# 1 Corinthians 14:9 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
In this verse, commentators emphasize the importance of clear and understandable speech. They highlight the contrast between speaking with the gift of tongues and speaking without being understood. The verse condemns the practice of speaking in a language not understood by the audience, exposing the futility of such communication. It emphasizes the need for plain, simple, and intelligible speech, as speaking without being understood is akin to speaking into empty space. Furthermore, the verse underscores the significance of effective communication in conveying the message and avoiding the loss of labor.
