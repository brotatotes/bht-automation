# 1 Corinthians 15:11 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
In this verse, the commentators highlight the unity and consistency in the preaching of the Gospel. It is not about individual identities or preferences, but about the message itself. The early Christian leaders, including Paul, proclaimed the same truths about Christ's death and resurrection. The emphasis is on the importance of unity in the body of Christ and the ongoing effort to share the Gospel with others. It also reminds us that our belief in these core truths is foundational to our faith and should be reflected in our actions.
