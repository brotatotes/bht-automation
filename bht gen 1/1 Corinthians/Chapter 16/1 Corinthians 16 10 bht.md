## BHT:
In this Bible verse, the apostle Paul urges the Corinthians to warmly receive Timothy, protecting him from any harm or persecution. Timothy's youth and potentially timid disposition might have made him vulnerable to being discredited, but Paul insists that he is deserving of their confidence and support. Timothy works diligently in the service of the Lord, faithfully preaching the same Gospel as Paul, and thus should be esteemed and welcomed by the Corinthians. This passage emphasizes the importance of valuing and upholding the ministry of pastors and ministers in the Church.
