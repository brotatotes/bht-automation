## BHT:
This verse concludes a severe Epistle with an expression of affection and love. The love mentioned here is not human or fleshly, but spiritual and in Christ Jesus. It is a love that embraces and extends to all believers, regardless of status or size, and is rooted in the grace and image of Christ. The author, Paul, desires and believes that this love will always be accepted by the Corinthians.
