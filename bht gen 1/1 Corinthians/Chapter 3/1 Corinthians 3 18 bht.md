# 1 Corinthians 3:18 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
Let no one deceive themselves with false hopes, for true wisdom comes from embracing the Gospel and denying worldly wisdom. The danger of self-deception lies in prioritizing the wisdom of the world over the wisdom of God. Therefore, let us become fools in the world's eyes in order to gain God's wisdom, recognizing that our worth and understanding are ultimately determined by Him.
