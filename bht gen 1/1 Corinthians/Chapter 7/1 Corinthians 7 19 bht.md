# 1 Corinthians 7:19 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
In this Bible verse, commentators discuss the insignificance of external rituals like circumcision and emphasize the importance of focusing on God's commandments. They highlight that circumcision or uncircumcision alone does not impact salvation. Rather, what truly matters is obeying God's commandments and having faith that is expressed through love towards others. This verse emphasizes that true righteousness is not achieved through external appearances or rituals, but through a genuine inward transformation and a life lived in accordance with God's instructions.
