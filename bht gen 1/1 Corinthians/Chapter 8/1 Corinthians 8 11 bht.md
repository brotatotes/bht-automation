# 1 Corinthians 8:11 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
In this verse, the commentators emphasize the responsibility of those with knowledge to consider the impact of their actions on weaker brothers in faith. They warn against using knowledge in a way that could cause harm or lead others astray. The irony is highlighted of valuing personal preferences over the well-being of others, especially when considering the immense sacrifice Christ made for all. It serves as a reminder of the inclusive nature of Christ's death and the importance of extending love and compassion to all, regardless of their response.
