# 1 Corinthians 9:1 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
In this verse, the writer expresses his independence and apostolic authority, firmly establishing his legitimacy as a messenger of God. He asserts that his encounter with Jesus Christ lends credibility to his apostleship, serving as a witness to the truth of the Gospel. The verse also highlights the transformative impact of his ministry, as he claims the Corinthians as his own work in the Lord, a testament to the effectiveness of his teaching and the power of God's work through him.
