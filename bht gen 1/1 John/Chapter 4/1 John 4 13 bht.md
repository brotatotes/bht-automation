# 1 John 4:13 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
In this verse, we are reminded of the intimate relationship between God and believers. The presence of the Holy Spirit within us is a testimony to the fact that God dwells in us and that we abide in Him. The Spirit's presence is evidenced by the fruits of love, and His indwelling gives us a profound assurance of our fellowship and relationship with God. Through the Spirit's influence, we experience communion with God and exercise faith and love towards Him, thus experiencing His love and grace in return.
