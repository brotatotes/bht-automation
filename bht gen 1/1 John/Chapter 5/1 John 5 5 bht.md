# 1 John 5:5 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
In this Bible verse, believers are described as triumphantly overcoming the world through their union with Jesus Christ, the Son of God. Their victory is not reliant on themselves or any other mere creature or thing, but solely on their faith and trust in Christ. This conquering of the world is not simply a one-time event, but a continual way of life, characterized by perseverance and a deep apprehension of Christ's power. The verse emphasizes the contrasts between Jesus as fully human and fully divine, and underscores the significance of experiencing this victory through genuine faith in Christ alone.
