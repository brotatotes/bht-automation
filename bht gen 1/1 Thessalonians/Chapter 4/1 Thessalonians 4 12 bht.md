# 1 Thessalonians 4:12 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
In this verse, believers are encouraged to conduct themselves honorably and to live in a way that brings credit and respect to Christianity in the eyes of the unbelieving world. They are instructed to provide for themselves and to be self-sufficient, not depending on others, while also being generous and ready to meet the needs of others. The verse highlights the importance of living in a manner that upholds the reputation of Christianity, dispels negative assumptions, and meets both personal and communal needs.

### Albert Barnes:

### Philip Schaff:

3. "A decorous and irreproachable demeanor, excellence in the virtues which the world acknowledges, diligence in the public service, these things commend the religion which enjoins them."

3. "People outside the churches have a right to watch the conduct of professing Christians" - The commentary reminds us that our behavior as Christians is not only observed by fellow believers but also by those outside the church, emphasizing the need for integrity in all our actions.
