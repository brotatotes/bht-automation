## BHT:
In this verse, the commentators emphasize the nature of God's call and its purpose in the lives of believers. They highlight that God's call is not a call to live in impurity or engage in sinful behavior, but rather a call to holiness and a life pleasing to God. They stress the importance of understanding and fulfilling the purposes of God's call, and the need to walk worthy of this calling. By emphasizing the contrast between God's holy call and the impurity of the world, the verse urges believers to live a life of moral purity and godliness.
