# 1 Timothy 1:8 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
The verse being described is a reminder that the law, when used lawfully, serves a rightful purpose. It is acknowledged that the law is good, morally excellent, and in agreement with God's holiness. Its proper use is to awaken the sense of sin in the ungodly and guide believers in righteous living. By pointing to Christ, even the ceremonial law finds its purpose. In essence, the law is a means to testify against sins and lead individuals to repentance and holiness.
