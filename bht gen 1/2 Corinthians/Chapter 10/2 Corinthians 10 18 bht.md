# 2 Corinthians 10:18 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
In this verse, it is clear that one's self-commendation holds no weight. Instead, what truly matters is being commended by the Lord. Seeking approval from God rather than relying on self-praise is emphasized. The person who is approved by the Lord is genuine, trustworthy, and accepted in His sight, indicating a high level of honor and commendation. This verse serves as a reminder that our worth and validation come from God alone, not from the accolades of others.
