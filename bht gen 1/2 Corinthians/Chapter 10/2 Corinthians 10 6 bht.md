# 2 Corinthians 10:6 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
This Bible verse speaks about the apostolic authority granted to the writer to enforce discipline against the disobedient. The writer, having the power to punish, waits for every opportunity for the disobedient to repent and join the ranks of the obedient. The verse emphasizes the readiness to take action against rebellion, while also recognizing the need for a gentler approach to bring about obedience before resorting to punishment. It highlights the distinction between the faithful and the rebellious, with the former being the writer's first concern.
