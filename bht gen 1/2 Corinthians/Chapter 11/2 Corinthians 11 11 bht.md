# 2 Corinthians 11:11 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
In this verse, the Apostle Paul defends his decision to not accept any material support from the Corinthians. He refutes the notion that his refusal is due to a lack of love for them, emphasizing that love is not measured by what one receives, but by what one gives. He reassures the Corinthians that his love for them is selfless and not contingent on receiving anything in return. Paul appeals to God's omniscience, acknowledging that God knows the sincerity of his love and the deeper reasons behind his decision.
