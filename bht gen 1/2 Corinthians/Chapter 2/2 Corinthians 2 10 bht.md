## BHT:
In this verse, the commentator makes it clear that forgiveness is being extended to a specific individual, emphasizing the personal and intimate nature of Christ's involvement. It is highlighted that forgiveness is not only for the sake of the offender but also for the benefit and well-being of the church. The verse also emphasizes the authority and confidence of the speaker in granting forgiveness. Ultimately, the commentator assures the readers that forgiveness is not a hesitation but a definite and confirmed act.
