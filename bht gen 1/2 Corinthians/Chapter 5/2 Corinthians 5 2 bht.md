# 2 Corinthians 5:2 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
In this verse, Paul expresses his earnest desire to be "clothed upon" with a heavenly body, beyond the limitations and sufferings of his current earthly body. The commentators highlight the transformation and heavenly origin of this new habitation. They explain that the glorified body is compared to a house or clothing, representing a spiritual dwelling place that is incorruptible and immortal. This desire, guided by the Spirit, reflects the longing for heavenly glory and the ultimate union of the soul with its perfected form.
