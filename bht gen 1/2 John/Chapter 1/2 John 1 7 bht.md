# 2 John 7 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
The verse being described is a warning against deceivers who deny the truth of Jesus Christ's incarnation. These deceivers, often bearing the Christian name, maliciously seek to lead others astray by denying key aspects of Christ's nature. It is crucial to hold fast to the teachings that have been passed down and to acknowledge Jesus as the incarnate Son of God. Those who deny this truth are not only deceivers but are also actively opposing the mission of Christ.
