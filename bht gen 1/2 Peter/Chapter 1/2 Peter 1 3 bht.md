# 2 Peter 1:3 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
This verse speaks of the comprehensive provision that God has given to believers for both their earthly and spiritual lives. It emphasizes the divine power and grace that enables believers to live a godly life. The verse also highlights the importance of diligently pursuing faith, knowledge, and the personal knowledge of Christ. The passage indicates that believers have been granted all things necessary for life and godliness, with the recognition that the source of these blessings is Christ Himself.

### Albert Barnes:
1. "All the effects of the gospel on the human heart are, in the Scriptures, traced to the power of God."
2. "The phrase 'according as' seems to be connected with the sentence...we are bound...to give all diligence to add to our faith, knowledge, etc."
3. "By a proper acquaintance with him, or by the right kind of knowledge of him."

These quotes highlight the power of God in the gospel, the importance of diligence in pursuing faith and knowledge, and the significance of knowing God personally.
