## BHT:
In this verse, the commentators paint a vivid picture of individuals who deviate from the path of righteousness. They describe these individuals as resembling irrational animals, exhibiting destructive passions, and engaging in blasphemous speech. These individuals are portrayed as destined for destruction, lacking the ability to understand and appreciate higher truths. The commentators emphasize the contrast between these individuals and the angels, highlighting the moral and spiritual depths to which they have sunk. Overall, the verse provides a stark warning against succumbing to base instincts and the consequences that await those who do so.
