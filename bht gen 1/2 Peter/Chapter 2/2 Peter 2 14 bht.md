1. "having eyes full of an adulteress" - implying that their eyes are constantly filled with lust and desire, like that of an adulteress. This highlights their immoral and sinful nature.

2. "laying baits for unstable souls" - suggesting that they actively set traps and temptations to ensnare those who are easily swayed or lacking in firm faith. This emphasizes their manipulative and deceitful behavior.
