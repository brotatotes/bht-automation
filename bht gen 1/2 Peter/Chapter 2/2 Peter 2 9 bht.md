## BHT:
This verse speaks of the Lord's omniscience and ability to rescue the faithful from temptation. It emphasizes God's understanding of the best way to deliver his people, even when they cannot see a way out. The verse also highlights God's justice, as he reserves punishment for the unrighteous until the day of judgment. In his wisdom, God knows how to protect and deliver the godly, ensuring that they will not be overwhelmed by temptation.
