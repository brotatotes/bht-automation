## BHT:
In this Bible verse, the commentators shed light on the commendation and boasting of the Thessalonians' faith. Their enduring faith in the midst of persecutions and afflictions is highlighted as a testimony to the power and impact of the gospel. The concept of boasting is emphasized, indicating that their faith serves as a pattern for other churches, inspiring and encouraging them. Patience and unwavering confidence in God are identified as fruits of their faith, sustaining them in tribulations. Endurance, as an active choice, holds the ultimate goal of being counted worthy of the kingdom of God.

### Marvin Vincent:

2. "The simple verb kaucasqai to boast": Paul frequently uses this verb to emphasize the act of boasting, highlighting the importance of acknowledging and proclaiming one's accomplishments or blessings.
3. "The kindred nouns kauchma ground of boasting, and kauchsiv act of boasting": These related words further emphasize the concept of boasting, indicating that there are specific reasons or actions that serve as the basis for one's pride and exultation.
