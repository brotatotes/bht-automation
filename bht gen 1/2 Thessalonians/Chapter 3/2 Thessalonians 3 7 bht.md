# 2 Thessalonians 3:7 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
In this verse, the apostle Paul calls the Thessalonians to imitate his example of living. He emphasizes the importance of following in the footsteps of those who are faithfully following God's Word. Paul specifically highlights the need to avoid disorderly behavior and idleness, highlighting the value of hard work, dedication, and a productive lifestyle. By imitating the apostles and their way of life, the Thessalonians are encouraged to walk in a manner that reflects their commitment to God and others.
