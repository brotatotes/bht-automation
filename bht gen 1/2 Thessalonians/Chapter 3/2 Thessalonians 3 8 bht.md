# 2 Thessalonians 3:8 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
In this verse, the speakers emphasize their self-sufficiency and independence. They did not rely on others for their sustenance, as they bought their own food and worked hard to support themselves. Their actions were in stark contrast to false teachers who sought personal gain. The phrase "eat bread" is used to represent the act of sustaining themselves through their own efforts. Overall, the verse portrays the apostles' dedication to spreading the Gospel without burdening others or seeking financial gain.
