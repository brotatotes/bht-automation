### John Gill:
1. "For his name's sake they went forth" - The disciples were driven out of Judea for professing the name of Christ, showing their dedication to spreading the Gospel.

3. "Lest it should be thought they sought their own worldly interest" - The disciples were mindful of not giving any opportunity for others to question their intentions or hinder the progress of the Gospel by seeking personal gain.
