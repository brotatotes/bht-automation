# Acts 10:12 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
In this vision, it appeared to Peter as if all of creation was gathered together, symbolizing the breaking down of barriers and the inclusion of all creatures in God's plan of salvation. The mixing of clean and unclean animals challenged traditional understanding and highlighted the radical inclusivity of God's kingdom. This divine revelation demonstrated that the distinctions and differences that had existed before were now done away with and that all nations, Jews and Gentiles alike, were welcome in the church of Christ.
