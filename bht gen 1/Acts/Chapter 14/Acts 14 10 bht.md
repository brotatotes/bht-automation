## BHT:
In this powerful biblical account, a man who had been lame from birth finds himself in the midst of a life-altering moment. With a loud voice, the apostle Paul speaks, capturing the attention of all who are present. ### Jamieson Fausset Brown:
1. "A certain man, a cripple from his mother's womb, heard Paul speak." This highlights the miraculous nature of the healing that is about to take place. The man's condition from birth emphasizes the severity of his disability and sets the stage for the extraordinary power of God to be displayed through Paul's ministry.

2. "The same heard Paul speak—in the open air and to a crowd of people." This emphasizes the public nature of the healing and the impact it had on the surrounding audience. The healing was not done in secret but was witnessed by many, further validating the power and authority of Paul's message.

3. "The same heard Paul speak." This simple statement underscores the importance of hearing and receiving the word of God. The man's physical healing was a result of his willingness to listen and respond to Paul's message. It serves as a reminder that faith comes by hearing, and it is through hearing the word that transformation and miracles can occur.

### Albert Barnes:
1. "Said with a loud voice" - This emphasizes the authority and power behind the words spoken by the individual. It signifies a strong and commanding presence.
2. "And he leaped" - This suggests a sudden and joyful movement, indicating a miraculous healing or restoration of physical ability.
3. "Compare Isaiah 35:6" - This reference to Isaiah 35:6 highlights the connection between the leaping mentioned in the verse and the prophecy of the future restoration of the physically impaired. It adds a deeper layer of significance to the event.
