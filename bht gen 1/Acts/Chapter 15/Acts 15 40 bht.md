### Albert Barnes:
1. "Being recommended" refers to the act of entrusting oneself or someone else to God's care through prayer. It is a way of acknowledging our dependence on God and seeking His guidance and protection.

2. This act of commendation is not just a casual request, but a deliberate and intentional act of faith. It demonstrates our trust in God's sovereignty and His ability to work in and through our lives.

3. The reference to Acts 14:26 reminds us of the example set by the early Christians, who consistently committed themselves and their endeavors to God's care through prayer. It serves as a reminder for us to follow their example and seek God's guidance and blessing in all that we do.
