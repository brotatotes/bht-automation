## BHT:
In this Bible verse, the jailer, responsible for the prisoners' safekeeping, is shaken awake by an earthquake. Thinking that the prisoners had escaped, he is ready to end his own life to avoid the consequences. This act shows the severity of the punishment he would face. It is important to note the historical context in which suicide was viewed as a means of escape from trouble. This verse serves as a reminder of the weight of responsibility and the desperate measures one might consider.
