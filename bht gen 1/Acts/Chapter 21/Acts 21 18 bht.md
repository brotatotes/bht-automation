# Acts 21:18 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
In this passage, we see that James, the brother of the Lord, played a significant role in the early Christian community as the president of the church at Jerusalem. His leadership is evident through his prominence in the book of Acts, as well as in Paul's letters. Paul's visit to James and the elders further highlights the respect and unity within the early church, while the absence of other apostles emphasizes Paul's distinct mission and ministry. Overall, James, known as the Lord's brother, held a position of authority and respect among the early disciples.
