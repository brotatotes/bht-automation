# Acts 25:24 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
In this Bible verse, the shift from chaos to order is highlighted as the Jews, both in Jerusalem and Caesarea, gather in a more organized and official representation before the authorities. The verse reflects the impact and attention that Paul, the prisoner, had gained, as the influential men express their curiosity and desire to personally meet and hear him. The tumultuous outcry of the Jewish multitude is described as they strongly urge for Paul's condemnation, demanding with loud voices that he should not live any longer.

3. "‘And all here’ speaks for the unanimity of the Cæsarean Jews in this matter with their Jerusalem brethren."
