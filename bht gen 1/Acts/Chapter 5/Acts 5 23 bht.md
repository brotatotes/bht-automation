# Acts 5:23 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
In this verse, we see the miraculous escape of the apostles from a securely locked and guarded prison. The closed doors and presence of vigilant guards emphasize the impossibility of their escape. The astonishment of the guards upon discovering the empty prison highlights the supernatural intervention of God. This event serves as a powerful demonstration of God's extraordinary power and His ability to defy natural laws.
