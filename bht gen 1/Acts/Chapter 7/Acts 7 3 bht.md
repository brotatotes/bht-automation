# Acts 7:3 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
In this verse, we see Abraham's act of simple confidence in God, illustrating what man is always required to do at the command of God. Abraham's obedience, even in leaving his hometown, demonstrates his trust in God's promise. This serves as an emblem of the calling of the saints out of the world, emphasizing the importance of trust and obedience in our relationship with God. It is a powerful reminder that sometimes God calls us to step out into the unknown, relying on His guidance and provision as we follow Him on our journey.
