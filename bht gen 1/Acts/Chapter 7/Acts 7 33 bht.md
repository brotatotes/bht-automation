# Acts 7:33 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
In this verse, the commentators emphasize the act of putting off shoes as a mark of reverence and humility. They explain that this act was common in ancient times to show respect in the presence of a superior or in holy places. The commentators also emphasize that the ground itself becomes holy as a result of the divine presence. This verse reminds us to approach sacred spaces with reverence in our hearts and with external signs of veneration.
