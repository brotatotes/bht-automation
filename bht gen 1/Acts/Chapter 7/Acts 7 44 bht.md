# Acts 7:44 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
The verse describes a tabernacle that serves as a witness of God's presence and the proper way of worship. This portable temple carried the testimony or declaration of God's will, symbolized by the tablets of the Ten Commandments in the ark. The tabernacle, according to the divine pattern revealed to Moses, showcased the sanctity and divine approval. It contrasts with idolatry, highlighting the importance of faithfully following God's appointed form of worship.
