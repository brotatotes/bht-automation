# Acts 9:21 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
In a surprising and dramatic turn of events, a man known for his zealous persecution of the church experiences a sudden change of heart. The commentators remark on the astonishment and wonder of those who witness this transformation. The man, previously intent on laying waste and destroying the church, now preaches the name of Christ passionately, leaving the observers in awe. The power of this conversion is emphasized as it defies expectations and signifies a divine intervention.
