## BHT:
In this Bible verse, a man who had been paralyzed for eight years is healed by Jesus Christ through the ministry of Peter. The emphasis is on the immediacy and urgency of the healing, as well as the power and authority of Jesus in bringing about restoration. Peter, realizing that the healing is not his own doing but a work of Christ, instructs the man to make his own bed as a sign of his complete restoration. This miraculous healing serves as a testimony to the divine intervention and the glory of Jesus as the Messiah.
