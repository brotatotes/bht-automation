# Ephesians 3:14 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
In this passage, the apostle is seen humbly bowing his knees in prayer, expressing his earnest desire for the perseverance of the saints. This serves as a reminder for believers to also pray for themselves and to approach God with a heart of humility and sincerity, rather than focusing solely on the outward posture. The emphasis lies not on the physical act, but on the genuine heart attitude in communion with God.
