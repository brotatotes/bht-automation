# Ephesians 5:33 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
In this passage, we are reminded of the profound responsibility that both husbands and wives have in a marriage. The husband is called to love his wife as he loves himself, recognizing the deep unity they share as one flesh. Meanwhile, the wife is encouraged to reverence her husband, understanding the importance of willing submission within the marriage relationship. These instructions apply to every individual within a marriage, emphasizing the significance of mutual love, respect, and obedience in building a strong and harmonious bond.
