# Ephesians 6:23 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
This verse speaks of a peace that goes beyond mere tranquility, instead being a deep sense of well-being bestowed by God. Love is intimately connected with faith, as both are regarded as essential components of the believer's life. Peace, love, and faith all find their source in God the Father and the Lord Jesus Christ, demonstrating the divine origins of these blessings. This verse encourages believers to pursue peace, love, and faith, continuously seeking their growth and manifestation in their lives.
