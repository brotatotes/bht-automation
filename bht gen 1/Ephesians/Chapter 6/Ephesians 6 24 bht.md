## BHT:
This verse highlights the significance of having a love for the Lord Jesus Christ that is pure, incorruptible, and eternal. It contrasts the temporary and corruptible things of the world with the enduring qualities of a true believer. It serves as the culmination of the Epistle, emphasizing the importance of this sincere and incorruptible love. This love for Christ should be genuine and untainted by corruption, moving in a sphere that is perennial and immutable. The grace of God is necessary to produce and sustain such an incorruptible love, which will abide forever.
