## BHT:
This Bible verse describes the vivid portrayal of Jesus Christ being crucified and its impact on the Galatians. Through faithful preaching, the image of Jesus on the cross is presented before them, serving as a powerful reminder of his sacrifice. The commentator emphasizes the bewilderment and astonishment at the Galatians' tendency to turn away from the freedom of the gospel and fall back into the bondage of the law.
