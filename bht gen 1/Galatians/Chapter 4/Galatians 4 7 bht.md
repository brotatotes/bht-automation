# Galatians 4:7 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
Through faith in Christ, believers are no longer bound as servants under the law but are adopted into God's family as His children. They are heirs of all the promises and enjoy a relationship with the Triune God. This verse emphasizes the personal nature of this truth, inviting those who believe in Christ to embrace their identity as children of God and heirs of His blessings.
