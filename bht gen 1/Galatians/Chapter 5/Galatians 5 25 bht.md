# Galatians 5:25 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
In this verse, the commentators highlight the significance of living in and being guided by the Holy Spirit. They emphasize that our spiritual life depends on the Spirit's influence and that our actions should align with this inner life. The exhortation is to yield wholly to the agency of the Holy Spirit, allowing Him to govern our conduct and manifest Himself through our outward effects. It is a call to intentional and continuous commitment, walking in accordance with the Spirit's guidance and allowing our actions to reflect the life of the Spirit within us.
