## BHT:
In this verse, the commentators emphasize that God cannot be deceived or ridiculed by human actions. Our works, whether good or bad, are seeds sown for eternity, and God will judge us accordingly. The verse warns against making excuses for withholding what is due to others or trying to reap different results than what we have sown. It ultimately reminds us of the futility and foolishness of mocking God, highlighting the importance of recognizing the consequences of our actions.
