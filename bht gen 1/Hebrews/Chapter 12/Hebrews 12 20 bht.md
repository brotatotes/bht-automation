## BHT:
The verse describes a moment when the people were unable to bear the stern and terrifying commands given to them. The severity of the command is emphasized, as even the touch of the mountain was punished with stoning. The commentators discuss the consequences of disobedience, the sacredness of the object being touched, and the inability of the people to endure the weight of the law's demands. This verse serves as a reminder of the human limitations and the need for grace and redemption.
