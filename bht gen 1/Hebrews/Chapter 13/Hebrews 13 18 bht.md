## BHT:
The writer of this verse humbly requests the prayers of the readers, acknowledging the weight and importance of their role as guides and governors in the ministry. They express their confidence in having a good conscience, one that is sanctified by the Spirit of God and focused on the upright discharge of their ministerial duties.
