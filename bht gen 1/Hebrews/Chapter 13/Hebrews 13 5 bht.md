## BHT:
In this verse, believers are urged to live a life free from the love of money, content with what they currently have. The commentators emphasize the danger of covetousness, considering it a great evil that hinders true religion. They highlight the importance of upright conduct, both in personal and public life, and emphasize the need for believers to generously communicate and provide for their families.
