## BHT:
In this verse, the commentators emphasize the dangerous nature of an evil heart of unbelief. They warn against the rebellion and disobedience that can arise from it, and highlight the need to guard against losing faith in God. They describe unbelief as the root cause of all evil and emphasize the dishonor it brings to God. Departing from God and yielding to unbelief can lead one astray from the source of life, holiness, and happiness. The verse serves as a solemn warning to remain steadfast in faith and trust in the living God.
