### Archibald T Robertson:
1. "Let us therefore give diligence...to be eager and alert." 
2. "The exhortation has a warning...that no man fall." 
3. "The unbelief is like that seen in the Israelites...the Israelites set a terrible example and it is so easy to copy the bad examples."

### John Gill:
1. "Not eternal rest; this is not to be entered into now; nor is an entrance into it to be obtained by labour; salvation is not by works; eternal life is a free gift; good works do not go before to prepare heaven for the saints, but follow after."
2. "But the Gospel rest is here meant, that rest which believers now enter into, and is at this present time for them."
3. "And this is to be laboured for by prayer, hearing the word, and attendance on ordinances; and this requires strength, diligence, and industry; and supposes difficulties and discouragements, through the corruptions of the heart, and the temptations of Satan."

### John Wesley:

3. "The warning against falling serves as a reminder that even those who are firmly rooted in their faith should not become complacent or overconfident, but should continually strive to grow and mature in their relationship with God."
