### Jamieson-Fausset-Brown:

3. "Not agreeably to a statute is Christ appointed, but according to an inward living power."

### Albert Barnes:
1. "A strong proof of the necessity of such a change of the priesthood was furnished from the fact that the Messiah was to be of the tribe of Judah."
2. "But a much stronger, because 'as a priest' he was to be of the order of Melchizedek - that is, he was of the same rank with one who did not even belong to that tribe."
3. "Resembling; that is, he was to be of the order of Melchizedek."
