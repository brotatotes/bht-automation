# John 10:17 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
In this remarkable verse, lies the mystery of the love of the Father for the Son. The Son, in his ultimate act of love for the Father, willingly lays down his life to accomplish his great purposes of benevolence. Through his sacrificial death and resurrection, Jesus demonstrates the inseparable bond between the Father and the Son, and the Father's deep love for humanity. This act of selfless love not only atones for the sins of humanity but also provides a way for their redemption and justification.
