## BHT:
In this verse, we witness the significance of belief, as Jesus rejoices in the opportunity for the disciples to strengthen their faith. The commentators emphasize the divine power of Jesus, highlighting how the presence of the Prince of Life prevents death from reaching those in His midst. The deep bond between Jesus and Lazarus transcends even death, as the Lord maintains a personal relationship with His friend.
