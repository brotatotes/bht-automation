## BHT:
In this Bible verse, a man who had been dead came forth from his tomb, still bound in grave clothes. The commentators describe how Lazarus was tightly wrapped in bandages, his limbs constrained, and his face covered with a napkin. This vividly depicts the physical state of Lazarus, emphasizing the miracle of his resurrection. The commentators also highlight the contrast between Lazarus' entombment and his subsequent emergence, underscoring the power of the resurrection and the remarkable nature of this event.
