### John Calvin:
1. "His commandment is eternal life." - This highlights the importance of following God's commandments, as they lead to eternal life. It emphasizes the eternal nature of God's teachings and the significance of obedience to them.

3. "Wicked men should feel the vengeance of God." - This statement implies that those who reject God and His commandments will face the consequences of their actions. 3. "The Gospel... is a means of quickening sinners with a spiritual life, which issues in an eternal one; it is the savour of life unto life, and the Spirit which giveth life, and is the ministration of it."
