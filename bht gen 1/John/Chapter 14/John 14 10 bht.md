## BHT:
In this Bible verse, commentators highlight the profound relationship between Jesus and God the Father. They emphasize the unity and oneness shared by the Father and Son, with Jesus being the perfect reflection and revelation of the Father. Both Jesus' words and works bear the divine imprint of the Father, underscoring the inseparable bond between them. This verse showcases the deep connection and unity in nature between Jesus and God, affirming Jesus' divine nature and the authority and truth of his teachings.

### Marvin Vincent:
1. "The Father abiding in me doeth His works." - This quote highlights the close relationship between Jesus and the Father, emphasizing that the works Jesus performs are not of his own accord but are a result of the Father's presence within him.

3. "His works are the works of the Father abiding in Him; therefore the Father is in Him." - This quote summarizes the previous points by stating that the works performed by Jesus are a direct result of the Father's presence within him, thus affirming the indwelling of the Father in Jesus.
