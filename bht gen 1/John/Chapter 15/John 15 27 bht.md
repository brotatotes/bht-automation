The verse under consideration speaks of a unified witness of the Spirit through believers, who are called to bear witness to the truth of Jesus as the Messiah. These witnesses, chosen and taught by the Holy Spirit, have acquired qualifications for their role through their experiences with Jesus. Through firsthand accounts of His life, teachings, sufferings, death, resurrection, and ascension, they are equipped to testify to His person, doctrines, and works. 1. "You shall be witnesses to the world to urge on them the evidences that the Lord Jesus is the Messiah." This quote highlights the role of believers in testifying to the truth of Jesus as the Messiah and sharing the evidence of his life and teachings.

2. "They had for more than three years seen his works, and were therefore qualified to bear witness of his character and doctrines." This quote emphasizes the importance of firsthand experience in being able to effectively bear witness to Jesus' character and teachings.

3. "From the beginning - From his entrance on the public work of the ministry." This quote points out that the disciples were witnesses from the very beginning of Jesus' ministry, indicating their unique perspective and insight into his mission and message.

3. "They were chosen to be with Christ": The disciples were specifically chosen by Jesus to be with Him and witness His ministry firsthand. This emphasizes the significance of being in close relationship with Christ and having a deep understanding of His teachings.
