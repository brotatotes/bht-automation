# John 16:15 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
In this verse, we are given a glimpse into the essential relations of the Blessed Trinity. The Son glorifies the Father by revealing Him to us, showcasing the intimacy and unity between them. The Son, being equal with the Father, actively and intentionally receives all things from Him. This verse provides evidence for the orthodox doctrine of the Holy Trinity and highlights the eternal connection between the Son and the Spirit. Through the Spirit's revelation, we are guided into a deeper understanding of the truth about the Father, ultimately leading us to the blessings and doctrines of grace.
