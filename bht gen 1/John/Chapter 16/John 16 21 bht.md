# John 16:21 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
The verse describes a profound and universal experience, likened to the birth of a child, bringing both pain and joy. The woman represents all of humanity, experiencing birth pangs of anguish that lead to the newness of life. Through the anguish and sorrow, a renewal of hope and eternal joy is possible. This passage emphasizes the transformative power of pain, where sorrow gives way to joy and all anxieties dissolve. It serves as a reminder that challenges and suffering can lead to a deeper understanding and appreciation of life's blessings.
