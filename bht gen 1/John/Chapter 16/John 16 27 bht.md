### Albert Barnes:
1. "To love Jesus is to obey Him, and to obey Him is to love Him. The two are inseparable and interdependent."

### John Calvin:
1. "The only bond of our union with God is to be united to Christ; and we are united to him by a faith which is not reigned, but which springs from sincere affection, which he describes by the name of love."

3. "God loves men in a secret way, before they are called, if they are among the elect; for he loves his own before they are created; but, as they are not yet reconciled, they are justly accounted enemies of God."
