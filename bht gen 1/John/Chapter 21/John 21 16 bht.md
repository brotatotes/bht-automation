# John 21:16 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
In this Bible verse, Jesus examines Peter's love for Him, asking him if he truly loves Him. Jesus then instructs Peter to take care of His followers and nurture the younger members of the flock. The repetition of Peter's love and his humility in response highlight the sincerity and depth of his devotion. This verse emphasizes the importance of love, care, and guidance in shepherding God's people.
