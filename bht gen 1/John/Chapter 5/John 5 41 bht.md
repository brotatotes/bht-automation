## BHT:
In this verse, Jesus emphasizes that he does not seek or receive honor from men. His worth and glory come from a different source, beyond the opinions and recognition of humans. While some may strive for earthly recognition, Jesus is motivated by a higher calling and finds his true glory in his loving fellowship with God. He is not concerned with seeking the applause or honor of others, but rather embodies a selflessness that is focused on fulfilling his divine mission and seeking the glory of his Father.
