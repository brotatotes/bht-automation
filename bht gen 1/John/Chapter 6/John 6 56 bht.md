# John 6:56 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
In this verse, the intimate connection between believers and Christ is emphasized. Through abiding in Him, believers find sustenance, guidance, and strength. This mutual indweling manifests in a deep spiritual union, where Christ becomes a part of believers, transforming them from within. The focus lies not on external rituals, but on the continual mystical fellowship and communion with Christ. Dwelling in Him means remaining in the belief of His doctrine and participating in the benefits of His death. It is through faith and the reception of His Spirit that believers become His habitation, with Christ dwelling in their hearts.
