# John 6:61 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
This verse describes Jesus' divine knowledge and self-sufficiency. He possesses omniscience within Himself, not needing external sources for wisdom. His question challenges the listeners' ability to accept his teachings and highlights the importance of being receptive to spiritual truths. It reminds us to be mindful of our actions and words, which can either build others up or cause them to stumble. Despite any initial doubts or difficulties in understanding, we can seek guidance from Jesus himself to find clarity and resolve.
