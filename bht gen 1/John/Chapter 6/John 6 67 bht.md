# John 6:67 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
In this verse, Jesus poses a question to his disciples, seeking their unwavering commitment amidst the desertion of others. As commentators suggest, Jesus asks this question knowing the response he expects, reflecting his reliance on their faith and attachment. Although some may have shown sympathy towards those leaving or felt restlessness, Jesus reassures his loyal disciples that their steadfastness is greatly valued, contrasting their unwavering devotion with the departure of others. Jesus' concern for their response displays his tender regard and strong affection, emphasizing the voluntary nature of their discipleship.
