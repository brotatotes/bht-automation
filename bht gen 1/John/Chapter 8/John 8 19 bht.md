### Marvin Vincent:
1. "The phrase 'where' indicates that the people were seeking physical evidence or a tangible sign to validate their belief. They were not content with the testimony of an unseen and unheard witness."
2. "This verse highlights the human tendency to rely on empirical evidence rather than faith. It reveals the struggle to trust in something intangible and unseen."
3. "The desire for concrete proof is a common challenge faced by believers throughout history. It reminds us of the importance of cultivating a strong faith that can withstand the temptation to seek constant validation."
