# John 9:27 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
This verse captures the ironic exchange between the Pharisees and a man who was healed by Jesus. The Pharisees, who had resisted Jesus, were questioned by the man if they, too, desired to become Jesus' disciples. The passage reveals the stubbornness of the Pharisees, their unwillingness to believe the man's account, and their deep-seated opposition to Jesus. It also showcases the all-encompassing invitation to discipleship, demonstrating that anyone can choose to follow Jesus, regardless of their background or previous beliefs.
