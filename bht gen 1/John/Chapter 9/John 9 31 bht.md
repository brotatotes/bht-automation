# John 9:31 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
In this Bible verse, commentators elaborate on the concept that God does not hear sinners, emphasizing that this refers to impenitent sinners and that being a worshipper of God is a prerequisite to be heard. They point out that Jesus, being sinless, is not excluded from God's hearing. Moreover, they stress the importance of sincerity, truth, and obedience in prayer, highlighting that God hears those who cry to Him, repentant sinners, and those who do His will. The verse examines the distinction between mere lip service and genuine worship.
