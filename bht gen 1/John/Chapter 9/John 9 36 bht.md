## BHT:
This seeker, driven by an eagerness to believe in the Son of God, seeks further information and is ready to embrace him as soon as he is pointed out. Though still lacking clear knowledge, he demonstrates obedience, trust, and a genuine desire for instruction. With an attitude of respect and acknowledgement of Jesus' authority, this open-hearted individual pleads to know the one in whom they have fullest confidence, exemplifying the readiness to receive Jesus' words before fully understanding his identity. Faith precedes knowledge, and this follower, though still learning, is poised to receive the truth from Christ.

### Albert Barnes:
1. "He inquired, therefore, who the person was, or wished that he might be pointed out to him, that he might see him. This passage shows that he was disposed to believe, and had a strong desire to see and hear the Son of God."
2. "It is clear that the man did not know that it was the Lord Jesus that addressed him, and he therefore replied to him in the common language of respect, and asked him to point out to him the Son of God."
3. "The word translated 'Lord' here is rendered 'Sir' in John 4:11; John 20:15; John 12:21; Acts 16:30; Matthew 27:63. It should have been also here, and in many other places."

### Marvin Vincent:
