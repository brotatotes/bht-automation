Fear not, for you have found favor with God. The angel's proclamation of good tidings to Mary calms her alarm, assuring her of the eminent favor granted to her by God himself. In this moment, grace is not only poured into her lips but also expressed through the gracious words of Christ, embodying kindness and favor. This announcement of an inestimable blessing overrides any fear or confusion, highlighting the free, spontaneous, and absolute loving kindness of God towards humanity.
