## BHT:
Luke's Gospel was written with the purpose of providing Theophilus with a comprehensive understanding and certainty about the teachings and truths of Christianity. Luke wanted Theophilus to have a firm foundation and secure knowledge of the faith he had embraced, to be steadfast and secure against error. The Gospel serves as an assurance, strengthening faith, and proving the truth of the accounts heard by word of mouth, aiming to preserve Theophilus from doubt and defection.
