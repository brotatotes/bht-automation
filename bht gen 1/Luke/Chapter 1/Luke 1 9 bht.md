As part of his service, he would remove the ashes of the previous offering and make intercession for the people through prayer. ### Jamieson Fausset Brown:
1. "His lot was to burn incense" - The assignment of tasks to the priests was determined by lot, and Zacharias was chosen for the honorable duty of burning incense. This was considered the most distinguished part of the service.
2. "To remove the ashes of the former service" - One of the tasks of the priests involved clearing away the ashes from the previous service. This highlights the importance of maintaining cleanliness and order in the temple.
3. "To make intercession for the people" - While the incense was being burned, the priest would offer prayers on behalf of the people. This demonstrates the role of the priest as a mediator between God and the people, interceding on their behalf.
