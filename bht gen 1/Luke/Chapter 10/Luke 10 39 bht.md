## BHT:
In this verse, a woman named Mary is described as sitting at the feet of Jesus, showing her desire to learn and grow as his disciple. This posture of sitting at someone's feet was the ancient posture of learners or disciples. Mary's choice to sit at Jesus' feet symbolizes a posture of humility, submission, and a desire to learn from Him. Her act expresses great affection for Christ, her humble deportment, and close attention to His teachings.
