### Marvin Vincent:
1. "The familiar rendering, daily, which has prevailed uninterruptedly in the Western Church from the beginning, is a fairly adequate representation of the original." 
2. "The rendering in the margin of Rev. is, our bread for the coming day."
3. "If the prayer were said in the evening, no doubt it would mean the following day; but supposing it to be used before dawn, it would designate the day then breaking."

### Philip Schaff:
1. "This verse may be thus more exactly translated: ‘our sufficient (or needful) bread give us for the day.’" 
2. "The request for 'sufficient bread' acknowledges that our daily sustenance ultimately comes from God, and that we are dependent on Him for our basic needs." 
3. "By asking for bread 'for the day,' we are reminded of the importance of living in the present moment and trusting God to provide for our needs each day."
