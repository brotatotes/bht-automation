# Luke 11:39 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
This passage describes Jesus' confrontation with the Pharisees, exposing their hypocritical behavior. Although they appeared pious and adhered to religious laws, Jesus reveals their lack of inner purity and their hearts filled with greed and wickedness. This serves as a reminder that true righteousness goes beyond external appearances and requires a genuine transformation of the heart. Jesus challenges the Pharisees' emphasis on rituals, calling for a focus on true inner righteousness.
