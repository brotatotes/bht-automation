### Albert Barnes:
1. "By giving to the poor, you may show your repentance for your crimes in amassing money in this manner."
2. "They may show, by giving to others, that they do not think their gains acquired honestly, and that they truly repent."
3. "Thus may they show that they disapprove of their former conduct; and thus may be seen one great principle of God’s government - 'that good finally comes out of evil.'"

These quotes highlight the idea that giving to the poor can serve as a way to repent for past wrongdoings and demonstrate genuine remorse. It also emphasizes the transformative power of using ill-gotten wealth for good, showing that good can come out of evil actions.

### John Calvin:
