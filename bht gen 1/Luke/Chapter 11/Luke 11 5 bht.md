# Luke 11:5 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
In this verse, we see the power of prayer and the willingness of God to provide for His people. The commentators emphasize that even when faced with reluctance or seemingly unseasonable times, God is ready to listen and answer our prayers. The urgency and uncertainty in the request highlight the vulnerability and dependence of the person making the request. Through this passage, we are reminded that God is not bound by human limitations and can use our small offerings and talents to accomplish great things.

3. "Allegorical interpretations abound, but must be accepted with caution. A reference to the Bread of Life is most probable."
