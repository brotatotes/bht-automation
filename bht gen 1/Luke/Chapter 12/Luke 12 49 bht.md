2. "To spread the fire of heavenly love over all the earth" - Jesus' mission is not limited to a specific group or region, but encompasses the entire earth. His purpose is to ignite the fire of God's love in every corner of the world, bringing salvation and transformation to all who receive it.

3. "The fire of heavenly love" - This phrase emphasizes the divine nature of the love that Jesus brings. It is not a human love, but a love that comes from heaven itself. This love is pure, powerful, and transformative, capable of changing lives and bringing about the kingdom of God on earth.
