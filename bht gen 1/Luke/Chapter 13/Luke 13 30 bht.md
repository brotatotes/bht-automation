## BHT:
In this verse, the commentators emphasize the dramatic reversal of fortunes in God's kingdom. The first to believe, such as the Jews, may fall from their high place while the Gentiles, who were initially cast off, will obtain the first rank. It is a reminder that success and importance in God's kingdom are not based on worldly standards. The verse challenges our understanding of greatness and highlights the significance of humility and faithfulness.
