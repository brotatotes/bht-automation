### Albert Barnes:
1. "Tell that fox - A fox is an emblem of slyness, of cunning, and of artful mischief."
2. "Behold, I cast out devils ... - Announce to him the fact that I am working miracles in his territory, and that I shall continue to do it."
3. "Today and tomorrow - A little time. The words seem here to be used not strictly, but proverbially - to denote a short space of time."

### Marvin Vincent:
