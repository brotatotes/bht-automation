### Albert Barnes:
1. "None of those men - This cannot be understood as meaning that no 'Jews' would be saved, but that none of those who had 'treated him, in that manner' - none who had so decidedly rejected the offer of the gospel - would be saved."
2. "We may here see how dangerous it is 'once' to reject the gospel; how dangerous to grieve away the Holy Spirit."
3. "How often God forsakes forever the sinner who has been once awakened, and who grieves the Holy Spirit."
