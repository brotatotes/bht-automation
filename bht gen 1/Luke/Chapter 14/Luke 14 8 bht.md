# Luke 14:8 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
In this verse, commentators highlight the deeper meaning behind the imagery of a wedding feast. The mention of a wedding indicates the significance and joyousness of the occasion, symbolizing the abundance and generosity of God's provision in His kingdom. The presence of Jesus at the wedding signifies His approval and blessing on the institution of marriage, reminding us of His interest in every aspect of our lives. The verse also emphasizes the importance of humility, cautioning against seeking the highest places of honor and encouraging a posture of true humility.
