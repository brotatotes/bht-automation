## BHT:
In this Bible verse, commentators describe the seriousness of causing others to stumble in their faith and the lasting impact of our behavior and influence on others. It is emphasized that it is inadmissible and impossible to lead others astray, as this will lead to severe consequences. This serves as a solemn warning to protect and nurture the faith of those around us, being mindful of the stumbling blocks we may become. Woe unto those who cause others to stumble.

### Archibald T Robertson:
1. "It is impossible (ανενδεκτον εστιν). The meaning is inadmissible, unallowable."

3. "Τα σκανδαλα is simply the accusative of general reference. Literally, the not coming as to occasions of stumbling."
