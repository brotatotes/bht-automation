# Luke 18:15 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
In this verse, parents brought their infants and children to Jesus, seeking His touch and blessing. The commentators note the inclusion of both infants and young children, highlighting the tender age of the children and the power of physical touch. Jesus rebuked those who tried to prevent the children from coming to Him, expressing His love for the marginalized. By embracing and blessing the children, Jesus challenged societal norms and emphasized the significance of humility, innocence, and dependence on God. This encounter teaches us that the kingdom of God belongs to those who approach it with childlike faith and trust.
