## BHT:
In this Bible verse, a woman persistently seeks justice from a judge for her defenseless and oppressed situation. Her plea is not for personal revenge, but for deliverance from the burden of injustice. The justice of her cause is implied, as the judge is bound by his duty to do what is right.
