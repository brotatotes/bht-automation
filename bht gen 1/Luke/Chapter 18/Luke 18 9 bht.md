## BHT:
This verse describes individuals who arrogantly trusted in their own righteousness and disregarded others. They considered themselves to be blameless, relying on their own works, moral behavior, and religious practices. Their confidence stemmed from their ignorance of God's justice and the imperfection of their own hearts. This self-righteousness led them to despise and make nothing of others, displaying an attitude of arrogance and pride.
