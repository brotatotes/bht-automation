# Luke 19:11 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
In this passage, commentators highlight the misconceptions and expectations surrounding the kingdom of God. The disciples, influenced by their own carnal senses, anticipated a glorious and tangible kingdom that would bring them rest and happiness. However, Jesus had a different purpose in mind, aiming to correct their notion through a parable. The parable serves to refute the idea of an immediate and visible kingdom, emphasizing the need for faithful service and labor in the spiritual nature of the kingdom of God.
