In this verse, Jesus chooses to celebrate the Passover in a humble guest-chamber, highlighting his willingness to identify with the common people and serve all. The room, prepared by the owner of the house as an honor, symbolizes Jesus' invitation for all people to partake in the spiritual feast of salvation.
