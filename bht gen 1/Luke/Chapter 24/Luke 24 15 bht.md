# Luke 24:15 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
As the disciples communed and questioned each other about the truth and credibility of a recent report, Jesus himself drew near. He actively approached them, not only participating in their discussions but also walking alongside them. His intentional presence shows His desire to engage and journey with His followers, even in moments of doubt and uncertainty. Jesus seeks to establish a deep and meaningful connection with those who seek Him, meeting them in their questions and concerns.
