## BHT:
In this verse, we see the disciples compelling Jesus to stay with them through their persistent and urgent entreaties. Their genuine desire for His fellowship is evident as they passionately request His presence. Despite Jesus initially intending to continue His journey, the disciples' faith and compelling arguments convince Him to remain. This encounter reminds us of the power of fervent prayer and the compassionate response of Jesus to the heartfelt pleas of His followers.
