## BHT:
Led by the forceful and guiding hand of the Holy Spirit, Jesus was driven into the wilderness where he abstained from food. Throughout this intense conflict, Jesus wielded the Word of God under the guidance of the Spirit, remaining continuously under the Spirit's guidance and filled with supernatural power. In this uninhabited and dangerous place, Jesus was fortified against temptations, fully equipped and prepared for his ministry.
