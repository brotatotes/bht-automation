# Luke 5:17 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
In this passage, we see Jesus teaching amidst a crowd of people, which includes Pharisees and teachers of the law who are increasingly feeling the need to make a judgment about Him. The gathering is packed, with some authorities emphasizing that there was not even room at the door. Despite the opposition, the power of the Lord is present to heal both physical and spiritual ailments, drawing people from various villages and districts to seek His healing touch.

### Philip Schaff:
1. "Pharisees and teachers of the law. Peculiar to Luke; but the other Evangelists speak of the 'scribes' as objecting." 
2. "Out of every Village, etc. From all parts, not necessarily from each and every village."
3. "And the power of the Lord was with him to heal. Some authorities read: 'that he should heal them.' 'Lord' refers to God, although Luke often applies the term to our Lord."

### John Gill:
1. "There was not room for them, neither within the house, nor about the door." 
2. "They came from all parts to watch and observe him, and to take all opportunities and advantages against him."
3. "The power of the Lord was present to heal them."

These quotes highlight the crowded gathering of people eager to hear Jesus teach, the presence of those who sought to oppose him, and the power of God to heal those in need.
