# Luke 7:24 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
In this Bible verse, Jesus responds to messengers sent by John the Baptist, who questioned if Jesus truly was the Messiah. Jesus, aware of the people's perception of John, challenges their expectations of him. He emphasizes that John was not a wavering or inconsistent man, but rather someone to be revered. Jesus addresses the people's curiosity about John's character and actions, reminding them to focus on the significance of his message rather than superficial aspects. To avoid any potential misunderstandings or flattery, Jesus deliberately waits until after the messengers leave to commend John publicly.
