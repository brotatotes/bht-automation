## BHT:
In this Bible verse, Jesus responds to a Pharisee named Simon who secretly doubted his prophetic authority. Jesus not only addresses Simon personally, exposing his hidden thoughts, but also reveals his omniscience by speaking to the true character of a woman present. Despite Simon's judgemental attitude, Jesus responds with kindness and respect, exemplifying his ability to approach others with love and understanding, showing that even proud Pharisees are not exempt from his tender and courteous address.
