## BHT:
In this verse, Jesus gathers his disciples and confronts them about their ambition for power and recognition, exposing their desire for superiority and preeminence. He highlights that such aspirations are prominent among the Gentiles who lack knowledge of God, emphasizing the need for humility and servant leadership. Jesus aims to correct their pride and vanity, redirecting their focus towards God's kingdom rather than seeking personal authority.
