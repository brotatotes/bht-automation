### John Wesley:
1. "This verse highlights the transformation that will occur in the resurrection. It emphasizes that the relationships and dynamics we experience in this earthly life will no longer be applicable in the eternal realm."
2. "The absence of marriage in the resurrection does not imply a loss or deprivation, but rather a fulfillment of our deepest desires for unity and communion with God and one another."
3. "By stating that there will be no marriage, Jesus is emphasizing that our ultimate union will be with Him, and our primary focus will be on worshiping and glorifying God for eternity."
