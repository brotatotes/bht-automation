# Mark 13:22 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
Some commentators observe that in this verse, there is a warning about false figures who will deceive and mislead others, even the chosen ones. These deceivers will use signs and wonders to trick people, showcasing their apparent power. It is emphasized that their intention is to deliberately mislead and deceive, posing a danger to those they aim to confuse. However, there is reassurance that the elect will be protected by the power and grace of God.
