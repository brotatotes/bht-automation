## BHT:
In this Bible verse, Jesus expresses astonishment and wonder at the unbelief of his countrymen. Despite his divine knowledge and insight into the human heart, he is taken aback by their lack of faith. It is surprising that even in his own hometown and among his kinspeople, there is a lack of belief in him. Jesus, in his humanity, experiences the full range of emotions, including marveling at the disbelief of those who should have recognized his divine mission and qualifications. This verse highlights the importance of faith and the impact it has on Jesus' ministry.
