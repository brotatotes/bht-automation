## BHT:
In this verse, Jesus addresses a crowd gathered near Caesarea Philippi in heathen territory. He beckons them to come closer and reveals a truth of great importance. Jesus teaches that whoever desires to follow Him must be willing to deny themselves, embracing God's will even in the midst of hardship and challenges, just as Jesus Himself did on the cross. It is a call to a spiritual commitment and a philosophy of life that contrasts with worldly desires and expectations.
