# Mark 9:19 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
In this passage, Jesus expresses frustration towards the lack of faith among the people, longing for them to believe in Him. He demonstrates His authority and ability to address their concerns by commanding the child to be brought to Him. The father's doubt is also highlighted, emphasizing the importance of unwavering faith in Jesus. This verse serves as a reminder for readers to trust in Jesus and bring their problems to Him, knowing that He can provide solutions.
