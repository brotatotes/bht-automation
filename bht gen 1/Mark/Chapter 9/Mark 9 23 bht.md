# Mark 9:23 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
This Bible verse emphasizes the power of belief and faith as the determining factor for what is possible. Jesus challenges the individual's faith, highlighting that the limitation lies in their own disbelief rather than in His power. The verse portrays the struggle to develop faith, with Jesus aiding in its birth. It also emphasizes that all blessings and assistance come from God alone, and that faith in Christ is essential for the manifestation of God's power in one's life.
