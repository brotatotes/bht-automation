# Matthew 10:20 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
In this verse, we see the future mission of the Apostles, as they speak not on their own authority but are guided by the Spirit of God. It highlights the divine inspiration and the indwelling presence of the Holy Spirit, who speaks through believers. Their words, directed by God, carry power and authority.
