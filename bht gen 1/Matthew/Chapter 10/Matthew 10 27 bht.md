# Matthew 10:27 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
In this verse, Jesus instructs his disciples to boldly share the teachings he has given them. He emphasizes the contrast between the private nature of their current learning and the public proclamation that is to come. Jesus urges them to openly declare what they have been taught, emphasizing that the truth is meant to be shared without fear. This verse serves as an encouragement for the disciples to confidently spread the teachings they have received, both in private and from rooftops.
