# Matthew 10:28 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
In this verse, Jesus encourages His disciples to prioritize their spiritual well-being over their physical safety. He reminds them that while physical death is a small matter compared to the eternal destiny of their souls. The ultimate fear should be reserved for God alone, who has the power to destroy both body and soul in hell. This verse serves as a powerful reminder to fear God, not people, and to trust in His divine providence and protection.
