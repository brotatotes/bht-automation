## BHT:
In this verse, the instruction given by Jesus to his disciples is to trust in God's provision rather than relying on material wealth. They are urged not to acquire or procure gold, silver, or other possessions for their journey. Instead, they are to go without preparing and trust in God's faithfulness. The emphasis is on a mindset of dependence, letting go of earthly possessions, and learning to trust in God for their needs.
