1. "The kingdom of heaven, the Gospel, and the ministry of it, first by John, then by Christ and his apostles, suffereth violence; or 'comes with force', and power upon the souls of men."

2. "Men rush into my kingdom" - This vivid imagery suggests a sense of urgency and determination in seeking to be a part of God's kingdom.

3. "With a violence like that of those who are taking a city by storm" - This comparison highlights the intensity and fervor with which people are pursuing God's kingdom, emphasizing the importance and value they place on it.
