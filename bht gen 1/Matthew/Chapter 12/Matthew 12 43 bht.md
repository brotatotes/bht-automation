### Philip Schaff:
1. "The figure in Matthew 12:43-45 refers primarily to the Jewish people, but is applicable also in the history of Christianity and to individuals."
2. "The return into the man is against the view that the abode of the wicked is meant; but a state of greater dissatisfaction and unrest is plainly indicated."
3. "Our Lord's words, while in one sense an accommodation to this view, allude to the place whither the demons go, without stating where it is."

### John Wesley:
