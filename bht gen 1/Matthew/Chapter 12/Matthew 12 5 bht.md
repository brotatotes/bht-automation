# Matthew 12:5 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
In this verse, commentators discuss the actions of the priests in the temple and their observance of the Sabbath. They point out the seemingly contradictory nature of the priests working on the Sabbath while still being considered blameless. They highlight the inconsistency in the application of Sabbath laws, contrasting the criticism of Jesus' disciples with the blamelessness of the priests.
