## BHT:
In this verse, the commentators paint a vivid picture of a gathering taking place in the kingdom of heaven. They describe how stumbling-blocks, whether they be people or things that cause offense and hinder others, will be gathered and removed. These stumbling-blocks include the ungodly, sinners, and those who do iniquity. The commentators emphasize the importance of separating these obstacles from the righteous, creating an environment where the good seed can flourish and bear fruit. It is a gathering that signifies the removal of anything that obstructs the growth and hinders the children of God.
