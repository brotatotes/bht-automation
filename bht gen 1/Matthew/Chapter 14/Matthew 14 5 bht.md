# Matthew 14:5 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
In this Bible verse, the character of John the Baptist serves as a powerful force, restraining Herod from taking his life. Herod's fear of his own conscience, respect for John as a good man, and the fear of the multitude who held John in high esteem, all contributed to preventing his execution. This demonstrates how God can use different factors to restrain the wickedness of individuals, even those in positions of power.
