# Matthew 15:10 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
In this verse, Jesus dismisses the hypocritical leaders who misunderstand the true meaning of the law, turning instead to the multitude who are open to his teachings. He emphasizes that genuine pollution is not external, but rather a matter of the heart. Jesus invites the common people to draw near, recognizing their potential for growth and receptiveness to his message. The verse underscores the significance of true understanding and interpretation of the law, contrasting it with blind adherence to rituals and traditions.
