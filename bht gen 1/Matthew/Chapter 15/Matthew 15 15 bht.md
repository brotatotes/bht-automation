# Matthew 15:15 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
In this passage, Jesus delivers a parable that challenges the conventional beliefs and traditions of the Pharisees. Peter, as the spokesperson for the disciples, seeks clarification on the meaning of the parable, indicating their eagerness to understand Jesus' teachings. Jesus responds by emphasizing that true defilement does not come from external things, but from the condition of one's heart. This prompts the disciples to reflect on their own understanding and confront the conflict between Jesus' teachings and societal expectations.
