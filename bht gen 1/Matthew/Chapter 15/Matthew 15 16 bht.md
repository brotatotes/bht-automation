# Matthew 15:16 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
In this verse, Jesus expresses disappointment towards his disciples' lack of understanding, comparing them to the Pharisees. He reproves them for their ignorance and dulness, highlighting their failure to grasp the plain and easy teachings that he has been imparting to them for a long time. Despite their close proximity to him and the extensive instruction they have received, they still struggle to comprehend the truths he is revealing. This emphasizes the need for further growth and spiritual insight among the disciples.
