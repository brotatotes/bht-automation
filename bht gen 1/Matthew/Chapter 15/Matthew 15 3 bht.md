# Matthew 15:3 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
In this verse, Jesus confronts the religious leaders about their prioritization of man-made traditions over the commandments of God. He points out their transgression in neglecting the duties that God had commanded. Jesus emphasizes that following human traditions should not undermine the authority of God's law. He exposes the hypocrisy of those who hold others accountable for breaking traditions while failing to observe and uphold the commandments of God themselves. Ultimately, Jesus advocates for true righteousness and freedom from the bondage of mere ceremonialism and tradition.
