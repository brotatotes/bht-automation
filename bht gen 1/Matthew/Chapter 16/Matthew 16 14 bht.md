In this Bible verse, we see the varied opinions and beliefs surrounding Jesus. Some believed Him to be the Son of David or a remarkable personage. Others thought He was John the Baptist risen from the dead, Jeremiah, or another ancient prophet. Though there were divisions and controversy, the popular opinion in Galilee was shifting.
