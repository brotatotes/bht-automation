# Matthew 16:5 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
In this passage, Jesus and his disciples are traveling by boat and they realize that they forgot to bring bread, likely due to the distraction of their journey. The commentators highlight the disciples' forgetfulness and lack of provision, emphasizing the importance of staying focused and prepared. Additionally, the mention of yeast serves as a metaphor for the potential spread and corruption of false teachings and hypocrisy. Through this verse, we're reminded to be attentive, discerning, and to guard against influences that may lead us away from the truth.

### John Wesley:
