## BHT:
This verse speaks of the disciples' struggle to cast out a demon, highlighting their need for greater faith and dependence on God's power. The failure of the disciples reminds us that even those chosen and close to Jesus still face challenges and must rely on His authority. It points to the presence of unbelief, both in the disciples and in the father of the afflicted child.
