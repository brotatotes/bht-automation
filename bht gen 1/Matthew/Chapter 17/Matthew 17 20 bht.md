# Matthew 17:20 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
This verse speaks about the importance of having faith, even if it is as small as a mustard seed. The commentators emphasize that genuine faith, regardless of its size, has the power to accomplish great things and overcome obstacles. It highlights the vitality and growth of faith, urging believers to have increasing and expanding faith. However, they caution against the hindrance of unbelief or a lack of faith, emphasizing that faith should be accompanied by actions and love.
