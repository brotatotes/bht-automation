## BHT:
In this verse, we are instructed on how to address the faults of our fellow believers. We are urged to take a proactive approach, going to them rather than waiting for them to come to us. It is emphasized that addressing faults should be done privately and with the intention to convince and rebuke. The aim is not only justice but also to restore the person and regain their love and fellowship. Ultimately, by following this instruction, we can prevent offense and uphold the honor of the Christian name.
