## BHT:
Where two or three are gathered together in my name, there is a powerful spiritual bond and unity among believers. In these assemblies, decisions are made and prayers are offered with the authority and assistance of Christ. The presence of God is manifested among them, guiding their counsels and answering their petitions through the active work of the Holy Spirit.
