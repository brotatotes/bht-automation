# Matthew 18:34 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
In this Bible verse, a servant incurs enormous debt, but is shown great mercy and forgiven by his master. However, the servant fails to extend the same mercy to others and is subsequently condemned to severe punishment. The verse emphasizes the consequences of withholding forgiveness and serves as a reminder of the importance of compassion and justice in our interactions with one another. The punishment faced by the servant is depicted as imprisonment and torment until the debt is fully paid, highlighting the severity of the treatment deserved for his actions.
