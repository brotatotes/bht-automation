## BHT:
In this verse, the commentators emphasize the importance of avoiding offense and removing anything that may cause us to stumble in our faith. They highlight that giving offense to Christ's disciples is equally harmful and should be avoided to prevent eternal damnation. By heeding these cautions, we can maintain our fellowship with Christ and prioritize our eternal relationship with Him.
