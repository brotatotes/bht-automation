# Matthew 19:25 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
The verse under consideration unveils the astonishment of the disciples, prompted by the difficulty for the wealthy to enter the kingdom of God. Their questioning arises from an awareness of the potential hindrance that material possessions and earthly desires pose to one's spiritual journey. Recognizing that their understanding of the kingdom was tainted by worldly perspectives, the disciples grapple with the implications of salvation for both the rich and the poor. This passage serves as a reminder that true salvation transcends material wealth, emphasizing the need for humility and detachment from earthly attachments.
