# Matthew 19:25 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
In this Bible verse, commentators highlight the disciples' astonishment at the difficulties of attaining salvation and the misconceptions they held due to societal beliefs. The disciples were struck with amazement, unable to comprehend Jesus' teachings. Their surprise stemmed from their assumption that wealth and status were indications of God's favor. However, Jesus challenges this notion, emphasizing the need for humility and reliance on God rather than material possessions for one's salvation.
