# Matthew 20:21 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
In this verse, the disciples express their misguided belief in a temporal kingdom, expecting positions of honor and authority in Jesus' earthly reign. Their ambitious request reflects their inability to fully comprehend the spiritual nature of Jesus' kingdom that surpassed worldly power and riches. Despite Jesus' teachings about the true nature of his kingdom, the disciples still held onto their worldly expectations, unaware of the magnitude of Jesus' mission and the spiritual transformation it would bring.
