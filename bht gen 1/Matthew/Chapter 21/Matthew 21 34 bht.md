## BHT:
In this Bible verse, God sends his servants, the prophets and other messengers, to the husbandmen, symbolizing the responsibility of producing fruits and the expectation of receiving them. The trust and investment God has placed in his people, particularly those in positions of authority, call for a fruitful return. The context suggests the long-awaited expectation for righteousness from the Jewish nation, emphasizing the role of prophets in instructing, exhorting, reproving, and encouraging repentance to bring forth the desired fruits.
