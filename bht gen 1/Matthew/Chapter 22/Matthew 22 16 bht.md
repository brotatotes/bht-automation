## BHT:
In this verse, the commentators provide insights that enrich our understanding. They highlight the Pharisees' deceptive intentions, portraying themselves as sincere seekers of truth. This verse reminds us to seek the pure word of God and walk uprightly, putting aside worldly judgments.
