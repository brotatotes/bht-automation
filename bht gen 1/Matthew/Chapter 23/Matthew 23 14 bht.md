### Albert Barnes:

2. "Even while, the force of ‘and’ is best represented thus."

### John Gill:
1. "For ye devour widows' houses; that is, the goods in the houses of such as were left with fatherless children, and but little to support them; who being left alone, and none to advise them, and being weak, and prone to superstition; these greedy dogs, as Isaiah calls them, who could never have enough, easily imposed upon them, wormed them out of all their substance, stripped them bare of the necessaries of life."

### John Wesley:
