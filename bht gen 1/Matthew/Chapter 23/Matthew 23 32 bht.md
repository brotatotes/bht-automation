## BHT:
In this verse, the current generation is given permission to continue in the sinful ways of their ancestors, as they are told to "fill up the measure of your fathers." The concept of divine judgment is emphasized, as the sins of the past are seen as reaching their full extent. The speaker acknowledges that they are leaving the people to follow their own hearts, implying a sense of abandonment. Ultimately, this verse highlights the consequences of persistent rebellion and the giving over of individuals to their own desires.
