# Matthew 24:51 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
In this Bible verse, the consequences of hypocrisy are vividly depicted. The verse speaks of a dreadful sundering of the conscience and practice, where the hypocrites' soul and body are separated, and their gifts and talents are taken away. It warns against the dangers of claiming righteousness while living in hypocrisy, emphasizing that even the formerly upright can become the worst of sinners. This verse specifically addresses ministers of Christ, highlighting the baseness of hypocrisy and calling for genuine transformation to avoid damnation.
