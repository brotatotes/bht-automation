# Matthew 25:13 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
This verse encourages believers to be vigilant and prepared for the return of Jesus, as the exact timing is unknown. Maintaining a strong spiritual life and relying on the Holy Spirit are essential in serving the kingdom of God. Watchfulness involves being alert in every aspect of our lives, guarding against sin, false teachings, and worldly distractions. It is a constant state of readiness, with a heart that remains awake even in times of rest. Though peaceful, the vigilant person acknowledges the uncertainty of the future and remains cautious.
