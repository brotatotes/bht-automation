# Matthew 25:15 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
In this parable, individuals are entrusted with different capacities and abilities, given according to their own specific capacity for business. Each person is called to faithfully utilize their unique skills and talents, recognizing the value of spiritual gifts over merely official ones. Despite variations in opportunities and responsibilities, all are expected to make the most of what has been given to them. The parable emphasizes the importance of acknowledging and utilizing individual capacities to contribute to the overall success and productivity of the tasks at hand.

### Albert Barnes:
