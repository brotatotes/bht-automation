## BHT:
In this verse, the righteous and faithful are exalted on the right hand, receiving honor and favor from God. They are represented by sheep, symbolizing their care and charge by Christ, the good shepherd who laid down his life for them. In contrast, the wicked and unfaithful goats are placed on the left, signifying dishonor and separation from God. The division between sheep and goats emphasizes the eternal separation between the righteous and the wicked, underlining the importance of living a righteous and faithful life.
