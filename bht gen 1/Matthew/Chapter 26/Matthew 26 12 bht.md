# Matthew 26:12 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
In this verse, Jesus interprets Mary's act of anointing him as a symbolic preparation for his impending death. He sees it as a confirmation of his previous teachings to the disciples about his approaching crucifixion. Although Mary may not have fully understood the depth of her action, Jesus views her anointing as a representation of the embalming process that would typically accompany a burial. Through her act, Mary unknowingly becomes a vessel for reinforcing the significance of his upcoming sacrifice.
