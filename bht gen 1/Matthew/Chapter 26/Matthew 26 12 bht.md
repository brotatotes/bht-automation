In this verse, Jesus affirms that Mary's act of anointing his feet with precious ointment was a symbolic preparation for his burial. By interpreting her action as a foreshadowing of his impending death, Jesus confirms his teachings about his crucifixion. Through her anointing, Jesus establishes a profound connection between his impending death and the salvation it would bring to the whole world.
