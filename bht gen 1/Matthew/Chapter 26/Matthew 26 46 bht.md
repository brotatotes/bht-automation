# Matthew 26:46 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
In this verse, we see Jesus demonstrating both bravery and foresight. He calls his disciples to action, not to retreat from the impending danger, but to meet it head-on, showcasing his courage as a human. Additionally, Jesus displays his deep understanding by knowing not only who will betray him, but also when and where it will happen. Despite the weight of this knowledge, Jesus remains composed, showing a remarkable calmness of spirit.
