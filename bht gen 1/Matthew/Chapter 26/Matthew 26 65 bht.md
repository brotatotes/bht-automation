# Matthew 26:65 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
In this Bible verse, the high priest reacts to hearing what he perceives as blasphemy. His dramatic tearing of clothes expresses his horror and grief at the alleged offense. Though forbidden to do so, the high priest breaks the rules, emphasizing the seriousness of the situation. This action signifies the profound distress caused by the claim of divinity and serves as a powerful symbol of the high priest's zeal for the honor of God. The tearing of clothes reflects the gravity of the offense and the high priest's response to it as a public calamity.
