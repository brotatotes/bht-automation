# Matthew 27:39 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
In this verse, we see the extreme abasement of the Son of God, as He endures mockery and scorn during His crucifixion. Passers-by wag their heads in derision, exemplifying the contempt and ridicule directed towards Him. This pain and humiliation highlight the cost of our salvation, emphasizing the depth of Jesus' suffering. Despite the cruelty and insults, Jesus remains silent, teaching us patience and providing consolation in His enduring love for us.
