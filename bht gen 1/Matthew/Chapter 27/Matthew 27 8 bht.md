# Matthew 27:8 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
This verse describes a field that gained its name, "the field of blood," due to its association with the betrayal of Judas and the shedding of innocent blood. It was purchased with blood money, serving as a perpetual memorial to the consequences of betrayal. The name "Aceldama" is also used to refer to this field, emphasizing the stain of the blood money that remains even unto the present day. It is believed to have been located on the south side of Mount Sion.
