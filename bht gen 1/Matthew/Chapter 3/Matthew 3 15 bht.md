# Matthew 3:15 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
In this verse, Jesus proclaims the significance of fulfilling God's righteous ordinances, encompassing baptism. He acknowledges the role of John as His forerunner and emphasizes the importance of obedience for messengers of God in upholding His righteous laws. Jesus recognizes baptism as a means to completely fulfill the righteous law of God and accomplish His divine mission. The ministries of John and Jesus are intertwined, marking a transition from the old covenant to the new and exhibiting Jesus' submission to God's will.
