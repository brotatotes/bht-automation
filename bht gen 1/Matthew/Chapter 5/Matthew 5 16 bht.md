# Matthew 5:16 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
In this passage, Christians are encouraged to let their light shine through their good works, so that others may see and be inspired to glorify God. The metaphor of Christians being the light of the world emphasizes the importance of being visible and impactful in society. By living in a way that reflects the goodness and grace of God, Christians have the opportunity to lead others towards redemption and transformation. The ultimate goal is to bring glory to God and inspire others to love and serve Him.
