# Matthew 7:11 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
In this verse, we see a general declaration of the corruption of man and a strong affirmation of original sin. The argument moves from the lesser to the greater, emphasizing the logic of God's goodness. Though our fallen nature is evident, the Father of all fathers has a deep affection towards His children. By imitating the God of love and following His example, we fulfill the law and the prophets, receiving all good things from our Heavenly Father who is ready to answer our prayers.
