# Matthew 7:2 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
This verse reminds us of the reciprocal nature of judgment. Just as we judge others, we will also be judged. It emphasizes the importance of self-reflection and self-judgment in our interactions with others. The verse cautions against harsh judgment and encourages us to be kind and fair in our judgments, as the same standard will be applied to us. Ultimately, it highlights the idea that how we judge others will determine how we are judged in return.

This serves as a cautionary reminder to be mindful of the impact our judgments have on our relationships.
