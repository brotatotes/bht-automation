# Matthew 7:8 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
This verse emphasizes the promise that those who ask, seek, and knock will receive, find, and have doors opened for them. It highlights the importance of approaching God with faith and a sincere desire to use what is received for a righteous purpose. When we lack wisdom, we can turn to God for guidance, but it is crucial to ask in faith without wavering.
