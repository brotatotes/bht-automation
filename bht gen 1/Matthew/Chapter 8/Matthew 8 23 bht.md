## BHT:
In this Bible verse, we are presented with the powerful metaphor of a storm on the Sea of Galilee. Through the disciples' fear and panic, we are reminded of our own human frailty and tendency to doubt. However, this passage also reassures us of Jesus' presence amidst our trials, as he is ready to calm the storm and bring us peace. Additionally, his authority over the forces of nature is displayed, emphasizing his ability to bring order and tranquility to any chaotic situation.
