## BHT:
In this Bible verse, we see the people's misplaced priorities and worldly concerns over spiritual matters. They were more interested in their material possessions and the loss of their property than in the miraculous healing and powerful presence of Jesus. Fearing further loss, punishment, and disruption to their way of life, they preferred the presence of their swine over the transformative power and salvation brought by Christ.
