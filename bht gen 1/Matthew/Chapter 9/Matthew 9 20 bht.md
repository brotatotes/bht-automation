# Matthew 9:20 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
In this verse, a woman with a chronic issue of blood approaches Jesus from behind, with a humble and reverent attitude. She has heard of Jesus' miraculous healings and believes that she too can be healed. Despite her superstitious faith, Jesus honors her belief and cures her. This incident serves as a reminder for all believers to approach God with humility and awe, submitting ourselves to His authority and seeking His guidance.

### John Calvin:

### John Gill:

### John Wesley:

3. It reminds us that we are not to assert ourselves or demand attention, but to humbly submit ourselves to God's authority and seek His guidance and direction.
