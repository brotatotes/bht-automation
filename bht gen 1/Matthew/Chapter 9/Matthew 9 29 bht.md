## BHT:
The referenced verse describes an encounter where Jesus healed two blind men. Commentators believe that Jesus tested and rewarded the faith of the blind men, as their level of faith determined the extent of their cure. Jesus touched their eyes as a symbol of his power and compassion. By touching their eyes, he demonstrated his favor and willingness to heal them. The healing was not solely dependent on the individuals' faith, but it was a confirmation of their belief in Jesus' ability to restore their sight.
