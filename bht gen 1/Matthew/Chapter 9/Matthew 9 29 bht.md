The verse emphasizes the importance of faith, not the size or strength of it, in receiving the cure. The restored vision serves as a visible sign and confirmation of their faith in Jesus, both spiritually and physically. Jesus' act of touching their eyes not only demonstrates his compassion but also serves as a physical manifestation of his healing power. Through faith and the touch of Jesus, their sight is restored, a gracious seal of their belief in him.
