# Revelation 16:3 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
The verse describes a powerful judgment upon the world of sin and corruption, symbolized by the pouring out of a phial upon the sea. The sea undergoes a transformation, becoming discolored like the blood of a dead man, symbolizing the consequences of sin which lead to decay and death. This judgment affects every living soul, stressing the universal impact of sin and the all-encompassing nature of the judgment. The parallel with the plagues of Egypt further emphasizes the severity of the calamity and the need for repentance.
