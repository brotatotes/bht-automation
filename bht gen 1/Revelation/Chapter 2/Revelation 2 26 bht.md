1. "And—implying the close connection of the promise to the conqueror that follows, with the preceding exhortation."

### Albert Barnes:
1. "And keepeth my works unto the end - The works that I command and that I require, to the end of his life." 
2. "To him will I give power over the nations - ...they would partake of the final triumph and glory of the Saviour, and be associated with him."
3. "It is not said that they would have exclusive power over the nations, or that they would hold offices of trust under him during a personal reign on the earth; but the meaning is, that they would be associated with him in his future glory."

### Philip Schaff:
1. "They are not simply the works which Jesus commands, but those which He does, a fresh illustration of that close identification of Jesus with His people which marks the writings of St. John."
2. "By the ‘nations’ we are not to understand the nations as such, but the nations as opposed to the true Israel of God, and as alienated from God, properly the Gentiles."
3. "The believer shall not merely have power, but rightful power, authority, over them."
