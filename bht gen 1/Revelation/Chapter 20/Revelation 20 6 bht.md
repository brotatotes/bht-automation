### Albert Barnes:
1. "Blessed - That is, his condition is to be regarded as a happy or a favored one. This is designed apparently to support and encourage those who, in the time of John, suffered persecution, or who might suffer persecution afterward."
2. "That hath part in the first resurrection - That participated in it; that is, who is associated with those who are thus raised up."
3. "On such the second death hath no power - The 'second death' is properly the death which the wicked will experience in the world of woe. See Revelation 20:14. The meaning here is, that all who are here referred to as having part in the first resurrection will be secure against that."
