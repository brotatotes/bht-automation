## BHT:
In this verse, a scene unfolds where an angel takes burning coals from the altar and scatters them on the earth, symbolizing the imminent answer of God's vengeance to the prayers of the saints. The coals represent the judgments about to be inflicted, accompanied by thunders, lightnings, voices, and earthquakes. The prayers offered are not for mercy or deliverance but for the vindication of God's cause, resulting in the fulfillment of His righteous judgment upon the earth. This passage depicts the profound significance of the actions and symbols used, marking the divine response to the saints' pleas for justice.
