## BHT:
The author expresses a deep longing to connect with the readers, not for his own benefit, but to impart something of great value - a spiritual gift. This gift is not a supernatural power, but rather practical and applicable to their faith journey. The purpose behind this desire is to establish and strengthen them, both in their Christian character and in their understanding of the blessings of the gospel. Through face-to-face interactions and the sharing of spiritual truths, the author hopes to confirm and solidify their faith.
