### Albert Barnes:

### Philip Schaff:
1. "The idea of salvation is analyzed; it comprises two facts: being justified and being saved (in the full sense of the word)."

3. "True faith always leads to confession; confession is nothing without true faith."

### John Gill:
1. "True faith does not lie in the bare assent of the mind to the Gospel, or any truth contained in it, but in the heart; it is not a notional knowledge of things to be believed; but it is heart work, a believing with all the heart."

3. ### John Wesley:
