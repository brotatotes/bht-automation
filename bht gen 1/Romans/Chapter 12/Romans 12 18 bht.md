3. "Our responsibility extends as far as our ability to keep the peace."

### John Gill:
1. "Or be at peace, seek after peace, pursue it, and cultivate it."
2. "Peace should rule in their hearts."
3. "They should live peaceably and quietly, in all godliness and honesty."
