# Romans 14:18 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
In this passage, commentators emphasize the importance of righteousness, peace, and joy in the life of a believer. These qualities are seen as a reflection of the excellence of Christ's religion and are pleasing to God. When someone demonstrates righteousness, peace, and joy, their actions are not only accepted by God, but also approved by others. This complete expression of the Kingdom of God is marked by the acceptance of God and the approval of men, and reveals the flourishing of the Kingdom within the believer.
