### John Wesley:

2. "Their goddess Hope, like their other idols, was nothing" - Highlighting the emptiness and insignificance of the heathens' worship.
3. "It was, indeed, built again not long after, but was again burned to the ground" - Demonstrating the futility of placing hope in earthly things that are temporary and subject to destruction.
