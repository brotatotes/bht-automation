# Romans 15:29 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
The Apostle Paul's unwavering confidence in bringing the blessing of Christ to the believers in Rome is evident. He believed not only in his coming to Rome but also in the manner of his arrival. Paul was positive in bringing the Gospel, which he considered a welcome and necessary message of salvation for all. He trusted that he would come in the fullness of the blessing of Christ, bringing the complete message and withholding nothing profitable for their spiritual growth.
