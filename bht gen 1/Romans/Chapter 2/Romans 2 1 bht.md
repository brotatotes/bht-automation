# Romans 2:1 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
In this verse, the commentators highlight the universal nature of human guilt and the inexcusability of sinful actions. They emphasize that no one can escape God's judgment, whether they are moral or not. The verse challenges a censorious spirit and reminds us that passing judgment on others condemns ourselves. It warns against mere knowledge without action and encourages self-examination.

### John Gill:
