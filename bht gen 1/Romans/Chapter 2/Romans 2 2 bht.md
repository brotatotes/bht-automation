## BHT:
In this verse, the author introduces an undoubted truth, emphasizing the certainty and universal nature of God's judgment. This judgment, solely carried out by God, stands in opposition to the flawed judgments of humans. It encompasses all hypocrisy and unrighteousness, and is aligned with the truth, making no exceptions. This divine judgment goes beyond external actions, reaching deep into the heart and exposing the true nature of individuals.

### John Gill:
1. "The judgment of God" refers to the judgment that will occur after death, which is certain, universal, and carried out solely by God. It is definitive and stands in opposition to the judgment of men.

3. We can be sure of this judgment and its adherence to truth based on reason, Scripture, and the being and perfections of God.
