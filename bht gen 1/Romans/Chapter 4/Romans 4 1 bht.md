## BHT:
This verse highlights the significance of Abraham's faith in finding acceptance with God. It distinguishes between his fleshly efforts and his spiritual connection with God. By emphasizing that Abraham's faith was counted as righteousness due to his trust in God's promises and reliance on His grace, the verse emphasizes the importance of a genuine and deep faith in God.
