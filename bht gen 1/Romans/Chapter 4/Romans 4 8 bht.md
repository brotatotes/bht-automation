## BHT:
This verse speaks of the incredible blessing and assurance that comes with having our sins forgiven by the Lord. It emphasizes that God will not count our sins against us, treating us as if the sins had never been committed. Through the righteousness of Christ, believers are seen as faultless and beyond reproach in the eyes of God, and He will never use our sins against us. This verse reveals the complete forgiveness and acceptance we receive through Christ's sacrifice, offering hope and peace to those who trust in Him.
