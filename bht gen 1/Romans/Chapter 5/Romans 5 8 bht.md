## BHT:
The verse being described is a powerful declaration of God's love for humanity. Through the death of Christ, God demonstrates His tremendous love for us, surpassing any love that humans can show. It is emphasized that Christ died for us even when we were sinners and enemies of God, illustrating the depth of His love and the extent He went to save us. ### John Gill:

### John Wesley:
1. "God recommendeth - A most elegant expression. Those are wont to be recommended to us, who were before either unknown to, or alienated from, us."
