# Romans 6:8 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
In this verse, we see the profound connection between believers and Christ. It speaks of our spiritual union with Him, our participation in His death, and our hope in sharing in His resurrection. Through our death with Christ, we have died to sin and are no longer slaves to it. This verse emphasizes the transformation and newness of life that believers are called to pursue, as they crucify their old selves and live in obedience to God. It speaks of the intimate and uninterrupted communion with Christ, where believers experience the highest pleasure and consummate happiness.
