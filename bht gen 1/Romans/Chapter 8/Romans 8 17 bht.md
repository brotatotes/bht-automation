# Romans 8:17 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
As joint heirs with Christ, we are partakers of a great inheritance from God, one that matches the one given to His Son. If we willingly and cheerfully suffer with Him, we become rightful participants in this divine inheritance. It is a new proposition that entails a deep connection and new understanding of our relationship with God.

### Albert Barnes:
1. "An heir is one who succeeds to an estate. The meaning here is, that if we sustain the relation of sons to God, that we shall be treated as such, and admitted to share his favors."
2. "This expression means that we shall be partakers of that inheritance which God confers on his people. That inheritance is his favor here, and eternal life hereafter."
3. "Christians are united to him; they are his friends; and they are thus represented as destined to partake with him of his glory."

These quotes highlight the idea that as children of God, we are treated as heirs and will share in his favors and eternal life. It emphasizes the unity and connection between Christians and Christ, and the promise of partaking in his glory.
