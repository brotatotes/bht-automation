# Romans 8:24 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
This Bible verse reminds us of the integral role of hope in our salvation. It speaks to the future expectation of salvation, highlighting that hope is not based on what we can already see or possess. Our faith plays a central role in this salvation, as it apprehends the object in both present and future aspects. Hope serves as the anchor that keeps us steadfast on our journey towards salvation, trusting in the promises of God.
