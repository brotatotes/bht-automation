# Romans 8:8 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
Those who live according to their sinful nature, being under its control and influence, are unable to please God. They prioritize their own desires and pursue worldly pleasures, rather than submitting to His will. As a result, they are enslaved to sin, separated from God, and incapable of experiencing true spiritual life. This verse highlights the inherent inability of the sinful nature to please God and emphasizes the need for transformation through the Holy Spirit to align one's actions and desires with His will.

3. "Those who are in this state should turn from it without delay." - This statement urges individuals who are living in sin to repent and turn away from their sinful ways.
