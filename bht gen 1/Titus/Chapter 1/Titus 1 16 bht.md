# Titus 1:16 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
This verse speaks of a group of individuals who confidently profess to know and acknowledge God, but their actions contradict their profession. They are described as abominable, disobedient, and worthless, indicating their lack of genuine faith. Though they claim to have knowledge of God, their knowledge is superficial and theoretical. Their hypocrisy and failure to live out their faith will ultimately render them rejected by God.
