# Titus 1:9 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
The passage describes the importance of holding firmly to the faithful word and teachings of the apostles. It emphasizes the need for elders to exhort believers in sound doctrine, guiding them towards holiness and edification. The phrase "as he has been taught" stresses the adherence to authoritative instruction, while the reference to Acts 2:42 underscores the significance of the apostles' teaching in the early Christian community. Thus, the verse emphasizes the faithful preservation and transmission of essential teachings for the spiritual growth and unity of believers.
