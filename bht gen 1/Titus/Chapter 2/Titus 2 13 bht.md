# Titus 2:13 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
Believers are encouraged to eagerly anticipate and desire the glorious appearing of Jesus, which is their blessed hope. This hope is not merely an abstract concept but the eternal glory and happiness that believers are looking forward to. It is a reminder that Jesus is not a small God, but the great God who will manifest His glory at His Second Advent.

2. "The blessed hope and manifestation of the glory."
