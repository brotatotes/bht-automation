# 1 Timothy 3:9 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
The mystery of the faith, which is the truth revealed by the Spirit, is to be held in a pure conscience. This means that there should be no mixture of anything base or foreign in one's faith. It is not just about having an orthodox belief, but also about being sincere and pure in heart. Holding firmly to the great doctrines of the Christian religion is important, as it is the basis of one's faith. This emphasizes the need for steadfastness in faith and holiness in heart and life.
