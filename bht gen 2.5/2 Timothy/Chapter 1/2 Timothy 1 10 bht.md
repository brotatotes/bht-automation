# 2 Timothy 1:10 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
The death of the body is temporary, while the resurrection brings the abolition of death into full effect. Christ is the life and resurrection, making it possible for the dead to live and rise. The divine counsel was hidden until the fitting time, and through the Incarnation, Christ abolished death and brought life and incorruption to light.
