# Galatians 3:4 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
Paul questions the Galatian Christians if their sufferings were in vain, suggesting that if the gospel they embraced was false, their sufferings would have been fruitless. The sufferings could refer to persecutions or challenges faced in adhering to the gospel. However, there is hope that they will not abandon the gospel and lose the benefits of their spiritual experiences. The purpose of suffering is not to drive individuals to despair but to lead them to a better course.
