# Hebrews 7:13 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
The insights from various sources highlight that a change in the law and priesthood is confirmed. It is emphasized that the Lord Jesus, belonging to a different tribe, is the one being referred to. This change in the priesthood is distinct from the Aaronic priesthood and sets aside the law. The emphasis is on the fact that no one from the tribe of Judah has ever been involved in the service of the priesthood. This change from one tribe to another is evident in the Lord Jesus, who is the antitype of Melchizedek and the eternal priest of God.
