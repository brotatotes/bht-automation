## BHT:
When Jesus declared, I am He, the soldiers and officials who came to arrest Him were struck down by a supernatural power, overwhelmed by the glorious effulgence of His majesty. His divine majesty and innocence produced such awe that they fell before Him. Jesus' open and bold profession, along with His unequalled power in reaching the consciences of men, made them feel they were in the presence of more than a mortal man. This display of power and mercy should have deterred them from further assault, but they persisted in their wicked attempt.
