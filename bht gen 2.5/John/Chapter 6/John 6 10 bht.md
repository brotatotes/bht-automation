## BHT:
The disciples are directed to have the people sit down in order to prepare for the feast that the Lord is about to provide. The mention of much grass indicates that it is the season of early spring, and the grass is flourishing and abundant. The men are specifically mentioned, possibly because they sat down first according to Eastern custom, and the number of women and children is not likely to be very large. The people obediently sit down, even though they are uncertain about the outcome, demonstrating their readiness to obey.
