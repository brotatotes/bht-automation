# Jude 1:25 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
God, the Father, is our Savior through Jesus Christ our Lord. He has dominion and legitimate power, both now and for all eternity. The word Savior can be applied to God because He is the great Author of salvation. We should desire for God to have supreme authority in our lives and for His dominion to be established over all the earth. In our own sins and sorrows, we should look forward to the time when His reign will be established over our souls. Jesus Christ is truly God and the source of all wisdom and salvation. To Him belongs all glory, both now and forever.
