# Luke 10:39 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
Mary sat at Jesus' feet, as His disciple, while He was discoursing. It does not appear that the meal had begun; far rather is it likely that Martha was busy about preparing it. Mary sat at Jesus' feet, as His disciple, while He was discoursing. She took her seat by the feet of Jesus and went on listening to his talk. This was the ancient posture of disciples or learners. They sat at the 'feet' of their teachers - that is, beneath them, in a humble place. When it is said that Mary sat at Jesus' feet, it means that she was 'a disciple' of his; that she listened attentively to his instructions, and was anxious to learn his doctrine.
