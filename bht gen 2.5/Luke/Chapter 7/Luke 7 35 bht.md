# Luke 7:35 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
Wisdom, personified as a mother, is slandered by her own children, yet her worth and rank remain unimpaired. Despite apostates revolting from the Church, the faith of the Gospel will always remain uninjured among the true elect. Christ, the wisdom of God, calls sinners to repentance and saves them, making them His children. These children, given to Christ by the Father, are redeemed and called to live lives that reflect the teachings of Christ, denying ungodliness and worldly lusts.
