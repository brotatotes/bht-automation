# Matthew 12:6 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
Jesus declares that there is something greater than the temple. This something refers to Christ Himself, who is the true Temple of God on earth. The comparison with the temple highlights the significance of Jesus and His disciples, who are of greater importance than the temple. The use of the neuter form of the adjective greater adds a solemn and impressive sense to the statement. Jesus asserts His authority to declare works of necessity on the Sabbath, just as the priests in the temple were authorized to do so.
