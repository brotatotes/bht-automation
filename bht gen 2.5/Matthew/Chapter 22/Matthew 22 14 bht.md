## BHT:
The phrase For many are called, but few are chosen highlights the distinction between those who are invited and those who are ultimately chosen by God. This statement emphasizes that not everyone who hears the invitation will respond with genuine faith. It is not enough to have an external profession of faith; true chosenness is demonstrated by a transformed life. This phrase serves as a reminder to examine oneself and ensure that one's faith is genuine and not merely superficial.
