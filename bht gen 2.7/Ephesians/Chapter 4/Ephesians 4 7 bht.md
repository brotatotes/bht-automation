## BHT:
Each believer receives grace according to the gift of Christ. This distribution of gifts is intended for the well-being of the whole body of believers. No one is overlooked or dispensable for the edification of the church. Unity is compatible with the diverse gifts and roles within the church. 1. "The 'grace' referred to here, most probably means 'the gracious influences of the Holy Spirit,' or his operations on the heart in connection with the use of the means which God has appointed."

### Marvin Vincent:

### John Calvin:

3. "These blessings are said to be the gift of Christ; for, as the apostle, first of all, mentioned the Father, so his aim, as we shall see, is to represent all that we are, and all that we have, as gathered together in Christ."
