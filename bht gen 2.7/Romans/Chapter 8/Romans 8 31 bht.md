1. "If God be for us - Be on outside, or is our friend, as he has shown himself to be"

### John Calvin:
1. "But Paul loudly exclaims, that a deeper principle ought to be inquired after, and that they reason absurdly who confine themselves to the sad spectacle of our present warfare."
2. "If God be for us, etc. This is the chief and the only support which can sustain us in every temptation."
3. "There is no power either under or above the heavens, which can resist the arm of God. Having him then as our defender, we need fear no harm whatever."

### Archibald T. Robertson:
1. "For these things... Paul has made a triumphant presentation of the reasons for the certainty of final sanctification of the sons of God."
