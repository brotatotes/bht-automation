## BHT:
To be in the spirit means to be under the dominion of our renewed mind, for the indwelling of God's Spirit signifies our union with Christ. The Spirit of Christ, the Holy Spirit, possesses believers, infusing them with His gracious disposition. If a person lacks the Spirit of Christ, they do not belong to Him. This condition assumes the truth and calls for self-examination of our connection to Christ.
