# 1 John 2:29 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
Every person who truly practices righteousness from a heart of belief and love is born of God. This means that those who are born of God share in his righteous nature and reflect his character through their actions. In other words, their faith in God's righteousness and their obedience to his commandments are evidence that they have been regenerated by the Spirit of God.

## Choicest Commentary Quotes:
### Henry Alford:
1. "If ye know that He is righteous...ye know also every one who doeth righteousness is born of Him." 
2. "The proposition will bear converting: not logically, but theologically, ποιῶν, for all righteousness is energetic: it springs out of holiness, truth, love."
3. "God is righteous. This is our axiom, from which we set out. And if so, then the source of righteousness."

### Jamieson-Fausset-Brown:
1. "Love is the grand feature and principle of 'righteousness' selected for discussion."
2. "We argue from his doing righteousness, to his being born of God."
3. "Her much love evinced that her sins were already forgiven; not, were the condition of her sins being forgiven."

### Albert Barnes:
1. "Everyone who has any true acquaintance with God, must have the fullest conviction that he is a righteous Being."
2. "No one who is not, in the proper sense of the term, a righteous man, can have any wellfounded pretensions to being regarded as a child of God."
3. "If we are righteous, in the true and proper sense, doing that which is right toward God and toward people, to ourselves, to our families, to our neighbors, to the world at large, to the Saviour who died for us, then we are true Christians."

### John Calvin:
1. "The first argument is, that we are spiritually begotten after the likeness of Christ; it hence follows, that no one is born of Christ but he who lives righteously."
2. "It is at the same time uncertain whether he means Christ or God, when he says that they who are born of him do righteousness...they are born of Christ, who are renewed by his Spirit."
3. "Hence we must conclude that the righteous one spoken of here, who together with the Son is mentioned in the 22d verse, is the Father."

### Philip Schaff:
1. "He first dilates on its glory as a birth of God; as the design of His love; as including both the privileges and the reality of sonship; as awaiting its full dignity at the revelation of Christ; and as inspiring through hope the energy of personal sanctification."
2. "He dwells on the absolute incompatibility between the regenerate life and sin: as the destruction of sin is the object of Christ’s atoning; manifestation; as sin is inconsistent with abiding in Him; and as sin is the mark of communion with the devil."
3. "He passes to the essential connection between regeneration and brotherly love: showing that brotherly love is the mark of regeneration; and, finally, that our love to each other has one supreme standard, the sacrifice of Christ for us."

### Archibald T. Robertson:
1. "If ye know by intuitive or absolute knowledge that Christ is righteous..."
2. "Know ye by experimental knowledge..."
3. "Doing righteousness is proof of the new birth."

### John Gill:
1. "Christ, who is righteous as God in his nature and in his works; and as man in his obedience, life, and conversation."
2. "Everyone that doeth righteousness... intends such an one who with the heart believes unto righteousness, and lays hold by faith, and lives upon the righteousness of Christ for justification; and who performs good works in faith, and from a principle of love."
3. "Such an one is born of him;... being regenerated by his Spirit, having his grace implanted in him, as appears by his faith in his righteousness and by his works of righteousness, as fruits of faith."

### John Wesley:
1. Every one — And none else.Who practiseth righteousness — From a believing, loving heart.Is born of him — For all his children are like himself.
