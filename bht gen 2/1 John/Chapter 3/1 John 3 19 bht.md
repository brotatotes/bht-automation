# 1 John 3:19 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
By loving one another in deed and truth, we can have confident assurance that we are born of God and belong to Jesus Christ. This love is not mere words or superficial, but a genuine, operative love that comes from the Spirit of God within us. Through this love, we have a clear proof of our true faith and our status as children of God. As we walk in this love, we can enjoy the assurance of God's favor and have a peaceful heart before Him.

## Choicest Commentary Quotes:
### Jamieson-Fausset-Brown:
1. "We shall know [the truth], namely, if we fulfil the command."
2. "Having herein the truth radically, we shall be sure not to love merely in word and tongue."
3. "Assurance is designed to be the ordinary experience and privilege of the believer."

### Albert Barnes:
1. "To be of the truth stands opposed to cherishing false and delusive hopes."
2. "The meaning here seems to be, that we shall in this way allay the doubts and trouble of our minds, and produce a state of quiet and peace, to wit, by the evidence that we are of the truth."
3. "A true Christian does not attempt to conceal the fact that there is much for which his own heart and conscience might justly accuse him but he finds, notwithstanding all this, evidence that he is a child of God, and he is persuaded that all will be well."

### Marvin Vincent:
1. "Shall assure [πεισομεν]...Render as A.V., and Rev. as sure."
2. "Before Him [εμπροσθεν αυτου]...before Him we shall assure our heart."
3. "These words are to be kept in mind as the key - note of what follows."

### John Calvin:
1. "If we, in truth, love our neighbors, we have an evidence that we are born of God, who is truth."
2. "Love is accessory or an inferior aid, a prop to our faith, not a foundation on which it rests."
3. "A genuine assurance before God we cannot have, except his Spirit produces in us the fruit of love."

### Archibald T. Robertson:
1. "Shall we know by this, that we are of the truth."
2. "In the very presence of God we shall have confident assurance because God understands us."

### John Gill:
1. "By the saints loving one another in deed and in truth, they know, as the cause is known by the effect, that they are of God."
2. "They are the children of God, and are born of him, since they love those that are, and every like loves its like."
3. "They are of Jesus Christ, who is the way, the truth, and the life; that they belong to him, are his, since they have his Spirit, as appears by his fruits in them, and this, among the rest, love to the brethren."

### John Wesley:
1. "And hereby we know — We have a farther proof by this real, operative love."
2. "That we are of the truth — That we have true faith, that we are true children of God."
3. "And shall assure our hearts before him — Shall enjoy the assurance of his favour, and the 'testimony of a good conscience toward God.'"
