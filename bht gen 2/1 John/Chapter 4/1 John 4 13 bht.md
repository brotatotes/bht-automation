# 1 John 4:13 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
Our understanding of our connection with God is confirmed by the presence of His Spirit within us. This serves as a testimony to our fellowship with Him and the communication of His love and grace to us. Through the indwelling of the Spirit, we are empowered to exercise faith and love towards God, creating a mutual dwelling with Him. Moreover, the gifts of the Spirit given to us further demonstrate God's presence and support in our lives, enabling us to contribute to the growth and benefit of the community of believers.
