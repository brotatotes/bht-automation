# 1 Timothy 4:9 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
Godliness has promises attached to it, a faithful saying confirms. These promises are specifically for those who believe in God, their Savior, in both the present life and the life to come. Therefore, it is important to accept this truth, and let it encourage us in exercising godliness.

## Choicest Commentary Quotes:
### Henry Alford:
1. "Piety has the promise of that life attached to it, according to the well-known Christian saying which follows."
2. "We toil and suffer reproach because we have fixed our hope on the living God, who is the Saviour of all men."
3. "He is the same Saviour towards and of all: but these alone appropriate His salvation."

### Jamieson-Fausset-Brown:
1. "faithful is the saying, "
	&c. confirms the assertion as to the "promise" attached
	to "godliness,"
2. "God
	is the Saviour specially of those that believe" (2 Timothy 2:11- :), both as to "the life that now is," and also as
	to "the life which is to come" (2 Timothy 2:11- :).

### Albert Barnes:
1. This is a faithful saying - see the notes on 1 Timothy 1:15.

### John Calvin:
1. "God supplies his people, in this world, with everything that is necessary for a happy and joyful life; since they are often destitute of all good things, and, on that account, appear to be forsaken by God."
2. "Not satisfied with the simple doctrine, he wards off all opposing temptations by this shield, and in this manner instructs believers to open the door to the grace of God, which our unbelief shuts out."
3. "Undoubtedly if we were willing to receive God’s benefits, he would use greater liberality toward us."

### Philip Schaff:
1. "This is a faithful saying."
2. "At first it might seem as if the words referred to what had immediately preceded."
3. "The rule in all other cases is that they precede the truth to which they refer."

### Archibald T. Robertson:
1. See 1 Timothy 1:15 for these very words, but here the phrase points to the preceding words, not to the following as there. 

### John Gill:
1. "This is a faithful saying,.... A true one, and to be believed, that godliness has such promises annexed to it."
2. "Worthy of all acceptation; by all godly persons, to encourage them to the exercise of godliness."
