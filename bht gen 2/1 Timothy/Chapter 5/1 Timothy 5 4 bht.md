## BHT:
Let these individuals understand the importance of reciprocating the care and support shown to them by their parents. It is their responsibility to provide for their own families, rather than relying on the church for assistance. Neglecting their parents in old age would be an act of ingratitude, while caring for them is a way of showing appreciation for the love and sacrifices bestowed upon them. This act of filial piety is not only commendable but also pleasing and acceptable in the sight of God.
