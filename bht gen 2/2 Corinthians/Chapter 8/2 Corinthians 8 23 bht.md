# 2 Corinthians 8:23 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
Titus is more than just a colleague to the apostle. They share a deep partnership, not only in their shared ministry but also in their hardships and trials. Similarly, the messengers sent by the churches serve not only as preachers of the Gospel but also as caretakers of the needy. They are chosen to faithfully represent Christ and to bring glory to His name through their selfless service. Ultimately, these individuals are instrumental in advancing the glory and honor of Christ.

## Choicest Commentary Quotes:
### Henry Alford:
1. "Whether concerning Titus, he is my partner and my fellow-worker towards you."
2. "Whether our brethren are Apostles of the churches, the glory of Christ."

### Jamieson-Fausset-Brown:
1. "Fellow worker towards you."
2. "Messengers...in the less strict sense."
3. "Sent by the churches, as we are by the Lord."

### Albert Barnes:
1. "If any inquiry is made from any quarter about him, or if it is necessary from any cause to say anything about him, I would say he is my partner."
2. "He partakes with me in preaching the gospel, and in establishing and organizing churches."
3. "They have the entire confidence of the churches, having been selected and appointed by them to a work of labor and responsibility."

### John Calvin:
1. "They are called also the glory of Christ, for this reason, that as he alone is the glory of believers, so he ought also to be glorified by them in return."
2. "Hence, all that excel in piety and holiness are the glory of Christ, because they have nothing but by Christ’s gift."

### Philip Schaff:
1. "Whether any inquire about Titus. ‘Need I say anything in commendation of him?’ he is my partner, and my fellow-worker to you-ward therefore, in relation to the apostle and his work, above the other two."
2. "The messengers of the churches, they are the glory of Christ (as we should say) 'They are an honour to Christ;' their walk and work bring glory to Him. Noble testimony this!"

### Archibald T. Robertson:
1. "He endorses Titus up to the hilt."
2. "He is 'my partner' (κοινωνος εμος) and 'fellow-worker' (συνεργος)."
3. "The glory of Christ (δοξα Χριστου). Financial agents, please observe."

### John Gill:
1. "He [Titus] is my partner; he had been his companion in his travels, a partner with him in preaching the Gospel, as well as in the troubles and persecutions he had met with; and was one with whom he had had sweet communion and fellowship."
2. "They [the messengers] were chosen and sent forth by the churches, not only to preach the Gospel, but particularly to take care of the ministration to the poor saints."
3. "The messengers of them [the churches], on whom the image of Christ was stamped, who faithfully performed the work of Christ, in all things sought his glory and not their own; and in and by whom his glory was displayed, and held forth to others."

### John Wesley:
1. My partner — In my cares and labours.The glory of Christ — Signal instruments of advancing his glory.
