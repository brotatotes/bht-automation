# 2 Thessalonians 1:1 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
Paul, Silvanus, and Timotheus address their letter as a reminder that they are in God, the loving Father who cares for them. This statement emphasizes the intimate relationship between the believers and God. The Church in God and Christ is not just a gathering based on faith, but a community established and built by both the Father and Christ. Furthermore, when God adopts and regenerates us, we are brought into Christ. This introduction highlights the importance of recognizing our position in God and Christ, as well as the divine work of adoption and regeneration.
