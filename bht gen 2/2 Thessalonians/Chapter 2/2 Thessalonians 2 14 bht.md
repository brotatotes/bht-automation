# 2 Thessalonians 2:14 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
God chose you to be saved through the gospel message that we preached, which was accompanied by the power of his Spirit. This calling is not only to believe in the truth, but also to live a holy life, as both faith and holiness are connected to the goal of obtaining the glory of our Lord Jesus Christ.

## Choicest Commentary Quotes:
### Henry Alford:
1. "He (God) called you through our Gospel, in order to acquisition of the glory of our Lord Jesus Christ."
2. "Your sharing in the glory which He has."
3. "The whole context has for its purpose the lot of the Thessalonians as contrasted with that of those spoken of."

### Jamieson-Fausset-Brown:
1. "by our gospel—"through" the Gospel which we preach."
2. "to . . . glory—In it was 'salvation,' that is, deliverance from all evil, of body and soul; here it is positive good, even 'glory', and that 'the glory of our Lord Jesus' Himself, which believers are privileged to share with Him."

### Albert Barnes:
1. "He made the gospel as preached by us the means of calling you to salvation."
2. "God has chosen you to salvation from eternity, and has made the gospel as preached by us the means of carrying that eternal purpose into effect."
3. "That you may partake of the same glory as the Saviour in heaven."

### Marvin Vincent:
1. Our gospel. See on 1 Thessalonians 1:5.

### John Calvin:
1. "For the sons of God are not called otherwise than to the belief of the truth."
2. "He accordingly puts himself forward as a surety, that the Thessalonians may not doubt that the gospel, in which they had been instructed by him, is the safety-bringing voice of God."
3. "And thus it will be a second means of confirmation that he will defend them, as being nothing less than his own inheritance, and, in maintaining their salvation, will stand forward in defense of his own glory."

### Philip Schaff:
1. "Calling, in Paul’s sense, cannot fail or remain barren."
2. "In truth, calling and election are one and the same thing."
3. "The glory of our Lord Jesus Christ...of which all His people are to partake."

### Archibald T. Robertson:
1. "The goal, that is the final salvation..."
2. "God called the Thessalonians through Paul's preaching as he calls men now through the heralds of the Cross..."
3. "Probably correct translation rather than possession."

### John Gill:
1. "Salvation being appointed as the end in the decree of election, and sanctification of the Spirit, and belief of the truth, as means."
2. "The elect in the effectual calling are called to the participation of each of these; first to the one, and then to the other; to grace here, and glory hereafter."
3. "And by this they were called to the obtaining of the glory of our Lord Jesus Christ; not his essential glory, though he will be seen and known in the glory of his person as he is, so far as creatures in a state of perfection will be capable of."

### John Wesley:
1. To which — Faith and holiness.He hath called you by our gospel — That which we preached, accompanied with the power of his Spirit.
