# 2 Timothy 1:1 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
The Apostle Paul, being called by the divine will, emphasized his name and apostleship to establish the authority behind his ministry. The promise of life mentioned refers to the eternal life freely given by God in his grace, which is found in Christ Jesus. As Gospel ministers, it is their duty to guide individuals towards the way of life and salvation through Christ alone.
