# 2 Timothy 2:1 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
Believers are encouraged to be strong in the grace found in Christ Jesus. This means not only having faith in it, but also boldly preaching and defending it against error and heresy. The grace of Christ is the source of strength, and believers should always rely on it and exercise their faith in it.

## Choicest Commentary Quotes:
### Henry Alford:
1. "The grace of Christ, the empowering influence in the Christian life, being necessary for its whole course and progress, is regarded as the element in which it is lived."
2. "The true keeping of the deposit entrusted to thee will involve thy handing it on unimpaired to others, who may in their turn hand it on again."
3. "Scripture has been God’s way of fixing tradition, and rendering it trustworthy at any distance of time; of obviating the very danger which in this Epistle we see so imminent, viz. of one of those teachers, who were links in this chain of transmission, becoming inefficient and transmitting it inadequately."

### Jamieson-Fausset-Brown:
1. "Thou therefore—following my example (2 Timothy 1:8; 2 Timothy 1:12), and that of ONESIPHORUS (2 Timothy 1:16-18), and shunning that of those who forsook me (2 Timothy 1:16-55.1.18- :)."
2. "my son—Children ought to imitate their father."
3. "be strong—literally, 'be invested with power.' Have power, and show thyself to have it; implying an abiding state of power."

### Albert Barnes:
1. "Thou therefore - In view of the fact stated in the previous chapter, that many had turned away from the apostle, and had forsaken the paths of truth."
2. "Be strong in the grace which is in Christ Jesus; - compare the notes at Ephesians 6:10."
3. "The meaning is, Be strong, relying on the grace which the Lord Jesus only can impart."

### Marvin Vincent:
1. "Be strong...be strengthened inwardly." 
2. "Grace is the inward source of strength." 
3. "The association of grace and strength."

### John Calvin:
1. "Be strong in the grace...he intends to shake off sloth and indifference."
2. "What God demands from us by his word he likewise bestows by his Spirit...we are strengthened in the grace which he has given to us."
3. "This kind appellation...tends much to gain the affections, that the doctrine may more effectually obtain admission into the heart."

### Philip Schaff:
1. "Be strong. The Greek verb, passive and not middle, implies at once the being strengthened, and the continuance in the state thus reached."
2. "This process and state find the element of their life in the grace which is found in Christ."

### Archibald T. Robertson:
1. "Be strengthened... keep on being empowered, keep in touch with the power." 
2. "Christ is the dynamo for power only when and while we keep in touch with him."

### John Gill:
1. "he would have him be strong in the grace that is in Christ Jesus;"
2. "to be strong in it, is to preach it boldly, to defend it bravely, and courageously oppose every error and heresy"
3. "grace in Christ is what believers should always have recourse unto, and exercise faith on"
