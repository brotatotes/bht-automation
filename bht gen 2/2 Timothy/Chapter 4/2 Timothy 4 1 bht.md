# 2 Timothy 4:1 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
At his appearing and his kingdom, Christ will judge the quick and the dead. This refers to a future event, the last day, where Christ will appear in person in a most glorious manner. Currently, Christ's kingdom is of a spiritual nature, but it will be more evident in the future through the spread of the Gospel and the revival of godliness. The general judgment, executed with righteousness and impartiality, is certain. Believers should eagerly await Christ's second appearance and focus on both reading and hearing Scripture, without neglecting the ministry of pastors.
