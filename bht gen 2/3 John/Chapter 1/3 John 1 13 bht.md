# 3 John 13 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
The apostle acknowledges that he had many things he desired to communicate, demonstrating the depth and breadth of his intended message. This indicates that there are important matters he wishes to address. His focus is not only on the churches as a whole, but also on addressing specific individuals within those communities. In choosing not to rely on the conventional method of writing with ink and pen, he emphasizes the significance and uniqueness of his message, suggesting that it transcends the limitations of the written word alone.
