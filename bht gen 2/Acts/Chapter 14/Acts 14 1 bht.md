## Choicest Commentary Quotes:
### Henry Alford:
1. "ὁμοῦ, Hesych: not, 'in the same manner,' as Wolf and others."
2. "οὕτως ὥστε, as in E. V.; not ἐγένετο.… ὥστε…, as Vater."
3. "Probably here these are the σεβόμενοι τὸν θεόν, those of the uncircumcised who were more or less attached to the Jewish religion."

### Jamieson-Fausset-Brown:
1. "Though Paul was now the prominent speaker and actor, yet in everything Barnabas went along with him."
2. "A multitude of the Greeks believed— meaning probably the religious proselytes, as opposed to 'the Gentiles' mentioned."

### Albert Barnes:
1. "In Iconium - See the notes on Acts 13:51. In this place, and in Antioch and Lystra, Timothy became acquainted with Paul and his manner of life, 2 Timothy 3:10-11."
2. "So spake - Spake with such power - their preaching was attended so much with the influence of the Spirit."
3. "And also of the Greeks - Probably proselytes from the Greeks, who were in the habit of attending the synagogue."

### John Calvin:
1. "they consider that the Lord had called them upon that condition, that they should do their duty though the whole world and Satan did say nay."
2. "that which was once spoken to Jeremiah is common to all the prophets and ministers of God, 'They shall fight against thee, but they shall not prevail.'"
3. "they carried with them the same courage still; whereby it appeareth that they were not only furnished for one combat, but even for continual warfare."

### Philip Schaff:
1. "The success of Paul’s preaching appears to have been unusually great in this place."
2. "The rapid spread of the doctrines preached by the apostles in Iconium and its neighbourhood."
3. "The reputation of Paul very likely attracted many of the dwellers in Iconium who had no connection with Judaism."

### Archibald T. Robertson:
1. "They entered together."

### John Gill:
1. "Such words, and doctrines of grace, with so much power, authority, and demonstration of the Spirit, with so much plainness, clearness, and evidence, as well as with so much boldness and courage."
2. "These converts laid the foundation of a Gospel church state in this place."
3. "Here, according to the Roman martyrology, Tryphena and Tryphosa, mentioned in Romans 16:12 heard the Apostle Paul preach; and here the famous virgin and martyr, Thecla, was converted."
