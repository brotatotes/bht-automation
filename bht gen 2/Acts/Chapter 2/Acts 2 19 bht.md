## BHT:
The wonders and signs mentioned signify the miraculous occurrences and prodigies that preceded the downfall of Jerusalem. These extraordinary events served as warnings of God's displeasure and impending judgment. The description of bloodshed, fire, and pillars of smoke symbolizes the warfare, destruction, and rising smoke that accompanied this judgment. It reminds us that rejecting God's grace can result in severe consequences.
