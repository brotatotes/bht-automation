# Acts 20:31 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
The Apostle Paul's unwavering commitment to warn and admonish everyone day and night is truly remarkable. He tirelessly devote himself to this task, shedding tears and offering heartfelt pleas to ensure that no one is left unguarded against false teachings. His diligence serves as a powerful example for all who proclaim the Gospel, inspiring us to be vigilant and diligent in warning others against spiritual dangers.

## Choicest Commentary Quotes:
### Henry Alford:
1. "ὅτι is only (retf.) used by Paul."
2. "νουθετῶν (reff.) is used only by Paul."
3. "On the three years spoken of in this verse, see note, ch. Acts 19:10."

### Jamieson-Fausset-Brown:
1. "I ceased not to warn everyone night and day with tears—What an appeal to be able to make!"
2. "And if this was an apostle's part, how much more a pastor's!"

### Albert Barnes:
1. "Therefore watch - Matthew 24:42. In view of the dangers which beset yourselves...be on your guard."
2. "Remember - Recall my counsels and admonitions in reference to these dangers."
3. "I ceased not - I continued to do it. To warn - To admonish; to place before the mind...setting the danger and duty of each individual before him."

### Marvin Vincent:
1. "Watch [γρηγορειτε]. To warn [νουθετων]."
2. "Its fundamental idea is the well-intentioned seriousness with which one would influence the mind and disposition of another by advice, admonition, warning, putting right, according to circumstances."

### John Calvin:
1. "They have need of great attention to beware; and that it is an unseemly thing that they should be wearied who had seen his singular patience by the space of three years."
2. "He speaketh of his tears, which did add no small efficacy to his exhortations."
3. "Their studies must be kindled, not only with this speech which he now maketh, but that it is meet that they remember those often exhortations which he did continually beat in by the space of three years, and that with many tears."

### Philip Schaff:
1. "Therefore watch, and remember, that by the space of three years I ceased not to warn every one night and day with tears."
2. "The approving words...tell us that the earnest wishes and the affectionate pleadings of the apostle were not in vain."
3. "I know thy works, and thy labour, and thy patience, and how thou canst not bear them which are evil...and hast not fainted."

### Archibald T. Robertson:
1. "Because of which thing. The very command of Jesus concerning the perils before his return."

### John Gill:
1. "And remember that by the space of three years;...he was constantly and continually cautioning them against false teachers."
2. "shedding tears at the thoughts of what mischief would be done, and how many souls would be ruined by them; which gives a lively idea of the apostle, and his ministry, of his affection, zeal, and diligence."
3. "very worthy of the imitation of all the preachers of the Gospel."

### John Wesley:
1. I ceased not to warn every one night and day — This was watching indeed! Who copies after this example?
