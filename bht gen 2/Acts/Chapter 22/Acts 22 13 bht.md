### Marvin Vincent:
1. "Stood [επιστας] . More correctly, as Rev., "standing by [επι] ."
2. "Receive thy sight [αναβλεψον] . Better, look up."

### Philip Schaff:

3. "The sight came back, and 'he looked upon Ananias.'"
