# Acts 3:25 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
Ye are the children of the prophets and the covenant, heirs of the prophecies and the promises. You belong to the lineage that received the prophecies about the Messiah and the blessings that come through Him. Just as the covenant was made with our fathers, you are now heirs of it. The blessings of God extend to all the chosen people, regardless of their background, through Christ. Take hold of this promise and embrace the richness of your spiritual inheritance.

## Choicest Commentary Quotes:
### Henry Alford:
1. "He applies this to them, as being inheritors of the promises. They were descendants, according to the flesh, and fellow-partakers, according to the spirit."
2. "For a full comment on this promise made to Abraham, see Galatians 3:16."
3. "This is cited freely from the LXX, which for οἱ πατριαί has τὰ ἔθνη."

### Jamieson-Fausset-Brown:

	covenant—and so the natural heirs of its promises. 
	in thy seed, &c.—(See
	on  :-, &c.).

### Albert Barnes:
1. "Ye are the sons of the prophets...They professed to follow the prophets as their teachers and guides." 
2. "Ye are the sons of the covenant; that is, you are of the posterity of Abraham, with whom the covenant was made."
3. "The promise to which Peter refers in the passage before us is in Genesis 22:18; Genesis 12:3."

### Marvin Vincent:
1. "Covenant [διαθηκης] . See on Matthew 26:28."
2. "The noun covenant is derived from the verb diatiqnmi, originally to distribute or arrange."
3. "Hence, to arrange or settle mutually; to make a covenant with."

### John Calvin:
1. "God omitteth nothing whereby he may bring us unto himself."
2. "We must also note diligently this course of teaching, where Peter showeth that the gospel is assigned and appointed unto the Jews."
3. "Let us not suffer this promise to be wrung from us, which is, as it were, a solemn declaration, whereby the Lord maketh us his heirs together with the fathers."

### Philip Schaff:
1. "Children in this sense is a very common expression in Hebrew thought."
2. "They were children of the prophets, for the promise of their prophets was in the first instance to them."
3. "They were children of the covenant as the heirs of Abraham, with whom God made a covenant when He chose him and his descendants for a peculiar people."

### Archibald T. Robertson:
1. "The covenant which God made... Literally, 'the covenant which God covenanted.'"
2. "The covenant (agreement between two)... was with Abraham and repeated at various times."
3. "In Hebrews 9:15-18 the word is used both for covenant and will."

### John Gill:
1. "Ye are the children of the prophets...to them belonged the prophecies of the Old Testament concerning the Messiah, and the promises of him." 
2. "They were heirs of them;and of the covenant which God made with our fathers."
3. "All the chosen of God among the Gentiles, in every nation, and of every kindred and family among them, are blessed in Christ, with all spiritual blessings."

### John Wesley:
1. Ye are the sons of the prophets and of the covenant — That in, heirs of the prophecies. To you properly, as the first heirs, belong the prophecies and the covenant. Genesis 12:3.
