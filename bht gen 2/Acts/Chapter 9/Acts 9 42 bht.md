## Choicest Commentary Quotes:
### Jamieson-Fausset-Brown:
1. "Peter put them all forth, and kneeled down - the one in imitation of his Master's way; the other, in striking contrast with it." 
2. "The kneeling became the lowly servant, but not the Lord Himself, of whom it is never once recorded that he knelt in the performance of a miracle."
3. "The graphic minuteness of detail here imparts to the narrative an air of charming reality."

### Albert Barnes:
1. "And many believed ... - A similar effect followed when Jesus raised up Lazarus."
2. "This was the first miracle of this kind that was performed by the apostles."
3. "It was not merely a work of benevolence, in restoring to life one who contributed largely to the comfort of the poor, but it was a means of extending and establishing, as it was designed doubtless to do, the kingdom of the Saviour."

### John Calvin:
1. "Now appear manifold fruits of the miracle, for God comforted the poor, a godly matron was restored to the Church, in whose death it suffered great loss."
2. "Many are called unto the faith; for although Peter were [had been] a minister of so great power, yet he keepeth not the men in [on] himself; but doth rather direct them unto Christ."

### John Gill:
1. "The report of such a miracle, and wrought upon a person of note, was soon spread all over the place."
2. "[...] throughout which the fame of this miracle might pass."
3. "Many believed in the Lord Jesus Christ, whom Peter preached, and the saints in Joppa professed, and in whose name, and by whose power, this miracle was done."
