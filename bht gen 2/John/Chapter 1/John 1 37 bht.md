## Choicest Commentary Quotes:
### Henry Alford:
1. "We must not understand ἠκολ. in the narrower sense which it bears when they left all and followed Him." 
2. "Here only of mechanical going after Him." 
3. "βουλόμενοι πεῖραν λαβεῖν αὐτοῦ."

### Albert Barnes:
1. "This shows that John was sincere; that he was not desirous of forming a party or of building up a sect; that he was willing that all those whom he had attracted to himself by his ministry should become followers of Christ."
2. "The object of ministers should not be to build up their own interests or to extend their own fame. It is to point men to the Saviour."
3. "We should not delay nor debate the matter, but leave at once all our old teachers, guides and companions, and follow the Lamb of God."

### Philip Schaff:
1. "The witness of the Baptist has its proper effect, an effect, we cannot doubt, foreseen and designed by himself." 
2. "Those who listen to it turn from him, and follow Jesus."

### Archibald T. Robertson:
1. "Heard him speaking" (kind of indirect discourse).
2. John had disciples (μαθητα, learners).
3. They followed Jesus (ηκολουθησαν τω Ιησου).
4. These two disciples of the Baptist (Andrew and John) took him at his word and acted on it.
5. John the Baptist had predicted and portrayed the Messiah, had baptized him, had interpreted him, and now for the second time had identified him.

### John Gill:
1. "Faith in Christ came by hearing them."
2. "They found their affections, and the desires of their souls, to be after Christ."
3. "They followed Jesus... in order to get some acquaintance with him, and receive some instructions from him."

### John Wesley:
1. They followed Jesus — They walked after him, but had not the courage to speak to him.
