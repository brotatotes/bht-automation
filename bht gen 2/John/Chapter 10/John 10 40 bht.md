## BHT:
Jesus goes back to the place where John first baptized, referred to as beyond Jordan in the region called Peroea. This location holds significance as it is where John bore witness to Jesus. Jesus takes this opportunity to continue His ministry, preach the Gospel, and work miracles. His purpose is not self-indulgence but to bring many souls to believe in Him.
