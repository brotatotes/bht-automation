## BHT:
In this pivotal moment, Pilate, disclaiming all responsibility, delivers Jesus to the chief priests to be crucified, knowing that nothing will satisfy them but his death. Pilate passes sentence on him and gives him up to their will, recognizing that Jesus, though innocent, must bear the insatiable cruelty of the people. Throughout it all, Jesus displays utmost patience, meekness, and resignation in the face of his unjust fate.
