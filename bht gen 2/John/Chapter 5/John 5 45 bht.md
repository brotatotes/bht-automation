## BHT:
Though Christ does not come to condemn or accuse, there is one that does - the Law itself. The Law, in which the Jews placed their hope, stands as an accuser, charging them with the violation of its precepts. Sin, as a transgression of the law, brings about the accusations brought forth by the very law they have trusted in.
