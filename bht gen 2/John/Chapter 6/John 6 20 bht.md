# John 6:20 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
We learn from this passage that in the presence of Christ, we have every reason to be confident and at ease, for He alone is our source of comfort and security. Believers who have received Him as their Savior can boldly face any challenge, for they have been delivered from death to life. Just as Jesus calmed the storm and reassured his disciples, He is able to remove the cause of our fears and give us peace.
