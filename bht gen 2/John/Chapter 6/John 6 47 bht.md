# John 6:47 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
Those who believe in Jesus are assured of eternal life. This truth is emphasized by the repeated phrase verily, verily, which indicates certainty and dependability. Through faith, believers not only have the promise of everlasting life, but they also experience glimpses of this life in their souls. Their faith grants them a foretaste of what is to come, assuring them of the future fulfillment of eternal life.

### John Calvin:
1. "For we are taught by these words that it is when we believe in Christ that God is made known to us; for then do we begin to see, as in a mirror, or as in a bright and lively image, God who was formerly invisible."

3. "I acknowledge that Christ is not eaten but by faith; but the reason is, because we receive him by faith, that he may dwell in us, and that we may be made partakers of him, and thus may be one with him. To eat him, therefore, is an effect or work of faith."

3. "He desires to express in the most forcible manner the state of mind which formed the strongest possible contrast to that of the Jews."
