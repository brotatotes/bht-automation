# John 8:32 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
The truth shall make you free by freeing you from guilt, sin, misery, and Satan. The Spirit of God writes the truth in our hearts, leading us to experience freedom from the bondage of sin. It is through the truth that we are set free.

## Choicest Commentary Quotes:
### Henry Alford:
1. "The knowing of the truth answers to the feeding on Christ; is the inner realization of it in the man."
2. "True freedom from all fear and error and bondage comes in the continuing increase of this knowing of the truth."

### Albert Barnes:
1. "The doctrines of the true religion shall make you free - that is, it will free you from the slavery of evil passions, corrupt propensities, and grovelling views."
2. "The condition of a sinner is that of a captive or a slave to sin."
3. "The effect of the gospel is to break this hard bondage to sin and to set the sinner free."

### John Calvin:
1. "Wherefore, whatever progress any of us have made in the Gospel, let him know that he needs new additions."
2. "It is the same unvarying truth which Christ teaches his followers from the beginning to the end, but on those who were at first enlightened by him, as it were with small sparks, he at length pours a full light."
3. "The truth shall make you free. He commends the knowledge of the Gospel from the fruit which we derive from it, or — which is the same thing — from its effect, namely, that it restores us to freedom."

### Philip Schaff:
1. "If they shall abide in the word of Jesus, it will be shown that they have begun a true discipleship, and the word in which they abide shall make known to them the truth."
2. "He came as Saviour, not as Teacher only... Here the figure is changed from that of future death to that of present and continued bondage: 'the truth' shall be the means of giving freedom."
3. "Such appropriation of the truth found in the words of Jesus is but another representation of faith in Him who is the Giver of freedom."

### Archibald T. Robertson:
1. "Truth is one of the marks of Christ... But it will be for them knowledge to be learned by doing God's will."
2. "The freedom of which Jesus here speaks is freedom from the slavery of sin... This freedom is won alone by Christ."
3. "Freedom (intellectual, moral, spiritual) is only attainable when we are set free from darkness, sin, ignorance, superstition and let the Light of the World shine on us and in us."

### John Gill:
1. "they should have a larger knowledge of it, while others are ever learning, and never come to the knowledge of the truth"
2. "the spirit of truth should lead them into all truth, and cause them to grow and increase in Gospel light and knowledge"
3. "the truth shall make you free; from ignorance and error, and the prejudices of education, under which the whole nation laboured, and from the thraldom of the law."

### John Wesley:
1. "The truth...shall make you free - From guilt, sin, misery, Satan."
2. "The Spirit of God...written in your hearts."
3. "The truth...shall set you free."
