## Choicest Commentary Quotes:
### Albert Barnes:
1. "Fear not, Mary - Do not be alarmed at this appearance of an angel."
2. "He only comes to announce to you good tidings."
3. "Thou hast found favour with God - Eminent favor or mercy in being selected to be the mother of the Messiah."

### Marvin Vincent:
1. "Grace [χαριν] . From the same root as cairw, to rejoice. Primarily that which gives joy or pleasure; and hence outward beauty, loveliness, something which delights the beholder." 
2. "As a beautiful or agreeable sentiment felt and expressed toward another; kindness, favor, good will." 
3. "The higher Christian signification, based on the emphasis of freeness in the gift or favor, and, as commonly in New Testament, denoting the free, spontaneous, absolute loving kindness of God toward men, and so contrasted with debt, law, works, sin."

### John Calvin:
1. "Whenever the feeblest ray of the Divine glory bursts upon us, we cannot avoid being alarmed."
2. "Fear gives rise to trembling, until God manifests himself as a Father."
3. "Thou hast found favor, is used by Luke instead of, 'God has been merciful to thee:’ for a person is said to find favor, not when he has sought it, but when it has been freely offered to him."

### Philip Schaff:
1. Luke 1:30.  Favor, or, ‘grace.’ This verse also opposes the dogma of the Immaculate Conception.
	

### Archibald T. Robertson:
1. "To find favour is a common O.T. phrase."
2. "Χαρις is a very ancient and common word with a variety of applied meanings."
3. "It is a favourite word for Christianity, the Gospel of the grace of God in contrast with law or works."

### John Gill:
1. "He calls her by her name, signifying that she was well known to him, as the saints are to the ministering angels, who are often sent unto them, encamp about them, and do them many good offices."
2. "He had no ill design upon her, nor brought any ill news to her."
3. "For thou has found favor, or 'found grace with God'; and what that particular grace and favor was, is expressed in the following verses."
