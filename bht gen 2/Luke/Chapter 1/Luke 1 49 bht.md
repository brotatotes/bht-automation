# Luke 1:49 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
Mary acknowledges the greatness of God's works, recognizing that He is powerful and has done great things for her. These works are not performed by any ordinary power, but by the almighty power of God Himself. Consequently, His name is to be held in reverence and holiness, for it is through His name that these wondrous acts are accomplished.
