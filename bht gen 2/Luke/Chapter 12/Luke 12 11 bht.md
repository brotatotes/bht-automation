# Luke 12:11 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
When faced with the need to give a defense, do not be anxious or concerned about how you will respond, or even about the manner in which you will defend your beliefs. Let go of any worry or apprehension about what to say or how to say it. Remember that in moments like these, it is not about the eloquence of your words, but rather the sincerity and faithfulness of your heart that will shine through. Trust in the guidance of the Holy Spirit and rely on God's wisdom to speak through you.
