# Luke 12:42 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
God emphasizes the value of faithfulness and wisdom in those who serve in His family. These dedicated stewards, such as the ministers of His word, are entrusted with the responsibility of managing the resources and provisions of God's household. Their faithfulness and wisdom are essential as they govern the church, which is considered God's household, according to the principles and teachings of Christ. This passage highlights the importance of upholding these qualities in leaders and servants who play a role in the care and guidance of God's people.

## Choicest Commentary Quotes:
### Henry Alford:
1. "Our Lord does not answer the question directly, but proceeds with His discourse, so as to furnish it with an answer; viz. that in its highest sense it applies to his Apostles and ministers, inasmuch as to them most has been given as the οἰκονόμοι but that its application is gradationally downwards through all those who know their Master’s will, even to the lowest, whose measure both of responsibility and of reward is more limited." (quote 1)
2. "For the comment on Luk 12:42-46 see on Matthew 24:45-51." (quote 2) 
3. "Notice that  ἀπίστων here = ὑποκριτῶν in Matt." (quote 3)

### Jamieson-Fausset-Brown:
1. "Fidelity is the first requisite in a servant, wisdom the next."
2. "To you certainly in the first instance, representing the 'stewards' of the 'household' I am about to collect, but generally to all 'servants' in My house."
3. "The steward, whose it was to distribute to the servants their allotted portion of food, will deem fit to be made."

### Marvin Vincent:
1. "That faithful and wise steward."
2. "Household [θεραπειας] . From its original meaning of waiting on, attendance (Luke 9:11), it comes to mean the retinue of attendants; the body of household servants."
3. "In due season. At the appointed time for distributing rations."

### Archibald T. Robertson:
1. "Peter is this wise steward, each of the Twelve is, anyone is who acts thus." 
2. "A steward is house manager... Each man is a steward in his own responsibilities."
3. "Late word from σιτομετρεω (Genesis 47:12) for the Attic τον σιτον μετρεω, to measure the food, the rations."

### John Gill:
1. "Such are stewards in Christ's family, they are entrusted with the stores and provisions of his house, and 'faithfulness' and 'wisdom' are requisite in them."
2. "Christ's 'household', or family, is his church, over which the ministers of the Gospel are appointed 'rulers', to govern the house according to the laws of Christ."
3. "They are faithful who give out the whole portion allotted, without adulterating it, or keeping back any part of it; and they are wise, who deliver it to them in proper time and season."

### John Wesley:
1. "Who is that faithful and wise steward...the ministers of his word."
2. "Whom his lord shall make ruler over his household - For his wisdom and faithfulness."
