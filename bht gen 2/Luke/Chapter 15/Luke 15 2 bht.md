# Luke 15:2 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
Jesus welcomes sinners into his presence with open arms, inviting them into his love and protection. He not only shares meals with them, but also offers them spiritual nourishment by allowing them to partake in the provisions of his house and feast on the bread of life. In this intimate communion, he delights in the exercise of the very graces that he has graciously bestowed upon them. By receiving sinners into his fellowship, Jesus demonstrates his loving desire for them to experience true communion with him and to live by faith in him.

1. "What a truth of unspeakable preciousness do their lips, as on other occasions, unconsciously utter."
