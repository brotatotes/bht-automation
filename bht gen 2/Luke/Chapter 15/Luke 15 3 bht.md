# Luke 15:3 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
These parables demonstrate how God views and receives sinners, contrasting with the judgmental attitude of the Pharisees and scribes. The lost sheep, the lost coin, and the prodigal son all represent the immense value that God places on each individual and His relentless pursuit to seek out and restore those who have gone astray. These stories emphasize that God passionately desires to reconcile with sinners, rejoicing over their return and welcoming them into His loving embrace.
