## Choicest Commentary Quotes:
### Marvin Vincent:
1. "Understanding [συνεσει] . From sunihmi, to bring together. Hence that quality of mind which combines : understanding not only of facts, but of facts in their mutual relations."
2. "the love of a well pondered and duly considered resolution which determines the whole person; the love which clearly understands itself" [χρεμερ].

### John Calvin:
1. "They reckoned it a miracle, that a child should frame his questions with such correctness and propriety."
2. "They heard Christ, and thus acted the part rather of scholars than of teachers."
3. "He already began to tax their perverse way of teaching."

### Philip Schaff:
1. "His answers...indicates the wisdom of His words."
2. "There is nothing premature, forced, or unbecoming His age, and yet a degree of wisdom and an intensity of interest in religion, which rises far above a purely human youth."

### Archibald T. Robertson:
1. "Were amazed (εξισταντο). Imperfect indicative middle, descriptive of their continued and repeated astonishment."
2. "The boy had a holy thirst for knowledge (Plummer), and he used a boy's way of learning."
3. "His answers (ταις αποκρισεσιν αυτου). It is not difficult to ask hard questions, but this boy had astounding answers to their questions, revealing his amazing intellectual and spiritual growth."

### John Gill:
1. "All in the sanhedrim, both the doctors, and their disciples, were amazed, at his understanding."
2. "His answers...were made with so much wisdom and judgment, that it was surprising in one of his years."
