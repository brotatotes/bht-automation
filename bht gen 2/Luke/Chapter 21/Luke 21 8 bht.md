# Luke 21:8 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
Take heed that ye be not deceived, for many shall come in my name, saying, I am the Christ, warns Jesus. The seducers will deceive many by proclaiming themselves as the Messiah and misleading people with false teachings. However, Jesus assures that the time is near when He will deliver His followers from all their enemies. These deceptive words should not be followed, for they lead to destruction.
