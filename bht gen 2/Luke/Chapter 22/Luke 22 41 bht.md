### Philip Schaff:
1. "Withdrew himself, lit, ‘was himself withdrawn.’ Drawn by internal anguish, some suppose."
2. "About a stone’s east. Not so far as to be out of hearing. This was probably the distance from the three disciples (Matthew, Mark), not from the main body, since the next clause refers to what took place in His solitude, and Luke 22:45-46, to the three disciples."

3. "Prayed (προσηυχετο). Imperfect middle, was praying, kept on praying."
