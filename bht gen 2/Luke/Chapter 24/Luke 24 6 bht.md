# Luke 24:6 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
Remember how he spake to you, saying, The Son of man must be delivered. These words serve as a reminder of what Jesus had previously said to them before his crucifixion. It is interesting to note that Jesus never refers to himself as the Son of man after his resurrection, indicating the unique significance of this title in relation to his earthly mission.

## Choicest Commentary Quotes:
### Jamieson-Fausset-Brown:

### Philip Schaff:
1. "Remember, etc. This they had forgotten naturally enough in the circumstances."
2. "When he was yet in Galilee, i.e., with them in Galilee, their home."
3. "This verse has occasioned difficulty, in view of the fact that according to Matthew and Mark Galilee was spoken of by the angel in a different connection. But we suppose that this reminder preceded the direction of Mark 10:7."

### Archibald T. Robertson:
1. "He is not here, but is risen." 
2. "The words are genuine...in Mark 16:6; Matthew 28:7."
3. "Jesus plainly foretold this fact."

### John Gill:
1. "He is not here, but is risen" - Matthew 28:6
2. "Remember how he spake unto you when he was yet in Galilee" - Matthew 17:22
3. "These women that followed him from Galilee were along with the disciples"

### John Wesley:
1. "Remember how he spake to you, saying, The Son of man must be delivered."
2. "This is only a repetition of the words which our Lord had spoken to them before his passion."
3. "But it is observable, he never styles himself the Son of man after his resurrection."
