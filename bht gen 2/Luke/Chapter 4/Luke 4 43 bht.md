## BHT:
Jesus explained that it was his duty to preach the kingdom of God not only in Capernaum but also in other cities. He emphasized that the preaching of the Gospel was a crucial aspect of his work, encompassing the doctrines and ordinances of the kingdom of God on earth, as well as the preparation for and attainment of the kingdom of glory. Jesus declared that spreading the message of the kingdom was part of his divine commission and the purpose for which he was sent by the Father.

### Archibald T. Robertson:
1. "Jesus felt the urge to go with the work of evangelism 'to the other cities also,' to all, not to a favoured few."
2. "Christ is the great Apostle of God to men."

### John Gill:
1. "I must preach the kingdom of God to other cities also; as well as to Capernaum; and though they did not express their desire of his continuance with them, in order to preach the Gospel to them; yet Christ signifies, that a principal part of his work lay in preaching the things concerning the kingdom of God, the Gospel dispensation on earth, the doctrines and ordinances of it..."
2. "...as well as the things which relate to the kingdom of glory; as what is the saints meetness for it, their regeneration, and their right unto it, which lies in his righteousness..."
3. "...and that as he had preached these things at Capernaum, there was a necessity upon him to preach them in other cities of Galilee and Judea: for therefore am I sent; as he was by his heavenly Father, and had himself also undertook and engaged to do it."
