# Luke 5:18 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
Four men brought a paralyzed man and sought ways to bring him into the house where Jesus was, laying him at his feet in hopes of moving his compassion and receiving a cure for his condition. This man's paralysis was described as palsy in Matthew 9:2 and paralytic in Mark 2:3, the latter being the technical medical term used by Luke, in line with the usage of other medical writers. These passages highlight the efforts made by the four men to bring their friend to Jesus, emphasizing their faith and determination to seek healing for the paralyzed man.
