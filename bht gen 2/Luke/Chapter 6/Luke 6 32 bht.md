## BHT:
Luke 6:31 prompts us to reconsider the nature of our gratitude and grace. It urges us to move beyond mere reciprocation and conditional love, as even sinners are capable of such acts. Our Lord regards this form of love as a lower expression of natural virtue, unworthy of receiving genuine thanks. Therefore, true grace and gratitude should transcend these limitations, reflecting the transformative power of God's grace within us. It calls us to a higher standard, one that goes beyond what is expected or deserved.
