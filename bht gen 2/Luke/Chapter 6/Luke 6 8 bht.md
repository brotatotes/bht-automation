### Marvin Vincent:
1. He knew [ηδει] . Imperfect. He was all along aware. Thoughts [διαλογισμους] . See on James 2:4; Matthew 14:19.

### John Calvin:
1. "They had openly declared by their language what was in their minds." 
2. "Christ replies not to their secret thoughts, but to express words." 
3. "Christ discerned their secret thoughts; for they did not openly avow their designs."

### Archibald T. Robertson:
1. "Jesus, in contrast to these spies, read their intellectual processes like an open book." 
2. "Christ worked right out in the open where all could see." 
3. "It was a moment of excitement when the man stepped forth there before them all."

### John Gill:
1. "Being the omniscient God; though they had said nothing of their intentions, he knew what they designed..."
2. "And said to the man which had the withered hand, rise up, and stand forth in the midst."
3. "He rose up from his seat, and stood up in the midst of the synagogue, and of the people, that he might be seen of all."
