## BHT:
As Christ descended into the waters of the Jordan, He was baptized by John and emerged from the immersion, symbolizing a new beginning. The Spirit, appearing as a dove, descended upon Him, signifying the presence and empowerment of God. This image of the dove as representative of the Holy Spirit fittingly captures His purity, gentleness, and peace.
