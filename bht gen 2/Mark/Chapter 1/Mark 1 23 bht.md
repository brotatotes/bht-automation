In their synagogue, there was a man with an unclean spirit, someone who was completely under the power of the impure spirit. This evil spirit, often associated with demons, signifies a state of estrangement from God. The man cried out, overwhelmed by the presence and authority of Jesus, and fearful of being cast out of the person he possessed.
