### Archibald T. Robertson:
1. "The sabbath ended at sunset and so the people were now at liberty to bring their sick to Jesus."
2. "The news about the casting out of the demon and the healing of Peter's mother-in-law had spread all over Capernaum."

### John Gill:
1. "At which time the Jews' sabbath was ended; for this was a sabbath day, when according to them, it was not lawful to heal."
2. "The last of the two evening days being come, and the sun set, the sabbath was over; and therefore being under no restraint on account of that, they brought unto him all that were diseased."
3. "And them that were possessed with devils. The Persic version renders it 'epileptics', such as were troubled with the falling sickness, as many of those were, whose bodies the devils possessed."

### John Wesley:
