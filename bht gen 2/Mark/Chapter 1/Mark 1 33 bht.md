The people of Capernaum, having witnessed Jesus' power and authority in the synagogue, were eager to see what other miracles He would perform. ### Archibald T. Robertson:
1. "The whole city was gathered together there. Mark alone mentions this vivid detail. He is seeing with Peter's eyes again." 
2. "Peter no doubt watched the beautiful scene with pride and gratitude as Jesus stood in the door and healed the great crowds in the glory of that sunset." 
3. "All sorts of sick folk came and were healed."

### John Gill:
1. "The inhabitants of the city of Capernaum...got together in great crowds about the door of the house of Simon and Andrew." 
2. "Having either heard of, or seen the dispossession of the unclean spirit in the synagogue...they were willing to see what miraculous cures might be wrought by Christ."
3. "Being willing to see... the diseased and possessed that were brought to him."

### John Wesley:
1. "And the whole city was gathered together at the door - O what a fair prospect was here!"
2. "Who could then have imagined that all these blossoms would die away without fruit?"
