# Mark 10:50 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
When Bartimeus heard that Jesus was near, he sprang up, casting away his garment as he rushed to meet him. This detail is a sign of an eyewitness account, describing Bartimeus' urgency to approach Jesus without any hindrances. Bartimeus did not stop to worry about the cloak that might be lost; his focus was solely on reaching Jesus. This account emphasizes the transformative power of encountering Christ, as we are called to cast off our old ways and embrace a new life in Him.

### John Gill:
1. "Such who are effectually called by the grace of Christ... put off the old man, as to the former conversation, being called by an holy God, with an holy calling, to holiness in heart and life."
2. "These also cast away the garment of their own righteousness... and come nakedly to Christ, for righteousness, and renounce their own in point of justification, that being an hinderance to their coming to him for his."
3. "The Gospel reveals a better righteousness to them than their own, more suitable to them, who are called from the dunghill, to sit among princes, and to inherit the throne of glory."
