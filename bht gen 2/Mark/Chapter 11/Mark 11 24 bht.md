# Mark 11:24 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
Believe that you have already received what you asked for, for this assurance is meant to encourage prayer and is especially important for miraculous and extraordinary requests. Be fully convinced in your belief, as if you have already received it, and it shall be granted to you. This level of faith is powerful and it is the essence of praying with faith.

## Choicest Commentary Quotes:
### Henry Alford:
1. "Believe that when you asked, you received, and the fulfilment shall come."

### Jamieson-Fausset-Brown:
1. "This verse only generalizes the assurance of Mark 11:23; which seems to show that it was designed for the special encouragement of evangelistic and missionary efforts..."
2. "...this is a directory for prevailing prayer in general."

### Marvin Vincent:
1. Receive [ελαβετε] . More lit., received. Rev., have received.

### Archibald T. Robertson:
1. "Believe that ye have received them (πιστευετε οτ ελαβετε). That is the test of faith, the kind that sees the fulfillment before it happens."
2. "Ελαβετε is second aorist active indicative, antecedent in time to πιστευετε, unless it be considered the timeless aorist when it is simultaneous with it."
3. "For this aorist of immediate consequence see John 15:6."

### John Gill:
1. "For encouragement in prayer more particularly, without which nothing should be attempted, and especially which is above the power of nature, and is of a miraculous kind."
2. "Be as much assured of having them, as if you had already received them, and you shall have them."
3. "And great faith it is so to believe; and this is the prayer of faith."
