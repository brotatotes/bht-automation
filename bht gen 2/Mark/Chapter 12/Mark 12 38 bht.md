Beware of the scribes who love to go in long clothing and seek salutations in marketplaces. Jesus sternly warns against these individuals, recognizing the necessity to caution his followers repeatedly. Their desire to display their supposed importance through distinct attire and extravagant greetings is an embodiment of their pride.
