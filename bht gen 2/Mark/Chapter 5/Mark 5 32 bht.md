# Mark 5:32 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
Christ, knowing all things, was well aware of who she was even before she approached Him. His purpose in looking around was not to satisfy His curiosity, but rather to publicly acknowledge and commend her faith and actions. By observing her, He showed His satisfaction with her belief and wanted to encourage others to have the same kind of trust in Him. His intention was not to draw attention to Himself, but to highlight her faith and inspire others to place their confidence in Him.

3. "Not in an ostentatious way, to gain glory to himself, but to commend her faith and encourage others in the exercise of it on him."
