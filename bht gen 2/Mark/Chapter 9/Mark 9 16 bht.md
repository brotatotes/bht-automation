### Jamieson-Fausset-Brown:
1. "Ere they had time to reply, the father of the boy, whose case had occasioned the dispute, himself steps forward and answers the question; telling a piteous tale of deafness, and dumbness, and fits of epilepsy."

### Philip Schaff:
1. Mark 9:16.  Asked them. Probably the scribes. The opposition was thus transferred from the disciples to our Lord.
	 What question ye with them? About what, what is the subject of discussion?
	

### Archibald T. Robertson:
