### John Calvin:
1. "This scarcity of teaching indicated a wretched state of disorder."
2. "...he had been commanded to discharge the duties of a public teacher, and was therefore bound to look upon all the Jews, for the time being, as belonging to the flock of God and to the Church..."

### Philip Schaff:
1. "His compassion manifested itself in healing their sick, and in giving them instruction."

### Archibald T. Robertson:

### John Gill:
1. "And Jesus went forth,.... Either from the mountain where he sat with his disciples, John 6:3 or out of the desert, where he had retired for secrecy; or out of the ship, which seems best, the company having got thither before his landing." 
2. "And was moved with compassion toward them: partly on account of their bodily infirmities, which were very many and great; and partly on account of the bad situation they were in, through want of spiritual pastors to feed them with the bread of life; for Mark gives this as the reason, moving his compassion, 'because they were as sheep, not having a shepherd.'"
3. "And he healed their sick; which they brought along with them, and that without the use of any medicine, by a word speaking: so that as the former phrase gives proof of his humanity, this attests his divinity."
