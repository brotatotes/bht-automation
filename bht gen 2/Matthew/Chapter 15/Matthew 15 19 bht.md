### Jamieson-Fausset-Brown:
1. "The first shape which the evil that is in the heart takes, when it begins actively to stir, is that of 'considerations' or 'reasonings' on certain suggested actions."

### Marvin Vincent:
1. "For all good and evil, whether in the body or in human nature, originates, as he declared, in the soul, and overflows from thence, as from the head into the eyes; and therefore, if the head and body are to be well, you must begin by curing the soul. That is the first thing."
2. "Thoughts, or disputings, like the captious questioning of the Pharisees about washing hands."

### John Calvin:
1. "Out of the heart of man proceeds all that is sinful and that corrupts by its pollution."
2. "All sins proceed from the wicked and corrupt affections of the heart."
3. "An unholy heart pollutes the eyes by making them the ministers, or organs, of wicked desires."

### Philip Schaff:
1. "For out of the heart proceed evil thoughts, i.e., reasonings, purposes, not mere notions."

### John Gill:
1. "For out of the heart proceed evil thoughts....which take their rise from, and are devised, and forged, in the corrupt heart of man." 
2. "Murders; inveterate hatred of men's persons, malice prepense, schemes to take away life, all angry and wrathful words, and actual effusion of man's blood."
3. "Adulteries; uncleanness committed between married persons, both in thought, and deed: fornications; unlawful copulations of persons in a single state: thefts; taking away from others by force or fraud, what is their right and property."

### John Wesley:
