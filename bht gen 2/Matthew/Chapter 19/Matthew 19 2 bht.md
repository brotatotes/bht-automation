1. "And great multitudes followed him,.... The Persic version adds, 'of the sick and diseased'; but all that followed him were not such, though some were: these came not only from Galilee, but from the adjacent parts, from the country beyond Jordan, and the coasts of Judea, where he had been formerly."

3. "And he healed them there; in the above mentioned places, even as many of them as were sick and diseased."

### John Wesley:
