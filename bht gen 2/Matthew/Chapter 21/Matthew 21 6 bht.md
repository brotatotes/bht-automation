The disciples' unwavering faith and obedience are commendable in this instance. Despite the potential objections and difficulties they may have faced, they had complete trust in Jesus' command and promise. Their willingness to follow through, without any hesitation, serves as a testament to their deference towards their Master. Their example encourages us to press forward, even in the face of adversity, and demonstrate the obedience that the Lord demands from us.

3. "Let us also learn by their example to press forward through every kind of difficulty, so as to render to the Lord the obedience which he demands from us."
