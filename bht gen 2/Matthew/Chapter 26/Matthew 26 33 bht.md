# Matthew 26:33 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
Peter's reply to Jesus demonstrates his misguided confidence and failure to understand the true nature of his own weaknesses. While he speaks with sincerity and deep affection, his overconfidence and pride prevent him from recognizing the prophecies about Jesus' resurrection. As a result, Peter underestimates the vulnerability of the other disciples and overestimates his own fidelity. This serves as a reminder of the importance of humility and relying on God's strength rather than our own. Peter's subsequent denials, lasting over an hour, further highlight the magnitude of his betrayal and the consequences of self-reliance.
