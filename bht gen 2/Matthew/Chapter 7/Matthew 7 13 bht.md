# Matthew 7:13 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
The first step in religion requires us to triumph over our natural inclinations, embarking on a narrow and thorny road. Christ reminds his disciples that the gate is at the beginning, not the end, emphasizing the entrance to the narrow path. This path, though challenging, leads to life. On the other hand, the wide gate is easily entered and the broad way easily trodden, leading to destruction. The broad way appeals to many, but its end is ruin. The narrow gate and road, though difficult, lead to true life and should be chosen over the easy path to destruction.
