# Philippians 2:5 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
Let this mind be in you, which was also in Christ Jesus. The apostle urges the believers to imitate the humility of Christ, who sought not his own glory but humbled himself. He presents Jesus as the perfect example of humility and encourages the Philippians to adopt the same attitude in order to maintain unity. By embodying the spirit of Christ's selflessness and humility, they can reflect his character and bring glory to God.

## Choicest Commentary Quotes:
### Jamieson-Fausset-Brown:
1. "Have this mind in you," &c. He does not put forward himself as an example, but Christ, THE ONE pre-eminently who sought not His own, but "humbled Himself."
2. Christ humbled Himself in taking on our nature and further humbling Himself in that nature.

### Albert Barnes:
1. "Let this mind be in you, which was also in Christ Jesus."
2. "The object of this reference to the example of the Saviour is particularly to enforce the duty of humility."
3. "The point here is, that he left a state of inexpressible glory, and took upon him the most humble form of humanity, and performed the most lowly offices, that he might benefit us."

### Marvin Vincent:
1. Let this mind be in you [τουτο φρονεισθω εν υμιν] . Lit., let this be thought in you. The correct reading, however, is froneite, lit., "think this in yourselves." Rev., have this mind in you.

### John Calvin:
1. "He now recommends, from the example of Christ, the exercise of humility, to which he had exhorted them in words."
2. "He exhorts every one to have the same disposition that was in Christ."
3. "He afterwards shews what a pattern of humility has been presented before us in Christ."

### Philip Schaff:
1. "Have this mind in yourselves which was also in Christ Jesus."
2. "He has already said 'be of the same mind,' 'be of one mind,' and thus pointed towards a grand ideal."
3. "He now sets forth that ideal, as it has once been seen embodied in Christ, the great Exemplar."

### Archibald T. Robertson:
1. "Have this mind in you which was also in Christ Jesus."
2. "Humility."
3. "Paul presents Jesus as the supreme example of humility."
4. "He urges humility on the Philippians as the only way to secure unity."

### John Gill:
1. "The apostle proposes Christ as the great pattern and exemplar of humility."
2. "Engage the saints to lowliness of mind, in imitation of him."
3. "Let the same condescending spirit and humble deportment appear in you as in him."
