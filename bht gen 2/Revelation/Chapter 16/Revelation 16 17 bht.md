# Revelation 16:17 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
The seventh angel poured out his phial upon the air, symbolizing the kingdom of Satan and the powers of darkness. This act encompasses the entire earth, showing the magnitude of the final overthrow of the beast. The pouring out of this phial seemed to take up more time than the others, indicating its significant impact and far-reaching consequences. As the phial was poured out, it signified the completion of God's commands and the fulfillment of all the plagues. Truly, it is done.

## Choicest Commentary Quotes:
### Jamieson-Fausset-Brown:
1. "angel—so ANDREAS."
2. "into—so ANDREAS (Greek, 'eis'). But A and B, 'upon' (Greek, 'epi')."
3. "great—so B, Vulgate, Syriac, Coptic, and ANDREAS."
4. "of heaven—so B and ANDREAS. But A, Vulgate, Syriac, and Coptic omit."
5. "It is done—'It is come to pass.'"
6. "God's voice as to the final consummation, as Jesus' voice on the cross when the work of expiation was completed, 'It is finished.'"

### Albert Barnes:
1. "This introduces the final catastrophe in regard to the 'beast' - his complete and utter overthrow, accompanied with tremendous judgments."
2. "The most probable supposition as to the idea intended to be represented is, that, as storms and tempests seem to be engendered in the air, so this destruction would come from some supernatural cause, as if the whole atmosphere should be filled with wind and storm; and a furious and desolating whirlwind should be aroused by some invisible power."
3. "The series of judgments is about to be completed; the dominion of the beast is about to come to an end forever. The meaning here is, that that destruction was so certain, that it might be spoken of as now actually accomplished."

### Marvin Vincent:
1. Temple of heaven. Omit of heaven.

### Philip Schaff:
1. "The air is the dwelling-place of the powers of darkness, whose head is ‘the prince of the power of the air.’"
2. "The voice is from God, and from His very throne."
3. "It is done, i.e all God's purposes are accomplished: all the plagues are poured out; the end is reached."

### Archibald T. Robertson:
1. "Upon the air. All men breathe the air and this is worse than the smiting of the earth, the sea, the fresh waters, the sun."
2. "A great voice. The voice of God as in Revelation 16:1."
3. "It is done. The whole series of plagues is now complete."

### John Gill:
1. "By which is meant the kingdom of Satan, he being the prince of the power of the air."

### John Wesley:
1. "And the seventh poured out his phial upon the air — Which encompasses the whole earth."
2. "This is the most weighty phial of all, and seems to take up more time than any of the preceding."
3. "It is done — What was commanded, verse 1. Revelation 16:1 The phials are poured out."
