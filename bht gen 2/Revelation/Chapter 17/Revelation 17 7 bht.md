# Revelation 17:7 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
The angel in Revelation 17:7 proceeds to explain the mystery or hidden meaning behind what St. John had seen. The interpretation is given in an obscure manner, only understood by those with spiritual wisdom. Similarly, the mystery of the woman is revealed by the angel in Revelation 17:18, while the beast and its symbolic elements are explained in a mystical manner in Revelation 17:8. Through these explanations, a deeper understanding of the symbols and their significance can be attained.

## Choicest Commentary Quotes:
### Albert Barnes:
1. "The question asked him, 'why' he wondered, was designed to show him that the cause of his surprise would be removed or lessened, for that he would proceed so to explain this that he might have a correct view of its design."
2. "The sense is, 'I will explain what is meant by the symbol - the hidden meaning that is couched under it.'"
3. "That is, he would so far explain it that a just view might be obtained of its signification."

### Philip Schaff:
1. Revelation 17:7. The angel proceeds to explain what St. John had seen, taking the two parts of the vision in inverted order; first, the beast (Revelation 17:8-14), and secondly, the woman (Revelation 17:15-18).
	

### Archibald T. Robertson:
1. "I will tell thee the mystery (εγω ερω σο το μυστηριον)." 
2. "The angel gives his interpretation of the woman and the beast (Revelation 17:7-18)."
3. "Ερω is the future active of ειπον (defective verb), to tell, to say."

### John Gill:
1. "Till it was made known to John by the angel, it was a mystery to him; and when it was revealed, the interpretation is given in such an obscure manner, that it is only understood by the mind that has spiritual wisdom; and still remains a mystery to carnal men, just as the Gospel itself does." 
2. "The hidden meaning of this woman, or the mystery of her, is told by the angel in Revelation 17:18."
3. "The mystical sense of the beast, its heads and horns, and which is also delivered in a mysterious manner, is given in Revelation 17:8."

### John Wesley:
1. I will tell thee the mystery — The hidden meaning of this.
