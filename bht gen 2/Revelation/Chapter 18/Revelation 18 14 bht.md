## BHT:
The paragraph speaks to the loss of the fruits and luxuries that were longed for and desired. These included the desired and delectable fruits that were imported into Rome, such as pears, peaches, nectarines, apples, and plums. It also encompasses all the things that were pleasing to the taste and sight, like sumptuous clothing, majestic buildings, and opulent furniture. These desirable things will no longer be found or regained, as they will cease to exist.
