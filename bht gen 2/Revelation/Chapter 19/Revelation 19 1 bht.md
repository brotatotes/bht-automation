The destruction of Babylon by the power of God elicits a grand response of praise from the heavenly hosts. The word Hallelujah is used here, signifying a call to praise the Lord. This call for praise is fitting in the presence of God and in light of the ultimate triumph of the gospel over the enemies of the church.
