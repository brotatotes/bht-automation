# Revelation 19:15 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
And he shall rule them with a rod of iron, signifies that those who escape his initial judgment will still be subject to his authority and strictness. The nations have already experienced his righteous punishment, but they will face an even greater retribution when they face his iron rod in the future. This will cause them to acknowledge and submit to their rightful Lord.

## Choicest Commentary Quotes:
### Jamieson-Fausset-Brown:
1. "Here in its avenging power, 2 Thessalonians 2:8, 'consume with the Spirit of His mouth' (2 Thessalonians 2:8- :, to which there is allusion here); not in its convicting and converting efficacy."
2. "He, who would have shepherded them with pastoral rod and with the golden scepter of His love, shall dash them in pieces, as refractory rebels, with 'a rod of iron.'"
3. "The fierceness of Christ's wrath against His foes will be executed with the resources of omnipotence."

### Albert Barnes:
1. "The sword seems to be an emblem of his words or doctrines, as penetrating the hearts of people."
2. "His enemies would be certainly crushed before him - as grapes are crushed under the feet of him that treads in the winevat."

### Marvin Vincent:
1. "Sword. See on ch. Revelation 1:16."
2. "Shall rule [ποιμανει] . See on ch. Revelation 2:27."
3. "Of the fierceness and wrath [του θυμου και της οργης] . Omit and, and render, as Rev., the fierceness of the wrath."

### Philip Schaff:
1. "On the sharp sword mentioned in this verse comp. chaps. Revelation 1:16, Revelation 2:12; Revelation 2:16."
2. "On the tending as a shepherd comp. Revelation 2:27, Revelation 12:5."
3. "The heaping up of words of judgment in the last clause is very striking, the winepress of the wine of the fierceness of the wrath of Almighty God. For the ‘winepress’ comp. chap. Revelation 14:19-20."

### Archibald T. Robertson:
1. "A sharp sword...a figure here for forensic and judicial condemnation." 
2. "And he shall rule them...as Shepherd." 
3. "The winepress of the fierceness of the wrath of Almighty God...these images are here combined."

### John Gill:
1. "The word of God, or the judiciary sentence of Christ according to it, and which he will fully execute, to the utter destruction of all his enemies."
2. "And he shall rule them with a rod of iron; use them with the utmost severity."
3. "The wicked antichristian party are likened to clusters of grapes; who being ripe for destruction, are cast into it, and pressed, squeezed, and trodden down by the mighty power of Christ."

### John Wesley:
1. "And he shall rule them — Who are not slain by his sword."
2. "The nations have long ago felt his 'iron rod,' partly while the heathen Romans, after their savage persecution of the Christians, themselves groaned under numberless plagues and calamities, by his righteous vengeance."
3. "For although the cruelty, for example, of the Spaniards in America, was unrighteous and detestable, yet did God therein execute his righteous judgment on the unbelieving nations; but they shall experience his iron rod as they never did yet, and then will they all return to their rightful Lord."
