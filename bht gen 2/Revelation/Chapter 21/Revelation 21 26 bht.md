# Revelation 21:26 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
The New Jerusalem, described in Revelation, will be a glorious and excellent city. It will gather all that was valued and pure on earth. This city will receive the best of all the nations, presented by their kings, representing the various orders among the redeemed. Just as the nations on earth form one great human family, the City of God will unite all believers from diverse backgrounds. The glory of the nations will contribute to the honor and magnificence of this splendid state.
