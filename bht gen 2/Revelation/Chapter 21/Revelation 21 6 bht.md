# Revelation 21:6 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
The end has come, and everything that was prophesied has been fulfilled. God, the Alpha and Omega, the beginning and the end, has brought it all to completion. He offers the water of life to all who are spiritually thirsty.

## Choicest Commentary Quotes:
### Jamieson-Fausset-Brown:
1. "All is as sure as if it actually had been fulfilled for it rests on the word of the unchanging God."
2. "When the consummation shall be, God shall rejoice over the work of His own hands, as at the completion of the first creation."
3. "As gratuitous as was man's hatred of God, so gratuitous is God's love to man: there was every cause in Christ why man should love Him, yet man hated Him; there was every cause in man why (humanly speaking) God should have hated man, yet God loved man."

### Albert Barnes:
1. "The great work is accomplished; the arrangement of human affairs is complete. The redeemed are gathered in; the wicked are cut off; truth is triumphant, and all is now complete - prepared for the eternal state of things."
2. "The meaning of the language as used here, regarded as spoken by the Redeemer at the consummation of all things, and as his people are about entering into the abodes of blessedness, is, 'I am now indeed the Alpha and the Omega - the first and the last.'"
3. "The freeness and the fulness of salvation will be one of the most striking things made manifest when the immense hosts of the redeemed shall be welcomed to their eternal abodes."

### Marvin Vincent:
1. "It is done [γεγονεν]. The correct reading is gegonan they are come to pass; i e., these words."
2. "Alpha and Omega. Both have the article, "the alpha," etc. See on ch. Revelation 1:8."
3. "Unto him that is athirst. Compare Isaiah 55:1. Fountain [πηγης]. See on John 4:6. Of the water of life. See John 4:10, John 4:14. Compare Isaiah 12:3."

### Philip Schaff:
1. "The future for which the saints of God have longed, and of which the prophets spoke, has come." 
2. "God is the unchangeable, the everlasting, One; the first cause, the last end, of all things."
3. "The redeemed not only find their first life in Christ: they draw from Him continually those ever fresh supplies of grace by which they are sustained in spiritual life and joy."

### Archibald T. Robertson:
1. "They have come to pass." 
2. "I am the Alpha and the Omega, the beginning and the end."
3. "God is the bountiful Giver of the Water of Life."

### John Gill:
1. "The end of all things is come; it is all over with the first heaven and earth; these are no more, and the new heaven and earth are finished."
2. "The whole election of grace is completed; every individual vessel of mercy is called by grace; all the saints are brought with Christ, and their bodies raised, and living saints changed, and all together are as a bride prepared for her husband."
3. "He that is athirst is one that is so not in a natural, much less in a sinful, but in a spiritual sense; who as he has thirsted after Christ, and salvation by him; after pardon of sin, and a justifying righteousness; after communion with Christ, and conformity to him, and a greater degree of knowledge of him; so after the glories of his kingdom, and the happiness of a future state."

### John Wesley:
1. "It is done - All that the prophets had spoken; all was spoken." 
2. "I am the Alpha and the Omega, the beginning and the end - The Everlasting."
3. "I will give to him that thirsteth - The Lamb saith the same."
