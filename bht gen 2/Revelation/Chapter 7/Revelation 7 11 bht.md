## BHT:
Just as the angels in heaven stood in waiting, surrounding the throne and the elders, they too are filled with joy and adoration for the salvation of mankind. In their countless numbers, they see the work of God's perfections and rejoice that the church is now brought triumphantly to heaven. ### Albert Barnes:
1. "The redeemed, it is manifest, occupied the inner circle, and were near the throne, though their precise location is not mentioned."
2. "The angels sympathize with the church redeemed and triumphant, as they did with the church in its conflicts and trials, and they now appropriately unite with that church in adoring and praising God."
3. "They see in that redemption new displays of the character of God, and they rejoice that that church is rescued from its troubles, and is now brought triumphant to heaven."
