# Revelation 8:4 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
The ascending smoke, accompanied by the prayers of the saints, is a profound symbol of acceptance. This signifies that the prayers of the saints, though imperfect, are made acceptable and presented before God. The smoke itself represents the virtue of Christ's mediation, which covers any deficiencies in their prayers. It serves as a beautiful reminder of Christ's loving care for His church and His people.
