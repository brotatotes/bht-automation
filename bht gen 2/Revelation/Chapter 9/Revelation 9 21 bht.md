1. "Neither repented they of their murders... for which the Turks as a scourge were let in upon it, and destroyed it; and yet the western papacy, who did not suffer in these calamities, took no warning by them, did not repent of their sins, and reform their practices..."
2. "Jezebel the whore of Rome has been famous for... necromancers, given to the magic art, and have entered into covenant, and have had familiarity with the devil..."
