## BHT:
The Lord will swiftly fulfill His word in righteousness, cutting it short and completing His work upon the earth. This will result in a great consumption and apparent ruin. However, the remnant that remains after this destruction will serve as a testimony of God's righteousness. The Apostle applies this prophecy to the salvation of the remnant of Israel, emphasizing their assurance and the certainty of God's plan.
