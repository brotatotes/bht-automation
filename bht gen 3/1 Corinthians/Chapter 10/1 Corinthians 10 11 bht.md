## BHT:
Living in the last dispensation, our guilt is greater if we fall short of our privileges, as exemplified by past generations. These historical and figurative examples serve as a warning, indicating the closeness of God's judgment. Written for our admonition, they are applicable to Christians of all ages. The gospel era represents the culmination of God's dispensation, where punishments and rewards converge.
