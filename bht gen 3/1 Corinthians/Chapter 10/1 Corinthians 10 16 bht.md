## BHT:
The cup of blessing, representing the blood of Christ, is consecrated by the minister as the representative of the congregation. Through the joint blessing and drinking of the cup, believers enter into a communion, a spiritual participation in the blood of Christ. In a similar way, the bread signifies a communion with the body of Christ. By partaking in the cup and the bread, Christians share in the blessings and benefits of Christ's sacrifice, united to him and to each other as one body.
