# 1 Corinthians 10:17 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
When believers come together and partake of the communion bread, they become one bread with Christ and one body with each other. This unity is symbolized by the shared consumption of the same loaf, signifying their spiritual connection to Christ and one another. By eating the bread, believers visibly express their oneness and bond. In contrast, participating in idol feasts aligns individuals with idolatry, underscoring the importance of maintaining unity with Christ. Through communion, believers establish and outwardly demonstrate their shared society and familial identity as followers of Christ.
