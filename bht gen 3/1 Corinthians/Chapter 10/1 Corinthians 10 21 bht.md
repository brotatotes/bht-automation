## BHT:

The cup of devils represents the pagan feasts, and the table of the Lord refers to the Lord's Supper. Joining in the worship of demons contradicts the devotion owed to Christ. It's an incongruous and impossible fellowship to be both Christ's and the devil's. Thus, it is improper and wicked to drink from the cup of the Lord and the cup of devils.
