## BHT:
Paul addresses the Corinthian plea of all things are lawful by highlighting the importance of edification in the Christian body. He emphasizes that while certain actions may be permissible, not all contribute to building up the spiritual temple of God. True Christian freedom is not simply about what is allowed, but about considering the well-being and growth of others. Believers should regulate their choices, using their liberty in a way that promotes edification and the welfare of fellow Christians.
