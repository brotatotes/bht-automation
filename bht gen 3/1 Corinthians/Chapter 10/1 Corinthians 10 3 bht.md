# 1 Corinthians 10:3 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
The Christian Sacraments, symbolizing the body and blood of Christ, are of great importance for membership in Christ. The manna and miraculous stream in the wilderness anticipated these elements, serving as a testimony to their significance as more than mere signs or remembrances. Just as the Israelites were nourished by the spiritual manna, we are likewise nourished by the true bread from heaven, which is Christ. It is crucial not to demean or deny the necessity of these Sacraments, which are essential for our spiritual growth.

### Albert Barnes:
