# 1 Corinthians 10:31 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
In all things, our aim should be the glory of God, setting aside self-regard and avoiding anything that hinders His glory. Our actions, whether in eating, drinking, or any other conduct, should be guided by the edification of our neighbor and the advancement of God's kingdom. Even the most ordinary actions of life, like eating and drinking, can be done to the glory of God when we recognize His provision and consider every mercy as coming from Him. May our lives reflect God's glory and advance the knowledge and love of Him in ourselves and others.
