## BHT:
The warning in this text is about the danger of pushing God to His limit by tempting Him with rebellious conduct and sin. Just as the Israelites provoked Him with their complaints and flirted with idolatry, the Corinthians were at risk of doing the same with their overconfidence in knowledge. The consequence of this temptation can be seen in the example of the Israelites perishing by the serpents, serving as a reminder to not test the Lord's patience.
