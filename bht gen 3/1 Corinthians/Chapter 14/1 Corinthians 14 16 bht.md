## BHT:
If you pray or bless in a foreign language that is not understood by the unlearned, there will be no fellowship and your prayer or blessing will not be public. No one can join in with an Amen if they do not comprehend what you are saying. The practice of saying Amen aloud was borrowed from the synagogue worship, where it was expected that all worshippers would enthusiastically respond. The audible response of the congregation allows them to express their whole-hearted agreement with the prayers being offered.
