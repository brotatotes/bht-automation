# 1 Corinthians 14:4 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
When someone exercises their spiritual gift in accordance with the prompting of the Spirit, it is edifying not only for themselves but also for the church. Even though the words may be unintelligible to others, the intensity of the prayer or praise can still be edifying to the individual. However, it is important that the person is able to interpret their own tongue in order to edify themselves and the church. Paul emphasizes that it is not about showmanship or self-glorification, but about the edification of the entire church community.
