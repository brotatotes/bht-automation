# 1 Corinthians 4:15 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
Paul reminds the Corinthians that, despite the abundance of tutors and instructors they may have had, he alone holds the position of their spiritual father. These tutors played a role in their education, but they did not have the rights or the same affection of a father. Paul emphasizes that he begot them spiritually in Christ Jesus through the gospel, highlighting the profound significance and closeness of their relationship. The Corinthians should value and acknowledge this unique bond they have with Paul, recognizing that he holds a special place as their spiritual father.
