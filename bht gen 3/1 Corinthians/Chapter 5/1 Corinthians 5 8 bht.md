# 1 Corinthians 5:8 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
Let us keep the feast, a perpetual feast, and keep the leaven out. With sincerity and truth, let us engage in the service of God, putting away all evil. Just as the Israelites celebrated the Passover with unleavened bread, we should celebrate our Christian life as a lifelong Paschal celebration of our 'eternal redemption' by the death of Jesus. We must bring sincerity and truth to the feast, renouncing malice and wickedness, partaking in the rich feast of the flesh and blood of Christ.
