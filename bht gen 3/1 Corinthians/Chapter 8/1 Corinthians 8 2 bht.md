## BHT:
True knowledge can only be attained through humility and love, as without them, knowledge is mere appearance. To truly know, one must acknowledge their own ignorance and approach knowledge with a spirit of love. Pride and self-sufficiency stem from knowledge without love, hindering growth and edification. The truly learned embrace their own ignorance and recognize that genuine knowledge is marked by genuine love.
