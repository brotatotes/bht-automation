# 1 Corinthians 8:7 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
In addressing the issue of eating meat that had been offered to idols, Paul acknowledges that not all believers possess the same level of knowledge. The Gentile converts, who had been steeped in idol worship, still regarded the offerings as made to something real and felt eating such meat was sinful. It is essential for those with greater knowledge to consider the weaker brethren, not causing them to stumble or defile their conscience. Even if lacking knowledge, one must act according to their conscience as a sensitive gauge to their spiritual condition.
