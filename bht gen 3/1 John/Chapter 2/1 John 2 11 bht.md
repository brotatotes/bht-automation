### Jamieson-Fausset-Brown:
1. "is" marks his continuing STATE: he has never come out of "the darkness" (so Greek); "walketh" marks his OUTWARD WALK and acts.
2. "where"; including not only the destination to which, but the way whereby.
3. Darkness not only surrounds, but blinds him, and that a blindness of long standing.
