## BHT:
Abide in Christ so that when He is revealed, we can confidently approach Him without shame. This means not fleeing from His presence, which would cause shame. Believers of all stages and ages are called to abide in Him, being rooted and grounded. The reference to the second coming of Christ reminds us that it could happen at any time. Therefore, we should abide in Him, having faith, hope, and love, so that we will not be put to shame before Him at His coming.
