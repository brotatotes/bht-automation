## BHT:
The one who purifies themselves as a response to the hope resting upon God demonstrates a will to be cleansed, not separate from this hope but consistently interconnected with it. This purification goes beyond simply being freed from impure actions, encompassing liberation from all forms of defilement, both physical and spiritual. Ultimately, the hope at hand entails the aspiration to be transformed into God's likeness, conformed to the image of God in Christ. This process of purification involves the work of both God and man, presupposing justification through faith, resting upon the grace and righteousness of Christ.
