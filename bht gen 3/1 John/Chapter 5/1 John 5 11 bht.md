# 1 John 5:11 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
God has given us eternal life through His Son, a gift that is not merely promised but given once for all. This life is found in Jesus Himself, and it is secured to us through Him. By believing in Christ, we become partakers of this life, which is a pure grace gift from God the Father. It is rooted in His sovereign will and manifests His abundant love for us. Through this gift of eternal life, we are assured that we are now children of God.
