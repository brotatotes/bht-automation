# 1 John 5:13 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
The Apostle writes to emphasize the importance of knowing and having the assurance of eternal life. This knowledge brings joy in God, and it is not a superficial certainty. Believing in the name of the Son of God is the key to attaining this assurance. By knowing that they have eternal life, believers can confidently hold on to their faith and continue to trust in Christ. It is both a gift from God and a duty for every Christian to live in the enjoyment of this assurance.
