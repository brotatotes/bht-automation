Those who are born again do not continually sin, as their heavenly birth endures, safeguarding them from the wicked one. This warns against misusing the previous statements to justify complacency. Believers of all stages are included in this assurance and are kept from evil by God. Christ protects those begotten of God, preventing the devil from snatching them away.
