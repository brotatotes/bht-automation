## BHT:
To you believers belongs the honor...but to the disobedient...the stone which the builders rejected, this has become for a head corner stone. Unbelief leads to disobedience, as faith is contrasted with disobedience throughout Scripture. Believers find value in Christ as the precious cornerstone, while unbelievers reject Him, unaware that their rejection only contributes to His exaltation.
