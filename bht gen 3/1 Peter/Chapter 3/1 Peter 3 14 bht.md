## BHT:
If you should suffer for righteousness' sake, remember that the highest good gained by Christ in the Gospel is a heart certified of God's grace, bringing tranquility in all adversities. This suffering does not take away your blessedness, but rather promotes it. So do not be terrified by the fear that others try to strike into you. Instead, trust in God, for He is your protector and can vanquish all your foes. By conquering immoderate terrors through faith, you can firmly stand and fulfill your duty.
