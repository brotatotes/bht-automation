# 1 Peter 4:12 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
Beloved, do not be astonished at the passing through the fire which is taking place in your case for a trial to you. Think it not a thing alien from you but realize that some strange thing were happening unto you. It is not a strange and untoward chance, but rather it is taking place with the gracious design of trying you and proving you. Therefore, do not be amazed or consider it a strange thing. Instead, be prepared and steadfast, knowing that these fiery trials are permitted by the wisdom of God for your testing.

### Henry Alford:
1. "Beloved, be not astonished at the passing through the fire which is taking place in your case for a trial to you." 
2. "Think it not a thing alien from you, in which you are not at home."

### Jamieson-Fausset-Brown:
1. "They might think it strange that God should allow His chosen children to be sore tried."
2. "The Greek adds, 'in your case.'"
3. "Instead of its 'happening to you' as some strange and untoward chance, it 'is taking place' with the gracious design of trying you; God has a wise design in it."

### Albert Barnes:
1. "Beloved, think it not strange - Do not consider it as anything which you had no reason to expect; as anything which may not happen to others also."
2. "Concerning the fiery trial which is to try you - Referring, doubtless, to some severe persecution which was then impending."
3. "As though some strange thing happened unto you - Something unusual; something which did not occur to others."

### Marvin Vincent:

3. "The trial as actually in progress...taking place...with a view to probation."

### John Gill:

2.
