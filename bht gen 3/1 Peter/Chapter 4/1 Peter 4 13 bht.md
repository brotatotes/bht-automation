# 1 Peter 4:13 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
Rejoice insofar as you are partakers of sufferings and may rejoice exulting in your identification with Christ, even in suffering. These sufferings are the same kind that Christ endured, and by willingly suffering for His sake, you enter into fellowship with Him. The capacity to find joy in your present sufferings, as you share in the sufferings of Christ, is the condition for experiencing the radiant joy of future glory. As you suffer for His sake, rejoice in the hope of the more abundant glory that awaits you, for the measure of glory corresponds to the measure of suffering.
