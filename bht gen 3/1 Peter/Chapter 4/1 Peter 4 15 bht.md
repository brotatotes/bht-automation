## BHT:
In his letter to the early Christians, Peter advises them to avoid suffering as wrongdoers, which includes refraining from acts like murder or theft that are punishable by law. Additionally, he cautions against meddling in the affairs of others, acting as self-appointed overseers who excessively involve themselves in the lives of their Gentile neighbors.
