# 1 Peter 4:5 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
For the comfort of Christians who are unjustly slandered, they can find solace in the fact that there is a Judge, Christ Himself, who is ready to judge both the living and the dead. Those who falsely accuse believers will have to give account for their evil-speaking and be justly condemned. This judgment is not only near in time but also signifies a near judgment in proximity to believers who will have Christ at God's right hand in heaven. So Christians should patiently endure opposition, knowing that the Lord will vindicate them when He comes to judge.
