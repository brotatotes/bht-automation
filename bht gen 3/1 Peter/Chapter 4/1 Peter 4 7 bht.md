# 1 Peter 4:7 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
The end of all things is at hand, indicating that we are in the final dispensation before the Lord returns. In view of this, we should be of sound mind and temperate, encouraging watchfulness and promoting prayer. The nearness of the end should prompt us to be ready and prayerful, seeking strength from the Lord. Let us be sober and watchful, engaging in various forms of prayer, both private and public, as we await the culmination of all things.
