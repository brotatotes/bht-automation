### Albert Barnes:
1. "As there was an allusion in that verse, by the use of the word, to age, so there is in this verse to the fact that they sustained an office in the church."
2. "You are not to attempt to lord it over one another, but are to treat each other with deference and respect."
3. "They were to be willing to take any place, and to perform any office, however humble, in order to serve and benefit others."

### Marvin Vincent:
1. "Put on and wrap yourselves about with humility, so that the covering of humility cannot possibly be stripped from you."
2. "Pride calls out God's armies. No wonder, therefore, that it 'goeth before destruction.'"
3. "To the humble."

### John Calvin:
1. "He bids every one that is inferior in age to obey the counsels of the elders, and to be teachable and humble; for the age of youth is inconstant, and requires a bridle."
2. "In short, all ranks in society have to defend the whole body, which cannot be done, except all the members are joined together by the bond of mutual subjection."
3. "He yet intimates that no ornament is more beautiful or more becoming, than when we submit one to another."

### Philip Schaff:
1. "The exhortation clearly is to the cherishing of a spirit of deference on the part of one class to another." 
2. "Gird yourselves with humility [...] the grace of humility being not the girdle that fastens other things, but the thing which is girt firmly about one."
3. "His sweet dews and showers of grace slide off the mountains of pride, and fall on the low valleys of humble hearts, and make them pleasant and fertile."

### Archibald T. Robertson:
1. "All ages, sexes, classes."
2. "Gird yourselves with humility."
3. "Peter here is thinking of what Jesus did...the lesson of humility."

### John Gill:

2. "And it is a saying of R. Meir, 'he that loves God loves men; he that makes God glad makes men glad; and it (the law) clothes him with humility and fear.'"

### John Wesley:
1. "Ye younger, be subject to the elder."
2. "And be all, subject to each other."
3. "Be clothed with humility."
