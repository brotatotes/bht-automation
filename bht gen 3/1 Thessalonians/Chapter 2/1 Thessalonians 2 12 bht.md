# 1 Thessalonians 2:12 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
To prevent God's name from being blasphemed among the Gentiles, we are called to walk worthily in the present divine grace. Reflecting the sum total of God's perfections, we honor Him through our actions. This involves continuously responding to His call, living in a manner that reflects His holiness and grace. As children of God, we are called to imitate His works of kindness, righteousness, and holiness. By walking in the light and conducting ourselves honestly, we bring honor to God, who has chosen us to be His own.
