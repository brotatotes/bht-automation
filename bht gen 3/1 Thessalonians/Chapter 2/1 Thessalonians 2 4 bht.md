# 1 Thessalonians 2:4 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
As preachers, it is through the free choice and grace of God that we are approved and pleasing in His sight. We should strive to please Him rather than seeking the approval of men. God tests our hearts, and His approval is the only one that truly matters. The gospel is not about trickery, but about declaring the truth and obtaining the approval of God. Our responsibility lies in faithfully fulfilling the trust God has given us and speaking as men accountable to Him.
