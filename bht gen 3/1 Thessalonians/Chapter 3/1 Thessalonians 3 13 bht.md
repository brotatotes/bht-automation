# 1 Thessalonians 3:13 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
May your hearts be established and blameless in holiness, not only in the eyes of men but also before God Himself. It is important to cultivate holiness, for it is through holiness that we are numbered among the saints and justified in the day of judgment. True holiness is not mere external adherence to rules, but a genuine transformation of the heart, brought about by the Spirit of God. Let us strive for increased holiness, knowing that at the second coming of Christ, we will be united with Him and all His holy angels, standing blameless before them all.
