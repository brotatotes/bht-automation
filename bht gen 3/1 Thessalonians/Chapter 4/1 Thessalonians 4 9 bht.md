# 1 Thessalonians 4:9 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
In this context, brotherly love refers to acts of kindness towards poor brethren. The Thessalonians are gently reproved for not fully practicing the love they have knowledge of. Paul emphasizes the greatness of love, stating there is no need to write more about it because they are already taught by God. Divine teachings are exemplified through love, which is seen as a kinship in the love of Christ. Love extends even to enemies, serving as evidence of being taught by God and enlightening the conscience.
