## BHT:
In all things, we are encouraged to test and examine what is presented to us, avoiding blind acceptance. This requires discernment and the scrutiny of spiritual gifts and manifestations. We should not be influenced by popularity or appearance, but put everything to the test, holding fast to what is good according to reason and God's Word. It is crucial to abstain from any form of evil, even if it may seem good. Through the guidance of the Holy Spirit, we can properly discern, relying on the Scriptures as our standard of truth.
