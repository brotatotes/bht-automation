## BHT:
Paul's closing words in this letter emphasize the importance of grace, which he begins and ends with. The omission of Amen in some manuscripts likely indicates the Church's response after hearing the Epistle read. The subscription stating that the letter was written from Athens is a later addition, as Paul actually wrote it from Corinth. Instead of the customary farewell, Paul chooses to use the noble word grace.
