## BHT:
The call to be watchful and sober in this passage highlights the contrast between the spiritually indifferent world and believers who are urged to maintain a state of wakefulness. By refraining from worldly indulgence and embracing a calm, sober-minded approach, we can prevent ourselves from being caught off guard when the Lord returns.
