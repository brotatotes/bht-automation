# 1 Timothy 3:13 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
Those who faithfully serve in the office of deacon are acquiring for themselves a strong foundation and confidence in their faith. They serve as the pillar and foundation of truth, proclaiming the doctrines with their actions. By performing their duties well, they secure a good standing-place against the day of judgment and gain boldness rooted in their own faith. Through their service, they purchase a noble position and influence within the church, potentially leading to higher office. This honorable office brings great boldness in the faith, enabling them to confidently proclaim the truths of Christ.

3. "'in faith,' that is, boldness resting on their own faith."

### Albert Barnes:
1. "The 'word' implies nothing as to the exact nature of the office."
2. "By exercising this office well, a deacon would secure additional respectability and influence in the church."
3. "By the faithful performance of the duties of the office of a deacon, and by the kind of experience which a man would have in that office, he would establish a character of firmness in the faith, which would show that he was a decided Christian."

### Marvin Vincent:
1. "Purchase is unfortunate from the point of modern usage; but it is employed in its original sense of to win, acquire, without any idea of a bargain."

3. "An assured position and blameless reputation in the church, with a pure conscience, would assure boldness of speech and of attitude in the Christian community and elsewhere."
