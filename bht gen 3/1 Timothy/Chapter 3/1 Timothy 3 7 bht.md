# 1 Timothy 3:7 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
A minister must have a good reputation from both within and outside the church. Without a favorable testimony from the world, there is a risk of falling into reproach and becoming ensnared by the devil. The former sins and shame surrounding a bishop must not continue to surround them, as it may lead to recklessness or despair. Paul's concern for the witness of outsiders is evident, as he cautioned against the snares set by the devil. It is crucial for ministers to be viewed as upright and honest individuals, preventing any opportunity for accusations or reproach.
