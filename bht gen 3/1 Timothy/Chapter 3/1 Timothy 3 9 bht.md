## BHT:
Office-bearers in the Church must hold the mystery of the faith in a pure conscience, embracing the truth revealed in Christ with conscientious purity. This emphasizes the association of faith with ethics, as the pure conscience becomes the vessel in which the mystery of the faith is preserved.
