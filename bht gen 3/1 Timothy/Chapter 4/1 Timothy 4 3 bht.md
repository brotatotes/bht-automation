## BHT:
Sensuality leads to false spiritualism, as the heretics claimed higher moral perfection through abstinence from marriage and certain foods. This asceticism was influenced by the Essenes and later developed by the Gnostics. Paul does not condemn marriage, and those who know the truth understand that all foods are now clean and can be received with thanksgiving. This prohibition is a hypocritical deception perpetuated by some, such as the Papists, who claim holiness while living in debauchery. Nevertheless, all foods were created by God and are good, to be enjoyed with a pure conscience and gratitude.
