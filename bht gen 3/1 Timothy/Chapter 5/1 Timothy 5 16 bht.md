# 1 Timothy 5:16 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
If any believing woman has widows in her family who are dependent in any way, she should relieve them to prevent burdening the church and to prioritize assistance for widows who are truly in need. The emphasis is on diligence in good works, including the care of the afflicted. It is unreasonable to leave dependent widows to be supported by others when there is an ability to provide for them. Relatives and the church have a responsibility to support poor widows, allowing the church's resources to be used for those who are genuinely in need.
