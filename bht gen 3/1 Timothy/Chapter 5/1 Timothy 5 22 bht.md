# 1 Timothy 5:22 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
Be cautious when ordaining someone to the ministry, ensuring they have a good character before laying hands on them, lest you become a partaker in their sins. Take heed not to introduce individuals to the ministry who are living in sin or embracing dangerous error, as this would make you a patron of their wrongdoing. By adhering to these guidelines and observing a strict discipline, you can safeguard yourself and maintain your own purity, free from the sins of others.
