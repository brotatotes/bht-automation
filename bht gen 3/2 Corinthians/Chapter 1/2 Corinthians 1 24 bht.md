# 2 Corinthians 1:24 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
Our desire is to bring you joy, not to exert dominance over your faith. Like buttresses supporting a sacred building, we lean on each other as helpers of your joy. It is through faith that you stand, and because of this, we are diligent in assisting your faith, which is the source of true joy. We correct any misapprehensions with gentleness, for our goal is to promote your highest happiness. Faith must be free and exempt from the control of men, and faith ultimately stands in submission only to God.
