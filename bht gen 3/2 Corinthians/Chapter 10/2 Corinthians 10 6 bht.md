# 2 Corinthians 10:6 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
Paul's aim in addressing the Corinthian Church is to stress the significance of obedience without singling out specific individuals. He assumes that most of the church will act obediently, allowing rebels an opportunity to join the obedient before enacting punishment. Paul holds punishment in readiness but waits until all chances for obedience have been given. The goal is to complete the number of obedient believers before dealing with the disobedient.
