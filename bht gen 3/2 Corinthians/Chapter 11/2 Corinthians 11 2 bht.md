# 2 Corinthians 11:2 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
Paul's deep concern for the Corinthians is evident as he jealously compares their relationship to that of a betrothed couple. He hopes to present them as a pure and faithful bride to Christ. This zeal is a godly jealousy rooted in a love for God's honor. Paul sees himself as intimately connected to Christ, understanding that his ministry is ultimately the work of Christ himself. Like a bridegroom, he shares in Christ's love and concern for the Corinthians, desiring their faithfulness and purity.
