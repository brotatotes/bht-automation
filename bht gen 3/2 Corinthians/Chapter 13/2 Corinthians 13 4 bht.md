# 2 Corinthians 13:4 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
Through His crucifixion, Christ willingly embraced weakness and set an example for His followers. It was His voluntary abnegation of power that allowed for His crucifixion to take place. In our union with Him, we may also experience weakness, yet we find solace in knowing that we will live with Him through the power of God. This power is available to us, empowering us in our weakness and sustaining us on our journey with Christ.
