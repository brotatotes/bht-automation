# 2 Corinthians 5:12 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
The Apostle Paul writes to the Corinthians, aiming to equip them with a foundation of substance against those who boast in external achievements without sincerity of heart. His purpose is not to commend himself, but to give them a reason to glory on his behalf, showing the authenticity of his preaching. Paul wants their consciences to affirm his sincerity, in contrast to the false teachers who pride themselves in outward displays. This enables the Corinthians to respond to those who value external reputation but lack genuine sincerity.
