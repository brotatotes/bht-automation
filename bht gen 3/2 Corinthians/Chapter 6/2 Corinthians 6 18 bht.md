# 2 Corinthians 6:18 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
God promises to be our Father and us to be His sons and daughters. This relationship is even more endearing than being God's people. The greatness of the Promiser emphasizes the greatness of the promise. We are adopted into God's family, an honor greater than being friends with the rich or regarded as children of nobles and princes. As children of God, we have the privilege of calling the Almighty our Father, with unlimited access to His aid in all difficulties and supply in all our needs. This divine adoption is of immeasurable worth and dignity.
