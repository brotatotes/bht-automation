# 2 Corinthians 7:12 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
Paul's purpose in writing to the Corinthians was driven by his earnest care for the Church rather than personal considerations. He sought to bring out their zeal and obedience to God, making it evident to themselves and in God's sight. He emphasized that his writing was not solely for the sake of the individual offender, but to manifest the Church's fidelity and zeal towards him as God's minister. His main objective was to protect the welfare of the entire Church and prevent it from being harmed by tolerating wrongdoing.
