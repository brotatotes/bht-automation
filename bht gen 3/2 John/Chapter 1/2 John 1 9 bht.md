## BHT:
Those who go beyond Christ's teaching and do not abide in His doctrine have lost their connection with God. True progress lies in moving closer to Christ, not away from Him. By remaining in His doctrine, we not only have knowledge of the Father and the Son, but also enjoy fellowship with them. Let us cling to the teachings of Christ, for in doing so, we hold on to God Himself.
