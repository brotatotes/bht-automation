# 2 Peter 1:3 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
Through His divine power, God has graciously given us all things necessary for life and godliness. The concept of godliness encompasses both moral and natural attributes, representing virtue and glory. We are called to diligently pursue these qualities, as they are gifts from God Himself. True godliness is rooted in spiritual life and stands in opposition to corruption and lust. The word grant emphasizes the graciousness of God's gift, while worship entails reverent reverence for worth. Christ's divine power combats false teachings, enabling us to partake in the abundant grace available through the gospel.
