# 2 Peter 1:6 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
Let discriminating knowledge bear fruit through self-control and patient endurance. It is not merely stoical endurance, but endurance united with trust in God. Self-control moves weakness and imparts strength, preventing the mind from weakening due to incontinence. Patient endurance should be accompanied by godliness, a continual sense of God's presence, and a filial fear and confidence in Him. By cultivating these virtues, believers can bear all conditions and events, love others, and practice true piety.

3. "Christian self-control is to be practiced in and along with the spirit of patient endurance, which saves it from harshness and fitfulness, confirms it into constancy, and mellows it into tenderness and humility."
