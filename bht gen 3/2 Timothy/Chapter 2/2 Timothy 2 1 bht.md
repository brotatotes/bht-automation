# 2 Timothy 2:1 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
In order to live the Christian life, believers are to rely on the empowering influence of the grace of Christ. This grace must be passed on to faithful individuals who can teach others. Believers are called to be invested with power and demonstrate that they have it, as grace is the inward source of strength. They are to keep in touch with Christ, who is the dynamo for power, and remain rooted and grounded in His grace.
