## BHT:
For the sake of the elect and the full expansion of salvation, the apostle actively endures all things, knowing that God is carrying on His work. Through their sufferings, ministers work towards the salvation of the elect, allowing them to obtain the salvation in Christ Jesus with eternal glory.
