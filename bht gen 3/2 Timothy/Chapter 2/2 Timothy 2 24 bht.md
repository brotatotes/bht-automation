## BHT:
The servant of the Lord must imitate his master in not striving contentiously, though uncompromising in earnestly contending for the faith. He is to be gentle so that he may not occasion any evils, and patient so that he may endure evils. The servant of the Lord must be apt to teach, showing solid teaching, ease in teaching, and patience and assiduity in it.
