# 2 Timothy 2:5 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
The soldier must serve without dividing his service, striving lawfully and observing all the conditions of the contest. The Christian minister is engaged in a struggle for the crown, striving in the games and running the race set before them in the right way. Just as the victor in the athletic contests was crowned, the Christian must strive to obtain the crown of life. The apostle emphasizes the importance of fulfilling the laws and rules, as failure to comply would result in disqualification from the reward.

### John Calvin:

3. "If any man, therefore, wearied with the conflict, immediately withdraw from the arena to enjoy repose, he will be condemned for indolence instead of being crowned."

### Philip Schaff:

3. Failure to keep to the appointed diet and discipline, no less than taking an unfair advantage at the time, excluded the competitor from his reward.
