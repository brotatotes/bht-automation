# 2 Timothy 3:12 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
Those who determine to live godly in Christ Jesus shall be persecuted, for it is a mark of the pious and a necessary consequence of living according to His will. The nature of the Christian doctrine, which condemns other religions and requires adherence to seemingly unreasonable precepts, predicts that persecutions will be faced. There is no godliness outside of Christ, and those who desire to live godly lives will be hunted and subjected to various forms of persecution. This is the price of loyal service to Christ, as there is no exception.
