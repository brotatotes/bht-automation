## BHT:
Paul urges Timothy to visit him before winter, considering the challenges of travel during that season. He mentions the greetings sent by Eubulus, Pudens, Linus, and Claudia, as well as all the brethren in Rome. Claudia, a Briton, was sent for education, symbolizing her father's commitment to the faith. It is even said that Timothy, a son of Pudens, participated in converting the Britons.
