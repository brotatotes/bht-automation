### Albert Barnes:

### Marvin Vincent:
1. "Men will not be won to the truth by scolding's. They should understand what they hear, and learn by perceive why they are rebuked."
2. "In periods of unsettled faith, skepticism, and mere curious speculation in matters of religion, teachers of all kinds swarm like the flies in Egypt. The demand creates the supply. The hearers invite and shape their own preachers."
3. "Some come to hear, not to learn, just as we go to the theater, for pleasure, to delight our ears with the speaking or the voice or the plays."

### Philip Schaff:
