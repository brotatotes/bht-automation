## Choicest Commentary Quotes:
### Jamieson-Fausset-Brown:
1. "I know you are prospering in the concerns of your soul. I wish you similar prosperity in your body."
2. "Perhaps John had heard from the brethren that Gaius was in bad health, and was tried in other ways, to which the wish refers."
3. "Prosper in general, be in health in particular."

### Albert Barnes:
1. "In every respect, I wish that it may go as well with you as it does with your soul; that in your worldly prosperity, your comfort, and your bodily health, you may be as prosperous as you are in your religion."
2. "It is right to wish that our friends may have success in the works of their hands and their plans of life."
3. "It argues a high state of piety when we can, as the expression of our highest desire for the welfare of our friends, express the hope that they may be in all respects as much prospered as they are in their spiritual concerns."

### Marvin Vincent:
1. "I pray that in all things thou mayst prosper."
2. "Be in health."
3. "The soul is the principle of individuality, the seat of personal impressions."

### Archibald T. Robertson:
1. "In all things (περ παντων). To be taken with ευοδουσθα and like περ in 1 Corinthians 16:1, 'concerning all things.'"
2. "Thou mayest prosper (σε ευοδουσθα)... Ευοδοω is old verb...to have a good journey, to prosper, in LXX, in N.T. only this verse (twice), 1 Corinthians 16:2; Romans 1:10."
3. "Even as thy soul prospereth (καθως ευοδουτα σου η ψυχη). A remarkable comparison which assumes the welfare...of his soul (ψυχη here as the principle of the higher life as in John 12:27, not of the natural life as in Matthew 6:25)."

### John Gill:
1. "Beloved, I wish above all things that thou mayest prosper,.... Or succeed in all temporal affairs, in the business of life."
2. "The soul is diseased with sin, and may be said to be in good health when all its iniquities are forgiven."
3. "The soul may be said to prosper when it has a spiritual appetite for the Gospel, feeds upon it, and grows in grace and knowledge."
