# Acts 11:12 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
In response to the accusation of associating with uncircumcised men, Peter brings up the six brethren who had witnessed the events firsthand. Notably, he avoids mentioning Cornelius' name or high position, instead emphasizing the uncircumcised man to whom he had been divinely sent. Peter acknowledges the charge against him and any potential doubt, but affirms that it was the Spirit who commanded him to go with them.
