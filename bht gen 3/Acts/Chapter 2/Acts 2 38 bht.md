## BHT:
Repent and be baptized in the name of Jesus for the remission of sins and the gift of the Holy Spirit. This involves a change of mind, turning away from considering Jesus an impostor, and embracing Him as Lord. Baptism serves as the visible seal of the remission of sins obtained through faith in Jesus. True repentance and baptism express our need for purification, the cleansing of our souls by God's influences, and the grace of the Spirit connected to baptism.
