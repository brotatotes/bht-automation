## BHT:
Baptism is a visible seal of remission, symbolizing the washing away of sins by the blood of Christ. It is not a mere ceremonial rite, but rather an act that invites the presence of the Lord and expresses a deep faith in Him. While baptism itself does not possess salvific power, it serves as a means and seal of God's forgiveness. Therefore, it is crucial not to delay obedience to the command of baptism, for God often uses this ordinance to lead His children to the cleansing power of Christ's blood.
