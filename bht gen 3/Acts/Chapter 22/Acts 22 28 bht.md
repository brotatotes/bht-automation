### John Calvin:

### Philip Schaff:
1. "The word, as Plumptre well remarks, expressing not the transition from bondage to freedom, but from the position of an alien to that of a citizen."

### Archibald T. Robertson:
1. "The sale of the Roman citizenship was resorted to by the emperors as a means of filling the exchequer, much as James I. made baronets."

3. "He was educated by the greatest of the Rabbis; he was at an early age entrusted by the Jewish authorities with an important commission; his nephew could gain ready access to the Roman tribune; he was treated as a person of consequence by Felix, Festus, Agrippa, and Julius."

### John Wesley:
