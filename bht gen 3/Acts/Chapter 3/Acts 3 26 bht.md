# Acts 3:26 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
Peter, inspired by the Spirit, reveals that the true blessing the Messiah brings is not worldly prosperity, but rather the turning away from sin. By confronting his listeners with their guilt in crucifying the Lord, Peter offers the assurance of forgiveness and a glorious future to those who turn to Him. He warns them from their own Scriptures to submit to Christ and receive the blessings of salvation. This blessing is not confined to the Jews, but extends to all, as the glory of the Lord shines upon everyone.
