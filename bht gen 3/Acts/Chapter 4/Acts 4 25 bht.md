### Jamieson-Fausset-Brown:
1. "This fierce confederacy our praying disciples see in full operation, in the 'gathering together of Herod and Pilate, the Gentiles (the Roman authority), and the people of Israel, against God's holy Child ('Servant') Jesus."
2. "David's spirit sees with astonishment 'the heathen, the people, the kings and princes of the earth,' in deadly combination against the sway of Jehovah and His Anointed (his Messiah, or Christ), and asks 'why' it is."
3. "The best ancient copies read, after 'were gathered together,' 'in this city,' which probably answers to 'upon my holy hill of Zion,' in the Psalm."
