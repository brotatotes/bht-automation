# Acts 4:29 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
Recognizing the threatenings as a declaration of war, the early Christians calmly asked God to look upon their threatenings and grant them deliverance. Seeking divine attestation to their testimony, they prayed for fearless courage to testify for their Master. Their request for bravery and constancy revealed their deep understanding that preaching the word of God required not only the gift of speech but also the ability to do so fearlessly and faithfully. They desired the freedom to boldly proclaim the gospel in all places, undeterred by the persecution they faced.
