# Acts 7:27 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
In this account, we witness the continued resistance of Israel to the Spirit. Moses, whose life was divided into three forty-year periods, felt a strong inclination to visit his brethren and deliver them from bondage. However, the Israelites rejected his efforts and challenged his authority as their ruler and judge. This rejection revealed the prevailing sentiment towards Moses at that time. It is often the wrongdoer who resists reconciliation, not God. Despite comprehending their words, Moses faced opposition in fulfilling his divine calling as God's chosen instrument.
