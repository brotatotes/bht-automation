# Colossians 2:5 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
Though absent in the flesh, in the Spirit I rejoice at the orderly, harmonized church you have established. Your solid faith in Christ serves as a firm foundation against false teachings. Your good order and steadfastness resemble a well-organized army, standing firm against the attacks of Gnosticism. Even though a few stragglers may have gone astray, your church remains intact, a solid front that maintains an honorable profession of faith. It is truly delightful to see a church walking together in Gospel order, how beautiful it is!
