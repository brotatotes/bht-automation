## BHT:
Christ is given as the Head over all things to the Church, benefiting her. He is above everything, and the Church cannot be separated from Him. By putting all things in subjection to Himself, He ensures the Church's safety. Christ is a gift to the Church, guiding and governing every member, providing them with growth and strength. The Church's unity comes from within, through the freedom of individual diversity, rather than external uniformity.
