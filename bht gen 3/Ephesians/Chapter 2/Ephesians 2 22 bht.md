## BHT:
Believers are being built together in the Spirit as a permanent dwelling for God. Through the fellowship and indwelling of the Spirit, they grow into a holy temple in the Lord. This grace of God imparts spiritual life and transforms them into the mystical body of Christ, the temple of God. The Spirit's involvement in the building process signifies that the church becomes a spiritual house, a fitting habitation for God. As believers, we are called to continue growing in the faith of Christ, relying on the Spirit's power, and fostering love and unity within the church.
