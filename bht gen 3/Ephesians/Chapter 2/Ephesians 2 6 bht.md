# Ephesians 2:6 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
Our union with Christ establishes the groundwork for both our present spiritual and future bodily resurrection and ascension. As believers, we are already positioned with Him in heavenly places, sharing in His honors and glories. This intimate connection assures us of our future place in heaven and grants us a foretaste of the blessings to come.
