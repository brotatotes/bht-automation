Believers are urged to cease walking in the emptiness of their intellect and the vanity of the Gentiles. This vanity stems from a departure from the knowledge of the true God, resulting in a waste of rational powers on worthless objects. The call for believers to abandon this way of life is part of a broader exhortation to live worthily and exemplify the teachings of their Savior. Through the doctrine of salvation, believers have been enlightened and ought not to participate in the futile pursuits of those who remain in darkness.
