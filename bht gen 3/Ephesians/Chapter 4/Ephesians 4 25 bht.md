# Ephesians 4:25 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
As members of one another in Christ, it is imperative that we put away falsehood and speak truth each one with his neighbor. Our mutual duties are grounded in our union with Christ, and this calls for honesty and integrity in all our interactions. Lying is contrary to the truth shining in our minds and is designed to deceive others. Let us speak truth in every aspect of our lives, both in secular and spiritual matters, reflecting the righteousness of the new man we have become in Christ.
