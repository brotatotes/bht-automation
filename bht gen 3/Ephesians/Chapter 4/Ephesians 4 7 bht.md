## BHT:
In the body of Christ, each believer is bestowed a unique gift from Christ to be used for the benefit of the entire Church. This grace is not self-generated but given by Christ in varying measures. The unity of the Church is not compromised by the diversity of gifts and offices but rather reflects the one grace of God manifesting itself in different ways. Believers are called to depend on one another and contribute their individual gifts for the collective support and growth of the body of Christ.
