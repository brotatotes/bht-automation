## BHT:
We are called in Ephesians to walk in love as Christ loved us and gave Himself for us as an offering and a sacrifice to God. Christ's sacrificial love is seen as an odor of sweet smell, an offering that is pleasing to God. By imitating God's love, we imitate Christ's sacrificial offering and become a sweet-smelling savor to God. Our love should not be mere words, but a genuine and continuous display of self-sacrifice, mirroring Christ's own view of His atoning death.
