# Galatians 1:3 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
May the grace and peace of God be with you. In his salutation, Paul genuinely wishes for the divine grace and peace even upon those he is about to upbraid. Grace encompasses the fullness of the gospel blessing, while peace signifies the personal enjoyment and happiness derived from it. The association of God the Father and our Lord Jesus Christ highlights the divinity of Christ, who serves as the mediator of saving grace and inward peace. Without Christ, neither grace nor true prosperity can be obtained.
