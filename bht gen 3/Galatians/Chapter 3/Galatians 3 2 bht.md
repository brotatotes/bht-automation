# Galatians 3:2 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
Paul's challenge to the Galatians centers around whether they received the Holy Spirit through the works of the law or through the message of faith. By highlighting that faith is not about laboring but about receiving, Paul exposes their error in deviating from the gospel. The fact that they received the Spirit through the preaching of the gospel demonstrates the true source of spiritual gifts. Paul's aim is to remind them of the power of the Holy Spirit accompanying his teaching and the diverse gifts bestowed upon believers for the edification of the Church.
