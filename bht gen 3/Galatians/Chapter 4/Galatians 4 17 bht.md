## BHT:
Beware of the false teachers who pretend to be your friends while they secretly aim to separate you from the truth. They flatter and court you only to advance their own selfish agendas, not seeking your spiritual welfare but to draw you away from the genuine community. Their zealous actions are not honorable, and their motives are not sincere; they wish to exclude you from the blessings of the gospel. Therefore, do not be deceived by their outward show of affection and zeal.
