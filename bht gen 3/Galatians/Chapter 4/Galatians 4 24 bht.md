# Galatians 4:24 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
The allegory of the two covenants, symbolized by Sarah and Hagar, reveals a deeper meaning to those who grasp the law as a system of prophetic figures. It goes beyond the bare historical fact and unveils the inner character of God's dealings with humanity. By contrasting the children born from these covenants, it emphasizes that the free covenant of promise brings freedom, while the legal covenant of bondage does not. This allegory serves as a reminder to those burdened by the law of the grace and freedom found in the Gospel.

### Albert Barnes:
1. "Allegories are in words what hieroglyphics are in painting."
