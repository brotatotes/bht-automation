# Galatians 5:1 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
For freedom, Christ made us free from the yoke of bondage, the law. It is for freedom that Christ set us free, not in a state of bondage. Therefore, we should stand fast and be not entangled again in a yoke of legalism. This freedom is our inheritance, a gift from Christ's redemption. Let us remain truly free and avoid any burdens or actions that would hinder our freedom in Christ.

3. "Every doctrine and ordinance of men is a yoke of bondage which should not be submitted to; ... any action whatever, performed in a religious way and in order for a man's acceptance with God... is a yoke of bondage."
