# Galatians 5:15 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
When Christians engage in contentious disputes, it becomes a destructive cycle that devours their strength, health, character, and resources. The ferociousness of their partisan hatred leads to mutual injury, endangering the entire Christian community. This behavior, likened to wild animals biting and devouring each other, can destroy the organic life of the body itself. Let us remember that slandering, accusing, and unjust acts arising from disputes can only lead to the ruin and consumption of the whole body. We must guard against allowing our disagreements to result in mutual destruction.
