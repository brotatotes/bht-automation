# Galatians 5:18 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
If you are led by the Spirit, you are not under the law. The works of the flesh and the law are in the same category, while the Spirit and the law are opposites. Giving ourselves up to be led by the Spirit frees us from the works of the flesh and the bondage of the law. This freedom is granted by the Spirit, who emancipates us from the yoke of the law and leads us into the blessings and promises of the covenant of grace. The Spirit's guidance is for our profit and leads us out of sin and into the presence of God.
