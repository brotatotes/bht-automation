# Galatians 6:15 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
Boasting in external factors holds no value when compared to the transformative power of the new birth through the Spirit. This results in becoming a new creature in Christ, a phrase carrying profound spiritual meaning. Acceptance in the kingdom of Christ depends on being created anew by the Spirit, which nullifies external distinctions and the shadows of the law. The emphasis lies not on conformity to customs, but on the genuine transformation experienced by being united with Christ.
