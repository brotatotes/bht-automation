## BHT:
Christ is anointed by God with the oil of gladness, symbolizing His triumph and joy resulting from His righteousness and hatred of sin. This anointing denotes His consecration as Messiah, Priest, and King, elevating Him above all others. Christ's love for righteousness is evident in His obedience to God's law and His future role as the judge of the world. God, as the anointer, supports and prepares Christ, revealing His position as the God of Christ. Christ's infinite purity and holiness establish His supremacy among all.
