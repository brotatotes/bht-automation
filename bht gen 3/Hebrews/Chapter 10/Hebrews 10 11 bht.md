# Hebrews 10:11 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
The priests in the old Jewish system, standing in a servile attitude, offered sacrifices that could never fully strip off guilt or take away sins. Christ, as the exalted Savior, offered himself once for all, contrasting the wearisome round of daily offerings. This highlights the imperfection of the old priesthood and the sufficiency of Christ's sacrifice. By placing our faith in him, we find true sanctification and can rest in his victory.
