## BHT:
Through the sacrifice of Christ, the objective perfection provided in His self-sacrifice is gradually becoming subjective in believers as they are being sanctified. This sanctification is perfect in Christ and does not require any further sacrifices. The great High Priest's reign is uninterrupted by the duty of sacrifice because He has completed what the old sacrifices could not. The offering of Christ has laid the foundation for the eternal perfection of those who are being sanctified. By one offering, He has perfected forever those who are being sanctified, ensuring their full reconciliation with God.
