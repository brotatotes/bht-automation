## BHT:
Through the blood of Jesus, our High Priest and perfect sacrifice, we can boldly enter into God's presence. Our access to Him is grounded in His blood, which accomplished atonement and propitiation. This newfound freedom to approach God directly is unique to believers in Christ, as no one before Him dared or could come so fearlessly and joyfully. We no longer need a priestly caste as a mediator; we come boldly with loving confidence through Christ. It is in the blood of Jesus that our boldness to enter is grounded.
