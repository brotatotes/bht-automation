## BHT:
Let us consider one another with continuous care, contemplating the characters and needs of our fellow believers, in order to render mutual help and counsel. This mutual care must be carried out with a view to provoking one another to love. It is in the context of the new economy of grace that mutual care is demanded from the members of the Christian community, stirring up each other's affections and ministries.
