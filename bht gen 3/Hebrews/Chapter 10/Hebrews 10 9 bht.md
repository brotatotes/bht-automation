# Hebrews 10:9 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
The rejection of legal sacrifices involves Jesus voluntarily offering himself as a pleasing sacrifice to God, taking away the animal sacrifices that God does not will and establishing the offering of an obedient will. This act of removing the first, the system of animal sacrifices, to establish the second, doing God's will, is the author's exegesis. Christ's coming was necessary as no human offering would avail, while the sacrifices of beasts are replaced by the priesthood of Christ. The Gospel, guided by the will of God, replaces insignificant acts of sacrifice with true obedience.
