## BHT:
Do not forget the exhortation that urges you to not despise the correction of the Lord or faint when you are rebuked by Him. His discipline is given in kindness to remove impurities and strengthen your faith. Instead of resisting or resenting His correction, endure it with patience and trust in His loving guidance.
