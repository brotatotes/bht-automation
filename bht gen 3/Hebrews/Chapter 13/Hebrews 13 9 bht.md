## BHT:
Beware of teachings that deviate from the one faith in Jesus Christ, denying his messiahship and superseding his saving work. True spiritual nourishment is found in Christ's sacrifice, the foundation of our assurance and the means of all grace. Jewish ceremonialism fails to build up the spiritual life, emphasizing the importance of establishing our hearts in God's grace. We must guard against the winds of various theories and cling to the pure knowledge of Christ to combat strange doctrines. The doctrine of grace establishes our hearts in God's love, while ritual observances hold no spiritual value.
