# Hebrews 2:1 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
Hebrews 2:1 highlights the superiority of Christ compared to prophets and angels, prompting us to pay careful attention to the new revelation that He brings. The cautionary picture of drifting past the sure anchorage of the Gospel emphasizes the perils of inattention. We are urged not to let the truths slip away, but to grasp them tightly, for it is all too easy to drift away from truth and goodness through negligence. The exhortation reminds us of the necessity to earnestly heed the things we have heard.
