## BHT:
For it is said, 'Today if you hear His voice, harden not your hearts, as in the provocation.' This reminder emphasizes the need to avoid the wicked heart of unbelief and hold onto our confidence in Christ until the end. The invitation given in the ninety-fifth Psalm should not remain a mere invitation, but rather a lived experience. The fulfillment of this condition lies in the Scripture itself, which calls us daily and necessitates our continual growth in faith. Therefore, let us encourage one another, cling to Christ and His Gospel, and willingly obey His teachings every day.
