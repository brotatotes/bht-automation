## BHT:
Those who partake of milk, lacking in understanding and experience, are like infants. They need to progress from the basic principles of the Gospel to a more mature understanding of righteousness. This involves not only the doctrine of justification but also the perfection of the doctrine of Christ. It is crucial to move beyond the milk of the Gospel and strive for a deeper understanding of God's truth.
