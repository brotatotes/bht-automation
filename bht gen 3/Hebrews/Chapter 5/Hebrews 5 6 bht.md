# Hebrews 5:6 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
Jesus is a Priest unlike any other, standing alone without equal. He is both a High Priest in the Aaronic tradition and, by opening access to God, a High Priest to believers as well. Melchizedek, a man, priest, and king, prophetically foreshadows Christ's eternal priesthood, surpassing the Levitical priests. The phrase after the order of Melchizedek highlights Jesus' divine appointment as Priest, signifying His superiority. This establishes His greater role as Priest, superior to Aaron, showcasing the change in priesthood and the law.
