## BHT:
Believers are urged to diligently cultivate the full assurance of hope until the end, imitating those who inherit the promises through faith and endurance. The full assurance of hope is obtained through persevering in holy living and serves as a safeguard against falling away. Each individual is encouraged to exhibit the same diligence, developing their hope into full assurance, even amidst trials. This assurance of faith and hope is not a mere opinion, but a divine evidence given by the power of the Holy Spirit.
