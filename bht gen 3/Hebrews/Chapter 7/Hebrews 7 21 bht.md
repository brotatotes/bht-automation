## BHT:
For they indeed, without the solemn promise on oath, are made priests. But he, with the taking of an oath, became a priest. The existing legal priests lacked the oath that solidified Jesus' priesthood and emphasized its unchangeable nature. This oath provided comfort to the heirs of promise, assuring them of God's unwavering commitment. Jesus' priesthood, established through an unalterable oath, surpasses the Levitical priesthood. It signifies God's steadfastness and the everlasting character of Jesus' role as High Priest.
