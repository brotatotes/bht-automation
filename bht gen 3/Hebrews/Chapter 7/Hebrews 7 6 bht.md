# Hebrews 7:6 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
Melchisedec, the one whose genealogy is never reckoned, received tithes from Abraham and blessed him in an official capacity. This implies that Melchisedec, without a legal right, was much above the Levitical priests. The act of Abraham offering tithes to Melchisedec as a voluntary gift signifies his recognition of Melchisedec's personal greatness. Despite being the possessor of divine promises, Abraham willingly accepted the blessing from Melchisedec, showcasing the significance of this acceptance. Thus, Melchisedec must be considered greater than the Levitical priests.
