# Hebrews 8:2 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
Through His glorified body as the tabernacle, Christ enters the heavenly Holy of Holies, interceding for us. This genuine tabernacle in heaven is the ideal counterpart to the earthly tabernacle. Our Great High Priest, the Lord Jesus, has entered the true tabernacle in heaven. By His sacrifice, the veil is removed, granting us free access to God. With our thoughts raised to the kingdom of God, we see Christ as the minister who mediates in His various offices, presenting our sacrifices and bestowing His gifts upon us.
