## BHT:
The Author elucidates the symbolism of the Holy of holies and the altar of incense, highlighting the connection between the earthly sanctuary and the heavenly realm. The altar of incense, positioned by the veil, is considered part of the Holy of holies. The second veil, also referred to as a tabernacle, separates the Holy Place from the Holy of holies. This veil represents the barrier between God and humanity, which is overcome by the death of Christ, signified by the rending of the veil, providing an open pathway to heaven.
