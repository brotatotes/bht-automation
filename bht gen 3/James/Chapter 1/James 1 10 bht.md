# James 1:10 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
The caution given is to the rich against boasting in their worldly riches, for they will pass away. Rather, they should rejoice in their humility and lowliness, as it is through the Gospel that they are freed from the temptations of wealth. The Cross of Christ brings the proud low and exalts the poor. In light of the fleeting nature of earthly glory, finding contentment in both prosperity and poverty reminds us that true worth is found in eternity.
