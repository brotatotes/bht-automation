## BHT:
Be not deceived, my beloved brethren, and do not err in attributing temptation to evil to God, for He is the author of all good. The way of sin is to deceive and kill, and the devil is skilled at blinding our eyes. However, God is the source of every good and perfect gift. To ascribe evil to God denies His holiness and is a damnable heresy that brings swift destruction. Let us guard against this deception and abhor any notion that tarnishes His name and glory.
