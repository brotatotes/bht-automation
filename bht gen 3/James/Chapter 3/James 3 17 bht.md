# James 3:17 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
True wisdom calls us to prioritize purity and sanctity, both in our relationship with God and how we interact with others. It is characterized by a genuine desire for peace, rejecting hypocrisy and ambitious pursuits. This wisdom is marked by mercy and compassion, showing kindness to all without showing partiality. Pursuing this wisdom leads to a pure heart and life, free from impure desires, and enables us to yield to the judgments and reasonings of others. It bears good fruits in our thoughts and actions, harmonizing with the heavenly truth revealed in Christ.
