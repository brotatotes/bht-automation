# James 4:10 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
To be exalted by the Lord, one must have their mind deep-rooted in humility. By recognizing His presence in all aspects of life, humbling ourselves before Him, and acknowledging our meanness and sinfulness, we can seek His grace and mercy. Walking humbly with God, depending on His grace, and ascribing all we have and are unto it, enable us to be raised from brokenness to become forgiven children. God's usual way is to lift up the meek and exalt those who humble themselves.
