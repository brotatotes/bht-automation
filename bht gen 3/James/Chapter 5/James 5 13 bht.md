## BHT:
If anyone is troubled, let them pray; if anyone is joyful, let them sing praises. Express your feelings in a Christian and sober manner, seeking solace and comfort through prayer. Instead of swearing in rash impatience, let us continue to pray. When we are merry and joyous, let us express it through singing psalms of praise. Afflictions should spur us to pray, while moments of joy should inspire us to offer praises.
