# James 5:16 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
Confess your sins openly and honestly to one another, not only in the case of sickness, but universally confess. This includes confessing wrongs done to others and seeking counsel and intercessory prayers. A righteous person's prayer is powerful, especially when they possess a special gift of the Spirit. The purpose of confession and prayer is for healing, both physically and spiritually. By confessing and praying for one another, we can experience the restoration of peace to troubled souls and find forgiveness and strength from God.

### Jamieson-Fausset-Brown:
1. "Confess, THEREFORE, not only in the particular case of sickness, but universally confess."
2. 3. "A righteous man's prayer is always heard generally, but his particular request for the healing of another was then likely to be granted when he was one possessing a special charism of the Spirit."

### Albert Barnes:
1. "The particular reason for doing it which is here specified is, that it would contribute to a restoration to health - 'that ye may be healed.'" 
2. "Disease is often greatly aggravated by the trouble of mind which arises from conscious guilt; and, in such a case, nothing will contribute more directly to recovery than the restoration of peace to the soul agitated by guilt and by the dread of a judgment to come."
3. "We may ask him for what we desire, and he has graciously said that such asking may effect much for our own good and the good of our fellow-men."
