### Albert Barnes:
1. "Probably the true meaning is, that it was one of their characteristics that they had been guilty of wrong towards good men."
2. "The sense may be either that they could not oppose them - the rich men being so powerful, and they who were oppressed so feeble; or that they bore their wrongs with meekness, and did not attempt it."
3. "The sins, therefore, condemned in these verses James 5:1-6... are these four: hoarding up money when it was unnecessary... keeping back the wages... giving themselves up to a life of ease, luxury, and sensual indulgence... wronging and oppressing good and just men."
