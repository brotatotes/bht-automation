# John 14:13 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
Prayer is not limited to the Father alone, for when we pray, we are also praying to the Son. It is through the Spirit of grace and supplication that Jesus brings down upon the Church that we are empowered to do greater works. By accomplishing these works of grace and triumph, the Father's glory is made evident in and through the Son. When we pray in Jesus' name, our prayers must be consistent with His character and contribute to our salvation. Approaching God in the name of His Son grants us the privilege of receiving blessings and salvation.
