# John 15:20 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
Jesus instructs His disciples to remember His words and the inevitable truth that if they persecuted Him, they will also persecute His followers. The verb persecuted signifies a swift and hostile pursuit, involving harassment and maltreatment. Although the world may commend Jesus's teachings, it hesitates to fully embrace them. The disciples are called to recognize their role in following Jesus, facing the same duties and trials as their Master. They must remember and be prepared for the opposition they may encounter, as the world's response will mirror the treatment Jesus received.

### John Calvin:
