## BHT:
This verse serves as a striking testimony against the error of falling from grace and emphasizes the consequences of not abiding in Christ. Just like a branch that dries up without the life-giving sap, those who do not remain connected to Jesus wither away and become useful only as fuel. The imagery used here vividly illustrates the certainty and immediacy of the judgment that awaits those who do not truly abide in Christ. It is a sobering warning against presumption and a reminder of the importance of staying in vital union with Jesus, the true vine.
