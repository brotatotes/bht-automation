## BHT:
The more knowledge we have, the more we are compelled to pray in the name of Jesus. Jesus assures his disciples that his intercession does not exclude them from access to the Father, but rather ensures it. By asking in the name of Jesus, the disciples are assured that God will not refuse their requests. This demonstrates the special love of God for his disciples and their ability to approach the Father with confidence, knowing he is ready and willing to bestow upon them the blessings they need.
