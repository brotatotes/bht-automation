# John 2:2 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
Jesus commences his ministry by attending a wedding, distinguishing himself from the austerity of John the Baptist. This signifies his rejection of giving up occasions we struggle to purify. His presence at the wedding embodies the importance and honor he assigns to marriage. He is invited, potentially at his mother's urging and accompanied by his disciples. These disciples serve as the initial core of an extensive following, recognizing Jesus as their Teacher and Lord. Jesus' attendance demonstrates his affability, willingness to partake, and sanctifying influence on human society.
