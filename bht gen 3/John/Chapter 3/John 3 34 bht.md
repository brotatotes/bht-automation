## BHT:
Those who have received and believe in the testimony of Jesus confirm that God is true, while those who reject Him oppose God openly. The Holy Spirit was given without measure to Jesus, enabling Him to speak the words of God with divine authority. Therefore, the words of Jesus are not mere human words, but the very words of God Himself.
