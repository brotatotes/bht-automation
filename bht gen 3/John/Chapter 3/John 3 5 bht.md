# John 3:5 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
To be born of water refers to baptism, the outward sign, while being born of the Spirit signifies the inward grace. The symbol of water and the operation of the Spirit are brought together in Ezekiel's prophecy, and baptism with water and the Spirit is the admission into the kingdom of God. Jesus affirms the obligation of the outward rite of baptism while also emphasizing the necessary presence of the Holy Spirit. Through water and the Spirit, one enters into the outward fellowship and the vision and fellowship of the kingdom of God.
