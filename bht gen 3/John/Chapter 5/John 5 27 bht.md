## BHT:
Man is to be judged by Man, by the appointed Son of Man who is the inclusive Head of humanity. The appointment of a Judge in our own nature is a beautiful arrangement of divine wisdom in redemption. The Son of Man, who partakes of human nature, is given the authority to pass judgment on humanity. As a son of man, He judges, having fought the same battle and endured the same trials as those standing before Him. It is through this appointment as Judge that He imparts to us what He has received from the Father.
