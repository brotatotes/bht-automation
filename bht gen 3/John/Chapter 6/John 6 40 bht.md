## BHT:
To truly look upon Jesus is more than just seeing Him; it is the awakening of attention that precedes faith. It is through this act of beholding Him with the eye of faith that one can obtain eternal life. The Father's will is to preserve and raise up all who believe in the Son, ensuring that none are lost. This act of contemplation and belief in Jesus leads to the promise of being raised up on the last day, fulfilling the will of God.
