# John 8:21 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
Jesus warns that those who reject Him will die in their sins and be unable to come to Him. This is not about the destruction of Jerusalem, but about individual perdition. He emphasizes His departure and their separation, expressing the collective state of estrangement from God. In despair, they will seek Him when it's too late, resulting in their tragic realization. Jesus focuses on the judgment they will face, sealing their fate of utter ruin, both temporally and eternally, while others enter the kingdom of heaven.
