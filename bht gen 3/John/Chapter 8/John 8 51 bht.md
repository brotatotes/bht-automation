# John 8:51 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
If a man keeps Jesus' word and continues in faith, he shall be rescued from the power of the devil. To keep His word is not merely outward obedience, but endurance and obedience of faith. The promise that he shall never see death confirms Jesus' authority as Lord of eternal life. This promise does not mean freedom from physical death, but the assurance of living eternally with death having no lasting power. By keeping His word, the believer embraces life and rejects death.
