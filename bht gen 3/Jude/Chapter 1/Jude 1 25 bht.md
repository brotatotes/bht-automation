## BHT:
To the only God who is our Savior through Jesus Christ our Lord, be glory, majesty, might, and power before all time, and now, and to all the ages. Amen. We acknowledge that God is the great Author of our salvation, and therefore the word Savior is appropriately applied to Him. In all our affections and aspirations, may God be supreme, and in the face of sin and suffering in this world, may we eagerly anticipate the establishment of His dominion over all the earth. Let us also look forward to the time when God's reign will be fully realized over our own souls, free from sin and sorrow. Through Jesus Christ, who is truly God and the only Savior of His people, we give glory to the Father, the one who is crowned with glory and honor.
