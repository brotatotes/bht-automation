## BHT:
Those who reject the mission and message of Christ's messengers will suffer the same fate as the cities of Chorazin, Bethsaida, and Capernaum. It is a grave responsibility to be co-workers with Christ, as rejecting the Seventy or the Gospel is to despise God Himself. Embracing the Gospel is the highest form of worship, for he who hears the messengers of Christ hears Christ Himself. Any contempt shown towards the messengers is ultimately directed at God the Father, and will be answered for on the day of judgement.
