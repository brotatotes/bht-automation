# Luke 11:2 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
Luke 11:2 presents a more definite version of the Lord's Prayer than the one in Matthew, with the shorter form considered the original. The omission of the doxology in Luke indicates that this particular form was never used liturgically. These instructions from Jesus pertain to all disciples, urging us to not only imitate this prayer but to use this very form. Luke 11:2 provides a specific and authentic rendition of the Lord's Prayer for believers to follow faithfully in their own prayers.
