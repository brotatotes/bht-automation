### Philip Schaff:
1. "Therefore also said the wisdom of God... Christ represents Himself as 'the wisdom of God.'"  
2. "That passage speaks of the sending of prophets and their rejection... This is on the whole preferable." 
3. "The notion that Luke is quoting Matthew 23:34... is a mere assumption."

### Archibald T. Robertson:
1. "Jesus here speaks with confident knowledge of the Divine counsels."
2. "They will act as their fathers did. They will kill and persecute."
3. "Jesus is the wisdom of God."

### John Gill:
1. "The infinite wisdom of God said within himself, determined in his own breast, to do what follows."
2. "Christ is intended, who, as God, is the essential wisdom of God and, as man and mediator, has the spirit of wisdom resting on him, and the treasures of wisdom and knowledge hid in him."
3. "By whom are meant the apostles of Christ, and the ministers of the Gospel."

### John Wesley:
1. "The wisdom of God, agreeably to this, hath said..."
