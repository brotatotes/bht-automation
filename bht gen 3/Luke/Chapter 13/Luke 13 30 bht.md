# Luke 13:30 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
In this prophetic declaration, it is hinted that those who were initially chosen may lose their high place, while the previously cast-off Gentiles will attain the first rank. This warns the Jews against placing their confidence solely in their chosen status. Instead, it reveals that the Gentiles, who were called last, will be first and share in the glory and happiness of the patriarchs and prophets. Conversely, the Jews, who anticipated the kingdom, may end up rejected and despised, highlighting that rewards are not solely based on order of calling.
