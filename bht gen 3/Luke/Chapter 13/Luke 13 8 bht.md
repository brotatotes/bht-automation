# Luke 13:8 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
Like a diligent gardener, Christ pleads for a brief respite, unwilling to see the seemingly barren tree cut down until every effort is made to nurture its potential. This symbolizes changes in divine treatment and the freshening of spiritual culture for the impenitent. Christ intercedes not only for the elect but also for the unconverted, desiring their conversion and growth in sanctification. The digging represents exposing hidden sins and the lack of spiritual root, while dunging signifies the need for warmth and love for Christ.
