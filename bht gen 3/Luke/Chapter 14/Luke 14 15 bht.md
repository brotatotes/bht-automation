## BHT:
The great Feast is prepared and invitations are issued, yet many decline. This man's seemingly pious exclamation lacks the true understanding of living a righteous life. He simply wishes that everything is right in the end, without considering the present. Jesus uses the imagery of a banquet to describe the spiritual bliss of heaven, which was a common figure among the rabbis. The man's remark about being blessed to eat bread in the kingdom of God reveals his misconception of a sumptuous and carnal feast, while the true blessing lies in being in the presence of God.
