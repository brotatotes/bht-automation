# Luke 14:23 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
This parable prophetically contemplates the extension of the kingdom of God to the whole world. It directs the Gospel invitations to be carried to the lowest strata and be brought in contact with the outermost circles of human society. Ministers of Christ are given a powerful directive to go out with missionary zeal and bring the message of salvation to all people. The grace of God is not merely offered but is accompanied by exhortations and even threats to compel people to draw near to Him through the force of His word.
