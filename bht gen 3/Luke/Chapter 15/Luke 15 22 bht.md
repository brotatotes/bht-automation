# Luke 15:22 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
The father's acts of clothing his son in a fine robe, giving him a ring, and providing shoes serve as symbols of freedom and honor. These garments are expressions of the father's overwhelming love, extending beyond the worthiness of the son. The best robe may allude to the righteousness of Christ, while the ring and shoes represent a declaration of God's love and restoration. By interrupting the son's confession, the father demonstrates his display of pardoning love, enveloping him in forgiveness and restoration.
