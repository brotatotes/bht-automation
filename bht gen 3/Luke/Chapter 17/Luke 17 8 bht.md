# Luke 17:8 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
Jesus speaks about what we should expect in our state of service to Him. He contrasts this with the wonders of His grace that we will receive in our state of freedom and adoption. It is a question of what is our right versus what is favor. The emphasis is on the affirmation of the affirmative answer. As the master, He expects us to make ready for Him, saying Thou shalt eat and drink. This implies that as servants, we should be ready and prepared to serve Him willingly and diligently.
