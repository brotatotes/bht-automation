# Luke 18:10 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
Two men went up to the temple to pray - a Pharisee and a tax collector. The Pharisee, standing and full of pride, boasts about his righteous deeds, while the tax collector, aware of his unworthiness, approaches God with confession and trembling. Jesus teaches that the Pharisee's self-righteousness leads to rejection, while the tax collector's humble prayer is accepted by God. This parable warns against prideful prayer and highlights the significance of humility and genuine repentance in our approach to God.
