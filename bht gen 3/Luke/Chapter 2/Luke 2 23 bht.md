# Luke 2:23 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
Luke 2:23 highlights the fact that Jesus was made under the law, fulfilling the requirements written in the law of the Lord. Luke repeatedly emphasizes the obedience of Joseph and Mary to the law, specifically in terms of the purification of the mother and the presentation of the son. This obedience to the law is further supported by Paul's statement in Galatians 4:4. Jesus' adherence to the law allows him to redeem us from the bondage and condemnation of the law.
