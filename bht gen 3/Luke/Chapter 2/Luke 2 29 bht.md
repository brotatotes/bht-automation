### John Calvin:
1. "The Spirit of God enlightened his eyes by faith, to perceive, under a mean and poor dress, the glory of the Son of God."

3. "If Simeon had said exactly, 'Now I shall die with a composed and easy mind, because I have seen the Son of God,' this expression would have indicated the weakness of his faith; but, as he had the word, he might have refused to die until the coming of Christ."
