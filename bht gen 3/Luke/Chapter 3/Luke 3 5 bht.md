# Luke 3:5 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
The proclamation in Luke 3:5 calls for the levelling and smoothing of valleys, an imagery borrowed from the book of Isaiah. The exhortation to fill the valleys and bring low the crooked paths signifies the preparation for the coming of the Lord. John the Baptist, acting as the Messiah's herald, uses Isaiah's description of this preparation with great force. The valleys represent souls that are lowly and humble, who will be raised and directed to believe in Christ. The removal of hinderances paves the way for the reception of the Messiah.
