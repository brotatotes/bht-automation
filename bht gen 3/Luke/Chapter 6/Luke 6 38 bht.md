# Luke 6:38 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
When Jesus says, Give, and it will be given to you. A good measure, pressed down, shaken together, and running over will be poured into your lap, He uses the picture of a full measure of corn to illustrate His point. Just as a generous measure of corn is poured into the bosom of the recipient, so too will blessings be poured out in abundance upon those who give generously. This act of giving is not solely about material possessions, but also encompasses acts of kindness and compassion towards those in need. By giving liberally, we open ourselves up to the overflowing abundance of blessings that God desires to pour into our lives. As we give, we can trust that God will measure back to us with the same generosity that we have shown towards others. So let us be generous and compassionate in our giving, knowing that God will pour out His blessings upon us in return.
