# Luke 7:30 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
The lawyers, interpreters and doctors of the Mosaic law, rejected the baptism of John and refused to confess their sins. In doing so, they set aside God's purpose of grace for themselves, and by their disobedience, they annulled His counsel. Though they didn't outright contradict, their hidden pride and aversion to baptism demonstrated their despising of God's command. Sadly, these highly esteemed and learned individuals rejected the gracious design of God and missed out on His love and blessings.
