## Choicest Commentary Quotes:
### Henry Alford:
1. "The enquiry proceeds from our Lord Himself."
2. "Addressed to Philip, and answered by Philip and Andrew."
3. "The Three agree in their account, and St. John differs from them."

### Marvin Vincent:
1. "And when the day began to wear away...to decline. Wyc., very literally, to bow down." 
2. "Lodge...Primarily the verb means to break up or dissolve. Hence often in New Testament to destroy." 
3. "Victuals...Only here in New Testament. Properly a stock of provisions. Thus Xenophon."

### Archibald T. Robertson:
1. "The sun was turning down towards setting."
2. "The verb 'to dissolve, destroy, overthrow' then of travellers to break a journey, to lodge."
3. "Victuals, only here in the N.T., though common in ancient Greek, especially for provisions for a journey (snack)."

### John Gill:
1. "When the day began to wear away... when the sun was declining... when the evening time was come."
2. "Send the multitude away, that they may go into the towns and country round about."
3. "For we are here in a desert place; which afforded no conveniency for lodging, nor any supply of food."

### John Wesley:
