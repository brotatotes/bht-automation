# Mark 12:27 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
God is the God of the living, not the dead. No human being is truly dead to God, as all mankind sustains a conscious relationship with Him. Those accounted worthy to obtain the world to come enter into an unbreakable covenant with God. Therefore, it is a grave error to think otherwise. The solemn, severe, but kindly close in Mark emphasizes the seriousness of this mistake. To be the God of someone indicates a relation to them as living, pointing to the certainty of a future blessedness and bodily resurrection.
