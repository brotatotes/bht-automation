## BHT:
As Jesus sat down opposite the treasury, he observed thoughtfully how the multitude cast money into it. He watched intently as the rich were casting in their offerings. Mark draws this immortal picture of the weary Christ, who had just taken his leave of the temple, sitting by the treasury and beholding with pleasure how people, both rich and poor, were contributing.
