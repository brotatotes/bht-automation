## BHT:
The image of Jesus as the porter or doorkeeper conveys the idea of being watchful and giving warning of approaching danger. Ministers are called to be vigilant and fulfill their duty as watchmen for God's church. The parable speaks to Jesus ascending to heaven, leaving the church under the care of the apostles.
