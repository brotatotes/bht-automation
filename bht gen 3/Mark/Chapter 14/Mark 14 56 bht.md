## BHT:
The testimonies against Him were not equal or consistent with one another, which was necessary for a legal conviction. Even if just two of them had agreed, it would have been enough to bring about His arrest. It is remarkable how the enemies of Jesus managed to fumble their attempts to produce credible witnesses. While they were asserting that God had abandoned Him, God, His ultimate Witness, was watching over Him. There were many false witnesses who spoke against Him, but none of them could provide joint testimony that would justify a capital sentence.
