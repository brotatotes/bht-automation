# Mark 9:33 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
After their journey to Capernaum, Jesus allowed his disciples some privacy to ponder the weighty news he had shared with them. During this time, the disciples had been occupied with a different matter altogether. Rather than considering the impending death of their Master, they were debating amongst themselves about their individual rankings in the political kingdom they expected Jesus to establish. Jesus, aware of their discussion, used the opportunity to address their pride and ambition. He desired to teach them humility and give them insight into the nature of his kingdom and its subjects.

### Archibald T. Robertson:
1. "They had been disputing, not about the coming death of the Master, but about the relative rank of each of them in the political kingdom which they were expecting him to establish."
2. "Jesus had suspected the truth about them and they had apparently kept it up in the house."
3. "Probably they asked Jesus first and then he pushed the matter further and deeper to see if this had not been the occasion of the somewhat heated discussion on the way in."
