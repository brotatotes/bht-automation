When entering a house, one should extend the usual civilities as a sign of respect. As you enter, simultaneously offer your salutations, which act as an opening to a conversation and a test of the house's worthiness. The act of saluting is a way to make trial and see if your entertainers will willingly listen to you. If they reject, withdraw your salutation and depart from them immediately.
