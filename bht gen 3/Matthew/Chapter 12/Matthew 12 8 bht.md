# Matthew 12:8 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
The Son of Man, as the representative and Head of humanity, has complete authority over the Sabbath. By merging it with the Lord's Day, he imparts an air of liberty, love, and eternal rest to it. This claim of Messiahship affirms his solidarity with mankind and his right to interpret and preside over the Sabbath. Jesus' authority over the Sabbath proves his divinity and allows him to dispense with and interpret the laws. His ultimate act of authority is seen in changing the Sabbath from the seventh to the first day of the week.
