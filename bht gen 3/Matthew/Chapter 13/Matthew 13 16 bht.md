# Matthew 13:16 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
Jesus declares, Blessed are your eyes, for they see; and your ears, for they hear. These words are a beatitude for the disciples who willingly opened their eyes and ears to receive the divine light. Through the Gospel, they perceive the glory of the only-begotten Son of God, recognizing Him as the Redeemer. Their eyes truly behold His personal and transcendent glories, while their ears hear the words of truth that lead them to understanding and bearing fruitful belief. Indeed, their blessed eyes and ears are privileged to receive divine revelation.
