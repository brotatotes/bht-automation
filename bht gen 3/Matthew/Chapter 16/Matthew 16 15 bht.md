## BHT:
The confession that Jesus is the Christ, the Son of the living God, acknowledges both the human and divine nature of the Lord. It surpasses the Jewish Messianic concept of a chosen man. Jesus wanted to hear his disciples' belief about his identity, and Peter's emphatic response recognized him as the anointed Son of God. This confession necessitates a resolute expression of faith, both in belief and outward proclamation.
