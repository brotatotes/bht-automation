# Matthew 18:12 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
In this parable, Jesus emphasizes the diligent efforts of the good Shepherd to find and save even one lost sheep. The Shepherd's reluctance to lose a sheep is seen in His act of leaving the ninety-nine on the mountains to find the one that wandered away. The reference to wandering stars illustrates those who have strayed from the path. As disciples, it is our duty to gather and bring back those who have gone astray, rejoicing when they return to the path of duty.
