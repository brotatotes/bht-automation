# Matthew 18:5 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
The Lord accentuates the significance of children and their representation of humility, revealing the honor they hold in His heavenly realm. Believers are urged to receive these little ones in His name, signifying their participation in Christian love and association with Christ. This applies to both tangible and spiritual children. By welcoming disciples who exemplify meekness and humility, one essentially receives Christ Himself. Thus, believers are encouraged to esteem and appreciate one another by humbling themselves, as the respect shown to the modest and lowly is comparable to receiving Christ.
