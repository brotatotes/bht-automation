# Matthew 19:11 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
Not everyone can accept that staying unmarried is better, as it is a saying meant only for those divinely intended to receive it. Jesus recognizes that this demand surpasses the capacity of many and appeals to the spiritual intelligence of his disciples to understand his true meaning. While it might be good in certain circumstances, it is not universally applicable. This high ideal can only be grasped and lived out by those who receive illumination and power from God, for it is not a natural inclination but a gift of grace.
