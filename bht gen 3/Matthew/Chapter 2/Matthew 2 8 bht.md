# Matthew 2:8 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
In this account, deceitful Herod cunningly sends the wise men on a mission to search out and gather accurate information about the child. Disguised as a safe conduct, Herod's true intention is to destroy Jesus. However, God intervenes to protect His Son from Herod's jealous rage. The absence of Jewish witnesses or Herod's courtiers accompanying the wise men raises questions about the credibility of Herod's actions.
