# Matthew 22:16 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
Approached by the Herodians, followers of Herod, Jesus faced deceptive flattery implying He was a reckless simpleton. However, their acknowledgment of His independence and sincerity was a temptation. Nonetheless, Jesus, as a true teacher, fearlessly taught the ways of God and the true interpretation of Scripture. He did not show favoritism based on wealth or power and remained unwavering in his commitment to truth, unaffected by flattery or threats. The loyalty of the Herodians to Herod and the Roman government did not sway Jesus' dedication to teaching the truth.
