## BHT:
Woe unto you, scribes and Pharisees, hypocrites! for ye devour widows' houses... The scribes and Pharisees took advantage of the helpless widows, exploiting their confiding nature to seize their property. This behavior is reminiscent of the Romish clergy, who are described as the successors of these scribes. Not only did they defraud the widows, but they also made a pretense of piety, engaging in long prayers as a cover for their deceit. Jesus condemns them for their insincerity and warns of the greater damnation that awaits them. Their actions reveal the impurity that tainted even their worship of God.
