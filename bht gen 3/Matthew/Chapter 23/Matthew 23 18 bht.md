# Matthew 23:18 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
The phrase he is guilty in the context of the altar of burnt-offerings implies a state of indebtedness or obligation. The rendering of this word as oweth or debtor suggests this sense. The significance and sanctity of the temple and the offerings on the altar are emphasized by the reversed order of hallowed things, indicating their equal holiness. Jesus' intention is to direct the purpose of swearing to its lawful end. Thus, if one swears by the gift on the altar, fulfilling the oath becomes obligatory.
