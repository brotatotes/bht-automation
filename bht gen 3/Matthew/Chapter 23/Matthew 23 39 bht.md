# Matthew 23:39 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
Jesus declares that He will not reveal Himself until the people repent and recognize Him as their Messiah. This prophetic moment will occur when all of Israel acknowledges Him with joyful hearts, saying, Blessed is He who comes in the name of the Lord. The departure of Jesus signifies the desertion and desolation of the temple, for the glory of God has departed from Jerusalem. The desolation will persist until the appointed time when Israel will be converted and seek the Lord their God, recognizing Jesus as their Redeemer and Judge.
