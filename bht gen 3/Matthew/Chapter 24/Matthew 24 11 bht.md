## BHT:
False prophets, warned against by Jesus Himself, still exist today. They are the ones who claim to be prophets or foretellers of future events. Throughout history, starting from the time of the siege of Jerusalem, there have always been those who deceive others with their false teachings. Pious minds need not be dismayed by these imposters, but rather stand firm against their attacks.
