## BHT:
The parable of the ten virgins highlights the distinction between the wise and the foolish in their readiness for the coming of the Bridegroom. The foolish virgins, lacking oil, hurried to obtain some, but found the door shut and their chance for entrance lost. This vivid depiction of almost being saved but ultimately lost serves as a powerful warning. The parable underscores the importance of being prepared and ready for Christ's coming, as those who are ready will be admitted while the door closes for the unprepared.
