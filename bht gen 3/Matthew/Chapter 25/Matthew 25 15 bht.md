## BHT:
Each person in this parable has been given talents according to their individual character and abilities, all of which are gifts from God. It is not about what a person deserves, but rather about the opportunities they have been given to serve and advance the kingdom of the Lord. The word talents here signifies not only natural gifts, but also the spiritual endowments and ministerial gifts that equip individuals to serve as stewards of God's grace. We are reminded that these gifts are not to be hoarded or wasted, but used faithfully to bring glory to God.
