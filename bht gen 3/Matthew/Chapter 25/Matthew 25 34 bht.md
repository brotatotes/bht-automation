### Albert Barnes:
1. "Christ says to the righteous that the kingdom was prepared for 'them.' Of course, God meant to confer it on 'them.' They were individuals, and it follows that He intended to bestow His salvation on them as individuals."
2. "The phrase 'from the foundation of the world' is used to denote that this was appointed for them in the beginning; that God has no new plan; that the rewards which he will now confer on them he always intended to confer."
3. "The salvation of His people is universally represented as the result of the free gift of God, according to His own pleasure, bestowed on individuals, and by a plan which is eternal."

3. "It is no slight persuasive to patience, when men are fully convinced that they do not run in vain; and therefore, lest our minds should be cast down by the pride of the ungodly... let us always remember the inheritance which awaits us in heaven."
