## BHT:
The Coming One will baptize with the Holy Spirit and fire, a power surpassing John's water baptism. This baptism signifies the fiery character of the Spirit's operations on the soul, searching, consuming, refining, and sublimating. John humbly compares himself to the lowliest of servants, feeling unworthy to even take off the sandals of the Coming One. The baptism of the Holy Spirit refers to the inward work of conversion, purification, and guidance. The powerful and purifying influences of the Spirit were evident on the day of Pentecost, symbolized by the appearance of fire.
