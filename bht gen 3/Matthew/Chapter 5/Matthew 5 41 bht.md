## BHT:
When compelled to go a mile, our Savior advises going the extra mile, emphasizing peace and a willingness to submit to unreasonable demands. This is exemplified by an official forcing a traveler to carry a letter, similar to Simon of Cyrene carrying the cross for Christ.
