## BHT:
Jesus cautioned against praying in a manner that seeks public attention and praise, warning against the hypocrites who love to pray in synagogues and on street corners that they may be seen of men. While the standing posture in prayer was common, it was the inward motive of seeking approval that was condemned. Jesus emphasized that true prayer should be sincere and private, focusing on the relationship with God rather than the applause of others.
