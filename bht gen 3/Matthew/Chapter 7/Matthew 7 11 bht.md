## BHT:
The fallen nature and corruption within us are evidence of original sin; however, our gracious Father in heaven does not extinguish His love for His pleading children. Earthly fathers, imperfect as they are, know how to give good gifts to their children, so how much more will our Heavenly Father, who is omniscient and omnipotent, give good things to those who ask Him?
