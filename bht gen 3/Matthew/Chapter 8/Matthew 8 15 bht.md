### Archibald T. Robertson:
1. "Touched her hand... in loving sympathy as the Great Physician and like any good doctor today."
2. "Ministered... 'Began to minister' at once to Jesus at table in gratitude and love."

### John Gill:
1. "Just as he did the winds and sea, having all diseases, as well as the elements, at his beck and control." 
2. "The former of these actions is a proof of her being restored to health and strength, in so much that she could rise and walk about of herself; whereas generally, persons after fevers continue very weak a considerable time; which shows what a miracle was wrought upon her by Christ." 
3. "The latter of them expresses her gratitude, for the mercy she had received; she rises and serves him and his friends, preparing proper and suitable provisions for them."
