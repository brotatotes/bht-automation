### John Gill:
1. "For she said within herself,.... That is, she thought within herself, she reasoned the matter in her mind, she concluded upon it, and firmly believed it..."
2. "Her faith was, that if she might be allowed, or if she could by any means come at him, to touch any part of his garment, she should have a cure..."
3. "I shall be whole, or 'I shall be saved'; that is, from her disease, from which she could have no deliverance, by the advice and prescriptions of all her former physicians, and by all the means she had made use of."
