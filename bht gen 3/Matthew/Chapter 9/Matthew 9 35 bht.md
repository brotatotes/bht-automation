### Jamieson-Fausset-Brown:
1. "To these fruitful journeyings of the Redeemer, 'with healing in His wings,' Peter no doubt alludes, when, in his address to the household of Cornelius, he spoke of 'How God anointed Jesus of Nazareth with the Holy Ghost and with power: who went about doing good, and healing all that were oppressed of the devil: for God was with Him'." 
2. "Jesus Compassionating the Multitudes, Asks Prayer for Help." 
3. "Soon after this, it should seem, the multitudes still hanging on Him, Jesus is touched with their wretched and helpless condition, and acts as is now to be described."

### Albert Barnes:
1. "Jesus went about all the cities and villages."
2. "His labors were, as yet, confined to that part of Palestine."
3. "Compare the notes at Matthew 4:24-25."

### John Calvin:
1. "The whole ministry of Christ is not minutely described, for he was constantly employed in the discharge of his office; that is, in proclaiming the doctrine of salvation, and in confirming it by the addition of miracles."
2. "The gospel of the kingdom is a designation given to it from its effect, for in this way God gathered to himself a people sadly scattered, that he might reign in the midst of them."
3. "We must be subject to God, in order that we may be exalted by him to the heavenly glory."

### Philip Schaff:
1. "An appropriate introduction to what follows, as well as a fitting close to this account of the leading miracles performed by our Lord."
2. "Almost identical with Matthew 4:23, which precedes the Sermon on the Mount, describing a customary course of action."
3. "This third circuit seems to have begun before the Apostles were sent out and to have continued until their return."
