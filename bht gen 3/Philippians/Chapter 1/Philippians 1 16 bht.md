## BHT:
These verses highlight the contrasting motivations behind preaching Christ. Some do so sincerely with pure motives and pure intentions, out of love for Paul and love for Christ. On the other hand, there are those who preach in pretense, driven by ambition and a desire to exalt themselves. Their intentions are not genuine, as they seek to cause trouble and stir up strife.
