## BHT:
Confident in his prophetic intimations, Paul knows he will continue with the Philippians for their advancement and joy in faith. He desires to promote their growth and increase their rejoicing in belief. Though he lacks a clear revelation of the outcome, he is persuaded of the significance of his presence among them.
