## BHT:

The grumblings and profitless disputes with our fellow men are a result of vain glory and moral rebellion against God. Instead, let us conduct ourselves agreeably among others, without making ourselves odious. We should do what is agreeable to the will of God without dispute or hesitation, even if it goes against our desires.
