## BHT:
Believers are called to be like heavenly luminaries, holding forth the light of Christ's word. Christians are the light of the world, reflecting the light from Christ and shining in the darkness where it is needed. By holding fast to the word of life and making it known to others, believers bring glory and joy to those who labor for their spiritual growth. ### Albert Barnes:
1. "The duty here enjoined is that of making the gospel known to others, and of thus keeping up the knowledge of it in the world."

### Marvin Vincent:
1. "The verb means literally to hold upon or apply. Hence to fix attention upon."
2. "In classical Greek, to hold out, present, as to offer wine to a guest or the breast to an infant."
3. "Lit., for a cause of glorying unto me."

### John Calvin:

3. "The greater the exploits, the triumph will be the more splendid."

### Philip Schaff:
