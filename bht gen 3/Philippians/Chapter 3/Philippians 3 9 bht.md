# Philippians 3:9 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
Paul acknowledges that his righteousness, according to the law, is insufficient and defective. Instead, he seeks a righteousness obtained through faith in Christ, which is perfect and glorious. By being found in Christ and united with Him in both time and eternity, Paul finds a true and intimate relationship that surpasses mere profession. This righteousness is a gift from God, received through His grace, contrasting his own righteousness under the law. It emphasizes the distinction between human effort and the righteousness that comes from God through faith.
