# Revelation 11:10 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
When the two prophets are silenced, the inhabitants of the earth rejoice and exchange gifts in a jubilant celebration. These prophets have tormented the dwellers on the earth with their uncompromising testimony against prevailing errors. The followers of the Antichrist find relief in the perceived silence of righteousness, which leads to their exultation. This mockery highlights the pleasure that earthly-minded individuals derive from the persecution and removal of faithful messengers, mirroring the rejoicing of the ungodly in the destruction of those who stand for truth.
