# Revelation 13:18 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
Here is wisdom: let him who hath understanding calculate the number of the beast. The number 666, representing the beast, confounds interpreters and escapes easy solutions. Some propose the word lateinos, but the true calculation remains uncertain. It takes special sagacity and uncommon skill to compute and ascertain the exact import of the number, revealing the name and nature of the antichristian beast. Spiritual wisdom is needed to solve the mystery of iniquity, so as not to be beguiled by it.

### Jamieson-Fausset-Brown:
1. "Spiritual wisdom is needed to solve the mystery of iniquity, so as not to be beguiled by it."
2. "The number six is the world number; in 666 it occurs in units, tens, and hundreds. It is next neighbor to the sacred seven, but is severed from it by an impassable gulf."
3. "Papal Rome has similarly substituted the standard of the Keys for the standard of the Cross; so on the papal coinage (the image of power). The two first letters of 'Christ', Ch, R, represent seven hundred, the perfect number. The Ch, X, St represent an imperfect number, a triple falling away (apostasy) from septenary perfection."

### Albert Barnes:
1. "The idea is, either that there would be need of special sagacity in determining what the 'number' of the 'beast' or of his 'name' was, or that special 'wisdom' was shown by the fact that the number could be thus expressed."
2. "It implied that it was practicable to 'count the number of the name'; and that it would require uncommon skill to do it."
3. "The word here means 'compute'; that is, ascertain the exact import of the number, so as to identify the beast."

2. "He that understands. 'The one having intelligence' in such matters."
