## BHT:
Come out of her, my people...that ye partake not in her sins, and that ye receive not of her plagues. This urging to separate from Babylon is an invitation that God extends to His people, recognizing their presence even in the midst of corruption. Just as Lot's wife was caught up in the destruction of Sodom due to her lingering attachment, believers must emerge from apostate or world-conforming churches. By answering this call, they ensure they have no fellowship with the sins of Babylon and escape the impending judgment.
