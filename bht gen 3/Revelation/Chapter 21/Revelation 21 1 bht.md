# Revelation 21:1 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
In this glimpse of the eternal kingdom, God dwells with His glorified people on a purified and renewed earth, where the sea no longer symbolizes unrest. The absence of the sea signifies a state of solid peace, reflecting the perfect state of man in which the sea would detract. The old physical world is gone, replaced by a glorious picture of bliss for the saints, a transformation that appears freshly created. This new creation represents a higher state of being, where righteousness prevails and all former things are done away, making all things new.
