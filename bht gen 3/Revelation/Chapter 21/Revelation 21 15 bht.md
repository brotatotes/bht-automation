## BHT:
The measuring of the city, gates, and walls with a golden reed represents the consecration of every part and God's guardianship over it. The use of a golden reed emphasizes the dignity of God's service. This measuring reveals the city's proportions and highlights its noble dimensions. Not a man is the measurer, and the measures themselves are precise and real, signifying the perfection and exactness of everything in this glorious church state.
