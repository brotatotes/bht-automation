The foundation-stones of the city's wall are adorned with every precious stone, including the sky-blue sapphire and the agate from Chalcedon. These precious stones constituted the 'foundations' and visibly represent the radiance and superb structure. They closely correspond with the twelve stones on the high priest's breastplate, signifying the worth and preciousness of Christ, the one and only foundation.
