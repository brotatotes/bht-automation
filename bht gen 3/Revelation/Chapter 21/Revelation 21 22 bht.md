# Revelation 21:22 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
In this heavenly city, the Lord God Almighty Himself is the temple. The inhabitants no longer need a physical place of worship or sacrifice, as the great Sacrifice Himself is present. Means of grace and church ordinances will cease, replaced by uninterrupted, direct communion with God and the Lamb. The entire city becomes a holy temple, sanctified by the eternal presence of God and the Lamb. In this perfected state, God will be immediately present, filling the new heaven and earth, and sanctifying all within.
