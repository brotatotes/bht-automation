### Henry Alford:

1. "All that was truly glorious and excellent in the earth and its converted nations shall be gathered into it." 
2. "While all shall form one Bride, there shall be various orders among the redeemed."

### Albert Barnes:
1. And they shall bring ... - See the notes on Revelation 21:24. That blessed world shall be made up of all that was truly valuable and pure on the earth.


### Philip Schaff:
1. "The nations shall stream into the city with their gifts, to lay their best upon its altars, and to enjoy in turn its rest and peace and security and light."
2. "The New Jerusalem receives freely, and possesses for ever, the glory and honour of the lungs of the earth."
3. "She receives without seeking it all that Babylon had become a harlot to obtain, and could not keep."

### Archibald T. Robertson:
1. "Rome gathered the merchandise of the world."
2. "The City of God will have the best of all the nations."
3. "An expansion of verse Revelation 21:24."

### John Gill:
1. "The glory of the nations are the people of God, who, though they are reckoned the filth of the world, are the excellent in the earth."
2. "These will be brought into this city, and be presented to the King of it, in a glorious manner, and that by the kings of the earth."
3. "Whatever is great and glorious in the nations of the world, will be counted as nothing in comparison of the glory and magnificence of this state."

### John Wesley:
1. "They shall bring the glory of the nations into it." 
2. "A select part of each nation... all which can contribute to make this city honorable and glorious shall be found in it."
3. "All that was rich and precious throughout the world was brought into one city."
