# Revelation 22:12 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
Behold, I come quickly, and my reward is with me, spoken by our Lord, emphasizes the imminence of Christ's second coming and the certainty of the reward awaiting each individual. Just as the Lord rewards both the wicked and the righteous, every person will receive their rightful recompense according to their deeds. Let us, therefore, be prepared and diligent in presenting works that are pleasing to the Lord, for each one will be given their due reward based on their own actions.
