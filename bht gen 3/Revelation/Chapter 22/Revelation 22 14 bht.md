## BHT:
Blessed are they that wash their robes...in the Blood of the Lamb... Those who wash their robes in the blood of the Lamb are granted the privilege to partake in the tree of life and enter into the city. This privilege of access is not earned through personal merit, but rather bestowed through God's grace. It is a reminder that salvation is not achieved by human works, but by the sacrifice of Christ. As believers, we are united as one in the Church and have authority over the tree of life.
