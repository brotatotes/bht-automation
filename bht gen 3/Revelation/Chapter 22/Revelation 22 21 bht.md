# Revelation 22:21 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
May the grace of the Lord Jesus be with the saints, a fitting benediction that concludes this apocalyptic book, meant to be read in the churches. It is a good word for the close of this marvelous picture of God's gracious provision for his people on earth and in heaven. Let a sense of the love of Christ abide upon you, as you see your interest in the redeeming grace of Christ and trust in its fullness. May the free love of the Lord Jesus and all its fruits be with all who long for his appearing.

Amen."
