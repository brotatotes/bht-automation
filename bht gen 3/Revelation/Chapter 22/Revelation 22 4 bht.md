## BHT:
In the future New Jerusalem, the redeemed shall have the privilege of seeing God's face and know Him intimately as they are known by Him. There shall be no need for artificial light, as the Lord God Himself shall shine upon them. Mutual love among the members of Christ's family will flow freely, unencumbered by suspicion.
