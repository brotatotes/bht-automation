## BHT:
Those who conquer and endure as Christ's soldiers and servants will receive the reward of white garments, representing transfigured spiritual bodies shining with the likeness of Christ. Their names, if found in the book of life, will never be erased. This book serves as a register of God's chosen ones, a list of those who have overcome sin and remained undefiled. As they confess Christ on earth, He will confess their names before the Father in the great day, granting them eternal glory, happiness, and prosperity.
