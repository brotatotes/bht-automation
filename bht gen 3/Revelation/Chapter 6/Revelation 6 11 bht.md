# Revelation 6:11 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
These blessed ones, dressed in white robes, rest for a little time in peaceful victory. This resting period will last until their fellow servants who will face persecution are fulfilled and join them. The number of martyrs is divinely determined, and when that number is complete, God will pour out His wrath on those who have persecuted them. These white robes are not exclusive to the martyrs, but are bestowed upon all who overcome, symbolizing purity and victory.
