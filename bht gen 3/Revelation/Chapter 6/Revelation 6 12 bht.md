# Revelation 6:12 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
In this vision, the heavens are shaken, as well as the sea and the dry land. The sun becomes black as sackcloth of hair, and the whole moon turns into blood. This imagery depicts the immediate coming of the day of the Lord, signaling a necessary preliminary to the establishment of unshakable things. The parallelism with Matthew 24:29 further underscores the significance of this event, emphasizing the shaking of the powers of the heavens. The darkness of the sun symbolizes the removal of pagan gods, while the moon turning into blood suggests the downfall of idolatry.
