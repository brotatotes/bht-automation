## BHT:
The cataclysmic disruption portrayed here presents a vivid image of the sky being rolled up and separated, indicating a complete removal. This powerful imagery suggests a pre-Adamic-like upheaval, preparing the earth for its current inhabitants and hinting at the creation of a new world.
