# Revelation 7:15 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
Blessed are those who wash their robes, for they can enter the heavenly city and serve God day and night with a spiritual worship. God's dwelling among them signifies a sacred fellowship and His protection. The redeemed will have intimate communion with God, having come out of great affliction and having cleansed their robes in His blood. Their service will entail praising and adoring God's nature and works in the new Jerusalem state.
