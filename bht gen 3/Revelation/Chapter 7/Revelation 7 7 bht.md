## BHT:
The tribe of Levi, known for being joined together in love and holy fellowship with full purpose of heart, serves as a reminder of the rewards of grace bestowed on faithful servants in times of tribulation. Similarly, the tribe of Issachar exemplifies the practical hearing and obedience of the Gospel, receiving the hire and rewards of grace from God. While Levi was once set apart in the Levitical ceremonies, it is now on an equal level with the other tribes, displaying the unity of God's people.

3. "The interpretation of this name [Issachar] is 'hire' or 'reward'...and may design those rewards of grace which God bestows upon his faithful servants, in times of tribulation."
