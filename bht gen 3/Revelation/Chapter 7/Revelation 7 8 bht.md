# Revelation 7:8 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
The sealing of twelve thousand from each tribe, including Zabulon, Joseph, and Benjamin, is a significant sign. Zabulon signifies dwelling, reflecting God's gracious inhabitation among the sealed ones. Joseph signifies adding, implying the increase of numbers and spiritual gifts in the churches of that time. Benjamin, meaning the Son of the right hand, signifies that the sealed ones are as dear to God as a man's right hand is to him. However, Dan is not mentioned, possibly due to early idolatry and being reduced to a single family.

### John Gill:
1. "Of the tribe of Zabulon [were] sealed twelve thousand,.... Which signifies 'dwelling'... and may denote his gracious inhabitation, as well as that of God the Father, and of the Spirit, among the saints and sealed ones."
2. "Of the tribe of Joseph [were] sealed twelve thousand; whose name signifies 'adding'... and may intend the additions both of numbers, and of gifts and graces to the churches of those times."
3. "Of the tribe of Benjamin [were] sealed twelve thousand; this word signifies 'the Son of the right hand'... these sealed ones are as a signet on God's right hand, and are as near and dear unto him as a man's right hand is to him."
