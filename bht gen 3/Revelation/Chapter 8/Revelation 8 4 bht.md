## BHT:
The smoke of the incense, given to the angel by Christ the High Priest, ascended along with the prayers of the saints. The smoke accompanies the prayers, making them more acceptable to God. This act of burning incense represents the priestly role of the saints, with the angels serving as their fellow servants.
