# Romans 1:19 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
God has revealed himself to all individuals, not only in what they might have known, but in what they did know. This knowledge has been conveyed to them through their senses, as the phenomena of nature serve as a testimony to the Creator. Every person has this universal objective knowledge of God as the Creator, which is present in their heart and conscience. Therefore, God's wrath is revealed against those who suppress this knowledge. Through the creation of the world, God has manifested his existence and attributes to all mankind, leaving them without excuse for their ignorance. The light of nature has made it known that there is one God, glorious and full of majesty, who has endowed humanity with reason and conscience to perceive his works and learn of his perfections and will.
