## BHT:
Romans 10:15 underscores the importance and dignity of preachers, whose feet are regarded as beautiful because of the message they convey. This Messianic prophecy from Isaiah signifies the universality of their preaching, leaving no excuse for those who do not heed it. The preachers' rapid and vigorous approach is emphasized, reflecting the urgency of their mission. The gospel is not received passively; it is actively brought by those who are sent, a reminder that there is no lawful preaching without a proper mission.
