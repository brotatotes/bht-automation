## BHT:
Boast not against the branches, for it is not thou that bearest the root, but the root thee. Remember that your blessings and standing in the family of God come solely from your union with the spiritual church, whose root is the Father of the faithful. Do not scorn the branches, for you, O Gentile, are like a branch in relation to the root. ### Jamieson-Fausset-Brown:

### Albert Barnes:
1. "The ingrafted shoot, deriving all its vigor and fruitfulness from the stock of another tree, ought not to boast against the branches."
2. "If there were occasion for boasting, it would rather be in the root or stock which sustains the branches; least of all can it be in those which were grafted in, having been before wholly unfruitful."
3. "The tree, even with a part of the branches removed. and others ingrafted, retains its identity, and is never regarded as a different tree."
