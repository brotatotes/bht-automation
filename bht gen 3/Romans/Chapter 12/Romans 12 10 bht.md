# Romans 12:10 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
In brotherly love, Christians are called to show affectionate regard for one another, outdoing each other in showing honor. This contrasts with the prevailing morality of the world and is only possible for those who have been transformed by the love of Christ. The term brotherly love signifies a close bond between believers, resembling that of blood relatives. Therefore, believers are exhorted to love one another as if they were family by nature, demonstrating tender affection. By esteeming others above themselves and taking the initiative to honor one another, believers exemplify the love of Christ.
