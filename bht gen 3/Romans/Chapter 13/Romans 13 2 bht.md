## BHT:
Those who resist the civil power resist the ordinance of God and shall receive punishment from Him. This refers to resisting the authority that God has established, not magistrates being above the laws. We must be cautious not to oppose God's providence in this way, for those who contend with Him in this manner will not go unpunished.
