# Romans 9:23 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
Chosen and prepared beforehand by God, the vessels of mercy serve as instruments through which His divine mercy is exercised, magnifying His name. These vessels demonstrate the magnitude of divine mercy towards the elect and highlight the infinite worthiness of praise as they are used to display God's glorious perfections - His love, grace, wisdom, power, faithfulness, justice, and holiness. Through this, God reveals the riches of His glory, showing His mercy towards sinners and preparing them for eternal happiness, all in accordance with His holiness and justice.
