# Romans 9:8 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
Being children of God is not determined by physical descent, but by being children of the promise, born through divine promise rather than naturally. This applies to all individuals, as privileges weren't solely based on lineage but on God's promises. Believers are the true children of God, born again through the power of God, His Spirit, and His grace. The distinction extends beyond Isaac and Ishmael, illustrating that the promise is specially fulfilled only in some individuals, emphasizing that believers are the children of God.
