# Titus 2:10 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
Servants are instructed to not appropriate what does not belong to them and to show all good fidelity, meaning they are to be faithful in their work and take care of their masters' property. By doing so, they are adorning the doctrine of God our Saviour in all things, showing the fair influence of religion on their lives. This exhortation applies to all, including slaves, as the influence of their example is consequential to religion. Through their faithful and honest conduct, they contribute to the beauty and honor of the Christian name and mirror the glory of God.
