# Titus 3:6 Commentary Help Text

## BHT:
The pouring out of the Holy Spirit is not a matter of debate, as it encompasses the grace received before, during, and after baptism. This pouring out occurs abundantly, as seen in the great Pentecost and in the individual experiences of believers. It is through Jesus Christ our Savior that this pouring out is made possible. The Holy Spirit, whom the Father pours out, is the author of our salvation. This pouring out is compared to the metaphor of water, and through the mediation and merits of Christ, the Holy Spirit is bestowed on those who are members of Christ.

2. "Abundantly; literally, 'richly'."

### Archibald T. Robertson:
