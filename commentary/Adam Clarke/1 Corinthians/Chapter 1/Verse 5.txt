<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="1co+1:5">1 Corinthians 1:5</span>. <i><b>Ye are enriched</b></i><b> - ye abound - </b><i><b>in all utterance</b></i> — εν παντι λογω, <i>In all doctrine</i>; for so the word should certainly be translated and understood. All the truths of God relative to their salvation had been explicitly declared to them; and they had <i>all knowledge</i>; so that they perfectly <i>comprehended</i> the doctrines which they had heard.</p>
