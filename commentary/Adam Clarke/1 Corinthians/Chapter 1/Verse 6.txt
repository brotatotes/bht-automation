<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="1co+1:6">1 Corinthians 1:6</span>. <i><b>As the testimony of Christ</b></i>, &amp;c.] The testimony of Christ is the <i>Gospel</i> which the apostle had preached, and which had been <i>confirmed</i> by various gifts of the Holy Spirit, and miracles wrought by the apostle.</p>
