<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="1co+1:8">1 Corinthians 1:8</span>. <i><b>Who shall - confirm you</b></i> — As the testimony of Christ was <i>confirmed</i> among you, so, in conscientiously believing and obeying, God will <i>confirm</i> you through that testimony. See <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="1co+1:6">1 Corinthians 1:6</span>.</p>
<p><i><b>In the day of our Lord Jesus</b></i> — In the day that he comes to judge the world, according to some; but, in the day in which he comes to destroy the Jewish polity, according to others. While God destroys them who are disobedient, he can save you who believe.</p>
