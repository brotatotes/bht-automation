<p>Verse 23. <i><b>All things are lawful for me</b></i> — I may lawfully eat all kinds of food, <i>but all are not expedient</i>; ου παντα συμφερει. It would not be <i>becoming</i> in me to eat of all, because I should by this offend and grieve many weak minds.
