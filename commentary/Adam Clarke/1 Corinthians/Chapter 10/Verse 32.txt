<p>Verse 32. <i><b>Give none offence</b></i>, &amp;c.] Scrupulously avoid giving any cause of offence either to the unconverted <i>Jews</i> or the unconverted <i>Gentiles</i>, so as to prejudice them against Christianity: <i>nor to the Church of God</i>, made up of converts from the above parties.</p>
