<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="1co+11:9">1 Corinthians 11:9</span>. <i><b>Neither was the man created</b></i>, c.] και γαρ ουκ εκτισθη. for the man was not created upon the woman's account. The reason is plain from what is mentioned above and from the original creation of woman she was made <i>for</i> the man, to be his proper or suitable helper.</p>
