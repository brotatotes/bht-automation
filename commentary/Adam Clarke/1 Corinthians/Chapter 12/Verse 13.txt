<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="1co+12:13">1 Corinthians 12:13</span>. <i><b>For by one Spirit are we all baptized</b></i>, c.] As the body of man, though composed of many members, is informed and influenced by one soul so the Church of Christ, which is his body, though composed of many members, is informed and influenced by one Spirit, the Holy Ghost; actuating and working by his spiritual body, as the human soul does in the body of man.</p>

<p>On this verse there is a great profusion of various readings, which may be found in <i>Griesbach</i>, but cannot be conveniently noticed here.</p>
