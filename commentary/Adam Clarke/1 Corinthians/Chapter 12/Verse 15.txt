<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="1co+12:15">1 Corinthians 12:15</span>. <i><b>If the foot shall say</b></i>, c.] As all the members of the body are necessarily dependent on each other, and minister to the general support of the system, so is it in the Church. All the private members are intimately connected among themselves, and also with their pastors without which union no Church can subsist.</p>
