<p>Verse 29. <i><b>Are all apostles</b></i>, c.] That is: All are <i>not</i> apostles, all are not <i>prophets</i>, c. God has distributed his various gifts among various persons, each of whom is necessary for the complete edification of the body of Christ. On these subjects see the notes on <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="1co+12:7-10">1 Corinthians 12:7-10</span>.</p>
