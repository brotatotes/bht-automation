<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="1co+12:5">1 Corinthians 12:5</span>. <i><b>Differences of administrations</b></i> — διακονιων. Various offices in the Church, such as <i>apostle, prophet</i>, and <i>teacher</i>; under which were probably included <i>bishop</i> or <i>presbyter, pastor,</i> <i>deacon</i>, c. the <i>qualifications</i> for such offices, as well as the <i>appointments</i> themselves, coming immediately from the one Lord Jesus Christ.</p>
