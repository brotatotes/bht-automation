<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="1co+14:10">1 Corinthians 14:10</span>. <i><b>There are, it may be</b></i> — ει τυχοι, For example.</p>
<p><i><b>So many kinds of voices</b></i> — So many different languages, each of which has its distinct articulation, pronunciation, emphasis, and meaning; or there may be so many different nations, each possessing a different language, &amp;c.</p>
