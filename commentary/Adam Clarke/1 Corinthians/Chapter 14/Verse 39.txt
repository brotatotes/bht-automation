<p>Verse 39. <i><b>Covet to prophesy</b></i> — Let it be your endeavour and prayer to be able to <i>teach the way of God</i> to the ignorant; this is the most <i>valuable</i>, because the most <i>useful</i> gift of the Spirit.</p>
<p><i><b>And forbid not to speak with tongues.</b></i> — Let every gift have its own <i>place</i> and <i>operation</i>; let none envy another; nor prevent him from doing that part of the work to which God, by giving the <i>qualification</i>, has evidently called him.</p>
