<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="1co+14:4">1 Corinthians 14:4</span>. <i><b>He that speaketh in an</b></i><b> unknown </b><i><b>tongue</b></i> — In the <i>Hebrew</i> for instance, the knowledge of the depth and power of which he has got by a Divine revelation, <i>edifieth himself</i> by that knowledge.</p>
<p><i><b>But he that prophesieth</b></i> — Has the gift of preaching.</p>
<p><i><b>Edifieth the Church.</b></i> — Speaketh unto men to <i>edification,</i> <i>exhortation</i>, and <i>comfort</i>, <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="1co+14:3">1 Corinthians 14:3</span>.</p>
