<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="1co+15:2">1 Corinthians 15:2</span>. <i><b>By which also ye are saved</b></i> — That is, ye are now in a salvable state; and are saved from your Gentilism, and from your former sins.</p>
<p><i><b>If ye keep in memory</b></i> — Your future salvation, or being brought finally to glory, will now depend on your <i>faithfulness</i> to the grace that ye have received.</p>
