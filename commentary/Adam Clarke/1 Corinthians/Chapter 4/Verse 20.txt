<p>Verse 20. <i><b>For the kingdom of God</b></i> — The religion of the Lord Jesus is <i>not in word</i>-in human eloquence, excellence of speech, or even in <i>doctrines; but in power</i>, εν δυναμει, in the mighty energy of the Holy Spirit; enlightening, quickening, converting, and sanctifying believers; and all his genuine apostles are enabled, on all necessary occasions, to demonstrate the truth of their calling by <i>miracles</i>; for this the original word often means.</p>
