<p><i><b>Shall I then take</b></i>, &amp;c.] Shall we, who profess to be members of his body, of his flesh, and of his bones, connect ourselves with <i>harlots</i>, and thus dishonour and pollute the bodies which are members of Christ? <i>God forbid</i>! These passages admit of a more literal interpretation. This, if given at all, I must give in a strange language.</p>
