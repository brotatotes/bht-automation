<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="1co+6:7">1 Corinthians 6:7</span>. <i><b>There is utterly a fault among you</b></i> — There is a most manifest <i>defect</i> among you,</p>
<p>1. Of <i>peaceableness</i>;</p>
<p>2. Of <i>brotherly</i> <i>love</i>;</p>
<p>3. Of <i>mutual confidence</i>; and</p>
<p>4. Of <i>reverence</i> for <i>God</i>, and concern for the <i>honour of his cause</i>.</p>
<p><i><b>Why do ye not rather take wrong?</b></i> — Better suffer an injury than take a method of redressing yourselves which must injure your own peace, and greatly dishonour the cause of God.</p>
