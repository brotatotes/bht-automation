Verse 1 Corinthians 6:7. There is utterly a fault among you — There is a most manifest defect among you,1. Of peaceableness;2. Of brotherly love;3. Of mutual confidence; and4. Of reverence for God, and concern for the honour of his cause.Why do ye not rather take wrong? — Better suffer an injury than take a method of redressing yourselves which must injure your own peace, and greatly dishonour the cause of God.
