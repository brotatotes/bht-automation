<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="1co+9:15">1 Corinthians 9:15</span>. <i><b>Neither have I written</b></i>, c.] Though I might plead the authority of God in the law, of Christ in the Gospel, the common consent of our own doctors, and the usages of civil society, yet I have not availed myself of my privileges nor do I now write with the intention to lay in my claims.</p>
