<p>Verse 24. <i><b>Let that therefore abide in you</b></i> — Continue in the doctrines concerning the incarnation, passion, death, resurrection, ascension, and intercession of the Lord Jesus, which you have heard preached from the beginning by us his apostles.</p>
<p><i><b>Ye also shall continue in the Son, and in the Father.</b></i> — Ye who are preachers shall not only be acknowledged as ministers of the Church of Christ, but be genuine children of God, by faith in the Son of his love; and ye all, thus continuing, shall have fellowship with the Father and with the Son.</p>
