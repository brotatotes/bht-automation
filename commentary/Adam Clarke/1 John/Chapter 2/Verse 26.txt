<p>Verse 26. <i><b>These</b></i><b> things </b><i><b>have I written</b></i> — Either meaning the whole epistle, or what is contained in the preceding verses, from the beginning of the 18th to the end of the 25th. <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="1jo+2:18-25">1 John 2:18-25</span></p>
<p><i><b>Them that seduce you.</b></i> — Περι των πλανωντων ὑμας· That is, the <i>deceivers that were among them</i>, and who were labouring to pervert the followers of Christ.</p>
