<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="1jo+4:3">1 John 4:3</span>. <i><b>Every spirit</b></i> — Every teacher, <i>that confesseth not</i> <i>Jesus, is not of God</i> - has not been inspired by God. The words εν σαρκιεληλυθοτα, <i>is come in the flesh</i>, are wanting in AB, several others, both the <i>Syriac</i>, the Polyglot <i>Arabic, AEthiopic, Coptic,</i> <i>Armenian</i>, and <i>Vulgate</i>; in <i>Origen, Cyril, Theodoret, Irenaeus</i>, and others. <i>Griesbach</i> has left them out of the text.</p>
<p>Spirit <i><b>of antichrist</b></i> — All the opponents of Christ's <i>incarnation</i>, and consequently of his <i>passion, death</i>, and <i>resurrection</i>, and the benefits to be derived from them.</p>
<p><i><b>Ye have heard that it should come</b></i> — See <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="2th+2:7">2 Thessalonians 2:7</span>.</p>
<p><i><b>Even now already is it in the world.</b></i> — Is working powerfully both among the Jews and Gentiles.</p>
