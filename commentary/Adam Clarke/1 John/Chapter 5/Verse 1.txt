<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="1jo+5:1">1 John 5:1</span>. <i><b>Whosoever believeth</b></i>, c.] Expressions of this kind are to be taken in <i>connection with the subjects necessarily</i> <i>implied in them. He that believeth that Jesus is the Messiah</i>, and confides in him for the remission of sins, <i>is begotten of God</i> and they who are pardoned and begotten of God love him in return for his love, and love all those who are his children.</p>
