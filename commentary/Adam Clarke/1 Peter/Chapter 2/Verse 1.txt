<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="1pe+2:1">1 Peter 2:1</span>. <i><b>Wherefore, laying aside</b></i> — This is in close connection with the preceding chapter, from which it should not have been separated, and the subject is continued to the end of the 10th verse. <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="1pe+2:10">1 Peter 2:10</span></p>
<p><i><b>Laying aside all malice</b></i> — See the notes on <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="eph+4:22-31">Ephesians 4:22-31</span>. These tempers and dispositions must have been common among the Jews, as they are frequently spoken against: Christianity can never admit of such they show the mind, not of Christ, but of the old murderer.</p>
