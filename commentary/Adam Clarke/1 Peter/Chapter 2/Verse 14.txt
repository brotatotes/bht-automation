<p>Verse 14. <i><b>Or unto governors</b></i> — By <i>king as supreme</i>, the Roman <i>emperor</i> is meant; and by <i>governors</i>, ηγεμοσιν, are meant, leaders, governors, presidents, proconsuls, and other chief magistrates, sent by him into the provinces dependent on the Roman empire.</p>
<p><i><b>For the punishment of evil doers</b></i> — This was the object of their mission; they were to punish delinquents, and encourage and protect the virtuous.</p>
