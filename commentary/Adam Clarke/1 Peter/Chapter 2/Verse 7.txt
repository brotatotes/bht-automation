Verse 7. Unto you therefore which believe — You, both Jews and Gentiles.He is precious — Ὑμιν ουν ἡ τιμη τοις πιστευουσιν· The honour is to you who believe; i.e. the honour of being in this building, and of having your souls saved through the blood of the Lamb, and becoming sons and daughters of God Almighty.Them which be disobedient — The Jews, who continue to reject the Gospel; that very person whom they reject is head of the corner - is Lord over all, and has all power in the heavens and the earth.
