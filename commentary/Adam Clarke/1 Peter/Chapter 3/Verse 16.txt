<p>Verse 16. <i><b>Having a good conscience</b></i> — The testimony of God in your own soul, that in simplicity and godly sincerity you have your conversation in the world. See on the term <i>conscience</i> at the end of Hebrews.</p>
<p><i><b>Whereas they speak evil of you</b></i> — See the same sentiment in <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="1pe+2:11">1 Peter 2:11</span>, and the note there.</p>
