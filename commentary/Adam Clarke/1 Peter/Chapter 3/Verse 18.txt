<p>Verse 18. <i><b>Christ also hath once suffered</b></i> — <span class="_0000ff"><span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ro+5:6">Romans 5:6</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ro+5:6">Romans 5:6</span></span>; <i>"</i><span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="heb+9:28">Hebrews 9:28</span><i>"</i>.</p>
<p><i><b>Put to death in the flesh</b></i> — In his human nature.</p>
<p><i><b>But quickened by the Spirit</b></i> — That very dead body revived by the power of his Divinity. There are various opinions on the meaning of this verse, with which I need not trouble the reader, as I have produced that which is most likely.</p>
