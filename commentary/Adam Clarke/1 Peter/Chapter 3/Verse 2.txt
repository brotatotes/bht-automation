<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="1pe+3:2">1 Peter 3:2</span>. <i><b>Chaste conversation - with fear.</b></i> — While they see that ye join modesty, chastity, and the purest manners, to the fear of God. Or perhaps <i>fear</i>, φοβος, is taken, as in <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="eph+5:33">Ephesians 5:33</span>, for the <i>reverence</i> due to the husband.</p>
