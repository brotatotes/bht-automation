<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="1pe+4:4">1 Peter 4:4</span>. <i><b>They think it strange</b></i> — ξενιζονται. They <i>wonder</i> and are <i>astonished</i> at you, that ye can renounce these gratifications of the flesh for a spiritual something, the good of which <i>they</i> cannot see.</p>
<p><i><b>Excess of riot</b></i> — ασωτιας αναχυσιν. <i>Flood of profligacy</i>; bearing down all rule, order, and restraints before it.</p>
<p><i><b>Speaking evil of</b></i> you] βλασφημουντες. Literally, <i>blaspheming</i>; i.e. speaking <i>impiously</i> against <i>God</i>, and <i>calumniously</i> of <i>you.</i></p>
