<p>Verse 12. <i><b>That ye would walk worthy of God</b></i> — That they should, in every respect, act up to their high calling, that it would not be a reproach to the God of holiness to acknowledge them as his sons and daughters. <span class="_0000ff"><span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="eph+4:1">Ephesians 4:1</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="eph+4:1">Ephesians 4:1</span></span>; <i>"</i><span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="php+1:27">Philippians 1:27</span><i>"</i>; and <i>"</i><span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="col+1:10">Colossians 1:10</span><i>"</i>.</p>
<p><i><b>His Kingdom and glory.</b></i> — His <i>Church</i> here, for that is the <i>kingdom of God</i> among men; and his <i>glory</i> hereafter, for that is the state to which the dispensations of grace in his Church lead. The words, how ever, may be a <i>hendiadys</i>, and signify his <i>glorious</i> <i>kingdom</i>.</p>
