Verse 12. That ye would walk worthy of God — That they should, in every respect, act up to their high calling, that it would not be a reproach to the God of holiness to acknowledge them as his sons and daughters. Ephesians 4:1; Ephesians 4:1; "Philippians 1:27"; and "Colossians 1:10".His Kingdom and glory. — His Church here, for that is the kingdom of God among men; and his glory hereafter, for that is the state to which the dispensations of grace in his Church lead. The words, how ever, may be a hendiadys, and signify his glorious kingdom.
