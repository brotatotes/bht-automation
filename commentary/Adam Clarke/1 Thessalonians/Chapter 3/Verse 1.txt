<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="1th+3:1">1 Thessalonians 3:1</span>. <i><b>Wherefore, when we could no longer</b></i>, c.] The apostle was anxious to hear of their state, and as he could obtain no information without sending a messenger express, he therefore sent Timothy from Athens choosing rather to be left alone, than to continue any longer in uncertainty relative to their state.</p>
