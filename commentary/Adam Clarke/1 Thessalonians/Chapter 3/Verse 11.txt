<p>Verse 11. <i><b>Now God himself and our Father</b></i> — That is: God who is our Father, who has <i>adopted</i> us into the <i>heavenly family</i>, and called us his <i>sons</i> and <i>daughters</i>.</p>
<p><i><b>Direct our way</b></i> — As he was employed in God's work he dared not consult his own inclinations, he looked for continual directions from God, <i>where, when</i>, and <i>how</i> to do his Master's work.</p>
