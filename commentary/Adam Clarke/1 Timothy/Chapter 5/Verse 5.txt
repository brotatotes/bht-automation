<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="1ti+5:5">1 Timothy 5:5</span>. <i><b>And desolate</b></i> — και μεμονωμενη. <i>Left entirely</i> <i>alone</i>-having neither children nor relatives to take care of her.</p>
<p><i><b>Trusteth in God</b></i> — Finding she has no other helper, she <i>continues in prayer and supplication</i>, that she may derive that from God which, in the course of his providence, he has deprived her of among men.</p>
