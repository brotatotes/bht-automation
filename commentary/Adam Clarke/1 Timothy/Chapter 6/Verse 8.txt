<p>Verse 8. <i><b>Having food and raiment, let us be therewith content.</b></i> — αρκεσθησομεθα. Let us consider this a <i>competency</i>. And it is evident that the apostle considers this a competency, and by these words explains what he said <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="1ti+6:6">1 Timothy 6:6</span>.</p>
<p>The word ακεπασματα, which we translate <i>raiment</i>, signifies <i>covering</i> in general; and here means <i>house</i> or <i>lodging</i>, as well as <i>clothing</i>.</p>
