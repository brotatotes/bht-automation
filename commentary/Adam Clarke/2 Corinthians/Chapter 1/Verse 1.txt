<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="2co+1:1">2 Corinthians 1:1</span>. <i><b>Paul, an apostle</b></i> — Paul, commissioned immediately by Jesus Christ himself, <i>according to the will of God</i>, to preach the Gospel to the Gentiles. <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="1co+1:1">1 Corinthians 1:1</span>.</p>
<p><i><b>In all Achaia</b></i> — The whole of the <i>Peloponnesus</i>, or that country separated from the main land by the <i>Isthmus of Corinth</i>. From this we may learn that this epistle was not only sent to the Church at <i>Corinth</i>, but to all the Churches in that country.</p>
