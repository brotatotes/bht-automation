<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="2co+1:13">2 Corinthians 1:13</span>. <i><b>Than what ye read</b></i> — Viz. In the first epistle which he had sent them.</p>
<p><i><b>Or acknowledge</b></i> — To be the truth of God; and which he hoped they would continue to acknowledge, and not permit themselves to be turned aside from the hope of the Gospel.</p>
