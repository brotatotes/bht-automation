<p>Verse 19. <i><b>For the Son of God</b></i>, &amp;c.] If I could have changed my purpose through <i>carnal</i> or <i>secular</i> interests then I must have had the same interest in view when I first preached the Gospel to you, with Silvanus and Timotheus. But did not the whole of our conduct prove that we neither <i>had</i>, nor could <i>have</i> such interest in view?</p>
