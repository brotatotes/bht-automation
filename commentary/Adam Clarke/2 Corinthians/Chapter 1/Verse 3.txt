<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="2co+1:3">2 Corinthians 1:3</span>. <i><b>Blessed</b></i><b> be </b><i><b>God</b></i> — Let God have universal and eternal praise:</p>
<p>1. Because he is the <i>Father of our Lord Jesus Christ</i>, who is the gift of his endless love to man, <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="joh+1:16">John 1:16</span>.</p>
<p><span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="joh+1:2">John 1:2</span>. Because he is the <i>Father of mercies</i>, ὁ Πατηρ των οικτιρμων, the source whence <i>all</i> mercy flows, whether it respect the body or the soul, time or eternity; the <i>source of tender mercy</i>; for so the word implies. <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ro+12:1">Romans 12:1</span>. And,</p>
<p>3. Because he is <i>the God of all comfort</i>-the Fountain whence all consolation, happiness, and bliss flow to angels and to men.</p>
