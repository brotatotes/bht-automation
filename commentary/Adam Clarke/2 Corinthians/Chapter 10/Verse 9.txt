<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="2co+10:9">2 Corinthians 10:9</span>. <i><b>That I may not seem</b></i>, c.] This is an elliptical sentence, and may be supplied thus: "I have not used this authority nor will I add any more concerning this part of the subject, lest I should seem, as my adversary has insinuated, to wish to terrify you by my letters.</p>
