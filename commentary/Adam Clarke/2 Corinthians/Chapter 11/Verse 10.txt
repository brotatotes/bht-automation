<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="2co+11:10">2 Corinthians 11:10</span>. <i><b>As the truth of Christ is in me</b></i> — εστιν αληθεια χριστου εν εμοι. <i>The truth of Christ is in me</i>. That is: I speak as becomes a Christian man, and as influenced by the Gospel of Christ. It is a <i>solemn form</i> of <i>asseveration</i>, if not to be considered in the sense of an <i>oath</i>.</p>
<p><i><b>In the regions of Achaia.</b></i> — The whole of the <i>Peloponnesus</i>, or <i>Morea</i>, in which the city of Corinth stood. From this it appears that he had received no help from any of the other Churches in the whole of that district.</p>
