Verse 2 Corinthians 11:13. For such are false apostles — Persons who pretend to be apostles, but have no mission from Christ.Deceitful workers — They do preach and labour, but they have nothing but their own emolument in view.Transforming themselves — Assuming as far as they possibly can, consistently with their sinister views, the habit, manner, and doctrine of the apostles of Christ.
