<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="2co+11:13">2 Corinthians 11:13</span>. <i><b>For such</b></i><b> are </b><i><b>false apostles</b></i> — Persons who <i>pretend</i> to be apostles, but have no mission from Christ.</p>
<p><i><b>Deceitful workers</b></i> — They <i>do</i> preach and labour, but they have nothing but their <i>own emolument</i> in view.</p>
<p><i><b>Transforming themselves</b></i> — Assuming as far as they possibly can, <i>consistently with their sinister views</i>, the habit, manner, and doctrine of the apostles of Christ.</p>
