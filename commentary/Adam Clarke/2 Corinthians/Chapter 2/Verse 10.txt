<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="2co+2:10">2 Corinthians 2:10</span>. <i><b>To whom ye forgive any thing</b></i> — Here he farther shows them that his sole object in the punishment inflicted on the transgressor, was his amendment and therefore promises to <i>ratify</i>, in the <i>name</i> and <i>authority</i> of <i>Christ</i>, the free pardon which he exhorts them to dispense.</p>
<p><i><b>In the person of Christ</b></i> — As I believe Christ acts towards his penitent soul, so do I. Christ forgives his sin, and takes him to his favour; let us forgive him his offence against the Church, and restore him to its communion.</p>
