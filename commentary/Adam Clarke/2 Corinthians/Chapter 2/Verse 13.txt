<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="2co+2:13">2 Corinthians 2:13</span>. <i><b>I had no rest in my spirit</b></i> — I was so concerned for you, through the love I bear you, that I was greatly distressed because I did not find Titus returned to give me an account of your state.</p>
<p><i><b>But taking my leave of them</b></i> — I went thence into Macedonia, expecting to find him there; and thither he did come; and gave me a joyous account of your state. See <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="2co+8:6">2 Corinthians 8:6</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="2co+8:7">2 Corinthians 8:7</span>.</p>
