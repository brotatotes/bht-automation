<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="2co+3:2">2 Corinthians 3:2</span>. <i><b>Ye are our epistle</b></i> — I bear the most ardent love to you. I have no need to be put in remembrance of you by any epistles or other means; <i>ye are written in my heart </i>- I have the most affectionate remembrance of you.</p>
<p><i><b>Known and read of all men</b></i> — For wherever I go I mention you; speak of your various gifts and graces; and praise your knowledge in the Gospel.</p>
