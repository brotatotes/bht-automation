<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="2co+5:3">2 Corinthians 5:3</span>. <i><b>If so be that being clothed</b></i> — That is, <i>fully prepared</i> in this life for the glory of God;</p>
<p><i><b>We shall not be found naked.</b></i> — Destitute in that future state of that Divine image which shall render us capable of enjoying an endless glory.</p>
