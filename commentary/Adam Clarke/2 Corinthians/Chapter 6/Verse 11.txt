<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="2co+6:11">2 Corinthians 6:11</span>. <i><b>O ye Corinthians, our mouth is open unto you</b></i> — I speak to you with the utmost <i>freedom</i> and <i>fluency</i>, because of my affection for you.</p>
<p><i><b>Our heart is enlarged.</b></i> — It is expanded to take you and all your interests in; and to keep you in the most affectionate remembrance.</p>
<p>The preceding verses contain a very fine specimen of a very powerful and commanding <i>eloquence</i>.</p>
