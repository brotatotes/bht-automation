<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="2co+6:3">2 Corinthians 6:3</span>. <i><b>Giving no offence</b></i> — The word προσκοπη, read προσκομμα, <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ro+14:13">Romans 14:13</span>, signifies a <i>stumbling block</i> in general, or any thing over which a man stumbles or falls; and here means any <i>transgression</i> or <i>scandal</i> that might take place among the ministers, or the Christians themselves, whereby either Jews or Gentiles might take occasion of offence, and vilify the Gospel of Christ.</p>
