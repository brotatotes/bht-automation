<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="2co+6:8">2 Corinthians 6:8</span>. <i><b>By honour and dishonour</b></i> — By going through both; sometimes respected, sometimes despised.</p>
<p><i><b>By evil report and good report</b></i> — Sometimes praised, at other times calumniated.</p>
<p><i><b>As deceivers</b></i> — Said to carry about a false doctrine for our secular emolument.</p>
<p><i><b>And</b></i><b> yet </b><i><b>true</b></i> — Demonstrated by the nature of the doctrine, as well as by our life and conversation, that we are true men; having nothing in view but God's glory and the salvation of the world.</p>
