<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="2co+7:7">2 Corinthians 7:7</span>. <i><b>He told us your earnest desire</b></i> — To see me, and correct what was amiss among yourselves.</p>
<p><i><b>Your mourning</b></i> — Because you had sinned.</p>
<p><i><b>Your fervent mind</b></i> — The zeal you felt to testify your affectionate regard for me.</p>
