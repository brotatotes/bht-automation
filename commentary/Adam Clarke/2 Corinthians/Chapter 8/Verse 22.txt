<p>Verse 22. <i><b>We have sent with them</b></i> — <i>Titus</i> and, probably, <i>Luke,</i> <i>our brother</i>, probably <i>Apollos</i>.</p>
<p><i><b>Now much more diligent</b></i> — Finding that I have the fullest confidence in your complete reformation and love to me, he engages in this business with alacrity, and exceeds even his former diligence.</p>
