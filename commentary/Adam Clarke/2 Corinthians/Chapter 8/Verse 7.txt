<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="2co+8:7">2 Corinthians 8:7</span>. <i><b>As ye abound in every</b></i><b> thing</b>] See the note on <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="1co+1:5">1 Corinthians 1:5</span>. <i>In faith</i>, crediting the whole testimony of God; <i>in utterance</i>, λογω, <i>in doctrine</i>, knowing what to teach: <i>knowledge</i> of God's will, and prudence to direct you in teaching and doing it; <i>in</i> <i>diligence</i>, to amend all that is wrong among you, and to do what is right; <i>and in love to us</i>, whom <i>now</i> ye prize as the apostles of the Lord, and your pastors in him.</p>
<p><i><b>Abound in this grace also.</b></i> — Be as eminent for your <i>charitable</i> <i>disposition</i> as ye are for your <i>faith, doctrine, knowledge,</i> <i>diligence</i>, and <i>love</i>.</p>
