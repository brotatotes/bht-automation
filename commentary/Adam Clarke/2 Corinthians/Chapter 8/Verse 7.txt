Verse 2 Corinthians 8:7. As ye abound in every thing] See the note on 1 Corinthians 1:5. In faith, crediting the whole testimony of God; in utterance, λογω, in doctrine, knowing what to teach: knowledge of God's will, and prudence to direct you in teaching and doing it; in diligence, to amend all that is wrong among you, and to do what is right; and in love to us, whom now ye prize as the apostles of the Lord, and your pastors in him.Abound in this grace also. — Be as eminent for your charitable disposition as ye are for your faith, doctrine, knowledge, diligence, and love.
