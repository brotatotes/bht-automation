<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="2co+9:2">2 Corinthians 9:2</span>. <i><b>I know the forwardness of your mind</b></i> — You have already <i>firmly purposed</i> to contribute to the support of the poor and suffering saints.</p>
<p><i><b>That Achaia was ready a year ago</b></i> — The whole of the <i>Morea</i> was anciently called <i>Achaia</i>, the capital of which was <i>Corinth</i>. The apostle means, not only <i>Corinth</i>, but other Churches in different parts about Corinth; we know there was a Church at <i>Cenchrea</i>, one of the ports on the Corinthian Isthmus.</p>
<p><i><b>Your zeal hath provoked very many.</b></i> — Hearing that the Corinthians were so intent on the relief of the sufferers in Palestine, other Churches, and especially they of <i>Macedonia</i>, came forward the more promptly and liberally.</p>
