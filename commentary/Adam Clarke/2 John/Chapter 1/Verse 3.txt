<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="2jo+1:3">2 John 1:3</span>. <i><b>Grace be with you</b></i> — This is addressed to <i>her</i>, her <i>household</i>, and probably that part of the <i>Church</i> which was more immediately under her care.</p>
<p><i><b>The Son of the Father</b></i> — The apostle still keeps in view the <i>miraculous conception</i> of Christ; a thing which the <i>Gnostics</i> absolutely denied; a doctrine which is at the ground work of our salvation.</p>
