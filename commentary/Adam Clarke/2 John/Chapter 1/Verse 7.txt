<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="2jo+1:7">2 John 1:7</span>. <i><b>For many deceivers</b></i>, c.] Of these he had spoken before, see <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="1jo+4:1">1 John 4:1</span>, &amp;c. And these appear to have been <i>Gnostics</i>, for they denied that <i>Jesus was come in the flesh</i>. And this doctrine, so essential to salvation, none could deny but a <i>deceiver</i> and an <i>antichrist</i>. Instead of εισηλθον <i>are entered in</i>, many excellent MSS. and versions have εξηλθον, <i>are gone out</i>. The sense is nearly the same.</p>
