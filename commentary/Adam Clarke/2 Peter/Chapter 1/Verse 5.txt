<p>Verse 5. <i><b>And beside this</b></i> — Notwithstanding what God hath done for you, in order that ye may not receive the grace of God in vain;</p>
<p><i><b>Giving all diligence</b></i> — <i>Furnishing</i> all <i>earnestness</i> and <i>activity</i>: the original is very emphatic.</p>
<p><i><b>Add to your faith</b></i> — επιχορηγησατε. <i>Lead up hand in hand</i>; alluding, as most think, to the <i>chorus</i> in the Grecian dance, who danced with joined hands. <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="2co+9:10">2 Corinthians 9:10</span>.</p>
<p><i>Your faith </i>- That faith in Jesus by which ye have been led to embrace the whole Gospel, and by which ye have the evidence of things unseen.</p>
<p><i><b>Virtue</b></i> — αρετην. <i>Courage</i> or <i>fortitude</i>, to enable you to profess the faith before men, in these times of persecution.</p>
<p><i><b>Knowledge</b></i> — True wisdom, by which your faith will be increased, and your courage directed, and preserved from degenerating into <i>rashness</i>.</p>
