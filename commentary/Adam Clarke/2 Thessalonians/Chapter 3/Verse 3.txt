<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="2th+3:3">2 Thessalonians 3:3</span>. <i><b>From evil.</b></i> — Απο του πονηρου may be translated, <i>from</i> <i>the devil</i> or <i>from the evil one</i>. They had disorderly men, wicked men, and the evil one or the devil, to contend with; God alone could support and give them the victory; he had <i>promised</i> to do it, and he might ever be <i>confided</i> in as being invariably <i>faithful</i>.</p>
