<p>Verse 11. <i><b>Whereunto I am appointed a preacher</b></i> — κηρυξ, a <i>herald</i>. <span class="_0000ff"><span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="mt+3:17">Matthew 3:17</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="mt+3:17">Matthew 3:17</span></span>.</p>
<p><i><b>And an apostle</b></i> — Sent immediately from God to man.</p>
<p><i><b>A teacher</b></i> — One whose business it is to <i>instruct</i> men, and particularly the Gentiles, to whom he was especially sent; to proclaim the doctrines of eternal life, the resurrection and final incorruptibility of the human body; and, in a word, the salvation both of the body and soul of man by Christ Jesus.</p>
