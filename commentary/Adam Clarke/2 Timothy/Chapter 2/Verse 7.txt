<p>Verse 7. <i><b>Consider what I say</b></i> — Apply my metaphors and similitudes in a proper manner.</p>
<p><i><b>And the Lord give thee understanding</b></i> — But instead of δωη, <i>may</i> <i>he give</i>, ACDEFG, several others, besides <i>versions</i> and <i>fathers</i>, have δωσει <i>he will give</i>. Consider thou properly, and God <i>will</i> give thee a proper understanding of all things that concern thy own peace, and the peace and prosperity of his Church. <i>Think</i> as well as <i>read</i>.</p>
