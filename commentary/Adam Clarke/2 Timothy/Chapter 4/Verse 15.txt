<p>Verse 15. <i><b>Of whom be thou ware also</b></i> — It seems that this rabbin travelled about from place to place for the purpose of opposing the Gospel, <i>the Jews putting him forward</i>, as it is said, <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ac+19:33">Acts 19:33</span>.</p>
<p><i><b>He hath greatly withstood our words.</b></i> — Has been a constant opposer of the Christian doctrines.</p>
