<p>Verse 19. <i><b>Salute Prisca and Aquila</b></i> — Several MSS., <i>versions</i>, and <i>fathers</i> have <i>Priscilla</i> instead of Prisca: they are probably the same as those mentioned <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ac+18:18">Acts 18:18</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ac+18:26">Acts 18:26</span>.</p>
<p><i><b>The household of Onesiphorus.</b></i> — See <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="2ti+1:16">2 Timothy 1:16</span>. Onesiphorus was probably at this time dead: his <i>family</i> still remained at Ephesus.</p>
