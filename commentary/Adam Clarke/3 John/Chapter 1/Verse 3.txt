<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="3jo+1:3">3 John 1:3</span>. <i><b>When the brethren came</b></i> — Probably the same of whom he speaks in the fifth <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="3jo+1:5">3 John 1:5</span> and following verses, and who appear to have been itinerant evangelists.</p>
<p><i><b>The truth that is in thee</b></i> — The soundness of thy faith and the depth of thy religion.</p>
