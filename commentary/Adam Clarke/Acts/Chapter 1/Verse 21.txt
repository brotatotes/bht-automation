<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ac+1:21">Acts 1:21</span>. <i><b>Which have companied with us</b></i> — They judged it necessary to fill up this blank in the apostolate by a person who had been an <i>eye witness</i> of the acts of our Lord.</p>
<p><i><b>Went in and out</b></i> — A phrase which includes all the actions of life.</p>
