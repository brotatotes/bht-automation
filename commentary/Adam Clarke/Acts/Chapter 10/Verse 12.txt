<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ac+10:12">Acts 10:12</span>. <i><b>All manner of four-footed beasts</b></i>, c.] Every species of <i>quadrupeds</i>, whether <i>wild</i> or <i>domestic</i> all <i>reptiles</i>, and all <i>fowls</i>. Consequently, both the <i>clean</i> and <i>unclean</i> were present in this visionary representation: those that the Jewish law allowed to be sacrificed to God, or proper for food; as well as those which that law had prohibited in both cases: such as the <i>beasts</i> that do not chew the <i>cud; fish</i> which have <i>no scales;</i> <i>fowls</i> of prey and such others as are specified in <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="le+11:1">Leviticus 11:1</span>, &amp;c., where see the notes.</p>
