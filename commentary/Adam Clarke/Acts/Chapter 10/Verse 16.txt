<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ac+10:16">Acts 10:16</span>. <i><b>This was done thrice</b></i> — For the greater certainty, and to make the deeper impression on the apostle's mind.</p>
<p><i><b>And the vessel was received up again into heaven.</b></i> — Both Jews and Gentiles came equally <i>from</i> God; and to him, <i>both</i>, by the preaching of the Gospel, shall again <i>return</i>.</p>
