<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ac+10:28">Acts 10:28</span>. <i><b>Ye know how that it is an unlawful thing</b></i>, c.] He addressed the whole company, among whom, it appears, there were persons well acquainted with Jewish customs probably some of them were Jewish proselytes.</p>
<p><i><b>But God hath showed me</b></i>, c.] He now began to understand the import of the vision which he saw at Joppa. A Gentile is not to be avoided because he is a Gentile God is now taking down the partition wall which separated them from the Jews.</p>
