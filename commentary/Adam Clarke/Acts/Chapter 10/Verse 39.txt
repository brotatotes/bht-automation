<p>Verse 39. <i><b>We are witnesses of all</b></i> — In this speech St. Peter may refer, not only to the twelve apostles, but to the six brethren whom he had brought with him.</p>
<p><i><b>Whom they slew</b></i> — As the truth of the <i>resurrection</i> must depend on the reality of the <i>death</i> of Christ, it was necessary that this should be stated, and shown to rest on the most indubitable evidence.</p>
