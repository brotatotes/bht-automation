<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ac+11:17">Acts 11:17</span>. <i><b>God gave them the like gift</b></i>, c.] Viz. the Holy Spirit, and its various gifts and graces, in the same way and in the same measure in which he gave them to us Jews. What was I, that I could withstand God? It was not I who called them to salvation: it was God and the thing is proved to be from God alone, for none other could dispense the Holy Spirit.</p>
