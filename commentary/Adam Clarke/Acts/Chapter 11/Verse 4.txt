<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ac+11:4">Acts 11:4</span>. <i><b>But Peter rehearsed</b></i> the matter <i>from the beginning, and</i> <i>expounded</i> it <i>by order</i>] εξετιθετο αυτοις καθεξης. This is the very <i>style</i> of St. Luke; see his Gospel, <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="lu+1:3">Luke 1:3</span>. To remove their prejudice, and to give them the fullest reasons for his conduct, he thought it best to give them a simple relation of the whole affair; which he does, as we have seen in the preceding chapter, with a few <i>additional</i> circumstances here. See the notes before.</p>
