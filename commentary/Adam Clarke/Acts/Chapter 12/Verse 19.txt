<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ac+12:19">Acts 12:19</span>. <i><b>Commanded that they should be put to death.</b></i> — He believed, or pretended to believe, that the escape of Peter was owing to the negligence of the keepers: jailers, watchmen, &amp;c., ordinarily suffered the same kind of punishment which should have been inflicted on the prisoner whose escape they were supposed to have favoured.</p>
<p><i><b>He went down from Judea to Caesarea</b></i> — How soon he went down, and how long he stayed there, we know not.</p>
