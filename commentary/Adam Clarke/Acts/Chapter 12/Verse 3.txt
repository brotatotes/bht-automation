<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ac+12:3">Acts 12:3</span>. <i><b>He proceeded - to take Peter also.</b></i> — He supposed that these two were pillars on which the infant cause rested; and that, if these were removed, the building must necessarily come down.</p>
<p><i><b>The days of unleavened bread.</b></i> — About the latter end of March or beginning of April; but whether in the third or fourth year of the Emperor Claudius, or earlier or later, cannot be determined.</p>
