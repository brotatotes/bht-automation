<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ac+12:5">Acts 12:5</span>. <i><b>Prayer was made without ceasing</b></i> — The Greek word εκτενης signifies both <i>fervour</i> and <i>earnestness</i>, as well as <i>perseverance</i>. These prayers of the Church produced that miraculous interference mentioned below, and without which Peter could not have thus escaped from the hands of this ruthless king.</p>
