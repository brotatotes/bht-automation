<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ac+13:14">Acts 13:14</span>. <i><b>They came to Antioch in Pisidia</b></i> — This place is mentioned thus to distinguish it from <i>Antioch</i> in <i>Syria</i>, with which it had nothing in common but the name. There were several cities and towns in various districts of these countries called <i>Antioch</i>: some have reckoned up not less than <i>twelve</i>. Pisidia, in which this was situated, was a province of Asia Minor, near to Pamphylia, having Phrygia on the north, and Pamphylia on the south. The position of all these places may be seen on the <i>map</i>.</p>
<p><i><b>Into the synagogue on the Sabbath day</b></i> — Though Paul was now on a special mission to the <i>Gentiles</i>, yet he availed himself of every opportunity, in every place, of making the <i>first</i> offer of salvation to the <i>Jews</i>.</p>
