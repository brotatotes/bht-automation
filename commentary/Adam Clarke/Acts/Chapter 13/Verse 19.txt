<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ac+13:19">Acts 13:19</span>. <i><b>Destroyed seven nations</b></i> — The <i>Canaanites, Hittites,</i> <i>Girgasites, Amorites, Hivites, Peresites</i>, and <i>Jebusites</i>. The rabbins frequently call them שבעה אומות <i>Shebaah Omoth</i>, the <i>Seven</i> <i>Nations</i>.</p>
