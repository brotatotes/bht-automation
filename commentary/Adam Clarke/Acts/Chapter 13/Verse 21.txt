<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ac+13:21">Acts 13:21</span>. <i><b>Saul the son of Cis</b></i> — In all proper names quoted from the Old Testament, we should undoubtedly follow, as nearly as possible, the same orthography: קיש <i>Kish</i>, was the name of this king's father, and so we spell it in the Old Testament, and yet have transformed it into <i>Cis</i> in the New, where the orthography is almost entirely lost.</p>
<p><i><b>The space of forty years.</b></i> — Reckoning from the time of his anointing by Samuel to the time of his death, from A.M. 2909 to 2949.</p>
