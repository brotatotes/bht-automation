Verse Acts 13:26. Men and brethren — This should have been translated brethren simply. Acts 7:2.Children of the stock of Abraham — All ye that are Jews.And whosoever among you feared God — That is, all ye who are Gentiles, and are now proselytes to the Jewish religion.The word of this salvation — The doctrine that contains the promise of deliverance from sin, and the means by which it is brought about; all which is founded on Jesus, of the stock of David, dying and rising again for the salvation of Jews and Gentiles.
