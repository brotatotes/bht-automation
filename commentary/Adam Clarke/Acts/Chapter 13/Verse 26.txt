<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ac+13:26">Acts 13:26</span>. <i><b>Men</b></i><b> and </b><i><b>brethren</b></i> — This should have been translated brethren simply. <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ac+7:2">Acts 7:2</span>.</p>
<p><i><b>Children of the stock of Abraham</b></i> — All ye that are <i>Jews</i>.</p>
<p><i><b>And whosoever among you feared God</b></i> — That is, all ye who are <i>Gentiles</i>, and are now <i>proselytes</i> to the Jewish religion.</p>
<p><i><b>The word of this salvation</b></i> — The <i>doctrine</i> that contains the promise of <i>deliverance from sin</i>, and the means by which it is brought about; all which is founded on Jesus, of the stock of David, dying and rising again for the salvation of <i>Jews</i> and Gentiles.</p>
