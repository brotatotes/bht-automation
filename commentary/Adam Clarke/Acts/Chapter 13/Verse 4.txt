<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ac+13:4">Acts 13:4</span>. <i><b>Being sent forth by the Holy Ghost</b></i> — By his <i>influence,</i> <i>authority</i>, and under his <i>continual direction</i>. Without the <i>first</i>, they were not <i>qualified</i> to go; without the <i>second</i>, they had no <i>authority</i> to go; and without the <i>third</i>, they could not <i>know</i> <i>where to go</i>.</p>
<p><i><b>Departed, unto Seleucia</b></i> — This is generally understood to be <i>Seleucia</i> of <i>Pieria</i>, the first city on the coast of <i>Syria</i>, coming from <i>Cilicia</i>; near the place where the river <i>Orontes</i> pours itself into the sea.</p>
<p><i><b>They sailed to Cyprus.</b></i> — A well known island in the Mediterranean Sea. <span class="_0000ff"><span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ac+4:36">Acts 4:36</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ac+4:36">Acts 4:36</span></span>.</p>
