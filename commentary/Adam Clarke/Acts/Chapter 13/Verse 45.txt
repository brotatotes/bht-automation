<p>Verse 45. <i><b>The Jews - were filled with envy</b></i> — <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ac+5:17">Acts 5:17</span>. These could not bear the Gentiles, who believed in Christ, to be <i>equal</i> with them; and yet; according to the Gospel, it was really the case.</p>
<p><i><b>Contradicting</b></i> — The arguments and statements brought forward by the disciples; <i>and blaspheming</i>, speaking impiously and injuriously of Jesus Christ. This is probably what is meant.</p>
