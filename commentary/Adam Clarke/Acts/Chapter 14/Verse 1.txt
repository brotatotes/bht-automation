<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ac+14:1">Acts 14:1</span>. <i><b>In Iconium</b></i> — See the conclusion of the preceding chapter.</p>
<p><i><b>So spake</b></i> — και λαλησαι ουτως. With such power and demonstration of the Spirit, <i>that a great multitude both of the Jews</i>, genuine descendants of one or other of the twelve tribes, <i>and also of the</i> <i>Greeks</i>, ελληνων, probably such as were proselytes of the gate, <i>believed</i>, received the Christian religion as a revelation from God, and confided in its Author for salvation, according to the apostles' preaching.</p>
