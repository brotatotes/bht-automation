<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ac+14:2">Acts 14:2</span>. <i><b>Stirred up the Gentiles</b></i> — των εθνων, Such as were mere <i>heathens</i>, and thus distinguished from the <i>Jews</i>, and the <i>Greeks</i> who were proselytes.</p>
<p><i><b>Evil affected</b></i> — εκακωσαν, <i>Irritated</i> or <i>exasperated their</i> <i>minds against the brethren</i>, the disciples of Christ; one of their appellations before they were called <i>Christians</i> at Antioch. <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ac+11:26">Acts 11:26</span>.</p>
