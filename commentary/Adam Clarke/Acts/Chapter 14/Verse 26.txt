<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ac+14:26">Acts 14:26</span>. <i><b>And thence sailed to Antioch</b></i> — This was Antioch in <i>Syria</i>; and to reach which, by sea, they were obliged to coast a part of the Mediterranean Sea, steering between <i>Cyprus</i> and <i>Cilicia</i>; though they might have gone the whole journey by <i>land</i>.</p>
<p><i><b>Whence they had been recommended-for the work which they</b></i><b> </b><i><b>fulfilled.</b></i> — The reader will recollect that it was from this Antioch they had been sent to preach the Gospel to the heathen in Asia Minor: see <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ac+13:1-2">Acts 13:1-2</span>; and that they <i>fulfilled</i> that <i>work</i>: see in the same chapter, <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ac+13:48">Acts 13:48</span>; and the circumstantial account of their travels and preaching given in this chapter.</p>
