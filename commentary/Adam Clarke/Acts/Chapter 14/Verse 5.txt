<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ac+14:5">Acts 14:5</span>. <i><b>An assault made</b></i> — ορμη, A <i>desperate attempt</i> was made by <i>their rulers</i>, i.e. by the heathen rulers of the people, and the rulers of the synagogue.</p>
<p><i><b>To use</b></i><b> them </b><i><b>despitefully</b></i> — To expose them, bring them into contempt, and make them appear as monsters, or movers of sedition; and then to <i>stone them</i> for this falsely alleged crime.</p>
