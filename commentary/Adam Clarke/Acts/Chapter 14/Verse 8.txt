<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ac+14:8">Acts 14:8</span>. <i><b>Impotent in his feet</b></i> — αδυνατος τοις ποσιν, He had no muscular power, and probably his ancle bones were dislocated; or he had what is commonly termed <i>club feet</i>; this is the more likely, as he is said to have been <i>lame from his mother's womb</i>, and to have <i>never walked</i>.</p>
