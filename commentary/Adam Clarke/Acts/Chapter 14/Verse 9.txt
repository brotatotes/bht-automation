<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ac+14:9">Acts 14:9</span>. <i><b>That he had faith to be healed</b></i> â€” How did this faith come to this poor heathen? Why, by <i>hearing the word of God preached</i>: for it is said, <i>the same heard Paul speak</i>. And it appears that he credited the doctrine he heard, and believed that Jesus <i>could</i>, if he <i>would</i>, make him whole. Besides, he must have heard of the miracles which the apostles had wrought, see <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ac+14:3">Acts 14:3</span>; and this would raise his expectation of receiving a cure.</p>
