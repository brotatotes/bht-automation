<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ac+15:19">Acts 15:19</span>. <i><b>Wherefore my sentence is</b></i> — Διο εγω κρινω, <i>Wherefore I</i> <i>judge</i>. There is an authority here that does not appear in the speech of St. Peter; and this authority was felt and bowed to by all the council; and the decree proposed by St. James adopted.</p>
