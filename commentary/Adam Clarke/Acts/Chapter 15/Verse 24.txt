<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ac+15:24">Acts 15:24</span>. <i><b>Certain which went out from us</b></i> — So the persons who produced these doubtful disputations at Antioch, c., had gone out from the apostles at Jerusalem, and were of that Church: persons zealous for the law, and yet, strange to tell, so conscientiously attached to the Gospel that they risked their personal safety by professing it.</p>
<p><i><b>To whom we gave no such commandment</b></i> — As, therefore, they went out from that Church, they should have taught nothing which was not owned and taught by it much less should they have taught in opposition to it.</p>
