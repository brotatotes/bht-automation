Verse 32. Judas and Silas, being prophets — That is, being teachers in the Church. This signification of the word prophet we have often already seen. See the notes on Acts 11:27; Acts 13:1.Exhorted the brethren — To abide steadily attached to God, and to each other, in peace, love, and unity.And confirmed them.] In the blessed truths they had already received.
