<p>Verse 32. <i><b>Judas and Silas, being prophets</b></i> — That is, being <i>teachers</i> in the Church. This signification of the word <i>prophet</i> we have often already seen. See the notes on <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ac+11:27">Acts 11:27</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ac+13:1">Acts 13:1</span>.</p>
<p><i><b>Exhorted the brethren</b></i> — To abide steadily attached to God, and to each other, in peace, love, and unity.</p>
<p><i><b>And confirmed</b></i> them.] In the blessed truths they had already received.</p>
