<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ac+15:4">Acts 15:4</span>. <i><b>They were received of the Church</b></i> — The whole body of Christian believers.</p>
<p><i><b>The apostles</b></i> — Either the whole or part of the <i>twelve</i>; though we read of none but <i>John, Peter</i>, and <i>James</i>. See <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ga+2:9">Galatians 2:9</span>.</p>
<p><i><b>And elders</b></i> — Those who were <i>officers</i> in the Church, under the apostles.</p>
<p><i><b>They declared</b></i> — To this council they gave a succinct account of the great work which God had wrought by them among the Gentiles. This was St. Paul's third journey to Jerusalem after his conversion. See an account of his <i>first</i> journey, <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ac+9:26">Acts 9:26</span>, and of his <i>second</i> in <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ac+11:30">Acts 11:30</span>.</p>
