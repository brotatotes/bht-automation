At this time Timothy must have been very young; for, several years after, when appointed to superintend the Church at Crete, he appears to have been then so young that there was a danger of its operating to the prejudice of his ministry: <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="1ti+4:12">1 Timothy 4:12</span>, <i>Let no man despise thy youth</i>. He had a very early religious education from his godly mother <i>Eunice</i>, and his not less pious grandmother <i>Lois</i>; and, from his religious instructions, was well prepared for the work to which God now called him.</p>
