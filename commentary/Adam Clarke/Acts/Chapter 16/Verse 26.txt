<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ac+16:26">Acts 16:26</span>. <i><b>There was a great earthquake</b></i> — Thus God bore a miraculous testimony of approbation to his servants; and, by the earthquake, and loosing the bonds of the prisoners, showed, in a symbolical way, the nature of that religion which they preached: while it shakes and terrifies the guilty, it proclaims deliverance to the captives, and the opening of the prison-doors to them that are bound; and sets at liberty them that are bruised.</p>
<p><i><b>Every one's bands were loosed.</b></i> — And yet so eminently did God's providence conduct every thing, that not one of the prisoners made his escape, though the doors were open, and his bolts off!</p>
