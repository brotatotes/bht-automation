<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ac+17:10">Acts 17:10</span>. <i><b>Sent away Paul and Silas by night</b></i> — Fearing some farther machinations of the Jews and their associates.</p>
<p><i><b>Berea</b></i> — This was another city of Macedonia, on the same gulf with Thessalonica; and not far from Pella, the birth place of <i>Alexander</i> <i>the Great</i>.</p>
