<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ac+17:7">Acts 17:7</span>. <i><b>These all do contrary to the decrees of Caesar</b></i> — Persecutors always strive to affect the <i>lives</i> of the objects of their hatred, by accusing them of <i>sedition</i>, or plots against the <i>state</i>.</p>
<p><i><b>That there is another king</b></i><b>, one </b><i><b>Jesus.</b></i> — How malevolent was this saying! The apostles proclaimed Jesus as king-that is true; but never once insinuated that his kingdom <i>was</i> of <i>this world</i>. The <i>reverse</i> they always maintained.</p>
