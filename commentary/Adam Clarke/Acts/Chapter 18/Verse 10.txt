<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ac+18:10">Acts 18:10</span>. <i><b>No man shall set on thee</b></i> — Και ουδεις επιθησεται σοι, No man shall be permitted to <i>lay violent hands</i> upon thee. It is very likely that the Jews had conspired his death; and his preservation was an act of the especial interposition of Divine Providence.</p>
<p><i><b>I have much people in this city.</b></i> — Εν τῃ πολει ταυτῃ, <i>In this</i> <i>very city</i>: there are many here who have not resisted my Spirit, and consequently are now under its teachings, and are ready to embrace my Gospel as soon as thou shalt declare it unto them.</p>
