<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ac+18:11">Acts 18:11</span>. <i><b>He continued there a year and six months</b></i> — He was now confident that he was under the <i>especial</i> protection of God, and therefore continued <i>teaching the word</i>, τον λογον, the doctrine <i>of</i> <i>God</i>. It is very likely, that it was during his stay here that he wrote his <i>first</i> epistle to the <i>Thessalonians</i>, and the <i>second</i> not long after; and some think that the epistle to the <i>Galatians</i> was written during his stay at Corinth.</p>
