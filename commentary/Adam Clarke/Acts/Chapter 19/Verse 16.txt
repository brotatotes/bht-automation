<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ac+19:16">Acts 19:16</span>. <i><b>And the man in whom the evil spirit was</b></i>, c.] Thus we find that one man was more powerful than these <i>seven brothers</i> so that he stripped them of their upper garments, and beat and wounded the whole! Was not this a proof that he derived his strength from the evil spirit that dwelt in him?</p>
