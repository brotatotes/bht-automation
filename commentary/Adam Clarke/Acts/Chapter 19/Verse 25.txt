<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ac+19:25">Acts 19:25</span>. <i><b>By this craft we have our wealth.</b></i> — The word ευπορια not only signifies <i>wealth</i>, but also <i>abundance</i>. It was a most lucrative trade; and he plainly saw that, if the apostles were permitted to go on thus preaching, the worship of Diana itself would be destroyed; and, consequently, all the gain that he and his fellows derived from it would be brought to nought.</p>
