<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ac+19:6">Acts 19:6</span>. <i><b>They spake with tongues, and prophesied.</b></i> — They received the miraculous gift of different languages; and in those languages they <i>taught</i> to the people the great doctrines of the Christian religion; for this appears to be the meaning of the word προεφητευον, <i>prophesied</i>, as it is used above.</p>
