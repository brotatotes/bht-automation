<p>Verse 33. <i><b>By the right hand of God exalted</b></i> — Raised by <i>omnipotence</i> to the highest dignity in the realms of glory, to sit at the right hand of God, and administer the laws of both worlds.</p>
<p><i><b>The promise of the Holy Ghost</b></i> — This was the promise that he had made to them a little before he suffered, as may be seen in <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="joh+14:16">John 14:16</span>, &amp;c., <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="joh+16:7">John 16:7</span>, &amp;c., and after he had risen from the dead. <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="lu+24:49">Luke 24:49</span>, and which as the apostle says was <i>now shed forth</i>.</p>
