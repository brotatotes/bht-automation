<p>Verse 37. <i><b>When they heard</b></i><b> this, </b><i><b>they were pricked in their</b></i><b> </b><i><b>heart</b></i> — This powerful, intelligent, consecutive, and interesting discourse, supported every where by <i>prophecies</i> and corresponding <i>facts</i>, left them without reply and without excuse; and they plainly saw there was no hope for them, but in the mercy of him whom they had rejected and crucified.</p>
<p><i><b>What shall we do?</b></i> — How shall we escape those judgments which we now see hanging over our heads?</p>
