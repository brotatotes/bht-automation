<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ac+2:4">Acts 2:4</span>. <i><b>To speak with other tongues</b></i> — At the building of <i>Babel</i> the <i>language</i> of the people was <i>confounded</i>; and, in consequence of this, they became scattered over the face of the earth: at this <i>foundation</i> of the <i>Christian Church</i>, the gift of various languages was given to the apostles, that the scattered nations might be <i>gathered</i>; and united under one shepherd and superintendent (επισκοπος) of all souls.</p>
<p><i><b>As the Spirit gave them utterance.</b></i> — The word αποφθεγγεσθαι seems to imply such utterance as proceeded from immediate inspiration, and included oracular communications.</p>
