<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ac+20:19">Acts 20:19</span>. <i><b>Serving the Lord with all humility</b></i>, c.] This relates not only to his zealous and faithful performance of his <i>apostolic</i> <i>functions</i>, but also to his <i>private walk</i> as a Christian and shows with what carefulness this apostle himself was obliged to walk, in order to have his calling and election, as a Christian, ratified and made firm.</p>
