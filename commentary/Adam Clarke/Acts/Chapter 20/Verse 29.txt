<p>Verse 29. <i><b>After my departing</b></i> — Referring, most likely, to his <i>death</i>; for few of these evils took place during his life.</p>
<p><i><b>Grievous wolves</b></i> — Persons professing to be <i>teachers</i>; Judaizing Christians, who, instead of feeding the flock, would feed themselves, even to the oppression and ruin of the Church.</p>
