<p>Verse 33. <i><b>I have coveted no man's silver</b></i>, c.] And from this circumstance they would be able to discover the grievous wolves, and the perverters for these had nothing but their <i>own interests</i> in view; whereas the genuine disciples of Christ neither coveted nor had worldly possessions. St. Paul's account of his own disinterestedness is very similar to that given by Samuel of his, <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="1sa+12:3-5">1 Samuel 12:3-5</span>.</p>
