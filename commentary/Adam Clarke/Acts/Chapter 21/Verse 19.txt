<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ac+21:19">Acts 21:19</span>. <i><b>Declared particularly</b></i>, c.] He no doubt had heard that they were prejudiced against him and, by declaring what God had done by him among the Gentiles, showed how groundless this prejudice was: for, were he a bad man, or doing any thing that he should not do, God would not have made him such a singular instrument of so much good.</p>
