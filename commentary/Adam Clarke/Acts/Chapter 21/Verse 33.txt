<p>Verse 33. <i><b>And took him</b></i> — With <i>great violence</i>, according to <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ac+24:7">Acts 24:7</span>, probably meaning an <i>armed force</i>.</p>
<p><i><b>To be bound with two chains</b></i> — To be bound between two soldiers; his right hand chained to the left hand of the one, and his left hand to the right of the other. <span class="_0000ff"><span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ac+12:6">Acts 12:6</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ac+12:6">Acts 12:6</span></span>.</p>
