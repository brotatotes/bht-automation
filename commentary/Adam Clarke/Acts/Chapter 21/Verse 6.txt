<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ac+21:6">Acts 21:6</span>. <i><b>Taken - leave</b></i> — ασπασαμενοι; <i>Having given each other the</i> <i>kiss of peace</i>, as was the constant custom of the Jews and primitive Christians.</p>
<p><i><b>They returned home</b></i> — That is, the men, their wives, and their children.</p>
