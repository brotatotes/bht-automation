Verse Acts 21:8. We that were of Paul's company — Οἱ περι τον Παυλον· This clause is wanting in ABCE, and many others; the Syriac, Coptic, Vulgate, Armenian, &c.Came unto Caesarea — This was Caesarea of Palestine, already sufficiently described, Acts 8:40.Philip the evangelist — One of the seven deacons, who seems to have settled here after he had baptized the eunuch.
