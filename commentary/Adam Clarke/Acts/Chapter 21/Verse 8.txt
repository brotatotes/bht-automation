<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ac+21:8">Acts 21:8</span>. <i><b>We that were of Paul's company</b></i> — Οἱ περι τον Παυλον· This clause is wanting in ABCE, and many others; the <i>Syriac,</i> <i>Coptic, Vulgate, Armenian</i>, &amp;c.</p>
<p><i><b>Came unto Caesarea</b></i> — This was Caesarea of <i>Palestine</i>, already sufficiently described, <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ac+8:40">Acts 8:40</span>.</p>
<p><i><b>Philip the evangelist</b></i> — One of the seven deacons, who seems to have settled here after he had baptized the eunuch. <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ac+8:40">Acts 8:40</span>.</p>
