<p><i><b>Away with such a</b></i> fellow] According to the law of Moses, he who attempted to seduce the people to any strange worship was to be <i>stoned</i>, <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="de+13:15">Deuteronomy 13:15</span>. The Jews wished to insinuate that the apostle was guilty of this crime, and that therefore he should be stoned, or put to death.</p>
