<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ac+22:25">Acts 22:25</span>. <i><b>And as they bound him</b></i>, &amp;c.] They were going to tie him to a post, that they might scourge him.</p>
<p><i><b>Is it lawful</b></i>, &amp;c.] The Roman law absolutely forbade the <i>binding</i> of a Roman citizen. <span class="_0000ff"><span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ac+16:37">Acts 16:37</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ac+16:37">Acts 16:37</span></span>.</p>
