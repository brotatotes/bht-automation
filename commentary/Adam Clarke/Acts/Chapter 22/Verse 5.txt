<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ac+22:5">Acts 22:5</span>. <i><b>The high priest doth bear me witness</b></i>, c.] He probably referred to the <i>letters of authority</i> which he had received from the high priest, and the <i>whole estate of the elders</i>, παν το πρεσβυτεριον, <i>the whole of the presbytery</i>, that is, the <i>sanhedrin</i> and it is likely, that he had those letters to produce. This zeal of his against Christianity was an ample proof of his <i>sincerity</i> as a Pharisaical Jew.</p>
