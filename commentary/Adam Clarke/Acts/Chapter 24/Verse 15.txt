<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ac+24:15">Acts 24:15</span>. <i><b>And have hope toward God</b></i>, c.] I not only do not hold any thing by which the general creed of this people might be altered, in reference to the <i>present state</i> but, also, I hold nothing different from their belief in reference to a <i>future</i> <i>state</i>; for, if I maintain the doctrine of the <i>resurrection of</i> <i>the dead</i>, it is what <i>themselves allow</i>.</p>
