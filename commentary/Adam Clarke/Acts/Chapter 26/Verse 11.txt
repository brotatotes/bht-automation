<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ac+26:11">Acts 26:11</span>. <i><b>Being exceedingly mad against them</b></i> — Only a <i>madman</i> will persecute another because of his differing from him in religious opinion; and the fiercest persecutor is he who should be deemed the most furious madman.</p>
<p><i><b>Unto strange cities.</b></i> — Places out of the jurisdiction of the Jews, such as Damascus, which he immediately mentions.</p>
