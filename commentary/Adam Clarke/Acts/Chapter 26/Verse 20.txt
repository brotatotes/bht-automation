<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ac+26:20">Acts 26:20</span>. <i><b>But showed first unto them of Damascus</b></i> — He appears to have preached at Damascus, and in the neighbouring parts of Arabia Deserta, for about three years; and afterwards he went up to Jerusalem. See <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ga+1:17-18">Galatians 1:17-18</span>; and <span class="_0000ff"><span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ac+9:23">Acts 9:23</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ac+9:23">Acts 9:23</span></span>.</p>
<p><i><b>That they should repent</b></i> — Be deeply humbled for their past iniquities, and <i>turn to God</i> as their <i>Judge</i> and <i>Saviour</i>, avoiding all <i>idolatry</i> and all <i>sin</i>; and thus <i>do works meet for repentance</i>; that is, show by their <i>conduct</i> that they had <i>contrite hearts</i>, and that they sincerely sought salvation from God alone. For the meaning of the word <i>repentance</i>, <span class="_0000ff"><span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="mt+3:2">Matthew 3:2</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="mt+3:2">Matthew 3:2</span></span>.</p>
