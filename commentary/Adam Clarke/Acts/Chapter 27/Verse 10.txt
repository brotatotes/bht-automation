<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ac+27:10">Acts 27:10</span>. <i><b>I perceive that this voyage will be with hurt</b></i>, &amp;c.] Paul might either have had this intimation from the Spirit of God, or from his own knowledge of the state of this sea after the autumnal equinox, and therefore gave them this prudent warning.</p>
