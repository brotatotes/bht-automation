<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ac+27:13">Acts 27:13</span>. <i><b>When the south wind blew softly</b></i> — Though this wind was not very favourable, yet, because it blew <i>softly</i>, they supposed they might be able to make their passage.</p>
<p><i><b>They sailed close by Crete.</b></i> — Kept as near the coast as they could. See the track on the map.</p>
