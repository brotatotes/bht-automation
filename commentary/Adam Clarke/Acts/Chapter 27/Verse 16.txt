<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ac+27:16">Acts 27:16</span>. <i><b>A certain island - called Clauda</b></i> — Called also <i>Gaudos</i>; situated at the south-western extremity of the island of Crete, and now called <i>Gozo</i>, according to Dr. Shaw.</p>
<p><i><b>Much work to come by the boat</b></i> — It was likely to have been washed overboard; or, if the boat was in <i>tow</i>, at the stern of the vessel, which is probable, they found it very difficult to save it from being <i>staved</i>, or broken to pieces.</p>
