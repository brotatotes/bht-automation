<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ac+27:27">Acts 27:27</span>. <i><b>Driven up and down in Adria</b></i> — <span class="_0000ff"><span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ac+27:17">Acts 27:17</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ac+27:17">Acts 27:17</span></span>.</p>
<p><i><b>Deemed that they drew near to some country</b></i> — They judged so, either by the <i>smell of land</i>, which those used to the sea can perceive at a considerable distance, or by the agitation of the sea, <i>rippling</i> of the tide, flight of sea-birds, &amp;c.</p>
