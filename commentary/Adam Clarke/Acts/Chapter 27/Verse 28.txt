<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ac+27:28">Acts 27:28</span>. <i><b>And sounded</b></i> — βολισαντες, <i>Heaving the lead</i>.</p>
<p><i><b>Twenty fathoms</b></i> — οργυιας εικοσι, About <i>forty yards</i> in depth. The οργυια is thus defined by the Etymologicon: Σημαινει την εκτασιν των χειρων, συν τῳ πλατει του Ϛηθους· <i>It signifies the</i> <i>extent of the arms, together with the breadth of the breast</i>. This is exactly the quantum of our <i>fathom</i>.</p>
