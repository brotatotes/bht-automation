<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ac+27:8">Acts 27:8</span>. <i><b>The Fair Havens</b></i> — This port still remains, and is known by the same name; it was situated towards the northern extremity of the island.</p>
<p><i><b>Was the city</b></i><b> of </b><i><b>Lasea.</b></i> — There is no city of this name now remaining: the Codex Alexandrinus reads αλασσα, <i>Alassa</i>; probably <i>Lysia</i>, near the port of <i>Gortyna</i>, to the eastward.</p>
