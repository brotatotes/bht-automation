<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ac+4:1">Acts 4:1</span>. <i><b>The priests</b></i> — These persons had evidenced the most implacable enmity against Christ from the beginning.</p>
<p><i><b>The captain of the temple</b></i> — See this office particularly explained in the note, <span class="_0000ff"><span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="lu+22:4">Luke 22:4</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="lu+22:4">Luke 22:4</span></span>.</p>
<p><i><b>The Sadducees</b></i> — Whose whole system was now in danger by the preaching of the <i>resurrection</i> of Christ; for they believed not in the immortality of the soul, nor in any future world. These made a common cause with the priests, &amp;c., to suppress the evidence of Christ's resurrection, and silence the apostles.</p>
