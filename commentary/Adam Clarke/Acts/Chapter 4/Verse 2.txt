<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ac+4:2">Acts 4:2</span>. <i><b>Being grieved</b></i> — διαπονουμενοι, They were <i>thoroughly</i> <i>fatigued</i> with the continuance of this preaching; their minds suffered more <i>labour, through vexation</i> at the success of the apostles, than the bodies of the apostles did in their fatiguing exercise of preaching during the whole day.</p>
