<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ac+4:24">Acts 4:24</span>. <i><b>Lord, thou art God</b></i> — Δεσποτα, συ ὁ Θεος, <i>Thou God</i> art the <i>sovereign Lord</i>. Thy rule is universal, and thy power unlimited; for thou hast the heaven and its glories, the earth and the sea, and their endlessly varied and numerous inhabitants, under thy direction and control.</p>
