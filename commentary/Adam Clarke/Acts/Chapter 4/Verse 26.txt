<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ac+4:26">Acts 4:26</span>. <i><b>Against the Lord and against his Christ.</b></i> — κατα του χριστου αυτου should be translated, <i>against his ANOINTED</i>, because it particularly agrees with ονεχρισας, <i>whom thou hast ANOINTED</i>, in the succeeding verse.</p>
