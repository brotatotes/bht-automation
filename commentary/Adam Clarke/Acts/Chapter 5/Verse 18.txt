<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ac+5:18">Acts 5:18</span>. <i><b>Put them in the common prison.</b></i> — It being too late in the evening to bring them to a hearing. To this verse the <i>Codex</i> <i>Bezae</i> adds, και επορευθη εἱς ἑκαστος εις τα ιδια, <i>And each of them</i> <i>went to his own house</i>.</p>
