<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ac+5:21">Acts 5:21</span>. <i><b>Called the council together</b></i> — συνεδριον The <i>sanhedrin,</i> <i>all the senate</i>; την γερουσιαν, the <i>elders</i>, or what we would call <i>the aldermen</i>. How these differed from the πρεσβυτεριον, <i>presbytery</i>, if they did differ, is not now known.</p>
