<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ac+5:6">Acts 5:6</span>. <i><b>The young men arose</b></i> — Some of the stout young men belonging to the disciples then present, who were the fittest to undertake a work of this kind, which required considerable bodily exertion.</p>
<p><i><b>Buried him.</b></i> — This was on the same day in which he died. It was a clear case that he was dead, and dead by a judgment of God that would not be revoked. As therefore it was no case of <i>suspended</i> <i>animation</i>, there was no reason to <i>delay</i> the burial.</p>
