<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ac+7:25">Acts 7:25</span>. <i><b>He supposed his brethren would have understood</b></i>, c.] He probably imagined that, as he felt from the Divine influence he was appointed to be their deliverer, they would have his Divine appointment signified to them in a similar way and the act of justice which he now did in behalf of his oppressed countryman would be sufficient to show them that <i>he</i> was now ready to enter upon his office, if <i>they</i> were willing to concur.</p>
