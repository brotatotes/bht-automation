<p>Verse 38. <i><b>With the angel which spake to him</b></i> — Stephen shows that Moses received the law by the ministry of angels; and that he was only a mediator between the angel of God and them.</p>
<p><i><b>The lively oracles</b></i> — λογια ζωντα, The <i>living oracles</i>. The <i>doctrines</i> of <i>life</i>, those <i>doctrines</i>-obedience to which entitled them, by the promise of God, to a <i>long life upon earth</i>, which spoke to them of that <i>spiritual life</i> which every true believer has in union with his God, and promised that <i>eternal life</i> which those who are faithful unto death shall enjoy with him in the realms of glory.</p>
<p>The Greek word λογιον, which we translate <i>oracle</i>, signifies a <i>Divine revelation</i>, a <i>communication from God himself</i>, and is here applied to the <i>Mosaic law</i>; to the <i>Old Testament</i> in general, <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ro+3:2">Romans 3:2</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="heb+5:12">Hebrews 5:12</span>; and to <i>Divine revelation</i> in general, <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="1pe+4:11">1 Peter 4:11</span>.</p>
