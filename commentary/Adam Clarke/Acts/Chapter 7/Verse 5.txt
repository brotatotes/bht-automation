<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ac+7:5">Acts 7:5</span>. <i><b>Gave him none inheritance</b></i> — Both Abraham and Jacob had small parcels of land in Canaan; but they had them by <i>purchase</i>, not by God's gift; for, as Abraham was obliged to <i>buy a</i> <i>burying-place</i> in Canaan, <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ge+23:3-18">Genesis 23:3-18</span>, it is obvious he had no <i>inheritance</i> there.</p>
<p><i><b>And to his seed after him</b></i> — See <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ge+12:7">Genesis 12:7</span> (note); <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ge+13:15">Genesis 13:15</span>, and the notes there.</p>
