<p>Verse 55. <i><b>Saw the glory of God</b></i> — The <i>Shekinah</i>, the splendour or manifestation of the Divine Majesty.</p>
<p><i><b>And Jesus standing on the right hand of God</b></i> — In his official character, as Mediator between God and man.</p>
<p>Stephen had this revelation while in the Sanhedrin; for as yet he had not been <i>forced out of the city</i>. See <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ac+7:58">Acts 7:58</span>.</p>
