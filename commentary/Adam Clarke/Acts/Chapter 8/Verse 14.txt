<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ac+8:14">Acts 8:14</span>. <i><b>The word of God</b></i> — The doctrine of the Lord Jesus Christ.</p>
<p><i><b>They sent unto them Peter and John</b></i> — There was no individual <i>ruler</i> among the apostles-there was not even a <i>president</i> of the council; and Peter, far from being <i>chief</i> of the apostles, is one of those sent, with the same commission and authority as John, to confirm the Samaritans in the faith.</p>
