<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ac+8:22">Acts 8:22</span>. <i><b>Repent therefore of this thy wickedness</b></i> — St. Peter did not suppose his case to be utterly <i>hopeless</i>; though his sin, considered in its <i>motives</i> and <i>objects</i>, was of the most heinous kind.</p>
<p><i><b>If perhaps the thought of thine heart may be forgiven thee.</b></i> — His sin, as yet, only existed in <i>thought</i> and <i>purpose</i>; and therefore it is said, <i>if perhaps the thought of thine heart may be forgiven</i>.</p>
