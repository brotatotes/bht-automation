<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ac+9:14">Acts 9:14</span>. <i><b>And here he hath authority</b></i>, c.] Ananias had undoubtedly heard of Saul's coming, and the commission he had received from the chief priests and he was about to urge this as a reason why he should have no connection with so dangerous a man.</p>
