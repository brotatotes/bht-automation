<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ac+9:22">Acts 9:22</span>. <i><b>Confounded the Jews</b></i> — συνεχυνε, Overwhelmed them so with his arguments that they were obliged to <i>blush</i> for the weakness of their own cause.</p>
<p><i><b>Proving that this</b></i> — ουτος, This person, viz. JESUS, <i>is very</i> <i>Christ</i>; εστιν ο χριστος, IS THE CHRIST, or <i>Messiah</i>. <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ac+9:21">Acts 9:21</span>.</p>
