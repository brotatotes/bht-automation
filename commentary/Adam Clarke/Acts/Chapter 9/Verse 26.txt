<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ac+9:26">Acts 9:26</span>. <i><b>He assayed to join himself to the disciples</b></i> — επειρατο κολλασθαι, He <i>endeavoured to get closely united to them</i>, to be in religious fellowship with them.</p>
<p><i><b>Believed not that he was a disciple.</b></i> — They did not suppose it possible that such a person could be converted to the faith of Christ. The full power of Divine grace, in the conversion of the soul, was not yet completely known.</p>
