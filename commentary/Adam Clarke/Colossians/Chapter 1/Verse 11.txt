<p>Verse 11. <i><b>Strengthened with all might</b></i> — That they might be able to walk worthy of the Lord, bring forth fruit, &amp;c. <span class="_0000ff"><span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="eph+3:13">Ephesians 3:13</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="eph+3:13">Ephesians 3:13</span></span>, &amp;c.</p>
<p><i><b>According to his glorious power</b></i> — According to that sufficiency of strength which may be expected from him who has all power both in the heavens and in the earth.</p>
<p><i><b>Unto all patience</b></i> — Relieving, hoping, and enduring all things.</p>
<p><i><b>With joyfulness</b></i> — Feeling the continual testimony that ye please God, which will be a spring of perpetual comfort. <span class="_0000ff"><span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="eph+4:2">Ephesians 4:2</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="eph+4:2">Ephesians 4:2</span></span>.</p>
