Verse 11. Strengthened with all might — That they might be able to walk worthy of the Lord, bring forth fruit, &c. Ephesians 3:13; Ephesians 3:13, &c.According to his glorious power — According to that sufficiency of strength which may be expected from him who has all power both in the heavens and in the earth.Unto all patience — Relieving, hoping, and enduring all things.With joyfulness — Feeling the continual testimony that ye please God, which will be a spring of perpetual comfort. Ephesians 4:2; Ephesians 4:2.
