<p>Verse 26. <i><b>The mystery which hath been hid</b></i> — The mystery is this: that God had designed to grant the Gentiles the same privileges with the Jews, and make them his people who were not his people. That this is what St. Paul means by the <i>mystery</i>, see <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="eph+3:3">Ephesians 3:3</span>, c.</p>
<p><i><b>Made manifest to his saints</b></i> — It is fully known to all who have embraced the doctrine of Christ crucified to all Christians.</p>
