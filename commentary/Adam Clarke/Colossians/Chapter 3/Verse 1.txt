<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="col+3:1">Colossians 3:1</span>. <i><b>If ye then</b></i> — ει ουν. <i>Seeing then that ye are risen</i> <i>with Christ</i>; this refers to what he had said, <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="col+2:12">Colossians 2:12</span>: <i>Buried with him in baptism, wherein also ye are risen with him</i>. As, therefore, ye have most cordially received the doctrine of Christ; and profess to be partakers of a spiritual religion, that promises spiritual and eternal things; seek those things, and look to be prepared for the enjoyment of them.</p>
