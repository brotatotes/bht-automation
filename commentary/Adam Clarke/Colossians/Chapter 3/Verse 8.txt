<p>Verse 8. <i><b>But now ye also put on all these</b></i> — <span class="_0000ff"><span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="eph+4:22">Ephesians 4:22</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="eph+4:22">Ephesians 4:22</span></span>. Being now converted, sin had no more dominion over them.</p>
<p><i><b>Anger, wrath</b></i>, c.] They had not only lived in the evils mentioned <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="col+3:5">Colossians 3:5</span>, but also in those enumerated here and they had not only laid aside the former, but they had laid aside the latter also. They retained no bosom, no easily besetting, sin. They were risen with Christ, and they sought the things which were above.</p>
<p><i><b>Blasphemy</b></i> — The word seems here to mean <i>injurious</i> and <i>calumnious</i> <i>speaking</i>.</p>
