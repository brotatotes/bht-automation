<p>Verse 12. <i><b>That we</b></i> — Jews, now apostles and messengers of God, to whom the first offers of salvation were made, and who were the <i>first</i> that <i>believed in Christ</i>.</p>
<p><i><b>Should be to the praise of his glory</b></i> — By being the means of preaching Christ crucified to the <i>Gentiles</i>, and spreading the Gospel throughout the world.</p>
