<p>Verse 16. <i><b>Cease not to give thanks</b></i> — The apostle intimates, so fully satisfied was he of the genuineness of their conversion, and of their steadiness since their conversion, that it was to him a continual cause of <i>thanksgiving</i> to God, who had brought them into that state of salvation; and of <i>prayer</i>, that they might be preserved blameless to the end.</p>
<p><i><b>Making mention of you</b></i> — While praying for the prosperity of the Christian cause generally, he was led, from his particular affection for them, to <i>mention them by name</i> before God.</p>
