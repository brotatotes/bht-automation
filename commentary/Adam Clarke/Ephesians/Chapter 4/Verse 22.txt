Verse 22. That ye put off — And this has been one especial part of our teaching, that ye should abandon all these, and live a life totally opposite to what it was before.The old man — Romans 6:6, and especially the notes on Romans 13:13; Romans 13:14.Which is corrupt — The whole of your former life was corrupt and abominable; ye lived in the pursuit of pleasure and happiness; ye sought this in the gratification of the lusts of the flesh; and were ever deceived by these lusts, and disappointed in your expectations.
