<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="eph+4:4">Ephesians 4:4</span>. <b>There is </b><i><b>one body</b></i> — Viz. of Christ, which is his Church.</p>
<p><i><b>One Spirit</b></i> — The Holy Ghost, who animates this body.</p>
<p><i><b>One hope</b></i> — Of everlasting glory, to which glory ye have been called by the preaching of the Gospel; through which ye have become the body of Christ, instinct with the energy of the Holy Ghost.</p>
