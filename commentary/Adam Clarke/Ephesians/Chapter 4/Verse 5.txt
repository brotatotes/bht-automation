<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="eph+4:5">Ephesians 4:5</span>. <i><b>One Lord</b></i> — Jesus Christ, who is the governor of this Church.</p>
<p><i><b>One faith</b></i> — One system of religion, proposing the same objects to the faith of all.</p>
<p><i><b>One baptism</b></i> — Administered in the name of the holy Trinity; indicative of the influences, privileges, and effects of the Christian religion.</p>
