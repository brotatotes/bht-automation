<p>Verse 20. <i><b>Giving thanks always</b></i> — God is continually loading you with his benefits; you <i>deserve</i> nothing of his kindness; therefore give him thanks for his unmerited bounties.</p>
<p><i><b>God and the Father</b></i> — That is: God, who is your Father, and the Father of mercies. See the observations on the <i>Greek article</i> at the end of this epistle. <i>"</i><span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="eph+6:24">Ephesians 6:24</span><i>"</i></p>
<p><i><b>In the name of our Lord Jesus</b></i> — He is the only <i>mediator</i>; and through him alone can ye approach to God; and it is for his sake only that God will hear your <i>prayers</i> or receive your <i>praises</i>.</p>
