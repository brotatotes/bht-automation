<p>Verse 8. <i><b>Whatsoever good thing any man doeth</b></i> — Though your masters should fail to give you the due reward of your fidelity and labour, yet, as ye have done your work as unto the Lord, he will take care to give you the proper recompense.</p>
<p><i><b>Whether</b></i><b> he be </b><i><b>bond</b></i> — A <i>slave</i>, bought with money;</p>
<p><i><b>Or free.</b></i> — A person who has <i>hired</i> himself of his own free accord.</p>
