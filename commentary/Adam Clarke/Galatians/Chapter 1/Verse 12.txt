<p>Verse 12. <i><b>I neither received it of man</b></i> — By means of any <i>apostle</i>, as was remarked <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ga+1:1">Galatians 1:1</span>. No man taught me what I have preached to you.</p>
<p><i><b>But by the revelation of Jesus Christ.</b></i> — Being commissioned by himself alone; receiving the knowledge of it from Christ crucified.</p>
