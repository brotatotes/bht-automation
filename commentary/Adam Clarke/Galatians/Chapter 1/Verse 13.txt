<p>Verse 13. <i><b>Ye have heard of my conversation</b></i> — τηνεμην αναστροφην. My <i>manner of life</i>; the <i>mode</i> in which I <i>conducted</i> <i>myself</i>.</p>
<p><i><b>Beyond measure I persecuted the Church</b></i> — For proofs of this the reader is referred to <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ac+9:1-2">Acts 9:1-2</span> (note); <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ac+22:4">Acts 22:4</span> (note), and the notes there. The apostle tells them that <i>they had heard this</i>, because, being <i>Jews</i>, they were acquainted with what had taken place in Judea, relative to these important transactions.</p>
