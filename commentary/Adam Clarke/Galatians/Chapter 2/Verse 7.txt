<p>Verse 7. <i><b>But contrariwise</b></i> — They were so far from wishing me to alter my plan, or to introduce any thing <i>new</i> in my doctrine to the Gentiles, that they saw plainly that my doctrine was the <i>same</i> as their own, coming immediately from the same source; and therefore gave to me and to Barnabas the right hand of fellowship.</p>
<p><i><b>The Gospel of the uncircumcision</b></i> — They saw, to their utmost satisfaction, that I was as expressly sent by God to preach the Gospel to the Gentiles, as Peter was to preach it to the Jews.</p>
