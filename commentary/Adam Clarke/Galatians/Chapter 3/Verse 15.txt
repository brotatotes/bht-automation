<p>Verse 15. <i><b>I speak after the manner of men</b></i> — I am about to produce an example taken from civil transactions. <i>If it be</i> <i>confirmed</i>-If an agreement or bond be signed, sealed, and witnessed, and, in this country, <i>being first duly stamped</i>;</p>
<p><i><b>No man disannulleth</b></i> — It stands under the protection of the civil law, and nothing can be legally erased or added.</p>
