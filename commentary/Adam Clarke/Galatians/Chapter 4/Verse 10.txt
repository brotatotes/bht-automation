<p>Verse 10. <i><b>Ye observe days</b></i> — Ye superstitiously regard the Sabbaths and particular days of your own appointment;</p>
<p><i><b>And months</b></i> — New moons; <i>times</i>-festivals, such as those of tabernacles, dedication, passover, &amp;c.</p>
<p><i><b>Years.</b></i> — Annual atonements, sabbatical years, and jubilees.</p>
