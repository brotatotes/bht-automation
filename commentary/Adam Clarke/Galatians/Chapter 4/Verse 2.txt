<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ga+4:2">Galatians 4:2</span>. <i><b>But is under tutors</b></i> — επιτροπους. <i>Guardians</i> and <i>governors</i>; οικονομους. those who have the <i>charge</i> of the <i>family.</i> These words are nearly similar; but we may consider the first as <i>executor</i>, the last as the person who <i>superintends the concerns of</i> <i>the family and estate</i> till the heir become of age; such as we call trustee.</p>
<p><i><b>Until the time appointed of the father.</b></i> — The time mentioned in the father's will or testament.</p>
