Verse 29. But as then he — Ishmael, who was born after the flesh-whose birth had nothing supernatural in it, but was according to the ordinary course of nature,Persecuted him — Isaac, who was born after the Spirit-who had a supernatural birth, according to the promise, and through the efficacy, of the Holy Spirit, giving effect to that promise-Sarah shall have a son, Genesis 17:16-21; Genesis 21:1, c.Persecuted him the persecution here referred to is that mentioned Genesis 21:9. It consisted in mocking his brother Isaac.Even so it is now. — So the Jews, in every place, persecute the Christians; and show thereby that they are rather of the posterity of Hagar than of Sarah.
