<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ga+4:5">Galatians 4:5</span>. <i><b>To redeem them</b></i> — εξαγοραση. To <i>pay down a price</i> for them, and thus <i>buy them off</i> from the necessity of observing circumcision, offering <i>brute sacrifices</i>, performing different <i>ablutions</i>, c., &amp;c.</p>
<p><i><b>That we might receive the adoption of sons.</b></i> — Which adoption we could not obtain by the law for it is the Gospel only that puts us among the children, and gives us a place in the heavenly family. On the nature of <i>adoption</i> <span class="_0000ff"><span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ro+8:15">Romans 8:15</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ro+8:15">Romans 8:15</span></span>.</p>
