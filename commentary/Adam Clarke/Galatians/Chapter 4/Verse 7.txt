<p>Verse 7. <i><b>Thou art no more a servant</b></i> — Thou who hast believed in Christ art no longer a <i>slave</i>, either under the <i>dominion of sin</i> or under <i>obligation</i> to the Mosaic ritual; <i>but a son</i> of God, adopted into the heavenly family.</p>
<p><i><b>And if a son, then an heir</b></i> — Having a right to the <i>inheritance</i>, because <i>one</i> of the <i>family</i>, for none can <i>inherit</i> but the <i>children</i>; but this heirship is the most extraordinary of all: it is not an heirship of any <i>tangible possession</i>, either in <i>heaven</i> or <i>earth</i>; it is not to possess a <i>part</i> or even the <i>whole</i> of <i>either,</i> it is to possess <i>Him</i> who made all things; not God's <i>works</i>, but God <i>himself</i>: heirs of GOD through Christ.</p>
