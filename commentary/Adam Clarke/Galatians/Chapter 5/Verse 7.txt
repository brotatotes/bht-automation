<p>Verse 7. <i><b>Ye did run well</b></i> — Ye once had the faith that worked by love-ye were genuine, active, useful Christians.</p>
<p><i><b>Who doth hinder</b></i> — Who prevented you from continuing to obey the truth? Ye could only be turned aside by your own consent. St. Paul here, as in <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="1co+9:24">1 Corinthians 9:24</span>, compares Christianity to a <i>race</i>. See the notes on the above text.</p>
