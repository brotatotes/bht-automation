<p>Verse 28. <i><b>He that despised Moses' law</b></i> — αθετησας. He that rejected it, <i>threw it aside</i>, and denied its Divine authority by presumptuous sinning, <i>died without mercy</i>-without any extenuation or mitigation of punishment; <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="nu+15:30">Numbers 15:30</span>.</p>
<p><i><b>Under two or three witnesses</b></i> — That is, when convicted by the testimony of two or three respectable witnesses. See <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="de+17:6">Deuteronomy 17:6</span>.</p>
