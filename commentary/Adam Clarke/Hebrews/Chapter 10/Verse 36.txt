Verse 36. Ye have need of patience — Having so great a fight of sufferings to pass through, and they of so long continuance. God furnishes the grace; you must exercise it. The grace or principle of patience comes from God; the use and exercise of that grace is of yourselves. Here ye must be workers together with God. Patience and perseverance are nearly the same.Have done the will of God — By keeping the faith, and patiently suffering for it.
