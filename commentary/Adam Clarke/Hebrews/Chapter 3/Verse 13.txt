<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="heb+3:13">Hebrews 3:13</span>. <i><b>But exhort one another daily</b></i> — This supposes a state of chose Church fellowship, without which they could not have had access to each other.</p>
<p><i><b>While it is called to-day</b></i> — Use time while you have: it, for by and by there will be no more present time; all will be future; all will be eternity. <i>Daily</i> signifies time continued. <i>To-day</i>, all present time. Your fathers said: Let us make ourselves a captain, and return back unto Egypt, <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="nu+14:4">Numbers 14:4</span>. Thus <i>they</i> exhorted each other <i>to depart from the living God</i>. Be <i>ye</i> warned by their example; let not that unbelieving heart be in <i>you</i> that was in <i>them</i>; exhort each other daily to cleave to the living God; lest, if ye, do not, <i>ye</i>, like <i>them</i>, may be hardened through the deceitfulness of sin.</p>
