<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="heb+4:4">Hebrews 4:4</span>. <i><b>For he spake in a certain place</b></i> — This <i>certain place</i> or <i>somewhere</i>, που, is probably <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ge+2:2">Genesis 2:2</span>; and refers to the completion of the work of creation, and the setting apart the <i>seventh day</i> as a day of <i>rest</i> for man, and a <i>type</i> of everlasting felicity. <i>See the notes on "</i><span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ge+2:1">Genesis 2:1</span><i>"</i>, &amp;c., and <i>see here "</i><span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="heb+2:6">Hebrews 2:6</span><i>"</i>.</p>
