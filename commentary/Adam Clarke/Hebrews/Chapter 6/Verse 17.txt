<p>Verse 17. <i><b>The heirs of promise</b></i> — All the believing posterity of Abraham, and the nations of the earth or Gentiles in general.</p>
<p><i><b>The immutability of his counsel</b></i> — His unchangeable purpose, to call the Gentiles to salvation by Jesus Christ; to justify every penitent by faith; to accept faith in Christ for justification in place of personal righteousness; and finally to bring every persevering believer, whether Jew or Gentile, to eternal glory.</p>
