Verse 17. The heirs of promise — All the believing posterity of Abraham, and the nations of the earth or Gentiles in general.The immutability of his counsel — His unchangeable purpose, to call the Gentiles to salvation by Jesus Christ; to justify every penitent by faith; to accept faith in Christ for justification in place of personal righteousness; and finally to bring every persevering believer, whether Jew or Gentile, to eternal glory.
