<p>Verse 24. <i><b>But this</b></i> — οδε, <i>But he</i>, that is, Christ, <i>because</i> <i>he continueth ever</i> i s eternal, <i>hath an unchangeable priesthood</i>, απαραβατον ιερωσυνην, a <i>priesthood that passeth not away</i> from him; he lives for ever, and he lives a <i>priest</i> for ever.</p>
