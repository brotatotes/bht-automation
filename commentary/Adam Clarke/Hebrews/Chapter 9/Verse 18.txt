<p>Verse 18. <i><b>Whereupon</b></i> — οθεν. <i>Wherefore</i>, as a <i>victim</i> was required for the ratification of every covenant, the first covenant made between God and the Hebrews, by the mediation of Moses, was <i>not dedicated</i>, εγκεκαινισται, renewed or solemnized, <i>without blood</i>-without the death of a victim, and the aspersion of its blood.</p>
