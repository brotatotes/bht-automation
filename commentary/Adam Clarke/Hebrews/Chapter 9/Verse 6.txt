<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="heb+9:6">Hebrews 9:6</span>. <i><b>When these thing were thus ordained</b></i> — When the tabernacle was made, and its furniture placed in it, according to the Divine direction.</p>
<p><i><b>The priests went always into the first Tabernacle</b></i> — That is, into the <i>first part</i> of the tabernacle, or holy place, into which he went <i>every day twice, accomplishing the services</i>, τας λατρειας επιτελουντες, which included his burning the incense at the morning and evening sacrifice, dressing the lamps, removing the old show-bread and laying on the new, and sprinkling the blood of the sin-offerings before the veil <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="le+4:6">Leviticus 4:6</span>: and for these works he must have <i>constant access to</i> the place.</p>
