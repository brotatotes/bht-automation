<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="jas+1:2">James 1:2</span>. <i><b>Count it all joy</b></i> — The word πειρασμος, which we translate <i>temptation</i>, signifies affliction, persecution, or <i>trial</i> of any kind; and in this sense it is used here, not intending diabolic suggestion, or what is generally understood by the word temptation.</p>
