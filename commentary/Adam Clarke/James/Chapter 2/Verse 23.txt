<p>Verse 23. <i><b>The scripture was fulfilled</b></i> — He believed God; this faith was never inactive, it was accounted to him for righteousness: and being justified by thus believing, his life of obedience showed that he had not received the grace of God in vain. <span class="_0000ff"><span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ge+15:6">Genesis 15:6</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ge+15:6">Genesis 15:6</span></span>; <i>"</i><span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ro+4:3">Romans 4:3</span><i>"</i>; <i>"</i><span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ga+3:6">Galatians 3:6</span><i>"</i>; where this subject is largely explained.</p>
<p><i><b>The friend of God.</b></i> — The highest character ever given to man. As among friends every thing is in common; so God took Abraham into intimate communion with himself, and poured out upon him the choicest of his blessings: for as God can never be in want, because he possesses all things; so Abraham his friend could never be destitute, because God was his friend.</p>
