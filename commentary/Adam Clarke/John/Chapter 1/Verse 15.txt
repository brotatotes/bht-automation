<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="joh+1:15">John 1:15</span>. <i><b>Of him</b></i> — The glorious personage before mentioned: <i>John</i> <i>the Baptist</i>, whose history was well known to the persons to whom this Gospel came in the beginning, <i>bare witness; and he</i> <i>cried</i>, - being deeply convinced of the importance and truth of the subject, he delivered his testimony with the utmost zeal and earnestness, - saying, <i>This is he of whom I spake, He that cometh</i> <i>after me </i>- for I am no other than the <i>voice</i> of the <i>crier</i> in the wilderness, <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="isa+40:3">Isaiah 40:3</span>, the forerunner of the Messiah.</p>
<p><i><b>Was before me.</b></i> — Speaking by the prophets, and warning your fathers to repent and return to God, as I now warn you; <i>for he was</i> <i>before me</i>-he was from eternity, and from him I have derived both my <i>being</i> and my <i>ministry</i>.</p>
