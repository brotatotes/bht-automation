<p>Verse 35. <i><b>The next day</b></i> — After that mentioned <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="joh+1:29">John 1:29</span>.</p>
<p><i><b>Two of his disciples</b></i> — One of them was <i>Andrew</i>, <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="joh+1:40">John 1:40</span>, and it is very likely that <i>John</i> himself was the other; in every thing in which he might receive honour he studiously endeavours to conceal his own name.</p>
