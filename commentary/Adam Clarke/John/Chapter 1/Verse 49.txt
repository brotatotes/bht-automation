<p>Verse 49. <i><b>Rabbi</b></i> — That is, <i>Teacher</i>! and so this word should be translated.</p>
<p><i><b>Thou art the Son of God</b></i> — The promised Messiah.</p>
<p><i><b>Thou art the King of Israel.</b></i> — The real descendant of David, who art to sit on that spiritual throne of which the throne of David was the type.</p>
