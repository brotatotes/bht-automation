<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="joh+10:18">John 10:18</span>. <i><b>I have power</b></i> — Or, <i>authority</i>, εξουσιαν. Our Lord speaks of himself here as <i>man</i>, or the <i>Messiah</i>, as being God's messenger, and sent upon earth to fulfil the Divine will, in dying and rising again for the salvation of men.</p>
<p><i><b>This commandment have I received</b></i> — That is, I act according to the Divine commandment in executing these things, and giving you this information.</p>
