<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="joh+10:19">John 10:19</span>. <i><b>There was a division</b></i> — σχισμα, <i>a schism</i>, a <i>rent</i>. They were divided in their opinions; one part received the light, and the other resisted it.</p>
<p><i><b>Again</b></i> — There was a dissension of this kind before among the same people; see <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="joh+9:16">John 9:16</span>.</p>
