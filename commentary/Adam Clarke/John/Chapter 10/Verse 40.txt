<p><i><b>Where John at first baptized</b></i> — That is, at <i>Bethabara</i>: see <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="joh+1:28">John 1:28</span>. <i>Afterwards</i>, John baptized at <i>AEnon</i>: <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="joh+3:23">John 3:23</span>.</p>
