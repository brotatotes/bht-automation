<p>Verse 27. <i><b>Yea, Lord: I believe</b></i> — πεπιστευκα, <i>I have believed</i>. Either meaning that she had believed this for some time past, or that, since he began to teach her, her faith had been considerable increased; but verbs <i>preter</i>, in Greek, are often used to signify the <i>present</i>. Martha here acknowledges Christ for the Messiah promised to their fathers; but her faith goes no farther; and, having received some hope of her brother's present resurrection, she waited for no farther instruction, but ran to call her sister.</p>
