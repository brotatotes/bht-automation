<p>Verse 23. <i><b>The hour is come, that the Son of man</b></i>, c.] The time is just at hand in which the Gospel shall be preached to all nations, the middle wall of partition broken down, and Jews and Gentiles united in one fold. But this could not be till after his death and resurrection, as the succeeding verse teaches. The <i>disciples</i> were the first fruits of the <i>Jews</i> these <i>Greeks</i>, the first fruits of the <i>Gentiles</i>.</p>
