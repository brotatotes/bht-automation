Verse 42. Among the chief rulers - many believed on him — We only know the names of two of them, Nicodemus, and Joseph of Arimathea.But-they did not confess him — Or it: they were as yet weak in the faith, and could not bear the reproach of the cross of Christ. Besides, the principal rulers had determined to excommunicate every person who acknowledged Christ for the Messiah; see John 9:22.
