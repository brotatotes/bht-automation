<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="joh+12:47">John 12:47</span>. <i><b>And believe not</b></i> — και μη φυλαξη, <i>And keep them not</i>, is the reading of ABL, <i>seven</i> others; <i>Syriac, Wheelock's Persian</i>, two of the <i>Arabic, Coptic, Sahidic, AEthiopic, Armenian</i>, later <i>Syriac, Vulgate, six</i> of the <i>Itala</i>, and some of the fathers.</p>
<p>A man must <i>hear</i> the words of Christ in order to <i>believe</i> them; and he must <i>believe</i>, in order to <i>keep</i> them; and he must <i>keep</i> them in order to his <i>salvation</i>.</p>
<p><i><b>I judge him not</b></i> — I need not do it: the words of Moses and the prophets judge and condemn him. See the notes on <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="joh+3:17">John 3:17</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="joh+5:45">John 5:45</span>.</p>
