<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="joh+12:5">John 12:5</span>. <i><b>Three hundred pence</b></i> — Or <i>denarii</i>: about 9<i>£</i>. 13<i>s</i>. 9<i>d</i>. of our money; reckoning the denarius at 7 3/4<i>d</i>. One of my MSS. of the <i>Vulgate</i> (a MS. of the 14th century) reads, <i>cccc</i> <i>denarii</i>.</p>
