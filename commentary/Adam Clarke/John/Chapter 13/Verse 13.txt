<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="joh+13:13">John 13:13</span>. <i><b>Ye call me Master and Lord</b></i> — Ὁ διδασκαλος και ὁ κυριος, similar to רבי <i>Rabbi</i>, and מר <i>Mar</i>, titles very common among the Jewish doctors, as may be seen in <i>Schoettgen</i>. This double title was not given except to the most accredited teachers, רבי ומורי <i>Rabbi vemore</i>, my master, my lord!</p>
