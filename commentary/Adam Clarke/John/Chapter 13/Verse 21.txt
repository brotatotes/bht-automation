<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="joh+13:21">John 13:21</span>. <i><b>Was troubled in spirit.</b></i> — <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="joh+11:33">John 11:33</span>.</p>
<p><i><b>And testified</b></i> — Spoke with great earnestness.</p>
<p><i><b>Shall betray me.</b></i> — παραδωσει με, <i>Will deliver me up</i>. Judas had already <i>betrayed</i> our blessed Lord, and he was now on the point of <i>delivering him up</i> into the hands of the chief priests. By all these warnings, did not our Lord intend that <i>Judas</i> should be benefited?-that he should repent of his iniquity, and turn and find mercy?</p>
