<p>Verse 32. <i><b>And shall straightway glorify him.</b></i> — Or, <i>glorify him</i>, ευθυς, <i>immediately</i>; "he did, not only in the miracles wrought at his death, but also in that remarkable case mentioned, <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="joh+18:6">John 18:6</span>, when the whole crowd that came to seize him were driven back with a <i>word</i> of his mouth, and fell to the ground.</p>
