<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="joh+14:10">John 14:10</span>. <i><b>I am in the Father, and the Father in me?</b></i> — We are essentially one; and those who have seen <i>me</i> have seen <i>him</i> who sent me.</p>
<p><i><b>He doeth the works.</b></i> — We are not only <i>one</i> in <i>nature</i>, but <i>one</i> also in <i>operation</i>. The works which I have done bear witness of the infinite perfection of my nature. Such miracles as I have wrought could only be performed by unlimited power.</p>
