<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="joh+15:15">John 15:15</span>. <i><b>Henceforth I call you not servants</b></i> — Which he at least indirectly had done, <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="joh+13:16">John 13:16</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="mt+10:24-25">Matthew 10:24-25</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="lu+17:10">Luke 17:10</span>.</p>
<p><i><b>I have called you friends</b></i> — I have admitted you into a state of the most intimate fellowship with myself; and have made known unto you whatsoever I have heard from the Father, which, in your present circumstances, it was necessary for you to be instructed in.</p>
