Verse John 15:15. Henceforth I call you not servants — Which he at least indirectly had done, John 13:16; Matthew 10:24-25; Luke 17:10.I have called you friends — I have admitted you into a state of the most intimate fellowship with myself; and have made known unto you whatsoever I have heard from the Father, which, in your present circumstances, it was necessary for you to be instructed in.
