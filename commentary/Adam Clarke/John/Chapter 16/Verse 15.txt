<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="joh+16:15">John 16:15</span>. <i><b>All things that the Father hath are mine</b></i> — If Christ had not been equal to God, could he have said this without blasphemy?</p>
<p><i><b>And show</b></i><b> it </b><i><b>unto you.</b></i> — As Christ is represented the <i>Ambassador</i> of the Father, so the Holy Spirit is represented the <i>ambassador</i> of the Son, coming vested with his authority, as the interpreter and executor of <i>his</i> will.</p>
