Our Lord's meaning appears to have been this: that his resurrection should be so <i>completely demonstrated</i> to them, that they should never have a doubt concerning it; and consequently that their joy should be great and permanent, knowing that the atonement was made, the victory gained, and the kingdom of heaven opened to all believers. Therefore it is said, <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ac+4:33">Acts 4:33</span>, <i>that with great power did the apostles give witness of the</i> <i>resurrection of the Lord Jesus</i>.</p>
