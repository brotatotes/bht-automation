<p>Verse 25. <i><b>In Proverbs</b></i> — That is, words which, besides their plain, literal meaning, have another, viz. a spiritual or figurative one. I have represented heavenly things to you through the medium of earthly.</p>
<p><i><b>The time cometh</b></i> — viz. the interval from his resurrection to his ascension, which consisted of forty days, during which he instructed his disciples in the most sublime mysteries and truths of his kingdom. <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ac+1:3">Acts 1:3</span>.</p>
