<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="joh+17:25">John 17:25</span>. <i><b>The world hath not known thee</b></i> — Has not acknowledged me. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="joh+1:11-12">John 1:11-12</span>.</p>
<p><i><b>And these have known</b></i> — Here our Lord, returning to the disciples, speaks: 1st. Of their having <i>received him</i> as the Messiah; 2dly. Of his <i>making the Father known</i> unto them; 3dly. Of his purpose to <i>continue</i> to influence them by the <i>Spirit of truth</i>, that they might be perfectly <i>united</i> to God, by an <i>indwelling Saviour</i> for ever.</p>
