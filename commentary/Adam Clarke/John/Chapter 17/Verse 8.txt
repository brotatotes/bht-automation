<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="joh+17:8">John 17:8</span>. <i><b>I have given - them the words</b></i> — I have delivered thy doctrine to them, so that they have had a pure teaching immediately from heaven: neither Jewish fables nor fictions of men have been mingled with it.</p>
<p><i><b>And have known surely</b></i> — Are fully convinced and acknowledge that I am the promised Messiah, and that they are to look for none other; and that my mission and doctrine are all Divine, <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="joh+17:7-8">John 17:7-8</span>.</p>
