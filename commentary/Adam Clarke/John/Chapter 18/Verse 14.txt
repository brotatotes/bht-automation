<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="joh+18:14">John 18:14</span>. <i><b>Caiaphas was he which gave counsel</b></i>, &amp;c.] Therefore he was an improper person to sit in judgment on Christ, whom he had <i>prejudged</i> and <i>precondemned</i>: see on <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="joh+11:50-52">John 11:50-52</span>. But Christ must not be treated according to the rules of <i>justice</i>: if he had, he could not have been put to death.</p>
