<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="joh+18:4">John 18:4</span>. <i><b>Jesus knowing all things</b></i>, c.] He had gone through all his preaching, working of miracles, and passion, and had nothing to do now but to offer up himself on the cross he therefore went forth to meet them, to deliver himself up to death.</p>
