<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="joh+19:15">John 19:15</span>. <i><b>Away with him</b></i> — αρον: probably this means, <i>kill him</i>. In <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="isa+57:1">Isaiah 57:1</span>, it is said, και ανδρες, δικαιοι αιρονται, <i>and just men are taken away</i>; that is, according to some, by a violent death.</p>
