<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="joh+19:6">John 19:6</span>. <i><b>Crucify HIM</b></i> — αυτον, which is necessary to the text, and which is wanting in the common editions, and is supplied by our version in Italics, is added here on the authority of almost every MS. and version of importance. As it is omitted in the common editions, it affords another proof, that they were not taken from the best MSS.</p>
