<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="joh+2:11">John 2:11</span>. <i><b>This beginning of miracles</b></i> — It was probably the first he ever wrought:-at any rate, it was the first he wrought <i>after</i> <i>his baptism</i>, and the first he wrought <i>publicly</i>.</p>
<p><i><b>His glory</b></i> — His supreme Divinity: <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="joh+1:14">John 1:14</span>.</p>
<p><i><b>His disciples believed on him.</b></i> — Were more abundantly confirmed in their faith, that he was either the promised Messiah, or a most extraordinary prophet, in the fullest intercourse with the ever blessed God.</p>
