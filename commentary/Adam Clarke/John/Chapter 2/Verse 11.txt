Verse John 2:11. This beginning of miracles — It was probably the first he ever wrought:-at any rate, it was the first he wrought after his baptism, and the first he wrought publicly.His glory — His supreme Divinity: John 1:14.His disciples believed on him. — Were more abundantly confirmed in their faith, that he was either the promised Messiah, or a most extraordinary prophet, in the fullest intercourse with the ever blessed God.
