<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="joh+2:5">John 2:5</span>. <i><b>His mother saith</b></i>, &amp;c.] The virgin seems to have understood our Lord as hinted above. It was not yet time to grant them a supply, because the want had not as yet been generally <i>felt</i>. But, silently receiving the respectful caution, she saw that the miracle should be wrought when it best suited the purposes of the Divine wisdom.</p>
