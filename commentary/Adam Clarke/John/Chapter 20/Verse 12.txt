<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="joh+20:12">John 20:12</span>. <i><b>Seeth two angels</b></i> — <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="joh+20:6">John 20:6</span>. She knew these to be angels by their <i>white</i> and <i>glistening</i> robes. Matthew and Mark mention but one angel-probably that one only that spoke, <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="joh+20:13">John 20:13</span>.</p>
<p><i><b>One at the head, and the other at the feet</b></i> — So were the cherubim placed at each end of the mercy-seat: <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ex+25:18-19">Exodus 25:18-19</span>. <i>Lightfoot</i>.</p>
