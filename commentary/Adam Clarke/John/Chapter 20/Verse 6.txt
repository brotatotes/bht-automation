<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="joh+20:6">John 20:6</span>. <i><b>Seeth the linen clothes lie</b></i> — θεωπει: from θεαομαι, <i>to</i> <i>behold</i>, and οραω, <i>to see </i>- to <i>look steadily</i> at any thing, so as to discover what it is, and to be <i>satisfied</i> with viewing it.</p>
