<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="joh+20:8">John 20:8</span>. <i><b>That other disciple</b></i> — John.</p>
<p><i><b>Saw</b></i> — That the body was not there.</p>
<p><i><b>And believed.</b></i> — That it had been taken away, as Mary had said; but he did not believe that he was risen from the dead. See what follows.</p>
