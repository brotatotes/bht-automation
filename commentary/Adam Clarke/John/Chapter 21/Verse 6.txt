<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="joh+21:6">John 21:6</span>. <i><b>And ye shall find.</b></i> — The <i>AEthiopic, three</i> copies of the <i>Itala</i>, and St. <i>Cyril</i>, add, <i>They said therefore unto him, we have</i> <i>laboured all the night and caught nothing, nevertheless at thy</i> <i>command we will let down the net</i>. This is borrowed from <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="lu+5:5">Luke 5:5</span>.</p>
<p><i><b>For the multitude of fishes.</b></i> — This was intended as an emblem of the immense number of souls which should be converted to God by their ministry; according to the promise of Christ, <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="mt+4:19">Matthew 4:19</span>.</p>
