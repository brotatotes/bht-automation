<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="joh+21:9">John 21:9</span>. <i><b>They saw a fire</b></i>, c.] This appears to have been a new miracle. It could not have been a fire which the disciples had there, for it is remarked as something <i>new</i> besides, they had caught no fish, <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="joh+21:5">John 21:5</span>, and here was a small fish upon the coals, and a loaf of bread provided to eat with it. The whole appears to have been miraculously prepared by Christ.</p>
