<p>Verse 25. <i><b>John's disciples and the Jews</b></i> — Instead of Ιουδαιων, <i>Jews</i>, ABELS. M. BV, nearly 100 others, some versions and fathers, read Ιουδαιου, <i>a Jew</i>, which <i>Griesbach</i> has admitted into the text. The <i>person</i> here spoken of was probably one who had been baptized by the disciples of our Lord; and the subject of debate seems to have been, whether the baptism of John, or that of Christ, was the most efficacious towards <i>purifying</i>.</p>
