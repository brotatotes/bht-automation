<p>Verse 30. <i><b>They went out of the city</b></i> — Such effect had the simple testimony of the woman on their minds.</p>
<p><i><b>And came unto him.</b></i> — Or, <i>Were coming to him</i>; for they did not reach him immediately; all that discourse between him and his disciples, mentioned <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="joh+4:31-39">John 4:31-39</span>, inclusive, having taken place before the people of Sychar got to the well. See <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="joh+4:40">John 4:40</span>.</p>
