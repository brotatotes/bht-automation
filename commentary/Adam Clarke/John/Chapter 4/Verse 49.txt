<p>Verse 49. <i><b>Sir, come down</b></i>, c.] He did not think our Lord could cure him without being <i>present</i>, and seems here to feel himself hurt, because our Lord did not come at his first entreaty. It is difficult for a <i>proud</i> man, or a <i>man in office</i>, to humble himself, or to treat even God Almighty with proper respect. The spirit of this man seems not much unlike to that of Naaman the Syrian, <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="2ki+5:11">2 Kings 5:11</span>.</p>
