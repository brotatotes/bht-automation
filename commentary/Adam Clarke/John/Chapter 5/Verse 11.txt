<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="joh+5:11">John 5:11</span>. <i><b>He that made me whole</b></i>, c.] The poor man reasoned conclusively:- He who could work such a miracle must be at least the <i>best</i> of <i>men</i>: now a good man will neither <i>do evil</i> himself, nor <i>command</i> others to <i>do</i> it: but he who cured me ordered me to carry my bed therefore, there can be no evil in it.</p>
