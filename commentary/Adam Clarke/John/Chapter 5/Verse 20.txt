<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="joh+5:20">John 5:20</span>. <i><b>Greater works than these</b></i> — Two of these he immediately mentions: <i>Raising the dead</i>, <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="joh+5:21">John 5:21</span>. And <i>judging the world</i>, <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="joh+5:22">John 5:22</span>.</p>
<p><i><b>That ye may marvel.</b></i> — Or, <i>So as to make you wonder</i>. Our Lord sometimes speaks of himself as God, and sometimes as the ambassador of God. As he had a human and Divine nature, this distinction was essentially necessary. Many errors have originated from want of attention to this circumstance.</p>
