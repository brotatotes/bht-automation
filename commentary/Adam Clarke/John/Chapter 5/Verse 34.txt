Verse 34. But I receive not testimony from man [only.] I have no need of John's testimony: the works that I do bear sufficient testimony to me, John 5:36.But these things I say, c.] You believed John to be a prophet - a prophet cannot lie: he bore testimony that I am the Lamb of God, that beareth away the sin of the world, John 1:29 therefore, that ye may be saved by believing in me as such, I have appealed to John's testimony.
