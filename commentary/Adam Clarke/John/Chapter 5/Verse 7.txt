<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="joh+5:7">John 5:7</span>. <i><b>Sir, I have no man</b></i> — Ναι, Κυριε - "Yes, sir; but I have no man:" - this is the reading of C*GH, <i>fourteen</i> others, both the <i>Syriac</i>, later <i>Persic, Arabic</i>, and <i>Chrysostom</i>. Reader, be thankful to God for health and outward comforts. When <i>long</i> affliction has been allied to <i>deep</i> poverty, how deplorable is the state!</p>
