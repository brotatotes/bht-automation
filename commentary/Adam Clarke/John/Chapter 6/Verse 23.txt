<p>Verse 23. <i><b>There came other boats</b></i> — After Jesus and his disciples had departed.</p>
<p><i><b>From Tiberias</b></i> — Herod Antipas built this city near the lake of Genesaret, in the best parts of Galilee, and called it <i>Tiberias</i>, in honour of <i>Tiberius</i>, the Roman emperor: see Jos. Ant. book xviii. chap. 2. sect. 3.</p>
