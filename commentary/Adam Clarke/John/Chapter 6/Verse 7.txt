<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="joh+6:7">John 6:7</span>. <i><b>Two hundred pennyworth</b></i> — This sum, rating the <i>denarius</i> at 7 3/4<i>d</i>., would amount to 6<i>£</i>. 9<i>s</i>. 2<i>d</i>. of our money, and appears to have been more than our Lord and all his disciples were worth of this world's goods. See the notes on <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="mt+18:28">Matthew 18:28</span>.</p>
