<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="joh+8:21">John 8:21</span>. <i><b>Then said Jesus again unto them</b></i> — He had said the same things to them the day before. See <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="joh+7:34">John 7:34</span>.</p>
<p><i><b>Ye shall seek me</b></i> — When your calamities come upon you, ye shall in vain seek for the help of the Messiah, whom ye now reject, and whom ye shall shortly crucify.</p>
