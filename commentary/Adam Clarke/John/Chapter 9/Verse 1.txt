<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="joh+9:1">John 9:1</span>. <i><b>And as Jesus passed by</b></i> — This chapter is a continuation of the preceding, and therefore the word <i>Jesus</i> is not in the Greek text: it begins simply thus - <i>And passing along</i>, και παραγων, &amp;c. Having left the temple, where the Jews were going to stone him, (<span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="joh+8:59">John 8:59</span>,) it is probable our Lord went, according to his custom, to the mount of Olives. The next day, which was the Sabbath, <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="joh+9:14">John 9:14</span>, he met a man who had been born blind, sitting in some public place, and asking alms from those who passed by, <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="joh+9:8">John 9:8</span>.</p>
