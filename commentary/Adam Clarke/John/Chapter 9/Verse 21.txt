<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="joh+9:21">John 9:21</span>. <i><b>He is of age</b></i> — ηλικιαν εχει, literally, <i>he has</i> <i>stature</i>, i.e. he is a full-grown man; and in this sense the phrase is used by the best Greek writers. See <i>Kypke</i> and <i>Raphelius</i>. <i>Mature age</i> was fixed among the Jews at <i>thirty</i> years.</p>
