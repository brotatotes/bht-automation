<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="lu+1:14">Luke 1:14</span>. <i><b>Thou shalt have joy</b></i>, c.] εσται χαρα σοι, <i>He will be</i> <i>joy and gladness to thee</i>. A child of prayer and faith is likely to be a source of comfort to his parents. Were proper attention paid to this point, there would be fewer <i>disobedient</i> children in the world and the number of <i>broken-hearted</i> parents would be lessened. But what can be expected from the majority of matrimonial connections, connections <i>begun</i> without the <i>fear</i> of God, and <i>carried on</i> without his <i>love</i>.</p>
<p><i><b>Many shall rejoice at his birth.</b></i> — He shall be the minister of God for good to multitudes, who shall, through his preaching, be turned from the error of their ways, and converted to God their Saviour.</p>
