Verse 28. And the angel came in unto her — Some think that all this business was transacted in a vision; and that there was no personal appearance of the angel. When Divine visions were given, they are announced as such, in the sacred writings; nor can we with safety attribute any thing to a vision, where a Divine communication is made, unless it be specified as such in the text.Hail — Analogous to, Peace be to thee - May thou enjoy all possible blessings!Highly favoured — As being chosen in preference to all the women upon earth, to be the mother of the Messiah. Not the mother of God, for that is blasphemy.The Lord is with thee — Thou art about to receive the most convincing proofs of God's peculiar favour towards thee.Blessed art thou among women. — That is, thou art favoured beyond all others.
