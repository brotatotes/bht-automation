<p>Verse 39. <i><b>In those days</b></i> — As soon as she could conveniently fit herself out for the journey.</p>
<p><i><b>Hill country</b></i> — <i>Hebron</i>, the city of the priests, <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="jos+21:11">Joshua 21:11</span>, which was situated in the tribe of Judah, about forty miles south of Jerusalem, and upwards of seventy from Nazareth.</p>
<p><i><b>With haste</b></i> — This probably refers to nothing else than the <i>earnestness</i> of her mind to visit her relative Elisabeth, and to see what the Lord had wrought for <i>her</i>.</p>
