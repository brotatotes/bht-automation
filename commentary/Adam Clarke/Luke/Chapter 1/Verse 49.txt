<p>Verse 49. <i><b>He that is mighty hath done to me great things</b></i> — Or, <i>miracles</i>, μεγαλεια. As God <i>fills</i> her with his goodness, she <i>empties</i> herself to him in praises; and, sinking into her own nothingness, she ever confesses that God alone is all in all.</p>
<p><i><b>Holy</b></i><b> is </b><i><b>his name</b></i> — Probably the word which Mary used was חסד <i>chesed</i>, which though we sometimes translate <i>holy</i>, see <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ps+86:2">Psalms 86:2</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ps+145:17">Psalms 145:17</span>, yet the proper meaning is <i>abundant goodness, exuberant kindness</i>; and this well agrees with the following clause.</p>
