<p>Verse 57. <i><b>Now Elisabeth's full time came</b></i>, &amp;c.] But, according to others, we are to understand the three months of Mary's visit as preceding the birth of John, which would complete the time of Elisabeth's pregnancy, according to <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="lu+1:36">Luke 1:36</span>, and the only difficulty is to ascertain whether Mary went immediately to Hebron after her salutation, or whether she tarried nearly three months before she took the journey.</p>
