<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="lu+1:9">Luke 1:9</span>. <i><b>His lot was</b></i>, &amp;c.] We are informed in the <i>Talmud</i>, that it was the custom of the priests to divide the different functions of the sacerdotal office among themselves by <i>lot</i>: and, in this case, the decision of the lot was, that Zacharias should at that time burn the incense before the Lord, in the holy place.</p>
