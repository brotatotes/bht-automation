<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="lu+10:41">Luke 10:41</span>. <i><b>Thou art careful and troubled</b></i> — <i>Thou art distracted</i>, μεριμνας, <i>thy mind is divided</i>, (<span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="mt+13:22">Matthew 13:22</span>,) in consequence of which, τυρβαζη, <i>thou art disturbed</i>, thy spirit is thrown into a <i>tumult</i>.</p>
<p><i><b>About many things.</b></i> — Getting a <i>variety</i> of things ready for this entertainment, much more than are necessary on such an occasion.</p>
