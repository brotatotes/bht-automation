<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="lu+11:4">Luke 11:4</span>. <i><b>Lead us not into temptation</b></i>, c.] Dr. Lightfoot believes that this petition is intended against the visible apparitions of the devil, and his actual obsessions he thinks that the meaning is too much softened by our translation. <i>Deliver</i> <i>us from evil</i>, is certainly a very inadequate rendering of ῥυσαι ἡμας απο του πονηρου; literally, <i>Deliver us from the wicked one</i>.</p>
