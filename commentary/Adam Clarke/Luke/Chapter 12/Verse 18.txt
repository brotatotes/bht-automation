<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="lu+12:18">Luke 12:18</span>. <i><b>I will pull down</b></i>, &amp;c.] The rich are full of <i>designs</i> concerning this life, but in general take no thought about eternity till the time that their <i>goods</i> and their <i>lives</i> are both taken away.</p>
