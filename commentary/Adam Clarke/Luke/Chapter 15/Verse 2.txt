<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="lu+15:2">Luke 15:2</span>. <i><b>Receiveth sinners</b></i> — προσδεχεται. He receives them <i>cordially, affectionately </i>- takes them to <i>his bosom</i>; for so the word implies. What mercy! Jesus receives sinners in the most <i>loving, affectionate</i> manner, and saves them unto eternal life! Reader, give glory to God for ever!</p>
