<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="lu+15:23">Luke 15:23</span>. <i><b>The fatted calf, and kill</b></i> it] θυσατε, <i>Sacrifice it</i>. In ancient times the animals provided for public feasts were first sacrificed to God. The blood of the beast being poured out before God, by way of atonement for sin, the flesh was considered as <i>consecrated</i>, and the guests were considered as feeding on Divine food. This custom is observed among the Asiatics to this day.</p>
