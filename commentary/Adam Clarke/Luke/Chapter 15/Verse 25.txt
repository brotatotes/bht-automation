<p>Verse 25. <i><b>His elder son</b></i> — Meaning probably persons of a <i>regular</i> <i>moral life</i>, who needed no repentance in comparison of the prodigal already described.</p>
<p><i><b>In the field</b></i> — Attending the concerns of the farm.</p>
<p><i><b>He heard music</b></i> — συμφωνιας, a number of sounds <i>mingled</i> together, as in a <i>concert</i>.</p>
<p><i><b>Dancing.</b></i> — χορων. But Le Clerc denies that the word means <i>dancing</i> at all, as it properly means a <i>choir of singers</i>. The <i>symphony</i> mentioned before may mean the musical instruments which accompanied. the choirs of singers.</p>
