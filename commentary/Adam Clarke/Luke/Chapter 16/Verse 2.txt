<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="lu+16:2">Luke 16:2</span>. <i><b>Give an account of thy</b></i>, &amp;c.] Produce thy books of receipts and disbursements, that I may see whether the accusation against thee be true or false. The original may be translated, <i>Give up the business</i>, τον λογον, <i>of the stewardship</i>.</p>
