<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="lu+17:12">Luke 17:12</span>. <i><b>Ten - lepers</b></i> — Concerning the leprosy see the note on <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="mt+8:2">Matthew 8:2</span>; and on <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="le+13:1">Leviticus 13:1</span>, c. and <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="le+14:1">Leviticus 14:1</span>, c..</p>
<p><i><b>Which stood afar off</b></i> — They kept at a distance, because forbidden by law and custom to come near to those who were sound, for fear of infecting them. See <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="le+13:46">Leviticus 13:46</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="nu+5:2">Numbers 5:2</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="2ki+15:5">2 Kings 15:5</span>.</p>
