<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="lu+17:15">Luke 17:15</span>. <i><b>One of them, when he saw that he was healed</b></i>, &amp;c.] It seems that he did not wait to go first to the priest, but turned immediately back, and gave public praise to the kind hand from which he had received his cure.</p>
