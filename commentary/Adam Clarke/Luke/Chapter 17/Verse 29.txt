<p>Verse 29. <i><b>It rained fire and brimstone</b></i> — Instead of <i>it</i> rained, <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ge+19:24">Genesis 19:24</span> justifies the insertion of the pronoun <i>he</i>, as implied in the verb εβρεξε; for it is there said that <i>Jehovah rained fire and brimstone from Jehovah out of heaven</i>.</p>
