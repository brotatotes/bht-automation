<p>Verse 33. <i><b>And the third day he shall rise again.</b></i> — See <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ho+6:2">Hosea 6:2</span>; and let the reader observe that the passage should be read thus: <i>In the third day he will raise HIM up</i>, (יקמנו) and we shall live <i>before him</i>: his resurrection shall be the <i>pledge, token</i>, and <i>cause</i> of ours.</p>
