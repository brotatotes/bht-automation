<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="lu+18:5">Luke 18:5</span>. <i><b>She weary me.</b></i> — υπωπιαζη με, <i>Stun me</i>. A metaphor taken from boxers, who <i>bruise</i> each other, and by beating each other about the face <i>blacken the eyes</i>. See <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="1co+9:27">1 Corinthians 9:27</span>.</p>
