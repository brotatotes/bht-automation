<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="lu+19:23">Luke 19:23</span>. <i><b>With usury?</b></i> — συν τοκω, <i>With its produce</i>, i.e. what the loan of the money is fairly worth, after paying the person sufficiently for using it: for, in lent money, both the <i>lender</i> and <i>borrower</i> are supposed to reap profit.</p>
