<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="lu+2:19">Luke 2:19</span>. <i><b>And pondered</b></i><b> them </b><i><b>in her heart.</b></i> — συμβαλλουσα, <i>Weighing them in her heart</i>. <i>Weighing</i> is an English translation of our word <i>pondering</i>, from the Latin <i>ponderare</i>. Every circumstance relative to her son's birth, Mary <i>treasured up</i> in her memory; and every new circumstance she <i>weighed</i>, or compared with those which had already taken place, in order to acquire the fullest information concerning the nature and mission of her son.</p>
