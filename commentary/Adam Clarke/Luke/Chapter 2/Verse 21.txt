<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="lu+2:21">Luke 2:21</span>. <i><b>When eight days were accomplished</b></i> — The law had appointed that every male should be circumcised at eight days old, or on the eighth day after its birth, <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ge+17:12">Genesis 17:12</span>; and our blessed Lord received circumcision in token of his subjection to the law, <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ga+4:4">Galatians 4:4</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ga+5:3">Galatians 5:3</span>.</p>
<p><i><b>His name was called JESUS</b></i> — <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ga+5:3- :">Galatians 5:3- :</span> and <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="joh+1:29">John 1:29</span>.</p>
