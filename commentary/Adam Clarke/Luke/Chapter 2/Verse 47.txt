<p>Verse 47. <i><b>Answers:</b></i> — The word αποκρισις here seems not to mean <i>answers</i> only, but what Jesus said by way of question to the doctors, <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="lu+2:46">Luke 2:46</span>. So in <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="re+7:13">Revelation 7:13</span>, one of the elders is said to have <i>answered, saying </i>- when he only asked a question. Bp. PEARCE.</p>
