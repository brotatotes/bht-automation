<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="lu+21:15">Luke 21:15</span>. <i><b>I will give you a mouth and wisdom</b></i> — στομα, <i>a mouth</i>, must appear plain to every person to be used here for a <i>ready</i> <i>utterance</i>, or <i>eloquence</i> in speaking. They shall have an abundance of wisdom to know what to say; and they shall have an irresistible eloquence to say what they ought.</p>
