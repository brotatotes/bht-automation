<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="lu+21:19">Luke 21:19</span>. <i><b>In your patience</b></i> — Rather, <i>your perseverance</i>, your faithful continuance in my word and doctrine. <i>Ye will preserve</i> <i>your souls</i>. Ye shall escape the Roman sword, and not one of you shall perish in the destruction of Jerusalem. Instead of κτησασθε, <i>possess</i>, or <i>preserve ye</i>, I read κτησεσθε, <i>ye shall preserve</i>. This reading is supported by AB-B, five others; both the <i>Syriac</i>, all the <i>Arabic, AEthiopic, Vulgate</i>, all the <i>Itala</i> except two, <i>Origen, Macarius</i>, and <i>Tertullian</i>.</p>
