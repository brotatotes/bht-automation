<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="lu+21:24">Luke 21:24</span>. <i><b>They shall fall by the edge of the sword</b></i> — Those who perished in the siege are reckoned to be not less than <i>eleven</i> <i>hundred thousand</i>. See <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="mt+24:22">Matthew 24:22</span>.</p>
<p><i><b>And shall be led away captive</b></i> — To the number of <i>ninety-seven</i> <i>thousand</i>. See Josephus, War, b. vi. c. ix. s. 2, 3, and on <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="mt+24:31">Matthew 24:31</span>.</p>
<p><i><b>Trodden down of the Gentiles</b></i> — Judea was so completely subjugated that the very land itself was sold by Vespasian; the Gentiles possessing it, while the Jews were either nearly all killed or led away into captivity.</p>
<p><i><b>Of the Gentiles be fulfilled.</b></i> — Till the different nations of the earth, to whom God shall have given the dominion over this land, have accomplished all that which the Lord hath appointed them to do; and till the time of their conversion to God take place. But when shall this be? We know not. The nations are still treading down Jerusalem, and the <i>end</i> is known only to the Lord. <span class="_0000ff"><span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="mt+24:31">Matthew 24:31</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="mt+24:31">Matthew 24:31</span></span>.</p>
