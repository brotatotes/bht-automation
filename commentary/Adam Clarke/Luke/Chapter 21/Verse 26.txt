<p>Verse 26. <i><b>Men's hearts failing them for fear</b></i> — Or, <i>Men fainting</i> <i>away through fear</i>, (αποψυχοντων), <i>being ready to die</i>.</p>
<p><i><b>Coming on the earth</b></i> — Or, <i>Coming upon this land</i>, οικουμενη. See this translation of the word vindicated in <i>Clarke's note on "</i><span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="lu+2:1">Luke 2:1</span><i>"</i>.</p>
