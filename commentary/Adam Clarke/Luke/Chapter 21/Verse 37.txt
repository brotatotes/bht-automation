<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="lu+21:37">Luke 21:37</span>. <i><b>And in the day time</b></i> — Or, <i>every day </i>- τας ημερας. This probably relates to the <i>four</i> last days of his life already mentioned.</p>
<p><i><b>Abode in the mount</b></i> — He taught all day in the temple, and withdrew every evening, and lodged in Bethany; a town at the foot, or on the declivity of the mount of Olives. <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="mt+21:17">Matthew 21:17</span>.</p>
