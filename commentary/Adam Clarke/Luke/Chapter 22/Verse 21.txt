<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="lu+22:21">Luke 22:21</span>. <i><b>The hand of him that betrayeth me</b></i>, c.] What can be desired more, says Dr. Lightfoot, as a demonstration that Judas was present at the eucharist? And, whereas the contrary is endeavoured to be proved out of <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="joh+13:0">John 13:0</span>, nothing is made out of nothing for there is not one syllable throughout that whole chapter of the <i>paschal</i> supper, but of a supper before the <i>feast of</i> <i>the passover</i>.</p>
