<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="lu+22:22">Luke 22:22</span>. <i><b>The Son of man goeth</b></i> — That is, he is about to die, απερχεσθαι, οιχεσθαι, <i>abire, going, going away</i>, and <i>departing</i>, are used, by the best <i>Greek</i> and <i>Latin</i> writers, for <i>death</i> and <i>dying</i>. See <i>Rosenmuller</i>.</p>
