<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="lu+24:18">Luke 24:18</span>. <i><b>Cleopas</b></i> — The same as Alpheus, father of the Apostle James, <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="mr+3:18">Mark 3:18</span>, and husband of the sister of the virgin. <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="joh+19:25">John 19:25</span>.</p>
<p><i><b>Art thou only a stranger</b></i> — As if he had said, What has been done it Jerusalem, within these few days, has been so public, so awful, and so universally known, that, if thou hadst been but a lodger in the city for a single night, I cannot conceive how thou couldst miss hearing of these things: indeed, thou appearest to be the <i>only</i> person unacquainted with them.</p>
