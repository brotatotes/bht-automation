His preaching had made a deep impression upon their hearts, <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="lu+24:32">Luke 24:32</span>, and now they feel it their greatest privilege to entertain the preacher.</p>
<p>This is a constant effect of the doctrine of Christ: wherever it is <i>felt</i>, the Author of it, the ever-blessed Jesus, is earnestly entreated to dwell in the heart; and he who preaches it, is amply provided with the necessaries of life by those who have received his testimony.</p>
