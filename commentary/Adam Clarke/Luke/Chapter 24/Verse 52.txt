<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="lu+24:52">Luke 24:52</span>. <i><b>They worshipped him</b></i> — Let it be observed that this worship was not given by way of civil respect, for it was <i>after</i> he was parted from them, and carried back into heaven, that they offered it to him; but acts of civil respect are always performed in the presence of the person. They adored him as their God, and were certainly too much <i>enlightened</i> to be capable of any species of <i>idolatry</i>.</p>
<p><i><b>Returned to Jerusalem with great joy</b></i> — Having the fullest proof that Jesus was the promised Messiah; and that they had a full commission to preach repentance and remission of sin to mankind, and that they should be Divinely qualified for this great work by receiving the promise of the Father, <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="lu+24:49">Luke 24:49</span>.</p>
