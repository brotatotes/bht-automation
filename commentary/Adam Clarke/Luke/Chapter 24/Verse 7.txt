<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="lu+24:7">Luke 24:7</span>. <i><b>Sinful men</b></i> — Or <i>heathens</i>, ανθρωπων ἁμαρτωλων, i.e. the <i>Romans</i>, by whom only he could be put to death; for the Jews themselves acknowledged that this power was now vested in the hands of the Roman governor alone. See <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="joh+19:15">John 19:15</span>.</p>
