<p>Verse 32. <i><b>His word was with power.</b></i> — εν εξουσια, <i>With authority</i>. He assumed the tone and manner of a new Lawgiver; and uttered all his <i>doctrines</i>, not in the way of exhortation or advice, but in the form of precepts and commands, the unction of the Holy Spirit accompanying all he said. <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="mr+1:22">Mark 1:22</span>.</p>
