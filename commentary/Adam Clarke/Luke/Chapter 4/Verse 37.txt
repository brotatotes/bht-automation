<p>Verse 37. <i><b>The fame</b></i> — ηχος, the <i>sound</i>. This is a very elegant metaphor. The people are represented as <i>struck</i> with <i>astonishment</i>, and the <i>sound</i> goes out through all the coasts; in allusion to the propagation of sound, by a smart stroke upon any substance, by which the air is suddenly agitated, and conveys the <i>report</i> made by the stroke to distant places. So this miracle was told to others by those who saw it, and they to others still, till it was heard through all the coasts of Galilee, <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="mr+1:28">Mark 1:28</span>.</p>
