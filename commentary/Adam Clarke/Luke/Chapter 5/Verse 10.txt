<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="lu+5:10">Luke 5:10</span>. <i><b>Thou shalt catch men.</b></i> — ανθρωπους εση ζωγρων, <i>Thou</i> <i>shalt catch men alive</i>; this is the proper signification of the word. <i>Fear not</i>: these discoveries of God tend to <i>life</i>, not to <i>death</i>; and ye shall become the instruments of <i>life</i> and <i>salvation</i> to a lost world. <i>These</i> fish are taken to be <i>killed</i> and fed on; but those who are converted under your ministry shall be preserved unto <i>eternal life</i>. <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="mt+4:18">Matthew 4:18</span>, &amp;c., where this subject is considered more at large.</p>
