Verse Luke 5:12. A certain city — This was some city of Galilee; probably Chorazin or Bethsaida.A man full of leprosy — See this disease, and the cure, largely explained on Matthew 8:2-4; and see it particularly applied to the use of public preaching, Mark 1:40, &c.
