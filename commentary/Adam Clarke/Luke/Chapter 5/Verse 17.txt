<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="lu+5:17">Luke 5:17</span>. <i><b>On a certain day</b></i> — This was when he was at Capernaum. See <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="mr+2:1">Mark 2:1</span>.</p>
<p><i><b>The power of the Lord</b></i> — Δυναμις Κυριου The <i>mighty</i> or <i>miraculous</i> power of the Lord, i.e. of Jesus, was there to heal them - as many as were diseased either in body or soul. Where the <i>teaching</i> of <i>Christ</i> is, there also is the <i>power</i> of Christ to <i>redeem</i> and <i>save</i>.</p>
