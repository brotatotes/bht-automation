<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="lu+7:18">Luke 7:18</span>. <i><b>The disciples of John showed him</b></i>, c.] It is very likely that John's disciples attended the ministry of our Lord at particular times and this, we may suppose, was a common case among the disciples of different Jewish teachers. Though bigotry existed in its most formidable shape between the <i>Jews</i> and <i>Samaritans</i>, yet we do not find that it had any place between Jews and Jews, though they were of different sects, and attached to different teachers.</p>
