<p>Verse 35. <i><b>Wisdom is justified</b></i>, c.] Probably the <i>children of</i> <i>wisdom</i> is a mere Hebraism here for the <i>products</i> or <i>fruits of</i> <i>wisdom</i> hence the <i>Vatican</i> MS., one other, and some <i>versions</i>, have εργων, <i>works</i>, instead of τεκνων, <i>sons</i>, in the parallel place, <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="mt+11:19">Matthew 11:19</span>. True wisdom shows itself by its works; <i>folly</i> is never found in the <i>wise man's</i> way, any more than <i>wisdom</i> is in the path of a <i>fool</i>. <i>Theophylact's</i> note on this place should not be overlooked. Εδικαιωθη, τουτ' εστιν ετιμηθη, <i>Wisdom IS</i> <i>JUSTIFIED</i>, that is, <i>IS HONOURED, by all her children</i>.</p>
