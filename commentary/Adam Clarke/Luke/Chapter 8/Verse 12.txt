<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="lu+8:12">Luke 8:12</span>. <i><b>Those by the way side</b></i> — Bishop PEARCE thinks that Luke by ον here means σποροι, the <i>seeds</i>, though he acknowledges that he has never found such a word as σποροι in the plural number signifying <i>seeds</i>.</p>
