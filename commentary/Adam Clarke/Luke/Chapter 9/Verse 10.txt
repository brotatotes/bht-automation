<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="lu+9:10">Luke 9:10</span>. <i><b>Told him all</b></i> — <i>Related distinctly </i>- διηγησαντο, from δια, <i>through</i>, and ηγεομαι, <i>I declare</i>: hence the whole of this Gospel, because of its relating every thing so <i>particularly</i>, is termed διηγησις, <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="lu+1:1">Luke 1:1</span>, a <i>particular</i> and <i>circumstantially detailed narration</i>. <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="mr+6:30">Mark 6:30</span>.</p>
