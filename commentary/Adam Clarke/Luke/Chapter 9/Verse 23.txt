<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="lu+9:23">Luke 9:23</span>. <i><b>If any</b></i><b> man </b><i><b>will come after me</b></i> — <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="mt+16:24">Matthew 16:24</span>, and <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="mr+8:34">Mark 8:34</span>, where the nature of <i>proselytism</i> among the Jews is explained.</p>
<p><i><b>Daily</b></i> — καθ ημεραν is omitted by many reputable MSS., versions, and fathers. It is not found in the parallel places, <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="mt+16:24">Matthew 16:24</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="mr+8:34">Mark 8:34</span>.</p>
