<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="lu+9:4">Luke 9:4</span>. <i><b>There abide, and thence depart.</b></i> — That is, remain in that lodging till ye depart from that city. Some MSS. and versions add μη, which makes the following sense: <i>There remain, and depart </i> <i>NOT thence</i>. <span class="_0000ff"><span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="mt+10:11">Matthew 10:11</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="mt+10:11">Matthew 10:11</span></span>.</p>
