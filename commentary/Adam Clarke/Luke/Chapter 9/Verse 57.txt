<p>Verse 57. <i><b>A certain</b></i><b> man</b>] He was a scribe. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="mt+8:19-22">Matthew 8:19-22</span>. It is probable that this took place when Christ was at <i>Capernaum</i>, as Matthew represents it, and not on the way to <i>Jerusalem</i> through <i>Samaria</i>.</p>
