<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="mr+1:1">Mark 1:1</span>. <i><b>The beginning of the Gospel</b></i> — It is with the utmost propriety that Mark begins the Gospel dispensation by the preaching of John the Baptist, he being the <i>forerunner</i> of Jesus Christ, and the first proclaimer of the incarnated Messiah. <i>Gospel</i>-for the meaning of the word see the preface to Matthew. <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="mt+1:1">Matthew 1:1</span></p>
<p><i><b>Son of God</b></i> — To point out his Divine origin; and thus glancing at his miraculous conception. This was an essential character of the Messiah. See <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="mt+16:16">Matthew 16:16</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="mt+26:63">Matthew 26:63</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="lu+22:67">Luke 22:67</span>, &amp;c.</p>
