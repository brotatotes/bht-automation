<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="mr+1:16">Mark 1:16</span>. <i><b>As he walked by the sea</b></i>, &amp;c.] See Clarke on <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="mt+4:18-22">Matthew 4:18-22</span>.</p>
<p><i><b>Andrew his brother</b></i> — Instead of the common reading, αδελφον αυτου, <i>his brother</i>, the best MSS. and versions have αδελφουτου σιμωνος, <i>the brother of Simon</i>, which should be received into the text. The most eminent critics approve of this reading.</p>
