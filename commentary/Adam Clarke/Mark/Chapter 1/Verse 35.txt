Verse 35. In the morning a great while before day — By πρωι, the morning, is to be understood the whole space of three hours, which finished the fourth watch of the night.And there prayed. — Not that he needed any thing, for in him dwelt all the fulness of the Godhead bodily; but that he might be a pattern to us. Every thing that our blessed Lord did he performed either as our pattern, or as our sacrifice.
