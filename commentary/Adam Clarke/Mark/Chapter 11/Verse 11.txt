<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="mr+11:11">Mark 11:11</span>. <i><b>When he had looked round about upon all things</b></i> — <i>He</i> <i>examined every thing </i>- to see if the matters pertaining to the Divine worship were properly conducted; to see that nothing was <i>wanting</i>-nothing <i>superfluous</i>.</p>
<p><i><b>And now the eventide was come</b></i> — The time in which he usually left Jerusalem, to go to Bethany.</p>
