<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="mr+11:15">Mark 11:15</span>. <i><b>And they come</b></i> — Several MSS. and versions have παλιν, <i>again</i>. This was the next day after our Lord's triumphant entry into Jerusalem; for on the evening of that day he went to <i>Bethany</i>, and lodged there, <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="mr+11:11">Mark 11:11</span>, and <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="mt+21:17">Matthew 21:17</span>, and returned the next morning to Jerusalem.</p>
