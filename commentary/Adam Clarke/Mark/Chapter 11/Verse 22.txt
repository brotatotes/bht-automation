<p>Verse 22. <i><b>Have faith in God</b></i> — Εχετε πιϚιν θεου is a mere Hebraism: <i>have the faith of God</i>, i.e. have <i>strong</i> faith, or the <i>strongest faith</i>, for thus the Hebrews expressed the <i>superlative</i> degree; so the <i>mountains of God</i> mean exceeding <i>great</i> mountains-the <i>hail of God</i>, exceeding <i>great hail</i>, &amp;c.</p>
