<p>Verse 26. At the end of this verse, the 7th and 8th verses of <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="mt+7:0">Matthew 7:0</span>:, <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="mt+7:7-8">Matthew 7:7-8</span> Ask and ye shall receive, &amp;c., are added by M, and sixteen other MSS.</p>
<p>The 26th verse is wanting in BLS, seven others, some editions, the <i>Coptic</i>, one <i>Itala</i>, and <i>Theophyact</i>.</p>
