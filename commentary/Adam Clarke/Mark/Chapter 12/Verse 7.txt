<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="mr+12:7">Mark 12:7</span>. <i><b>This is the heir</b></i> — So they appear to have acknowledged in their consciences that this was the Messiah, the <i>heir of all</i> <i>things</i>.</p>
<p><i><b>The inheritance shall be ours.</b></i> — By slaying him we shall maintain our <i>authority</i>, and keep possession of our <i>revenues</i>.</p>
