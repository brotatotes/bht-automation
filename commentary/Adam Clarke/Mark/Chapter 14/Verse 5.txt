<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="mr+14:5">Mark 14:5</span>. <i><b>It might have been sold</b></i> — το μυρον, <i>This ointment</i>, is added by ABCDKL, thirty-five others, <i>AEthiopic, Armenian, Gothic</i>, all the <i>Itala</i> except one. <i>Griesbach</i> has received it into the text. The <i>sum</i> mentioned here would amount to nearly 10<i>£</i> sterling.</p>
