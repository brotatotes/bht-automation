<p>Verse 54. <i><b>Peter followed</b></i> — On Peter's denial, see <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="mt+26:57">Matthew 26:57</span>, &amp;c.</p>
<p><i><b>At the fire.</b></i> — προς τ οφως, literally, <i>at the light</i>, i.e. a fire that cast considerable light, in consequence of which, the maid servant was the better able to distinguish him: see <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="mr+14:67">Mark 14:67</span>.</p>
