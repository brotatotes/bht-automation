<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="mr+14:8">Mark 14:8</span>. <i><b>To anoint my body to the burying.</b></i> — εις τον ενταφιασμον, <i>against</i>, or <i>in reference to</i>, its <i>embalmment</i>, thus pointing out my death and the embalmment of my body, for the bodies of persons of distinction were wrapped up in aromatics to preserve them from putrefaction. <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="mt+26:12">Matthew 26:12</span>.</p>
