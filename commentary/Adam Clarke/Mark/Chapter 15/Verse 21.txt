<p>Verse 21. <i><b>A Cyrenian</b></i> — One of <i>Cyrene</i>, a celebrated city in the <i>Pentapolis</i> of <i>Libya</i>.</p>
<p><i><b>The father of Alexander and Rufus</b></i> — It appears that these two persons were well known among the first disciples of our Lord. It is not unlikely that this is the same <i>Alexander</i> who is mentioned, <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ac+19:33">Acts 19:33</span>, and that the other is the <i>Rufus</i> spoken of by St. Paul, <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ro+16:13">Romans 16:13</span>.</p>
