<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="mr+15:8">Mark 15:8</span>. <i><b>The multitude crying aloud</b></i> — αναβοησας. The word itself strongly marks the <i>vociferations</i>, or, to come nearer the original word, the <i>bellowing</i> of the multitude. It signifies, properly, a loud and long cry, such as Christ emitted on the cross. See the whole history of these proceedings against our Lord treated at large, on <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="mt+27:0">Matthew 27:0</span>. <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="mt+27:1-26">Matthew 27:1-26</span>, &amp;c.</p>
