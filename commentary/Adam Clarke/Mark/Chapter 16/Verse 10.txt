<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="mr+16:10">Mark 16:10</span>. <i><b>Them that had been with him</b></i> — Not only the <i>eleven</i> disciples, but several others who had been the occasional companions of Christ and the apostles.</p>
<p><i><b>Mourned and wept.</b></i> — Because they had lost their Lord and Master, and had basely abandoned him in his extremity.</p>
