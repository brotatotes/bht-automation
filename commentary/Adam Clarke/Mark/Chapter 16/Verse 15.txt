<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="mr+16:15">Mark 16:15</span>. <i><b>Go ye into all the world</b></i> — <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="mt+28:19">Matthew 28:19</span>.</p>
<p><i><b>And preach the Gospel to every creature.</b></i> — <i>Proclaim the glad</i> <i>tidings </i>- of Christ crucified; and raised from the dead - <i>to all the</i> <i>creation</i>, πασῃ τῃ κτισει - to the <i>Gentile</i> worid; for in this sense בריות <i>berioth</i>, is often understood among the rabbins; because HE, <i>through the grace of God, hath tasted death for</i> EVERY <i>man</i>, <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="heb+2:9">Hebrews 2:9</span>. And on the rejection of the Gospel by the Jews, it was sent to the whole <i>Gentile</i> world.</p>
