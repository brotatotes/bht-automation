<p>Verse 17. <i><b>These signs shall follow</b></i> — Or rather, <i>accompany</i>; this is the proper import of the original word παρακολουθησει, from παρα <i>with</i>, and ακολουθεω, <i>I follow</i>.</p>
<p><i><b>Them that believe</b></i> — <i>The believers</i>, as we express it; i.e. the <i>apostles</i>, and all those who in those <i>primitive</i> times were endued with <i>miraculous</i> powers, for the confirmation of the doctrines they preached.</p>
<p><i><b>In my name</b></i> — That is, by the <i>authority</i> and <i>influence</i> of the almighty Jesus.</p>
<p><i><b>Cast out devils</b></i> — Whose kingdom Jesus Christ was manifested to destroy.</p>
<p><i><b>Speak with new tongues</b></i> — This was most literally fulfilled on the day of pentecost, <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ac+2:4-19">Acts 2:4-19</span>.</p>
