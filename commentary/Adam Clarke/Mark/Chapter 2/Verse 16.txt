Verse Mark 2:16. Sinners — By αμαρτωλοι, the Gentiles or heathens are generally to be understood in the Gospels, for this was a term the Jews never applied to any of themselves, Matthew 9:10; Matthew 9:10.How is it that he eateth — Some very good MSS., several versions, with Chrysostom and Augustin, read, Why doth YOUR MASTER eat?
