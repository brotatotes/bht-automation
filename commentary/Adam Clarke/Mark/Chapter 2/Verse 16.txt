<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="mr+2:16">Mark 2:16</span>. <i><b>Sinners</b></i> — By αμαρτωλοι, the Gentiles or heathens are generally to be understood in the Gospels, for this was a term the Jews never applied to any of themselves, <span class="_0000ff"><span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="mt+9:10">Matthew 9:10</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="mt+9:10">Matthew 9:10</span></span>.</p>
<p><i><b>How is it that he eateth</b></i> — Some very good MSS., several versions, with <i>Chrysostom</i> and <i>Augustin</i>, read, <i>Why doth</i> YOUR MASTER <i>eat</i>?</p>
