<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="mr+2:2">Mark 2:2</span>. <i><b>So much as about the door</b></i> — Meaning the yard or court before the house.</p>
<p><i><b>Preached</b></i><b> THE WORD</b>] τον λογον. The <i>doctrine</i> of the kingdom of God; for so ο λογος is repeatedly used.</p>
