<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="mr+2:3">Mark 2:3</span>. <i><b>One sick of the palsy</b></i> — <i>A paralytic person</i>.</p>
<p><span class="_0000ff"><span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="mt+9:2">Matthew 9:2</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="mt+9:2">Matthew 9:2</span></span>, &amp;c.</p>
<p><i><b>Borne of four.</b></i> — Four men, one at each corner of the sofa or couch on which he lay: this sick man appears to have been too feeble to come himself, and too weak to be carried in any other way.</p>
