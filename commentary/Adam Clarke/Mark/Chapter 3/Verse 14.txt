<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="mr+3:14">Mark 3:14</span>. <i><b>He ordained twelve</b></i> — εποιησε, he <i>made</i> twelve. Here is nothing of what we call <i>ordaining</i>. Christ simply appointed them to be with him; and that he might send them occasionally to preach, &amp;c.</p>
<p><i><b>To preach</b></i> — The <i>Codex Bezae, Saxon</i>, and all the <i>Itala</i>, except one, add το ευαγγελιον, <i>the Gospel</i>.</p>
