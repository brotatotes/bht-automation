<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="mr+3:9">Mark 3:9</span>. <i><b>A small ship</b></i> — πλοιαριον. <i>The lytil boot</i>, Old English MS. It was doubtless something of the <i>boat</i> kind, which probably belonged to some of the disciples. Our Lord was at this time teaching by the sea of Galilee. The word <i>ship</i> is utterly improper in many places of our translation, and tends to mislead the people.</p>
