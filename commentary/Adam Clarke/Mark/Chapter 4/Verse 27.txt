<p>Verse 27. <i><b>And should sleep and rise night and day</b></i> — That is, he should <i>sleep</i> by <i>night</i>, and <i>rise</i> by <i>day</i>; for so the words are obviously to be understood.</p>
<p><i><b>He knoweth not how.</b></i> — How a plant grows is a mystery in nature, which the wisest philosopher in the universe cannot fully explain.</p>
