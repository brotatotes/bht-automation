<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="mr+4:4">Mark 4:4</span>. <i><b>The fowls</b></i> — του ουρανου, <i>of the air</i>, is the common reading; but it should be omitted, on the authority of nine <i>uncial</i> MSS., upwards of <i>one hundred</i> others, and almost all the <i>versions</i>. <i>Bengel</i> and <i>Griesbach</i> have left it out of the text. It seems to have been inserted in Mark, from <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="lu+8:5">Luke 8:5</span>.</p>
