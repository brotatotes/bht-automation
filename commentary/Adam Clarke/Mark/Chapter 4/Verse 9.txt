<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="mr+4:9">Mark 4:9</span>. <i><b>And he said - He that hath ears to hear, let him hear.</b></i> — The <i>Codex Bezae</i>, later <i>Syriac</i> in the margin, and seven copies of the <i>Itala</i>, add, και ὁ συνιων συνιετω, <i>and whoso understandeth, let</i> <i>him understand</i>.</p>
