<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="mr+5:12">Mark 5:12</span>. <b>ALL </b><i><b>the devils</b></i> — παντες, <i>all</i>, is omitted by many MSS. and versions; <i>Griesbach</i> leaves it out of the text. οι δαιμονες is omitted also by several: <i>Griesbach</i> leaves it doubtful. Probably it should be read thus, <i>And they besought him, saying</i>.</p>
