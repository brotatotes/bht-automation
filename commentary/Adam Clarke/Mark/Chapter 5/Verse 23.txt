<p>Verse 23. <i><b>My little daughter</b></i> — το θυγατριον μου, <i>that little</i> <i>daughter of mine</i>. The words express much tenderness and concern. Luke observes, <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="lu+8:42">Luke 8:42</span>, that she was his <i>only daughter</i>, and was about <i>twelve years of age</i>.</p>
<p><i><b>At the point of death</b></i> — εσχατως εχει, <i>in the last extremity,</i> <i>the last gasp</i>. <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="mt+9:18">Matthew 9:18</span>.</p>
