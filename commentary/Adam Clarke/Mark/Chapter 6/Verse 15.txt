<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="mr+6:15">Mark 6:15</span>. OR, <i><b>as one of the prophets.</b></i> — η, <i>or</i>, is omitted by ABCEGHKLMS-BHV, and one hundred others, <i>Syriac</i>, all the <i>Arabic</i>, all the <i>Persic, Coptic, AEthiopic, Gothic, Slavonic, Vulgate</i>, two <i>Itala, Origen, Victor</i>, and <i>Theophylact. Bengel, Wetstein</i>, and <i>Griesbach</i> leave it out of the text: the omission of it mends the sense much.</p>
