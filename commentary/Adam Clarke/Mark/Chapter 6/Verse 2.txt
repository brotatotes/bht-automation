<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="mr+6:2">Mark 6:2</span>. <i><b>Were astonished</b></i> — επι τῃ διδαχῃ αυτου, <i>at his doctrine</i>, or <i>teaching</i>. This is added by the <i>Codex Bezae</i> and <i>eight</i> others, later <i>Syriac, Armenian, Vulgate</i>, and all the <i>Itala</i>.</p>
