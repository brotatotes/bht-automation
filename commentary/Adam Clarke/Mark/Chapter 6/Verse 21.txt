<p>Verse 21. <i><b>Lords</b></i> — μεγιστασιν, probably governors of particular districts.</p>
<p><i><b>High captains</b></i> — χιλιαρχοις; literally, <i>chiefs</i> or <i>captains</i> over a <i>thousand men</i>, military chiefs.</p>
<p><i><b>Chief estates</b></i> — πρωτοις; probably such as might be called <i>nobles</i> by <i>title</i> only, having no <i>office</i> civil or military; probably <i>magistrates</i>. See KYPKE an the place.</p>
