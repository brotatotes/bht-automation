<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="mr+6:53">Mark 6:53</span>. <i><b>The land of Gennesaret</b></i> — This country lay on the coast of the sea of Galilee: it is described by Josephus as being exceedingly pleasant and fertile. It had its name of Gennesaret from גן, <i>gen</i>, a <i>garden</i>, and סר <i>sar</i>, a <i>prince</i>, either because the <i>king</i> had a <i>garden</i> there, or because of its great <i>fertility</i>.</p>
