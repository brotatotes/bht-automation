<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="mr+7:14">Mark 7:14</span>. <i><b>When he had called all the people</b></i> — But instead of παντα, <i>all</i>, παλιν, <i>again</i>, is the reading of BDL, later <i>Syriac</i> in the margin, <i>Coptic, AEthiopic, Saxon, Vulgate</i>, all the <i>Itala</i> but one. <i>Mill</i> and <i>Griesbach</i> approve of this reading.</p>
