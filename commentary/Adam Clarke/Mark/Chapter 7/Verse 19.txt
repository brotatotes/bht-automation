<p>Verse 19. <i><b>Into the draught</b></i> — <span class="_0000ff"><span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="mt+15:17">Matthew 15:17</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="mt+15:17">Matthew 15:17</span></span>.</p>
<p><i><b>Purging all meats?</b></i> — For what is separated from the different aliments taken into the stomach, and thrown out of the body, is the innutritious parts of <i>all</i> the <i>meats</i> that are eaten; and thus they are purged, nothing being left behind but what is proper for the support of the body.</p>
