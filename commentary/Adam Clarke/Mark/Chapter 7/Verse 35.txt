<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="mr+7:35">Mark 7:35</span>. <i><b>He spake plain.</b></i> — ορθως, <i>distinctly, without</i> <i>stammering</i>. One MS. has, <i>And he spoke, praising God</i>. There is no doubt of this: but the evangelist, I think, did not write these words.</p>
