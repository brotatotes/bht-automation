<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="mr+7:8">Mark 7:8</span>. <i><b>Washing of pots and cups</b></i>, &amp;c.] This whole clause is wanting in BL, <i>five</i> others, and the <i>Coptic</i>: one MS. omits this and the whole of the ninth verse. The eighth verse is not found in the parallel place of <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="mt+15:7-9">Matthew 15:7-9</span>.</p>
