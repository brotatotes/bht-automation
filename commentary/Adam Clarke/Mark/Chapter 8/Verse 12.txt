<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="mr+8:12">Mark 8:12</span>. <i><b>And he sighed deeply in his spirit</b></i> — Or <i>having</i> <i>deeply groaned </i>- so the word αναστεναξας properly means. He was exceedingly affected at their <i>obstinacy</i> and <i>hardness</i> of <i>heart</i>. See <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="mt+16:1-4">Matthew 16:1-4</span>.</p>
