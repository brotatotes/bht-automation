<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="mr+8:32">Mark 8:32</span>. <i><b>And he spake that saying</b></i> — Concerning the <i>certainty</i> and <i>necessity</i> of his sufferings - <i>openly: with great plainness</i>, παρρησια, <i>confidence</i>, or <i>emphasis</i>, so that the disciples now began fully to understand him. This is an <i>additional</i> observation of St. Mark. For Peter's reproof, <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="mt+16:22">Matthew 16:22</span>, &amp;c.</p>
