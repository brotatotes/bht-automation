<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="mr+9:2">Mark 9:2</span>. <i><b>And after six days Jesus taketh</b></i> with him <i>Peter</i>, c.] For a full account of the <i>nature</i> and <i>design</i> of the transfiguration, <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="mt+17:1">Matthew 17:1</span>, c.</p>
<p><i><b>A high mountain</b></i> — I have conjectured, <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="mt+17:1">Matthew 17:1</span>, that this was one of the mountains of <i>Galilee</i>: some say <i>Hermon</i>, some <i>Tabor</i> but Dr. Lightfoot thinks a mountain near Cesarea Philippi to be more likely.</p>
<p><i><b>Was transfigured</b></i> — Four good MSS. and Origen add here, AND WHILE THEY WERE PRAYING <i>he was transfigured</i> but this appears to be added from <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="lu+9:29">Luke 9:29</span>.</p>
