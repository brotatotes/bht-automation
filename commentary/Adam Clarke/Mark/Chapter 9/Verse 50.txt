<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="mr+9:50">Mark 9:50</span>. <i><b>If the salt have lost his saltness</b></i> — <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="mt+5:13">Matthew 5:13</span>.</p>
<p><i><b>Have salt in yourselves</b></i> — See that ye have at all times the <i>preserving</i> principle of Divine grace in your hearts, and give that proof of it which will satisfy your own minds, and convince or silence the world: live in brotherly kindness and <i>peace with each</i> <i>other</i>: thus shall all men see that you are free from ambition, (see <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="mr+9:34">Mark 9:34</span>), and that you are my disciples indeed. That it is possible for the salt to lose its <i>savour</i>, and yet retain its <i>appearance</i> in the most perfect manner, see proved on the note on <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="mt+5:13">Matthew 5:13</span>.</p>
