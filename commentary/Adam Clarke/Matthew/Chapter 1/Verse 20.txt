<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="mt+1:20">Matthew 1:20</span>. <i><b>That which is conceived</b></i> (or formed)<b> </b><i><b>in her</b></i> — So I think γεννηθεν should be translated in this place: as it appears that the human nature of Jesus Christ was a <i>real creation</i> in the womb of the virgin, by the power of the Holy Spirit. The <i>angel of</i> <i>the Lord</i> mentioned here was probably the angel Gabriel, who, six months before, bad been sent to Zacharias and Elisabeth, to announce the birth of Christ's forerunner, <i>John the Baptist</i>. See <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="lu+1:36">Luke 1:36</span>.</p>
