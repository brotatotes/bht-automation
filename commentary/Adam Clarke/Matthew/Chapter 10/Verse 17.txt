<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="mt+10:17">Matthew 10:17</span>. <i><b>But beware of men</b></i> — Or, be on your guard against <i>men</i>, των ανθρωπων THESE men; i.e. your <i>countrymen</i>; those from whom you might have reasonably expected comfort and support; and especially those in <i>power</i>, who will abuse that power to oppress you.</p>
<p><i><b>Councils</b></i> — συνεδρια, <i>sanhedrins</i> and <i>synagogues</i>. <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="mt+5:22">Matthew 5:22</span>. "By <i>synagogues</i> we may understand here, not the places of public worship, but assemblies where <i>three</i> magistrates, chosen out of the principal members of the synagogue, presided to adjust differences among the people: these had power, in certain cases, to condemn to the <i>scourge</i>, but not to <i>death</i>. See <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ac+22:19">Acts 22:19</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="2co+11:24">2 Corinthians 11:24</span>, compared with <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="lu+12:11">Luke 12:11</span>." See <i>Lightfoot</i>.</p>
