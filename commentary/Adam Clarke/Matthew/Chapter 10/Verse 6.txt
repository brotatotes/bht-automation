<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="mt+10:6">Matthew 10:6</span>. <i><b>But go rather to the lost sheep</b></i>, c.] The Jewish Church was the ancient <i>fold</i> of God but the sheep had wandered from their Shepherd, and were lost. Our blessed Lord sends these under-shepherds to seek, find, and bring them back to the Shepherd and Overseer of their souls.</p>
