<p>Verse 42. <i><b>The queen of the south</b></i> — In <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="1ki+10:1">1 Kings 10:1</span>, this queen is said to be of <i>Saba</i>, which was a city and province of Arabia Felix, to the <i>south</i>, or <i>south-east</i>, of Judea.</p>
<p><i><b>Uttermost parts of the earth</b></i> — περατων της γης - a form of speech which merely signifies, <i>a great distance</i>. See <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="de+28:49">Deuteronomy 28:49</span>.</p>
