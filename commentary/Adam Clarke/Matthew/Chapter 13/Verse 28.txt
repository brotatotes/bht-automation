<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="mt+13:28">Matthew 13:28</span>. <i><b>An enemy hath done this</b></i> — It is the interest of Satan to introduce hypocrites and wicked persons into religious societies, in order to discredit the work of God, and to favour his own designs.</p>
<p><i><b>Wilt thou then that we go and gather them up?</b></i> — A zeal which is rash and precipitate is as much to be feared as the total lack of strict discipline.</p>
