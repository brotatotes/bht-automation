Plainness, mildness, and modesty, are qualifications necessary to be observed when we reprove the great. The best service a subject can render his prince is to lay before him, in the plainest but most respectful manner, what the <i>law</i> of God <i>requires</i> of him, and what it <i>forbids</i>.
