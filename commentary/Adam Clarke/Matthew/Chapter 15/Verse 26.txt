<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="mt+15:26">Matthew 15:26</span>. <i><b>The children's bread</b></i> — The salvation provided for the Jews, who were termed <i>the children of the kingdom</i>. And cast it to the κυναριοις, <i>little dogs - to the curs</i>; such the <i>Gentiles</i> were reputed by the Jewish people, and our Lord uses that form of speech which was common among his countrymen. What terrible repulses! and yet she still perseveres!</p>
