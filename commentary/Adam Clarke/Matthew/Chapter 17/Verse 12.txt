<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="mt+17:12">Matthew 17:12</span>. <i><b>Knew him not</b></i> — Or, ουκ επιγνωσαν αυτον, <i>They have not</i> <i>acknowledged him</i>. That is, the <i>Jewish rulers</i> have not acknowledged him, did not receive him as the forerunner of the Messiah. But it appears that all the rest acknowledged him as <i>such</i>; and some, from the power and demonstration of his preaching, were inclined to think he was <i>more</i>, even the <i>Messiah</i> himself: see <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="lu+3:15">Luke 3:15</span>.</p>
