<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="mt+17:4">Matthew 17:4</span>. <i><b>Peter said - let us make</b></i>, &amp;c.] That is, when he saw Moses and Elijah ready to depart from the mount, <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="lu+9:33">Luke 9:33</span>, he wished to detain them, that he might always enjoy their company with that of his Lord and Master, still supposing that Christ would set up a temporal kingdom upon earth.</p>
