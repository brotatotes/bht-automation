It was a maxim among the Jews never to forgive more than thrice: Peter enlarges this charity more than <i>one half</i>; and our Lord makes even his enlargement <i>septuple</i>, see <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="mt+18:22">Matthew 18:22</span>. <i>Revenge</i> is natural to man, i.e. man is naturally a <i>vindictive</i> <i>being</i>, and, in consequence, nothing is more difficult to him than <i>forgiveness</i> of injuries.</p>
