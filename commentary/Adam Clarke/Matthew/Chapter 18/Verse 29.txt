<p>Verse 29. <i><b>Fell down at his feet</b></i> — This clause is wanting in several ancient MSS., versions, and fathers. Several printed editions also have omitted it; Griesbach has left it out of the text.</p>
<p><i><b>Pay thee all.</b></i> — παντα, <i>all</i>, is omitted by a multitude of MSS., versions, and fathers.</p>
