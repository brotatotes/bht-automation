<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="mt+20:3">Matthew 20:3</span>. <i><b>The third hour</b></i> — <i>Nine o'clock</i> in the morning.</p>
<p><i><b>Market-place</b></i> — Where labourers usually stood till they were hired. I have often seen labourers standing in the market places of large towns in these countries, waiting to be employed.</p>
