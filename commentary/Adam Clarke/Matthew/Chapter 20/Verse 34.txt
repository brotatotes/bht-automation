<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="mt+20:34">Matthew 20:34</span>. <i><b>So Jesus had compassion</b></i><b> on them</b>] επλαγχνιαθεις, <i>He</i> <i>was moved with tender pity</i>. The tender pity of Christ met the earnest cry of the blind men, and their immediate cure was the result.</p>
<p><i><b>They followed him.</b></i> — As a proof of the <i>miracle</i> that was wrought, and of the <i>gratitude</i> which they felt to their benefactor. For other particulars of this miraculous cure, <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="mr+10:46">Mark 10:46</span>, c.</p>
<p>Reader, whosoever thou art, act in behalf of thy soul as these blind men did in behalf of their sight, and thy salvation is sure. Apply to the Son of David lose not a moment; he is <i>passing by</i>, and thou art <i>passing</i> into <i>eternity</i>, and probably wilt never have a more favourable opportunity than the present. The Lord increase thy earnestness and faith!</p>
