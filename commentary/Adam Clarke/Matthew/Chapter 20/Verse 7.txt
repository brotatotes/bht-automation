<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="mt+20:7">Matthew 20:7</span>. <i><b>No man hath hired us.</b></i> — This was the reason why they were all the day <i>idle</i>.</p>
<p><i><b>And whatsoever is right</b></i><b>, that </b><i><b>shall ye receive.</b></i> — Ye may expect payment in proportion to your <i>labour</i>, and the <i>time</i> ye spend in it; but this clause is wanting in some of the best MSS., versions, and fathers.</p>
