<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="mt+20:8">Matthew 20:8</span>. <i><b>When the even was come</b></i> — <i>Six o'clock</i>, the time they ceased from labour, and the workmen came to receive their wages.</p>
<p><i><b>Steward</b></i> — επιτρωπος. A <i>manager of the household concerns</i> under the master. The rabbinical writers use the very same word, in Hebrew letters, for the same office, אפיטרופוס <i>epitropos</i>. See <i>Kypke</i>.</p>
