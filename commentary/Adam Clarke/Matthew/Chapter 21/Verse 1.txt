<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="mt+21:1">Matthew 21:1</span>. <i><b>Bethphage</b></i> — A place on the west declivity of Mount <i>Olivet</i>, from which it is thought the whole declivity and part of the valley took their name. It is supposed to have derived its name from the <i>fig-trees</i> which grew there; בית beeth, signifying a <i>region</i> as well as a <i>house</i>, and פג <i>phag</i>, a <i>green fig</i>.</p>
