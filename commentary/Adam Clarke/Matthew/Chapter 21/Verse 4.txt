<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="mt+21:4">Matthew 21:4</span>. <i><b>All this was done</b></i> — The word <i>all</i>, in this clause, is omitted by some MSS., versions, and fathers.</p>
<p><i><b>Which was spoken</b></i> — The Spirit of God, which predicted those things that concerned the Messiah, took care to have them literally fulfilled:</p>
<p>1. To show the truth of prophecy in general; and,</p>
<p>2. To designate Christ as the person intended by that prophecy.</p>
<p>See the note on <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="mt+2:23">Matthew 2:23</span>.</p>
