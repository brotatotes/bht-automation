Verse Matthew 21:4. All this was done — The word all, in this clause, is omitted by some MSS., versions, and fathers.Which was spoken — The Spirit of God, which predicted those things that concerned the Messiah, took care to have them literally fulfilled:1. To show the truth of prophecy in general; and,2. To designate Christ as the person intended by that prophecy.See the note on Matthew 2:23.
