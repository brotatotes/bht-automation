<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="mt+22:15">Matthew 22:15</span>. <i><b>In</b></i><b> his </b><i><b>talk.</b></i> — εν λογω, <i>by discourse</i>: intending to ask him <i>subtle</i> and <i>ensnaring</i> questions; his answers to which might involve him either with the Roman government, or with the great Sanhedrin.</p>
