<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="mt+22:23">Matthew 22:23</span>. <i><b>The same day</b></i> — Malice is ever <i>active</i>; let it be defeated ever so often, it returns to the charge. Jesus and his Gospel give no quarter to vice; the vicious will give no quarter to him or it.</p>
