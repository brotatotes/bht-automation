<p>Verse 34. <i><b>They were gathered together.</b></i> — επι το αυτο - <i>they came</i> <i>together with one accord</i>, or, <i>for the same purpose</i>; i.e. of <i>ensnaring</i> him in his <i>discourse</i>, as the Sadducees had done, <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="mt+22:23">Matthew 22:23</span>.</p>
<p>The Codex Bezae and several of the <i>Itala</i> have επαυτον, <i>against him</i>. <i>Camen togidre into oon. </i>- Old MS. Eng, Bib.</p>
