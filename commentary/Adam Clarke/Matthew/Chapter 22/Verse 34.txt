Verse 34. They were gathered together. — επι το αυτο - they came together with one accord, or, for the same purpose; i.e. of ensnaring him in his discourse, as the Sadducees had done, Matthew 22:23.The Codex Bezae and several of the Itala have επαυτον, against him. Camen togidre into oon. - Old MS. Eng, Bib.
