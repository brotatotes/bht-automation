<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="mt+22:7">Matthew 22:7</span>. <i><b>But when the king</b></i> — HIMSELF or, <i>this very king</i>. I have added εκεινος on the authority of <i>nine</i> of the most ancient MSS. and nearly one hundred others; the later <i>Syriac, six</i> copies of the <i>Itala</i>, and some of the <i>fathers</i>. Several printed editions have it, and <i>Griesbach</i> has received it into the text.</p>
