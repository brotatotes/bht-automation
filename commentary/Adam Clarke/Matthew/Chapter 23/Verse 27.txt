<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="mt+23:27">Matthew 23:27</span>. <i><b>For ye are like</b></i> — παρομοιαζετε, <i>ye exactly</i> <i>resemble </i>- the parallel is complete.</p>
<p><i><b>Whited sepulchres</b></i> — <i>White-washed tombs</i>. As the law considered those unclean who had touched any thing belonging to the dead, the Jews took care to have their tombs white-washed each year, that, being easily <i>discovered</i>, they might be consequently <i>avoided</i>.</p>
