It was once the Lord's <i>temple, God's</i> OWN <i>house</i>; but now he says, YOUR <i>temple</i> or <i>house </i>- to intimate that God had <i>abandoned</i> it. <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="mt+23:21">Matthew 23:21</span>; <i>see also Clarke on "</i><span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="lu+13:35">Luke 13:35</span><i>"</i>.</p>
