<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="mt+23:9">Matthew 23:9</span>. <i><b>Call no</b></i><b> man </b><i><b>your</b></i><b> FATHER</b>] Our Lord probably alludes to the AB, or father of the Sanhedrin, who was the <i>next</i> after the <i>nasi</i>, or president. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="mt+20:21">Matthew 20:21</span>. By which he gives his disciples to understand that he would have no SECOND, after himself, established in his Church, of which he alone was the <i>head</i>; and that perfect equality must subsist among them.</p>
