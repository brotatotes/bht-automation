<p>Verse 36. <i><b>But of that day and hour</b></i> — ωρα, here, is translated <i>season</i> by many eminent critics, and is used in this sense by both sacred and profane authors. As the <i>day</i> was not known, in which Jerusalem should be invested by the Romans, therefore our Lord advised his disciples to pray that it might not be on a <i>Sabbath</i>; and as the <i>season</i> was not known, therefore they were to pray that it might not be in the <i>winter</i>; <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="mt+24:20">Matthew 24:20</span>. <span class="_0000ff"><span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="mr+13:32">Mark 13:32</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="mr+13:32">Mark 13:32</span></span>.</p>
