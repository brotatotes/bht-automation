<p>The SECOND sign, <i>wars</i> and <i>commotions</i>.</p>
<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="mt+24:6">Matthew 24:6</span>. The next signs given by our Lord are <i><b>wars and rumours</b></i><b> </b><i><b>of wars</b></i><b>, c.</b>] These may be seen in <i>Josephus</i>, Ant. b. xviii. c. 9 War, b. ii. c. 10; especially as to the <i>rumours of wars</i>, when Caligula ordered his statue to be set up in the temple of God, which the Jews having refused, had every reason to expect a war with the Romans, and were in such consternation on the occasion that they even neglected to till their land.</p>
