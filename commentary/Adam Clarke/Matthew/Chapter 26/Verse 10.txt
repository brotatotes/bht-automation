<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="mt+26:10">Matthew 26:10</span>. <i><b>Why trouble ye the woman?</b></i> — Or, <i>Why do ye put the</i> <i>woman to pain</i>? See this sense of κοπους παρεχειν, established by <i>Kypke</i> in loco. A generous mind is ever <i>pained</i> when it is denied the opportunity of doing good, or when its proffered kindness is refused.</p>
