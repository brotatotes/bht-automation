<p>Verse 30. <i><b>And when they had sung a hymn</b></i> — υμνησαντες means, probably, no more than a kind of <i>recitative</i> reading or chanting. As to the <i>hymn</i> itself, we know, from the universal consent of Jewish antiquity, that it was composed of <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ps+113:0">Psalms 113:0</span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ps+114:0">Psalms 114:0</span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ps+115:0">Psalms 115:0</span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ps+116:0">Psalms 116:0</span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ps+117:0">Psalms 117:0</span>, and <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ps+118:0">Psalms 118:0</span>, termed by the Jews הלל <i>halel</i>, from הללו־יה <i>halelu-yah</i>, the first word in <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ps+113:0">Psalms 113:0</span>. These six Psalms were always sung at every paschal solemnity. They sung this great hillel on account of the <i>five</i> great benefits referred to in it; viz.</p>
<p style="margin-left: 0.9em; "> 1. The Exodus from Egypt, <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ps+114:1">Psalms 114:1</span>.</p>
<p style="margin-left: 0.9em; "> <i>When Israel went out of Egypt</i>, &amp;c.</p>
<p style="margin-left: 0.9em; "> </p>
<p style="margin-left: 0.9em; "> 2. The miraculous division of the Red Sea, <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ps+114:3">Psalms 114:3</span>.</p>
<p style="margin-left: 0.9em; "> <i>The sea saw it and fled</i>.</p>
<p style="margin-left: 0.9em; "> </p>
<p style="margin-left: 0.9em; "> 3. The promulgation of the law, <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ps+114:4">Psalms 114:4</span>.</p>
<p style="margin-left: 0.9em; "> <i>The mountains skipped like lambs</i>.</p>
<p style="margin-left: 0.9em; "> </p>
<p style="margin-left: 0.9em; "> 4. The resurrection of the dead, <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ps+116:9">Psalms 116:9</span>.</p>
<p style="margin-left: 0.9em; "> <i>I will walk before the Lord in the land of the living</i>.</p>
<p style="margin-left: 0.9em; "> </p>
<p style="margin-left: 0.9em; "> 5. The passion of the Messiah, <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ps+115:1">Psalms 115:1</span>.</p>
<p style="margin-left: 0.9em; "> <i>Not unto us, O Lord, not unto us</i>, &amp;c. </p>
