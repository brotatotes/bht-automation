<p>Verse 50. <i><b>Jesus said - Friend</b></i> — Rather, <i>companion</i>, εταιρε, (<i>not</i> FRIEND,) <i>wherefore</i>, rather, <i>against whom</i> (εφ' ὃ, the reading of all the best MSS.) <i>art thou come</i>? How must these words have cut his very soul, if he had any sensibility left! Surely, thou, who hast so long been my <i>companion</i>, art not come against <i>me</i>, thy Lord, Teacher and Friend! What is the human heart not capable of, when abandoned by God, and influenced by Satan and the love of money!</p>
<p><i><b>Laid hands on Jesus</b></i> — But not before they had felt that proof of his sovereign power by which they had all been struck down to the earth, <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="joh+18:6">John 18:6</span>. It is strange that, after this, they should dare to approach him; but the Scriptures must be fulfilled.</p>
