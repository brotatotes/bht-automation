<p>Verse 55. <i><b>Are ye come out as against a thief</b></i> — At this time Judea was much infested by robbers, so that armed men were obliged to be employed against them-to this our Lord seems to allude. <span class="_0000ff"><span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="mt+26:52">Matthew 26:52</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="mt+26:52">Matthew 26:52</span></span>.</p>
<p><i><b>I sat daily with you</b></i> — Why come in this hostile manner? Every day, for four days past, ye might have met with me in the temple, whither I went to teach you the way of salvation. <span class="_0000ff"><span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="mt+21:17">Matthew 21:17</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="mt+21:17">Matthew 21:17</span></span>.</p>
