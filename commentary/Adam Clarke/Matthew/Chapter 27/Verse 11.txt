<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="mt+27:11">Matthew 27:11</span>. <i><b>Before the governor</b></i> — My old MS. English Bible translates ηγημων <i>Meyr cheef justyse, Presedent</i>.</p>
<p><i><b>Art thou the Xing of the Jews?</b></i> — The Jews had undoubtedly delivered him to Pilate as one who was rising up against the imperial authority, and assuming the regal office. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="mt+27:2">Matthew 27:2</span>.</p>
