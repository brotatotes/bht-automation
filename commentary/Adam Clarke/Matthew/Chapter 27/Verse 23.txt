Verse Matthew 27:23. What evil hath he done? — Pilate plainly saw that there was nothing laid to his charge for which, consistently with the Roman laws, he could condemn him.But they cried out the more — What strange fury and injustice! They could not answer Pilate's question, What evil hath he done? He had done none, and they knew he had done none; but they are determined on his death.
