<p>Verse 49. After this verse, BCL and five others add, <i>Another,</i> <i>taking a spear, pierced his side, and there came out blood and</i> <i>water</i>. Several of the fathers add the same words here: they appear, however, to be an interpolation from <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="joh+19:34">John 19:34</span>.</p>
