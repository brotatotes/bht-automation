<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="mt+27:64">Matthew 27:64</span>. <i><b>Lest his disciples come by night</b></i> — Νυκτος, <i>by night</i>, is wanting in <i>ten</i> of the uncial MSS., and in several others, and in most of the versions. <i>Erasmus, Aldus, Bengel</i>, and <i>Boghard</i>, with <i>Griesbach</i>, leave it out of the text.</p>
