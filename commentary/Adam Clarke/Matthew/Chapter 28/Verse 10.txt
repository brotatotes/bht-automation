Verse Matthew 28:10. Be not afraid — They were seized with fear at the sight of the angel; and this was now renewed by this unexpected appearance of Christ. Matthew 28:8; Matthew 28:8.Go, tell my brethren — This is the first time our Lord called his disciples by this endearing name: they no doubt thought that their Lord would reproach them with their past cowardice and infidelity; but, in speaking thus, he gives them a full assurance, in the most tender terms, that all that was passed was as buried for ever.
