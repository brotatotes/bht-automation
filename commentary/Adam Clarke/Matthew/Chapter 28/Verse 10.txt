<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="mt+28:10">Matthew 28:10</span>. <i><b>Be not afraid</b></i> — They were seized with fear at the sight of the angel; and this was now renewed by this unexpected appearance of Christ. <span class="_0000ff"><span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="mt+28:8">Matthew 28:8</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="mt+28:8">Matthew 28:8</span></span>.</p>
<p><i><b>Go, tell my brethren</b></i> — This is the <i>first</i> time our Lord called his disciples by this endearing name: they no doubt thought that their Lord would reproach them with their past cowardice and infidelity; but, in speaking thus, he gives them a full assurance, in the most tender terms, that all that was passed was as buried for ever.</p>
