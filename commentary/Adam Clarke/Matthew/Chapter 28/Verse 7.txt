<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="mt+28:7">Matthew 28:7</span>. <i><b>Go quickly and tell his disciples</b></i> — Thus these faithful women proclaim the Gospel to those who were afterwards to be the <i>teachers</i> of the whole human race! Behold what honour God puts upon those who <i>persevere</i> in his truth, and continue to <i>acknowledge him before men</i>!</p>
<p><i><b>That he is risen from the dead</b></i> — There is a remarkable saying of <i>R. Judah Hakkodesh</i>, which some critics quote on this subject: "After THREE DAYS the SOUL of the Messiah shall RETURN to its body, and he shall GO OUT of that STONE in which he shall be BURIED."</p>
<p><i><b>Goeth before you into Galilee</b></i> — As himself promised, <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="mt+26:32">Matthew 26:32</span>.</p>
