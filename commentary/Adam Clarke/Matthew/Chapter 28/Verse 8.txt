Verse Matthew 28:8. They departed quickly from the sepulchre — At the desire of the angel they went into the tomb, to have the fullest certainty of the resurrection.Fear and great joy — Fear, produced by the appearance of this glorious messenger of God; and great joy occasioned by the glad tidings of the resurrection of their Lord and Master. At the mention of unexpected good news, fear and joy are generally intermingled.  TERANT. Andr. v. 945.  "I am almost beside myself, my mind is so agitated with fear, hope, and joy, at this unexpected good news."
