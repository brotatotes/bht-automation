<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="mt+28:8">Matthew 28:8</span>. <i><b>They departed quickly from the sepulchre</b></i> — At the desire of the angel they went <i>into</i> the tomb, to have the fullest certainty of the resurrection.</p>
<p><i><b>Fear and great joy</b></i> — <i>Fear</i>, produced by the <i>appearance</i> of this glorious messenger of God; and <i>great joy</i> occasioned by the <i>glad</i> <i>tidings</i> of the resurrection of their Lord and Master. At the mention of unexpected good news, fear and joy are generally intermingled. </p>
<p style="margin-left: 0.9em; "> TERANT. <i>Andr</i>. v. 945. </p>
<p style="margin-left: 0.9em; "><br/> </p>
<p>"I am almost beside myself, my mind is so agitated with <i>fear,</i> <i>hope</i>, and <i>joy</i>, at this unexpected good news."</p>
