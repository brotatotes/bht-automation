<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="mt+3:14">Matthew 3:14</span>. <i><b>John forbad him</b></i> — Earnestly and pressingly opposed him: this is the proper import of the words διεκωλευεν αυτον. I have observed that δια, in composition, most <i>frequently</i>, if not <i>always</i>, strengthens the signification in classic authors. - <i>Wakefield</i>.</p>
