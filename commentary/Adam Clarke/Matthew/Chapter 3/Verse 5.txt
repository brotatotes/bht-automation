<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="mt+3:5">Matthew 3:5</span>. <i><b>Jordan</b></i> — Many of the best MSS. and versions, with <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="mr+1:5">Mark 1:5</span>, add ποταμω, the <i>river</i> Jordan; but the definitive article, with which the word is generally accompanied, both in the Hebrew and the Greek, is, sufficient; and our article <i>the</i>, which should ever be used in the translation, expresses the force of the other.</p>
