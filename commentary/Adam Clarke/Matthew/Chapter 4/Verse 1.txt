<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="mt+4:1">Matthew 4:1</span>. <i><b>Then was Jesus led up of the Spirit</b></i> — This transaction appears to have taken place immediately after Christ's baptism; and this bringing up of Christ was through the influence of the Spirit of God; that Spirit which had rested upon him in his baptism.</p>
<p><i><b>To be tempted</b></i> — The first act of the ministry of Jesus Christ was a combat with Satan. Does not this receive light from <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ge+3:17">Genesis 3:17</span>. <i>I will put enmity between the woman's seed and thy seed: it shall</i> <i>bruise thy head, and thou shalt bruise his heel</i>.</p>
