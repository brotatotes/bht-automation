<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="mt+5:27">Matthew 5:27</span>. <i><b>Ye have heard that it was said by them of old</b></i> — By <i>the ancients</i>, τοις αρχαιοις, is omitted by nearly a <i>hundred</i> MSS., and some of them of the very greatest antiquity and authority; also by the <i>Coptic, Ethiopic, Armenian, Gothic</i>, and <i>Sclavonian</i> versions; by <i>four</i> copies of the old <i>Itala</i>; and by <i>Origen, Cyril</i>, <i>Theophylact, Euthymius</i>, and <i>Hilary</i>. On this authority <i>Wetstein</i> and <i>Griesbach</i> have left it out of the text.</p>
