<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="mt+8:1">Matthew 8:1</span>. <i><b>From the mountain</b></i> — That mountain on which he had delivered the preceding inimitable sermon.</p>
<p><i><b>Great multitudes followed him.</b></i> — Having been deeply impressed with the glorious doctrines which they had just heard.</p>
