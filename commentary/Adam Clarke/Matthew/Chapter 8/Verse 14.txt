<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="mt+8:14">Matthew 8:14</span>. <i><b>Peter's house</b></i> — That Peter lived at Capernaum, and that Christ lodged with him, is fully evident from this verse compared with <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="mt+17:24">Matthew 17:24</span>.</p>
<p><i><b>Peter's - wife's mother</b></i> — Learn hence, says Theophylact, that <i>marriage</i> is no hinderance to <i>virtue</i>, since the chief of the apostles had his wife. Marriage is one of the first of Divine institutions, and is a positive command of God. He says, the state of celibacy is not GOOD, <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ge+2:18">Genesis 2:18</span>. Those who pretend to say that the <i>single</i> state is more holy than the <i>other</i> slander their Maker, and say in effect, "We are too holy to keep the commandments of God."</p>
