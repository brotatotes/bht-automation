Verse Matthew 9:9. Named Matthew — Generally supposed to be the same who wrote this history of our blessed Lord. Mathai signifies a gift in Syriac; probably so named by his parents as implying a gift from God.The receipt of custom — The custom-house, τελωνιον - the place where the taxes levied by the Romans of the Jews, were collected.Follow me. — That is, become my disciple.And he arose, and followed him. — How blessed it is to be obedient to the first call of Christ-how much happiness and glory are lost by delays, though conversion at last may have taken place!
