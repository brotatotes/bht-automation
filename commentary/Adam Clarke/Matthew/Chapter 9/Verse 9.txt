<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="mt+9:9">Matthew 9:9</span>. <i><b>Named Matthew</b></i> — Generally supposed to be the same who wrote this history of our blessed Lord. <i>Mathai</i> signifies a <i>gift</i> in Syriac; probably so named by his parents as implying a <i>gift</i> <i>from God</i>.</p>
<p><i><b>The receipt of custom</b></i> — The custom-house, τελωνιον - the place where the taxes levied by the <i>Romans</i> of the <i>Jews</i>, were collected.</p>
<p><i><b>Follow me.</b></i> — That is, become my disciple.</p>
<p><i><b>And he arose, and followed him.</b></i> — How blessed it is to be obedient to the first call of Christ-how much happiness and glory are lost by <i>delays</i>, though conversion at last may have taken place!</p>
