<p>Verse 14. <i><b>Waxing confident</b></i> — Finding the effect produced by the public defence which the apostle made, they were greatly encouraged, and the more boldly and openly proclaimed the doctrine of Christ crucified.</p>
<p><i><b>The word</b></i> — The doctrine of Christ; several excellent MSS. and <i>versions</i> add, some θεου, others κυριου, the <i>word of God</i>, or <i>the word of the Lord</i>. This is a respectable reading, and is probably genuine.</p>
