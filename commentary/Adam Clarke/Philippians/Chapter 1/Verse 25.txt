<p>Verse 25. <i><b>Having this confidence, I know that I shall abide</b></i> — Convinced that it is necessary that I should live longer, for the spreading and defence of the Gospel, I am persuaded that I shall now be liberated. This was in fact the case, for, after having been two years in bonds at Rome, he was released.</p>
<p><i><b>For your furtherance</b></i> — In the way of righteousness.</p>
<p><i><b>And joy of faith</b></i> — And happiness in that way. The farther a man proceeds in the way of truth, the stronger his faith will be; and the stronger his faith, the greater his joy or happiness.</p>
