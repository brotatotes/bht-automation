<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="php+2:2">Philippians 2:2</span>. <i><b>Fulfil ye my joy</b></i> — Ye ought to complete my joy, who have suffered so much to bring you into the possession of these blessings, by being <i>like-minded</i> with myself, having <i>the same love</i> to God, his cause, and me, as I have to him, his cause, and you.</p>
<p><b>Being </b><i><b>of one accord</b></i> — Being perfectly agreed in labouring to promote the honour of your Master; and of one mind, being constantly <i>intent</i> upon this great subject; keeping your eye fixed upon it in all you say, do, or intend.</p>
