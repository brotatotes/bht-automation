Verse 25. Epaphroditus, my brother, c.] Here is a very high character of this minister of Christ he was,1. A brother-one of the Christian family; a thorough convert to God, without which he could not have been a preacher of the Gospel.2. He was a companion in labour; he laboured, and laboured in union with the apostle in this great work.3. He was a fellow soldier; the work was a work of difficulty and danger, they were obliged to maintain a continual warfare, fighting against the world, the devil, and the flesh.4. He was their apostle-a man whom God had honoured with apostolical gifts, apostolical graces, and apostolical fruits; and,5. He was an affectionate friend to the apostle; knew his soul in adversity, acknowledged him in prison, and contributed to his comfort and support.
