<p>Verse 7. <i><b>But made himself of no reputation</b></i> — εαυτονεκενωσε. <i>He emptied himself </i>- did not appear in his glory, for he assumed the form of a servant, being made in the likeness of man. And his being made in the likeness of man, and assuming the form of a servant, was a <i>proof</i> that he had <i>emptied</i> himself - laid aside the effulgence of his glory.</p>
