<p>Verse 17. <i><b>Brethren, be followers - of me</b></i> — In the things of Christ let me be your <i>line</i>, and my writings preaching, and conduct, your <i>rule</i>.</p>
<p><i><b>And mark them</b></i> — σκοπειτε. Still alluding to the <i>line</i> in the stadium, keep your eye steadily fixed on those <i>who walk</i>-live, <i>as</i> <i>ye have us</i>-myself, Timothy, and Epaphroditus, <i>for an ensample</i>.</p>
