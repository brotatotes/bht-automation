<p>Verse 13. <i><b>I can do all things</b></i> — It was not a <i>habit</i> which he had acquired by <i>frequent exercise</i>, it was a disposition which he had by <i>grace</i>; and he was enabled to do all by the power of an indwelling Christ. <i>Through Him who strengtheneth me</i> is the reading of some of the best MSS., versions, and fathers; the word χριστω, <i>Christ</i>, being omitted.</p>
