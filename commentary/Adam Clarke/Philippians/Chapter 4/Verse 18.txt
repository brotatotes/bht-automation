<p>Verse 18. <i><b>I have all</b></i> — Ye have now sent me so much by Epaphroditus, that I abound in all the necessaries of life.</p>
<p><i><b>Having received - the things</b></i> — Probably a supply of <i>clothes</i> and such like <i>necessaries</i>, as well as of <i>money</i>.</p>
<p><i><b>An odour of a sweet smell</b></i> — Alluding to the sacrifices offered up under the law. With what ye have done to me, his servant, God is well pleased. See <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="eph+5:2">Ephesians 5:2</span>, and the note there.</p>
