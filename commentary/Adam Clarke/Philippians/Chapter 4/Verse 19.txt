<p>Verse 19. <i><b>My God shall supply all your need</b></i> — As you have given to me in my distress, God will never suffer you to want without raising up help to you, as he raised you up for help to me.</p>
<p><i><b>According to his riches</b></i> — His fulness is infinite; and through Christ, whose followers we are, he will dispense every requisite blessing of <i>providence, grace</i>, and <i>glory</i>, to you.</p>
