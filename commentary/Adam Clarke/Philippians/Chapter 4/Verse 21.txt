Verse 21. Salute every saint — Remember to present my affectionate wishes to every Christian at Philippi.The brethren which are with me — Those who were fellow labourers with him, generally supposed to be Aristarchus, Mark, Justus, Epaphras, Luke, and Demas. See the end of the epistles to the Colossians (Colossians 4:17 note), and to Philemon (Philemon 1:25).
