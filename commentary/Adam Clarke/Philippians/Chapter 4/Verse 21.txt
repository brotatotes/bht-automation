<p>Verse 21. <i><b>Salute every saint</b></i> — Remember to present my affectionate wishes to every Christian at Philippi.</p>
<p><i><b>The brethren which are with me</b></i> — Those who were fellow labourers with him, generally supposed to be Aristarchus, Mark, Justus, Epaphras, Luke, and Demas. See the end of the epistles to the <i>Colossians</i> (<span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="col+4:17">Colossians 4:17</span> note), and to <i>Philemon</i> (<span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="phm+1:25">Philemon 1:25</span>).</p>
