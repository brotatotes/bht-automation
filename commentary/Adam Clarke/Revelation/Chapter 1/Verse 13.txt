<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="re+1:13">Revelation 1:13</span>. <i><b>Like unto the Son of man</b></i> — This seems a reference to <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="da+7:13">Daniel 7:13</span>. This was our blessed Lord himself, <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="re+1:18">Revelation 1:18</span>.</p>
<p><i><b>Clothed with a garment down to the foot</b></i> — This is a description of the <i>high priest</i>, in his sacerdotal robes. See these described at large in the notes on <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ex+28:4">Exodus 28:4</span>, &amp;c., Jesus is our high priest, even in heaven. He is still discharging the sacerdotal functions before the throne of God.</p>
<p><i><b>Golden girdle.</b></i> — The emblem both of <i>regal</i> and <i>sacerdotal</i> dignity.</p>
