Verse Revelation 1:13. Like unto the Son of man — This seems a reference to Daniel 7:13. This was our blessed Lord himself, Revelation 1:18.Clothed with a garment down to the foot — This is a description of the high priest, in his sacerdotal robes. See these described at large in the notes on Exodus 28:4, &c., Jesus is our high priest, even in heaven. He is still discharging the sacerdotal functions before the throne of God.Golden girdle. — The emblem both of regal and sacerdotal dignity.
