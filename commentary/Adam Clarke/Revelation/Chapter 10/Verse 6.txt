<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="re+10:6">Revelation 10:6</span>. <i><b>By him that liveth for ever and ever</b></i> — The eternal, self-existent Jehovah, the Maker of all things.</p>
<p><i><b>That there should be time no longer</b></i> — That the great counsels relative to the events already predicted should be immediately fulfilled, and that there should be no longer <i>delay</i>. This has no reference to the day of judgment.</p>
