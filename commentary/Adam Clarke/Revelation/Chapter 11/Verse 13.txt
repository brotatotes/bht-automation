<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="re+11:13">Revelation 11:13</span>. <i><b>A great earthquake</b></i> — Violent commotions among the persecutors, and revolutions of states.</p>
<p><i><b>Slain of men seven thousand</b></i> — Many perished in these popular commotions.</p>
<p><i><b>The remnant were affrighted</b></i> — Seeing the hand of God's judgments so remarkably stretched out.</p>
<p><i><b>Gave glory</b></i> — Received the pure doctrines of the Gospel, and glorified God for <i>his</i> judgments and <i>their</i> conversion.</p>
