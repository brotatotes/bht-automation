<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="re+11:17">Revelation 11:17</span>. <i><b>O Lord God Almighty, which art</b></i> — This gives a proper view of God in his eternity; all <i>times</i> are here comprehended, the <i>present</i>, the <i>past</i>, and the <i>future</i>. This is the infinitude of God.</p>
<p><i><b>Hast taken to thee</b></i> — Thou hast <i>exercised</i> that power which thou ever hast; and thou hast broken the power of thy enemies, and exalted thy Church.</p>
