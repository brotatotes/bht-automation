<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="re+11:18">Revelation 11:18</span>. <i><b>The nations were angry</b></i> — Were enraged against thy Gospel, and determined to destroy it.</p>
<p><i><b>Thy wrath is come</b></i> — The time to avenge thy servants and to destroy all thy enemies.</p>
<p><i><b>The time of the dead, that they should be judged</b></i> — The word κρινειν, <i>to judge</i>, is often used in the sense of <i>to avenge</i>. The dead, here, may mean those who were <i>slain for the testimony of</i> <i>Jesus</i>, and the <i>judging</i> is the <i>avenging</i> of their blood.</p>
<p><i><b>Give reward unto thy servants</b></i> — Who have been faithful unto death.</p>
<p><i><b>The prophets</b></i> — The faithful <i>teachers</i> in the Church, <i>the</i> <i>saints</i>-the Christians.</p>
<p><i><b>And them that fear thy name</b></i> — All thy sincere followers.</p>
<p><i><b>Destroy them which destroy the earth.</b></i> — All the authors, fomenters, and encouragers of bloody wars.</p>
