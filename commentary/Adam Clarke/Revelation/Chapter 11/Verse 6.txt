<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="re+11:6">Revelation 11:6</span>. <i><b>These have power to shut heaven</b></i> — As <i>Elijah</i> did, <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="1ki+17:1-46">1 Kings 17:1-46</span>.</p>
<p><i><b>To turn them to blood</b></i> — As Moses did, <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ex+7:19-25">Exodus 7:19-25</span>. They shall have power to afflict the land with plagues, similar to those which were inflicted on the Egyptians.</p>
