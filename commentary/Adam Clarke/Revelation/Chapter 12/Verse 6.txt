<p>NOTES ON CHAP. XII., BY J. E. C.</p>
<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="re+12:6">Revelation 12:6</span>. <i><b>And the woman fled into the wilderness</b></i> — The account of the woman's flying into the wilderness immediately follows that of her child being caught up to the throne of God, to denote the great and <i>rapid</i> increase of heresies in the Christian Church after the time that Christianity was made the religion of the empire.</p>
