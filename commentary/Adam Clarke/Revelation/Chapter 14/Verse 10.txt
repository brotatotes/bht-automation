<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="re+14:10">Revelation 14:10</span>. <i><b>The wine of the wrath of God</b></i> — As they have drunk the intoxicating wine of idolatry or spiritual fornication, they shall now drink the wine of God's wrath, which is poured out into the cup of his indignation. This is an allusion to the <i>poisoned cup</i>, which certain criminals were obliged to drink, on which ensued speedy death. <span class="_0000ff"><span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="heb+2:9">Hebrews 2:9</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="heb+2:9">Hebrews 2:9</span></span>.</p>
<p><i><b>Shall be tormented with fire and brimstone</b></i> — An allusion to the punishment of <i>Sodom</i> and <i>Gomorrha</i> for their unnatural crimes.</p>
<p><i><b>Presence of the holy angels, and-of the Lamb</b></i> — These being the <i>instruments</i> employed in their destruction; the <i>Lamb</i>-the Lord Jesus Christ, acting as judge.</p>
