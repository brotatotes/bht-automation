<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="re+14:14">Revelation 14:14</span>. <i><b>A white cloud</b></i> — It is supposed that, from this verse to the end of the chapter, the <i>destruction</i> of Rome is represented under the symbols of <i>harvest</i> and <i>vintage</i>; images very frequent among the ancient prophets, by which they represented the destruction and excision of nations. See <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="joe+3:12-14">Joel 3:12-14</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="isa+17:5">Isaiah 17:5</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="isa+63:1">Isaiah 63:1</span>; and <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="mt+13:37">Matthew 13:37</span>.</p>
<p><i><b>A golden crown</b></i> — In token of victory and regal power.</p>
