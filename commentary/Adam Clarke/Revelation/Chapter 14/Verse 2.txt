<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="re+14:2">Revelation 14:2</span>. <i><b>The voice of many waters</b></i> — That is, of multitudes of various nations.</p>
<p><i><b>The voice of harpers</b></i> — Though the sounds were many and apparently confused, yet both <i>harmony</i> and <i>melody</i> were preserved.</p>
