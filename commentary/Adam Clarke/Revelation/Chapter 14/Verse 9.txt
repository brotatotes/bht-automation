<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="re+14:9">Revelation 14:9</span>. <i><b>And the third angel followed</b></i> — Bishop Bale considers these three angels as three descriptions of preachers, who should bear their testimony against the corruptions of the <i>papal Church</i>.</p>
<p><i><b>The beast and his image</b></i> — See the notes on <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="re+13:1-18">Revelation 13:1-18</span>.</p>
<p><i><b>Mark in his forehead</b></i> — Such as the <i>sectarial marks</i> of the idolatrous Hindoos, as has been observed before.</p>
