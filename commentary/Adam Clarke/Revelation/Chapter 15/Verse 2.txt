<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="re+15:2">Revelation 15:2</span>. <i><b>A sea of glass</b></i> — A spacious lucid plain around the throne, from which fiery coruscations were continually emitted: or, the reflection of the light upon this lucid plain produced the prismatic colours of the most vivid <i>rainbow</i>.</p>
