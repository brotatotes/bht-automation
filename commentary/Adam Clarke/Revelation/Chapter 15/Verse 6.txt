<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="re+15:6">Revelation 15:6</span>. <i><b>The seven angels came out of the temple</b></i> — To show that they were sent from God himself.</p>
<p><i><b>Clothed in pure and white linen</b></i> — Habited as <i>priests</i>. For these habits see <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ex+28:6">Exodus 28:6</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ex+28:8">Exodus 28:8</span>; and <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="re+1:13">Revelation 1:13</span>.</p>
