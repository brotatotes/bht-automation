Verse Revelation 16:10. The seat of the beast — επι τον θρονον του θηριου Upon the throne of the wild beast. The regal family was smitten by the fourth vial; they did not repent: then the fifth angel pours out his vial on the throne of the wild beast, or antichristian idolatrous power.Was full of darkness — Confusion, dismay, and distress.
