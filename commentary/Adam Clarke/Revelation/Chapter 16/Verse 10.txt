<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="re+16:10">Revelation 16:10</span>. <i><b>The seat of the beast</b></i> — επι τον θρονον του θηριου <i>Upon</i> <i>the throne of the wild beast</i>. The regal family was smitten by the fourth vial; they did not repent: then the fifth angel pours out his vial on the throne of the wild beast, or antichristian idolatrous power.</p>
<p><i><b>Was full of darkness</b></i> — Confusion, dismay, and distress.</p>
