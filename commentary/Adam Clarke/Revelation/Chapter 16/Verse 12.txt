<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="re+16:12">Revelation 16:12</span>. <i><b>Upon the great river Euphrates</b></i> — Probably meaning the people in the vicinity of this river; though some think that the <i>Tiber</i> is intended.</p>
<p><i><b>The water thereof was dried up</b></i> — The <i>people</i> discomfited, and all impediments removed.</p>
<p><i><b>The kings of the east</b></i> — There seems to be an allusion here to the ruin of Babylon by Cyrus, predicted by the Prophet Jeremiah, <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="jer+50:1-64">Jeremiah 50:1-64</span>. But what city or people is pointed out by this Babylon it is in vain to conjecture.</p>
