<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="re+16:17">Revelation 16:17</span>. <i><b>Poured out his vial into the air</b></i> — To signify that this plague was to be widely diffused, and perhaps to intimate that pestilences and various deaths would be the effect of this vial. But possibly <i>air</i> in this place may have some emblematical meaning.</p>
<p><i><b>It is done.</b></i> — It is said, <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="re+10:7">Revelation 10:7</span>, that in the days of the seventh trumpet <i>the mystery of God should be finished</i>; so here we find it <i>completed</i>. γεγονε All's over! Fuimus Troes! Ilium fuit! Once there were Trojans, and they had a city; but now all are extinct.</p>
