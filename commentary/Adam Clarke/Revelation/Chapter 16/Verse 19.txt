<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="re+16:19">Revelation 16:19</span>. <i><b>The great city</b></i> — Some say Jerusalem, others <i>Rome pagan</i>, others <i>Rome papal</i>.</p>
<p><i><b>The cup of the wine of the fierceness of his wrath.</b></i> — Alluding to the mode of putting certain criminals to death, by making them drink a cup of poison. <span class="_0000ff"><span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="heb+2:9">Hebrews 2:9</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="heb+2:9">Hebrews 2:9</span></span>.</p>
