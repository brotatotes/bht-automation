<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="re+16:21">Revelation 16:21</span>. <i><b>A great hail-about the weight of a talent</b></i> — Has this any reference to <i>cannon balls</i> and <i>bombs</i>? It is very doubtful; we are all in the dark in these matters.</p>
<p>The words ως ταλαντιαια, <i>as a talent</i>, are used to express something <i>great, excessively oppressive</i>; as νοσηματων ταλαντιαιων, <i>terrible diseases</i>, not diseases of the <i>weight</i> of a <i>talent</i>. See <i>Rosenmuller</i>.</p>
