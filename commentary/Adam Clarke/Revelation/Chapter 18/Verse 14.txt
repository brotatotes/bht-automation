<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="re+18:14">Revelation 18:14</span>. <i><b>And the fruits that thy soul lusted after.</b></i> — και ἡ οπωρα της επιθυμιας της ψυχης σου. As οπωρα signifies <i>autumn</i>, any and all kinds of <i>autumnal fruits</i> may be signified by the word in the above clause.</p>
<p><i><b>Dainty and goodly</b></i> — τα λιπαρα <i>Delicacies</i> for the <i>table</i>. τα λαμπρα,, what is <i>splendid</i> and <i>costly</i> in <i>apparel</i>.</p>
