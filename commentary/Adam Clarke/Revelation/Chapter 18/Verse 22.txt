<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="re+18:22">Revelation 18:22</span>. <i><b>The voice of harpers</b></i>, &amp;c.] This seems to indicate not only a total destruction of influence, &amp;c., but also of <i>being</i>. It seems as if this city was to be <i>swallowed up by an earthquake</i>, or <i>burnt up by fire from heaven</i>.</p>
