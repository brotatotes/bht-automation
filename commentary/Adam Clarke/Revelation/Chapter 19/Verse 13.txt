<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="re+19:13">Revelation 19:13</span>. <i><b>He</b></i><b> was </b><i><b>clothed with a vesture dipped in blood</b></i> — To show that he was just come from recent slaughter. The description is taken from <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="isa+63:2-3">Isaiah 63:2-3</span>, where Judas Maccabaeus, or some other conqueror, is described.</p>
<p><i><b>The Word of God.</b></i> — Written in the Targum, and in other Jewish writings, מימרא דיי <i>meimera daiya</i>, "the word of Jehovah;" by which they always mean a <i>person</i>, and not a <i>word spoken</i>. <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="joh+1:1">John 1:1</span>, &amp;c.</p>
