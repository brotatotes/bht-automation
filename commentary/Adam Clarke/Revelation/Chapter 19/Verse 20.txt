<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="re+19:20">Revelation 19:20</span>. <i><b>And the beast was taken, and-the false prophet</b></i> — <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="re+17:8">Revelation 17:8</span>, c.</p>
<p><i><b>That worshipped his image.</b></i> — The <i>beast</i> has been represented as the <i>Latin empire</i> the <i>image of the beast</i>, the <i>popes of Rome</i>; and the <i>false prophet</i>, the <i>papal clergy</i>.</p>
<p><i><b>Were cast alive into a lake of fire</b></i> — Were discomfited when <i>alive</i>-in the zenith of their power, and destroyed with an utter destruction.</p>
