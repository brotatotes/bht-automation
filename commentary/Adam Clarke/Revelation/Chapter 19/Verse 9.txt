<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="re+19:9">Revelation 19:9</span>. <i><b>Blessed are they which are called unto the marriage</b></i> <i>supper</i>] This is an evident allusion to the <i>marriage of the king's</i> <i>son</i>, <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="mt+22:2">Matthew 22:2</span>, &amp;c., where the incarnation of our Lord, and the calling of Jews and Gentiles, are particularly pointed out. See the notes there. <i>Blessed</i> are all they who hear the Gospel, and are thus invited to lay hold on everlasting life.</p>
