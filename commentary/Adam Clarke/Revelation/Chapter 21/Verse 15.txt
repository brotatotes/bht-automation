<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="re+21:15">Revelation 21:15</span>. <i><b>Had a golden reed</b></i> — Several excellent MSS. add μετρον, a <i>measure</i>; he had a <i>measuring rod made of gold</i>. This account of measuring the city seems to be copied, with variations, from <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="eze+40:3">Ezekiel 40:3</span>, &amp;c.</p>
