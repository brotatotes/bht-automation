<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="re+21:5">Revelation 21:5</span>. <i><b>Behold, I make all things new.</b></i> — As the creation of the world at the beginning was the work of God alone, so this new creation.</p>
<p><i><b>These words are true and faithful.</b></i> — <i>Truth</i> refers to the <i>promise</i> of these changes; <i>faithfulness</i>, to the <i>fulfilment</i> of these promises.</p>
