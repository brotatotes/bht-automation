<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="re+21:6">Revelation 21:6</span>. <i><b>It is done.</b></i> — All is determined, and shall be fulfilled in due time. The great drama is finished, and what was intended is now completed; referring to the period alluded to by the angel.</p>
<p><i><b>I am Alpha and Omega</b></i> — <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="re+1:8">Revelation 1:8</span>.</p>
<p><i><b>The fountain of the water of life</b></i> — See on <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="joh+4:10">John 4:10</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="joh+4:14">John 4:14</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="joh+7:37">John 7:37</span>, &amp;c.</p>
<p>The rabbins consider <i>the fountain of the world to come</i> as one of the particular blessings of a future state. In <i>Sanhedrim, Aboth R.</i> <i>Nathan</i>, c. 31, it is said, "He will show them the excellency of the <i>fountain of the future world</i>, that they may accurately see and consider, and say, Wo to us! what good have we lost! and our race is cut off from the face of the earth."</p>
