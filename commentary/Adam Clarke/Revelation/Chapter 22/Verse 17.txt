Verse Revelation 22:17. The Spirit and the bride — All the prophets and all the apostles; the Church of God under the Old Testament, and the Church of Christ under the New.Say, Come. — Invite men to Jesus, that by him they may be saved and prepared for this kingdom.Let him that heareth — Let all who are privileged with reading and hearing the word of God, join in the general invitation to sinners.Him that is athirst — He who feels his need of salvation, and is longing to drink of the living fountain.And whosoever will — No soul is excluded: Jesus died for every man; every man may be saved; therefore let him who wills, who wishes for salvation, come and take the water of life freely-without money or price!
