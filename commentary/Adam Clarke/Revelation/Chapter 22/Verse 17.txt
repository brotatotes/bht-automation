<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="re+22:17">Revelation 22:17</span>. <i><b>The Spirit and the bride</b></i> — All the <i>prophets</i> and all the <i>apostles</i>; the Church of God under the Old Testament, and the Church of Christ under the New.</p>
<p><i><b>Say, Come.</b></i> — Invite men to Jesus, that by him they may be saved and prepared for this kingdom.</p>
<p><i><b>Let him that heareth</b></i> — Let all who are privileged with <i>reading</i> and <i>hearing</i> the word of God, join in the general invitation to <i>sinners</i>.</p>
<p><i><b>Him that is athirst</b></i> — He who feels his need of salvation, and is longing to drink of the living fountain.</p>
<p><i><b>And whosoever will</b></i> — No soul is excluded: Jesus died for every man; every man may be saved; therefore let him who <i>wills</i>, who <i>wishes</i> for salvation, come and take the <i>water of life</i> <i>freely</i>-without money or price!</p>
