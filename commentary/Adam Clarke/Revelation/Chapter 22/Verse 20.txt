<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="re+22:20">Revelation 22:20</span>. <i><b>Surely I come quickly</b></i> — This may be truly said to every person in every age; Jesus the Judge is at the door!</p>
<p><i><b>Even so, come, Lord Jesus.</b></i> — The wish and desire of the suffering Church, and of all the followers of God, who are longing for the coming of his kingdom.</p>
