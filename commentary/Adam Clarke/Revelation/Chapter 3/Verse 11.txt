<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="re+3:11">Revelation 3:11</span>. <i><b>Behold, I come quickly</b></i> — These things will shortly take place; and I am coming with <i>consolations</i> and <i>rewards</i> to my faithful followers, and with <i>judgments</i> to my adversaries.</p>
<p><i><b>Take thy crown.</b></i> — God has provided mansions for you; let none through your fall occupy those seats of blessedness.</p>
