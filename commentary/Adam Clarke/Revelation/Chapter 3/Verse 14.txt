<p><i>Epistle to the Church of the Laodiceans.</i></p>
<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="re+3:14">Revelation 3:14</span>. <i><b>These things saith the Amen</b></i> — That is, He who is true or faithful; from אמן <i>aman</i>, he was tree; immediately interpreted, <i>The faithful and true witness</i>. See <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="re+1:5">Revelation 1:5</span>.</p>
<p><i><b>The beginning of the creation of God</b></i> — That is, the head and governor of all creatures: the king of the creation. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="col+1:15">Colossians 1:15</span>. By his <i>titles</i>, here, he prepares them for the humiliating and awful truths which he was about to declare, and the <i>authority</i> on which the declaration was founded.</p>
