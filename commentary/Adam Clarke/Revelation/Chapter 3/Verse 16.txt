<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="re+3:16">Revelation 3:16</span>. <i><b>Because thou art lukewarm</b></i> — Irresolute and undecided.</p>
<p><i><b>I will spue thee out of my mouth.</b></i> — He alludes here to the known effect of <i>tepid water</i> upon the <i>stomach</i>; it generally produces a <i>nausea</i>. I wilt cast thee off. Thou shalt have no interest in me. Though thou hast been near to my heart, yet now I must pluck thee thence, because slothful, careless, and indolent; thou art not in earnest for thy soul.</p>
