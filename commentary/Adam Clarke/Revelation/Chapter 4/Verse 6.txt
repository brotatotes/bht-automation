<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="re+4:6">Revelation 4:6</span>. <i><b>Four beasts</b></i> — τεσσαρα ζωα <i>Four living creatures</i> or <i>four animals</i>. The word <i>beast</i> is very improperly used here and elsewhere in this description. <i>Wiclif</i> first used it, and translators in general have followed him in this uncouth rendering. A <i>beast</i> before the throne of God in heaven sounds oddly.</p>
