<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="re+5:11">Revelation 5:11</span>. <i><b>The voice of many angels</b></i> — These also are represented as joining in the chorus with <i>redeemed mortals</i>.</p>
<p><i><b>Ten thousand times ten thousand</b></i> — "Myriads of myriads and chiliads of chiliads;" that is, an infinite or innumerable multitude. This is in reference to <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="da+7:10">Daniel 7:10</span>.</p>
