<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="re+5:3">Revelation 5:3</span>. <i><b>And no man</b></i> — ουδεις No <i>person</i> or <i>being</i>.</p>
<p><i><b>In heaven</b></i> — Among all the angels of God.</p>
<p><i><b>Nor in the earth</b></i> — No <i>human</i> being.</p>
<p><i><b>Neither under the earth</b></i> — No <i>disembodied spirit</i>, nor any <i>demon</i>. Neither <i>angels, men</i>, nor <i>devils</i>, can fathom the decrees of God.</p>
<p><i><b>Neither to look thereon.</b></i> — None can <i>look</i> into it unless it be opened, and none can <i>open</i> it unless the <i>seals</i> be unloosed.</p>
