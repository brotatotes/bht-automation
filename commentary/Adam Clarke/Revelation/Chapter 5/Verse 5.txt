Verse Revelation 5:5. The Lion of the tribe of Juda — Jesus Christ, who sprang from this tribe, as his genealogy proves; see on Matthew 1:2; Matthew 1:3 and Luke 3:33. There is an allusion here to Genesis 49:9, Judah is a lion's whelp; the lion was the emblem of this tribe, and was supposed to have been embroidered on its ensigns.The Root of David — See Isaiah 11:1. Christ was the root of David as to his Divine nature; he was a branch out of the stem of Jesse as to his human nature.Hath prevailed — By the merit of his incarnation, passion, and death.To open the book — To explain and execute all the purposes and decrees of God, in relation to the government of the world and the Church.
