<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="re+6:12">Revelation 6:12</span>. <i><b>The sixth seal</b></i> — This seal also is opened and introduced by Jesus Christ alone.</p>
<p><i><b>A great earthquake</b></i> — A most stupendous change in the civil and religious constitution of the world. If it refer to Constantine the Great, the change that was made by his conversion to Christianity might be very properly represented under the emblem of an <i>earthquake</i>, and the other symbols mentioned in this and the following verses.</p>
<p>The <i>sun</i>-the ancient <i>pagan government</i> of the Roman empire, was totally darkened; and, like a <i>black hair sackcloth</i>, was degraded and humbled to the dust.</p>
<p>The <i>moon</i>-the <i>ecclesiastical</i> state of the same empire, became as <i>blood</i>-was totally ruined, their sacred rites abrogated, their priests and religious institutions desecrated, their altars cast down, their temples destroyed, or turned into places for Christian worship.</p>
