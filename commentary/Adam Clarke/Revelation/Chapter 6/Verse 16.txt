<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="re+6:16">Revelation 6:16</span>. <i><b>Said to the mountains and rocks</b></i> — Expressions which denote the strongest perturbation and alarm. They preferred any kind of death to that which they apprehended from this most awful revolution.</p>
<p><i><b>From the face of him that sitteth on the throne</b></i> — They now saw that all these terrible judgments came from the <i>Almighty</i>; and that <i>Christ</i>, the author of Christianity, was now judging, condemning, and destroying them for their cruel persecutions of his followers.</p>
