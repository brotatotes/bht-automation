Verse Revelation 6:4. Another horse-red — The emblem of war; perhaps also of severe persecution, and the martyrdom of the saints.Him that sat thereon — Same say, Christ; others, Vespasian; others, the Roman armies; others, Artabanus, king of the Parthians, &c., &c.Take peace from the earth — To deprive Judea of all tranquillity.They should kill one another — This was literally the case with the Jews, while besieged by the Romans.A great sword. — Great influence and success, producing terrible carnage.
