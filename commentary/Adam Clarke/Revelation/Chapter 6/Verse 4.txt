<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="re+6:4">Revelation 6:4</span>. <i><b>Another horse-red</b></i> — The emblem of <i>war</i>; perhaps also of <i>severe persecution</i>, and the <i>martyrdom</i> of the saints.</p>
<p><i><b>Him that sat thereon</b></i> — Same say, <i>Christ</i>; others, <i>Vespasian</i>; others, the <i>Roman armies</i>; others, <i>Artabanus</i>, king of the Parthians, &amp;c., &amp;c.</p>
<p><i><b>Take peace from the earth</b></i> — To deprive Judea of all tranquillity.</p>
<p><i><b>They should kill one another</b></i> — This was literally the case with the <i>Jews</i>, while besieged by the Romans.</p>
<p><i><b>A great sword.</b></i> — Great influence and success, producing terrible carnage.</p>
