<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="re+6:5">Revelation 6:5</span>. <i><b>The third beast</b></i> — That which had the face of a <i>man</i>.</p>
<p><i><b>A black horse</b></i> — The emblem of <i>famine</i>. Some think that which took place under <i>Claudius</i>. See <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="mt+24:7">Matthew 24:7</span>; the same which was predicted by <i>Agabus</i>, <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ac+11:28">Acts 11:28</span>.</p>
<p><i><b>A pair of balances</b></i> — To show that the <i>scarcity</i> would be such, that every person must be put under an <i>allowance</i>.</p>
