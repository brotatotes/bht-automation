<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="re+7:11">Revelation 7:11</span>. <i><b>All the angels</b></i>, &amp;c.] As there is joy in the presence of God among these holy spirits when one sinner repents, no wonder that they take such an interest in the gathering together of such innumerable multitudes who are fully saved from their sins.</p>
