<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="re+7:15">Revelation 7:15</span>. <i><b>Therefore</b></i> — Because they are washed in the blood of the Lamb, <i>are they before the throne</i>-admitted to the immediate presence, <i>of God</i>.</p>
<p><i><b>And serve him day and night</b></i> — Without ceasing; being filled with the spirit of prayer, faith, love, and obedience.</p>
<p><i><b>Shall dwell among them.</b></i> — He lives in his own Church, and in the heart of every true believer.</p>
