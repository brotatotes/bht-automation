<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="re+7:2">Revelation 7:2</span>. <i><b>The seal of the living God</b></i> — This angel is represented as the chancellor of the supreme King, and as <i>ascending from the</i> <i>east</i>, απο ανατολης ηλιου, <i>from the rising of the sun</i>. Some understand this of Christ, who is called ανατολη, the <i>east</i>, <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="lu+1:78">Luke 1:78</span>.</p>
<p><i><b>Four angels, to whom it was given to hurt</b></i> — Particular <i>agents</i> employed by Divine providence in the management of the affairs of the earth; but whether <i>spiritual</i> or <i>material</i> we know not.</p>
