<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="re+7:5-8">Revelation 7:5-8</span>. <i><b>Of the tribe of Juda</b></i>, c.] <i>First</i>, we are to observe that the tribe of <i>Levi</i> is here mentioned, though that tribe had no inheritance in Israel but they now belonged to the spiritual priesthood. <i>Secondly</i>, That the tribe of <i>Dan</i>, which had an inheritance, is here <i>omitted</i>; as also the tribe of <i>Ephraim</i>. <i>Thirdly</i>, That the tribe of <i>Joseph</i> is here <i>added</i> in the place of <i>Ephraim</i>. <i>Ephraim</i> and <i>Dan</i>, being the principal promoters of idolatry, are left out in this enumeration.</p>
