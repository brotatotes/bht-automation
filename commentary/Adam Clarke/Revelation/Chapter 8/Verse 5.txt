<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="re+8:5">Revelation 8:5</span>. <i><b>Cast</b></i><b> it </b><i><b>into the earth</b></i> — That is, upon the land of Judea; intimating the judgments and desolations which were now coming upon it, and which appear to be farther opened in the sounding of the seven trumpets.</p>
<p><i><b>There were voices</b></i> — All these seem to point out the confusion, commotions, distresses, and miseries, which were coming upon these people in the <i>wars</i> which were at hand.</p>
