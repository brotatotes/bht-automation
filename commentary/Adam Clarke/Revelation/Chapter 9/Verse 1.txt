<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="re+9:1">Revelation 9:1</span>. <i><b>A star fall from heaven</b></i> — An angel encompassed with light suddenly descended, and seemed like a <i>star</i> falling from heaven.</p>
<p><i><b>The key of the bottomless pit.</b></i> — Power to inundate the earth with a flood of temporal calamities and moral evils.</p>
