<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="re+9:11">Revelation 9:11</span>. <i><b>A king over them</b></i> — A supreme head; some think Mohammed, some think Vespasian.</p>
<p><i><b>The angel of the bottomless pit</b></i> — The chief envoy of Satan.</p>
<p><i><b>Abaddon</b></i> — From אבד <i>abad</i>, he destroyed.</p>
<p><i><b>Apollyon.</b></i> — From απο, <i>intensive</i>, and ολλυω, <i>to destroy</i>. The meaning is the same both in the Hebrew and Greek.</p>
