<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="re+9:2">Revelation 9:2</span>. <i><b>He opened the bottomless pit</b></i> — το φρεαρ της αβυσσου <i>The pit of the bottomless deep</i>. Some think the angel means <i>Satan</i>, and the bottomless pit <i>hell</i>. Some suppose <i>Mohammed</i> is meant; and Signior <i>Pastorini</i> professes to believe that <i>Luther</i> is intended!</p>
<p><i><b>There arose a smoke</b></i> — False doctrine, obscuring the true light of heaven.</p>
