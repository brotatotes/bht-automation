<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ro+1:10">Romans 1:10</span>. <i><b>Making request</b></i>, c.] By this we see how earnestly the apostle longed to see Rome. It had long been a subject of continual prayer to God, that he might have a prosperous journey to, or rather <i>meeting</i> with, them, for so we should understand the word ευοδωθησμαι: that he had a prosperous <i>meeting</i> with them we cannot doubt that he had a disastrous journey to them the 27th of the Acts fully proves.</p>
