<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ro+1:13">Romans 1:13</span>. <i><b>But was let hitherto</b></i> — The word <i>let</i>, from the Anglo-Saxon, [Anglo-Saxon] to <i>hinder</i>, signifies <i>impediment</i> or <i>hinderance</i> of any kind: but it is likely that the original word, εκωλυθην, <i>I was forbidden</i>, refers to a <i>Divine prohibition</i>:-he would have visited them long before, but God did not see right to <i>permit</i> him.</p>
