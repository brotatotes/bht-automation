<p>Verse 24. <i><b>God-gave them up</b></i>, c.] They had filled up the measure of their iniquities, and God, by permitting them to plunge into all manner of irregularities, thus, by one species of sin, inflicted punishment on another.</p>
<p><i><b>Dishonour their own bodies</b></i> — Probably alluding here to what is more openly expressed, <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ro+1:26-27">Romans 1:26-27</span>.</p>
<p><i><b>Between themselves</b></i> — ενεαυτοις, <i>Of themselves, of their own</i> <i>free accord</i> none <i>inciting</i>, none <i>impelling</i>.</p>
