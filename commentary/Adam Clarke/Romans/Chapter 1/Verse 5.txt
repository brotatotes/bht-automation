<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ro+1:5">Romans 1:5</span>. <i><b>Grace and apostleship</b></i> — The peculiar influence and the essential qualifications which such an <i>office</i> requires. Without the GRACE, <i>favour</i>, and peculiar help of God, he could not have been an apostle: he had an extraordinary conversion, and an extraordinary call to preach the Gospel. Probably χαριν και αποστολην, <i>grace and apostleship</i>, mean the same as χαριν της αποστολης, the <i>apostolical office</i>; for so the word χαρις means in <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ro+12:3">Romans 12:3</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ro+15:15">Romans 15:15</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="1co+3:10">1 Corinthians 3:10</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="eph+3:8">Ephesians 3:8</span>. See the various acceptations of the word <i>grace</i>, <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ro+1:7">Romans 1:7</span>.</p>
<p><i><b>For obedience to the faith</b></i> — That by this <i>office</i>, which I have received from God, and the <i>power</i> by which it is accompanied, I might proclaim the <i>faith</i>, the Gospel of Jesus; and show all nations the necessity of believing in it, in order to their salvation. Here is:</p>
<p>1. The Gospel of the Son of God.</p>
<p>2. An apostle divinely commissioned and empowered to preach it.</p>
<p>3. The necessity of faith in the name of Jesus, as the only Saviour of the world.</p>
<p>4. Of obedience, as the necessary consequence of genuine faith. And,</p>
<p>5. This is to be proclaimed among all nations; that all might have the opportunity of believing and being saved.</p>
