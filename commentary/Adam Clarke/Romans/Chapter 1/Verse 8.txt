<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ro+1:8">Romans 1:8</span>. <i><b>First, I thank my God</b></i> — From this to the end of <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ro+1:17">Romans 1:17</span> belongs to the <i>preface</i>, in which the apostle endeavours to conciliate the good opinion of the Christians at Rome, and to prepare their minds for his reproofs and exhortations.</p>
<p><i><b>Your faith is spoken</b></i> — καταγγελλεται, is celebrated, <i>throughout</i> <i>the whole world</i>-in every place where the Christian religion is professed, through all parts of the <i>Roman dominions</i>; for in this sense we should understand the words, the <i>whole world</i>.</p>
