<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ro+10:9">Romans 10:9</span>. <i><b>That if thou shalt confess</b></i>, c.] Acknowledge the Lord Jesus Christ as the only Saviour. <i>Believe in thy heart</i> that he who died for thy offences has been <i>raised</i> for thy justification and depend solely on him for that justification, <i>and thou shalt be saved</i>.</p>
