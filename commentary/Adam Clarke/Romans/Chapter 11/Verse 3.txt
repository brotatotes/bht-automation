Verse Romans 11:3. Lord, they have killed thy prophets — They will not permit any person to speak unto them in thy name; and they murder those who are faithful to the commission which they have received from thee.Digged down thine altars — They are profligate and profane beyond example, and retain not the slightest form of religion.I am left alone — There is no prophet besides myself left, and they seek to destroy me.
