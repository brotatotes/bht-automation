<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ro+11:3">Romans 11:3</span>. <i><b>Lord, they have killed thy prophets</b></i> — They will not permit any person to speak unto them in thy name; and they murder those who are faithful to the commission which they have received from thee.</p>
<p><i><b>Digged down thine altars</b></i> — They are profligate and profane beyond example, and retain not the slightest form of religion.</p>
<p><i><b>I am left alone</b></i> — There is no prophet besides myself left, and they seek to destroy me.</p>
