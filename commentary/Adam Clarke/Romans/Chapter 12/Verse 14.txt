<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ro+12:14">Romans 12:14</span>. <i><b>Bless them which persecute you</b></i> — ευλογειτε, Give <i>good words</i>, or <i>pray</i> for them that give you <i>bad words</i>, καταρασθε, who make <i>dire imprecations</i> against you. <i>Bless</i> them, pray for them, and on no account curse them, whatever the provocation may be. Have the loving, forgiving mind that was in your Lord.</p>
