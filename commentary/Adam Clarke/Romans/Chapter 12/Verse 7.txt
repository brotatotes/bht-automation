<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ro+12:7">Romans 12:7</span>. <i><b>Or ministry</b></i> — διακονια simply means the <i>office</i> of a <i>deacon</i>; and what this office was, <i>see in Clarke's note on "</i><span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ac+6:4">Acts 6:4</span><i>"</i>, where the subject is largely discussed.</p>
<p><i><b>Or he that teacheth</b></i> — The teacher, διδασκαλος, was a person whose office it was to instruct others, who thereby catechizing, or simply explaining the grand truths of Christianity.</p>
