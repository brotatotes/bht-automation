<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ro+13:2">Romans 13:2</span>. <i><b>Whosoever resisteth the power</b></i> — ο αντιτασσομενος, He who sets himself in <i>order</i> against this <i>order</i> of God; τη του θεου διαταγη, and <i>they who resist</i>, οι ανθεστηκοτες, they who obstinately, and for no right reason, oppose the ruler, and strive to unsettle the constitution, and to bring about illegal changes,</p>
<p><i><b>Shall receive to themselves damnation.</b></i> — κριμα, <i>condemnation</i>; shall be <i>condemned</i> both by the spirit and letter of that constitution, which, under pretence of defending or improving, they are indirectly labouring to subvert.</p>
