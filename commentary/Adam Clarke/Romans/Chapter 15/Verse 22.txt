<p>Verse 22. <i><b>For which cause</b></i>, c.] My considering it <i>a point of</i> <i>honour to build on no other man's foundation</i> and, finding that the Gospel has been long ago planted at Rome, I have been prevented from going thither, purposing rather to spend my time and strength in preaching where Christ has not, as yet, been proclaimed.</p>
