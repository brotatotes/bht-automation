<p>Verse 27. <i><b>For if the Gentiles have been made partakers</b></i>, c.] It was through and by means of the Jews that the Gentiles were brought to the knowledge of God and the Gospel of Christ. These were the <i>spiritual things</i> which they had received and the pecuniary contribution was the <i>carnal things</i> which the Gentiles were now returning.</p>
