<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ro+16:14">Romans 16:14</span>. <i><b>Salute Asyncritus</b></i>, c.] Who these were we know not. Hermas was probably the same to whom a work called the <i>Shepherd</i> is attributed a work with this title is still extant, and may be found among the writings of the <i>apostolical</i> fathers. But it is in vain to look for <i>identity</i> of <i>persons</i> in <i>similarity</i> of <i>names</i>; for, among the Greeks and Romans at this time there were many persons who bore the same names mentioned in this chapter.</p>
