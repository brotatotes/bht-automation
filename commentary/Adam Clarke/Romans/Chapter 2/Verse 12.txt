<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ro+2:12">Romans 2:12</span>. <i><b>For as many as have sinned without law</b></i>, c.] They, viz. the <i>Gentiles</i>, who shall be found to have transgressed against the mere <i>light of nature</i>, or rather, <i>that true light that lighteth</i> <i>every man that cometh into the world</i>, <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="joh+1:9">John 1:9</span>, shall not come under the same rule with those, the <i>Jews</i>, who have in <i>addition</i> to this enjoyed an extraordinary <i>revelation</i> but they shall be dealt with according to the inferior dispensation, under which they lived: while those, the <i>Jews</i>, who have <i>sinned against the law</i>-the positive Divine revelation granted to them, <i>shall be judged by</i> <i>that law</i>, and punished proportionably to the abuse of such an extraordinary advantage.</p>
