<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ro+2:2">Romans 2:2</span>. <i><b>We are sure that the judgment of God</b></i>, &amp;c.] God is impartial, and will punish sin wheresoever he finds it. <i>Transgression</i> in a <i>Jew</i> is not less criminal than <i>iniquity</i> in a <i>Gentile</i>.</p>
