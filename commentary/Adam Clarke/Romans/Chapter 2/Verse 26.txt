<p>Verse 26. <i><b>Therefore if the uncircumcision</b></i>, &amp;c.] If the <i>Gentile</i> be found to act according to the <i>spirit</i> and <i>design</i> of the <i>law</i>, his acting thus uprightly, according to the light which God has afforded him, will be reckoned to him as if he were circumcised and walked agreeably to the law.</p>
