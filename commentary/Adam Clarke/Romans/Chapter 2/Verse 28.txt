<p>Verse 28. <i><b>For he is not a Jew</b></i> — A genuine <i>member of the Church</i> of God, who has only an <i>outward</i> profession.</p>
<p><i><b>Neither</b></i><b> is that </b><i><b>circumcision</b></i> — Circumcision is a rite which represents a <i>spiritual</i> thing, viz. the change and purification of the heart, as may be seen, <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="jer+4:4">Jeremiah 4:4</span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="jer+4:6">Jeremiah 4:6</span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="jer+4:10">Jeremiah 4:10</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="jer+9:26">Jeremiah 9:26</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="eze+44:7">Ezekiel 44:7</span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="eze+44:9">Ezekiel 44:9</span>.</p>
