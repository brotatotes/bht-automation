<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ro+2:7">Romans 2:7</span>. <i><b>To them</b></i>, &amp;c.] In this manner will God, in the great day, dispense punishments and rewards:</p>
<p>1. He will give eternal life to them who, in all the <i>trials</i> and <i>difficulties</i> of the present state, have <i>persevered</i> in <i>well doing</i>-seeking for and expecting <i>glory, honour</i>, and <i>immortality</i>.</p>
