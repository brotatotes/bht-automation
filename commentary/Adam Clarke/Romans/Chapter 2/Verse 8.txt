<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ro+2:8">Romans 2:8</span>. <i><b>But unto them</b></i>, &amp;c.]</p>
<p>2. He will manifest his <i>indignation</i>, and inflict <i>wrath</i> - punishment, on all who are <i>contentious</i> - who obstinately dispute against the truth, and <i>obey</i> <i>unrighteousness</i> - who act under the influence of the principle of sin, and not under the influence of the Spirit of God.</p>
