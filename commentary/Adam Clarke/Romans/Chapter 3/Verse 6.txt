<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ro+3:6">Romans 3:6</span>. APOSTLE. <i><b>God forbid</b></i> — μη γενοιτο, by no means. God cannot be <i>unjust</i>; were he unjust, he could not be qualified to judge the world, nor inflict that punishment on the unfaithful Jews, to which I refer.</p>
