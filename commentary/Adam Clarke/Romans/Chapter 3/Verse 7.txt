<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ro+3:7">Romans 3:7</span>. JEW. <i><b>For if the truth of God</b></i>, &amp;c.] But to resume my reasoning (<span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ro+3:5">Romans 3:5</span>:) If the faithfulness of God in keeping his promise made to our fathers is, through our unfaithfulness, made far more glorious than it otherwise would have been, why should we then be <i>blamed</i> for that which must redound so much to the honour of God?</p>
