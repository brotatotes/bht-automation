<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ro+3:8">Romans 3:8</span>. APOSTLE. <i><b>And not</b></i><b> rather, c.</b>] <i>And</i> why do you <i>not</i> <i>say</i>, seeing you assume this ground, that in all cases we should do wickedly, because God, by freely pardoning, can so glorify his own grace? This is a most impious sentiment, but it follows from your reasoning it has, indeed, been most injuriously laid to the charge of us apostles, who preach the doctrine of <i>free pardon,</i> <i>through faith, without the merit of works</i>; but this is so manifest a perversion of the truth that a just punishment may be expected to fall on the propagators of such a slander.</p>
