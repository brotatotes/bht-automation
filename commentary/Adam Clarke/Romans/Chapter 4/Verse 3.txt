<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ro+4:3">Romans 4:3</span>. <i><b>For, what saith the Scripture?</b></i> — The Scriptural account of this transaction, <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ge+15:6">Genesis 15:6</span>, is decisive; for there it is said, <i>Abraham believed God, and it was counted</i>, ελογισθη, <i>it</i> <i>was reckoned to him for righteousness</i>, ειςδικαιοσυνην, <i>for</i> <i>justification</i>.</p>
