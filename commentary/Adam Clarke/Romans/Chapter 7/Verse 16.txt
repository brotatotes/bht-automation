<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ro+7:16">Romans 7:16</span>. <i><b>If then I do that which I would not</b></i>, c.] Knowing that the law condemns it, and that therefore it must be <i>evil</i>. <i>I consent unto the law</i> I show by this circumstance that I acknowledge the law to be good.</p>
