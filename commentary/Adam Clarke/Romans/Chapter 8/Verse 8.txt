<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="ro+8:8">Romans 8:8</span>. <i><b>So then</b></i> — Because this carnal mind is enmity against God, <i>they that are in the flesh</i>-who are under the power of the workings of this carnal mind, (which every soul is that has not received redemption in the blood of the Lamb,)-</p>
<p><i><b>Cannot please God.</b></i> — Because of the rebellious workings of this principle of <i>rebellion</i> and <i>hatred</i>. And, if they cannot <i>please</i> God, they must be <i>displeasing</i> to him; and consequently in the broad road to final perdition.</p>
