<p>Verse 28. <i><b>For he will finish the work, and cut it short</b></i>, c.] These appear to be forensic terms, and refer to the <i>conclusion</i> of a <i>judicial proceeding</i> the Lord has <i>tried</i> and <i>found them guilty</i>, and will <i>immediately execute</i> upon them the punishment due to their transgressions.</p>
