Verse.13. This witness is true. — What Epimenides said of them nearly 600 years before continued still to be true. Their original character had undergone no moral change.Rebuke them sharply — αποτομως. Cuttingly, severely; show no indulgence to persons guilty of such crimes.That they may be sound in the faith — That they may receive the incorrupt doctrine, and illustrate it by a holy and useful life.
