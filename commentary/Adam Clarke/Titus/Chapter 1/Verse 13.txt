<p>Verse.13. <i><b>This witness is true.</b></i> — What Epimenides said of them nearly 600 years before continued still to be true. Their original character had undergone no moral change.</p>
<p><i><b>Rebuke them sharply</b></i> — αποτομως. <i>Cuttingly, severely</i>; show no indulgence to persons guilty of such crimes.</p>
<p><i><b>That they may be sound in the faith</b></i> — That they may receive the incorrupt doctrine, and illustrate it by a holy and useful life.</p>
