<p>Verse 14. <i><b>Not giving heed to Jewish fables</b></i> — See on <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="1ti+1:4">1 Timothy 1:4</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="1ti+4:7">1 Timothy 4:7</span>.</p>
<p><i><b>Commandments of men</b></i> — The injunctions of the scribes and Pharisees, which they added to the law of God.</p>
<p><i><b>That turn from the truth.</b></i> — For such persons made the word of God of none effect by their traditions. Sometimes the verb αποστρεφομαι signifies to be <i>averse from, slight</i>, or <i>despise</i>. So, here, the persons in question despised the truth, and taught others to do the same.</p>
