<p>Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="acc" lang="eng" ref="tit+3:2">Titus 3:2</span>. <i><b>To speak evil of no man</b></i> — μηδενα βλασφημειν. To blaspheme no person, to reproach none, to speak nothing to any man's injury; but, on the contrary, bearing reproach and contumely with patience and meekness.</p>
