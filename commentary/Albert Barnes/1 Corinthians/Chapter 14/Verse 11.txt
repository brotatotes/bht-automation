<p style=" "><b>The meaning of the voice - </b>Of the language that is uttered, or the sounds that are made.</p>
<p style=" "><b>I shall be unto him ... - </b>What I say will be unintelligible to him, and what he says will be unintelligible to me. We cannot understand one another any more than people can who speak different languages.</p>
<p style=" "><b>A barbarian - </b>See the note at <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="ro+1:14">Romans 1:14</span>. The word means one who speaks a different, or a foreign language.</p>
