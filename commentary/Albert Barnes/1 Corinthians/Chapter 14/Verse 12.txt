<p style=" "><b>Even so ye - </b>Since you desire spiritual gifts, I may urge it upon you to seek to he able to speak in a clear and intelligible manner, that you may edify the church. This is one of the most valuable endowments of the Spirit; and this should be earnestly desired.</p>
<p style=" "><b>Forasmuch as ye are zealous - </b>Since you earnestly desire; See the note at <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="1co+12:31">1 Corinthians 12:31</span>.</p>
<p style=" "><b>Spiritual gifts - </b>The endowments conferred by the Holy Spirit; See the note at <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="1co+12:1">1 Corinthians 12:1</span>.</p>
<p style=" "><b>Seek that ye may excel ... - </b>Seek that you may be able to convey truth in a clear and plain manner; seek to be distinguished for that. It is one of the most rare and valuable endowments of the Holy Spirit.</p>
