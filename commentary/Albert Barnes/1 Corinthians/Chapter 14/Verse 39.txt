<p style=" "><b>Covet to prophesy - </b>See the note at <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="1co+14:1">1 Corinthians 14:1</span>. This is the “summing up” of all that he had said. It was “desirable” that a man should wish to be able to speak, under the teaching of the Holy Spirit, in such a manner as to edify the church.</p>
<p style=" "><b>And forbid not ... - </b>Do not suppose that the power of speaking foreign languages is useless, or is to be despised, or that it is to be prohibited. “In its own place” it is a valuable endowment; and on proper occasions the talent should be exercised; see in <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="1co+14:22">1 Corinthians 14:22</span>.</p>
