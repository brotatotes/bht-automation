<p style=" "><b>For if the dead rise not ... - </b>This is a repetition of what is said in <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="1co+15:13">1 Corinthians 15:13</span>. It is repeated here, evidently, because of its importance. It was a great and momentous truth which would “bear” repetition, that if there was no resurrection, as some held, then it would follow that the Lord Jesus was not raised up.</p>
