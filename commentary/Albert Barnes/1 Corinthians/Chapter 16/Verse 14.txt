<p style=" "><b>Let all your things ... - </b>All that you do. This direction is repeated on account of its great importance, and because it is a summing up of all that he had said in this Epistle; see <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="1co+13:1-13">1 Corinthians 13:1-13</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="1co+14:1">1 Corinthians 14:1</span>. Here he says, that charity, or love, was to regulate all that they did. This was a simple rule; and if this was observed, every thing would be done well.</p>
