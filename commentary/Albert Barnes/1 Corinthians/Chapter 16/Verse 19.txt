<p style=" "><b>The churches of Asia - </b>The word “Asia” in the New Testament usually denotes Asia Minor in general; see the note on <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="ac+2:9">Acts 2:9</span>. It was sometimes used in a more limited sense, to denote the region around Ephesus, and of which Ephesus was the center and capital; see note, <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="ac+16:6">Acts 16:6</span>. This is the region undoubtedly which is intended here.</p>
<p style=" "><b>Salute you - </b>Greet you; send respectful and affectionate Christian regards; see the note at <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="ro+16:3">Romans 16:3</span>.</p>
<p style=" "><b>Aquila and Priscilla - </b>See the note on <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="ac+18:26">Acts 18:26</span>.</p>
<p style=" "><b>Much in the Lord - </b>With affectionate Christian salutations; or as Christians. Wishing the blessing and favor of the Lord.</p>
<p style=" "><b>With the church that is in their house - </b>See the note at <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="ro+16:5">Romans 16:5</span>.</p>
