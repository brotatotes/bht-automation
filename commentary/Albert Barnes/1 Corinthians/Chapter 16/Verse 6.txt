<p style=" "><b>That ye may bring me on my journey - </b>That you may accompany me, or aid me, and furnish me the means of going on my journey. It was customary for the apostles to be attended by some members of the churches and friends in their travels. See the note at <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="ac+10:23">Acts 10:23</span>.</p>
<p style=" "><b>On my journey ... - </b>Probably to Judea. This was evidently his intention. But wherever he should go, it would be gratifying to him to have their aid and companionship.</p>
