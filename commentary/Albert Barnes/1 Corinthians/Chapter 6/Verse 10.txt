<p style=" "><b>Nor covetous; - </b>See the note at <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="1co+5:10">1 Corinthians 5:10</span>. It is remarkable that the apostle always ranks “the covetous” with the most abandoned classes of people.</p>

<p style=" "><b>Nor extortioners - </b>See the note at <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="1co+5:11">1 Corinthians 5:11</span>.</p>
