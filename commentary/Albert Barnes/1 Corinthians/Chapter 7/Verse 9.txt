<p style=" "><b>But if they cannot contain - </b>If they have not the gift of continence; if they cannot be secure against temptation; if they have not strength of virtue enough to preserve them from the danger of sin, and of bringing reproach and scandal on the church.</p>
<p style=" "><b>It is better - </b>It is to be preferred.</p>
<p style=" "><b>Than to burn - </b>The passion here referred to is often compared to a fire; see Virgil, <i>Aeneas</i> 4:68. It is better to marry, even with all the inconveniences attending the marriage life in a time of distress and persecution in the church <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="1co+7:26">1 Corinthians 7:26</span>, than to be the prey of raging, consuming, and exciting passions.</p>
