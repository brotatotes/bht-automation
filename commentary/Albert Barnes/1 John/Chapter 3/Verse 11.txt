<p style=" "><b>For this is the message - </b>Margin, “commandment.” In the received text, this is <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ἀγγελία</span></span> angelia - “a message brought;” in several manuscripts, and in later editions, it is <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ἐπαγγελία</span></span> epangelia - “annunciation, announcement;” an order given, or a commandment, <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="ac+23:21">Acts 23:21</span>. It is not very material which reading is followed. The word “command” or “rule” would express the sense with sufficient clearness. The reference is to the law given by the Saviour as a permanent direction to his disciples.</p>
