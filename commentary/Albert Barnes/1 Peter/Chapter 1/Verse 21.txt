<p style=" "><b>Who by him do believe in God - </b>Faith is sometimes represented particularly as exercised in God, and sometimes in Christ. It is always a characteristic of true religion that a man has faith in God. Compare the notes at <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="mr+11:22">Mark 11:22</span>.</p>
<p style=" "><b>That raised him up from the dead - </b>See the <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="ac+2:24">Acts 2:24</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="ac+3:15">Acts 3:15</span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="ac+3:26">Acts 3:26</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="ac+4:10">Acts 4:10</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="ac+5:30">Acts 5:30</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="ac+13:30">Acts 13:30</span> notes; <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="ro+4:24">Romans 4:24</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="ro+6:4">Romans 6:4</span> notes; <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="1co+15:15">1 Corinthians 15:15</span> note.</p>
<p style=" "><b>And gave him glory - </b>By exalting him at his own right hand in heaven, <span class="_0000ff"><u>Phi 2:9</u></span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="1ti+3:16">1 Timothy 3:16</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="eph+1:20-21">Ephesians 1:20-21</span>.</p>
<p style=" "><b>That your faith and hope might be in God - </b>That is, by raising up the Lord Jesus, and exalting him to heaven, he has laid the foundation of confidence in his promises, and of the hope of eternal life. Compare the notes at <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="1pe+1:3">1 Peter 1:3</span>.
