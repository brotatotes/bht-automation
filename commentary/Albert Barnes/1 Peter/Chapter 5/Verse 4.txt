<p style=" "><b>And when the chief Shepherd shall appear - </b>The prince of the pastors - the Lord Jesus Christ. “Peter, in the passage above, ranks himself with the elders; here he ranks Christ himself with the pastors” - Benson. See the notes at <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="1pe+2:25">1 Peter 2:25</span>. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="heb+13:20">Hebrews 13:20</span>.</p>
<p style=" "><b>Ye shall receive a crown of glory - </b>A glorious crown or diadem. Compare the notes at <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="2ti+4:8">2 Timothy 4:8</span>.</p>
<p style=" "><b>That fadeth not away - </b>This is essentially the same word, though somewhat different in form, which occurs in <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="1pe+1:4">1 Peter 1:4</span>. See the notes at that verse. The word occurs nowhere else in the New Testament. Compare the notes at <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="1co+9:25">1 Corinthians 9:25</span>.</p>
