<p style=" "><b>And the Lord make you to increase and abound in love - </b>compare notes, <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="2co+9:8">2 Corinthians 9:8</span>. The word “Lord” here probably refers to the Lord Jesus, as this is the name by which he is commonly designated in the New Testament; see the notes on <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="ac+1:24">Acts 1:24</span>. If this be so, then this is a petition to the Lord Jesus as the fountain of all grace and goodness.</p>
