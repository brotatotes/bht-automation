<p style=" "><b>Unto Timothy - </b>For an account of Timothy, see Intro. Section 1.</p>
<p style=" "><b>My own son in the faith - </b>Converted to the Christian faith by my instrumentality, and regarded by me with the affection of a father; see notes, <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="1co+4:15">1 Corinthians 4:15</span>. Paul had no children of his own, and he adopted Timothy as a son, and uniformly regarded and treated him as such. He had the same feeling also toward Titus; <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="tit+1:4">Titus 1:4</span>; compare <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="ga+4:19">Galatians 4:19</span> note; <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="1th+2:7">1 Thessalonians 2:7</span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="1th+2:11">1 Thessalonians 2:11</span> notes; and <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="phm+1:10">Philemon 1:10</span> note.</p>
<p style=" "><b>Grace, mercy, and peace, ... - </b>See the notes, <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="ro+1:7">Romans 1:7</span>.</p>
