<p style=" "><b>God that comforteth those that are cast down - </b>Whose characteristic is, that be gives consolation to those who are anxious and depressed. All his consolation was in God; and by whatever instrumentality comfort was administered, he regarded and acknowledged God as the author; see the note, <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="2co+1:4">2 Corinthians 1:4</span>.</p>
<p style=" "><b>By the coming of Titus - </b>To Macedonia. He rejoiced not only in again seeing him, but especially in the intelligence which he brought respecting the success of his Epistle, and the conduct of the church at Corinth.</p>
