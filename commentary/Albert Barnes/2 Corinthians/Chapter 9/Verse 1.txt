<p style=" "><b>For as touching the ministering to the saints - </b>In regard to the collection that was to be taken up for the aid of the poor Christians in Judea; see the notes on <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="ro+15:26">Romans 15:26</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="1co+16:1">1 Corinthians 16:1</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="2co+8:0">2 Corinthians 8:0</span>.</p>
<p style=" "><b>It is superfluous ... - </b>It is needless to urge that matter on you, because I know that you acknowledge the obligation to do it, and have already purposed it.</p>
<p style=" "><b>For me to write to you - </b>That is, to write more, or to write largely on the subject. It is unnecessary for me to urge arguments why it should be done; and all that is proper is to offer some suggestions in regard to the manner in which it shall be accomplished.</p>
