<p style=" "><b>Yet have I sent the brethren - </b>The brethren referred to in <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="2co+8:18">2 Corinthians 8:18</span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="2co+8:22-23">2 Corinthians 8:22-23</span>.</p>
<p style=" "><b>Lest our boasting of you - </b>That you were disposed to contribute, and that you were already prepared, and that the contribution was ready.</p>
<p style=" "><b>Should be in vain - </b>Lest anything should have occurred to prevent the collection. I have sent them that they may facilitate it, and that it may be secure and certain.</p>
<p style=" "><b>In this behalf - </b>In this respect. That is, lest our boasting of you, in regard to your readiness to contribute to relieve the needs of others, should be found to have been ill-grounded.</p>
