<p style=" "><b>And this is love, that we walk after his commandments - </b>This is the proper expression or evidence of love to God. See the notes at <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="joh+14:15">John 14:15</span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="joh+14:21">John 14:21</span>.</p>
<p style=" "><b>This is the commandment - </b>That is, this is his great and special commandment; the one by which his disciples are to be especially characterized, and by which they are to be distinguished in the world. See the notes at <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="joh+13:34">John 13:34</span>.</p>
