<p style=" "><b>And to godliness brotherly kindness - </b>Love to Christians as such. See the <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="joh+13:34">John 13:34</span> note; <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="heb+13:1">Hebrews 13:1</span> note.</p>
<p style=" "><b>And to brotherly kindness charity - </b>Love to all mankind. There is to be a special affection for Christians as of the same family; there is to be a true and warm love, however, for all the race. See the notes at <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="1co+13:0">1 Corinthians 13:0</span>.</p>
