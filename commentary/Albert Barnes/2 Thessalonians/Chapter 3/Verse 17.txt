<p style=" "><b>The salutation of Paul with mine own hand; - </b>See the notes, <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="1co+16:21">1 Corinthians 16:21</span>. “Which is the token in every epistle.” Greek: “sign.” That is, this signature is a sign or proof of the genuineness of the epistle; compare the notes on <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="ga+6:11">Galatians 6:11</span>.</p>
<p style=" "><b>So I write - </b>Referring, probably, to some mark or method which Paul had of signing his name, which was well known, and which would easily be recognized by them.</p>
