<p style=" "><b>Thou therefore - </b>In view of the fact stated in the previous chapter, that many had turned away from the apostle, and had forsaken the paths of truth.</p>
<p style=" "><b>Be strong in the grace which is in Christ Jesus; - </b>compare the notes at <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="eph+6:10">Ephesians 6:10</span>. The meaning is, Be strong, relying on the grace which the Lord Jesus only can impart.</p>
