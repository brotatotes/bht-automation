<p style=" "><b>It is a faithful saying - </b>Or, rather, that which he was about to say was worthy of entire credence and profound attention; see the notes at <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="1ti+1:15">1 Timothy 1:15</span>. The object is to encourage Timothy to bear trials by the hope of salvation.</p>
<p style=" "><b>For if we be dead with him - </b>see the notes at <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="ro+6:8">Romans 6:8</span>.</p>
<p style=" "><b>We shall also live with him - </b>This was a sort of maxim, or a settled point, which is often referred to in the Bible; see the <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="ro+6:3-5">Romans 6:3-5</span> notes; <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="joh+11:25">John 11:25</span> note; <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="1th+4:14">1 Thessalonians 4:14</span> note.</p>
