<p style=" "><b>If we suffer, we shall also reign with him - </b>The meaning is, that the members will be treated as the Head is. We become united with him by faith, and, if we share his treatment on earth, we shall share his triumphs in heaven; see the notes at <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="ro+8:17">Romans 8:17</span>.</p>
<p style=" "><b>If we deny him, he also will deny us; - </b>see the notes at <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="mt+10:32-33">Matthew 10:32-33</span>.</p>
