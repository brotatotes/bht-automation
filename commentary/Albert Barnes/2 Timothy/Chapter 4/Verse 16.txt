On the meaning of this word, see the notes on <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="ro+4:3">Romans 4:3</span>, and <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="phm+1:18">Philemon 1:18</span>. The prayer of the apostle here breathes the very spirit of Christ; see the notes on <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="lu+23:34">Luke 23:34</span>; compare <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="ac+7:60">Acts 7:60</span>.</p>
