<p style=" "><b>Salute Prisca and Aquila - </b>Prisca, or Priscilla, was the wife of Aquila, though her name is sometimes mentioned first. In regard to their history, see the notes at <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="ro+16:3">Romans 16:3</span>. They were at Rome when Paul wrote his Epistle to the Romans, but afterward went into Asia Minor, which was the native place of Aquila <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="ac+18:2">Acts 18:2</span>, and where they probably died.</p>
