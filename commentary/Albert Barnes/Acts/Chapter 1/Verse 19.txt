<p style=" "><b>It was known ... - </b>, <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="mt+27:8">Matthew 27:8</span>. The scene in the temple; the acts of the priests in purchasing the field, etc., would make it known; and the name of the field would preserve the memory of the guilt of Judas.</p>
<p style=" "><b>Their proper tongue - </b>The language spoken by the Jews the Syro-Chaldaic.</p>
<p style=" "><b>Aceldama - </b>This is composed of two Syro-Chaldaic words, and means literally, the field of blood.</p>
