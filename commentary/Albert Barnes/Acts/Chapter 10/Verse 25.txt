<p style=" "><b>Fell down at his feet - </b>This was an act of profound regard for him as an ambassador of God. In Oriential countries it was usual for persons to prostrate themselves at length on the ground before men of rank and honor. “Worshipped him” This does not mean religious homage, but civil respect - the homage, or profound regard which was due to one in honor. See the notes on <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="mt+2:2">Matthew 2:2</span>.</p>
