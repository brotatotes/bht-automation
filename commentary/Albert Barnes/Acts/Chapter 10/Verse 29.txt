<p style=" "><b>Without gainsaying - </b>without “saying anything against it”; without hesitation or reluctance.</p>
<p style=" "><b>I ask, therefore ... - </b>The main design for which Cornelius had sent for him had been mentioned to Peter by the messenger, <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="ac+10:22">Acts 10:22</span>. But Peter now desired from his own lips a more particular statement of the considerations which had induced him to send for him.</p>
<p style=" "><b>For what intent - </b>For what purpose or design.</p>
