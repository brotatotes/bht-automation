<p style=" "><b>That through his name ... - </b>This was implied in what the prophets said. See <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="ro+10:11">Romans 10:11</span>. It was not, indeed, expressly affirmed that they who believed in him should be pardoned, but this was implied in what they said. They promised a Messiah, and their religion consisted mainly in believing in a Messiah to come.
