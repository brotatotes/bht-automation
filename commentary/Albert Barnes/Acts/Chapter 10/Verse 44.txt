<p style=" "><b>The Holy Ghost fell ... - </b>Endowing them with the power of speaking with other tongues, <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="ac+10:46">Acts 10:46</span>. Of this the apostle Peter makes much in his argument in <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="ac+11:17">Acts 11:17</span>. By this, God showed that the Gentiles were to be admitted to the same privileges with the Jews, and to the blessings of salvation in the same manner. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="ac+2:1-4">Acts 2:1-4</span>.</p>
<p style=" "><b>Which heard the word - </b>The Word of God; the message of the gospel.</p>
