<p style=" "><b>And all thy house - </b>Thy family. This is a circumstance which his omitted in the account in <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="ac+10:0">Acts 10:0</span>: It is said, however, in <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="ac+10:2">Acts 10:2</span>, that Cornelius feared God with all his house. It is evident from <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="ac+10:48">Acts 10:48</span> that the family also received the ordinance of baptism, and was received into the church.</p>
