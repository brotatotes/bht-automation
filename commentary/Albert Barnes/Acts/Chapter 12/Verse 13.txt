<p style=" "><b>At the door of the gate - </b>Rather the door of the vestibule, or principal entrance into the house. The house was entered through such a porch or vestibule, and it was the door opening into this which is here intended. See the notes on <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="mt+9:2">Matthew 9:2</span>.</p>
<p style=" "><b>A damsel - </b>A girl.</p>
<p style=" "><b>Came to hearken - </b>To hear who was there.</p>
<p style=" "><b>Named Rhoda - </b>This is a Greek name signifying a rose. It was not unusual for the Hebrews to give the names of flowers, etc., to their daughters. Thus, Susanna, a lily; Hadessa, a myrtle; Tamar, a palm-tree, etc. (Grotius).</p>
