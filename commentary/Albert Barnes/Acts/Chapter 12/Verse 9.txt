<p style=" "><b>And wist not - </b>Knew not.</p>
<p style=" "><b>That it was true - </b>That it was real.</p>
<p style=" "><b>But thought he saw a vision - </b>He supposed that it was a representation made to his mind similar to what he had seen before. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="ac+10:11-12">Acts 10:11-12</span>. It was so astonishing, so unexpected, so wonderful, that he could not realize that it was true.</p>
