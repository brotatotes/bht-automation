<p style=" "><b>And as John fulfilled his course - </b>As he was engaged in completing his work. His ministry is called a course or race, that which was to be run, or completed.</p>
<p style=" "><b>He said ... - </b>These are not the precise words which the evangelists have recorded, but the sense is the same. See the <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="joh+1:20">John 1:20</span> note; <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="mt+3:11">Matthew 3:11</span> note.</p>
