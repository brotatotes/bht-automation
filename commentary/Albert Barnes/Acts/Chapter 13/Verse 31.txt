<p style=" "><b>And he was seen - </b>See the notes at the end of Matthew.</p>
<p style=" "><b>Many days - </b>Forty days, <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="ac+1:3">Acts 1:3</span>.</p>
<p style=" "><b>Of them which came up - </b>By the apostles particularly. He was seen by others; but they are especially mentioned as having been chosen for this object, to bear witness to him, and as having been particularly qualified for it.</p>
