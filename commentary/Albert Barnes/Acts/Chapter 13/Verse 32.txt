<p style=" "><b>And we - </b>We who are here present. Paul and Barnabas.</p>
<p style=" "><b>Declare unto you glad tidings - </b>We preach the gospel the good news. To a Jew, nothing could be more grateful intelligence than that the Messiah had come; to a sinner convinced of his sins nothing can be more cheering than to hear of a Saviour.</p>
<p style=" "><b>The promise ... - </b>The promise here refers to all that had been spoken in the Old Testament respecting the advent, sufferings, death, and resurrection of Christ.</p>
