<p style=" "><b>But he, whom God raised again - </b>The Lord Jesus.</p>
<p style=" "><b>Saw no corruption - </b>Was raised without undergoing the usual change that succeeds death. As David had returned to corruption, and the Lord Jesus had not, it followed that this passage in <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="ps+16:1-11">Psalms 16:1-11</span> referred to the Messiah.</p>
