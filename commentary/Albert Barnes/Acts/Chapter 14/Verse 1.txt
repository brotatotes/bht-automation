<p style=" "><b>In Iconium - </b>See the notes on <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="ac+13:51">Acts 13:51</span>. In this place, and in Antioch and Lystra, Timothy became acquainted with Paul and his manner of life, <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="2ti+3:10-11">2 Timothy 3:10-11</span>.</p>

<p style=" "><b>And also of the Greeks - </b>Probably proselytes from the Greeks, who were in the habit of attending the synagogue.</p>
