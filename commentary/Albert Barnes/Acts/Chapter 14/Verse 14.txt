<p style=" "><b>Which, when the apostles - </b>Barnabas is called an apostle because he was sent forth by the church on a particular message (<span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="ac+13:3">Acts 13:3</span>; compare <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="ac+14:26">Acts 14:26</span>), not because he had been chosen to the special work of the apostleship - to Dear witness to the life and resurrection of Christ. See the notes on <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="ac+1:22">Acts 1:22</span>.</p>
<p style=" "><b>They rent their clothes - </b>As an expression of their abhorrence of what the people were doing, and of their deep grief that they should thus debase themselves by offering worship to human beings. See the notes on <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="mt+26:65">Matthew 26:65</span>.</p>
