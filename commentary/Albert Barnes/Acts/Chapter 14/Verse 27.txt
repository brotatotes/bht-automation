<p style=" "><b>They rehearsed ... - </b><span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="ac+11:4">Acts 11:4</span>. They related what had happened; their dangers and their success. This they did because they had been sent out by the church, and it was proper that they should give an account of their work; and because it furnished a suitable occasion of gratitude to God for his mercy.</p>
<p style=" "><b>All that God had done ... - </b>In protecting, guarding them, etc. All was traced to God.</p>
<p style=" "><b>Had opened the door of faith - </b>Had furnished an opportunity of preaching the gospel to the Gentiles, <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="1co+16:9">1 Corinthians 16:9</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="2co+2:12">2 Corinthians 2:12</span>.</p>
