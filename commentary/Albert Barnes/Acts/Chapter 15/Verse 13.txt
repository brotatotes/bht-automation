<p style=" "><b>James answered - </b>James the Less, son of Alpheus. See the notes on <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="ac+12:1">Acts 12:1</span>.</p>
<p style=" "><b>Hearken unto me - </b>This whole transaction shows that Peter had no such authority in the church as the papists pretend, for otherwise his opinion would have been followed without debate. James had an authority not less than that of Peter. It is possible that he might have been next in age (compare <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="1co+15:7">1 Corinthians 15:7</span>); and it seems morally certain that he remained for a considerable part of his life in Jerusalem, <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="ac+12:17">Acts 12:17</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="ac+21:18">Acts 21:18</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="ga+1:19">Galatians 1:19</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="ga+2:9">Galatians 2:9</span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="ga+2:12">Galatians 2:12</span>.</p>
