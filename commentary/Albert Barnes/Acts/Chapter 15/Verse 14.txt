<p style=" "><b>Simeon - </b>This is a Hebrew name. The Greek mode of writing it commonly was Simon. It was one of the names of Peter, <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="mt+4:18">Matthew 4:18</span>.</p>
<p style=" "><b>To take out of them a people - </b>To choose from among the Gentries those who should be his friends.</p>
