<p style=" "><b>My sentence - </b>Greek: I judge <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κρίνω</span></span> krinō that is, I give my opinion. It is the usual language in which a judge delivers his opinion; but it does not imply here that James assumed authority to settle the case, but merely that he gave his opinion, or counsel.</p>
<p style=" "><b>That we trouble not them - </b>That we do not molest, disturb, or oppress them by imposing on them unnecessary fires and ceremonies.</p>
