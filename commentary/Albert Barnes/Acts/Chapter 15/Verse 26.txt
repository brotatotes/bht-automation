<p style=" "><b>Men that have hazarded their lives ... - </b>See <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="ac+14:0">Acts 14:0</span>: This was a noble testimony to the character of Barnabas and Paul. It was a commendation of them to the confidence of the churches, and an implied expression that they wished their authority to be regarded in the establishment and organization of the church.</p>
<p style=" "><b>For the name - </b>In the cause of the Lord Jesus.</p>
