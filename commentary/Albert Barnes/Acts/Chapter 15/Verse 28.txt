<p style=" "><b>For it seemed good to the Holy Ghost - </b>This is a strong and undoubted claim to inspiration. It was with special reference to the organization of the church that the Holy Spirit had been promised to them by the Lord Jesus, <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="mt+18:18-20">Matthew 18:18-20</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="joh+14:26">John 14:26</span>.</p>
<p style=" "><b>No greater burden - </b>To impose no greater restraints to enjoin no other observances. See the notes on <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="ac+15:10">Acts 15:10</span>.</p>
<p style=" "><b>Than these necessary things - </b>Necessary:</p>
<p style=" ">(1) In order to preserve the peace of the church.</p>
<p style=" ">(2) To conciliate the minds of the Jewish converts, <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="ac+15:21">Acts 15:21</span>.</p>
<p style=" ">(3) In their circumstances particularly, because the crime which is specified - licentiousness was one to which all early converts were especially exposed. See the notes on <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="ac+15:20">Acts 15:20</span>.</p>
