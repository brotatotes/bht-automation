<p style=" "><b>Being prophets - </b>See the notes on <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="ac+11:27">Acts 11:27</span>. This evidently implies that they had been preachers before they went to Antioch. What was the precise nature of the office of a prophet in the Christian church it is not easy to ascertain. Possibly it may imply that they were teachers of unusual or remark able ability. Compare the notes on <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="ro+12:6">Romans 12:6</span>.</p>
<p style=" "><b>Confirmed them - </b>Strengthened them; that is, by their instructions and exhortations. See the notes on <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="ac+14:22">Acts 14:22</span>.</p>
