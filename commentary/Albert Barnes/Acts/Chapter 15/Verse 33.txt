<p style=" "><b>A space - </b>For some time.</p>
<p style=" "><b>They were let go in peace - </b>An expression implying that they departed with the affectionate regard of the Christians to whom they had ministered, and with their highest wishes for their prosperity, <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="1co+16:11">1 Corinthians 16:11</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="2jo+1:10">2 John 1:10</span>.</p>
<p style=" "><b>Unto the apostles - </b>At Jerusalem. Many mss., however, instead of “unto the apostles,” read “unto those who had sent them.” The sense is not materially different.</p>
