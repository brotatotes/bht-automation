<p style=" "><b>They were received of the church - </b>By the church, in a hospitable and friendly manner. They were acknowledged as Christian brethren, and received with Christian kindness. See <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="ga+2:9">Galatians 2:9</span>.</p>
<p style=" "><b>And they declared - </b>Paul and Barnabas, and those with them. That is, they stated the case; the remarkable conversion of the Gentiles, the evidence of their piety, and the origin of the present dispute.</p>
