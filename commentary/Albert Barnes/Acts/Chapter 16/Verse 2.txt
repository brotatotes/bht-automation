<p style=" "><b>Which - </b>That is, Timothy. The connection requires us to understand this of him. Of the character of his father nothing is known.</p>
<p style=" "><b>Was well reported of - </b>Was esteemed highly as a young man of piety and promise. Compare the notes on <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="ac+6:3">Acts 6:3</span>. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="1ti+5:10">1 Timothy 5:10</span>. Timothy had been religiously educated. He was carefully trained in the knowledge of the holy Scriptures, and was therefore the better qualified for his work, <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="2ti+3:15">2 Timothy 3:15</span>.</p>
