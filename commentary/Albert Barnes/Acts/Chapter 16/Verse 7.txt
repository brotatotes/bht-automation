<p style=" "><b>Mysia - </b>This was a province of Asia Minor, having Propontis on the north, Bithynia on the east, Lydia on the south, and the Aegean Sea on the west.</p>
<p style=" "><b>They assayed - </b>They endeavored; they attempted.</p>
<p style=" "><b>Into Bithynia - </b>A province of Asia Minor lying east of Mysia.</p>
