<p style=" "><b>And he continued ... - </b>Paul was not accustomed to remain long in a place. At Ephesus, indeed, he remained three years <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="ac+20:31">Acts 20:31</span>; and his stay at Corinth was caused by his success, and by the necessity of placing a church, collected out of such corrupt and dissolute materials, on a firm foundation.</p>
