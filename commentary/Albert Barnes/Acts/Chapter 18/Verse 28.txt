<p style=" "><b>For he mightily convinced the Jews - </b>He did it by strong arguments; he bore down all opposition, and effectually silenced them.</p>
<p style=" "><b>And that publicly - </b>In his public preaching in the synagogue and elsewhere.</p>
<p style=" "><b>Showing by the scriptures - </b>Proving from the Old Testament. Showing that Jesus of Nazareth corresponded with the account of the Messiah given by the prophets. See the notes on <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="joh+5:39">John 5:39</span>.</p>
<p style=" "><b>That Jesus was Christ - </b>See the margin. That Jesus of Nazareth was the Messiah.</p>
