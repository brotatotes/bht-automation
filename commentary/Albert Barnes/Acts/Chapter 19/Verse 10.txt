<p style=" "><b>This continued - </b>This public instruction.</p>
<p style=" "><b>By the space ... - </b>For two whole years.</p>
<p style=" "><b>So that all - </b>That is, the great mass of the people.</p>
<p style=" "><b>Which dwelt in Asia - </b>In that province of Asia Minor of which Ephesus was the principal city. The name Asia was used sometimes to denote that single province. See the notes on <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="ac+2:9">Acts 2:9</span>. Ephesus was the capital; and there was, of course, a constant and large influx of people there for the purposes of commerce and worship.</p>
<p style=" "><b>Heard the word of the Lord Jesus - </b>Heard the doctrine respecting the Lord Jesus.</p>
