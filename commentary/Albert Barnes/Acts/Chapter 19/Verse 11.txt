<p style=" "><b>Special miracles - </b>Miracles that were remarkable; that were not common, or that were very unusual (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">οὐ τὰς τυχών</span></span> ou tas tuchōn). This expression is Classical Greek. Thus, Longinus says of Moses that he was no common man - <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">οὐχ ̓ ὁ τύχων ἀνήρ</span></span> ouch ho tuchōn anēr.</p>
