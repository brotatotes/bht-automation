<p style=" "><b>After these things were ended - </b>After the gospel was firmly established at Ephesus, so that his presence there was no longer necessary.</p>
<p style=" "><b>Purposed in the spirit - </b>Resolved in his mind.</p>
<p style=" "><b>When he had passed through Macedonia and Achaia - </b>In these places he had founded flourishing churches. It is probable that his main object in this visit was to take up a collection for the poor saints at Jerusalem. See the notes on <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="ro+15:25-26">Romans 15:25-26</span>.</p>
<p style=" "><b>To go to Jerusalem - </b>To bear the contribution of the Gentile churches to the poor and oppressed Christians in Judea.</p>
<p style=" "><b>I must also see Rome - </b>See the notes on <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="ro+15:24">Romans 15:24</span>. He did go to Rome, but he went in chains, as a prisoner.</p>
