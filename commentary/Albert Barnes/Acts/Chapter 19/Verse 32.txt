<p style=" "><b>Some therefore cried one thing ... - </b>This is an admirable description of a mob, assembled for what purpose they knew not; but agitated by passions, and strifes, and tumults.</p>
<p style=" "><b>And the more part knew not ... - </b>The greater part did not know. They had been drawn together by the noise and excitement, and but a small part would know the real cause of the commotion. This is usually the case in tumultuous meetings.</p>
