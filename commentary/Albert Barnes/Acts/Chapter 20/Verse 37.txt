<p style=" "><b>Wept sore - </b>Wept much. Greek: “There was a great weeping of all.”</p>
<p style=" "><b>And fell on Paul’s neck - </b>Embraced him, as a token of tender affection. The same thing Joseph did when he met his aged father Jacob, <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="ge+46:29">Genesis 46:29</span>.</p>
<p style=" "><b>And kissed him - </b>This was the common token of affection.
