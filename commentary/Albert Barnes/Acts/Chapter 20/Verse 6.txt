<p style=" "><b>After the days of unleavened bread - </b>After the seven days of the Passover, during which they ate only unleavened bread. See <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="ex+12:0">Exodus 12:0</span>.</p>
<p style=" "><b>In five days - </b>They crossed the Aegean Sea. Paul, when he crossed it on a former occasion, did it in two days <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="ac+16:11-12">Acts 16:11-12</span>; but the navigation of the sea is uncertain, and they were now probably hindered by contrary winds.</p>
