<p style=" "><b>For thou shalt be his witness ... - </b>As an apostle - to testify to all people that the Messiah has come, that he has died, that he has risen, and that he is the Saviour of the world.</p>
<p style=" "><b>Of what thou hast seen and heard - </b>Of the remarkable proof which has been furnished you of the divine mission and character of the Lord Jesus.</p>
