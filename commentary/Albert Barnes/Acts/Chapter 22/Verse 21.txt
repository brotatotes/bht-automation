<p style=" "><b>And he said unto me, Depart - </b>Because the Christians at Jerusalem would not receive him.</p>
<p style=" "><b>Far hence - </b>Paul traveled far in the pagan nations. A large part of his ministry was spent in remote countries, and in the most distant regions then known. See <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="ro+15:19">Romans 15:19</span>.</p>
