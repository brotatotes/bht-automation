<p style=" "><b>Bound him with thongs - </b>With cords, preparatory to scourging.</p>
<p style=" "><b>Is it lawful ... - </b>It was directly contrary to the Roman law to bind and scourge a Roman citizen. See the notes on <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="ac+16:36-37">Acts 16:36-37</span>.</p>
