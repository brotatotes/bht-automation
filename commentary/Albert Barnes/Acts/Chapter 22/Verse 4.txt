<p style=" "><b>And I persecuted - </b><span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="ac+8:3">Acts 8:3</span>.</p>
<p style=" "><b>This way - </b>Those who were of this mode of worshipping God; that is, Christians. See the notes on <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="ac+9:2">Acts 9:2</span>.</p>
<p style=" "><b>Unto the death - </b>Intending to put them to death. He did not probably put any to death himself, but he committed them to prison; he sought their lives; he was the agent employed in arresting them; and when they were put to death, he tells us that he gave his voice against them <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="ac+26:10">Acts 26:10</span>; that is, he joined in, and approved of their condemnation.</p>
