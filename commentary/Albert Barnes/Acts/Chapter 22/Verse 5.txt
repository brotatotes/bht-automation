<p style=" "><b>As also the high priest ... - </b>See the notes on <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="ac+9:2">Acts 9:2</span>.</p>
<p style=" "><b>All the estate of the elders - </b>Greek: all the presbytery; that is, the whole body of the Sanhedrin, or Great Council of the nation.</p>
<p style=" "><b>Unto the brethren - </b>The Jewish brethren who were at Damascus. Paul here speaks as a Jew, and regards his countrymen as his brethren.</p>
