<p style=" "><b>Which had made this conspiracy - </b>This oath <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">συνωμοσίαν</span></span> sunōmosian, this agreement, or compact. This large number of desperate men, bound by so solemn an oath, would be likely to be successful, and the life of Paul was therefore in special danger. The manner in which they purposed to accomplish their design is stated in <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="ac+23:15">Acts 23:15</span>.</p>
