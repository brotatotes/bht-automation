<p style=" "><b>And he said ... - </b>In what way this young man had received intelligence of this, we can only conjecture. It is not improbable that he was a student under some one of the Jewish teachers, and that he might have learned it of him. It is not at all probable that the purpose of the 40 men would be very closely kept. Indeed, it is evident that they were not themselves very anxious about concealing their oath, as they mentioned it freely to the chief priests and elders, <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="ac+23:14">Acts 23:14</span>.</p>
