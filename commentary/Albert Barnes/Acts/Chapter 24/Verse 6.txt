<p style=" "><b>Who also hath gone about - </b>Who has endeavored.</p>
<p style=" "><b>To profane the temple - </b>This was a serious, but unfounded charge. It arose from the gross calumny of the Jews, when they pretended that he had introduced Greeks into that sacred place, <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="ac+21:28">Acts 21:28</span>. To this charge he replies in <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="ac+24:18">Acts 24:18</span>.</p>
<p style=" "><b>And would have judged - </b>That is, would have condemned and punished.</p>
<p style=" "><b>According to our law - </b>Their law, which forbade the introduction of strangers into the temple.</p>
