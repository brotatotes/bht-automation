<p style=" "><b>With great pomp - </b>Greek: “with much phantasy” <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">φαντασίας</span></span> phantasias; with much show, parade, and splendor. It was an occasion on which he could exhibit much of the splendor of royalty, and he chose to do it.</p>
<p style=" "><b>Into the place of hearing - </b>The court-room, or the place where the judges heard and tried causes.</p>
<p style=" "><b>With the chief captains - </b>Greek: the chiliarchs; the commanders of 1,000 men. It means here that the military officers were assembled. “The principal men of the city.” The civil officers, or the men of reputation and influence.</p>
