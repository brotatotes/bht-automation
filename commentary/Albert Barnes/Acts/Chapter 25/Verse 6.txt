<p style=" "><b>More than ten days - </b>See the margin. The Syriac reads it, “eight or ten.” The Vulgate, “not more than eight or ten.” The Coptic, “eight or ten.” Griesbach supposes this to be the true reading, and has admitted it into the text.</p>
<p style=" "><b>Sitting in the judgment seat - </b>On the tribunal; or holding a court for the trial of Paul.</p>
<p style=" "><b>Commanded Paul to be brought - </b>To be brought up for trial. He had been secured, but was placed in the care of a soldier, who was commanded to let him have all the freedom that was consistent with his security.</p>
