<p style=" "><b>My manner of life - </b>My opinions, principles, and conduct.</p>
<p style=" "><b>From my youth - </b>Paul was born in Tarsus; but at an early period he had been sent to Jerusalem for the purpose of education in the school of Gamaliel, <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="ac+22:3">Acts 22:3</span>.</p>
<p style=" "><b>Which was at the first - </b>Which was from the beginning; the early part of which; the time when the opinions and habits are formed.</p>
<p style=" "><b>Know all the Jews - </b>It is not at all improbable that Paul was distinguished in the school of Gamaliel for zeal in the Jewish religion. The fact that he was early entrusted with a commission against the Christians <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="ac+9:0">Acts 9:0</span> shows that he was known. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="php+3:4-6">Philippians 3:4-6</span>. He might appeal to them, therefore, in regard to the early part of his life, and, doubtless, to the very men who had been his violent accusers.</p>
