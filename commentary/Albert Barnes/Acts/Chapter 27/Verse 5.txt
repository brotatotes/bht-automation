<p style=" "><b>The sea of Cilicia and Pamphylia - </b>The sea which lies off the, coast from these two regions. For their situation, see the notes on <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="ac+6:9">Acts 6:9</span>, and <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="ac+13:13">Acts 13:13</span>.</p>
<p style=" "><b>We came to Myra, a city of Lycia - </b>Lycia was a province in the southwestern part of Asia Minor, having Phrygia and Pisidia on the north, the Mediterranean on the south, Pamphylia on the east, and Carla on the west.</p>
