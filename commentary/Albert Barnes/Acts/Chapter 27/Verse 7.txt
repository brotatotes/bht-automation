<p style=" "><b>Had sailed slowly - </b>By reason of the prevalence of the western winds, <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="ac+27:4">Acts 27:4</span>.</p>
<p style=" "><b>Over against Cnidus - </b>This was a city standing on a promontory of the same name in Asia Minor, in the part of the province of Caria called Doris, and a little northwest of the island of Rhodes.</p>
<p style=" "><b>The wind not suffering us - </b>The wind repelling us in that direction; not permitting us to hold on a direct course, we were driven off near to Crete.</p>
<p style=" "><b>We sailed under Crete - </b>See <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="ac+27:4">Acts 27:4</span>. We lay along near to Crete, so as to break the violence of the wind. For the situation of Crete, see the notes on <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="ac+2:11">Acts 2:11</span>.</p>
<p style=" "><b>Over against Salmone - </b>Near to Salmone. This was the name of the promontory which formed the eastern extremity of the island of Crete.</p>
