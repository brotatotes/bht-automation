<p style=" "><b>Who also honoured us - </b>As people who were favored by heaven, and who had been the means of conferring important benefits on them in healing the sick, etc. Probably the word “honors” here means “gifts, or marks of favor.”</p>
<p style=" "><b>They laded us - </b>They gave us, or conferred on us. They furnished us with such things as were necessary for us on our journey.</p>
