<p style=" "><b>What thou thinkest - </b>What your belief is; or what are the doctrines of Christians respecting the Messiah.</p>
<p style=" "><b>This sect - </b>The sect of Christians.</p>
<p style=" "><b>Spoken against - </b>Particularly by Jews. This was the case then, and, to a great extent, is the case still. It has been the common lot of the followers of Christ to be spoken “against.” Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="ac+24:5">Acts 24:5</span>.</p>
