<p style=" "><b>And had great reasoning - </b>Great discussion or debates. That is, the part which believed that Jesus was the Messiah <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="ac+28:24">Acts 28:24</span> discussed the subject warmly with those who did not believe. This whole verse is missing in the Syriac version, and in some Greek mss., and is supposed by Mill and Griesbach to be spurious.</p>
