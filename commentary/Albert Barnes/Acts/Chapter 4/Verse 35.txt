<p style=" "><b>And laid them down ... - </b>That is, they committed the money received for their property to the disposal of the apostles, to distribute it as was necessary among the poor. This soon became a burdensome and inconvenient office, and they therefore appointed men who had special charge of it, <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="ac+6:1-2">Acts 6:1-2</span>, etc.</p>
