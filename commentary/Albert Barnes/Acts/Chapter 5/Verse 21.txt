<p style=" "><b>Early in the morning - </b>Greek: at the break of day. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="lu+24:1">Luke 24:1</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="joh+8:2">John 8:2</span>.</p>
<p style=" "><b>Called the council together - </b>The Sanhedrin, or the Great Council of the nation. This was clearly for the purpose of “trying” the apostles for disregarding their commandments.</p>
<p style=" "><b>And all the senate - </b>Greek: “eldership.” Probably these were not a part of the Sanhedrin, but were people of age and experience, who in <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="ac+4:8">Acts 4:8</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="ac+25:15">Acts 25:15</span>, are called “elders of the Jews,” and who were present for the sake of counsel Canal advice in a case of emergency.</p>
