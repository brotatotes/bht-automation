<p style=" "><b>The captain of the temple - </b>See the notes on <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="ac+4:1">Acts 4:1</span>.</p>
<p style=" "><b>Doubted of them - </b>They were in “perplexity” about these things. The word rendered “doubted” denotes “that state of anxiety which arises when a person has lost his way, or when he does not know what to do to escape from a difficulty.” See <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="lu+9:7">Luke 9:7</span>.</p>
<p style=" "><b>Whereunto this would grow - </b>What this “would be”; or, what would be the result or end of these events. For:</p>
<p style="margin-left: 2.0em;  "> (3)The doctrines of the apostles were gaining ground.</p>
<p style="margin-left: 2.0em;  "> (4)Their efforts to resist them had been in vain. They need “not” have doubted; but sinners are not disposed to be convinced of the truth of religion.</p>
