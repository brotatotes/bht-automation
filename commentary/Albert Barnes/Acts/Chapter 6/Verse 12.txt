<p style=" "><b>And they stirred up the people - </b>They “excited” the people, or alarmed their fears, as had been done before when they sought to put the Lord Jesus to death, <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="mt+27:20">Matthew 27:20</span>.</p>
<p style=" "><b>The elders - </b>The members of the Sanhedrin, or Great Council.</p>
<p style=" "><b>Scribes - </b>See the notes on <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="mt+2:4">Matthew 2:4</span>.</p>
<p style=" "><b>To the council - </b>To the Sanhedrin, or the Great Council of the nation, which claimed jurisdiction in the matters of religion. See the notes on <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="mt+2:4">Matthew 2:4</span>.</p>
