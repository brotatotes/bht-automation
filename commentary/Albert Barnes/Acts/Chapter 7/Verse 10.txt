<p style=" "><b>And delivered him ... - </b>That is, restored him to liberty from his servitude and humiliation, and raised him up to high honors and offices in Egypt.</p>
<p style=" "><b>Favour and wisdom - </b>The favor was the result of his wisdom. His wisdom was particularly evinced in interpreting the dreams of Pharaoh, <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="ge+41:0">Genesis 41:0</span>.</p>
<p style=" "><b>And he made him governor ... - </b><span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="ge+41:40">Genesis 41:40</span>.</p>
<p style=" "><b>All his house - </b>All the family, or all the court and government of the nation.</p>
