<p style=" "><b>I have seen ... - </b>The repetition of this word is in accordance with the usage of the Hebrew writers when they wish to represent anything emphatically.</p>
<p style=" "><b>Their groaning - </b>Under their oppressions.</p>
<p style=" "><b>Am come down - </b>This is spoken in accordance with human conceptions. It means that God was about to deliver them.</p>
<p style=" "><b>I will send thee ... - </b>This is a mere summary of what is expressed at much greater length in <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="ex+3:7-10">Exodus 3:7-10</span>.</p>
