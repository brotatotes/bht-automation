<p style=" "><b>And they made a calf - </b>This was made of the ear-rings and ornaments which they had brought from Egypt, <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="ex+32:2-4">Exodus 32:2-4</span>. Stephen introduces this to remind them how prone the nation had been to reject God, and to walk in the ways of sin.</p>
