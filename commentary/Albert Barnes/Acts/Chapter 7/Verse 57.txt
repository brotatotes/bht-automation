<p style=" "><b>Then they cried out - </b>That is, probably, “the people,” not the members of the council It is evident he was put to death in a popular tumult. They had charged him with blasphemy; and they regarded what he had now said as full proof of it.</p>
<p style=" "><b>And stopped their ears - </b>That they might hear no more blasphemy.</p>
<p style=" "><b>With one accord - </b>In a tumult; unitedly.</p>
