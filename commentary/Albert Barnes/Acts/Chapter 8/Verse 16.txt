<p style=" "><b>He was fallen - </b>This expression is several times applied to the Holy Spirit, <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="ac+10:44">Acts 10:44</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="ac+11:15">Acts 11:15</span>. It does not differ materially from the common expression, “The Holy Spirit descended.” It means that he came from heaven; and the expression “to fall,” applied to his influences, denotes the “rapidity” and “suddenness” of his coming. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="ac+19:2">Acts 19:2</span>.</p>

See also <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="ac+10:48">Acts 10:48</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="ac+19:5-6">Acts 19:5-6</span>.</p>
