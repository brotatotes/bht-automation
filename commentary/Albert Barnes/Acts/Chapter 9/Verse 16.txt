<p style=" "><b>For I will show him ... - </b>This seems to be added to encourage Ananias. He had feared Saul. The Lord now informs him that Saul, hitherto his enemy, would ever after be his friend. He would not merely profess repentance, but would manifest the sincerity of it by encountering trials and reproaches for his sake. The prediction here was fully accomplished, <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="ac+20:23">Acts 20:23</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="2co+11:23-27">2 Corinthians 11:23-27</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="2ti+1:11-12">2 Timothy 1:11-12</span>.</p>
