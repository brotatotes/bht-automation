<p style=" "><b>Whom, when they had washed - </b>Among most people it has been customary to wash the body before it is buried or burned. They prepared her in the usual manner for interment.</p>
<p style=" "><b>In an upper chamber - </b>See the notes on <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="ac+1:13">Acts 1:13</span>. There is no evidence that they expected that Peter would raise her up to life.</p>
