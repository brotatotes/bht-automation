<p style=" "><b>And many believed ... - </b>A similar effect followed when Jesus raised up Lazarus. See <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="joh+12:11">John 12:11</span>.</p>
<p style=" ">This was the first miracle of this kind that was performed by the apostles. The effect was that many believed. It was not merely a work of benevolence, in restoring to life one who contributed largely to the comfort of the poor, but it was a means of extending and establishing, as it was designed doubtless to do, the kingdom of the Saviour.</p>
