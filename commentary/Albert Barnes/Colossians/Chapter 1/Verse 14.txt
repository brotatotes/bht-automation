<p style=" "><b>In whom we have redemption; - </b>See this explained in the notes at <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="eph+1:7">Ephesians 1:7</span>. The passage here proves that we obtain forgiveness of sins through the blood of Christ; but it does not prove that this is all that we obtain through that blood.</p>
