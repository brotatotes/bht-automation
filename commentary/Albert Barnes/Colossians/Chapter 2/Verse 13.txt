<p style=" "><b>And you, being dead in your sins - </b>Notes, <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="eph+2:1">Ephesians 2:1</span>.</p>
<p style=" "><b>And the uncircumcision of your flesh - </b>That is, Gentiles, and giving unrestrained indulgence to the desires of the flesh. They lived as those who had not by any religious rite or covenant brought themselves under obligations to lead holy lives.</p>
<p style=" "><b>Hath he quickened - </b>Notes, <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="eph+2:1">Ephesians 2:1</span>.</p>
<p style=" "><b>Together with him - </b>In virtue of his being restored to life. That is, the resurrection of the Lord Jesus was the means of imparting to us spiritual life.</p>
