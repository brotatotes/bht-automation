<p style=" "><b>But now ye also put off all these - </b>All these which follow, as being also inconsistent with the Christian calling.</p>
<p style=" "><b>Anger, wrath - </b>Notes, <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="eph+4:26">Ephesians 4:26</span>.</p>
<p style=" "><b>Malice - </b>Notes, <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="eph+4:31">Ephesians 4:31</span>.</p>
<p style=" "><b>Blasphemy - </b>Notes, <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="mt+9:3">Matthew 9:3</span>. The word here seems to mean all injurious and calumnious speaking - whether against God or man.</p>
<p style=" "><b>Filthy communication out of your mouth - </b>Lewd, indecent, and immodest discourse; Notes, <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="eph+4:29">Ephesians 4:29</span>. The conversation of the pagan everywhere abounds with this. A pure method of conversation among men is the fruit of Christianity.</p>
