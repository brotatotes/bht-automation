<p style=" "><b>Continue in prayer - </b>That is, do not neglect it; observe it at all stated times; maintain the spirit of prayer, and embrace all proper occasions to engage in it; compare the <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="lu+18:1">Luke 18:1</span> note; <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="eph+6:18">Ephesians 6:18</span> note; <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="1th+4:17">1 Thessalonians 4:17</span> note.</p>
<p style=" "><b>And watch in the same with thanksgiving - </b>Watch for favorable opportunities; watch that your mind may be in a right frame when you pray: and watch, that when your mind is in a right frame you may not neglect to pray; see the <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="eph+6:18">Ephesians 6:18</span> note; <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="php+4:6">Philippians 4:6</span>.</p>
