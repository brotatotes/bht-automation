<p style=" "><b>And came and preached peace - </b>That is, the system of religion which he proclaimed, was adapted to produce peace with God. This he preached personally to those who “were nigh,” that is, the Jews; to those who were “afar off “ - the Gentiles - he preached it by his apostles. He was the author of the system which proclaimed salvation to both.</p>
<p style=" ">The word “peace” here refers to reconciliation with God.</p>
