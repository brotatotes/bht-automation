<p style=" "><b>He that descended is the same also that ascended - </b>The same Redeemer came down from God, and returned to him. It was not a different being, but the same.</p>
<p style=" "><b>Far above all heavens - </b>see the notes on <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="eph+1:20-23">Ephesians 1:20-23</span>; compare <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="heb+7:26">Hebrews 7:26</span>. He is gone above the visible heavens, and has ascended into the highest abodes of bliss; see the notes on <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="2co+12:2">2 Corinthians 12:2</span>.</p>
<p style=" "><b>That he might fill all things - </b>Margin, “fulfil.” The meaning is, “that he might fill all things by his influence, and direct and overrule all by his wisdom and power.” Doddridge. See the notes on <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="eph+1:23">Ephesians 1:23</span>.</p>
