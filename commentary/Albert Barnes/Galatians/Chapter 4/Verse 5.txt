<p style=" "><b>To redeem them - </b>By his death as an atoning sacrifice; see the note at <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="ga+3:13">Galatians 3:13</span>.</p>
<p style=" "><b>Them that were under the law - </b>Sinners, who had violated the Law, and who were exposed to its dread penalty.</p>
<p style=" "><b>That we might receive the adoption of sons - </b>Be adopted as the sons or the children of God; see <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="joh+1:12">John 1:12</span>, note; <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="ro+8:15">Romans 8:15</span>, note.</p>
