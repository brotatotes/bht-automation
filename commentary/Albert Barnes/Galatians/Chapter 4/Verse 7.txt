<p style=" "><b>But a son - </b>A child of God, adopted into his family, and to be treated as a son.</p>
<p style=" "><b>And if a son ... - </b>Entitled to all the privileges of a son, and of course to be regarded as an heir through the Redeemer, and with him. See the sentiment here expressed explained in the the note at <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="ro+8:17">Romans 8:17</span>.</p>
