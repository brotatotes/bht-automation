<p style=" "><b>With the affections - </b>Margin, “Passions.” All corrupt desires.</p>
<p style=" "><b>And lusts - </b>See the note at <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="ro+1:24">Romans 1:24</span>.</p>
