<p style=" "><b>That ye be not slothful - </b>Indolent; inactive. This was what he was especially desirous of guarding them against. By diligent and strenuous effort only could they secure themselves from the danger of apostasy.</p>
<p style=" "><b>But followers - </b>Imitators - that you may live as they lived.</p>
<p style=" "><b>Of them who through faith and patience - </b>By faith, or confidence in God, and by patience in suffering - referring to those who in times of trial had remained faithful to God, and had been admitted to heaven. In <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="heb+11:0">Hebrews 11:0</span>, the apostle has given a long list of such persevering and faithful friends of God; see the notes on that chapter.</p>
<p style=" "><b>The promise - </b>The promise of heaven.</p>
