<p style=" "><b>Saying, Surely blessing I will bless thee - </b>That is, I will certainly bless thee. The phrase is a Hebrew mode of expression, to denote emphasis or certainty - indicated by the repetition of a word; compare <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="ge+14:23">Genesis 14:23</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="ex+8:10">Exodus 8:10</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="joe+3:14">Joel 3:14</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="jdg+5:30">Judges 5:30</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="jdg+15:16">Judges 15:16</span>.</p>
<p style=" "><b>Multiplying I will multiply thee - </b>I will greatly increase thee - I will grant thee an exceedingly numerous posterity.</p>
