<p style=" "><b>For then must he often have suffered - </b>That is, if his blood had no more efficacy than what the Jewish high priest offered, and which was so often repeated, it would have been necessary that Christ should have often died.</p>
<p style=" "><b>But now once - </b>Once for all; once in the sense that it is not to be repeated again - <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ἅπαξ</span></span> hapax.</p>
<p style=" "><b>In the end of the world - </b>In the last dispensation or economy; that under which the affairs of the world will be wound up; see the phrase fully explained in <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="heb+1:2">Hebrews 1:2</span> note, and <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="ac+2:17">Acts 2:17</span> note; <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="1co+10:11">1 Corinthians 10:11</span>, and <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="isa+2:2">Isaiah 2:2</span>.</p>
<p style=" "><b>Hath he appeared - </b>He has been manifested in human form.</p>

<p style="margin-left: 2.0em;  "> (2)To remove the stain of sin from the soul; see the notes on <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="heb+9:14">Hebrews 9:14</span>.</p>
