<p style=" "><b>Doth a fountain send forth at the same place - </b>Margin, “hole.” The Greek word means “opening, fissure,” such as there is in the earth, or in rocks from which a fountain gushes.</p>
<p style=" "><b>Sweet water and bitter - </b>Fresh water and salt, <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="jas+3:12">James 3:12</span>. Such things do not occur in the works of nature, and they should not be found in man.</p>
