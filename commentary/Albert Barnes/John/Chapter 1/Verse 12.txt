<p style=" "><b>To as many as received him - </b>The great mass; the people; the scribes and Pharisees rejected him. A few in his lifetime received him, and many more after his death. “To receive him,” here, means to “believe” on him. This is expressed at the end of the verse.</p>
<p style=" "><b>Gave he power - </b>This is more appropriately rendered in the margin by the word “right” or “privilege.” Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="ac+1:7">Acts 1:7</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="ac+5:4">Acts 5:4</span>; <span class="_0000ff"><u>Rom 9:21</u></span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="1co+7:37">1 Corinthians 7:37</span>; <span class="_0000ff"><u>1Co 8:9</u></span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="1co+9:4-5">1 Corinthians 9:4-5</span>.</p>
