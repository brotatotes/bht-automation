<p style=" "><b>As the Father knoweth me ... - </b>See the <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="mt+11:27">Matthew 11:27</span> note; also <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="lu+10:22">Luke 10:22</span> note.</p>
