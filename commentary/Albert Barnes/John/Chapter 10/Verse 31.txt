<p style=" "><b>The Jews took up stones - </b>Stoning was the punishment of a blasphemer, <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="le+24:14-16">Leviticus 24:14-16</span>. They considered him guilty of blasphemy because he made himself equal with God, <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="joh+10:33">John 10:33</span>.</p>
<p style=" "><b>Again - </b>They had before plotted against his life <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="joh+5:16">John 5:16</span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="joh+5:18">John 5:18</span>, and once at least they had taken up stones to destroy him, <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="joh+8:59">John 8:59</span>.</p>
