<p style=" "><b>By me - </b>By my instruction and merits.</p>
<p style=" "><b>Shall be saved - </b>See <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="joh+5:24">John 5:24</span>.</p>
<p style=" "><b>Shall go in and out ... - </b>This is language applied, commonly to flocks. It meant that he shall be well supplied, and defended, and led “beside the still waters of salvation.”</p>
