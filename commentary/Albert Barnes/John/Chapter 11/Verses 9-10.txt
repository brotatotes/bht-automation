<p style=" "><b>Twelve hours - </b>The Jews divided the day from sunrise to sunset into twelve equal parts. A similar illustration our Saviour uses in <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="joh+9:4-5">John 9:4-5</span>. See the notes at that place.</p>
<p style=" "><b>If any man walk - </b>If any man travels. The illustration here is taken from a traveler. The conversation was respecting a journey into Judea, and our Lord, as was his custom, took the illustration from the case before him.</p>
<p style=" "><b>He stumbleth not - </b>He is able, having light, to make his journey safely. He sees the obstacles or dangers and can avoid them.</p>
<p style=" "><b>The light of this world - </b>The light by which the world is illuminated that is, the light of the sun.</p>
<p style=" "><b>In the night - </b>In darkness he is unable to see danger or obstacles, and to avoid them. His journey is unsafe and perilous, or, in other words, it is not a proper time to travel.</p>
