<p style=" "><b>The people - </b>A part of the people.</p>
<p style=" "><b>It thundered - </b>The unexpected sound of the voice would confound and amaze them; and though there is no reason to doubt that the words were spoken distinctly <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="mt+3:17">Matthew 3:17</span>, yet some of the people, either from amazement or envy, would suppose that this was a mere natural phenomenon.</p>
<p style=" "><b>An angel spake - </b>It was the opinion of many of the Jews that God did not speak to men except by the ministry of angels, <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="heb+2:2">Hebrews 2:2</span>; “The word spoken by angels;” <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="ga+3:19">Galatians 3:19</span>; “It was ordained by angels in the hand of a mediator.”</p>
