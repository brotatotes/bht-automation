<p style=" "><b>The chief rulers - </b>Members of the Sanhedrin - Nicodemus, Joseph, and others like them.</p>
<p style=" "><b>Because of the Pharisees - </b>The Pharisees were a majority of the council.</p>
<p style=" "><b>Did not confess him - </b>Did not openly avow their belief that he was the Messiah. Two of them, however, did afterward evince their attachment to him. These were Joseph and Nicodemus, <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="joh+19:38-39">John 19:38-39</span>. That Joseph was one of them appears from <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="mr+15:43">Mark 15:43</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="lu+23:50-51">Luke 23:50-51</span>.</p>
<p style=" "><b>Put out of the synagogue - </b>Excommunicated. See the notes at <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="joh+9:22">John 9:22</span>.</p>
