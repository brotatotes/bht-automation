<p style=" "><b>Ye call me Master - </b>Teacher.</p>
<p style=" "><b>And Lord - </b>This word is applied to one who rules, and is often given to God as being the Proprietor and Ruler of all things. It is given to Christ many hundred times in the New Testament.</p>
<p style=" "><b>Ye say well ... - </b><span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="mt+23:8">Matthew 23:8</span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="mt+23:10">Matthew 23:10</span>.</p>
<p style=" "><b>So I am - </b>That is, he was their Teacher and Instructor, and he was their Sovereign and King.</p>
