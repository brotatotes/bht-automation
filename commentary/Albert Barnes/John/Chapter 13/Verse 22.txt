<p style=" "><b>Doubting of whom ... - </b>The word translated “doubting” denotes that kind of anxiety which a man feels when he is in perplexity, and knows not what to say or do. We should say they were at a loss. See the notes at <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="mt+26:22">Matthew 26:22</span>.</p>
