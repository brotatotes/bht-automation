<p style=" "><b>Now is the Son of man glorified - </b>The last deed is done that was necessary to secure the death of the Son of man, the glory that shall result to him from that death, the wonderful success of the gospel, the exaltation of the Messiah, and the public and striking attestation of God to him in the view of the universe. See the notes at <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="joh+12:32">John 12:32</span>.</p>
