<p style=" "><b>No man at the table knew - </b>This shows that Jesus had signified to John only who it was that should betray him.</p>
<p style=" "><b>The bag - </b>The traveling-bag in which they put their common property. See the notes at <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="joh+12:6">John 12:6</span>.</p>
<p style=" "><b>Have need of against the feast - </b>The feast of the Passover. This feast continued seven days, and they supposed that Jesus had directed him to make preparation for their needs on those days.</p>
