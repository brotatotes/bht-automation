<p style=" "><b>This is my commandment - </b>The special law of Christianity, called hence “the new commandment.” See the notes at <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="joh+13:34">John 13:34</span>.</p>
<p style=" "><b>As I have loved you - </b>That is, with the same tender affection, willing to endure trials, to practice self-denials, and, if need be, to lay down your lives for each other, <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="1jo+3:16">1 John 3:16</span>.</p>
