<p style=" "><b>Ye also shall bear witness - </b>You shall be witnesses to the world to urge on them the evidences that the Lord Jesus is the Messiah.</p>
<p style=" "><b>Have been with me - </b>They had for more than three years seen his works, and were therefore qualified to bear witness of his character and doctrines.</p>
<p style=" "><b>From the beginning - </b>From his entrance on the public work of the ministry, <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="mt+4:17-22">Matthew 4:17-22</span>. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="ac+1:21-22">Acts 1:21-22</span>.</p>
