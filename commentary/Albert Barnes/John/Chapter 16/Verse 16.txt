A little while - His death would occur in a short time. It took place the next day. See John 14:19.Ye shall not see me - That is, he would be concealed from their view in the tomb.And again a little while - After three days he would rise again and appear to their view.Because I go ... - Because it is a part of the plan that I should ascend to God, it is necessary that I should rise from the grave, and then you will see me, and have evidence that I am still your Friend. Compare John 7:33. Here are three important events foretold for the consolation of the disciples; yet they were stated in such a manner that, in their circumstances and with their prejudices, it appeared difficult to understand him.
