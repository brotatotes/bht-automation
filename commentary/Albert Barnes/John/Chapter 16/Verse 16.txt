<p style=" "><b>A little while - </b>His death would occur in a short time. It took place the next day. See <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="joh+14:19">John 14:19</span>.</p>
<p style=" "><b>Ye shall not see me - </b>That is, he would be concealed from their view in the tomb.</p>
<p style=" "><b>And again a little while - </b>After three days he would rise again and appear to their view.</p>
<p style=" "><b>Because I go ... - </b>Because it is a part of the plan that I should ascend to God, it is necessary that I should rise from the grave, and then you will see me, and have evidence that I am still your Friend. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="joh+7:33">John 7:33</span>. Here are three important events foretold for the consolation of the disciples; yet they were stated in such a manner that, in their circumstances and with their prejudices, it appeared difficult to understand him.</p>
