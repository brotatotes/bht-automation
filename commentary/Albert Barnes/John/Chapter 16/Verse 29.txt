<p style=" "><b>Now speakest thou plainly - </b>What he had said that perplexed them was that which is contained in <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="joh+16:16">John 16:16</span>. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="joh+16:17-19">John 16:17-19</span>; “A little while and ye shall not see me,” etc. This he had now explained by saying <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="joh+16:28">John 16:28</span>, “Again, I leave the world, and go to the Father.” In this there was no ambiguity, and they expressed themselves satisfied with this explanation.</p>
