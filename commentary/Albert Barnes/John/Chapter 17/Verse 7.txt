<p style=" "><b>They have known - </b>They have been taught that and have believed it.</p>
<p style=" "><b>Hast given me - </b>This refers, doubtless, to the doctrine of Christ, <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="joh+17:8">John 17:8</span>. They are assured that all my instructions are of God.</p>
