<p style=" "><b>Saw that he was dead - </b>Saw by the indications of death on his person, and perhaps by the testimony of the centurion, <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="mt+27:54">Matthew 27:54</span>. The death of Jesus was doubtless hastened by the intense agony of the garden, and the special sufferings endured as an atonement for sin on the cross. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="mt+27:46">Matthew 27:46</span>.</p>
