<p style=" "><b>Though Jesus himself baptized not - </b>The reason why Jesus did not baptize was probably because, if He had baptized, it might have made unhappy divisions among his followers: those might have considered themselves most worthy or honored who had been baptized by Him. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="1co+1:17">1 Corinthians 1:17</span>.</p>
