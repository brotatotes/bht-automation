<p style=" "><b>That saying - </b>That proverb. This proverb is found in some of the Greek writers (Grotius). Similar proverbs were in use among the Jews. See <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="isa+65:21-22">Isaiah 65:21-22</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="le+26:16">Leviticus 26:16</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="mic+6:15">Micah 6:15</span>.</p>
<p style=" "><b>One soweth ... - </b>One man may preach the gospel, and with little apparent effect; another, succeeding him, may be crowned with eminent success. The seed, long buried, may spring up in an abundant harvest.</p>
