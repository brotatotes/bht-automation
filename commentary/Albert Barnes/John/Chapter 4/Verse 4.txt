<p style=" "><b>And he must needs go through Samaria - </b>Samaria was between Judea and Galilee. The direct and usual way was to pass through Samaria. Sometimes, however, the Jews took a circuitous route on the east side of the Jordan. See the notes at <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="mt+2:22">Matthew 2:22</span>.</p>
