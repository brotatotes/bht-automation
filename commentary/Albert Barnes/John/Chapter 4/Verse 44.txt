<p style=" "><b>For Jesus himself testified ... - </b>See the notes at <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="mt+13:57">Matthew 13:57</span>. The connection of this verse with the preceding may be thus explained: “Jesus went to Galilee, but not to Nazareth, for he testified,” etc. Or, “Jesus went to Galilee, ‘although’ he had said that a prophet had no honor in his own country; yet, because he foreknew that the Galileans would many of them believe on him, he went at this time.”</p>
