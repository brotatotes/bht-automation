<p style=" "><b>His word abiding in you - </b>His law does not abide in you - that is, you do not regard or obey it. This was the “third” thing that he charged them with.</p>
<p style=" "><b>For whom he hath sent - </b>God had foretold that the Messiah would come. He had now given evidence that Jesus was he; but now they rejected him, and this was proof that they did not regard the word of God.</p>
