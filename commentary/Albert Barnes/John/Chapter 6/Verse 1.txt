<p style=" "><b>Jesus went over - </b>Went to the east side of the sea. The place to which he went was Bethsaida, <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="lu+9:10">Luke 9:10</span>. The account of this miracle of feeding the five thousand is recorded also in <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="mt+14:13-21">Matthew 14:13-21</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="mr+6:32-44">Mark 6:32-44</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="lu+9:10-17">Luke 9:10-17</span>. John has added a few circumstances omitted by the other evangelists.</p>
