<p style=" "><b>Immediately - </b>Quickly. Before a long time. How far they were from the land we know not, but there is no evidence that there was a miracle in the case. The word translated “immediately” does not of necessity imply that there was no interval of time, but that there was not a long interval. Thus, in <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="mt+13:5">Matthew 13:5</span>, in the parable of the sower, “and immediately (the same word in Greek) they sprung up,” etc., <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="mr+4:17">Mark 4:17</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="mt+24:29">Matthew 24:29</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="3jo+1:14">3 John 1:14</span>.</p>
