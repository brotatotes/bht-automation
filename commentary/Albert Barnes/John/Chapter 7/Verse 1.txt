<p style=" "><b>After these things - </b>After the transactions which are recorded in the last chapters had taken place, and after the offence he had given the Jews. See <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="joh+5:18">John 5:18</span>.</p>
<p style=" "><b>Jesus walked - </b>Or Jesus lived, or taught. He traveled around Galilee teaching.</p>
<p style=" "><b>In Jewry - </b>In Judea, the southern division of Palestine. Compare the notes at <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="joh+4:3">John 4:3</span>.</p>
<p style=" "><b>The Jews sought - </b>That is, the rulers of the Jews. It does not appear that the common people ever attempted to take his life.</p>
