<p style=" "><b>One work - </b>The healing of the man on the Sabbath, <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="joh+5:0">John 5:0</span>.</p>
<p style=" "><b>Ye all marvel - </b>You all wonder or are amazed, and particularly that it was done on the Sabbath. This was the particular ground of astonishment, that he should dare to do what they esteemed a violation of the Sabbath.</p>
