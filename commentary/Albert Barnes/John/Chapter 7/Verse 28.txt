<p style=" "><b>Ye know whence I am - </b>You have sufficient evidence of my divine mission, and that I am the Messiah.</p>
<p style=" "><b>Is true - </b>Is worthy to be believed. He has given evidence that I came from him, and he is worthy to be believed. Many read this as a question - Do ye know me, and know whence I am? I have not come from myself, etc.</p>
