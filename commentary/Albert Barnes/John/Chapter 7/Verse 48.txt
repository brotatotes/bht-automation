<p style=" "><b>The rulers - </b>The members of the Sanhedrin, who were supposed to have control over the religious rites and doctrines of the nation.</p>
<p style=" "><b>The Pharisees - </b>The sect possessing wealth, and office, and power. The name Pharisees sometimes denotes those who were high in honor and authority.</p>
