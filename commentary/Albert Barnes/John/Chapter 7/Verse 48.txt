The rulers - The members of the Sanhedrin, who were supposed to have control over the religious rites and doctrines of the nation.The Pharisees - The sect possessing wealth, and office, and power. The name Pharisees sometimes denotes those who were high in honor and authority.Believed on him - Is there any instance in which those who are high in rank or in office have embraced him as the Messiah?
