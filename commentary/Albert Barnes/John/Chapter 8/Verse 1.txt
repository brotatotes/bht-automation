<p style=" "><b>Mount of Olives - </b>The mountain about a mile directly east of Jerusalem. See the notes at <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="mt+21:1">Matthew 21:1</span>. This was the place in which he probably often passed the night when attending the feasts at Jerusalem. The Garden of Gethsemane, to which he was accustomed to resort <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="joh+18:2">John 18:2</span>, was on the western side of that mountain, and Bethany, the abode of Martha and Mary, on its east side, <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="joh+11:1">John 11:1</span>.</p>
