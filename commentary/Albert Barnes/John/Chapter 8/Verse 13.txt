<p style=" "><b>Thou bearest record of thyself - </b>Thou art a witness for thyself, or in thy own case. See <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="joh+5:31">John 5:31</span>. The law required two witnesses in a criminal case, and they alleged that as the only evidence which Jesus had was his own assertion, it could not be entitled to belief.</p>
<p style=" "><b>Is not true - </b>Is not worthy of belief, or is not substantiated by sufficient evidence.</p>
