<p style=" "><b>Where is thy Father? - </b>This question was asked, doubtless, in derision. Jesus had often given them to understand that by his Father he meant God, <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="joh+5:6">John 5:6</span>; They professed to be ignorant of this, and probably looked round in contempt for his Father, that he might adduce him as a witness in the case.</p>
