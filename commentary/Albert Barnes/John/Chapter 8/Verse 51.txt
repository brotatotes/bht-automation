<p style=" "><b>If a man keep my saying - </b>If he believes on me and obeys my commandments.</p>
<p style=" "><b>He shall never see death - </b>To see death, or to taste of death, is the same as to die, <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="lu+2:26">Luke 2:26</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="mt+16:28">Matthew 16:28</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="mr+9:1">Mark 9:1</span>. The sense of this passage is, “He shall obtain eternal life, or he shall be raised up to that life where there shall be no death.” See <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="joh+6:49-50">John 6:49-50</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="joh+3:36">John 3:36</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="joh+5:24">John 5:24</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="joh+11:25-26">John 11:25-26</span>.</p>
