<p style=" "><b>If I honour myself - </b>If I commend or praise myself. If I had no other honor and sought no other honor than that which proceeds from a desire to glorify myself.</p>
<p style=" "><b>My honour is nothing - </b>My commendation or praise of myself would be of no value. See the notes at <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="joh+5:31">John 5:31</span>.</p>
