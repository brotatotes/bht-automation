<p style=" "><b>The people waited - </b>That is, beyond the usual time.</p>
<p style=" "><b>Marvelled - </b>Wondered. The priest, it is said, was not accustomed to remain in the temple more than half an hour commonly. Having remained on this occasion a longer time, the people became apprehensive of his safety, and wondered what had happened to him.</p>
