<p style=" "><b>And Mary arose - </b>The word “arose” here is equivalent to “setting out,” or starting on a journey.</p>
<p style=" "><b>The hill country - </b>The region in the vicinity of Jerusalem, commonly called the hill country of Judea.</p>
<p style=" "><b>City of Juda - </b>What city is meant is not known. Some have supposed it to be Jerusalem, others Hebron; but all is conjecture. It was probably a Levitical city, and the residence of Zechariah when he was not employed in the temple.</p>
