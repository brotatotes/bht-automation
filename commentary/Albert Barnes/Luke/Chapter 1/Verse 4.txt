<p style=" "><b>The certainty - </b>Have full evidence or proof of.</p>
<p style=" "><b>Been instructed - </b>By the preachers of the gospel. The original word is the one from which is derived our word “catechism - been catechized;” but it does not denote here the “manner” in which the instruction was imparted, as it does with us, but simply the fact that he had been taught those things.</p>
