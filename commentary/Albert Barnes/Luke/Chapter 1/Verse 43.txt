<p style=" "><b>And whence is this to me? - </b>An expression of humility. Why is it that the mother of my Lord should come to me, as if to honor me?</p>
<p style=" "><b>Mother of my Lord - </b>The word “Lord” sometimes denotes “divinity,” and sometimes superior, master, teacher, or governor. It was given by the Jews to their expected Messiah; but whether they understood it as denoting divinity cannot now be ascertained. It is clear only that Elizabeth used it as denoting great dignity and honor.</p>
