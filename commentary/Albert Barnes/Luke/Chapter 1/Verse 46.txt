<p style=" "><b>My soul doth magnify the Lord - </b>To “magnify” means to “make great,” and then to “extol,” to “praise,” to “celebrate.” It does not mean here strictly to “make great,” but to increase “in our estimation” - that is, to praise or extol. See <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="ps+34:3">Psalms 34:3</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="2sa+7:26">2 Samuel 7:26</span>.</p>
