<p style=" "><b>On the eighth day - </b>This was the day on which it was required to circumcise children, <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="ge+21:4">Genesis 21:4</span>.</p>
<p style=" "><b>And they called him Zacharias - </b>The name of the child was commonly given at the time of the circumcision, <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="ge+21:3-4">Genesis 21:3-4</span>. The name “commonly” given to the eldest son was that of the father.</p>
