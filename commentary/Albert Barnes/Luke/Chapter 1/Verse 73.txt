<p style=" "><b>The oath - </b>This oath is recorded in <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="ge+22:16-17">Genesis 22:16-17</span>. It was an oath in which God swore by himself (because he could swear by no greater, <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="heb+6:13-14">Hebrews 6:13-14</span>) that he would surely bless Abraham and his posterity. That promise was now to be entirely fulfilled by the coming of the Messiah.</p>
