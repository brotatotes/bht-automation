<p style=" "><b>Might serve him - </b>Might obey, honor, and worship him. This was regarded as a “favor.” This was what was promised, and for this Zechariah praised God.</p>
<p style=" "><b>Without fear - </b>Fear of death, of spiritual enemies, or of external foes. In the sure hope of God’s “eternal” favor beyond the grave.</p>
