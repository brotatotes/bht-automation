<p style=" "><b>Waxed strong in spirit - </b>That is, in courage, understanding, and purposes of good, fitting him for his future work. The word “wax” means to “increase, to grow,” from an old Saxon word.</p>
<p style=" "><b>In the deserts - </b>In Hebron, and in the hill country where his father resided. He dwelt in obscurity, and was not known publicly by the people.</p>
<p style=" "><b>Until the day of his showing - </b>Until he entered on his public ministry, as recorded in <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="mt+3:0">Matthew 3:0</span> - that is, probably, until he was about 30 years of age. See <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="lu+3:0">Luke 3:0</span>.</p>
