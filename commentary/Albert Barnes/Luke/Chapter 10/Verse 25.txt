A certain lawyer - One who professed to be well skilled in the laws of Moses, and whose business it was to explain them.Stood up - Rose - came forward to address him.Tempted him - Feigned a desire to be instructed, but did it to perplex him, or to lead him, if possible, to contradict some of the maxims of the law.Inherit eternal life - Be saved. This was the common inquiry among the Jews. “They” had said that man must keep the commandments - the written and oral law.
