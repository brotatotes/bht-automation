<p style=" "><b>By chance - </b>Accidentally, or as it happened. It means that he did not do it with a “design” to aid the man that was wounded.</p>
<p style=" "><b>A certain priest - </b>It is said that not less than 12,000 priests and Levites dwelt at Jericho; and as their business was at Jerusalem, of course there would be many of them constantly traveling on that road.</p>
<p style=" "><b>When he saw him - </b>He saw him lie, but came not near him.</p>
<p style=" "><b>Passed by on the other side - </b>On the farther side of the way. Did not turn out of his course even to come and see him.</p>
