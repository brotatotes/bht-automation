<p style=" "><b>A certain village - </b>Bethany. See <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="joh+11:1">John 11:1</span>. It was on the eastern declivity of the Mount of Olives. See the notes at <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="mt+21:1">Matthew 21:1</span>.</p>
<p style=" "><b>Received him - </b>Received him kindly and hospitably. From this it would seem that “Martha” was properly the mistress of the house. Possibly she was a widow, and her brother Lazarus and younger sister Mary lived with her; and as “she” had the care of the household, this will also show why she was so diligently employed about domestic affairs.</p>
