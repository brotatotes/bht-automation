<p style=" "><b>The son of peace - </b>That is, if the “house” or “family” be “worthy,” or be disposed to receive you in “peace” and kindness. See <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="mt+10:13">Matthew 10:13</span>. “The son of peace” means one “disposed” to peace, or peaceful and kind in his disposition. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="mt+1:1">Matthew 1:1</span>.</p>
