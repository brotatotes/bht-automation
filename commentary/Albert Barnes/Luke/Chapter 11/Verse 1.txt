<p style=" "><b>As he was praying - </b>Luke has taken notice of our Saviour’s praying often. Thus, at his baptism <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="lu+3:21">Luke 3:21</span>; in the wilderness <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="lu+5:16">Luke 5:16</span>; before the appointment of the apostles, he continued all night in prayer <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="lu+6:12">Luke 6:12</span>; he was alone praying <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="lu+9:18">Luke 9:18</span>; his transfiguration also took place when he went up to pray <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="lu+9:28-29">Luke 9:28-29</span>.</p>
