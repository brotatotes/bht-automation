<p style=" "><b>The wisdom of God - </b>By the “wisdom of God,” here, is undoubtedly meant the Saviour himself. What he immediately says is not written in the Old Testament. Jesus is called “the word of God” <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="joh+1:1">John 1:1</span>, because he is the medium by which God “speaks” or makes his will known. He is called “the wisdom of God,” because by him God makes his wisdom known in creation (<span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="col+1:13-18">Colossians 1:13-18</span> and in redemption <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="1co+1:30">1 Corinthians 1:30</span>. Many have also thought that the Messiah was referred to in the <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="pr+8:1">Proverbs 8:1</span> of Proverbs, under the name of Wisdom.</p>
