<p style=" "><b>Intending to build a tower - </b>See <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="mt+21:33">Matthew 21:33</span>. A tower was a place of defense or observation, erected on high places or in vineyards, to guard against enemies. It was made “high,” so as to enable one to see an enemy when he approached; and “strong,” so that it could not be easily taken.</p>
<p style=" "><b>Counteth the cost - </b>Makes a calculation how much it will cost to build it.</p>
