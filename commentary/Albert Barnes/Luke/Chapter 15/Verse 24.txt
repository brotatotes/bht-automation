<p style=" "><b>Was dead - </b>This is capable of two significations:</p>
<p style=" ">The word is not unfrequently thus used. See <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="1ti+5:6">1 Timothy 5:6</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="mt+8:22">Matthew 8:22</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="ro+6:13">Romans 6:13</span>. Hence, to be restored to “virtue” is said to be restored again to life, <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="ro+6:13">Romans 6:13</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="re+3:1">Revelation 3:1</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="eph+2:1">Ephesians 2:1</span>. It is probable that this latter is the meaning here. See <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="lu+15:32">Luke 15:32</span>.</p>
<p style=" "><b>Was lost - </b>Had wandered away from home, and we knew not where he was.</p>
