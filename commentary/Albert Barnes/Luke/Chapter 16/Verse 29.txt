<p style=" "><b>They have Moses - </b>The writings of Moses. The first five books of the Bible.</p>
<p style=" "><b>The prophets - </b>The remainder of the Old Testament. What the prophets had written.</p>
<p style=" "><b>Hear them - </b>Hear them speak in the Scriptures. Read them, or hear them read in the synagogues, and attend to what they have delivered.</p>
