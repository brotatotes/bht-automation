<p style=" "><b>By the prophets - </b>Those who foretold the coming of the Messiah, and whose predictions are recorded in the Old Testament.</p>
<p style=" "><b>Son of man - </b>The Messiah. They predicted that certain things would take place respecting the Messiah that was to come. See the <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="da+9:25-27">Daniel 9:25-27</span> notes; <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="isa+53:0">Isaiah 53:0</span> notes. “These things,” Jesus said, would be accomplished “in him,” he being the Son of man, or the Messiah.</p>
