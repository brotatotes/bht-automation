<p style=" "><b>By the Spirit - </b>By the direction of the Spirit.</p>
<p style=" "><b>Into the temple - </b>Into that part of the temple where the public worship was chiefly performed - into the court of the women. See the notes at <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="mt+21:12">Matthew 21:12</span>.</p>
