<p style=" "><b>Twelve years old - </b>All males among the Hebrews were required to appear three times a year before God, to attend on the ordinances of religion in the temple, and it is probable that this was the age at which they first went up to Jerusalem, <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="ex+23:14-17">Exodus 23:14-17</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="de+16:16">Deuteronomy 16:16</span>.</p>
<p style=" "><b>To Jerusalem - </b>Where the feasts of the Jews were all held. This was a journey from Nazareth of about 70 miles.</p>
<p style=" "><b>After the custom of the feast - </b>According to the usual manner of the feast.</p>
