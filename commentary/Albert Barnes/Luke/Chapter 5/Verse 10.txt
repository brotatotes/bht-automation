<p style=" "><b>Fear not - </b>He calmed their fears. With mildness and tenderness he stilled all their troubled feelings, and to their surprise announced that henceforward they should be appointed as heralds of salvation.</p>
<p style=" "><b>From henceforth - </b>Hereafter.</p>
<p style=" "><b>Shalt catch men - </b>Thou shalt be a minister of the gospel, and thy business shall be to win people to the truth that they may be saved.</p>
