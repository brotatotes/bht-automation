<p style=" "><b>Vexed - </b>The word “vex” with us means to provoke, or irritate by petty provocations. Here it means, however, to “afflict,” to “torment” - denoting deep and heavy trials.</p>
<p style=" "><b>Unclean spirits - </b>Demons that were impure and unholy, having a delight in tormenting, and in inflicting painful and loathsome diseases.</p>
