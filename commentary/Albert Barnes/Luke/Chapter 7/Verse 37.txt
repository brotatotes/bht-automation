<p style=" "><b>In the city - </b>What city is meant is unknown. Some have supposed it was Nain; some Capernaum; some Magdala; and some Jerusalem.</p>
<p style=" "><b>Which was a sinner - </b>Who was depraved or wicked. This woman, it seems, was known to be a sinner - perhaps an abandoned woman or a prostitute. It is certain that she had much to be forgiven, and she had probably passed her life in crime. There is no evidence that this was the woman commonly called Mary Magdalene.</p>
