<p style=" "><b>A certain creditor - </b>A man who had lent money or sold property, the payment for which was yet due.</p>
<p style=" "><b>Five hundred pence - </b>About 69 dollars 26 cents, or 14 British pounds, 11 shilling, 8d. See the notes at <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="mt+18:28">Matthew 18:28</span>.</p>
<p style=" "><b>Fifty - </b>About 7 dollars, or 1 British pound, 9 shillings, and 2d.</p>
