<p style=" "><b>Beware of the scribes - </b>Be on your guard. Be cautious about hearing them or following them.</p>
<p style=" "><b>Scribes - </b>The learned men of the Jewish nation.</p>
<p style=" "><b>Which love to go in long clothing - </b>In long, flowing robes, as significant of their consequence, leisure, and learning.</p>
