<p style=" "><b>How in the bush - </b>At the burning bush. See <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="ex+3:16">Exodus 3:16</span>. The meaning is, “in that part of the book of Exodus which contains the account of the burning bush. When there were no chapters and verses, it was the easiest way of quoting a book of the Old Testament “by the subject,” and in this way it was often done by the Jews.</p>
