<p style=" "><b>Watch ye - </b>Be diligent, faithful, and waiting for the return of your Lord, who will come at an unexpected hour.</p>
<p style=" "><b>Master of the house - </b>Denoting here the Lord Jesus.</p>
<p style=" "><b>At even, or at midnight, or ... - </b>This refers to the four divisions into which the Jews divided the night.</p>
