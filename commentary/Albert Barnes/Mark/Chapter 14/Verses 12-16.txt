<p style=" "><b>A large upper room - </b>The word used here denotes the upper room devoted to purposes of prayer, repose, and often of eating. See the notes at <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="mt+9:1-8">Matthew 9:1-8</span>.</p>
<p style=" "><b>Furnished and prepared - </b>Literally, “spread” and “ready.” Spread with a carpet, or with “couches” such as were used in eating. See the notes at <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="mt+23:6">Matthew 23:6</span>.</p>
