<p style=" "><b>And Pilate marveled if - </b>Wondered if he was dead, or wondered that he was so soon dead. It was not common for persons crucified to expire under two or three days, sometimes not until the sixth or seventh. Joseph had asked for the “body,” implying that he was dead. That he was, had been ascertained by the soldiers. See <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="joh+19:33">John 19:33</span>.</p>
