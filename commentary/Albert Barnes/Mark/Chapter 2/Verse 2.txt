<p style=" "><b>So much as about the door - </b>In the “court” or “yard” before the door. They could not get near enough to hear him.</p>
<p style=" "><b>Preached the word unto them - </b>The word of God; the revelation or doctrine which he came to deliver, called “the Word,” and “the Word of God,” because it was spoken or revealed by God. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="ac+6:2-7">Acts 6:2-7</span>.</p>
