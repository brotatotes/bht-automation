<p style=" "><b>And the scribes ... - </b>See the notes at <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="mt+12:24-32">Matthew 12:24-32</span>. The occasion of their saying this was, that he had healed a man possessed with a devil. The scribes, who came from Jerusalem to watch his conduct, charged him with having made a compact or agreement with the prince of the devils.</p>
