<p style=" "><b>In Decapolis - </b>See the notes at <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="mt+4:25">Matthew 4:25</span>.</p>
<p style=" "><b>How great things ... - </b>This was the natural expression of right feeling at being cured of such a calamity. So the desire of sinners freed from sin is to honor Jesus, and to invite the world to participate in the same salvation, and to join them in doing honor to the Son of God. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="ps+66:16">Psalms 66:16</span>.</p>
