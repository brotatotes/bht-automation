<p style=" "><b>A desert place - </b>A retired place, across the sea from Capernaum, where they would be free from interruption.</p>
<p style=" "><b>There were many coming and going - </b>Coming to be healed and retiring, or coming to hear him preach. It means that they were “thronged,” or that there was a vast multitude attending his preaching.</p>
