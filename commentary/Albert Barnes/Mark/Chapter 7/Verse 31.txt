<p style=" "><b>Came unto the sea of Galilee - </b>The Sea of Tiberias. See the notes at <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="mt+4:18">Matthew 4:18</span>.</p>
<p style=" "><b>Decapolis - </b>See the notes at <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="mt+4:25">Matthew 4:25</span>. He did not go immediately into Capernaum, or any city where he was known, but into the retired regions around the Sea of Galilee. This was done to avoid the designs of the Pharisees, who sought his life.</p>
