<p style=" "><b>They bring - </b>That is, his friends brought, or the people brought.</p>
<p style=" "><b>One that was deaf, and had an impediment in his speech - </b>Not entirely mute, but who spoke indistinctly or with difficulty. His deafness might not have been of long standing, and his speech, therefore, not entirely ruined.</p>
<p style=" "><b>To put his hand upon him - </b>That is, to cure him. Blessings were commonly imparted by laying on the hands.</p>
