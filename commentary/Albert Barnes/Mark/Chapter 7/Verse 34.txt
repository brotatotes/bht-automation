<p style=" "><b>Looked up to heaven - </b>To lift up the eyes to heaven is an act imploring aid from God, and is an attitude of prayer, <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="ps+121:1-2">Psalms 121:1-2</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="mr+6:41">Mark 6:41</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="joh+11:41">John 11:41</span>.</p>
<p style=" "><b>He sighed - </b>Pitying the sufferings of the man who stood before him.</p>
<p style=" "><b>Ephphatha - </b>This word is “Syriac,” the language which our Lord used in addressing the man, and means “Be opened.”</p>
