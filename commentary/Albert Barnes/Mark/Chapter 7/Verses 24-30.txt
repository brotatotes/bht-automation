<p style=" "><b>A Greek - </b>The Jews called all persons “Greeks” who were not of their nation. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="ro+1:14">Romans 1:14</span>. The whole world was considered as divided into Jews and Greeks. Though she might not have been strictly a “Greek,” yet she came under this general appellation as a foreigner.</p>
