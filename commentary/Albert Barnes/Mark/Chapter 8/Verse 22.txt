<p style=" "><b>And they bring a blind man unto him - </b>The healing of the blind man of Bethsaida is recorded only by Mark.</p>
<p style=" "><b>Besought him to touch him - </b>That is, to heal him, for they believed that his touch would restore his sight.</p>
