<p style=" "><b>Now the birth of Jesus Christ - </b>The circumstances attending his birth.</p>
<p style=" "><b>Was on this wise - </b>In this manner.</p>
<p style=" "><b>Espoused - </b>Betrothed, or engaged to be married. There was commonly an interval of ten or twevle months, among the Jews, between the contract of marriage and the celebration of the nuptials (see <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="ge+24:55">Genesis 24:55</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="jdg+14:8">Judges 14:8</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="de+20:7">Deuteronomy 20:7</span>), yet such was the nature of this engagement, that unfaithfulness to each other was deemed adultery. See <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="de+22:25">Deuteronomy 22:25</span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="de+22:28">Deuteronomy 22:28</span>.</p>
