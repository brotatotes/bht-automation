<p style=" "><b>Freely ye have received, freely give - </b>That is, they were not to sell their favors of healing, preaching, etc. They were not to make a money-making business of it, to bargain specifically to heal for so much, and to cast out devils for so much. This, however, neither then nor afterward precluded them from receiving a competent support. See <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="lu+10:7">Luke 10:7</span>; <span class="_0000ff"><u>1Co 9:8-14</u></span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="1ti+5:18">1 Timothy 5:18</span>.</p>
