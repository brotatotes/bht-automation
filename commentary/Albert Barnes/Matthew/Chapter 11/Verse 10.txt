<p style=" "><b>For this is he ... - </b>The passage of Scripture here quoted is found in <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="mal+3:1">Malachi 3:1</span>. The substance of it is contained also in <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="isa+40:3">Isaiah 40:3</span>.</p>
<p style=" "><b>Prepare thy way - </b>That is, to prepare “the people;” to make them ready, by proper instructions, to receive the Messiah.</p>
