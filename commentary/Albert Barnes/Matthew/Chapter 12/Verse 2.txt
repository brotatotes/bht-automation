<p style=" "><b>Upon the Sabbath day - </b>The Pharisees, doubtless desirous of finding fault with Christ, said that in plucking the grain on the “Sabbath day” they had violated the commandment. Moses had commanded the Hebrews to abstain from all servile work on the Sabbath, <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="ex+20:10">Exodus 20:10</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="ex+35:2-3">Exodus 35:2-3</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="nu+15:32-36">Numbers 15:32-36</span>. On any other day this would have been clearly lawful, for it was permitted, <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="de+23:25">Deuteronomy 23:25</span>.</p>
