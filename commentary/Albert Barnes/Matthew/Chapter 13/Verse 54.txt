<p style=" "><b>Into his own country - </b>That is, into Nazareth. Mark, who has also recorded this <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="mr+6:1-6">Mark 6:1-6</span>, says that it took place on the Sabbath. It was common for our Saviour to speak in the synagogues. Any Jew had a fight to address the people, if called on by the minister; and our Saviour often availed himself of the right to instruct the people and declare his doctrines. See <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="mt+4:23">Matthew 4:23</span>.</p>
