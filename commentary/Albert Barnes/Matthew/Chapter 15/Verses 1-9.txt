<p style=" "><b>In vain do they worship me - </b>That is, their attempts to worship are “vain,” or are not real worship - they are mere “forms.”</p>
<p style=" "><b>Teaching for doctrines ... - </b>The word “doctrines,” here, means the requirements of religion - things to be believed and practiced in religion.</p>
<p style=" ">God only has a right to declare what shall be done in his service; but they held their traditions to be superior to the written word of God, and taught them as doctrines binding the conscience. See the notes at <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="isa+29:13">Isaiah 29:13</span>.</p>
