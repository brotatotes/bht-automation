<p style=" "><b>Sea of Galilee - </b>That is, the Lake of Gennesaret. For an account of the principal diseases mentioned here, see the notes at <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="mt+4:24">Matthew 4:24</span>.</p>
<p style=" "><b>Maimed - </b>Those to whom a hand or foot was wanting. See <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="mt+18:8">Matthew 18:8</span>. To cure them - that is, to restore a hand or foot - was a direct act of creative power. It is no wonder, therefore, that the people wondered.</p>
<p style=" "><b>And they glorified the God of Israel - </b>To glorify here means to praise; to acknowledge his power and goodness. The God of Israel was the God that the Israelites or Jews worshipped.</p>
