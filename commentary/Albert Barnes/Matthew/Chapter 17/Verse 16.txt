<p style=" "><b>And I brought him to thy disciples ... - </b>That is, not to the apostles, for they had power over unclean spirits <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="mt+10:8">Matthew 10:8</span>, but to others of his followers who attempted to work miracles. It is probable that many of his disciples attempted this who were not personal attendants on his ministry, <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="mr+9:38">Mark 9:38</span>.</p>
