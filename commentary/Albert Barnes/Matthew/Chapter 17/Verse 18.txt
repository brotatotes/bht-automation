<p style=" "><b>And Jesus rebuked the devil - </b>The word “rebuke” has the combined force of reproving and commanding. He reproved him for having afflicted the child, and he commanded him to come out of him. Mark <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="mr+9:25">Mark 9:25</span> has recorded the words which he used words implying reproof and command: “Thou dumb and deaf spirit, I charge thee come out of him, and enter no more into him.” And the spirit cried, and with a mighty convulsion came out, leaving the child apparently dead. Jesus lifted him up by the hand (Mark), and gave him to his father (Luke).</p>
