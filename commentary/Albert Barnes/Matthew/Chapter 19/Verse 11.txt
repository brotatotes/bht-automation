<p style=" "><b>All men cannot receive this saying - </b>The minds of people are not prepared for this. This saying evidently means what the disciples had just said that it was good for a man not to marry. It might be good in certain circumstances - in times of persecution and trial, or for the sake of laboring in the cause of religion without the care and burden of a family. It might be good for many to live, as some of the apostles did, without marriage, but it was not given to all people, <span class="_0000ff"><u>1Co 7:1</u></span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="1co+7:7">1 Corinthians 7:7</span>,<span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="1co+7:9">1 Corinthians 7:9</span>. To be married, or unmarried, might be lawful, according to circumstances, <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="1co+7:26">1 Corinthians 7:26</span>.</p>
