<p style=" "><b>They received every man a penny - </b>There was no agreement how much they should receive, but merely that justice should be done, <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="mt+20:4-5">Matthew 20:4-5</span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="mt+20:7">Matthew 20:7</span>. The householder supposed they had earned it, or chose to make a present to them to compensate for the loss of the first part of the day, when they were willing to work, but could not find employment.</p>
