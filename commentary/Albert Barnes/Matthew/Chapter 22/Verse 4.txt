<p style=" "><b>Other servants - </b>Who might press it on their attention. So God repeats his message to sinners when they reject it.</p>
<p style=" "><b>My dinner - </b>This word literally denotes the meal taken about noon. It is also taken for a meal in general. As marriages were, among Eastern nations, in the evening, it refers here to a meal taken at that time.</p>
<p style=" "><b>Fatlings - </b>This word does not refer to any particular species of animals. It denotes any fat animals. As oxen are also mentioned, however, it refers here, probably, to lambs or calves, <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="2sa+6:13">2 Samuel 6:13</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="1ch+15:26">1 Chronicles 15:26</span>.</p>
