<p style=" "><b>But when the king heard ... - </b>This doubtless refers to the Jews and to Jerusalem. They were murderers, having slain the prophets; and God was about to send forth the armies of the Romans under his providential direction, and to burn up their city. See the notes at <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="mt+24:0">Matthew 24:0</span>.</p>
<p style=" "><b>Wroth - </b>Angry; displeased.</p>
