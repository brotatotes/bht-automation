<p style=" "><b>The temple - </b>See the notes at <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="mt+21:12">Matthew 21:12</span>.</p>
<p style=" "><b>It is nothing - </b>It amounts to nothing - it is not binding.</p>
<p style=" "><b>The gold of the temple - </b>Either the golden vessels in the temple - the candlestick, etc.; or the gold with which the doors and other parts of the temple were covered; or the gold in the treasury. This, it seems, they considered far more sacred than any other part of the temple, but it is not known why.</p>
<p style=" "><b>He is a debtor - </b>He is bound to keep his oath. He is guilty if he violates it.</p>
