<p style=" "><b>The altar - </b>The altar of burnt-offerings, in the court of the priests. See the notes at <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="mt+21:12">Matthew 21:12</span>. It was made of brass, about 30 feet in length and breadth, and 15 feet in height, <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="2ch+4:1">2 Chronicles 4:1</span>. On this altar were offered all the beasts and bloody oblations of the temple.</p>
<p style=" "><b>The gift that is upon it - </b>The gift or offering made to God, so called because it was devoted or “given” to him. The gift upon this altar was always beasts and birds.</p>
