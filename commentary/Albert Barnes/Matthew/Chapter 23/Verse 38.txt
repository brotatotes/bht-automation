<p style=" "><b>Your house - </b>The temple. The house of worship of the Jews. The chief ornament of Jerusalem.</p>
<p style=" "><b>Desolate - </b>About to be desolate or destroyed. To be forsaken as a place of worship, and delivered into the hands of the Romans, and destroyed. See the notes at <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="mt+24:0">Matthew 24:0</span>.</p>
