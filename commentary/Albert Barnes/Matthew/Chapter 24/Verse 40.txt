<p style=" "><b>Then shall two be in the field ... - </b>The calamity will come suddenly. There will be no escape for those whom it overtakes.</p>
<p style=" "><b>One shall be taken - </b>The word “taken” may mean either to be taken away from the danger - that is, rescued, as Lot was <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="lu+17:28-29">Luke 17:28-29</span>, or to be taken away “by death.” Probably the latter is the meaning.</p>
