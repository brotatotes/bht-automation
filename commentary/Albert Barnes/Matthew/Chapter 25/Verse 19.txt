<p style=" "><b>After a long time ... - </b>By the return of the lord of those servants to reckon with them is denoted the return of Christ to call people to an account for the manner in which they have improved their talents. See <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="ro+14:12">Romans 14:12</span>; <span class="_0000ff"><u>2Co 5:10</u></span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="1th+4:16">1 Thessalonians 4:16</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="ac+1:11">Acts 1:11</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="ac+17:31">Acts 17:31</span>.</p>
<p style=" "><b>Reckon with them - </b>To reckon is to settle accounts. Here it means to inquire into their faithfulness, and to reward or punish them accordingly.</p>
