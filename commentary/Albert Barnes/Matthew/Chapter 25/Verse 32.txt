<p style=" "><b>And before him ... - </b>At his coming to judgment the world will be burned up, <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="2pe+3:10">2 Peter 3:10</span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="2pe+3:12">2 Peter 3:12</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="re+20:11">Revelation 20:11</span>. The dead in Christ that is, all true Christians - will be raised up from their graves, <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="1th+4:16">1 Thessalonians 4:16</span>. The living will be changed - i. e., will be made like the glorified bodies of those that are raised from the dead, <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="1co+15:52-54">1 Corinthians 15:52-54</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="1th+4:17">1 Thessalonians 4:17</span>. All the wicked will rise and come forth to judgment, <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="joh+5:28-29">John 5:28-29</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="da+12:2">Daniel 12:2</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="mt+13:41-42">Matthew 13:41-42</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="re+20:13">Revelation 20:13</span>. Then shall the world be judged, the righteous saved, and the wicked punished.</p>
<p style=" "><b>And he shall separate ... - </b>Shall determine respecting their character, and shall appoint them their doom accordingly.</p>
