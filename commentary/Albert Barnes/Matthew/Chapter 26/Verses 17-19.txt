<p style=" "><b>They made ready the passover - </b>That is, they procured a lamb, multitudes of which were kept for sale in the temple; they had it killed and flayed by the priests, and the blood poured by the altar; they roasted the lamb, and prepared the bitter herbs, the sauce, and the unleavened bread.</p>
<p style=" ">This was done, it seems, while our Lord was absent, by the two disciples.</p>
