<p style=" "><b>When he was accused ... - </b>To wit, of perverting the nation, and of forbidding to give tribute to Caesar, <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="lu+23:2">Luke 23:2</span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="lu+23:5">Luke 23:5</span>. Probably this was done in a tumultuous manner and in every variety of form.</p>
<p style=" "><b>He answered nothing - </b>He was conscious of his innocence. He knew that they could not prove these charges. They offered no testimony to prove them, and, in conscious innocence, he was silent.</p>
