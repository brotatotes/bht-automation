<p style=" "><b>We remember - </b>They had either heard him say this, or, more probably, had understood that this was one of his doctrines.</p>
<p style=" "><b>That deceiver - </b>One of the charges against him was that he deceived the people, <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="joh+7:12">John 7:12</span>. By this title they still chose to designate him, thinking that his death had fully confirmed the truth of the charges against him.</p>
