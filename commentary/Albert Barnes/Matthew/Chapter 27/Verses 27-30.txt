<p style=" "><b>And they spit upon him - </b>This was a token of the deepest contempt and insult.</p>
<p style=" ">See the notes at <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="mt+26:67">Matthew 26:67</span>.</p>
<p style=" "><b>And took the reed - </b>The cane, probably so large as to inflict a heavy blow.</p>
<p style=" "><b>And smote him on the head - </b>Not merely to injure him by the force of the blow, but to press the “thorns” into his head, and thus to add cruelty to insult.</p>
