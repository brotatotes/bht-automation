<p style=" "><b>The governor’s ears - </b>To Pilate. If it is reported to him that Jesus was stolen while you slept.</p>
<p style=" "><b>We will persuade him - </b>We will convince or satisfy him, so that he shall not punish you. This they might promise with safety; for,</p>
<p style=" ">He would not be disposed, “of himself,” to take any further trouble about the matter. He would feel that all that could be demanded of him had been done, and would not be disposed further to interfere, unless the Sanhedrin should demand it. This, of course, they would not do.</p>
