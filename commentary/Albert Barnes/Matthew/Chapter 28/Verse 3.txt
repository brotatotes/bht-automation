<p style=" "><b>His countenance - </b>In our language the word “countenance” refers to the “face only;” in the original it refers to his “whole person.” His “general aspect, or the appearance of the angel himself,” was, etc.</p>
<p style=" "><b>Like lightning - </b>Peculiarly bright and shining.</p>
<p style=" "><b>His raiment white as snow - </b>Celestial beings are usually represented as clothed in white, <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="ac+1:10">Acts 1:10</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="da+7:9">Daniel 7:9</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="re+3:4-5">Revelation 3:4-5</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="re+4:4">Revelation 4:4</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="re+7:13-14">Revelation 7:13-14</span>. White, among the Jews, was the symbol of “purity or innocence.”</p>
