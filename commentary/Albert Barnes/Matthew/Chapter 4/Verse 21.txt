<p style=" "><b>And going on from thence - </b>From the place where he had found Peter and Andrew, <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="mt+4:18">Matthew 4:18</span>.</p>
<p style=" "><b>Saw two other brothers - </b>They were men engaged in the same employment, as it is probable that there were many such in the neighborhood of the lake.</p>
<p style=" "><b>In a ship - </b>A small vessel. In fact, it was little more, probably, than a sail-boat.</p>
<p style=" "><b>Mending their nets - </b>A very common employment when they were not actually engaged in fishing.</p>
