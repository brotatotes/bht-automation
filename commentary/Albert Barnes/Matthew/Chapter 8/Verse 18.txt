<p style=" "><b>Unto the other side - </b>Jesus was now in Capernaum, a city at the northwest corner of the Sea of Tiberias, or Sea of Galilee. See the notes at <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="mt+4:18">Matthew 4:18</span>. The country to which he purposed to go was the region on the east of the Sea of Tiberias.</p>
