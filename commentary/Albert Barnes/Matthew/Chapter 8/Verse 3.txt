<p style=" "><b>And Jesus ...touched him - </b>It was an offence to the Jews to “touch” a leprous person, and was regarded as making him who did it ceremonially impure, <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="le+13:3">Leviticus 13:3</span>. The act of putting forth his hand and “touching” him, therefore, expressed the intention of Jesus to cure him, and was a pledge that he “was,” in fact, already cured.</p>
