<p style=" "><b>There came unto him a centurion - </b>A centurion was the commander of 100 men in the Roman armies. Judea was a Roman province, and garrisons were kept there to preserve the people in subjection. This man was probably by birth a pagan. See <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="mt+8:10">Matthew 8:10</span>.</p>
