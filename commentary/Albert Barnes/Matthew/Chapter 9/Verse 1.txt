<p style=" "><b>And he entered into a ship ... - </b>Jesus acceded to the request of the people of Gadara <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="mt+8:34">Matthew 8:34</span>, recrossed the Lake of Gennesareth, and returned to his own city. By “his own city” is meant Capernaum <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="mr+2:1">Mark 2:1</span>, the city which was at that time his home, or where he had his dwelling. See the notes at <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="mt+4:13">Matthew 4:13</span>. This same account, with some additional circumstances, is contained in <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="mr+2:3-12">Mark 2:3-12</span>, and <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="lu+5:18-26">Luke 5:18-26</span>.</p>
