<p style=" "><b>And when the devil was cast out, the dumb spake - </b>The miracle is narrated in the briefest terms; but the effect was immediate and the restoration was complete.</p>
<p style=" "><b>It was never so seen in Israel - </b>Never was there in our land - among the Jews - such a succession of wonders, so striking, so marvelous, so full of the power of God. This was literally true.</p>
