<p style=" "><b>Jesus, knowing their thoughts - </b>Mark says, “Jesus perceived “in his spirit” that they so reasoned.” The power of searching the heart, and of knowing the thoughts of people, belongs only to God, <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="1ch+28:9">1 Chronicles 28:9</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="ro+8:27">Romans 8:27</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="re+2:23">Revelation 2:23</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="jer+17:10">Jeremiah 17:10</span>. In claiming this, as Jesus did here, and often elsewhere, he gave clear proofs of his omniscience, <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="joh+2:24-25">John 2:24-25</span>.</p>
