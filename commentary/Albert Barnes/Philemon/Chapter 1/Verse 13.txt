<p style=" "><b>Whom I would have retained with me, that in thy stead - </b>“That he might render me the service which I know you would if you were here.” The Greek is: “for thee” <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ὑπὲρ σοῦ</span></span> huper sou; that is, what he should do for Paul might be regarded as done by Philemon himself.</p>
<p style=" "><b>He might have ministered unto me - </b>He might have rendered me assistance (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">διακονῇ</span></span> diakonē); to wit, in such a way as one who was in bonds would need.</p>
