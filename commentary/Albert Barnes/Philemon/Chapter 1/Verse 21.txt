<p style=" "><b>Having confidence in thy obedience - </b>That you would comply with all my expressed desires.</p>
<p style=" "><b>I wrote unto thee - </b>“I have written to you;” to wit, in this Epistle.</p>
<p style=" "><b>Knowing that thou wilt also do more than I say - </b>In all the respects which he had mentioned - in receiving Onesimus, and in his kind treatment of him. He had asked a great favor of him, but he knew that he would go even beyond what he had asked.</p>
