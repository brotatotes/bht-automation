<p style=" "><b>And that every tongue should confess - </b>Everyone should acknowledge him. On the duty and importance of confessing Christ, see the notes at <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="ro+10:9-10">Romans 10:9-10</span>.</p>
<p style=" "><b>That Jesus Christ is Lord - </b>The word “Lord,” here, is used in its primitive and proper sense, as denoting owner, ruler, sovereign; compare the notes at <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="ro+14:9">Romans 14:9</span>. The meaning is, that all should acknowledge him as the universal sovereign.</p>
<p style=" "><b>To the glory of God the Father - </b>Such a universal confession would honor God; see the notes at <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="joh+5:23">John 5:23</span>, where this sentiment is explained.</p>
