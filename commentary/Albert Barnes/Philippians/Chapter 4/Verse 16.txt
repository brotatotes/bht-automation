<p style=" "><b>For even in Thessalonica; - </b>see the notes, <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="ac+17:1">Acts 17:1</span>. Paul remained there long enough to establish a flourishing church. He met, indeed, with much opposition and persecution there; and, hence, it was necessary that his wants should be supplied by others.</p>
