<p style=" "><b>And the angel which I saw stand ... - </b><span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="re+10:2">Revelation 10:2</span>. That is, John saw him standing in this posture when he made the oath which he proceeds to record.</p>
<p style=" "><b>Lifted up his hand to heaven - </b>The usual attitude in taking an oath, as if one called heaven to witness. Compare the notes on <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="da+12:7">Daniel 12:7</span>.</p>
