<p style=" "><b>And the seven angels - </b>See the notes on <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="re+15:1">Revelation 15:1</span>.</p>
<p style=" "><b>Came out of the temple - </b>Were seen to come from the temple; that is, from the immediate presence of God.</p>
<p style=" "><b>Having the seven plagues - </b>See the notes on <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="re+15:1">Revelation 15:1</span>. Each one entrusted with a single “plague” to be executed upon the earth. The meaning here is, that they were designated or appointed to execute those plagues in judgments. The symbols of their office - the golden vials - were given to them afterward, <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="re+15:7">Revelation 15:7</span>.</p>
<p style=" "><b>Clothed in pure and white linen - </b>The emblem of holiness - the common representation in regard to the heavenly inhabitants. See the notes on <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="re+3:4">Revelation 3:4</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="re+7:13">Revelation 7:13</span>. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="mt+17:2">Matthew 17:2</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="lu+9:29">Luke 9:29</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="mr+16:5">Mark 16:5</span>.</p>
<p style=" "><b>And having their breasts girded with golden girdles - </b>See the notes on <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="re+1:13">Revelation 1:13</span>. The meaning is, that they were attired in a manner befitting their rank and condition.</p>
