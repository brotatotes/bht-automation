<p style=" "><b>And there were voices, and thunders, and lightnings - </b>Accompanying the voice that was heard from the throne. See the notes on <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="re+4:5">Revelation 4:5</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="re+11:19">Revelation 11:19</span>.</p>
<p style=" "><b>And there was a great earthquake, ... - </b>See the notes on <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="re+6:12">Revelation 6:12</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="re+11:19">Revelation 11:19</span>. The meaning is, that a judgment followed as if the world were shaken by an earthquake, or which would be properly represented by that.</p>
<p style=" "><b>So mighty an earthquake, and so great - </b>All this is intensive, and is designed to represent the severity of the judgment that would follow.</p>
