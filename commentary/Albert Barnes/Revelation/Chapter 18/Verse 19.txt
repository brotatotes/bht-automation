<p style=" "><b>And they cast dust on their heads - </b>A common sign of lamentation and mourning among the Orientals. See the notes on <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="job+2:12">Job 2:12</span>.</p>
<p style=" "><b>By reason of her costliness - </b>The word rendered “costliness” - <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τιμιότητος</span></span> timiotētos - means, properly, “preciousness, costliness”; their magnificence, costly merchandise. The luxury of a great city enriches many individuals, however much it may impoverish itself.</p>
<p style=" "><b>For in one hour is she made desolate - </b>So it seemed to them. See the notes on <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="re+18:17">Revelation 18:17</span>.</p>
