<p style=" "><b>And there shall in no wise - </b>On no account; by no means. This strong language denotes the absolute exclusion of all that is specified in the verse.</p>
<p style=" "><b>Anything that defileth - </b>Literally, anything “common.” See the notes on <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="ac+10:14">Acts 10:14</span>. It means here that nothing will be found in that blessed abode which is unholy or sinful. It will be a pure world, <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="2pe+3:13">2 Peter 3:13</span>.</p>
<p style=" "><b>Neither whatsoever worketh abomination, or maketh a lie - </b>See the notes on <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="re+21:8">Revelation 21:8</span>.</p>
<p style=" "><b>But they which are written in the Lamb’s book of life - </b>Whose names are there recorded. See the notes on <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="re+3:5">Revelation 3:5</span>. Compare the notes on <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="re+21:8">Revelation 21:8</span>.</p>
