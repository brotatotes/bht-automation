<p style=" "><b>And he that sat upon the throne said - </b>Probably the Messiah, the dispenser of the rewards of heaven. See the notes on <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="re+20:11">Revelation 20:11</span>.</p>
<p style=" "><b>Behold, I make all things new - </b>A new heaven and new earth <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="re+21:1">Revelation 21:1</span>, and an order of things to correspond with that new creation. The former state of things when sin and death reigned will be changed, and the change consequent on this must extend to everything.</p>
<p style=" "><b>And he said unto me, Write - </b>Make a record of these things, for they are founded in truth, and they are adapted to bless a suffering world. Compare the notes on <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="re+14:13">Revelation 14:13</span>. See also <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="re+1:19">Revelation 1:19</span>.</p>
<p style=" "><b>For these words are true and faithful - </b>They are founded in truth, and they are worthy to be believed. See the notes on <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="re+19:9">Revelation 19:9</span>. Compare also notes on <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="da+12:4">Daniel 12:4</span>.</p>
