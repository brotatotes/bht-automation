<p style=" "><b>He that overcometh - </b>See the notes on <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="re+2:7">Revelation 2:7</span>.</p>
<p style=" "><b>Shall inherit all things - </b>Be an heir of God in all things. See the notes on <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="ro+8:17">Romans 8:17</span>. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="re+2:7">Revelation 2:7</span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="re+2:11">Revelation 2:11</span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="re+2:17">Revelation 2:17</span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="re+2:26">Revelation 2:26</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="re+3:5">Revelation 3:5</span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="re+3:12">Revelation 3:12</span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="re+3:21">Revelation 3:21</span>.</p>
<p style=" "><b>And I will be his God - </b>That is, forever. He would be to them all that is properly implied in the name of God; he would bestow upon them all the blessings which it was appropriate for God to bestow. See the <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="2co+6:18">2 Corinthians 6:18</span> note; <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="heb+8:10">Hebrews 8:10</span> note.</p>
<p style=" "><b>And he shall be my son - </b>He shall sustain to me the relation of a son, and shall be treated as such. He would ever onward sustain this relation, and be honored as a child of God.</p>
