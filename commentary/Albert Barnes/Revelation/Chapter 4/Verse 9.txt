<p style=" "><b>And when those beasts give glory ... - </b>As often as those living beings ascribe glory to God. They did this continually <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="re+4:8">Revelation 4:8</span>; and, if the above explanation be correct, then the idea is that the ways and acts of God in his providential government are continually of such a nature as to honor him.</p>
