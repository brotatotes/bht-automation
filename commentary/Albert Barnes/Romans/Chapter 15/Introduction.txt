<p style=" ">It may be of importance to state that between the last verse of the preceding chapter and the first verse of <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="ro+15:0">Romans 15:0</span>, the Arabic version, some manuscripts and many of the Greek fathers, as Chrysostom, Theodoret, Theophylact, etc. have introduced <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="ro+16:25-27">Romans 16:25-27</span>. Why this was done, has been a matter of controversy. The discussion, however, is of no practical importance, and most critics concur in the opinion that the present arrangement of the Greek text is genuine.</p>
