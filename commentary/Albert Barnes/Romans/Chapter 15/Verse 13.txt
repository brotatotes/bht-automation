<p style=" "><b>Now the God of hope - </b>The God who “inspires,” or “produces” the Christian hope.</p>
<p style=" "><b>All joy and peace - </b><span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="ro+14:17">Romans 14:17</span>. If they were filled with this, there would be no strife and contention.</p>
<p style=" "><b>In believing - </b>The effect of believing is to produce this joy and peace.</p>
<p style=" "><b>That ye may abound ... - </b>That your hope may be steadfast and strong.</p>
<p style=" "><b>Through the power ... - </b>By means of the powerful operation of the Holy Spirit. It is by his power alone that the Christian has the hope of eternal life; see <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="eph+1:13-14">Ephesians 1:13-14</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="ro+8:24">Romans 8:24</span>.</p>
