<p style=" "><b>Whose mouth - </b><span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="ps+10:7">Psalms 10:7</span>. The apostle has not quoted this literally, but has given the sense. David in the psalm is describing his bitter enemies.</p>
<p style=" "><b>Cursing - </b>Reproachful and opprobrious language, such as Shimei used in relation to David; <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="2sa+16:5">2 Samuel 16:5</span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="2sa+16:7-8">2 Samuel 16:7-8</span>.</p>
<p style=" "><b>Bitterness - </b>In the psalm, deceits. The word “bitterness” is used to denote severity, harshness, cruelty; reproachful and malicious words.</p>
