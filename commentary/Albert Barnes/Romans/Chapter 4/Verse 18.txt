<p style=" "><b>Who against hope - </b>Who against all apparent or usual ground of hope. He refers here to the prospect of a posterity; see <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="ro+4:19-21">Romans 4:19-21</span>.</p>
<p style=" "><b>Believed in hope - </b>Believed in what was promised to excite his hope. Hope here is put for the object of his hope - what was promised.</p>
<p style=" "><b>According to what was spoken - </b><span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="ge+15:5">Genesis 15:5</span>.</p>
<p style=" "><b>So shall thy seed be - </b>That is, as the stars in heaven for multitude. Thy posterity shall be very numerous.</p>
