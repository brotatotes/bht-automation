<p style=" "><b>So then if ... - </b>compare <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="mt+5:32">Matthew 5:32</span>.</p>
<p style=" "><b>She shall be called - </b>She will be. The word used here <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">χρηματίσει</span></span> chrēmatisei is often used to denote being called by an oracle or by divine revelation. But it is here employed in the simple sense of being commonly called, or of being so regarded.</p>
