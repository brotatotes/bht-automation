<p style=" "><b>Not as though ... - </b>Not as though the promise of God had entirely failed. Though I grieve thus <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="ro+9:2-3">Romans 9:2-3</span>, though I am deeply apprehensive for the nation, yet I do not affirm that all the nation is to be destroyed. The promise of God will not entirely fail.</p>
<p style=" "><b>Not all Israel - </b>Not all the descendants of Jacob have the true spirit of Israelites, or are Jews in the scriptural sense of the term; see the note at <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="ro+2:28-29">Romans 2:28-29</span>.</p>
