<p style=" "><b>For this is the word of promise - </b>This is the promise made to Abraham. The design of the apostle, in introducing this, is doubtless to show to whom the promise appertained; and by specifying this, he shows that it had not reference to Ishmael, but to Isaac.</p>
<p style=" "><b>At this time - </b>Greek, According to this time; see <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="ge+18:10">Genesis 18:10</span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="ge+18:14">Genesis 18:14</span>. Probably it means at the exact time promised; I will fulfil the prediction at the very time; compare <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="2ki+4:16">2 Kings 4:16</span>.</p>
