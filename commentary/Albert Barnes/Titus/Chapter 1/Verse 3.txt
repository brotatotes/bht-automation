<p style=" "><b>But hath in due times - </b>At the proper time; the time which he had intended; the best time: see the notes at <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="1ti+2:6">1 Timothy 2:6</span>; compare the notes at <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="mt+2:2">Matthew 2:2</span>.</p>
<p style=" "><b>Manifested his word through preaching - </b>See the notes at <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="2ti+2:10">2 Timothy 2:10</span>. The meaning here is, that he has made known his eternal purpose through the preaching of the gospel; compare the notes at <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="ro+10:14-15">Romans 10:14-15</span>.</p>
<p style=" "><b>Which is committed unto me - </b>Not exclusively, but in common with others; see the notes at <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="2ti+1:11">2 Timothy 1:11</span>.</p>
<p style=" "><b>According to the commandment of God our Saviour - </b>Paul always claimed to be divinely commissioned, and affirmed that he was engaged in the work of preaching by the authority of God; see <span class="_0000ff"><u>Gal 1:1-12</u></span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="1co+1:1">1 Corinthians 1:1</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="ro+1:1-4">Romans 1:1-4</span>.</p>
