<p style=" "><b>Mine own son - </b>Notes, <span class="scriptRef" com="bnb" lang="eng" ref="1ti+1:2">1 Timothy 1:2</span>.</p>
<p style=" "><b>After the common faith - </b>The faith of all Christians; - equivalent to saying “my son in the gospel.” That is, Paul had been the means of converting him by preaching that gospel which was received by all who were Christians.</p>
