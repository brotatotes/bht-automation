 We write  (γραφομεν ημεις). Literary plural present active indicative of γραφω, which see in the singular in 1 John 2:12-14.May be fulfilled  (η πεπληρωμενη). Periphrastic perfect passive subjunctive of πληροω, stressing the state of completion in the purpose (ινα), remain full, precisely as in John 16:24. See aorist subjunctive in John 15:11 and perfect indicative in John 17:13. The MSS. differ as often between ημων (our) and υμων (your).
