 And if the righteous is scarcely saved  (κα ε ο δικαιος μολις σωζετα). First-class condition again with ε and present passive indicative of σωζω. Quotation from Proverbs 11:31. See 1 Peter 3:12; 1 Peter 3:14; Matthew 5:20. But the Christian is not saved by his own righteousness (Philippians 3:9; Revelation 7:14). For μολις see Acts 14:18 and for ασεβης (ungodly, without reverence) see Romans 4:5; 2 Peter 2:5.Will appear  (φανειτα). Future middle of φαινω, to show. For the question see Mark 10:24-26.
