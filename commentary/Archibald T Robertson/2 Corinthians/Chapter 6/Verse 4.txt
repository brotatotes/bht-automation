 But in everything commending ourselves  (αλλ' εν παντ συνιστανοντες εαυτους). Paul gives a marvellous summary of his argument about the dignity and glory of ministers of Christ asministers of God  (ως θεου διακονο) under three aspects, the first within  (εν) verses 2 Corinthians 6:3-7, the second withby  (δια) verses 2 Corinthians 6:7; 2 Corinthians 6:8, the third withas  (ως) verses 2 Corinthians 6:9-10. The negative view with εν we have in verse 2 Corinthians 6:3, then the positive in verses 2 Corinthians 6:4-7. Each word carries a story that can be filled in from Paul's own life as a preacher with an echo in that of us all.In distresses  (εν στενοχωριαις). In tight places (2 Corinthians 12:10). Late word from στενοχωρεω (see on 2 Corinthians 4:8).
