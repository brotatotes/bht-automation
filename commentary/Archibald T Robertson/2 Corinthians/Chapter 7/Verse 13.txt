 We joyed the more exceedingly  (περισσοτερως μαλλον εχαρημεν). Double comparative (pleonastic use of μαλλον, more, with περισσοτερως, more abundantly) as is common in the Koine (Mark 7:36; Philippians 1:23).For the joy of Titus  (επ τη χαρα Τιτου). On the basis of (επ) the joy of Titus who was proud of the outcome of his labours in Corinth.Hath been refreshed  (αναπεπαυτα). Perfect passive indicative of αναπαυω. Cf. 1 Corinthians 16:18 for this striking verb.
