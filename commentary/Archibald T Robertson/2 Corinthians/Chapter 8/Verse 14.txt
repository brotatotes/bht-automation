 By equality  (εξ ισοτητος). Old word from ισος, fair, equal. In N.T. only here and Colossians 4:1.Abundancy  (περισσευμα). Late word from περισσευω like περισσεια (verse 2 Corinthians 8:2) Cf. Matthew 12:34.Want  (υστερημα). Late word from υστερεω, to be in want. See also 2 Corinthians 9:12; Luke 21:4 (cf. υστερησις in Mark 12:44).
