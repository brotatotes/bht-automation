 We have sent with him  (συνεπεμψαμεν μετ' αυτου). Epistolary aorist.The brother  (τον αδελφον). This may be, probably is, Luke who may also be the brother of Titus (see also 2 Corinthians 12:18) according to a common Greek idiom where the article is used as "his." But this idiom is not necessary. As a matter of fact, we do not know who this brother is.Is spread through all the churches  (δια πασων των εκκλησιων). No verb in the Greek (ellipsis).
