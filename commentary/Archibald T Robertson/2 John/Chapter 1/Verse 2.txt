 For the truth's sake  (δια την αληθειαν). Repetition of the word, one of which John is very fond (1 John 1:6, "the truth, as revealed by the Christ, and gradually unfolded by the Spirit, who is truth" (Brooke).Which abideth in us  (την μενουσαν εν ημιν). See John 17:19 for "sanctified in truth" and 1 John 2:6 for abiding in Christ, and so it includes all who are in Christ.It shall be with us  (μεθ' ημων εστα). Confident assertion, not a mere wish. Note the order of the words, "With us it shall be" (εστα future middle of ειμ).
