 Temperance  (την εγκρατειαν). Self-control. Old word (from εγκρατης, εν and κρατος, one holding himself in as in Titus 1:8), in N.T. only here, Acts 24:25; Galatians 5:23. The opposite of the πλεονεξια of the heretics.Patience  (την υπομονην). For which see James 1:3.Godliness  (την ευσεβειαν). For which see verse 2 Peter 1:3.
