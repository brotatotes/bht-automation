 Whereas  (οπου). Loose use of οπου (in Xenophon) = "wherein."Though greater  (μειζονες οντες). Than the evil δοξα. Concessive participle and comparative adjective.In might and strength  (ισχυ κα δυναμε). Locative case. Both indwelling strength (ισχυς, Mark 12:30) and ability (δυναμις, Matthew 25:15).Railing judgment  (βλασφεμον κρισιν). "Blasphemous accusation."Against them  (κατ' αυτων). The evil angels (δοξα).Before the Lord  (παρα κυριω). In God's presence. See Jude 1:9 and possibly Enoch 9.
