 Ignorant  (απαιδευτους). Old verbal, here only in N.T. (α privative and παιδευω). Untrained, uneducated, "speculations of a half-educated mind" (Parry).Refuse  (παραιτου). See 1 Timothy 4:7.They gender strifes  (γεννωσιν μαχας). Present active indicative of old and common verb γενναω (Romans 9:11). "They beget battles." See 2 Timothy 2:14.
