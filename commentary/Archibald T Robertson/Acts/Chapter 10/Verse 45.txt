 They of the circumcision which believed  (ο εκ περιτομης πιστο). The believing ones of the circumcision, more exactly.Were amazed  (εξεστησαν). Second aorist active indicative, intransitive, of εξιστημ. They stood out of themselves.On the Gentiles also  (κα επ τα εθνη). Or, even upon the Gentiles.Was poured out  (εκκεχυτα). Present perfect passive retained in indirect discourse of εκχεω or εκχυνω, old verb, used metaphorically of the Holy Spirit also in Acts 2:17 (from Joel 2:28), Acts 2:33.
