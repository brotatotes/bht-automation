 They entered together  (κατα το αυτο εισελθειν). Like επ το αυτο in Acts 3:1. The infinitive εισελθειν is the subject of εγενετο.So spake that  (λαλησα ουτως ωστε). Infinitive again parallel to εισελθειν. With the result that, actual result here stated with ωστε and the aorist infinitive πιστευσα (Robertson, Grammar, pp. 999f.) rather than ωστε and the indicative like John 3:16. It was a tremendous first meeting.
