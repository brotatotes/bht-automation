 Being themselves also prophets  (κα αυτο προφητα οντες). As well as Paul and Barnabas and like Agabus (Acts 11:27-30), for-speakers for Christ who justify the commendation in the letter (verse Acts 15:27) "with many words" (δια λογου πολλου), "with much talk," and no doubt with kindly words concerning the part played at the Conference by Paul and Barnabas.Confirmed  (επεστηριξαν). See on Acts 14:22. It was a glorious time with no Judaizers to disturb their fellowship as in Acts 15:1-3.
