 Which knoweth the heart  (καρδιογνωστης). Late word from καρδια (heart) and γνωστης (known, γινωσκω). In the N.T. only here and Acts 1:24 which see.Giving them the Holy Spirit  (δους το πνευμα το αγιον). And before their baptism. This was the Lord's doing. They had accepted (Acts 11:18) this witness of God then and it was true now of these other Gentile converts.
