 When he had so said  (τουτο αυτου λαλουντος). Genitive absolute of present participle (Westcott and Hort) rather than aorist (ειποντος). While he was saying this.A dissension  (στασις). This old word for standing or station (Hebrews 9:8) from ιστημ, to place, we have seen already to mean insurrection (Acts 19:40 which see). Here it is strife as in Acts 15:2.Was divided  (εσχισθη). See on Acts 14:4.
