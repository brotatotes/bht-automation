 But we  (εμεις δε). In contrast to the work given the seven.The ministry of the word  (τη διακονια του λογου). The same word διακονια employed in verse Acts 6:1, but here about preaching as the special ministry with which the apostles were concerned. For "continue steadfastly" (προσκαρτερησομεν) see on Acts 2:42.
