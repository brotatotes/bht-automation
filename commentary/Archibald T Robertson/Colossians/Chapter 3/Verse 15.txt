 The peace of Christ  (η ειρηνη του Χριστου). The peace that Christ gives (John 14:27).Rule  (βραβευετω). Imperative active third singular of βραβευω, to act as umpire (βραβευς), old verb, here alone in N.T. See 1 Corinthians 7:15 for called in peace.In one body  (εν εν σωματ). With one Head (Christ) as in Colossians 1:18; Colossians 1:24.Be ye thankful  (ευχαριστο γινεσθε). "Keep on becoming thankful." Continuous obligation.
