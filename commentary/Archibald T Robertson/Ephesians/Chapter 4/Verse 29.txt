 Corrupt  (σαπρος). Rotten, putrid, like fruit (Matthew 7:17), fish (Matthew 13:48), here the opposite of αγαθος (good).For edifying as the need may be  (προς οικοδομην της χρειας). "For the build-up of the need," "for supplying help when there is need." Let no other words come out.That it may give  (ινα δω). For this elliptical use of ινα see on Ephesians 5:33.
