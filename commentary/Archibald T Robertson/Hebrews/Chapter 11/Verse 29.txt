 Which assaying to do  (ης πιεραν λαβοντες). Literally, "of which taking trial" (second aorist active participle of λαμβανω, to take). The idiom πειραν λαμβανειν occurs in Deuteronomy 28:56, in N.T. only here and verse Hebrews 11:36, though a classical idiom (Demosthenes, etc.).Were swallowed up  (κατεποθησαν). First aorist passive indicative of καταπινω, to drink down, to swallow down (Matthew 23:24).
