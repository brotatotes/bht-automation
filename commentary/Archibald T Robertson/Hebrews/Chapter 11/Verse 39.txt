 These all  (ουτο παντες). The whole list in verses Hebrews 11:5-38. Cf. verse Hebrews 11:13.Through their faith  (δια πιστεως). Here rather than πιστε as so often.Received not the promise  (ουκ εκομισαντο την επαγγελιαν). First aorist middle of κομιζω. The Messianic promise they did not live to see (Hebrews 11:13), though they had individual special promises fulfilled as already shown (Hebrews 11:33).
