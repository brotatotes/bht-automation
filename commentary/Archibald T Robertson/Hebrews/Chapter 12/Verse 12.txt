 Wherefore  (διο). Because of the chastening.Lift up  (ανορθωσατε). First aorist active imperative of ανορθοω, old compound (from ανα, ορθος) to make straight, in N.T. here and Luke 13:13; Acts 15:16.Hang down  (παρειμενας). Perfect passive participle of παριημ, old verb to let pass, to relax, in N.T. only here and Luke 11:42.Palsied  (παραλελυμενα). Perfect passive participle of παραλυω, old verb to loosen on the side, to dissolve, to paralyze (Luke 5:18; Luke 5:24).
