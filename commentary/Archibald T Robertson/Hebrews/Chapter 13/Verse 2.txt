 As bound with them  (ως συνδεδεμενο). Perfect passive participle of συνδεω, old verb, here only in N.T. For sympathy with prisoners see Hebrews 10:34.As being yourselves also in the body  (ως κα αυτο οντες εν σωματ). And so subject to evil treatment. See Hebrews 11:37 for κακουχεω and Hebrews 11:25 for συνκακουχεω.
