 How great  (πηλικος). Geometrical magnitude in contrast to arithmetical (ποσος), here only in N.T., "how distinguished." He received tithes from Abraham (verses Hebrews 7:4-6) and he blessed Abraham (Hebrews 7:6) and even Levi is included (verses Hebrews 7:8-10).Out of the chief spoils  (εκ των ακροθινιων). Old word from ακρος, top, and θις, a heap (the top of the pile).Patriarch  (πατριαρχης). LXX word (πατρια, tribe, αρχω, to rule) transferred to N.T. (Acts 2:29).
