 To him that knoweth  (ειδοτ). Dative case of second perfect participle ειδως (from οιδα), and with the infinitive to know how, "to one knowing how."To do good  (καλον ποιειν). "To do a good deed."And doeth it not  (κα μη ποιουντ). Dative again of the present active participle of ποιεω, "and to one not doing it." Cf. "not a doer" (James 1:23) and Matthew 7:26.Sin  (αμαρτια). Unused knowledge of one's duty is sin, the sin of omission. Cf. Matthew 23:23.
