 Again on the morrow  (τη επαυριον παλιν). Third day since verse John 1:19.Was standing  (ιστηκε). Past perfect of ιστημ, intransitive, and used as imperfect in sense. See same form in John 7:37.Two  (δυο). One was Andrew (verse John 1:40), the other the Beloved Disciple (the Apostle John), who records this incident with happy memories.
