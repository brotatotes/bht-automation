 If any man serve me  (εαν εμο τις διακονη). Condition of third class again (εαν with present active subjunctive of διακονεω, keep on serving with dative εμο).Let him follow me  (εμο ακολουθειτω). "Me (associative instrumental case) let him keep on following" (present active imperative of ακολουθεω).Where ... there  (οπου ... εκε). In presence and spiritual companionship here and hereafter. Cf. John 14:3; John 17:24; Matthew 28:20.Shall honour  (τιμησε). Future active of τιμαω, but it may be the kind of honour that Jesus will get (verse John 12:23).
