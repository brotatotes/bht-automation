 A little while  (μικρον). The brief period now till Christ's death as in John 7:33; John 13:33; John 14:19.Again a little while  (παλιν μικρον). The period between the death and the resurrection of Jesus (from Friday afternoon till Sunday morning).Ye shall see me  (οψεσθε με). Future middle of οπτομα, the verb used in John 1:51; John 16:22 as here of spiritual realities (Bernard), though θεωρεω is so used in John 20:14.
