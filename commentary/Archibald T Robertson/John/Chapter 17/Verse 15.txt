 Shouldest take  (αρηις). First aorist active subjunctive of αιρω (liquid verb).From the evil one  (εκ του πονηρου). Ablative case with εκ, but can mean the evil man, Satan, or the evil deed. See same ambiguity in Matthew 6:13. But in 1 John 5:18 ο πονηρος is masculine (the evil one). Cf. Revelation 3:10.
