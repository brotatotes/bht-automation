 Was standing  (ιστηκε). Same form in verse John 18:5 which see. So also ιστηκεισαν in John 18:18. Picture of Peter standing outside by the door.Unto the high priest  (του αρχιερεως). Objective genitive here, but dative in verse John 18:15.Unto her that kept the door  (τη θυρωρω). Old word (θυρα, door, ωρα, care), masculine in John 10:3, feminine here, door-keeper (male or female).
