 A garden  (κηπος). See John 18:1; John 18:26.New  (καινον). Fresh, unused.Was never yet laid  (ουδεπω ην τεθειμενος). Periphrastic past perfect passive of τιθημ. It was Joseph's mausoleum, a rock tomb hewn out of the mountain side (Mark 15:56; Matthew 27:60; Luke 23:53), a custom common with the rich then and now. For royal tombs in gardens see 2 Kings 21:18; 2 Kings 21:26; Nehemiah 3:16.
