 Was standing  (ιστηκε). Past perfect of ιστημ as imperfect as in John 19:25.At the tomb  (προς τω μνημειω). Προς (in front of) with locative while παρα (by the side of) with locative in John 19:25. Pathetic and common picture of a woman weeping by the tomb. See John 11:31.As she wept  (ως εκλαιεν). Imperfect, "as she was weeping."She stooped and looked  (παρεκυψεν). Aorist active indicative of παρακυπτω for which see verse John 20:5. Mary "peeped into" the tomb, but did not enter.
