 But he answered  (ος δε απεκριθη). Demonstrative ος (But this one) and deponent use of απεκριθη (first aorist passive indicative of αποκρινομα with no passive force).The same  (εκεινος). "That one," emphatic demonstrative as often in John (John 1:18; John 1:33; John 9:37; John 10:1, etc.). The man did not know who Jesus was nor even his name. He quotes the very words of Jesus.Whole  (υγιη). Predicate accusative agreeing with με (me).
