 They were willing therefore  (ηθελον ουν). Inchoative imperfect, "they began to be willing." This does not contradict Mark 6:51 as Bernard thinks. Both Jesus and Peter climbed into the boat.Whither they were going  (εις ην υπηγον). Progressive imperfect active, "to which land they had been going" (intransitive use of υπαγω, to lead under, to go under or away as in verse John 6:67; John 7:33; John 12:11; John 18:8.
