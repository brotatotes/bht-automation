 Thou hast both seen him  (κα εωρακας αυτον). Perfect active indicative (double reduplication) of οραω. Since his eyes were opened.And he it is that speaketh with thee  (κα ο λαλων μετα σου εκεινος εστιν). "And the one speaking with thee is that man." See John 19:35 for εκεινος used of the speaker. In John 4:26 Jesus reveals himself in like manner to the Samaritan woman as Messiah while here as the Son of Man (or the Son of God).
