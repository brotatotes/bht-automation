 Tender mercy  (σπλαγχνα ελεους). Bowels of mercy literally (1 Peter 3:8; James 3:11). Revised margin has it, hearts of mercy.The dayspring from on high  (ανατολη εξ υψους). Literally, rising from on high, like the rising sun or stars (Isaiah 60:19). The word is used also of a sprouting plant or branch (Jeremiah 23:5; Zechariah 6:12), but that does not suit here.Shall visit  (επεσκεψετα), correct text, cf. Luke 1:68.
