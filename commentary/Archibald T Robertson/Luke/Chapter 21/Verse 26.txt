 Men fainting  (αποψυχοντων ανθρωπων). Genitive absolute of αποψυχω, to expire, to breathe off or out. Old word. Here only in N.T.Expectation  (προσδοκιας). Old word from προσδοκαω, to look for or towards. In the N.T. only here and Acts 12:11.The world  (τη οικουμενη). Dative case, "the inhabited" (earth, γη).
