 Look up  (ανακυψατε). First aorist active imperative of ανακυπτω, to raise up. Here of the soul as in John 8:7; John 8:10, but in Luke 13:11 of the body. These the only N.T. examples of this common verb.Redemption  (απολυτρωσις). Act of redeeming from απολυτροω. The final act at the second coming of Christ, a glorious hope.
