 As for these things  (ταυτα). Accusative of general reference.One stone upon another  (λιθος επ λιθω). Stone upon stone (locative). Here both Mark 13:2; Matthew 24:2 have επ λιθον (accusative). Instead of ουκ αφεθησετα (future passive) they both have ου μη αφεθη (double negative with aorist passive subjunctive). It was a shock to the disciples to hear this after the triumphal entry.
