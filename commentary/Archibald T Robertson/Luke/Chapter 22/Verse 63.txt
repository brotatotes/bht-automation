 That held  (ο συνεχοντες). See on Luke 8:45; Luke 19:43 for this verb συνεχω. Here alone in the N.T. for holding a prisoner (holding together). The servants or soldiers, not the Sanhedrin.Mocked  (ενεπαιζον). Imperfect active, were mocking, inchoative, began to mock, to play like boys.And beat him  (δεροντες). Present active participle of δερω, to flay, tan, or hide. Literally, "beating."
