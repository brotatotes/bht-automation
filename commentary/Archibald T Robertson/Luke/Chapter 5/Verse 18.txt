 That was palsied  (ος ην παραλελυμενος). Periphrastic past perfect passive where Mark 2:3; Matthew 9:2 have παραλυτικον (our paralytic). Luke's phrase is the technical medical term (Hippocrates, Galen, etc.) rather than Mark's vernacular word (Ramsay, Luke the Physician, pp. 57f.).They sought  (εζητουν). Conative imperfect.
