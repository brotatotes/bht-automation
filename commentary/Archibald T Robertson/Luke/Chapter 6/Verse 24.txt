 But woe unto you that are rich  (Πλην ουα υμιν τοις πλουσιοις). Sharp contrast (πλην). As a matter of fact the rich Pharisees and Sadducees were the chief opposers of Christ as of the early disciples later (James 5:1-6).Ye have received  (απεχετε). Receipt in full απεχω means as the papyri show.Consolation  (παρακλησιν). From παρακαλεω, to call to one's side, to encourage, to help, to cheer.
