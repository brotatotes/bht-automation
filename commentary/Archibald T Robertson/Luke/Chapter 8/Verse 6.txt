 Upon the rock  (επ την πετραν). Mark 4:5 "the rocky ground" (επ το πετρωδες), Matthew 13:5 "the rocky places.As soon as it grew  (φυεν). Second aorist passive participle of φυω, an old verb to spring up like a sprout.Withered away  (εξηρανθη). First aorist passive indicative of ζηραινω, old verb, to dry up.Moisture  (ικμαδα). Here only in the N.T., though common word.
