 Looking on them  (εμβλεψας αυτοις). So in Matthew 19:26. Their amazement increased (Mark 10:26).But not with God  (αλλ' ου παρα θεω). Locative case with παρα (beside). The impossible by the side of men (παρα ανθρωποις) becomes possible by the side of God. That is the whole point and brushes to one side all petty theories of a gate called needle's eye, etc.
