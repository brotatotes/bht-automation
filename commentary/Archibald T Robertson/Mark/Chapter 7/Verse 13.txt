 Making void the word of God by your tradition  (ακυρουντες τον λογον του θεου τη παραδοσε υμων). See on Matthew 15:6 for the word ακυρουντες, invalidating, a stronger word than αθετειν, to set aside, in verse Mark 7:9. See both used in Galatians 3:15; Galatians 3:17. Setting aside does invalidate.
