 Bring him unto me  (φερετε αυτον προς με). The disciples had failed and their unbelief had led to this fiasco. Even the disciples were like and part of thefaithless  (απιστος, unbelieving) generation in which they lived. The wordfaithless  does not here mean treacherous as it does with us. But Jesus is not afraid to undertake this case. We can always come to Jesus when others fail us.
