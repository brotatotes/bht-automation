 Was speechless  (εψιμωθη). Was muzzled, dumb from confusion and embarrassment. It is used of the ox (1 Timothy 5:18).The outer darkness  (το σκοτος το εξωτερον). See Matthew 8:12. All the blacker from the standpoint of the brilliantly lighted banquet hall.There shall be  (εκε εστα). Out there in the outer darkness.
