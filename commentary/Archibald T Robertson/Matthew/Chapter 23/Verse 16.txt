 Ye blind guides  (οδηγο τυφλο). Note omission of "Scribes and Pharisees, hypocrites" with this third woe. In Matthew 15:14 Jesus had already called the Pharisees "blind guides" (leaders). They split hairs about oaths, as Jesus had explained in Matthew 5:33-37, between the temple and the gold of the temple.He is a debtor  (οφειλε). He owes his oath, is bound by his oath. A.V.,is guilty , is old English, obsolete sense of guilt as fine or payment.
