 Is past  (απηλθεν). Second aorist active indicative of απερχομα. See Revelation 9:12 for this use and Revelation 21:1; Revelation 21:4. The second woe (η ουα η δευτερα) is the sixth trumpet (Revelation 9:12) with the two episodes attached (Revelation 10:1-11).The third woe  (η ουα η τριτη, feminine as in Revelation 9:12) is the seventh trumpet, which now "cometh quickly" (ερχετα ταχυ), for which phrase see Revelation 2:16; Revelation 3:11; Revelation 22:7; Revelation 22:12; Revelation 22:20. Usually pointing to the Parousia.
