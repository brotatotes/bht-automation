 He shall rule  (ποιμανε). Future active of ποιμαινω, to shepherd (from ποιμην, shepherd), also from Psalms 2:8. See again Revelation 7:17; Revelation 12:5; Revelation 19:15.With a rod of iron  (εν ραβδω σιδηρα). Continuing the quotation. Instrumental use of εν. Ραβδος (feminine) is the royal sceptre and indicates rigorous rule.The vessels of the potter  (τα σκευη τα κεραμικα). Old adjective, belonging to a potter (κεραμευσ, κεραμος), here only in N.T.Are broken to shivers  (συντριβετα). Present passive indicative of συντριβω, old verb, to rub together, to break in pieces (Mark 14:3).
