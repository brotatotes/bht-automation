 As I also have received  (ως καγω ειληφα). Perfect active indicative of λαμβανω. Christ still possesses the power from the Father (Acts 2:33; Psalms 2:7).The morning star  (τον αστερα τον πρωινον). "The star the morning one." In Revelation 22:16 Christ is the bright morning star. The victor will have Christ himself.
