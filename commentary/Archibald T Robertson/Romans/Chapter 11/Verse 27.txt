 My covenant  (η παρ' εμου διαθηκη). "The from me covenant," "my side of the covenant I have made with them" (Sanday and Headlam). Cf. Jeremiah 31:31. Not a political deliverance, but a religious and ethical one.When I shall take away  (οταν αφελωμα). Second aorist middle subjunctive of αφαιρεω, old and common verb, to take away.
