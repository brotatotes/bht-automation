 Who hath known?  (τις εγνω?). Second aorist active indicative of γινωσκω, a timeless aorist, did know, does know, will know. Quotation from Isaiah 40:13. Quoted already in 1 Corinthians 2:16.Counsellor  (συμβουλος). Old word from συν and βουλη. Only here in N.T.His  (αυτου). Objective genitive, counsellor to him (God). Some men seem to feel competent for the job.
