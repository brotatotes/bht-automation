 Of him  (εξ αυτου),through him  (δι' αυτου),unto him  (εις αυτον). By these three prepositions Paul ascribes the universe (τα παντα) with all the phenomena concerning creation, redemption, providence to God as theSource  (εξ), theAgent  (δ), theGoal  (εις).For ever  (εις τους αιωνας). "For the ages." Alford terms this doxology in verses Romans 11:33-36 "the sublimest apostrophe existing even in the pages of inspiration itself."
