Because of meat (δια βρωμα). "Because of food."In love  (κατα αγαπην). "According to love" as the regulating principle of life. See Romans 14:1 where Paul pleads for love in place of knowledge on this point.Destroy not  (μη απολλυε). Present active imperative of απολλυω, the very argument made in 1 Corinthians 8:10.With thy meat  (τω βρωματ σου). Instrumental case, "with thy food." It is too great a price to pay for personal liberty as to food.
