 But also glorying in God  (αλλα κα καυχωμενο εν τω θεω). Basis of all the exultation above (verses Romans 5:1-5).Through whom we have now received the reconciliation  (δ ου νυν την καταλλαγην ελαβομεν). Second aorist active indicative of λαμβανω, looked at as a past realization, "now" (νυν) in contrast with the future consummation and a sure pledge and guarantee of it.
