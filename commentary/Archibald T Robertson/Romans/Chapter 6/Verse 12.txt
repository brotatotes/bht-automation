 Reign  (βασιλευετω). Present active imperative, "let not sin continue to reign" as it did once (Romans 5:12).Mortal  (θνητο). Verbal adjective from θνησκω, subject to death. The reign of sin is over with you. Self-indulgence is inconsistent with trust in the vicarious atonement.That ye should obey  (εις το υπακουειν). With a view to obeying.
