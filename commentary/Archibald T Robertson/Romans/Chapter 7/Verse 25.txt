 I thank God  (χαρις τω θεω). "Thanks to God." Note of victory over death through Jesus Christ our Lord."So then I myself  (αρα ουν αυτος εγω). His whole self in his unregenerate state gives a divided service as he has already shown above. In Romans 6:1-7 Paul proved the obligation to be sanctified. In Romans 7:7-8 he discusses the possibility of sanctification, only for the renewed man by the help of the Holy Spirit.
