 Hath said before  (προειρηκεν). Perfect active indicative of προειπον (defective verb). Stands on record in Isaiah 1:9.Had left  (εγκατελιπεν). Second aorist active indicative of old verb εγκαταλειπω, to leave behind. Condition of second class, determined as unfulfilled, with αν εγενηθημεν and αν ωμοιωθημεν as the conclusions (both first aorist passives of γινομα and ομοιοω, common verbs).A seed  (σπερμα). The remnant of verse Romans 9:27.
