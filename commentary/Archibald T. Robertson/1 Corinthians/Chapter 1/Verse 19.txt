<p><b> I will destroy </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">απολω</span></span>). Future active indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">απολλυμ</span></span>. Attic future for <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">απολεσω</span></span>. Quotation from <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="isa+29:14">Isaiah 29:14</span> (LXX). The failure of worldly statesmanship in the presence of Assyrian invasion Paul applies to his argument with force. The wisdom of the wise is often folly, the understanding of the understanding is often rejected. There is such a thing as the ignorance of the learned, the wisdom of the simple-minded. God's wisdom rises in the Cross sheer above human philosophizing which is still scoffing at the Cross of Christ, the consummation of God's power. </p>
