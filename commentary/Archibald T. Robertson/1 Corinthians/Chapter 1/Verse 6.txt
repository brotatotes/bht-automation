<p><b> Even as </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">καθως</span></span>). In proportion as (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1th+1:5">1 Thessalonians 1:5</span>) and so inasmuch as (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="php+1:7">Philippians 1:7</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="eph+1:4">Ephesians 1:4</span>).</p>
<p><b>The testimony of Christ </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">το μαρτυριον του Χριστου</span></span>). Objective genitive, the testimony to or concerning Christ, the witness of Paul's preaching.</p>
<p><b>Was confirmed in you </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εβεβαιωθη εν υμιν</span></span>). First aorist passive of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">βεβαιοω</span></span>, old verb from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">βεβαιος</span></span> and that from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">βαινω</span></span>, to make to stand, to make stable. These special gifts of the Holy Spirit which they had so lavishly received (ch. <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1co+1:1">1 Corinthians 1:1</span>) were for that very purpose. </p>
