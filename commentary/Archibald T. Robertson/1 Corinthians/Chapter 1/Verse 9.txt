<p><b> God is faithful </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πιστος ο θεος</span></span>). This is the ground of Paul's confidence as he loves to say (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1th+5:24">1 Thessalonians 5:24</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1co+10:13">1 Corinthians 10:13</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+8:36">Romans 8:36</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="php+1:16">Philippians 1:16</span>). God will do what he has promised.</p>
<p><b>Through whom </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δι' ου</span></span>). God is the agent (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δι'</span></span>) of their call as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+11:36">Romans 11:36</span> and also the ground or reason for their call (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δι' ον</span></span>) in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="heb+2:10">Hebrews 2:10</span>.</p>
<p><b>Into the fellowship </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εις κοινωνιαν</span></span>). Old word from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κοινωνος</span></span>, partner for partnership, participation as here and <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2co+13:13">2 Corinthians 13:13</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="php+2:1">Philippians 2:1</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="php+3:10">Philippians 3:10</span>. Then it means fellowship or intimacy as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+2:42">Acts 2:42</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ga+2:9">Galatians 2:9</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2co+6:14">2 Corinthians 6:14</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1jo+1:3">1 John 1:3</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1jo+1:7">1 John 1:7</span>. And particularly as shown by contribution as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2co+8:4">2 Corinthians 8:4</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2co+9:13">2 Corinthians 9:13</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="php+1:5">Philippians 1:5</span>. It is high fellowship with Christ both here and hereafter. </p>
