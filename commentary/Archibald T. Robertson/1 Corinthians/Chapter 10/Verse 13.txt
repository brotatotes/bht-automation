 Hath taken  (ειληφεν). Perfect active indicative of λαμβανω.But such as man can bear  (ε μη ανθρωπινος). Except a human one. Old adjective meaning falling to the lot of man.Above that ye are able  (υπερ ο δυνασθε). Ellipsis, but plain. There is comfort in that God is faithful, trustworthy (πιστος).The way of escape  (την εκβασιν). "The way out" is always there right along with (συν) the temptation. This old word only here in N.T. and Hebrews 13:7 about death. It is cowardly to yield to temptation and distrustful of God.
