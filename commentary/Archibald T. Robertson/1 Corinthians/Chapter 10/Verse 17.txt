<p><b> One bread </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εις αρτος</span></span>). One loaf.</p>
<p><b>Who are many </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ο πολλο</span></span>). The many.</p>
<p><b>We all </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ο παντες</span></span>). We the all, the whole number, <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ο παντες</span></span> being in apposition with the subject</p>
<p><b>we </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ημεις</span></span> unexpressed).</p>
<p><b>Partake </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μετεχομεν</span></span>). Have a part with or in, share in. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1co+9:12">1 Corinthians 9:12</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="heb+2:14">Hebrews 2:14</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="heb+5:13">Hebrews 5:13</span> (partaking of milk).</p>
<p><b>Of the one bread </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">του ενος αρτου</span></span>). Of the one loaf, the article <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">του</span></span> referring to one loaf already mentioned.</p>
<p><b>One body </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εν σωμα</span></span>). Here the mystical spiritual body of Christ as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1co+12:12">1 Corinthians 12:12</span>, the spiritual kingdom or church of which Christ is head (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="col+1:18">Colossians 1:18</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="eph+5:23">Ephesians 5:23</span>). </p>
