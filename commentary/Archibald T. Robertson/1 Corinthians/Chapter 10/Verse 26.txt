<p>This verse gives the reason for Paul's advice. It is a quotation from <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ps+24:1">Psalms 24:1</span> and was a common form of grace before meals.</p>
<p><b>Fulness </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πληρωμα</span></span>). Old word from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πληροω</span></span>, to fill, here that with which a thing is filled, whatever fills the earth. </p>
