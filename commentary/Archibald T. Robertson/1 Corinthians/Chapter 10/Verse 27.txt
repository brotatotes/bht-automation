<p><b> Biddeth you </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">καλε υμας</span></span>). To a general banquet, but not to a temple feast (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1co+8:10">1 Corinthians 8:10</span>) which is prohibited. If a pagan invites Christians to their homes to a banquet, one is to act like a gentleman. </p>
