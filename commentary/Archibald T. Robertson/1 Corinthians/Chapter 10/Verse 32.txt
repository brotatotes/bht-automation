<p><b> Give no occasion of stumbling </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">απροσκοπο</span></span>). Late word and in papyri, only three times in N.T. (here; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="php+1:10">Philippians 1:10</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+24:16">Acts 24:16</span>). See on <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+24:16">Acts 24:16</span>. Here in active sense, not tripping others by being a stumbling-block, as in Sirach 32:21, but passive in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+24:16">Acts 24:16</span>. </p>
