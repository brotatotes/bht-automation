 Mine own profit  (το εμουτου συμφερον). Old word from συμφερω, to bear together, and explains use of verb in verse 1 Corinthians 10:23.That they may be saved  (ινα σωθωσιν). First aorist passive subjunctive of σωζω, to save, with ινα purpose clause with same high motive as in 1 Corinthians 9:22. This is the ruling passion of Paul in his dealings with men.
