<p><b> Mine own profit </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">το εμουτου συμφερον</span></span>). Old word from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">συμφερω</span></span>, to bear together, and explains use of verb in verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1co+10:23">1 Corinthians 10:23</span>.</p>
<p><b>That they may be saved </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ινα σωθωσιν</span></span>). First aorist passive subjunctive of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">σωζω</span></span>, to save, with <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ινα</span></span> purpose clause with same high motive as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1co+9:22">1 Corinthians 9:22</span>. This is the ruling passion of Paul in his dealings with men. </p>
