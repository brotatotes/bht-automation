<p><b> Howbeit </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πλην</span></span>). This adversative clause limits the preceding statement. Each sex is incomplete without (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">χωρις</span></span>, apart from, with the ablative case) the other.</p>
<p><b>In the Lord </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εν Κυριω</span></span>). In the sphere of the Lord, where Paul finds the solution of all problems. </p>
