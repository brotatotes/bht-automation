<p><b> Of </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εκ</span></span>)</p>
<p><b>--by </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δια</span></span>). Ever since the first creation man has come into existence by means of (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δια</span></span> with genitive) the woman. The glory and dignity of motherhood. Cf. <i>The Fine Art of Motherhood</i> by Ella Broadus Robertson. </p>
