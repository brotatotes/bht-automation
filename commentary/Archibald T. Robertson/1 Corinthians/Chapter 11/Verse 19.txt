<p><b> Must be </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δε εινα</span></span>). Since moral conditions are so bad among you (cf. chapters 1 to 6). Cf. <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+18:7">Matthew 18:7</span>.</p>
<p><b>Heresies </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αιρεσεις</span></span>). The schisms naturally become</p>
<p><b>factions </b> or</p>
<p><b>parties </b>. Cf. strifes (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εριδες</span></span>) in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1co+1:11">1 Corinthians 1:11</span>. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+15:5">Acts 15:5</span> for <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αιρεσεις</span></span>, a choosing, taking sides, holding views of one party, heresy (our word). "Heresy is theoretical schism, schism practical heresy." Cf. <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="tit+3:10">Titus 3:10</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2pe+2:1">2 Peter 2:1</span>. In Paul only here and <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ga+5:20">Galatians 5:20</span>.</p>
<p><b>That </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ινα</span></span>). God's purpose in these factions makes</p>
<p><b>the proved ones </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ο δοκιμο</span></span>) become</p>
<p><b>manifest </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">φανερο</span></span>). "These <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αιρεσεις</span></span> are a magnet attracting unsound and unsettled minds" (Findlay). It has always been so. Instance so-called Christian Science, Russellism, New Thought, etc., today. </p>
