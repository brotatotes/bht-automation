<p><b> Wait one for another </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αλληλους εκδεχεσθε</span></span>). As in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+5:3">John 5:3</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+17:16">Acts 17:16</span>. That is common courtesy. Wait in turn. Vulgate has <i>invicem expectate</i>. </p>
