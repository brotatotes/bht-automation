<p><b> For the woman </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δια την γυναικα</span></span>). Because of (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δια</span></span> with accusative case) the woman. The record in Genesis gives the man (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ανηρ</span></span>) as the origin (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εκ</span></span>) of the woman and the reason for (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δια</span></span>) the creation (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εκτισθη</span></span>, first aorist passive of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κτιζω</span></span>, old verb to found, to create, to form) of woman. </p>
