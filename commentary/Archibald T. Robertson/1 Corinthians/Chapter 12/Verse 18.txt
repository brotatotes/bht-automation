<p><b> But now </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">νυν δε</span></span>). But as things are, in contrast to that absurdity.</p>
<p><b>Hath God set </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ο θεος εθετο</span></span>). Second aorist middle indicative. God did it and of himself.</p>
<p><b>Even as it pleased him </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">καθως ηθελησεν</span></span>). Why challenge God's will? Cf. <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+9:20">Romans 9:20</span>. </p>
