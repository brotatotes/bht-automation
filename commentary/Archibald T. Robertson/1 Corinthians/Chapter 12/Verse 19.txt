<p><b> One member </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εν μελος</span></span>). Paul applies the logic of verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1co+12:17">1 Corinthians 12:17</span> to any member of the body. The application to members of the church is obvious. It is particularly pertinent in the case of a "church boss." </p>
