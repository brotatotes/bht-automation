<p><b> Diversities </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">διαιρεσεις</span></span>). Old word for distinctions, differences, distributions, from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">διαιρεω</span></span>, to distribute, as <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">διαιρουν</span></span> (dividing, distributing) in verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1co+12:11">1 Corinthians 12:11</span>. Only here in the N.T.</p>
<p><b>Of gifts </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">χαρισματων</span></span>). Late word and chiefly in Paul (cf. <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+12:6">Romans 12:6</span>) in N.T. (except <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1pe+4:19">1 Peter 4:19</span>), but some examples in papyri. It means a favour (from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">χαριζομα</span></span>) bestowed or received without any merit as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+1:11">Romans 1:11</span>. </p>
