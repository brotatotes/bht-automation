<p><b> Of ministrations </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">διακονιων</span></span>). This old word is from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">διακονος</span></span> and has a general meaning of service as here (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+11:13">Romans 11:13</span>) and a special ministration like that of Martha (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+10:40">Luke 10:40</span>) and the collection (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1co+16:15">1 Corinthians 16:15</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2co+8:4">2 Corinthians 8:4</span>). </p>
