<p><b> Manifestation </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">φανερωσις</span></span>). Late word, in papyri, in N.T. only here and <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2co+4:2">2 Corinthians 4:2</span>, from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">φανεροω</span></span>, to make manifest (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">φανερος</span></span>). Each instance of the whole (verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1co+12:6">1 Corinthians 12:6</span>) is repeatedly given (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">διδοτα</span></span>, present passive indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">διδωμ</span></span>).</p>
<p><b>To profit withal </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">προς το συμφερον</span></span>). See on <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1co+6:12">1 Corinthians 6:12</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1co+10:23">1 Corinthians 10:23</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1co+10:33">1 Corinthians 10:33</span> for Paul's guiding principle in such matters. </p>
