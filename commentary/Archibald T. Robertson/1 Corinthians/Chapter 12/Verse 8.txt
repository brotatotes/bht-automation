<p><b> To one </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ω μεν</span></span>). Demonstrative <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ος</span></span> with <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μεν</span></span> in dative case, to this one. The distribution or correlation is carried on by <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αλλω δε</span></span> (verses <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1co+12:8">1 Corinthians 12:8</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1co+12:9">1 Corinthians 12:9</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1co+12:10">1 Corinthians 12:10</span>), <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ετερω δε</span></span> (verses <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1co+12:9">1 Corinthians 12:9</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1co+12:10">1 Corinthians 12:10</span>) for variety, nine manifestations of the Spirit's work in verses <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1co+12:8-10">1 Corinthians 12:8-10</span>.</p>
<p><b>The Word of wisdom </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">λογος σοφιας</span></span>). Old words. <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">Λογος</span></span> is reason, then speech. Wisdom is intelligence, then practical action in accord with it. Here it is speech full of God's wisdom (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1co+2:7">1 Corinthians 2:7</span>) under the impulse of the Spirit of God. This gift is placed first (revelation by the Spirit).</p>
<p><b>The word of knowledge </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">λογος γνωσεως</span></span>). This gift is insight (illumination) according to (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κατα</span></span>) the same Spirit. </p>
