 It may be  (ε τυχο). Condition of fourth class (ε and aorist optative of τυγχανω), if it should happen. Common enough idiom. Cf. τυχον in 1 Corinthians 16:6.Without signification  (αφωνον). Old adjective (α privative and φωνη). Without the faculty of speech (1 Corinthians 12:2; Acts 8:32; 2 Peter 2:16).
