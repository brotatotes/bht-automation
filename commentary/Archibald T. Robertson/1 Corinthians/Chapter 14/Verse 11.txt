<p><b> The meaning of the voice </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">την δυναμιν της φωνης</span></span>). The power (force) of the voice.</p>
<p><b>A barbarian </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">βαρβαρος</span></span>). Jargon, <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">βαρ-βαρ</span></span>. The Egyptians called all <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">βαρβαρους</span></span> who did not speak their tongue. The Greeks followed suit for all ignorant of Greek language and culture. They divided mankind into Hellenes and Barbarians.</p>
<p><b>Unto me </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εν εμο</span></span>). In my case, almost like a dative. </p>
