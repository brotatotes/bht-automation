<p><b> Howbeit in church </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αλλα εν εκκλησια</span></span>). Private ecstasy is one thing (cf. <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2co+12:1-9">2 Corinthians 12:1-9</span>) but not in church worship.</p>
<p><b>That I may instruct </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ινα κατηχησω</span></span>). Final clause with <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ινα</span></span>. For the rare verb <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κατηχεω</span></span> see on <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+1:4">Luke 1:4</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+18:25">Acts 18:25</span>. </p>
