<p><b> For no man understandeth </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ουδεις γαρ ακουε</span></span>). Literally, hears, gets the sense, understands. Verb <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ακουω</span></span> used either of hearing the sound only or getting the idea (cf. <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+9:7">Acts 9:7</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+22:9">Acts 22:9</span>).</p>
<p><b>Mysteries </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μυστηρια</span></span>). Unexplained mysteries (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1co+2:7">1 Corinthians 2:7</span>). </p>
