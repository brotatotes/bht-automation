<p><b> But if there be no interpreter </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εαν δε μη η διερμηνευτης</span></span>). Third class condition. Earliest known instance and possibly made by Paul from verb in verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1co+14:27">1 Corinthians 14:27</span>. Reappears in Byzantine grammarians.</p>
<p><b>Keep silence in church </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">σιγατω εν εκκλησια</span></span>). Linear action (present active imperative). He is not even to speak in a tongue once. He can indulge his private ecstasy with God. </p>
