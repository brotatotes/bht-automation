<p><b> Edification </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">οικοδομην</span></span>). Building up.</p>
<p><b>Comfort </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">παρακλησιν</span></span>). Encouragement, calling to one's side.</p>
<p><b>Consolation </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">παραμυθιαν</span></span>). Old word (from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">παρα, μυθοσ, παραμυθεομα</span></span> <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1th+2:12">1 Thessalonians 2:12</span> which see, a stimulating word), nowhere else in N.T., but <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">παραμυθιον</span></span> in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="php+2:1">Philippians 2:1</span> with <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">παρακλησις</span></span> as here. Edification, cheer, incentive in these words. </p>
