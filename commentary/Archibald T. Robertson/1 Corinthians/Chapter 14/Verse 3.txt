 Edification  (οικοδομην). Building up.Comfort  (παρακλησιν). Encouragement, calling to one's side.Consolation  (παραμυθιαν). Old word (from παρα, μυθοσ, παραμυθεομα 1 Thessalonians 2:12 which see, a stimulating word), nowhere else in N.T., but παραμυθιον in Philippians 2:1 with παρακλησις as here. Edification, cheer, incentive in these words.
