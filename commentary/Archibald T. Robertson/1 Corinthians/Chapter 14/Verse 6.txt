<p><b> If I come </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εαν ελθω</span></span>). Third class condition, supposable case (aorist subjunctive).</p>
<p><b>What shall I profit you </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τ υμας ωφελησω</span></span>). Two accusatives with this verb (see <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1co+13:3">1 Corinthians 13:3</span>).</p>
<p><b>Unless I speak </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εαν μη λαλησω</span></span>). Second condition (also third class) with the one conclusion (cf. <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1ti+2:5">1 Timothy 2:5</span>). </p>
