<p><b> What I am </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ο ειμ</span></span>). Not,</p>
<p><b>who </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ος</span></span>), but</p>
<p><b>what </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ο</span></span>), neuter singular. His actual character and attainments. All "by the grace of God" (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">χαριτ θεου</span></span>).</p>
<p><b>I laboured more abundantly than they all </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">περισσοτερον αυτων παντων εκοπιασα</span></span>). This is sober fact as shown by the Acts and Paul's Epistles. He had tremendous energy and used it. Genius is work, Carlyle said. Take Paul as a specimen. </p>
