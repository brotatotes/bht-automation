<p><b> Then also </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αρα κα</span></span>). Inevitable inference.</p>
<p><b>Have perished </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">απωλοντο</span></span>). Did perish. Second aorist middle indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">απολλυμ</span></span>, to destroy, middle, to perish (delivered up to eternal misery). Cf. <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1co+8:11">1 Corinthians 8:11</span>. </p>
