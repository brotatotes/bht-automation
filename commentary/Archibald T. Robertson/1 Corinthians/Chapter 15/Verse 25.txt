<p><b> Till he hath put </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αχρ ου θη</span></span>). Second aorist active subjunctive of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τιθημ</span></span>, "till he put" (no sense in saying "hath put," merely effective aorist tense for climax. <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">Αχρ (ου), μεχρ (ου), εως (ου)</span></span> all are used for the same idea of indefinite future time. </p>
