<p><b> Awake up righteously </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εκνηψατε δικαιως</span></span>). Wake up as if from drunkenness. <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">Εκνηφω</span></span>, only here in N.T. sin not (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μη αμαρτανετε</span></span>). Stop sinning.</p>
<p><b>No knowledge of God </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αγνωσιαν θεου</span></span>). Old word for ignorance, in N.T. only here and <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1pe+2:15">1 Peter 2:15</span>. Ignorance of God, agnosticism. Some today (agnostics) even take pride in it instead of shame (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εντροπην</span></span>, turning in on oneself). See on <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1co+6:5">1 Corinthians 6:5</span> for <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εντροπη</span></span>. </p>
