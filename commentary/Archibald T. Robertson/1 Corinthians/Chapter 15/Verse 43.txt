<p><b> In weakness </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εν ασθενεια</span></span>). Lack of strength as shown in the victory of death.</p>
<p><b>In power </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εν δυναμε</span></span>). Death can never conquer this new body, "conformed to the body of His glory" (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="php+3:21">Philippians 3:21</span>). </p>
