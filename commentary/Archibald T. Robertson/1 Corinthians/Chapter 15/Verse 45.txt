<p><b> Became a living soul </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εγενετο εις ψυχην ζωσαν</span></span>). Hebraistic use of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εις</span></span> in predicate from LXX. God breathed a soul (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ψυχη</span></span>) into "the first man."</p>
<p><b>The last Adam became a life-giving spirit </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ο εσχατος Αδαμ εις πνευμα ζωοποιουν</span></span>). Supply <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εγενετο</span></span> (became). Christ is the crown of humanity and has power to give us the new body. In <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+5:12-19">Romans 5:12-19</span> Paul calls Christ the Second Adam. </p>
