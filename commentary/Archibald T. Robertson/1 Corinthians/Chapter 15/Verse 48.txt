<p><b> As is the earthly </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">οιος ο χοικος</span></span>). Masculine gender because of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ανθρωπος</span></span> and correlative pronouns (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">οιοσ, τοιουτο</span></span>) of character or quality. All men of dust (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">χοικο</span></span>) correspond to "the man of dust" (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ο χοικος</span></span>), the first Adam.</p>
