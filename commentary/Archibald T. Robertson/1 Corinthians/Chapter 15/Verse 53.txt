<p><b> Must put on </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δε ενδυσασθα</span></span>). Aorist (ingressive) middle infinitive, put on as a garment.</p>
<p><b>Immortality </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αθανασιαν</span></span>). Old word from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αθανατος</span></span>, undying, and that from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">α</span></span> privative and <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">θνησκω</span></span>, to die. In N.T. only here and <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1ti+6:16">1 Timothy 6:16</span> where God is described as having immortality. </p>
