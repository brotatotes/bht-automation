 Victory  (νικος). Late form of νικη.O death  (θανατε). Second instance. Here Paul changes Hades of the LXX for Hebrew Sheol (Hosea 13:14) to death. Paul never uses Hades.Thy sting  (σου το κεντρον). Old word from κεντρεω, to prick, as in Acts 26:14. In Revelation 9:10 of the sting of locusts, scorpions. The serpent death has lost his poison fangs.
