<p><b> Be ye steadfast, unmovable </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εδραιο γινεσθε, αμετακινητο</span></span>). "Keep on becoming steadfast, unshaken." Let the sceptics howl and rage. Paul has given rational grounds for faith and hope in Christ the Risen Lord and Saviour. Note practical turn to this great doctrinal argument.</p>
<p><b>Work </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εργον</span></span>),</p>
<p><b>labour </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κοπος</span></span>, toil). The best answer to doubt is work. </p>
