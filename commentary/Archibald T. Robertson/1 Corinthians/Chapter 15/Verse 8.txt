<p><b> As unto one born out of due time </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ωσπερε τω εκτρωματ</span></span>). Literally, as to the miscarriage (or untimely birth). Word first occurs in Aristotle for abortion or miscarriage and occurs in LXX (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="nu+12:12">Numbers 12:12</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="job+3:16">Job 3:16</span>) and papyri (for miscarriage by accident). The verb <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τιτρωσκω</span></span> means to wound and <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εκ</span></span> is out. Paul means that the appearance to him came after Jesus had ascended to heaven. </p>
