<p><b> Watch ye </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">γρηγορειτε</span></span>). Stay awake. Late present from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εγρηγορα</span></span> second perfect of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εγειρω</span></span>, to awake.</p>
<p><b>Quit you like men </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ανδριζεσθε</span></span>). Play the man. Middle voice, show yourselves men. From <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ανηρ</span></span>, a man. </p>
