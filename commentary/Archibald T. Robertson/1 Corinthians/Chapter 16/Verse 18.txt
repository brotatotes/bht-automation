<p><b> For they refreshed my spirit and yours </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ανεπαυσαν γαρ το εμον πνευμα κα το υμων</span></span>). They did both. The very verb used by Jesus in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+11:28">Matthew 11:28</span> for the refreshment offered by him to those who come to him, fellowship with Jesus, and here fellowship with each other. </p>
