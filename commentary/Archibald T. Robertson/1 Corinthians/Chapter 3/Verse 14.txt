<p><b> If any man's work shall abide </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ε τινος το εργον μενε</span></span>). Condition of the first class with future indicative, determined as fulfilled, assumed as true. When the fire has done its work, what is left? That is the fiery test that the work of each of us must meet. Suitable reward (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+20:8">Matthew 20:8</span>) will come for the work that stands this test (gold, silver, precious stones) </p>
