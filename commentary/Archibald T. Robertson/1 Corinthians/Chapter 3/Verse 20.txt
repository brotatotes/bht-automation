<p><b> And again </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κα παλιν</span></span>). Another confirmatory passage from <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ps+94:11">Psalms 94:11</span>.</p>
<p><b>Reasonings </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">διαλογισμους</span></span>). More than <i>cogitationes</i> (Vulgate), sometimes disputations (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="php+2:14">Philippians 2:14</span>). Paul changes "men" of LXX to wise (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">σοφων</span></span>) in harmony with the Hebrew context.</p>
<p><b>Vain </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ματαιο</span></span>). Useless, foolish, from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ματη</span></span>, a futile attempt. </p>
