 So then neither--neither--but  (Hωστε ουτε--ουτε--αλλ'). Paul applies his logic relentlessly to the facts. He had askedwhat  (τ) is Apollos or Paul (verse 1 Corinthians 3:5). The answer is here.Neither is anything  (τ)the one who plants nor the one who waters . God is the whole and we are not anything.
