<p><b> With a rod </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εν ραβδω</span></span>). The so-called instrumental use of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εν</span></span> like the Hebrew (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1sa+17:43">1 Samuel 17:43</span>). The shepherd leaned on his rod, staff, walking stick. The paedagogue had his rod also.</p>
<p><b>Shall I come? </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ελθω;</span></span>). Deliberative subjunctive. Paul gives them the choice. They can have him as their spiritual father or as their paedagogue with a rod. </p>
