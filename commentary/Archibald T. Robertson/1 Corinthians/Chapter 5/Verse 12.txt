<p><b> For what have I to do? </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τ γαρ μοι;</span></span>). "For what is it to me (dative) to judge those without (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τους εξο</span></span>)?" They are outside the church and not within Paul's jurisdiction. God passes judgment on them. </p>
