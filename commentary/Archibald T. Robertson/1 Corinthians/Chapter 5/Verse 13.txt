<p><b> Put away the wicked man </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εξαρατε τον πονηρον</span></span>). By this quotation from <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="de+17:7">Deuteronomy 17:7</span> Paul clinches the case for the expulsion of the offender (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1co+5:2">1 Corinthians 5:2</span>). Note <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εξ</span></span> twice and effective aorist tense. </p>
