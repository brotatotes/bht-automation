<p><b> Will raise up us </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ημας εξεγερε</span></span>). Future active indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εξεγειρω</span></span> though the MSS. vary greatly, some having the present and some even the aorist. But the resurrection of the body gives added weight to Paul's argument about the dignity and destiny of the body (<i>quanta dignitas</i>, Bengel) which should not be prostituted to sensuality. </p>
