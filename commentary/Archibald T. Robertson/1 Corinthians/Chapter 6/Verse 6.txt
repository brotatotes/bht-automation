<p><b> And that before unbelievers </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κα τουτο επ απιστων</span></span>). Climactic force of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κα</span></span>. The accusative of general reference with <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τουτο</span></span>. "That there should be disputes about <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">βιωτικα</span></span> is bad; that Christian should go to law with Christian is worse; that Christians should do this before unbelievers is worst of all" (Robertson and Plummer). </p>
