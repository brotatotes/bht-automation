<p><b> Which hath an unbelieving husband </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ητις εχε ανδρα απιστον</span></span>). Relative clause here, while a conditional one in verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1co+7:12">1 Corinthians 7:12</span> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ε τις</span></span>, if any one). Paul is perfectly fair in stating both sides of the problem of mixed marriages. </p>
