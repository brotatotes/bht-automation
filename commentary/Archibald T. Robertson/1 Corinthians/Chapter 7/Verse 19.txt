<p><b> But the keeping of the commandments of God </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αλλα τηρησις εντολων θεου</span></span>). Old word in sense of watching (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+4:3">Acts 4:3</span>). Paul's view of the worthlessness of circumcision or of uncircumcision is stated again in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ga+5:6">Galatians 5:6</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ga+6:15">Galatians 6:15</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+2:25-29">Romans 2:25-29</span> (only the inward or spiritual Jew counts). </p>
