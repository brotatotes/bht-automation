<p><b> Because of fornications </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δια τας πορνειας</span></span>). This is not the only reason for marriage, but it is a true one. The main purpose of marriage is children. Mutual love is another. The family is the basis of all civilization. Paul does not give a low view of marriage, but is merely answering questions put to him about life in Corinth. </p>
