<p><b> Render the due </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">την οφειλην αποδιδοτω</span></span>). Marriage is not simply not wrong, but for many a duty. Both husband and wife have a mutual obligation to the other. "This dictum defends marital intercourse against rigorists, as that of ver. <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1co+7:1">1 Corinthians 7:1</span> commends celibacy against sensualists" (Findlay). </p>
