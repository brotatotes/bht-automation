<p><b> As though they possessed not </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ως μη κατεχοντες</span></span>). See this use of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κατεχω</span></span>, old verb to hold down (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+14:9">Luke 14:9</span>), to keep fast, to possess, in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2co+6:10">2 Corinthians 6:10</span>. Paul means that all earthly relations are to hang loosely about us in view of the second coming. </p>
