<p><b> Doeth well </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">καλως ποιε</span></span>). So Paul commends the father who gives his daughter in marriage (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">γαμιζε</span></span>). This verb <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">γαμιζω</span></span> has not been found outside the N.T. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+22:30">Matthew 22:30</span>.</p>
<p><b>Shall do better </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κρεισσον ποιησε</span></span>). In view of the present distress (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1co+7:26">1 Corinthians 7:26</span>) and the shortened time (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1co+7:29">1 Corinthians 7:29</span>). And yet, when all is said, Paul leaves the whole problem of getting married an open question to be settled by each individual case. </p>
