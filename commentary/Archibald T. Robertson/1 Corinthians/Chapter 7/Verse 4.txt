<p><b> The wife </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">η γυνη</span></span>). The wife is mentioned first, but the equality of the sexes in marriage is clearly presented as the way to keep marriage undefiled (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="heb+13:4">Hebrews 13:4</span>). "In wedlock separate ownership of the person ceases" (Robertson and Plummer). </p>
