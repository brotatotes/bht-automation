<p><b> Is it a great matter? </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μεγα;</span></span>). The copula <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εστιν</span></span> has to be supplied. Note two conditions of first class with <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ε</span></span>, both assumed to be true. On <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πνευματικα</span></span> and <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">σαρκικα</span></span> see on <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1co+2:14">1 Corinthians 2:14</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1co+3:3">1 Corinthians 3:3</span>. This point comes out sharply also in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ga+6:6">Galatians 6:6</span>. </p>
