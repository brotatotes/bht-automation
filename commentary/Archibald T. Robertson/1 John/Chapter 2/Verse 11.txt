<p><b> Blinded </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ετυφλωσεν</span></span>). First aorist active indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τυφλοω</span></span>, the very verb and form used in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2co+4:4">2 Corinthians 4:4</span> of the god of this age to keep men from beholding the illumination of the gospel of the glory of Christ who is the image of God. The first part of the verse repeats verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1jo+2:9">1 John 2:9</span>, but adds this vivid touch of the blinding power of darkness. In the Mammoth Cave of Kentucky the fish in Echo River have eye-sockets, but no eyes. </p>
