<p><b> Hath not the Father </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ουδε τον πατερα εχε</span></span>). "Not even does he have the Father" or God (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2jo+1:9">2 John 1:9</span>).</p>
<p><b>He that confesseth the Son </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ο ομολογων τον υιον</span></span>). Because the Son reveals the Father (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+1:18">John 1:18</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+14:9">John 14:9</span>). Our only approach to the Father is by the Son (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+14:6">John 14:6</span>). Confession of Christ before men is a prerequisite for confession by Christ before the Father (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+10:32">Matthew 10:32</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+12:8">Luke 12:8</span>). </p>
