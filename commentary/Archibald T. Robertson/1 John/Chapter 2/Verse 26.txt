<p><b> Concerning them that would lead you astray </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">περ των πλανωντων υμας</span></span>). "Concerning those that are trying to lead you astray" (conative use of the present active articular participle of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πλαναω</span></span>. See <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1jo+1:8">1 John 1:8</span> for this verb. John is doing his part to rescue the sheep from the wolves, as Paul did (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+20:29">Acts 20:29</span>). </p>
