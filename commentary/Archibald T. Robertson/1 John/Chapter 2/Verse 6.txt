<p><b> Himself also to walk </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κα αυτος περιπατειν</span></span>). Present active infinitive after <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">οφειλε</span></span> (ought), "Himself also to keep on walking," a continuous performance, not a spasmodic spurt.</p>
<p><b>Even as he walked </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">καθως εκεινος περιεπατησεν</span></span>). Constative aorist active indicative summing up the life of Christ on earth with the emphatic use of the demonstrative <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εκεινος</span></span> in reference to Christ as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1jo+3:3">1 John 3:3</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1jo+3:5">1 John 3:5</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1jo+3:7">1 John 3:7</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1jo+3:16">1 John 3:16</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1jo+4:17">1 John 4:17</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+7:11">John 7:11</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+9:12">John 9:12</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+9:28">John 9:28</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+19:21">John 19:21</span>. </p>
