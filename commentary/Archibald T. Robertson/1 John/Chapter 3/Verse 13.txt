<p><b> If </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ε</span></span>). Common construction after <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">θαυμαζω</span></span> (wonder) rather than <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">οτ</span></span> (that, because). Present imperative here with <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μη</span></span> means "cease wondering." Note <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μη θαυμασηις</span></span> (do not begin to wonder) in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+3:6">John 3:6</span> (an individual case). See this same condition and language in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+15:18">John 15:18</span>. </p>
