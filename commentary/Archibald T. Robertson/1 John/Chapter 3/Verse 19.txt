<p><b> Shall we know </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">γνωσομεθα</span></span>). Future middle indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">γινωσκω</span></span>, at any future emergency, we shall come to know by this (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εν τουτω</span></span>) "that we are of the truth" (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">οτ εκ της αληθειας εσμεν</span></span>).</p>
<p><b>Before him </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εμπροσθεν αυτου</span></span>). In the very presence of God we shall have confident assurance (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πεισομεν την καρδιαν ημων</span></span>, either we shall persuade our heart or shall assure our heart) because God understands us. </p>
