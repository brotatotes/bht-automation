<p><b> If our heart condemn us not </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εαν η καρδια μη καταγινωσκη</span></span>). Condition of third class with <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εαν μη</span></span> and present active subjunctive. The converse of the preceding, but not a claim to sinlessness, but the consciousness of fellowship in God's presence.</p>
<p><b>Boldness toward God </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">παρρησιαν προς τον θεον</span></span>). Even in prayer (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="heb+4:16">Hebrews 4:16</span>). See also <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1jo+2:28">1 John 2:28</span>. </p>
