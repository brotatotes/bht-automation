 If our heart condemn us not  (εαν η καρδια μη καταγινωσκη). Condition of third class with εαν μη and present active subjunctive. The converse of the preceding, but not a claim to sinlessness, but the consciousness of fellowship in God's presence.Boldness toward God  (παρρησιαν προς τον θεον). Even in prayer (Hebrews 4:16). See also 1 John 2:28.
