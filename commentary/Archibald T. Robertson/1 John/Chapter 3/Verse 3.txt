<p><b> Set on him </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επ' αυτω</span></span>). Resting upon (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επ</span></span>) with locative rather than <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εις</span></span>, looking to, <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+24:15">Acts 24:15</span>. That is upon Christ (Brooke), upon God (D. Smith), upon God in Christ (Westcott).</p>
<p><b>Purifieth himself </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αγνιζε εαυτον</span></span>). Present active indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αγνιζω</span></span>, old verb, from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αγνος</span></span> (pure from contamination), used of ceremonial purifications (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+11:55">John 11:55</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+21:24">Acts 21:24</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+21:26">Acts 21:26</span> as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ex+19:10">Exodus 19:10</span>) and then of personal internal cleansing of heart (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="jas+4:8">James 4:8</span>), soul (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1pe+1:22">1 Peter 1:22</span>), self (here). Cf. <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="php+2:12">Philippians 2:12</span> the work of both God and man.</p>
<p><b>As he is pure </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">καθως εκεινος αγνος εστιν</span></span>). As in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1jo+2:6">1 John 2:6</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1jo+3:9">1 John 3:9</span> <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εκεινος</span></span> (emphatic demonstrative) refers to Christ. Christ can be termed <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αγνος</span></span> "in virtue of the perfection of his humanity" (Westcott). Our destiny is to be conformed to the image of God in Christ (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+8:29">Romans 8:29</span>). </p>
