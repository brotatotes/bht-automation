 Let no man lead you astray  (μηδεις πλανατω υμας). Present active imperative of πλαναω, "let no one keep on leading you astray." See 1 John 1:8; 1 John 2:26. Break the spell of any Gnostic charmer.He that doeth righteousness  (ο ποιων την δικαιοσυνην). "He that keeps on doing (present active participle of ποιεω) righteousness." For this idiom with ποιεω see 1 John 1:6; 1 John 3:4.He  (εκεινος). Christ as in verse 1 John 3:5.
