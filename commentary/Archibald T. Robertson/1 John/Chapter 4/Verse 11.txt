 If God so loved us  (ε ουτως ο θεος ηγαπησεν ημας). Condition of first class with ε and the first aorist active indicative. As in John 3:16, so here ουτως emphasises the manifestation of God's love both in its manner and in its extent (Romans 8:32).Ought  (οφειλομεν). As in 1 John 2:6. Noblesse oblige. "Keep on loving," (αγαπαιν) as in 1 John 3:11.
