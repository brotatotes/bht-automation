<p><b> If a man say </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εαν τις ειπη</span></span>). Condition of third class with <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εαν</span></span> and second aorist active subjunctive. Suppose one say. Cf. <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1jo+1:6">1 John 1:6</span>.</p>
<p><b>I love God </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">Αγαπω τον θεον</span></span>). Quoting an imaginary disputant as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1jo+2:4">1 John 2:4</span>.</p>
<p><b>And hateth </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κα μισε</span></span>). Continuation of the same condition with <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εαν</span></span> and the present active subjunctive, "and keep on hating." See <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1jo+2:9">1 John 2:9</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1jo+3:15">1 John 3:15</span> for use of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μισεω</span></span> (hate) with <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αδελφος</span></span> (brother). A liar (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ψευστης</span></span>). Blunt and to the point as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1jo+1:10">1 John 1:10</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1jo+2:4">1 John 2:4</span>.</p>
<p><b>That loveth not </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ο μη αγαπων</span></span>). "The one who does not keep on loving" (present active negative articular participle).</p>
<p><b>Hath seen </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εωρακεν</span></span>). Perfect active indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">οραω</span></span>, the form in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+1:18">John 1:18</span> used of seeing God.</p>
<p><b>Cannot love </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ου δυνατα αγαπαιν</span></span>). "Is not able to go on loving," with which compare <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1jo+2:9">1 John 2:9</span>, <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ου δυνατα αμαρτανειν</span></span> (is not able to go on sinning). The best MSS. do not have <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πως</span></span> (how) here. </p>
