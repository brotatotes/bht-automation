<p><b> Believeth on </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πιστευων εις</span></span>). John draws a distinction between "not believing God" (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μη πιστευων τω θεω</span></span>) in next clause, the testimony of God about his Son, and surrender to and reliance on the Son as here (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εις</span></span> and the accusative). See the same distinction less clearly drawn in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+6:30">John 6:30</span>. See also <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εις την μαρτυριαν</span></span> after <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πεπιστευκεν</span></span> in this same verse and <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+2:23">John 2:23</span>.</p>
<p><b>In him </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εν αυτω</span></span>). "In himself," though the evidence is not decisive between <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αυτω</span></span> and <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αυτω</span></span>.</p>
<p><b>Hath made </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πεποιηκεν</span></span>). Perfect active indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ποιεω</span></span> like <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μεμαρτυρηκεν</span></span> and <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πεπιστευκεν</span></span>, permanent state.</p>
<p><b>A liar </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ψευστην</span></span>). As in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1jo+1:10">1 John 1:10</span>, which see.</p>
<p><b>Because he hath not believed </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">οτ ου πεπιστευκεν</span></span>). Actual negative reason with negative <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ου</span></span>, not the subjective reason as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+3:18">John 3:18</span>, where we have <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">οτ μη πεπιστευκεν</span></span>). The subjective negative is regular with <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ο μη πιστευων</span></span>. Relative clause here repeats close of verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1jo+5:9">1 John 5:9</span>. </p>
