<p><b> Toward him </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">προς αυτον</span></span>). Fellowship with (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">προς</span></span>, face to face) Christ. For boldness see <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1jo+2:28">1 John 2:28</span>.</p>
<p><b>That </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">οτ</span></span>). Declarative again, as in verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1jo+5:11">1 John 5:11</span>.</p>
<p><b>If we ask anything </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εαν τ αιτωμεθα</span></span>). Condition of third class with <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εαν</span></span> and present middle (indirect) subjunctive (personal interest as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="jas+4:3">James 4:3</span>, though the point is not to be pressed too far, for see <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+20:20">Matthew 20:20</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+20:22">Matthew 20:22</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+16:24">John 16:24</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+16:26">John 16:26</span>).</p>
<p><b>According to his will </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κατα το θελημα αυτου</span></span>). This is the secret in all prayer, even in the case of Jesus himself. For the phrase see <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1pe+4:19">1 Peter 4:19</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ga+1:4">Galatians 1:4</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="eph+1:5">Ephesians 1:5</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="eph+1:11">Ephesians 1:11</span>.</p>
<p><b>He heareth us </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ακουε ημων</span></span>). Even when God does not give us what we ask, in particular then (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="heb+5:7">Hebrews 5:7</span>). </p>
