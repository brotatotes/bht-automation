<p><b> And if we know </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κα εαν οιδαμεν</span></span>). Condition of first class with <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εαν</span></span> (usually <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ε</span></span>) and the perfect active indicative, assumed as true. See <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1th+3:8">1 Thessalonians 3:8</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+8:31">Acts 8:31</span> for the indicative with <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εαν</span></span> as in the papyri. "An amplification of the second limitation" (D. Smith).</p>
<p><b>Whatsoever we ask </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ο εαν αιτωμεθα</span></span>). Indefinite relative clause with modal <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εαν</span></span> (=<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αν</span></span>) and the present middle (as for ourselves) subjunctive of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αιτεω</span></span>. This clause, like <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ημων</span></span>, is also the object of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ακουε</span></span>.</p>
<p><b>We know that we have </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">οιδαμεν οτ εχομεν</span></span>). Repetition of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">οιδαμεν</span></span>, the confidence of possession by anticipation.</p>
<p><b>The petitions </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τα αιτηματα</span></span>). Old word, from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αιτεω</span></span>, requests, here only in John, elsewhere in N.T. <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+23:24">Luke 23:24</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="php+4:6">Philippians 4:6</span>. We have the answer already as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mr+11:24">Mark 11:24</span>.</p>
<p><b>We have asked </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ηιτηκαμεν</span></span>). Perfect active indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αιτεω</span></span>, the asking abiding. </p>
