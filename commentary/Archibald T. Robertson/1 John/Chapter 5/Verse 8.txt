<p><b> The Spirit and the water and the blood </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">το πνευμα κα το υδωρ κα το αιμα</span></span>). The same three witnesses of verses <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1jo+5:6">1 John 5:6</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1jo+5:7">1 John 5:7</span> repeated with the Spirit first.</p>
<p><b>The three </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ο τρεις</span></span>). The resumptive article.</p>
<p><b>Agree in one </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εις το εν εισιν</span></span>). "Are for the one thing," to bring us to faith in Jesus as the Incarnate Son of God, the very purpose for which John wrote his Gospel (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1jo+20:31">1 John 20:31</span>). </p>
