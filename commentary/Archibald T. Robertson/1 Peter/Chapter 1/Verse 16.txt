<p><b> Because it is written </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">διοτ γεγραπτα</span></span>). "Because (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">διοτ</span></span> stronger than <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">οτ</span></span> below) it stands written" (regular formula for O.T. quotation, perfect passive indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">γραφω</span></span>). The quotation is from <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="le+11:44">Leviticus 11:44</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="le+19:2">Leviticus 19:2</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="le+20:7">Leviticus 20:7</span>. Reenforced by Jesus in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+5:48">Matthew 5:48</span>. The future <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εσεσθε</span></span> here is volitive like an imperative. </p>
