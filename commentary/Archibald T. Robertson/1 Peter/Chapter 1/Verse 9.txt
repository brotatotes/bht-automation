<p><b> Receiving </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κομιζομενο</span></span>). Present middle participle of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κομιζω</span></span>, old verb, to receive back, to get what is promised (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1pe+5:4">1 Peter 5:4</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="heb+10:36">Hebrews 10:36</span>).</p>
<p><b>The end of your faith </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">το τελος της πιστεως</span></span>). The conclusion, the culmination of faith (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2co+3:13">2 Corinthians 3:13</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+2:21">Romans 2:21</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+10:4">Romans 10:4</span>). See <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="heb+12:2">Hebrews 12:2</span> of Jesus as "Pioneer and Perfecter of Faith."</p>
<p><b>Even the salvation of your souls </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">σωτηριαν ψυχων</span></span>). No "even" in the text, just the accusative of apposition with <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τελος</span></span>, viz., final salvation. </p>
