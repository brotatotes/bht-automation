<p><b> Putting away therefore </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αποθεμενο ουν</span></span>). Second aorist middle participle of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αποτιθημ</span></span>, old and common verb, in metaphorical sense either to cleanse defilements (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1pe+3:21">1 Peter 3:21</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="jas+1:21">James 1:21</span>) or to put off clothing (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+13:12">Romans 13:12</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="col+3:5">Colossians 3:5</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="eph+4:22">Ephesians 4:22</span>). Either sense suits here. Therefore (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ουν</span></span>) because of the new birth (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1pe+1:23">1 Peter 1:23</span>) and the new life demanded.</p>
<p><b>Wickedness </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κακιαν</span></span>). This old word, from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κακος</span></span> (evil), in the ancients meant vice of any kind and note <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πασαν</span></span> (all) here.</p>
<p><b>Guile </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δολον</span></span>). Old word (from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δελω</span></span>, to catch with bait), deceit.</p>
<p><b>Hypocrisies </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">υποκρισεις</span></span>). Singular (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">υποκρισιν</span></span>) in the best MSS. See <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1pe+1:22">1 Peter 1:22</span> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ανυποκριτον</span></span>) and <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mr+7:6">Mark 7:6</span> for Christ's denunciation of hypocrites which the disciples did not understand, including Peter (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+15:16">Matthew 15:16</span>).</p>
<p><b>Envies </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">φθονους</span></span>). Genuine here, not <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">φονους</span></span> (murders), as B has it. For the word see <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+27:18">Matthew 27:18</span>.</p>
<p><b>Evil speakings </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">καταλαλιας</span></span>). Late word (from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">καταλαλος</span></span>, defamer, <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+1:30">Romans 1:30</span>), in N.T. only here and <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2co+12:20">2 Corinthians 12:20</span>. "Backbitings." For verb see <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1pe+2:12">1 Peter 2:12</span>. </p>
