<p><b> When he was reviled </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">λοιδορουμενος</span></span>). Present passive participle of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">λοιδορεω</span></span>, old verb (from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">λοιδορος</span></span>, reviler, <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1co+5:11">1 Corinthians 5:11</span>) as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+9:28">John 9:28</span>.</p>
<p><b>Reviled not again </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ουκ αντελοιδορε</span></span>). Imperfect active (for repeated incidents) of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αντιλοιδορεω</span></span>, late and rare compound (Plutarch, Lucian, one papyrus example with compound following the simplex verb as here, Moulton and Milligan's <i>Vocabulary</i>), here only in N.T. Idiomatic use of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αντ</span></span> (in turn, return, back).</p>
<p><b>Threatened not </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ουκ ηπειλε</span></span>). Imperfect again (repeated acts) of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">απειλεω</span></span>, old compound (from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">απειλη</span></span>, threat, <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+9:1">Acts 9:1</span>), in N.T. only here and <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+4:17">Acts 4:17</span>.</p>
<p><b>But committed himself </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">παρεδιδου δε</span></span>). Imperfect active again (kept on committing himself) of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">παραδιδωμ</span></span>, to hand over, usually of one to a judge, but here not of another (as the Sanhedrin), but himself (supply <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εαυτον</span></span>), for Jesus uses this very idea in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+23:46">Luke 23:46</span> as he dies. Jesus thus handed himself and his cause over to the Father who judges righteously (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τω κρινοντ δικαιως</span></span>, dative of present active articular participle of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κρινω</span></span>). </p>
