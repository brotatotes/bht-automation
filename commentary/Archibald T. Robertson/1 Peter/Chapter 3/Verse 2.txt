<p><b> Beholding </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εποπτευσαντες</span></span>). First aorist active participle of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εποπτευω</span></span>, for which see <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1pe+2:12">1 Peter 2:12</span>. See <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1pe+2:12">1 Peter 2:12</span> also for <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αναστροφην</span></span> manner of life).</p>
<p><b>Chaste </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αγνην</span></span>). Pure because "in fear" (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εν φοβω</span></span>), no word in the Greek for "coupled," fear of God, though in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="eph+5:33">Ephesians 5:33</span> fear (reverence for) of the husband is urged. </p>
