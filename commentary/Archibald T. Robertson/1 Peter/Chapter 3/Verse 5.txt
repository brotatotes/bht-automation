<p><b> Adorned themselves </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εκοσμουν εαυτας</span></span>). Imperfect active of customary action, "used to adorn themselves." <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">Κοσμεω</span></span> is old verb from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κοσμος</span></span> in the sense in verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1pe+3:3">1 Peter 3:3</span>. See <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="heb+11:11">Hebrews 11:11</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="heb+11:35">Hebrews 11:35</span> for like tribute to holy women of the O.T. The participle <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">υποτασσομενα</span></span> repeats verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1pe+3:1">1 Peter 3:1</span>. </p>
