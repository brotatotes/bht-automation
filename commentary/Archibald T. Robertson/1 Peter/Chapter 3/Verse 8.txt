<p><b> Finally </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">το τελος</span></span>). Adverbial accusative. Conclusion, not of the Epistle, but only of the addresses to various classes. No verb (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εστε</span></span> imperative, be) here.</p>
<p><b>Likeminded </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ομοφρονες</span></span>). Old compound (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ομοσ, φρην</span></span>), here only in N.T.</p>
<p><b>Compassionate </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">συμπαθεις</span></span>). Old adjective (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">συν, πασχω</span></span>), in N.T. only here and <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+12:15">Romans 12:15</span>. Our "sympathetic" in original sense.</p>
<p><b>Loving as brethren </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">φιλαδελφο</span></span>). Old compound (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">φιλοσ, αδελφος</span></span>), here only in N.T.</p>
<p><b>Tender-hearted </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ευσπλαγχνο</span></span>). Late and rare compound (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ευ</span></span> and <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">σπλαγχνον</span></span>), in Hippocrates, Apocrypha, in N.T. only here and <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="eph+4:32">Ephesians 4:32</span>.</p>
<p><b>Humble minded </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ταπεινοφρονες</span></span>). Late compound (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ταπεινοσ, φρην</span></span>), in Plutarch, <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="pr+29:23">Proverbs 29:23</span>, here only in N.T. </p>
