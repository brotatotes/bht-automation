<p><b> And if the righteous is scarcely saved </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κα ε ο δικαιος μολις σωζετα</span></span>). First-class condition again with <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ε</span></span> and present passive indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">σωζω</span></span>. Quotation from <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="pr+11:31">Proverbs 11:31</span>. See <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1pe+3:12">1 Peter 3:12</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1pe+3:14">1 Peter 3:14</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+5:20">Matthew 5:20</span>. But the Christian is not saved by his own righteousness (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="php+3:9">Philippians 3:9</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+7:14">Revelation 7:14</span>). For <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μολις</span></span> see <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+14:18">Acts 14:18</span> and for <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ασεβης</span></span> (ungodly, without reverence) see <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+4:5">Romans 4:5</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2pe+2:5">2 Peter 2:5</span>.</p>
<p><b>Will appear </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">φανειτα</span></span>). Future middle of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">φαινω</span></span>, to show. For the question see <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mr+10:24-26">Mark 10:24-26</span>. </p>
