<p><b> Above all things </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">προ παντων</span></span>). See this phrase in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="jas+5:12">James 5:12</span>.</p>
<p><b>Being fervent </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εκτενη εχοντες</span></span>). Present active participle of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εχοντες</span></span> and predicate accusative of adjective <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εκτενης</span></span> (from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εκτεινω</span></span>, to stretch out), stretched out, here only in N.T., "holding intent you love among yourselves."</p>
<p><b>For love covereth a multitude of sins </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">οτ αγαπη καλυπτε πληθος αμαρτιων</span></span>). See <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="jas+5:20">James 5:20</span> for meaning, sins of the one loved, not of the one loving. </p>
