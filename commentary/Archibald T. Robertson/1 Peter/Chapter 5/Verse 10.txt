<p><b> The God of all grace </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ο θεος της χαριτος</span></span>). See <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1pe+4:10">1 Peter 4:10</span> for <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ποικιλης χαριτος θεου</span></span> (of the variegated grace of God).</p>
<p><b>In Christ </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εν Χριστω</span></span>). A Pauline phrase (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2co+5:17-19">2 Corinthians 5:17-19</span>), but Petrine also. For God's "calling" us (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">καλεσας</span></span>) see <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1th+5:23">1 Thessalonians 5:23</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1co+1:8">1 Corinthians 1:8</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+8:29">Romans 8:29</span>.</p>
<p><b>After that ye have suffered a little while </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ολιγον παθοντας</span></span>). Second aorist active participle of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πασχω</span></span>, antecedent to the principal verbs which are future active (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">καταρτισε</span></span>, to mend, <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mr+1:19">Mark 1:19</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ga+6:1">Galatians 6:1</span>, <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">στηριξε</span></span>, for which see <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+9:51">Luke 9:51</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+22:32">Luke 22:32</span>, <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">σθενωσε</span></span> from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">σθενος</span></span> and so far a <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">απαξ λεγομενον</span></span> like <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ενισχυω</span></span> according to Hesychius). For <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ολιγον</span></span> see <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1pe+1:6">1 Peter 1:6</span>. </p>
