<p><b> With a kiss of love </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εν φιληματ αγαπης</span></span>). As in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1co+16:20">1 Corinthians 16:20</span>. The abuse of this custom led to its confinement to men with men and women with women and to its final abandonment (<i>Apost. Const</i>. ii. 57, 12).</p>
<p><b>That are in Christ </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τοις εν Χριστω</span></span>). This is the greatest of all secret orders and ties, one that is open to all who take Christ as Lord and Saviour. </p>
