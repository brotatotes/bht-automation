<p><b> Casting </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επιριψαντες</span></span>). First aorist active participle of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επιριπτω</span></span>, old verb, to throw upon, in N.T. only here and <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+19:35">Luke 19:35</span> (casting their clothes on the colt), here from <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ps+55:22">Psalms 55:22</span>. For <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μεριμνα</span></span> see <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+6:25">Matthew 6:25</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+6:31">Matthew 6:31</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+6:34">Matthew 6:34</span>.</p>
<p><b>He careth </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αυτω μελε</span></span>). Impersonal verb <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μελε</span></span> (present active indicative) with dative <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αυτω</span></span>, "it is a care to him." God does care (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+21:18">Luke 21:18</span>). </p>
