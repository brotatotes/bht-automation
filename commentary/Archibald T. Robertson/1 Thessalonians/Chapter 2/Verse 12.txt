<p><b> To the end that </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εις το</span></span>). Final use of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εις</span></span> and the articular infinitive, common idiom in the papyri and Paul uses <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εις</span></span> to and the infinitive fifty times (see again in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1th+3:2">1 Thessalonians 3:2</span>), some final, some sub-final, some result (Robertson, <i>Grammar</i>, pp. 989-91).</p>
<p><b>Walk worthily of God </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">περιπατειν αξιως του θεου</span></span>). Present infinitive (linear action), and genitive case with adverb <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αξιως</span></span> as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="col+1:10">Colossians 1:10</span> (cf. <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="php+1:27">Philippians 1:27</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="eph+4:1">Ephesians 4:1</span>), like a preposition.</p>
<p><b>Calleth </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">καλουντος</span></span>). Present active participle, keeps on calling. Some MSS. have <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">καλεσαντος</span></span>, called.</p>
<p><b>Kingdom </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">βασιλειαν</span></span>) here is the future consummation because of glory (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δοξαν</span></span>) as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2th+1:5">2 Thessalonians 1:5</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1co+6:9">1 Corinthians 6:9</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1co+15:50">1 Corinthians 15:50</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ga+5:21">Galatians 5:21</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2ti+4:1">2 Timothy 4:1</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2ti+4:18">2 Timothy 4:18</span>), but Paul uses it for the present kingdom of grace also as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1co+4:20">1 Corinthians 4:20</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+14:17">Romans 14:17</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="col+1:13">Colossians 1:13</span>. </p>
