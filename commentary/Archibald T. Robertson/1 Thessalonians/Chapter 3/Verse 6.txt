<p><b> Even now </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αρτ</span></span>). Just now, Timothy having come (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ελθοντος Τιμοθεου</span></span>, genitive absolute). Why Silas is not named is not clear, unless he had come from Beroea or elsewhere in Macedonia.</p>
<p><b>Glad tidings of </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ευαγγελισαμενου</span></span>). First aorist middle participle of the verb for evangelizing (gospelizing).</p>
<p><b>Good remembrance </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μνειαν</span></span>). Same word used by Paul <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1th+1:2">1 Thessalonians 1:2</span>.</p>
<p><b>Longing to see us </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επιποθουντες ημας ιδειν</span></span>). Old and strong verb, <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επι-</span></span>, directive, to long after. Mutual longing that pleased Paul ("we also you"). </p>
