<p><b> That ye may walk honestly </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ινα περιπατητε ευσχημονως</span></span>). Present subjunctive (linear action). Old adverb from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ευσχημων</span></span> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ευ, σχημα</span></span>, Latin <i>habitus</i>, graceful figure), becomingly, decently. In N.T. only here and <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+13:13">Romans 13:13</span>. This idea includes honest financial transactions, but a good deal more. People outside the churches have a right to watch the conduct of professing Christians in business, domestic life, social life, politics. </p>
