<p><b> Not for uncleanness, but in sanctification </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επ ακαθαρσια αλλ' εν αγιασμω</span></span>). Sharp contrast made still sharper by the two prepositions <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επ</span></span> (on the basis of) and <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εν</span></span> (in the sphere of). God has "called" us all for a decent sex life consonant with his aims and purposes. It was necessary for Paul to place this lofty ideal before the Thessalonian Christians living in a pagan world. It is equally important now. </p>
