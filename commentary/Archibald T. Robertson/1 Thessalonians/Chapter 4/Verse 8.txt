<p><b> Therefore </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τοιγαρουν</span></span>). This old triple compound particle (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τοι, γαρ, ουν</span></span>) is in the N.T. only here and <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="heb+12:1">Hebrews 12:1</span>. Paul applies the logic of the case.</p>
<p><b>He that rejecteth </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ο αθετων</span></span>). This late verb (Polybius and LXX) is from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">α-θετος</span></span> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">α</span></span> privative and verbal of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τιθημ</span></span>, to proscribe a thing, to annul it.</p>
<p><b>But God </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αλλα τον θεον</span></span>). Paul sees this clearly and modern atheists see it also. In order to justify their licentiousness they do not hesitate to set aside God. </p>
