<p><b> Know perfectly </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ακριβως οιδατε</span></span>). Accurately know, not "the times and the seasons," but their own ignorance.</p>
<p><b>As a thief in the night </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ως κλεπτης εν νυκτ</span></span>). As a thief at night, suddenly and unexpectedly. Reminiscence of the word of Jesus (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+24:43">Matthew 24:43</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+12:39">Luke 12:39</span>), used also in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2pe+3:10">2 Peter 3:10</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+3:3">Revelation 3:3</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+16:15">Revelation 16:15</span>.</p>
<p><b>Cometh </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ερχετα</span></span>). Prophetic or futuristic present tense. </p>
