<p><b> Prove all things </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">παντα [δε] δοκιμαζετε</span></span>). Probably <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δε</span></span> (but) is genuine. Even the gift of prophecy has to be tested (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1co+12:10">1 Corinthians 12:10</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1co+14:29">1 Corinthians 14:29</span>) to avoid error. Paul shows fine balance here.</p>
<p><b>Hold fast that which is good </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">το καλον κατεχετε</span></span>). Keep on holding down the beautiful (noble, morally beautiful). Present imperative <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κατ-εχω</span></span> (perfective use of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κατα-</span></span> here). </p>
