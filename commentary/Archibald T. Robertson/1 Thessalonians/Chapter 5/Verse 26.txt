<p><b> With a holy kiss </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εν φιληματ αγιω</span></span>). With a kiss that is holy (Milligan) a token of friendship and brotherly love (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1co+16:20">1 Corinthians 16:20</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2co+13:12">2 Corinthians 13:12</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+16:16">Romans 16:16</span>). In <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1pe+5:14">1 Peter 5:14</span> it is "with a kiss of love." This was the customary salutation for rabbis. </p>
