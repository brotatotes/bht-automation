<p>This noble doxology is a burst of gratitude for God's grace to Paul. For other doxologies see <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ga+1:5">Galatians 1:5</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+11:36">Romans 11:36</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+16:27">Romans 16:27</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="php+4:20">Philippians 4:20</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="eph+3:21">Ephesians 3:21</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1ti+6:16">1 Timothy 6:16</span>. White suggests that Paul may have often used this doxology in his prayers. Lock suggests "a Jewish liturgical formula" (a needless suggestion in view of Paul's wealth of doxologies seen above). For God's creative activity (King of the ages) see <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1co+10:11">1 Corinthians 10:11</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="eph+2:7">Ephesians 2:7</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="eph+3:9">Ephesians 3:9</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="eph+3:11">Ephesians 3:11</span>.</p>
<p><b>Incorruptible </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αφθαρτω</span></span>). As an epithet of God also in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+1:23">Romans 1:23</span>.</p>
<p><b>Invisible </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αορατω</span></span>). Epithet of God in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="col+1:15">Colossians 1:15</span>.</p>
<p><b>The only God </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μονω θεω</span></span>). So <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+16:27">Romans 16:27</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+5:44">John 5:44</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+17:3">John 17:3</span>.</p>
<p><b>For ever and ever </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εις τους αιωνας των αιωνων</span></span>). "Unto the ages of ages." Cf. <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="eph+3:21">Ephesians 3:21</span> "of the age of the ages." </p>
