<p><b> True </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">γνησιω</span></span>). Legitimate, not spurious. Old word from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">γινομα</span></span>, but Pauline only in N.T. (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="php+4:3">Philippians 4:3</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2co+8:8">2 Corinthians 8:8</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="tit+1:4">Titus 1:4</span>). In <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="php+2:20">Philippians 2:20</span> the adverb <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">γνησιως</span></span> occurs and of Timothy again.</p>
