<p><b> Gain to themselves </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εαυτοις περιποιουντα</span></span>). Present middle indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">περιποιεω</span></span>, old verb, to make besides (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">περ</span></span>, around, over), to lay by. Reflexive (indirect) middle with reflexive pronoun (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εαυτοις</span></span>) repeated as often happens in the <i>Koine</i>. In N.T. only here, <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+17:33">Luke 17:33</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+20:28">Acts 20:28</span> (Paul also, quoting <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="isa+43:21">Isaiah 43:21</span>).</p>
<p><b>A good standing </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">βαθμον καλον</span></span>). Late word from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">βαινω</span></span>, in LXX for steps at a door (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1sa+5:5">1 Samuel 5:5</span>). In plural the steps of a stair. In the inscriptions it means a good foothold or standing. The ecclesiastical writers (Theodoret) take it to be a higher grade or rank, but it is doubtful if Paul means that here.</p>
<p><b>Much boldness </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πολλην παρρησιαν</span></span>). A Pauline phrase (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2co+3:12">2 Corinthians 3:12</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2co+7:4">2 Corinthians 7:4</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="php+1:20">Philippians 1:20</span>).</p>
<p><b>In the faith which is in Christ Jesus </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εν πιστε τη εν Χριστω Ιησου</span></span>). Pauline phrase again (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+26:18">Acts 26:18</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ga+3:26">Galatians 3:26</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="col+1:4">Colossians 1:4</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="eph+1:15">Ephesians 1:15</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2ti+1:13">2 Timothy 1:13</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2ti+3:15">2 Timothy 3:15</span>). </p>
