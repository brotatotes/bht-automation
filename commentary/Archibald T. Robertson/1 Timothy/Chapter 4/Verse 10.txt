<p><b> To this end </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εις τουτο</span></span>). The godliness (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ευσεβεια</span></span>) of verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1ti+4:8">1 Timothy 4:8</span>. See <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2co+6:10">2 Corinthians 6:10</span> as Paul's own commentary.</p>
<p><b>We labour </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κοπιωμεν</span></span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="col+1:29">Colossians 1:29</span>)</p>
<p><b>and strive </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κα αγωνιζομεθα</span></span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="col+1:29">Colossians 1:29</span>). Both Pauline words.</p>
<p><b>Because we have set our hope </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">οτ ελπικαμεν</span></span>). Perfect active indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ελπιζω</span></span> (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+15:12">Romans 15:12</span>).</p>
<p><b>Saviour of all men </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">σωτηρ παντων ανθρωπων</span></span>). See <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1ti+1:1">1 Timothy 1:1</span> for <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">σωτηρ</span></span> applied to God as here. Not that all men "are saved" in the full sense, but God gives life (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1ti+6:13">1 Timothy 6:13</span>) to all (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+17:28">Acts 17:28</span>).</p>
<p><b>Specially of them that believe </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μαλιστα πιστων</span></span>). Making a distinction in the kinds of salvation meant. "While God is potentially Saviour of all, He is actually Saviour of the <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πιστο</span></span>" (White). So Jesus is termed "Saviour of the World" (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+4:42">John 4:42</span>). Cf. <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ga+6:10">Galatians 6:10</span>. </p>
