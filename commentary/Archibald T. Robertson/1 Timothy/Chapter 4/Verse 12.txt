<p><b> Despise </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">καταφρονειτω</span></span>). Imperative active third singular of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">καταφρονεω</span></span>, old verb, to think down on, to despise (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+2:4">Romans 2:4</span>).</p>
<p><b>Thy youth </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">σου της νεοτητος</span></span>). Genitive case of old word (from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">νεος</span></span>) as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mr+10:20">Mark 10:20</span>.</p>
<p><b>Be thou </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">γινου</span></span>). Present middle imperative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">γινομα</span></span>. "Keep on becoming thou."</p>
<p><b>An ensample </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τυπος</span></span>). Old word from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τυπτω</span></span>, a type. Pauline use of the word (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1th+1:7">1 Thessalonians 1:7</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2th+3:9">2 Thessalonians 3:9</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="php+3:17">Philippians 3:17</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="tit+2:7">Titus 2:7</span>).</p>
<p><b>To them that believe </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">των πιστων</span></span>). Objective genitive.</p>
<p><b>In word </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εν λογω</span></span>). In conversation as well as in public speech.</p>
<p><b>In manner of life </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εν αναστροφη</span></span>). "In bearing" (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ga+1:13">Galatians 1:13</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="eph+4:22">Ephesians 4:22</span>).</p>
<p><b>In purity </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εν αγνεια</span></span>). Old word from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αγνευω</span></span> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αγνος</span></span>). Sinlessness of life. Used of a Nazirite (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="nu+6:2">Numbers 6:2</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="nu+6:21">Numbers 6:21</span>). Only here and <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1ti+5:2">1 Timothy 5:2</span> in N.T. </p>
