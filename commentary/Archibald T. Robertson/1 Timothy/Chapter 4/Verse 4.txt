<p><b> Creature </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κτισμα</span></span>). Late word from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κτιζω</span></span>, result of creating. See <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ge+1:31">Genesis 1:31</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mr+7:15">Mark 7:15</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+14:14">Romans 14:14</span> for the idea stated.</p>
<p><b>To be rejected </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αποβλητον</span></span>). Old verbal adjective in passive sense from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αποβαλλω</span></span>, to throw away, here only in N.T.</p>
<p><b>If it be received </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">λαμβανομενον</span></span>). "Being received." Present passive participle of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">λαμβανω</span></span>, in conditional sense, "with thanksgiving." </p>
