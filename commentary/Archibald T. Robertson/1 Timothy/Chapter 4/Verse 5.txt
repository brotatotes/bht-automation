<p><b> It is sanctified </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αγιαζετα</span></span>). Present passive indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αγιαζω</span></span>, here "rendered holy" rather than "declared holy." Cf. verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1ti+4:4">1 Timothy 4:4</span>.</p>
<p><b>Through the word of God and prayers </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δια λογου θεου κα εντευξεως</span></span>). See <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1ti+2:1">1 Timothy 2:1</span> for <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εντευξις</span></span>. Paul seems to refer to Genesis 1. It is almost a hendiadys "by the use of Scripture in prayer." </p>
