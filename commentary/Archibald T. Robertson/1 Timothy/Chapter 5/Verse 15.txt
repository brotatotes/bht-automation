<p><b> Are turned aside </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εξετραπησαν</span></span>). Second aorist (effective) passive indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εκτρεπω</span></span>. See <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1ti+1:6">1 Timothy 1:6</span>.</p>
<p><b>After Satan </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">οπισω του Σατανα</span></span>). "Behind Satan." Late use of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">οπισω</span></span> (behind) as a preposition. Used by Jesus of disciples coming behind (after) him (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+16:24">Matthew 16:24</span>). </p>
