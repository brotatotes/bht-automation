<p><b> Against an elder </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κατα πρεσβυτερου</span></span>). In the official sense of verses <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1ti+5:17">1 Timothy 5:17</span>.</p>
<p><b>Receive not </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μη παραδεχου</span></span>). Present middle imperative with <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μη</span></span> (prohibition) of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">παραδεχομα</span></span>, to receive, to entertain. Old verb. See <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+22:18">Acts 22:18</span>.</p>
<p><b>Accusation </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κατηγοριαν</span></span>). Old word (from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κατηγορος</span></span>). In N.T. only here, <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="tit+1:6">Titus 1:6</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+18:29">John 18:29</span> in critical text.</p>
<p><b>Except </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εκτος ε μη</span></span>). For this double construction see <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1co+14:5">1 Corinthians 14:5</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1co+15:2">1 Corinthians 15:2</span>.</p>
<p><b>At the mouth of </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επ</span></span>). Idiomatic use of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επ</span></span> (upon the basis of) as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2co+13:1">2 Corinthians 13:1</span>. </p>
