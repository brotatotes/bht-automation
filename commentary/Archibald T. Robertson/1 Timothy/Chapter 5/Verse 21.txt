<p><b> The elect angels </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">των εκλεκτων αγγελων</span></span>). For this triad of God, Christ, angels, see <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+9:26">Luke 9:26</span>. "Elect" in the sense of the "holy" angels who kept their own principality (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="jude+1:6">Jude 1:6</span>) and who did not sin (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2pe+2:4">2 Peter 2:4</span>). Paul shows his interest in angels in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1co+4:9">1 Corinthians 4:9</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1co+11:10">1 Corinthians 11:10</span>.</p>
<p><b>Observe </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">φυλαξηις</span></span>). First aorist active subjunctive of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">φυλασσω</span></span>, to guard, to keep (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+2:26">Romans 2:26</span>). Subfinal use of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ινα</span></span>.</p>
<p><b>Without prejudice </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">χωρις προκριματος</span></span>). Late and rare word (from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">προκινω</span></span>, to judge beforehand), three times in the papyri, here only in N.T. "Without prejudgment."</p>
<p><b>By partiality </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κατα προσκλισιν</span></span>). Late word from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">προσκλινω</span></span>, to incline towards one (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+5:36">Acts 5:36</span>), only here in N.T. </p>
