<p><b> O man of God </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ω ανθρωπε θεου</span></span>). In N.T. only here and <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2ti+3:17">2 Timothy 3:17</span>, there general and here personal appeal to Timothy. Cf. <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="de+33:1">Deuteronomy 33:1</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1sa+2:27">1 Samuel 2:27</span>.</p>
<p><b>Flee </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">φευγε</span></span>),</p>
<p><b>follow after </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">διωκε</span></span>). Vivid verbs in present active imperative. The preacher can not afford to parley with such temptations.</p>
