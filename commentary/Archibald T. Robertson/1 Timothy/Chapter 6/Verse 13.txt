<p><b> Who quickeneth all things </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">του ζωογονουντος τα παντα</span></span>). Present active participle of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ζωογονεω</span></span> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ζωογονος</span></span>, from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ζωοσ, γενω</span></span>), late word to give life, to bring forth alive, in N.T. only here and <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+7:19">Acts 7:19</span>. See <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1sa+2:6">1 Samuel 2:6</span>.</p>
<p><b>Before Pontius Pilate </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επ Ποντιου Πειλατου</span></span>). Not "in the time of," but "in the presence of."</p>
<p><b>Witnessed </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μαρτυρησαντος</span></span>). Note <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μαρτυρεω</span></span>, not <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ομολογεω</span></span> as in verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1ti+6:12">1 Timothy 6:12</span>. Christ gave his evidence as a witness to the Kingdom of God. Evidently Paul knew some of the facts that appear in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1ti+6:18">1 Timothy 6:18</span>. </p>
