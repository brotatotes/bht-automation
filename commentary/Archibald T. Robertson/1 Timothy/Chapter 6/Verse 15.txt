 In its own times  (καιροις ιδιοις). Locative case. May be "in his own times." See 1 Timothy 2:6. Clearly not for us to figure out.Who is the blessed and only Potentate  (ο μακαριος κα μονος δυναστης). "The happy and alone Potentate." Δυναστης, old word, in N.T. only here, Luke 1:52; Acts 8:27 (the Eunuch). See 1 Timothy 1:11 for μακαριος.The King of kings  (ο βασιλευς των βασιλευοντων). "The King of those who rule as kings." Oriental title. So with "Lord of lords." See Revelation 10:16.
