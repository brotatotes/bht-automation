<p><b> That they do good </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αγαθοεργειν</span></span>). Late word (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αγαθος</span></span>, <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εργω</span></span>), in N.T. only here and <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+14:17">Acts 14:17</span>.</p>
<p><b>Rich in good works </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πλουτειν εν εργοις καλοις</span></span>). See <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+12:21">Luke 12:21</span> "rich toward God" and <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+6:19">Matthew 6:19</span> for "treasures in heaven."</p>
<p><b>Ready to distribute </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ευμεταδοτους</span></span>). Late and rare verbal (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ευ, μετα, διδωμ</span></span>). Free to give, liberal. Only here in N.T.</p>
<p><b>Willing to communicate </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κοινωνικους</span></span>). Old adjective, ready to share, gracious, liberal again. Only here in N.T. See <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ga+6:6">Galatians 6:6</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="php+4:15">Philippians 4:15</span>. </p>
