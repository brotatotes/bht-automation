<p><b> Laying up in store </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αποθησαυριζοντας</span></span>). Late literary word (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">απο</span></span> and <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">θησαυριζω</span></span>), only here in N.T. Same paradox as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+6:19">Matthew 6:19</span>, "laying up in store" by giving it away.</p>
<p><b>Which is life indeed </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">της οντως ζωης</span></span>). See <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1ti+5:3">1 Timothy 5:3</span> for <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">οντως</span></span>. This life is merely the shadow of the eternal reality to come. </p>
