<p><b> Guard that which is committed unto thee </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">την παραθηκην φυλαξον</span></span>). "Keep (aorist of urgency) the deposit." <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">Παραθηκην</span></span> (from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">παρατιθημ</span></span>, to place beside as a deposit, <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2ti+2:2">2 Timothy 2:2</span>), a banking figure, common in the papyri in this sense for the Attic <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">παρακαταθηκη</span></span> (Textus Receptus here, <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2ti+1:12">2 Timothy 1:12</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2ti+1:14">2 Timothy 1:14</span>). See substantive also in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2ti+1:12">2 Timothy 1:12</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2ti+1:14">2 Timothy 1:14</span>.</p>
<p><b>Turning away from </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εκτρεπομενος</span></span>). Present middle participle of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εκτρεπω</span></span>, for which see <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1ti+1:6">1 Timothy 1:6</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1ti+5:15">1 Timothy 5:15</span>.</p>
<p><b>Babblings </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κενοφωνιας</span></span>). From <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κενοφωνος</span></span>, uttering emptiness. Late and rare compound, in N.T. only here and <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2ti+2:16">2 Timothy 2:16</span>.</p>
<p><b>Oppositions </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αντιθεσεις</span></span>). Old word (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αντι, θεσις</span></span>), antithesis, only here in N.T.</p>
<p><b>Of the knowledge which is falsely so called </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">της ψευδωνυμου γνωσεως</span></span>). "Of the falsely named knowledge." Old word (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ψευδησ, ονομα</span></span>). Our "pseudonymous." Only here in N.T. </p>
