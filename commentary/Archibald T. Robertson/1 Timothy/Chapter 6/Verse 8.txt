<p><b> Food </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">διατροφας</span></span>). Plural, supports or nourishments (from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">διατρεφω</span></span>, to support). Old word, here only in N.T.</p>
<p><b>Covering </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">σκεπασματα</span></span>). Plural, "coverings." Late word from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">σκεπαζω</span></span>, to cover. Here only in N.T.</p>
<p><b>We shall be content </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αρκεσθησομεθα</span></span>). First future passive of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αρκεω</span></span>, to be content. Old word. See <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2co+12:9">2 Corinthians 12:9</span>. This is the <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αυταρκεια</span></span> of verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1ti+6:6">1 Timothy 6:6</span>.</p>
<p><b>There with </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τουτοις</span></span>). Associative instrumental case, "with these." </p>
