<p><b> Desire to be rich </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">βουλομενο πλουτειν</span></span>). The will (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">βουλομα</span></span>) to be rich at any cost and in haste (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="pr+28:20">Proverbs 28:20</span>). Some MSS. have "trust in riches" in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mr+10:24">Mark 10:24</span>. Possibly Paul still has teachers and preachers in mind.</p>
<p><b>Fall into </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εμπιπτουσιν εις</span></span>). See on <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1ti+3:6">1 Timothy 3:6</span> for <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εν -- εις</span></span> and <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1ti+3:7">1 Timothy 3:7</span> for <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">παγιδα</span></span> (snare).</p>
<p><b>Foolish </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ανοητους</span></span>). See <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ga+3:1">Galatians 3:1</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ga+3:3">Galatians 3:3</span>.</p>
<p><b>Hurtful </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">βλαβερας</span></span>). Old adjective from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">βλαπτω</span></span>, to injure, here alone in N.T.</p>
<p><b>Drown </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">βυθιζουσιν</span></span>). Late word (literary <i>Koine</i>) from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">βυθος</span></span> (bottom), to drag to the bottom. In N.T. only here and <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+5:7">Luke 5:7</span> (of the boat). Drown in the lusts with the issue "in destruction and perdition" (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εις ολεθρον κα απωλειαν</span></span>). Not annihilation, but eternal punishment. The combination only here, but for <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ολεθρος</span></span>, see <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1th+5:3">1 Thessalonians 5:3</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2th+1:9">2 Thessalonians 1:9</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1co+5:5">1 Corinthians 5:5</span> and for <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">απωλεια</span></span>, see <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2th+2:3">2 Thessalonians 2:3</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="php+3:19">Philippians 3:19</span>. </p>
