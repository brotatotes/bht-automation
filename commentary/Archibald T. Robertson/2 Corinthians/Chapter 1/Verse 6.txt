<p><b> Whether </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ειτε</span></span>)</p>
<p><b>--or </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ειτε</span></span>). The alternatives in Paul's experience (afflicted <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">θλιβομεθα</span></span>, comforted <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">παρακαλουμεθα</span></span>) work out for their good when they are called on to endure like sufferings "which we also suffer" (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ων κα ημεις πασχομεν</span></span>). The relative <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ων</span></span> is attracted from neuter accusative plural <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">α</span></span> to genitive case of the antecedent <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">παθηματων</span></span> (sufferings). </p>
