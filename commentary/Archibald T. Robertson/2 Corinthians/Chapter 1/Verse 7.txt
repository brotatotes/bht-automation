<p><b> Our hope for you </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">η ελπις ημων υπερ υμων</span></span>). The old word <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ελπις</span></span>, from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ελπιζω</span></span>, to hope, has the idea of waiting with expectation and patience. So here it is "steadfast" (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">βεβαια</span></span>, stable, fast, from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">βαινω</span></span>, to plant the feet down).</p>
<p><b>Partakers </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κοινωνο</span></span>). Partners as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+5:10">Luke 5:10</span>. </p>
