<p><b> What we are </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">οιο εσμεν</span></span>). Rather, "what sort" (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">οιο</span></span>), not <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ο</span></span> (what) nor <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ο</span></span> (who). Literary plural. <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">Hοιος</span></span> is qualitative just as <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τοιουτο</span></span> (such). Paul's quality in his letters when absent (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αποντες</span></span>) and in his deeds when present (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">παροντες</span></span>) is precisely the same. </p>
