<p><b> Even unto the parts beyond you </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εις τα υπερεκεινα υμων</span></span>). Compound adverb (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">υπερ, εκεινα</span></span>, beyond those places) used as preposition. Found only here and in ecclesiastical writers.</p>
<p><b>Things ready to our hand </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τα ετοιμα</span></span>). He had a plenty besides that he could use. </p>
