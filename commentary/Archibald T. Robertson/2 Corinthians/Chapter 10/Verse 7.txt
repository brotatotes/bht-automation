<p><b> Ye look </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">Βλεπετε</span></span>). Either indicative or imperative. Either makes sense but the indicative the best sense.</p>
<p><b>Before your face </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κατα προσωπον</span></span>). They ought to look below the surface. If it is imperative, they should see the facts.</p>
<p><b>That he is Christ's </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">Χριστου εινα</span></span>). Predicate genitive in indirect discourse). </p>
