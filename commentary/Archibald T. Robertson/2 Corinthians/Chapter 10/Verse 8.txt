<p><b> Somewhat abundantly </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">περισσοτερον τ</span></span>). Comparative, "somewhat more abundantly" than I have, in order to show that he is as true a minister of Christ as his accusers are. Concessive (conditional) clause of third class. For <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εαν τε</span></span> see <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+14:8">Romans 14:8</span>.</p>
<p><b>I shall not be put to shame </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ουκ αισχυνθησομα</span></span>). As a convicted impostor or pretentious boaster (Plummer). First future passive, singular number (not literary plural as in verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2co+10:7">2 Corinthians 10:7</span>). </p>
