<p><b> No man shall stop me of this glorying </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">η καυχησις αυτη ου φραγησετα εις εμε</span></span>). More exactly, "This glorying shall not be fenced in as regards me." Second future passive of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">φρασσω</span></span>, to fence in, to stop, to block in. Old verb, only here in N.T.</p>
<p><b>In the regions of Achaia </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εν τοις κλιμασιν της Αχαιας</span></span>). <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">Κλιμα</span></span> from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κλινω</span></span>, to incline, is <i>Koine</i> word for declivity slope, region (our climate). See chapter <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2co+11:1">2 Corinthians 11:1</span> for Paul's boast about preaching the gospel without cost to them. </p>
