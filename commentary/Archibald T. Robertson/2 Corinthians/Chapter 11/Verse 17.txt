<p><b> Not after the Lord </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ου κατα Κυριον</span></span>). Not after the example of the Lord. He had appealed to the example of Christ in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2co+10:1">2 Corinthians 10:1</span> (the meekness and gentleness of Christ). Paul's conduct here, he admits, is not in keeping with that. But circumstances force him on. </p>
