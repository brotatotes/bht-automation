<p><b> With a godly jealousy </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">θεου ζηλω</span></span>). Instrumental case of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ζηλος</span></span>. With a jealousy of God.</p>
<p><b>I espoused </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ηρμοσαμην</span></span>). First aorist middle indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αρμοζω</span></span>, old verb to join, to fit together (from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αρμος</span></span>, joint). Common for betrothed, though only here in N.T. The middle voice indicates Paul's interest in the matter. Paul treats the Corinthians as his bride. </p>
