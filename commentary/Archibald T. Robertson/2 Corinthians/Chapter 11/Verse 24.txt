<p><b> Five times received I forty stripes save one </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πεντακις τεσσερακοντα παρα μιαν ελαβον</span></span>). The Acts and the Epistles are silent about these Jewish floggings (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+27:36">Matthew 27:36</span>). See on <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+12:47">Luke 12:47</span> for omission of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πληγας</span></span> (stripes). Thirty-nine lashes was the rule for fear of a miscount (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="de+25:1-3">Deuteronomy 25:1-3</span>). Cf. Josephus (<i>Ant</i>. IV. 8, 1, 21). </p>
