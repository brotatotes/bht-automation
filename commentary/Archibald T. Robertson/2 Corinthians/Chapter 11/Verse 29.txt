<p><b> I burn </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πυρουμα</span></span>). Present passive indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πυροω</span></span>, old verb to inflame (from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πυρ</span></span>, fire). When a brother stumbles, Paul is set on fire with grief. </p>
