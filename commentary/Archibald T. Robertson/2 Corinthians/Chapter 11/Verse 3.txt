<p><b> The serpent beguiled Eve </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ο οφις εξηπατησεν Hευαν</span></span>). Paul's only mention of the serpent in Eden. The compound <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εξαπαταω</span></span> means to deceive completely.</p>
<p><b>Lest by any means </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μη πως</span></span>). Common conjunction after verbs of fearing.</p>
<p><b>Corrupted </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">φθαρη</span></span>). Second aorist passive subjunctive with <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μη πως</span></span> of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">φθειρω</span></span>, to corrupt. </p>
