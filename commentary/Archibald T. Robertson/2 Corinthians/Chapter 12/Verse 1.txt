<p><b> I must needs glory </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">καυχασθα δε</span></span>). This is the reading of B L Latin Syriac, but Aleph D Bohairic have <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δε</span></span> while K M read <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δη</span></span>. The first is probably correct. He must go on with the glorying already begun, foolish as it is, though it is not expedient (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ου συμφερον</span></span>).</p>
<p><b>Visions </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">οπτασιας</span></span>). Late word from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">οπταζω</span></span>. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+1:22">Luke 1:22</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+26:19">Acts 26:19</span>.</p>
<p><b>Revelations of the Lord </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αποκαλυψεις Κυριου</span></span>). Unveilings (from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αποκαλυπτω</span></span> as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+1:1">Revelation 1:1</span>). See on <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2th+1:7">2 Thessalonians 1:7</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1co+1:7">1 Corinthians 1:7</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1co+14:26">1 Corinthians 14:26</span>. Paul had both repeated visions of Christ (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+9:3">Acts 9:3</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+16:9">Acts 16:9</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+18:9">Acts 18:9</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+22:17">Acts 22:17</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+27:23">Acts 27:23</span>) and revelations. He claimed to speak by direct revelation (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1co+11:23">1 Corinthians 11:23</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1co+15:3">1 Corinthians 15:3</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ga+1:12">Galatians 1:12</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="eph+3:3">Ephesians 3:3</span>, etc.). </p>
