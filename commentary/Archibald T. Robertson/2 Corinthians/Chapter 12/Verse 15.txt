<p><b> I will most gladly spend and be spent </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ηδιστα δαπανησω κα εκδαπανηθησομα</span></span>). Both future active of old verb <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δαπαναω</span></span> (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mr+5:26">Mark 5:26</span>) to spend money, time, energy, strength and the future passive of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εκδαπαναω</span></span>, late compound to spend utterly, to spend out, (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εκ-</span></span>), to spend wholly. Only here in N.T. </p>
