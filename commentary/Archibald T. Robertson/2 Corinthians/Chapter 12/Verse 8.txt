<p><b> Concerning this thing </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">υπερ τουτου</span></span>). More likely, "concerning this messenger of Satan."</p>
<p><b>That it might depart from me </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ινα αποστη αφ' εμου</span></span>). Second aorist active (intransitive) subjunctive of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αφιστημ</span></span> in final clause, "that he stand off from me for good." </p>
