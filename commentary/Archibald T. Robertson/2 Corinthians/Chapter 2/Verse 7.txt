<p><b> So that on the contrary </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ωστε τουναντιον</span></span>). The natural result expressed by <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ωστε</span></span> and the infinitive. <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">Τουναντιον</span></span> is by crasis for <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">το εναντιον</span></span> and accusative of general reference.</p>
<p><b>Rather </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μαλλον</span></span>). Absent in some MSS.</p>
<p><b>Lest by any means </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μη πως</span></span>). Negative purpose.</p>
<p><b>Swallowed up </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">καταποθη</span></span>). First aorist passive subjunctive of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">καταπινω</span></span>, to drink down (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1co+15:54">1 Corinthians 15:54</span>).</p>
<p><b>With his overmuch sorrow </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τη περισσοτερα λυπη</span></span>). Instrumental case, "by the more abundant sorrow" (comparative of adjective <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">περισσος</span></span>). </p>
