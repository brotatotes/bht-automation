<p><b> Passeth away </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">καταργουμενον</span></span>). In process of disappearing before the gospel of Christ.</p>
<p><b>Remaineth </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μενον</span></span>). The new ministry is permanent. This claim may be recommended to those who clamour for a new religion. Christianity is still alive and is not dying. Note also <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εν δοξη</span></span>, in glory, in contrast with <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δια δοξης</span></span>, with glory.</p>
<p><b>Boldness </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">παρρησια</span></span>). Instrumental case after <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">χρωμεθα</span></span>. Old word, <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πανρησισ=παρρησις</span></span>, telling it all, absolute unreservedness. Surely Paul has kept nothing back here, no mental reservations, in this triumphant claim of superiority. </p>
