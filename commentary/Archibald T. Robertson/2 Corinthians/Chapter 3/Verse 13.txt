<p><b> Put a veil upon his face </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ετιθε καλυμμα επ το προσωπον αυτου</span></span>). Imperfect active of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τιθημ</span></span>, used to put (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ex+34:33">Exodus 34:33</span>).</p>
<p><b>That the children of Israel should not look steadfastly </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">προς το μη ατενισα τους υιους</span></span>). Purpose expressed by <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">προς</span></span> and the articular infinitive with negative <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μη</span></span> and the accusative of general reference. The Authorized Version had a wrong translation here as if to hide the glory on his face. </p>
