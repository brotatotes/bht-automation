<p><b> Whensoever Moses is read </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ηνικα αν αναγινωσκητα Μωυσης</span></span>). Indefinite temporal clause with <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ηνικα</span></span> an and the present passive subjunctive.</p>
<p><b>A veil lieth upon their heart </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επ την καρδιαν αυτων κειτα</span></span>). Vivid and distressing picture, a fact that caused Paul agony of heart (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+9:1-5">Romans 9:1-5</span>). With wilful blindness the rabbis set aside the word of God by their tradition in the time of Jesus (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mr+7:8">Mark 7:8</span>). </p>
