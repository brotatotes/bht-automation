<p><b> Of ourselves </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αφ' εαυτων</span></span>). Starting from ourselves (reflexive pronoun).</p>
<p><b>As from ourselves </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ως εξ αυτων</span></span>). He says it over again with preposition <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εξ</span></span> (out of). He has no originating power for such confidence.</p>
<p><b>Sufficiency </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ικανοτης</span></span>). Old word, only here in N.T. </p>
