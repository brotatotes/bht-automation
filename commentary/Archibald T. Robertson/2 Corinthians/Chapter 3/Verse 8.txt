<p><b> How shall not rather? </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πως ουχ μαλλον?</span></span>). <i>Argumentum a minore ad majus</i> (from the less to the greater).</p>
<p><b>Of the spirit </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">του πνευματος</span></span>). Marked by the spirit. Picture of the Christian ministry now. </p>
