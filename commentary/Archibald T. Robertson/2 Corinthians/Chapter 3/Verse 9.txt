<p><b> Of condemnation </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">της κατακρισεως</span></span>). Genitive, that brings condemnation because unable to obey the law.</p>
<p><b>Is glory </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δοξα</span></span>). No copula, but makes the figure bolder. Paul freely admits the glory for the old dispensation.</p>
<p><b>Of righteousness </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">της δικαιοσυνης</span></span>). Marked by and leading to righteousness. See <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2co+11:15">2 Corinthians 11:15</span>.</p>
<p><b>Much more </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πολλω μαλλον</span></span>). Instrumental case, by much more.</p>
<p><b>Exceed </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">περισσευε</span></span>). Overflow. </p>
