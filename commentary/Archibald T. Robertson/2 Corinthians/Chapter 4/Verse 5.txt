<p><b> For we preach not ourselves </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ου γαρ εαυτους κηρυσσομεν</span></span>). Surely as poor and disgusting a topic as a preacher can find.</p>
<p><b>But Christ Jesus as Lord </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αλλα Χριστον Ιησουν Κυριον</span></span>). <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">Κυριον</span></span> is predicate accusative in apposition.</p>
<p><b>As your servants for Jesus' sake </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δουλους υμων δια Ιησουν</span></span>). Your bond-slave for the sake of Jesus. This is the sufficient reason for any preacher's sacrifice, "for Jesus' sake." </p>
