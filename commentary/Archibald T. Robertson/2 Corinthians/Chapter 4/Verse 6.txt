 God who said  (ο θεος ο ειπων). Paraphrase of Genesis 1:3.Who shined  (ος ελαμψεν). Like a lamp in the heart (cf. Matthew 5:15). Miners carry a lamp on the forehead, Christians carry one in their hearts lit by the Spirit of God.To give the light  (προς φωτισμον). For the illumination.In the face of Jesus Christ  (εν προσωπω Ιησου Χριστου). The Christian who looks on the face of Jesus Christ as Moses looked upon the glory of God will be able to give the illumination of the knowledge of the glory of God. See 2 Corinthians 2:10 for προσωπον.
