<p><b> God who said </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ο θεος ο ειπων</span></span>). Paraphrase of <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ge+1:3">Genesis 1:3</span>.</p>
<p><b>Who shined </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ος ελαμψεν</span></span>). Like a lamp in the heart (cf. <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+5:15">Matthew 5:15</span>). Miners carry a lamp on the forehead, Christians carry one in their hearts lit by the Spirit of God.</p>
<p><b>To give the light </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">προς φωτισμον</span></span>). For the illumination.</p>
<p><b>In the face of Jesus Christ </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εν προσωπω Ιησου Χριστου</span></span>). The Christian who looks on the face of Jesus Christ as Moses looked upon the glory of God will be able to give the illumination of the knowledge of the glory of God. See <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2co+2:10">2 Corinthians 2:10</span> for <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">προσωπον</span></span>. </p>
