<p><b> Forsaken </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εγκαταλειπομενο</span></span>). Double compound of old verb <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εγ-κατα-λειπω</span></span>, to leave behind, to leave in the lurch.</p>
<p><b>Smitten down </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">καταβαλλομενο</span></span>). As if overtaken.</p>
<p><b>Destroyed </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">απολλυμενο</span></span>). Perishing as in verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2co+4:3">2 Corinthians 4:3</span>. Was Paul referring to Lystra when the Jews stoned him and thought him dead? </p>
