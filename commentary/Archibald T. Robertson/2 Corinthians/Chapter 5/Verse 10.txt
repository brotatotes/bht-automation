 Before the judgment-seat of Christ  (εμπροσθεν του βηματος του Χριστου). Old word βημα, a step (from βαινω), a platform, the seat of the judge (Matthew 27:19). Christ is Saviour, Lord, and Judge of us all (τους παντας, the all).That each may receive  (ινα κομισητα εκαστος). Receive as his due, κομιζω means, old verb. See on Matthew 25:27.Bad  (φαυλον). Old word, akin to German faul, worthless, of no account, base, wicked.
