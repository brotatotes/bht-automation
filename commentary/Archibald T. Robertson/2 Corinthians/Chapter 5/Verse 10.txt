<p><b> Before the judgment-seat of Christ </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εμπροσθεν του βηματος του Χριστου</span></span>). Old word <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">βημα</span></span>, a step (from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">βαινω</span></span>), a platform, the seat of the judge (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+27:19">Matthew 27:19</span>). Christ is Saviour, Lord, and Judge of us all (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τους παντας</span></span>, the all).</p>
<p><b>That each may receive </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ινα κομισητα εκαστος</span></span>). Receive as his due, <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κομιζω</span></span> means, old verb. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+25:27">Matthew 25:27</span>.</p>
<p><b>Bad </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">φαυλον</span></span>). Old word, akin to German <i>faul</i>, worthless, of no account, base, wicked. </p>
