<p><b> Being clothed </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ενδυσαμενο</span></span>). First aorist middle participle, having put on the garment.</p>
<p><b>Naked </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">γυμνο</span></span>). That is, disembodied spirits, "like the souls in Sheol, without form, and void of all power of activity" (Plummer). </p>
