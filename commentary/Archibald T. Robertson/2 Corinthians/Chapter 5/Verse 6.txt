<p><b> At home in the body </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ενδημουντες εν τω σωματ</span></span>). Rare verb <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ενδημεω</span></span> from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ενδημος</span></span> (one among his own people as opposed to <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εκδημος</span></span>, one away from home). Both <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εκδημεω</span></span> (more common in the old Greek) and <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ενδημεω</span></span> occur in the papyri with the contrast made by Paul here. </p>
