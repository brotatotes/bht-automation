<p><b> Agreement </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">συνκαταθεσις</span></span>). Fifth of these words. Late word, but common, though here only in N.T. Approved by putting together the votes. In the papyri <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εκ συνκαταθεσεως</span></span> means "by agreement." On the temple of God and idols see <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1co+10:14-22">1 Corinthians 10:14-22</span>. See <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+23:51">Luke 23:51</span> for the verb <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">συνκατατιθημ</span></span>.</p>
<p><b>For we are the temple of the living God </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ημεις γαρ ναος θεου εσμεν ζωντος</span></span>). We, not temples (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+7:48">Acts 7:48</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+17:24">Acts 17:24</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1co+3:16">1 Corinthians 3:16</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1co+6:19">1 Corinthians 6:19</span>).</p>
<p><b>As God said </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">καθως ειπεν ο θεος</span></span>). A paraphrase and catena of quotations, what J. Rendel Harris calls <i>Testimonia</i> (from <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="le+26:11">Leviticus 26:11</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="isa+52:11">Isaiah 52:11</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="eze+20:34">Ezekiel 20:34</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="eze+37:27">Ezekiel 37:27</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2sa+7:8">2 Samuel 7:8</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2sa+7:14">2 Samuel 7:14</span>). Plummer notes that at the beginning "I will dwell in them" (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ενοικησω εν αυτοις</span></span>) is not in any of them. "As God said" points to <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="le+26:12">Leviticus 26:12</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="eze+37:27">Ezekiel 37:27</span>. </p>
