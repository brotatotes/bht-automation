<p><b> Saith the Lord Almighty </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">λεγε Κυριος παντοκρατωρ</span></span>). <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2sa+7:8">2 Samuel 7:8</span>. This use of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εις</span></span> is a Hebraism for Hebrew <i>le</i> instead of predicate nominative. <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">Παντοκρατωρ</span></span> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πασ, κρατεω</span></span>, Ruler of all) is common in the LXX. Occurs also in the inscriptions and papyri. In the N.T. only here and in Revelation. </p>
