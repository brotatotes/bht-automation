<p><b> In stripes </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εν πληγαις</span></span>). In blows, wounds (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+10:30">Luke 10:30</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+12:48">Luke 12:48</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+16:23">Acts 16:23</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+16:33">Acts 16:33</span>). Our plague.</p>
<p><b>In tumults </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εν ακαταστασιαις</span></span>). See on <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1co+14:33">1 Corinthians 14:33</span>). Instabilities, often from politics.</p>
