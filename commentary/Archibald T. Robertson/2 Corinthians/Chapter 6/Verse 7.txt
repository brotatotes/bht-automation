<p><b> On the right hand and on the left </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">των δεξιων κα αριστερων</span></span>). Offensive weapons (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">οπλων</span></span>) on the right, defensive on the left. See <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1th+5:8">1 Thessalonians 5:8</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="eph+6:11">Ephesians 6:11</span> for Paul's description of the panoply of God and <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+6:13">Romans 6:13</span> for the phrase "weapons of righteousness," the only kind that will stand the strain. See also Book of Wisdom 5:18ff. </p>
