<p><b> This selfsame thing </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αυτο τουτο</span></span>). "This very thing," "the being made sorry according to God" (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">το κατα θεον λυπηθηνα</span></span>, articular first aorist passive infinitive with which <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αυτο τουτο</span></span> agrees and the proleptic subject of the verb <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κατειργασατο</span></span>.</p>
<p><b>Earnest care </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">σπουδην</span></span>). Diligence, from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">σπευδω</span></span>, to hasten. Cf. <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+12:11">Romans 12:11</span>.</p>
<p><b>Yea </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αλλα</span></span>). Not adversative use of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αλλα</span></span>, but copulative as is common (half dozen examples here).</p>
<p><b>Clearing of yourselves </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">απολογια</span></span>). In the old notion of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">απολογια</span></span> (self-vindication, self-defence) as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1pe+3:15">1 Peter 3:15</span>.</p>
<p><b>Indignation </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αγανακτησιν</span></span>). Old word, only here in N.T. From <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αγανακτεο</span></span> (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mr+10:14">Mark 10:14</span>, etc.).</p>
<p><b>Avenging </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εκδικησιν</span></span>). Late word from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εκδικεω</span></span>, to avenge, to do justice (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+18:5">Luke 18:5</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+21:22">Luke 21:22</span>), vindication from wrong as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+18:7">Luke 18:7</span>, to secure punishment (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1pe+2:14">1 Peter 2:14</span>).</p>
<p><b>Pure </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αγνους</span></span>). Kin to <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αγιος</span></span> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αζω</span></span>, to reverence), immaculate. </p>
