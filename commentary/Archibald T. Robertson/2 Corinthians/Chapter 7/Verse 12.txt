<p><b> But that your earnest care for us might be made manifest </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αλλ' εινεκεν του φανερωθηνα την σπουδην υμων την υπερ ημων</span></span>). So the correct text, not "our care for you." Easy to interchange Greek <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">υμων</span></span> (your) and <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ημων</span></span> (our). Usual construction with preposition <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ενεκεν</span></span> and genitive of articular infinitive with accusative of general reference. </p>
