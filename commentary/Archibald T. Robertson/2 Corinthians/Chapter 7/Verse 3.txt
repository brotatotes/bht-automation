<p><b> Not to condemn you </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">προς κατακρισιν ου</span></span>). "Not for condemnation." Late word from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κατακρινω</span></span>, found in Vettius Valens, and here only in N.T.</p>
<p><b>To die together and live together </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εις το συναποθανειν κα συνζηιν</span></span>). "For the dying together (second aorist ingressive active infinitive of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">συναποθνησκω</span></span>) and living together (present active infinitive)." One article (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">το</span></span>) with both infinitives. You are in our hearts to share death and life. </p>
