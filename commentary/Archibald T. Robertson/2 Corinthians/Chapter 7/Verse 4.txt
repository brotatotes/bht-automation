<p><b> I overflow with joy in all our affliction </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">υπερπερισσευομα τη χαρα επ παση τη θλιψε ημων</span></span>). A thoroughly Pauline sentiment. <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">Περισσευω</span></span> means to overflow, as we have seen. <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">Hυπερ-περισσευω</span></span> (late word, so far only here and Byzantine writers) is to have a regular flood. Vulgate <i>superabundo</i>. </p>
