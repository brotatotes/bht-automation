<p><b> Cormforteth </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">παρακαλων</span></span>). See on <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2co+1:3-7">2 Corinthians 1:3-7</span> for this word.</p>
<p><b>The lowly </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τους ταπεινους</span></span>). See on <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+11:29">Matthew 11:29</span>. Literally, low on the ground in old sense (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="eze+17:24">Ezekiel 17:24</span>). Low in condition as here; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="jas+1:9">James 1:9</span>. In <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2co+10:1">2 Corinthians 10:1</span> regarded as abject. In this sense in papyri. "Humility as a sovereign grace is the creation of Christianity" (Gladstone, <i>Life</i>, iii, p. 466).</p>
