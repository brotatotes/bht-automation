<p><b> The readiness to will </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">η προθυμια του θελειν</span></span>). Old word from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">προθυμος</span></span> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">προ, θυμος</span></span>), forwardness, eagerness (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+17:11">Acts 17:11</span>). They were quick to pledge.</p>
<p><b>The completion also </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κα το επιτελεσα</span></span>). The finishing also (articular first aorist active infinitive).</p>
<p><b>Out of your ability </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εκ του εχειν</span></span>). "Out of the having," literally, and so, "out of what you can give" (verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2co+8:12">2 Corinthians 8:12</span>). </p>
