<p><b> Proof </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δοκιμη</span></span>). Tests as of metals as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2co+2:9">2 Corinthians 2:9</span>.</p>
<p><b>Abundance </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">περισσεια</span></span>). Late word from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">περισσευω</span></span>, to overflow.</p>
<p><b>Their deep poverty </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">η κατα βαθους πτωχεια αυτων</span></span>). <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">Πτωχεια</span></span> is old word from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πτωχευω</span></span>, to be a beggar, as of Jesus in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2co+8:9">2 Corinthians 8:9</span> (from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πτωχος</span></span>, cowering in fear and poverty, as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+14:13">Luke 14:13</span>, but ennobled by Christ as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+5:3">Matthew 5:3</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2co+8:9">2 Corinthians 8:9</span>). Poverty down deep. Strabo (LX 419) has <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κατα βαθους</span></span>, down to the bottom.</p>
<p><b>Liberality </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">απλοτητος</span></span>). From <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">απλους</span></span>, single, simple (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+6:22">Matthew 6:22</span>). "The passage from single-mindedness or simplicity to liberality is not quite obvious" (Plummer). Perhaps "heartiness" supplies the connecting link. See also <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2co+9:11-13">2 Corinthians 9:11-13</span>. </p>
