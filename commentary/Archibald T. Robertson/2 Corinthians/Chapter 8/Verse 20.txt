<p><b> Avoiding this </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">στελλομενο τουτο</span></span>). Present middle participle of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">στελλω</span></span>, old verb, to set, to arrange. So "arranging for ourselves this."</p>
<p><b>That any man should blame us </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μη τις ημας μωμησητα</span></span>). Literally, "lest any one blame us" (negative purpose with <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μη</span></span> and first aorist middle subjunctive of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μωμεομα</span></span>. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2co+6:3">2 Corinthians 6:3</span>, only other N.T. example).</p>
<p><b>Bounty </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αδροτητ</span></span>). Old word from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αδρος</span></span>, thick, stout, ripe, rich, great as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1ki+1:9">1 Kings 1:9</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2ki+10:6">2 Kings 10:6</span>. Only here in N.T. </p>
