<p><b> About Titus </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">υπερ Τιτου</span></span>). There is no verb expressed. Supply "inquire." He endorses Titus up to the hilt. He is "my partner" (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κοινωνος εμος</span></span>) and "fellow-worker" (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">συνεργος</span></span>).</p>
<p><b>Messengers of the churches </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αποστολο εκκλησιων</span></span>). Apostles in the general sense of "sent ones" (from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αποστελλω</span></span>, to send) by the churches and responsible to the churches for the handling of the funds.</p>
<p><b>The glory of Christ </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δοξα Χριστου</span></span>). Financial agents, please observe. </p>
