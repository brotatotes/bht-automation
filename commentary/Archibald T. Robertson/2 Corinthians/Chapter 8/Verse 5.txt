<p><b> We had hoped </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ηλπισαμεν</span></span>). First aorist active indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ελπιζω</span></span>. "Expected," he means. They went beyond his hopes about them.</p>
<p><b>First they gave their own selves </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εαυτους εδωκαν πρωτον</span></span>). First aorist active indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">διδωμ</span></span> (k aorist). "Themselves they gave first." That is the explanation of the generous giving. </p>
