<p><b> Proving </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δοκιμαζων</span></span>). Testing and so proving.</p>
<p><b>The sincerity also of your love </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κα το της υμετερας αγαπης γνησιον</span></span>). Old adjective, contraction of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">γενεσιος</span></span> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">γινομα</span></span>), legitimately born, not spurious. A collection is a test of one's love for Christ, not the only test, but a real one. </p>
