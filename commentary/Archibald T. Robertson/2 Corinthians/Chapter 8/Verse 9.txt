 Though he was rich  (πλουσιος ων). Concessive present participle ων from ειμ, to be.Be became poor  (επτωχευσεν). Ingressive aorist active indicative of πτωχευω (see verse 2 Corinthians 8:2 on πτωχεια).Through his poverty  (τη εκεινου πτωχεια). Instrumental case, by means of.Might become rich  (πλουτησητε).
