 I glory  (καυχωμα). Present middle indicative. I still am glorying, in spite of the poor performance of the Corinthians.Hath been prepared  (παρεσκευαστα). Perfect passive indicative of παρασκευαζω, to make ready, "stands prepared."Stirred up  (ηρεθισε). First aorist active indicative of ερεθιζω (from ερεθω, to excite), to excite in a good sense here, in a bad sense in Colossians 3:21, the only N.T. examples.Very many of them  (τους πλειονας). The more, the majority.
