<p><b> Sparingly </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">φειδομενως</span></span>). Late and rare adverb made from the present middle participle <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">φειδομενος</span></span> from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">φειδομα</span></span>, to spare. It occurs in Plutarch (Alex. 25). </p>
