<p><b> Of thine elect sister </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">της αδελφης σου της εκλεκτης</span></span>). Same word <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εκλεκτη</span></span> as in verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2jo+1:1">2 John 1:1</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+17:4">Revelation 17:4</span>. Apparently children of a deceased sister of the lady of verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2jo+1:1">2 John 1:1</span> who lived in Ephesus and whom John knew as members of his church there. </p>
