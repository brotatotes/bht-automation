<p><b> Beseech </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ερωτω</span></span>). For pray as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1jo+5:16">1 John 5:16</span>.</p>
<p><b>Lady </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κυρια</span></span>). Vocative case and in the same sense as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2jo+1:1">2 John 1:1</span>.</p>
<p><b>As though I wrote </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ως γραφων</span></span>). Common idiom <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ως</span></span> with the participle (present active) for the alleged reason.</p>
<p><b>New </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">καινην</span></span>). As in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1jo+2:7">1 John 2:7</span>, which see.</p>
<p><b>We had </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ειχαμεν</span></span>). Imperfect active (late <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">-α</span></span> form like <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ειχαν</span></span> in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mr+8:7">Mark 8:7</span>) of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εχω</span></span> and note <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ειχετε</span></span> with <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">απ' αρχης</span></span> in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1jo+2:7">1 John 2:7</span>. Not literary plural, John identifying all Christians with himself in this blessing.</p>
<p><b>That we love one another </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ινα αγαπωμεν αλληλους</span></span>). Either a final clause after <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ερωτω</span></span> as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+17:15">John 17:15</span> or an object clause in apposition with <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εντολην</span></span>, like <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1jo+2:27">1 John 2:27</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1jo+3:23">1 John 3:23</span> and like verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2jo+1:6">2 John 1:6</span>. </p>
