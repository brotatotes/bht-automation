<p><b> Wherefore </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">διο</span></span>). Because of the exhortation and argument in verses <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2pe+1:5-9">2 Peter 1:5-9</span>.</p>
<p><b>Give the more diligence </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μαλλον σπουδασατε</span></span>). "Become diligent (first aorist ingressive active imperative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">σπουδαζω</span></span> as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2ti+2:15">2 Timothy 2:15</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2pe+1:15">2 Peter 1:15</span>) the more" (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μαλλον</span></span>, not less).</p>
<p><b>To make </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ποιεισθα</span></span>). Present middle infinitive of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ποιεω</span></span>, to make for yourselves.</p>
<p><b>Calling and election </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κλησιν κα εκλογην</span></span>). Both words (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κλησιν</span></span>, the invitation, <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εκλογην</span></span>, actual acceptance). See for <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εκλογη</span></span> <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1th+1:4">1 Thessalonians 1:4</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+9:11">Romans 9:11</span>.</p>
<p><b>If ye do </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ποιουντες</span></span>). Present active circumstantial (conditional) participle of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ποιεω</span></span>, "doing."</p>
<p><b>Ye shall never stumble </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ου μη πταισητε ποτε</span></span>). Strong double negative (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ου μη ποτε</span></span>) with first aorist active subjunctive of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πταιω</span></span>, old verb to stumble, to fall as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="jas+2:10">James 2:10</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="jas+3:2">James 3:2</span>. </p>
