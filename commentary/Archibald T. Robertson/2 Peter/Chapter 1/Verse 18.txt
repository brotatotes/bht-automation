<p><b> This voice </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ταυτην την φωνην</span></span>). The one referred to in verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2pe+1:17">2 Peter 1:17</span>.</p>
<p><b>We heard </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ηκουσαμεν</span></span>). First aorist active indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ακουω</span></span>, a definite experience of Peter.</p>
<p><b>Brought </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ενεχθεισαν</span></span>). "Borne" as in verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2pe+1:17">2 Peter 1:17</span>.</p>
<p><b>When we were with him </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">συν αυτω οντες</span></span>). Present active participle of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ειμ</span></span>, "being with him."</p>
<p><b>In the holy mount </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εν τω αγιω ορε</span></span>). Made holy by the majestic glory. See <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="eze+28:14">Ezekiel 28:14</span> for "holy mount of God," there Sinai, this one probably one of the lower slopes of Hermon. Peter's account is independent of the Synoptic narrative, but agrees with it in all essentials. </p>
