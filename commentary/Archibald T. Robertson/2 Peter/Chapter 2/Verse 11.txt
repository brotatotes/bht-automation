<p><b> Whereas </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">οπου</span></span>). Loose use of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">οπου</span></span> (in Xenophon) = "wherein."</p>
<p><b>Though greater </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μειζονες οντες</span></span>). Than the evil <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δοξα</span></span>. Concessive participle and comparative adjective.</p>
<p><b>In might and strength </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ισχυ κα δυναμε</span></span>). Locative case. Both indwelling strength (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ισχυς</span></span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mr+12:30">Mark 12:30</span>) and ability (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δυναμις</span></span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+25:15">Matthew 25:15</span>).</p>
<p><b>Railing judgment </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">βλασφεμον κρισιν</span></span>). "Blasphemous accusation."</p>
<p><b>Against them </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κατ' αυτων</span></span>). The evil angels (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δοξα</span></span>).</p>
<p><b>Before the Lord </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">παρα κυριω</span></span>). In God's presence. See <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="jude+1:9">Jude 1:9</span> and possibly Enoch 9. </p>
