<p><b> Forsaking </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">καταλειποντες</span></span>). Present active participle of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">καταλειπω</span></span> (continually leaving) or <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">καταλιποντες</span></span> (second aorist active), having left.</p>
<p><b>The right way </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ευθειαν οδον</span></span>). "The straight way" of <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1sa+12:23">1 Samuel 12:23</span> (cf. <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+7:13">Matthew 7:13</span> for this use of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">οδος</span></span>), "the way of truth" (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2pe+2:2">2 Peter 2:2</span>).</p>
<p><b>They went astray </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επλανηθησαν</span></span>). First aorist passive indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πλαναω</span></span>, like <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mr+12:24">Mark 12:24</span>.</p>
<p><b>The way of Balaam </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τη οδω του Βαλααμ</span></span>). Associative instrumental case after <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εξακολουθησαντες</span></span>, for which verb see <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2pe+1:16">2 Peter 1:16</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2pe+2:2">2 Peter 2:2</span>. These false teachers, as shown in verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2pe+2:13">2 Peter 2:13</span>, followed the way of Balaam, "who loved the hire of wrong-doing" (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ος μισθον αδικιας ηγαπησεν</span></span>). </p>
