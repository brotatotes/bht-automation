<p><b> For </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">γαρ</span></span>). Parenthetical explanation in verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2pe+2:8">2 Peter 2:8</span> of the remark about Lot.</p>
<p><b>Dwelling </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ενκατοικων</span></span>). Present active participle of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ενκατοικεω</span></span>, old but rare double compound, here only in N.T.</p>
<p><b>In seeing and hearing </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">βλεμματ κα ακοη</span></span>). "By sight (instrumental case of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">βλεμμα</span></span>, old word, from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">βλεπω</span></span> to see, here only in N.T.) and hearing" (instrumental case of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ακοη</span></span> from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ακουω</span></span>, to hear, common as <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+13:14">Matthew 13:14</span>).</p>
<p><b>From day to day </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ημεραν εξ ημερας</span></span>). "Day in day out." Accusative of time and ablative with <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εξ</span></span>. Same idiom in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ps+96:2">Psalms 96:2</span> for the more common <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εξ ημερας εις ημεραν</span></span>.</p>
<p><b>Vexed </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εβασανιζεν</span></span>). Imperfect active (kept on vexing) of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">βασανιζω</span></span>, old word, to test metals, to torment (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+8:29">Matthew 8:29</span>).</p>
<p><b>With their lawless deeds </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ανομοις εργοις</span></span>). Instrumental case of cause, "because of their lawless (contrary to law) deeds." For <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ανομος</span></span> see <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2th+2:8">2 Thessalonians 2:8</span>. </p>
