<p><b> Knowing these things beforehand </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">προγινωσκοντες</span></span>). Present active participle of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">προγινωσκω</span></span> as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1pe+1:20">1 Peter 1:20</span>. Cf. <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πρωτον γινωσκω</span></span> (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2pe+1:20">2 Peter 1:20</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2pe+3:1">2 Peter 3:1</span>). Hence they are without excuse for misunderstanding Peter or Paul on this subject.</p>
<p><b>Beware </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">φυλασσεσθε</span></span>). Present middle imperative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">φυλασσω</span></span>, common verb, to guard.</p>
<p><b>Lest </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ινα μη</span></span>). Negative purpose, "that not."</p>
<p><b>Being carried away </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">συναπαχθεντες</span></span>). First aorist passive participle of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">συναπαγω</span></span>, old verb double compound, to carry away together with, in N.T. only here and <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ga+2:13">Galatians 2:13</span>.</p>
<p><b>With the error </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τη πλανη</span></span>). Instrumental case, "by the error" (the wandering).</p>
<p><b>Of the wicked </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">των αθεσμων</span></span>). See on <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2pe+2:7">2 Peter 2:7</span>.</p>
<p><b>Ye fall from </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εκπεσητε</span></span>). Second aorist active subjunctive with <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ινα μη</span></span> of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εκπιπτω</span></span>, old verb, to fall out of, with the ablative here (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">στηριγμου</span></span>, steadfastness, late word from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">στηριζω</span></span>, here alone in N.T.) as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ga+5:4">Galatians 5:4</span> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">της χαριτος εξεπεσατε</span></span>, ye fell out of grace). </p>
