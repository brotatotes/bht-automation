<p><b> By which means </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δι' ων</span></span>). The two waters above or the water and the word of God. Mayor against the MSS. reads <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δι' ου</span></span> (singular) and refers it to <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">λογω</span></span> alone.</p>
<p><b>Being overshadowed </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κατακλυσθεις</span></span>). First aorist passive participle of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κατακλυζω</span></span>, old compound, here only in N.T., but see <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κατακλυσμος</span></span> in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2pe+2:5">2 Peter 2:5</span>.</p>
<p><b>With water </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">υδατ</span></span>). Instrumental case of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">υδωρ</span></span>.</p>
<p><b>Perished </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">απωλετο</span></span>). Second aorist middle indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">απολλυμ</span></span>. </p>
