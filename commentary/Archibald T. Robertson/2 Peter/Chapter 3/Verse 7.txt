<p><b> That now are </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">νυν</span></span>). "The now heavens" over against "the then world" (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ο τοτε κοσμος</span></span> verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2pe+3:6">2 Peter 3:6</span>).</p>
<p><b>By the same word </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τω αυτω λογω</span></span>). Instrumental case again referring to <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">λογω</span></span> in verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2pe+3:6">2 Peter 3:6</span>.</p>
<p><b>Have been stored up </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τεθησαυρισμενο εισιν</span></span>). Perfect passive indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">θησαυριζω</span></span>, for which verb see <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+6:19">Matthew 6:19</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+12:21">Luke 12:21</span>.</p>
<p><b>For fire </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πυρ</span></span>). Dative case of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πυρ</span></span>, not with fire (instrumental case). The destruction of the world by fire is here pictured as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joe+2:30">Joel 2:30</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ps+50:3">Psalms 50:3</span>.</p>
<p><b>Being reserved </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τηρουμενο</span></span>). Present passive participle of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τηρεω</span></span>, for which see <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2pe+2:4">2 Peter 2:4</span>.</p>
<p><b>Against </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εις</span></span>). Unto. As in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2pe+2:4">2 Peter 2:4</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2pe+2:9">2 Peter 2:9</span> and see <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1pe+1:4">1 Peter 1:4</span> for the inheritance reserved for the saints of God. </p>
