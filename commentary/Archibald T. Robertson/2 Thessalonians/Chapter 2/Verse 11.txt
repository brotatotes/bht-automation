<p><b> And for this reason God sendeth them </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κα δια τουτο πεμπε αυτοις ο θεος</span></span>). Futuristic (prophetic) present of the time when the lawless one is revealed. Here is the definite judicial act of God (Milligan) who gives the wicked over to the evil which they have deliberately chosen (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+1:24">Romans 1:24</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+1:26">Romans 1:26</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+1:28">Romans 1:28</span>).</p>
<p><b>A working of error </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ενεργειαν πλανης</span></span>). Terrible result of wilful rejection of the truth of God.</p>
<p><b>That they should believe a lie </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εις το πιστευσα αυτους τω ψευδε</span></span>). Note <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εις το</span></span> again and <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τω ψευδε</span></span> (the lie, the falsehood already described), a contemplated result. Note <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+1:25">Romans 1:25</span> "who changed the truth of God into the lie." </p>
