<p>See <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2th+1:3">2 Thessalonians 1:3</span> for same beginning.</p>
<p><b>Beloved of the Lord </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ηγαπημενο υπο κυριου</span></span>). Perfect passive participle of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αγαπαω</span></span> with <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">υπο</span></span> and the ablative as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1th+1:4">1 Thessalonians 1:4</span>, only here <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κυριου</span></span> instead of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">θεου</span></span>, the Lord Jesus rather than God the Father.</p>
<p><b>Because that God chose you </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">οτ ειλατο υμας ο θεος</span></span>). First aorist middle indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αιρεω</span></span>, to take, old verb, but uncompounded only in N.T. here, <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="php+1:22">Philippians 1:22</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="heb+11:25">Hebrews 11:25</span>, and here only in sense of</p>
<p><b>choose </b>, that being usually <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εξαιρεομα</span></span> or <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">προοριζω</span></span>.</p>
<p><b>From the beginning </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">απ' αρχης</span></span>). Probably the correct text (Aleph D L) and not <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">απαρχην</span></span> (first fruits, B G P), though here alone in Paul's writings and a hard reading, the eternal choice or purpose of God (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1co+2:7">1 Corinthians 2:7</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="eph+1:4">Ephesians 1:4</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2ti+1:9">2 Timothy 1:9</span>), while <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">απαρχην</span></span> is a favourite idea with Paul (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1co+15:20">1 Corinthians 15:20</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1co+15:23">1 Corinthians 15:23</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1co+16:15">1 Corinthians 16:15</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+8:23">Romans 8:23</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+11:16">Romans 11:16</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+16:5">Romans 16:5</span>).</p>
<p><b>Unto salvation </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εις σωτηριαν</span></span>). The ultimate goal, final salvation.</p>
<p><b>In sanctification of the Spirit </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εν αγιασμω πνευματος</span></span>). Subjective genitive <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πνευματος</span></span>, sanctification wrought by the Holy Spirit.</p>
<p><b>And belief of the truth </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κα πιστε αληθειας</span></span>). Objective genitive <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αληθειας</span></span>, belief in the truth. </p>
