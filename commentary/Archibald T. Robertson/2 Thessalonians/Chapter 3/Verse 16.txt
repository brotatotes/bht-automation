<p><b>Give you peace </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δοιη υμιν την ειρηνην</span></span>). Second aorist active optative (<i>Koine</i>) of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">διδωμ</span></span>, not <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δωη</span></span> (subjunctive). So also <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+15:5">Romans 15:5</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2ti+1:16">2 Timothy 1:16</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2ti+1:18">2 Timothy 1:18</span>. The Lord Jesus whose characteristic is peace, can alone give real peace to the heart and to the world. (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+14:27">John 14:27</span>). </p>
