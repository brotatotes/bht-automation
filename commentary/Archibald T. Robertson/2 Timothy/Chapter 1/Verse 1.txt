<p><b> According to the promise of the life which is in Christ Jesus </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κατ' επαγγελιαν ζωης της εν Χριστω Ιησου</span></span>). "With a view to the fulfilment of the promise." See <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="tit+1:1">Titus 1:1</span> for this same use of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κατα</span></span>. For <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κατ' επαγγελιαν</span></span> see <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ga+3:29">Galatians 3:29</span>. See <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1ti+4:8">1 Timothy 4:8</span> for the phrase "promise of life." Here or there "life that in Christ Jesus" includes the present as well as the future. </p>
