<p><b> When he was in Rome </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">γενομενος εν Ρομη</span></span>). Second aorist middle participle of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">γινομα</span></span> (coming to Rome, happening in Rome).</p>
<p><b>He sought me diligently and found me </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">σπουδαιως εζητησεν με κα ευρεν</span></span>). Effective aorists both of them (first of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ζητεω</span></span>, second of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ευρισκω</span></span>). He did it at the risk of his own life apparently. </p>
