<p><b> Grant to him to and mercy </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δωιη αυτω ευρειν ελεος</span></span>). Second aorist active optative in wish for the future again as in verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2ti+1:16">2 Timothy 1:16</span>. Find mercy from the Lord (Jesus) as he found me.</p>
<p><b>Thou knowest very well </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">βελτιον συ γινωσκεις</span></span>). Literally, "thou knowest better (than I)," for he did those things in Ephesus where thou art. Only N.T. example of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">βελτιον</span></span>, in D text of <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+10:28">Acts 10:28</span>. </p>
