<p><b> A spirit of fearfulness </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πνευμα δειλιας</span></span>). Here <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πνευμα</span></span> is the <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">χαρισμα</span></span> of verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2ti+1:6">2 Timothy 1:6</span>, the human spirit as endowed by the Holy Spirit (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+8:15">Romans 8:15</span>). <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">Δειλια</span></span> is an old word (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δειλοσ, δειδω</span></span>) and always in a bad sense of cowardice, only here in N.T.</p>
<p><b>Of power </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δυναμεως</span></span>). One of Paul's characteristic words (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+1:16">Romans 1:16</span>).</p>
<p><b>Of love </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αγαπης</span></span>). One of the gifts of the Spirit (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ga+5:22">Galatians 5:22</span>). "Which drives out fear" (Lock) as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1jo+4:18">1 John 4:18</span>.</p>
<p><b>Of discipline </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">σωφρονισμου</span></span>). Late <i>Koine</i> word (from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">σωφρονιζω</span></span>, to control), self-control, here only in N.T. See <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1ti+2:9">1 Timothy 2:9</span> for <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">σωφροσυνη</span></span>. </p>
