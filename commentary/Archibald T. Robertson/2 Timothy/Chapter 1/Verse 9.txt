<p><b> Called us with a holy calling </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">καλεσαντος κλησε αγια</span></span>). Probably dative, "to a holy calling." <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">Κλησις</span></span> here apparently not the invitation, but the consecrated service, "the upward calling" (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="php+3:14">Philippians 3:14</span>). See <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1co+7:20">1 Corinthians 7:20</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="eph+4:1">Ephesians 4:1</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="eph+4:4">Ephesians 4:4</span> for the use of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">καλεω</span></span> with <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κλησις</span></span>. Paul often uses <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">καλεω</span></span> of God's calling men (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1th+2:12">1 Thessalonians 2:12</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1co+1:9">1 Corinthians 1:9</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ga+1:6">Galatians 1:6</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+8:20">Romans 8:20</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+9:11">Romans 9:11</span>).</p>
<p><b>Purpose </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">προθεσιν</span></span>). See <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+9:11">Romans 9:11</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="eph+1:11">Ephesians 1:11</span> for <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">προθεσιν</span></span>.</p>
<p><b>Which was given </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">την δοθεισαν</span></span>). First aorist passive articular participle agreeing with <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">χαρις</span></span> (grace), a thoroughly Pauline expression (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1co+3:10">1 Corinthians 3:10</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+12:3">Romans 12:3</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+12:6">Romans 12:6</span>, etc.), only here in Pastoral Epistles.</p>
<p><b>Before times eternal </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">προ χρονων αιωνιων</span></span>). See <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="tit+1:2">Titus 1:2</span>. </p>
