<p><b> Be strengthened </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ενδυναμου</span></span>). Present passive imperative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ενδυναμοω</span></span>. See already <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1ti+1:12">1 Timothy 1:12</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+4:20">Romans 4:20</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="php+4:13">Philippians 4:13</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="eph+6:10">Ephesians 6:10</span>. "Keep on being empowered," "keep in touch with the power."</p>
<p><b>In the grace that is in Christ Jesus </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εν τη χαριτ τη εν Χριστω Ιησου</span></span>). Where the power is located. Christ is the dynamo for power only when and while we keep in touch with him. </p>
