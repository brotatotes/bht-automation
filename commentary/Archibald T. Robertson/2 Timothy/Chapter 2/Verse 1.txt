 Be strengthened  (ενδυναμου). Present passive imperative of ενδυναμοω. See already 1 Timothy 1:12; Romans 4:20; Philippians 4:13; Ephesians 6:10. "Keep on being empowered," "keep in touch with the power."In the grace that is in Christ Jesus  (εν τη χαριτ τη εν Χριστω Ιησου). Where the power is located. Christ is the dynamo for power only when and while we keep in touch with him.
