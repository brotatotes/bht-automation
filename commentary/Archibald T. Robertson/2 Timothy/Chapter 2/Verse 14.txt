 That they strive not about words  (μη λογομαχειν). Word apparently coined by Paul from λογομαχια (1 Timothy 6:4 which see), a back formation in that case. A mere war of words displeases Paul. (Titus 3:9).Useful  (χρησιμον). Late and rare word from χραομα, here only in N.T.To the subverting  (επ καταστροφη). Old word (from καταστρεφω, to turn down or over), here only in N.T. (except 2 Peter 2:6 in some MSS., not in Westcott and Hort)." Because of the overthrow" (result επ, not aim), useless for this reason. Such war of words merely upsets the hearers.
