<p><b> Howbeit </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μεντο</span></span>). Strong adversative, "however."</p>
<p><b>Firm </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">στερεος</span></span>). Old adjective, solid, compact, in N.T. only here, <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1pe+5:9">1 Peter 5:9</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="heb+5:12">Hebrews 5:12</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="heb+5:14">Hebrews 5:14</span>. See <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">στερεωμα</span></span> in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="col+2:5">Colossians 2:5</span>. For <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">θεμελιος</span></span> see <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1co+3:11">1 Corinthians 3:11</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+15:20">Romans 15:20</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1ti+6:19">1 Timothy 6:19</span>. Cf. <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εδραιωμα</span></span> in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1ti+3:15">1 Timothy 3:15</span>.</p>
<p><b>Seal </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">σφραγις</span></span>). See <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1co+9:2">1 Corinthians 9:2</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+4:11">Romans 4:11</span>.</p>
<p><b>Knoweth </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εγνω</span></span>). Timeless aorist active indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">γινωσκω</span></span>. Quotation from <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="nu+16:5">Numbers 16:5</span>.</p>
<p><b>Let every one depart </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αποστητω πας</span></span>). Paraphrase of <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="nu+16:27">Numbers 16:27</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="isa+26:13">Isaiah 26:13</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="isa+52:11">Isaiah 52:11</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="jer+20:9">Jeremiah 20:9</span>. Second aorist active imperative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αφιστημ</span></span> (intransitive use), "Let every one stand off from." Probably another echo of the rebellion of Korah. </p>
