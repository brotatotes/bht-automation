<p><b> Youthful </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">νεωτερικας</span></span>). Literary <i>Koine</i> word (Polybius, Josephus), only here in N.T. There are lusts peculiar to flaming youth.</p>
<p><b>Flee </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">φευγε</span></span>). Present active imperative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">φευγω</span></span>, old and common verb. In this sense see <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1co+6:18">1 Corinthians 6:18</span>.</p>
<p><b>Follow after </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">διωκε</span></span>). Present active imperative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">διωκω</span></span> as if in a chase for which sense see <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1th+5:15">1 Thessalonians 5:15</span>. Steady pursuit of these virtues like those in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ga+5:22">Galatians 5:22</span>.</p>
<p><b>Call on the Lord </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επικαλουμενον τον κυριον</span></span>). See <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1co+1:2">1 Corinthians 1:2</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+10:12-14">Romans 10:12-14</span>. </p>
