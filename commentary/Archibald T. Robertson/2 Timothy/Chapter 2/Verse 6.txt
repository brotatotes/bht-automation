 The husbandman that laboureth  (τον κοπιωντα γεωργον). "The toiling tiller of the soil" (γεωργον, from γη and εργω, worker of the earth). See γεωργιον (field) in 1 Corinthians 3:9 and also 1 Corinthians 9:7.First  (πρωτον). As is natural and right.To partake  (μεταλαμβανειν). Old word as in Acts 2:46 to share in. Paul elsewhere uses μετεχω as in 1 Corinthians 9:12.
