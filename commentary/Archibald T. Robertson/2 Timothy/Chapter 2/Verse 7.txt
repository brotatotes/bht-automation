<p><b> Consider </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">νοε</span></span>). Present active imperative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">νοεω</span></span>, old verb, to put your mind (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">νους</span></span>) on. See <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="eph+3:4">Ephesians 3:4</span> and like command in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1co+10:15">1 Corinthians 10:15</span>.</p>
<p><b>Understanding </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">συνεσιν</span></span>). "Comprehension" (from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">συνιημ</span></span>, to send together, to grasp). See <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="col+1:9">Colossians 1:9</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="col+2:2">Colossians 2:2</span>. This is a blessed promise that calls for application. </p>
