<p><b> Wherein </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εν ω</span></span>). In my gospel.</p>
<p><b>I suffer hardship </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κακοπαθω</span></span>). "I suffer evil." Old compound (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κακον, πασχω</span></span>), elsewhere in N.T., <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2ti+4:5">2 Timothy 4:5</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="jas+5:13">James 5:13</span>.</p>
<p><b>Unto bonds </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μεχρ δεσμων</span></span>). "Up to bonds." A common experience with Paul (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2co+11:23">2 Corinthians 11:23</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="php+1:7">Philippians 1:7</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="php+1:13">Philippians 1:13</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="php+1:14">Philippians 1:14</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="col+4:18">Colossians 4:18</span>).</p>
<p><b>As a malefactor </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ως κακουργος</span></span>), old compound (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κακον, εργω</span></span>, doer of evil), in N.T. only here and <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+23:32">Luke 23:32</span> (of the robbers). One of the charges made against Paul.</p>
<p><b>Is not bound </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ου δεδετα</span></span>). Perfect passive indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δεω</span></span>, to bind. Old verb. See <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1co+7:27">1 Corinthians 7:27</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1co+7:39">1 Corinthians 7:39</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+7:2">Romans 7:2</span>. I am bound with a chain, but no fetters are on the word of God (Pauline phrase; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1th+2:13">1 Thessalonians 2:13</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1co+14:36">1 Corinthians 14:36</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2co+2:17">2 Corinthians 2:17</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="php+1:14">Philippians 1:14</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="tit+2:5">Titus 2:5</span>). </p>
