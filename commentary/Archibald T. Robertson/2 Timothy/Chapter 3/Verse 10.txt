 Didst follow  (παρηκολουθησας). First aorist active indicative of παρακολουθεω, for which see 1 Timothy 4:6. Some MSS. have perfect active παρηκολουθηκας (thou hast followed). Nine associative-instrumental cases here after the verb ( teaching , διδασκαλια, Romans 12:7;conduct , αγωγη, old word here only in N.T.;purpose , προθεσε, Romans 8:28;faith , πιστε, 1 Thessalonians 3:6;longsuffering , μακροθυμια, Colossians 1:11;persecutions , διωγμοις, 2 Thessalonians 1:4;sufferings , παθημασιν, 2 Corinthians 1:6). The two last items belong to verse 2 Timothy 3:11.
