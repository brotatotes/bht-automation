<p><b> Didst follow </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">παρηκολουθησας</span></span>). First aorist active indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">παρακολουθεω</span></span>, for which see <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1ti+4:6">1 Timothy 4:6</span>. Some MSS. have perfect active <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">παρηκολουθηκας</span></span> (thou hast followed). Nine associative-instrumental cases here after the verb (<b> teaching </b>, <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">διδασκαλια</span></span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+12:7">Romans 12:7</span>;</p>
<p><b>conduct </b>, <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αγωγη</span></span>, old word here only in N.T.;</p>
<p><b>purpose </b>, <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">προθεσε</span></span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+8:28">Romans 8:28</span>;</p>
<p><b>faith </b>, <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πιστε</span></span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1th+3:6">1 Thessalonians 3:6</span>;</p>
<p><b>longsuffering </b>, <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μακροθυμια</span></span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="col+1:11">Colossians 1:11</span>;</p>
<p><b>persecutions </b>, <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">διωγμοις</span></span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2th+1:4">2 Thessalonians 1:4</span>;</p>
<p><b>sufferings </b>, <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">παθημασιν</span></span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2co+1:6">2 Corinthians 1:6</span>). The two last items belong to verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2ti+3:11">2 Timothy 3:11</span>. </p>
