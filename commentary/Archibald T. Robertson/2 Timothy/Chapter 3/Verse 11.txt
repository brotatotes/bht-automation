<p><b> What things befell me </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">οια μο εγενετο</span></span>). Qualitative relative (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">οια</span></span>) referring to actual experiences of Paul (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εγενετο</span></span>, second aorist middle indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">γινομα</span></span>) more fully described in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2co+11:30-33">2 Corinthians 11:30-33</span>. The Acts of the Apostles tell of his experiences in Antioch in Pisidia (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+13:14">Acts 13:14</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+13:45">Acts 13:45</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+13:50">Acts 13:50</span>), in Iconium (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+14:1-5">Acts 14:1-5</span>), in Lystra (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+14:6-19">Acts 14:6-19</span>). See also <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ga+2:11">Galatians 2:11</span>.</p>
<p><b>What persecutions I endured </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">οιους διωγμους υπηνεγκα</span></span>). Qualitative relative again with <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">διωγμους</span></span>. The verb is first aorist active indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">υποφερω</span></span>, old verb, to bear under as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1co+10:13">1 Corinthians 10:13</span>.</p>
<p><b>Delivered me </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">με ερυσατο</span></span>). First aorist middle of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ρυομα</span></span>, old verb, with <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εκ</span></span> here as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1th+1:10">1 Thessalonians 1:10</span>. Used again of the Lord Jesus in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2ti+4:18">2 Timothy 4:18</span>. </p>
