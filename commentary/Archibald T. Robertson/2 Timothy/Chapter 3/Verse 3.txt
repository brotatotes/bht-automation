<p><b> Implacable </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ασπονδο</span></span>). Truce-breakers. Old word, only here in N.T. though in MSS. in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+1:31">Romans 1:31</span> (from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">α</span></span> privative and <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">σπονδη</span></span>, a libation). </p>
<p><b> Slanderers </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">διαβολο</span></span>). See <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1ti+3:11">1 Timothy 3:11</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="tit+2:3">Titus 2:3</span>.</p>
<p><b>Without self-control </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ακρατεις</span></span>). Old word (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">α</span></span> privative and <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κρατος</span></span>), here only in N.T.</p>
<p><b>Fierce </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ανημερο</span></span>). Old word (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">α</span></span> privative and <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ημερος</span></span>, tame), only here in N.T.</p>
<p><b>No lovers of good </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αφιλαγαθο</span></span>). Found only here (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">α</span></span> privative and <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">φιλαγαθος</span></span>, for which see <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="tit+1:8">Titus 1:8</span>). See also <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="php+4:8">Philippians 4:8</span>. A papyrus describes Antoninus as <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">φιλαγαθος</span></span> and has <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αφιλοκαγαθια</span></span>. </p>
