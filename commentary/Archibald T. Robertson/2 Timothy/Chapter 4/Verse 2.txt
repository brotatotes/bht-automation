<p><b> Preach the word </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κηρυξον τον λογον</span></span>). First aorist active imperative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κηρυσσω</span></span>. For "the word" used absolutely, see <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1th+1:6">1 Thessalonians 1:6</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ga+6:6">Galatians 6:6</span>.</p>
<p><b>Be instant in season, out of season </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επιστηθ ευκαιρως ακαιρως</span></span>). Second aorist (ingressive) active imperative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εφιστημ</span></span> (intransitive use), "take a stand," "stand upon it or up to it," "carry on," "stick to it." The Vulgate has "<i>insta</i>." The two adverbs are like a proverb or a play (pun) on the word <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">καιρος</span></span>. There are all sorts of seasons (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">καιρο</span></span>), some difficult (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">χαλεπο</span></span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2ti+3:1">2 Timothy 3:1</span>), some easy (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ευκαιρη</span></span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1co+16:12">1 Corinthians 16:12</span>).</p>
<p><b>Reprove </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ελεγξον</span></span>). First aorist active imperative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ελεγχω</span></span>. "Bring to proof." <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="eph+5:11">Ephesians 5:11</span>.</p>
<p><b>Rebuke </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επιτιμησον</span></span>). First aorist active imperative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επιτιμαω</span></span>, to give honour (or blame) to, to chide. Common in the Gospels (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+17:3">Luke 17:3</span>).</p>
<p><b>Exhort </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">παρακαλεσον</span></span>). First aorist active imperative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">παρακαλεω</span></span>, common Pauline word. </p>
