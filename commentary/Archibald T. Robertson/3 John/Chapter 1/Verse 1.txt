<p><b> The beloved </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τω αγαπητω</span></span>). Four times in this short letter this verbal adjective is used of Gaius (here, <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="3jo+1:2">3 John 1:2</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="3jo+1:5">3 John 1:5</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="3jo+1:11">3 John 1:11</span>). See <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2jo+1:1">2 John 1:1</span> for the same phrase here, "whom I love in truth." </p>
