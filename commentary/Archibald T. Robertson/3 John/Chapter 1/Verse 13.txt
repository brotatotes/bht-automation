<p><b> I had </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ειχον</span></span>). Imperfect active of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εχω</span></span>, when I began to write (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">γραψα</span></span>, ingressive aorist active infinitive of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">γραφω</span></span>).</p>
<p><b>I am unwilling to write </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ου θελω γραφειν</span></span>). "I do not wish to go on writing them.</p>
<p><b>With ink and pen </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δια μελανος κα καλαμου</span></span>), "by means of (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δια</span></span>) black (ink) and reed (used as pen)." See <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2jo+1:12">2 John 1:12</span> for <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μελανος</span></span> and <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+11:7">Matthew 11:7</span> for <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">καλαμος</span></span>, used for papyrus and parchment, as <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">γραφειον</span></span> (a sharp stilus) for wax tablets. </p>
