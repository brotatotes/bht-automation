<p><b> They put forward two </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εστησαν δυο</span></span>). First aorist active indicative (transitive) of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ιστημ</span></span> (not intransitive second aorist, though same form in the third person plural). Somebody nominated two names, Justus and Matthias. </p>
