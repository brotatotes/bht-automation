 As they were looking  (βλεποντων αυτων). Genitive absolute. The present participle accents the fact that they were looking directly at Jesus.He was taken up  (eprth). First aorist passive indicative of επαιρω, old and common verb meaning to lift up. In Luke 24:51 we have "he was borne up" (ανεφερετο) and in Acts 1:2; Acts 1:11; 1 Timothy 3:6 "was received up" (ανελημπθη).Received  (υπελαβεν). Second aorist active indicative of υπολαμβανω, literally here "took under him." He seemed to be supported by the cloud. "In glory" Paul adds in 1 Timothy 3:16.Out of their sight  (απο των οφθαλμων αυτων). From their eyes (απο with ablative case).
