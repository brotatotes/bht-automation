<p><b> Were </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">υπηρχεν</span></span>). Imperfect of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">υπαρχω</span></span> in sense of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ην</span></span>, to exist, be. Fish are not mentioned, perhaps because the sheet had no water, though they were clean and unclean also (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="le+11:9">Leviticus 11:9</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="de+14:9">Deuteronomy 14:9</span>).</p>
<p><b>All manner of </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">παντα</span></span>). Literally, all, but clearly all varieties, not all individuals. Both clean and unclean animals are in the sheet. </p>
