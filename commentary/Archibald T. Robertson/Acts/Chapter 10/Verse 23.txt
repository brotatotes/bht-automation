<p><b> Lodged them </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εξενισεν</span></span>). Active voice here rather than passive as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+10:6">Acts 10:6</span>.</p>
<p><b>Accompanied him </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">συνηλθαν αυτω</span></span>). Associative instrumental case after verb. The wisdom of having these half dozen Jewish Christians from Joppa with Peter in the house of Cornelius in Caesarea becomes manifest in Jerusalem (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+11:12">Acts 11:12</span>). </p>
