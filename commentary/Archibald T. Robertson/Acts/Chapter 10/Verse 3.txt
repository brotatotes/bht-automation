<p><b> Coming in </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εισελθοντα</span></span>). Ingressive second aorist active participle, not present. So punctiliar, "saw come," not "saw coming." So also "say" or "speak," not "saying." Luke repeats the account of this vision to Cornelius twice (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+10:30">Acts 10:30</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+11:13">Acts 11:13</span>) and also the story of the vision to Peter (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+10:1-16">Acts 10:1-16</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+10:28">Acts 10:28</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+11:5">Acts 11:5</span>). </p>
