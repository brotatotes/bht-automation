<p><b> Four days ago </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">απο τεταρτης ημερας</span></span>). From the fourth day, reckoning backwards from this day.</p>
<p><b>I was keeping the ninth hour of prayer </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ημην την ενατην προσευχομενος</span></span>). Periphrastic middle imperfect and accusative of extension of time (all the ninth hour). </p>
