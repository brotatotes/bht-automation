<p><b> Fetch </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μεταπεμψα</span></span>). First aorist middle (indirect, for one's self) imperative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μεταπεμπω</span></span>, usual voice in ancient Greek with this verb in sense of sending another for one's own sake. Only in Acts in the N.T. See also <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+10:22">Acts 10:22</span>. </p>
