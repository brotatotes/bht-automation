<p><b> Making no distinction </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μηδεν διακριναντα</span></span>). So Westcott and Hort (first aorist active participle) instead of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μηδεν διακρινομενον</span></span> "nothing doubting" (present middle participle) like <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+10:20">Acts 10:20</span>. The difference in voice shows the distinction in meaning.</p>
<p><b>We entered into the man's house </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εισηλθομεν εις τον οικον του ανδρος</span></span>). Peter confesses it, but shows that the other six went in also. He avoids mention of Cornelius's name and office. </p>
