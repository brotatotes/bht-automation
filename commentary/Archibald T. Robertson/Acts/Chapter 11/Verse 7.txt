<p><b> A voice saying </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">φωνης λεγουσης</span></span>). Genitive case after <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ηκουσα</span></span> (cf. <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+9:7">Acts 9:7</span> and accusative <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+9:4">Acts 9:4</span> which see for discussion). Participle <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">λεγουσης</span></span> (present active of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">λεγω</span></span>) agreeing with <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">φωνης</span></span>, a kind of indirect discourse use of the participle. </p>
