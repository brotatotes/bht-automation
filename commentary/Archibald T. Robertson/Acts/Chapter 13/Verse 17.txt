<p><b> Chose </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εξελεξατο</span></span>). First aorist middle (indirect), selected for himself. Israel was the chosen people.</p>
<p><b>Exalted </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">υψωσεν</span></span>). From <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">υψοω</span></span>, late verb from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">υψος</span></span> so often used of Christ.</p>
<p><b>When they sojourned </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εν τη παροικια</span></span>). In the sojourn. Late word from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">παροικος</span></span> (sojourner, dweller, <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+7:6">Acts 7:6</span>) common in LXX. In N.T. only here and <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1pe+1:17">1 Peter 1:17</span>.</p>
<p><b>With a high arm </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μετα βραχιονος υψηλου</span></span>). Vivid picture from the LXX (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ex+6:1">Exodus 6:1</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ex+6:6">Exodus 6:6</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="de+5:15">Deuteronomy 5:15</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ps+136:12">Psalms 136:12</span>). </p>
