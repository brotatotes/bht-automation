<p><b> Suffered he their manners </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ετροποφορησεν</span></span>). First aorist active indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τροποφορεω</span></span>, late word from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τροπος</span></span>, manner, and <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">φερω</span></span>, reading of Aleph B D and accepted by Westcott and Hort. But A C Sahidic Bohairic read <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ετροφοφορησεν</span></span> from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τροφοφορεω</span></span> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τροφος</span></span>, a nurse, and <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">φερω</span></span>,) late word (II Macc. 7:27), probably correct word here and <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="de+1:31">Deuteronomy 1:31</span>. </p>
