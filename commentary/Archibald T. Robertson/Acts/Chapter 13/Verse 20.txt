<p><b> And after these things </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κα μετα ταυτα</span></span>). That is, the time of the Judges then began. Cf. <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="jdg+2:16">Judges 2:16</span>.</p>
<p><b>Until Samuel the prophet </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εως Σαμουηλ προφητου</span></span>). The <i>terminus ad quem</i>. He was the last of the judges and the first of the prophets who selected the first king (Saul) under God's guidance. Note the absence of the Greek article with <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">προφητου</span></span>. </p>
