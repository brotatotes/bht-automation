<p><b> They asked </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ηιτησαντο</span></span>). First aorist indirect middle indicative, they asked for themselves. They were tired of a theocracy. Cf. <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1sa+8:5">1 Samuel 8:5</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1sa+10:1">1 Samuel 10:1</span>. Paul mentions with pride that Benjamin was the tribe of Saul (his name also), but he does not allude to Saul's sin (Furneaux).</p>
<p><b>For the space of forty years </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ετη τεσσερακοντα</span></span>). Accusative of extent of time. Not in the O.T., but in Josephus, <i>Ant</i>. VI. 14, 9. </p>
