<p><b> Now no more to return to corruption </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μηκετ μελλοντα υποστρεφειν εις διαφθοραν</span></span>). No longer about to return as Lazarus did. Jesus did not die again and so is the first fruits of the resurrection (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1co+15:23">1 Corinthians 15:23</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+6:9">Romans 6:9</span>).</p>
<p><b>He hath spoken </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ειρηκεν</span></span>). Present perfect active indicative, common way of referring to the permanent utterances of God which are on record in the Scriptures.</p>
<p><b>The holy and sure blessings of David </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τα οσια Δαυειδ τα πιστα</span></span>). See <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2sa+7:13">2 Samuel 7:13</span>. Literally, "the holy things of David the trustworthy things." He explains "the holy things" at once. </p>
