<p><b> Was spread abroad </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">διεφερετο</span></span>). Imperfect passive of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">διαφερω</span></span>, to carry in different directions (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δια</span></span>). By the recent converts as well as by Paul and Barnabas. This would seem to indicate a stay of some months with active work among the Gentiles that bore rich fruit.</p>
<p><b>Throughout all the region </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δι' ολης της χωρας</span></span>). Antioch in Pisidia as a Roman colony would be the natural centre of a Roman <i>Regio</i>, an important element in Roman imperial administration. There were probably other <i>Regiones</i> in South Galatia (Ramsay, <i>St. Paul the Traveller and Roman Citizen</i>, pp. 102-12). </p>
