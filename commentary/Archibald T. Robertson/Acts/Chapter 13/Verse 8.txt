<p><b> Withstood them </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ανθιστατο αυτοις</span></span>). Imperfect middle of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ανθιστημ</span></span>, to stand against (face to face). Dative case (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αυτοις</span></span>). He persisted in his opposition and was unwilling to lose his great prize. There may have been a public discussion between Elymas and Saul.</p>
<p><b>To turn aside </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">διαστρεψα</span></span>). First aorist active infinitive of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">διαστρεφω</span></span>, old verb to turn or twist in two, to distort, to pervert (cf. <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+17:17">Matthew 17:17</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+23:2">Luke 23:2</span>). </p>
