<p><b> Whose temple was before the city </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">του οντος προ της πωλεως</span></span>). The god (Zeus) is identified with his temple. He had a statue and temple there.</p>
<p><b>Oxen and garlands </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ταυρους κα στεμματα</span></span>). Probably garlands to put on the oxen before they were slain. It was common to sacrifice bullocks to Jupiter and Mercury.</p>
<p><b>Would have done sacrifice </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ηθελεν θυειν</span></span>). Imperfect indicative, wanted to offer sacrifice. He was planning to do it, and his purpose now became plain to Paul and Barnabas. </p>
