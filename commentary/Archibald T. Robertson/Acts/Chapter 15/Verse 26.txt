<p><b> Have hazarded their lives </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">παραδεδωκοσ τας ψυχας αυτων</span></span>). Perfect active participle dative plural of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">παραδιδωμ</span></span>, old word, to hand over to another, and with <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ψυχας</span></span>, to hand over to another their lives. The sufferings of Paul and Barnabas in Pisidia and Lycaonia were plainly well-known just as the story of Judson in Burmah is today. On the use of "name" here see on <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+3:6">Acts 3:6</span>. </p>
