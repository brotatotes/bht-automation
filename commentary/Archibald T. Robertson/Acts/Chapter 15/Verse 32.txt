<p><b> Being themselves also prophets </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κα αυτο προφητα οντες</span></span>). As well as Paul and Barnabas and like Agabus (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+11:27-30">Acts 11:27-30</span>), for-speakers for Christ who justify the commendation in the letter (verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+15:27">Acts 15:27</span>) "with many words" (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δια λογου πολλου</span></span>), "with much talk," and no doubt with kindly words concerning the part played at the Conference by Paul and Barnabas.</p>
<p><b>Confirmed </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επεστηριξαν</span></span>). See on <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+14:22">Acts 14:22</span>. It was a glorious time with no Judaizers to disturb their fellowship as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+15:1-3">Acts 15:1-3</span>. </p>
