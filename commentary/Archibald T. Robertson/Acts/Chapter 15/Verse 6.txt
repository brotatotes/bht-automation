<p><b> Were gathered together </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">συνηχθησαν</span></span>). First aorist (effective) passive indicative. The church is not named here as in verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+15:4">Acts 15:4</span>, but we know from verses <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+15:12-22">Acts 15:12-22</span> that the whole church came together this time also along with the apostles and elders.</p>
<p><b>Of this matter </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">περ του λογου τουτου</span></span>). Same idiom in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+8:21">Acts 8:21</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+19:38">Acts 19:38</span>. They realized the importance of the issue. </p>
