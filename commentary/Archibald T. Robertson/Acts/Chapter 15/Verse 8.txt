<p><b> Which knoweth the heart </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">καρδιογνωστης</span></span>). Late word from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">καρδια</span></span> (heart) and <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">γνωστης</span></span> (known, <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">γινωσκω</span></span>). In the N.T. only here and <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+1:24">Acts 1:24</span> which see.</p>
<p><b>Giving them the Holy Spirit </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δους το πνευμα το αγιον</span></span>). And before their baptism. This was the Lord's doing. They had accepted (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+11:18">Acts 11:18</span>) this witness of God then and it was true now of these other Gentile converts. </p>
