<p><b> Trembling for fear </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εντρομος γενομενος</span></span>). "Becoming terrified." The adjective <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εντρομος</span></span> (in terror) occurs in N.T. only here and <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+7:32">Acts 7:32</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="heb+12:21">Hebrews 12:21</span>.</p>
<p><b>Fell down </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">προσεπεσεν</span></span>). Second aorist active indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">προσπιπτω</span></span>, old verb. An act of worship as Cornelius before Peter (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+10:25">Acts 10:25</span>), when <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">προσεκυνησεν</span></span> is used. </p>
