<p><b> They spake the word of God </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ελαλησαν τον λογον του θεου</span></span>). So Paul and Silas gave fuller exposition of the way of life to the jailor "with all that were in his house." It was a remarkable service with keenest attention and interest, the jailor with his warden, slaves, and family. </p>
