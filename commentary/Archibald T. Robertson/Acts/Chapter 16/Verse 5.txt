<p><b> Were strengthened </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εστερεουντο</span></span>). Imperfect passive of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">στερεοω</span></span>, old verb to make firm and solid like the muscles (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+3:7">Acts 3:7</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+3:16">Acts 3:16</span>), these three the only examples in the N.T.</p>
<p><b>Increased </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επερισσευον</span></span>). Imperfect active of the old and common verb <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">περισσευω</span></span> from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">περισσος</span></span> (overplus). The blessing of God was on the work of Paul, Silas, and Timothy in the form of a continuous revival. </p>
