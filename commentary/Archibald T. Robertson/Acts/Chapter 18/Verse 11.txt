<p><b> A year and six months </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ενιαυτον κα μηνας εξ</span></span>). Accusative of extent of time. How much time before this incident he had been there we do not know. He was in Corinth probably a couple of years in all. His work extended beyond the city (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2co+11:10">2 Corinthians 11:10</span>) and there was a church in Cenchreae (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+16:1">Romans 16:1</span>). </p>
