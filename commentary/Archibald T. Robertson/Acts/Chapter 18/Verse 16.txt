<p><b> He drave them </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">απηλασεν αυτους</span></span>). First aorist active indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">απελαυνω</span></span>, old word, but here alone in the N.T. The Jews were stunned by this sudden blow from the mild proconsul and wanted to linger to argue the case further, but they had to go. </p>
