<p><b> When they asked him </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ερωτωντων αυτων</span></span>). Genitive absolute of present participle of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ερωταω</span></span>, old verb to ask a question, common in <i>Koine</i> to make a request as here.</p>
<p><b>He consented not </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ουκ επενευσεν</span></span>). First aorist active indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επινευω</span></span>, old verb to express approval by a nod, only here in the N.T. </p>
