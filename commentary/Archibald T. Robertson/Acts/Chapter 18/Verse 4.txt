<p><b> He reasoned </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">διελεγετο</span></span>). Imperfect middle, same form as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+17:17">Acts 17:17</span> about Paul's work in Athens, here only on the Sabbaths.</p>
<p><b>Persuaded </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επειθεν</span></span>). Imperfect active, conative, he tried to persuade both Jews and Greeks (God-fearers who alone would come). </p>
