<p><b> Jesus I know </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τον Ιησουν γινωσκω</span></span>). "The (whom you mention) Jesus I recognize (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">γινωσκω</span></span>)" and "the (whom you mentioned) Paul I am acquainted with (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τον Παυλον επισταμα</span></span>)." Clear distinction between <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">γινωσκω</span></span> and <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επισταμα</span></span>.</p>
<p><b>But who are ye? </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">υμεις δε τινες εστε?</span></span>). But you, who are you? Emphatic prolepsis. </p>
