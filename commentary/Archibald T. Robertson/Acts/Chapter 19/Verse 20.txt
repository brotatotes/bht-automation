<p><b> Mightily </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κατα κρατος</span></span>). According to strength. Only here in N.T., common military term in Thucydides. Such proof of a change counted.</p>
<p><b>Grew and prevailed </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ηυξανεν κα ισχυεν</span></span>). Imperfect actives, kept growing and gaining strength. It was a day of triumph for Christ in Ephesus, this city of vast wealth and superstition. Ephesus for centuries will be one of the centres of Christian power. Timothy will come here and John the Apostle and Polycarp and Irenaeus. </p>
