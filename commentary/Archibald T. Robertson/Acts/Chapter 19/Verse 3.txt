<p><b> Into what </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εις τ</span></span>). More properly,</p>
<p><b>Unto what </b> or</p>
<p><b>on what basis </b> (Robertson, <i>Grammar</i>, p. 592). Clearly, Paul felt they had received a poor baptism with no knowledge of the Holy Spirit.</p>
<p><b>John's baptism </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">το Ιωανου βαπτισμα</span></span>). Last mention of John the Baptist in the N.T. They had been dipped in other words, but they had not grasped the significance of the ordinance. </p>
