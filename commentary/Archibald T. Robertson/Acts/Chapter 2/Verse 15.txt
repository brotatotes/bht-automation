<p><b> As ye suppose </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ως υμεις υπολαμβανετε</span></span>). Note use of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">υμεις</span></span> (ye) for decided emphasis.</p>
<p><b>The third hour </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ωρα τριτη</span></span>). Three o'clock in the day Jewish time, nine Roman. Drunkenness belongs to the night (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1th+5:7">1 Thessalonians 5:7</span>). It was a quick, common sense reply, and complete answer to their suspicion. </p>
