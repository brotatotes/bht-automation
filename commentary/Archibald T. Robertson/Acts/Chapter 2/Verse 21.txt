<p><b> Shall call on </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επικαλεσητα</span></span>). First aorist middle subjunctive of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επικαλεω</span></span>, common verb, to call to, middle voice for oneself in need. Indefinite relative clause with <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εαν</span></span> and so subjunctive, punctiliar idea, in any single case, and so aorist. </p>
