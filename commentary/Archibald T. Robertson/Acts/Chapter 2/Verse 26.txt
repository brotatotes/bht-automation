<p><b> Was glad </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ηυφρανθη</span></span>). First aorist (timeless here like the Hebrew perfect) passive indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ευφραινω</span></span> (cf. <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+15:32">Luke 15:32</span>). Timeless also is "rejoiced" (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ηγαλλιασατο</span></span>).</p>
<p><b>Shall dwell </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κατασκηνωσε</span></span>). Shall tabernacle, pitch a tent, make one's abode (cf. <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+13:32">Matthew 13:32</span>). See on <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+8:20">Matthew 8:20</span> about <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κατασκηνωσεις</span></span> (nests)</p>
<p><b>In hope </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επ' ελπιδ</span></span>). On hope, the hope of the resurrection. </p>
