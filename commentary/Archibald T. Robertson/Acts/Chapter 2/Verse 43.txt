<p><b> Came </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εγινετο</span></span>). Imperfect middle, kept on coming.</p>
<p><b>Were done </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εγινετο</span></span>). Same tense. Awe kept on coming on all and signs and wonders kept on coming through the apostles. The two things went on <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">παρ πασσυ</span></span>, the more wonders the more fear. </p>
