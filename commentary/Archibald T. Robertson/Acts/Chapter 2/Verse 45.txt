<p><b> Sold </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επιπρασκον</span></span>). Imperfect active, a habit or custom from time to time. Old and common verb, <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πιπρασκω</span></span>.</p>
<p><b>Parted </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">διεμεριζον</span></span>). Imperfect again of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">διαμεριζω</span></span>, old verb for dividing or distributing between (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δια</span></span>) people.</p>
<p><b>According as any man had need </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">καθοτ αν τις χρειαν ειχεν</span></span>). Regular Greek idiom for comparative clause with <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αν</span></span> and imperfect indicative corresponding precisely with the three preceding imperfects (Robertson, <i>Grammar</i>, p. 967). </p>
