<p><b> They all wept sore </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ικανος κλαυθμος εγενετο παντων</span></span>). Literally, There came considerable weeping of all (on the part of all, genitive case).</p>
<p><b>Kissed him </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κατεφιλουν αυτον</span></span>). Imperfect active of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">καταφιλεω</span></span>, old verb, intensive with <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κατα</span></span> and repetition shown also by the tense: They kept on kissing or kissed repeatedly, probably one after the other falling on his neck. Cf. also <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+26:49">Matthew 26:49</span>. </p>
