<p><b> Many lights </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">λαμπαδες ικανα</span></span>). It was dark at night since the full moon (passover) was three weeks behind. These lamps were probably filled with oil and had wicks that flickered and smoked. They would not meet in the dark.</p>
