<p><b> I could not see </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ουκ ενεβλεπον</span></span>). Imperfect active of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εμβλεπω</span></span>, I was not seeing, same fact stated in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+9:8">Acts 9:8</span>. Here the reason as "for the glory of that light" (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">απο της δοξης του φωτος εκεινου</span></span>).</p>
<p><b>Being led by the hand </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">χειραγωγουμενος</span></span>). Present passive participle of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">χειραγωγεω</span></span>, the same verb used in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+9:8">Acts 9:8</span> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">χειραγωγουντες</span></span>) which see. Late verb, in the N.T. only in these two places. In LXX. </p>
