<p><b> A devout man according to the law </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ευλαβης κατα τον νομον</span></span>). See on <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+2:5">Acts 2:5</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+8:2">Acts 8:2</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+2:25">Luke 2:25</span> for the adjective <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ευλαβης</span></span>. Paul adds "according to the law" to show that he was introduced to Christianity by a devout Jew and no law-breaker (Lewin). </p>
