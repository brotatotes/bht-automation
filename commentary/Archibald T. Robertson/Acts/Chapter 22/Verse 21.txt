<p><b> I will send thee forth far hence unto the Gentiles </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">Εγω εις εθνη μακραν εξαποστελω σε</span></span>). Future active of the double (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εξ</span></span>, out, <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">απο</span></span>, off or away) compound of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εξαποστελλω</span></span>, common word in the <i>Koine</i> (cf. <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+24:49">Luke 24:49</span>). This is a repetition by Jesus of the call given in Damascus through Ananias (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+9:15">Acts 9:15</span>). Paul had up till now avoided the word Gentiles, but at last it had to come, "the fatal word" (Farrar). </p>
