<p><b> Departed from him </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">απεστησαν απ' αυτου</span></span>). Second aorist active indicative (intransitive) of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αφιστημ</span></span>, stood off from him at once.</p>
<p><b>Was afraid </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εφοβηθη</span></span>). Ingressive aorist passive indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">φοβεομα</span></span>, became afraid. He had reason to be.</p>
<p><b>That he was a Roman </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">οτ Ρομαιος εστιν</span></span>). Indirect assertion with tense of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εστιν</span></span> retained.</p>
<p><b>Because he had bound him </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">οτ αυτον ην δεδεκως</span></span>). Causal <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">οτ</span></span> here after declarative <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">οτ</span></span> just before. Periphrastic past perfect active of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δεω</span></span>, to bind. </p>
