<p><b> Of Nazareth </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ο Ναζωραιος</span></span>). The Nazarene, not in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+9:5">Acts 9:5</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+26:15">Acts 26:15</span> and here because Jesus is mentioned now for the first time in the address. The form <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">Ναζωραιος</span></span> as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+2:23">Matthew 2:23</span> (which see) is used also in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+24:5">Acts 24:5</span> for the followers of Jesus instead of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">Ναζαρηνος</span></span> as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mr+1:24">Mark 1:24</span>, etc. (which see). </p>
