<p><b> Called unto him </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">προσκαλεσαμενος</span></span>). First aorist participle indirect middle, calling to himself. <p><b>Bring </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">απαγε</span></span>). "Take away." </p>
