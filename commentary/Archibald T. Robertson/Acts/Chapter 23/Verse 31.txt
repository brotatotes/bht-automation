<p><b> As it was commanded them </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κατα το διατεταγμενον αυτοις</span></span>). "According to that which was commanded them," perfect passive articular participle of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">διατασσω</span></span>.</p>
<p><b>By night </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δια νυκτος</span></span>). Through the night, travelling by night forty miles from Jerusalem to Antipatris which was founded by Herod the Great and was on the road from Jerusalem to Caesarea, a hard night's ride. </p>
