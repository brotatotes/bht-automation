<p><b> Prove </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">παραστησα</span></span>). First aorist active infinitive of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">παριστημ</span></span>, to place beside. They have made "charges," mere assertions. They have not backed up these charges with proof, "nor can they," says Paul.</p>
<p><b>Now </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">νυν</span></span>). As if they had changed their charges from the cries of the mob in Jerusalem which is true. Paul has no hired lawyer to plead for him, but he has made a masterly plea for his freedom. </p>
