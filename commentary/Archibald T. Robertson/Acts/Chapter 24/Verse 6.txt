<p><b> Assayed to profane </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επειρασεν βεβηλωσα</span></span>). A flat untruth, but the charge of the Asian Jews (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+21:28-30">Acts 21:28-30</span>). <i>Verbum optum ad calumnian</i> (Bengel).</p>
<p><b>We seized </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εκρατησαμεν</span></span>). As if the Sanhedrin had arrested Paul, Tertullus identifying himself with his clients. But it was the mob (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+21:28-31">Acts 21:28-31</span>) that attacked Paul and Lysias who rescued him (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+21:32">Acts 21:32</span>). </p>
