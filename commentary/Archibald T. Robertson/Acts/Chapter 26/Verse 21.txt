<p><b> Assayed to kill me </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επειρωντο διαχειρισασθα</span></span>). Conative imperfect middle of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πειραω</span></span>, the old form of the later <i>Koine</i> <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πειραζω</span></span> so common in the <i>Koine</i>, but in N.T. here only. Some MSS. have it in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+9:26">Acts 9:26</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="heb+4:15">Hebrews 4:15</span>. The old verb <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">διαχειριζω</span></span>, to take in hand, middle to lay hands on, to slay, occurs in N.T. only here and <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+5:30">Acts 5:30</span> which see. </p>
