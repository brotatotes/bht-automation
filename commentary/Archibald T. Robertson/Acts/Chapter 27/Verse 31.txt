<p><b> Except these abide in the ship </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">Εαν μη ουτο μεινωσιν εν τω πλοιω</span></span>). Condition of the third class (undetermined, but with hope, etc.). Paul has no hesitancy in saying this in spite of his strong language in verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+27:24">Acts 27:24</span> about God's promise. He has no notion of lying supinely down and leaving God to do it all. Without the sailors the ship could not be properly beached. </p>
