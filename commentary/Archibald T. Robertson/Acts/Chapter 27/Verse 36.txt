<p><b> Then were they all of good cheer </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ευθυμο δε γενομενο</span></span>). More exactly, "Then all becoming cheerful," because of Paul's words and conduct.</p>
<p><b>Took food </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">προσελαβοντο τροφης</span></span>). Partitive genitive here (some food), not accusative as verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+27:33">Acts 27:33</span>. Paul's courage was contagious. </p>
