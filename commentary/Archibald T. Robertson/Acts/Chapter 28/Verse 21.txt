<p><b> Letters </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">γραμματα</span></span>). Official documents from the Sanhedrin about the charges against Paul.</p>
<p><b>Any harm of thee </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τ περ σου πονηρον</span></span>).</p>
<p><b>Evil </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πονηρον</span></span>). The three aorists (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εδεξαμεθα, απηγγειλεν, ελαλησεν</span></span>) cover the past. These Jews do not mean to say that they had never heard of Paul. It is hardly likely that they had heard of his appeal to Caesar, "for how could the news have reached Rome before Paul?" (Page). </p>
