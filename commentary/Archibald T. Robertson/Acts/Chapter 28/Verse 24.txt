<p><b> Some believed </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ο μεν επειθοντο</span></span>). Imperfect passive indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πειθω</span></span>. More exactly, "some began to be persuaded" (inchoative).</p>
<p><b>Some disbelieved </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ο δε ηπιστουν</span></span>). Imperfect active of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">απιστεω</span></span>, to disbelieve, continued to disbelieve. It is usually so. </p>
