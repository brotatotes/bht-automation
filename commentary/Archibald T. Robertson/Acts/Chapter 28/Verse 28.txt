<p><b> This salvation </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τουτο το σωτηριον</span></span>). Adjective from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">σωτηρ</span></span> (Saviour), saving, bringing salvation. Common in the old Greek. The neuter as here often in LXX (as <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ps+67:2">Psalms 67:2</span>) as substantive like <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">σωτηρια</span></span> (cf. <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+3:6">Luke 3:6</span>).</p>
<p><b>They will also hear </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αυτο κα ακουσοντα</span></span>). <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">Αυτο</span></span> as opposed to the rejection by the Jews, "vivid and antithetical" (Page). </p>
