<p><b> Shook off </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αποτιναξας</span></span>). First aorist active participle of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αποτινασσω</span></span>, to shake off. Rare word (Euripides, Galen, LXX). In N.T. only here and <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+9:5">Luke 9:5</span>. </p>
