<p><b> Gave heed unto them </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επειχεν αυτοις</span></span>). Imperfect active of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επεχω</span></span>, to hold to. For the idiom with <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τον νουν</span></span> understood see <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+7:14">Acts 7:14</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1ti+4:16">1 Timothy 4:16</span>. He held his eyes right on Peter and John with great eagerness "expecting to receive something" (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">προσδοκων τ λαβειν</span></span>). He took Peter's invitation as a promise of a large gift. </p>
