<p><b>He stood </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εστη</span></span>). Second aorist active.</p>
<p><b>Walked </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">περιεπατε</span></span>). Went on walking, imperfect active. He came into the temple repeating these new exercises (walking, leaping, praising God). </p>
