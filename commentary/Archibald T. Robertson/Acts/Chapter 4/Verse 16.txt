<p><b> What shall we do? </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">Τ ποιησωμεν</span></span>). Deliberative aorist active subjunctive (ingressive and urgent aorist).</p>
<p><b>Notable miracle </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">γνωστον σημειον</span></span>). Or sign. It was useless to deny it with the man there.</p>
<p><b>We cannot deny it </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ου δυναμεθα αρνεισθα</span></span>). That is, it will do no good. </p>
