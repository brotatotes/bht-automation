<p><b> To their own company </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">προς τους ιδιους</span></span>). Their own people as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+1:11">John 1:11</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+13:1">John 13:1</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+24:23">Acts 24:23</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1ti+5:8">1 Timothy 5:8</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="tit+3:14">Titus 3:14</span>, not merely the apostles (all the disciples). In spite of Peter's courageous defiance he and John told the brotherhood all that had been said by the Sanhedrin. They had real apprehension of the outcome. </p>
