<p><b> Foreordained </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">προωρισεν</span></span>). First aorist active indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">προοριζω</span></span>, "They rise above sight and seem to see the Hand which 'shapes men's ends, rough hew them how they will'" (Furneaux). </p>
