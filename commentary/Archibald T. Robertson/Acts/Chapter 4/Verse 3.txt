 In ward  (εις τηρησιν). Probably in one of the chambers of the temple. In safe keeping (from τηρεω, to guard). Old word, in the N.T. only here and Acts 5:18; 1 Corinthians 7:19. So in papyri.Now eventide  (εσπερα ηδη). Hence no trial could take place before the next day, a regulation violated in the case of Jesus.
