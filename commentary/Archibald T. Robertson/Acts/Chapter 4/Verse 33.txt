<p><b> Gave their witness </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">απεδιδουν το μαρτυριον</span></span>). Imperfect active of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αποδιδωμ</span></span>, old verb to give back, to pay back a debt (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+7:42">Luke 7:42</span>), but a late omega form instead of the usual <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">απεδιδοσαν</span></span>. They kept on giving their witness with power after the answer to their prayer (verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+4:31">Acts 4:31</span>).</p>
<p><b>Of the resurrection </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">της αναστασεως</span></span>). It was on this issue that the Sadducees had arrested them (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+4:1-3">Acts 4:1-3</span>). </p>
