<p><b> Distribution was made </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">διεδιδετο</span></span>). Imperfect passive of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">διαδιδωμ</span></span>, late omega form for <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">διεδιδοτο</span></span> (the stem vowel <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ο</span></span> displaced by <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ε</span></span>). Impersonal use of the verb here.</p>
<p><b>According as any one had need </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">καθοτ αν τις χρειαν ειχεν</span></span>). Imperfect active of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εχω</span></span> with <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">καθοτ</span></span> and <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αν</span></span> with the notion of customary repetition in a comparative clause (Robertson, <i>Grammar</i>, p. 967). </p>
