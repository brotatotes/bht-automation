<p><b> Rulers and elders and scribes </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τους αρχοντας κα τους πρεσβυτερους κα τους γραμματεις</span></span>). The three classes composing the Sanhedrin (rulers=chief priests who were Sadducees, the scribes usually Pharisees, the elders not in either class: 24 priests, 24 elders, 22 scribes).</p>
<p><b>Were gathered together </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">συναχθηνα</span></span>). First aorist passive infinitive of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">συναγω</span></span> with accusative of general reference and the subject of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εγενετο</span></span>. </p>
