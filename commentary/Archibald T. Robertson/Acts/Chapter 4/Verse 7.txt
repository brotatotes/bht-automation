 In the midst  (εν τω μεσω). The Sanhedrin sat in a semicircle.They inquired  (επυνθανοντο). Imperfect middle, began to inquire.Or in what name  (η εν ποιω ονοματ). As if by some magical formula such as exorcists practised (Acts 19:13) as if to catch them by (Deuteronomy 13:1).Have ye done this  (εποιησατε τουτο υμεις). Note emphatic use of υμεις (ye).
