<p><b> In the midst </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εν τω μεσω</span></span>). The Sanhedrin sat in a semicircle.</p>
<p><b>They inquired </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επυνθανοντο</span></span>). Imperfect middle, began to inquire.</p>
<p><b>Or in what name </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">η εν ποιω ονοματ</span></span>). As if by some magical formula such as exorcists practised (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+19:13">Acts 19:13</span>) as if to catch them by (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="de+13:1">Deuteronomy 13:1</span>).</p>
<p><b>Have ye done this </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εποιησατε τουτο υμεις</span></span>). Note emphatic use of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">υμεις</span></span> (ye). </p>
