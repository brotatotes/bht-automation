<p><b> Concerning a good deed done to an impotent man </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επ ευεργεσια ανθρωπου ασθενους</span></span>). Objective genitive. Note <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ευεργεσια</span></span> (old word, in the N.T. only here and <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1ti+6:2">1 Timothy 6:2</span>), as a benefactor, not a malefactor. Skilful turn made by Peter.</p>
<p><b>Is made whole </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">σεσωστα</span></span>). Perfect passive indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">σωζω</span></span>, stands whole. </p>
