<p><b> Came together </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">συνηρχετο</span></span>). Imperfect middle, kept on coming.</p>
<p><b>Round about </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">περιξ</span></span>). Old adverb, strengthened form of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">περ</span></span>, only here in the N.T.</p>
<p><b>Vexed </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">οχλουμενους</span></span>). Present passive participle of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">οχλεω</span></span>, to excite a mob (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">οχλος</span></span>) against one, to trouble, annoy. Old word, only here in the N.T., though <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ενοχλεω</span></span> in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+6:18">Luke 6:18</span>.</p>
<p><b>Were healed every one </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εθεραπευοντο απαντες</span></span>). Imperfect passive, were healed one at a time, repetition. </p>
