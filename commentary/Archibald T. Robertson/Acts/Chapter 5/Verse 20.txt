<p><b> And stand </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κα σταθεντες</span></span>). First aorist passive participle (intransitive, ingressive aorist), take a stand. Bold and pictorial command.</p>
<p><b>All the words of this life </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">παντα τα ρηματα της ζωης ταυτης</span></span>). Not just a Hebraism for "all these words of life." Probably "this life" which the Sadducees deny and of which the angel is now speaking, this eternal life. (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+6:63">John 6:63</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+6:68">John 6:68</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1co+15:19">1 Corinthians 15:19</span>). </p>
