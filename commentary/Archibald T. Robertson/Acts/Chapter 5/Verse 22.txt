<p><b> The officers </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ο υπηρετα</span></span>). Under-rowers, literally (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+5:25">Matthew 5:25</span>). The servants or officers who executed the orders of the Sanhedrin.</p>
<p><b>Shut </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κεκλεισμενον</span></span>). Perfect passive participle of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κλειω</span></span>. Shut tight.</p>
<p><b>Standing at the doors </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εστωτας επ των θυρων</span></span>). Graphic picture of the sentinels at the prison doors. </p>
