<p><b> Ye slew </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">διεχειρισασθε</span></span>). First aorist middle indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">διαχειριζομα</span></span>, old verb from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δια</span></span> and <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">χειρ</span></span> (hand), to take in hand, manage, to lay hands on, manhandle, kill. In the N.T. only here and <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+26:21">Acts 26:21</span>.</p>
<p><b>Hanging him upon a tree </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κρεμασαντες επ ξυλου</span></span>). First aorist active participle of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κρεμαννυμ</span></span> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κρεμαννυω</span></span> seen already in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+18:6">Matthew 18:6</span> and <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+23:39">Luke 23:39</span>). Peter refers to <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="de+21:23">Deuteronomy 21:23</span> as Paul does in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ga+3:13">Galatians 3:13</span>, the curse pronounced on every one who "hangs upon a tree." </p>
