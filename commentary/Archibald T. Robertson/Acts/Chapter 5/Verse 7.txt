<p><b> And it was about the space of three hours after </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εγενετο δε ως ωρων τριων διαστημα</span></span>). Literally "Now there came an interval (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">διαστημα</span></span>, distance, space between) of about (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ως</span></span>) three hours."</p>
<p><b>When </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κα</span></span>). This use of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κα</span></span> after <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εγενετο</span></span> is characteristic of Luke's style in the Gospel.</p>
<p><b>Not knowing </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μη ειδυια</span></span>). Feminine singular of second perfect active participle of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">οιδα</span></span>. <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">Μη</span></span> usual negative of the participle in the <i>Koine</i>. </p>
