<p><b> Ye have agreed together </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">συνεφωνηθη υμιν</span></span>). First aorist passive indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">συμφωνεω</span></span> (to voice together, symphony), impersonal with dative; It was agreed together by you (or for you). "Your souls were allured together respecting this deceit" (Vincent).</p>
<p><b>To tempt the Spirit of the Lord </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πειρασα το πνευμα κυριου</span></span>). Like "Thou shalt not tempt the Lord thy God." It was close to the unpardonable sin which was attributing the manifest work of the Holy Spirit to Beelzebub.</p>
<p><b>The feet </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ο ποδες</span></span>). Graphic picture by Peter as he heard the steps of the young men at the door. </p>
