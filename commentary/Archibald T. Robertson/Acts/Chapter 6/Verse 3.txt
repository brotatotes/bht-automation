<p><b> Of good report </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μαρτυρουμενους</span></span>). Present passive participle of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μαρτυρεω</span></span>, to bear witness to. Men with a good reputation as well as with spiritual gifts (the Holy Spirit and wisdom).</p>
<p><b>We may appoint </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">καταστησομεν</span></span>). Future active indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">καθιστημ</span></span>, we shall appoint. The action of the apostles follows the choice by the church, but it is promised as a certainty, not as a possibility. The Textus Receptus has a first aorist active subjunctive here (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">καταστησωμεν</span></span>). </p>
