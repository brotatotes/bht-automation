<p><b> But we </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εμεις δε</span></span>). In contrast to the work given the seven.</p>
<p><b>The ministry of the word </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τη διακονια του λογου</span></span>). The same word <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">διακονια</span></span> employed in verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+6:1">Acts 6:1</span>, but here about preaching as the special ministry with which the apostles were concerned. For "continue steadfastly" (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">προσκαρτερησομεν</span></span>) see on <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+2:42">Acts 2:42</span>. </p>
