<p><b> Found no sustenance </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ουχ ηυρισκον χορτασματα</span></span>). Imperfect active, kept on not finding.</p>
<p><b>Chortasmata </b> is from</p>
<p><b>chortazo </b>, originally to feed with grass (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">χορτος</span></span>) or herbs. Old word, but only here in the N.T. and includes food for both men and animals. In <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ge+24:25">Genesis 24:25</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ge+24:32">Genesis 24:32</span> it is fodder for the cattle, a first necessity for owners of herds of cattle. </p>
