<p><b> At the second time </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εν τω δευτερω</span></span>). This expression only here in the N.T. This second visit is recorded in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ge+45:1">Genesis 45:1</span>.</p>
<p><b>Became manifest </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">φανερον εγενετο</span></span>). In <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ge+41:12">Genesis 41:12</span> the fact that Joseph was a Hebrew had been incidentally mentioned to Pharaoh, but now it was made clear to him. </p>
