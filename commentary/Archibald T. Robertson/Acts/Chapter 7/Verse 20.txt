<p><b> Exceeding fair </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αστειος τω θεω</span></span>). Ethical dative, fair to God (as God looked at him). <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">Αστειος</span></span> is from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αστυ</span></span>, city, and so means "of the city," with city manners and polish. Old word, only twice in the N.T. (here and <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="heb+11:23">Hebrews 11:23</span>) and both times about Moses and taken from <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ex+2:2">Exodus 2:2</span>.</p>
<p><b>He was nourished </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ανετραφη</span></span>). Second aorist passive indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ανατρεφω</span></span>. He was brought up at home for three months in defiance of the new Pharaoh. </p>
