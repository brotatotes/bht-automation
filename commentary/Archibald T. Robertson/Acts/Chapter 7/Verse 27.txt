<p><b> Thrust him away </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">απωσατο αυτον</span></span>). First aorist middle indicative (<i>Koine</i> for Attic <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">απεωσατο</span></span>) of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">απωθεω</span></span>, to push away from oneself in middle voice as here, common in old Greek. Again in verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+7:39">Acts 7:39</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+13:46">Acts 13:46</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+11:1">Romans 11:1</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1ti+1:19">1 Timothy 1:19</span>. It is always the man who is doing the wrong who is hard to reconcile. </p>
