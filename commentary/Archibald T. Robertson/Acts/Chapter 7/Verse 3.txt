<p><b> Which I shall shew thee </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ην αν σο δειξω</span></span>). Indefinite relative clause with <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αν</span></span> and the aorist active subjunctive (same form in first person singular as the future active indicative). Abraham followed on as God led him. </p>
