<p><b> Holy ground </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">γη αγια</span></span>). The priests were barefooted when they ministered in the temple. Moslems enter their mosques barefooted today. Cf. <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="jos+5:15">Joshua 5:15</span>.</p>
<p><b>Sandal </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">υποδημα</span></span>, bound under) is here "a distributive singular" (Hackett). Even the ground near the bush was "holy," a fine example for Stephen's argument. </p>
