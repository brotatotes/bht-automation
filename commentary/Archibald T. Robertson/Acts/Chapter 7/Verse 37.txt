<p><b> Like unto me </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ως εμε</span></span>). This same passage Peter quoted to the crowd in Solomon's Porch (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+3:22">Acts 3:22</span>). Stephen undoubtedly means to argue that Moses was predicting the Messiah as a prophet like himself who is no other than Jesus so that these Pharisees are in reality opposing Moses. It was a neat turn. </p>
