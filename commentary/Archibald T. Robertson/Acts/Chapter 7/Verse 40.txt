<p><b> Gods which shall go before us </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">θεους ο προπορευσοντα ημων</span></span>). <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ex+32:1">Exodus 32:1</span>. As guides and protectors, perhaps with some allusion to the pillar of fire and of cloud that had gone before them (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ex+13:21">Exodus 13:21</span>). The future indicative here with <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ο</span></span> (relative) expresses purpose.</p>
<p><b>Ye wot not </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ουκ οιδαμεν</span></span>). We do not know. How quickly they had forgotten both God and Moses while Moses was absent in the mount with God.</p>
<p><b>Become of him </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εγενετο αυτω</span></span>). Happened to him. "This" (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ουτος</span></span>) here is a contemptuous allusion to Moses by the people. </p>
