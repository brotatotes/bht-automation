<p><b> And Jesus standing </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κα Ιησουν εστωτα</span></span>). Full of the Holy Spirit, gazing steadfastly into heaven, he saw God's glory and Jesus "standing" as if he had risen to cheer the brave Stephen. Elsewhere (save verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+7:56">Acts 7:56</span> also) he is pictured as sitting at the right hand of God (the Session of Christ) as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+26:64">Matthew 26:64</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mr+16:19">Mark 16:19</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+2:34">Acts 2:34</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="eph+1:20">Ephesians 1:20</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="col+3:1">Colossians 3:1</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="heb+1:3">Hebrews 1:3</span>. </p>
