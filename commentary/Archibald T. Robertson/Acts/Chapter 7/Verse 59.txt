<p><b> They stoned </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ελιθοβολουν</span></span>). Same verb and tense repeated, they kept on stoning, they kept it up as he was calling upon the Lord Jesus and making direct prayer to him as "Lord Jesus" (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">Κυριε Ιησου</span></span>).</p>
<p><b>Receive my spirit </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δεξα το πνευμα μου</span></span>). Aorist middle imperative, urgency, receive it now. Many have followed Stephen into death with these words upon their dying lips. See, <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+9:14">Acts 9:14</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+9:21">Acts 9:21</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+22:16">Acts 22:16</span>. </p>
