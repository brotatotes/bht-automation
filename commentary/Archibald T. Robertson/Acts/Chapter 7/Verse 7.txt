<p><b> Will I judge </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κρινω εγω</span></span>). Future (accent on <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ω</span></span>) active indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κρινω</span></span> and <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εγω</span></span> (I) expressed is emphatic.</p>
<p><b>In this place </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εν τω τοπω τουτω</span></span>). Quoted from <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ex+3:12">Exodus 3:12</span> and referring to Sinai or Horeb, but Stephen applies it to the Promised Land. </p>
