<p><b> Moved with jealousy </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ζηλωσαντες</span></span>). First aorist active participle of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ζηλοω</span></span>, old verb from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ζηλος</span></span> (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+5:17">Acts 5:17</span>), to burn or boil with zeal, and then with envy as here (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+17:5">Acts 17:5</span>, etc.) and <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ge+37:11">Genesis 37:11</span>. </p>
