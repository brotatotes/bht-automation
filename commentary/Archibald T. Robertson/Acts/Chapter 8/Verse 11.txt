<p><b> Because that of long time he had amazed them with his sorceries </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δια το ικανω χρονω ταις μαγιαις εξεστακενα αυτους</span></span>). Causal use of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δια</span></span> with the accusative articular infinitive (perfect active <i>Koine</i> form and transitive, <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εξεστακενα</span></span>). Same verb as in verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+8:9">Acts 8:9</span> participle <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εξιστανων</span></span> and in verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+8:13">Acts 8:13</span> imperfect passive <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εξιστατο</span></span> (cf. also <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+2:7">Acts 2:7</span> already). <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">Χρονω</span></span> is associative instrumental and <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μαγιαις</span></span> instrumental case. </p>
