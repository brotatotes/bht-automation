<p><b> They were baptized </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εβαπτιζοντο</span></span>). Imperfect passive (repetition, from time to time), while</p>
<p><b>believed </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επιστευσαν</span></span>) is constative aorist antecedent to the baptism. Note dative case of Philip with <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επιστευσαν</span></span>. Note the gospel of Philip "concerning the kingdom of God, and the name of Jesus Christ." </p>
