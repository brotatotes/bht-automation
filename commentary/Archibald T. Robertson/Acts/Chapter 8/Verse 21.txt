<p><b> Lot </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κληρος</span></span>). Same idea as "part" (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μερις</span></span>), only as a figure.</p>
<p><b>Matter </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">λογο</span></span>). Literally, word or subject (as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+1:4">Luke 1:4</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+15:6">Acts 15:6</span>), the power of communicating the Holy Spirit. This use of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">λογος</span></span> is in the ancient Greek.</p>
<p><b>Straight </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ευθεια</span></span>). Quotation from <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ps+78:37">Psalms 78:37</span>. Originally a mathematically straight line as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+9:11">Acts 9:11</span>, then moral rectitude as here. </p>
