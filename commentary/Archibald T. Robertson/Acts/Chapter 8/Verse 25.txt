<p><b> They therefore </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ο μεν ουν</span></span>). Demonstrative <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ο</span></span> with <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μεν</span></span> (no following <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δε</span></span>) and the inferential <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ουν</span></span> (therefore) as often in Acts (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+1:6">Acts 1:6</span>, etc.).</p>
<p><b>Returned </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">υπεστρεφον</span></span>). Imperfect active picturing the joyful journey of preaching (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ευηγγελιζοντο</span></span>, imperfect middle) to the Samaritan villages. Peter and John now carried on the work of Philip to the Samaritans. This issue was closed. </p>
