<p><b> Was reading </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ανεγινωσκεν</span></span>). Imperfect active descriptive, not periphrastic like the two preceding verbs (was returning and sitting). He was reading aloud as Philip "heard him reading" (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ηκουσεν αυτον αναγινωσκοντος</span></span>), a common practice among orientals. He had probably purchased this roll of Isaiah in Jerusalem and was reading the LXX Greek text. See imperfect again in verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+8:32">Acts 8:32</span>. </p>
