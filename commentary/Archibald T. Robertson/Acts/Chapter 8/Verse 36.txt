<p><b> What doth hinder me to be baptized? </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">Τ κωλυε με βαπτισθηναι?</span></span>). Evidently Philip had said something about baptism following faith and conversion. Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+8:37">Acts 8:37</span> is not a genuine part of Acts, a western addition. Later baptismal liturgies had it. </p>
