<p><b> He preached the gospel </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ευηγγελιζετο</span></span>). Imperfect middle describing the evangelistic tour of Philip "till he came to Caesarea" (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εως του ελθειν αυτον</span></span>, genitive articular infinitive with the preposition <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εως</span></span> and the accusative of general reference) where he made his home and headquarters thereafter (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+21:28">Acts 21:28</span>) and was known as the Evangelist. </p>
