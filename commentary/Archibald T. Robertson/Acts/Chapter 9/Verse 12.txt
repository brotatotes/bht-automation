<p><b> Coming in and laying </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εισελθοντα κα επιθεντα</span></span>). Second aorist (ingressive) active participles picturing the punctiliar act as a sort of indirect discourse after verbs of sensation (Robertson, <i>Grammar</i>, pp. 1040-2). Some ancient documents do not have "in a vision" here.</p>
<p><b>Receive his sight </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αναβλεψε</span></span>). First aorist active subjunctive with <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">οπως</span></span> (purpose). See again as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+9:17">Acts 9:17</span>. </p>
