<p><b> Hath authority </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εχε εξουσιαν</span></span>). Probably Ananias had received letters from the Christians left in Jerusalem warning him of the coming of Saul. The protest of Ananias to Jesus against any dealing with Saul is a fine illustration of our own narrow ignorance in our rebellious moods against the will of God. </p>
