<p><b> Was strengthened </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ενισχυθη</span></span>). First aorist passive indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ενισχυω</span></span>, to receive strength (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ισχυς</span></span>), comparatively late verb and here only in the N.T. save <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+22:43">Luke 22:43</span> where it is doubtful. Poor verse division. This clause belongs in sense to verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+9:18">Acts 9:18</span>.</p>
<p><b>Some days </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ημερας τινας</span></span>). An indefinite period, probably not long, the early period in Damascus before Saul left for Arabia (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ga+1:13-24">Galatians 1:13-24</span>). </p>
