<p><b> In an upper chamber </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εν υπερωιω</span></span>). See on <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+1:13">Acts 1:13</span>. Also in verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+9:39">Acts 9:39</span>. In that house. This service was rendered by the women, though Luke has <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">λουσαντες</span></span> (masculine plural aorist active participle of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">λουω</span></span>), a general way of saying "they washed." The interment was not hurried as in Jerusalem (Ananias and Sapphira) and the upper room is where the body was usually placed. </p>
