<p><b> Stood by him </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">παρεστησαν αυτω</span></span>). Second aorist active indicative, intransitive, of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">παριστημ</span></span>). Vivid picture of this group of widows as they stood around Peter, weeping (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κλαιουσα</span></span>) and showing (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επιδεικνυμενα</span></span>, present middle as belonging to themselves, pointing with pride to) the very inner garments (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">χιτωνας</span></span>) and outer garments (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ιματια</span></span>), like the Latin <i>tunica</i> and <i>toga</i>, which she made from time to time (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εποιε</span></span>, imperfect active, repeated action). It was a heart-breaking scene. </p>
