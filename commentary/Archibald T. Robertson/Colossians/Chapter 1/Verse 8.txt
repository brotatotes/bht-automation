<p><b> Who also declared </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ο κα δηλωσας</span></span>). Articular first aorist active participle of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δηλοω</span></span>, old verb, to make manifest. Epaphras told Paul about their "love in the Spirit," grounded in the Holy Spirit. </p>
