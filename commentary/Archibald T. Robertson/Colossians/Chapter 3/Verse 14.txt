<p><b> And above all these things </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επ πασιν δε τουτοις</span></span>). "And upon all these things."</p>
<p><b>Put on love </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">την αγαπην</span></span>). See <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+3:20">Luke 3:20</span>. The verb has to be supplied (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ενδυσασθε</span></span>) from verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="col+3:12">Colossians 3:12</span> as the accusative case <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αγαπην</span></span> shows.</p>
<p><b>Which is </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ο εστιν</span></span>). Neuter singular of the relative and not feminine like <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αγαπη</span></span> (the antecedent) nor masculine like <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">συνδεσμος</span></span> in the predicate. However, there are similar examples of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ο εστιν</span></span> in the sense of <i>quod est</i> (<i>id est</i>), "that is," in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mr+14:42">Mark 14:42</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mr+15:42">Mark 15:42</span>, without agreement in gender and number. So also <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="eph+5:5">Ephesians 5:5</span> where <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ο εστιν</span></span> = "which thing."</p>
<p><b>The bond of perfectness </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">συνδεσμος της τελειοτητος</span></span>). See <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="col+2:19">Colossians 2:19</span> for <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">συνδεσμος</span></span>. Here it is apparently the girdle that holds the various garments together. The genitive (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τελειοτητος</span></span>) is probably that of apposition with the girdle of love. In a succinct way Paul has here put the idea about love set forth so wonderfully in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="col+3:1">Colossians 3:1</span>. </p>
