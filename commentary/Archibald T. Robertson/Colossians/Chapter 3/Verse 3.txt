<p><b> For ye died </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">απεθανετε γαρ</span></span>). Definite event, aorist active indicative, died to sin (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+6:2">Romans 6:2</span>).</p>
<p><b>Is hid </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κεκρυπτα</span></span>). Perfect passive indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κρυπτω</span></span>, old verb, to hide, remains concealed, locked "together with" (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">συν</span></span>) Christ, "in" (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εν</span></span>) God. No hellish burglar can break that combination. </p>
