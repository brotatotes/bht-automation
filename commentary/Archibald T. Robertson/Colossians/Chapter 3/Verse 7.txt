<p><b> Walked aforetime </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">περιεπατησατε ποτε</span></span>). First aorist (constative) indicative referring to their previous pagan state.</p>
<p><b>When ye lived </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">οτε εζητε</span></span>). Imperfect active indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ζαω</span></span>, to live, "ye used to live" (customary action). Sharp distinction in the tenses. </p>
