<p><b> And for them in Hierapolis </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κα των εν Hιερα Πολε</span></span>). The third of the three cities in the Lycus Valley which had not seen Paul's face (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="col+2:1">Colossians 2:1</span>). It was across the valley from Laodicea. Probably Epaphras had evangelized all three cities and all were in peril from the Gnostics. </p>
