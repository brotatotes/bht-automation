 Of me Paul with mine own hand  (τη εμη χειρ Παυλου). More precisely, "with the hand of me Paul." The genitive Παυλου is in apposition with the idea in the possessive pronoun εμη, which is itself in the instrumental case agreeing with χειρ. So also 2 Thessalonians 3:17; 1 Corinthians 16:21.My bonds  (μου των δεσμων). Genitive case with μνεμονευετε (remember). The chain (εν αλυσε Ephesians 6:20) clanked afresh as Paul took the pen to sign the salutation. He was not likely to forget it himself
