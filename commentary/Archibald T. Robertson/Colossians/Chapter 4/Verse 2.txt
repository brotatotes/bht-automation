<p><b> Continue steadfastly </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">προσκαρτερειτε</span></span>). See <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mr+3:9">Mark 3:9</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+2:42">Acts 2:42</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+2:46">Acts 2:46</span> for this interesting word from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">προς</span></span> and <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">καρτερος</span></span> (strong), common in the <i>Koine</i>.</p>
<p><b>Watching </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">γρηγορουντες</span></span>). Present active participle of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">γρηγορεω</span></span>, late present made on perfect active stem <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εγρηγορα</span></span> with loss of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ε-</span></span>, found first in Aristotle. </p>
