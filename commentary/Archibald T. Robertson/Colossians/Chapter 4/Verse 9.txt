<p><b> Together with Onesimus </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">συν Ονησιμω</span></span>). Co-bearer of the letter with Tychicus and praised on a par with him, runaway slave though he is.</p>
<p><b>Who is one of you </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ος εστιν εξ υμων</span></span>). Said not as a reproach to Colossae for having such a man, but as a privilege to the church in Colossae to give a proper welcome to this returning converted slave and to treat him as a brother as Paul argues to Philemon. </p>
