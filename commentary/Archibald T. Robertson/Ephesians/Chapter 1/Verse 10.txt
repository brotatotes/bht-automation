<p><b> Unto a dispensation of the fulness of the times </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εις οικονομιαν του πληρωματος των καιρων</span></span>). See <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="col+1:25">Colossians 1:25</span> for <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">οικονομιαν</span></span>. In <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ga+4:4">Galatians 4:4</span> "the fulness of the time" (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">το πληρωμα του χρονου</span></span>) the time before Christ is treated as a unit, here as a series of epochs (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">καιρων</span></span>). Cf. <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mr+1:15">Mark 1:15</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="heb+1:1">Hebrews 1:1</span>. On <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πληρωμα</span></span> see also <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+11:26">Romans 11:26</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="eph+3:19">Ephesians 3:19</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="eph+4:13">Ephesians 4:13</span>.</p>
<p><b>To sum up </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ανακεφαλαιωσασθα</span></span>). Purpose clause (amounting to result) with first aorist middle infinitive of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ανακεφαλαιοω</span></span>, late compound verb <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ανα</span></span> and <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κεφαλαιοω</span></span> (from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κεφαλαιον</span></span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="heb+8:1">Hebrews 8:1</span>, and that from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κεφαλη</span></span>, head), to head up all things in Christ, a literary word. In N.T. only here and <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+13:9">Romans 13:9</span>. For the headship of Christ in nature and grace see <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="col+1:15-20">Colossians 1:15-20</span>. </p>
