<p><b> The exceeding greatness of his power </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">το υπερβαλλον μεγεθος της δυναμεως αυτου</span></span>). <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">Μεγεθος</span></span> is an old word (from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μεγας</span></span>), but here only in N.T. <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">Hυπερβαλλον</span></span>, present active participle of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">υπερβαλλω</span></span>, reappears in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="eph+2:7">Ephesians 2:7</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="eph+3:19">Ephesians 3:19</span> and seen already in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2co+3:10">2 Corinthians 3:10</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2co+9:14">2 Corinthians 9:14</span>. To enlightened eyes the greatness of God's power is even more "surpassing." </p>
