<p><b> Preached peace </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ευηγγελισατο ειρηνην</span></span>). First aorist middle of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ευαγγελιζω</span></span>. "He gospelized peace" to both Jew and Gentile, "to the far off ones" (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τοις μακραν</span></span>) and "to the nigh ones" (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τοις εγγυς</span></span>). By the Cross and after the Cross Christ could preach that message. </p>
