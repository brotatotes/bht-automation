<p><b> Through him </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δι' αυτου</span></span>). Christ.</p>
<p><b>We both </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ο αμφοτερο</span></span>). "We the both" (Jew and Gentile).</p>
<p><b>Our access </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">την προσαγωγην</span></span>). The approach, the introduction as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+5:2">Romans 5:2</span>.</p>
<p><b>In one Spirit </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εν εν πνευματ</span></span>). The Holy Spirit.</p>
<p><b>Unto the Father </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">προς τον πατερα</span></span>). So the Trinity as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="eph+1:13">Ephesians 1:13</span>. The Three Persons all share in the work of redemption. </p>
