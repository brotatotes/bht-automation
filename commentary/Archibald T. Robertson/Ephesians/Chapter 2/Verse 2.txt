<p><b> According to the course of this world </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κατα τον αιωνα του κοσμου τουτου</span></span>). Curious combinations of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αιων</span></span> (a period of time), <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κοσμος</span></span> (the world in that period). See <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1co+1:20">1 Corinthians 1:20</span> for "this age" and <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1co+3:9">1 Corinthians 3:9</span> for "this world."</p>
<p><b>The prince of the power of the air </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τον αρχοντα της εξουσιας του αερος</span></span>). <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">Αηρ</span></span> was used by the ancients for the lower and denser atmosphere and <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αιθηρ</span></span> for the higher and rarer. Satan is here pictured as ruler of the demons and other agencies of evil. Jesus called him "the prince of this world" (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ο αρχων του κοσμου τουτου</span></span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+16:11">John 16:11</span>).</p>
<p><b>That now worketh </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">του νυν ενεργουντος</span></span>). Those who deny the existence of a personal devil cannot successfully deny the vicious tendencies, the crime waves, in modern men. The power of the devil in the lives of men does explain the evil at work "in the sons of disobedience" (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εν τοις υιοις της απεθιας</span></span>). In <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="eph+5:6">Ephesians 5:6</span> also. A Hebrew idiom found in the papyri like "sons of light" (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1th+5:5">1 Thessalonians 5:5</span>). </p>
