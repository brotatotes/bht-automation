On <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">οικονομιαν</span></span> (stewardship, dispensation) see <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="eph+1:9">Ephesians 1:9</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="eph+3:9">Ephesians 3:9</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="col+1:25">Colossians 1:25</span>. </p>
