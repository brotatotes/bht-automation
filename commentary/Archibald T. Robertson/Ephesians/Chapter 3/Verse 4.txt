<p><b> Whereby </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">προς ο</span></span>). "Looking to which," "according to which."</p>
<p><b>When ye read </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αναγινωσκοντες</span></span>). This Epistle will be read in public.</p>
<p><b>My understanding in the mystery of Christ </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">την συνεσιν μου εν τω μυστηριω του Χριστου</span></span>). My "comprehension" (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">συνεσιν</span></span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="col+1:9">Colossians 1:9</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="col+2:2">Colossians 2:2</span>). Every sermon reveals the preacher's grasp of "the mystery of Christ." If he has no insight into Christ, he has no call to preach. </p>
