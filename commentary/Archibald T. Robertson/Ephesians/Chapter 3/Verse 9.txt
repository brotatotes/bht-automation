<p><b> To make see </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">φωτισα</span></span>). First aorist active infinitive of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">φοτιζω</span></span>, late verb, to turn the light on. With the eyes of the heart enlightened (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="eph+1:18">Ephesians 1:18</span>) one can then turn the light for others to see. See <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="col+1:26">Colossians 1:26</span>. </p>
