<p><b> For the perfecting </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">προς τον καταρτισμον</span></span>). Late and rare word (in Galen in medical sense, in papyri for house-furnishing), only here in N.T., though <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">καταρτισις</span></span> in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2co+13:9">2 Corinthians 13:9</span>, both from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">καταρτιζω</span></span>, to mend (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+4:21">Matthew 4:21</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ga+6:1">Galatians 6:1</span>). "For the mending (repair) of the saints."</p>
<p><b>Unto the building up </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εις οικοδομην</span></span>). See <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="eph+2:21">Ephesians 2:21</span>. This is the ultimate goal in all these varied gifts, "building up." </p>
