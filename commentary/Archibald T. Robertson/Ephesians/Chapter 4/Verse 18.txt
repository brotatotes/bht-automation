<p><b> Being darkened </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εσκοτωμενο οντες</span></span>). Periphrastic perfect passive participle of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">σκοτοω</span></span>, old verb from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">σκοτος</span></span> (darkness), in N.T. only here and <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+9:2">Revelation 9:2</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+16:10">Revelation 16:10</span>.</p>
<p><b>In their understanding </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τη διανοια</span></span>). Locative case. Probably <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">διανοια</span></span> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δια, νους</span></span>) includes the emotions as well as the intellect (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">νους</span></span>). It is possible to take <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">οντες</span></span> with <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">απηλλοτριωμενο</span></span> (see <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="eph+2:12">Ephesians 2:12</span>) which would then be periphrastic (instead of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εσκοτωμενο</span></span>) perfect passive participle.</p>
<p><b>From the life of God </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">της ζωης του θεου</span></span>). Ablative case <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ζωης</span></span> after <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">απηλλοτριωμενο</span></span> (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="eph+2:12">Ephesians 2:12</span>).</p>
<p><b>Because of the ignorance </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δια την αγνοιαν</span></span>). Old word from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αγνοεω</span></span>, not to know. Rare in N.T. See <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+3:17">Acts 3:17</span>.</p>
<p><b>Hardening </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πωρωσιν</span></span>). Late medical term (Hippocrates) for callous hardening. Only other N.T. examples are <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mr+3:5">Mark 3:5</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+11:25">Romans 11:25</span>. </p>
