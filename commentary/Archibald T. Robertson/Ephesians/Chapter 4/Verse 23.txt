<p><b> That ye be renewed </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ανανεουσθα</span></span>). Present passive infinitive (epexegetical, like <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αποθεσθα</span></span>, of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αληθεια εν τω Ιησου</span></span>) and to be compared with <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ανακαινουμενον</span></span> in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="col+3:10">Colossians 3:10</span>. It is an old verb, <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ανανεοω</span></span>, to make new (young) again; though only here in N.T.</p>
<p><b>The spirit </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τω πνευματ</span></span>). Not the Holy Spirit, but the human spirit. </p>
