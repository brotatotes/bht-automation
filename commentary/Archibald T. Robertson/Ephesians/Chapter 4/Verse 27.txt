<p><b> Neither give place to the devil </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μηδε διδοτε τοπον τω διαβολω</span></span>). Present active imperative in prohibition, either stop doing it or do not have the habit. See <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+12:19">Romans 12:19</span> for this idiom. </p>
