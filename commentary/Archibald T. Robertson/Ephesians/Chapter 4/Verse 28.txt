<p><b> Steal no more </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μηκετ κλεπτετω</span></span>). Clearly here, cease stealing (present active imperative with <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μηκετ</span></span>).</p>
<p><b>The thing that is good </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">το αγαθον</span></span>). "The good thing" opposed to his stealing and "with his hands" (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ταις χερσιν</span></span>, instrumental case) that did the stealing. See <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2th+3:10">2 Thessalonians 3:10</span>. Even unemployment is no excuse for stealing.</p>
<p><b>To give </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μεταδιδονα</span></span>). Present active infinitive of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μεταδιδωμ</span></span>, to share with one. </p>
