<p><b> The unity </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">την ενοτητα</span></span>). Late and rare word (from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εις</span></span>, one), in Aristotle and Plutarch, though in N.T. only here and verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="eph+4:13">Ephesians 4:13</span>.</p>
<p><b>In the bond of peace </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εν τω συνδεσμω της ειρηνης</span></span>). In <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="col+3:14">Colossians 3:14</span> <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αγαπη</span></span> (love) is the <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">συνδεσμος</span></span> (bond). But there is no peace without love (verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="eph+4:2">Ephesians 4:2</span>). </p>
