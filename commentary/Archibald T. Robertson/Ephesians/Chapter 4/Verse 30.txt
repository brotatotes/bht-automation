<p><b> Grieve not the Holy Spirit of God </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μη λυπειτε το πνευμα το αγιον του θεου</span></span>). "Cease grieving" or "do not have the habit of grieving." Who of us has not sometimes grieved the Holy Spirit?</p>
<p><b>In whom </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εν ω</span></span>). Not "in which."</p>
<p><b>Ye were sealed </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εσφραγισθητε</span></span>). See <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="eph+1:13">Ephesians 1:13</span> for this verb, and <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="eph+1:14">Ephesians 1:14</span> for <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">απολυτρωσεως</span></span>, the day when final redemption is realized. </p>
