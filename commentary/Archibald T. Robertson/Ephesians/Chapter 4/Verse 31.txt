<p><b> Bitterness </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πικρια</span></span>). Old word from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πικρος</span></span> (bitter), in N.T. only here and <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+8:23">Acts 8:23</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+3:14">Romans 3:14</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="heb+12:15">Hebrews 12:15</span>.</p>
<p><b>Clamour </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κραυγη</span></span>). <p><b>Be put away </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αρθητω</span></span>). First aorist passive imperative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αιρω</span></span>, old verb, to pick up and carry away, to make a clean sweep. </p>
