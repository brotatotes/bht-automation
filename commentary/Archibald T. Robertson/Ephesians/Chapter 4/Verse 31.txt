 Bitterness  (πικρια). Old word from πικρος (bitter), in N.T. only here and Acts 8:23; Romans 3:14; Hebrews 12:15.Clamour  (κραυγη). Old word for outcry (Matthew 25:6; Luke 1:42). See Colossians 3:8 for the other words.Be put away  (αρθητω). First aorist passive imperative of αιρω, old verb, to pick up and carry away, to make a clean sweep.
