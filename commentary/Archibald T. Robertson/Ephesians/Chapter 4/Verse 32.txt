<p><b> Be ye kind to one another </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">γινεσθε εις αλληλους χρηστο</span></span>). Present middle imperative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">γινομα</span></span>, "keep on becoming kind (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">χρηστος</span></span>, used of God in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+2:4">Romans 2:4</span>) toward one another." See <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="col+3:12">Colossians 3:12</span>.</p>
