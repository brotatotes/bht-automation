 One body  (εν σωμα). One mystical body of Christ (the spiritual church or kingdom, cf. Ephesians 1:23; Ephesians 2:16).One Spirit  (εν πνευμα). One Holy Spirit, grammatical neuter gender (not to be referred to by "it," but by "he").In one hope  (εν μια ελπιδ). The same hope as a result of their calling for both Jew and Greek as shown in chapter Ephesians 4:2.
