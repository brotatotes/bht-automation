<p><b> One body </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εν σωμα</span></span>). One mystical body of Christ (the spiritual church or kingdom, cf. <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="eph+1:23">Ephesians 1:23</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="eph+2:16">Ephesians 2:16</span>).</p>
<p><b>One Spirit </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εν πνευμα</span></span>). One Holy Spirit, grammatical neuter gender (not to be referred to by "it," but by "he").</p>
<p><b>In one hope </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εν μια ελπιδ</span></span>). The same hope as a result of their calling for both Jew and Greek as shown in chapter <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="eph+4:2">Ephesians 4:2</span>. </p>
