<p><b> Have no fellowship with </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μη συνκοινωνειτε</span></span>). No partnership with, present imperative with <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μη</span></span>. Followed by associative instrumental case <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εργοις</span></span> (works).</p>
<p><b>Unfruitful </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ακαρποις</span></span>). Same metaphor of verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="eph+5:9">Ephesians 5:9</span> applied to darkness (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">σκοτος</span></span>).</p>
<p><b>Reprove </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ελεγχετε</span></span>). Convict by turning the light on the darkness. </p>
