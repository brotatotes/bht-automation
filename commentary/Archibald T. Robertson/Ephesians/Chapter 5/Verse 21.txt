<p><b> Subjecting yourselves to one another </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">υποτασσομενο αλληλοις</span></span>). Present middle participle of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">υποτασσω</span></span>, old military figure to line up under (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="col+3:18">Colossians 3:18</span>). The construction here is rather loose, coordinate with the preceding participles of praise and prayer. It is possible to start a new paragraph here and regard <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">υποτασσομενο</span></span> as an independent participle like an imperative. </p>
