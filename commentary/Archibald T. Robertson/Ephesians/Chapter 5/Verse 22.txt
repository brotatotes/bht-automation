But the case of ανδρασιν (dative) shows that the verb is understood from verse Ephesians 5:21 if not written originally. Ιδιοις (own) is genuine here, though not in Colossians 3:18.As unto the Lord  (ως τω Κυριω). So here instead of ως ανηκεν εν Κυριω of Colossians 3:18.
