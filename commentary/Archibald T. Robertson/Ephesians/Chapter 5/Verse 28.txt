<p><b> Even so ought </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ουτως οφειλουσιν</span></span>). As Christ loves the church (his body). How can one say that after reading <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="eph+5:22-33">Ephesians 5:22-33</span> where the noblest picture of marriage ever drawn is given? </p>
