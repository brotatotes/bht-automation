<p><b> Or covetousness </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">η πλεονεξια</span></span>). In bad company surely. Debasing like sensuality.</p>
<p><b>As becometh saints </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">καθως πρεπε αγιοις</span></span>). It is "unbecoming" for a saint to be sensual or covetous. </p>
