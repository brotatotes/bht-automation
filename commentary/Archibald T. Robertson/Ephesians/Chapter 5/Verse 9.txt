<p><b> The fruit of light </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ο καρπος του φωτος</span></span>). Two metaphors (fruit, light) combined. See <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ga+5:22">Galatians 5:22</span> for "the fruit of the Spirit." The late MSS. have "spirit" here in place of "light."</p>
<p><b>Goodness </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αγαθοσυνη</span></span>). Late and rare word from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αγαθος</span></span>. See <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2th+1:11">2 Thessalonians 1:11</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ga+5:22">Galatians 5:22</span>. </p>
