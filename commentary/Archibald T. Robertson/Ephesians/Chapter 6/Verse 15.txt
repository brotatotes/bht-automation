<p><b> Having shod </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">υποδησαμενο</span></span>). "Having bound under" (sandals). First aorist middle participle of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">υποδεω</span></span>, old word, to bind under (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mr+6:9">Mark 6:9</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+12:8">Acts 12:8</span>, only other N.T. example).</p>
<p><b>With the preparation </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εν ετοιμασια</span></span>). Late word from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ετοιμαζω</span></span>, to make ready, only here in N.T. Readiness of mind that comes from the gospel whose message is peace. </p>
