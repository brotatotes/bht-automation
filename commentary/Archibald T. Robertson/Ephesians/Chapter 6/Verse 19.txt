<p><b> That utterance may be given unto me </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ινα μο δοθη λογος</span></span>). Final clause with <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ινα</span></span> and first aorist passive subjunctive of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">διδωμ</span></span>, to give. See a like request in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="col+4:3">Colossians 4:3</span>. Paul wishes their prayer for courage for himself. </p>
