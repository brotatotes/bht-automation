<p><b> That ye may know </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ινα γνωτε</span></span>). Second aorist active subjunctive of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">γινωσκω</span></span>. Just as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="col+4:8">Colossians 4:8</span> he had not written <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ινα ειδητε</span></span> in verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="eph+6:21">Ephesians 6:21</span>.</p>
