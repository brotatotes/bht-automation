<p><b> And I was still unknown </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ημην δε αγνουμενος</span></span>). Periphrastic imperfect passive of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αγνοεω</span></span>, not to know.</p>
<p><b>By face </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τω προσωπω</span></span>). Associative instrumental case.</p>
<p><b>Of Judea </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">της Ιουδαιας</span></span>). As distinct from Jerusalem, for he had once scattered the church there and had revisited them before coming to Tarsus (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+9:26-30">Acts 9:26-30</span>). In <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+9:31">Acts 9:31</span> the singular of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εκκλησια</span></span> is used, but in a geographic sense for Judea, Samaria, and Galilee. </p>
