<p><b> They only heard </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μονον ακουοντες ησαν</span></span>). Periphrastic imperfect, "They were only hearing from time to time."</p>
<p><b>That once persecuted us </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ο διωκων ημας ποτε</span></span>). Present active articular participle, a sort of participle of antecedent time suggested by <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ποτε</span></span>, "the one who used to persecute us once upon a time."</p>
<p><b>The faith </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">την πιστιν</span></span>). Here used in the sense of "the gospel" as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+6:7">Acts 6:7</span>. </p>
