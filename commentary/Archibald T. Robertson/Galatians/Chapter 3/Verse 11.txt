<p><b> In the sight of God </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">παρα τω θεω</span></span>). By the side of (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">παρα</span></span>) God, as God looks at it, for the simple reason that no one except Jesus has ever kept <i>all</i> the law, God's perfect law. </p>
