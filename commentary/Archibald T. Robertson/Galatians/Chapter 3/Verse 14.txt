 That upon the Gentiles  (ινα εις τα εθνη). Final clause (ινα and γενητα, aorist middle subjunctive).That we might receive  (ινα λαβωμεν). Second final clause coordinate with the first as in 2 Corinthians 9:3. So in Christ we all (Gentile and Jew) obtain the promise of blessing made to Abraham, through faith.
