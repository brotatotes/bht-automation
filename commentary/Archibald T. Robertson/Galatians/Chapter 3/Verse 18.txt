<p><b> The inheritance </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">η κληρονομια</span></span>). Old word from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κληρονομος</span></span>, heir (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κλερος</span></span>, lot, <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">νεμομα</span></span>, to distribute). See on <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+21:38">Matthew 21:38</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+7:5">Acts 7:5</span>. This came to Israel by the promise to Abraham, not by the Mosaic law. So with us, Paul argues.</p>
<p><b>Hath granted </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κεχαριστα</span></span>). Perfect middle indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">χαριζομα</span></span>. It still holds good after the law came. </p>
