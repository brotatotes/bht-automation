<p><b> Foreseeing </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">προιδουσα</span></span>). Second aorist active participle of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">προοραω</span></span>. The Scripture is here personified. Alone in this sense of "sight," but common with <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">λεγε</span></span> or <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ειπεν</span></span> (says, said) and really in verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ga+3:22">Galatians 3:22</span> "hath shut up" (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">συνεκλεισεν</span></span>).</p>
<p><b>Would justify </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δικαιο</span></span>). Present active indicative, "does justify."</p>
<p><b>Preached the gospel beforehand </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">προευηγγελισατο</span></span>). First aorist middle indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">προευαγγελιζομα</span></span> with augment on <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">α</span></span> though both <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">προ</span></span> and <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ευ</span></span> before it in composition. Only instance in N.T. It occurs in Philo. and Schol. Soph. This Scripture announced beforehand the gospel on this point of justification by faith. He quotes the promise to Abraham in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ge+12:3">Genesis 12:3</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ge+18:18">Genesis 18:18</span>, putting <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">παντα τα εθνη</span></span> (all the nations) in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ga+18:18">Galatians 18:18</span> for <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πασα α φυλα</span></span> (all the tribes) of the earth. It is a crucial passage for Paul's point, showing that the promise to Abraham included all the nations of the earth. The verb <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ενευλογεω</span></span> (future passive here) occurs in the LXX and here only in N.T. (not <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+3:25">Acts 3:25</span> in correct text).</p>
<p><b>In thee </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εν σο</span></span>). "As their spiritual progenitor" (Lightfoot). </p>
