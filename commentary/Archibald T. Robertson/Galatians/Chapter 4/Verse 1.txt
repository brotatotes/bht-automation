<p><b> So long as </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εφ' οσον χρονον</span></span>). "For how long a time," incorporation of the antecedent (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">χρονον</span></span>) into the relative clause.</p>
<p><b>The heir </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ο κληρονομος</span></span>). Old word (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κληρος</span></span>, lot, <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">νεμομα</span></span>, to possess). Illustration from the law of inheritance carrying on the last thought in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ga+3:29">Galatians 3:29</span>.</p>
<p><b>A child </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">νηπιος</span></span>). One that does not talk (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">νη, επος</span></span>, word). That is a minor, an infant, immature intellectually and morally in contrast with <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τελειο</span></span>, full grown (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1co+3:1">1 Corinthians 3:1</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1co+14:20">1 Corinthians 14:20</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="php+3:15">Philippians 3:15</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="eph+4:13">Ephesians 4:13</span>).</p>
<p><b>From a bondservant </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δουλου</span></span>). Slave. Ablative case of comparison after <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">διαφερε</span></span> for which verb see on <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+6:26">Matthew 6:26</span>.</p>
<p><b>Though he is lord of all </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">Κυριος παντων ων</span></span>). Concessive participle <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ων</span></span>, "being legally owner of all" (one who has the power, <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ο εχων κυρος</span></span>). </p>
