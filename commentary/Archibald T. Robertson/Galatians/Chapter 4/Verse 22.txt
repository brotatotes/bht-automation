<p><b> By the handmaid </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εκ της παιδισκης</span></span>). From <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ge+16:1">Genesis 16:1</span>. Feminine diminutive of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">παις</span></span>, boy or slave. Common word for damsel which came to be used for female slave or maidservant (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+12:45">Luke 12:45</span>) or doorkeeper like <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+26:29">Matthew 26:29</span>. So in the papyri. </p>
