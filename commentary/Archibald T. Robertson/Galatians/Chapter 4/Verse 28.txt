<p><b> Now we </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ημεις δε</span></span>). Some MSS. have <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">υμεις δε</span></span> (now ye). In either case Paul means that Christians (Jews and Gentiles) are children of the promise as Isaac was (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κατα Ισαακ</span></span>, after the manner of Isaac). </p>
