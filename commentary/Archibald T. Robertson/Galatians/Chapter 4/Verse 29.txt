<p><b> Persecuted </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εδιωκεν</span></span>). Imperfect active of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">διωκω</span></span>, to pursue, to persecute. <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ge+21:9">Genesis 21:9</span> has in Hebrew "laughing," but the LXX has "mocking." The Jewish tradition represents Ishmael as shooting arrows at Isaac.</p>
<p><b>So now </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ουτος κα νυν</span></span>) the Jews were persecuting Paul and all Christians (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1th+2:15">1 Thessalonians 2:15</span>). </p>
