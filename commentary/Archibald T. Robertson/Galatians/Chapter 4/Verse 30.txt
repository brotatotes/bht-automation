<p><b> Cast out </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εκβαλε</span></span>). Second aorist active imperative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εκβαλλω</span></span>. Quotation from <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ge+21:10">Genesis 21:10</span> (Sarah to Abraham) and confirmed in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ga+21:12">Galatians 21:12</span> by God's command to Abraham. Paul gives allegorical warning thus to the persecuting Jews and Judaizers.</p>
<p><b>Shall not inherit </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ου μη κληρονομησε</span></span>). Strong negative (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ου μη</span></span> and future indicative). "The law and the gospel cannot co-exist. The law must disappear before the gospel" (Lightfoot). See <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ga+3:18">Galatians 3:18</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ga+3:29">Galatians 3:29</span> for the word "inherit." </p>
