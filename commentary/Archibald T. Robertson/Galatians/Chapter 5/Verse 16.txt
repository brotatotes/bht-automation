<p><b> Ye shall not fulfil </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ου μη τελεσητε</span></span>). Rather, "Ye will not fulfil." Strong double negative with aorist active subjunctive.</p>
<p><b>The lust of the flesh </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επιθυμιαν σαρκος</span></span>). Bad sense here as usual in Paul, but not so in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1th+2:17">1 Thessalonians 2:17</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="php+1:23">Philippians 1:23</span>. The word is just craving or longing (from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επι, θυμος</span></span>, yearning after). </p>
