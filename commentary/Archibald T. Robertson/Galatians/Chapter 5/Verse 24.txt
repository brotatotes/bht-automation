<p><b> Crucified the flesh </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">την σαρκα εσταυρωσαν</span></span>). Definite event, first aorist active indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">σταυροω</span></span> as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ga+2:19">Galatians 2:19</span> (mystical union with Christ). Paul uses <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">σαρξ</span></span> here in the same sense as in verses <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ga+5:16">Galatians 5:16</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ga+5:17">Galatians 5:17</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ga+5:19">Galatians 5:19</span>, "the force in men that makes for evil" (Burton).</p>
<p><b>With </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">συν</span></span>). "Together with," emphasizing "the completeness of the extermination of this evil force" and the guarantee of victory over one's passions and dispositions toward evil. </p>
