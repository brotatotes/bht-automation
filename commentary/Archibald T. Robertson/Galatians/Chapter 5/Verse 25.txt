<p><b> By the Spirit let us also walk </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πνευματ κα στοιχωμεν</span></span>). Present subjunctive (volitive) of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">στοιχεω</span></span>, "Let us also go on walking by the Spirit." Let us make our steps by the help and guidance of the Spirit. </p>
