<p><b> Availeth anything </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ισχυε τ</span></span>). Old word to have strength (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ισχυς</span></span>). See on <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+5:13">Matthew 5:13</span>. Neither Jew nor Greek has any recommendation in his state. See <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ga+3:28">Galatians 3:28</span>. All stand on a level in Christ.</p>
<p><b>Faith working through love </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πιστις δι' αγαπης ενεργουμενη</span></span>). Middle voice of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ενεργεω</span></span> and "through love," "the moral dynamic" (Burton) of Paul's conception of freedom from law. </p>
