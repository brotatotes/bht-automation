<p><b> As we have opportunity </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ως καιρον εχωμεν</span></span>). Indefinite comparative clause (present subjunctive without <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αν</span></span>). "As we have occasion at any time."</p>
<p><b>Let us work that which is good </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εργαζωμεθα το αγαθον</span></span>). Volitive present middle subjunctive of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εργαζομα</span></span>, "Let us keep on working the good deed."</p>
<p><b>Of the household of faith </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τους οικειους της πιστεως</span></span>). For the obvious reason that they belong to the same family with necessary responsibility. </p>
