<p><b> Lord </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">Κυριε</span></span>). In the LXX, not in the Hebrew. Quotation (the sixth) from <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ps+102:26-28">Psalms 102:26-28</span> through verses <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="heb+1:10-12">Hebrews 1:10-12</span>. Note emphatic position of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">συ</span></span> here at the beginning as in verses <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="heb+1:11-12">Hebrews 1:11-12</span> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">συ δε</span></span>). This Messianic Psalm pictures the Son in his Creative work and in his final triumph.</p>
<p><b>Hast laid the foundation </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εθεμελιωσας</span></span>). First aorist active of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">θεμελιοω</span></span>, old verb from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">θεμελιος</span></span> (foundation) for which see <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="col+1:23">Colossians 1:23</span>. </p>
