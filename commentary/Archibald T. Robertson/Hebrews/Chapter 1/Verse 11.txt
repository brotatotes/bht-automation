<p><b> They </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αυτο</span></span>). The heavens (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ουρανο</span></span>).</p>
<p><b>Shall perish </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">απολουντα</span></span>). Future middle of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">απολλυμ</span></span>. Modern scientists no longer postulate the eternal existence of the heavenly bodies.</p>
<p><b>But thou continuest </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">συ δε διαμενεις</span></span>). This is what matters most, the eternal existence of God's Son as Creator and Preserver of the universe (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+1:1-3">John 1:1-3</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="col+1:14">Colossians 1:14</span>).</p>
<p><b>Shall wax old </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">παλαιωθησοντα</span></span>). First future passive indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">παλαιοω</span></span>, from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">παλαιος</span></span>, for which see <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+12:33">Luke 12:33</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="heb+8:13">Hebrews 8:13</span>. </p>
