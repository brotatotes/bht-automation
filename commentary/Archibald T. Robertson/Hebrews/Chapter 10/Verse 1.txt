<p><b> Shadow </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">σκιαν</span></span>). The contrast here between <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">σκια</span></span> (shadow, shade caused by interruption of light as by trees, <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mr+4:32">Mark 4:32</span>) and <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εικων</span></span> (image or picture) is striking. Christ is the <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εικων</span></span> of God (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2co+4:4">2 Corinthians 4:4</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="col+1:15">Colossians 1:15</span>). In <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="col+2:17">Colossians 2:17</span> Paul draws a distinction between <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">σκια</span></span> for the Jewish rites and ceremonies and <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">σωμα</span></span> for the reality in Christ. Children are fond of shadow pictures. The law gives only a dim outline of the good things to come (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="heb+9:11">Hebrews 9:11</span>).</p>
<p><b>Continually </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εις το διηνεκες</span></span>). See this phrase also in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="heb+7:3">Hebrews 7:3</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="heb+9:12">Hebrews 9:12</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="heb+9:14">Hebrews 9:14</span>. Nowhere else in N.T. From <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">διηνεγκα</span></span> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">διαφερω</span></span>), to bear through.</p>
<p><b>They can </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δυναντα</span></span>). This reading leaves <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ο νομος</span></span> a <i>nominativus pendens</i> (an anacoluthon). But many MSS. read <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δυνατα</span></span> (it--the law--can). For the idea and use of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τελειωσα</span></span> see <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="heb+9:9">Hebrews 9:9</span>. </p>
