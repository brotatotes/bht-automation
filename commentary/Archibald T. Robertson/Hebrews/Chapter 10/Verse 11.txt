<p><b> Standeth </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εστηκεν</span></span>). Perfect active indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ιστημ</span></span> (intransitive), vivid picture.</p>
<p><b>Ministering and offering </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">λειτουργων κα προσφερων</span></span>). Present active participles graphically describing the priest.</p>
<p><b>Take away </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">περιελειν</span></span>). Second aorist active infinitive of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">περιαιρεω</span></span>, old verb to take from around, to remove utterly as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+27:20">Acts 27:20</span>. </p>
