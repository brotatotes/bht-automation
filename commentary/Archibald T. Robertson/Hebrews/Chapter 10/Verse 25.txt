<p><b> Not forsaking </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μη εγκαταλειποντες</span></span>). "Not leaving behind, not leaving in the lurch" (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2ti+4:10">2 Timothy 4:10</span>).</p>
<p><b>The assembling of yourselves together </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">την επισυναγωγην εαυτων</span></span>). Late double compound from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επισυναγω</span></span>, to gather together (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">συν</span></span>) besides (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επ</span></span>) as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+23:37">Matthew 23:37</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+17:27">Luke 17:27</span>. In N.T. only here and <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2th+2:1">2 Thessalonians 2:1</span>. In an inscription 100 B.C. for collection of money (Deissmann, <i>Light</i>, etc., p. 103).</p>
<p><b>As the custom of some is </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">καθως εθος τισιν</span></span>). "As is custom to some." For <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εθος</span></span> (custom) see <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+22:39">Luke 22:39</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+19:40">John 19:40</span>. Already some Christians had formed the habit of not attending public worship, a perilous habit then and now.</p>
<p><b>So much the more as </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τοσουτω μαλλον οσω</span></span>). Instrumental case of measure or degree, "by so much the more as," both with <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τοσουτω</span></span> and <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">οσω</span></span>.</p>
<p><b>The day drawing nigh </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εγγιζουσαν την ημεραν</span></span>). The Second Coming of Christ which draws nearer all the time (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+13:12">Romans 13:12</span>). </p>
