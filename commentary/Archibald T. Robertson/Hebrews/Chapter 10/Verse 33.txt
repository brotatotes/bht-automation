<p><b> Partly </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τουτο μεν</span></span>)</p>
<p><b>and partly </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τουτο δε</span></span>). Accusative of general reference (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τουτο</span></span>) with <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μεν</span></span> and <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δε</span></span> for contrast.</p>
<p><b>Being made a gazing-stock </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">θεατριζομενο</span></span>). Late verb to bring upon the stage, to hold up to derision. See Paul's use of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">θεατρον</span></span> of himself in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1co+4:9">1 Corinthians 4:9</span>.</p>
<p><b>By reproaches and afflictions </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ονειδισμοις τε κα θλιψεσιν</span></span>). Instrumental case. See <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+15:3">Romans 15:3</span>.</p>
<p><b>Partakers </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κοινωνο</span></span>). Partners (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+5:10">Luke 5:10</span>) with those (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">των</span></span> objective genitive).</p>
<p><b>So used </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ουτως αναστρεφομενων</span></span>). Present middle articular participle of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αναστρεφω</span></span>, to conduct oneself (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2co+1:12">2 Corinthians 1:12</span>). </p>
