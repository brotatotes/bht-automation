<p><b> If he shrink back </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εαν υποστειλητα</span></span>). Condition of third class with <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εαν</span></span> and the first aorist middle subjunctive of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">υποστελλω</span></span>, old verb to draw oneself under or back, to withdraw, as already in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+20:20">Acts 20:20</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+20:27">Acts 20:27</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ga+2:12">Galatians 2:12</span>. See <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+1:17">Romans 1:17</span> for the quotation also of "the just shall live by faith." </p>
