<p><b> Thou hadst no pleasure </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ουκ ευδοκησας</span></span>). First aorist active indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ευδοκεω</span></span>, common for God's good pleasure (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+3:17">Matthew 3:17</span>). God took no pleasure in the animal offering (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">θυσιαν</span></span>), the meal-offering (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">προσφοραν</span></span>), the burnt-offering (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ολοκαυτωματα</span></span>), the sin-offering (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">περ αμαρτιας</span></span>, concerning sin). </p>
