<p><b> To conceive seed </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εις καταβολην σπερματος</span></span>). For deposit of seed. See <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="heb+4:3">Hebrews 4:3</span> for <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">καταβολη</span></span>.</p>
<p><b>Past age </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">παρα καιρον ηλικιας</span></span>). Beyond (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">παρα</span></span> with the accusative) the season of age.</p>
<p><b>Since she counted him faithful who had promised </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επε πιστον ηγησατο τον επαγγειλαμενον</span></span>). Sarah herself (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αυτη--Σαρρα</span></span>). Even Sarah, old as she was, believed God who had promised. Hence she received power. </p>
