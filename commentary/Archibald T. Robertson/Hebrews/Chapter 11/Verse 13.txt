<p><b> In faith </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κατα πιστιν</span></span>). Here a break in the routine <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πιστε</span></span> (by faith), "according to faith," either for literary variety "or to suggest <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πιστις</span></span> as the sphere and standard of their characters" (Moffatt).</p>
<p><b>These all </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ουτο παντες</span></span>). Those in verses <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="heb+11:9-12">Hebrews 11:9-12</span> (Abraham, Sarah, Isaac, Jacob).</p>
<p><b>Not having the promises </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μη κομισαμενο τας επαγγελιας</span></span>). First aorist middle participle of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κομιζω</span></span>, to obtain, as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="heb+10:36">Hebrews 10:36</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="heb+11:39">Hebrews 11:39</span>. And yet the author mentions Abraham (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="heb+6:15">Hebrews 6:15</span>) as having obtained the promise. He received the promise of the Messiah, but did not live to see the Messiah come as we have done. It is in this sense that we have "better promises."</p>
<p><b>Greeted them </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ασπασαμενο</span></span>). First aorist middle participle of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ασπαζομα</span></span>, to salute (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+5:47">Matthew 5:47</span>). Abraham rejoiced to see Christ's day in the dim distance (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+8:56">John 8:56</span>).</p>
<p><b>Strangers </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ζενο</span></span>). Foreigners. "To reside abroad carried with it a certain stigma" (Moffatt). But they "confessed" it (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ge+23:4">Genesis 23:4</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ge+47:9">Genesis 47:9</span>).</p>
<p><b>Pilgrims </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">παρεπιδημο</span></span>). Late double compound (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">παρα, επι, δημος</span></span>), a sojourner from another land, in N.T. only here and <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1pe+1:1">1 Peter 1:1</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1pe+2:11">1 Peter 2:11</span>. </p>
