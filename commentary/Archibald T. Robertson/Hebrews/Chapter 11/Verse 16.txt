<p><b> They desire </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ορεγοντα</span></span>). Present middle indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ορεγω</span></span>, old word for stretching out after, yearning after as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1ti+3:1">1 Timothy 3:1</span>.</p>
<p><b>Their God </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">θεος αυτων</span></span>). Predicate nominative with the epexegetic infinitive <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επικαλεισθα</span></span> (to be called) used with <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ουκ επαισχυνετα</span></span> (is not ashamed). </p>
