<p><b> Therein </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εν ταυτη</span></span>). That is, "in faith," feminine demonstrative referring to <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πιστις</span></span>.</p>
<p><b>The elders </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ο πρεσβυτερο</span></span>). More nearly like "the fathers," not the technical sense of elders (officers) usual in the N.T., but more like "the tradition of the elders" (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mr+7:3">Mark 7:3</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mr+7:5">Mark 7:5</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+15:2">Matthew 15:2</span>).</p>
<p><b>Had witness borne to them </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εμαρτυρηθησαν</span></span>). First aorist passive of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μαρτυρεω</span></span> (cf. <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="heb+7:8">Hebrews 7:8</span>), "were testified to." </p>
