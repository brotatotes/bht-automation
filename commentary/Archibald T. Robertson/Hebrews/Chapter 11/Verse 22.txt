 When his end was nigh  (τελευτων). Present active participle of τελευταω, to finish or close (Matthew 2:19), "finishing his life."Of the departure  (περ της εξοδου). Late compound for way out, exit as here, metaphorically of death as here (Luke 9:31; 2 Peter 1:15).Concerning his bones  (περ των οστεων αυτου). Uncontracted form as in Matthew 23:27.
