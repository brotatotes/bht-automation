<p><b> Was hid </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εκρυβη</span></span>). Second aorist passive indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κρυπτω</span></span>, to hide, as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+5:14">Matthew 5:14</span>.</p>
<p><b>Three months </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τριμηνον</span></span>). Old adjective used as neuter substantive in accusative case for extent of time, here only in N.T.</p>
<p><b>A goodly child </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αστειον το παιδιον</span></span>). Literally, "the child was goodly" (predicate adjective). Old adjective from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αστυ</span></span> (city), "of the city" ("citified"), of polished manners, genteel. In N.T. only here and <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+7:20">Acts 7:20</span>, about Moses both times. Quoted from <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ex+2:2">Exodus 2:2</span> <b> The king's commandment </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">το διαταγμα του βασιλεως</span></span>). Late compound for injunction from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">διατασσω</span></span>, only here in the N.T. </p>
