<p><b> When he was grown up </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μεγας γενομενος</span></span>). "Having become great" (from <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ex+2:11">Exodus 2:11</span>).</p>
<p><b>Refused </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ηρνεσατο</span></span>). First aorist middle indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αρνεομα</span></span>, to deny, to refuse. He was of age and made his choice not from ignorance.</p>
<p><b>Son </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">υιος</span></span>). Predicate nominative with <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">λεγεσθα</span></span> (to be spoken of, present passive infinitive, of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">λεγω</span></span>). </p>
