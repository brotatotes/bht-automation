<p><b> He kept </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πεποιηκεν</span></span>). Perfect active indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ποιεω</span></span>, to make, "he has made," emphasizing the permanent nature of the feast.</p>
<p><b>The sprinkling of the blood </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">την προσχυσιν του αιματος</span></span>). Rather, "the pouring of the blood" (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">προσχυσις</span></span> from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">προσχεω</span></span>, to pour upon), only here in the N.T. (earliest known example). An allusion to the command in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ex+12:7">Exodus 12:7</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ex+12:22">Exodus 12:22</span> but in the LXX <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">προσχεω</span></span> is the usual term for the act (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ex+24:6">Exodus 24:6</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ex+29:16">Exodus 29:16</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="le+1:5">Leviticus 1:5</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="le+1:11">Leviticus 1:11</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="de+16:6">Deuteronomy 16:6</span>).</p>
<p><b>That the destroyer of the first-born should not touch them </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ινα μη ο ολοθρευων τα πρωτοτοκα θιγη αυτων</span></span>). Negative final clause with <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ινα μη</span></span> and the second aorist active subjunctive of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">θιγγανω</span></span>, old verb to touch with genitive, in the N.T. only here, <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="heb+12:20">Hebrews 12:20</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="col+2:21">Colossians 2:21</span>. The articular participle <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ο ολοθρευων</span></span> is from <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ex+11:23">Exodus 11:23</span>. For <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πρωτοτοκα</span></span> see <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+2:7">Luke 2:7</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ex+12:29">Exodus 12:29</span>. </p>
