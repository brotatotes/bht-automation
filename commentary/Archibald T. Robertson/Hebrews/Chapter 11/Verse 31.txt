<p><b> Having received the spies with peace </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δεξαμενη τους κατασκοπους μετ' ειρηνης</span></span>). First aorist middle participle of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δεχομα</span></span>, to welcome (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+10:8">Luke 10:8</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+10:10">Luke 10:10</span>). <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">Κατασκοπος</span></span> is an old compound (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κατασκοπεω</span></span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ga+2:4">Galatians 2:4</span>), used of scout or spy, in LXX, here only in N.T. </p>
