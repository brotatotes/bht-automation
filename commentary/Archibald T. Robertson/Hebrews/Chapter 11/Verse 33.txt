<p><b> Through faith </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δια πιστεως</span></span>). Change thus from the routine <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πιστε</span></span> used so far.</p>
<p><b>Subdued kingdoms </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κατηγωνισαντο βασιλειας</span></span>). First aorist middle indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">καταγωνιζομα</span></span>, <i>Koine</i> verb to struggle against, to overcome, here alone in the N.T. Used by Josephus of David's conquests. The author has here (verses <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="heb+11:33">Hebrews 11:33</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="heb+11:34">Hebrews 11:34</span>), "nine terse clauses" (Moffatt) with no connective (asyndeton) with great rhetorical and oratorical force (sledge-hammer style). For "wrought righteousness" (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ηργασαντο δικαιοσυνην</span></span>, first aorist middle indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εργαζομα</span></span>) see <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+10:35">Acts 10:35</span>.</p>
<p><b>Obtained promises </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επετυχον επαγγελιων</span></span>). Second aorist active indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επιτυγχανω</span></span>, old verb (already in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="heb+6:15">Hebrews 6:15</span>) with genitive. But they did not see the fulfilment of the Messianic promise (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="heb+11:39">Hebrews 11:39</span>).</p>
<p><b>Stopped the mouths of lions </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εφραξαν στοματα λεοντων</span></span>). First aorist active indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">φρασσω</span></span>, old verb to fence in, to block up. See <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="da+6:18-23">Daniel 6:18-23</span>. </p>
