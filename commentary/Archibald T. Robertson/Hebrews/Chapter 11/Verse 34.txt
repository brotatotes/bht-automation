<p><b> Quenched the power of fire </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εσβεσαν δυναμιν πυρος</span></span>). First aorist active indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">σβεννυμ</span></span> (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+12:20">Matthew 12:20</span>). See <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="da+3:19-28">Daniel 3:19-28</span>.</p>
<p><b>Escaped the edge of the sword </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εφυγον στοματα μαχαιρης</span></span>). Second aorist active indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">φευγω</span></span>, old verb to flee. "Mouths (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">στοματα</span></span>) of the sword" (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+21:24">Luke 21:24</span>). See <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1sa+18:11">1 Samuel 18:11</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1ki+19:2">1 Kings 19:2</span>.</p>
<p><b>Were made strong </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εδυναμωθησαν</span></span>). First aorist passive indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δυναμοω</span></span>, late verb from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δυναμις</span></span> as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="col+1:11">Colossians 1:11</span>.</p>
<p><b>Waxed mighty in war </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εγενηθησαν ισχυρο εν πολεμω</span></span>). "Became strong in battle" (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ps+18:34">Psalms 18:34</span>).</p>
<p><b>Armies of aliens </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">παρεμβολας αλλοτριων</span></span>). Late compound (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">παρα, εν, βαλλω</span></span>) for encampment (Polybius, Plutarch), barracks (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+21:34">Acts 21:34</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+21:37">Acts 21:37</span>), armies in battle line (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+20:9">Revelation 20:9</span> and here as in LXX and Polybius). Apparently a reference to the campaigns of Judas Maccabeus. </p>
