<p><b> A more excellent sacrifice </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πλειονα θυσιαν</span></span>). Literally, "more sacrifice" (comparative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πολυς</span></span>, much). For this rather free use of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πλειων</span></span> with the point implied rather than stated see <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+6:25">Matthew 6:25</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+10:31">Luke 10:31</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+12:23">Luke 12:23</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="heb+3:3">Hebrews 3:3</span>.</p>
<p><b>Than Cain </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">παρα Καιν</span></span>). For this use of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">παρα</span></span> after comparative see <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="heb+1:4">Hebrews 1:4</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="heb+1:9">Hebrews 1:9</span>. For the incident see <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ge+4:4">Genesis 4:4</span>.</p>
<p><b>Through which </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δι' ης</span></span>). The sacrifice (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">θυσια</span></span>).</p>
<p><b>He had Witness borne to him </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εμαρτυρηθη</span></span>). First aorist passive indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μαρτυρεω</span></span> as in verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="heb+11:2">Hebrews 11:2</span>, "he was witnessed to."</p>
<p><b>That he was righteous </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εινα δικαιος</span></span>). Infinitive in indirect discourse after <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εμαρτυρηθη</span></span>, personal construction of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δικαιος</span></span> (predicate nominative after <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εινα</span></span>) agreeing with the subject of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εμαρτυρηθη</span></span> (cf. <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+1:22">Romans 1:22</span>, <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εινα σοφο</span></span>).</p>
<p><b>God bearing witness </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μαρτυρουντος του θεου</span></span>). Genitive absolute with present active participle of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μαρτυρεω</span></span>.</p>
<p><b>Through it </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δι' αυτης</span></span>). Through his faith (as shown by his sacrifice). Precisely why Abel's sacrifice was better than that of Cain apart from his faith is not shown.</p>
<p><b>Being dead </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αποθανων</span></span>). Second aorist active participle of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αποθνησκω</span></span>, "having died."</p>
<p><b>Yet speaketh </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ετ λαλε</span></span>). Cf. <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ge+4:10">Genesis 4:10</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="heb+12:24">Hebrews 12:24</span>. Speaks still through his faith. </p>
