<p><b> Impossible </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αδυνατον</span></span>). Strong word as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="heb+6:4">Hebrews 6:4</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="heb+6:18">Hebrews 6:18</span>. See <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+8:8">Romans 8:8</span> for same idea with <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αρεσα</span></span> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αρεσκω</span></span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ga+1:10">Galatians 1:10</span>).</p>
<p><b>Must believe </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πιστευσα δε</span></span>). Moral necessity to have faith (trust, <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πιστευω</span></span>). This is true in business also (banks, for instance).</p>
<p><b>That he is </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">οτ εστιν</span></span>). The very existence of God is a matter of intelligent faith (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+1:19">Romans 1:19</span>) So that men are left without excuse.</p>
<p><b>He is a rewarder </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μισθαποδοτης γινετα</span></span>). Rather, "becomes a rewarder" (present middle indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">γινομα</span></span>, not of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ειμ</span></span>). Only N.T. example of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μισθαποδοτης</span></span>, late and rare double compound (one papyrus example, from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μισθος</span></span> (reward) and <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αποδιδωμ</span></span> (to pay back) like <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μισθαποδοσια</span></span> (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="heb+10:35">Hebrews 10:35</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="heb+11:26">Hebrews 11:26</span>).</p>
<p><b>Seek after </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εκζητουσιν</span></span>). That seek out God. </p>
