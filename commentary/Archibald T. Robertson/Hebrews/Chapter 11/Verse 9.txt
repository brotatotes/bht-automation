<p><b> Became a sojourner </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">παρωικησεν</span></span>). First aorist active indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">παροικεω</span></span>, old verb to dwell (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">οικεω</span></span>) beside (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">παρα</span></span>), common in LXX, in N.T. only here and <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+24:18">Luke 24:18</span>. Called <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">παροικον</span></span> (sojourner) in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+7:6">Acts 7:6</span>.</p>
<p><b>In the land of promise </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εις γην της επαγγελιας</span></span>). Literally, "land of the promise." The promise made by God to him (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ge+12:7">Genesis 12:7</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ge+13:15">Genesis 13:15</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ge+17:8">Genesis 17:8</span>).</p>
<p><b>As in a land not his own </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ως αλλοτριαν</span></span>). For <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αλλοτριος</span></span> (belonging to another) see <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="heb+9:25">Hebrews 9:25</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="heb+11:34">Hebrews 11:34</span>.</p>
<p><b>The heirs with him of the same promise </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">των συνκληρομενων της επαγγελιας της αυτης</span></span>). Late double compound (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">συν, κληροσ, νεμομα</span></span>), found in Philo, inscriptions and papyri, in N.T. only here, <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+8:17">Romans 8:17</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="eph+3:6">Ephesians 3:6</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1pe+3:7">1 Peter 3:7</span>. "Co-heirs" with Abraham. </p>
