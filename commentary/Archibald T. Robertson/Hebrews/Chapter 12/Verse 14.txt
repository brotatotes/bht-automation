<p><b> Follow after peace </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ειρηνην διωκετε</span></span>). Give peace a chase as if in a hunt.</p>
<p><b>With all men </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μετα παντων</span></span>). Like Paul's use of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">διωκω</span></span> with <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ειρηνην</span></span> in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+14:19">Romans 14:19</span> and his to <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εξ υμων</span></span> (so far as proceeds from you) in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="heb+12:18">Hebrews 12:18</span>. This lesson the whole world needs including Christians.</p>
<p><b>Sanctification </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αγιασμον</span></span>). Consecration as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1th+4:7">1 Thessalonians 4:7</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+6:19">Romans 6:19</span>, etc.</p>
<p><b>Without which </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ου χωρις</span></span>). Ablative case of the relative with <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">χωρις</span></span> (post positive here). About seeing God compare <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+5:8">Matthew 5:8</span> where we have <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">καθαρο</span></span>. </p>
