<p><b> Profane </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">βεβηλος</span></span>). Trodden under foot, unhallowed (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1ti+1:9">1 Timothy 1:9</span>).</p>
<p><b>For one mess of meat </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αντ βρωσεως μιας</span></span>). Idea of exchange, "for one act of eating" (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1co+8:4">1 Corinthians 8:4</span>).</p>
<p><b>Sold </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">απεδετο</span></span>). Second aorist middle indicative from <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ge+25:31">Genesis 25:31</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ge+25:33">Genesis 25:33</span>, and with irregular form for <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">απεδοτο</span></span> (regular <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μ</span></span> form).</p>
<p><b>His own birthright </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τα πρωτοτοκια εαυτου</span></span>). From Genesis also and in Philo, only here in N.T. From <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πρωτοτοκος</span></span> (first born, <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="heb+1:6">Hebrews 1:6</span>). </p>
