 Profane  (βεβηλος). Trodden under foot, unhallowed (1 Timothy 1:9).For one mess of meat  (αντ βρωσεως μιας). Idea of exchange, "for one act of eating" (1 Corinthians 8:4).Sold  (απεδετο). Second aorist middle indicative from Genesis 25:31; Genesis 25:33, and with irregular form for απεδοτο (regular μ form).His own birthright  (τα πρωτοτοκια εαυτου). From Genesis also and in Philo, only here in N.T. From πρωτοτοκος (first born, Hebrews 1:6).
