<p><b> For they could not endure </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ουκ εφερον γαρ</span></span>). Imperfect active of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">φερω</span></span>, "for they were not enduring (bearing)."</p>
<p><b>That which was enjoined </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">το διαστελλομενον</span></span>). Present passive articular participle of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">διαστελλω</span></span>, old verb to distinguish, to dispose, to order. The quotation is from <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ex+19:12">Exodus 19:12</span>. The people appealed to Moses (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ex+20:19">Exodus 20:19</span>) and the leaders did so also (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="de+5:23">Deuteronomy 5:23</span>), both in terror.</p>
<p><b>If even </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">καν</span></span>). "Even if." Condition of third class with second aorist active subjunctive of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">θιγγανω</span></span> as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="heb+11:28">Hebrews 11:28</span>, followed by genitive <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ορους</span></span> (mountain).</p>
<p><b>It shall be stoned </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">λιθοβοληθησετα</span></span>). From <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ex+19:13">Exodus 19:13</span>. Late compound verb from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">λιθοβολος</span></span> (from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">λιθοσ, βαλλω</span></span>) as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+21:35">Matthew 21:35</span>. </p>
