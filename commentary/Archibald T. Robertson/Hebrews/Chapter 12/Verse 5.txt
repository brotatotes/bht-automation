<p><b> Ye have forgotten </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εκλελησθε</span></span>). Perfect middle indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εκλανθανω</span></span>, to cause to forget, old verb, here only in the N.T. with genitive case as usual.</p>
<p><b>Reasoneth with you </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">υμιν διαλεγετα</span></span>). Present middle indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">διαλεγομα</span></span>, old verb to ponder different (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δια-</span></span>) things, to converse, with dative. Cf. <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+19:8">Acts 19:8</span>. The quotation is from <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="pr+3:11">Proverbs 3:11</span>.</p>
<p><b>Regard not lightly </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μη ολιγωρε</span></span>). Prohibition with <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μη</span></span> and the present active imperative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ολιγωρεω</span></span>, old verb from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ολιγωρος</span></span> and this from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ολιγος</span></span> (little) and <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ωρα</span></span> (hour), old verb, here only in N.T.</p>
<p><b>Chastening </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">παιδειας</span></span>). Old word from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">παιδευω</span></span>, to train a child (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">παις</span></span>), instruction (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2ti+3:16">2 Timothy 3:16</span>), which naturally includes correction and punishment as here. See also <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="eph+6:4">Ephesians 6:4</span>.</p>
<p><b>Nor faint </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μηδε εκλυου</span></span>). Prohibition with <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μη</span></span> and present passive imperative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εκλυω</span></span> (see verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="heb+12:3">Hebrews 12:3</span>). </p>
