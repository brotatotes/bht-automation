<p><b> If ye are without chastening </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ε χωρις εστε παιδειας</span></span>). Condition of first class, determined as fulfilled. Note position of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εστε</span></span> (are) between the preposition <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">χωρις</span></span> and <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">παιδειας</span></span> (ablative case).</p>
<p><b>Have been made </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">γεγονασιν</span></span>). Perfect active indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">γινομα</span></span>.</p>
<p><b>Partakers </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μετοχο</span></span>). Partners (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="heb+3:14">Hebrews 3:14</span>).</p>
<p><b>Then </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αρα</span></span>). Accordingly, correspondingly.</p>
<p><b>Bastards </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">νοθο</span></span>). Old word, here only in N.T. Illegitimate. </p>
