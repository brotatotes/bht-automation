 If ye are without chastening  (ε χωρις εστε παιδειας). Condition of first class, determined as fulfilled. Note position of εστε (are) between the preposition χωρις and παιδειας (ablative case).Have been made  (γεγονασιν). Perfect active indicative of γινομα.Partakers  (μετοχο). Partners (Hebrews 3:14).Then  (αρα). Accordingly, correspondingly.Bastards  (νοθο). Old word, here only in N.T. Illegitimate.
