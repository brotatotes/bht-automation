<p><b> We have an altar </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εχομεν θυσιαστηριον</span></span>). We Christians have a spiritual altar (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">θυσιαστηριον</span></span>), not a literal one (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="heb+7:13">Hebrews 7:13</span>). This metaphor is carried out.</p>
<p><b>Whereof </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εξ ου</span></span>). Our spiritual altar.</p>
<p><b>The tabernacle </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τη σκηνη</span></span>). Dative case with <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">λατρευοντες</span></span> (serve), <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">σκηνη</span></span> being used for "the whole ceremonial economy" (Vincent) of Judaism. </p>
