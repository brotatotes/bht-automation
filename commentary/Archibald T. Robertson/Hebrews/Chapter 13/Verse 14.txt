<p><b> An abiding city </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μενουσαν πολιν</span></span>). Jerusalem has lost its charm for followers of Christ. Vincent rightly argues that the Epistle must have been written before the destruction of Jerusalem else a reference to that event could hardly have been avoided here. We are now where Abraham was once (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="heb+11:10">Hebrews 11:10</span>). </p>
