<p><b> As bound with them </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ως συνδεδεμενο</span></span>). Perfect passive participle of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">συνδεω</span></span>, old verb, here only in N.T. For sympathy with prisoners see <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="heb+10:34">Hebrews 10:34</span>.</p>
<p><b>As being yourselves also in the body </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ως κα αυτο οντες εν σωματ</span></span>). And so subject to evil treatment. See <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="heb+11:37">Hebrews 11:37</span> for <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κακουχεω</span></span> and <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="heb+11:25">Hebrews 11:25</span> for <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">συνκακουχεω</span></span>. </p>
