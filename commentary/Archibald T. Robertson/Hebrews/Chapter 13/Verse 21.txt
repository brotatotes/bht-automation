 Make you perfect  (καταρτισα). First aorist active optative of καταρτιζω, to equip, as in Hebrews 10:5. A wish for the future. See 1 Corinthians 1:10; 2 Corinthians 13:11; 2 Timothy 3:17.Working in us  (ποιων εν εμιν). "Doing in us." Some MSS. read "in you."Well-pleasing  (ευαρεστον). Compound adjective (ευ, αρεστος). Usually with the dative (Romans 12:2), here with ενωπιον αυτου more like the Hebrew. This is one of the noblest doxologies in the N.T.
