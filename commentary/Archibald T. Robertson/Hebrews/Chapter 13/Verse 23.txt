<p><b> Hath been set at liberty </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">απολελυμενον</span></span>). Perfect passive participle of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">απολυω</span></span>, to set free, in indirect discourse after <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">γινωσκετε</span></span>. Possibly from prison if he came to Rome at Paul's request (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2ti+4:11">2 Timothy 4:11</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2ti+4:21">2 Timothy 4:21</span>).</p>
<p><b>Shortly </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ταχειον</span></span>). Same comparative as in verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="heb+13:19">Hebrews 13:19</span>, "sooner" than I expect (?). </p>
