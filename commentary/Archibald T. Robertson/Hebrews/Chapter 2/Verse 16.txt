<p><b> Verily </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δε που</span></span>). "Now in some way," only here in N.T.</p>
<p><b>Doth he take hold </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επιλαμβανετα</span></span>). Present middle indicative and means to lay hold of, to help, like <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">βοηθησα</span></span> in verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="heb+2:18">Hebrews 2:18</span>.</p>
<p><b>The seed of Abraham </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">σπερματος Αβρααμ</span></span>). The spiritual Israel (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ga+3:29">Galatians 3:29</span>), children of faith (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+9:7">Romans 9:7</span>). </p>
