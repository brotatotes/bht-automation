 With them that sinned  (τοις αμαρτησασιν). Dative masculine plural after προσωχθισεν (cf. verse Hebrews 3:10) of the articular first aorist active participle of αμαρτανω (αμαρτησας, not αμαρτων).Carcases  (κωλα). Old word for members of the body like the feet, in LXX a dead body (Numbers 14:29), here only in N.T.
