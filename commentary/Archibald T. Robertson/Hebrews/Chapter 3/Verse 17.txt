<p><b> With them that sinned </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τοις αμαρτησασιν</span></span>). Dative masculine plural after <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">προσωχθισεν</span></span> (cf. verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="heb+3:10">Hebrews 3:10</span>) of the articular first aorist active participle of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αμαρτανω</span></span> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αμαρτησας</span></span>, not <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αμαρτων</span></span>).</p>
<p><b>Carcases </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κωλα</span></span>). Old word for members of the body like the feet, in LXX a dead body (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="nu+14:29">Numbers 14:29</span>), here only in N.T. </p>
