<p><b> That they should not enter </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μη εισελευσεσθα</span></span>). Negative <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μη</span></span> (cf. <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ε</span></span> in verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="heb+3:11">Hebrews 3:11</span>) and the future middle infinitive in indirect discourse.</p>
<p><b>To them that were disobedient </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τοις απειθησασιν</span></span>).
