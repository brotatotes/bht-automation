<p><b> Hath been counted worthy of more glory than Moses </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πλειονος δοξης παρα Μωυσην ηξιωτα</span></span>). Perfect passive indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αξιοω</span></span>, to deem worthy, permanent situation described with definite claim of Christ's superiority to Moses. <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">Δοξης</span></span> in genitive case after <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ηξιωτα</span></span>. For <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">παρα</span></span> after the comparative <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πλειονος</span></span> see <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="heb+1:4">Hebrews 1:4</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="heb+1:9">Hebrews 1:9</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="heb+2:7">Hebrews 2:7</span>.</p>
<p><b>By so much as </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">καθ' οσον</span></span>). A proportionate measurement (common use of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κατα</span></span> and the quantitative relative <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">οσος</span></span>).</p>
<p><b>Than the house </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">του οικου</span></span>). Ablative case of comparison after <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πλειονα</span></span>. The architect is superior to the house just as Sir Christopher Wren is superior to St. Paul's Cathedral. The point in the argument calls for Jesus as the builder (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ο κατασκευασας</span></span>, first aorist active participle of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κατασκευαζω</span></span>, to found or build). But it is God's house as <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αυτου</span></span> means (verses <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="heb+3:2">Hebrews 3:2</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="heb+3:5">Hebrews 3:5</span>) and <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ου</span></span> in verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="heb+3:6">Hebrews 3:6</span>. This house of God existed before Moses (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="heb+11:2">Hebrews 11:2</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="heb+11:25">Hebrews 11:25</span>). Jesus as God's Son founded and supervised this house of God. </p>
