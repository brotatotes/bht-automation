<p><b> Harden not </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μη σκληρυνητε</span></span>). Prohibition with <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μη</span></span> and first aorist (ingressive) active subjunctive of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">σκληρυνω</span></span>, late verb from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">σκληρος</span></span> (dried up, stiff, hard) as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+19:9">Acts 19:9</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+9:18">Romans 9:18</span>.</p>
<p><b>As in the provocation </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ως ην τω παραπικρασμω</span></span>). Late compound from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">παραπικραινω</span></span>, late verb to embitter (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">παρα, πικρος</span></span>), found only in LXX and here and verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="heb+3:15">Hebrews 3:15</span>. It means embitterment, exasperation. For the simple verb <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πικραινω</span></span>, to make bitter, see <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="col+3:19">Colossians 3:19</span>. The reference is to <i>Meribah</i> (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="heb+17:1-7">Hebrews 17:1-7</span>).</p>
<p><b>Like as in the day </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κατα την ημεραν</span></span>). "According to the day" as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+12:1">Acts 12:1</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+19:23">Acts 19:23</span>.</p>
<p><b>Of the temptation </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">του πειρασμου</span></span>). The reference is to <i>Massah</i> which took place at Rephidim. </p>
