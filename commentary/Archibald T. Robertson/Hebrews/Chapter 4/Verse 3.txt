<p><b> Do enter </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εισερχομεθα</span></span>). Emphatic futuristic present middle indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εισερχομα</span></span>. We are sure to enter in, we who believe.</p>
<p><b>He hath said </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ειρηκεν</span></span>). Perfect active indicative for the permanent value of God's word as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="heb+1:13">Hebrews 1:13</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="heb+4:4">Hebrews 4:4</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="heb+10:9">Hebrews 10:9</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="heb+10:13">Hebrews 10:13</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="heb+13:5">Hebrews 13:5</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+13:34">Acts 13:34</span>. God has spoken. That is enough for us. So he quotes again what he has in verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="heb+4:11">Hebrews 4:11</span> from <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="heb+4:95">Hebrews 4:95</span>.</p>
<p><b>Although the works were finished </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">καιτο των εργων γενηθεντων</span></span>). Genitive absolute with concessive use of the participle. Old particle, in N.T. only here and <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+14:17">Acts 14:17</span> (with verb).</p>
<p><b>From the foundation of the world </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">απο καταβολης κοσμου</span></span>). <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">Καταβολη</span></span>, late word from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">καταβαλλω</span></span>, usually laying the foundation of a house in the literal sense. In the N.T. usually with <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">απο</span></span> (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+25:44">Matthew 25:44</span>) or <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">προ</span></span> (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+17:24">John 17:24</span>) about the foundation of the world. </p>
