<p><b> He again defineth a certain day </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">παλιν τινα οριζε ημεραν</span></span>). Present active indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">οριζω</span></span>, old verb to set a limit (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ορος</span></span>, horizon) as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+17:26">Acts 17:26</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+1:4">Romans 1:4</span>.</p>
<p><b>In David </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εν Δαυειδ</span></span>). Attributing the Psalm to David or in the Psalter at any rate.</p>
<p><b>Hath been before said </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">προειρητα</span></span>). Perfect passive indicative referring to the quotation in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="heb+3:7">Hebrews 3:7</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="heb+3:15">Hebrews 3:15</span>.</p>
<p><b>After so long a time </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μετα τοσουτον χρονον</span></span>). The time between Joshua and David. </p>
