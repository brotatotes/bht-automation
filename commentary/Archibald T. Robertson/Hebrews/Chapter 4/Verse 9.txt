<p><b> A sabbath rest </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">σαββατισμος</span></span>). Late word from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">σαββατιζω</span></span> (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ex+16:30">Exodus 16:30</span>) to keep the Sabbath, apparently coined by the author (a doubtful passage in Plutarch). Here it is parallel with <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">καταπαυσις</span></span> (cf. <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+14:13">Revelation 14:13</span>).</p>
<p><b>For the people of God </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τω λαω του θεου</span></span>). Dative case of blessed personal interest to the true Israel (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ga+6:16">Galatians 6:16</span>). </p>
