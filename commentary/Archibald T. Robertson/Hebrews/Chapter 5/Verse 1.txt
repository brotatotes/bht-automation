<p><b> In things pertaining to God </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τα προς τον θεον</span></span>). Accusative of general reference as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="heb+2:17">Hebrews 2:17</span> (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+15:17">Romans 15:17</span>). The two essential points about any high priest are human sympathy (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="heb+5:1-3">Hebrews 5:1-3</span>) and divine appointment (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="heb+5:4">Hebrews 5:4</span>). He is taken from men and appointed in behalf of men.</p>
<p><b>That he may offer </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ινα προσφερη</span></span>). Purpose clause with <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ινα</span></span> and present active subjunctive of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">προσφερω</span></span>, "that he keep on offering (from time to time)."</p>
<p><b>Both gifts </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δωρα</span></span>)</p>
<p><b>and sacrifices </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κα θυσιας</span></span>). General term (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δωρα</span></span>) and bloody offerings, but the two together are inclusive of all as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="heb+8:3">Hebrews 8:3</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="heb+9:9">Hebrews 9:9</span> (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1ki+8:64">1 Kings 8:64</span>).</p>
<p><b>For sins </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">υπερ αμαρτιων</span></span>). His own included (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="heb+7:27">Hebrews 7:27</span>) except in the case of Jesus. </p>
