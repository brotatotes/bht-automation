<p><b> Without experience </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">απειρος</span></span>). Old adjective (alpha privative and <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πειρα</span></span>, trial). Inexperienced. The babe (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">νηπιος</span></span>, old word, negative <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">νη</span></span> and <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επος</span></span>, word like Latin <i>infans</i>, infant, not able to talk), not able to chew if one uses only milk and is without teeth. Perhaps moral truth is meant by "word of righteousness" (cf. <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="heb+1:2">Hebrews 1:2</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="heb+2:3">Hebrews 2:3</span> for the word spoken by Christ). </p>
