<p><b> For himself </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">περ εαυτου</span></span>). Note <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">περ</span></span> three times here (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">περ του λαου, περ εαυτου, περ αμαρτιων</span></span>), but in verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="heb+5:1">Hebrews 5:1</span> <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">υπερ ανθρωπων, υπερ αμαρτιων</span></span>. In the <i>Koine</i> this interchange of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">περ</span></span> (around) and <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">υπερ</span></span> (over) is common (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+26:28">Matthew 26:28</span>). </p>
