<p><b> Made promise </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επαγγειλαμενος</span></span>). First aorist middle participle of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επαγγελλω</span></span>. Could swear by none greater (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κατ' ουδενος ειχεν μειζονος ομοσα</span></span>). Imperfect active of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εχω</span></span> in sense of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εδυνατο</span></span> as often with <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ομοσα</span></span> (first aorist active infinitive of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ομνυω</span></span>) and <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ωμοσεν</span></span> (he sware) is first aorist active indicative. </p>
