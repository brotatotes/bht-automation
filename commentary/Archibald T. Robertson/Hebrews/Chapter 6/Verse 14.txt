<p><b> Surely </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ε μην</span></span>). By itacism for <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">η μην</span></span> (Deissmann, <i>Bible Studies</i>, p. 205). The quotation is from <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ge+22:16">Genesis 22:16</span>. (the promise renewed to Abraham with an oath after offering of Isaac).</p>
<p><b>Blessing </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ευλογων</span></span>). Hebraism (present active participle) for the Hebrew infinitive absolute and so with <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πληθυνων</span></span> (multiplying). </p>
