<p><b> To shew </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επιδειξα</span></span>). First aorist active infinitive of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επιδεικνυμ</span></span>, to show in addition (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επι-</span></span>) to his promise "more abundantly" (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">περισσοτερον</span></span>).</p>
<p><b>The immutability of his counsel </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">το αμεταθετον της βουλης αυτου</span></span>). Late compound verbal neuter singular (alpha privative and <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μετατιθημ</span></span>, to change), "the unchangeableness of his will."</p>
<p><b>Interposed </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εμεσιτευσεν</span></span>). First aorist active indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μεσιτευω</span></span>, late verb from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μεσιτης</span></span>, mediator (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="heb+8:6">Hebrews 8:6</span>), to act as mediator or sponsor or surety, intransitively to pledge one's self as surety, here only in the N.T.</p>
<p><b>With an oath </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ορκω</span></span>). Instrumental case of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ορκος</span></span> (from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ερκος</span></span>, an enclosure), <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+14:7">Matthew 14:7</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+14:9">Matthew 14:9</span>. </p>
