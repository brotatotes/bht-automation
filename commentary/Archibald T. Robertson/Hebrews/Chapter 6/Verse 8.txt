 If it beareth  (εκφερουσα). Present active participle of εκφερω, conditional participle. For "thorns and thistles" see Matthew 7:16 for both words (ακανθας κα τριβολους). Roman soldiers scattered balls with sharp iron spikes, one of which was called tribulus, to hinder the enemy's cavalry.Rejected  (αδοκιμος). See 1 Corinthians 9:27; Romans 1:28. For καταρας εγγυς (nigh unto a curse) see Galatians 3:10.To be burned  (εις καυσιν). "For burning." Common sight in clearing up ground.
