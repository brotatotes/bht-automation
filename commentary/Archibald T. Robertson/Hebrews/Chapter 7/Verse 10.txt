<p><b> In the loins of his father </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εν τη οσφυ του πατρος</span></span>). Levi was not yet born. The reference is to Abraham, the forefather (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πατρος</span></span>) of Levi. This is a rabbinical imaginative refinement appealing to Jews. </p>
