<p><b> Perfection </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τελειωσις</span></span>). Abstract substantive of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τελειοω</span></span>. More the act than the quality or state (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τελειοτης</span></span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="heb+6:1">Hebrews 6:1</span>). The condition is of the second class, "if there were perfection, etc." The Levitical priesthood failed to give men "a perfectly adequate relation to God" (Moffatt).</p>
<p><b>Priesthood </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ιεροσυνης</span></span>). Old word, in N.T. only here, verses <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="heb+7:12">Hebrews 7:12</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="heb+7:24">Hebrews 7:24</span>. Cf. <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ιερετια</span></span> in verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="heb+7:5">Hebrews 7:5</span>. The adjective <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">Λευειτικη</span></span> occurs in Philo.</p>
<p><b>Received the law </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">νενομοθετητα</span></span>). Perfect passive indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">νομοθετεω</span></span>, old compound to enact law (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">νομοσ, τιθημ</span></span>), to furnish with law (as here), only other N.T. example in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="heb+8:6">Hebrews 8:6</span>.</p>
<p><b>What further need was there? </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τις ετ χρεια;</span></span>). No copula expressed, but it would normally be <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ην αν</span></span>, not just <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ην</span></span>: "What need still would there be?"</p>
<p><b>Another priest </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ετερον ιερεα</span></span>). Of a different line (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ετερον</span></span>), not just one more (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αλλον</span></span>). Accusative of general reference with the infinitive <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ανιστασθα</span></span> (present middle of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ανιστημ</span></span> intransitive).</p>
<p><b>And not to be reckoned </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κα ου λεγεσθα</span></span>). The negative <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ου</span></span> belongs rather to the descriptive clause than just to the infinitive. </p>
