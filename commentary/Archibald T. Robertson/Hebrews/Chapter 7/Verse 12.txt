<p><b> The priesthood being changed </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μετατιθεμενης της ιεροσυνης</span></span>). Genitive absolute with present passive participle of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μετατιθημ</span></span>, old word to transfer (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ga+1:6">Galatians 1:6</span>).</p>
<p><b>A change </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μεταθεσις</span></span>). Old substantive from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μετατιθημ</span></span>. In N.T. only in Heb. (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="heb+7:12">Hebrews 7:12</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="heb+11:5">Hebrews 11:5</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="heb+12:27">Hebrews 12:27</span>). God's choice of another kind of priesthood for his Son, left the Levitical line off to one side, forever discounted, passed by "the order of Aaron" (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">την ταξιν Ααρων</span></span>). </p>
