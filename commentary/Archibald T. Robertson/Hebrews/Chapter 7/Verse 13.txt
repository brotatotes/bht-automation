<p><b> Belongeth to another tribe </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">φυλης ετερας μετεσχηκεν</span></span>). <p><b>Hath given attendance at </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">προσεσχηκεν</span></span>). Perfect active indicative (watch perfects in Hebrews, not "for" aorists) of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">προσεχω</span></span>, old verb, here with either <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">νουν</span></span> (mind) or self (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εαυτον</span></span>) understood with dative case (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τω θυσιαστηριω</span></span>, the altar, for which word see <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+5:23">Matthew 5:23</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+1:11">Luke 1:11</span>). </p>
