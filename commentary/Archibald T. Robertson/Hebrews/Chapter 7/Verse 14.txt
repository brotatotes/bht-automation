<p><b> It is evident </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">προδηλον</span></span>). Old compound adjective (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">προ, δηλος</span></span>), openly manifest to all, in N.T. only here and <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1ti+5:24">1 Timothy 5:24</span>.</p>
<p><b>Hath sprung </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ανατεταλκεν</span></span>). Perfect active indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ανατελλω</span></span>, old compound to rise up like the sun (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+5:45">Matthew 5:45</span>). </p>
