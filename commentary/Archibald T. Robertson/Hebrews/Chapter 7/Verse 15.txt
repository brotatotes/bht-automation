 Yet more abundantly evident  (περισσοτερον ετ καταδηλον). Only N.T. instance of the old compound adjective καταδηλος thoroughly clear with ετ (still) added and the comparative περισσοτερον (more abundantly) piling Ossa on Pelion like Philippians 1:23.Likeness  (ομοιοτητα). See Hebrews 4:15, only N.T. examples. Cf. the verb in verse Hebrews 7:3.Ariseth another priest  (ανιστατα ιερευς ετερος). As said in verse Hebrews 7:11, now assumed in condition of first class.
