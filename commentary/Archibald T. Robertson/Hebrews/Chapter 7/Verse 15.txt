<p><b> Yet more abundantly evident </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">περισσοτερον ετ καταδηλον</span></span>). <p><b>Likeness </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ομοιοτητα</span></span>). See <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="heb+4:15">Hebrews 4:15</span>, only N.T. examples. Cf. the verb in verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="heb+7:3">Hebrews 7:3</span>.</p>
<p><b>Ariseth another priest </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ανιστατα ιερευς ετερος</span></span>). As said in verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="heb+7:11">Hebrews 7:11</span>, now assumed in condition of first class. </p>
