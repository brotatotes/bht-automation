<p><b> Carnal </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">σαρκινης</span></span>). "Fleshen" as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1co+3:1">1 Corinthians 3:1</span>, not <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">σαρκικης</span></span> (fleshlike, <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1co+3:3">1 Corinthians 3:3</span>). The Levitical priests became so merely by birth.</p>
<p><b>Of an endless life </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ζωης ακαταλυτου</span></span>). Late compound (alpha privative and verbal adjective from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">καταλυω</span></span>, to dissolve, as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2co+4:1">2 Corinthians 4:1</span>), indissoluble. Jesus as priest lives on forever. He is Life. </p>
