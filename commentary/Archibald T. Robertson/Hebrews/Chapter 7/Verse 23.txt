<p><b> Many in number </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πλειονες</span></span>). Comparative predicate adjective, "more than one," in succession, not simultaneously.</p>
<p><b>Because they are hindered </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δια το κωλυεσθα</span></span>). Articular infinitive (present passive) with <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δια</span></span> and the accusative case, "because of the being hindered."</p>
<p><b>By death </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">θανατω</span></span>). Instrumental case.</p>
<p><b>From continuing </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">παραμενειν</span></span>). Present active infinitive of the compound (remain beside) as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="php+1:25">Philippians 1:25</span> and in the ablative case. </p>
