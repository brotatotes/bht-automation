 First  (προτερον). Regular adverb for comparison between two, though πρωτον often occurs also (John 1:41), with επειτα (then) following.For the sins  (τον). Only the article in the Greek with repetition of υπερ or of αμαρτιων.When he offered up himself  (εαυτον ανενεγκας). First aorist active participle of αναφερω, to offer up. See same idea in Hebrews 9:14 where εαυτον προσηνεγκεν is used. Old verb for sacrifice to place on the altar (1 Peter 2:5; 1 Peter 2:24).
