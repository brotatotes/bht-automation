<p><b> How great </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πηλικος</span></span>). Geometrical magnitude in contrast to arithmetical (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ποσος</span></span>), here only in N.T., "how distinguished." He received tithes from Abraham (verses <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="heb+7:4-6">Hebrews 7:4-6</span>) and he blessed Abraham (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="heb+7:6">Hebrews 7:6</span>) and even Levi is included (verses <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="heb+7:8-10">Hebrews 7:8-10</span>).</p>
<p><b>Out of the chief spoils </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εκ των ακροθινιων</span></span>). Old word from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ακρος</span></span>, top, and <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">θις</span></span>, a heap (the top of the pile).</p>
<p><b>Patriarch </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πατριαρχης</span></span>). LXX word (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πατρια</span></span>, tribe, <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αρχω</span></span>, to rule) transferred to N.T. (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+2:29">Acts 2:29</span>). </p>
