<p><b> The priest's office </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">την ιερατειαν</span></span>). LXX and <i>Koine</i> word from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ιερευς</span></span>, in N.T. only here and <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+1:9">Luke 1:9</span>.</p>
<p><b>To take tithes </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αποδεκατοιν</span></span>). Present active infinitive (in <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">-οιν</span></span>, not <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">-ουν</span></span>, as the best MSS. give it) of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αποδεκατοω</span></span> a LXX word (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">απο, δεκατοω</span></span>), to take a tenth from (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">απο</span></span>).</p>
<p><b>Brethren </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αδελφους</span></span>). Accusative case in apposition with <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">λαον</span></span> (people) unaffected by the explanatory phrase <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τουτ' εστιν</span></span> (that is).</p>
<p><b>Though come out </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">καιπερ εξεληλυθοτας</span></span>). Concessive participle (cf. <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="heb+5:8">Hebrews 5:8</span>) with <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">καιπερ</span></span> (perfect active of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εξερχομα</span></span>). </p>
