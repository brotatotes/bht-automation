<p><b> Dispute </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αντιλογιας</span></span>). Ablative case with <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">χωρις</span></span>. For the word see <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="heb+6:16">Hebrews 6:16</span>. The writer makes a parenthetical generalization and uses the article and neuter adjective (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">το ελασσον</span></span>, the less, <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">υπο του κρειττονος</span></span>, by the better), a regular Greek idiom. </p>
