<p><b> So to say </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ως επος ειπειν</span></span>). An old idiom, here only in the N.T., common in Philo, used to limit a startling statement, an infinitive for conceived result with <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ως</span></span>.</p>
<p><b>Hath paid tithes </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δεδεκατωτα</span></span>). Perfect passive indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δεκατοω</span></span>, "has been tithed." This could only be true of Levi "so to speak." </p>
