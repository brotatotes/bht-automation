<p><b> Merciful </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ιλεως</span></span>). Old Attic adjective for <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ιλαος</span></span>, common in the LXX, only here in N.T., from which <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ιλασκομα</span></span> comes (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+18:13">Luke 18:13</span>).</p>
<p><b>Will I remember no more </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ου μη μνησθω ετ</span></span>). Double negative <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ου μη</span></span> with first aorist passive subjunctive (volitive) of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μιμνησκω</span></span>, to recall. </p>
