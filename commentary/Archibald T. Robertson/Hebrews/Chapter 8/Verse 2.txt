<p><b> Minister </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">λειτουργος</span></span>). See on <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+13:6">Romans 13:6</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="php+2:25">Philippians 2:25</span>.</p>
<p><b>Of the sanctuary </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">των αγιων</span></span>). "Of the holy places" (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τα αγια</span></span>), without any distinction (like <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="heb+9:8">Hebrews 9:8</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="heb+10:19">Hebrews 10:19</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="heb+13:11">Hebrews 13:11</span>) between the holy place and the most holy place as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="heb+9:2">Hebrews 9:2</span>.</p>
<p><b>Of the true tabernacle </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">της σκηνης της αληθινης</span></span>). By way of explanation of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">των αγιων</span></span>. For <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">σκηνη</span></span> see <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+17:4">Matthew 17:4</span> and <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">σκηνος</span></span> (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2co+5:1">2 Corinthians 5:1</span>), old word used here for the antitype or archetype of the tabernacle in the wilderness in which Aaron served, the ideal tabernacle in heaven of which the earthly tabernacle was a symbol and reproduced in the temple which merely copied the tabernacle. Hence it is the "genuine" tabernacle and see <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+1:9">John 1:9</span> for <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αληθινος</span></span>.</p>
<p><b>Pitched </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επηξεν</span></span>). First aorist active indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πηγνυμ</span></span>, old verb to fasten as the pegs of a tent, here only in the N.T. Cf. <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="nu+24:6">Numbers 24:6</span>. </p>
