<p><b> A tabernacle the first </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">σκηνη η πρωτη</span></span>). See <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="heb+8:2">Hebrews 8:2</span> for <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">σκηνη</span></span>. Large tents usually had two divisions (the outer and the inner or the first and the second). Note <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πρωτη</span></span> for the first of two as with the first covenant (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="heb+8:7">Hebrews 8:7</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="heb+8:13">Hebrews 8:13</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="heb+9:1">Hebrews 9:1</span>). The large outer tent was entered first and was called <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">Hαγια</span></span> (Holy), the first division of the tabernacle. The two divisions are here termed two tabernacles.</p>
<p><b>Was prepared </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κατεσκευασθη</span></span>). First aorist passive of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κατασκευαζω</span></span>. See <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="heb+3:3">Hebrews 3:3</span>. For the furniture see <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="heb+9:25">Hebrews 9:25</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="heb+9:26">Hebrews 9:26</span>. Three items are named here: the candlestick (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">η λυχνια</span></span>, late word for <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">λυχνιον</span></span>) or lampstand, necessary since there were no windows (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ex+25:31-39">Exodus 25:31-39</span>); the table (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">η τραπεζα</span></span>, old word, <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+15:27">Matthew 15:27</span>) for the bread (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ex+25:23-30">Exodus 25:23-30</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="le+24:6">Leviticus 24:6</span> of pure gold); the shewbread (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">η προθεσις των αρτων</span></span>) as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ex+25:30">Exodus 25:30</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ex+40:23">Exodus 40:23</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="le+24:5-9">Leviticus 24:5-9</span>. Probably a hendiadys for the table with the loaves of God's Presence. </p>
