<p><b> In like manner with the blood </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τω αιματ ομοιως</span></span>). Instrumental case of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αιμα</span></span> (blood). But the use of the article does not necessarily refer to the blood mentioned in verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="heb+9:19">Hebrews 9:19</span>. In <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ex+40:9">Exodus 40:9</span> Moses sprinkled the tabernacle with oil. It had not been erected at the time of <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ex+24:5">Exodus 24:5</span>. Josephus (<i>Ant</i>. III. 8, 6) gives a tradition that blood was used also at this dedication. Blood was used annually in the cleansing rites on the day of atonement. </p>
