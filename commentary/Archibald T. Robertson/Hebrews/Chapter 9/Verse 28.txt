<p><b> Once </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">απαξ</span></span>). "Once for all" (verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="heb+9:26">Hebrews 9:26</span>) as already stated.</p>
<p><b>Shall appear a second time </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εκ δευτερου οφθησετα</span></span>). Future passive indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">οραω</span></span>. Blessed assurance of the Second Coming of Christ, but this time "apart from sin" (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">χωρις αμαρτιας</span></span>, no notion of a second chance then).</p>
<p><b>Unto salvation </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εις σωτηριαν</span></span>). Final and complete salvation for "them that wait for him" (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τοις αυτον απεκδεχομενοις</span></span>). Dative plural of the articular participle present middle of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">απεκδεχομα</span></span>, the very verb used by Paul in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="php+3:20">Philippians 3:20</span> of waiting for the coming of Christ as Saviour. </p>
