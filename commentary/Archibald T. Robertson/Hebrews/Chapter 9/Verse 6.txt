 These things having been thus prepared  (τουτων ουτως κατεσκευασμενων). Genitive absolute with the perfect passive participle of κατασκευαζω for which verb see verse Hebrews 9:2. A mere summary has been made of the furniture.Go in  (εισιασιν). Present active indicative of εισειμ, to go in, old verb, in N.T. only here, Acts 3:3; Acts 21:18; Acts 21:26.Accomplishing  (επιτελουντες). Present active participle of επιτελεω for which see Hebrews 8:5.
