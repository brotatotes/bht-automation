<p><b> Be not deceived </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μη πλανασθε</span></span>). Prohibition with <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μη</span></span> and the present passive imperative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πλαναω</span></span>, common verb to lead astray. This is the way of sin to deceive and to kill (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+7:7-14">Romans 7:7-14</span>). The devil is a pastmaster at blinding men's eyes about sin (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2co+4:4">2 Corinthians 4:4</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+1:27">Romans 1:27</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="eph+4:14">Ephesians 4:14</span>; etc.). </p>
