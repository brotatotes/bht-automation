<p><b> Confusion </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ακαταστασια</span></span>). Late word (from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ακαταστατος</span></span>), <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="jas+1:8">James 1:8</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="jas+3:8">James 3:8</span>), a state of disorder (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1co+14:33">1 Corinthians 14:33</span>).</p>
<p><b>Vile </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">φαυλον</span></span>). Kin to German <i>faul</i>, first slight, ordinary, then bad. The steps are cheap, paltry, evil. Opposed to <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αγαθα</span></span> (good) in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+5:39">John 5:39</span>. </p>
