<p><b> One only </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εις</span></span>). No "only" in the Greek, but <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εις</span></span> here excludes all others but God.</p>
<p><b>The lawgiver </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ο νομοθετης</span></span>). Old compound (from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">νομοσ, τιθημ</span></span>), only here in N.T. In <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ps+9:20">Psalms 9:20</span>. Cf. <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">νομοθετεω</span></span> in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="heb+7:11">Hebrews 7:11</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="heb+8:6">Hebrews 8:6</span>.</p>
<p><b>To save </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">σωσα</span></span>, first aorist active infinitive of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">σωζω</span></span>)</p>
<p><b>and to destroy </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κα απολεσα</span></span>, first aorist active infinitive of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">απολλυμ</span></span> to destroy). Cf. the picture of God's power in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+10:28">Matthew 10:28</span>, a common idea in the O.T. (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="de+32:39">Deuteronomy 32:39</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1sa+2:16">1 Samuel 2:16</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2ki+5:7">2 Kings 5:7</span>).</p>
<p><b>But who art thou? </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">συ δε τις ει;</span></span>). Proleptic and emphatic position of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">συ</span></span> (thou) in this rhetorical question as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+9:20">Romans 9:20</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+14:4">Romans 14:4</span>.</p>
<p><b>Thy neighbour </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τον πλησιον</span></span>). "The neighbour" as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="jas+2:8">James 2:8</span>. </p>
