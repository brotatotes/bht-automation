<p><b> In your vauntings </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εν ταις αλαζονιαις υμων</span></span>). Old word for braggart talk (from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αλαζονευομα</span></span>, to act the <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αλαζων</span></span> empty boaster <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+1:30">Romans 1:30</span>), common in Aristophanes, in N.T. only here and <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1jo+2:16">1 John 2:16</span>.</p>
<p><b>Glorying </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">καυχησις</span></span>). Act of glorying, late word from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">καυχαομα</span></span>, good if for Christ (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1th+2:19">1 Thessalonians 2:19</span>), bad if for self as here. </p>
