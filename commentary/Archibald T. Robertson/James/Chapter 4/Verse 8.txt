<p><b> Draw nigh to God </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εγγισατε τω θεω</span></span>). First aorist active imperative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εγγιζω</span></span>, late verb from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εγγυς</span></span> (near) as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+3:2">Matthew 3:2</span>. With dative case again of personal relation. The priests in the sanctuary drew nigh to God (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ex+19:22">Exodus 19:22</span>), as we should now.</p>
<p><b>Cleanse your hands </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">καθαρισατε χειρας</span></span>). First aorist active imperative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">καθαριζω</span></span>, to cleanse, from dirt in a ritual sense (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ex+30:19-21">Exodus 30:19-21</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mr+7:3">Mark 7:3</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mr+7:19">Mark 7:19</span>). Here it is figurative, as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ho+1:16">Hosea 1:16</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ps+24:4">Psalms 24:4</span>. If we always had clean (from sin) hands and hearts?</p>
<p><b>Ye sinners </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αμαρτωλο</span></span>). A sharp term to strike the conscience, "a reproach meant to startle and sting" (Ropes).</p>
<p><b>Purify your hearts </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αγνισατε καρδιας</span></span>). First aorist active imperative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αγνιζω</span></span>, old verb from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αγνος</span></span> (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="jas+3:17">James 3:17</span>), ceremonially (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+21:24">Acts 21:24</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+21:26">Acts 21:26</span>), but here morally as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1pe+1:22">1 Peter 1:22</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1jo+3:3">1 John 3:3</span>. Anarthrous use of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">καρδιας</span></span> as of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">χειρας</span></span> (wash hands, purify hearts).</p>
<p><b>Ye double-minded </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">διψυχο</span></span>). As in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="jas+1:8">James 1:8</span>. </p>
