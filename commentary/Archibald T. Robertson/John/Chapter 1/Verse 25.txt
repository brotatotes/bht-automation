<p><b> Why then baptizest thou? </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">Τ ουν βαπτιζεισ;</span></span>). In view of his repeated denials (three here mentioned).</p>
<p><b>If thou art not </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ε συ ουκ ε</span></span>). Condition of first class. They did not interpret his claim to be "the voice" to be important enough to justify the ordinance of baptism. Abrahams (<i>Studies in Pharisaism and the Gospels</i>) shows that proselyte baptism was probably practised before John's time, but its use by John was treating the Jews as if they were themselves Gentiles. </p>
