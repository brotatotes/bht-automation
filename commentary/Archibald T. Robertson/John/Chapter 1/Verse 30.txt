<p><b> Of whom </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">υπερ ου</span></span>). Not <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">περ</span></span>, but <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">υπερ</span></span>. "On behalf of whom." John points to Jesus as he speaks: "This is he." There he is. See verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+1:15">John 1:15</span> for discussion of these words of John. </p>
