<p><b> Again on the morrow </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τη επαυριον παλιν</span></span>). Third day since verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+1:19">John 1:19</span>.</p>
<p><b>Was standing </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ιστηκε</span></span>). Past perfect of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ιστημ</span></span>, intransitive, and used as imperfect in sense. See same form in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+7:37">John 7:37</span>.</p>
<p><b>Two </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δυο</span></span>). One was Andrew (verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+1:40">John 1:40</span>), the other the Beloved Disciple (the Apostle John), who records this incident with happy memories. </p>
