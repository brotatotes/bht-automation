<p><b> There arose a division again </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">σχισμα παλιν εγενετο</span></span>). As in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+7:43">John 7:43</span> in the crowd (also in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+7:12">John 7:12</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+7:31">John 7:31</span>), so now among the hostile Jews (Pharisees) some of whom had previously professed belief in him (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+8:31">John 8:31</span>). The direct reference of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">παλιν</span></span> (again) may be to <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+9:16">John 9:16</span> when the Pharisees were divided over the problem of the blind man. Division of opinion about Jesus is a common thing in John's Gospel (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+6:52">John 6:52</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+6:60">John 6:60</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+6:66">John 6:66</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+7:12">John 7:12</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+7:25">John 7:25</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+8:22">John 8:22</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+9:16">John 9:16</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+10:19">John 10:19</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+10:24">John 10:24</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+10:41">John 10:41</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+11:41">John 11:41</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+12:19">John 12:19</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+12:29">John 12:29</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+12:42">John 12:42</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+16:18">John 16:18</span>). </p>
