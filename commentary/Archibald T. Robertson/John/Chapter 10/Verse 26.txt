<p><b> Because ye are not of my sheep </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">οτ εκ των προβατων μου</span></span>). This had been the point in the allegory of the Good Shepherd. In fact, they were the children of the devil in spirit and conduct (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+8:43">John 8:43</span>), pious ecclesiastics though they seemed, veritable wolves in sheep's clothing (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+7:15">Matthew 7:15</span>). </p>
