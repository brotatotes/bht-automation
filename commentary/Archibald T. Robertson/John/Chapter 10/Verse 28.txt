<p><b> And I give unto them eternal life </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">καγω διδωμ αυτοις ζωην αιωνιον</span></span>). This is the gift of Jesus now to his sheep as stated in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+6:27">John 6:27</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+6:40">John 6:40</span> (cf. <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1jo+2:25">1 John 2:25</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1jo+5:11">1 John 5:11</span>).</p>
<p><b>And they shall never perish </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κα ου μη απολωντα</span></span>). Emphatic double negative with second aorist middle (intransitive) subjunctive of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">απολλυμ</span></span>, to destroy. The sheep may feel secure (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+3:16">John 3:16</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+6:39">John 6:39</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+17:12">John 17:12</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+18:9">John 18:9</span>).</p>
<p><b>And no one shall snatch them out of my hand </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κα ουχ αρπασε τις αυτα εκ της χειρος μου</span></span>). Jesus had promised this security in Galilee (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+6:37">John 6:37</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+6:39">John 6:39</span>). No wolf, no thief, no bandit, no hireling, no demon, not even the devil can pluck the sheep out of my hand. Cf. <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="col+3:3">Colossians 3:3</span> (Your life is hid together with Christ in God). </p>
