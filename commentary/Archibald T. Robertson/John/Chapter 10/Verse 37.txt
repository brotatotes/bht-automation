<p><b> If I do not </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ε ου ποιω</span></span>). Condition of first class, assumed as true, with negative <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ου</span></span>, not <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ε μη</span></span>=unless.</p>
<p><b>Believe me not </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μη πιστευετε μο</span></span>). Prohibition with <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μη</span></span> and the present active imperative. Either "cease believing me" or "do not have the habit of believing me." Jesus rests his case on his doing the works of "my Father" (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">του πατρος μου</span></span>), repeating his claims to sonship and deity. </p>
