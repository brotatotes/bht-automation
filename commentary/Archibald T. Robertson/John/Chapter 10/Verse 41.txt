<p><b> Many came to him </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πολλο ηλθον προς αυτον</span></span>). Jesus was busy here and in a more congenial atmosphere than Jerusalem. John wrought no signs the crowds recall, though Jesus did many here (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+19:2">Matthew 19:2</span>). The crowds still bear the impress of John's witness to Christ as "true" (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αληθη</span></span>). Here was prepared soil for Christ. </p>
