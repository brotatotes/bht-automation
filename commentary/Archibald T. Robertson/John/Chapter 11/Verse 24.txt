<p><b> In the resurrection at the last day </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εν τη αναστασε εν τη εσχατη ημερα</span></span>). Did Jesus mean only that? She believed it, of course, and such comfort is often offered in case of death, but that idea did not console Martha and is not what she hinted at in verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+11:22">John 11:22</span>. </p>
