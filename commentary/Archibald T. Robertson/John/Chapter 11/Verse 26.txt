<p><b> Shall never die </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ου μη αποθανη εις τον αιωνα</span></span>). Strong double negative <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ου μη</span></span> with second aorist active subjunctive of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αποθνησκω</span></span> again (but spiritual death, this time), "shall not die for ever" (eternal death).</p>
<p><b>Believest thou this? </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πιστευεις τουτο;</span></span>) Sudden test of Martha's insight and faith with all the subtle turns of thought involved. </p>
