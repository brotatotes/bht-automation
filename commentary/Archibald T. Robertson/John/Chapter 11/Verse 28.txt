 Called Mary  (εφωνησεν Μαριαμ). First aorist active indicative of φωνεω. Out of the house and away from the crowd.Secretly  (λαθρα). Old adverb from λαθρος (λανθανω). To tell her the glad news.The Master  (ο διδασκαλος). "The Teacher." So they loved to call him as he was (John 13:13).Is here  (παρεστιν). "Is present."Calleth thee  (φωνε σε). This rouses Mary.
