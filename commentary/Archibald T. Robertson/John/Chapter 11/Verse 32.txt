<p><b> Fell down at his feet </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επεσεν αυτου προς τους ποδας</span></span>). Second aorist active of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πιπτω</span></span>, to fall. Note unusual position of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αυτου</span></span>. This impulsive act like Mary. She said precisely what Martha had said to Jesus (verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+11:21">John 11:21</span>). But she said no more, only wept (verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+11:33">John 11:33</span>). </p>
