<p><b> Beheld that which he did </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">θεασαμενο ο εποιησεν</span></span>). First aorist middle participle of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">θεαομα</span></span> and first aorist active indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ποιεω</span></span> in the relative (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ο</span></span>) clause. They were eye-witnesses of all the details and did not depend on hearsay.</p>
<p><b>Believed on him </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επιστευσαν εις αυτον</span></span>). Such a result had happened before (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+7:31">John 7:31</span>), and all the more in the presence of this tremendous miracle which held many to Jesus (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+12:11">John 12:11</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+12:17">John 12:17</span>). </p>
