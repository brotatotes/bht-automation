<p><b> Now Jesus loved </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ηγαπα δε</span></span>). Imperfect active of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αγαπαω</span></span> picturing the continued love of Jesus for this noble family where he had his home so often (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+10:38-42">Luke 10:38-42</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+12:1-8">John 12:1-8</span>). The sisters expected him to come at once and to heal Lazarus. </p>
