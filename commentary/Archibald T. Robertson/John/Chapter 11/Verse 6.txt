<p><b> That he was sick </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">οτ ασθενε</span></span>). Present active indicative retained in indirect discourse after a secondary tense (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ηκουσεν</span></span>).</p>
<p><b>Two days </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δυο ημερας</span></span>). Accusative of extent of time.</p>
<p><b>In the place where he was </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εν ω ην τοπω</span></span>). Incorporation of the antecedent <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τοπω</span></span> into the relative clause, "in which place he was." It was long enough for Lazarus to die and seemed unlike Jesus to the sisters. </p>
