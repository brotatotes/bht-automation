<p><b> Bare witness </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εμαρτυρε</span></span>). Imperfect active of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μαρτυρεω</span></span>. This crowning triumph of Jesus gave an added sense of importance to the crowds that were actually with Jesus when he called Lazarus out of the tomb and raised him from the dead. For this description of this portion of the crowd see <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+11:45">John 11:45</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+12:1">John 12:1</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+12:9-11">John 12:9-11</span>. </p>
