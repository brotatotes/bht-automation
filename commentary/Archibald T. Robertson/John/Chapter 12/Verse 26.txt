<p><b> If any man serve me </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εαν εμο τις διακονη</span></span>). Condition of third class again (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εαν</span></span> with present active subjunctive of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">διακονεω</span></span>, keep on serving with dative <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εμο</span></span>).</p>
<p><b>Let him follow me </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εμο ακολουθειτω</span></span>). "Me (associative instrumental case) let him keep on following" (present active imperative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ακολουθεω</span></span>).</p>
<p><b>Where ... there </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">οπου ... εκε</span></span>). In presence and spiritual companionship here and hereafter. Cf. <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+14:3">John 14:3</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+17:24">John 17:24</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+28:20">Matthew 28:20</span>.</p>
<p><b>Shall honour </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τιμησε</span></span>). Future active of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τιμαω</span></span>, but it may be the kind of honour that Jesus will get (verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+12:23">John 12:23</span>). </p>
