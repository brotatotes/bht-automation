<p><b> Though he had done so many signs before them </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τοσαυτα αυτου σημεια πεποιηκοτος εμπροσθεν αυτων</span></span>). Genitive absolute with perfect active participle in concessive sense of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ποιεω</span></span>.</p>
<p><b>Yet they believed not on him </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ουκ επιστευον εις αυτον</span></span>). No "yet" in the Greek. Negative imperfect active of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πιστευω</span></span>, "they kept on not believing on him," stubborn refusal in face of the light (verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+12:35">John 12:35</span>). </p>
