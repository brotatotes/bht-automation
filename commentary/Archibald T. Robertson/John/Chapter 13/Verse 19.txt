<p><b> From henceforth </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">απ' αρτ</span></span>). "From now on," as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+14:7">John 14:7</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+23:39">Matthew 23:39</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+14:13">Revelation 14:13</span>.</p>
<p><b>Before it come to pass </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">προ του γενεσθα</span></span>). <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">Προ</span></span> with ablative of the articular second aorist middle infinitive <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">γινομα</span></span> (before the coming to pass).</p>
<p><b>When it is come to pass </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">οταν γενητα</span></span>). Indefinite relative clause with <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">οταν</span></span> and the second aorist middle subjunctive of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">γινομα</span></span>, "whenever it does come to pass."</p>
<p><b>That ye may believe </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ινα πιστευητε</span></span>). Purpose clause with <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ινα</span></span> and present active subjunctive of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πιστευω</span></span>, "that ye may keep on believing." Cf. <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="isa+48:5">Isaiah 48:5</span>.</p>
<p><b>That I am he </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">οτ εγω ειμ</span></span>). As Jesus has repeatedly claimed to be the Messiah (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+8:24">John 8:24</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+8:58">John 8:58</span>, etc.). Cf. also <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+14:29">John 14:29</span> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πιστευσητε</span></span> here); <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+16:4">John 16:4</span>. </p>
