<p><b> Looked one on another </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εβλεπον εις αλληλους</span></span>). Inchoative imperfect of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">βλεπω</span></span>, "began to glance at one another in bewilderment (doubting, <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">απορουμενο</span></span>, present passive participle of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">απορεω</span></span>, to be at a loss, to lose one's way, <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">α</span></span> privative and <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πορος</span></span>, way). They recalled their strife about precedence and Judas betrayed nothing.</p>
