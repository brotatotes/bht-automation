<p><b> Having received the sop </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">λαβων το ψωμιον</span></span>). Second aorist active participle of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">λαμβανω</span></span>. Judas knew what Jesus meant, however ignorant the disciples. So he acted "straightway" (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ευθυς</span></span>).</p>
<p><b>And it was night </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ην δε νυξ</span></span>). Darkness falls suddenly in the orient. Out into the terror and the mystery of this dreadful night (symbol of his devilish work) Judas went. </p>
