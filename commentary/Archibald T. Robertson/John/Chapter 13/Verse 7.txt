 I ... thou  (εγω ... συ). Jesus repeats the pronouns used by Peter in similar contrast.Not now  (ουκ αρτ). Just now αρτ means (John 9:19; John 9:25). Used again by Jesus (verse John 13:33) and Peter (verse John 13:37).But thou shalt understand hereafter  (γνωση δε μετα ταυτα). Future middle of γινωσκω (instead of the verb οιδα) to know by experience. "Thou shalt learn after these things," even if slowly.
