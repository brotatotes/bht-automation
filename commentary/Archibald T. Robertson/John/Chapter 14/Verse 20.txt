<p><b> In that day </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εν εκεινη τη ημερα</span></span>). The New Dispensation of the Holy Spirit, beginning with Christ's Resurrection and the Coming of the Holy Spirit at pentecost.</p>
<p><b>Shall know </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">γνωσεσθε</span></span>). Future middle of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">γινωσκω</span></span>. Chapters 1 to 3 of Acts bear eloquent witness to these words. </p>
