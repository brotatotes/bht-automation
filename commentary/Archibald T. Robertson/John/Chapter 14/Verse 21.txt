<p><b> He it is that loveth me </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εκεινος εστιν ο αγαπων με</span></span>). Emphatic demonstrative pronoun <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εκεινος</span></span>: "that is the one who loves me."</p>
<p><b>And will manifest myself unto him </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κα εμφανισω αυτω εμαυτον</span></span>). Future active of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εμφανιζω</span></span>, old verb from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εμφανης</span></span> (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+10:40">Acts 10:40</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+10:20">Romans 10:20</span>). The Unseen and Risen Christ will be a real and spiritual Presence to the obedient and loving believer. </p>
