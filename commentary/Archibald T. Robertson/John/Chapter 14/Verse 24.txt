<p><b> He that loveth me not </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ο μη αγαπων με</span></span>). Present active articular participle of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αγαπαω</span></span> with negative <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μη</span></span>, "the one who keeps on not loving me."</p>
<p><b>Is not mine, but the Father's </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ουκ εστιν εμοσ, αλλα του πατρος</span></span>). Predicative possessive pronoun <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εμος</span></span> and the predicate genitive of possession <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πατρος</span></span>. </p>
