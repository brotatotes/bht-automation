<p><b> Have I spoken </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">λελαληκα</span></span>). Perfect active indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">λαλεω</span></span>, for permanent keeping (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τηρεω</span></span> verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+14:23">John 14:23</span>).</p>
<p><b>While yet abiding with you </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">παρ' υμιν μενων</span></span>). Present active participle, no "yet" (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ετ</span></span>) in the Greek, "while remaining beside (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">παρ'</span></span>) you" before departing for the coming of the other Paraclete. </p>
