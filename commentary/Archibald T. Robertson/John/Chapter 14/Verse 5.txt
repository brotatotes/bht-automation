<p><b> Whither </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">που</span></span>)--<b> how </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πως</span></span>). It is Thomas, not Peter (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+13:36">John 13:36</span>) who renews the doubt about the destination of Jesus including the path or way thither (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">την οδον</span></span>). Thomas is the spokesman for the materialistic conception then and now. </p>
