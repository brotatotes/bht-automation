<p><b> So long time </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τοσουτον χρονον</span></span>). Accusative of extent of time.</p>
<p><b>And dost thou not know me? </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κα ουκ εγνωκας με;</span></span>). Perfect active indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">γινωσκω</span></span>. Jesus patiently repeats his language to Philip with the crisp statement: "he that hath seen me hath seen the Father" (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ο εωρακως εμε εωρακεν τον πατερα</span></span>). Perfect active participle and perfect active indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">οραω</span></span>, state of completion.</p>
<p><b>Thou </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">συ</span></span>). Emphatic--After these years together. </p>
