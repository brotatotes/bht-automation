<p><b> That my joy may be in you </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ινα η χαρα η εμη εν υμιν η</span></span>). Purpose clause with <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ινα</span></span> and the present subjunctive <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">η</span></span> (some MSS. have <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μεινη</span></span>, may remain), Christ's permanent absolute joy in the disciples.</p>
<p><b>And that your joy be fulfilled </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">Κα η χαρα υμων πληρωθη</span></span>). Same construction with first aorist (effective) passive subjunctive of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πληροω</span></span>, consummation of the process preceding. </p>
