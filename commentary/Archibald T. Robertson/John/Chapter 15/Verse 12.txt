<p><b> That ye love one another </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ινα αγαπατε αλληλους</span></span>). Non-final use of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ινα</span></span>, introducing a subject clause in apposition with <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εντολη</span></span> (commandment) and the present active subjunctive of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αγαπαω</span></span>, "that ye keep on loving one another." See <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+13:34">John 13:34</span>. </p>
