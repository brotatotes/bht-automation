<p><b> My Father also </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κα τον πατερα μου</span></span>). Because Christ reveals God (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+14:9">John 14:9</span>) and to dishonour Christ is to dishonour God (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+5:23">John 5:23</span>).
