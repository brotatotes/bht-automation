<p><b> They have both seen and hated </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κα εωρακασιν κα μεμισηκασιν</span></span>). Perfect active indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">οραω</span></span> and <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μισεω</span></span>, permanent attitude and responsibility. The "world" and the ecclesiastics (Sanhedrin) had united in this attitude of hostility to Christ and in reality to God. </p>
