<p><b> And ye also bear witness </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κα υμεις δε μαρτυρειτε</span></span>). Present active indicative or imperative (do ye bear witness), same form of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μαρτυρεω</span></span>. "Ye also" as well as the Holy Spirit, ye also when filled with and taught by the Holy Spirit the things concerning Jesus. It is here that Christians fail most.</p>
<p><b>Have been </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εστε</span></span>). Progressive present of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ειμ</span></span>, "are with me from the beginning of my ministry as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+14:9">John 14:9</span>. They were chosen to be with Christ (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mr+3:14">Mark 3:14</span>). </p>
