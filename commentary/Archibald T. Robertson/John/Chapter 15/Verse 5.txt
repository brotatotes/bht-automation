<p><b> Ye the branches </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">υμεις τα κληματα</span></span>). Jesus repeats and applies the metaphor of verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+15:1">John 15:1</span>.</p>
<p><b>Apart from me </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">χωρις εμου</span></span>). See <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="eph+2:12">Ephesians 2:12</span> for <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">χωρις Χριστου</span></span>. There is nothing for a broken off branch to do but wither and die. For the cosmic relation of Christ see <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+1:3">John 1:3</span> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">χωρις αυτου</span></span>). </p>
