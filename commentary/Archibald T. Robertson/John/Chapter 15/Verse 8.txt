<p><b> Herein </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εν τουτω</span></span>). That is in the vital union and the much fruit bearing. It points here backwards and forwards.</p>
<p><b>Is glorified </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εδοξασθη</span></span>). Another gnomic or timeless first aorist passive indicative.</p>
<p><b>Bear </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">φερετε</span></span>). Present active subjunctive, "keep on bearing" much fruit.</p>
<p><b>And so shall ye be </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κα γενησεσθε</span></span>). Rather "become." Future middle indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">γινομα</span></span>, though B D L read <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">γενησθε</span></span> (after <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ινα</span></span> like <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">φερητε</span></span>).
