<p><b> Abide </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μεινατε</span></span>). Constative first aorist active imperative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μενω</span></span>, summing up the whole.</p>
<p><b>In my love </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εν τη αγαπη τη εμη</span></span>). Subjunctive possessive pronoun, "in the love that I have for you." Our love for Christ is the result of Christ's love for us and is grounded at bottom in the Father's love for the world (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+3:16">John 3:16</span>). John has <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εμος</span></span> 37 times and always in the words of Jesus (Bernard). But he uses <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μου</span></span> also (verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+15:10">John 15:10</span>). </p>
