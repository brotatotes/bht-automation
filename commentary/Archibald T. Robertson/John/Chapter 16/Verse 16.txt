<p><b> A little while </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μικρον</span></span>). The brief period now till Christ's death as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+7:33">John 7:33</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+13:33">John 13:33</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+14:19">John 14:19</span>.</p>
<p><b>Again a little while </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">παλιν μικρον</span></span>). The period between the death and the resurrection of Jesus (from Friday afternoon till Sunday morning).</p>
<p><b>Ye shall see me </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">οψεσθε με</span></span>). Future middle of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">οπτομα</span></span>, the verb used in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+1:51">John 1:51</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+16:22">John 16:22</span> as here of spiritual realities (Bernard), though <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">θεωρεω</span></span> is so used in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+20:14">John 20:14</span>. </p>
