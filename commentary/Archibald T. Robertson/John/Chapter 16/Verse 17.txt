<p><b> Some of the disciples </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εκ των μαθητων αυτου</span></span>). Ellipsis of time (some) before <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εκ</span></span> as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+7:40">John 7:40</span>. Jesus seemed to contradict himself, for the disciples took both verbs in the same sense and were still puzzled over the going to the Father of <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+14:3">John 14:3</span>. But they talk to one another, not to Jesus. </p>
