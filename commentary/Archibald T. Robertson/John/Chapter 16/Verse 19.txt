<p><b> Jesus perceived </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εγνω Ιησους</span></span>). Second aorist active indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">γινωσκω</span></span>.</p>
<p><b>That they were desirous to ask him </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">οτ ηθελον αυτον ερωταιν</span></span>). Imperfect active tense of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">θελω</span></span> in indirect discourse instead of the retention of the present <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">θελουσιν</span></span> (the usual idiom), just like our English. Their embarrassment was manifest after four inquiries already (Peter, Thomas, Philip, Judas). So Jesus takes the initiative. </p>
