<p><b> They shall put you out of the synagogues </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αποσυναγωγους ποιησουσιν υμας</span></span>). "They will make you outcasts from the synagogues." Predicate accusative of the compound adjective <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αποσυναγωγος</span></span> for which see <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+9:22">John 9:22</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+12:42">John 12:42</span>.</p>
<p><b>Yea </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αλλ'</span></span>). Use of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αλλα</span></span> as co-ordinating conjunction, not adversative.</p>
<p><b>That </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ινα</span></span>) not in the sense of "when" (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">οτε</span></span>), but as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+12:23">John 12:23</span> for God's purpose (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+2:34">Luke 2:34</span>, <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">οπως</span></span>).</p>
<p><b>Shall think </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δοξη</span></span>). First aorist active subjunctive of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δοκεω</span></span>. "So blind will he be" (Bernard).</p>
<p><b>That he offereth service unto God </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">λατρειαν προσφερειν τω θεω</span></span>). Infinitive (present active) indirect discourse after <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δοξη</span></span>. For the phrase see <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="heb+6:1">Hebrews 6:1</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="heb+8:3">Hebrews 8:3</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="heb+9:7">Hebrews 9:7</span>. The rabbis so felt when they crucified Jesus and when they persecuted the disciples (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+6:13">Acts 6:13</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+7:57">Acts 7:57</span>). No persecution is more bitter than when done by religious enthusiasts and bigots like the Spanish Inquisition. </p>
