<p><b> Do ye now believe? </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αρτ πιστευετε;</span></span>). For <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αρτ</span></span> (just now) see <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+9:19">John 9:19</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+13:33">John 13:33</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+13:37">John 13:37</span>. Their belief in Christ was genuine <i>as far as it went</i>, but perils await them of which they are ignorant. They are too self-confident as their despair at Christ's death shows. </p>
