<p><b> And none of you asketh me </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κα ουδεις εξ υμων ερωτα με</span></span>). Adversative use of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κα</span></span>="and yet" as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+1:10">John 1:10</span>. Now that they realize that Jesus is going, the thoughts of the disciples turn on themselves and they cease asking the query of Peter (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+13:36">John 13:36</span>). </p>
