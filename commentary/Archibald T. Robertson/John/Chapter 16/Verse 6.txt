<p><b> Sorrow hath filled </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">η λυπη πεπληρωκεν</span></span>). This word is not used of Jesus in the Gospels, in John only in this chapter. Perfect active indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πληροω</span></span>. They do not see their way to go on without Jesus. </p>
