<p><b> That they may have my joy fulfilled in themselves </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ινα εχωσιν την χαραν την εμην πεπληρωμενην εν εαυτοις</span></span>). Purpose clause with present active subjunctive of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εχω</span></span>, "that they may keep on having Christ's joy in their faithfulness realized in themselves." <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">Πεπληρωμενην</span></span> is the perfect passive participle of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πληροω</span></span> in the predicate position. For the use of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πληροω</span></span> with <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">χαρα</span></span> (joy) see <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+15:11">John 15:11</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+16:24">John 16:24</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="php+2:2">Philippians 2:2</span>. </p>
