<p><b> Sanctify </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αγιασον</span></span>). First aorist active imperative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αγιαζω</span></span>. To consecrate or set apart persons or things to God. See <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ex+28:41">Exodus 28:41</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ex+29:1">Exodus 29:1</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ex+29:36">Exodus 29:36</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ex+40:13">Exodus 40:13</span>. See Paul's prayer for the Thessalonians (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1th+5:23">1 Thessalonians 5:23</span>). This is done in the sphere (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εν</span></span>) of truth (God's truth), God's Word (not human speculation, but God's message to us). </p>
