<p><b> That they also may be in us </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ινα κα αυτο εν ημιν ωσιν</span></span>). Another purpose clause with <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ινα</span></span> and the present active subjunctive of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ειμ</span></span>. The only possible way to have unity among believers is for all of them to find unity first with God in Christ.</p>
<p><b>That the world may believe </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ινα ο κοσμος πιστευη</span></span>). Another purpose clause with <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ινα</span></span> and the present active subjunctive of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πιστευω</span></span>, "may keep on believing." Beyond a doubt, strife, wrangling, division are a stumblingblock to the outside world. </p>
