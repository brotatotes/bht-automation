<p><b> I will </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">θελω</span></span>). Perfect identity of his will with that of the Father in "this moment of spiritual exaltation" (Bernard), though in Gethsemane Jesus distinguishes between his human will and that of the Father (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mr+14:36">Mark 14:36</span>).</p>
<p><b>Where I am </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">οπου ειμ εγω</span></span>). That is heaven, to be with Jesus (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+12:26">John 12:26</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+13:36">John 13:36</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+14:3">John 14:3</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+8:17">Romans 8:17</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2ti+2:11">2 Timothy 2:11</span>).</p>
<p><b>That they may behold </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ινα θεωρωσιν</span></span>). Another purpose clause with <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ινα</span></span> and the present active subjunctive of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">θεωρεω</span></span>, "that they may keep on beholding," the endless joy of seeing Jesus "as he is" (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1jo+3:2">1 John 3:2</span>) in heaven.</p>
<p><b>Before the foundation of the world </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">προ καταβολης κοσμου</span></span>). This same phrase in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="eph+1:4">Ephesians 1:4</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1pe+1:20">1 Peter 1:20</span> and six other times we have <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">καταβολη κοσμου</span></span> (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+25:34">Matthew 25:34</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+11:50">Luke 11:50</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="heb+4:3">Hebrews 4:3</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="heb+9:26">Hebrews 9:26</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+13:8">Revelation 13:8</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+17:8">Revelation 17:8</span>). Here we find the same pre-incarnate consciousness of Christ seen in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+17:5">John 17:5</span>. </p>
