<p><b> Asked </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ηρωτησεν</span></span>). First aorist active indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ερωταω</span></span>, to question, usual meaning. This was Annas making a preliminary examination of Jesus probably to see on what terms Jesus made disciples whether as a mere rabbi or as Messiah. </p>
