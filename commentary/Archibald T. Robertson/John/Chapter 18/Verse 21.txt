<p><b> Ask them that have heard me </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ερωτησον τους ακηκοοτας</span></span>). First aorist (tense of urgent and instant action) active imperative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ερωταω</span></span> and the articular perfect active participle accusative masculine plural of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ακουω</span></span>, to hear. There were abundant witnesses to be had. Multitudes had heard Jesus in the great debate in the temple on Tuesday of this very week when the Sanhedrin were routed to the joy of the common people who heard Jesus gladly (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mr+12:37">Mark 12:37</span>). They still know. </p>
