<p><b> Therefore sent him </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">απεστειλεν ουν αυτον</span></span>). First aorist active of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αποστελλω</span></span>, not past perfect (had sent). The preliminary examination by Annas was over.</p>
<p><b>Bound </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δεδεμενον</span></span>). Perfect passive participle of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δεω</span></span>, to bind. Jesus was bound on his arrest (verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+18:12">John 18:12</span>) and apparently unbound during the preliminary examination by Annas. </p>
