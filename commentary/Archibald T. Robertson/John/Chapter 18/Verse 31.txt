<p><b> Yourselves </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">υμεις</span></span>). Emphatic. Pilate shrewdly turns the case over to the Sanhedrin in reply to their insolence, who have said nothing whatever about their previous trial and condemnation of Jesus. He drew out at once the admission that they wanted the death of Jesus, not a fair trial for him, but Pilate's approval of their purpose to kill him (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+7:1">John 7:1</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+7:25">John 7:25</span>). </p>
