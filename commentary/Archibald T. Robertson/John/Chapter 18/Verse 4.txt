<p><b> Knowing all the things that were coming upon him </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ειδως παντα τα ερχομενα επ' αυτον</span></span>). Mentioned already in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+13:1">John 13:1</span>. He was not taken by surprise.
