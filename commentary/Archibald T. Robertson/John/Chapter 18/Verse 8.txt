<p><b> Let these go their way </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αφετε τουτους υπαγειν</span></span>). Second aorist active imperative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αφιημ</span></span>. The verb <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">υπαγειν</span></span> means to withdraw (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+11:44">John 11:44</span>). Jesus shows solicitude for the eleven as he had warned them and prayed for them (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+22:31">Luke 22:31</span>). He is trying to help them. </p>
