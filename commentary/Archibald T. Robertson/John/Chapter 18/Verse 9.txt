<p><b> That might be fulfilled </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ινα πληρωθη</span></span>). The regular formula (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+17:12">John 17:12</span>) for Scripture, here applied to the prophecy of Jesus (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+17:12">John 17:12</span>) as in verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+18:32">John 18:32</span>. John treats the saying of Jesus as on a par with the O.T. </p>
