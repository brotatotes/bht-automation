<p><b> He delivered </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">παρεδωκεν</span></span>). Kappa aorist active of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">παραδιδωμ</span></span>, the very verb used of the Sanhedrin when they handed Jesus over to Pilate (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+18:30">John 18:30</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+18:35">John 18:35</span>). Now Pilate hands Jesus back to the Sanhedrin with full consent for his death (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+23:25">Luke 23:25</span>).</p>
<p><b>To be crucified </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ινα σταυρωθη</span></span>). Purpose clause with <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ινα</span></span> and the first aorist passive subjunctive of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">σταυροω</span></span>. John does not give the dramatic episode in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+27:24">Matthew 27:24</span> when Pilate washed his hands and the Jews took Christ's blood on themselves and their children. But it is on Pilate also. </p>
