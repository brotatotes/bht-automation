 They crucified  (εσταυρωσαν). The soldiers just as in Acts 22:24; the scourging of Paul was to be done by the soldiers.And Jesus in the midst  (μεσον δε τον Ιησουν). Predicate adjective μεσον. A robber (ληιστης, not a thief, κλεπτης) was on each side of Jesus (Mark 15:27; Matthew 27:38) like Barabbas (John 18:40) and probably members of his band, malefactors (κακουργο) Luke terms them (Luke 23:32).
