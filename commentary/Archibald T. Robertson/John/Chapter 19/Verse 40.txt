 In linen cloths  (οθονιοις). Late diminutive for the old οθονη, used for ships' sails, in N.T. here and Luke 24:12. Case here either locative or instrumental.With the spices  (μετα των αρωματων). Late word αρωμα for spices, from fumes.To bury  (ενταφιαζειν). Late verb, from ενταφια (εν, ταφος) the burial preparations of all sorts (flowers, perfumes, etc.), in N.T. only here and Matthew 26:12.
