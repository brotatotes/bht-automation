<p><b> In linen cloths </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">οθονιοις</span></span>). Late diminutive for the old <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">οθονη</span></span>, used for ships' sails, in N.T. here and <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+24:12">Luke 24:12</span>. Case here either locative or instrumental.</p>
<p><b>With the spices </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μετα των αρωματων</span></span>). Late word <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αρωμα</span></span> for spices, from fumes.</p>
<p><b>To bury </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ενταφιαζειν</span></span>). Late verb, from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ενταφια</span></span> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εν, ταφος</span></span>) the burial preparations of all sorts (flowers, perfumes, etc.), in N.T. only here and <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+26:12">Matthew 26:12</span>. </p>
