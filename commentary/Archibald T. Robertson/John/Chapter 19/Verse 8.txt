<p><b> He was the more afraid </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μαλλον εφοβηθη</span></span>). First aorist passive indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">φοβεομα</span></span>. He was already afraid because of his wife's message (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+27:19">Matthew 27:19</span>). The claim of Jesus to deity excited Pilate's superstitious fears. </p>
