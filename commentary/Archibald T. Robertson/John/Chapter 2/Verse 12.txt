<p><b> He went down to Capernaum </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κατεβη εις Καφαρναουμ αυτος</span></span>). Second aorist active indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">καταβαινω</span></span>. Cana was on higher ground. This brief stay (<b> not many days </b>, <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ου πολλας ημερας</span></span>) in this important city (Tell Hum) on the north shore of Galilee was with Christ's mother, brothers (apparently friendly at first) and the six disciples, all in the fresh glow of the glory manifested at Cana. Surely Mary's heart was full. </p>
