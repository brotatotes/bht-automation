<p><b> Was standing </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ιστηκε</span></span>). Past perfect of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ιστημ</span></span> as imperfect as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+19:25">John 19:25</span>.</p>
<p><b>At the tomb </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">προς τω μνημειω</span></span>). <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">Προς</span></span> (in front of) with locative while <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">παρα</span></span> (by the side of) with locative in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+19:25">John 19:25</span>. Pathetic and common picture of a woman weeping by the tomb. See <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+11:31">John 11:31</span>.</p>
<p><b>As she wept </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ως εκλαιεν</span></span>). Imperfect, "as she was weeping."</p>
<p><b>She stooped and looked </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">παρεκυψεν</span></span>). Aorist active indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">παρακυπτω</span></span> for which see verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+20:5">John 20:5</span>. Mary "peeped into" the tomb, but did not enter. </p>
