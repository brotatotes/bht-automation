<p><b> Didymus </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">Διδυμος</span></span>). The same expression applied to Thomas in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+11:16">John 11:16</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+21:2">John 21:2</span>, but nowhere else in N.T. Old word for twin (double), "the pessimist of the apostolic band" (Bernard). The term twelve is still applied to the group, though Judas, the traitor, is dead. </p>
