<p><b> For </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">γαρ</span></span>). Explanatory use of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">γαρ</span></span>.</p>
<p><b>The Scripture </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">την γραφην</span></span>). Probably <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ps+16:10">Psalms 16:10</span>. Jesus had repeatedly foretold his resurrection, but that was all forgotten in the great sorrow on their hearts. Only the chief priests and Pharisees recalled the words of Jesus (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+27:62">Matthew 27:62</span>).</p>
<p><b>Must </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δε</span></span>). For this use of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δε</span></span> concerning Christ's death and resurrection see <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mr+8:31">Mark 8:31</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+26:54">Matthew 26:54</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+9:22">Luke 9:22</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+17:25">Luke 17:25</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+22:37">Luke 22:37</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+24:7">Luke 24:7</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+24:26">Luke 24:26</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+24:44">Luke 24:44</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+3:14">John 3:14</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+12:34">John 12:34</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+1:16">Acts 1:16</span>. Jesus had put emphasis on both the fact and the necessity of his resurrection which the disciples slowly perceived. </p>
