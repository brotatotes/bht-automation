<p><b> Manifested himself </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εφανεροσεν εαυτον</span></span>). First aorist active indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">φανεροω</span></span> with the reflexive pronoun (cf. <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+7:4">John 7:4</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+13:4">John 13:4</span>). For the passive see <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+1:31">John 1:31</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+21:14">John 21:14</span>. Jesus was only seen during the forty days now and then (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+1:3">Acts 1:3</span>), ten instances being recorded. The word <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">φανεροω</span></span> is often used of Christ on earth (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+1:31">John 1:31</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+2:11">John 2:11</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1pe+1:20">1 Peter 1:20</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1jo+1:2">1 John 1:2</span>), of his works (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+3:5">John 3:5</span>), of the second coming (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1jo+2:28">1 John 2:28</span>), of Christ in glory (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="col+3:4">Colossians 3:4</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1jo+3:2">1 John 3:2</span>).</p>
<p><b>At </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επ</span></span>). By or upon.</p>
<p><b>Of Tiberias </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">της Τιβεριαδος</span></span>). As in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+6:1">John 6:1</span> instead of the usual "Sea of Galilee." Tiberias, the capital city of Galilee, gave this epithet to the Sea of Galilee. This is not the appearance in Galilee prearranged by Jesus (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mr+16:7">Mark 16:7</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+28:7">Matthew 28:7</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+28:16">Matthew 28:16</span>). </p>
