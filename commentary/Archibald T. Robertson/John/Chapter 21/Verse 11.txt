 Went up  (ανεβη). Into the little boat or dinghy.Drew  (ειλκυσεν). Same verb as ελκυσα in verse John 21:6. Peter now did what they had failed to do.Three  (τριων). The addition "three" to the "hundred and fifty" looks as if they were actually counted these "large" (μεγαλων) fish. It was a great fish story that John recalls vividly.Was not rent  (ουκ εσχισθη). First aorist passive indicative of σχιζω, to split (our word "schism").
