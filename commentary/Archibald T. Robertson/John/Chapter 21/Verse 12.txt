<p><b> Break your fast </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αριστησατε</span></span>). First aorist active imperative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αρισταω</span></span> from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αριστον</span></span>, first to breakfast, as here and then later to dine as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+11:37">Luke 11:37</span>. What a delightful breakfast of fresh broiled fish just caught (verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+21:10">John 21:10</span>) with the hush of joyful surprise in the presence of the Risen Lord.</p>
<p><b>Durst </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ετολμα</span></span>) Imperfect active of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τολμαω</span></span>. The restraint of silence continued. </p>
