<p><b> Now the third time </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">το ηδη τριτον</span></span>). "To the disciples" (apostles) John says, the two others being told by him (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+20:19">John 20:19</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+20:26">John 20:26</span>) on the two Sunday evenings. There were four other appearances already (to Mary Magdalene, to the group of women, to the two on the way to Emmaus, to Peter). </p>
