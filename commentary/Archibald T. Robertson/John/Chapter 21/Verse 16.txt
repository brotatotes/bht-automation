<p><b> Lovest thou me? </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αγαπαις με;</span></span>). This time Jesus drops the <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πλεον τουτων</span></span> and challenges Peter's own statement. Peter repeats the same words in reply.</p>
<p><b>Tend my sheep </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ποιμαινε τα προβατια</span></span>). Present active imperative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ποιμαινω</span></span>, old verb from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ποιμην</span></span> (shepherd), "shepherd my lambs" (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">προβατια</span></span>, diminutive of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">προβατον</span></span>, sheep). </p>
