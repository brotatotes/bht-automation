 Thou girdest thyself  (εζωννυες σεαυτον). Imperfect active of customary action of ζωννυω, old verb, in N.T. only here and Acts 12:8. So as to περιεπατεις (walkedst) and ηθελες (wouldest), two other imperfects of customary action.When thou shalt be old  (οταν γηρασηις). Indefinite temporal clause with οταν and the first aorist active subjunctive of γηρασκω, old verb to grow old, in N.T. only here and Hebrews 8:13, "whenever thou growest old."
