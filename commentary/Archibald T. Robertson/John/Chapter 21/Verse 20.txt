<p><b> Turning about </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επιστραφεις</span></span>). Second aorist passive participle of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επιστρεφω</span></span>, old verb, here a sudden turning round (ingressive aorist). For the simplex verb <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">στρεφω</span></span> see <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+20:14">John 20:14</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+20:16">John 20:16</span>.</p>
<p><b>Following </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ακολουθουντα</span></span>). Following both Jesus and Peter, perhaps having heard the graphic dialogue above. </p>
