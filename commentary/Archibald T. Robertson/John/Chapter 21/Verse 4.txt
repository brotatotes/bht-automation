 When day was now breaking  (πρωιας ηδη γινομενης). Genitive absolute and note present middle participle (dawn coming on and still dark). In Matthew 27:1 the aorist participle (γενομενης) means that dawn had come. For "beach" (αιγιαλον) see Matthew 13:2.Was  (εστιν). Present indicative retained in indirect assertion.
