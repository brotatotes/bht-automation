<p><b> When day was now breaking </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πρωιας ηδη γινομενης</span></span>). Genitive absolute and note present middle participle (dawn coming on and still dark). In <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+27:1">Matthew 27:1</span> the aorist participle (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">γενομενης</span></span>) means that dawn had come. For "beach" (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αιγιαλον</span></span>) see <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+13:2">Matthew 13:2</span>.</p>
<p><b>Was </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εστιν</span></span>). Present indicative retained in indirect assertion. </p>
