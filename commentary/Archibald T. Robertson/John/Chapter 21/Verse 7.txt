<p><b> It is the Lord </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ο κυριος εστιν</span></span>). John's quick insight appears again.</p>
<p><b>Girt his coat about him </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τον επενδυτην διεζωσατο</span></span>). First aorist middle (indirect) indicative with which note <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">διεζωσεν εαυτον</span></span> in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+13:4">John 13:4</span>. Apparently Peter threw on the upper garment or linen blouse (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επενδυτην</span></span>) worn by fishers over his waistcloth and tucked it under his girdle. </p>
