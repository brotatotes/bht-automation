<p><b> In the little boat </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τω πλοιαριω</span></span>). Locative case of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πλοιαριον</span></span> (diminutive) for the larger boat (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πλοιον</span></span>, verses <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+21:3">John 21:3</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+21:6">John 21:6</span>) could come no closer to shore. But the words seem interchangeable in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+6:17">John 6:17</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+6:19">John 6:19</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+6:21">John 6:21</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+6:22">John 6:22</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+6:24">John 6:24</span>.</p>
<p><b>About two hundred cubits off </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ως απο πηχων διακοσιων</span></span>). For <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πηχυς</span></span>, cubit, see <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+6:27">Matthew 6:27</span> and for <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ως απο</span></span> see <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+11:18">John 11:18</span>.</p>
<p><b>Dragging </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">συροντες</span></span>). Present active participle of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">συρω</span></span> for which see <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+8:3">Acts 8:3</span>. </p>
