<p><b> For John had not yet been cast into prison </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ουπω γαρ ην βεβλημενος εις την φυλακην Ιωανης</span></span>). Periphrastic past perfect indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">βαλλω</span></span> explaining (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">γαρ</span></span>) why John was still baptizing, the reason for the imprisonment having been given by Luke (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+3:19">Luke 3:19</span>). </p>
