<p><b> Hath set his seal </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εσφραγισεν</span></span>). First aorist active indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">σφραγιζω</span></span> for which verb see <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+27:66">Matthew 27:66</span>. The metaphor of sealing is a common one for giving attestation as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+6:27">John 6:27</span>. The one who accepts the witness of Jesus attests that Jesus speaks the message of God. </p>
