Jesus makes the same claim in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+5:19-30">John 5:19-30</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+11:27">Matthew 11:27</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+28:18">Matthew 28:18</span>. </p>
