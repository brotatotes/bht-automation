<p><b> Marvel not </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μη θαυμασηις</span></span>). "Do not begin to wonder" (ingressive first aorist active subjunctive with <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μη</span></span>), as clearly Nicodemus had done. In John the word <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">θαυμαζω</span></span> usually means "unintelligent wonder" (Bernard).</p>
<p><b>Ye must be born anew </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δε υμας γεννηθηνα ανωθεν</span></span>). Jesus repeats the point in verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+3:3">John 3:3</span> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δε</span></span> and the infinitive instead of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εαν μη</span></span> and the subjunctive) with <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ανωθεν</span></span> (from above) only and not <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εξ υδατος</span></span>. </p>
