<p><b> How? </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">Πωσ;</span></span>) Nicodemus is not helped either by the use of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">υδωρ</span></span> or <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πνευμα</span></span> to understand <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δε γεννηθηνα ανωθεν</span></span> (the necessity of the birth from above or regeneration). He falls back into his "stupid misunderstanding." There are none so dull as those who will not see. Preoccupation prevents insight. Literally one must often empty his mind to receive new truth. </p>
