<p><b> Art thou </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">Μη συ ε</span></span>). Expecting a negative answer.</p>
<p><b>Greater than our father Jacob </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μειζων ε του πατρος ημων Ιακωβ</span></span>). Ablative case <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πατρος</span></span> after the comparative adjective <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μειζων</span></span> (positive <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μεγας</span></span>). The Samaritans claimed descent from Jacob through Joseph (tribes of Ephraim and Manasseh).</p>
<p><b>Cattle </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">θρεμματα</span></span>). Old word from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τρεφω</span></span>, to nourish, nursling, child, flock, cattle. Only here in N.T. </p>
