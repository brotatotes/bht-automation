<p><b> Every one that drinketh </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πας ο πινων</span></span>). Present active articular participle with <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πας</span></span>, parallel to the indefinite relative with the second aorist active subjunctive (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ος αν πιη</span></span>) in verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+4:14">John 4:14</span>. With this difference in the tenses used (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πινων</span></span>, keep on drinking, <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πιη</span></span>, once for all). Note <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εκ</span></span> and the ablative both times, out of the water. Jesus pointed to the well ("this water"). </p>
