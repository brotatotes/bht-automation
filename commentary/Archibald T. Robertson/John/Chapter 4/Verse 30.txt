<p><b> They went out </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εξηλθον</span></span>). Second aorist (effective) indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εξερχομα</span></span>, at once and in a rush.</p>
<p><b>And were coming to him </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κα ηρχοντο προς αυτον</span></span>). Imperfect middle, graphically picturing the long procession as they approached Jesus. </p>
