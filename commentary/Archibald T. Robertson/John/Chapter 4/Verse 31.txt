<p><b> In the meanwhile </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εν τω μεταξυ</span></span>). Supply <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">καιρο</span></span> or <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">χρονο</span></span>. See <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">το μεταξυ Σαββατον</span></span>, "the next Sabbath" (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+13:42">Acts 13:42</span>) and <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εν τω μεταξυ</span></span> (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+8:1">Luke 8:1</span>). <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">Μεταξυ</span></span> means between.</p>
<p><b>Prayed him </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ηρωτων αυτον</span></span>). Imperfect active, "kept beseeching him." For this late (<i>Koine</i>) use of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ερωταω</span></span>, to beseech, instead of the usual sense to question see also verses <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+4:40">John 4:40</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+4:47">John 4:47</span>. Their concern for the comfort of Jesus overcame their surprise about the woman. </p>
