<p><b> So the father knew </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εγνω ουν ο πατηρ</span></span>). Second aorist active indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">γινωσκω</span></span>. Inferential use of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ουν</span></span>.</p>
<p><b>Himself believed </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επιστευσεν αυτος</span></span>). Not just the word of Jesus (verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+4:50">John 4:50</span>), but complete faith in Jesus himself as the Messiah, absolute use of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πιστευω</span></span> as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+1:7">John 1:7</span>.</p>
<p><b>And his whole house </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κα η οικια αυτου</span></span>). All his family, the first example of a whole family believing in Jesus like the later case of Crispus (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+18:8">Acts 18:8</span>). </p>
