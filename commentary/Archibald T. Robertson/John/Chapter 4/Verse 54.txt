<p><b> The second sign that </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δευτερον σημειον</span></span>). No article, simply predicate accusative, "This again a second sign did Jesus having come out of Judea into Galilee." The first one was also in Cana (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+2:1">John 2:1</span>), but many were wrought in Jerusalem also (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+2:23">John 2:23</span>). </p>
