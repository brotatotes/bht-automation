<p><b> Persecute </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εδιωκον</span></span>). Inchoative imperfect, "began to persecute" and kept it up. They took this occasion as one excuse (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δια τουτο</span></span>, because of this). They disliked Jesus when here first (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+2:18">John 2:18</span>) and were suspicious of his popularity (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+4:1">John 4:1</span>). Now they have cause for an open breach.</p>
<p><b>Because he did </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">οτ εποιε</span></span>). Imperfect active, not just this one act, but he was becoming a regular Sabbath-breaker. The Pharisees will watch his conduct on the Sabbath henceforth (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mr+2:23">Mark 2:23</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mr+3:2">Mark 3:2</span>). </p>
