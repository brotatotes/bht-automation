<p><b> He hath given all judgement unto the Son </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">την κρισιν πασαν δεδωκεν τω υιω</span></span>). Perfect active indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">διδωμ</span></span>, state of completion (as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+3:35">John 3:35</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+6:27">John 6:27</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+6:29">John 6:29</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+10:29">John 10:29</span>, etc.). See this prerogative claimed for Christ already in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+3:17">John 3:17</span>. See the picture of Christ as Judge of men in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+25:31-46">Matthew 25:31-46</span>. </p>
