<p><b> Were going </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ηρχοντο</span></span>). Picturesque imperfect.</p>
<p><b>It was now dark </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">σκοτια ηδη εγεγονε</span></span>). Past perfect active of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">γινομα</span></span>. While they were going, "darkness had already come."</p>
<p><b>And Jesus had not yet come to them </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κα ουκ εληλυθε προς αυτους ο Ιησους</span></span>). Another past perfect active of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ερχομα</span></span> with negative <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ουπω</span></span>. Darkness had come, but Jesus had not come, while they were going over the sea. The tenses in these verses are very graphic. </p>
