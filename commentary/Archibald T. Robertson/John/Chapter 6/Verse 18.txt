<p><b> And the sea was rising </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">η τε θαλασσα διεγειρετο</span></span>). Imperfect (without augment) passive of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">διεγειρω</span></span>, late compound to wake up thoroughly, to arouse.</p>
<p><b>By reason of a great wind that blew </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ανεμου μεγαλου πνεοντος</span></span>). Genitive absolute with present active participle of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πνεω</span></span>, to blow, "a great wind blowing." </p>
