<p><b> Not because ye saw signs </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ουχ οτ ειδετε σημεια</span></span>). Second aorist active indicative of the defective verb <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">οραω</span></span>. They had seen the "signs" wrought by Jesus (verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+6:2">John 6:2</span>), but this one had led to wild fanaticism (verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+6:14">John 6:14</span>) and complete failure to grasp the spiritual lessons.</p>
<p><b>But because ye ate of the loaves </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αλλ' οτ εφαγετε εκ των αρτων</span></span>). Second aorist active indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εσθιω</span></span>, defective verb.</p>
<p><b>Ye were filled </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εχορτασθητε</span></span>). First aorist passive indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">χορταζω</span></span>, from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">χορτος</span></span> (grass) as in verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+6:10">John 6:10</span>, to eat grass, then to eat anything, to satisfy hunger. They were more concerned with hungry stomachs than with hungry souls. It was a sharp and deserved rebuke. </p>
