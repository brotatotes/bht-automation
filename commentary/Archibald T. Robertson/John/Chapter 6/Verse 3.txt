<p><b> Into the mountain </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εις το ορος</span></span>). From the level of the Jordan valley up into the high hill on the eastern side. Mark (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mr+6:46">Mark 6:46</span>) and Matthew (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+14:23">Matthew 14:23</span>) mention that after the miracle Jesus went further up into the mountain to pray.</p>
<p><b>Sat </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εκαθητο</span></span>). Imperfect middle of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">καθημα</span></span>, was sitting, a picture of repose. </p>
