<p><b> That of all that which </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ινα παν ο</span></span>). Literally, "That all which" (see verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+6:37">John 6:37</span> for <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">παν ο</span></span>), but there is a sharp anacoluthon with <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">παν</span></span> left as <i>nominativus pendens</i>.</p>
<p><b>I should lose nothing </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μη απολεσω εξ αυτου</span></span>). Construed with <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ινα</span></span>, "that I shall not lose anything of it." <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">Απολεσω</span></span>, from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">απολλυμ</span></span>, can be either future active indicative or first aorist active subjunctive as is true also of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αναστησω</span></span> (from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ανιστημ</span></span>), "I shall raise up."</p>
<p><b>At the last day </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τη εσχατη εμερα</span></span>). Locative case without <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εν</span></span>. Only in John, but four times here (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+6:39">John 6:39</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+6:40">John 6:40</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+6:44">John 6:44</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+6:54">John 6:54</span>) "with the majesty of a solemn refrain." In <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+7:37">John 7:37</span> it is the last day of the feast of tabernacles, but in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+11:24">John 11:24</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+12:48">John 12:48</span> of the day of judgment as here. Christ is the Agent of the general resurrection in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+5:28">John 5:28</span> as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1co+15:22">1 Corinthians 15:22</span> while here only the resurrection of the righteous is mentioned. </p>
