<p><b> Should have eternal life </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εχη ζωην αιωνιον</span></span>). Present active subjunctive with <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ινα</span></span>, "that he may keep on having eternal life" as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+3:15">John 3:15</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+3:36">John 3:36</span>.</p>
<p><b>Beholdeth </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">θεωρων</span></span>). With the eye of faith as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+12:45">John 12:45</span>.</p>
<p><b>And I will raise him up </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κα αναστησω</span></span>). Future active indicative (volitive future, promise) as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+6:54">John 6:54</span>. </p>
