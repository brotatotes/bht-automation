<p><b> Murmured </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εγογγυζον</span></span>). Imperfect active of the onomatopoetic verb <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">γογγυζω</span></span>, late verb in LXX (murmuring against Moses), papyri (vernacular), like the cooing of doves or the buzzing of bees. These Galilean Jews are puzzled over what Jesus had said (verses <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+6:33">John 6:33</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+6:35">John 6:35</span>) about his being the bread of God come down from heaven. </p>
