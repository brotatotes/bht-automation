<p><b> This one has seen the Father </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ουτος εωρακεν τον πατερα</span></span>). Perfect active indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">οραω</span></span>. With the eyes no one has seen God (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+1:18">John 1:18</span>) save the Son who is "from God" in origin (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+1:1">John 1:1</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+1:14">John 1:14</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+7:29">John 7:29</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+16:27">John 16:27</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+17:8">John 17:8</span>). The only way for others to see God is to see Christ (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+14:9">John 14:9</span>). </p>
