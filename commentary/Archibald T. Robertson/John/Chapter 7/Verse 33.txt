<p><b> Yet a little while </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ετ χρονον μικρον</span></span>). Accusative of extent of time. It was only six months to the last passover of Christ's ministry and he knew that the end was near.</p>
<p><b>I go unto him that sent me </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">υπαγω προς τον πεμψαντα με</span></span>). See the same words in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+16:5">John 16:5</span>. <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">Hυπαγω</span></span>, old compound (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">υπο, αγω</span></span>), has the notion of withdrawing (literally, go under). See <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+16:7-10">John 16:7-10</span> for three words for going common in John (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πορευομα</span></span>, go for a purpose, <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">απερχομα</span></span>, to go away, <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">υπαγω</span></span>, to withdraw personally). <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">Hυπαγω</span></span> often in John of going to the Father or God (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+8:14">John 8:14</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+8:21">John 8:21</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+13:3">John 13:3</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+13:33">John 13:33</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+13:36">John 13:36</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+14:4">John 14:4</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+14:5">John 14:5</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+14:28">John 14:28</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+15:16">John 15:16</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+16:4">John 16:4</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+16:7">John 16:7</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+16:10">John 16:10</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+16:17">John 16:17</span>). See <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+6:21">John 6:21</span>. It was enigmatic language to the hearers. </p>
