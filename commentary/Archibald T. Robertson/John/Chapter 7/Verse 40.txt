<p><b> Some of the multitude </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εκ του οχλου</span></span>). <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">Τινες</span></span> (some) to be supplied, a common Greek idiom.</p>
<p><b>Of a truth </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αληθως</span></span>). "Truly." See <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+1:47">John 1:47</span>.</p>
<p><b>The prophet </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ο προφητης</span></span>). The one promised to Moses (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="de+18:15">Deuteronomy 18:15</span>) and long expected. See on John <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+1:21">John 1:21</span>. Proof of the deep impression made by Jesus. </p>
