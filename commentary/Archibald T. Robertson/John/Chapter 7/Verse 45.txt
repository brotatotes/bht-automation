<p><b> Why did ye not bring him? </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">Δια τ ουκ ηγαγετε αυτον;</span></span>). Second aorist active indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αγω</span></span>. Indignant outburst of the Sanhedrin (both Sadducees and Pharisees) at the failure of the (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τους</span></span>, note article here referring to verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+7:32">John 7:32</span>) temple police to arrest Jesus. "Apparently they were sitting in expectation of immediately questioning him" (Dods). They were stunned at this outcome. </p>
