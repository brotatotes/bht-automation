<p><b> He abode still in Galilee </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εμεινεν εν τη Γαλιλαια</span></span>). No "still" (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ετ</span></span>) in the Greek text. The constative aorist active indicative <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εμεινεν</span></span> covers a period of some days. </p>
