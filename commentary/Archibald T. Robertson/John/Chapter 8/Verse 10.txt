<p><b> Lifted up himself </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ανακυψας</span></span>). First aorist active participle of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ανακυπτω</span></span> as in verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+8:7">John 8:7</span>.</p>
<p><b>Where are they? </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">Που εισιν;</span></span>). Jesus had kept on writing on the ground as the accusers had slipped away one by one.</p>
<p><b>Did no man condemn thee? </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ουδεις σε κατεκρινεν;</span></span>). First aorist active indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κατακρινω</span></span>, old and common verb to give judgment against (down on) one, but not in John. No one dared to cast a stone at the woman on Christ's terms. </p>
