<p><b> Again </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">παλιν</span></span>). Probably <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">παλιν</span></span> (again) in verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+8:12">John 8:12</span> refers to a day after the feast is over since the last day is mentioned in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+7:37">John 7:37</span>. So then here again we probably move on to another day still beyond that in verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+8:12">John 8:12</span>.</p>
<p><b>And ye shall seek me </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κα ζητησετε με</span></span>). As in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+7:34">John 7:34</span>, "the search of despair" (Bernard), seeking for the Messiah when it is too late, the tragedy of Judaism today (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+1:11">John 1:11</span>).</p>
<p><b>And ye shall die in your sin </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κα εν τη αμαρτια υμων αποθανεισθε</span></span>). Future middle indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αποθνησκω</span></span> which is the emphatic word here (cf. <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="eze+3:18">Ezekiel 3:18</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="eze+18:18">Ezekiel 18:18</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="pr+24:9">Proverbs 24:9</span>). Note singular <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αμαρτια</span></span> (sin) here, but plural <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αμαρτιαις</span></span> (sins) when the phrase is repeated in verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+8:24">John 8:24</span> (sin in its essence, sin in its acts).</p>
<p><b>Ye cannot come </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">υμεις ου δυνασθε ελθειν</span></span>). Precise language of <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+7:34">John 7:34</span> to the Jews and to the apostles in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+13:33">John 13:33</span>. </p>
