<p><b> They perceived not </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ουκ εγνωσαν</span></span>). Second aorist active indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">γινωσκω</span></span>. "Preoccupied as they were with thoughts of an earthly deliverer" (Westcott) and prejudiced against recognizing Jesus as the one sent from God.</p>
<p><b>That he spake to them of the Father </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">οτ τον πατερα αυτοις ελεγεν</span></span>). Indirect assertion, but with the present indicative (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">λεγε</span></span>) changed to the imperfect (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ελεγεν</span></span>) as was sometimes done (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+2:25">John 2:25</span>) after a secondary tense. </p>
