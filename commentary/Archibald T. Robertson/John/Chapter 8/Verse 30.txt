<p><b> Many believed on him </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πολλο επιστευσαν εις αυτον</span></span>). Ingressive aorist active indicative, came to believe, nominally at any rate, as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+2:23">John 2:23</span>. But the tension was keen and Jesus proceeded to test the faith of these new believers from among the Pharisees. </p>
