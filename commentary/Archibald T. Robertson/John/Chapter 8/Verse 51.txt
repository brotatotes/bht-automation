<p><b> If a man keep my word </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εαν τις τον εμον λογον τηρηση</span></span>). Condition of third class with <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εαν</span></span> and constative aorist active subjunctive of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τηρεω</span></span>. Repeated in verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+8:52">John 8:52</span>. See verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+8:43">John 8:43</span> about hearing the word of Christ. Common phrase in John (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+8:51">John 8:51</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+8:52">John 8:52</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+8:55">John 8:55</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+14:23">John 14:23</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+14:24">John 14:24</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+15:20">John 15:20</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+17:6">John 17:6</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1jo+2:5">1 John 2:5</span>). Probably the same idea as keeping the commands of Christ (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+14:21">John 14:21</span>).</p>
<p><b>He shall never see death </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">θανατον ου μη θεωρηση εις τον αιονα</span></span>). Spiritual death, of course. Strong double negative <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ου μη</span></span> with first aorist active subjunctive of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">θεωρεω</span></span>. The phrase "see death" is a Hebraism (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ps+89:48">Psalms 89:48</span>) and occurs with <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ιδειν</span></span> (see) in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+2:26">Luke 2:26</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="heb+11:5">Hebrews 11:5</span>. No essential difference meant between <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">οραω</span></span> and <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">θεωρεω</span></span>. See <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+14:23">John 14:23</span> for the blessed fellowship the Father and the Son have with the one who keeps Christ's word. </p>
