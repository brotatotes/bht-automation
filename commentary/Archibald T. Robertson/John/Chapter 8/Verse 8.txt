<p><b> Again he stooped down </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">παλιν κατακυψας</span></span>). First aorist active participle of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κατακυπτω</span></span>, old and rare verb (in Epictetus II, 16. 22) instead of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κατω κυψας</span></span> in verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+8:6">John 8:6</span>.</p>
<p><b>With his finger </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τω δακτυλω</span></span>). Not genuine, only in D and Western class.</p>
<p><b>Wrote on the ground </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εγραφεν εις την γην</span></span>). Imperfect active of the simplex <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">γραφω</span></span>, not <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">καταγραφω</span></span>. The second picture of Jesus writing on the ground. </p>
