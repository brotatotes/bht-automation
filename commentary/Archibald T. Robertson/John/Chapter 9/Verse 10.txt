<p><b> How then were thine eyes opened? </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">Πως ουν ηνεωιχθησαν σου ο οφθαλμοι;</span></span>). Natural and logical (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ουν</span></span>) question. First aorist passive indicative (triple augment) of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ανοιγω</span></span>. These neighbours admit the fact and want the manner ("how") of the cure made clear. </p>
