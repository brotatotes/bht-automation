<p><b> The man that is called Jesus </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ο ανθρωπος ο λεγομενος Ιησους</span></span>). He does not yet know Jesus as the Messiah the Son of God (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+9:36">John 9:36</span>).</p>
<p><b>I received sight </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ανεβλεψα</span></span>). First aorist active indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αναβλεπω</span></span>, old verb to see again, to recover sight, not strictly true of this man who had never seen. He got back sight that he had never had. Originally the verb means to look up (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+14:19">Matthew 14:19</span>). </p>
