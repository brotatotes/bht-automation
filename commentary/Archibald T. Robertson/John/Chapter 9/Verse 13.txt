<p><b> They bring him </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αγουσιν αυτον</span></span>). Vivid dramatic present active of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αγω</span></span>. These neighbours bring him.</p>
<p><b>To the Pharisees </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">προς τους Φαρισαιους</span></span>). The accepted professional teachers who posed as knowing everything. The scribes were usually Pharisees.</p>
<p><b>Him that aforetime was blind </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τον ποτε τυφλον</span></span>). Simply, "the once blind man." </p>
