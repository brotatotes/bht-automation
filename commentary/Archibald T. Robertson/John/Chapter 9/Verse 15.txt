<p><b> Again </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">παλιν</span></span>). Besides the questioning of the neighbours (verses <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+9:8">John 9:8</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+9:9">John 9:9</span>).</p>
<p><b>Therefore </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ουν</span></span>). Since he has been brought to the Pharisees who must make a show of wisdom.</p>
<p><b>Also asked him </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ηρωτων αυτον κα</span></span>). Inchoative imperfect active of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ερωταω</span></span>, "began also to question him."</p>
<p><b>How he received his sight </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πως ανεβλεψεν</span></span>). No denial as yet of the fact, only interest in the "how."</p>
<p><b>He put </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επεθηκεν</span></span>). Genuine here, but see verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+9:6">John 9:6</span>.</p>
<p><b>And lo see </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κα βλεπω</span></span>). That is the overwhelming fact. </p>
