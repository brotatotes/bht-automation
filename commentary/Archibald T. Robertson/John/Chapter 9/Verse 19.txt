<p><b> Is this your son who ye say was born blind? how doth he now see? </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">Hουτος εστιν ο υιος υμων, ον υμεις ληγετε οτ τυφλος εγεννηθη; πως ουν βλεπε αρτι;</span></span>). It was shrewdly put with three questions in one in order to confuse the parents if possible and give the hostile Pharisees a handle. </p>
