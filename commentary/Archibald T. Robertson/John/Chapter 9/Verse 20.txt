<p><b> We know that this is our son, and that he was born blind </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">Οιδαμεν οτ ουτος εστιν ο υιος ημων κα οτ τυφλος εγεννηθη</span></span>). These two questions the parents answer clearly and thus cut the ground from under the disbelief of these Pharisees as to the fact of the cure (verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+9:18">John 9:18</span>). So these Pharisees made a failure here. </p>
