<p><b> Are we also blind? </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">Μη κα ημεις τυφλο εσμεν;</span></span>). Negative answer expected (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μη</span></span>) and yet these Pharisees who overheard the words of Jesus to the new convert vaguely suspected that Jesus was referring to them by the last clause. Up in Galilee Jesus had called the Pharisees blind guides who stumble into the pit (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+15:14">Matthew 15:14</span>). </p>
