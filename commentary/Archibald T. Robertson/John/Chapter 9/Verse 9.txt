<p><b> Nay but he is like him </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">Ουχι, αλλα ομοιος αυτω εστιν</span></span>). Vigorous denial (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ουχ</span></span>) and mere similarity suggested. Associative instrumental case <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αυτο</span></span> after <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ομοιος</span></span>. The crowd is divided.</p>
<p><b>He said </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εκεινος ελεγεν</span></span>). Emphatic demonstrative (as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+9:11">John 9:11</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+9:12">John 9:12</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+9:25">John 9:25</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+9:36">John 9:36</span>), "That one spake up." He knew. </p>
