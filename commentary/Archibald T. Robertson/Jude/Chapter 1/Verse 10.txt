<p><b> Whatsoever things they know not </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">οσα ουκ οιδασιν</span></span>). Here <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2pe+2:12">2 Peter 2:12</span> has <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εν οις αγνοουσιν</span></span>. The rest of the sentence is smoother than <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2pe+2:12">2 Peter 2:12</span>.</p>
<p><b>Naturally </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">φυσικως</span></span>). Here only in N.T. <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2pe+2:12">2 Peter 2:12</span> has <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">γεγεννημενα φυσικα</span></span>. Jude has the article <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τα</span></span> with <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αλογα ζωα</span></span> and the present passive <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">φθειροντα</span></span> instead of the future passive <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">φθαρησοντα</span></span>. </p>
