<p><b> To execute judgment </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ποιησα κρισιν</span></span>). "To do justice."</p>
<p><b>To convict </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ελεγξα</span></span>). First aorist (effective) active infinitive like <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ποιησα</span></span> before it.</p>
<p><b>Ungodly </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ασεβεις</span></span>). See verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="jude+1:4">Jude 1:4</span> and end of this verse.</p>
<p><b>Of ungodliness </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ασεβειας</span></span>). Old word as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+1:18">Romans 1:18</span>, plural in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="jude+1:18">Jude 1:18</span> as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+11:26">Romans 11:26</span>.</p>
<p><b>Which </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ων</span></span>). Genitive by attraction from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">α</span></span> (cognate accusative with <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ησεβησαν</span></span>, old verb, to act impiously, here alone in N.T. save some MSS. in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2pe+2:6">2 Peter 2:6</span>) to agree with the antecedent <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εργων</span></span> (deeds).</p>
<p><b>Hard things </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">σκληρων</span></span>). Harsh, rough things as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+6:60">John 6:60</span>.</p>
<p><b>Which </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ων</span></span>). Genitive by attraction from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">α</span></span> (object of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ελαλησαν</span></span>, first aorist active indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">λαλεω</span></span>) to the case of the antecedent <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">σκληρων</span></span>. Four times in this verse as a sort of refrain <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ασεβεις</span></span> (twice), <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ασεβειασ, ησεβησαν</span></span>. </p>
