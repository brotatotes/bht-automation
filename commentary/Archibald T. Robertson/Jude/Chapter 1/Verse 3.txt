<p><b> Beloved </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αγαπητο</span></span>). As in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="3jo+1:2">3 John 1:2</span>.</p>
<p><b>All diligence </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πασαν σπουδην</span></span>). As in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2pe+1:5">2 Peter 1:5</span>.</p>
<p><b>Of our common salvation </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">περ της κοινης ημων σωτηριας</span></span>). See this use of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κοινος</span></span> (common to all) in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="tit+1:4">Titus 1:4</span> with <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πιστις</span></span>, while in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2pe+1:1">2 Peter 1:1</span> we have <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ισοτιμον πιστιν</span></span>, which see.</p>
<p><b>I was constrained </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αναγκην εσχον</span></span>). "I had necessity" like <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+14:18">Luke 14:18</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="heb+7:27">Hebrews 7:27</span>.</p>
<p><b>To contend earnestly </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επαγωνιζεσθα</span></span>). Late and rare (in Plutarch, inscriptions) compound, here only in N.T. A little additional (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επ</span></span>) striving to the already strong <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αγωνιζεσθα</span></span> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αγων</span></span> contest). Cf. <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1ti+6:12">1 Timothy 6:12</span> <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αγωνιζου τον καλον αγωνα</span></span>.</p>
<p><b>For the faith </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τηι--πιστε</span></span>). Dative of advantage. Here not in the original sense of trust, but rather of the thing believed as in verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="jude+1:20">Jude 1:20</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ga+1:23">Galatians 1:23</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ga+3:23">Galatians 3:23</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="php+1:27">Philippians 1:27</span>.</p>
<p><b>Once for all delivered </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">απαξ παραδοθειση</span></span>). First aorist passive participle feminine dative singular of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">παραδιδωμ</span></span>, for which see <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2pe+2:21">2 Peter 2:21</span>. See also <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2th+2:15">2 Thessalonians 2:15</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1co+11:2">1 Corinthians 11:2</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1ti+6:20">1 Timothy 6:20</span>. </p>
