<p><b> Yet </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μεντο</span></span>). See <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+4:27">John 4:27</span>. In spite of these warnings.</p>
<p><b>In like manner </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ομοιως</span></span>). Like the cities of the plain.</p>
<p><b>These also </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κα ουτο</span></span>). The false teachers of verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="jude+1:4">Jude 1:4</span>.</p>
<p><b>In their dreamings </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ενυπνιαζομενο</span></span>). Present middle participle of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ενυπνιαζω</span></span>, to dream (from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ενυπνιον</span></span> dream, <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+2:17">Acts 2:17</span>, from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εν</span></span> and <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">υπνος</span></span>, in sleep), in Aristotle, Hippocrates, Plutarch, papyri, LXX (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joe+2:28">Joel 2:28</span>), here only in N.T. Cf. <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="col+2:18">Colossians 2:18</span>.</p>
<p><b>Defile </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μιαινουσιν</span></span>). Present active indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μιναινω</span></span>, old verb, to stain, with sin (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="tit+1:15">Titus 1:15</span>) as here. <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2pe+2:10">2 Peter 2:10</span> has <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μιασμου</span></span>.</p>
<p><b>Set at nought </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αθετουσιν</span></span>). Present active indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αθετεω</span></span>, to annul. Both <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κυριοτης</span></span> (dominion) and <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δοξα</span></span> (dignities) occur in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2pe+2:10">2 Peter 2:10</span>, which see for discussion. </p>
