<p><b> Gladness </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αγαλλιασις</span></span>). Only in the LXX and N.T. so far as known. A word for extreme exultation.</p>
<p><b>Rejoice </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">χαρησοντα</span></span>). Second future passive indicative. The coming of a prophet will indeed be an occasion for rejoicing. </p>
