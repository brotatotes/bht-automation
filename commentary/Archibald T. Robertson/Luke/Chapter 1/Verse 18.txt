<p><b> Whereby </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κατα τ</span></span>). According to what. It was too good to be true and Zacharias demanded proof and gives the reason (for, <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">γαρ</span></span>) for his doubt. He had prayed for this blessing and was now sceptical like the disciples in the house of Mary about the return of Peter (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+12:14">Acts 12:14</span>). </p>
