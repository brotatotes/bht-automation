<p><b> Thou shalt be silent </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εση σιωπων</span></span>). Volitive future periphrastic.</p>
<p><b>Not able to speak </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μη δυναμενος λαλησα</span></span>). Negative repetition of the same statement. His dumbness will continue "until" (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αχρ ης ημερας</span></span>) the events come to pass "because" (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ανθ' ων</span></span>). The words were to become reality in due season (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">καιρον</span></span>, not <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">χρονος</span></span>, time). </p>
