<p><b> Were waiting </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ην προσδοκων</span></span>). Periphrastic imperfect again. An old Greek verb for expecting. Appears in papyri and inscriptions. It denotes mental direction whether hope or fear.</p>
<p><b>They marvelled </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εθαυμαζον</span></span>). Imperfect tense, were wondering. The Talmud says that the priest remained only a brief time in the sanctuary.</p>
<p><b>While he tarried </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εν τω χρονιζειν</span></span>). See verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+1:8">Luke 1:8</span> for the same idiom. </p>
