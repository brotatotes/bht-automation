<p><b> Perceived </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επεγνωσαν</span></span>). Second aorist indicative. Clearly knew because he was not able to pronounce the benediction from the steps (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="nu+6:24-26">Numbers 6:24-26</span>).</p>
<p><b>Continued making signs </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ην διανευων</span></span>). Periphrastic imperfect again. He nodded and beckoned back and forth (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δια</span></span>, between). Further proof of a vision that caused his dumbness. </p>
