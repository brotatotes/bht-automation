<p><b> Was sent </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">απεσταλη</span></span>). Second aorist passive indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αποστελλω</span></span> from which <i>apostle</i> comes. The angel Gabriel is God's messenger to Mary as to Zacharias (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+1:19">Luke 1:19</span>). </p>
