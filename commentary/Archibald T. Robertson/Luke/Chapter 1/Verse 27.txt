<p><b> Betrothed </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εμνηστευμενην</span></span>). Perfect passive participle. Betrothal usually lasted a year and unfaithfulness on the part of the bride was punished with death (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="de+23:24">Deuteronomy 23:24</span>). </p>
