<p><b> Shall be no end </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ουκ εστα τελος</span></span>). Luke reports the perpetuity of this Davidic kingdom over the house of Jacob with no Pauline interpretation of the spiritual Israel though that was the true meaning as Luke knew. Joseph was of the house of David (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+1:27">Luke 1:27</span>) and Mary also apparently (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+2:5">Luke 2:5</span>). </p>
