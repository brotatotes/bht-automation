<p><b> Doth magnify </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μεγαλυνε</span></span>). Latin, <i>magnificat</i>. Harnack argues that this is also the song of Elisabeth because a few Latin MSS. have it so, but Mary is correct. She draws her material from the O.T. and sings in the noblest strain. </p>
