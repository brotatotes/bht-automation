<p><b> Fear </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">φοβουμενοις</span></span>). Dative of the present middle participle. Here it is reverential fear as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+10:2">Acts 10:2</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="col+3:22">Colossians 3:22</span>. The bad sense of dread appears in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+21:46">Matthew 21:46</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mr+6:20">Mark 6:20</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+12:4">Luke 12:4</span>. </p>
