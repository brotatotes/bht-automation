<p><b> Showed strength </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εποιησεν κρατος</span></span>). "Made might" (Wycliff). A Hebrew conception as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ps+118:15">Psalms 118:15</span>. Plummer notes six aorist indicatives in this sentence (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+1:51-63">Luke 1:51-63</span>), neither corresponding to our English idiom, which translates here by "hath" each time.</p>
<p><b>Imagination </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">διανοια</span></span>). Intellectual insight, moral understanding. </p>
