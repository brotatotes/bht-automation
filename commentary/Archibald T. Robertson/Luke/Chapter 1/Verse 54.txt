<p><b> Hath holpen </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αντελαβετο</span></span>). Second aorist middle indicative. A very common verb. It means to lay hold of with a view to help or succour.</p>
<p><b>Servant </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">παιδος</span></span>). Here it means "servant," not "son" or "child," its usual meaning. </p>
