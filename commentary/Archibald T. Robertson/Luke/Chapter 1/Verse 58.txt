<p><b> Had magnified </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εμεγαλυνεν</span></span>). Aorist active indicative. Same verb as in verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+1:46">Luke 1:46</span>.</p>
<p><b>Rejoiced with her </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">συνεχαιρον αυτη</span></span>). Imperfect tense and pictures the continual joy of the neighbours, accented also by <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">συν-</span></span> (cf. <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="php+2:18">Philippians 2:18</span>) in its mutual aspect. </p>
