<p><b> Righteous before God </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δικαιο εναντιον του θεου</span></span>). Old Testament conception and idiom. Cf. <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+2:25">Luke 2:25</span> about Simeon. Expanded in Old Testament language. Picture of "noblest product of Old Testament education" (Ragg) is Zacharias and Elisabeth, Mary and Joseph, Simeon and Anna who were "privileged to see with clear eyes the dawn of the New Testament revelation." </p>
