<p><b> Tablet </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πινακιδιον</span></span>). Diminutive of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πινακις</span></span>. In Aristotle and the papyri for writing tablet, probably covered with wax. Sometimes it was a little table, like Shakespeare's "the table of my memory" (Hamlet, i.5). It was used also of a physician's note-book.</p>
<p><b>Wrote, saying </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εγραψεν λεγων</span></span>). Hebrew way of speaking (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2ki+10:6">2 Kings 10:6</span>). </p>
