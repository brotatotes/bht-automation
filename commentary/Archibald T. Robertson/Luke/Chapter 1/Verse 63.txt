 Tablet  (πινακιδιον). Diminutive of πινακις. In Aristotle and the papyri for writing tablet, probably covered with wax. Sometimes it was a little table, like Shakespeare's "the table of my memory" (Hamlet, i.5). It was used also of a physician's note-book.Wrote, saying  (εγραψεν λεγων). Hebrew way of speaking (2 Kings 10:6).
