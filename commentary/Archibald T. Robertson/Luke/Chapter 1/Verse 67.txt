<p><b> Prophesied </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επροφητευσεν</span></span>). Under the guidance of the Holy Spirit. This <i>Benedictus</i> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">Ευλογητος</span></span>,</p>
<p><b>Blessed </b>) of Zacharias (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+1:68-79">Luke 1:68-79</span>) may be what is referred to in verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+1:64">Luke 1:64</span> "he began to speak blessing God" (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ευλογων</span></span>). Nearly every phrase here is found in the O.T. (Psalms and Prophets). He, like Mary, was full of the Holy Spirit and had caught the Messianic message in its highest meaning. </p>
