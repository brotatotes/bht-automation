<p><b> Hath visited </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επεσκεψατο</span></span>). An old Greek word with a Hebraic colouring to look into with a view to help. The papyri have plenty of examples of the verb in the sense of inspecting, examining.</p>
<p><b>Redemption </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">λυτρωσιν</span></span>) here originally referred to political redemption, but with a moral and spiritual basis (verses <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+1:75">Luke 1:75</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+1:77">Luke 1:77</span>). </p>
