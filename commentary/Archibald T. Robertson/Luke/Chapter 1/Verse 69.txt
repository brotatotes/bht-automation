<p><b> Horn of salvation </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κερας σωτηριας</span></span>). A common metaphor in the O.T. (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1sa+2:10">1 Samuel 2:10</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2sa+23:3">2 Samuel 23:3</span>, etc.). It represents strength like the horns of bulls. Cf. <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+132:17">Luke 132:17</span>. </p>
