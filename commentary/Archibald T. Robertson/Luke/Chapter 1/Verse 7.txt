<p><b> Because that </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">καθοτ</span></span>). Good Attic word, according to what. Only in Luke and Acts in the N.T. In the papyri.</p>
<p><b>Well stricken in years </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">προβεβηκοτες εν ταις ημεραις αυτων</span></span>). Wycliff has it right: "Had gone far in their days." Perfect active participle. See also verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+1:18">Luke 1:18</span>. </p>
