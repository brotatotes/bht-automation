<p><b> Yea and thou </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κα συ δε</span></span>). Direct address to the child with forecast of his life (cf. <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+1:13-17">Luke 1:13-17</span>).</p>
<p><b>Prophet </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">προφητης</span></span>). The word here directly applied to the child. Jesus will later call John a prophet and more than a prophet.</p>
<p><b>The Lord </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">Κυριου</span></span>). Jehovah as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+1:16">Luke 1:16</span>. </p>
