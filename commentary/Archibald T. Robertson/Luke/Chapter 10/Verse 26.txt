<p><b> How readest thou? </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πως αναγινωσκεισ;</span></span>). As a lawyer it was his business to know the facts in the law and the proper interpretation of the law. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+7:30">Luke 7:30</span> about <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">νομικος</span></span> (lawyer). The rabbis had a formula, "What readest thou?" </p>
