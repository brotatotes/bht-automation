<p><b> Proved neighbour to him that fell </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πλησιον γεγονενα του εμπεσοντος</span></span>). Second perfect infinitive of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">γινομα</span></span> and second aorist active participle of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εμπιπτω</span></span>. Objective genitive, became neighbour to the one, etc. Jesus has changed the lawyer's standpoint and has put it up to him to decide which of "these three" (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τουτων των τριων</span></span>, priest, Levite, Samaritan) acted like a neighbour to the wounded man. </p>
