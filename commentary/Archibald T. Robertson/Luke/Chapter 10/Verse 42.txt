<p><b> The good portion </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">την αγαθην μεριδα</span></span>). The best dish on the table, fellowship with Jesus. This is the spiritual application of the metaphor of the dishes on the table. Salvation is not "the good portion" for Martha had that also.</p>
<p><b>From her </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αυτης</span></span>). Ablative case after <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αφαιρηθησετα</span></span> (future passive indicative). Jesus pointedly takes Mary's side against Martha's fussiness. </p>
