<p><b> First say </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πρωτον λεγετε</span></span>). Say first. The adverb <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πρωτον</span></span> can be construed with "enter" (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εισελθητε</span></span>), but probably with <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">λεγετε</span></span> is right. The word spoken is the usual oriental salutation. </p>
