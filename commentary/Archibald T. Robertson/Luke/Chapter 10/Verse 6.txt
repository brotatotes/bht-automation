<p><b> A son of peace </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">υιος ειρηνης</span></span>). A Hebraism, though some examples occur in the vernacular <i>Koine</i> papyri. It means one inclined to peace, describing the head of the household.</p>
<p><b>Shall rest </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επαναπαησετα</span></span>). Second future passive of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επαναπαυω</span></span>, a late double compound (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επι, ανα</span></span>) of the common verb <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">παυω</span></span>.</p>
<p><b>It shall turn to you again </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εφ' υμας ανακαμψε</span></span>). Common verb <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ανακαμπτω</span></span>, to bend back, return. The peace in that case will bend back with blessing upon the one who spoke it. </p>
