<p><b> Because ye say </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">οτ λεγετε</span></span>). Jesus here repeats in indirect discourse (accusative and infinitive) the charge made against him in verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+11:15">Luke 11:15</span>. The condition is of the first class, determined as fulfilled. </p>
