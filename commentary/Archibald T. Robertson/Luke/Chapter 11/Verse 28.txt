<p><b> But he said </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αυτος δε ειπεν</span></span>). Jesus in contrast turns attention to others and gives them a beatitude (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μακαριο</span></span>). "The originality of Christ's reply guarantees its historical character. Such a comment is beyond the reach of an inventor" (Plummer). </p>
