<p><b> Whether not </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μη</span></span>). This use of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μη</span></span> in an indirect question is good Greek (Robertson, <i>Grammar</i>, p. 1045). It is a pitiful situation if the very light is darkness. This happens when the eye of the soul is too diseased to see the light of Christ. </p>
