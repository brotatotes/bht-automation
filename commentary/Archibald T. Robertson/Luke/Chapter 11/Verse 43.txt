<p><b> The chief seats in the synagogues </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">την πρωτοκαθεδριαν εν ταις συναγωγαις</span></span>). Singular here, plural in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+23:6">Matthew 23:6</span>. This semi-circular bench faced the congregation. <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+23:6">Matthew 23:6</span> has also the chief place at feasts given by Luke also in that discourse (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+20:46">Luke 20:46</span>) as well as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+14:7">Luke 14:7</span>, a marked characteristic of the Pharisees. </p>
