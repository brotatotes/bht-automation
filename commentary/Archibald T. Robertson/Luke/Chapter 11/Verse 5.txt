 At midnight  (μεσονυκτιου). Genitive of time.And say to him  (κα ειπη αυτω). This is the deliberative subjunctive, but it is preceded by two future indicatives that are deliberative also (εξει, πορευσετα).Lend me  (χρησον μο). First aorist active imperative second singular. Lend menow . From κιχρημ, an old verb, to lend as a matter of friendly interest as opposed to δανειζω, to lend on interest as a business. Only here in the N.T.
