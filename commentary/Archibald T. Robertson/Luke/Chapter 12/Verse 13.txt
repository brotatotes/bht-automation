<p><b> Bid my brother </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ειπε τω αδελφω μου</span></span>). This volunteer from the crowd draws attention to the multitude (verses <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+12:13-21">Luke 12:13-21</span>). He does not ask for arbitration and there is no evidence that his brother was willing for that. He wants a decision by Jesus against his brother. The law (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="de+21:17">Deuteronomy 21:17</span>) was two-thirds to the elder, one-third to the younger. </p>
