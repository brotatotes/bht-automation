<p><b> A judge or a divider </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κριτην η μεριστην</span></span>). Jesus repudiates the position of judge or arbiter in this family fuss. The language reminds one of <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ex+2:14">Exodus 2:14</span>. Jesus is rendering unto Caesar the things of Caesar (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+20:25">Luke 20:25</span>) and shows that his kingdom is not of this world (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+18:36">John 18:36</span>). The word for divider or arbiter (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μεριστης</span></span>) is a late word from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μεριζομα</span></span> (verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+12:13">Luke 12:13</span>) and occurs here only in the N.T. </p>
