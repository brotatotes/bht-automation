<p><b> The ravens </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τους κορακας</span></span>). Nowhere else in the N.T. The name includes the whole crow group of birds (rooks and jackdaws). Like the vultures they are scavengers. <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+6:26">Matthew 6:26</span> has simply "the birds" (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τα πετεινα</span></span>).</p>
<p><b>Storechamber </b> (tameion). Not in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+6:26">Matthew 6:26</span>. Means secret chamber in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+12:3">Luke 12:3</span>.</p>
<p><b>Of how much more </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ποσω μαλλον</span></span>). <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+6:26">Matthew 6:26</span> has question, <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ουχ μαλλον</span></span>. </p>
