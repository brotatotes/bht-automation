 The ravens  (τους κορακας). Nowhere else in the N.T. The name includes the whole crow group of birds (rooks and jackdaws). Like the vultures they are scavengers. Matthew 6:26 has simply "the birds" (τα πετεινα).Storechamber  (tameion). Not in Matthew 6:26. Means secret chamber in Luke 12:3.Of how much more  (ποσω μαλλον). Matthew 6:26 has question, ουχ μαλλον.
