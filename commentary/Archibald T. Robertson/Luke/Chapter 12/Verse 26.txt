<p><b> Not able to do even that which is least </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ουδε ελαχιστον δυνασθε</span></span>). Negative <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ουδε</span></span> in the condition of the first class. Elative superlative, very small. This verse not in Matthew and omitted in D. Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+12:27">Luke 12:27</span> as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+6:28">Matthew 6:28</span>, save that the verbs for toil and spin are plural in Matthew and singular here (neuter plural subject, <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τα κρινα</span></span>). </p>
