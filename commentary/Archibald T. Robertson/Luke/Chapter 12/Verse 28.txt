<p><b> Clothe </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αμφιαζε</span></span>). Late Greek verb in the <i>Koine</i> (papyri) for the older form <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αμφιεννυμ</span></span> (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+6:30">Matthew 6:30</span>). See Matthew for discussion of details. Matthew has "the grass of the field" instead of "the grass in the field" as here. </p>
