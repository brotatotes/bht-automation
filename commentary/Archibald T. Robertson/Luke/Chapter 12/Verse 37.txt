<p><b> He shall gird himself </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">περιζωσετα</span></span>). Direct future middle. Jesus did this (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+13:4">John 13:4</span>), not out of gratitude, but to give the apostles an object lesson in humility. See the usual course in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+17:7-10">Luke 17:7-10</span> with also the direct middle (verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+12:8">Luke 12:8</span>) of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">περισωννυω</span></span>. </p>
