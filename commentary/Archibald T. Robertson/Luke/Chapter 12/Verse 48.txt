<p><b> To whomsoever much is given </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">παντ δε ω εδοθη πολυ</span></span>). Here is inverse attraction from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ο</span></span> to <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">παντ</span></span> (Robertson, <i>Grammar</i>, pp. 767f.). Note <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">παρ' αυτου</span></span> (from him) without any regard to <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">παντ</span></span>.</p>
<p><b>They commit </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">παρεθεντο</span></span>). Second aorist middle indicative, timeless or gnomic aorist. Note the impersonal plural after the passive voice just before. </p>
