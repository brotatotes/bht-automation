<p><b> Till thou have paid </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εως αποδωις</span></span>). Second aorist active subjunctive of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αποδιδωμ</span></span>, to pay back in full.</p>
<p><b>The last mite </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">το εσχατον λεπτον</span></span>). From <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">λεπω</span></span>, to peel off the bark. Very small brass coin, one-eighth of an ounce. In the N.T. only here and <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+21:2">Luke 21:2</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mr+12:42">Mark 12:42</span> (the poor widow's mite) which see. </p>
