 Till thou have paid  (εως αποδωις). Second aorist active subjunctive of αποδιδωμ, to pay back in full.The last mite  (το εσχατον λεπτον). From λεπω, to peel off the bark. Very small brass coin, one-eighth of an ounce. In the N.T. only here and Luke 21:2; Mark 12:42 (the poor widow's mite) which see.
