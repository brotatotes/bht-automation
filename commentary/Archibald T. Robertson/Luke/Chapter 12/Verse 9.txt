<p><b> Shall be denied </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">απαρνηθησετα</span></span>). First future passive of the compound verb <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">απαρνεομα</span></span>. Here <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+10:33">Matthew 10:33</span> has <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αρνησομα</span></span> simply. Instead of "in the presence of the angels of God" (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εμπροσθεν των αγγελων του θεου</span></span>) <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+10:33">Matthew 10:33</span> has "before my Father who is in heaven." </p>
