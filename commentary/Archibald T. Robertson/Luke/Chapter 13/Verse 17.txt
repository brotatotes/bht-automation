<p><b> Were put to shame </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κατηισχυνοντο</span></span>). Imperfect passive of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">καταισχυνω</span></span>, old verb, to make ashamed, make one feel ashamed. Passive here, to blush with shame at their predicament.</p>
<p><b>Rejoiced </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εχαιρεν</span></span>). Imperfect active. Sharp contrast in the emotions of the two groups.</p>
<p><b>Were done </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">γινομενοις</span></span>). Present middle participle, were continually being done. </p>
