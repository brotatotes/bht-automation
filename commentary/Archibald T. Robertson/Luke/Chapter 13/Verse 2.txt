<p><b> Sinners above all </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αμαρτωλο παρα παντας</span></span>). <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">Παρα</span></span> means "beside," placed beside all the Galileans, and so beyond or above (with the accusative).</p>
<p><b>Have suffered </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πεπονθασιν</span></span>). Second perfect active indicative third plural from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πασχω</span></span>, common verb, to experience, suffer. The tense notes that it is "an irrevocable fact" (Bruce). </p>
