<p><b> Are they few that be saved? </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ε ολιγο ο σωζομενοι;</span></span>). Note use of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ε</span></span> as an interrogative which can be explained as ellipsis or as <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ει=η</span></span> (Robertson, <i>Grammar</i>, p. 1024). This was an academic theological problem with the rabbis, the number of the elect. </p>
