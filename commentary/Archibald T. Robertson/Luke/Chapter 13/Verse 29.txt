<p><b> Shall sit down </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ανακλιθησοντα</span></span>). Future passive indicative third plural. Recline, of course, is the figure of this heavenly banquet. Jesus does not mean that these will be saved in different ways, but only that many will come from all the four quarters of the earth. </p>
