<p><b> Except ye repent </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εαν μη μετανοητε</span></span>). Present active subjunctive of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μετανοεω</span></span>, to change mind and conduct, linear action, keep on changing. Condition of third class, undetermined, but with prospect of determination.</p>
<p><b>Ye shall perish </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">απολεισθε</span></span>). Future middle indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">απολλυμ</span></span> and intransitive. Common verb. </p>
