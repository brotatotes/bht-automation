<p><b> Till I shall dig </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εως οτου σκαψω</span></span>). First aorist active subjunctive like <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">βαλω</span></span> (second aorist active subjunctive of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">βαλλω</span></span>), both common verbs.</p>
