<p><b> And if it bear fruit thenceforth </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κ'αν μεν ποιηση καρπον εις το μελλον</span></span>). Aposiopesis, sudden breaking off for effect (Robertson, <i>Grammar</i>, p. 1203). See it also in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mr+11:32">Mark 11:32</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+23:9">Acts 23:9</span>. Trench (<i>Parables</i>) tells a story like this of intercession for the fig tree for one year more which is widely current among the Arabs today who say that it will certainly bear fruit this time. </p>
