 When he went  (εν τω ελθειν αυτον). Luke's favourite temporal clause = "on the going as to him."That  (κα). Another common Lukan idiom, και=οτ after εγενετο, like Hebrew wav.They  (αυτο). Emphatic.Were watching  (ησαν παρατηρουμενο). Periphrastic imperfect middle. Note force of αυτο, middle voice, and παρα-. They were themselves watching on the side (on the sly), watching insidiously, with evil intent as in Mark 3:2 (active).
