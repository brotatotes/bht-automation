<p><b> When thou makest a feast </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">οταν δοχην ποιηις</span></span>). <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">Hοταν</span></span> and the present subjunctive in an indefinite temporal clause. <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">Δοχη</span></span> means reception as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+5:29">Luke 5:29</span>, late word, only in these two passages in the N.T. Note absence of article with these adjectives in the Greek (poor people, maimed folks, lame people, blind people). </p>
