<p><b> His servant </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τον δουλον αυτου</span></span>). His bondservant. <i>Vocator</i> or Summoner (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="es+5:8">Esther 5:8</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="es+6:14">Esther 6:14</span>). This second summons was the custom then as now with wealthy Arabs. Tristram (<i>Eastern Customs</i>, p. 82) says: "To refuse the second summons would be an insult, which is equivalent among the Arab tribes to a declaration of war." </p>
