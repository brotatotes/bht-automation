<p><b> Which had the dropsy </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">υδρωπικος</span></span>). Late and medical word from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">υδωρ</span></span> (water), one who has internal water (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">υδρωπς</span></span>). Here only in the N.T. and only example of the disease healed by Jesus and recorded. </p>
