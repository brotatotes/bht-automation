<p><b> And he turned </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κα στραφεις</span></span>). Second aorist passive participle of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">στρεφω</span></span>, common verb. It is a dramatic act on the part of Jesus, a deliberate effort to check the wild and unthinking enthusiasm of the crowds who followed just to be following. Note "many multitudes" (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">οχλο πολλο</span></span>) and the imperfect tense <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">συνεπορευοντο</span></span>, were going along with him. </p>
