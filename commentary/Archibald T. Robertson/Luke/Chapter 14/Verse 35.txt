<p><b> Dunghill </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κοπριαν</span></span>). Later word in the <i>Koine</i> vernacular. Here only in the N.T., though in the LXX.</p>
<p><b>Men cast it out </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εξω βαλλουσιν αυτο</span></span>). Impersonal plural. This saying about salt is another of Christ's repeated sayings (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+5:13">Matthew 5:13</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mr+9:50">Mark 9:50</span>). Another repeated saying is the one here about having ears to hear (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+8:8">Luke 8:8</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+14:35">Luke 14:35</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+11:15">Matthew 11:15</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+13:43">Matthew 13:43</span>). </p>
