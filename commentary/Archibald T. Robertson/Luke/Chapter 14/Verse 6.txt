<p><b> Could not answer again </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ουκ ισχυσαν ανταποκριθηνα</span></span>). Did not have strength to answer back or in turn (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αντι-</span></span>) as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+9:20">Romans 9:20</span>. They could not take up the argument and were helpless. They hated to admit that they cared more for an ox or ass or even a son than for this poor dropsical man. </p>
