<p><b> Sit not down </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μη κατακλιθηις</span></span>). First aorist (ingressive) passive subjunctive of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κατακλινω</span></span>, to recline. Old verb, but peculiar to Luke in the N.T. (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+7:36">Luke 7:36</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+9:14">Luke 9:14</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+14:8">Luke 14:8</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+24:30">Luke 24:30</span>).</p>
<p><b>Be bidden </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">η κεκλημενος</span></span>). Periphrastic perfect passive subjunctive of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">καλεω</span></span> after <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μη ποτε</span></span>. </p>
