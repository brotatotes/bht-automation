<p><b> There is joy </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">γινετα χαρα</span></span>). More exactly, joy arises. Futuristic present of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">γινομα</span></span> (cf. <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εστα</span></span> in verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+15:7">Luke 15:7</span>).</p>
<p><b>In the presence of the angels of God </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ενωπιον των αγγελων του θεου</span></span>). That is to say, the joy of God himself. The angels are in a sense the neighbours of God. </p>
