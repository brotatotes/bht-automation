<p><b> I will arise and go </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αναστας προρευσομα</span></span>). This determination is the act of the will after he comes to himself and sees his real condition.</p>
<p><b>I did sin </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ημαρτον</span></span>). That is the hard word to say and he will say it first. The word means to miss the mark. I shot my bolt and I missed my aim (compare the high-handed demand in verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+15:12">Luke 15:12</span>). </p>
