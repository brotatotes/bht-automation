<p><b> But he was angry </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ωργισθη</span></span>). First aorist (ingressive) passive indicative. But he became angry, he flew into a rage (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">οργη</span></span>). This was the explosion as the result of long resentment towards the wayward brother and suspicion of the father's partiality for the erring son.</p>
<p><b>Would not go in </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ουκ ηθελεν εισελθειν</span></span>). Imperfect tense (was not willing, refused) and aorist active (ingressive) infinitive.</p>
<p><b>Entreated </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">παρεκαλε</span></span>). Imperfect tense, he kept on beseeching him. </p>
