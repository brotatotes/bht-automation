<p><b> Her friends and neighbours </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τας φιλας κα γειτονας</span></span>). Note single article and female friends (feminine article and <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">φιλας</span></span>). <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">Hεως ου ευρη</span></span> here as in verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+15:4">Luke 15:4</span>, only <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ου</span></span> added after <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εως</span></span> (until which time) as often.</p>
<p><b>Which I lost </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ην απωλεσα</span></span>). First aorist active indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">απολλυμ</span></span>. She lost the coin (note article). The shepherd did not lose the one sheep. </p>
