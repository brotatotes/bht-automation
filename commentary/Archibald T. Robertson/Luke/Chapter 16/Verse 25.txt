 Receivedst  (απελαβες). Second aorist indicative of απολαμβανω, old verb to get back what is promised and in full. See also Luke 6:34; Luke 18:30; Luke 23:41.Evil things  (τα κακα). Not "his," but "the evil things" that came upon him.Thou art in anguish  (οδυνασα). Like καυχασα in Romans 2:17. They contracted -αεσα without the loss of ς. Common in the Koine.
