<p><b> Beside all this </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εν πασ τουτοις</span></span>).</p>
<p><b>In all these things </b> (or regions).</p>
<p><b>Gulf </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">χασμα</span></span>). An old word from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">χαινω</span></span>, to yawn, our chasm, a gaping opening. Only here in the N.T.</p>
<p><b>Is fixed </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εστηρικτα</span></span>). Perfect passive indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">στηριζω</span></span>, old verb (see on <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+9:51">Luke 9:51</span>). Permanent chasm.</p>
<p><b>May not be able </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μη δυνωντα</span></span>). Present middle subjunctive of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δυναμα</span></span>. The chasm is there on purpose (<b> that not </b>, <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">οπως μη</span></span>) to prevent communication. </p>
