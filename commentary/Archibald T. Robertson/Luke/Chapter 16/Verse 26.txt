 Beside all this  (εν πασ τουτοις).In all these things  (or regions).Gulf  (χασμα). An old word from χαινω, to yawn, our chasm, a gaping opening. Only here in the N.T.Is fixed  (εστηρικτα). Perfect passive indicative of στηριζω, old verb (see on Luke 9:51). Permanent chasm.May not be able  (μη δυνωντα). Present middle subjunctive of δυναμα. The chasm is there on purpose ( that not , οπως μη) to prevent communication.
