<p><b> That he may testify </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">οπως διαμαρτυρητα</span></span>). An old verb for solemn and thorough (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δια-</span></span>) witness. The Rich Man labours under the delusion that his five brothers will believe the testimony of Lazarus as a man from the dead. </p>
