<p><b> Within himself </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εν εαυτω</span></span>). As soon as he had time to think the thing over carefully. He knew that he was guilty of embezzlement of the Master's funds.</p>
<p><b>Taketh away </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αφαιρειτα</span></span>). Present (linear) middle indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αφαιρεω</span></span>, old verb to take away. Here the middle present means, He is taking away for himself.</p>
<p><b>To beg I am not ashamed </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επαιτειν αισχυνομα</span></span>). The infinitive with <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αισχυνομα</span></span> means ashamed to begin to beg. The participle, <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επαιτων αισχυνομα</span></span> would mean, ashamed while begging, ashamed of begging while doing it. </p>
