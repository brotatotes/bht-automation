<p><b> They will repent </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μετανοησουσιν</span></span>). The Rich Man had failed to do this and he now sees that it is the one thing lacking. It is not wealth, not poverty, not alms, not influence, but repentance that is needed. He had thought repentance was for others, not for all. </p>
