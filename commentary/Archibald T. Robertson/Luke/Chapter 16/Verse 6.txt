<p><b> Measures </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">βατους</span></span>). Transliterated word for Hebrew <i>bath</i>, between eight and nine gallons. Here alone in the N.T. Not the same word as <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">βατος</span></span> (<b> bush </b>) in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+6:44">Luke 6:44</span>.</p>
<p><b>Thy bond </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">σου τα γραμματα</span></span>). Thy writings, thy contracts, thy note.</p>
<p><b>Quickly </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ταχεως</span></span>). It was a secret arrangement and speed was essential. </p>
