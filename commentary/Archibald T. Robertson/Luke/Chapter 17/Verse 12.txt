<p><b> Which stood afar off </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ο ανεστησαν πορρωθεν</span></span>). The margin of Westcott and Hort reads simply <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εστησαν</span></span>. The compound read by B means "rose up," but they stood at a distance (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="le+13:45">Leviticus 13:45</span>). The first healing of a leper (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+5:12-16">Luke 5:12-16</span>) like this is given by Luke only. </p>
