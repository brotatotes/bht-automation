<p><b> And he was a Samaritan </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κα αυτος ην Σαμαρειτης</span></span>). This touch colours the whole incident. The one man who felt grateful enough to come back and thank Jesus for the blessing was a despised Samaritan. The <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αυτος</span></span> has point here. </p>
