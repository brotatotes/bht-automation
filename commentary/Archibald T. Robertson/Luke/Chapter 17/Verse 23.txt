<p><b> Go not away nor follow after them </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μη απελθητε μηδε διωξητε</span></span>). Westcott and Hort bracket <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">απελθητε μηδε</span></span>. Note aorist subjunctive with <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μη</span></span> in prohibition, ingressive aorist. Do not rush after those who set times and places for the second advent. The Messiah was already present in the first advent (verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+17:21">Luke 17:21</span>) though the Pharisees did not know it. </p>
