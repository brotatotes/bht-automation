<p><b> Lighteneth </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αστραπτουσα</span></span>). An old and common verb, though only here and <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+24:4">Luke 24:4</span> in the N.T. The second coming will be sudden and universally visible. There are still some poor souls who are waiting in Jerusalem under the delusion that Jesus will come there and nowhere else. </p>
