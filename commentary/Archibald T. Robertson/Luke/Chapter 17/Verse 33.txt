<p><b> Shall preserve it </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ζωογονησε αυτην</span></span>). Or save it alive. Here only in the N.T. except <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1ti+6:13">1 Timothy 6:13</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+7:19">Acts 7:19</span>. It is a late word and common in medical writers, to bring forth alive (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ζωοσ, γενω</span></span>) and here to keep alive. </p>
