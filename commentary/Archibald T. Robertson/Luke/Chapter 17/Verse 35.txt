<p><b> Shall be grinding </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εσοντα αληθουσα</span></span>). <p><b>Together </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επ το αυτο</span></span>). In the same place, near together as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+2:1">Acts 2:1</span>. </p>
