<p><b> Twice in the week </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δις του σαββατου</span></span>). One fast a year was required by the law (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="le+16:29">Leviticus 16:29</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="nu+29:7">Numbers 29:7</span>). The Pharisees added others, twice a week between passover and pentecost, and between tabernacles and dedication of the temple.</p>
<p><b>I get </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κτωμα</span></span>). Present middle indicative, not perfect middle <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κεκτημα</span></span> (I possess). He gave a tithe of his income, not of his property. </p>
