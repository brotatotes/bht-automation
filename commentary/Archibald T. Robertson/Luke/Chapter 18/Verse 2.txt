<p>Regarded not (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μη εντρεπομενος</span></span>). Present middle participle of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εντρεπω</span></span>, old verb, to turn one on himself, to shame one, to reverence one. This was a "hard-boiled" judge who knew no one as his superior. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+21:37">Matthew 21:37</span>. </p>
