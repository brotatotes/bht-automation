<p><b> Then who </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κα τις</span></span>). Literally,</p>
<p><b>and who </b>. The <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κα</span></span> calls attention to what has just been said. Wealth was assumed to be mark of divine favour, not a hindrance to salvation. </p>
