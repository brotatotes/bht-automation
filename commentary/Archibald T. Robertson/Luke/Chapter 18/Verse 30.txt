<p><b> Shall not receive </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ουχ μη λαβη</span></span>). Very strong double negative with aorist active subjunctive of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">λαμβανω</span></span>.</p>
<p><b>Manifold more </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πολλαπλασιονα</span></span>). Late Greek word, here alone in the N.T. save <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+19:29">Matthew 19:29</span> where Westcott and Hort have it though many MSS. there read <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εκατονπλασιονα</span></span> (a hundredfold) as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mr+10:30">Mark 10:30</span>. </p>
