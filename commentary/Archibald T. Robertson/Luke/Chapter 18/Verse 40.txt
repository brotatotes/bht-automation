<p><b> Stood </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">σταθεις</span></span>). First aorist passive where <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mr+10:49">Mark 10:49</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+20:32">Matthew 20:32</span> have <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">στας</span></span> (second aorist active) translated "stood still." One is as "still" as the other. The first is that Jesus " stopped."</p>
<p><b>Be brought </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αχθηνα</span></span>). First aorist infinitive in indirect command. </p>
