<p><b> The unrighteous judge </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ο κριτης της αδικιας</span></span>). The judge of unrighteousness (marked by unrighteousness), as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+16:8">Luke 16:8</span> we have "the steward of unrighteousness," the same idiom. </p>
