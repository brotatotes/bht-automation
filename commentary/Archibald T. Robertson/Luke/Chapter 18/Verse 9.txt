<p><b> Set all others at naught </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εξουθενουντας τους λοιπους</span></span>). A late verb <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εξουθενεω</span></span>, like <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ουδενεω</span></span>, from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ουθεν</span></span> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ουδεν</span></span>), to consider or treat as nothing. In LXX and chiefly in Luke and Paul in the N.T. </p>
