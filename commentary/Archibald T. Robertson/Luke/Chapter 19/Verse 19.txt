<p><b> Be thou also over </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κα συ επανο γινου</span></span>). Present middle imperative. Keep on becoming over. There is no real reason for identifying this parable of the pounds with the parable of the talents in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+19:25">Luke 19:25</span>. The versatility of Jesus needs to be remembered by those who seek to flatten out everything. </p>
