<p><b> Reign </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">βασιλευσα</span></span>). First aorist active infinitive, ingressive aorist, come to rule.</p>
<p><b>Slay </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κατασφαξατε</span></span>). First aorist active imperative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κατασφαζω</span></span>, to slaughter, an old verb, but only here in the N.T. </p>
