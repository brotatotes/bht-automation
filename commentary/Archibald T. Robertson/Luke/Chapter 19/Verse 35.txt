<p><b> Set Jesus thereon </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επεβιβασαν τον Ιησουν</span></span>). First aorist active. Old verb, to cause to mount, causative verb from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">βαινω</span></span>, to go. In the N.T. only here and <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+10:34">Luke 10:34</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+23:24">Acts 23:24</span>. </p>
