<p><b> If thou hadst known </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ε εγνως</span></span>). Second aorist active indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">γινωσκω</span></span>. Second-class condition, determined as unfulfilled.</p>
<p><b>Even thou </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κα συ</span></span>). Emphatic position of the subject.</p>
<p><b>But now </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">νυν δε</span></span>). Aposiopesis. The conclusion is not expressed and the sudden breaking off and change of structure is most impressive.</p>
<p><b>They are hid </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εκρυβη</span></span>). Second aorist passive indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κρυπτω</span></span>, common verb, to hide. </p>
