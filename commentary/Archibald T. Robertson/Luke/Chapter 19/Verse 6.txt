<p><b> He made haste and came down </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">σπευσας κατεβη</span></span>). Luke repeats the very words of Jesus with the same idiom.</p>
<p><b>Received him joyfully </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">υπεδεξατο αυτον χαιρων</span></span>). The very verb used of Martha's welcome to Jesus (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+10:38">Luke 10:38</span>). "Joyfully" is the present active participle, "rejoicing" (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">χαιρων</span></span>). </p>
