<p><b> Murmured </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">διεγογγυζοντο</span></span>). Imperfect middle of this compound onomatopoetic word <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δια-γογγυζω</span></span>. In <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+5:30">Luke 5:30</span> we have the simple <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">γογγυζω</span></span>, a late word like the cooing doves or the hum of bees. This compound with <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δια-</span></span> is still rarer, but more expressive.</p>
<p><b>To lodge </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">καταλυσα</span></span>). Jesus was the hero of this crowd from Galilee on their way to the passover. But here he had shocked their sensibilities and those of the people of Jericho by inviting himself to be the guest of this chief publican and notorious sinner who had robbed nearly everybody in the city by exorbitant taxes. </p>
