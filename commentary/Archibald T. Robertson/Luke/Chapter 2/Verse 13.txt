<p><b> Host </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">στρατιας</span></span>). A military term for a band of soldiers common in the ancient Greek. Bengel says: "Here the army announces peace."</p>
<p><b>Praising </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αινουντων</span></span>). Construction according to sense (plural, though <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">στρατιας</span></span> is singular). </p>
