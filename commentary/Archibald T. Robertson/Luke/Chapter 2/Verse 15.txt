<p><b> Said to one another </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ελαλουν προς αλληλους</span></span>). Imperfect tense, inchoative, "began to speak," each to the other. It suggests also repetition, they kept saying,</p>
<p><b>Now </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δη</span></span>). A particle of urgency.</p>
<p><b>This thing </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">το ρημα τουτο</span></span>). A Hebraistic and vernacular use of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ρημα</span></span> (something said) as something done. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+1:65">Luke 1:65</span>. The ancient Greek used <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">λογος</span></span> in this same way. </p>
