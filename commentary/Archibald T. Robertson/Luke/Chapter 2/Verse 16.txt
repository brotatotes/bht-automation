<p><b> With haste </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">σπευσαντες</span></span>). Aorist active participle of simultaneous action.</p>
<p><b>Found </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ανευραν</span></span>). Second aorist active indicative of a common Greek verb <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ανευρισκω</span></span>, but only in Luke in the N.T. The compound <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ανα</span></span> suggests a search before finding. </p>
