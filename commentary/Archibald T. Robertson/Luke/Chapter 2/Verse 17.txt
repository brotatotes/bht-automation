<p><b> Made known </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εγνωρισαν</span></span>). To others (verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+2:18">Luke 2:18</span>) besides Joseph and Mary. The verb is common from Aeschylus on, from the root of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">γινωσκω</span></span> (to know). It is both transitive and intransitive in the N.T. </p>
