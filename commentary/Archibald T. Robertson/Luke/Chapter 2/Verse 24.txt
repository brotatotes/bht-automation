<p><b> A pair of turtledoves, or two young pigeons </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">Ζευγος τρυγονων η δυο νοσσους περιστερων</span></span>). The offspring of the poor, costing about sixteen cents, while a lamb would cost nearly two dollars. The "young of pigeons" is the literal meaning. </p>
