<p><b> Each to his own city </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εκαστος εις την εαυτου πολιν</span></span>). A number of papyri in Egypt have the heading enrolment by household (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">απογραφη κατ' οικιαν</span></span>). Here again Luke is vindicated. Each man went to the town where his family register was kept. </p>
