<p><b> Of all the peoples </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">παντων των λαων</span></span>). Not merely Jews. Another illustration of the universality of Luke's Gospel seen already in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+1:70">Luke 1:70</span> in the hymn of Zacharias. The second strophe of the song according to Plummer showing what the Messiah will be to the world after having shown what the Messiah is to Simeon. </p>
