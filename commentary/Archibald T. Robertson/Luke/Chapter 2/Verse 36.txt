<p><b> One Anna a prophetess </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">Hαννα προφητις</span></span>). The word <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">προφητις</span></span> occurs in the N.T. only here and <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+2:20">Revelation 2:20</span>. In old Greek writers it means a woman who interprets oracles. The long parenthesis into verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+2:37">Luke 2:37</span> tells of her great age. Montefiore makes it 106 as she was 15 when married, married 7 years, a widow 84. </p>
