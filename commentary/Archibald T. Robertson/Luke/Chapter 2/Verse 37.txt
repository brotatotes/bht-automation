<p><b> Which departed not </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">η ουκ αφιστατο</span></span>). Imperfect indicative middle. She kept on not leaving. The Spirit kept her in the temple as he led Simon to the temple (Plummer). The case of "the temple" (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">του ιερου</span></span>) is ablative.</p>
<p><b>Night and day </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">νυκτα κα ημεραν</span></span>). Accusative of duration of time, all night and all day. She never missed a service in the temple. </p>
