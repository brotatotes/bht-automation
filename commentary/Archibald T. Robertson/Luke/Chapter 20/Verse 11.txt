<p><b> He sent yet another </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">προσεθετο ετερον πεμψα</span></span>). Literally,</p>
<p><b>he added to send another </b>. A clear Hebraism repeated in verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+20:12">Luke 20:12</span> and also in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+19:11">Luke 19:11</span>. </p>
