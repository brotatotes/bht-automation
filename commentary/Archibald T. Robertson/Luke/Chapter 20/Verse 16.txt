<p><b> God forbid </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μη γενοιτο</span></span>). Optative of wish about the future with <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μη</span></span>. Literally,</p>
<p><b>may it not happen </b>. No word "God" in the Greek. This was the pious protest of the defeated members of the Sanhedrin who began to see the turn of the parable against themselves. </p>
