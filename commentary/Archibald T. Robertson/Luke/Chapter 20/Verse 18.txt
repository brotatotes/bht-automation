<p><b> Shall be broken to pieces </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">συνθλασθησετα</span></span>). Future passive indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">συνθλαω</span></span>, a rather late compound, only here in the N.T. unless <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+21:44">Matthew 21:44</span> is genuine. It means to shatter.</p>

From <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">λικμαω</span></span>, an old verb to winnow and then to grind to powder.
