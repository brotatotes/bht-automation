<p><b> Tribute </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">φορον</span></span>). Old word for the annual tax on land, houses, etc. Mark and Matthew have <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κηνσον</span></span>, which see for this Latin word in Greek letters. The picture on the coin may have been that of Tiberius. </p>
