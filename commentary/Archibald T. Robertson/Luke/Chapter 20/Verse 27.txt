<p><b> There is no resurrection </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αναστασιν μη εινα</span></span>). Accusative and infinitive with negative <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μη</span></span> in indirect assertion. The Sadducees rally after the complete discomfiture of the Pharisees and Herodians. They had a stock conundrum with which they had often gotten a laugh on the Pharisees. So they volunteer to try it on Jesus. For discussion of details here see on <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+22:23-33">Matthew 22:23-33</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mr+12:18-27">Mark 12:18-27</span>. Only a few striking items remain for Luke. </p>
