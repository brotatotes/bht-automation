<p><b> Equal unto the angels </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ισαγγελο</span></span>). A rare and late word from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ισος</span></span>, equal, and <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αγγελος</span></span>. Only here in the N.T. Mark and Matthew have "as angels" (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ως αγγελο</span></span>). Angels do not marry, there is no marriage in heaven.</p>
<p><b>Sons of God, being sons of the resurrection </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">υιο θεου της αναστασεως υιο οντες</span></span>). This Hebraistic phrase, "sons of the resurrection" defines "sons of God" and is a direct answer to the Sadducees. </p>
