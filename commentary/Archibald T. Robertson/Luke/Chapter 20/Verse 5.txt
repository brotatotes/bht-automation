 They reasoned with themselves  (συνελογισαντο). First aorist middle of συλλογιζομα, to bring together accounts, an old word, only here in the N.T. Mark and Matthew have διελογιζοντο (imperfect middle of διαλογιζομα, a kindred verb, to reckon between one another, confer). This form (διελογιζοντο) in verse Luke 20:14 below.If we shall say  (εαν ειπωμεν). Third-class condition with second aorist active subjunctive. Suppose we say! So in verse Luke 20:6.
