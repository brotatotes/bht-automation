<p><b> It shall turn unto you </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αποβησετα υμιν</span></span>). Future middle of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αποβαινω</span></span>. It will come off, turn out for you (dative of advantage).</p>
<p><b>For a testimony </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εις μαρτυριον</span></span>). To their loyalty to Christ. Besides, "the blood of the martyrs is the seed of the church." </p>
