<p><b> Your adversaries </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ο αντικειμενο υμιν</span></span>). Those who stand against, line up face to face with (note <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αντι-</span></span>).</p>
<p><b>To withstand or to gainsay </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αντιστηνα η αντειπειν</span></span>). Two second aorist active infinitives with <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αντι-</span></span> in composition again. But these "antis" will go down before the power of Christ. </p>
