<p><b> Men fainting </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αποψυχοντων ανθρωπων</span></span>). Genitive absolute of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αποψυχω</span></span>, to expire, to breathe off or out. Old word. Here only in N.T.</p>
<p><b>Expectation </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">προσδοκιας</span></span>). Old word from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">προσδοκαω</span></span>, to look for or towards. In the N.T. only here and <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+12:11">Acts 12:11</span>.</p>
<p><b>The world </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τη οικουμενη</span></span>). Dative case, "the inhabited" (earth, <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">γη</span></span>). </p>
