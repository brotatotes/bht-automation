<p><b> And then shall they see </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κα τοτε οψοντα</span></span>). As much as to say that it will be not till then. Clearly the promise of the second coming of the Son of man in glory here (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mr+13:26">Mark 13:26</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+24:30">Matthew 24:30</span>) is pictured as not one certain of immediate realization. The time element is left purposely vague. </p>
