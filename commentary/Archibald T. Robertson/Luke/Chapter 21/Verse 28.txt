<p><b> Look up </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ανακυψατε</span></span>). First aorist active imperative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ανακυπτω</span></span>, to raise up. Here of the soul as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+8:7">John 8:7</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+8:10">John 8:10</span>, but in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+13:11">Luke 13:11</span> of the body. These the only N.T. examples of this common verb.</p>
<p><b>Redemption </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">απολυτρωσις</span></span>). Act of redeeming from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">απολυτροω</span></span>. The final act at the second coming of Christ, a glorious hope. </p>
