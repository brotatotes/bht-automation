<p><b> Shoot forth </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">προβαλωσιν</span></span>). Second aorist active subjunctive of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">προβαλλω</span></span>, common verb, but in the N.T. only here and <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+19:33">Acts 19:33</span>.</p>
<p><b>Summer </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">θερος</span></span>). Not harvest, but summer. Old word, but in the N.T. only here (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mr+13:28">Mark 13:28</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+24:32">Matthew 24:32</span>). </p>
