<p><b> Coming to pass </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">γινομενα</span></span>). Present middle participle of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">γινομα</span></span> and so descriptive of the process.</p>
<p><b>Nigh </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εγγυς</span></span>). The consummation of the kingdom is here meant, not the beginning. </p>
