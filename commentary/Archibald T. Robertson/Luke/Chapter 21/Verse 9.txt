<p><b> Be not terrified </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μη πτοηθητε</span></span>). First aorist passive subjunctive with <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μη</span></span> from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πτοεω</span></span> an old verb to terrify, from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πτοα</span></span>, terror. In the N.T. only here and <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+24:37">Luke 24:37</span>.</p>
<p><b>First </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">Πρωτον</span></span>). It is so easy to forget this and to insist that the end is "immediately" in spite of Christ's explicit denial here.
