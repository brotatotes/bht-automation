<p><b> When you are entered </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εισελθοντων υμων</span></span>). Genitive absolute.</p>
<p><b>Meet you </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">συναντησε υμιν</span></span>). An old verb <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">συνανταω</span></span> (from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">συν</span></span>, with, and <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ανταω</span></span>, to face, <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αντ</span></span>) with associative instrumental (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">υμιν</span></span>). See on <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mr+14:13">Mark 14:13</span> about the "man bearing a pitcher of water." </p>
