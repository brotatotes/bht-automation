 The fruit of the vine  (του γενηματος της αμπελου). So Mark 14:25; Matthew 26:29 and not οινος though it was wine undoubtedly. But the language allows anything that is "the fruit of the vine."Come  (ελθη). Second aorist active subjunctive with εως as in verse Luke 22:16. Here it is the consummation of the kingdom that Jesus has in mind, for the kingdom had already come.
