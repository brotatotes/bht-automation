<p><b> As it hath been determined </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κατα το ωρισμενον</span></span>). Perfect passive participle of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">οριζω</span></span>, to limit or define, mark off the border, our "horizon." But this fact does not absolve Judas of his guilt as the "woe" here makes plain. </p>
