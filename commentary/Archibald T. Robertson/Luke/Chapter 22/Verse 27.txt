<p><b> But I </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">Εγω δε</span></span>). Jesus dares to cite his own conduct, though their leader, to prove his point and to put a stop to their jealous contention for the chief place at this very feast, a wrangling that kept up till Jesus had to arise and give them the object lesson of humility by washing their feet (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+13:1-20">John 13:1-20</span>). </p>
