<p><b> Until thou shalt thrice deny that thou knowest me </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εως τρις με απαρνηση ειδενα</span></span>). "Thrice" is in all four Gospels here for they all give this warning to Peter (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mr+14:30">Mark 14:30</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+26:34">Matthew 26:34</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+22:34">Luke 22:34</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+18:38">John 18:38</span>). Peter will even deny knowing Jesus (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ειδενα</span></span>). </p>
