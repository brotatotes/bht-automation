 Were glad  (εχαρησαν). Second aorist passive indicative of χαιρω as in Mark 14:11. Ingressive aorist, a natural exultation that one of the Twelve had offered to do this thing.Covenanted  (συνεθεντο). Second aorist indicative middle of συντιθημ. An old verb to put together and in the middle with one another. In the N.T. outside of John 9:22 only in Luke (here and Acts 23:20; Acts 24:9). Luke only mentions "money" (αργυριον), but not "thirty pieces" (Matthew 26:15).
