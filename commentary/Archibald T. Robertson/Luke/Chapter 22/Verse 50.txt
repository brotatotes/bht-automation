<p><b> His right ear </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">το ους αυτου το δεξιον</span></span>). <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mr+14:47">Mark 14:47</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+26:51">Matthew 26:51</span> do not mention "right," but Luke the Physician does. <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+18:10">John 18:10</span> follows Luke in this item and also adds the names of Peter and of Malchus since probably both were dead by that time and Peter would not be involved in trouble. </p>
