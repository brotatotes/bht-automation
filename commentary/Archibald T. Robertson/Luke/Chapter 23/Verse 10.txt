<p><b> Stood </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ιστηκεισαν</span></span>). Second perfect active intransitive of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ιστημ</span></span> with sense of imperfect. They stood by while Herod quizzed Jesus and when he refused to answer, they broke loose with their accusations like a pack of hounds with full voice (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ευτονως</span></span>, adverb from adjective <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ευτονος</span></span>, from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ευ</span></span>, well, and <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τεινω</span></span>, to stretch, well tuned). Old word, but in the N.T. only here and <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+18:28">Acts 18:28</span>. </p>
