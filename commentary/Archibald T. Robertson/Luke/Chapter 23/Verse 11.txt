<p><b> Set him at nought </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εξουθενησας</span></span>). First aorist active participle from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εξουθενεω</span></span>, to count as nothing, to treat with utter contempt, as zero.</p>
<p><b>Arraying him in gorgeous apparel </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">περιβαλων εσθητα λαμπραν</span></span>). Second aorist active participle of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">περιβαλλω</span></span>, to fling around one. <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">Λαμπραν</span></span> is brilliant, shining as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="jas+2:2">James 2:2</span>, so different from the modest dress of the Master. This was part of the shame. </p>
