<p><b> For before they were at enmity between themselves </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">προυπηρχον γαρ εν εχθρα οντες προς εαυτους</span></span>). A periphrastic imperfect of the double compound <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">προυπερχω</span></span>, an old verb, to exist (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">υπαρχω</span></span>) previously (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">προ-</span></span>), here alone in the N.T., with <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">οντες</span></span> (participle of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ειμ</span></span>) added. </p>
