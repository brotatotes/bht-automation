<p><b> No nor yet </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αλλ' ουδε</span></span>). But not even.</p>
<p><b>Hath been done by him </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εστιν πεπραγμενον αυτω</span></span>). Periphrastic perfect passive indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πρασσω</span></span>, common verb, to do. The case of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αυτω</span></span> can be regarded as either the dative or the instrumental (Robertson, <i>Grammar</i>, pp. 534,542). </p>
