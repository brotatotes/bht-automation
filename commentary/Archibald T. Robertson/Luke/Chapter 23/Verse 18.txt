<p><b> All together </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πανπληθε</span></span>). An adverb from the adjective <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πανπληθης</span></span>, all together. Used by Dio Cassius. Only here in the N.T.</p>
<p><b>Away </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αιρε</span></span>). Present active imperative, Take him on away and keep him away as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+21:36">Acts 21:36</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+22:22">Acts 22:22</span>, of Paul. But</p>
<p><b>release </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">απολυσον</span></span>) is first aorist active imperative, do it now and at once. </p>
