<p><b> Whom they asked for </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ον ηιτουντο</span></span>). Imperfect middle, for whom they had been asking for themselves. Luke repeats that Barabbas was in prison "for insurrection and murder."</p>
<p><b>To their will </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τω θεληματ αυτων</span></span>). This is mob law by the judge who surrenders his own power and justice to the clamour of the crowd. </p>
