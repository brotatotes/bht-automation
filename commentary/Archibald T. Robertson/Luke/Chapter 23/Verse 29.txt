<p><b> Blessed </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μακαρια</span></span>). A beatitude to the barren, the opposite of the hopes of Jewish mothers. Childless women are commiserated (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+1:25">Luke 1:25</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+1:36">Luke 1:36</span>).</p>
<p><b>To the hills </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τοις βουνοις</span></span>). A Cyrenaic word. In the N.T. only here and <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+3:5">Luke 3:5</span>. Quotation from <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ho+10:8">Hosea 10:8</span>. </p>
