<p><b> In the green tree </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εν υγρω ξυλω</span></span>). Green wood is hard to burn and so is used for the innocent.</p>
<p><b>In the dry </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εν τω ξηρω</span></span>). Dry wood kindles easily and is a symbol for the guilty. This common proverb has various applications. Here the point is that if they can put Jesus to death, being who he is, what will happen to Jerusalem when its day of judgment comes?</p>
<p><b>What shall be done </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τ γενητα</span></span>). Deliberative subjunctive. </p>
