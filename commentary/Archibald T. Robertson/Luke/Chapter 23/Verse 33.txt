<p><b> The skull </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">το κρανιον</span></span>). Probably because it looked like a skull. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+27:33">Matthew 27:33</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mr+15:22">Mark 15:22</span>.</p>
<p><b>There they crucified him </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εκε εσταυρωσαν</span></span>). There between the two robbers and on the very cross on which Barabbas, the leader of the robber band, was to have been crucified.</p>
