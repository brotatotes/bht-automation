<p><b> Railed </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εβλασφημε</span></span>). Imperfect active, implying that he kept it up. His question formally calls for an affirmative answer (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ουχ</span></span>), but the ridicule is in his own answer: "Save thyself and us." It was on a level with an effort to break prison. Luke alone gives this incident (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+23:39-43">Luke 23:39-43</span>), though <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mr+15:32">Mark 15:32</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+27:44">Matthew 27:44</span> allude to it. </p>
