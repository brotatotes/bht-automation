<p><b> Rebuking </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επιτιμων</span></span>). From what Mark and Matthew say both robbers sneered at Jesus at first, but this one came to himself and turned on his fellow robber in a rage.</p>
<p><b>Dost thou not even fear God? </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">Ουδε φοβη τον θεον;</span></span>). <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">Ουδε</span></span> here goes with the verb. <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">Φοβη</span></span> (second person singular present indicative middle of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">φοβεομα</span></span>. Both of you will soon appear before God. Jesus has nothing to answer for and you have added this to your other sins. </p>
