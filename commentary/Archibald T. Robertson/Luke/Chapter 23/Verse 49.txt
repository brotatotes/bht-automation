 Stood afar off  (ιστηκεισαν απο μακροθεν). Same verb as in verse Luke 23:35. Melancholy picture of the inner circle of the acquaintances of Jesus and the faithful band of women from Galilee.Seeing these things  (ορωσα ταυτα). And helpless either to prevent them or to understand them. They could only stand and look with blinded eyes.
