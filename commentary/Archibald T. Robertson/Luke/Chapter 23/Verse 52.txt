<p><b> Asked for </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ηιτησατο</span></span>). First aorist middle (indirect) indicative as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mr+15:43">Mark 15:43</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+27:58">Matthew 27:58</span>. The middle voice shows that Joseph of Arimathea asked the body of Jesus as a personal favour. </p>
