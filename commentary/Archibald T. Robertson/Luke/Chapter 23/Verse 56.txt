<p><b> On the sabbath they rested </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">το σαββατον ησυχασαν</span></span>). They returned and prepared spices before the sabbath began. Then they rested all during the sabbath (accusative of extent of time, <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">το σαββατον</span></span>). </p>
