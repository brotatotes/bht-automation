<p><b> As idle talk </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ως ληρος</span></span>). Old word for nonsense, only here in the N.T. Medical writers used it for the wild talk of those in delirium or hysteria.</p>
<p><b>Disbelieved </b> (pistoun). Imperfect active of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">απιστεω</span></span>, old verb from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">απιστος</span></span>, without confidence or faith in. They kept on distrusting the story of the women. </p>
