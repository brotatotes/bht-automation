<p><b> They communed </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ωμιλουν</span></span>). Imperfect active of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ομιλεω</span></span>, old and common verb (from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ομιλος</span></span>, in company with). In the N.T. only here (and verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+24:15">Luke 24:15</span>) and <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+20:11">Acts 20:11</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+24:26">Acts 24:26</span>. Our word homiletics is derived from this word for preaching was at first largely conversational in style and not declamatory. </p>
