<p><b> While they communed and questioned together </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εν τω ομιλειν αυτους κα συνζητειν</span></span>). Same idiom as in verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+24:14">Luke 24:14</span>, which see. Note <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">συνζητειν</span></span>; each questioned the other.</p>
<p><b>Jesus himself </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αυτος Ιησους</span></span>). In actual person.</p>
<p><b>Went with them </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">συνεπορευετο αυτοις</span></span>). Imperfect middle, was going along with them. </p>
