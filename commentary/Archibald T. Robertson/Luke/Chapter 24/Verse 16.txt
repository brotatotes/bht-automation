<p><b> Were holden that they should not know him </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εκρατουντο του μη επιγνωνα αυτον</span></span>). Imperfect passive of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κρατεω</span></span>, continued being held, with the ablative case of the articular infinitive, "from recognizing him," from knowing him fully (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επι-γνωνα</span></span>, ingressive aorist of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επιγινωσκο</span></span>). The <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μη</span></span> is a redundant negative after the negative idea in <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εκρατουντο</span></span>. </p>
