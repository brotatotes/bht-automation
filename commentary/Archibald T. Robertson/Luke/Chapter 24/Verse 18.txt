<p><b> Dost thou alone sojourn? </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">συ μονος παροικεισ;</span></span>). <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">Μονος</span></span> is predicate adjective. "Hast thou been dwelling alone (all by thyself)?"</p>
<p><b>And not know? </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κα ουκ εγνωσ;</span></span>). Second aorist active indicative and difficult to put into English as the aorist often is. The verb <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">παροικεω</span></span> means to dwell beside one, then as a stranger like <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">παροικο</span></span> (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="eph+2:19">Ephesians 2:19</span>). In Jerusalem everybody was talking about Jesus. </p>
