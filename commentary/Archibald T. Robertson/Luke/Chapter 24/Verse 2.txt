<p><b> Rolled away </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αποκεκυλισμενον</span></span>). Perfect passive participle of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αποκυλιω</span></span>, late verb and in the N.T. only in this context (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mr+16:3">Mark 16:3</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+28:2">Matthew 28:2</span>) while <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+20:1">John 20:1</span> has <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ηρμενον</span></span> (taken away). </p>
