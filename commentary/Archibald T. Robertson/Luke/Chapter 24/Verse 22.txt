<p><b> Amazed us </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εξεστησαν ημας</span></span>). First aorist active (transitive) indicative with accusative <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ημας</span></span> of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εξιστημ</span></span>. The second aorist active is intransitive.</p>
<p><b>Early </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ορθρινα</span></span>). A poetic and late form for <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ορθριος</span></span>. In the N.T. only here and <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+24:22">Revelation 24:22</span>. Predicate adjective agreeing with the women. </p>
