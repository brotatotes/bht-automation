<p><b> Foolish men </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ανοητο</span></span>). Literally without sense (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">νους</span></span>), not understanding. Common word.</p>
<p><b>Slow of heart </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">βραδεις τη καρδια</span></span>). Slow in heart (locative case). Old word for one dull, slow to comprehend or to act.</p>
<p><b>All that </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πασιν οις</span></span>). Relative attracted from the accusative <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">α</span></span> to the case of the antecedent <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πασιν</span></span> (dative). They could only understand part of the prophecies, not all. </p>
