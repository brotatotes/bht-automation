<p><b> Made as though </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">προσεποιησατο</span></span>). First aorist active middle (Some MSS. have <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">προσεποιειτο</span></span> imperfect) indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">προσποιεω</span></span>, old verb to conform oneself to, to pretend. Only here in the N.T. Of course he would have gone on if the disciples had not urged him to stay. </p>
