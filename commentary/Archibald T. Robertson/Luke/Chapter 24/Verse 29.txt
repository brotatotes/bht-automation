<p><b> Constrained </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">παρεβιασαντο</span></span>). Strong verb <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">παραβιαζομα</span></span>, to compel by use of force (Polybius and LXX). In the N.T. only here and <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+16:15">Acts 16:15</span>. It was here compulsion of courteous words.</p>
<p><b>Is far spent </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κεκλικεν</span></span>). Perfect active indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κλινω</span></span>. The day "has turned" toward setting. </p>
