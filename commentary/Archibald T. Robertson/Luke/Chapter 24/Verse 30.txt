<p><b> When he had sat down </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εν τω κατακλιθηνα αυτον</span></span>). Luke's common idiom as in verses <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+24:4">Luke 24:4</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+24:15">Luke 24:15</span>. Note first aorist passive infinitive (on the reclining as to him).</p>
<p><b>Gave </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επεδιδου</span></span>). Imperfect, inchoative idea, began to give to them, in contrast with the preceding aorist (punctiliar) participles. </p>
