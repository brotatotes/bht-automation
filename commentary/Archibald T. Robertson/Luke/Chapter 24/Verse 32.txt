<p><b> Was not our heart burning? </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">Ουχ η καρδια εμων καιομενη ην;</span></span>). Periphrastic imperfect middle.</p>
<p><b>Spake </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ελαλε</span></span>). Imperfect active, was speaking. This common verb <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">λαλεω</span></span> is onomatopoetic, to utter a sound, <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">λα-λα</span></span> and was used of birds, children chattering, and then for conversation, for preaching, for any public speech.</p>
<p><b>Opened </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">διηνοιγεν</span></span>). Imperfect active indicative of the same verb used of the eyes in verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+24:31">Luke 24:31</span>. </p>
