<p><b> That very hour </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αυτη τη ωρα</span></span>). Locative case and common Lukan idiom, at the hour itself. They could not wait.</p>
<p><b>Gathered </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ηθροισμενους</span></span>). Perfect passive participle of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αθροιζω</span></span>, old verb from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αθροος</span></span> (copulative <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">α</span></span> and <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">θροος</span></span>, crowd). Only here in the N.T. </p>
