<p><b> Rehearsed </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εξηγουντο</span></span>). Imperfect middle indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εξηγεομα</span></span>, verb to lead out, to rehearse. Our word exegesis comes from this verb. Their story was now confirmatory, not revolutionary. The women were right then after all.</p>
<p><b>Of them </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αυτοις</span></span>). To them, dative case. They did not recognize Jesus in his exegesis, but did in the breaking of bread. One is reminded of that saying in the <i>Logia of Jesus</i>: "Raise the stone and there thou shalt find me, cleave the wood and there am I." </p>
