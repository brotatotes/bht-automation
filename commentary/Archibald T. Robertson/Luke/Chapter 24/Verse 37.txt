 Terrified  (πτοηθεντες). First aorist passive participle of πτοεω, old verb and in the N.T. only here and Luke 21:9 which see.Affrighted  (εμφοβο γενομενο). Late adjective from εν and φοβος (fear). Both these terms of fear are strong.Supposed  (εδοκουν). Imperfect active of δοκεω, kept on thinking so.
