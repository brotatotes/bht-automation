<p><b> Terrified </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πτοηθεντες</span></span>). First aorist passive participle of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πτοεω</span></span>, old verb and in the N.T. only here and <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+21:9">Luke 21:9</span> which see.</p>
<p><b>Affrighted </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εμφοβο γενομενο</span></span>). Late adjective from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εν</span></span> and <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">φοβος</span></span> (fear). Both these terms of fear are strong.</p>
<p><b>Supposed </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εδοκουν</span></span>). Imperfect active of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δοκεω</span></span>, kept on thinking so. </p>
