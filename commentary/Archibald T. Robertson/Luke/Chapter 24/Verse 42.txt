<p><b> A piece of broiled fish </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ιχθυος οπτου μερος</span></span>). <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">Οπτος</span></span> is a verbal from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">οπταω</span></span>, to cook, to roast, to broil. Common word, but only here in the N.T. The best old documents omit "and a honeycomb" (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κα απο μελισσιου κηριου</span></span>). </p>
