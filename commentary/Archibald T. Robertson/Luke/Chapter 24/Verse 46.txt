<p><b> It is written </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">γεγραπτα</span></span>). Perfect passive indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">γραφω</span></span>, to write, the usual phrase for quoting Scripture. Jesus now finds in the Old Testament his suffering, his resurrection, and the preaching of repentance and forgiveness of sins to all nations. Note the infinitives <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">παθειν, αναστηναι, κηρυχθηνα</span></span>. </p>
