<p><b> As they were affrighted </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εμφοβων γενομενων αυτων</span></span>). Genitive absolute with second aorist middle of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">γινομα</span></span>, to become. Hence,</p>
<p><b>when they became affrighted </b>. They had utterly forgotten the prediction of Jesus that he would rise on the third day. </p>
