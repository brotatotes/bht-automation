<p><b> He parted from them </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">διεστη απ' αυτων</span></span>). Second aorist active (intransitive) indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">διιστημ</span></span>. He stood apart (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δια</span></span>) and he was gone. Some manuscripts do not have the words "and was carried into heaven." But we know that Jesus was taken up into heaven on a cloud (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+1:9">Acts 1:9</span>). </p>
