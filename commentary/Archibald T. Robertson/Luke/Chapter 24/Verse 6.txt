 He is not here, but is risen  (ουκ εστιν ωδε, αλλα ηγερθη). Another Western non-interpolation according to Westcott and Hort. The words are genuine at any rate in Mark 16:6; Matthew 28:7.The third day rise again  (τη τριτη ημερα αναστηνα). See Luke 9:22; Luke 18:32; Luke 18:33 where Jesus plainly foretold this fact. And yet they had forgotten it, for it ran counter to all their ideas and hopes.
