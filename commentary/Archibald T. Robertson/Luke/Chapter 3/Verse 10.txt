 Asked  (επηρωτων). Imperfect tense, repeatedly asked.What then must we do?  (τ ουν ποιησωμεν;). Deliberative aorist subjunctive. More exactly,What then are we to do, What then shall we do?  Same construction in verses Luke 3:12; Luke 3:14. The ουν refers to the severe things already said by John (Luke 3:7-9).
