<p><b> Many other exhortations </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πολλα μεν ουν κα ετερα</span></span>). Literally, many and different things did John <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εςανγελιζε</span></span>, <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ευαγγελιζετο</span></span>, to the people. Luke has given a bare sample of the wonderful messages of the Baptist. Few as his words preserved are they give a definite and powerful conception of his preaching. </p>
