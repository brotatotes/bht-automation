<p><b> To the multitude that went out </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τοις εξπορευομενοις οχλοις</span></span>). Plural,</p>
<p><b>Multitudes </b>. The present participle also notes the repetition of the crowds as does <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ελεγεν</span></span> (imperfect), he used to say. <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+3:7-10">Matthew 3:7-10</span> singles out the message of John to the Pharisees and Sadducees, which see for discussion of details. Luke gives a summary of his preaching to the crowds with special replies to these inquiries: the multitudes, <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+3:10">Luke 3:10</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+3:11">Luke 3:11</span>, the publicans <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+3:12">Luke 3:12</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+3:13">Luke 3:13</span>, the soldiers <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+3:14">Luke 3:14</span>.</p>
<p><b>To be baptized of him </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">βαπτισθηνα υπ' αυτου</span></span>). This is the purpose of their coming. <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+3:7">Matthew 3:7</span> has simply "to his baptism." John's metaphors are from the wilderness (vipers, fruits, axe, slave boy loosing sandals, fire, fan, thrashing-floor, garner, chaff, stones).</p>
<p><b>Who warned you? </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τις επεδειξεν υμιν;</span></span>). The verb is like our "suggest" by proof to eye, ear, or brain (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+6:47">Luke 6:47</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+12:5">Luke 12:5</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+9:16">Acts 9:16</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+20:35">Acts 20:35</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+3:7">Matthew 3:7</span>). Nowhere else in the N.T. though common ancient word (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">υποδεικνυμ</span></span>, show under, point out, give a tip or private hint). </p>
