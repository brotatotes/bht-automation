<p><b> Three years and six months </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ετη τρια κα μηνας εξ</span></span>). Accusative of duration of time without <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επ</span></span> (doubtful). The same period is given in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="jas+5:17">James 5:17</span>, the popular Jewish way of speaking. In <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1ki+18:1">1 Kings 18:1</span> the rain is said to have come in the third year. But the famine probably lasted still longer. </p>
