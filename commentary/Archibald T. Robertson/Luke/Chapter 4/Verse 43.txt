<p><b> I must </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">με δε</span></span>). Jesus felt the urge to go with the work of evangelism "to the other cities also," to all, not to a favoured few.</p>
<p><b>For therefore was I sent </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">οτ επ τουτο απεσταλην</span></span>). "A phrase of Johannine ring" (Ragg). Second aorist passive indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αποστελλω</span></span>. Christ is the great Apostle of God to men. </p>
