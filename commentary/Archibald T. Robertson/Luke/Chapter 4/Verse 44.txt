<p><b> Was preaching </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ην κηρυσσων</span></span>). Periphrastic imperfect active, describing his first tour of Galilee in accord with the purpose just stated. One must fill in details, though <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mr+1:39">Mark 1:39</span> and <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+8:23-25">Matthew 8:23-25</span> tell of the mass of work done on this campaign. </p>
