 So much the more  (μαλλον). Mark 1:45 has only "much" (πολλα, many), but Mark tells more about the effect of this disobedience.Went abroad  (διηρχετο). Imperfect tense. The fame of Jesus kept going.Came together  (συνηρχοντο). Imperfect tense again. The more the report spread, the more the crowds came.
