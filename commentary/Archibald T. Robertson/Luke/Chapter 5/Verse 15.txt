<p><b> So much the more </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μαλλον</span></span>). <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mr+1:45">Mark 1:45</span> has only "much" (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πολλα</span></span>, many), but Mark tells more about the effect of this disobedience.</p>
<p><b>Went abroad </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">διηρχετο</span></span>). Imperfect tense. The fame of Jesus kept going.</p>
<p><b>Came together </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">συνηρχοντο</span></span>). Imperfect tense again. The more the report spread, the more the crowds came. </p>
