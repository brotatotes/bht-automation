<p><b> That was palsied </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ος ην παραλελυμενος</span></span>). Periphrastic past perfect passive where <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mr+2:3">Mark 2:3</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+9:2">Matthew 9:2</span> have <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">παραλυτικον</span></span> (our paralytic). Luke's phrase is the technical medical term (Hippocrates, Galen, etc.) rather than Mark's vernacular word (Ramsay, <i>Luke the Physician</i>, pp. 57f.).</p>
<p><b>They sought </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εζητουν</span></span>). Conative imperfect. </p>
