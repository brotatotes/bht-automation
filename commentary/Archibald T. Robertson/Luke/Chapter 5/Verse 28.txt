<p><b> He forsook all </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">καταλιπων παντα</span></span>). This detail in Luke alone. He left his profitable business for the service of Christ.</p>
<p><b>Followed him </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ηκολουθε αυτω</span></span>). Imperfect active, perhaps inchoative. He began at once to follow him and he kept it up. Both <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mr+2:14">Mark 2:14</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+9:9">Matthew 9:9</span> have the aorist (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ηκολουθησεν</span></span>), perhaps ingressive. </p>
