<p><b> They inclosed </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">συνεκλεισαν</span></span>). Effective aorist active indicative with perfective compound <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">συν</span></span>.</p>
<p><b>They shut together. Were breaking </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">διερησσετο</span></span>). Imperfect passive singular (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δικτυα</span></span> being neuter plural). This is the late form of the old verb <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">διαρηγνυμ</span></span>. The nets were actually tearing in two (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δια-</span></span>) and so they would lose all the fish. </p>
