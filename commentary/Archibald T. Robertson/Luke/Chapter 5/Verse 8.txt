<p><b> Fell down at Jesus' knees </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">προσεπεσεν τοις γονασιν Ιησου</span></span>). Just like Peter, from extreme self-confidence and pride (verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+5:5">Luke 5:5</span>) to abject humilation. But his impulse here was right and sincere. His confession was true. He was a sinful man. </p>
