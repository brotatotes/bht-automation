<p><b> In the same manner did their fathers </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τα αυτα εποιουν ο πατερες αυτων</span></span>). Literally, their fathers did the same things to the false prophets. That is they spoke well (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">καλως</span></span>), finely of false prophets. Praise is sweet to the preacher but all sorts of preachers get it.</p>
<p><b>Of you </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">υμας</span></span>). Accusative case after words of speaking according to regular Greek idiom, to speak one fair, to speak well of one. </p>
