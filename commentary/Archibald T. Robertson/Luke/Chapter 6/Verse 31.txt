<p><b> As ye would </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">καθως θελετε</span></span>). In <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+7:12">Matthew 7:12</span> the Golden Rule begins: <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">Παντα οσα εαν θελητε</span></span>. Luke has "likewise" (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ομοιως</span></span>) where Matthew has <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ουτως</span></span>. See on Matthew for discussion of the saying. </p>
