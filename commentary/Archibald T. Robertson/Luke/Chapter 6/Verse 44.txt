<p><b> Is known </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">γινωσκετα</span></span>). The fruit of each tree reveals its actual character. It is the final test. This sentence is not in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+7:17-20">Matthew 7:17-20</span>, but the same idea is in the repeated saying (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+7:16">Matthew 7:16</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+7:20">Matthew 7:20</span>): "By their fruits ye shall know them," where the verb</p>
<p><b>epignosesthe </b> means full knowledge. The question in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+7:16">Matthew 7:16</span> is put here in positive declarative form. The verb is in the plural for "men" or "people," <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">συλλεγουσιν</span></span>. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+7:16">Matthew 7:16</span>.</p>
<p><b>Bramble bush </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">βατου</span></span>). Old word, quoted from the LXX in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mr+12:26">Mark 12:26</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+20:37">Luke 20:37</span> (from <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ex+3:6">Exodus 3:6</span>) about the burning bush that Moses saw, and by Stephen (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+7:30">Acts 7:30</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+7:35">Acts 7:35</span>) referring to the same incident. Nowhere else in the N.T. "Galen has a chapter on its medicinal uses, and the medical writings abound in prescriptions of which it is an ingredient" (Vincent).</p>
<p><b>Gather </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τρυγωσιν</span></span>). A verb common in Greek writers for gathering ripe fruit. In the N.T. only here and <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+14:18">Revelation 14:18</span>.</p>
<p><b>Grapes </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">σταφυλην</span></span>). Cluster of grapes. </p>
