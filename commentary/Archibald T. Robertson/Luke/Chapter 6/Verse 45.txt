<p><b> Bringeth forth </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">προφερε</span></span>). In a similar saying repeated later. <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+12:34">Matthew 12:34</span> has the verb <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εκβαλλε</span></span> (throws out, casts out), a bolder figure. "When men are natural, heart and mouth act in concert. But otherwise the mouth sometimes professes what the heart does not feel" (Plummer). </p>
