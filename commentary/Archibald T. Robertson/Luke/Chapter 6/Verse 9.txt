<p><b> I ask you </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επερωτω υμας</span></span>). They had questions in their hearts about Jesus. He now asks in addition (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επ'</span></span>) an open question that brings the whole issue into the open.</p>
<p><b>A life </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ψυχην</span></span>). So the Revised Version. The rabbis had a rule: <i>Periculum vitae pellit sabbatum</i>. But it had to be a Jew whose life was in peril on the sabbath. The words of Jesus cut to the quick.</p>
<p><b>Or to destroy it </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">η απολεσα</span></span>). On this very day these Pharisees were plotting to destroy Jesus (verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+6:7">Luke 6:7</span>). </p>
