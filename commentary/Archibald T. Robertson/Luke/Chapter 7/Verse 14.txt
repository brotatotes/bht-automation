<p><b> Touched the bier </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ηψατο του σορου</span></span>). An urn for the bones or ashes of the dead in Homer, then the coffin (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ge+5:26">Genesis 5:26</span>), then the funeral couch or bier as here. Only here in the N.T. Jesus touched the bier to make the bearers stop, which they did (<b>stood still </b>, <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εστησαν</span></span>), second aorist active indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ιστημ</span></span>. </p>
