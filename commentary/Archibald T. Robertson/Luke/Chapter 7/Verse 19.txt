 Calling unto him  (προσκαλεσαμενος). First aorist middle (indirect) participle.Two  (δυο τινας). Certain two. Not in Matthew 11:2.Saying  (λεγων). John saying by the two messengers. The message is given precisely alike in Matthew 11:3, which see. In both we have ετερον for "another," either a second or a different kind. In verse Luke 7:20 Westcott and Hort read αλλον in the text, ετερον in the margin. Προσδοκωμεν, may be present indicative or present subjunctive (deliberative), the same contract form (αο= ω, αω ω).
