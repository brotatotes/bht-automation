<p><b> In that hour he cured </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εν εκεινη τη ορα εθεραπευσεν</span></span>). This item is not in Matthew. Jesus gave the two disciples of John an example of the direct method. They had heard. Then they saw for themselves.</p>
<p><b>Diseases </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">νοσων</span></span>),</p>
<p><b>plagues </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μαστιγων</span></span>),</p>
<p><b>evil spirits </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πνευματων πονηρων</span></span>), all kinds of bodily ills, and he singles out the</p>
<p><b>blind </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τυφλοις</span></span>) to whom in particular he bestowed sight (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εχαριζατο βλεπειν</span></span>), gave as a free gift (from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">χαρις</span></span>, grace) seeing (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">βλεπειν</span></span>). </p>
