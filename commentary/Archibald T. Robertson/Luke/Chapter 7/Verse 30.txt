<p><b> Rejected for themselves </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ηθετησαν εις εαυτους</span></span>). The first aorist active of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αθετεω</span></span> first seen in LXX and Polybius. Occurs in the papyri. These legalistic interpreters of the law refused to admit the need of confession of sin on their part and so set aside the baptism of John. They annulled God's purposes of grace so far as they applied to them.</p>
<p><b>Being not baptized by him </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μη βαπτισθεντες υπ' αυτου</span></span>). First aorist passive participle. <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">Μη</span></span> is the usual negative of the participle in the <i>Koine</i>. </p>
