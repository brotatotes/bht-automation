<p><b> Of all her children </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">απο παντων των τεκνων αυτης</span></span>). Here <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+11:19">Matthew 11:19</span> has "by her works" (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">απο των εργων αυτης</span></span>). Aleph has <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εργων</span></span> here. The use of "children" personifies wisdom as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+7:8">Luke 7:8</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+7:9">Luke 7:9</span>. </p>
