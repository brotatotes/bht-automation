<p><b> Answering </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αποκριθεις</span></span>). First aorist passive participle, redundant use with <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ειπεν</span></span>. Jesus answers the thoughts and doubts of Simon and so shows that he knows all about the woman also. Godet notes a tone of Socratic irony here. </p>
