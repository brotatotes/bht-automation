 A certain lender  (δανιστη τιν). A lender of money with interest. Here alone in the N.T. though a common word.Debtors  (χρεοφιλετα). From χρεω (debt, obligation) and οφειλω, to owe. Only here and Luke 16:5 in the N.T., though common in late Greek writers.Owed  (ωφειλεν). Imperfect active and so unpaid. Five hundred δηναρια and fifty like two hundred and fifty dollars and twenty-five dollars.
