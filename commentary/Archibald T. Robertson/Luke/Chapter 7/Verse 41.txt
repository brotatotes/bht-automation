<p><b> A certain lender </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δανιστη τιν</span></span>). A lender of money with interest. Here alone in the N.T. though a common word.</p>
<p><b>Debtors </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">χρεοφιλετα</span></span>). From <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">χρεω</span></span> (debt, obligation) and <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">οφειλω</span></span>, to owe. Only here and <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+16:5">Luke 16:5</span> in the N.T., though common in late Greek writers.</p>
<p><b>Owed </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ωφειλεν</span></span>). Imperfect active and so unpaid. Five hundred <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δηναρια</span></span> and fifty like two hundred and fifty dollars and twenty-five dollars. </p>
