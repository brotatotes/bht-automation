<p><b> Who even forgiveth sins </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ος κα αμαρτιας αφιησιν</span></span>). Present indicative active of same verb, <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αφιημ</span></span>. Once before the Pharisees considered Jesus guilty of blasphemy in claiming the power to forgive sins (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+5:21">Luke 5:21</span>). Jesus read their inmost thoughts as he always does. </p>
