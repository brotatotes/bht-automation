See on <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mr+4:22">Mark 4:22</span> for discussion of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κρυπτον</span></span> and <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αποκρυφον</span></span>. </p>
