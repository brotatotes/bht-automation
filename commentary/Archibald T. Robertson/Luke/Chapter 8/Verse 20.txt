<p><b> Was told </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">απηγγελη</span></span>). Second aorist passive indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">απαγγελλω</span></span>, to bring word or tidings. Common verb. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mr+3:32">Mark 3:32</span> and <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+12:47">Matthew 12:47</span> for details. </p>
