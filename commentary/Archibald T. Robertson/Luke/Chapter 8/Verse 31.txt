<p><b> Into the abyss </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εις την αβυσσον</span></span>). Rare old word common in LXX from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">α</span></span> privative and <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">βαθυς</span></span> (deep). So bottomless place (supply <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">χωρα</span></span>). The deep sea in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ge+1:2">Genesis 1:2</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ge+7:11">Genesis 7:11</span>. The common receptacle of the dead in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+10:7">Romans 10:7</span> and especially the abode of demons as here and <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+9:1-11">Revelation 9:1-11</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+11:7">Revelation 11:7</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+17:8">Revelation 17:8</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+20:1">Revelation 20:1</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+20:3">Revelation 20:3</span>. </p>
