<p><b> Rushed down the steep </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ωρμησεν κατα του κρημνου</span></span>). Ablative with <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κατα</span></span> as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mr+5:13">Mark 5:13</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+8:32">Matthew 8:32</span> and the same vivid verb in each account, to hurl impetuously, to rush.</p>
<p><b>Were choked </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">απεπνιγη</span></span>). Second aorist (constative) passive indicative third singular (collective singular) where <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mr+5:13">Mark 5:13</span> has the picturesque imperfect <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επνιγοντο</span></span>. </p>
