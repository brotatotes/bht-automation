<p><b> Saw what had come to pass </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ιδοντες το γεγονος</span></span>). This item only in Luke. Note the neat Greek idiom <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">το γεγονος</span></span>, articular second perfect active participle of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">γινομα</span></span>. Repeated in verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+8:35">Luke 8:35</span> and in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mr+5:14">Mark 5:14</span>. Note numerous participles here in verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+8:35">Luke 8:35</span> as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mr+5:15">Mark 5:15</span>. </p>
