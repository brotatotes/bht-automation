<p><b> Were holden with great fear </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">φοβω μεγαλω συνειχοντο</span></span>). Imperfect passive of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">συνεχω</span></span> with the instrumental case of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">φοβος</span></span>. See a similar use of this vigorous verb in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+12:50">Luke 12:50</span> of Jesus and in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="php+1:23">Philippians 1:23</span> of Paul. </p>
