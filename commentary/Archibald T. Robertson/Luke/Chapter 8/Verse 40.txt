<p><b> Welcomed </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">απεδεξατο</span></span>). Peculiar to Luke. To receive with pleasure, from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αποδεχομα</span></span>, a common verb.</p>
<p><b>For they were all waiting for him </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ησαν γαρ παντες προσδοκωντες αυτον</span></span>). Periphrastic imperfect active of</p>
<p><b>prosdokao </b>, an old verb for eager expectancy, a vivid picture of the attitude of the people towards Jesus. Driven from Decapolis, he is welcomed in Capernaum. </p>
