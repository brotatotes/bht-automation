 Welcomed  (απεδεξατο). Peculiar to Luke. To receive with pleasure, from αποδεχομα, a common verb.For they were all waiting for him  (ησαν γαρ παντες προσδοκωντες αυτον). Periphrastic imperfect active ofprosdokao , an old verb for eager expectancy, a vivid picture of the attitude of the people towards Jesus. Driven from Decapolis, he is welcomed in Capernaum.
