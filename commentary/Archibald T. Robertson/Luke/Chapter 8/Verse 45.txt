<p><b> Press thee and crush thee </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">συνεχουσιν σε κα αποθλιβουσιν</span></span>). Hold thee together, hold thee in (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">συνεχω</span></span>, see verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+8:37">Luke 8:37</span>).</p>
<p><b>Crush thee </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αποθλιβω</span></span>) here only in the N.T., a verb used of pressing out grapes in Diodorus and Josephus. <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mr+5:31">Mark 5:31</span> has <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">συνθλιβω</span></span>, to press together. </p>
