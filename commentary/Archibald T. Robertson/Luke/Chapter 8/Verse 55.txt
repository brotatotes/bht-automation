<p><b> Her spirit returned </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επεστρεψεν το πνευμα αυτης</span></span>). The life came back to her at once.</p>
<p><b>Be given her to eat </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αυτη δοθηνα φαγειν</span></span>). The first infinitive <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δοθηνα</span></span> is an indirect command. The second <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">φαγειν</span></span> (second aorist active of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εσθιω</span></span>) is epexegetic purpose. </p>
