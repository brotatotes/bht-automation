<p><b> Amidst the thorns </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εν μεσω των ακανθων</span></span>). <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mr+4:7">Mark 4:7</span> has <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εις</span></span> (among) and <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+13:7">Matthew 13:7</span> has <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επ</span></span> "upon."</p>
<p><b>Grew with it </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">συνφυεισα</span></span>). Same participle as <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">φυεν</span></span> above with <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">συν-</span></span> (together).</p>
