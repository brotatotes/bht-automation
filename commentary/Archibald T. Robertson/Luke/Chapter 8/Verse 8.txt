<p><b> A hundredfold </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εκατονπλασιονα</span></span>). Luke omits the thirty and sixty of <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mr+4:8">Mark 4:8</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+13:8">Matthew 13:8</span>.</p>
<p><b>He cried </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εφωνε</span></span>). Imperfect active, and in a loud voice, the verb means. The warning about hearing with the ears occurs also in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mr+4:9">Mark 4:9</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+13:9">Matthew 13:9</span>. </p>
