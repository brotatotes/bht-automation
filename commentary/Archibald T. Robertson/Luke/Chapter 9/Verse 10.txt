<p><b> Declared </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">διηγησαντο</span></span>). First aorist middle of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">διηγεομα</span></span>, to carry a narrative through to the end. Jesus listened to it all.</p>
<p><b>They had done </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εποιησαν</span></span>). Aorist active indicative, they did.</p>
<p><b>He took them </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">παραλαβων αυτους</span></span>). Second aorist active participle of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">παραλαμβανω</span></span>. Very common verb.</p>
<p><b>Bethsaida </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">Βηθσαιδα</span></span>). Peculiar to Luke. Bethsaida Julias is the territory of Philip, for it is on the other side of the Sea of Galilee (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+6:1">John 6:1</span>). </p>
