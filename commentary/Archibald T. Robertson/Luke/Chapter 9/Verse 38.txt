 Master  (Διδασκαλε). Teacher as in Mark 9:17.Lord  (κυριε, Matthew 17:15).To look upon  (επιβλεψα). Aorist active infinitive of επιβλεπω (επ, upon, βλεπω, look), common verb, but in the N.T. only here and James 2:3 except Luke 1:48 in quotation from LXX. This compound verb is common in medical writers for examining carefully the patient.Mine only child  (μονογενης μο). Only in Luke as already about an only child in Luke 7:12; Luke 8:42.
