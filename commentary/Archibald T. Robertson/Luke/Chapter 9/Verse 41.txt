 How long shall I be with you and bear with you?  (εως ποτε εσομα προς υμας κα ανεξομα υμων;). Here the two questions of Mark 9:19 (only one in Matthew 17:17) are combined in one sentence.Bear with  (ανεξομα, direct middle future) is, hold myself from you (ablative case υμων).Faithless  (απιστος) is disbelieving and perverse (διεστραμμενη, perfect passive participle of διαστρεφω), is twisted, turned, or torn in two.
