<p><b> Went </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">διηρχοντο</span></span>). Imperfect middle, continuous and repeated action made plainer also by three present participles (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εξερχομενοι, ευαγγελιζομενοι, θεραπευοντες</span></span>), describing the wide extent of the work through all the villages (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κατα τας κωμας</span></span>, distributive use of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κατα</span></span>) everywhere (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πανταχου</span></span>) in Galilee. </p>
