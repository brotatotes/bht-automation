<p><b> Thou art </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">συ ε</span></span>). So <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+3:22">Luke 3:22</span>. <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+3:17">Matthew 3:17</span> has</p>
<p><b>this is </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ουτος εστιν</span></span>) which see. So both Mark and Luke have "in thee," while Matthew has "in whom." </p>
