<p><b> Tearing him </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">σπαραξαν αυτον</span></span>). Margin,</p>
<p><b>convulsing him </b> like a spasm. Medical writers use the word for the rotating of the stomach. <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+4:35">Luke 4:35</span> adds "when the demon had thrown him down in the midst." Mark mentions the "loud voice" (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">φονη μεγαλη</span></span>), a screech, in fact. It was a moment of intense excitement. </p>
