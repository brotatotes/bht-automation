<p><b> The report of him </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">η ακοη αυτου</span></span>). Vulgate, <i>rumor</i>. See <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+14:1">Matthew 14:1</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+24:6">Matthew 24:6</span>. They had no telephones, telegraphs, newspapers or radio, but news has a marvellous way of spreading by word of mouth. The fame of this new teacher went out "everywhere" (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πανταχου</span></span>) throughout all Galilee. </p>
