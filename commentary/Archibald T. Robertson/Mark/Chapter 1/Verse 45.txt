<p><b> Began to publish it much </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ηρξατο κηρυσσειν πολλα</span></span>). <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+5:15">Luke 5:15</span> puts it, "so much the more" (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μαλλον</span></span>). One of the best ways to spread a thing is to tell people not to tell. It was certainly so in this case. Soon Jesus had to avoid cities and betake himself to desert places to avoid the crowds and even then people kept coming to Jesus (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ηρχοντο</span></span>, imperfect tense). Some preachers are not so disturbed by the onrush of crowds. </p>
