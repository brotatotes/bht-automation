<p><b> Then went out unto him </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εξεπορευετο προς αυτον</span></span>). Imperfect indicative describing the steady stream of people who kept coming to the baptism (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εβαπτιζοντο</span></span>, imperfect passive indicative, a wonderful sight).</p>
<p><b>In the river Jordan </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εν τω Ιορδανη ποταμω</span></span>). In the Jordan river, literally. </p>
