<p><b> Mightier than I </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ο ισχυροτερος μου</span></span>). In each of the Synoptics. Gould calls it a skeptical depreciation of himself by John. But it was sincere on John's part and he gives a reason for it.</p>
<p><b>The Latchet </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τον ιμαντα</span></span>). The thong of the sandal which held it together. When the guest comes into the house, performed by a slave before one enters the bath. Mark alone gives this touch. </p>
