<p><b> With water </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">υδατ</span></span>). So Luke (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+3:16">Luke 3:16</span>) the locative case,</p>
<p><b>in water </b>. Matthew (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+3:11">Matthew 3:11</span>) has <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εν</span></span> (in), both with (in) water and the Holy Spirit. The water baptism by John was a symbol of the spiritual baptism by Jesus. </p>
