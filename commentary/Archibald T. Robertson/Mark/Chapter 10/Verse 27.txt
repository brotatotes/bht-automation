<p><b> Looking on them </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εμβλεψας αυτοις</span></span>). So in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+19:26">Matthew 19:26</span>. Their amazement increased (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mr+10:26">Mark 10:26</span>).</p>
<p><b>But not with God </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αλλ' ου παρα θεω</span></span>). Locative case with <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">παρα</span></span> (beside). The impossible by the side of men (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">παρα ανθρωποις</span></span>) becomes possible by the side of God. That is the whole point and brushes to one side all petty theories of a gate called needle's eye, etc. </p>
