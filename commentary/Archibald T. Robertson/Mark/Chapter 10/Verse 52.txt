<p><b> Followed </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ηκολουθε</span></span>). Imperfect tense picturing joyful Bartimaeus as he followed the caravan of Jesus into the new Jericho.</p>
<p><b>Made thee whole </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">σεσωκεν</span></span>). Perfect active indicative. The word commonly means</p>
<p><b>save </b> and that may be the idea here. </p>
