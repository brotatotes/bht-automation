 When he had looked round about upon all things  (περιβλεψαμενος παντα). Another Markan detail in this aorist middle participle. Mark does not give what Luke 19:39-55 has nor what Matthew 21:10-17 does. But it is all implied in this swift glance at the temple before he went out to Bethany with the Twelve,it being now eventide  (οψε ηδη ουσης της ωρας). Genitive absolute, the hour being already late. What a day it had been! What did the apostles think now?
