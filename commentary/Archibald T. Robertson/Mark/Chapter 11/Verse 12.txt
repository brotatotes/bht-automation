<p><b> On the morrow </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τη επαυριον</span></span>). <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+21:18">Matthew 21:18</span> has "early" (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πρω</span></span>), often of the fourth watch before six A.M. This was Monday morning. The Triumphal Entry had taken place on our Sunday, the first day of the week. </p>
