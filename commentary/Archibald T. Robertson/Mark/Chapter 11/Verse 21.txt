<p><b> Peter calling to remembrance </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αναμνησθεις ο Πετρος</span></span>). First aorist participle, being reminded. Only in Mark and due to Peter's story. For his quick memory see also <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mr+14:72">Mark 14:72</span>.</p>
<p><b>Which thou cursedst </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ην κατηρασω</span></span>). First aorist middle indicative second person singular from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">καταραομα</span></span>. It almost sounds as if Peter blamed Jesus for what he had done to the fig tree. </p>
