<p><b> Shall not doubt in his heart </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μη διακριθη εν τη καρδια αυτου</span></span>). First aorist passive subjunctive with <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ος αν</span></span>. The verb means a divided judgment (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δια</span></span> from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δυο</span></span>, two, and <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κρινω</span></span>, to judge). Wavering doubt. Not a single act of doubt (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">διακριθη</span></span>), but continued faith (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πιστευη</span></span>).</p>
<p><b>Cometh to pass </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">γινετα</span></span>). Futuristic present middle indicative. </p>
