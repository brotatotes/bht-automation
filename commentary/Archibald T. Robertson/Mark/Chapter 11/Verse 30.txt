<p><b> Answer me </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αποκριθητε μο</span></span>). This sharp demand for a reply is only in Mark. See also verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mr+11:29">Mark 11:29</span>. Jesus has a right to take this turn because of John's direct relation to himself. It was not a dodge, but a home thrust that cleared the air and defined their attitude both to John and Jesus. They rejected John as they now reject Jesus. </p>
