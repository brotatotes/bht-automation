<p><b> We know not </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ουκ οιδαμεν</span></span>). It was for the purpose of getting out of the trap into which they had fallen by challenging the authority of Jesus. Their self-imposed ignorance, refusal to take a stand about the Baptist who was the Forerunner of Christ, absolved Jesus from a categorical reply. But he has no notion of letting them off at this point. </p>
