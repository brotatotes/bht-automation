<p><b> Certain of those that stood there </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τινες των εκε εστηκοτων</span></span>). Perfect active participle, genitive plural. Bystanders. <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+19:33">Luke 19:33</span> terms them "the owners thereof" (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ο κυριο αυτου</span></span>). The lords or masters of the colt. They make a natural protest. </p>
