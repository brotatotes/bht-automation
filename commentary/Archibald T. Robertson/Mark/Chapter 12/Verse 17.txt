<p><b> Marvelled greatly at him </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εξεθαυμαζον επ' αυτω</span></span>). Imperfect tense with perfective use of the preposition <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εξ</span></span>. Both Matthew and Luke use the ingressive aorist. Luke adds that they "held their peace" (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εσιγησαν</span></span>) while Matthew notes that they "went their way" (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">απηλθαν</span></span>), went off or away. </p>
