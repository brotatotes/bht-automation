<p><b> To wife </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">γυναικα</span></span>). Predicate accusative in apposition with "her" (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αυτην</span></span>). So Luke, but Matthew merely has "had her" (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εσχον αυτην</span></span>), constative aorist indicative active. </p>
