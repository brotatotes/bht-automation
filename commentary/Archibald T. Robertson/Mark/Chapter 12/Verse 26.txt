<p><b> In the place concerning the Bush </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επ του βατου</span></span>). This technical use of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επ</span></span> is good Greek, in the matter of, in the passage about, the Bush. <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">Βατος</span></span> is masculine here, feminine in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+20:37">Luke 20:37</span>. The reference is to <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ex+3:3-6">Exodus 3:3-6</span> (in the book of Moses, <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εν τη βιβλω</span></span>). </p>
