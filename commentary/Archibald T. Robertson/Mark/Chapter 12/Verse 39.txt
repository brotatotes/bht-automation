<p><b> First seats in the synagogues </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πρωτοκαθεδριας</span></span>). As a mark of special piety, seats up in front while now the hypocrites present in church prefer the rear seats.</p>
<p><b>Chief places at feasts </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πρωτοκλισιας εν τοις δειπνοις</span></span>). Recognizing proper rank and station. Even the disciples fall victims to this desire for precedence at table (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+22:24">Luke 22:24</span>). </p>
