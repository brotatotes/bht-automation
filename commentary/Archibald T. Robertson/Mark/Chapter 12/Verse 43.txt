<p><b> Called unto him </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">προσκαλεσαμενος</span></span>). Indirect middle voice. The disciples themselves had slipped away from him while the terrific denunciation of the scribes and Pharisees had gone on, puzzled at this turn of affairs.</p>
<p><b>More than all </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πλειον παντων</span></span>). Ablative of comparison (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">παντων</span></span>). It may mean, more than all the rich put together.</p>
<p><b>All that she had </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">παντα οσα ειχεν</span></span>). Imperfect tense.</p>
<p><b>Cast in </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εβαλεν</span></span>). Aorist tense, in sharp contrast.</p>
<p><b>All her living </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ολον τον βιον αυτης</span></span>). Her</p>
<p><b>livelihood </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">βιος</span></span>), not her life (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ζωη</span></span>). It is a tragedy to see a stingy saint pose as giving the widow's mite when he could give thousands instead of pennies. </p>
