<p><b> But take ye heed </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">Hυμεις δε βλεπετε</span></span>). Gullibility is no mark of a saint or of piety. Note emphatic position of you (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">υμεις</span></span>). Credulity ranks no higher than scepticism. God gave us our wits for self-protection. Christ has warned us beforehand. </p>
