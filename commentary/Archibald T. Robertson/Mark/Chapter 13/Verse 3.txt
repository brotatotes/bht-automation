<p><b> Over against the temple </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κατεναντ του ιερου</span></span>). In full view of the temple about which they had been speaking.</p>
<p><b>Privately </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κατ' ιδιαν</span></span>). Peter and James and John and Andrew (named only in Mark) had evidently been discussing the strange comment of Jesus as they were coming out of the temple. In their bewilderment they ask Jesus a bit to one side, though probably all the rest drew up as Jesus began to speak this great eschatological discourse. </p>
