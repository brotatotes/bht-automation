<p><b> When they sacrificed the passover </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">οτε το πασχα εθυον</span></span>). Imperfect indicative, customary practice. The paschal lamb (note <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πασχα</span></span>) was slain at 6 P.M., beginning of the fifteenth of the month (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ex+12:6">Exodus 12:6</span>), but the preparations were made beforehand on the fourteenth (Thursday). See on <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+26:17">Matthew 26:17</span> for discussion of "eat the passover." </p>
