<p><b> And he </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κα αυτος</span></span>). Emphatic, and he himself.</p>
<p><b>A large upper room </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αναγαιον μεγα</span></span>). Anything above ground (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">γη</span></span>), and particularly upstairs as here. Here and in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+22:12">Luke 22:12</span>. Example in Xenophon. Jesus wishes to observe this last feast with his disciples alone, not with others as was often done. Evidently this friend of Jesus was a man who would understand.</p>
<p><b>Furnished </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εστρωμενον</span></span>). Perfect passive participle of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">στρωννυμ</span></span>, state of readiness. "Strewed with carpets, and with couches properly spread" (Vincent). </p>
