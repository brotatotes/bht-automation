<p><b> Not during the feast </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">Μη εν τη εορτη</span></span>). They had first planned to kill him at the feast (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+11:57">John 11:57</span>), but the Triumphal Entry and great Tuesday debate (this very morning) in the temple had made them decide to wait till after the feast was over. It was plain that Jesus had too large and powerful a following. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+26:47">Matthew 26:47</span>. </p>
