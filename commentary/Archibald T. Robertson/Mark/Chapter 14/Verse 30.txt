<p><b> Twice </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δις</span></span>). This detail only in Mark. One crowing is always the signal for more. The Fayum papyrus agrees with Mark in having <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δις</span></span>. The cock-crowing marks the third watch of the night (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mr+13:35">Mark 13:35</span>). </p>
