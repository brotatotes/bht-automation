<p><b> Exceeding vehemently </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εκπερισσως</span></span>). This strong compounded adverb only in Mark and probably preserves Peter's own statement of the remark. About the boast of Peter see on <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+26:35">Matthew 26:35</span>. </p>
