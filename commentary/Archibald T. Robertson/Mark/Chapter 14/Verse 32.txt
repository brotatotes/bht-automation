<p><b> Which was named </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ου το ονομα</span></span>). Literally, "whose name was." On Gethsemane see on <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+26:36">Matthew 26:36</span>.</p>
<p><b>While I pray </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εως προσευξωμα</span></span>). Aorist subjunctive with <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εως</span></span> really with purpose involved, a common idiom. Matthew adds "go yonder" (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">απελθων εκε</span></span>). </p>
