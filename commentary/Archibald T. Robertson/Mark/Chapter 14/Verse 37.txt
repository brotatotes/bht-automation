<p><b> Simon, sleepest thou? </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">Σιμων, καθευδεισ;</span></span>). The old name, not the new name, Peter. Already his boasted loyalty was failing in the hour of crisis. Jesus fully knows the weakness of human flesh (see on <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+26:41">Matthew 26:41</span>). </p>
