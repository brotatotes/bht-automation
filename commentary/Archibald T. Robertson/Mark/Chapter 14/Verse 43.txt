<p><b> And the scribes </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κα των γραμματεων</span></span>). Mark adds this item while <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+18:3">John 18:3</span> mentions "Pharisees." It was evidently a committee of the Sanhedrin for Judas had made his bargain with the Sanhedrin (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mr+14:1">Mark 14:1</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+26:3">Matthew 26:3</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+22:2">Luke 22:2</span>). See discussion of the betrayal and arrest on <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+26:47-56">Matthew 26:47-56</span> for details. </p>
