<p><b> Linen cloth </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">σινδονα</span></span>). An old Greek word of unknown origin. It was fine linen cloth used often for wrapping the dead (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+27:59">Matthew 27:59</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mr+15:46">Mark 15:46</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+23:53">Luke 23:53</span>). In this instance it could have been a fine sheet or even a shirt. </p>
