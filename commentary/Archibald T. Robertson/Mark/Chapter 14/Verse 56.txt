<p><b> Their witness agreed not together </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ισα α μαρτυρια ουκ ησαν</span></span>). Literally, the testimonies were not equal. They did not correspond with each other on essential points.</p>
<p><b>Many were bearing false witness </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εψευδομαρτυρουν</span></span>, imperfect, repeated action)</p>
<p><b>against him </b>. No two witnesses bore joint testimony to justify a capital sentence according to the law (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="de+19:15">Deuteronomy 19:15</span>). Note imperfects in these verses (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mr+14:55-57">Mark 14:55-57</span>) to indicate repeated failures. </p>
