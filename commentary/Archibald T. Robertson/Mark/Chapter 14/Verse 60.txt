<p><b> Stood up in the midst </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αναστας εις μεσον</span></span>). Second aorist active participle. For greater solemnity he arose to make up by bluster the lack of evidence. The high priest stepped out into the midst as if to attack Jesus by vehement questions. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+26:59-68">Matthew 26:59-68</span> for details here. </p>
