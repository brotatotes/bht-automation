<p><b> Beneath in the court </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κατω εν τη αυλη</span></span>). This implies that Jesus was upstairs when the Sanhedrin met. <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+22:69">Matthew 22:69</span> has it</p>
<p><b>without in the court </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εξω εν τη αυλη</span></span>). Both are true. The open court was outside of the rooms and also below. </p>
