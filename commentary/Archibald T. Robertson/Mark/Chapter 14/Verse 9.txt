<p><b> For a memorial of her </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εις μνημοσυνον αυτης</span></span>). So in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+26:13">Matthew 26:13</span>. There are many mausoleums that crumble to decay. But this monument to Jesus fills the whole world still with its fragrance. What a hint there is here for those who wish to leave permanent memorials. </p>
