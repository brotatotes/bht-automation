<p><b> What each should take </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τις τ αρη</span></span>). Only in Mark. Note double interrogative, Who What? The verb <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αρη</span></span> is first aorist active deliberative subjunctive retained in the indirect question. The details in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mr+15:24-32">Mark 15:24-32</span> are followed closely by <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+27:35-44">Matthew 27:35-44</span>. See there for discussion of details. </p>
