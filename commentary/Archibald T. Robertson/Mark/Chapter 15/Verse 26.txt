<p><b> The superscription </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">η επιγραφη</span></span>). The writing upon the top of the cross (our word epigraph). <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+23:38">Luke 23:38</span> has this same word, but <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+27:37">Matthew 27:37</span> has "accusation" (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αιτιαν</span></span>). See Matthew for discussion. <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+19:19">John 19:19</span> has "title" (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τιτλον</span></span>). </p>
