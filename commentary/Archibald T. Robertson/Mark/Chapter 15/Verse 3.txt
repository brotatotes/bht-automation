<p><b> Accused him of many things </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κατηγορουν αυτου πολλα</span></span>). Imperfect tense, repeated accusations besides those already made. They let loose their venom against Jesus. One of the common verbs for speaking against in court (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κατα</span></span> and <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αγορευω</span></span>). It is used with the genitive of the person and the accusative of the thing. </p>
