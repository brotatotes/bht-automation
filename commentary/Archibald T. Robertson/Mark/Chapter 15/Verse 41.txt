<p><b> Followed him and ministered unto him </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ηκολουθουν κα διηκονουν αυτω</span></span>). Two imperfects describing the long Galilean ministry of these three women and many other women in Galilee (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+8:1-3">Luke 8:1-3</span>) who came up with him (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">α συναναβασα αυτω</span></span>) to Jerusalem. This summary description in Mark is paralleled in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+27:55">Matthew 27:55</span> and <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+23:49">Luke 23:49</span>. These faithful women were last at the Cross as they stood afar and saw the dreadful end to all their hopes. </p>
