<p><b> The preparation </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">παρασκευη</span></span>). Mark explains the term as meaning "the day before the sabbath" (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">προσαββατον</span></span>), that is our Friday, which began at sunset. See discussion on <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+27:57">Matthew 27:57</span>. The Jews had already taken steps to get the bodies removed (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+19:31">John 19:31</span>). </p>
