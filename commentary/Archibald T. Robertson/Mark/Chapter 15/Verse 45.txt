<p><b> Granted the corpse </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εδωρησατο το πτωμα</span></span>). This official information was necessary before the burial. As a matter of fact Pilate was probably glad to turn the body over to Joseph else the body would go to the potter's field. This is the only instance when <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πτωμα</span></span> (<i>cadaver</i>, corpse) is applied to the body (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">σωμα</span></span>) of Jesus, the term used in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+27:59">Matthew 27:59</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+23:53">Luke 23:53</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+19:40">John 19:40</span>). </p>
