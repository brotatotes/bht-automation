<p><b> Marvelled </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">θαυμαζειν</span></span>). Pilate was sure of the innocence of Jesus and saw through their envy (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mr+15:10">Mark 15:10</span>), but he was hoping that Jesus would answer these charges to relieve him of the burden. He marvelled also at the self-control of Jesus. </p>
