<p><b> Bound with them that had made insurrection </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μετα των στασιαστων δεδεμενος</span></span>). A desperate criminal, leader in the insurrection, sedition (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εν τη στασε</span></span>), or revolution against Rome, the very thing that the Jews up at Bethsaida Julias had wanted Jesus to lead (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+6:15">John 6:15</span>). Barabbas was the leader of these rioters and was bound with them.</p>
<p><b>Had committed murder </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">φονον πεποιηκεισαν</span></span>). Past perfect indicative without augment. Murder usually goes with such rioters and the priests and people actually chose a murderer in preference to Jesus. </p>
