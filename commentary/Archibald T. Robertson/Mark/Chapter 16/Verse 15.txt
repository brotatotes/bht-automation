<p><b> To the whole creation </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">παση τη κτισε</span></span>). This commission in Mark is probably another report of the missionary <i>Magna Charta</i> in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+28:16-20">Matthew 28:16-20</span> spoken on the mountain in Galilee. One commission has already been given by Christ (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+20:21-23">John 20:21-23</span>). The third appears in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+24:44-49">Luke 24:44-49</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+1:3-8">Acts 1:3-8</span>. </p>
