<p><b> They shall speak with new tongues </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">γλωσσαις λαλησουσιν [καιναισ]</span></span>). Westcott and Hort put <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">καιναις</span></span> (new) in the margin. Casting out demons we have seen in the ministry of Jesus. Speaking with tongues comes in the apostolic era (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+2:3">Acts 2:3</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+10:46">Acts 10:46</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+19:6">Acts 19:6</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1co+12:28">1 Corinthians 12:28</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1co+12:14">1 Corinthians 12:14</span>). </p>
