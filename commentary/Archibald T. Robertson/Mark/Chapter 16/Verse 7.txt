<p><b> And Peter </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κα τω Πετρω</span></span>). Only in Mark, showing that Peter remembered gratefully this special message from the Risen Christ. Later in the day Jesus will appear also to Peter, an event that changed doubt to certainty with the apostles (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+24:34">Luke 24:34</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1co+15:5">1 Corinthians 15:5</span>). See on <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+28:7">Matthew 28:7</span> for discussion of promised meeting in Galilee. </p>
