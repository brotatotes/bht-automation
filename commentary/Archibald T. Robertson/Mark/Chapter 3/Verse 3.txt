<p><b> Stand forth </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εγειρε εις το μεσον</span></span>). Step into the middle of the room where all can see. It was a bold defiance of the Christ's spying enemies. Wycliff rightly puts it:</p>
<p><b>They aspieden him </b>. They played the spy on Jesus. One can see the commotion among the long-bearded hypocrites at this daring act of Jesus. </p>
