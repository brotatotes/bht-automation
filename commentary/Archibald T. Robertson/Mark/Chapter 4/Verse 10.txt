<p><b> When he was alone </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">οτε εγενετο κατα μονας</span></span>). Only in Mark. Vivid recollection of Peter. Mark has also "they that were about him with the twelve" (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ο περ αυτον συν τοις δωδεκα</span></span>), Matthew and Luke simply "the disciples." They did not want the multitude to see that they did not understand the teaching of Jesus. </p>
