<p><b> The lusts of other things </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">α περ τα λοιπα επιθυμια</span></span>). All the passions or longings, sensual, worldly, "pleasures of this life" (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ηδονων του βιου</span></span>) as Luke has it (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+8:14">Luke 8:14</span>), the world of sense drowning the world of spirit. The word <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επιθυμια</span></span> is not evil in itself. One can yearn (this word) for what is high and holy (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+22:15">Luke 22:15</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="php+1:23">Philippians 1:23</span>). </p>
