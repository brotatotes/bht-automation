<p><b> Bear fruit </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">καρποφορουσιν</span></span>). Same word in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+13:23">Matthew 13:23</span> and <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+8:15">Luke 8:15</span>. Mark gives the order from thirty, sixty, to a hundred, while <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+13:23">Matthew 13:23</span> has it reversed. </p>
