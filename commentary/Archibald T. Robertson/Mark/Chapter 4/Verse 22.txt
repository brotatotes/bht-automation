<p><b> Save that it should be manifested </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εαν μη ινα φανερωθη</span></span>). Note <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εαν μη</span></span> and <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ινα</span></span>. <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+8:17">Luke 8:17</span> has it</p>
<p><b>that shall not be made manifest </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ο ου φανερον γενησετα</span></span>). Here in Mark it is stated that the temporary concealment is for final manifestation and a means to that end. Those who are charged with the secret at this time are given the set responsibility of proclaiming it on the housetops after Ascension (Swete). The hidden (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κρυπτον</span></span>) and the</p>
<p><b>secret </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αποκρυφον</span></span>) are to be revealed in due time. </p>
