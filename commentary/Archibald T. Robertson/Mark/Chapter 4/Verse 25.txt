<p><b> Even that which he hath </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κα ο εχε</span></span>). <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+8:18">Luke 8:18</span> has</p>
<p><b>even that which he thinketh that he hath or seemeth to have </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κα ο δοκε εχειν</span></span>). It is possible that <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εχε</span></span> here has the notion of acquiring. The man who does not acquire soon loses what he thinks that he has. This is one of the paradoxes of Jesus that repay thought and practice. </p>
