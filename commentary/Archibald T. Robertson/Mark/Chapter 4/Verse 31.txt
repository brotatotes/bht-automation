<p><b> When it is sown </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">οταν σπαρη</span></span>). Second aorist passive subjunctive of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">σπειρω</span></span>. Alone in Mark and repeated in verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mr+4:32">Mark 4:32</span>.</p>
<p><b>Less than all the seeds </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μικροτερον παντων των σπερματων</span></span>). Comparative adjective with the ablative case after it. Hyperbole, of course, but clearly meaning that from a very small seed a large plant grows, the gradual pervasive expansive power of the kingdom of God. </p>
