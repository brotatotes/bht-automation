<p><b> As they were able to hear it </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">καθως ηδυναντο ακουειν</span></span>). Only in Mark. Imperfect indicative. See <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+16:12">John 16:12</span> for <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ου δυνασθε βασταζειν</span></span>, not able to bear. Jesus used parables now largely, but there was a limit even to the use of them to these men. He gave them the mystery of the kingdom in this veiled parabolic form which was the only feasible form at this stage. But even so they did not understand what they heard. </p>
