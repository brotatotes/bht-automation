<p><b> Rebuked the wind </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επετιμησεν τω ανεμω</span></span>) as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+8:26">Matthew 8:26</span> and <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+8:24">Luke 8:24</span>. He spoke to the sea also. All three Gospels speak of the sudden calm (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">γαληνη</span></span>) and the rebuke to the disciples for this lack of faith. </p>
