<p><b> Growing up and increasing </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αναβαινοντα κα αυξανομενα</span></span>). In Mark alone. A vivid detail enlarging on the continued growth implied in the imperfect "yielded fruit" (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εδιδου καρπον</span></span>). It kept on yielding as it grew. Fruit is what matters. </p>
