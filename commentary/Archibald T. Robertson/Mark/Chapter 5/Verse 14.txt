<p><b> And in the country </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κα εις τους αγρους</span></span>). Mark adds this to "the city." In the fields and in the city as the excited men ran they told the tale of the destruction of the hogs. They came to see (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ηλθον ιδειν</span></span>). All the city came out (Matthew), they went out to see (Luke). </p>
