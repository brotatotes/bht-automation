<p><b> If I touch but his garments </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">Εαν αψωμα κ'αν των ιματιων αυτου</span></span>). She was timid and shy from her disease and did not wish to attract attention. So she crept up in the crowd and touched the hem or border of his garment (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κρασπεδον</span></span>) according to <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+9:20">Matthew 9:20</span> and <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+8:44">Luke 8:44</span>. </p>
