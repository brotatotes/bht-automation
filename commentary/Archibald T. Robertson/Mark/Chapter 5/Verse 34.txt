<p><b> Go in peace </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">Hυπαγε εις ειρηνην</span></span>). She found sympathy, healing, and pardon for her sins, apparently. Peace here may have more the idea of the Hebrew <i>shalom</i>, health of body and soul. So Jesus adds: "Be whole of thy plague" (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ισθ υγιης απο της μαστιγος σου</span></span>). Continue whole and well. </p>
