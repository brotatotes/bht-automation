<p><b> Wailing greatly </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αλαλαζοντας πολλα</span></span>). An onomatopoetic word from Pindar down. The soldiers on entering battle cried <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">Αλαλα</span></span>. Used of clanging cymbals (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1co+13:1">1 Corinthians 13:1</span>). Like <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ολολυζω</span></span> in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="jas+5:1">James 5:1</span>. It is used here of the monotonous wail of the hired mourners. </p>
