Second aorist active subjunctive, <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">γνο</span></span>. But would they keep still about it? There was the girl besides. <p><b>given her to eat </b>, (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δοθηνα αυτη φαγειν</span></span>), a natural care of the Great Physician. Two infinitives here (first aorist passive and second aorist active). "She could walk and eat; not only alive, but well" (Bruce). </p>
