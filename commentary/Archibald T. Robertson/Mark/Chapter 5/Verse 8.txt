<p><b> For he said </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ελεγεν γαρ</span></span>). For he had been saying (progressive imperfect). Jesus had already repeatedly ordered the demon to come out of the man whereat the demon made his outcry to Jesus and protested. <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+8:29">Matthew 8:29</span> had "before the time" (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">προ καιρου</span></span>) and <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mr+8:31">Mark 8:31</span> shows that the demons did not want to go back to the abyss (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">την αβυσσον</span></span>) right now. That was their real home, but they did not wish to return to the place of torment just now. </p>
