<p><b> There abide </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εκε μενετε</span></span>). So also <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+10:11">Matthew 10:11</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+9:4">Luke 9:4</span>. Only Matthew has city or village (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mr+10:11">Mark 10:11</span>), but he mentions house in verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mr+6:12">Mark 6:12</span>. They were to avoid a restless and dissatisfied manner and to take pains in choosing a home. It is not a prohibition against accepting invitations. </p>
