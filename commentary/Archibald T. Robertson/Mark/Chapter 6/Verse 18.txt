<p><b> Thy brother's wife </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">την γυναικα του αδελφου</span></span>). While the brother was alive (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="le+18:16">Leviticus 18:16</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="le+20:21">Leviticus 20:21</span>). After a brother's death it was often a duty to marry his widow. </p>
