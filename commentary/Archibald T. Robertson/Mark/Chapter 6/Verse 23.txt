<p><b> And he sware unto her </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κα ωμοσεν αυτη</span></span>). The girl was of marriageable age though called <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κορασιον</span></span> (cf. <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="es+2:9">Esther 2:9</span>). Salome was afterward married to Philip the Tetrarch. The swaggering oath to the half of the kingdom reminds one of <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="es+5:3">Esther 5:3</span>, the same oath made to Esther by Ahasuerus. </p>
