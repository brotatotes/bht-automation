<p><b> Into the country and villages round about </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εις τους κυκλω αγρους κα κωμας</span></span>). The fields (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αγρους</span></span>) were the scattered farms (Latin, <i>villae</i>). The villages (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κωμας</span></span>) may have included Bethsaida Julias not far away (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+9:10">Luke 9:10</span>). The other Bethsaida was on the Western side of the lake (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mr+6:45">Mark 6:45</span>).</p>
<p><b>Somewhat to eat </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τ φαγωσιν</span></span>). Literally,</p>
<p><b>what to eat </b>,</p>
<p><b>what they were to eat </b>. Deliberative subjunctive retained in the indirect question. </p>
