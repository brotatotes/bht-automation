<p><b> Men </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ανδρες</span></span>). Men as different from women as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+14:21">Matthew 14:21</span>. This remarkable miracle is recorded by all Four Gospels, a nature miracle that only God can work. No talk about accelerating natural processes will explain this miracle. And three eyewitnesses report it: the Logia of Matthew, the eyes of Peter in Mark, the witness of John the Beloved Disciple (Gould). The evidence is overwhelming. </p>
