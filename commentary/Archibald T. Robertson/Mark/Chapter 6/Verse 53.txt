<p><b> And moored to the shore </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κα προσωρμισθησαν</span></span>). Only here in the New Testament, though an old Greek verb and occurring in the papyri. <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">Hορμος</span></span> is roadstead or anchorage. They cast anchor or lashed the boat to a post on shore. It was at the plain of Gennesaret several miles south of Bethsaida owing to the night wind. </p>
