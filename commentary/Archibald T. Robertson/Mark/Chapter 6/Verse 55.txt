<p><b> Ran about </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">περιεδραμον</span></span>). Vivid constative aorist picturing the excited pursuit of Jesus as the news spread that he was in Gennesaret.</p>
<p><b>On their beds </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επ τοις κραβαττοις</span></span>). Pallets like that of the man let down through the roof (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mr+2:4">Mark 2:4</span>).</p>
<p><b>Where they heard he was </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">οπου ηκουον οτ εστιν</span></span>). Imperfect tense of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ακουω</span></span> (repetition), present indicative <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εστιν</span></span> retained in indirect discourse. </p>
