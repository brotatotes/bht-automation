<p><b> Making void the word of God by your tradition </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ακυρουντες τον λογον του θεου τη παραδοσε υμων</span></span>). See on <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+15:6">Matthew 15:6</span> for the word <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ακυρουντες</span></span>, invalidating, a stronger word than <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αθετειν</span></span>, to set aside, in verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mr+7:9">Mark 7:9</span>. See both used in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ga+3:15">Galatians 3:15</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ga+3:17">Galatians 3:17</span>. Setting aside does invalidate. </p>
