<p><b> When he was entered into the house from the multitude </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">οτε εισηλθεν εις οικον απο του οχλου</span></span>). This detail in Mark alone, probably in Peter's house in Capernaum. To the crowd Jesus spoke the parable of corban, but the disciples want it interpreted (cf. <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mr+4:10">Mark 4:10</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mr+4:33">Mark 4:33</span>). <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+15:15">Matthew 15:15</span> represents Peter as the spokesman as was usually the case. </p>
