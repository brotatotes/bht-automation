<p><b> Evil thoughts </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ο διαλογισμο ο κακο</span></span>). These come out of the heart (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εκ της καρδιας</span></span>), the inner man, and lead to the dreadful list here given like the crimes of a modern police court:</p>
<p><b>fornications </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πορνεια</span></span>, usually of the unmarried),</p>
<p><b>adulteries </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μοιχαια</span></span>, of the married),</p>
<p><b>thefts </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κλοπα</span></span>, stealings),</p>
<p><b>covetings </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πλεονεξια</span></span>, craze for more and more),</p>
<p><b>murders </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">φονο</span></span>, growing out of the others often),</p>
<p><b>wickednesses </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πονηρια</span></span>, from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πονος</span></span>, toil, then drudge, bad like our <i>knave</i>, serving boy like German <i>Knabe</i>, and then criminal),</p>
<p><b>deceit </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δολος</span></span>, lure or snare with bait),</p>
<p><b>lasciviousness </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ασελγεια</span></span>, unrestrained sex instinct),</p>
<p><b>evil eye </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">οφθαλμος πονηρος</span></span>) or eye that works evil and that haunts one with its gloating stare,</p>
<p><b>railing </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">βλασφημια</span></span>, blasphemy, hurtful speech),</p>
<p><b>pride </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">υπερηφανια</span></span>, holding oneself above others, stuck up),</p>
<p><b>foolishness </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αφροσυνη</span></span>, lack of sense), a fitting close to it all. </p>
