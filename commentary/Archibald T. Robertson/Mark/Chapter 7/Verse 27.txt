<p><b> Let the children first be filled </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αφες πρωτον χορτασθηνα τα παιδια</span></span>). The Jews had the first claim. See the command of Jesus in the third tour of Galilee to avoid the Gentiles and the Samaritans (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+10:5">Matthew 10:5</span>). Paul was the Apostle to the Gentiles, but he gave the Jew the first opportunity (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+2:9">Romans 2:9</span>). See on <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+15:24">Matthew 15:24</span>. </p>
