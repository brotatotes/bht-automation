<p><b> Ephphatha </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">διανοιχθητ</span></span>, be opened). Another one of Mark's Aramaic words preserved and transliterated and then translated into Greek. "Be thou unbarred" (<i>Braid Scots</i>). Jesus sighed (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εστεναξεν</span></span>) as he looked up into heaven and spoke the word <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εφφαθα</span></span>. Somehow he felt a nervous strain in this complex case (deaf, dumb, demoniac) that we may not quite comprehend. </p>
