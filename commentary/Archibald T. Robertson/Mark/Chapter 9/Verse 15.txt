<p><b> Were greatly amazed </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εξεθαμβηθησαν</span></span>). First aorist passive ingressive aorist with perfective compound <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εξ-</span></span>. The sudden and opportune appearance of Jesus in the midst of the dispute when no one was looking for him turned all eyes to him. He would not fail, however the disciples might do so. The people were awed for the moment and then running began to welcome him (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">προτρεχοντες ησπαζοντο</span></span>). Present participle and imperfect middle indicative. </p>
