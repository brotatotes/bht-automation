<p><b> Bring him unto me </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">φερετε αυτον προς με</span></span>). The disciples had failed and their unbelief had led to this fiasco. Even the disciples were like and part of the</p>
<p><b>faithless </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">απιστος</span></span>, unbelieving) generation in which they lived. The word</p>
<p><b>faithless </b> does not here mean treacherous as it does with us. But Jesus is not afraid to undertake this case. We can always come to Jesus when others fail us. </p>
