<p><b> Having torn much </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">σπαραξας</span></span>). The uncompounded verb used in verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mr+9:20">Mark 9:20</span>.</p>
<p><b>Became as one dead </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εγενετο ωσε νεκρος</span></span>). As if dead from the violence of the spasm. The demon did him all possible harm in leaving him. </p>
