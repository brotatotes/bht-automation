<p><b> But they held their peace </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">Hο δε εσιωπων</span></span>). Imperfect tense. Put thus to them, they felt ashamed that the Master had discovered their jealous rivalry. It was not a mere abstract query, as they put it to Jesus, but it was a canker in their hearts. </p>
