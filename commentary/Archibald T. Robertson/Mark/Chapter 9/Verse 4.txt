<p><b> Elijah with Moses </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">Ελειας συν Μωυσε</span></span>). Matthew and Luke have "Moses and Elijah." Both, as a matter of fact were prophets and both dealt with law. Both had mysterious deaths. The other order in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mr+9:5">Mark 9:5</span>. </p>
