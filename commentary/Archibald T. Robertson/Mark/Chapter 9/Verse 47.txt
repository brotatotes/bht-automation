<p><b> With one eye </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μονοφθαλμον</span></span>). Literally one-eyed. See also <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+18:9">Matthew 18:9</span>. Vernacular <i>Koine</i> and condemned by the Atticists. See <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+18:8">Matthew 18:8</span>. Mark has here "kingdom of God" where <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+18:9">Matthew 18:9</span> has "life." </p>
