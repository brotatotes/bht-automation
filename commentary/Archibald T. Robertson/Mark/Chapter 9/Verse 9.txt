<p><b> Save when </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ε μη οταν</span></span>). Matthew has "until" (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εως ου</span></span>).</p>
<p><b>Should have risen </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αναστη</span></span>). Second aorist active subjunctive. More exactly, "should rise" (punctiliar aorist and futuristic, not with any idea of perfect tense). <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+9:36">Luke 9:36</span> merely says that they told no man any of these things. It was a high and holy secret experience that the chosen three had had for their future good and for the good of all. </p>
