<p><b> Get you no gold </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μη κτησησθε</span></span>). It is not, "Do not possess" or "own," but "do not acquire" or "procure" for yourselves, indirect middle aorist subjunctive. Gold, silver, brass (copper) in a descending scale (nor even bronze).</p>
<p><b>In your purses </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εις τας ζωνας υμων</span></span>). In your girdles or belts used for carrying money. </p>
