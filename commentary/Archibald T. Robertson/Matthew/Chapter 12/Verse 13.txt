<p><b> Stretch forth thy hand </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εκτεινον σου την χειρα</span></span>). Probably the arm was not withered, though that is not certain. But he did the impossible. "He stretched it forth," straight, I hope, towards the Pharisees who were watching Jesus (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mr+3:2">Mark 3:2</span>). </p>
