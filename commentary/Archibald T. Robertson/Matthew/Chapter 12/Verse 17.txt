<p><b> That it might be fulfilled </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ινα πληρωθη</span></span>). The final use of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ινα</span></span> and the sub-final just before (verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+12:16">Matthew 12:16</span>). The passage quoted is <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="isa+42:1-4">Isaiah 42:1-4</span> "a very free reproduction of the Hebrew with occasional side glances at the Septuagint" (Bruce), possibly from an Aramaic collection of <i>Testimonia</i> (McNeile). Matthew applies the prophecy about Cyrus to Christ. </p>
