<p><b> Thy disciples do </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ο μαθητα σου ποιουσιν</span></span>). These critics are now watching a chance and they jump at this violation of their Pharisaic rules for Sabbath observance. The disciples were plucking the heads of wheat which to the Pharisees was reaping and were rubbing them in their hands (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+6:1">Luke 6:1</span>) which was threshing. </p>
