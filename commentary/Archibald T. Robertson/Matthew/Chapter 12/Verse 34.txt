<p><b> Ye offspring of vipers </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">γεννηματα εχιδνων</span></span>). These same terrible words the Baptist had used to the Pharisees and Sadducees who came to his baptism (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+3:7">Matthew 3:7</span>). But these Pharisees had deliberately made their choice and had taken Satan's side. The charge against Jesus of being in league with Satan reveals the evil heart within. The heart "spurts out" (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εκβαλλε</span></span>) good or evil according to the supply (treasure, <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">θησαυρου</span></span>) within. Verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+12:33">Matthew 12:33</span> is like <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+7:17-19">Matthew 7:17-19</span>. Jesus often repeated his crisp pungent sayings as every teacher does. </p>
