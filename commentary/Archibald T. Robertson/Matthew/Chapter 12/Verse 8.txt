<p><b> Lord of the Sabbath </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κυριος του σαββατου</span></span>). This claim that he as the Son of Man is master of the Sabbath and so above the Pharisaic regulations angered them extremely. By the phrase "the Son of man" here Jesus involves the claim of Messiahship, but as the Representative Man he affirms his solidarity with mankind, "standing for the human interest" (Bruce) on this subject. </p>
