<p><b> And all the multitude stood on the beach </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κα πας ο οχλος επ τον αιγιαλον ιστηκε</span></span>). Past perfect tense of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ιστημ</span></span> with imperfect sense, had taken a stand and so stood. Note accusative also with <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επ</span></span> upon the beach where the waves break one after the other (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αιγιαλος</span></span> is from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αλς</span></span>, sea, and <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αγνυμ</span></span>, to break, or from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αισσω</span></span>, to rush). Jesus had to get into a boat and sit down in that because of the crush of the crowd. </p>
