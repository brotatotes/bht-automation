<p><b> Explain unto us </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">διασαφησον ημιν</span></span>). Also in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+18:31">Matthew 18:31</span>. "Make thoroughly clear right now" (aorist tense of urgency). The disciples waited till Jesus left the crowds and got into the house to ask help on this parable. Jesus had opened up the Parable of the Sower and now they pick out this one, passing by the mustard seed and the leaven. </p>
