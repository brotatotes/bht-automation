<p><b> The rocky places </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τα πετρωδη</span></span>). In that limestone country ledges of rock often jut out with thin layers of soil upon the layers of rock.</p>
<p><b>Straightway they sprang up </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ευθεως εξανετειλεν</span></span>). "Shot up at once" (Moffatt). Double compound (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εξ</span></span>, out of the ground, <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ανα</span></span>, up). Ingressive aorist of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εξανατελλω</span></span>. </p>
