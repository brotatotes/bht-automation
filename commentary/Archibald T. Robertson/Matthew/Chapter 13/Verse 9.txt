<p><b> He that hath ears let him hear </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ο εχων ωτα ακουετω</span></span>), So also in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+11:15">Matthew 11:15</span> and <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+13:43">Matthew 13:43</span>. It is comforting to teachers and preachers to observe that even Jesus had to exhort people to listen and to understand his sayings, especially his parables. They will bear the closest thought and are often enigmatical. </p>
