<p><b> Their sick </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τους αρρωστους αυτων</span></span>). "Without strength" (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ρωννυμ</span></span> and <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">α</span></span> privative). <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">Εσπλαγχνισθη</span></span> is a deponent passive. The verb gives the oriental idea of the bowels (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">σπλαγχνα</span></span>) as the seat of compassion. </p>
