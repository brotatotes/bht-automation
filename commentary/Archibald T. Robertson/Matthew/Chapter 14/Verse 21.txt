<p><b> Beside women and children </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">χωρις γυναικων κα παιδιων</span></span>). Perhaps on this occasion there were not so many as usual because of the rush of the crowd around the head of the lake. Matthew adds this item and does not mean that the women and children were not fed, but simply that "the eaters" (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ο εσθιοντες</span></span>) included five thousand men (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ανδρες</span></span>) besides the women and children. </p>
