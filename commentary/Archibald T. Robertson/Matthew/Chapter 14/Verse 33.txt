<p><b> Worshipped him </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">προσεκυνησαν αυτω</span></span>). And Jesus accepted it. They were growing in appreciation of the person and power of Christ from the attitude in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+8:27">Matthew 8:27</span>. They will soon be ready for the confession of <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+16:16">Matthew 16:16</span>. Already they can say: "Truly God's Son thou art." The absence of the article here allows it to mean a Son of God as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+27:54">Matthew 27:54</span> (the centurion). But they probably mean "the Son of God" as Jesus was claiming to them to be. </p>
