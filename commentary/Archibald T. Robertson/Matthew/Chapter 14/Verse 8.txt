<p><b> Put forward </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">προβιβασθεισα</span></span>). See <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+19:33">Acts 19:33</span> for a similar verb (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">προβαλοντων</span></span>), "pushing forward." Here (Acts) the Textus Receptus uses <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">προβιβαζω</span></span>. "It should require a good deal of 'educating' to bring a young girl to make such a grim request" (Bruce).</p>
<p><b>Here </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ωδε</span></span>). On the spot. Here and now.</p>
<p><b>In a charger </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επ πινακ</span></span>). Dish, plate, platter. Why the obsolete "charger"? </p>
