<p><b> A Canaanitish woman </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">γυνη Χαναναια</span></span>). The Phoenicians were descended from the Canaanites, the original inhabitants of Palestine. They were of Semitic race, therefore, though pagan.</p>
<p><b>Have pity on me </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ελεησον με</span></span>). She made her daughter's case her own, "badly demonized." </p>
