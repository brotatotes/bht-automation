<p><b> I was not sent </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ουκ απεσταλην</span></span>). Second aorist passive indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αποστελλω</span></span>. Jesus takes a new turn with this woman in Phoenicia. He makes a test case of her request. In a way she represented the problem of the Gentile world. He calls the Jews "the lost sheep of the house of Israel" in spite of the conduct of the Pharisees. </p>
