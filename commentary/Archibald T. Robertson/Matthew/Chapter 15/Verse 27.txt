<p><b> Even the dogs </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κα τα κυναρια</span></span>). She took no offence at the implication of being a Gentile dog. The rather she with quick wit took Christ's very word for little dogs (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κυναρια</span></span>) and deftly turned it to her own advantage, for the little dogs eat of the crumbs (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ψιχιων</span></span>, little morsels, diminutive again) that fall from the table of their masters (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κυριων</span></span>), the children. </p>
