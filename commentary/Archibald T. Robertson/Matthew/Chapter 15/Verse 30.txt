<p><b> And they cast them down at his feet </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κα εριψαν αυτους παρα τους ποδας αυτου</span></span>). A very strong word, flung them down, "not carelessly, but in haste, because so many were coming on the same errand" (Vincent). It was a great day for "they glorified the God of Israel." </p>
