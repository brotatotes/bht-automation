<p><b> The borders of Magadan </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εις τα ορια Μαγαδαν</span></span>). On the eastern side of the Sea of Galilee and so in Galilee again. Mark terms it Dalmanutha (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mr+8:10">Mark 8:10</span>). Perhaps after all the same place as Magdala, as most manuscripts have it. </p>
