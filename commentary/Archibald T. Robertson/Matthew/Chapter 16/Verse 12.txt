<p><b> Then understood they </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τοτε συνηκαν</span></span>). First aorist active indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">συνιημ</span></span>, to grasp, to comprehend. They saw the point after this elaborate rebuke and explanation that by "leaven" Jesus meant "teaching." </p>
