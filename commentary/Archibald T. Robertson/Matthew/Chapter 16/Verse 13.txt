<p><b> Caesarea Philippi </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">Καισαριας της Φιλιππου</span></span>). Up on a spur of Mt. Hermon under the rule of Herod Philip.</p>
<p><b>He asked </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ηρωτα</span></span>). Began to question, inchoative imperfect tense. He was giving them a test or examination. The first was for the opinion of men about the Son of Man. </p>
