<p><b> They reasoned </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">διελογιζοντο</span></span>). It was pathetic, the almost jejune inability of the disciples to understand the parabolic warning against "the leaven of the Pharisees and Sadducees" (verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+16:6">Matthew 16:6</span>) after the collision of Christ just before with both parties in Magadan. They kept it up, imperfect tense. It is "loaves" (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αρτους</span></span>) rather than "bread." </p>
