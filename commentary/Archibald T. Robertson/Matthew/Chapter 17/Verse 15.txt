<p><b> Epileptic </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">σεληνιαζετα</span></span>). Literally, "moonstruck," "lunatic." The symptoms of epilepsy were supposed to be aggravated by the changes of the moon (cf. <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+4:24">Matthew 4:24</span>).</p>
<p><b>He has it bad </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κακως εχε</span></span>) as often in the Synoptic Gospels. </p>
