<p><b> Until </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εως ου</span></span>). This conjunction is common with the subjunctive for a future event as his Resurrection (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εγερθη</span></span>) was. Again (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mr+9:10">Mark 9:10</span>) they were puzzled over his meaning. Jesus evidently hopes that this vision of Moses and Elijah and his own glory might stand them in good stead at his death. </p>
