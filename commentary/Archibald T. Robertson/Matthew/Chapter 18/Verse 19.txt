<p><b> Shall agree </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">συμφωνησωσιν</span></span>). Our word "symphony" is this very root. It is no longer looked at as a concord of voices, a chorus in harmony, though that would be very appropriate in a church meeting rather than the rasping discord sometimes heard even between two brethren or sisters.</p>
<p><b>Of my Father </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">παρα του πατρος μου</span></span>). From the side of, "by my Father." </p>
