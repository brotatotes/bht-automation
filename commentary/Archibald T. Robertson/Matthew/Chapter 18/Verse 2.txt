<p><b> Called to him </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">προσκαλεσαμενος</span></span>). Indirect middle voice aorist participle. It may even be Peter's "little child" (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">παιδιον</span></span>) as it was probably in Peter's house (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mr+9:33">Mark 9:33</span>).</p>
<p><b>Set him </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εστησεν</span></span>). Transitive first aorist active indicative, not intransitive second aorist, <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εστη</span></span>.</p>
<p><b>In the midst of them </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εν μεσω αυτων</span></span>). Luke adds (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+9:47">Luke 9:47</span>) "by his side" (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">παρ' εαυτω</span></span>). Both are true. </p>
