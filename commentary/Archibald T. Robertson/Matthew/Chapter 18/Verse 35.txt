<p><b> From your hearts </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">απο των καρδιων υμων</span></span>). No sham or lip pardon, and as often as needed. This is Christ's full reply to Peter's question in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+18:21">Matthew 18:21</span>. This parable of the unmerciful servant is surely needed today. </p>
