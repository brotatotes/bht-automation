<p><b> Suffer </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αφετε</span></span>). "Leave them alone." Second aorist active imperative.</p>
<p><b>Forbid them not </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μη κωλυετε</span></span>). "Stop hindering them." The idiom of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μη</span></span> with the present imperative means just that.</p>
<p><b>Of such </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">των τοιουτων</span></span>). The childlike as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+18:3">Matthew 18:3</span>. </p>
