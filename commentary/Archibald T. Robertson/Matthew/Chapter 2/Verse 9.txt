<p><b> Went before them </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">προηγεν αυτους</span></span>). Imperfect tense, kept on in front of them, not as a guide to the town since they now knew that, but to the place where the child was, the inn according to <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+2:7">Luke 2:7</span>. Justin Martyr says that it was in a cave. The stall where the cattle and donkeys stayed may have been beneath the inn in the side of the hill. </p>
