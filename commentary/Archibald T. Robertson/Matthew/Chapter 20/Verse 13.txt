<p><b> To one of them </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εν αυτων</span></span>). Evidently the spokesman of the group. "Friend" (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εταιρε</span></span>). Comrade. So a kindly reply to this man in place of an address to the whole gang. <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ge+31:40">Genesis 31:40</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="job+27:21">Job 27:21</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ho+13:15">Hosea 13:15</span>. The word survives in modern Greek. </p>
