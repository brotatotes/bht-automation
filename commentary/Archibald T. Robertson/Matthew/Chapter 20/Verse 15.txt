<p><b> Is thine eye evil? </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ο οφθαλμος σου πονηρος εστιν?</span></span>) See on <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+6:22-24">Matthew 6:22-24</span> about the evil eye and the good eye. The complainer had a grudging eye while the householder has a liberal or generous eye. See <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+5:7">Romans 5:7</span> for a distinction between <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δικαιος</span></span> and <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αγαθος</span></span>. </p>
