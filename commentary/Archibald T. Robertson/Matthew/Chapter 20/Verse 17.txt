<p><b> Apart </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κατ' ιδιαν</span></span>). This is the prediction in Matthew of the cross (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+16:21">Matthew 16:21</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+17:22">Matthew 17:22</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+20:17">Matthew 20:17</span>). "Aside by themselves" (Moffatt). The verb is <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">παρελαβεν</span></span>. Jesus is having his inward struggle (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mr+10:32">Mark 10:32</span>) and makes one more effort to get the Twelve to understand him. </p>
