<p><b> Ye know not what ye ask </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ουκ οιδατε τ αιτεισθε</span></span>). How often that is true. <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">Αιτεισθε</span></span> is indirect middle voice, "ask for yourselves," "a selfish request."</p>
<p><b>We are able </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δυναμεθα</span></span>). Amazing proof of their ignorance and self-confidence. Ambition had blinded their eyes. They had not caught the martyr spirit. </p>
