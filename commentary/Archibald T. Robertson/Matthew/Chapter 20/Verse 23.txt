<p><b> Ye shall drink </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πιεσθε</span></span>). Future middle from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πινω</span></span>. Christ's cup was martyrdom. James was the first of the Twelve to meet the martyr's death (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+12:2">Acts 12:2</span>) and John the last if reports are true about him. How little they knew what they were saying. </p>
