<p><b> Touched their eyes </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ηψατο των ομματων</span></span>). A synonym for <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">οφθαλμων</span></span> in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mr+8:23">Mark 8:23</span> and here alone in the N.T. In the LXX and a common poetic word (Euripides) and occurs in the papyri. In modern Greek <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ματια μου</span></span> (abbreviation) means "light of my eye," "my darling." The verb <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">απτομα</span></span> is very common in the Synoptic Gospels. The touch of Christ's hand would sooth the eyes as they were healed. </p>
