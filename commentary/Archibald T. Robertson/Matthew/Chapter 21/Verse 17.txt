<p><b> To Bethany </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εις Βηθανιαν</span></span>). House of depression or misery, the Hebrew means. But the home of Martha and Mary and Lazarus there was a house of solace and comfort to Jesus during this week of destiny. He</p>
<p><b>lodged there </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ηυλισθη εκε</span></span>) whether at the Bethany home or out in the open air. It was a time of crisis for all. </p>
