<p><b> The baptism of John </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">το βαπτισμα το Ιωανου</span></span>). This represents his relation to Jesus who was baptized by him. At once the ecclesiastical leaders find themselves in a dilemma created by their challenge of Christ.</p>
<p><b>They reasoned with themselves </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">διελογιζοντο</span></span>). Picturesque imperfect tense describing their hopeless quandary. </p>
