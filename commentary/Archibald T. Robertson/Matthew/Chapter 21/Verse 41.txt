<p><b> He will miserably destroy those miserable men </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κακους κακως απολεσε αυτους</span></span>). The paronomasia or assonance is very clear. A common idiom in literary Greek. "He will put the wretches to a wretched death" (Weymouth).</p>
<p><b>Which </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">οιτινες</span></span>). Who, which very ones of a different character. </p>
