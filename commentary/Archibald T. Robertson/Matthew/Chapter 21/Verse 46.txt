<p><b> Took him </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ειχον</span></span>). Descriptive imperfect of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εχω</span></span>, to hold. This fear of the people was all that stayed the hands of the rabbis on this occasion. Murderous rage was in their hearts towards Jesus. People do not always grasp the application of sermons to themselves. </p>
