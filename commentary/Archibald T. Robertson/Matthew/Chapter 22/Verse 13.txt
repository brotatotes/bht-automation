<p><b> Was speechless </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εψιμωθη</span></span>). Was muzzled, dumb from confusion and embarrassment. It is used of the ox (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1ti+5:18">1 Timothy 5:18</span>).</p>
<p><b>The outer darkness </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">το σκοτος το εξωτερον</span></span>). See <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+8:12">Matthew 8:12</span>. All the blacker from the standpoint of the brilliantly lighted banquet hall.</p>
<p><b>There shall be </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εκε εστα</span></span>). Out there in the outer darkness. </p>
