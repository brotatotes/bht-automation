<p><b> He had put the Sadducees to silence </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εφιμωσεν τους Σαδδουκαιους</span></span>). Muzzled the Sadducees. The Pharisees could not restrain their glee though they were joining with the Sadducees in trying to entrap Jesus.</p>
<p><b>Gathered themselves together </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">συνηχθησαν επ το αυτο</span></span>). First aorist passive, were gathered together. <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">Επ το αυτο</span></span> explains more fully <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">συν-</span></span>. See also <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+2:47">Acts 2:47</span>. "Mustered their forces" (Moffatt). </p>
