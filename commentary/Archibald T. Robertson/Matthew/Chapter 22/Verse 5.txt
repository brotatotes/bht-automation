<p><b> Made light of it </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αμελησαντες</span></span>). Literally, neglecting, not caring for. They may even have ridiculed the invitation, but the verb does not say so. However, to neglect an invitation to a wedding feast is a gross discourtesy.</p>
