<p><b> The partings of the highways </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τας διεξοδους των οδων</span></span>). Vulgate, <i>exitus viarum</i>. <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">Διοδο</span></span> are cross-streets, while <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">διεξοδο</span></span> (double compound) seem to be main streets leading out of the city where also side-streets may branch off, "by-ways." </p>
