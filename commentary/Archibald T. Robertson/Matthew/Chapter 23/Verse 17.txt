<p><b> Ye fools </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μωρο</span></span>). In <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+5:22">Matthew 5:22</span> Jesus had warned against calling a man <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μωρος</span></span> in a rage, but here he so terms the blind Pharisees for their stupidity, description of the class. "It shows that not the word but the spirit in which it is uttered is what matters" (McNeile). </p>
