<p><b> Shall wax cold </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ψυγησετα</span></span>). Second future passive indicative from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ψυχω</span></span>. To breathe cool by blowing, to grow cold, "spiritual energy blighted or chilled by a malign or poisonous wind" (Vincent).</p>
<p><b>The love of many </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">η αγαπη των πολλων</span></span>). Love of the brotherhood gives way to mutual hatred and suspicion. </p>
