<p><b> In the wilderness </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εν τη ερημω</span></span>). Like Simon son of Gioras (Josephus, <i>War</i>, IV,9,5,&amp;7).</p>
<p><b>In the inner chambers </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εν τοις ταμειοις</span></span>). Like John of Giscala (Josephus, <i>War</i>, V,6,1). False Messiahs act the role of the Great Unseen and Unknown. </p>
