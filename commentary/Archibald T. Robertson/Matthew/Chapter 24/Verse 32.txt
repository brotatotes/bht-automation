<p><b> Putteth forth its leaves </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τα φυλλα εκφυη</span></span>). Present active subjunctive according to Westcott and Hort. If accented <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εκφυη</span></span> (last syllable), it is second aorist passive subjunctive (Erasmus). </p>
