<p><b> At the mill </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εν τω μυλω</span></span>). So Westcott and Hort and not <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μυλων</span></span> (millhouse) Textus Receptus. The millstone and then hand-mill which was turned by two women (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αληθουσα</span></span>) as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ex+11:5">Exodus 11:5</span>. This verb is a late form for <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αλεω</span></span>. There was a handle near the edge of the upper stone. </p>
