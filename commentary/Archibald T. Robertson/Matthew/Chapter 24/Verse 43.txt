<p><b> In what watch </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ποια φυλακη</span></span>). As in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+14:25">Matthew 14:25</span> (four watches of the night).</p>
<p><b>Broken through </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">διορυχθηνα</span></span>). Digged through the tile roof or under the floor (dirt in the poorer houses). </p>
