<p><b> And while they went away </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">απερχομενων δε αυτων</span></span>). Present middle participle, genitive absolute, while they were going away, descriptive linear action. Picture of their inevitable folly.</p>
<p><b>Was shut </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εκλεισθη</span></span>). Effective aorist passive indicative, shut to stay shut. </p>
