<p><b> Straightway </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ευθεως</span></span>). Beginning of verse 16, not the end of verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+25:15">Matthew 25:15</span>. The business temper of this slave is shown by his promptness.</p>
<p><b>With them </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εν αυτοις</span></span>). Instrumental use of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εν</span></span>. He worked (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ηργασατο</span></span>), did business, traded with them. "The virgins wait, the servants work" (Vincent).</p>
<p><b>Made </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εποιησεν</span></span>). But Westcott and Hort read <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εκερδησεν</span></span>, gained, as in verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+25:17">Matthew 25:17</span>. <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">Κερδος</span></span> means interest. This gain was a hundred per cent. </p>
