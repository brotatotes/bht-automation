<p><b> Maketh a reckoning </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">συναιρε λογον</span></span>). As in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+18:23">Matthew 18:23</span>. Deissmann (<i>Light from the Ancient East</i>, p. 117) gives two papyri quotations with this very business idiom and one Nubian ostracon with it. The ancient Greek writers do not show it. </p>
