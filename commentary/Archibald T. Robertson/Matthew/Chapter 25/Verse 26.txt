<p><b> Thou wicked and slothful servant </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πονηρε δουλε κα οκνηρε</span></span>). From <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πονος</span></span> (work, annoyance, disturbance, evil) and <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">οκνεω</span></span> (to be slow, "poky," slothful). Westcott and Hort make a question out of this reply to the end of verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+25:26">Matthew 25:26</span>. It is sarcasm. </p>
