<p><b> The unprofitable </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τον αχρειον</span></span>). Useless (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">α</span></span> privative and <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">χρειος</span></span>, useful) and so unprofitable, injurious. Doing nothing is doing harm. </p>
