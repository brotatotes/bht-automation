<p><b> Clothed me </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">περιεβαλετε με</span></span>). Second aorist middle indicative, cast something around me.</p>
<p><b>Visited me </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επεσκεψασθε με</span></span>). Looked after, came to see. Our "visit" is from Latin <i>viso, video</i>. Cf. our English "go to see." </p>
