<p><b> They all slumbered and slept </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ενυσταξαν πασα κα εκαθευδον</span></span>). They dropped off to sleep, nodded (ingressive aorist) and then went on sleeping (imperfect, linear action), a vivid picture drawn by the difference in the two tenses. Many a preacher has seen this happen while he is preaching. </p>
