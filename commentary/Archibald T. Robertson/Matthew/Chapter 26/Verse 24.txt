<p><b> Good were it for that man </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">καλον ην αυτω</span></span>). Conclusion of second-class condition even though <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αν</span></span> is not expressed. It is not needed with verbs of obligation and necessity. There are some today who seek to palliate the crime of Judas. But Jesus here pronounces his terrible doom. And Judas heard it and went on with his hellish bargain with the Sanhedrin. Apparently Judas went out at this stage (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+13:31">John 13:31</span>). </p>
