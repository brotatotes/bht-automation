<p><b> Sang a hymn </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">υμνησαντες</span></span>). The <i>Hallel</i>, part of <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+26:115-118">Matthew 26:115-118</span>. But apparently they did not go out at once to the Garden of Gethsemane. Jesus tarried with them in the Upper Room for the wonderful discourse and prayer in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+26:14-17">Matthew 26:14-17</span>. They may have gone out to the street after <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+14:31">John 14:31</span>. It was no longer considered obligatory to remain in the house after the passover meal till morning as at the start (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ex+12:22">Exodus 12:22</span>). Jesus went out to Gethsemane, the garden of the agony, outside of Jerusalem, toward the Mount of Olives. </p>
