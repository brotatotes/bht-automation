<p><b> Even if I must die with thee </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κν δεη με συν σο αποθανειν</span></span>). Third-class condition. A noble speech and meant well. His boast of loyalty is made still stronger by <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ου μη σε απαρνησομα</span></span>. The other disciples were undoubtedly embarrassed by Peter's boast and lightheartedly joined in the same profession of fidelity. </p>
