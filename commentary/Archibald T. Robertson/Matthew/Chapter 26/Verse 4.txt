<p><b> They took counsel together </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">συνεβουλευσαντο</span></span>). Aorist middle indicative, indicating their puzzled state of mind. They have had no trouble in finding Jesus (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+11:57">John 11:57</span>). Their problem now is how to</p>
<p><b>take Jesus by subtilty and kill him </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ινα τον Ιησουν δολω κρατησοσιν κα αποκτεινωσιν</span></span>). The Triumphal Entry and the Tuesday debate in the temple revealed the powerful following that Jesus had among the crowds from Galilee. </p>
