<p><b> What </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ουτως</span></span>). The Greek adverb is not interrogation or exclamatory <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τ</span></span>, but only "so" or "thus." There is a tone of sad disappointment at the discovery that they were asleep after the earnest plea that they keep awake (verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+26:38">Matthew 26:38</span>). "Did you not thus have strength enough to keep awake one hour?" Every word struck home. </p>
