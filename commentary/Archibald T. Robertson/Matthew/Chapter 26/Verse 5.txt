<p><b> A tumult </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">θορυβος</span></span>). They feared the uprising in behalf of Jesus and were arguing that the matter must be postponed till after the feast was over when the crowds had scattered. Then they could catch him "by craft" (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δολω</span></span>) as they would trap a wild beast. </p>
