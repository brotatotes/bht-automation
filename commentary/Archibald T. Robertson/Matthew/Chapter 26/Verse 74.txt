<p><b> Then began he to curse and to swear </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τοτε ηρξατο καταθεματιζειν κα ομνυειν</span></span>). He repeated his denial with the addition of profanity to prove that he was telling the truth instead of the lie that they all knew. His repeated denials gave him away still more, for he could not pronounce the Judean gutterals. He called down on himself (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">καταθεματιζειν</span></span>) imprecations in his desperate irritation and loss of self-control at his exposure.</p>
<p><b>The cock crew </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αλεκτων εφωνησεν</span></span>). No article in the Greek, just "a cock crew" at that juncture, "straightway" (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ευθυς</span></span>). But it startled Peter. </p>
