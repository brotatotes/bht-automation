<p><b> Now when morning was come </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πρωιας δε γενομενης</span></span>). Genitive absolute. After dawn came the Sanhedrin held a formal meeting to condemn Jesus and so ratify the illegal trial during the night (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mr+15:1">Mark 15:1</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+22:66-71">Luke 22:66-71</span>). Luke gives the details of this second ratification consultation. The phrase used,</p>
<p><b>took counsel </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">συμβουλιον ελαβον</span></span>) is a Latin idiom (<i>consilium ceperunt</i>) for <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">συνεβουλευσαντο</span></span>. </p>
