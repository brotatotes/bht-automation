<p><b> Watched him there </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ετηρουν αυτον εκε</span></span>). Imperfect tense descriptive of the task to prevent the possibility of rescue or removal of the body. These rough Roman soldiers casting lots over the garments of Christ give a picture of comedy at the foot of the Cross, the tragedy of the ages. </p>
