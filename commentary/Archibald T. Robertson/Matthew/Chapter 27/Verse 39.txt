<p><b> Wagging their heads </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κινουντες τας κεφαλας αυτων</span></span>). Probably in mock commiseration. "Jews again appear on the scene, with a malice like that shewn in the trial before the Sanhedrin" (McNeile). "To us it may seem incredible that even his worst enemies could be guilty of anything so brutal as to hurl taunts at one suffering the agonies of crucifixion" (Bruce). These passers-by (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">παρατηρουμενο</span></span>) look on Jesus as one now down and out. They jeer at the fallen foe. </p>
