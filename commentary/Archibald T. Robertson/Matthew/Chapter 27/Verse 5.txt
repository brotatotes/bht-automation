<p><b> Hanged himself </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">απηγξατο</span></span>). Direct middle. His act was sudden after he hurled the money into the sanctuary (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εις τον ναον</span></span>), the sacred enclosure where the priests were. The motives of Judas in the betrayal were mixed as is usually the case with criminals. The money cut a small figure with him save as an expression of contempt as the current price of a slave. </p>
