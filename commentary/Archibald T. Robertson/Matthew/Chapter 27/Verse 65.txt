<p><b> Make it as sure as you can </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ασφαλισασθε ως οιδατε</span></span>). "Make it secure for yourselves (ingressive aorist middle) as you know how."</p>
<p><b>Have a guard </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εχετε κουστωδιαν</span></span>), present imperative, a guard of Roman soldiers, not mere temple police. The Latin term <i>koustodia</i> occurs in an Oxyrhynchus papyrus of A.D. 22. "The curt permission to the Jews whom he despised is suitable in the mouth of the Roman official" (McNeile). </p>
