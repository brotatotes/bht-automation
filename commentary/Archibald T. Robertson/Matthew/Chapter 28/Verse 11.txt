<p><b> Told unto the chief priests </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">απηγγειλαν τοις αρχιερευσιν</span></span>). These Roman soldiers had been placed at the disposal of the Sanhedrin. They were probably afraid also to report to Pilate and tell him what had happened. They apparently told a truthful account as far as they understood it. But were the Sanhedrin convinced of the resurrection of Jesus? </p>
