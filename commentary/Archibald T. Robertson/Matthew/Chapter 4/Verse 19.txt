<p><b> Fishers of men </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αλεεις ανθρωπων</span></span>). Andrew and Simon were fishers by trade. They had already become disciples of Jesus (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+1:35-42">John 1:35-42</span>), but now they are called upon to leave their business and to follow Jesus in his travels and work. These two brothers promptly (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ευθεως</span></span>) accepted the call and challenge of Jesus. </p>
