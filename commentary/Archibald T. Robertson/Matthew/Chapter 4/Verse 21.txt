<p><b> Mending their nets </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">καταρτιζοντας τα δικτυα αυτων</span></span>). These two brothers, James and John, were getting their nets ready for use. The verb (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">καταρτιζω</span></span>) means to adjust, to articulate, to mend if needed (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+6:40">Luke 6:40</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+9:22">Romans 9:22</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ga+6:1">Galatians 6:1</span>). So they promptly left their boat and father and followed Jesus. They had also already become disciples of Jesus. Now there are four who follow him steadily. </p>
