<p><b> That have been persecuted for righteousness' sake </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ο δεδιωγμενο ενεκεν δικαιοσυνης</span></span>). Posing as persecuted is a favourite stunt. The kingdom of heaven belongs only to those who suffer for the sake of goodness, not who are guilty of wrong. </p>
