<p><b> The last farthing </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τον εσχατον κοδραντην</span></span>). A Latin word, <i>quadrans, 1/4 of an </i>as_ (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ασσαριον</span></span>) or two mites (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mr+12:42">Mark 12:42</span>), a vivid picture of inevitable punishment for debt. This is emphasized by the strong double negative <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ου μη</span></span> with the aorist subjunctive. </p>
