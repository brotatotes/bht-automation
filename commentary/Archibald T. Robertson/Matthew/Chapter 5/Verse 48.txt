<p><b> Perfect </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τελειο</span></span>). The word comes from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τελος</span></span>, end, goal, limit. Here it is the goal set before us, the absolute standard of our Heavenly Father. The word is used also for relative perfection as of adults compared with children. </p>
