<p><b> In secret </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εν τω κρυφαιω</span></span>). Here as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+6:4">Matthew 6:4</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+6:6">Matthew 6:6</span> the Textus Receptus adds <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εν τω φανερω</span></span> (openly), but it is not genuine. The word <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κρυφαιος</span></span> is here alone in the New Testament, but occurs four times in the Septuagint. </p>
