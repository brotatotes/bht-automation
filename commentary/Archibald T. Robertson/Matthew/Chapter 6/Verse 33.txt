<p><b> First his kingdom </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πρωτον την βασιλειαν</span></span>). This in answer to those who see in the Sermon on the Mount only ethical comments. Jesus in the Beatitudes drew the picture of the man with the new heart. Here he places the Kingdom of God and his righteousness before temporal blessings (food and clothing). </p>
