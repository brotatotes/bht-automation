<p><b> By their fruits ye shall know them </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">απο των καρπων αυτων επιγνωσεσθε</span></span>). From their fruits you will recognize them." The verb "know " (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">γινωσκω</span></span>) has <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επ</span></span> added, fully know. The illustrations from the trees and vines have many parallels in ancient writers. </p>
