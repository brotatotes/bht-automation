<p><b> And doeth them not </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κα μη ποιων αυτους</span></span>). The foolish builder put his house on the sands that could not hold in the storm. One is reminded of the words of Jesus at the beginning of the Sermon in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+5:19">Matthew 5:19</span> about the one "who does and teaches." Hearing sermons is a dangerous business if one does not put them into practice. </p>
