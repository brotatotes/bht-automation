<p><b> When even was come </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">οψιας γενομενης</span></span>). Genitive absolute. A beautiful sunset scene at the close of the Sabbath day (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mr+1:21">Mark 1:21</span>). Then the crowds came as Jesus stood in the door of Peter's house (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mr+1:33">Mark 1:33</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+8:14">Matthew 8:14</span>) as all the city gathered there with the sick, "all those who had it bad" (see on <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+4:24">Matthew 4:24</span>) and he healed them "with a word" (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">λογω</span></span>). It was a never to be forgotten memory for those who saw it. </p>
