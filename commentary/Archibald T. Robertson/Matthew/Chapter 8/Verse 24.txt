<p><b> But he was asleep </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αυτος δε εκαθευδεν</span></span>). Imperfect, was sleeping. Picturesque scene. The Sea of Galilee is 680 feet below the Mediterranean Sea. These sudden squalls come down from the summit of Hermon with terrific force (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">σεισμος μεγας</span></span>) like an earthquake. Mark (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mr+4:37">Mark 4:37</span>) and Luke (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+8:23">Luke 8:23</span>) term it a whirlwind (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">λαιλαπς</span></span>) in furious gusts. </p>
