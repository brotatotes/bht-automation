<p><b> That he would depart </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">οπως μεταβη</span></span>). The whole city was excited over the destruction of the hogs and begged Jesus to leave, forgetful of the healing of the demoniacs in their concern over the loss of property. They cared more for hogs than for human souls, as often happens today. </p>
