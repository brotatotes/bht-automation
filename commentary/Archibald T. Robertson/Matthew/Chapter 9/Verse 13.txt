<p><b> But go ye and learn </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πορευθεντες δε μαθετε</span></span>). With biting sarcasm Jesus bids these preachers to learn the meaning of <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ho+6:6">Hosea 6:6</span>. It is repeated in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+12:7">Matthew 12:7</span>. Ingressive aorist imperative (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μαθετε</span></span>). </p>
