<p><b> The sons of the bride-chamber </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ο υιο του νυμφωνος</span></span>). It is a late Hebrew idiom for the wedding guests, "the friends of the bridegroom and all the sons of the bride-chamber" (<i>Tos. Berak.</i> ii. 10). Cf. <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+2:29">John 2:29</span>. </p>
