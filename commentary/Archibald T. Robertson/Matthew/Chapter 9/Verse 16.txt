<p><b> Undressed cloth </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ρακους αγναφου</span></span>). An unfulled, raw piece of woollen cloth that will shrink when wet and tear a bigger hole than ever.</p>
<p><b>A worse rent </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">χειρον σχισμα</span></span>). Our word "schism." The "<b>patch</b>" (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πληρωμα</span></span>, filling up) thus does more harm than good. </p>
