 They brought  (προσεφερον). Imperfect, "were bringing," graphic picture made very vivid by the details in Mark 2:1-4 and Luke 5:17. " Lying on a bed " (stretched on a couch), perfect passive participle, a little bed or couch (κλινιδιον) in Luke 5:19, "a pallet" (κραβατος) in Mark 2:4; Mark 2:9; Mark 2:11.Thy sins are forgiven  (αφιεντα). Present passive indicative (aoristic present). Luke (Luke 5:21) has αφεωντα, Doric and Ionic perfect passive indicative for the Attic αφειντα, one of the dialectical forms appearing in the Koine.
