<p><b> As Jesus passed by </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">παραγοντ Ιησου</span></span>). Associative instrumental case with <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ηκολουθησαν</span></span>. It was the supreme opportunity of these two blind men. Note two demoniacs in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+8:28">Matthew 8:28</span> and two blind men in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+20:30">Matthew 20:30</span>. See the same word <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">παραγων</span></span> used of Jesus in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+9:9">Matthew 9:9</span>. </p>
