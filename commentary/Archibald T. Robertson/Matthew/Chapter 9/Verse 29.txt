<p><b> Touched their eyes </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ηψατο των οφθαλμων</span></span>). The men had faith (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+9:28">Matthew 9:28</span>) and Jesus rewards their faith and yet he touched their eyes as he sometimes did with kindly sympathy. </p>
