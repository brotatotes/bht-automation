<p><b> Let me have joy of thee </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εγω σου οναιμην</span></span>). Second aorist middle optative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ονινημ</span></span>, old verb, only here in N.T. Optative the regular construction for a wish about the future. "May I get profit from thee in the Lord."</p>
