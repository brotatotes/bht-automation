<p><b> But withal </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αμα δε</span></span>). Along with your kindly reception of Onesimus. On <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αμα</span></span>, see <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+24:26">Acts 24:26</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+27:40">Acts 27:40</span>.</p>
