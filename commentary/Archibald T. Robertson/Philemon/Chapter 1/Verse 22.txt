 But withal  (αμα δε). Along with your kindly reception of Onesimus. On αμα, see Acts 24:26; Acts 27:40.A lodging  (ξενιαν). Old word from ξενος, stranger. In N.T. only here and Acts 28:23.I shall be granted unto you  (χαρισθησομα υμιν). First future passive of χαριζομα. Used either as a favour as here and Acts 3:14 or for destruction (Acts 25:11).
