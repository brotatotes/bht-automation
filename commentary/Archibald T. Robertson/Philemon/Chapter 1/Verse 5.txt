<p><b> Hearing </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ακουων</span></span>). Through Epaphras (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="col+1:7">Colossians 1:7</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="col+1:8">Colossians 1:8</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="col+4:12">Colossians 4:12</span>), possibly from Onesimus also.</p>
<p><b>And towards all the saints </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κα εις παντας τους αγιους</span></span>). He spoke of "thy love and faith" (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">σου την αγαπην κα την πιστιν</span></span>) "towards the Lord Jesus" (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">προς τον Κυριον Ιησουν</span></span>) and by a sort of momentum (Vincent) he carries both words over to the saints, though it can be explained as chiasm (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ga+4:4">Galatians 4:4</span>) also. </p>
