<p><b> Earnest expectation </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αποκαραδοκιαν</span></span>). In Paul alone from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αποκαραδοκεω</span></span> (in papyri). See on <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+8:19">Romans 8:19</span> for only other example.</p>
<p><b>Shall be magnified </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μεγαλυνθησετα</span></span>). Future passive indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μεγαλυνω</span></span>, old verb, to make great, from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μεγας</span></span> (great). See <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+19:17">Acts 19:17</span>.</p>
<p><b>In my body </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εν τω σωματ μου</span></span>). See <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+12:1">Romans 12:1</span>. It is harder often to make Christ great in the body than in the spirit. </p>
