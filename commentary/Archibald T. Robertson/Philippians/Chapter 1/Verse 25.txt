<p><b> And abide with you all </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κα παραμενω πασιν υμιν</span></span>). Common Pauline idiom to repeat the simple verb (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μενω</span></span>) as a compound (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">παραμενω</span></span>, future active indicative), old verb, to remain beside followed by locative case. See same idiom in <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">χαιρω, συνχαιρω</span></span> (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="php+2:17">Philippians 2:17</span>). </p>
