<p><b> In Christ Jesus in me </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εν Χριστω Ιησου εν εμο</span></span>). "In Christ Jesus" as the basis for the glorying (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">καυχημα</span></span>), "in me" as the instance in point.</p>
<p><b>Through my presence </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δια της εμης παρουσιας</span></span>). The word so often used of the second coming of Christ, but here in its ordinary sense as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="php+2:12">Philippians 2:12</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1co+16:17">1 Corinthians 16:17</span>. </p>
