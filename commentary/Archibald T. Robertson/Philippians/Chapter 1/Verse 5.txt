<p><b> For your fellowship </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επ τη κοινωνια υμων</span></span>). "On the basis of your contribution" as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2co+8:4">2 Corinthians 8:4</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2co+9:13">2 Corinthians 9:13</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+2:42">Acts 2:42</span>. The particular kind of "partnership" or "fellowship" involved is the contribution made by the Philippians for the spread of the gospel (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="php+1:7">Philippians 1:7</span> <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">συγκοινωνους</span></span> and <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="php+4:14">Philippians 4:14</span> where <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">συγκοινωνησαντες</span></span> occurs).</p>
<p><b>In furtherance of the gospel </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εις το ευαγγελιον</span></span>). "For the gospel."</p>
<p><b>From the first day until now </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">απο της πρωτης ημερας αχρ του νυν</span></span>). As when in Thessalonica (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="php+4:15">Philippians 4:15</span>), in Corinth (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+18:5">Acts 18:5</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2co+11:7-10">2 Corinthians 11:7-10</span>), and now in Rome. </p>
