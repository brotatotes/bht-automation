<p><b> My witness </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μαρτυς μου</span></span>). Same solemn oath in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+1:9">Romans 1:9</span>.</p>
<p><b>I long after </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επιποθω</span></span>). Longing (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ποθος</span></span>) directed toward (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επ</span></span>) the Philippians. Old word, chiefly in Paul in N.T.</p>
<p><b>In the tender mercies </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εν σπλαγχνοις</span></span>). Literally "in the bowels" as the seat of the affections. </p>
