<p><b> Without murmurings </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">χωρις γογγυσμων</span></span>). See on <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+6:1">Acts 6:1</span> for this late onomatopoetic word from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">γογγυζω</span></span>, to mutter, to grumble.</p>
<p><b>Disputings </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">διαλογισμων</span></span>). Or questionings as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+24:38">Luke 24:38</span>. The grumblings led to disputes. </p>
