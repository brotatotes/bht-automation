<p><b> Likeminded </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ισοψυχον</span></span>). Old, but very rare adjective (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ισοσ, ψυχη</span></span>), like <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ισοτιμος</span></span> in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2pe+1:1">2 Peter 1:1</span>. Only here in N.T. Likeminded with Timothy, not with Paul.</p>
<p><b>Truly </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">γνησιως</span></span>). "Genuinely." Old adverb, only here in N.T., from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">γνησιος</span></span> (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="php+4:3">Philippians 4:3</span>), legitimate birth, not spurious. </p>
