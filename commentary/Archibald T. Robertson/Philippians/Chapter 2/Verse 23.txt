<p><b> So soon as I shall see </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ως αν αφιδω</span></span>). Indefinite temporal clause with <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ως αν</span></span> and the second aorist active subjunctive of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αφοραω</span></span>. The oldest MSS. (Aleph A B D) have <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αφιδω</span></span> (old aspirated form) rather than <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">απιδω</span></span>.</p>
<p><b>How it will go with me </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τα περ εμε</span></span>). On the force of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">απο</span></span> with <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">οραω</span></span> (look away) see <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="heb+12:2">Hebrews 12:2</span>. "The things concerning me," the outcome of the trial. Cf. <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1co+4:17">1 Corinthians 4:17</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1co+4:19">1 Corinthians 4:19</span>. </p>
