<p><b> I told you often </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πολλακις ελεγον</span></span>). Imperfect active, repetition in Paul s warnings to them.</p>
<p><b>Even weeping </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κα κλαιων</span></span>). Deep emotion as he dictated the letter and recalled these recreant followers of Christ (cf. <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2co+2:4">2 Corinthians 2:4</span>).</p>
<p><b>The enemies of the cross of Christ </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τους εχθρους του σταυρου του Χριστου</span></span>). Either the Judaizers who denied the value of the cross of Christ (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ga+5:11">Galatians 5:11</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ga+6:12">Galatians 6:12</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ga+6:14">Galatians 6:14</span>) or Epicurean antinomians whose loose living gave the lie to the cross of Christ (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1jo+2:4">1 John 2:4</span>). </p>
