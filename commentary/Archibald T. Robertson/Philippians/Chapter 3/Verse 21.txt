<p><b> Shall fashion anew </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μετασχηματισε</span></span>). Future active indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μετασχηματιζω</span></span> for which see <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1co+4:6">1 Corinthians 4:6</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2co+11:13">2 Corinthians 11:13</span>.</p>
<p><b>Conformed to </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">συμμορφον</span></span>). For which (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">συν, μορφη</span></span>) see <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+8:29">Romans 8:29</span>, only N.T. examples. With associative instrumental case. The body of our state of humiliation will be made suitable to associate with the body of Christ's glory (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1co+15:54">1 Corinthians 15:54</span>).</p>
<p><b>According to the working </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κατα την ενεργειαν</span></span>). "According to the energy." If any one doubts the power of Christ to do this transformation, Paul replies that he has power "even to subject all things unto himself." </p>
