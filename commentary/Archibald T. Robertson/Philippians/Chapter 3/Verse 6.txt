<p><b> As touching zeal </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κατα ζηλος</span></span>). So the old MSS. treating <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ζηλος</span></span> as neuter, not masculine. He was a zealot against Christianity, "persecuting the church" (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">διωκων την εκκλησιαν</span></span>). He was the ringleader in the persecution from the death of Stephen till his own conversion (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+8:1-9">Acts 8:1-9</span>).</p>
<p><b>Found blameless </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">γενομενος αμεμπτος</span></span>). "Having become blameless" (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ga+1:14">Galatians 1:14</span>). He knew and practised all the rules of the rabbis. A marvellous record, scoring a hundred in Judaism. </p>
