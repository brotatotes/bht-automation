<p><b> Again I will say </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">παλιν ερω</span></span>). Future active indicative of defective verb <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ειπον</span></span>.</p>
<p><b>Rejoice </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">χαιρετε</span></span>). Present active imperative as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="php+3:1">Philippians 3:1</span>, repeated for emphasis in spite of discouragements. Not in the sense of "Farewell" here. </p>
