<p><b> In me </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εν εμο</span></span>). Paul dares to point to his life in Philippi as an illustration of this high thinking. The preacher is the interpreter of the spiritual life and should be an example of it.</p>
<p><b>These things do </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ταυτα πρασσετε</span></span>). Practise as a habit (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πρασσω</span></span>, not <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ποιεω</span></span>). </p>
