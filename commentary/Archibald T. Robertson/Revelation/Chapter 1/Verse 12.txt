<p><b> To see the voice </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">βλεπειν την φωνην</span></span>). The voice put for the person speaking.</p>
<p><b>Having turned </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επιστρεψας</span></span>). First aorist active participle of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επιστρεφω</span></span>, from which also <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επεστρεψα</span></span>, just before, for which verb see <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+15:36">Acts 15:36</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+16:18">Acts 16:18</span>.</p>
<p><b>Seven golden candlesticks </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επτα λυχνιας χρυσας</span></span>). See <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+5:15">Matthew 5:15</span> for <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">λυχνια</span></span> (lampstand). Symbols of the seven churches as explained in verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+1:20">Revelation 1:20</span>. See <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ex+25:35">Exodus 25:35</span> for description of a seven-branched candlestick, but here the lampstands are separate. </p>
