 To see the voice  (βλεπειν την φωνην). The voice put for the person speaking.Having turned  (επιστρεψας). First aorist active participle of επιστρεφω, from which also επεστρεψα, just before, for which verb see Acts 15:36; Acts 16:18.Seven golden candlesticks  (επτα λυχνιας χρυσας). See Matthew 5:15 for λυχνια (lampstand). Symbols of the seven churches as explained in verse Revelation 1:20. See Exodus 25:35 for description of a seven-branched candlestick, but here the lampstands are separate.
