<p><b> As white wool </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ως εριον λευκον</span></span>). <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">Εριον</span></span> (wool) in N.T. only here and <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="heb+9:19">Hebrews 9:19</span>, though old word. The person of the Lord Jesus is here described in language largely from <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="da+7:9">Daniel 7:9</span> (the Ancient of Days).</p>
<p><b>White as snow </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ως χιων</span></span>). Just "as snow," also in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="da+7:9">Daniel 7:9</span>. In N.T. only here and <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+28:3">Matthew 28:3</span>.</p>
<p><b>As a flame of fire </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ως φλοξ πυρος</span></span>). In <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="da+7:9">Daniel 7:9</span> the throne of the Ancient of Days is <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">φλοξ πυρος</span></span>, while in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="da+10:6">Daniel 10:6</span> the eyes of the Ancient of Days are <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">λαμπαδες πυρος</span></span> (lamps of fire). See also <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+2:18">Revelation 2:18</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+19:12">Revelation 19:12</span> for this bold metaphor (like <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="heb+1:7">Hebrews 1:7</span>). </p>
