<p><b> Standing </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εστωτα</span></span>). Second perfect active participle of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ιστημ</span></span> (intransitive). John resumes the picture in verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+10:2">Revelation 10:2</span>.</p>
<p><b>Lifted up </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ηρεν</span></span>). First aorist active indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αιρω</span></span>, to lift up.</p>
<p><b>To heaven </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εις τον ουρανον</span></span>). Toward heaven, the customary gesture in taking a solemn oath (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ge+14:22">Genesis 14:22</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="de+32:40">Deuteronomy 32:40</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="da+12:7">Daniel 12:7</span>). </p>
