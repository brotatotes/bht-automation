<p><b> Sware </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ωμοσεν</span></span>). First aorist indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ομνυω</span></span> to swear.</p>
<p><b>By him that liveth </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εν τω ζωντ</span></span>). This use of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εν</span></span> after <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ομνυω</span></span> instead of the usual accusative (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="jas+5:12">James 5:12</span>) is like the Hebrew (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+5:34">Matthew 5:34</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+5:36">Matthew 5:36</span>). "The living one for ages of ages" is a common phrase in the Apocalypse for God as eternally existing (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+1:18">Revelation 1:18</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+4:9">Revelation 4:9</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+4:10">Revelation 4:10</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+15:7">Revelation 15:7</span>). This oath proves that this angel is not Christ.</p>
<p><b>Who created </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ος εκτισεν</span></span>). First aorist active indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κτιζω</span></span>, a reference to God's creative activity as seen in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ge+1:1">Genesis 1:1</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ex+20:11">Exodus 20:11</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="isa+37:16">Isaiah 37:16</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="isa+42:5">Isaiah 42:5</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ps+33:6">Psalms 33:6</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ps+145:6">Psalms 145:6</span>, etc.</p>
<p><b>That there shall be time no longer </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">οτ χρονος ουκετ εστα</span></span>). Future indicative indirect discourse with <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">οτ</span></span>. But this does not mean that <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">χρονος</span></span> (time), Einstein's "fourth dimension" (added to length, breadth, height), will cease to exist, but only that there will be no more delay in the fulfillment of the seventh trumpet (verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+10:7">Revelation 10:7</span>), in answer to the question, "How long?" (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+6:10">Revelation 6:10</span>). </p>
