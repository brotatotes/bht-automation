<p><b> Is past </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">απηλθεν</span></span>). Second aorist active indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">απερχομα</span></span>. See <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+9:12">Revelation 9:12</span> for this use and <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+21:1">Revelation 21:1</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+21:4">Revelation 21:4</span>. The second woe (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">η ουα η δευτερα</span></span>) is the sixth trumpet (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+9:12">Revelation 9:12</span>) with the two episodes attached (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+10:1-11">Revelation 10:1-11</span>).</p>
<p><b>The third woe </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">η ουα η τριτη</span></span>, feminine as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+9:12">Revelation 9:12</span>) is the seventh trumpet, which now "cometh quickly" (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ερχετα ταχυ</span></span>), for which phrase see <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+2:16">Revelation 2:16</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+3:11">Revelation 3:11</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+22:7">Revelation 22:7</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+22:12">Revelation 22:12</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+22:20">Revelation 22:20</span>. Usually pointing to the Parousia. </p>
