<p><b> Helped the woman </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εβοηθησεν τη γυναικ</span></span>). First aorist active indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">βοηθεω</span></span>, old verb with the dative as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="heb+2:18">Hebrews 2:18</span>, which see. Herodotus tells of the Lycus disappearing underground near Colossae. But this vivid symbol is not dependent on historical examples.</p>
<p><b>Swallowed up </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κατεπιεν</span></span>). Second aorist active indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">καταπινω</span></span>, literally "drank down." </p>
