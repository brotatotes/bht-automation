 Here is the patience of the saints  (Hωδε η υπομονη των αγιων εστιν). John's own comment as in Revelation 13:10; Revelation 17:9. In this struggle against emperor worship lay their opportunity (Romans 5:3). It was a test of loyalty to Christ.They that keep  (ο τηρουντες). In apposition with των αγιων (genitive), though nominative, a frequent anacoluthon in this book (Revelation 2:20, etc.). Cf. Revelation 12:17.The faith of Jesus  (την πιστιν Ιησου). "The faith in Jesus" (objective genitive) as in Revelation 2:13; Mark 11:22; James 2:1.
