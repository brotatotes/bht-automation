<p><b> He also </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κα αυτος</span></span>). As well as the Reaper on the cloud. This is the fifth angel who is God's messenger from heaven (temple where God dwells). This fifth angel with his sharp sickle is to gather the vintage (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+14:18-20">Revelation 14:18-20</span>) as Christ did the wheat. </p>
