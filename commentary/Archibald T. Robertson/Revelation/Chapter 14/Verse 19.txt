<p><b> Cast </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εβαλεν</span></span>). As in verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+14:16">Revelation 14:16</span>.</p>
<p><b>Gathered </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ετρυγησεν</span></span>). Like <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εθερισθη</span></span> in verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+14:16">Revelation 14:16</span>, in obedience to the instructions in verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+14:18">Revelation 14:18</span> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τρυγησον</span></span>).</p>
<p><b>The vintage of the earth </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">την αμπελον της γης</span></span>). "The vine of the earth." Here <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αμπελος</span></span> is used for the enemies of Christ collectively pictured.</p>
<p><b>And cast it </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εβαλεν</span></span>). Repeating <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εβαλεν</span></span> and referring to <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αμπελον</span></span> (vintage) just before.</p>
<p><b>Into the winepress the great winepress </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εις την ληνον τον μεγαν</span></span>). <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">Ληνος</span></span> is either feminine as in verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+14:20">Revelation 14:20</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+19:15">Revelation 19:15</span>, or masculine sometimes in ancient Greek. Here we have both genders, a solecism frequent in the Apocalypse (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+21:14">Revelation 21:14</span> <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">το τειχος εχων</span></span>). See <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+21:33">Matthew 21:33</span>. For this metaphor of God s wrath see <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+14:10">Revelation 14:10</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+15:1">Revelation 15:1</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+15:7">Revelation 15:7</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+16:1">Revelation 16:1</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+16:19">Revelation 16:19</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+19:15">Revelation 19:15</span>. </p>
