<p><b> And he saith </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">λεγων</span></span>). See above.</p>
<p><b>Fear God </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">φοβηθητε τον θεον</span></span>). First aorist passive (deponent) imperative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">φοβεομα</span></span>, here transitive with the accusative as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+12:5">Luke 12:5</span>. It is a call to judgment with no hope offered except by implication (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+14:15">Acts 14:15</span>).</p>
<p><b>Give him glory </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δοτε αυτω δοξαν</span></span>). Second aorist active indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">διδωμ</span></span>. For the phrase see <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+11:13">Revelation 11:13</span>.</p>
<p><b>The hour is come </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">η ωρα ηλθεν</span></span>). Second aorist (prophetic use) active indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ερχομα</span></span>. Common idiom in John's Gospel (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+2:4">Revelation 2:4</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+4:21">Revelation 4:21</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+4:23">Revelation 4:23</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+5:25">Revelation 5:25</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+5:28">Revelation 5:28</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+7:30">Revelation 7:30</span>, etc.).</p>
<p><b>Worship </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">προσκυνησατε</span></span>). First aorist active imperative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">προσκυνεω</span></span> with the dative case. Solemn call to the pagan world to worship God as Creator (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+4:11">Revelation 4:11</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+10:6">Revelation 10:6</span>), as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ps+96:6">Psalms 96:6</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+14:15">Acts 14:15</span>. For "the fountains of waters" see <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+8:10">Revelation 8:10</span>. </p>
