<p><b> The temple of the tabernacle of the testimony </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ο ναος της σκηνης του μαρτυριου</span></span>). Charles calls this "strange" language. Probably the tabernacle or tent of witness (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="nu+9:15">Numbers 9:15</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="nu+17:7">Numbers 17:7</span>) is in mind and the tent of meeting (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ex+27:21">Exodus 27:21</span> rather than the temple in Jerusalem.</p>
