<p><b> There came out </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εξηλθαν</span></span>). Second aorist active indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εξερχομα</span></span> with <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">-αν</span></span> rather than <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">-ον</span></span>. Proleptic and prophetic aorist.</p>
<p><b>The seven angels </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ο επτα αγγελο</span></span>). Those in verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+15:1">Revelation 15:1</span>.</p>
<p><b>The seven plagues </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τας επτα πληγας</span></span>). The bowls are not given them till verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+15:7">Revelation 15:7</span>.</p>
<p><b>Arrayed </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ενδεδυμενο</span></span>). Perfect passive participle of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ενδυω</span></span>.</p>
<p><b>With precious stone pure and bright </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">λιθον καθαρον λαμπρον</span></span>). Accusative case retained with verb of clothing as so often, literally "with a stone pure bright." For both adjectives together see <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+19:8">Revelation 19:8</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+19:14">Revelation 19:14</span>. Some MSS. read <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">λινον</span></span> (linen). For <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">λιθον</span></span> see <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+17:4">Revelation 17:4</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+18:16">Revelation 18:16</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="eze+28:13">Ezekiel 28:13</span>.</p>
<p><b>Girt </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">περιεζωσμενο</span></span>). Perfect passive participle of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">περιζωννυω</span></span>. See <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+1:13">Revelation 1:13</span> for both participles. For <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">στηθος</span></span> (breast) see <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+18:13">Luke 18:13</span>.</p>
<p><b>With golden girdles </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ζωνας χρυσας</span></span>). Accusative case after the perfect passive participle <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">περιεζωσμενο</span></span> as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+1:13">Revelation 1:13</span>. </p>
