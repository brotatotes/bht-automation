<p><b> Was filled with smoke </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εγεμισθη καπνου</span></span>). First aorist passive indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">γεμιζω</span></span> (from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">γεμω</span></span>), to fill full, and with the genitive <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">καπνου</span></span> (smoke). Smoke is here the symbol of God's presence (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ex+19:18">Exodus 19:18</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="isa+6:5">Isaiah 6:5</span>).</p>
<p><b>Till should be finished </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αχρ τελεσθωσιν</span></span>). Temporal clause for future time with <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αχρ</span></span> (equal to <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εως</span></span> in import) and the first aorist passive subjunctive of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τελεω</span></span>, a metaphorical and symbolic "smoke screen" to keep all out of the sanctuary for the time being. </p>
