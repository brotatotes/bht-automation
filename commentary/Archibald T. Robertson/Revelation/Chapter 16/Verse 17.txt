<p><b> Upon the air </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επ τον αερα</span></span>). All men breathe the air and this is worse than the smiting of the earth (verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+16:2">Revelation 16:2</span>), the sea (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+16:3">Revelation 16:3</span>), the fresh waters (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+16:4">Revelation 16:4</span>), the sun (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+16:8">Revelation 16:8</span>).</p>
<p><b>A great voice </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">φωνη μεγαλη</span></span>). The voice of God as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+16:1">Revelation 16:1</span>.</p>
<p><b>It is done </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">Γεγονεν</span></span>). Perfect active indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">γινομα</span></span>. Like <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">Γεγοναν</span></span> in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+21:6">Revelation 21:6</span>. The whole series of plagues is now complete. </p>
