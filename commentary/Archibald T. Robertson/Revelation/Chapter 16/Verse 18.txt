<p><b> And there were </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κα εγενοντο</span></span>). "And there came" (same verb <i>ginomai</i>). See <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+8:5">Revelation 8:5</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+11:19">Revelation 11:19</span> for this list of terrible sounds and lightnings, and for the great earthquake (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">σεισμος μεγας</span></span>) see <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+6:12">Revelation 6:12</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+11:13">Revelation 11:13</span> (cf. <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+21:11">Luke 21:11</span>).</p>
<p><b>Such as was not </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">οιος ουκ εγενετο</span></span>). Qualitative relative with <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">γινομα</span></span> again, "such as came not."</p>
<p><b>Since there were men </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αφ' ου ανθρωπο εγενοντο</span></span>). "Since which time (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">χρονου</span></span> understood) men came."</p>
<p><b>So great an earthquake, so mighty </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τηλικουτος σεισμος ουτω μεγας</span></span>). Quantitative correlative <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τηλικουτος</span></span> rather than the qualitative <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τοιουτος</span></span>, to correspond with <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">οιος</span></span> (not <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">οσος</span></span>). And then <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ουτω μεγας</span></span> repeats (redundant) <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τηλικουτος</span></span>. Cf. <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mr+13:19">Mark 13:19</span> for <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">οια--τοιαυτη</span></span> about like tribulation (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">θλιψις</span></span>). </p>
