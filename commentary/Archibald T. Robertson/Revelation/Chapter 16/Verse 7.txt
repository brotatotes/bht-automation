<p><b> O Lord God, the Almighty </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">Κυριε ο θεος ο παντοκρατωρ</span></span>). Just as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+15:3">Revelation 15:3</span> in the Song of Moses and of the Lamb, vocative with the article <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ο</span></span>. "Judgments" (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κρισεις</span></span>) here instead of "ways" (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">οδο</span></span>) there, and with the order of the adjectives reversed (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αληθινα κα δικαια</span></span>, true and righteous). </p>
