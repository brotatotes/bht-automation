<p><b> I will tell thee the mystery </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εγω ερω σο το μυστηριον</span></span>). The angel gives his interpretation of the woman and the beast (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+17:7-18">Revelation 17:7-18</span>). <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">Ερω</span></span> is the future active of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ειπον</span></span> (defective verb), to tell, to say. </p>
