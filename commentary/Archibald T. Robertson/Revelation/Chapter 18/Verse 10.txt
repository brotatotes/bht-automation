<p><b> Standing afar off </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">απο μακροθεν εστηκοτες</span></span>). Perfect active (intransitive) participle of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ιστημ</span></span>. Vivid picture of the terrible scene, fascinated by the lurid blaze (cf. Nero's delight in the burning of Rome in A.D. 64), and yet afraid to draw near. On <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">απο μακροθεν</span></span> see <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mr+5:6">Mark 5:6</span>. There is a weird charm in a burning city. They feared the same fate (cf. verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+18:7">Revelation 18:7</span> for <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">βασανισμου</span></span>, torment).</p>
<p><b>Woe, woe, the great city </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ουαι, ουαι, η πολις η μεγαλη</span></span>). Only example in the Apocalypse of the nominative with <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ουα</span></span> except verses <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+18:16">Revelation 18:16</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+18:19">Revelation 18:19</span>, though in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+6:25">Luke 6:25</span> and common in LXX (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="isa+5:7">Isaiah 5:7</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="isa+5:11">Isaiah 5:11</span>, etc.). For the dative see <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+8:13">Revelation 8:13</span>, once so "strong" (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">η ισχυρα</span></span>)!</p>
<p><b>In one hour </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μια ωρα</span></span>). Repeated in verses <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+18:16">Revelation 18:16</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+18:19">Revelation 18:19</span>, and like <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μια ημερα</span></span> (in one day) in verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+18:8">Revelation 18:8</span>. Some MSS. have here <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μιαν ωραν</span></span>, like <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ποιαν ωραν</span></span> (accusative of extent of time) in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+3:3">Revelation 3:3</span>. See verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+18:8">Revelation 18:8</span> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ο κρινας</span></span>) for <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">η κρισις σου</span></span> (thy judgment). This is the dirge of the kings. </p>
