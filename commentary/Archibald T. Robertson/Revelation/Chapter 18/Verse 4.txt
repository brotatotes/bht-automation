<p><b> Come forth, my people, out of her </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εξελθατε, ο λαος μου, εξ αυτης</span></span>). Second aorist (urgency) active imperative (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">-α</span></span> form) of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εξερχομα</span></span>. Like <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="isa+48:20">Isaiah 48:20</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="isa+52:11">Isaiah 52:11</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="jer+50:8">Jeremiah 50:8</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="jer+51:6">Jeremiah 51:6</span>, (about Babylon). See also the call of Abram (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ge+12:1">Genesis 12:1</span>). the rescue of Lot (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ge+19:12">Genesis 19:12</span>). In the N.T. see <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mr+13:4">Mark 13:4</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2co+6:14">2 Corinthians 6:14</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="eph+5:11">Ephesians 5:11</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1ti+5:11">1 Timothy 5:11</span>. <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">Hο λαος</span></span> is vocative with the form of the nominative.</p>
<p><b>That ye have no fellowship with her sins </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ινα μη συνκοινωνησητε ταις αμαρταις αυτης</span></span>). Purpose clause with <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ινα μη</span></span> and the first aorist active subjunctive of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">συνκοινωνεω</span></span>, old compound (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">συν</span></span>, together, <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κοινωνος</span></span>, partner), in N.T. only here, <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="php+4:14">Philippians 4:14</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="eph+5:11">Ephesians 5:11</span>. With associative instrumental case <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αμαρτιαις</span></span>.</p>
<p><b>And that ye receive not of her plagues </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κα εκ των πληγων αυτης ινα μη λαβητε</span></span>). Another purpose clause dependent on the preceding, with <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ινα μη</span></span> and the second aorist active subjunctive of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">λαμβανω</span></span>, and with proleptic emphatic position of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εκ των πληγων αυτης</span></span> before <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ινα μη</span></span>. </p>
