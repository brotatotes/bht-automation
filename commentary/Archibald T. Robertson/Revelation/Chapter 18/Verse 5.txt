<p><b> Have reached </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εκολληθησαν</span></span>). First aorist passive (deponent) indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κολλαω</span></span>, old verb (from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κολλα</span></span>, gluten, glue), to cleave to, to join one another in a mass "up to heaven" (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αχρ του ουρανου</span></span>). Cf. <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="jer+51:9">Jeremiah 51:9</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="zec+14:5">Zechariah 14:5</span>.</p>
<p><b>Hath remembered </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εμνημονευσεν</span></span>). First aorist (prophetic) active indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μνημονευω</span></span>, here with the accusative (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αδικηματα</span></span>, iniquities) instead of the genitive (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="col+4:18">Colossians 4:18</span>). </p>
