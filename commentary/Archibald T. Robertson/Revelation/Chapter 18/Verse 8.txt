<p><b> Therefore </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δια τουτο</span></span>). Because of her presumption added to her crimes.</p>
<p><b>In one day </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εν μια ημερα</span></span>). Symbolical term for suddenness like <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μια ωρα</span></span>, in one hour (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+18:10">Revelation 18:10</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+18:16">Revelation 18:16</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+18:19">Revelation 18:19</span>). John has in mind still <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="isa+47:7-9">Isaiah 47:7-9</span>.</p>
<p><b>Shall come </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ηξουσιν</span></span>). Future active of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ηκω</span></span>. Her plagues are named (death, mourning, famine).</p>
<p><b>She shall be utterly burned </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κατακαυθησετα</span></span>). Future passive of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κατακαιω</span></span> (perfective use of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κατα</span></span>).</p>
<p><b>With fire </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εν πυρ</span></span>). "In fire," as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+17:16">Revelation 17:16</span>.</p>
<p><b>Which judged her </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ο κρινας αυτην</span></span>). Articular first aorist active participle of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κρινω</span></span> referring to <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κυριος ο θεος</span></span> (the Lord God). The doom of Babylon is certain because of the power of God. </p>
