<p><b> For </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">οτ</span></span>). Because. The reason for God's judgments is given in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+15:3">Revelation 15:3</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+16:7">Revelation 16:7</span>. The doom of Babylon seen in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+14:7">Revelation 14:7</span> is now realized.</p>
<p><b>For </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">οτ</span></span>). Second use of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">οτ</span></span>, explaining the first.</p>
<p><b>He hath judged </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εκρινεν</span></span>). First aorist (prophetic and climacteric, effective) active indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κρινω</span></span>.</p>
<p><b>Which </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ητις</span></span>). The very one which.</p>
<p><b>Did corrupt </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εφθειρεν</span></span>). This is the terrible fact. First aorist active indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">φθειρω</span></span>. Cf. <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+11:18">Revelation 11:18</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+14:8">Revelation 14:8</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+17:2">Revelation 17:2</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+18:3">Revelation 18:3</span>.</p>
<p><b>And he hath avenged </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κα εξεδικησεν</span></span>). God has exacted vengeance for the blood of his servants from (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εκ</span></span>) her. Prophetic aorist again of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εκδικεω</span></span> with accusative and <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εκ</span></span> with ablative as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+6:10">Revelation 6:10</span>. </p>
