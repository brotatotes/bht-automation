<p><b> The rest </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ο λοιπο</span></span>). Of the enemy (the kings and their hosts of verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+19:19">Revelation 19:19</span>).</p>
<p><b>Were killed </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">απεκτανθησαν</span></span>).
