<p><b> A voice from the throne </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">φωνη απο του θρονου</span></span>). Not the voice of God, nor of the Lamb, nor <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εκ του ναου</span></span> (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+16:17">Revelation 16:17</span>), but from an angel of the Presence. This angel summons all the servants of God to join in the antiphonal praise to God.</p>
<p><b>Give praise to our God </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αινειτε τω θεω ημων</span></span>). Present active imperative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αινεω</span></span>, old verb, with the accusative elsewhere in N.T., but here with the dative as occasionally in the LXX (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1ch+16:36">1 Chronicles 16:36</span>, etc.). </p>
