<p><b> Repent therefore </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μετανοησον ουν</span></span>). First aorist (tense of urgency) active imperative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μετανοεω</span></span> with the inferential particle <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ουν</span></span> (as a result of their sin).</p>
<p><b>I come </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ερχομα</span></span>). Futuristic present middle indicative, "I am coming" (imminent), as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+2:5">Revelation 2:5</span> with <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ταχυ</span></span> as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+3:11">Revelation 3:11</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+11:14">Revelation 11:14</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+22:7">Revelation 22:7</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+22:12">Revelation 22:12</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+22:20">Revelation 22:20</span>. As with <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εν ταχε</span></span> (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+1:1">Revelation 1:1</span>), we do not know how soon "quickly" is meant to be understood. But it is a real threat.</p>
<p><b>Against them </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μετ' αυτων</span></span>). This proposition with <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πολεμεω</span></span> rather than <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κατα</span></span> (against) is common in the LXX, but in the N.T. only in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+2:16">Revelation 2:16</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+12:7">Revelation 12:7</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+13:4">Revelation 13:4</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+17:14">Revelation 17:14</span> and the verb itself nowhere else in N.T. except <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="jas+4:2">James 4:2</span>. "An eternal roll of thunder from the throne" (Renan). "The glorified Christ is in this book a Warrior, who fights with the sharp sword of the word" (Swete).</p>
<p><b>With </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εν</span></span>). Instrumental use of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εν</span></span>. For the language see <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+1:16">Revelation 1:16</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+2:12">Revelation 2:12</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+19:15">Revelation 19:15</span>. </p>
