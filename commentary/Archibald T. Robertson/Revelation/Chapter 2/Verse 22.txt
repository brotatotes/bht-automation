<p><b> I do cast </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">βαλλω</span></span>). Futuristic present active indicative rather than the future <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">βαλω</span></span>, since judgment is imminent.</p>
<p><b>Into a bed </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εις κλινην</span></span>). "A bed of sickness in contrast with the bed of adultery" (Beckwith).</p>
<p><b>Them that commit adultery with her </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τους μοιχευοντας μετ' αυτης</span></span>). Present active articular participle accusative plural of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μοιχευω</span></span>. The actual paramours of the woman Jezebel, guilty of both <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πορνεια</span></span> (fornication, verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+2:21">Revelation 2:21</span>) and <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μοιχεια</span></span> (adultery), works of Jezebel of old and of this Jezebel. There may be also an allusion to the spiritual adultery (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2co+11:2">2 Corinthians 11:2</span>) towards God and Christ as of old (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="jer+3:8">Jeremiah 3:8</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="jer+5:7">Jeremiah 5:7</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="eze+16:22">Ezekiel 16:22</span>).</p>
<p><b>Except they repent </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εαν μη μετανοησουσιν</span></span>). Condition of first class with <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εαν μη</span></span> and the future active indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μετανοεω</span></span>, put in this vivid form rather than the aorist subjunctive (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">-ωσιν</span></span>) third-class condition.</p>
<p><b>Of her works </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εκ των εργων αυτης</span></span>). <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">Αυτης</span></span> (her) correct rather than <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αυτων</span></span> (their). Jezebel was chiefly responsible. </p>
