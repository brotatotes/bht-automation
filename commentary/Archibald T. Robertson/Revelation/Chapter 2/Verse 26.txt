<p><b> He that overcometh and he that keepeth </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ο νικων κα ο τηρων</span></span>). Present active articular participles of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">νικαω</span></span> and <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τηρεω</span></span> in the nominative absolute (<i>nominativus pendens</i>) as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+3:12">Revelation 3:12</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+3:21">Revelation 3:21</span>, resumed by the dative <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αυτω</span></span> (to him), as in verses <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+2:7">Revelation 2:7</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+2:17">Revelation 2:17</span>.</p>
<p><b>Unto the end </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αχρ τελους</span></span>). That is, <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αχρ ου αν ηξο</span></span> above.</p>
<p><b>Authority over the nations </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εξουσιαν επ των εθνων</span></span>). From <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ps+2:8">Psalms 2:8</span>. The followers of the Messiah will share in his victory over his enemies (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+1:6">Revelation 1:6</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+12:5">Revelation 12:5</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+19:15">Revelation 19:15</span>). </p>
