<p><b> As I also have received </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ως καγω ειληφα</span></span>). Perfect active indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">λαμβανω</span></span>. Christ still possesses the power from the Father (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+2:33">Acts 2:33</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ps+2:7">Psalms 2:7</span>).</p>
<p><b>The morning star </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τον αστερα τον πρωινον</span></span>). "The star the morning one." In <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+22:16">Revelation 22:16</span> Christ is the bright morning star. The victor will have Christ himself. </p>
