<p><b> Coming down out of heaven </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">καταβαινοντα εκ του ουρανου</span></span>). As in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+10:1">Revelation 10:1</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+18:1">Revelation 18:1</span>.</p>
<p><b>The key of the abyss </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">την κλειν της αβυσσου</span></span>). As in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+9:1">Revelation 9:1</span>.</p>
<p><b>A great chain </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αλυσιν μεγαλην</span></span>). Paul wore a <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αλυσις</span></span> (alpha privative and <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">λυω</span></span>, to loose) in Rome (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2ti+1:16">2 Timothy 1:16</span>;, as did Peter in prison in Jerusalem (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+12:6">Acts 12:6</span>).</p>
<p><b>In his hand </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επ την χειρα αυτου</span></span>). "Upon his hand," ready for use. See <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επ</span></span> with the genitive in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+1:20">Revelation 1:20</span>. </p>
