<p><b> Was cast </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εβληθη</span></span>). First aorist (prophetic, affective) passive indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">βαλλω</span></span> (verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+20:3">Revelation 20:3</span>).</p>
<p><b>Into the lake of fire and brimstone </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εις την λιμνην του πυρος κα θειου</span></span>). As in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+19:20">Revelation 19:20</span> with the two beasts, as he adds, "where are also the beast and the false prophet" (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">οπου κα το θηριον κα ο ψευδοπροφητης</span></span>).</p>
<p><b>They shall be tormented </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">βασανισθησοντα</span></span>). Return to the prophetic future of verses <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+20:7">Revelation 20:7</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+20:8">Revelation 20:8</span>. For <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">βασανιζω</span></span> see <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+9:5">Revelation 9:5</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+14:10">Revelation 14:10</span>. For "day and night" (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ημερας κα νυκτος</span></span>) see <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+4:8">Revelation 4:8</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+7:15">Revelation 7:15</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+12:10">Revelation 12:10</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+14:11">Revelation 14:11</span>. For "for ever and ever" (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εις τους αιωνας τον αιωνων</span></span>) see <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+1:6">Revelation 1:6</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+1:18">Revelation 1:18</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+4:9">Revelation 4:9</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+4:10">Revelation 4:10</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+5:13">Revelation 5:13</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+7:12">Revelation 7:12</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+10:6">Revelation 10:6</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+11:15">Revelation 11:15</span>, etc. The devil was cast down from heaven (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+12:9">Revelation 12:9</span>), then imprisoned (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+20:2">Revelation 20:2</span>), now he received his final doom. </p>
