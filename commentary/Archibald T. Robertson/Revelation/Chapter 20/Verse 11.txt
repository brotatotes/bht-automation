<p><b> A great white throne </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">θρονον μεγαν λευκον</span></span>). Here <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μεγαν</span></span> (great) is added to the throne pictures in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+4:4">Revelation 4:4</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+20:4">Revelation 20:4</span>. The scene is prepared for the last judgment often mentioned in the N.T. (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+25:31-46">Matthew 25:31-46</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+14:10">Romans 14:10</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2co+5:10">2 Corinthians 5:10</span>). "The absolute purity of this Supreme Court is symbolized by the colour of the Throne" (Swete) as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="da+7:9">Daniel 7:9</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ps+9:1">Psalms 9:1</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ps+97:2">Psalms 97:2</span>. The name of God is not mentioned, but the Almighty Father sits upon the throne (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+4:2">Revelation 4:2</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+4:9">Revelation 4:9</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+5:1">Revelation 5:1</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+5:7">Revelation 5:7</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+5:13">Revelation 5:13</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+6:16">Revelation 6:16</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+7:10">Revelation 7:10</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+7:15">Revelation 7:15</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+19:4">Revelation 19:4</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+21:5">Revelation 21:5</span>), and the Son sits there with him (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="heb+1:3">Hebrews 1:3</span>) and works with the Father (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+5:19-21">John 5:19-21</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+10:30">John 10:30</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+25:31">Matthew 25:31</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+17:31">Acts 17:31</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2co+5:10">2 Corinthians 5:10</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2ti+4:1">2 Timothy 4:1</span>).</p>
<p><b>From whose face the earth and the heaven fled away </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ου απο προσωπου εφυγεν η γε κα ο ουρανος</span></span>). Second aorist (prophetic) active of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">φευγω</span></span>. See <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+16:20">Revelation 16:20</span>. The non-eternity of matter is a common teaching in the O.T. (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ps+97:5">Psalms 97:5</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ps+102:27">Psalms 102:27</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="isa+51:6">Isaiah 51:6</span>) as in the N.T. (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mr+13:31">Mark 13:31</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2pe+3:10">2 Peter 3:10</span>).</p>
<p><b>Was found </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ευρεθη</span></span>). First aorist passive indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ευρισκω</span></span>. All is now spiritual. Even scientists today are speaking of the non-eternity of the universe. </p>
