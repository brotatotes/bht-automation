<p><b> The building of the wall </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">η ενδωμησις του τειχους</span></span>). Or <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ενδομησις</span></span>, elsewhere so far only in Josephus (<i>Ant</i>. XV. 9. 6, a mole or breakwater) and in an inscription (<i>Syll</i>. 583 31), apparently from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ενδομεω</span></span>, to build in, and so the fact of building in. The wall had jasper (verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+21:11">Revelation 21:11</span>) built into it.</p>
<p><b>Was pure gold </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">χρυσιον καθαρον</span></span>). No copula <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ην</span></span> (was) expressed. The city shone like a mass of gold in contrast with the jasper lustre of the wall.</p>
<p><b>Pure glass </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">υαλω καθαρω</span></span>). Associative instrumental case after <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ομοιον</span></span>. <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">Hυαλος</span></span> (apparently from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">υε</span></span>, it rains, and so raindrop) in N.T. only <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+21:18">Revelation 21:18</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+21:21">Revelation 21:21</span>. </p>
