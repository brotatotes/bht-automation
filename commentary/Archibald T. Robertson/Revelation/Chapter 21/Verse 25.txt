<p><b> Shall in no wise be shut </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ου μη κλεισθωσιν</span></span>). Double negative with the first aorist passive subjunctive of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κλειω</span></span>.</p>
<p><b>By day </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ημερας</span></span>). Genitive of time. Mentioned alone without <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">νυκτος</span></span> (by night), "for there shall be no night there" (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">νυξ γαρ ουκ εστα εκε</span></span>). This looks like a continued picture of heaven. </p>
