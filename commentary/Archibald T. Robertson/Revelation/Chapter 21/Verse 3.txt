<p><b> The tabernacle of God is with men </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">η σκηνη του θεου μετα των ανθρωπων</span></span>). It is one of the angels of the Presence (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+16:17">Revelation 16:17</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+19:5">Revelation 19:5</span>) speaking.</p>
<p><b>And he shall dwell with them </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κα σκηνωσε μετ' αυτων</span></span>). Future active of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">σκηνοω</span></span>, already in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+7:15">Revelation 7:15</span> from <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="eze+37:27">Ezekiel 37:27</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="zec+2:10">Zechariah 2:10</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="zec+8:8">Zechariah 8:8</span> and used of the Incarnate Christ on earth by John (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+1:14">John 1:14</span>), now a blessed reality of the Father. The metaphor stands for the Shekinah Glory of God in the old tabernacle (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+7:15">Revelation 7:15</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+13:6">Revelation 13:6</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+15:5">Revelation 15:5</span>), the true tabernacle of which it was a picture (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="heb+8:2">Hebrews 8:2</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="heb+9:11">Hebrews 9:11</span>). God is now Immanuel in fact, as was true of Christ (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+1:23">Matthew 1:23</span>). </p>
