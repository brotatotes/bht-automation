<p><b> My reward is with me </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ο μισθος μου μετ' εμου</span></span>). It is Christ speaking again and he repeats his promise of coming quickly as in verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+22:7">Revelation 22:7</span>. He speaks now as the Rewarder (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ο μισθαποδοτης</span></span>) of <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="heb+11:6">Hebrews 11:6</span>. Cf. <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+11:18">Revelation 11:18</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="isa+40:10">Isaiah 40:10</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="isa+62:11">Isaiah 62:11</span>.</p>
<p><b>To render </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αποδουνα</span></span>). Second aorist active infinitive of purpose of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αποδιδωμ</span></span>, to give back. Each will receive the reward according to his own work (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+2:23">Revelation 2:23</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2co+5:10">2 Corinthians 5:10</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+2:26">Romans 2:26</span>). </p>
