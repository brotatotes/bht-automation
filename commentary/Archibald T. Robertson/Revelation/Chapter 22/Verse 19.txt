<p><b> If any man shall take away </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εαν τις αφελη</span></span>). Also condition of the third class with <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εαν</span></span> and second aorist active subjunctive of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αφαιρεω</span></span>, with <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">απο</span></span> repeated both in the condition and in the conclusion (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αφελε απο</span></span>, future active indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αφαιρεω</span></span> for the more usual <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αφαιρησε</span></span>).</p>
<p><b>Which are written in this book </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">των γεγραμμενων εν τω βιβλιω τουτω</span></span>). Ablative neuter plural articular perfect passive participle in apposition with <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εκ του ξυλου της ζωης</span></span> (from the tree of life) and <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εκ της πολεως της αγιας</span></span> (out of the holy city). Such a man is unworthy of his inheritance. </p>
