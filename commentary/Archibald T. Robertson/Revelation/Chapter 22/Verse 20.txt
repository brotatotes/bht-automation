<p><b> He which testifieth </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ο μαρτυρων</span></span>). That is Jesus (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+1:2">Revelation 1:2</span>) who has just spoken (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+22:18">Revelation 22:18</span>).</p>
<p><b>Yea: I come quickly </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">Ναι, ερχομα ταχυ</span></span>). Affirmation again of the promise in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+22:7">Revelation 22:7</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+22:12">Revelation 22:12</span>. On <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">Να</span></span> (Yes) see <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+1:7">Revelation 1:7</span> for the Lord's assent to the call. Then John expresses his absolute belief in the Lord's promise: "Amen: come, Lord Jesus" (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">Αμην, ερχου, Κυριε Ιησου</span></span>). On <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">Αμην</span></span> see <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+1:7">Revelation 1:7</span>. On <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ερχου</span></span> see <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+22:17">Revelation 22:17</span>. Note <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">Κυριε</span></span> with <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">Ιησου</span></span>. As in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1co+12:3">1 Corinthians 12:3</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="php+2:11">Philippians 2:11</span>. For Paul's confidence in the deity of Christ and the certainty of his second coming see <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="tit+2:13">Titus 2:13</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2ti+4:8">2 Timothy 4:8</span>. <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">Μαρανα θα</span></span> (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1co+16:22">1 Corinthians 16:22</span>). </p>
