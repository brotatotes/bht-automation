<p><b> There shall be no curse any more </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">παν καταθεμα ουκ εστα ετ</span></span>). No other example of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">καταθεμα</span></span> has been found outside of the <i>Didache</i> XVI. 5, though the verb <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">καταθεματιζω</span></span> occurs in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+26:74">Matthew 26:74</span>, meaning to curse, while we have <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αναθεματιζω</span></span> in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mr+14:71">Mark 14:71</span> in the same sense. It may be a syncopated form of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">καταναθεμα</span></span>. The usual <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αναθεμα</span></span> (curse) occurs in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1co+16:22">1 Corinthians 16:22</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ga+1:8">Galatians 1:8</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+9:3">Romans 9:3</span>. For <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">παν</span></span> with <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ουκ=ουδεν</span></span> see <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+21:27">Revelation 21:27</span>.</p>
<p><b>Shall do him service </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">λατρευσουσιν αυτω</span></span>). Future active of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">λατρευω</span></span>, linear idea, "shall keep on serving." See <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+7:15">Revelation 7:15</span> for present active indicative of this same verb with the dative <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αυτω</span></span> as here, picturing the worship of God in heaven. See <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+27:1">Revelation 27:1</span> for "the throne of God and of the Lamb." </p>
