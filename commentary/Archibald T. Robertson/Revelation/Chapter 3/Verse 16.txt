<p><b> Lukewarm </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">χλιαρος</span></span>). Tepid. Old adjective from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">χλιω</span></span>, to liquefy, to melt, here alone in N.T.</p>
<p><b>I will </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μελλω</span></span>). "I am about to," on the point of.</p>
<p><b>Spew thee </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">σε εμεσα</span></span>). First aorist active infinitive of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εμεω</span></span>, old verb to vomit, to reject with extreme disgust, here alone in N.T. </p>
