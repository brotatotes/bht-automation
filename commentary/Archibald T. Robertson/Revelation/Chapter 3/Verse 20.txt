<p><b> I stand at the door </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εστηκα επ την θυραν</span></span>). Perfect active of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ιστημ</span></span> (intransitive). Picture of the Lord's advent as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+24:33">Matthew 24:33</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="jas+5:9">James 5:9</span>, but true also of the individual response to Christ's call (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+12:36">Luke 12:36</span>) as shown in Holman Hunt's great picture. Some see a use also of <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="so+5:2">Song of Solomon 5:2</span>.</p>
<p><b>If any man hear--and open </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εαν τις ακουση κα ανοιξη</span></span>). Condition of third class with <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εαν</span></span> and first aorist (ingressive) active subjunctive of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ακουω</span></span> and <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ανοιγω</span></span>. See <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+10:3">John 10:3</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+18:37">John 18:37</span>. See the picture reversed (Swete) in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+13:25">Luke 13:25</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+25:10">Matthew 25:10</span>.</p>
<p><b>I will come in to him </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εισελευσομα</span></span>). Future middle of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εισερχομα</span></span>. See <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mr+15:43">Mark 15:43</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+11:3">Acts 11:3</span> for <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εισερχομα προς</span></span>, to go into a man's house. Cf. <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+14:23">John 14:23</span>.</p>
<p><b>Will sup </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δειπνησω</span></span>). Future active of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δειπνεω</span></span>, old verb, from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δειπνον</span></span> (supper), as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+17:8">Luke 17:8</span>. Fellowship in the Messianic kingdom (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+22:30">Luke 22:30</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mr+14:25">Mark 14:25</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+26:29">Matthew 26:29</span>). Purely metaphorical, as is plain from <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1co+6:13">1 Corinthians 6:13</span>. </p>
