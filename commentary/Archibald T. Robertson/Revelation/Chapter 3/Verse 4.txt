<p><b> A few names </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ολιγα ονοματα</span></span>). This use of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ονομα</span></span> for persons is seen in the <i>Koine</i> (Deissmann, <i>Bible Studies</i>, p. 196f.) as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+1:15">Acts 1:15</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+11:13">Revelation 11:13</span>.</p>
<p><b>Did not defile </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ουκ εμολυναν</span></span>). First aorist active indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μολυνω</span></span> (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1co+8:7">1 Corinthians 8:7</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1pe+1:4">1 Peter 1:4</span>), pollution.</p>
<p><b>They shall walk </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">περιπατησουσιν</span></span>). Future active of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">περιπατεω</span></span>, promise of fellowship with Christ (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μετ' εμου</span></span>, with me) "in white" (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εν λευκοις</span></span>), as symbols of purity (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+7:9">Revelation 7:9</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+7:13">Revelation 7:13</span>) like the angel (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+28:3">Matthew 28:3</span>), with possibly a reference to Enoch (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ge+5:22">Genesis 5:22</span>). For they are worthy (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">οτ αξιο εισιν</span></span>). To walk with Christ, not worthy in the same sense as God and Christ (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+4:11">Revelation 4:11</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+5:9">Revelation 5:9</span>), but in a relative sense. See <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+16:6">Revelation 16:6</span> for bad sense of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αξιος</span></span>. </p>
