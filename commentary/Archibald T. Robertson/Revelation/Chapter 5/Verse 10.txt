<p><b> Madest </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εποιησας</span></span>). First aorist active indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ποιεω</span></span>, a prophetic use anticipating the final result.</p>
<p><b>A kingdom and priests </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">βασιλειαν κα ιερεις</span></span>). As the correct text in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+1:6">Revelation 1:6</span>.</p>
<p><b>They reign </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">βασιλευουσιν</span></span>). Present active indicative, futuristic use, though Aleph P have the future <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">βασιλευσουσιν</span></span> (shall reign) as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+20:6">Revelation 20:6</span>. </p>
