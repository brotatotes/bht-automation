<p><b> And I saw </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κα ειδον</span></span>). A new feature introduced by the outer and vaster circle (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κυκλω</span></span>) of angels who catch up the new song of redemption in antiphonal singing, answering the song of the four living creatures and the twenty-four elders. Some MSS. read <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ως</span></span> (as if) before <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">φωνην</span></span> (voice). Ten thousand times ten thousand (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μυριαδες μυριαδων κα χιλιαδες χιλιαδων</span></span>). Literally, "myriads of myriads and thousands of thousands," a mild husteron-proteron. The regular order in I Enoch 40:I. See <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="da+7:10">Daniel 7:10</span> for <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">χιλια χιλιαδες</span></span> (thousand thousands) and <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μυρια μυριαδες</span></span> (countless myriads). They are all efforts to express the innumerable hosts of the angels. </p>
