<p><b> How long </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εως ποτε</span></span>). "Until when." Cf. <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+7:17">Matthew 7:17</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+10:24">John 10:24</span>.</p>
<p><b>O Master </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ο δεσποτης</span></span>). Nominative articular form, but used as vocative (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δεσποτα</span></span>) as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+4:11">Revelation 4:11</span> (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+20:28">John 20:28</span>). On <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δεσποτης</span></span> (correlative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δουλος</span></span>) see <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+2:29">Luke 2:29</span>. Here (alone in the Apocalypse) it is applied to God as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+2:29">Luke 2:29</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+4:24">Acts 4:24</span>, but to Christ in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="jdg+1:4">Judges 1:4</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2pe+2:1">2 Peter 2:1</span>.</p>
<p><b>The holy and true </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ο αγιος κα αληθινος</span></span>). See <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+3:7">Revelation 3:7</span> for these attributes of God.</p>
<p><b>Avenge our blood on them that dwell upon the earth </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εκδικεις το αιμα ημων εκ των κατοικουντων επ της γης</span></span>). This same idiom in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+19:2">Revelation 19:2</span> and see it also in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+18:7">Luke 18:7</span>, "a passage which goes far to answer many questions in theodicy" (Swete). We find <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εκδικεω</span></span>, late compound, used with <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εκ</span></span> as here in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="de+18:19">Deuteronomy 18:19</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1sa+24:13">1 Samuel 24:13</span>, but with <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">απο</span></span> in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+18:3">Luke 18:3</span>. For <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επ της γης</span></span> (upon the earth) see <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+3:10">Revelation 3:10</span>. </p>
