<p><b> Was removed </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">απεχωρισθη</span></span>). First aorist passive indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αποχωριζω</span></span>, to separate, to part (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+15:39">Acts 15:39</span>). "The heaven was parted."</p>
<p><b>As a scroll when it is rolled up </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ως βιβλιον ελισσομενον</span></span>). Present passive participle of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ελισσω</span></span>, old verb, to roll up, in N.T. only here (from <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="isa+34:4">Isaiah 34:4</span>) and <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="heb+1:12">Hebrews 1:12</span> (from <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ps+102:27">Psalms 102:27</span>). Vivid picture of the expanse of the sky rolled up and away as a papyrus roll (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+4:17">Luke 4:17</span>).</p>
<p><b>Were moved </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εκινηθησαν</span></span>). First aorist passive indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κινεω</span></span>, to move.</p>
<p><b>Out of their places </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εκ των τοπων αυτων</span></span>). See also <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+16:20">Revelation 16:20</span> for these violent displacements in the earth's crust. Cf. <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="na+1:5">Nahum 1:5</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="jer+4:24">Jeremiah 4:24</span>. Jesus spoke of faith removing mountains (of difficulty) as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mr+11:23">Mark 11:23</span> (cf. <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1co+13:2">1 Corinthians 13:2</span>). </p>
