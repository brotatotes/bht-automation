<p><b> They cry </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κραζουσ</span></span>). Vivid dramatic present.</p>
<p><b>With a great voice </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">φωνη μεγαλη</span></span>). As in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+6:10">Revelation 6:10</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+7:2">Revelation 7:2</span>. "The polyglott multitude shouts its praises as with one voice" (Swete).</p>
<p><b>Salvation </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">η σωτηρια</span></span>). As in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+12:10">Revelation 12:10</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+19:1">Revelation 19:1</span>. Nominative absolute. Salvation here is regarded as an accomplished act on the part of those coming out of the great tribulation (verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+7:14">Revelation 7:14</span>) and the praise for it is given to God (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τω θεω</span></span>, dative case) and to the Lamb (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τω αρνιω</span></span>, dative also). Both God and Christ are thus called <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">σωτηρ</span></span> as in the Pastoral Epistles, as to God (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1ti+1:1">1 Timothy 1:1</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1ti+2:3">1 Timothy 2:3</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="tit+1:3">Titus 1:3</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="tit+3:4">Titus 3:4</span>) and to Christ (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="tit+1:4">Titus 1:4</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="tit+2:13">Titus 2:13</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="tit+3:6">Titus 3:6</span>). For <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">η σωτηρια</span></span> see <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+4:22">John 4:22</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+4:12">Acts 4:12</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="jdg+1:3">Judges 1:3</span>. </p>
