<p><b> Were standing </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ιστηκεισαν</span></span>). Past perfect active of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ιστημ</span></span> intransitive and used like an imperfect as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+19:25">John 19:25</span>.</p>
<p><b>Round about </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κυκλω</span></span>). Preposition (in a circle) with genitive as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+4:6">Revelation 4:6</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+5:11">Revelation 5:11</span>. The angels here rejoice in the salvation of men (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+15:7">Luke 15:7</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+15:10">Luke 15:10</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1pe+1:12">1 Peter 1:12</span>).</p>
<p><b>Upon their faces </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επ τα προσωπα αυτων</span></span>). In reverential worship of God as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+11:16">Revelation 11:16</span>. For this worship (fell and worshipped) see also <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+4:10">Revelation 4:10</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+5:14">Revelation 5:14</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+11:16">Revelation 11:16</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+19:4">Revelation 19:4</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+19:10">Revelation 19:10</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+22:8">Revelation 22:8</span>. The dative <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τω θεω</span></span> (God) with <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">προσκυνεω</span></span> (to worship) is the usual construction for that meaning. When it means merely to do homage the accusative case is usual in this book (Charles). But in the Fourth Gospel the reverse order is true as to the cases with <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">προσκυνεω</span></span> (Abbott, <i>Joh. Vocab</i>. pp. 138-142). </p>
