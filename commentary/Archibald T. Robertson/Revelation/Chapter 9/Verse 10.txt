<p><b> Tails </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ουρας</span></span>). Old word, in N.T. only in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+9:10">Revelation 9:10</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+9:19">Revelation 9:19</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+12:4">Revelation 12:4</span>.</p>
<p><b>Like unto scorpions </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ομοιας σκορπιοις</span></span>). Aleph A wrongly have <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ομοιοις</span></span> (agreeing with <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">σκορπιοις</span></span> instead of with <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ουρας</span></span>). It is a condensed idiom for "like unto the tails of the scorpions" as we have it in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+13:11">Revelation 13:11</span> (cf. <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+5:20">Matthew 5:20</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1jo+2:2">1 John 2:2</span>).</p>
<p><b>Stings </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κεντρα</span></span>). Old word from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κεντρεω</span></span> (to prick, to sting), in N.T. only here, <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+26:14">Acts 26:14</span> (about Paul); <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1co+15:55">1 Corinthians 15:55</span> (about death). It is used "of the spur of a cock, the quill of the porcupine, and the stings of insects" (Vincent). It was the goad used for oxen (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="pr+26:3">Proverbs 26:3</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+26:14">Acts 26:14</span>).</p>
<p><b>In their tails </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εν ταις ουραις αυτων</span></span>). This locates "their power to hurt" (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">η εξουσια αυτων αδικησα</span></span>, infinitive here, <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ινα αδικησουσιν</span></span> in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+9:4">Revelation 9:4</span>) in their tails. It might have been in other organs. </p>
