<p><b> Of the horsemen </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">του ιππικου</span></span>). Old adjective <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ιππικος</span></span> from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ιππος</span></span> (horse), equestrian. The neuter articular singular <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">το ιππικον</span></span>, the horse or the cavalry in contrast with <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">το πεζικον</span></span> (the infantry), here only in N.T. For the numbers here see on <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+5:11">Revelation 5:11</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+7:4">Revelation 7:4</span>. </p>
