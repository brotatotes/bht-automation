<p><b> Opened </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ηνοιξεν</span></span>). First aorist active indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ανοιγνυμ</span></span>. With the "key" (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κλεις</span></span>).</p>
<p><b>As the smoke of a great furnace </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ως καπνος καμινου μεγαλης</span></span>). The plague of demonic locusts is here turned loose. <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">Καμινος</span></span> is old word for a smelting-furnace, already in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+1:15">Revelation 1:15</span>.</p>
<p><b>Were darkened </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εσκοτωθη</span></span>). First aorist passive indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">σκοτοω</span></span>, old causative verb from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">σκοτος</span></span>, in N.T. only here, <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+16:10">Revelation 16:10</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="eph+4:18">Ephesians 4:18</span>.</p>
<p><b>By reason of </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εκ</span></span>). "Out of," as a result of (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+8:13">Revelation 8:13</span>). </p>
