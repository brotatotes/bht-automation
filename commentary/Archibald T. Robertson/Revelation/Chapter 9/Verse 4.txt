<p><b> It was said </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ερρεθη</span></span>). First aorist passive indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ειπον</span></span>.</p>
<p><b>That they should not hurt </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ινα μη αδικησουσιν</span></span>). Sub-final (object clause subject of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ερρεθη</span></span>) with <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ινα μη</span></span> and the future active of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αδικεω</span></span> as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+3:9">Revelation 3:9</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+8:3">Revelation 8:3</span>. Vegetation had been hurt sufficiently by the hail (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+8:7">Revelation 8:7</span>).</p>
<p><b>But only such men as </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ε μη τους ανθρωπους οιτινες</span></span>). "Except (elliptical use of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ε μη</span></span>, if not, unless) the men who (the very ones who)." For this use of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">οστις</span></span> see <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+1:7">Revelation 1:7</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+2:24">Revelation 2:24</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+20:4">Revelation 20:4</span>.</p>
<p><b>The seal of God upon their foreheads </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">την σφραγιδα του θεου επ των μετωπων</span></span>). Provided for in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="re+7:3">Revelation 7:3</span>. "As Israel in Egypt escaped the plagues which punished their neighbours, so the new Israel is exempted from the attack of the locusts of the Abyss" (Swete). </p>
