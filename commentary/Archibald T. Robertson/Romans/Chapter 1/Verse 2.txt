<p><b> He promised afore </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">προεπηγγειλατο</span></span>). First aorist middle of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">προεπαγγελλω</span></span> for which verb see on <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2co+9:5">2 Corinthians 9:5</span>.</p>
<p><b>By </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δια</span></span>). Through, by means of, intermediate agency like <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+1:22">Matthew 1:22</span> which see.</p>
<p><b>In the holy scriptures </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εν γραφαις αγιαις</span></span>). No article, yet definite. Perhaps the earliest use of the phrase (Sanday and Headlam). Paul definitely finds God's gospel in the Holy Scriptures. </p>
