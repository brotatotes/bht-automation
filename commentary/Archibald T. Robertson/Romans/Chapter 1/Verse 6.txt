<p><b> Called to be Jesus Christ's </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κλητο Ιησου Χριστου</span></span>). Predicate genitive after <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κλητο</span></span> (verbal adjective from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">καλεω</span></span>, to call), though it is possible to consider it the ablative case, "called of (or from) Jesus Christ." </p>
