<p><b> How then shall they call? </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πως ουν επικαλεσωνται?</span></span>). Deliberative subjunctive (first aorist middle) of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επικαλεομα</span></span> (see verses <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+10:12">Romans 10:12</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+10:13">Romans 10:13</span>). The antecedent of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εις ον</span></span> (in whom) is not expressed.</p>
<p><b>How shall they believe? </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πος πιστευσωσιν?</span></span>). Deliberative subjunctive again (first aorist active of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πιστευω</span></span> just used). Each time Paul picks up the preceding verb and challenges that. Here again the antecedent <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εις τουτον</span></span> before <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ον</span></span> is not expressed.</p>
<p><b>How shall they hear? </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πος ακουσωσιν?</span></span>). Deliberative subjunctive (first aorist active of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ακουω</span></span>).</p>
<p><b>Without a preacher? </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">χωρις κηρυσσοντοσ?</span></span>). Preposition <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">χωρις</span></span> with ablative singular masculine present active participle of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κηρυσσω</span></span>, "without one preaching."</p>
<p><b>How shall they preach? </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πως κηρυξωσιν?</span></span>). Deliberative subjunctive again (first aorist active <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κηρυσσω</span></span>, to preach).</p>
<p><b>Except they be sent? </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εαν μη αποσταλωσιν?</span></span>). Second aorist passive deliberative subjunctive of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αποστελλω</span></span>, to send, from which verb <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αποστολος</span></span> apostle comes. Negative condition of third class. In graphic style Paul has made a powerful plea for missions. It is just as true today as then. </p>
