<p><b> How beautiful </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">Hως ωραιο</span></span>). A quotation from <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="isa+52:7">Isaiah 52:7</span> more like the Hebrew than the LXX, picturing the messengers of the restoration from the Jewish captivity. Paul assumes that the missionaries (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αποστολο</span></span>) have been sent as implied in verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+10:14">Romans 10:14</span>. </p>
