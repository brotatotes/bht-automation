 But they did not all hearken  (ου παντες υπηκουσαν). They heard, but did not heed. Some disbelieve now (Romans 3:3) as they did then. On obedience and disobedience see Romans 5:19; 1 Thessalonians 2:13; Galatians 3:2. He quotes Isaiah 53:1 to show how Isaiah felt.Report  (ακοη). Literally, "hearing" (Matthew 14:1; Mark 13:7).
