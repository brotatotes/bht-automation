<p><b> But they did not all hearken </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ου παντες υπηκουσαν</span></span>). They heard, but did not heed. Some disbelieve now (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+3:3">Romans 3:3</span>) as they did then. On obedience and disobedience see <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+5:19">Romans 5:19</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1th+2:13">1 Thessalonians 2:13</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ga+3:2">Galatians 3:2</span>. He quotes <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="isa+53:1">Isaiah 53:1</span> to show how Isaiah felt.</p>
<p><b>Report </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ακοη</span></span>). Literally, "hearing" (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+14:1">Matthew 14:1</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mr+13:7">Mark 13:7</span>). </p>
