<p><b> Did Israel not know? </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μη Ισραελ ουκ εγνω?</span></span>). "Did Israel fail to know?" See above.</p>
<p><b>First </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πρωτος</span></span>). Moses first before any one else. LXX quotation <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="de+32:21">Deuteronomy 32:21</span>. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1co+10:22">1 Corinthians 10:22</span> for <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">παραζηλωσω</span></span> (I will provoke you to jealousy).</p>
<p><b>With that which is no nation </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επ' ουκ εθνε</span></span>). The Jews had worshipped "no-gods" and now God shows favours to a "no-nation" (people).</p>
<p><b>Will I anger you </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">παροργιω υμας</span></span>). Future active (Attic future) of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">παροργιζω</span></span>, rare word, to rouse to wrath. </p>
