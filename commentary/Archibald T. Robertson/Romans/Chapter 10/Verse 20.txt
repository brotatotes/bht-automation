<p><b> Is very bold </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αποτολμα</span></span>). Present active indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αποτολμαω</span></span>, old word, to assume boldness (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">απο</span></span>, off) and only here in N.T. Isaiah "breaks out boldly" (Gifford). Paul cites <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="isa+65:1">Isaiah 65:1</span> in support of his own courage against the prejudice of the Jews. See <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+9:30-33">Romans 9:30-33</span> for illustration of this point.</p>
<p><b>I was found </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ευρεθην</span></span>). First aorist passive indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ευρισκω</span></span>. </p>
