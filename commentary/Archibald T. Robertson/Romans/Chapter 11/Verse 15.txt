<p><b> The casting away of them </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">η αποβολη αυτων</span></span>). Objective genitive (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αυτων</span></span>) with <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αποβολη</span></span>, old word from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αποβαλλω</span></span>, to throw off (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mr+10:50">Mark 10:50</span>), in N.T. only here and <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+27:22">Acts 27:22</span>.</p>
<p><b>The reconciling of the world </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">καταλλαγη κοσμου</span></span>). See <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+5:10">Romans 5:10</span> for <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">καταλλαγη</span></span> (reconciling). It explains verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+11:12">Romans 11:12</span>.</p>
<p><b>The receiving </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">η προσλημψις</span></span>). Old word from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">προσλαμβανω</span></span>, to take to oneself, only here in N.T.</p>
<p><b>Life from the dead </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ζωη εκ νεκρων</span></span>). Already the conversion of Jews had become so difficult. It is like a miracle of grace today, though it does happen. Many think that Paul means that the general resurrection and the end will come when the Jews are converted. Possibly so, but it is by no means certain. His language may be merely figurative. </p>
