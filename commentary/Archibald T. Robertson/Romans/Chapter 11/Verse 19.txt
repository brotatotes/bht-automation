<p><b> Thou wilt say then </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ερεις ουν</span></span>). A presumptuous Gentile speaks.</p>
<p><b>That I might be grafted in </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ινα εγω ενκεντρισθω</span></span>). Purpose clause with <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ινα</span></span> and first aorist passive subjunctive. He shows contempt for the cast-off Jews. </p>
