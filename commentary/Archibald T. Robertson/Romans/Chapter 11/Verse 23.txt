<p><b> If they continue not in their unbelief </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εαν μη επιμενωσ τη απιστια</span></span>). Third class condition with the same verb used in verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+11:22">Romans 11:22</span> of the Gentile. Locative case of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">απιστια</span></span> here (same form as the instrumental in verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+11:20">Romans 11:20</span>).</p>
<p><b>For God is able </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δυνατος γαρ εστιν ο θεος</span></span>). See this use of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δυνατος εστιν</span></span> in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+4:21">Romans 4:21</span> rather than <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δυνατα</span></span>. This is the <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">χρυξ</span></span> of the whole matter. God is able. </p>
