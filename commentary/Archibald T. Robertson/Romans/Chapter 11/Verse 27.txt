<p><b> My covenant </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">η παρ' εμου διαθηκη</span></span>). "The from me covenant," "my side of the covenant I have made with them" (Sanday and Headlam). Cf. <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="jer+31:31">Jeremiah 31:31</span>. Not a political deliverance, but a religious and ethical one.</p>
<p><b>When I shall take away </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">οταν αφελωμα</span></span>). Second aorist middle subjunctive of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αφαιρεω</span></span>, old and common verb, to take away. </p>
