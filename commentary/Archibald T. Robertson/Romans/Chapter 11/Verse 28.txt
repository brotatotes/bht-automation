<p><b> As touching the gospel </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κατα το ευαγγελιον</span></span>). "According to (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κατα</span></span> with the accusative) the gospel" as Paul has shown in verses <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+11:11-24">Romans 11:11-24</span>, the gospel order as it has developed.</p>
<p><b>Enemies </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εχθρο</span></span>). Treated as enemies (of God), in passive sense, because of their rejection of Christ (verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+11:10">Romans 11:10</span>), just as <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αγαπητο</span></span> (beloved) is passive.</p>
<p><b>As touching the election </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κατα την εκλογην</span></span>). "According to the election" (the principle of election, not as in verses <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+11:5">Romans 11:5</span> the elect or abstract for concrete).</p>
<p><b>For the fathers' sake </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δια τους πατερας</span></span>). As in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+9:4">Romans 9:4</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+11:16">Romans 11:16</span>. </p>
