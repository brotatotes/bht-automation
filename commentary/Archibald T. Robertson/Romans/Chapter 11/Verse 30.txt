<p><b> Ye in time past </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">υμεις ποτε</span></span>). Ye Gentiles (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+1:18-32">Romans 1:18-32</span>).</p>
<p><b>Were disobedient </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επειθησατε</span></span>). First aorist active indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">απειθεω</span></span>, to disbelieve and then to disobey. "Ye once upon a time disobeyed God."</p>
<p><b>By their disobedience </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τη τουτων απειθια</span></span>). Instrumental case, "by the disobedience of these" (Jews). Note "now" (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">νυν</span></span>) three times in this sentence. </p>
