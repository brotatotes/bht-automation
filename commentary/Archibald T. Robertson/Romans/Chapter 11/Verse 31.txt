<p><b> By the mercy shown to you </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τω υμετερω ελεε</span></span>). Objective sense of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">υμετερος</span></span> (possessive pronoun, your). Proleptic position also for the words go with <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ελεηθωσιν</span></span> (first aorist passive subjunctive of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ελεεω</span></span>, from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ελεος</span></span> with <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ινα</span></span>, purpose clause). God's purpose is for the Jews to receive a blessing yet. </p>
