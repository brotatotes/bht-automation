<p><b> First driven to him </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">προεδωκεν αυτω</span></span>). First aorist active indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">προδιδωμ</span></span>, to give beforehand or first. Old verb, here alone in N.T. From <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="job+41:11">Job 41:11</span>, but not like the LXX, Paul's own translation.</p>
<p><b>Shall be recompensed </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ανταποδοθησετα</span></span>). First future passive of double compound <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ανταποδιδωμ</span></span>, to pay back (both <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αντ</span></span> and <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">απο</span></span>), old word in good sense, as here and <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+14:14">Luke 14:14</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1th+3:9">1 Thessalonians 3:9</span> and in bad sense as <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2th+1:6">2 Thessalonians 1:6</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+12:19">Romans 12:19</span>. </p>
