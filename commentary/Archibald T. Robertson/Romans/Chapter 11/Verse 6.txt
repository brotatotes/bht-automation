<p><b> Otherwise </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επε</span></span>). Ellipse after <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επε</span></span> (since), "since, in that case."</p>
<p><b>Is no more </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ουκετ γινετα</span></span>). "No longer becomes" grace, loses its character as grace. Augustine: <i>Gratia nisi gratis sit gratia non est</i>. </p>
