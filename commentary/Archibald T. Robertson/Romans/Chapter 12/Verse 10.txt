<p><b> In love of the brethren </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τη φιλαδελφια</span></span>). Late word for brotherly love for which see <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1th+4:9">1 Thessalonians 4:9</span>.</p>
<p><b>Tenderly affectioned </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">φιλοστοργο</span></span>). Old compound adjective from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">φιλος</span></span> and <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">στοργη</span></span> (mutual love of parents and children), here alone in N.T. </p>
