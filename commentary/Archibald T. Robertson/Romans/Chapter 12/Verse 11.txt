<p><b> Slothful </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">οκνηρο</span></span>). Old adjective from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">οκνεω</span></span>, to hesitate, to be slow. Slow and "poky" as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+25:26">Matthew 25:26</span>. </p>
