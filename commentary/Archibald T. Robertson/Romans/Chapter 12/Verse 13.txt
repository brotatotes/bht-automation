<p><b> Communicating </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κοινωνουντες</span></span>). "Contributing." From <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κοινωνεω</span></span> for which see <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2co+9:13">2 Corinthians 9:13</span>. Paul had raised a great collection for the poor saints in Jerusalem.</p>
<p><b>Given to hospitality </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">την φιλοξενιαν διωκοντες</span></span>). "Pursuing (as if in a chase or hunt) hospitality" (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">φιλοξενια</span></span>, old word from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">φιλοξενος</span></span>, fond of strangers, <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">φιλος</span></span> and <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ξενος</span></span> as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1ti+3:2">1 Timothy 3:2</span>). In N.T. only here and <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="heb+13:2">Hebrews 13:2</span>. See <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2co+3:1">2 Corinthians 3:1</span>. They were to pursue (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">διωκω</span></span>) hospitality as their enemies pursued (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">διωκοντας</span></span>) them. </p>
