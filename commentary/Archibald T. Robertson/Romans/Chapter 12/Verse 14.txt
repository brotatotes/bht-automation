<p><b> And curse not </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κα μη καταρασθε</span></span>). Present middle imperative with <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μη</span></span>. Like <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+5:44">Matthew 5:44</span> in spirit, not a quotation, but a reminiscence of the words of Jesus. The negative addition gives emphasis. See <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+6:28">Luke 6:28</span> for the old verb <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">καταραομα</span></span> from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">καταρα</span></span> (curse). </p>
