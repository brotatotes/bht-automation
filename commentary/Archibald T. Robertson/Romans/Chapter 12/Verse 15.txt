<p><b> Rejoice </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">χαιρειν</span></span>). Present active infinitive of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">χαιρω</span></span>, absolute or independent use of the infinitive as if a finite verb as occurs sometimes (Robertson, <i>Grammar</i>, pp. 1092ff.). Literally here, "Rejoicing with rejoicing people, weeping with weeping people." </p>
