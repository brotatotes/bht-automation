<p><b> Render to no man </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μηδεν αποδιδοντες</span></span>). "Giving back to no man." Independent participle again.</p>
<p><b>Evil for evil </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κακον αντ κακου</span></span>). <p><b>Take thought of </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">προνοουμενο</span></span>). "Taking thought beforehand." Old word. See <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2co+8:21">2 Corinthians 8:21</span>. </p>
