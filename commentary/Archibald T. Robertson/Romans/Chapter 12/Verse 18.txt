<p><b> As much as in you lieth </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">το εξ υμων</span></span>). Accusative of general reference, "so far as what proceeds from you" ("the from you part"). See <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">το κατ' εμε</span></span> in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+1:15">Romans 1:15</span>. This phrase explains "if it be possible" (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ε δυνατον</span></span>). "All <i>your</i> part is to be peace" (Alford). For "be at peace" (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ειρηνευοντες</span></span>) see <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2co+13:11">2 Corinthians 13:11</span>. </p>
