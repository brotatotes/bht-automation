<p><b> Be not overcome of evil </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μη νικω υπο του κακου</span></span>). Present passive imperative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">νικαω</span></span>, to conquer. "Stop being conquered by the evil (thing or man),"</p>
<p><b>But overcome evil with good </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αλλα νικα εν τω αγαθω το κακον</span></span>). "But keep on conquering the evil in the good." Drown the evil in the good. Seneca: <i>Vincit malos pertinax bonitas</i>. </p>
