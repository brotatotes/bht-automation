<p><b> Is far spent </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">προεκοψεν</span></span>). First aorist active indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">προκοπτω</span></span>, to cut forward, to advance, old word for making progress. See <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+2:52">Luke 2:52</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ga+1:14">Galatians 1:14</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2ti+2:16">2 Timothy 2:16</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2ti+3:9">2 Timothy 3:9</span>.</p>
<p><b>Is at hand </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ηγγικεν</span></span>). Perfect active indicative, "has drawn nigh." Vivid picture for day-break.</p>
<p><b>Let us therefore cast off </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αποθωμεθα ουν</span></span>). Aorist middle subjunctive (volitive) of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αποτιθημ</span></span>, to put off from oneself "the works of darkness" (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τα εργα του σκοτους</span></span>) as we do our night-clothes.</p>
<p><b>Let us put on </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ενδυσωμεθα</span></span>). Aorist middle subjunctive (volitive) of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ενδυω</span></span>, to put on. For this same contrast between putting off (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αποτιθημ</span></span> and <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">απεκδυω</span></span>) and putting on (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ενδυω</span></span>) see <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="col+3:8-12">Colossians 3:8-12</span>.</p>
<p><b>The armour of light </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τα οπλα του φοτος</span></span>). The weapons of light, that belong to the light (to the day time). For the metaphor of the Christian armour see <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1th+5:8">1 Thessalonians 5:8</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2co+6:7">2 Corinthians 6:7</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+6:13">Romans 6:13</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="eph+6:13">Ephesians 6:13</span>. </p>
