<p><b> A terror </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">φοβος</span></span>). This meaning in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="isa+8:13">Isaiah 8:13</span>. Paul does not approve all that rulers do, but he is speaking generally of the ideal before rulers. Nero was Emperor at this time.</p>
