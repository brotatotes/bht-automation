 But thou, why dost thou judge?  (συ δε τ συ κρινεισ?). Referring to the conduct of the "weak" brother in verse Romans 14:3.Or thou again  (η κα συ). Referring to the "strong" brother.Shall stand before  (παραστησομεθα). Future middle of παριστημ and intransitive, to stand beside (παρα) with the locative case (τω βεματ, the judgment seat) as in Acts 27:24. See the same figure of God in 2 Corinthians 5:10.
