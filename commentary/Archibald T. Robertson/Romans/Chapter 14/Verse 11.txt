<p><b> As I live </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ζω εγω</span></span>). "I live." The LXX here (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="isa+45:23">Isaiah 45:23</span>) has <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κατ' εμαυτου ομννυω</span></span>, "I swear by myself."</p>
<p><b>Shall confess to God </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εξομολογησετα τω θεω</span></span>). Future middle of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εξομολογεω</span></span>, to confess openly (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εξ</span></span>) with the accusative as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+3:6">Matthew 3:6</span>. With the dative as here the idea is to give praise to, to give gratitude to (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+11:25">Matthew 11:25</span>). </p>
