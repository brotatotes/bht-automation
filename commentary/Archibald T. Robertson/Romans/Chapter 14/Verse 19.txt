<p><b> So then </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αρα ουν</span></span>). Two inferential particles, "accordingly therefore."</p>
<p><b>Let us follow after </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">διωκωμεν</span></span>). Present active subjunctive (volitive). "Let us pursue." Some MSS. have present indicative, "we pursue."</p>
<p><b>The things which make for peace </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τα της ειρηνης</span></span>). "The things of peace," literally, genitive case. So "the things of edification for one another" (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τα της οικοδομης της εις αλληλους</span></span>). </p>
