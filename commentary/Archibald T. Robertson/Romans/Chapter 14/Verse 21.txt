<p><b> Not to eat </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">το μη φαγειν</span></span>). "The not eating." Articular infinitive (second aorist active of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εσθιω</span></span>) and subject of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">καλον εστιν</span></span> (copula, understood).</p>
<p><b>Flesh </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κρεας</span></span>). Old word, in N.T. only here and <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1co+8:13">1 Corinthians 8:13</span>.</p>
<p><b>To drink </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πειν</span></span>). Shortened form for <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πιειν</span></span> (second aorist active infinitive of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πινω</span></span>).</p>
<p><b>Whereby </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εν ω</span></span>). "On which thy brother stumbleth" (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">προσκοπτε</span></span>). </p>
