<p><b> Whether--or </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εαν τε--εαν τε</span></span>). "Both if--and if" (condition of third class with present subjunctive (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ζωμεν--αποθνησκωμεν</span></span>). Both living and dying are "to the Lord." Paul repeats the idiom (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εαν τε--εαν τε</span></span>) with the conclusion "we are the Lord's (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">του κυριου εσμεν</span></span>). Predicate genitive, "we belong to the Lord." </p>
