<p><b> And lived again </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κα εζησεν</span></span>). First ingressive aorist active indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ζαω</span></span>, "he came to life."</p>
<p><b>Might be lord of </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κυριευσε</span></span>). Ingressive aorist active subjunctive of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κυριευω</span></span>, "become Lord of." Purpose clause with <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ινα</span></span> (that). Old verb from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κυριος</span></span>, lord. See <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+22:25">Luke 22:25</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+6:9">Romans 6:9</span>. </p>
