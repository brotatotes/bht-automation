<p><b> As it is written </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">καθως γεγραπτα</span></span>). From <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="isa+52:15">Isaiah 52:15</span>. Paul finds an illustration of his word about his own ambition in the words of Isaiah. Fritzsche actually argues that Paul understood Isaiah to be predicting his (Paul's) ministry! Some scholars have argued against the genuineness of verses <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+15:9-21">Romans 15:9-21</span> on wholly subjective and insufficient grounds. </p>
