<p><b> But now </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">νυν δε</span></span>). Repeats the very words used in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+15:23">Romans 15:23</span>.</p>
<p><b>I go </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πορευομα</span></span>). Futuristic present as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+14:2">John 14:2</span>.</p>
<p><b>Ministering unto the saints </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">διακονον τοις αγιοις</span></span>). Present active participle of purpose like <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ευλογουντα</span></span> in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+3:26">Acts 3:26</span>. This collection had been one of Paul's chief cares for over a year now (see <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+15:2">Romans 15:2</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+15:9">Romans 15:9</span>). See <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2co+8:4">2 Corinthians 8:4</span>. </p>
