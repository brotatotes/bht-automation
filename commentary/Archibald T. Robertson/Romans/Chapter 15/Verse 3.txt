<p><b> Pleased not himself </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ουχ εαυτω ηρεσεν</span></span>). Aorist active indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αρεσκω</span></span> with the usual dative. The supreme example for Christians. See <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+14:15">Romans 14:15</span>. He quotes <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ps+69:9">Psalms 69:9</span> (Messianic Psalm) and represents the Messiah as bearing the reproaches of others. </p>
