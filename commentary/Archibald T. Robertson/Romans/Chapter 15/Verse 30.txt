<p><b> By </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δια</span></span>). The intermediate agents of the exhortation (the Lord Jesus and the love of the Spirit) as <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δια</span></span> is used after <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">παρακαλω</span></span> in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+12:1">Romans 12:1</span>.</p>
<p><b>That ye strive together with me </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">συναγωνισασθα μο</span></span>). First aorist middle infinitive of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">συναγων ζομα</span></span>, old compound verb, only here in N.T., direct object of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">παρακαλω</span></span>, and with associative instrumental case <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μο</span></span>, the simplex <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αγωνιζομενος</span></span>, occurring in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="col+4:12">Colossians 4:12</span> of the prayers of Epaphras. For Christ's agony in prayer see <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+26:42">Matthew 26:42</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+22:44">Luke 22:44</span>. </p>
