<p><b> Apelles </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">Απελλην</span></span>). A name among Jews and a famous tragic actor also.</p>
<p><b>The approved </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τον δοκιμον</span></span>). The tried and true (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1co+11:19">1 Corinthians 11:19</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2co+10:18">2 Corinthians 10:18</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2co+13:7">2 Corinthians 13:7</span>).</p>
<p><b>Them which are of the household of Aristobulus </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τους εκ των Αριστοβουλου</span></span>). The younger Aristobulus was a grandson of Herod the Great. Lightfoot suggests that some of the servants in this household had become Christians, Aristobulus being dead. </p>
