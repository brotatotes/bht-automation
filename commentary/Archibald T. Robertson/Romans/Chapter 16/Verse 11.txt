<p><b> Herodion </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">Hερωιδιωνα</span></span>). Probably one belonging to the Herod family like that above.</p>
<p><b>Kinsman </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">συγγενη</span></span>). Merely fellow-countryman.</p>
<p><b>Them of the household of Narcissus </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τους εκ των Ναρκισσου</span></span>). "Narcissiani." There was a famous freedman of this name who was put to death by Agrippa. Perhaps members of his household. </p>
