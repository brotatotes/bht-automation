<p><b> Asyncritus </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">Ασυνκριτον</span></span>). There is an inscription of a freedman of Augustus with this name.</p>
<p><b>Phlegon </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">Φλεγοντα</span></span>). No light on this name till the historian of the second century A.D.</p>
<p><b>Hermes </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">Hερμην</span></span>). A very common slave name.</p>
<p><b>Patrobas </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">Πατροβαν</span></span>). Name of a freedman of Nero, abbreviated form of Patrobius.</p>
<p><b>Hermas </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">Hερμαν</span></span>). Not the author of the Shepherd of Hermas. Common as a slave name, shortened form of Hermagoras, Hermogenes, etc.</p>
<p><b>The brethren that are with them </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τους συν αυτοις αδελφους</span></span>). Perhaps a little church in the house of some one. </p>
