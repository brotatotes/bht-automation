<p><b> With a holy kiss </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εν φιληματ αγιω</span></span>). The near-east mode of salutation as hand-shaking in the Western. In China one shakes hands with himself. Men kissed men and women kissed women. See <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+16:1">Romans 16:1</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1co+16:20">1 Corinthians 16:20</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2co+13:12">2 Corinthians 13:12</span>. </p>
