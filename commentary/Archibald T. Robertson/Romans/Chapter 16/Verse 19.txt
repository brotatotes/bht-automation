<p><b> Is come abroad </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αφικετο</span></span>). Second aorist middle indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αφικνεομα</span></span>, old verb, to come from, then to arrive at, only here in N.T.</p>
<p><b>Over you </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εφ' υμιν</span></span>). "Upon you." Simple unto that which is evil (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ακεραιους εις το κακον</span></span>). Old adjective from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">α</span></span> privative and <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κεραννυμ</span></span>, to mix. Unmixed with evil, unadulterated. </p>
