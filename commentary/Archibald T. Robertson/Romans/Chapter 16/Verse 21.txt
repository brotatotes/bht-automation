<p>Verses <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+16:21-23">Romans 16:21-23</span> form a sort of postscript with greetings from Paul's companions in Corinth. Timothy was with Paul in Macedonia (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2co+1:1">2 Corinthians 1:1</span>) before he came to Corinth. Lucius may be the one mentioned in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+13:1">Acts 13:1</span>. Jason was once Paul's host (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+17:5-9">Acts 17:5-9</span>) in Thessalonica, Sosipater may be the longer form of Sopater of <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+20:4">Acts 20:4</span>. They are all Paul's fellow-countrymen (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">συγγενεις</span></span>). </p>
