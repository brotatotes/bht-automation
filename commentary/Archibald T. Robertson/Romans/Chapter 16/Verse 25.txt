<p>Verses <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+16:25-27">Romans 16:25-27</span> conclude the noble Epistle with the finest of Paul's doxologies.</p>
<p><b>To him that is able </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τω δυναμενω</span></span>). Dative of the articular participle of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δυναμα</span></span>. See similar idiom in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="eph+3:20">Ephesians 3:20</span>.</p>
<p><b>To stablish </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">στηριξα</span></span>). First aorist active infinitive of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">στηριζω</span></span>, to make stable.</p>
<p><b>According to my gospel </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κατα το ευαγγελιον μου</span></span>). Same phrase in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+2:16">Romans 2:16</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2ti+2:8">2 Timothy 2:8</span>. Not a book, but Paul's message as here set forth.</p>
<p><b>The preaching </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">το κηρυγμα</span></span>). The proclamation, the heralding.</p>
<p><b>Of Jesus Christ </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">Ιησου Χριστου</span></span>). Objective genitive, "about Jesus Christ."</p>
<p><b>Revelation </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αποκαλυψιν</span></span>). "Unveiling."</p>
<p><b>Of the mystery </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μυστηριου</span></span>). Once unknown, but now revealed.</p>
<p><b>Kept in silence </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">σεσιγημενου</span></span>). Perfect passive participle of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">σιγαω</span></span>, to be silent, state of silence.</p>
<p><b>Through times eternal </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">χρονοις αιωνιοις</span></span>). Associative instrumental case, "along with times eternal" (Robertson, <i>Grammar</i>, p. 527). See <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1co+2:6">1 Corinthians 2:6</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1co+2:7">1 Corinthians 2:7</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1co+2:10">1 Corinthians 2:10</span>. </p>
