<p><b> Urbanus </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">Ουρβανον</span></span>). "A common Roman slave name found among members of the household" (Sanday and Headlam). A Latin adjective from <i>urbs</i>, city (city-bred).</p>
<p><b>Stachys </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">Σταχυν</span></span>). A Greek name, rare, but among members of the imperial household. It means a head or ear of grain (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+12:1">Matthew 12:1</span>). </p>
