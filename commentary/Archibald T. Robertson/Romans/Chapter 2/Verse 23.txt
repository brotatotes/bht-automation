<p><b> Through thy transgression of the law </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δια της παραβασεως του νομου</span></span>). Old word for stepping across a line. Trench calls attention to "the mournfully numerous group of words" for the varieties of sin like <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αγνοημα</span></span>, ignorance, <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ανομια</span></span>, violation of law, <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αμαρτια</span></span>, missing the mark, <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εττημα</span></span>, falling short, <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">παραβασις</span></span>, passing over the line, <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">παρακοη</span></span>, disobedience to a voice, <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">παρανομια</span></span>, putting the law aside, <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">παραπτωμα</span></span>, falling down, <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πλημμελεια</span></span>, discord. </p>
