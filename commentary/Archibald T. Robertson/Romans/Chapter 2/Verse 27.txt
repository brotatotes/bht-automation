<p><b> If it fulfill the law </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τον νομον τελουσα</span></span>). <p><b>Judge thee </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κρινει--σε</span></span>). Unusual position of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">σε</span></span> (thee) so far from the verb <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κρινε</span></span>.</p>
