<p><b> That understandeth </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">συνιων</span></span>). Present active participle of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">συνιω</span></span>, late omega form of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">-μ</span></span> verb <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">συνιημ</span></span>, to send together, to grasp, to comprehend. Some MSS. have the article <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ο</span></span> before it as before <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εκζητων</span></span> (seeking out). </p>
