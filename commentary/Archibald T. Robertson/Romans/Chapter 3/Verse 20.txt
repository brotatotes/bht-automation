<p><b> Because </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">διοτ</span></span>, again, <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δια, οτ</span></span>).</p>
<p><b>By the works of the law </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εξ εργων νομου</span></span>). "Out of works of law." Mosaic law and any law as the source of being set right with God. Paul quotes <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ps+43:2">Psalms 43:2</span> as he did in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ga+2:16">Galatians 2:16</span> to prove his point.</p>
<p><b>The knowledge of sin </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επιγνωσις αμαρτιας</span></span>). The effect of law universally is rebellion to it (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1co+15:56">1 Corinthians 15:56</span>). Paul has shown this carefully in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ga+3:19-22">Galatians 3:19-22</span>. Cf. <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="heb+10:3">Hebrews 10:3</span>. He has now proven the guilt of both Gentile and Jew. </p>
