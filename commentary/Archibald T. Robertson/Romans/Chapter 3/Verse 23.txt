<p><b> Sinned </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ηρμαρτον</span></span>). Constative second aorist active indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αμαρτανω</span></span> as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+5:12">Romans 5:12</span>. This tense gathers up the whole race into one statement (a timeless aorist).</p>
<p><b>And fall short </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κα υστερουντα</span></span>). Present middle indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">υστερεω</span></span>, to be <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">υστερος</span></span> (comparative) too late, continued action, still fall short. It is followed by the ablative case as here, the case of separation. </p>
