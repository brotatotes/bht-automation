<p><b> It is excluded </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εξεκλεισθη</span></span>). First aorist (effective) passive indicative. "It is completely shut out." Glorying is on man's part.</p>
<p><b>Nay; but by a law of faith </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ουχι, αλλα δια νομου πιστεως</span></span>). Strong negative, and note "law of faith," by the principle of faith in harmony with God's love and grace. </p>
