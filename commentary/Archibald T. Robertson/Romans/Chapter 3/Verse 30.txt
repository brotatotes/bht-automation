<p><b> If so be that God is one </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ειπερ εις ο θεος</span></span>). Correct text rather than <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επειπερ</span></span>. It means "if on the whole." "By a species of rhetorical politeness it is used of that about which there is no doubt" (Thayer. Cf. <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1co+8:5">1 Corinthians 8:5</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1co+15:15">1 Corinthians 15:15</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+8:9">Romans 8:9</span>.</p>
<p><b>By faith </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εκ πιστεως</span></span>). "Out of faith," springing out of.</p>
<p><b>Through faith </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δια της πιστεως</span></span>). "By means of the faith" (just mentioned). <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">Εκ</span></span> denotes source, <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δια</span></span> intermediate agency or attendant circumstance. </p>
