<p><b> For then how </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επε πως</span></span>). There is a suppressed condition between <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επε</span></span> and <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πως</span></span>, an idiom occurring several times in the N.T. (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1co+15:29">1 Corinthians 15:29</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+11:6">Romans 11:6</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+11:22">Romans 11:22</span>). "Since, if that were true, how." </p>
