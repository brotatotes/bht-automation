<p><b> Be heirs </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κληρονομο</span></span>). No predicate in the Greek (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εισιν</span></span>). See on <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ga+4:1">Galatians 4:1</span>. If legalists are heirs of the Messianic promise to Abraham (condition of first class, assumed as true for argument's sake), the faith is emptied of all meaning (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κεκενωτα</span></span>, perfect passive indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κενοω</span></span>) and the promise to Abraham is made permanently idle (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κατηργητα</span></span>). </p>
