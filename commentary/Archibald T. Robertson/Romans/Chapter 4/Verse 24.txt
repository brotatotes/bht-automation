<p><b> Him that raised up Jesus </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τον εγειραντα Ιησουν</span></span>). First aorist active articular participle of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εγειρω</span></span>, to raise up. The fact of the Resurrection of Jesus is central in Paul's gospel (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1co+15:4">1 Corinthians 15:4</span>). </p>
