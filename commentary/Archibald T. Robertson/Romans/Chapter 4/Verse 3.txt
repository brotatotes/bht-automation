<p><b> It was reckoned unto him for righteousness </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ελογισθη εις δικαιοσυνην</span></span>). First aorist passive indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">λογιζομα</span></span>, old and common verb to set down accounts (literally or metaphorically). It was set down on the credit side of the ledger "for" (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εις</span></span> as often) righteousness. What was set down? His believing God (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επιστευσεν τω θεω</span></span>). </p>
