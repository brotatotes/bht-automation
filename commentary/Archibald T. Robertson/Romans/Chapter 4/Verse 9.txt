<p><b> Is this blessing then pronounced? </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ο μακαρισμος ουν ουτοσ?</span></span>). "Is this felicitation then?" There is no verb in the Greek. Paul now proceeds to show that Abraham was said in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ge+15:6">Genesis 15:6</span> to be set right with God by faith <i>before</i> he was circumcised. </p>
