<p><b> But also glorying in God </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αλλα κα καυχωμενο εν τω θεω</span></span>). Basis of all the exultation above (verses <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+5:1-5">Romans 5:1-5</span>).</p>
<p><b>Through whom we have now received the reconciliation </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δ ου νυν την καταλλαγην ελαβομεν</span></span>). Second aorist active indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">λαμβανω</span></span>, looked at as a past realization, "now" (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">νυν</span></span>) in contrast with the future consummation and a sure pledge and guarantee of it. </p>
