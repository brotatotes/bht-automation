<p><b> Through one that sinned </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δι' ενος αμαρτησαντος</span></span>). "Through one having sinned." That is Adam. Another contrast, difference in source (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εκ</span></span>).</p>
<p><b>Of one </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εξ ενος</span></span>). Supply <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">παραπτωματος</span></span>, Adam's one transgression.</p>
<p><b>Of many trespasses </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εκ πολλων παραπτωματων</span></span>). The gift by Christ grew out of manifold sins by Adam's progeny.</p>
<p><b>Justification </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δικαιωμα</span></span>). Act of righteousness, result, ordinance (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+1:32">Romans 1:32</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+2:26">Romans 2:26</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+8:4">Romans 8:4</span>), righteous deed (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+5:18">Romans 5:18</span>), verdict as here (acquittal). </p>
