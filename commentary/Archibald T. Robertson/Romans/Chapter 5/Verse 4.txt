<p><b> Knowing </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ειδοτες</span></span>). Second perfect participle of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ειδον</span></span> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">οιδα</span></span>), giving the reason for the previous exhortation to glory in tribulations. He gives a linked chain, one linking to the other (tribulation <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">θλιψις</span></span>, patience <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">υπομονη</span></span>, experience <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δοκιμη</span></span>, hope <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ελπις</span></span>) running into verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+5:5">Romans 5:5</span>. On <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δοκιμη</span></span>, see <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2co+2:9">2 Corinthians 2:9</span>. </p>
