<p><b> His own love </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">την εαυτου αγαπην</span></span>). See <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+3:16">John 3:16</span> as the best comment here.</p>
<p><b>While we were yet sinners </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ετ αμαρτωλων οντων</span></span>). Genitive absolute again. Not because we were Jews or Greeks, rich or poor, righteous or good, but plain sinners. Cf. <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+18:13">Luke 18:13</span>, the plea of the publican, "<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μο τω αμαρτωλω</span></span>." </p>
