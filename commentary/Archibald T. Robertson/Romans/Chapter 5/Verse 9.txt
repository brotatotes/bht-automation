<p><b> Much more then </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πολλω ουν μαλλον</span></span>). Argument from the greater to the less. The great thing is the justification in Christ's blood. The final salvation (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">σωθησομεθα</span></span>, future passive indicative) is less of a mystery. </p>
