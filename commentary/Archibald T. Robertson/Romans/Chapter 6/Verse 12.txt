<p><b> Reign </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">βασιλευετω</span></span>). Present active imperative, "let not sin continue to reign" as it did once (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+5:12">Romans 5:12</span>).</p>
<p><b>Mortal </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">θνητο</span></span>). Verbal adjective from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">θνησκω</span></span>, subject to death. The reign of sin is over with you. Self-indulgence is inconsistent with trust in the vicarious atonement.</p>
<p><b>That ye should obey </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εις το υπακουειν</span></span>). With a view to obeying. </p>
