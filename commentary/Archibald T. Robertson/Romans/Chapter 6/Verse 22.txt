<p><b> Ye have your fruit unto sanctification </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εχετε τον καρπον υμων εις αγιασμον</span></span>). Freedom from sin and slavery to God bring permanent fruit that leads to sanctification.</p>
<p><b>And the end eternal life </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">το δε τελος ζωην αιωνιον</span></span>). Note accusative case <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ζωην αιωνιον</span></span>, object of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εχετε</span></span> (ye have), though <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">θανατος</span></span> in contrast above is nominative. </p>
