<p><b> Wages </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">οψωνια</span></span>). Late Greek for wages of soldier, here of sin. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+3:14">Luke 3:14</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1co+9:7">1 Corinthians 9:7</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2co+11:8">2 Corinthians 11:8</span>. Sin pays its wages in full with no cut. But eternal life is God's gift (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">χαρισμα</span></span>), not wages. Both <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">θανατος</span></span> and <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ζωην</span></span> are</p>
<p><b>eternal </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αιωνιον</span></span>). </p>
