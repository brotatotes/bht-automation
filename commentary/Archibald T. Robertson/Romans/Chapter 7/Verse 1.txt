<p><b> To men that know the law </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">γινωσκουσιν νομον</span></span>). Dative plural of present active participle of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">γινωσκω</span></span>. The Romans, whether Jews or Gentiles, knew the principle of law.</p>
<p><b>A man </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">του ανθρωπου</span></span>). "The person," generic term <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ανθρωπος</span></span>, not <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ανηρ</span></span>. </p>
