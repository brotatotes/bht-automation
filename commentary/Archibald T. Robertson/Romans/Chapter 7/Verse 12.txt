<p><b> Holy, and righteous, and good </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αγια κα δικαια κα αγαθη</span></span>). This is the conclusion (wherefore, <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ωστε</span></span>) to the query in verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+7:7">Romans 7:7</span>. The commandment is God's and so holy like Him, just in its requirements and designed for our good. The modern revolt against law needs these words. </p>
