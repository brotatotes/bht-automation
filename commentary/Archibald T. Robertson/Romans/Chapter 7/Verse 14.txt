<p><b> Spiritual </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πνευματικος</span></span>). Spirit-caused and spirit-given and like the Holy Spirit. See <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1co+10:3">1 Corinthians 10:3</span>.</p>
<p><b>But I am carnal </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εγω δε σαρκινος ειμ</span></span>). "Fleshen" as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1co+3:1">1 Corinthians 3:1</span> which see, more emphatic even than <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">σαρκικος</span></span>," a creature of flesh."</p>
<p><b>Sold under sin </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πεπραμενος υπο την αμαρτιαν</span></span>). Perfect passive participle of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πιπρασκω</span></span>, old verb, to sell. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+13:46">Matthew 13:46</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+2:45">Acts 2:45</span>, state of completion. Sin has closed the mortgage and owns its slave. </p>
