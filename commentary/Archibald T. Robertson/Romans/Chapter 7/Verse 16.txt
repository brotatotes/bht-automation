<p><b> I consent unto the law </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">συνφημ τω νομω</span></span>). Old verb, here only in N.T., with associative instrumental case. "I speak with." My wanting (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">θελω</span></span>) to do the opposite of what I do proves my acceptance of God's law as good (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">καλος</span></span>). </p>
