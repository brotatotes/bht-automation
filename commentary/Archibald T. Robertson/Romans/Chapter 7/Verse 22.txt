<p><b> For I delight in </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">συνηδομα γαρ</span></span>). Old verb, here alone in N.T., with associative instrumental case, "I rejoice with the law of God," my real self "after the inward man" (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κατα τον εσω ανθρωπον</span></span>) of the conscience as opposed to "the outward man" (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2co+4:16">2 Corinthians 4:16</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="eph+3:16">Ephesians 3:16</span>). </p>
