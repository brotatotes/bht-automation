<p><b> I thank God </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">χαρις τω θεω</span></span>). "Thanks to God." Note of victory over death through Jesus Christ our Lord."</p>
<p><b>So then I myself </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αρα ουν αυτος εγω</span></span>). His whole self in his unregenerate state gives a divided service as he has already shown above. In <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+6:1-7">Romans 6:1-7</span> Paul proved the obligation to be sanctified. In <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+7:7-8">Romans 7:7-8</span> he discusses the possibility of sanctification, only for the renewed man by the help of the Holy Spirit. </p>
