<p><b> But now </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">νυν δε</span></span>). In the new condition.</p>
<p><b>Wherein we were holden </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εν ω κατειχομεθα</span></span>). Imperfect passive of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κατεχω</span></span>, picture of our former state (same verb in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+1:18">Romans 1:18</span>).</p>
<p><b>In newness of spirit </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εν καινοτητ πνευματος</span></span>). The death to the letter of the law (the old husband) has set us free to the new life in Christ. So Paul has shown again the obligation on us to live for Christ. </p>
