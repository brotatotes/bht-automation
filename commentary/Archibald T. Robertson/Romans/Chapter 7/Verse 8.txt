<p><b> Finding occasion </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αφορμην λαβουσα</span></span>). See <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2co+5:12">2 Corinthians 5:12</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2co+11:12">2 Corinthians 11:12</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ga+5:13">Galatians 5:13</span> for <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αφορμην</span></span>, a starting place from which to rush into acts of sin, excuses for doing what they want to do. Just so drinking men use the prohibition laws as "occasions" for violating them.</p>
<p><b>Wrought in me </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κατειργασατο εν εμο</span></span>). First aorist active middle indicative of the intensive verb <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κατεργαζομα</span></span>, to work out (to the finish), effective aorist. The command not to lust made me lust more.</p>
<p><b>Dead </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">νεκρα</span></span>). Inactive, not non-existent. Sin in reality was there in a dormant state. </p>
