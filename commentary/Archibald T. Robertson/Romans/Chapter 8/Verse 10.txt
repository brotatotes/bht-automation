<p><b> The body is dead </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">το μεν σωμα νεκρον</span></span>). Has the seeds of death in it and will die "because of sin."</p>
<p><b>The spirit is life </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">το δε πνευμα ζωη</span></span>). The redeemed human spirit. He uses <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ζωη</span></span> (life) instead of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ζωσα</span></span> (living), "God-begotten, God-sustained life" (Denney), if Christ is in you. </p>
