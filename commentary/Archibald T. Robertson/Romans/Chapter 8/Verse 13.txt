<p><b> Ye must die </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μελλετε αποθνησκειν</span></span>). Present indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μελλω</span></span>, to be about to do and present active infinitive of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αποθνησκω</span></span>, to die. "Ye are on the point of dying." Eternal death.</p>
<p><b>By the spirit </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πνευματ</span></span>). Holy Spirit, instrumental case.</p>
<p><b>Ye shall live </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ζησεσθε</span></span>). Future active indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ζαω</span></span>. Eternal life. </p>
