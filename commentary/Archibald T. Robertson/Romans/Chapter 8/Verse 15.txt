<p><b> The spirit of adoption </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πνευμα υιοθεσιας</span></span>). See on this term <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">υιοθεσια</span></span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ga+4:5">Galatians 4:5</span>. Both Jews and Gentiles receive this "adoption" into the family of God with all its privileges. "<b> Whereby we cry, Abba, Father </b>" (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εν η κραζομεν Αββα ο πατηρ</span></span>). See <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ga+4:6">Galatians 4:6</span> for discussion of this double use of Father as the child's privilege. </p>
