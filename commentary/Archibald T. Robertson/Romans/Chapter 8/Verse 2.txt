<p><b> The law of the Spirit of life </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ο νομος του πνευματος της ζωης</span></span>). The principle or authority exercised by the Holy Spirit which bestows life and which rests "in Christ Jesus."</p>
<p><b>Made me free </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ηλευθερωσεν με</span></span>). First aorist active indicative of the old verb <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ελευθεροω</span></span> for which see <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ga+5:1">Galatians 5:1</span>. Aleph B have <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">σε</span></span> (thee) instead of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">με</span></span>. It matters little. We are pardoned, we are free from the old law of sin and death (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+7:7-24">Romans 7:7-24</span>), we are able by the help of the Holy Spirit to live the new life in Christ. </p>
