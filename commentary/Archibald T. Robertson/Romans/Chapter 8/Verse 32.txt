<p><b> He that </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ος γε</span></span>). "Who as much as this" (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">γε</span></span> here magnifying the deed, intensive particle).</p>
<p><b>Spared not </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ουκ εφεισατο</span></span>). First aorist middle of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">φειδομα</span></span>, old verb used about the offering of Isaac in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ge+22:16">Genesis 22:16</span>. See <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+20:29">Acts 20:29</span>.</p>
<p><b>Also with him </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κα συν αυτω</span></span>). The gift of "his own son" is the promise and the pledge of the all things for good of verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+8:28">Romans 8:28</span>. Christ is all and carries all with him. </p>
