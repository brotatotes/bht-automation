 He that  (ος γε). "Who as much as this" (γε here magnifying the deed, intensive particle).Spared not  (ουκ εφεισατο). First aorist middle of φειδομα, old verb used about the offering of Isaac in Genesis 22:16. See Acts 20:29.Also with him  (κα συν αυτω). The gift of "his own son" is the promise and the pledge of the all things for good of verse Romans 8:28. Christ is all and carries all with him.
