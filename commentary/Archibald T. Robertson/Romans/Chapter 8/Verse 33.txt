<p><b> Who shall lay anything to the charge of God's elect? </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τις εγκαλεσε κατα εκλεκτων θεου?</span></span>). Future active indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εγκαλεω</span></span>, old verb, to come forward as accuser (forensic term) in case in court, to impeach, as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+19:40">Acts 19:40</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+23:29">Acts 23:29</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+26:2">Acts 26:2</span>, the only N.T. examples. Satan is the great Accuser of the brethren.</p>
<p><b>It is God that justifieth </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">θεος ο δικαιων</span></span>). God is the Judge who sets us right according to his plan for justification (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+3:21-31">Romans 3:21-31</span>). The Accuser must face the Judge with his charges. </p>
