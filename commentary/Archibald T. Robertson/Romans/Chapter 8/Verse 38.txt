<p><b> For I am persuaded </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πεπεισμα γαρ</span></span>). Perfect passive participle of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πειθω</span></span>, "I stand convinced." The items mentioned are those that people dread (life, death, supernatural powers, above, below, any creature to cover any omissions). </p>
