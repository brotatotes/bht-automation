 The ordinance of the law  (το δικαιωμα του νομου). "The requirement of the law."Might be fulfilled  (ινα πλερωθη). Purpose of the death of Christ by ινα and first aorist passive subjunctive of πληροω. Christ met it all in our stead (Romans 3:21-26).Not after the flesh, but after the Spirit  (μη κατα σαρκα αλλα κατα πνευμα). The two laws of life (κατα σαρκα in Romans 7:7-24, κατα πνευμα Romans 8:1-11). Most likely the Holy Spirit or else the renewed spirit of man.
