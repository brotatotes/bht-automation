<p><b> In Christ </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εν Χριστω</span></span>). Paul really takes a triple oath here so strongly is he stirred. He makes a positive affirmation in Christ, a negative one (not lying), the appeal to his conscience as co-witness (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">συνμαρτυρουσης</span></span>, genitive absolute as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+2:15">Romans 2:15</span> which see) "in the Holy Spirit." </p>
