<p><b> Having conceived of one </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εξ ενος κοιτην εχουσα</span></span>). By metonomy with cause for the effect we have this peculiar idiom (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κοιτη</span></span> being bed, marriage bed), "having a marriage bed from one" husband. One father and twins. </p>
