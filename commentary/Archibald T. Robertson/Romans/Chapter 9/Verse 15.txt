 For he says to Moses  (τω Μωυσε γαρ λεγε). He has an Old Testament illustration of God's election in the case of Pharaoh (Exodus 33:19).On whom I have mercy  (ον αν ελεω). Indefinite relative with αν and the present active subjunctive of ελεαω, late verb only here and Jude 1:23 in N.T. "On whomsoever I have mercy." The same construction in ον αν οικτειρω, "on whomsoever I have compassion."
