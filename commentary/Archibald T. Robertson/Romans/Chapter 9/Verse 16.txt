<p><b>It is not of </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ου</span></span>). We must supply <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εστιν ελεος</span></span> with <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ου</span></span>. "Mercy is not of." The articular participles (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">του θελοντοσ, του τρεχοντοσ, του ελεωντος</span></span>) can be understood as in the genitive with <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ελεος</span></span> understood (mercy is not a quality of) or as the predicate ablative of source like <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επιλυσεως</span></span> in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2pe+1:20">2 Peter 1:20</span>. Paul is fond of the metaphor of running. </p>
