<p><b> To Pharaoh </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τω Φαραω</span></span>). There is a national election as seen in verses <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+9:7-13">Romans 9:7-13</span>, but here Paul deals with the election of individuals. He "lays down the principle that God's grace does not necessarily depend upon anything but God's will" (Sanday and Headlam). He quotes <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ex+9:16">Exodus 9:16</span>.</p>
<p><b>Might be published </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">διαγγελη</span></span>). Second aorist passive subjunctive of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">διαγγελλω</span></span>. </p>
