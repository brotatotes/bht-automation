<p><b> Hath said before </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">προειρηκεν</span></span>). Perfect active indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">προειπον</span></span> (defective verb). Stands on record in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="isa+1:9">Isaiah 1:9</span>.</p>
<p><b>Had left </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εγκατελιπεν</span></span>). Second aorist active indicative of old verb <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εγκαταλειπω</span></span>, to leave behind. Condition of second class, determined as unfulfilled, with <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αν εγενηθημεν</span></span> and <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αν ωμοιωθημεν</span></span> as the conclusions (both first aorist passives of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">γινομα</span></span> and <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ομοιοω</span></span>, common verbs).</p>
<p><b>A seed </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">σπερμα</span></span>). The remnant of verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+9:27">Romans 9:27</span>. </p>
