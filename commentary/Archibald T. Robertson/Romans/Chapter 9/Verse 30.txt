<p><b> Attained </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κατελαβεν</span></span>). Second aorist active indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">καταλαμβανω</span></span>, old verb, to grasp, to seize, to overtake (carrying out the figure in <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">διωκω</span></span> (to pursue). It was a curious paradox.</p>
<p><b>Which is of faith </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">την εκ πιστεως</span></span>). As Paul has repeatedly shown, the only way to get the God-kind of righteousness. </p>
