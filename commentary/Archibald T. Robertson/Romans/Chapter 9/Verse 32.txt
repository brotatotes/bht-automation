<p>We must supply the omitted verb <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εδιωξα</span></span> (pursued) from verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+9:31">Romans 9:31</span>. That explains the rest.</p>
<p><b>They stumbled at the stone of stumbling </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">προσεκοψαν τω λιθω του προσκομματος</span></span>). The quotation is from <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="isa+8:14">Isaiah 8:14</span>. <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">Προσκοπτω</span></span> means to cut (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κοπτω</span></span>) against (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">προς</span></span>) as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mt+4:6">Matthew 4:6</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="joh+11:9">John 11:9</span>. The Jews found Christ a <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">σκανδαλον</span></span> (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1co+1:23">1 Corinthians 1:23</span>). </p>
