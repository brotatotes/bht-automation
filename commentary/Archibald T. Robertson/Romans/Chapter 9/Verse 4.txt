<p><b> Who </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">οιτινες</span></span>). The very ones who, inasmuch as they.</p>
<p><b>Israelites </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">Ισραηλειτα</span></span>). Covenant name of the chosen people.</p>
<p><b>Whose </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ων</span></span>). Predicate genitive of the relative, used also again with <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ο πατερες</span></span>. For "the adoption" (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">η υιοθεσια</span></span>) see <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+8:15">Romans 8:15</span>.</p>
<p><b>The glory </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">η δοξα</span></span>). The Shekinah Glory of God (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+3:23">Romans 3:23</span>) and used of Jesus in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="jas+2:1">James 2:1</span>.</p>
<p><b>The covenants </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">α διαθηκα</span></span>). Plural because renewed often (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ge+6:18">Genesis 6:18</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ge+9:9">Genesis 9:9</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ge+15:18">Genesis 15:18</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ge+17:2">Genesis 17:2</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ge+17:7">Genesis 17:7</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ge+17:9">Genesis 17:9</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ex+2:24">Exodus 2:24</span>).</p>
<p><b>The giving of the law </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">η νομοθεσια</span></span>). Old word, here only in N.T., from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">νομος</span></span> and <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τιθημ</span></span>.</p>
<p><b>The service </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">η λατρεια</span></span>). The temple service (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="heb+9:1">Hebrews 9:1</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="heb+9:6">Hebrews 9:6</span>).</p>
<p><b>The fathers </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ο πατερες</span></span>). The patriarchs (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+3:13">Acts 3:13</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+7:32">Acts 7:32</span>). </p>
