<p><b> The children of the promise </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τα τεκνα της επαγγελιας</span></span>). Not through Ishmael, but through Isaac. Only the children of the promise are "children of God" (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τεκνα του θεου</span></span>) in the full sense. He is not speaking of Christians here, but simply showing that the privileges of the Jews were not due to their physical descent from Abraham. Cf. <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+3:8">Luke 3:8</span>. </p>
