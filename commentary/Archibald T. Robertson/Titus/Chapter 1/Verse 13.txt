 Testimony  (μαρτυρια). Of the poet Epimenides. Paul endorses it from his recent knowledge.Sharply  (αποτομως). Old adverb from αποτομος (from αποτεμνω, to cut off), in N.T. only here and 2 Corinthians 13:10, "curtly," "abruptly." It is necessary to appear rude sometimes for safety, if the house is on fire and life is in danger.That they may be sound  (ινα υγιαινωσιν). Final clause with ινα and present active subjunctive of υγιαινω, for which verb see on 1 Timothy 1:10.
