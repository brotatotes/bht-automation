<p><b> Testimony </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μαρτυρια</span></span>). Of the poet Epimenides. Paul endorses it from his recent knowledge.</p>
<p><b>Sharply </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αποτομως</span></span>). Old adverb from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αποτομος</span></span> (from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αποτεμνω</span></span>, to cut off), in N.T. only here and <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2co+13:10">2 Corinthians 13:10</span>, "curtly," "abruptly." It is necessary to appear rude sometimes for safety, if the house is on fire and life is in danger.</p>
<p><b>That they may be sound </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ινα υγιαινωσιν</span></span>). Final clause with <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ινα</span></span> and present active subjunctive of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">υγιαινω</span></span>, for which verb see on <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1ti+1:10">1 Timothy 1:10</span>. </p>
