<p><b> In his own seasons </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">καιροις ιδιοις</span></span>). Locative case. See <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1ti+2:6">1 Timothy 2:6</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1ti+6:15">1 Timothy 6:15</span>.</p>
<p><b>In the message </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εν κηρυγματ</span></span>). See <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1co+1:21">1 Corinthians 1:21</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1co+2:4">1 Corinthians 2:4</span> for this word, the human proclamation (preaching) of God's word.</p>
<p><b>Wherewith I was intrusted </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ο επιστευθην</span></span>). Accusative relative <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ο</span></span> retained with the first aorist passive indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πιστευω</span></span> as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1ti+1:11">1 Timothy 1:11</span>. See <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1ti+2:7">1 Timothy 2:7</span>.</p>
<p><b>Of God our Saviour </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">του σωτηρος ημων θεου</span></span>). In verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="tit+1:4">Titus 1:4</span> he applies the words "<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">του σωτηρος ημων</span></span>" to Christ. In <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="tit+2:13">Titus 2:13</span> he applies both <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">θεου</span></span> and <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">σωτηρος</span></span> to Christ. </p>
