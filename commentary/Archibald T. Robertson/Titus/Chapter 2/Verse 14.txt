<p><b> Who gave himself for us </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ος εδωκεν εαυτον υπερ ημων</span></span>). Paul's great doctrine (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ga+1:4">Galatians 1:4</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ga+2:20">Galatians 2:20</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1ti+2:6">1 Timothy 2:6</span>).</p>
<p><b>That he might redeem us </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ινα λυτρωσητα</span></span>). Final clause, <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ινα</span></span> and the aorist middle subjunctive of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">λυτροω</span></span>, old verb from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">λυτρον</span></span> (ransom), in N.T. only here, <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+24:21">Luke 24:21</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1pe+1:18">1 Peter 1:18</span>.</p>
<p><b>Purify to himself </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">καθαριση εαυτω</span></span>). Final clause with first aorist active subjunctive of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">καθαριζω</span></span>, for which verb see <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="eph+5:26">Ephesians 5:26</span>.</p>
<p><b>Lawlessness </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ανομιας</span></span>). See <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2th+2:3">2 Thessalonians 2:3</span>.</p>
<p><b>A people for his own possession </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">λαον περιουσιον</span></span>). A late word (from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">περιειμ</span></span>, to be over and above, in papyri as well as <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">περιουσια</span></span>), only in LXX and here, apparently made by the LXX, one's possession, and so God's chosen people. See <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1pe+2:9">1 Peter 2:9</span> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">λαος εις περιποιησιν</span></span>).</p>
<p><b>Zealous of good works </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ζηλωτην καλων εργων</span></span>). "A zealot for good works." Substantive for which see <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1co+14:12">1 Corinthians 14:12</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ga+1:14">Galatians 1:14</span>. Objective genitive <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εργων</span></span>. </p>
