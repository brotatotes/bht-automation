<p><b> The younger men </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τους νεωτερους</span></span>). Just one item, besides "likewise" (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">οσαυτως</span></span> as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="tit+2:3">Titus 2:3</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="tit+2:1">Titus 2:1</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="tit+2:9">Titus 2:9</span>), "to be soberminded" (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">σωφρονειν</span></span>, old verb as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+12:3">Romans 12:3</span>). It is possible to take "in all things" (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">περ παντα</span></span>) with <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">σωφρονειν</span></span>, though the editors take it with verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="tit+2:7">Titus 2:7</span>. </p>
