<p><b> To be in subjection to rulers, to authorities, to be obedient </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αρχαις εξουσιαις υποτασσεσθα πειθαρχειν</span></span>). Remarkable double asyndeton, no <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κα</span></span> (and) between the two substantives or the two verbs. <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">Πειθαρχειν</span></span> (to obey), old verb (from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πειθομαι, αρχη</span></span>), in N.T. only here and <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ac+27:21">Acts 27:21</span>.</p>
