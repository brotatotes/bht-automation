<p><b> Is perverted </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εξεστραπτα</span></span>). Perfect passive indicative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εκστρεφω</span></span>, old word to turn inside out, to twist, to pervert. Only here in N.T.</p>
<p><b>Self-condemned </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αυτοκατακριτος</span></span>). Only known example of this double compound verbal adjective (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αυτοσ, κατα, κρινω</span></span>). </p>
