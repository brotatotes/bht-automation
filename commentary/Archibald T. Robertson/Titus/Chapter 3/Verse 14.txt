<p><b> Our people </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ο ημετερο</span></span>). "Our folks." The Cretan converts, not just Paul's friends.</p>
<p><b>Let learn </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μανθανετωσαν</span></span>). Present active imperative, keep on learning how.</p>
<p><b>To maintain </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">προιστασθα</span></span>). See verse <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="tit+3:8">Titus 3:8</span>.</p>
<p><b>For necessary uses </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εις αναγκαιας χρειας</span></span>). "For necessary wants." No idlers wanted.
