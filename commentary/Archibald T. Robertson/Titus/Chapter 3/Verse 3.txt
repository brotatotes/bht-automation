<p><b> Aforetime </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ποτε</span></span>). "Once" in our unconverted state as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="eph+2:3">Ephesians 2:3</span>.</p>
<p><b>Foolish </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ανοητο</span></span>). See <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+1:14">Romans 1:14</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+1:21">Romans 1:21</span>.</p>
<p><b>Disobedient </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">απειθεις</span></span>). See <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+1:30">Romans 1:30</span>.</p>
<p><b>Deceived </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πλανωμενο</span></span>). Present passive participle of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πλαναω</span></span> though the middle is possible.</p>
<p><b>Divers lusts </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ηδοναις ποικιλαις</span></span>). "Pleasures" (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ηδοναις</span></span> from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ηδομα</span></span>, old word, in N.T. only here, <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="lu+8:14">Luke 8:14</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="jas+4:1">James 4:1</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="jas+4:3">James 4:3</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2pe+2:13">2 Peter 2:13</span>). <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">Ποικιλαις</span></span> (old word) is many-coloured as in <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="mr+1:34">Mark 1:34</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="jas+1:2">James 1:2</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2ti+3:6">2 Timothy 3:6</span>, etc.</p>
<p><b>Living </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">διαγοντες</span></span>). See <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1ti+3:6">1 Timothy 3:6</span> (supply <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">βιον</span></span>).</p>
<p><b>In malice </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εν κακια</span></span>). See <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+1:29">Romans 1:29</span>.</p>
<p><b>Envy </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">φθονω</span></span>). See <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="ro+1:29">Romans 1:29</span>.</p>
<p><b>Hateful </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">στυγητο</span></span>). Late passive verbal from <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">στυγεω</span></span>, to hate. In Philo, only here in N.T.</p>
<p><b>Hating one another </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μισουντες αλληλους</span></span>). Active sense and natural result of being "hateful." </p>
