 Fightings about the law  (μαχας νομικας). "Legal battles." See 1 Timothy 6:4; 2 Timothy 2:23. Wordy fights about Mosaic and Pharisaic and Gnostic regulations.Shun  (περιιστασο). Present middle imperative of περιιστημ, intransitive, step around, stand aside (2 Timothy 2:16). Common in this sense in the literary Koine.Unprofitable  (ανωφελεις). Old compound adjective (α privative and οφελος), in N.T. only here and Hebrews 7:18.
