<p><b> Fightings about the law </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μαχας νομικας</span></span>). "Legal battles." See <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="1ti+6:4">1 Timothy 6:4</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2ti+2:23">2 Timothy 2:23</span>. Wordy fights about Mosaic and Pharisaic and Gnostic regulations.</p>
<p><b>Shun </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">περιιστασο</span></span>). Present middle imperative of <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">περιιστημ</span></span>, intransitive, step around, stand aside (<span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="2ti+2:16">2 Timothy 2:16</span>). Common in this sense in the literary <i>Koine</i>.</p>
<p><b>Unprofitable </b> (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ανωφελεις</span></span>). Old compound adjective (<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">α</span></span> privative and <span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">οφελος</span></span>), in N.T. only here and <span class="scriptRef" com="rwp" lang="eng" ref="heb+7:18">Hebrews 7:18</span>. </p>
