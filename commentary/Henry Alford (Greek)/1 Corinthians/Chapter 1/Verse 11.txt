<p><strong> 11.</strong> ] We cannot fill up τῶν Χλόης , not knowing whether they were <em> sons</em> , or <em> servants</em> , or other members of her family. Nor can we say whether Chloe was (Theophyl., al.) an <em> inhabitant of Corinth</em> , or <em> some Christian woman</em> (Estius) known to the Corinthians <em> elsewhere</em> , or (Michaelis, Meyer) <em> an Ephesian</em> , having friends who had been in Corinth.</p>
