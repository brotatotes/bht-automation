<p><strong> 6. </strong> <strong> τὸ μαρτ</strong> <strong> . </strong> <strong> τ</strong> <strong> . </strong> <strong> χριστοῦ</strong> ] the witness concerning Christ delivered by me.
	</p>
<p><strong> καθώς</strong> , <strong> as indeed</strong> , ‘siquidem.’
	</p>
<p><strong> ἐβεβ</strong> ., <strong> was confirmed</strong> , <em> took deep root</em> , among you; i.e. ‘as was to have been expected, from the impression made among you by my preaching of Christ.’ This confirmation was <em> internal</em> , by faith and permanence in the truth, not external, by miracles.</p>
