<p><strong> 14.</strong> ] <em> Conclusion from the above warning examples</em> : IDOLATRY IS BY ALL MEANS TO BE SHUNNED; not tampered with, but <em> fled from</em> .
	</p>
<p><strong> φεύγετε ἀπό</strong> (‘ <em> fugiendo discedite a</em> ,’ Meyer) expressing even more strongly than the accus, with φεύγω , the entire avoidance. This verse of itself would by inference forbid the Corinthians having any share in the idol feasts; but he proceeds to ground such prohibition on further special considerations.</p>
