<p><strong> 15.</strong> ] The <strong> ὅτι</strong> is rightly rendered in E. V. <strong> because</strong> .
	</p>
<p><strong> οὐ παρὰ</strong> τ . κ . τ . λ .] These words [may be taken, here and in the next verse, “ <em> it is not therefore not of the body</em> .” But they] are best taken as a question, appealing to the sense of the reader: they thus have more of the vigour of the Apostle’s style.
	</p>
