<p><strong> 22, 23.</strong> ] Nay, the relation between the members is so entirely different from this, that the <em> very disparagement</em> , conventionally, of <em> any member</em> , is the <em> reason why more care should be taken of it</em> . I understand by the τὰ δοκοῦντα μέλη τοῦ σώματος ἀσθενέστερα ὑπάρχειν , those members which in each man’s case appear to be <em> inheritors of disease</em> , or to have <em> incurred weakness</em> . By <em> this very fact</em> , their <em> necessity to him</em> is brought out much more than that of the others.</p>
