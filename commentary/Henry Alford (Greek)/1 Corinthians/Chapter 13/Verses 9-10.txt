<p><strong> 9, 10.</strong> ] <em> Reason given</em> ; that our knowledge, and our prophesying (utterance of divine things) are but <em> partial</em> , embracing <em> but a part</em> : but when that which is <em> perfect</em> (entire universal) shall have come, this <em> partial</em> shall be abolished superseded.
