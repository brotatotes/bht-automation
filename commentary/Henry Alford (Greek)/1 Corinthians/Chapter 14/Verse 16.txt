Thus in state matters, it is one out of office Δημοσθένει ὄντι ἰδιώτῃ , Thuc. iv. 2; in philosophy, one uneducated and rude ἡμεῖς μὴν οἱ ἰδιῶται οὐ δεδοίκαμεν , ὑμεῖς δὲ οἱ φιλόσοφοι δειλιᾶτε , Diog. Laert. Aristipp. ii. 71, &amp;c. &amp;c. See examples in Wetst. So here it <em> is, one who has not the gift of speaking and interpreting</em> .
