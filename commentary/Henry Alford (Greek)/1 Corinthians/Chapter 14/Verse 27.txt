<p><strong> 27.</strong> ] <strong> κατὰ δύο</strong> (scil. let it take place), <strong> by two</strong> (at each time, i.e. in one assembly: not more than two or three might speak with tongues <em> at each meeting</em> ) <strong> or at the most three, and by turn</strong> (one after another, not together): <strong> and let one</strong> (some one who has the gift, and <em> not more than one</em> ) <strong> interpret</strong> (what is said in the tongue).</p>
