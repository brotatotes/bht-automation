<p><strong> 8.</strong> ] <strong> ἄδηλον</strong> , <strong> uncertain</strong> , in its <em> meaning</em> : for a particular succession of notes of the trumpet then, as now, gave the signals for attack, and retreat, and the various evolutions of an army. The giving the signal for battle with the trumpet is called by Dio Cassius τὸ πολεμικὰν βοᾷν , by Ælian τὸ παρορμητικὸν ἐμπνεῖν : see Wetst., where many examples are to be found.</p>
