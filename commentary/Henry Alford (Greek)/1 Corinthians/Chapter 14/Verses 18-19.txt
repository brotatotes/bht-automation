<p><strong> 18, 19.</strong> ] <em> Declaration of his own feeling on the matter, highly endowed as he was with the gift</em> . <strong> I thank God, I speak with a tongue</strong> (have the gift of speaking with tongues) <strong> more than you all</strong> . This juxtaposition of two clauses, between which ‘ <em> that</em> ’ is to be supplied in the sense, is not unusual: βούλει σκοπῶμεν : ‘fac videas,’ Eur. Hippol. 567, ἐπίσχετ ʼ, αὐδὴν τῶν ἔσωθεν ἐκμάθω . Hom. Od. β . 195, Τηλεμάχῳ δ ʼ ἐν πᾶσιν ἐγὼν ὑποθήσομαι αὐτός , Μητέρα ἣν ἐς πατρὸς ἀνωγέτω ἀπονέεσθαι . See Hartung, Partikell. ii. p. 134.</p>
