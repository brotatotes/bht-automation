<p><strong> 12 19</strong> .] <em> On the fact of Christ’s Resurrection, announced in his preaching, and confessed in their belief, he grounds</em> ( <em> negatively</em> ) <em> the truth of the general Resurrection: If the latter be not to happen, neither has the former happened: and he urges the results of such a disproof of Christ’s Resurrection</em> .</p>
