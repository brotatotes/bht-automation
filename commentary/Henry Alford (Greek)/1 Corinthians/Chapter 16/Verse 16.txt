<p><strong> 16.</strong> ] <strong> καὶ ὑμεῖς</strong> , you in your turn, in return for their self-devotion.
	</p>
<p><strong> ὑποτάσσ</strong> .] viz. in honouring their advice and being ready to be directed by them: there is an allusion to ἔταξαν ἑαυτούς above.
	</p>
<p><strong> τοῖς τοιούτοις</strong> ] <em> to such persons</em> , meaning the individuals of Stephanas’s family, whom they knew. See the usage of ὁ τοιοῦτος in reff.
	</p>
<p><strong> συνεργοῦντι</strong> ] viz. with τοῖς τοιούτοις .</p>
