<p><strong> 10, 11</strong> .] <em> Recommendation of Timothy to their good reception and offices</em> . He had preceded Paul ( Act 19:22 ) in the journey to Macedonia. From <strong> ἐὰν ἔλθῃ</strong> , it would appear to have been <em> probable</em> , but not quite certain, that he would visit them. In ch. <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="1co+4:17">1 Corinthians 4:17</span> , he is described as sent on <em> for that purpose</em> : so that the ἐάν may merely refer to the uncertainties of the journey.</p>
