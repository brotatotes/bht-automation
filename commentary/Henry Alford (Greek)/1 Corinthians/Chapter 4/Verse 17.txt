<p><strong> τέκνον</strong> ] see <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="1ti+1:2">1 Timothy 1:2</span> ; <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="1ti+1:18">1 Timothy 1:18</span> ; <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="2ti+1:2">2 Timothy 1:2</span> . <p><strong> ἐν κυρίῳ</strong> points out the spiritual nature of the relationship.

<p><strong> ἀναμνήσει</strong> ] Timothy, by being himself a close imitator of the Christian virtues and teaching of his and their spiritual father, would bring to their minds his well-known character, and way of teaching, which they seemed to have well-nigh forgotten. See <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="2ti+3:10">2 Timothy 3:10</span> .
