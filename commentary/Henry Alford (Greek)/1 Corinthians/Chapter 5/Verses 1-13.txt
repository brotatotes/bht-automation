<p><strong> 1 13</strong> .] CONCERNING A GROSS CASE OF INCEST WHICH HAD ARISEN, AND WAS HARBOURED, AMONG THEM ( 1Co 5:1-8 ): AND QUALIFICATION OF A FORMER COMMAND WHICH HE HAD GIVEN THEM RESPECTING ASSOCIATION WITH GROSS SINNERS (9 13). The subject of this chapter is bound on to the foregoing by the question of ch. <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="1co+4:21">1 Corinthians 4:21</span> ; and it furnishes an instance of those things which required his apostolic discipline.</p>
