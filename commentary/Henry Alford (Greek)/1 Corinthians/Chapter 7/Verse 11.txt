<p><strong> 11</strong> .] ἐάν to καταλλαγήτω is parenthetical. It supposes a case of <em> actual</em> separation, contrary of course to Christ’s command: if such <em> have really taken place</em> ( καί , veritably: see note on <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="2co+5:3">2 Corinthians 5:3</span> , and Hartung, Partikell. i. 132), the additional sin of a new marriage ( Mat 5:32 ) must not be committed, but the breach healed as soon as possible.
