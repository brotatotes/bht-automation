<p><strong> 6 21</strong> .] THE THIRD AND LAST DIVISION OF THE EPISTLE. This portion falls naturally into two parts: <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="1jo+5:6-13">1 John 5:6-13</span> , and <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="1jo+5:14-21">1 John 5:14-21</span> ; the former of which treats of the concluding part of the argument, and the latter forms the close of the Epistle.</p>
