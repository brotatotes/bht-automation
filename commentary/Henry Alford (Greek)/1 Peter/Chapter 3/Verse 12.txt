<p><strong> 12</strong> .] <em> The citation continued, and a reason given for the foregoing conditions of prosperity</em> . <strong> Because the eyes of the Lord</strong> (Jehovah) <strong> are</strong> (directed, in a favourable sense, for good) <strong> upon righteous men</strong> (“inde vitam habent et dies bonos,” Bengel), <strong> and His ears</strong> (inclined) <strong> unto their supplication: but the face of the Lord is</strong> (directed, in an unfavourable sense, for wrath) <strong> upon men doing evil things</strong> .</p>
