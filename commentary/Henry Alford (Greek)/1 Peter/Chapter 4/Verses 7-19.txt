<p><strong> 7 5:11</strong> .] <em> General exhortations with reference to behaviour within the Christian body, in contemplation of the approaching end</em> . This portion of the Epistle falls into three sections: 7 11, <em> Christian and social duties</em> , in consideration of the end being at hand: 12 19, <em> Christian bearing of suffering</em> , in the same consideration: <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="1pe+5:1-11">1 Peter 5:1-11</span> , <em> ecclesiastical and general mutual ministrations</em> : passing off into fervent <em> general exhortations and aspirations</em> .</p>
