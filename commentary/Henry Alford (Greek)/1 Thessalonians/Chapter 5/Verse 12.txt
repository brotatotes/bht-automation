<p><strong> 12</strong> .] <strong> εἰδέναι</strong> in this sense is perhaps a Hebraism: the LXX (in ref. Prov.) express יָדַע by ἐπιγινώσκειν . The persons indicated by <strong> κοπιῶντας</strong> <strong> , </strong> <strong> προϊσταμένους</strong> , and <strong> νουθετοῦντας</strong> , are the same, viz. the πρεσβύτεροι or ἐπίσκοποι : see note on <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="ac+20:17">Acts 20:17</span> ; <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="ac+20:28">Acts 20:28</span> . <strong> ἐν ὑμ</strong> <strong> .</strong> is <strong> among you</strong> , not as Pelt, al. ‘(bestowing labour) <em> on</em> you.’
	</p>
<p><strong> ἐν κυρίῳ</strong> , as the element in which, the matter with regard to which, their presidency takes place: = ‘in divine things:’ οὐκ ἐν τοῖς κοσμικοῖς , ἀλλ ʼ ἐν τοῖς κατὰ κύριον . Thl.</p>
