<p><strong> προσεύχεσθε</strong> , not of the mere spirit of prayer, as Jowett: but, as in parallel, <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="eph+6:18">Ephesians 6:18</span> , of direct supplications to God. These may be unceasing, in the heart which is full of his presence and evermore communing with Him.</p>
