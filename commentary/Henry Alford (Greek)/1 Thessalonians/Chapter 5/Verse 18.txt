<p><strong> 18</strong> . <strong> ἐν παντί</strong> ] <strong> in every thing, every circumstance</strong> : see reff., and cf. ὑπὲρ πάντων , <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="eph+5:20">Ephesians 5:20</span> ; κατὰ πάντα , <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="col+3:22-23">Colossians 3:22-23</span> . Chrys., al., explain it ‘ <em> on every occasion</em> ’ ( καιρῷ ); but <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="2co+9:8">2 Corinthians 9:8</span> , ἐν παντὶ πάντοτε , precludes this. τοῦτο perhaps refers back to the three χαίρ ., προσεύχ ., εὐχαρ ., or perhaps, as Ellic. and most modern expositors, to εὐχαρ . alone.
	</p>
<p>After <strong> γάρ</strong> , supply ἐστίν , and understand <strong> θέλημα</strong> , not ‘ <em> decree</em> ,’ but <strong> will</strong> , in its practical reference to your conduct.
	</p>
<p><strong> ἐν χρ</strong> <strong> . </strong> <strong> Ἰησ</strong> <strong> .</strong> ] in, as its medium; Christ being the Mediator.</p>
