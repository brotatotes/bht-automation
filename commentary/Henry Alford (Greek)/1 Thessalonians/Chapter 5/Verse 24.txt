<p><strong> 24</strong> .] <em> Assurance from God’s faithfulness, that it will be so</em> .
	</p>
<p><strong> πιστός</strong> (reff.) true to His word and calling: ἀντὶ τοῦ ἀληθής , Thdrt.
	</p>

	</p>
<p><strong> ποιήσει</strong> , viz. that which was specified in the last verse.</p>
