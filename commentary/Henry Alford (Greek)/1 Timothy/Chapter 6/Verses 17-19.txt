<p><strong> 17 19</strong> .] <em> Precepts for the rich</em> . Not a supplement to the Epistle, as commonly regarded: the occurrence of a doxology is no sufficient ground for supposing that the Apostle intended to close with it: cf. ch. <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="1ti+1:17">1 Timothy 1:17</span> . Rather, the subject is resumed from <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="1ti+6:6-10">1 Timothy 6:6-10</span> . We may perhaps make an inference as to the late date of the Epistle, from the existence of wealthy members in the Ephesian church.</p>
