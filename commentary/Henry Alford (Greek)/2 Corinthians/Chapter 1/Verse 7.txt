<p><strong> 7.</strong> ] <strong> εἰδότες</strong> refers back to παρακαλούμεθα : <strong> we are comforted with the assurance that, &amp;c</strong> . After οὕτως καί understand not ἔσεσθε , but ἐστε : he is speaking generally, of the community of consolation <em> subsisting</em> mutually between himself and the Corinthians; and it was this thought which helped to console him.</p>
