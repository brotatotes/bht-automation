<p><strong> 16.</strong> ] <strong> ἔστω δέ</strong> ‘but let us suppose the former matter dismissed:’ <strong> let the fact be granted, that I myself</strong> (emphatic) <strong> did not burden</strong> (= κατενάρκησα ) <strong> you</strong> . Then the sense breaks off, and the force of the concession goes no farther, the following words making a new hypothesis. <strong> Nevertheless, being</strong> (by habit and standing, ὑπάρχ .) <strong> crafty</strong> (unprincipled, and versatile in devices), <strong> I caught yon with guile</strong> (with some more subtle way. <em> Caught</em> you, in order to practise upon you for my own ends; but ἔλαβον is not ἐπλεονέκτησα , as Chrys., Hom. xxviii. p. 633: see ref. and note).</p>
