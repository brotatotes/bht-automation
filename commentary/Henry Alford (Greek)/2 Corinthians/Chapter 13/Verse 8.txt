<p><strong> 8.</strong> ] <strong> For we have no power against the truth</strong> (of the Gospel, as Meyer; not <em> of the facts</em> , as Chrys., al., and De Wette, which might suit κατὰ τῆς ἀλ ., but comes in very lamely with ὑπὲρ τῆς ἀλ . ‘If you walk in the truth, we shall be at one with you and so have no opportunity of shewing our power’) <strong> but</strong> (only) <strong> on behalf of</strong> (in furtherance of the cause and spread of) <strong> the truth.</strong></p>
