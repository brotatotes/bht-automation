<p><strong> 4</strong> .] The connexion with the foregoing is immediate: he had just spoken of his consciousness of apostolic success among them (which assertion would be true also of other churches which he had founded) being his worldwide recommendation. It is <em> this confidence</em> of which he here speaks. <strong> Such confidence however we possess through Christ towards God</strong> : i.e. ‘it is no vain boast, but rests on power imparted to us through Christ in regard to God, in reference to God’s work and our own account to be given to Him:’</p>
