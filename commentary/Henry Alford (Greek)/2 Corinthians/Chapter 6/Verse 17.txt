<p><strong> 17.</strong> ] The necessity of separation from the heathen enforced by another citation, <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="isa+52:11">Isaiah 52:11</span> , freely given from memory; κἀγὼ εἰσδέξ . ὑμ . being moreover substituted, from <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="eze+20:34">Ezekiel 20:34</span> , for προπορεύσεται γὰρ πρότερος ὑμῶν κύριος , κ . ὁ ἐπισυνάγων ὑμᾶς θεὸς Ἰσραήλ . The ἀκάθαρτον must be understood of the <em> pollutions of heathenism generally</em> , not of any one especial polluted thing, as meat offered to idols.</p>
