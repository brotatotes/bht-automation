<p><strong> ἵνα</strong> , κ . τ . λ .] <strong> in order that ye might in nothing be damaged by us</strong> : not ἐκβατικῶς , <em> so that ye did not …</em> , as many Commentators: the <em> divine purpose</em> of their grief is indicated; ‘God so brought it about, in order that your grief occasioned by me might have, not an injurious, but a beneficial effect.’</p>
