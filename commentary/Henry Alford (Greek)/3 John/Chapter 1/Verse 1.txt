<p><strong> 1</strong> .] ADDRESS. <strong> The elder</strong> (see prolegg. to the two Epistles) <strong> to Caius the beloved</strong> (on Caius, see prolegg. Ἐν ἀληθείᾳ ἀγαπᾷ ὁ κατὰ κύριον ἀγαπῶν ἐνδιαθέτῳ ἀγάπῃ , Œc.).</p>
