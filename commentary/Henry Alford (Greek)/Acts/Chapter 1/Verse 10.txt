<p><strong> 10. </strong> <strong> ἀτενίζ</strong> <strong> . </strong> <strong> ἦσαν</strong> ] <strong> they were gazing</strong> , <em> stood gazing</em> .
	</p>
<p><strong> εἰς τ</strong> <strong> . </strong> <strong> οὐρ</strong> <strong> .</strong> belongs to ἀτενίζ ., not to πορευομ ., see reff.
	</p>
<p><strong> πορευομένου</strong> , not πορευθέντος : implying that the cloud remained visible for some time, probably ascending with Him.
	</p>
<p><strong> παρειστήκεισαν</strong> , <em> imperf</em> . in sense, as the perf. is <em> present</em> : <strong> were standing by them</strong> .
	</p>
<p><strong> ἄνδρες</strong> ] evidently angels. See <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="lu+24:4">Luke 24:4</span> ; <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="joh+20:12">John 20:12</span> .</p>
