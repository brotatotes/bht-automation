<p><strong> 16.</strong> ] We may enquire, by what change in mind and power Peter was able, <em> before the descent of the Spirit</em> , thus authoritatively to speak of Scripture and the divine purposes? The answer will be found in the peculiar gift of the Spirit to the Apostles, <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="joh+20:21">John 20:21</span> ; <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="joh+20:23">John 20:23</span> ; where see note.
	</p>
