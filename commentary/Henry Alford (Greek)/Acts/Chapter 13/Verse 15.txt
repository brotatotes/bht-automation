<p><strong> 15</strong> .] The divisions of the law and prophets at present in use among the Jews were probably not yet arranged. Before the time of Antiochus Epiphanes, the <em> Law only</em> was read in the synagogues: but, this having been forbidden by him, the Prophets were substituted: and, when the Maccabees restored the reading of the Law, that of the prophets continued as well.
	</p>
<p><strong> ἀπέστειλαν</strong> ] Then they were not sitting in the πρωτοκαθεδρίαι , <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="mt+23:6">Matthew 23:6</span> , but somewhere among the congregation. The message was probably sent to them as having previously to this taught in the city, and thus being known to have come for that purpose. See, as illustrating our narrative, <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="lu+4:17">Luke 4:17</span> ff. and notes.</p>
