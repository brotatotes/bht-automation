<p><strong> 31</strong> .] The νῦν gives peculiar force to the sentence. <strong> Who are at this moment witnesses</strong> , <em> living</em> witnesses; q. d. ‘I am not telling you a matter of the <em> past</em> merely, but one made present to the people of the Jews ( τῷ λαῷ ) by living and autoptic testimony.’</p>
