<p><strong> 33</strong> .] The reading ἐν τῷ πρώτεῳ ψαλμῷ is explained thus: “hic psalmus qui nobis secundus est olim primus fuit, quod is qui præcedit, tanquam proœmium, numeratus non esset.” Rosenm. Arg. <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="ps+2:0">Psalms 2:0</span> St. Paul refers the prophecy in its full completion to the <em> Resurrection</em> of our Lord: similarly in <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="ro+1:4">Romans 1:4</span> , ὁρισθέντος υἱοῦ θεοῦ ἐν δυνάμει … ἑξ ἀναστάσεως νεκρῶν .</p>
