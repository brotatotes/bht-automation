<p><strong> 45. </strong> <strong> ἀντιλ</strong> <strong> . </strong> <strong> καί</strong> ] These words (see var. readd.) form a graphic repetition, passing from the <em> particular thing which they did</em> , viz. contradict the words spoken by Paul, to <em> the spirit in which they did it</em> , viz. a contradictious and blaspheming one. It is no Hebraism.</p>
