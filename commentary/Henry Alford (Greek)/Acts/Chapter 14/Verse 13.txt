<p><strong> ταύρους κ</strong> <strong> . </strong> <strong> στέμματα</strong> ] Not for ταύρους ἐστεμμένους : the garlands may have been to hang on the doors of the house where the Apostles were: or for manifold purposes connected with the sacrifice. ‘Ipsæ denique fores, ipsæ hostiæ, ipsiæ aræ, ipsi ministri et sacerdotes eorum coronantur.’ Wetst.
	</p>
<p><strong> τοὺς πυλῶνας</strong> are not the gates of the city, but the <em> doors of the outer court of the house</em> : see ch. <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="ac+12:13">Acts 12:13</span> .</p>
