<p><strong> 19. </strong> <strong> πείσαντες τοὺς ὄχλ.</strong> ] ἄπιστοι γὰρ Λυκάονες , ὡς καὶ Ἀριστοτέλης μαρτυρεῖ . Schol. on Homer, Il. δ . 88, 92.
	</p>
<p>They stoned him, not in the Jewish method, but tumultuously and in the streets, dragging him out of the city afterwards.
	</p>
<p>He refers to this stoning, <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="2co+11:25">2 Corinthians 11:25</span> , ἅπαξ ἐλιθάσθην .</p>
