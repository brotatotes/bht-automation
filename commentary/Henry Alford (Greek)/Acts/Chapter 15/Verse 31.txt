<p><strong> 31. </strong> <strong> παρακλήσει</strong> ] It does not appear, because παρεκάλεσαν follows in the sense of ‘exhorted,’ that this word need mean ‘ <em> exhortation</em> .’ There was (De W.) very little <em> exhortation</em> in the letter: and it is much more natural to render it <strong> consolation</strong> here: it was the <em> matter of their joy</em> , which surely could not be said of the <em> orders to abstain</em> given in the letter. It has been observed by Mr. Pusey that syr. renders παρεκάλεσαν <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="ac+5:32">Acts 5:32</span> , by <em> comforted</em> .</p>
