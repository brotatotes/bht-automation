<p><strong> 17.</strong> ] <strong> ἔκραζεν</strong> <strong> , used to cry out</strong> : several occasions are referred to. The recognition of Paul and his company here by the spirit is strictly analogous to that of our Lord by the dæmons, <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="mt+8:29">Matthew 8:29</span> ; <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="lu+4:34">Luke 4:34</span> ; and the same account to be given of both: viz. that the <em> evil spirit knew and confessed</em> the power of God and His Christ, whether in His own Person or that of His servants.</p>
