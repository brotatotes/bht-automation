<p><strong> ἐν τῇ ἀγορᾷ</strong> ] Strabo (x. 1) speaking of the Eretrians in Eubœa says that some suppose them to have been named ἀπὸ τῆς Ἀθήνῃσιν Ἐρετρίας , ἣ νῦν ἐστιν ἀγορά (as distinguished from the Ceramicus, which was the <em> old</em> forum). It was the space before the στοὰ ποικίλη , where the Stoics held their διαλέξεις .</p>
