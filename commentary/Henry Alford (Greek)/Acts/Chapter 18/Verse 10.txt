<p><strong> λαός ἐστί μοι πολύς</strong> ] See <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="joh+10:16">John 10:16</span> . As our Lord <em> forewarned</em> Paul in Jerusalem that <em> they would not</em> receive his testimony concerning Him, so here He <em> encourages</em> him, by a promise of much success in Corinth. The word λαός , the express title beforetime <em> of the Jews</em> , is still used now, notwithstanding <em> their secession</em> .</p>
