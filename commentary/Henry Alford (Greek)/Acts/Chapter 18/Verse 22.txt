<p><strong> 22. </strong> <strong> ἀναβάς</strong> ] <em> To Jerusalem</em> : for (1) it would be out of the question to suppose that Paul made the long detour by Cæsarea <em> only to go up into the town from the beach</em> , as supposed by most of those who omit δεῖ … Ἱερος . in <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="ac+18:21">Acts 18:21</span> , and <em> salute the disciples</em> , and (2) the expression κατέβη εἰς Ἀντ ., which suits a journey from Jerusalem (ch. Act 11:27 ), would not apply to one from Cæsarea.
