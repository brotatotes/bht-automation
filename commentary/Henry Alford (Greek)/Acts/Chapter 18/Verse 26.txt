<p><strong> 26. </strong> <strong> ἀκριβέστερον</strong> ] The former accuracy was only in <em> facts</em> : this is the still more expanded accuracy of <em> doctrine</em> . That was merely τὰ περὶ τοῦ Ἰησοῦ , as He lived and ministered on earth: this included also the promise of the Spirit, and its performance.</p>
