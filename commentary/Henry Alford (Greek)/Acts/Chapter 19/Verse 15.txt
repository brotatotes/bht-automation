<p><strong> 15.</strong> ] The narrative, from describing the nature of the attempt, passes to a single case in which it was tried, and in which (see below) two only of the brothers were apparently concerned.
	</p>
<p>No difference between γινώσκω and ἐπίσταμαι must be pressed: the two verbs are apparently used as separating Jesus and Paul, so that they do not stand together in the same category: as in E. V., <strong> Jesus I know, and Paul I know</strong> : the One being God in heaven, the other man on earth.</p>
