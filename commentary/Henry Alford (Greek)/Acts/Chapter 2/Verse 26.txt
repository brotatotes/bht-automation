<p><strong> 26.</strong> <strong> ἡ γλῶσσά μου</strong> ] Heb. כְּבוֹדִי , ‘ <em> my glory</em> :’ so in <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="ps+108:1">Psalms 108:1</span> , where our prayer-book version renders “I will give praise with the best member that I have.” Cf. also <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="ps+57:8">Psalms 57:8</span> .</p>
