<p><strong> 27.</strong> <strong> διαφθοράν</strong> ] Heb. שַׁחַת , ‘ <em> corruption</em> ,’ from שָׁחַת , corrupit, or ‘ <em> the pit</em> ,’ from שׁוּחַ , subsidere. De Wette maintains the last to be the only right rendering: but the Lexicons give both, as above, and Meyer and Stier defend the other.</p>
