<p><strong> 42 47</strong> .] DESCRIPTION OF THE LIFE AND HABITS OF THE FIRST BELIEVERS. This description <em> anticipates</em> ; embracing a period extending beyond the next chapter. This is plain from <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="ac+2:43">Acts 2:43</span> ; for the miracle related in the next chapter was evidently the first which attracted any public attention: <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="ac+2:44-45">Acts 2:44-45</span> , again, are taken up anew at the end of chap. 4, where we have a very similar description, evidently applying to the same period.</p>
