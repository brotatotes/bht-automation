<p><strong> 17. </strong> <strong> οἱ ἀδελφοί</strong> ] The Christians generally: not the Apostles and elders, as Kuin., who imagines from <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="ac+21:20-21">Acts 21:20-21</span> , that ‘cœtus non favebat Paulo.’ But (1) this is by no means implied: and (2) James and the elders are not mentioned till <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="ac+21:18">Acts 21:18</span> .</p>
