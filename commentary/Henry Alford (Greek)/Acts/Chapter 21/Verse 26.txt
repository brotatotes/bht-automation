<p><strong> 26.</strong> ] Paul himself entered into the vow with them ( <strong> σὺν αὐτοῖς ἁγν</strong> <strong> .</strong> ), and the time settled (perhaps the least that could be assigned: the Mischna requires thirty days) for the completion of the vow, i.e. the offering and shaving of their heads, was seven days. No definite time is prescribed in <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="nu+6:0">Numbers 6:0</span> , but there seven days is the time of purification <em> in case of uncleanness during the period of the vow</em> .
