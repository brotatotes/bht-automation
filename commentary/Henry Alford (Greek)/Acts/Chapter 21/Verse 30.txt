<p><strong> 30.</strong> ] The Levites shut the doors to prevent profanation by a riot, and possibly bloodshed, in the temple: hardly, as Bengel, ‘ne templi tutela uteretur Paulus:’ the right of asylum was only ( Exo 21:13-14 ) for murder unawares (Meyer). But by Act 21:14 there, and by Joab’s fleeing to the altar, <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="1ki+2:28">1 Kings 2:28</span> ff., we see that it was resorted to on other occasions.</p>
