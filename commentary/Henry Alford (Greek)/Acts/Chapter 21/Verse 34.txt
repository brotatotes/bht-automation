<p><strong> 34. </strong> <strong> παρεμβ</strong> .] The camp or barracks attached to the tower Antonia; or perhaps ‘into the tower’ itself: but the other is the more usual meaning of παρεμβ . “For a full history and description of the fortress of Antonia, see Robinson, i. pp. 431, 435; Williams, Holy City, i. 99; ii. 403 411; Howson, ii. 311.” Wordsworth.</p>
