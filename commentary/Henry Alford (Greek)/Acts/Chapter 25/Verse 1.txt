<p><span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="ac+25:1">Acts 25:1</span> .] The term ἐπαρχία is properly used of a <em> province</em> , whether imperial or senatorial (see note on ch. Act 13:7 ), but is here loosely applied to Judæa, which was only a procuratorship, attached to the <em> province</em> of Syria. So also Josephus calls Festus ἔπαρχος , Antt. xx. 8. 11; as also Albinus, ib. 9. 1.</p>
