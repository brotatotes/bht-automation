<p><strong> 17. </strong> <strong> ἐξαιρούμενός σε</strong> ] <em> delivering thee from</em> , as E. V.: not, as Kuin., al., and Conyb., ‘ <em> choosing thee out of</em> :’ see reff.
	</p>
<p><strong> τοῦ λαοῦ</strong> ] as elsewhere, <strong> the Jewish people</strong> . ‘Hic armatur contra omnes metus qui eum manebant, et simul præparatur ad crucis tolerantiam.’ Calvin.
	</p>
<p><strong> εἰς οὕς</strong> ] to <em> both</em> , the people, and the Gentiles; not the Gentiles only.</p>
