<p><strong> κόλπον</strong> <strong> … </strong> <strong> ἔχοντ</strong> <strong> . </strong> <strong> αἰγιαλόν</strong> ] a <strong> creek having a sandy beach</strong> . Some Commentators suppose that it should be αἰγιαλὸν ἔχοντα κόλπον , since every creek must have a beach: but what is meant is, a creek with a <em> smooth, sandy beach</em> , as distinguished from a rocky inlet.
	</p>
<p><strong> ἐξῶσαι</strong> ] Not, ‘ <em> to thrust in</em> ,’ as E. V., but <strong> to strand</strong> , ‘ <em> to run a-ground</em> :’ so Thucyd., ref., and more in Wetst.</p>
