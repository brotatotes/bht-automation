<p><strong> 42.</strong> ] <strong> ἵνα</strong> gives not only the <em> purpose</em> , but the substance of the βουλή . <strong> Their counsel was</strong> , <strong> to kill</strong> , &amp;c.: this it <em> was</em> , and <em> to this it tended</em> .
	</p>
<p>διαφύγοι has probably been a correction to suit ἐγένετο . But the subjunctive after the past is merely a mixture of construction of the historic past with the historic present, and is used where the scene is intended to be vividly set before the reader.</p>
