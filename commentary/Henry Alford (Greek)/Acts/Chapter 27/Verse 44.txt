<p><strong> 44. </strong> <strong> τοὺς λοιπούς</strong> ] scil. ἐπὶ τῆν γῆν ἐξιέναι .
	</p>
<p><strong> τινων τῶν ἀπὸ τ</strong> <strong> . </strong> <strong> π</strong> <strong> .</strong> ] probably, as E. V., <strong> broken pieces of the ship</strong> : some of the parts of the ship: the σανίδες being <strong> whole planks</strong> , perhaps of the decks.
	</p>
<p><strong> διασωθ</strong> <strong> . </strong> <strong> ἐπί</strong> ] may be = διας . κ . ἀφικέσθαι ἐπί , a constructio prægnans, but this need not be, as διασωθῆναι is <em> to get safe through</em> , and ἐπί is simply the direction in which the act is carried out.</p>
