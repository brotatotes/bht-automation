<p><strong> 19.</strong> ] ‘My appeal was a defensive and necessary step not an offensive one, to complain of my nation.’
	</p>
<p>The inf. aor. of the rec. would point to <em> some one definite charge</em> : <strong> κατηγορεῖν</strong> means ‘to play the accuser against my nation in any thing:’ indicating the habit.</p>
