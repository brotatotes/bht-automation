<p><strong> 35</strong> .] The words ἐπὶ τ . ἀνθρ . τούτ . may be joined either with προσέχ . ἑαυτ ., or with τί μέλ . πράσς . The latter would give the more usual construction: and the transposition of words is not unexampled in the Acts, see ch. <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="ac+1:2">Acts 1:2</span> ; <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="ac+19:4">Acts 19:4</span> .</p>
