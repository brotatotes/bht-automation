<p><strong> 29.</strong> <strong> Μαδιάμ</strong> ] So LXX, <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="ex+2:15">Exodus 2:15</span> , for מִדְיָן . Winer (Realw. ‘Midian’) supposes <em> this Madian</em> to have been a <em> nomad</em> detachment of the more settled Midianites, which at that time was encamped in the neighbourhood of Sinai and Horeb. For Jethro, Moses’ father-in-law, is not found there, in <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="ex+18:1">Exodus 18:1</span> ff., but comes to visit Moses from a distance. See also <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="nu+10:29">Numbers 10:29</span> ff.
