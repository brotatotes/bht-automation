<p><strong> 33.</strong> ] See <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="jos+5:15">Joshua 5:15</span> . Putting off the sandals was a <em> mark of reverence</em> . The priests performed all their ministrations <em> barefooted</em> . The Arabs to this day continue the practice: they always enter their mosques barefooted. Among the Pythagoreans it was a maxim, ἀνυπόδητος θῦε κ . προσκύνει , Iamblich. vita Pythag 105 (Mey.). So Juvenal, Sat. vi. 158, ‘Observant ubi festa mero pede sabbata reges.’
	</p>
<p>On the sanctity of the place, Chrys. remarks, οὐδαμοῦ ναός , κ . ὁ τόπος ἅγιος τῇ ἐπιφανείᾳ κ . ἐνεργείᾳ τοῦ χριστοῦ .</p>
