<p><strong> κώμας τ</strong> <strong> . </strong> <strong> Σαμ</strong> .] It is interesting to recall <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="lu+9:52">Luke 9:52</span> , where on their entering into a κώμην Σαμ , <em> the same John</em> wishes to call down fire from heaven, καὶ ἀναλῶσαι αὐτούς . On constr. ( εὐαγγ . w. accus.), see reff.
	</p>
<p>The gradual sowing of the seed further and further from Jerusalem is advancing: not only is this eunuch to carry it to a far distant land, but Philip is sent to a desert road, away from town or village, to seek him. The imperfects (altered in the rec., see var. readd., into aorists) are significant. They were on their way back to Jerusalem, and were evangelizing the Samaritan villages, when the angel spake (aor.) to Philip.</p>
