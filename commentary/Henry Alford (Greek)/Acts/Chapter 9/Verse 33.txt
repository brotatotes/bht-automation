<p><strong> 33. </strong> <strong> Αἰνέαν</strong> ] Whether a believer or not, does not appear; from Peter’s visit being to the <em> saints</em> , it would seem that he was: but perhaps the indefinite ἄνθρωπόν τινα may imply the contrary, as also Peter’s words, announcing a free and unexpected gift from One whom he knew not.</p>
