<p><strong> 18</strong> . <strong> ὡς ἀνῆκεν</strong> ] The verb is in the imperfect as ἔδει and χρῆν , conveying always in its form a slight degree of blame, as implying the non-realization of the duty pointed out just as when we say, ‘It was your duty to,’ &amp;c. See Winer, § 40. 3, end. The words <strong> ἐν κυρίῳ</strong> belong to ἀνῆκεν , not to ὑποτάσεσθε ; as is shewn by the parallel expression in <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="col+3:20">Colossians 3:20</span> ; was fitting, in that element of life designated by ἐν κυρίῳ .</p>
