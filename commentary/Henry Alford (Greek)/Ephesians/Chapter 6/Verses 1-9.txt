<p><strong> 22 6:9</strong> .] The Church, in her relation to Christ, comprehending and hallowing those earthly relations on which all social unity (and hers also) is founded, the Apostle proceeds to treat of the <em> three greatest of those</em> : that of <em> husband and wife</em> ( Eph 5:22-33 ), that of <em> parent and child</em> (ch. Eph 6:1-4 ), that of <em> master and servant</em> ( Eph 6:5-9 ). See this expanded by Stier, in his very long note, ii. 316 329.</p>
