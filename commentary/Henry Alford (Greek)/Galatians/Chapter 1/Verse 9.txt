<p><strong> 9</strong> .] <strong> As we said before</strong> (referring, not to Gal 1:8 as most Commentators; for the word more naturally, as in <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="2co+13:2">2 Corinthians 13:2</span> (so προείπαμεν , 1Th 4:6 ), relates to something said on a former occasion, and the plural seems here to bind it to εὐηγγελισάμεθα , but to what he had said during his presence with them: see a similar reference, ch. <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="ga+5:3">Galatians 5:3</span> ; Gal 5:21 ), <strong> I also now say again, If any one is</strong> (no longer now a supposition, but an assumption of the fact: see Hermann, ut supra; and Ellic.’s note) <strong> evangelizing you</strong> (reff.) <strong> other</strong> (with another gospel) <strong> than that which ye received</strong> (from us), <strong> let him be accursed</strong> (see above).</p>
