<p><strong> οὐ μυκτηρ</strong> <strong> .</strong> ] <strong> is not mocked</strong> : though men subjectively mock God, this mocking has no objective existence: there is no such thing as mocking of God in reality. <strong> μυκτηρίζειν</strong> λέγομεν τοὺς ἐν τῷ διαπαίζειν τινὰς τοῦτό πως τὸ μέρος ( μυκτῆρα ) ἐπισπῶντας , Etym. Mag. (cited by Ellic.) Pollux quotes the word from Lysias: in medicine it is used for bleeding at the nose (Hippocrat. p. 1240 D).
