<p><strong> 26 31</strong> .] <em> Caution</em> , arising from the mention of that day, which will be not a day of grace, but a day of judgment, <em> of the fearful peril of falling away from Christ</em> . The passage finds a close parallel in ch. <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="heb+6:4">Hebrews 6:4</span> ff., and much of what was there said will apply here.</p>
