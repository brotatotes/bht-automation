<p><strong> 26 28</strong> .] <em> Further and concluding argument for the fact of Christ being such a High Priest: that such an one was necessary for us</em> . This necessity however is not pursued into its grounds, but only asserted, and then the description of His exalted perfections gone further into, and substantiated by facts in his own history and that of the priests of the law ( Heb 7:28 ).</p>
