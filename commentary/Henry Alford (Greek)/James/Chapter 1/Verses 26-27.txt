<p><strong> 26, 27</strong> .] The Apostle is still on the command in <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="jas+1:19">James 1:19</span> . As yet he has been exemplifying the ταχὺς εἰς τὸ ἀκοῦσαι in connexion with the βραδὺς εἰς ὀργήν . From this <em> he passes to that which is again so nearly connected with it, the</em> βραδὺς εἰς τὸ λαλῆσαι .</p>
