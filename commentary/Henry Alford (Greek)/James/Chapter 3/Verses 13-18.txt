<p><strong> 13 18</strong> .] <em> Wisdom must be shewn by meekness and peaceableness, not by contentiousness</em> . This paragraph is closely connected with the subject of the chapter as enounced in <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="jas+3:1">James 3:1</span> . Where that ambition, and rivalry to be teachers, existed, there was sure to be contentiousness and every evil thing.</p>
