<p><strong> 8</strong> .] <strong> be ye also patient</strong> (as well as, after the example of, the husbandman): <strong> establish</strong> (confirm, strengthen, both which are required for patience) <strong> your hearts, because the coming of the Lord is nigh</strong> (perf.: ‘hath (already) drawn near, and is therefore at hand,’ as the perfects ἕστηκα , ἔγνωκα , &amp;c. Calvin says, “Colligendum robur ad durandum: colligi autem melius non potest, quam ex spe et quasi intuitu propinqui adventus Domini”).</p>
