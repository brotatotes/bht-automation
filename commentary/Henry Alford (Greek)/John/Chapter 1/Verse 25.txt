<p><strong> 25.</strong> ] On <strong> οὐδὲ</strong> <strong> … </strong> <strong> οὐδέ</strong> <strong> ,</strong> see note on <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="joh+1:13">John 1:13</span> . This question shews probably that they did not interpret Isa 40:3 of any herald of the Messiah. They regarded baptism as a significant token of the approach of the Messianic Kingdom, and they asked, ‘Why baptizest thou, if thou art no forerunner of the Messiah?’</p>
