<p><strong> 6.</strong> ] The Evangelist now passes to the <em> historic manifestation</em> of the Word. μετεληλυθὼς ἐπὶ τὴν ἐπιφάνειαν τοῦ υἱοῦ , τίνα ἂν εὗρεν ἀρχὴν ἑτέραν , ἢ τὰ κατὰ τὸν Ἰωάννην ; (Theodor. Mopsuest [12] in loc. p. 729, ed. Migne.) He <em> enunciates</em> briefly in these <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="joh+1:6-7">John 1:6-7</span> , what he afterwards, <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="joh+1:19-36">John 1:19-36</span> , narrates with historical detail.
	</p>
