<p><strong> 6.</strong> ] <strong> οὖν</strong> connects with <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="joh+11:4">John 11:4</span> , ‘Having then said this, although He loved, &amp;c., He abode,’ &amp;c.: μέν pointing on to ἔπειτα μ . τ . in next verse.
	</p>
<p>In all probability Lazarus <em> was dead</em> , when He spoke the words <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="joh+11:4">John 11:4</span> ; or at all events before the messenger returned.</p>
