<p><strong> 3. </strong> <strong> λίτραν</strong> ] What weight is imported, is uncertain: hardly (see ch. Joh 19:39 ) so much as a Roman pound. The word, originally Greek, was adopted into the Aramaic, and is found in the Rabbinical writings as equivalent to a mina; see Friedlieb, Archäologie der Leidensgeschichte, p. 33.
