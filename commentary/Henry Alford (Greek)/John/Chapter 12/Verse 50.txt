<p>The <strong> ἐντολὴ αὐτοῦ</strong> <em> is</em> , results in, not as a means merely, but in its accomplishment and expansion, <em> eternal life:</em> see ch. <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="joh+3:15">John 3:15</span> ; <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="joh+5:24">John 5:24</span> ; <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="joh+6:40">John 6:40</span> .
	</p>
<p>Thus all who do not believe are without excuse; because Jesus is not come, and speaks not, of Himself, but of the Father, Whose will and commandment respecting Him is, that He should be, and give, Life to all. They who reject Him, reject Life, and (ch. Joh 3:19 ) prefer darkness to Light.</p>
