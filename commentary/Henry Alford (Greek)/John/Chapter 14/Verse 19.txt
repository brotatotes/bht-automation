<p><strong> 19.</strong> ] The <em> immediate reference</em> of this θεωρεῖτε is to the forty days (see Act 10:41 ) but only as leading on to its wider and deeper reference to the spiritual life.
	</p>
<p><strong> ζῶ</strong> <strong> ,</strong> not ζήσω the principle of Life being immanent in Him.
	</p>
<p><strong> ζήσετε</strong> <strong> ,</strong> in all its fulness, including the most blessed sense of <strong> ζωή</strong> <strong> ,</strong> the Life of the Spirit, here and hereafter. See Meyer’s note.</p>
