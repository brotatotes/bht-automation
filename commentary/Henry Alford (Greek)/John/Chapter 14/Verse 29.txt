<p><strong> 29.</strong> ] <strong> εἴρηκα</strong> viz. ‘the prophecies of My Resurrection and Ascension,’ &amp;c.
	</p>
<p><strong> πιστεύσητε</strong> ] See ch. <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="joh+13:19">John 13:19</span> , where ὅτι ἐγώ εἰμι is supplied. <strong> That ye may believe,</strong> in the fullest sense of the word. “Neque enim Eum Dei Filium non et ante credebant: sed cum in Illo factum esset quod ante prædixit, fides illa quæ tunc quando illis loquebatur fuit parva, et cum moreretur pæne jam nulla, et revixit et crevit.” Aug [202] in Joann. Tract. lxxix. 1.
	</p>
