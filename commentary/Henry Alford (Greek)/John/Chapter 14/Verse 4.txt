<p><strong> 4.</strong> ] <strong> And where</strong> (whither) <strong> I go ye know the way.</strong> They might have known, and doubtless did know in some sense; but, as Lampe remarks, “interdum quis laudatur ut officii sui moneatur.” We use thus ‘ <em> you know</em> ,’ leaving to be supplied, ‘ <em> if you would give the matter thought</em> .’
	</p>
<p><strong> ὅπου</strong> <strong> ,</strong> <em> to the Father;</em> <strong> τὴν ὁδόν</strong> (in our Lord’s own case, of which <em> this</em> verse treats), <em> His death</em> .</p>
