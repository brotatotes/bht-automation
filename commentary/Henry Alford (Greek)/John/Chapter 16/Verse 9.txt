<p><strong> 9.</strong> ] And the great opening of <em> Sin</em> to the world is to shew them that its root and essence is, <em> unbelief in Christ as the Son of God</em> . UNBELIEF: for, mankind being alien from God by nature, the first step towards their recovery must be to lay hold on that only safety which He has provided for them; and that laying hold is <em> faith</em> , and the not doing it, when revealed and placed before them, is <em> sin</em> . Beforetime, it was also <em> unbelief;</em> “The fool hath said in his heart, There is <em> no God:</em> ” but now, for we can only believe as God has revealed Himself, it is unbelief in <em> Christ the Son of God</em> , the οὐ <strong> θέλετε</strong> ἔρχεσθαι πρός με : see this pointedly asserted <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="1jo+5:10-12">1 John 5:10-12</span> . Remember, this unbelief is not a mere want of historical faith, but unbelief <em> in its very root</em> , the want of a personal and living recognition of Jesus as the Lord ( 1Co 12:3 ), which, wherever the Spirit has “opened His commission” by the planting of the visible Church, is <em> the condemning sin</em> of the world. Of this He shall <em> convince</em> those who are brought out of the world, and ultimately <em> convict</em> those who remain in it and die in their sins (see Hare, Mission of the Comforter, vol. ii. note Q).</p>
