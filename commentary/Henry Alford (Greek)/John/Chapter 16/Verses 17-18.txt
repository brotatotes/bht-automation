<p><strong> 17, 18.</strong> ] The disciples are perplexed by this μικρόν , as connected with what our Lord had before asserted <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="joh+16:10">John 16:10</span> , ὑπάγω πρὸς τ . πατέρα . <em> That</em> seemed to them a long and hopeless withdrawal: how was it then to be reconciled with what he now said of a short absence? What was this <strong> μικρόν</strong> ? This connexion not being observed has led to the insertion of ὅτι ἐγὼ ὑπάγω πρ . τ . πατ . in <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="joh+16:16">John 16:16</span> .</p>
