<p><strong> 19.</strong> ] This preliminary enquiry seems to have had for its object to induce the prisoner to criminate himself, and furnish matter of accusation before the Sanhedrim.
	</p>
<p><strong> τῶν μαθ</strong> <strong> .,</strong> His party, or adherents, as the High Priest would understand His disciples to be; how many, and who they were, and with what object gathered together; and what His customary teaching of them had been. Of <em> these</em> , Jesus says nothing: compare <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="joh+18:8-9">John 18:8-9</span> . But He substitutes for them ὁ κόσμος , to which He had spoken plainly.</p>
