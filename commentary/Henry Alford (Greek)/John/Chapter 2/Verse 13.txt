<p><strong> 13.</strong> ] No data are given to determine whether the reason of the short stay at Capernaum was the near approach of the Passover.
	</p>
<p>Nothing is said of those who accompanied Jesus: but at all events, <em> His already called disciples</em> would be with Him (see <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="joh+2:22">John 2:22</span> , and ch. Joh 3:22 ), and among them in all probability the Evangelist himself: but <em> not the rest of the Twelve</em> , who were not yet called. Of this visit, the synoptic narrative records nothing.</p>
