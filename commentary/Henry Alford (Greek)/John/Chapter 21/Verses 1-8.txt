<p><strong> 1 8</strong> ] <em> The significant draught of fishes</em> . I reserve the remarks on this chapter to the end, thereby better to put the reader in possession of the evidence which I shall there gather up into one, but which will present itself as we go on. I will only state here, that whether written by John himself or not, <em> it is evidently an appendix to the Gospel, which latter has already concluded by a formal review of its contents and object at</em> ch. <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="joh+20:30-31">John 20:30-31</span> .</p>
