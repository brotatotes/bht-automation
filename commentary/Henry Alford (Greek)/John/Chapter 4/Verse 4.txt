<p><strong> 4.</strong> ] If He was already on the borders of Samaria, not far from Ænon (see note on ch. Joh 3:23 ), the direct way was through Samaria. Indeed without this assumption, we know that the Galilæans ordinarily took this way (Jos. Antt. xx. 6. 1, beginning). But there was probably design also in the journey. It could not have been mere speed ( πάντως ἔδει τοὺς ταχὺ βουλομένους ἀπελθεῖν δι ʼ ἐκείνης πορεύεσθαι , Jos. Vit. 52), since He made two days’ stay on the way.</p>
