<p><strong> 45.</strong> ] They received Him, but in accordance with the proverbial saying just recorded; not for any honour in which they themselves held Him, or value which they had for His teaching; but <em> on account of His fame in Jerusalem</em> , the metropolis, which set them the fashion in their estimate of men and things.
	</p>
<p><strong> καὶ αὐτοὶ γάρ</strong> <strong> ,</strong> inserted for those readers who might not be aware of the practice of the Galilæans to frequent the feasts at Jerusalem.</p>
