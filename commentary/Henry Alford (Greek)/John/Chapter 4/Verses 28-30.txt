<p><strong> 28 30.</strong> ] She does not mention to the men <em> His own</em> announcement of Himself, but as is most natural under such circumstances, rests the matter on the testimony likely to weigh most with them, <em> her own</em> . We often, and that unconsciously, put before another not <em> our</em> strongest, but what is likely to be <em> his</em> strongest reason. At the same time she shews how the suspicion expressed in Joh 4:25 arose in her own mind.</p>
