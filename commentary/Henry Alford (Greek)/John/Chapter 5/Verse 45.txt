<p><strong> 45.</strong> ] The work of Christ is not <strong> κατηγορεῖν</strong> <strong> ,</strong> even as He is Judge; but <strong> κρίνειν</strong> <strong> ,</strong> by the appointment of the Father. And therefore though He has said so much of the unbelief of the Jews, and charged them in the last verse with breach of the central law of God <em> He will not accuse</em> them; nay, it is not needful; for Moses, whom they disbelieved, while vainly hoping in him (see above on Joh 5:39 ), ἐπαναπαυόμενοι νόμῳ , <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="ro+2:17">Romans 2:17</span> , <em> already accused them:</em> see <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="de+31:21">Deuteronomy 31:21</span> ; <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="de+31:26">Deuteronomy 31:26</span> , and ch. <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="joh+7:19">John 7:19</span> .</p>
