<p><strong> διὰ</strong> expresses the <em> efficient cause</em> . The Father is the Fountain of all Life: the Son lives in and by the Father: and all created being generally, lives ( <em> in the lower sense</em> ) in and by Him; but he that eateth Him shall ( <em> eternally and in the highest sense</em> ) live by Him.
	</p>
<p>Joh 6:58 forms the solemn conclusion of the discourse, referring back to the Bread with which it began and to its difference from the perishable food which they had extolled: and setting forth the infinite superiority of its effects over those of that sustenance.
