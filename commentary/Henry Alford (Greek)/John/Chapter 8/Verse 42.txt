<p><strong> 42.</strong> ] ‘If you were the children of God, the <em> ethical proof</em> (as Lutbardt well calls it) of such descent would be, that you would love Me, who am κατ ʼ ἐξοχήν the Son of God, and who am come by the mission, and bearing the character, of God.’
	</p>
<p><strong> ἥκω</strong> conveys the result of <strong> ἐξῆλθον</strong> <strong> ,</strong> as Meyer; who also remarks that mere <em> sending</em> will not exhaust ἐξῆλθον , which must be taken metaphysically, of the proceeding forth of the Eternal Son from the essence of the Father.</p>
