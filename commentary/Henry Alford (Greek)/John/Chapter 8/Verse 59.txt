<p><strong> 59.</strong> ] Probably there were stones (for building) lying about in the outer court of the temple, where these words seem to have been spoken. The reason of the Jews’ doing this is given by them on a similar occasion, ch. <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="joh+10:33">John 10:33</span> , ὅτι σὺ ἄνθρωπος ὢν <strong> ποιεῖς σεαυτὸν θεόν</strong> <strong> .
	</strong></p>
<p>There does not appear to be any <em> miraculous</em> escape intended here, although certainly the assumption of one is natural under the circumstances. Jesus was probably surrounded by His disciples, and might thus hide himself (see ch. Joh 12:36 ), and go out of the temple.</p>
