Euthym [142] In the economy of God’s Providence, his suffering had its place and aim, and this was to bring out the <strong> ἔργα τ</strong> <strong> . </strong> <strong> θεοῦ</strong> in his being healed by the Redeemer (see Rom 11:11 and note).
