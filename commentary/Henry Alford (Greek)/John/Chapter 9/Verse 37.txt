<p><strong> 37.</strong> ] These words <strong> καὶ ἑώρακας αὐτ</strong> <strong> .</strong> serve to remind the man of the benefit he has received, and to awaken in him the liveliest gratitude: compare <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="lu+2:30">Luke 2:30</span> . They do not refer to a <em> former seeing</em> , when he was healed: this was the first time that he had <em> seen</em> his Benefactor.</p>
