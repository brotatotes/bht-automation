<p><strong> 8.</strong> ] <strong> θεωροῦντες</strong> belongs to <strong> τὸ πρότερον</strong> <strong> ,</strong> and thus expresses the present relatively to that time, οἳ ἦσαν τὸ πρότ . θεωροῦντες . The choice of the word θεωροῦντες implies attention and habit.
	</p>
<p>The reading τυφλός was most likely a correction of some one who thought προσαίτης did not express plainly enough the change in him. The question of identity would be much more likely to turn on whether he was really <em> the person who had sat and begged</em> (the blindness being involved in it), than on the fact of his having been blind.</p>
