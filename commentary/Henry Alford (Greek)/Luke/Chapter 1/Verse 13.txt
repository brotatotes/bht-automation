<p><strong> 13.</strong> ] He had then <em> prayed for a son</em> but as appears below, long since for he now had ceased to look for an answer to his prayer. Many Commentators (Aug [6] , Thl., Euth., Grot., &amp;c.) have thought his prayer was for the salvation of Israel by the appearance of the Messiah: but the former view appears more probable.
	</p>
