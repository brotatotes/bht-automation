<p><strong> 20.</strong> ] We must not consider this dumbness <em> solely</em> as a punishment; it was also a <em> sign</em> , as Zacharias had required. It is impossible for us to say what the degree of unbelief in Zacharias was, and therefore we can be no judges as to his being deserving of the punishment (against Strauss and the rationalists).
