<p><strong> 21.</strong> ] It was customary for the priest at the time of prayer not to remain long in the holy place, for fear the people who were without might imagine that any vengeance had been inflicted on him for some informality; as he was considered the <em> representative of the people</em> . The words <strong> ἐθαύμαζον ἐν</strong> are best taken together, <strong> wondered at,</strong> as in ref. Sir. They <em> may</em> also be taken separately, taking ἐν as ‘ <em> during:</em> ’ and so Meyer: but this is not so probable.</p>
