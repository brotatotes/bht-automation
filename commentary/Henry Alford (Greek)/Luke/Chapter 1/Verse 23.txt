<p><strong> 23. </strong> <strong> ὡς ἐπλήσ</strong> <strong> .</strong> ] The week during which his course was on duty. Mr. Greswell, by much elaborate calculation, has made it probable, but only as one out of several alternatives, that this week was Tisri 18 25, = September 30 October 7, of the <em> sixth year before the Christian era</em> (Prolegg. p. 85 sqq.).
	</p>
<p>A deaf and dumb person, we thus see, was not precluded from some of the sacerdotal ministrations.</p>
