<p><strong> 43.</strong> ] The word <strong> κυρίου</strong> <strong> ,</strong> as applied to the unborn babe, can no otherwise be explained than as uttered in the spirit of prophecy, and expressing <em> the divine nature</em> of our Lord: see especially <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="ps+110:1">Psalms 110:1</span> , from which Bleek thinks the expression is adopted.</p>
