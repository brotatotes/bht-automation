<p><strong> 60.</strong> ] There is no reason for supposing, with Theophyl., Euthym [13] , Meyer, that <em> Elisabeth</em> had had the name supernaturally intimated to her. She must necessarily have learnt it, in the course of communication by writing, from her husband.
