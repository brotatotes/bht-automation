<p><strong> 31.</strong> ] Many priests journeyed this way, for Jericho was a priestly city; this man is perhaps represented as having been up to Jerusalem in the order of his course, and returning ( <strong> κατέβαινεν</strong> ).
	</p>
<p>The Law and Prophets enjoined the act of mercy which this priest refused (see <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="ex+23:4-5">Exodus 23:4-5</span> ; <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="de+22:1-4">Deuteronomy 22:1-4</span> ; Isa 58:7 ); not, it is true, <em> literally</em> , and therefore he neglected it.
	</p>

	</p>
