<p><strong> 29.</strong> ] This is now in answer to those who sought of Him a sign from Heaven.
	</p>
<p><strong> τῶν ὄχλ</strong> <strong> . </strong> <strong> ἐπαθρ</strong> <strong> .…</strong> perhaps in expectation, as He paused in His discourse, that the sign was now about to be shewn: see notes on Matt. for the main subject.
	</p>
<p>Here we have one part of the sign of Jonas brought out, which is not touched on in Matt., viz. his <em> preaching after his resurrection</em> to the Ninevites, announcing for that would necessarily be involved in that preaching the wonderful judgment of God in bringing him there, and thus <em> making his own deliverance</em> , that he might <em> preach to them, a sign</em> to that people; which sign ( Luk 11:32 ) they received, and repented; but a greater than Jonas, shewing and preaching a greater sign by far, this generation shall reject.</p>
