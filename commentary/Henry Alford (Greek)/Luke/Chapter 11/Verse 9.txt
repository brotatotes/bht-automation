<p><strong> 9.</strong> ] What follows is in the closest connexion, and will not bear the idea that it is transferred here merely as being appropriate. The αἰτεῖν , ζητεῖν , κρούειν , all answer to the <em> features of the parable</em> .
	</p>
<p>Luk 11:10 declares to us not merely a result observable here among men, (in which sense it is <em> not universally true</em> ,) but a <em> great law of our Father’s spiritual Kingdom:</em> a clause out of the eternal covenant, which cannot be changed.</p>
