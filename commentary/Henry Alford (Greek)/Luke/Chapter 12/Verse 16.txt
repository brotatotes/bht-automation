<p><strong> 16.</strong> ] Our Lord in this parable sets before us one arrived at the very height of worldly prosperity, and that by no unfair means; ‘non limite perturbato, non spoliato paupere, non circumvento simplice.’ Aug. Serm. 178, c. 2, vol. v. It was <em> by</em> <em> God’s blessing</em> that he became thus rich, which might have been a <em> real</em> blessing, if he had known how to use it.</p>
