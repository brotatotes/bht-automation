<p><strong> 11. </strong> <strong> πν</strong> <strong> . </strong> <strong> ἀσθ</strong> <strong> .</strong> ] Her weakness was the effect of permitted power of the evil one ( Luk 13:16 ); but whether we are to find here a direct instance of <em> possession</em> , seems very doubtful. There is nothing in our Lord’s words addressed to her, to imply it: and in such cases He did not <em> lay on His hands, or touch</em> , but only in cases of sickness or bodily infirmity.
