<p><strong> 12.</strong> ] There is no reason to suppose any eminence of faith in her though we may fairly conclude that she was there with <em> some expectation</em> of a cure: see <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="lu+13:14">Luke 13:14</span> .
	</p>
<p><strong> ἀπολέλ</strong> <strong> .</strong> expresses the setting free of her muscles from the power which bound them down, and then, <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="lu+13:13">Luke 13:13</span> , the laying on of the divine hands confers upon her strength to rise and stand upright. It would be, in such a case, one thing to be loosed from the stiffening of years, and another to have strength at once conferred to stand upright.</p>
