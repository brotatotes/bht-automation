<p><strong> 25.</strong> ] A reason why this ἀγωνίζεσθαι is so important: because there will be a day when the gate will be <em> shut</em> . The figure is the usual one, of a <em> feast</em> , at which the householder entertains (in this case) the members of his family. These being assembled, he rises and shuts the door, and none are afterwards admitted.
	</p>

	</strong></p>
