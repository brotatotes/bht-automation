<p><strong> 8.</strong> ] The whole of this has, besides its plain reference, a <em> deeper one</em> , linked into it by the pregnant word <strong> γάμους</strong> <strong> ,</strong> <em> relating to the Kingdom of God</em> . Both meanings are obvious, and only one remark needed; that all that <em> false</em> humility, by which men put themselves lowest and dispraise themselves <em> of set purpose to be placed higher</em> , is, by the very nature of our Lord’s parable, <em> excluded:</em> for that is not <em> bona fide</em> ταπεινοῦν ἑαυτόν . The exaltation at the hands of the Host is not to be a <em> subjective end</em> to the guests, but will follow true humility.</p>
