<p><strong> 27.</strong> ] This is the <em> believing and trembling</em> of <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="jas+2:19">James 2:19</span> . His eyes are now opened to the truth; and no wonder that his natural sympathies are awakened for his brethren.
	</p>
<p>That a <em> lost spirit</em> should feel and express such sympathy, is not to be wondered at; the misery of such will be very much heightened by the awakened and active state of those higher faculties and feelings which selfishness and the body kept down here.</p>
