<p><strong> 37.</strong> ] <strong> ποῦ</strong> <strong> ,</strong> not ‘ <em> how?</em> ’ (Kuinoel) but literal <strong> where shall this happen?</strong> The disciples know not the universality of this which our Lord is announcing to them, and which His dark and awful saying proclaims, see note on it, <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="mt+24:28">Matthew 24:28</span> .
	</p>
<p>Observe, there is <em> not a word</em> , except so far as the greater coming includes the lesser, in all this, of the destruction of Jerusalem. The future παρουσία of the Lord is the <em> only subject:</em> and thus it is an entirely distinct discourse from that in <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="mt+24:0">Matthew 24:0</span> , or our ch. 21.</p>
