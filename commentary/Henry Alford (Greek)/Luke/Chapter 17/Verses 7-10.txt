<p><strong> 7 10.</strong> ] The connexion is, ‘Ye are <em> servants</em> of your Master; and therefore endurance is required of you, faith and trust to endure out your day’s work before you enter into your rest. Your Master will enter into His, but your time will not yet come; and all the service which you can meanwhile do Him, is but that which is your bounden duty to do, seeing that your body, soul, and spirit are His.’</p>
