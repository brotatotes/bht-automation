<p><strong> 9.</strong> ] <strong> πρός</strong> <strong> ,</strong> to, not <em> concerning:</em> it was concerning them, it is true: but this word expresses that it was spoken <em> to</em> them. The usage of πρός in Luk 18:1 is no example for the sense <em> concerning</em> , for it is not there so used of <em> persons</em> , but <em> with a neuter article and infinitive:</em> εἶπεν πρὸς αὐτοὺς παρ . is too general a phrase, to allow of any other interpretation than the ordinary one, where the context will bear it.
