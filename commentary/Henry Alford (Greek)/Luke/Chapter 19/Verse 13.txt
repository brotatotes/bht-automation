<p><strong> 13. </strong> <strong> δέκα</strong> ] See on <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="mt+25:1">Matthew 25:1</span> . The giving the μνᾶ <em> to each</em> , is a totally different thing from giving to one <em> five</em> , to another <em> two</em> , and to a third <em> one</em> talent. The sums given are here all the <em> same</em> , and all <em> very small</em> . The (Attic) mina is 1/60 of a talent, and equal to about ₤3 of our money.
	</p>
<p>In Matt. the man given his <em> whole property</em> to his servants: here he makes trial of them with these small sums ( ἐλάχιστον , see Luk 19:17 ).
	</p>
