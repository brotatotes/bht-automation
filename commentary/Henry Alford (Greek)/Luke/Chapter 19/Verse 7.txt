<p><strong> 7.</strong> ] The murmurers are Jews who were accompanying Him to Jerusalem, on the road to which Zacchæus’s house lay (see Luk 19:1 ).
	</p>
<p><strong> παρὰ ἁμ</strong> <strong> . </strong> <strong> ἀνδρί</strong> belongs to <strong> καταλῦσαι</strong> . His profession in life, and perhaps an unprincipled exercise of his power in it, had earned him this name with his fellow-countrymen. Cf. his confession in the next verse.</p>
