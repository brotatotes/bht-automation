<p><strong> 38.</strong> ] The <strong> ἀνθ</strong> ωμολ . has been understood (by Erasm., Calv., Calov., alli [31] .) to refer to <em> Symeon’s also</em> having praised God: but Winer, Meyer, and Bleek more accurately regard the prep. as pointing to the retributive nature of the offering of praise.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:3em;"> [31] alli= some cursive mss.
	</p>
<p>It was possibly at the hour of prayer; as she spoke of Him to numbers, who would at such a time be flocking to the temple.</p>
