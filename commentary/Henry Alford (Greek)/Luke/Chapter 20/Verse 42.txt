<p><strong> 42.</strong> ] On <strong> ἐν βίβλῳ ψαλμ</strong> <strong> .,</strong> Wordsw. says, “added here as conveying information necessary to Gentile readers.” This might be well, did the words occur in the Evangelist’s narrative: but surely not, when they are in a discourse of our Lord.
