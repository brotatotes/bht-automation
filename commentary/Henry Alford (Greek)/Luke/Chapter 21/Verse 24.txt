<p>The <strong> καιρ</strong> <strong> . </strong> <strong> ἐθν</strong> <strong> .</strong> (see reff.) are the <em> end of the Gentile dispensation</em> , just as the καιρός of Jerusalem was the <em> end</em> , fulfilment, <em> of the Jewish dispensation:</em> the <em> great rejection of the Lord by the Gentile world</em> , <em> answering to its type, His rejection by the Jews, being finished, the</em> <strong> καιρός</strong> <em> shall come, of which the destruction of Jerusalem was a type</em> . καιροί = καιρός : no essential difference is to be insisted on.
