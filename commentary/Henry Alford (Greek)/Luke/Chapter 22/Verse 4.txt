<p><strong> 4.</strong> ] <strong> καὶ στρατηγοῖς</strong> is peculiar to Luke: the others have merely <em> the chief priests</em> .
	</p>
<p>On <strong> στρατ</strong> <strong> .,</strong> see <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="ac+4:1">Acts 4:1</span> . The Levitical guard of the temple would be consulted, because it had been of late especially <em> in the temple</em> that our Lord had become obnoxious to them (see Luk 22:53 and ch. Luk 21:37-38 ).</p>
