<p><strong> 51.</strong> ] <strong> ἐᾶτε ἕως τούτου</strong> I understand as addressed, not to the disciples, but to the multitude, or rather <em> to those who were holding Him;</em> His hands were held, and He says, <strong> Suffer, permit me, thus far:</strong> i.e. to touch the ear of the wounded person. If this interpretation be correct, it furnishes an additional token of the truthfulness of our narrative for the previous laying hold of Jesus has not been mentioned here, but in Matthew ( Mat 26:50 ) and Mark ( Mar 14:46 ).</p>
