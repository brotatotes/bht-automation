<p><strong> 15 18.</strong> ] <em> Peculiar to Luke</em> . The desire of our Lord to eat this His last Passover may be explained from ch. <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="lu+12:50">Luke 12:50</span> : not <em> merely</em> from his depth of love for His disciples, though this formed an element in it, see Joh 13:1 sq. The <strong> γάρ</strong> in Luk 22:16 gives us the leading reason.</p>
