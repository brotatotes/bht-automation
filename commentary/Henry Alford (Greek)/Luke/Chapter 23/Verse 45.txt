<p><strong> 45.</strong> ] The words <strong> ἐσκοτ</strong> <strong> . </strong> <strong> ὁ ἥλ</strong> <strong> .</strong> are probably added to give solemnity to the preceding, assigning its reason; so that the gloss τοῦ ἡλ . ἐκλείποντος shews a right apprehension of the words. It can hardly be, as Me [116] ., that the <em> earth</em> was darkened till the ninth hour, and <em> then</em> the <em> sun</em> became dark also.
