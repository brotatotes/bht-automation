<p><strong> 26. </strong> <strong> παθεῖν καὶ εἰσελθ</strong> <strong> .</strong> ] The sufferings were the appointed way by which Christ should enter into His glory. παθεῖν καὶ εἰσελθ . = παθόντα εἰσελθ . It was not the <em> entering into His glory</em> , but the <em> suffering</em> , about which they wanted persuading.</p>
