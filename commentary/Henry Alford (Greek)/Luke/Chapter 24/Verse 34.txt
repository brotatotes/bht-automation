<p><strong> 34.</strong> ] This <em> appearance to Simon</em> (i.e. Peter the other Simon would not be thus named without explanation: see ch. <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="lu+5:3">Luke 5:3</span> ff.) is only hinted at here, but is asserted again, <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="1co+15:5">1 Corinthians 15:5</span> , in immediate connexion with that which here follows. It is not clear whether it took place before or after that on the way to Emmaus.</p>
