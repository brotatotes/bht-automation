<p><strong> 42.</strong> ] This was done to convince them further of his real corporeity. The omission of the words <strong> καὶ</strong> <strong> … </strong> <strong> κηρίου</strong> in the best MSS. is remarkable: see var. readd. It may possibly have arisen from an idea in some transcriber that this meal is the same as that in <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="joh+21:9">John 21:9</span> . The words could hardly have been an interpolation.</p>
