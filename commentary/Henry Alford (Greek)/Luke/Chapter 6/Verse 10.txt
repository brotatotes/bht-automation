<p><strong> 10.</strong> ] Mark adds μετ ʼ ὀργῆς συλλυπούμενος ἐπὶ τῇ πωρώσει τῆς καρδίας αὐτῶν one of the most striking and graphic descriptions in the Gospels.
	</p>
<p>It was thus that He bare (see Mat 8:17 ), even while on earth, our sins and infirmities. <em> Their</em> hearts were hardened, but <em> He</em> grieved for it.</p>
