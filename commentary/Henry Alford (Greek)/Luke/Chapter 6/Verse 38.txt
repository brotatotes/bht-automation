<p><strong> δώσουσιν</strong> ] The subject of this verb answers to the unexpressed agents of ἀντιμετρηθήσεται ; such agents being indefinite, and the meaning thereby rendered solemn and emphatic: see on ch. <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="lu+12:20">Luke 12:20</span> . If we are to find a nom., it should be <em> the Angels</em> , who are in this matter the ministers of the divine purposes (so Meyer).
	</p>
<p>This saying is found with a totally different import <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="mr+4:24">Mark 4:24</span> ; one of the many instances how the Lord turned about, so to speak, the Light of Truth contained in His declarations, so as to shine upon different departments of life and thought.</p>
