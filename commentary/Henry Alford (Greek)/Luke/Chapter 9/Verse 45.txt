<p><strong> 45.</strong> ] <strong> ἵνα</strong> not to be evaded by forcing it to mean ‘ <em> so that they did not</em> …,’ but to be rendered <strong> that they might not,</strong> as in Mat 1:22 alli [77] . It was the <em> divine purpose</em> , that they should not at present be aware of the full significancy of these words.
	</p>
