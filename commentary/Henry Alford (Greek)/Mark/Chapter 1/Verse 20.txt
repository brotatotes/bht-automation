<p>Now may we not venture to say that both these accounts came <em> from Peter</em> originally? Matthew’s an earlier one, taught (or given in writing perhaps) without any definite idea of making it part of a larger work; but this carefully corrected and rendered accurate, even to the omitting the name <em> Peter</em> , which though generally known, and therefore mentioned in the <em> oral</em> account, was perhaps not yet formally given, and was therefore omitted in the <em> historical</em> .</p>
