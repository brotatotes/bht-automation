<p><strong> βάπτ</strong> <strong> . </strong> <strong> μετ</strong> <strong> .,</strong> the baptism <em> symbolic of</em> (“gen. of the characteristic quality,” Meyer) <em> repentance and forgiveness</em> of the death unto sin, and new birth unto righteousness. The <em> former</em> of these only comes properly into the notion of John’s baptism, which did not confer the Holy Spirit, <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="mr+1:8">Mark 1:8</span> .</p>
