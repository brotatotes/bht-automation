<p><strong> 1. </strong> <strong> καὶ πέραν</strong> ] Our Lord retired, after His discourses to the Jews in <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="joh+10:0">John 10:0</span> and before the raising of Lazarus, to Bethany (<span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="joh+1:28">John 1:28</span> ; Joh 10:40 ) beyond Jordan, and thence made his last journey to Jerusalem; so that in the strictest sense of the words He did come <strong> into the borders of Judæa and beyond Jordan.
	</strong></p>
<p>Matt. has πέραν τ . Ἰορ . without the copula. See <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="lu+17:11">Luke 17:11</span> . Here a large portion of the sayings and doings of Jesus is omitted: cf. <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="mt+18:10">Matthew 18:10</span> ; <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="mt+19:3">Matthew 19:3</span> ; Luk 9:51 to <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="lu+18:15">Luke 18:15</span> ; <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="joh+7:1">John 7:1</span> ff.</p>
