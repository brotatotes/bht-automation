<p><strong> 13. </strong> <strong> παιδία</strong> ] Not only <em> children</em> , but as in Luke, <strong> infants</strong> ( βρέφη ): and our Lord was not to <em> teach</em> them, but only to touch, and pray over them. This simple, seemingly superstitious application of οἱ προσφέροντες (perhaps not the mothers only) the disciples, interrupted in their converse on high and important subjects, despise and reprove.</p>
