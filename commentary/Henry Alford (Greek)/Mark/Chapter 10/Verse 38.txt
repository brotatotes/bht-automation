<p><strong> 38.</strong> ] Observe the present tenses, <strong> πίνω</strong> and <strong> βαπτίζομαι</strong> <strong> .</strong> The Lord had already the cup of His suffering at His lips: was already, so to speak, sprinkled with the first drops of spray of His baptism of blood [or they may be merely official, ‘ <em> that I am to drink of and to be baptized with</em> ’].</p>
