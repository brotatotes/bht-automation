<p><strong> 52.</strong> ] In Matt. only, Jesus <em> touches</em> him. The account here and in Luke seems to correspond better with the wonderful strength of his faith. Our Lord healed <em> by a word</em> in such cases, see <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="mt+8:10-13">Matthew 8:10-13</span> ; ch. <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="mr+7:29">Mark 7:29</span> , and other places. Luke adds, δοξάζων τὸν θεόν , and that <em> all the people seeing him gave glory to God</em> : see also <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="lu+19:37">Luke 19:37</span> .</p>
