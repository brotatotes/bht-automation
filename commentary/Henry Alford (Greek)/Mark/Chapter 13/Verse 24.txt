<p><strong> 24.</strong> ] <strong> ἀλλά</strong> is to be noticed. It is more than the simple ‘ <em> but</em> :’ and is best rendered by <strong> nevertheless:</strong> qu. d., though I have forewarned you of all things, yet some of those shall be so terrible as to astound even the best prepared among you.
	</p>
