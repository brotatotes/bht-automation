<p><strong> 28.</strong> ] <strong> αὐτῆς</strong> <strong> ,</strong> emphatic, when <em> her</em> branch … conveying an <em> a fortiori</em> in the application. If in so humble an example as the fig-tree you discern the nearness of a season, much rather should you in these sure and awful signs discern the approach of the end.</p>
