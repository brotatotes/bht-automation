<p><strong> 20.</strong> ] This description of the traitor here again does not seem to <em> designate one</em> especially, nor to describe an action at that moment proceeding, but, as before, pathetically to describe the near relation of the betrayer to the Betrayed. Now however the relation pointed out is still closer than before it is that of one dipping in the same dish one of those nearest and most trusted.</p>
