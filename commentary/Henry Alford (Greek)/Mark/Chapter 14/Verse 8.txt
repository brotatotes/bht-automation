Mar 12:44 πάντα ὅσα εἶχεν ἔβαλεν . We have also the expression <strong> προέλαβεν μυρίσαι</strong> <strong> ,</strong> shewing, as I have observed on Matt., that the act was one of <em> prospective</em> love, grounded on the deepest apprehension of the reality of our Lord’s announcement of His approaching death.</p>
