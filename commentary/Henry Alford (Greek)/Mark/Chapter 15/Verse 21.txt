There is a Rufus saluted <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="ro+16:13">Romans 16:13</span> . The words <strong> ἐρχόμ</strong> <strong> . </strong> <strong> ἀπ</strong> <strong> ʼ </strong> <strong> ἀγρ</strong> <strong> .</strong> determine nothing as to its being a working day or otherwise, any more than οἱ παραπορευόμενοι , <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="mt+27:39">Matthew 27:39</span> : nothing is said as to the <em> distance</em> from whence he came.</p>
