<p><strong> 29.</strong> ] <strong> οὐά</strong> <strong> ,</strong> an expression of <em> reproach:</em> sometimes one of admiration and respect, as in Dio Cassius, lxiii. 20, where the Romans shout after Nero, on his triumphal entry after his victories in the Grecian games, ὀλυμπιονίκα , οὐά , πυθιονίκα , οὐὰ αὔγουστε , αὔγουστε .</p>
