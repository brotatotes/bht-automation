<p><strong> 26 29.</strong> ] PARABLE OF THE SEED GROWING WE KNOW NOT HOW. <em> Peculiar to Mark</em> . By Commentators of the Straussian school it is strangely supposed to be the same as the parable of the tares, <em> with the tares left out</em> . If so, a wonderful and most instructive parable has arisen out of the fragments of the other, in which the idea is <em> a totally different one</em> . It is, the <em> growth</em> of the once-deposited seed by the combination of its own development with the genial power of the earth, all of course under the creative hand of God, but independent of human care and anxiety during this time of growth.</p>
