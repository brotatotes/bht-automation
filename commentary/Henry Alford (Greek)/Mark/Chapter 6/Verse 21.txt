<p><strong> μεγιστᾶνες</strong> <strong> ,</strong> a Macedonian word, which came into use at the Alexandrine conquest. See Lobeck on Phrynichus, p. 197. He adduces the nom. form μεγιστᾶνος from Anna Comnena, xi. 324 c.</p>
