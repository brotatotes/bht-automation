<p><strong> 52.</strong> ] Peculiar to Mark.
	</p>
<p><strong> οὐ γὰρ συν</strong> <strong> .</strong> ] They did not, from the miracle which they had seen, infer the power of the Lord over nature.
	</p>
<p><strong> ἐπί</strong> <strong> ,</strong> hardly as Kuinoel, alli [24] ., <em> post</em> , but rather denoting, as usual, close superposition of the preceding on the following: there was no intelligent comprehension <em> founded on</em> the miracle of the loaves.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:3em;"> [24] alli= some cursive mss.</p>
