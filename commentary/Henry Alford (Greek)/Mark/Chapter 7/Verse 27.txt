<p><strong> 27.</strong> ] <strong> ἄφες πρῶτον</strong> <strong> .…</strong> ] This important addition in Mark sets forth the whole ground on which the present refusal rested. The Jews were <em> first</em> to have the Gospel offered to them, for their acceptance or rejection; it was <em> not yet time</em> for the Gentiles.</p>
