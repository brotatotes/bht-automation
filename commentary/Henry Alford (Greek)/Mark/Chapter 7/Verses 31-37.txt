<p><strong> 31 37.</strong> ] HEALING OF A DEAF AND DUMB PERSON. <em> Peculiar to Mark</em> .
	</p>
<p>A miracle which serves a most important purpose; that of clearly distinguishing between the cases of the <em> possessed</em> and the <em> merely diseased</em> or <em> deformed</em> . This man was what we call ‘deaf and dumb;’ the union of which maladies is often brought about by the inability of him who never has heard sounds to utter them plainly: or, as here apparently, by some accompanying physical infirmity of the organs of speech.</p>
