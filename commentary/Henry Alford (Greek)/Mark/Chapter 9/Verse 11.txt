<p><strong> 11.</strong> ] The <strong> ὅτι</strong> may be merely recitantis, ‘ <em> they asked him, saying</em> ( <em> that</em> ) <em> the Scribes say, that Elias must first come</em> :’ leaving <strong> ἐπηρώτων</strong> to find its application in the difficulty thus suggested by them. But it is better to take it in the unusual sense (undoubted there) of <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="mr+9:28">Mark 9:28</span> [see Moulton on Winer, p. 208, note 4]: see further on in this note.</p>
