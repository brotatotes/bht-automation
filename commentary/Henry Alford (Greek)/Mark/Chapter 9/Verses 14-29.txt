<p><strong> 14 29.</strong> ] HEALING OF A POSSESSED LUNATIC. <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="mt+17:14-21">Matthew 17:14-21</span> .<span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="lu+9:37-42">Luke 9:37-42</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="lu+9:37-42">Luke 9:37-42</span> . The account of Mark is by far the most copious: and here, which is very rarely the case in the official life of our Lord, the three accounts appear to have been <em> originally different and independent</em> . The descent from the mountain was on the <em> day following</em> the transfiguration, <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="lu+9:37">Luke 9:37</span> .</p>
