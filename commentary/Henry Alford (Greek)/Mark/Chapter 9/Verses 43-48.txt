<p><strong> 43 48.</strong> ] These solemn repetitions of former declarations (see <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="mt+5:29">Matthew 5:29</span> ; Mat 18:8-9 ) are by no means to be regarded as arbitrary insertions by this or that Evangelist, but as the truth of what was uttered by our Lord: see Prolegomena.
	</p>
<p><span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="mr+9:44">Mark 9:44</span> <strong> ; <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="mr+9:46">Mark 9:46</span></strong> <strong> ; </strong> Mar 9:48 are only in Mark; they are cited from Isaiah (see reff.), where the prophecy is of the <em> carcases of those who have transgressed against the Lord</em> . This triple repetition gives sublimity, and leaves no doubt of the discourse having been <em> verbatim</em> thus uttered. See note on <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="mt+5:22">Matthew 5:22</span> .</p>
