<p><strong> 37.</strong> ] Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="de+33:9">Deuteronomy 33:9</span> , and <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="ex+32:26-29">Exodus 32:26-29</span> , to which passages this verse is a reference.
