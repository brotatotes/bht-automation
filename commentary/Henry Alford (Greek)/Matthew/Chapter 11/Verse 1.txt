<p>Mat 10:1 <strong> to <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="mt+11:1">Matthew 11:1</span></strong> <strong> .]</strong> MISSION OF THE TWELVE APOSTLES. <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="mr+6:7-13">Mark 6:7-13</span> ; <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="lu+9:1-6">Luke 9:1-6</span> , for the <em> sending out</em> of the Apostles: <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="mr+3:13-19">Mark 3:13-19</span> ; <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="lu+6:13-16">Luke 6:13-16</span> , for their <em> names</em> . On the characteristic differences between this discourse and that delivered to the Seventy (<span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="lu+10:1">Luke 10:1</span> ff.) see notes there.
	</p>
<p>Notice, that this is not the <em> choosing</em> , but merely the <em> mission</em> of the twelve. The choosing had taken place some time before, but is not any where distinctly detailed by the Evangelists.</p>
