<p><strong> 30.</strong> ] <strong> χρηστός</strong> <strong> , easy,</strong> ‘not exacting;’ answering to ‘kind,’ spoken of persons, <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="lu+6:35">Luke 6:35</span> . See <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="1jo+5:3">1 John 5:3</span> . Owing to the conflict with evil ever incident to our corrupt nature even under grace, the ἀνάπαυσις which Christ gives is yet to be viewed as a yoke and a burden, seen on this its painful side, of conflict and sorrow: but it is a <em> light yoke;</em> the inner rest in the soul giving a peace which passeth understanding, and bearing it up against all. See <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="2co+4:16">2 Corinthians 4:16</span> .</p>
