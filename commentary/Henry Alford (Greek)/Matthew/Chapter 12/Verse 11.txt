<p><strong> 11.</strong> ] The construction of this verse is involved: there is a double question, as in ch. <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="mt+7:9">Matthew 7:9</span> .
	</p>
<p>Our Lord evidently asks this as being a thing allowed and done at the time when He spoke: but subsequently (perhaps, suggests Stier, on account of these words of Christ), it was forbidden in the Gemara; and it was only permitted to <em> lay planks for the beast to come out</em> .</p>
