<p><strong> 5.</strong> ] The priests were ordered to offer double offerings on the Sabbath ( Num 28:9-10 ), and to place fresh ( <em> hot</em> , and therefore baked that day) shewbread. In performing these commands they must commit many of what the Pharisees would call profanations of the Sabbath. So that, as Stier ( Mat 2:4 ), not only does the sacred <em> history</em> furnish examples of exception to the law of the Sabbath from <em> necessity</em> , but the <em> Law itself ordains</em> work to be done on the Sabbath as a <em> duty</em> .</p>
