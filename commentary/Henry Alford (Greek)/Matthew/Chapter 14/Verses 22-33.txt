<p><strong> 22 33.</strong> ] JESUS WALKS ON THE SEA. <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="mr+6:45-52">Mark 6:45-52</span> . (Luke omits this incident.) <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="joh+6:16-21">John 6:16-21</span> . The conviction of the people after the foregoing miracle was, that Jesus was the Messiah; and their disposition, to take Him by force, and make Him a king. See <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="joh+6:14-15">John 6:14-15</span> . For this reason he constrained His disciples to leave Him, because they were but too anxious to second this wish of the multitude; and <em> their</em> dismissal was therefore an important step towards the other.</p>
