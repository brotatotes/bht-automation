<p><strong> 16.</strong> ] The saying in Mat 15:11 was spoken for the multitude, who were exhorted ἀκούετε κ . συνίετε : much more then ought the disciples to have understood it.
	</p>
<p><strong> ἀκμήν</strong> = <em> adhuc</em> is a later Greek word: Phrynichus (p. 123, ed. Lobeck) says that Xenophon uses it once (ref.): but this is not in the sense of ἔτι , but ἄρτι , ‘ <em> even now</em> ,’ ‘ <em> in articulo</em> ;’ see Lobeck’s note, where he gives more examples.</p>
