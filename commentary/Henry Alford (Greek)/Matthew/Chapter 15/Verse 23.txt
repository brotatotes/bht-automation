<p><strong> 23.</strong> ] The reason alleged by the disciples must be coupled with our Lord’s unwillingness to be known, stated by Mark ( Mar 7:24 ), and means, ‘ <em> she will draw the attention of all upon us</em> .’ The word <strong> ἀπόλυσον</strong> does not necessarily imply <em> granting</em> her request, nor the contrary; but simply <strong> dismiss her,</strong> leaving the method to our Lord Himself.</p>
