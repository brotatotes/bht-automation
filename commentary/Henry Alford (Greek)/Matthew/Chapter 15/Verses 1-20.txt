<p><strong> 1 20.</strong> ] DISCOURSE CONCERING EATING WITH UNWASHED HANDS. <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="mr+7:1-23">Mark 7:1-23</span> . From Mark it appears that these Scribes and Pharisees had come <em> expressly</em> from Jerusalem <em> to watch our Lord:</em> most probably after that Passover which was nigh at the time of feeding the five thousand, <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="joh+6:4">John 6:4</span> .</p>
