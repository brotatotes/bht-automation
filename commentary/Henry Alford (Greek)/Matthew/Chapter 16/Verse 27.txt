<p><strong> 27.</strong> ] A further revelation of this important chapter respecting the Son of Man. He is to be JUDGE OF ALL and, as in ch. <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="mt+13:41">Matthew 13:41</span> , is to appear with <em> His</em> angels, and in the glory of His Father the δόξα ἣν δέδωκάς μοι , <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="joh+17:22">John 17:22</span> . Mark and Luke place here, not this declaration, but that of our ch. <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="mt+10:33">Matthew 10:33</span> . Our Lord doubtless joined the two. Compare ch. <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="mt+24:30">Matthew 24:30</span> ; <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="mt+25:31">Matthew 25:31</span> .
	</p>
