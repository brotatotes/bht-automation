<p><strong> 5.</strong> ] This crossing of the lake was not the voyage to Magadan mentioned in ch. <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="mt+15:39">Matthew 15:39</span> , for after the dialogue with the Pharisees, Mark adds ( Mar 8:13 ), πάλιν ἐμβὰς ἀπῆλθεν εἰς τὸ πέραν .
	</p>
<p><strong> ἐπελάθ</strong> <strong> .</strong> ] not for a pluperfect: <strong> After they had come to the other side, they forgot to take bread;</strong> viz. <em> on their land journey further</em> . This is also to be understood in Mark ( Mar 8:14 ), who states their having only one loaf in the ship, not to shew that they <em> had</em> forgotten to take bread before starting, but as a reason why they should have provided some on landing.</p>
