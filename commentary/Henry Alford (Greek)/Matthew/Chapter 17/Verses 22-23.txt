<p><strong> 22, 23.</strong> ] OUR LORD’S SECOND ANNOUNCEMENT OF HIS DEATH AND RESURRECTION. <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="mr+9:30-32">Mark 9:30-32</span> .<span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="lu+9:43-45">Luke 9:43-45</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="lu+9:43-45">Luke 9:43-45</span> . This followed immediately after the miracle ( Mar 9:30 ); our Lord went privately through Galilee; ἐδίδασκεν <strong> γὰρ</strong> κ . τ . λ .: the imparting of this knowledge more accurately to His disciples, which He had begun to do in the last chapter, was the reason for His privacy. For more particulars, see <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="lu+9:45">Luke 9:45</span> ; <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="mr+9:32">Mark 9:32</span> .</p>
