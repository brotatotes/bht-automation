<p><strong> 7.</strong> ] See <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="1co+11:19">1 Corinthians 11:19</span> . Stier suggests that Judas, who took offence at the anointing in Bethany, may have been on other occasions the man by whom the offence came, and so this may have been said with special reference to him. Still its <em> general</em> import is undeniable and plain. See also <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="ac+2:23">Acts 2:23</span> .</p>
