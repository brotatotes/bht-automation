<p><strong> ἐπὶ τὰ ὄρη</strong> belongs to ἀφείς , not to πορευθ . See var. read. The preposition of motion, ἐπί , gives the idea of the wandering and scattering of the flock over the mountains. If we join the words to πορευθείς , we give them an unmeaning emphasis, besides destroying the elegance of the sentence.</p>
