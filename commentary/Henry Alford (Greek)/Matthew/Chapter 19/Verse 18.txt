<p><strong> 18.</strong> ] De Wette observes well, that our Lord gives this enumeration of the commandments to <em> bring out the self-righteous spirit</em> of the young man, which He before saw. He only mentions those of the second table, having in <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="mt+19:17">Matthew 19:17</span> , in His declaration respecting ἀγαθός , included those of the first. Mark has the addition of μὴ ἀποστερήσῃς , representing probably the tenth commandment.</p>
