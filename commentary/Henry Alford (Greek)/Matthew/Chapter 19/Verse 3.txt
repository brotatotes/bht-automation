<p><strong> 3.</strong> ] This was a question of dispute between the rival Rabbinical schools of Hillel and Shammai; the former asserting the right of arbitrary divorce, from <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="de+24:1">Deuteronomy 24:1</span> , the other denying it except in case of adultery. It was also, says De Wette, a delicate question in the place where our Lord now was, in the dominions of Herod Antipas.
