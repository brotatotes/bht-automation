<p><strong> 43.</strong> ] Our Lord here <em> returns to the parable</em> , and more plainly than ever before announces to them their rejection by God. The ἀμπελών is now ἡ βασ . τ . θ . The <strong> ἔθνος</strong> here spoken of is not the Gentiles in general, but <em> the Church of the truly faithful</em> , the ἔθνος ἅγιον , λαὸς εἰς περιποίησιν of <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="1pe+2:9">1 Peter 2:9</span> ; see <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="ac+15:14">Acts 15:14</span> .</p>
