<p><strong> 7.</strong> ] The occurrence of this verse before the opening of the Feast to the Gentiles has perplexed some interpreters: but it is strictly exact: for although the Gospel was preached to the Gentiles forty years before the destruction of Jerusalem, yet the final rejection of the Jews and the <em> substitution of the Gentiles</em> did not take place till that event.
