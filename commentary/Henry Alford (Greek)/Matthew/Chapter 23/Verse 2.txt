<p><strong> 2.</strong> ] <strong> Moses’ seat</strong> is the office of judge and lawgiver of the people: see <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="ex+2:13-25">Exodus 2:13-25</span> ; <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="de+17:9-13">Deuteronomy 17:9-13</span> . Our Lord says, ‘In so far as the Pharisees and Scribes enforce the law and precepts of Moses, obey them: but imitate not their conduct.’
	</p>
<p><strong> ἐκάθισαν</strong> must not be pressed too strongly, as conveying blame, ‘ <em> have seated themselves</em> ;’ it is merely stated here as a <em> matter of fact</em> . <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="mt+23:8">Matthew 23:8</span> ; Mat 23:10 however apply to their <em> leadership</em> as well as their faults; and declare that <em> among Christians</em> there are to be <em> none sitting on the seat of Christ</em> .</p>
