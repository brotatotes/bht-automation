<p><strong> 13.</strong> ] The <em> primary</em> meaning of this seems to be, that whosoever remained faithful till the destruction of Jerusalem, should be preserved from it. No Christian, that we know of, perished in the siege or after it: see below. But it has <em> ulterior</em> meanings, according to which τέλος will signify, to an individual, the <em> day of his death</em> (see Rev 2:10 ), his martyrdom, as in the case of some of those here addressed, to the Church, <em> endurance in the faith to the end of all things</em> . See <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="lu+21:19">Luke 21:19</span> , and note.</p>
