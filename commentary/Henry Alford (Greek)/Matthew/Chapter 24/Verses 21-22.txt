<p>Our Lord still has in view the prophecy of Daniel (ch. Mat 12:1 ), and this citation clearly shews the <em> intermediate</em> fulfilment, by the destruction of Jerusalem, of that which is yet future in its <em> final</em> fulfilment: for Daniel is speaking of the end of all things. Then only will these words be accomplished in their full sense: although Josephus (but he only in a figure of rhetoric) has expressed himself in nearly the same language (B. J. proœm. § 4): τὰ γοῦν πάντων ἀπ ʼ αἰῶνος ἀτυχήματα πρὸς τὰ Ἰουδαίων ἡττᾶσθαί μοι δοκεῖ κατὰ σύγκρισιν .</p>
