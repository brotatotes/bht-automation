<p><strong> 42 44.</strong> ] Our Lord here resumes the tone of direct exhortation with which He commenced. To the secure and careless He will come as a thief in the night: to His own, as their Lord. See <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="ob+1:5">Obadiah 1:5</span> ; <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="re+3:3">Revelation 3:3</span> ; Rev 16:15 ; <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="1th+5:1-10">1 Thessalonians 5:1-10</span> , where the idea is expanded at length. Compare Mat 24:7 there with our <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="mt+24:49">Matthew 24:49</span> , and on the distinction between those who are of the day, and those who are of the night, see notes, there.</p>
