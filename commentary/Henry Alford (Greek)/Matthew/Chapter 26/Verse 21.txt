<p><strong> 21.</strong> ] This announcement is common to Matt., Mark, and John. In the part of the events of the supper <em> which relates to Judas</em> , St. Luke <em> is deficient</em> , giving no further report of them than <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="mt+26:21-23">Matthew 26:21-23</span> . The whole minute detail is given by St. John, who bore a considerable part in it.</p>
