<p><strong> 22.</strong> ] In the accounts of Luke and John, this enquiry is made πρὸς ἑαυτούς or εἰς ἀλλήλους . The real enquiry <em> from the Lord was made by John himself, owing to a sign from Peter</em> . This part of John’s narrative stands in the highest position for accuracy of detail, and the facts related in it are evidently the ground of the other accounts.</p>
