<p><strong> 30.</strong> ] The <strong> ὕμνος</strong> was in all probability the last part of the Hallel, or great Hallel, which consisted of <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="ps+115:1-18">Psalms 115:1-18</span> ; <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="ps+116:1-19">Psalms 116:1-19</span> ; <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="ps+117:1-2">Psalms 117:1-2</span> ; <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="ps+118:1-29">Psalms 118:1-29</span> ; the former part (Ps. 113. 114.) having been sung during the meal. It is unlikely that this took place <em> after</em> the solemn prayer in <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="joh+17:1-26">John 17:1-26</span> .
