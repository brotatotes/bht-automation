<p><strong> 35.</strong> ] This <strong> ἂν δέῃ</strong> again appears to have the precision of a repeated asseveration. Mark has the stronger expression ἐκ περισσοῦ ἔλεγεν , which even more clearly indicates that the συναποθανεῖν was not now first said. The rest said it, but not so earnestly perhaps; at all events, Peter’s confidence cast theirs into the shade.</p>
