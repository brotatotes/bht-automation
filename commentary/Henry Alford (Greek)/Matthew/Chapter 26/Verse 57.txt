<p><strong> 57. </strong> <strong> Καϊάφαν τὸν ἀρχ</strong> <strong> .</strong> ] He was ἀρχιερεὺς τοῦ ἐνιαυτοῦ ἐκείνου , Annas having been deposed, and since then the High-priests having been frequently changed by the Roman governors.
	</p>
<p><strong> ὅπου οἱ γρ</strong> <strong> .</strong> ] Probably they had assembled by a preconcerted design, expecting their prisoner. This was <em> a</em> meeting of the Sanhedrim, but not the regular assembly, which condemned him and handed Him over to Pilate. That took place <em> in the morning</em> , <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="lu+22:66-71">Luke 22:66-71</span> (where see note).</p>
