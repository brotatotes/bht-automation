<p><strong> 11.</strong> ] Before this Pilate had come out and demanded the cause of his being delivered up; the Jews not entering the Prætorium.
	</p>
<p>The primary accusation against Him seems to have been that He ἔλεγεν ἑαυτὸν χριστὸν βασιλέα εἶναι . This is presupposed in the enquiry of this verse.
	</p>
<p><strong> σὺ λέγεις</strong> is not to be rendered as a <em> doubtful answer</em> much less with Theophylact, as meaning, ‘ <em> Thou sayest it, not I</em> :’ but as a <em> strong affirmative</em> . See above on ch. <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="mt+26:64">Matthew 26:64</span> .</p>
