<p><strong> 25.</strong> ] <strong> αἶμα</strong> λέγουσι τὴν τοῦ αἵματος καταδίκην , Euthym [181] : but more probably with a much wider reference as the <em> adherence of blood to the hands of a murderer</em> is an idea not bearing any necessary reference to <em> punishment</em> , only to <em> guilt</em> .
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:3em;"> [181] Euthymius Zigabenus, 1116</p>
