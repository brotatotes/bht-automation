	</p>
<p><strong> ἐπ</strong> <strong> ʼ </strong> <strong> αὐτόν</strong> ] <strong> to Him</strong> <em> to make sport with Him</em> . This happened in the guard-room of the cohort: and the narrative of it we may well believe may have come from the centurion or others (see Mat 27:54 ), who were afterwards deeply impressed at the crucifixion.</p>
