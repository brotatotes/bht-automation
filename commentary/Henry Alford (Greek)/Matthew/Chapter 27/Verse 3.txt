<p><strong> 3.</strong> ] Observe it was <strong> τὰ τρ</strong> <strong> . </strong> <strong> ἀργ</strong> <strong> .</strong> which he brought back clearly <em> the price</em> of the Lord’s betrayal, not <em> earnest-money</em> merely; for by this time, nay when he delivered his Prisoner at the house of Annas, he would have in that case received the <em> rest</em> .
