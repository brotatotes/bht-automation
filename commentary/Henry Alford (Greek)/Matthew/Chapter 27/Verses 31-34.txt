Joh 19:16-17 . The four accounts are still essentially and remarkably distinct. Matthew’s and Mark’s are <em> from the same source</em> , but varied in expression, and in detail; Luke’s and John’s stand <em> each alone</em> ; Luke’s being the fullest, and giving us the deeply interesting address to the daughters of Jerusalem.</p>
