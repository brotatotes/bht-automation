<p><strong> 51 56.</strong> ] SIGNS FOLLOWING HIS DEATH. <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="mr+15:38-41">Mark 15:38-41</span> .<span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="lu+23:47-49">Luke 23:47-49</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="lu+23:47-49">Luke 23:47-49</span> . The three narratives are essentially distinct. That of Luke is more general giving only the <em> sense</em> of the centurion’s words twice using the indefinite πάντες and not specifying the women. The whole is omitted by John.</p>
