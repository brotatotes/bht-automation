<p><strong> 13 17.</strong> ] JESUS HIMSELF BAPTIZED BY HIM. <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="mr+1:9-11">Mark 1:9-11</span> .<span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="lu+3:21-22">Luke 3:21-22</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="lu+3:21-22">Luke 3:21-22</span> . It does not appear exactly <em> when the baptism of our Lord took place</em> . If the comparative age of the Baptist is taken into account, we should suppose it to have been about six months after this latter began his ministry. But this is no sure guide. The <em> place</em> was <em> Bethany</em> (the older reading), <em> beyond Jordan;</em> <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="joh+1:28">John 1:28</span> .</p>
