<p><strong> 36. </strong> <strong> οὐ δύνασαι μίαν τρ</strong> <strong> . </strong> <strong> λ</strong> <strong> . </strong> <strong> π</strong> <strong> . </strong> <strong> ἢ μ</strong> <strong> .</strong> ] Thou hast no control over the appearance of grey hairs on thy head thy head is not thine own; thou swearest then by a creature of God, whose destinies and changes are in God’s hand; so that <em> every oath is an appeal to God</em> . And, indeed, men generally regard it as such now, even unconsciously.</p>
