<p><strong> 17.</strong> ] i.e. ‘ <em> appear as usual:</em> ’ ‘seem to men the same as if thou wert not fasting.’ It has been observed that this precept applies only to voluntary and private fasts, (such as are mentioned <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="lu+18:12">Luke 18:12</span> ,) not to public and enjoined ones. But this distinction does not seem to be necessary; the one might afford just as much occasion for ostentation as the other.</p>
