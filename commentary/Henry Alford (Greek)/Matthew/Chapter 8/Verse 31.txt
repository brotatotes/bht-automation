<p><strong> 31. </strong> <strong> ἀπόστειλον ἡμ</strong> <strong> .</strong> ] St. Mark and St. Luke give, as the ground of this request, that they might not be sent <em> out of the land = into the abyss</em> , i.e. out of their permitted residence on earth to βάσανος πρὸ καιροῦ in the ἄβυσσος .
