<p><strong> 25.</strong> ] <strong> ἐκρ</strong> <strong> . </strong> <strong> τῆς χ</strong> <strong> . </strong> <strong> αὐ</strong> <strong> .</strong> is common to the three Evangelists. From Luke we learn that our Lord said ἡ παῖς , ἔγειρε : from Mark we have the words He actually uttered, ταλιθὰ κοῦμ : from both we learn that our Lord only took with him Peter, James, and John, and the father and mother of the maiden, that she was twelve years old, and that our Lord commanded that something should be given her to eat. She was an <em> only</em> daughter, <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="lu+8:42">Luke 8:42</span> .</p>
