<p><strong> 36. </strong> <strong> τοὺς ὄχλους</strong> ] Wherever He went, in all the cities.
	</p>
<p><strong> ἐσκυλμένοι</strong> ] ‘ <em> Vexati</em> ,’ <strong> harassed,</strong> <strong> plagued,</strong> viz. literally, with weariness in following Him; or spiritually, with the tyranny of the Scribes and Pharisees, their φορτία βαρέα , ch. <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="mt+23:4">Matthew 23:4</span> .
	</p>
<p><strong> ἐριμμένοι</strong> ] ‘ <em> Temere projecti,’ ‘abjecti,’ ‘neglecti</em> ,’ as sheep would be who had wandered from their pasture. The context shews that our Lord’s compassion was excited by their being without competent spiritual leaders and teachers.</p>
