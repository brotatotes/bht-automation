<p><strong> 8. </strong> <strong> τοῖς ἀνθρώποις</strong> ] Not plur. for sing. ‘ <em> to a man</em> ,’ nor ‘ <em> for the benefit of men;</em> ’ but <strong> to mankind.</strong> They regarded this wonder-working as something by God granted to men to mankind; and without supposing that <em> they</em> had before them the full meaning of their words, those words were true in the very highest sense. See <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="joh+17:8">John 17:8</span> . In Mark they say, ὅτι οὕτως οὐδέποτε εἴδαμεν : in Luke, ὅτι εἴδομεν παράδοξα σήμερον .</p>
