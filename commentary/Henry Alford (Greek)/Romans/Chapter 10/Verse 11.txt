<p><strong> 11</strong> .] <strong> For</strong> (proof of the former part of Rom 10:10 ) <strong> the Scripture saith, Every one who believeth on Him shall not be ashamed</strong> . πᾶς is neither in the LXX nor the Heb., but is implied in the indefinite participle. The Apostle seems to use it here as taking up παντὶ τῷ πιστεύοντι , <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="ro+10:4">Romans 10:4</span> . See ch. <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="ro+9:33">Romans 9:33</span> .</p>
