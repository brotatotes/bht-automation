<p><strong> 18</strong> .] The εἰ δυνατόν , as well remarked by Thol. and De Wette, is <em> objective only</em> not ‘ <em> if you can</em> ,’ but <strong> if it be possible if</strong> <em> others will allow it</em> . And this is further defined by τὸ ἐξ ὑμῶν : all YOUR <em> part is to be peace</em> : whether you actually live peaceably or not, will depend then solely on how <em> others</em> behave towards <em> you</em> .</p>
