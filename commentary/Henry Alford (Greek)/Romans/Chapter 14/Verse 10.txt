<p><strong> 10</strong> .] He returns to the duty of abstaining, the <em> weak</em> , from <em> judging</em> his stronger brother; the <em> strong</em> , from <em> despising</em> the weaker. It seems probable that χριστοῦ has been substituted for θεοῦ in the later MSS. from <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="2co+5:10">2 Corinthians 5:10</span> . The fact of Origen <em> once</em> citing it, decides nothing, in the presence of the expression βήματος τοῦ χριστοῦ in 2 Cor.</p>
