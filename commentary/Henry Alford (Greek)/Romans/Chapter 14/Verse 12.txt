<p><strong> 12.</strong> ] The stress is on <strong> περὶ ἑαυτοῦ</strong> : and the next verse refers back to it, laying the emphasis on ἀλλήλους . ‘Seeing that our account to God will be of <em> each man’s own self</em> , let us take heed lest by judging <em> one another</em> ( <strong> κρίνομεν</strong> here in the general sense of ‘pass judgment on,’ including both the ἐξουθενεῖν of the strong and the κρίνειν of the weak) we <em> incur the guilt</em> of ἀπολλύειν one another.’</p>
