<p><strong> 7.</strong> ] <strong> Wherefore</strong> (on which account, viz. that the wish of the last verse may be accomplished) <strong> receive</strong> (see ch. Rom 14:1 ) <strong> one another, as Christ also received you, with a view to God’s glory</strong> (that this is the meaning of εἰς δόξαν τοῦ θεοῦ , appears by <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="ro+15:9">Romans 15:9</span> , τὰ δὲ ἔθνη ὑπὲρ ἐλέους <strong> δοξάσαι τὸν θεόν</strong> ).
	</p>
<p>The Apostle does not expressly name <em> Jewish and Gentile converts</em> as those to whom he addresses this exhortation, but it is evident from the next verse that it is so.</p>
