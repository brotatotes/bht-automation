	</p>
<p><strong> νόμον</strong> , not τὸν νόμον , <strong> πράσσῃς</strong> , because the latter would import <em> the perfect fulfilment of the whole law</em> : whereas the supposition is of acting according to the law, <strong> doing the law.</strong> παραβάτης νόμου here, not τοῦ νόμου , the παραβάτης νόμου , like ἀκροατὴς - νόμου and ποιητὴς - νόμου , <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="ro+2:13">Romans 2:13</span> , being a designation generally of a <em> law-breaker</em> , as <em> those</em> of a <em> law-hearer</em> and <em> law-fulfiller</em> .
	</p>
<p><strong> ἀκροβ</strong> <strong> . </strong> <strong> γέγ</strong> <strong> .</strong> ] <em> counts for nothing</em> : the Jewish transgressor is no better off than the Gentile transgressor.</p>
