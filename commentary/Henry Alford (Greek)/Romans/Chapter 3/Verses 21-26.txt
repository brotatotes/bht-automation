<p><strong> 21 26.</strong> ] <em> The Apostle resumes the declaration</em> of ch. <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="ro+1:17">Romans 1:17</span> (having proved that man has no righteousness of his own resulting from the observance of God’s law): viz. <em> that God’s righteousness is revealed by Christ, whose atoning Death is, consistently with God’s justice, sufficient for the pardon of sin to those who believe in Him</em> .</p>
