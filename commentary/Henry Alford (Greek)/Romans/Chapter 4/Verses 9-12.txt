<p><strong> 9 12.</strong> ] <em> This declaration of blessedness applies to circumcised and uncircumcised alike. For Abraham himself was thus Justified when in</em> UNCIRCUMCISION, <em> and was then pronounced the father of the faithful, uncircumcised as well as circumcised</em> .
	</p>
<p><strong> μακαρισμός</strong> of course includes the <em> fact, on account of which</em> the congratulation is pronounced, the <em> justification itself</em> .</p>
