<p><strong> 8.</strong> ] <strong> Now</strong> (continuing the train of argument) <strong> if we died with Christ, we believe that we shall also</strong> (the future as in <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="ro+6:5">Romans 6:5</span> , because the life with Him though here begun, is not here completed: and the πιστεύομεν used more of <em> dogmatic belief</em> , than of <em> trust</em> , though the latter meaning is not altogether absent) <strong> live with Him.</strong></p>
