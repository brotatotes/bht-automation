<b> 11.</b>] We cannot fill up them [<b>which are of the house</b>]<b> of Chloe</b> (simply<b> them of Chloe</b> in the original), not knowing whether they were<i> sons or servants</i>, or other members of her family. Nor can we say whether Chloe was an<i> inhabitant of Corinth, or some Christian woman</i> known to the Corinthians<i> elsewhere</i>,<i> or an Ephesian</i>, having friends who had been in Corinth.
