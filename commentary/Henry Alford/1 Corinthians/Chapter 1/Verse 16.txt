<p><strong> 16.</strong> ] He subsequently recollects having baptized Stephanas and his family (see ch. <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="1co+16:15">1 Corinthians 16:15</span> ; 1Co 16:17 ), perhaps from information derived <em> from Stephanas himself</em> , who was with him: and he leaves an opening for any others whom he may possibly have baptized and have forgotten it. The last clause is important as against those who maintain the <em> absolute omniscience</em> of the inspired writers on <em> every topic which they handle</em> .</p>
