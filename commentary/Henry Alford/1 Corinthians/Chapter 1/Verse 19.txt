<b> 19.]</b> For (continuation of reason for not preaching in wisdom of speech: because it was prophesied that such wisdom should be brought to nought by God)<b> it is written, &c.</b><br><br> The citation is after the Septuagint, with the exception of “<i>I will destroy</i>,” for “<i>I will hide</i>.” The Hebrew is ‘the wisdom of the wise shall perish, and the prudence of the prudent shall disappear.’ But as Calvin truly says, “the wisdom perishes because the Lord destroys it; the prudence disappears because it is blotted out and annihilated by God.”
