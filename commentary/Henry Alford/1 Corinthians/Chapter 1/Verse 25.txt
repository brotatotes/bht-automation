<p><strong> 25.</strong> ] <strong> Because</strong> (reason why Christ (crucified) is the power and wisdom of God) <strong> the foolishness of God</strong> (that act of God which men think foolish) <strong> is wiser than men</strong> (surpasses in wisdom, not only <em> all which they call</em> by that name, but <em> men</em> , all possible wisdom of mankind); <strong> and the weakness of God</strong> (that act of God which men think weak) <strong> is stronger than men</strong> (not only surpasses in might all which <em> they think powerful</em> , but <em> men themselves</em> , all human might whatsoever. For the construction of the genitives, see reff.). The latter clause introduces a fresh thought, the way for which however has been prepared by δύναμις , 1Co 1:18 ; <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="1co+1:24">1 Corinthians 1:24</span> . The Jews required a proof of <em> divine Might</em> : we give them <em> Christ crucified</em> , which is to them a thing ἀσθενές : but this ἀσθενὲς τοῦ θεοῦ is stronger than <em> men</em> .</p>
