 4.  τ  .   θεῷ μου ] so in reff. Rom. Phil.
	 πάντοτε ] expanded in Php 1:4 into πάντοτε ἐν πάσῃ δεήσει μου .
	The ἡ χάρις ἡ δοθεῖσα = τὰ χαρίσματα τὰ δοθέντα (see below on 1Co 1:7 ) a metonymy which has passed so completely into our common parlance, as to be almost lost sight of as such. ‘  Grace ’ is properly  in God : the  gifts of grace in us, given by that grace.
	 ἐν ] not, as Chrys., Theophyl., Œcum., for διὰ , [nor =  by as E. V.,] but as usually in this connexion,  in Christ , i.e.  to you as members of Christ . So also below.
