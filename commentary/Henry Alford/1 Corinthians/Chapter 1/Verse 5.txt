<p><strong> 5. </strong> <strong> ἐν παντί</strong> ] general: particularized by ἐν παντὶ λόγῳ κ . πάσῃ γνώσει , <strong> in all teaching and all knowledge</strong> . <strong> λόγος</strong> (obj.), <em> the truth preached</em> . <strong> γνῶσις</strong> (subj.), <em> the truth apprehended</em> . They were rich in the <em> preaching</em> of the word, had among them able preachers, and rich in the <em> apprehension</em> of the word, were themselves intelligent hearers. See <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="2co+8:7">2 Corinthians 8:7</span> , where to these are added πίστις , σπουδή , and ἀγάπη .</p>
