<b> 7.</b>]<b> so that ye come not behind</b> (others)<b> in any gift [of grace]</b>;<b>—gift [of grace]</b> here has its widest sense,<i> of that which is the effect of grace</i>,—not meaning ‘spiritual gifts,’ in the narrower sense, as in ch. xii. 4. This is plain from the whole strain of the pas-sage, which dwells not on outward gifts, but on the inward graces of the Christian life.
