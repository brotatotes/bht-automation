<b> 2.] were baptized:</b> literally,<b> baptized themselves unto Moses</b>; entered by the act of such immersion into a solemn covenant with God, and became His church under the law as given by Moses, God’s servant,—just as we Christians by our baptism are bound in a solemn covenant with God, and enter His church under the Gospel as brought in by Christ, God’s eternal Son; see Heb. iii. 5, 6.
