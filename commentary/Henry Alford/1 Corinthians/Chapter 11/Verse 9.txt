<p><strong> 9.</strong> ] <strong> For also</strong> ( <em> parallel</em> with 1Co 11:8 <em> another</em> reason: not <em> subordinate</em> to it, as Meyer, who renders ἐκ in <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="1co+11:8">1 Corinthians 11:8</span> , ‘dependent on,’ and regards this verse as giving the reason) <strong> the man was not created</strong> (emphasis on ἐκτίσθη , as before on ἐστιν ) <strong> on account of the woman, &amp;c.</strong> In this verse, besides the <em> manner</em> of creation, <strong> ἐκ</strong> τοῦ ἀνδρός , the <em> occasion</em> of creation, <strong> διὰ</strong> τὸν ἄνδρα , is insisted on; see <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="ge+2:18">Genesis 2:18</span> ff.</p>
