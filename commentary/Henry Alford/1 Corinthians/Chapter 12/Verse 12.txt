<p><strong> 12.</strong> ] The <em> organic unity</em> of the various members in one body, is predicated also of CHRIST, i.e. <em> the Church as united in Him</em> , see ch. <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="1co+6:15">1 Corinthians 6:15</span> . The γάρ confirms the preceding ἓν κ . τὸ αὐτὸ πνεῦμα , by an analogy. By the repetition, τὸ σῶμα , … τοῦ σώματος …, σῶμα , the unity of the members as an organic whole is more strongly set forth.</p>
