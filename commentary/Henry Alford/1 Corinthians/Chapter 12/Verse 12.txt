<b> 12.</b>] The<i> organic unity</i> of the various members in one body, is predicated also of CHRIST, i.e.<i> the church as united in Him</i>, see ch. vi. 15. The word<b> for</b> confirms the preceding “<i>one</i><i> and the selfsame Spirit</i>,” by an analogy. By the repetition,— “the body”.... “<i>of the body</i>”.... “<i>one body,</i>” the unity of the members as an organic whole is more strongly set forth.
