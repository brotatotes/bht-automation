<b> 29. miracles</b>] The words [<i>workers of</i>], supplied in the English text, have no representative in the original. The Apostle has above placed the concrete,<i> apostles, prophets, teachers,</i> in apposition with<i> miraculous powers,</i> and<i> gifts of healings</i>; and now proceeds with the same arrangement till he comes to<i> gifts of healings</i>, which being too palpably unpredicable of persons, gives rise to the change of construction,<b> have</b><i> all gifts of healings.</i>
