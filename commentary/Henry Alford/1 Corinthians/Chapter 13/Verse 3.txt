<b> 3.</b>] “The true and most significant sense is ‘though I dole away in mouthfuls all my property or estates.’ Who that has witnessed the almsgiving in a Catholic monastery, or the court of a Spanish or Sicilian bishop’s or archbishop’s palace, where immense revenues are syringed away in farthings to herds of beggars, but must feel the force of the Apostle’s half-satirical expression?” MS. note by Coleridge, quoted by Stanley.
