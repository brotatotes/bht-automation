<p><strong> 17.</strong> ] <strong> καλῶς</strong> is not ironical, but concessive: it is not the act of <em> thanksgiving in a tongue</em> that the Apostle blames, for that is <em> of itself good</em> , being dictated by the Spirit: but the doing it <em> not to the edification of others</em> .
	</p>
