	 δύο ἢ τρεῖς , viz.  at one assemblings ; not  together ; this is plainly prohibited, 1 Corinthians 14:30 . There is no τὸ πλεῖστον as in the other case, because he does not wish to seem as if he were limiting this most edifying of the gifts.
	 οἱ ἄλλοι , scil. προφῆται , or perhaps, any person possessing the gift of διακρίσεις πνευμάτων , mentioned ch. 1Co 12:10 in immediate connexion with προφητεία . Such would exercise that gift, to determine  whether the spirit was of God : see ch. 1 Corinthians 12:3 ; 1 John 4:1-3 .
