	 ἐκκλησίαν ] [i.e. the assembled Christians: see note on ch. 1Co 11:18 ] not, as Meyer,  a congregation , but = τὴν ἐκκλησίαν : the art. being often omitted when a noun in government has an emphatic place before the verb: accordingly in 1 Corinthians 14:5 , it is ἡ ἐκκλ . which is edified.
