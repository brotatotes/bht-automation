<b> 11.</b>] He resumes the subject after the digression respecting himself:—<b>it matters not whether it were I or they</b> (the other Apostles)—SUCH<b> is the purport of our preaching</b>—SUCH<b> was your belief:—so, after this manner,</b> viz.,<i> that Christ died, was buried, and rose again</i>, as verses 3, 4.
