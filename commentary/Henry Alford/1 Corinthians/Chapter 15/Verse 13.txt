<b> 13.</b>]<b> But</b> is the argumentative particle frequent in mathematical demonstrations.<br><br><b> then neither is Christ risen</b>] This inference depends on the maxim, “that if the<i> genus</i> disappear, the<i> species</i> goes with it;” the Resurrection of Christ being an<i> instance</i> of the<i> rule</i>, that dead<i> men</i> rise; inasmuch as<i> He is man</i>, this is enlarged on, vv. 20–22.
