<b> 21.</b>] MAN<i> the bringer-in both of death and life: explanation</i> (not proof)<i> of Christ being the</i> “firstfruits of them that sleep:” and (1)<i> in that</i><i> He is</i> MAN: it being necessary that the firstfruit should be<i> as</i> the lump. The verity lying at the root of this verse is, that<i> by</i> MAN ONLY can<i> general effects pervading the whole human race</i> be introduced.
