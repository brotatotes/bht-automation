<b> 31.</b>] To<b> die day by day</b> is a strong expression for to be daily in sight of death and expecting it. See 2 Cor. iv. 11.—This he strengthens by an asseveration, grounded on his boast of them as his work in Christ: not that this is immediately or proximately at stake in the matter, but much as we should say, “As I love you, it is true.” He would not think of deceiving those of whom he boasted before God in connexion with Christ.
