<b> 49.</b>] The difference between the two readings (see margin) is occasioned, as in Rom. v. 1 (where see note), by the confusion of the long and short<i> o</i> in Greek. An exhortation would certainly here be entirely out of place; and the confusion of the two letters is so common, that it may he doubted whether we have a right to call this a various reading at all.
