<p><strong> 55</strong> .] TRIUMPHANT EXCLAMATION <em> of the Apostle realizing in his mind that glorious time</em> : expressed nearly in the terms of the prophetic announcement of Hosea, ποῦ ἡ δίκη σου , θάνατε ; ποῦ τὸ κέντρον σου , ᾅδη ;
	</p>
<p>The figure of death as a <em> venomous beast</em> is natural, from the <em> serpent</em> , <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="ge+3:0">Genesis 3:0</span> <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="nu+21:0">Numbers 21:0</span> .
	</p>
<p>The souls in Hades being freed by the resurrection, Death’s victory is gone: sin being abolished by the change of the animal body (the source of sin) to the spiritual, his sting is powerless. For a discussion of the quotation, see Stanley’s note.</p>
