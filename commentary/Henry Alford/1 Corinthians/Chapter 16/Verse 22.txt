<b> 22.</b>] He adds, as in Col. iv. 18; Eph. vi. 24, some exhortation, or solemn sentence,<i> in his own hand</i>, as having especial weight. On the distinction between the two words used for “<i>to love,</i>” see notes on John xxi. 15. The negation here of the feeling of personal affection, “has no love in his heart for,” is worthy of note, as connected with the curse which follows.
