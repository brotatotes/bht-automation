 10, 11 .]  Recommendation of Timothy to their good reception and offices . He had preceded Paul ( Act 19:22 ) in the journey to Macedonia. From  ἐὰν ἔλθῃ , it would appear to have been  probable , but not quite certain, that he would visit them. In ch. 1 Corinthians 4:17 , he is described as sent on  for that purpose : so that the ἐάν may merely refer to the uncertainties of the journey.
