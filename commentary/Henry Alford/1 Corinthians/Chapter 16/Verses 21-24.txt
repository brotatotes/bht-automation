<p><strong> 21 24</strong> .] <em> Autograph conclusion</em> . <strong> ὁ ἀσπασμός</strong> is the <em> final greeting</em> , which, according to ref. 2 Thess., was <em> always in his own hand</em> , the rest having been written (see Rom 16:22 ) by an amanuensis.
	</p>
<p><strong> Παύλου</strong> is in apposition with ἐμοῦ implied in ἐμῇ , as Il. ρ . 226, ὑμέτερον δὲ ἑκάστου θυμὸν ἀέξω : ἐμὸς τοῦ ἀθλίου βίος , and the like. See Kühner, § 499. 4.</p>
