<p><strong> 6 16.</strong> ] <em> Yet the Apostles spoke wisdom among the perfect, but of a kind higher than the wisdom of this world; a wisdom revealed from God by the Spirit, only intelligible by the spiritual man, and not by the unspiritual</em> ( ψυχικός ). The Apostle rejects the imputation, that the Gospel and its preaching is <em> inconsistent</em> with <em> wisdom</em> , rightly understood: nay, shews that the wisdom of the Gospel is of a far higher order than that of the wise in this world, and far above their comprehension.</p>
