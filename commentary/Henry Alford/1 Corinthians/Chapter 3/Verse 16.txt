<b> 16.]</b> The foregoing figures, with the occasion to which they referred, are now dropped, and the<b> building of God</b> recalled, to do further service. This<i> building</i> is now, as in Mal. iii. 1, and as indeed by implication in the foregoing verses, the<i> temple</i> of God (the stress on the word<i> temple</i>, not on “God”), the<i> habitation of His Spirit</i>.
