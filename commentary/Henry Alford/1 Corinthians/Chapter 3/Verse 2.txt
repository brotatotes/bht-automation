<p><strong> οὔπω γὰρ ἐδύνασθε</strong> ] Either, <strong> for ye were not yet able</strong> (scil. βρῶμα ἐσθίειν ), or, <strong> for ye were not yet strong</strong> , δύναμαι being used absolutely, as in Demosth. 1187. 8, δυνάμενος τῷ τε πράττειν κ . τῷ εἰπεῖν , and 484. 25, τῶν πολιτευομένων τινὲς δυνηθέντες , and see other reff. in Meyer. In the former case, the ellipsis is harsh: the latter meaning seems preferable, though not found elsewhere in the N. T.
