 5 15. ]  He takes occasion, by example of himself and Apollos, to explain to them the true place and office of Christian teachers: that they are in themselves nothing ( 1Co 3:5-8 ),  but work for God ( 1Co 3:9-10 ),  each in his peculiar department (1 Corinthians 3:10 ; cf. 1Co 3:6 ),  each requiring serious care as to the manner of his working, seeing that a searching trial of its worth will be made in the day of the Lord ( 1Co 3:10-15 ).
