<p><strong> 18.</strong> ] <strong> ὡς μὴ ἐρχομένου</strong> forms <em> one</em> idea, and the δέ is in consequence placed after it all: so Thucyd. i. 6, ἐν τοῖς πρῶτοι δὲ Ἀθηναῖοι : Isocr. περὶ εἰρ ., p. 160, ὅτι ἂν τύχῃ δὲ γενησόμενον . Hartung, Partikellehre, i. 190.
	</p>
<p><strong> ὡς</strong> expresses the assumption in their minds: the present part. ἐρχομένου refers to their saying οὐκ ἔρχ <strong> εται</strong> , as Meyer.</p>
