<p><strong> γνώσομαι</strong> <strong> ] I will inform myself of not the words of those who are puffed up</strong> ( <em> those</em> I care not for), <strong> but their power</strong> : whether they are really mighty in the Spirit, or not. This general reference of δύν . must be kept, and not narrowed, as Chrys., Theophyl., to [the] <em> power of working miracles</em> : or “quantum apud vos sua scientia et doctrina quam jactant profecerint,” Est.; or <em> virtuous lives</em> (Theodoret, al.), or <em> energy in the work of the gospel</em> (Meyer): he leaves it general and indefinite.</p>
