<p><strong> 14 21</strong> .] <em> Conclusion of this part of the Epistle: in what spirit the has written these words of blame: viz. in a spirit of admonition, as their father in the faith, whom they ought to imitate. To this end he sent Timothy to remind them of his ways of teaching, would soon, however, come himself, in mildness, or to punish, as the case might require</em> .</p>
