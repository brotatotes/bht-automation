<b> 6.]</b> ‘How inconsistent with your harbouring such an one, appear your high flown conceits of yourselves!’<br><br><b> Your glorying</b>: literally,<b> your matter of glorying. Are you not aware that a little leaven imparts a character to the whole lump?</b> That this is the meaning, and not, ‘that a little leaven<i> will</i>, if not purged out, leaven the whole lump,’ is manifest from the point in hand, viz. the inconsistency sistency of their<i> boasting</i>: which would not appear by their<i> danger of corruption</i><i> hereafter</i>, but by their<i> character</i> being<i> actually lost</i>. One of them was a fornicator of a fearfully depraved kind, tolerated and harbored: by this fact, the<i> character of the whole was tainted</i>.
