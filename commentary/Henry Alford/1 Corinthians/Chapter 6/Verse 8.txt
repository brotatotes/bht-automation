The account of this emphatic ὑμεῖς is to be found in an ellipsis after ἀποστερεῖσθε to the effect, ‘as our Lord commanded us His disciples,’ or ‘as it behoves the followers of Christ.’ Then ὑμεῖς comes in contrast: YOU  on the contrary ( ἀλλά , see above 1Co 6:6 )  do wrong, and defraud, and that (your)  brethren.
