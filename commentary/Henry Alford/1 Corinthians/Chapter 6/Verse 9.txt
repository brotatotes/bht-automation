<b> 9.]</b> ‘<i>Ye commit wrong</i>:’ this looks as if you had forgotten the rigid seclusion from the kingdom of God of<i> all wrong-doers</i> of every kind; see Gal. v. 21.<br><br><b> Be not deceived]</b> This caution would be most salutary and needful in a dissolute place like Corinth. It is similarly, and with an express reference to evil communications, ch. xv. The mention of<b> fornicators</b> refers back to ch. v., and is taken up again, verses 12 ff.<br><br><b> drunkards</b>, see on ch. v. 11.
