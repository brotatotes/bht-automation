<p><strong> 12.</strong> ] <strong> τοῖς λοιποῖς</strong> , <strong> the rest</strong> , perhaps in respect of their <em> letter of enquiry</em> , the <em> only ones not yet dealt with</em> . At all events, the meaning is plain, being those who are involved in mixed marriages with unbelievers.
	</p>
<p><strong> ἐγώ</strong> <strong> , </strong> <strong> οὐχ ὁ κύρ</strong> <strong> .</strong> ] <strong> I</strong> , i.e. I Paul, in my apostolic office, under the authority of the Holy Spirit (see above on 1Co 7:10 ), <strong> not the Lord</strong> , i.e. not Christ by any direct command spoken by Him: it was a question with which HE <em> did not deal</em> , in His recorded discourses. In the right arrangement of the words (txt) the stress is not on ἐγώ , but on λέγω : <strong> But to the rest I say (I, not the Lord)</strong> .
	</p>
<p><strong> συν</strong> ευδοκεῖ presupposes <em> his own</em> wish to continue united.
	</p>
