<b> 18.] Was any one called [being] circumcised</b>, i.e. at the time of his conversion.<br><br><b> become uncircumcised]</b> By a surgical operation; see 1 Macc. i. 15. The practice usually was adopted by those who wished to appear like the Gentiles, and to cast off their ancient faith and habits. Among the Christians a strong anti-Judaistic feeling might lead to it.
