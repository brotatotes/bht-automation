<p><strong> 9.</strong> ] <strong> but if they are incontinent</strong> … οὐκ must be joined not with εἰ , which would require μή , but with the verb. So reff. and Soph. Aj. 1131, εἰ τοὺς θανόντας οὐκ ἐᾷς θάπτειν παρών , ‘ <em> vetas</em> .’ See other examples in Hartung, Partikellehre, ii. 122 f.
	</p>
<p>ἐγκρατεύω is said by Lobeck, ad Phryn. p. 44, not to be found except in the LXX and N. T. But both Phrynichus and Thom. Mag. say ἀκρατεύεσθαι μηδαμῶς εἴπῃς , ἀλλὰ οὐκ ἐγκρατεύεσθαι . See in Wetst.
