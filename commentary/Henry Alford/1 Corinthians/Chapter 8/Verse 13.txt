<p><strong> 13</strong> .] <em> Fervid expression of his own resolution consequent on these considerations, by way of an example to them</em> .
	</p>
<p><strong> βρῶμα</strong> , <strong> food</strong> , i.e. <em> any</em> article of food, as <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="1co+8:8">1 Corinthians 8:8</span> ; purposely indefinite here; ‘if such a matter as food.…,’ but presently particularized.
	</p>
<p><strong> οὐ μὴ φάγω</strong> , strong future, <strong> I surely will not eat</strong> ; ‘there is no chance that I eat.’
	</p>
<p><strong> κρέα</strong> ] ‘Quo certius vitarem carnem idolo immolatam, <em> toto genere</em> carnium abstinerem.’ Bengel.
	</p>
<p><strong> σκανδαλίσω</strong> ] <strong> be the means of offending</strong> ; “commutatur persona: modo dixit <em> si cibus offendit</em> .” Bengel. “Non autem hoc dicit quod hoc aliquo casu opus sit, sed ut ostendat multo graviora quam de quibus hic agitur sustinenda pro proximorum salute.” Grot.</p>
