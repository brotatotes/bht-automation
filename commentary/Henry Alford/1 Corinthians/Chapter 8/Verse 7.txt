By  ἕως ἄρτι , it is shewn that these ἀσθενεῖς must have belonged to the  Gentile part of the Corinthian church: to those who had  once , before their conversion, held these idols to be veritable gods. Had they been  Jewish converts, it would not have been συνείδησις τοῦ εἰδώλου which would have troubled them, but apparent violation of the Mosaic law.
