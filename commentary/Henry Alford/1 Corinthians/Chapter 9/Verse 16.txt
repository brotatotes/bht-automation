<p><strong> 16</strong> ff.] The reason why he made so much of this <em> materies gloriandi:</em> viz. that his <em> mission itself</em> gave him no advantage this way, being an office <em> entrusted to him</em> , and for which he was solemnly accountable: but in this thing only had he an <em> advantage</em> so as to be able to boast of it, that he preached the gospel <em> without charge</em> .
	</p>
