 16 ff.] The reason why he made so much of<i> this matter of boasting</i>, viz. that<i> his mission</i> itself gave him no advantage this way, being an office<i> entrusted</i> to him, and for which he was solemnly accountable: but in this thing only had he an<i> advantage</i> to be able to boast of it, that he preached the gospel<i> without charge</i>. The English reader must beware not to ion “<i>preach the gospel</i>,” as if it made a distinction between preaching “the gospel” and preaching<i> something else</i>: it simply represents the word “<i>evangelize</i>,” i.e. perform the work of a Christian missionary.
