<b> 20</b>.]<b> unto the Jews I became as a Jew]</b> See examples, Acts xvi. 3; xxi. 26. The<i> Jews</i> here are not<i> Jewish converts</i>, who would be already<i> won</i> in the sense of this passage.<br><br><b> to them that are under the law....]</b> These again are not Jewish converts (see above); nor<i> proselytes</i>, who would not be thus<i> distinguished</i> from other Jews, but are much the same as the last-mentioned, the<i> Jews</i>, only to the number of these the Apostle<i> did not belong</i>, not being himself under the law, whereas he was nationally a Jew<i>.</i>
