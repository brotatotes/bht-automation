<p><strong> 21</strong> .] And besides this argument from common sense, there is another most powerful one, which the Apostle here adds. “Quomodo diligis eum, cujus odisti præceptum?” as Aug [73] <strong> And this commandment we have from Him</strong> (God: not, Christ: see below), <strong> that he who loveth God, love also his brother</strong> (where have we this commandment? In the great summary of the law, “Thou shalt love the Lord thy God with all thy heart, … and thy neighbour as thyself,” so often cited by our Lord; see Mat 22:37-39 ).
