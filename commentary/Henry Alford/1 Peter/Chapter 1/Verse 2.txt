The allusion is to Exod. xxiv. 8, where the covenant was inaugurated by sprinkling the blood on the people. This was the only occasion on which the blood was thus sprinkled on<i> persons</i>: for on the great day of atonement, only the sacred vessels were thus sprinkled: So also in Heb. ix. 13.
