<b>moreover</b> (brings up a new particular, belonging to a different sphere of conduct)<b> let him turn away from</b> (in act, that is)<b> evil, and do good; let him seek peace, and pursue it</b> (because it is not always to be found, and when not immediately found, may require diligent pursuit: compare Heb. xii. 14, and St. Paul’s command, Rom. xii. 18, The ancient gloss is good: “let him search for peace as a thing hidden, and pursue it as a thing fugitive”).
