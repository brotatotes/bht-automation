<b> 10.</b>]<b> Ye are witnesses</b>, of the outward appearance.<br><br><b> God</b>, of the heart.<br><br><b> holiness</b> is more a quality having respect to God:<b> justice</b>, to men. This distinction, perhaps precarious where the words occur separately, or seem to require no very precise application, is requisite here where both divine and human testimony is appealed to.<br><br><b> toward you that believe</b>] The former verse having referred to<i> external</i> occupation, in which he must have consorted with<i> unbelievers</i>, he here narrows the circle, to speak of his behaviour among the brethren them-selves.
