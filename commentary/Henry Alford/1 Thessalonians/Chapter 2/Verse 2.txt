This word reproduces the feeling with which Paul and Silas opened their ministry among them.<b></b><br><br><b> of God</b> is expressed for solemnity, to add to the weight of their<i> entering in</i>.<br><br><b> in</b> (amidst)<b> much conflict</b>, viz. under outward circumstances conflicting much with our work: and therefore that work could be no<i> empty thing</i>, which was thus maintained.
