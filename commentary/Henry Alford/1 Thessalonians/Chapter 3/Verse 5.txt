<b> 5.</b>]<b> For this cause</b>, because tribulation had verily begun among you ( “<i>as it came to pass</i>”).<br><br> The words<b> I also</b> seem to convey a delicate hint that Timothy also was anxious respecting them: or it may have the same reference as “<i>we also</i>,” ch. ii. 13,—viz. to the other Christians who had heard of their tribulation.
