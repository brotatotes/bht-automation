<p><strong> 7</strong> .] <strong> διὰ τοῦτο</strong> , viz. on account of what has just been mentioned, from ἄρτι …; <strong> τοῦτο</strong> combining the whole of the good news in one.
	</p>
<p><strong> ἐφ</strong> <strong> ʼ </strong> <strong> ὑμῖν</strong> , <strong> with reference to you</strong> : as we say, <strong> over you</strong> . <em> You</em> were the object of our consolation: the faith which you shewed was the means whereby that object was applied to our minds.
	</p>
