<p><strong> 20</strong> .] On <strong> προφητείας</strong> , see <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="1co+12:10">1 Corinthians 12:10</span> , note. They were liable to be despised in comparison with the more evidently miraculous gift of tongues: and hence in <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="1co+14:5">1 Corinthians 14:5</span> , &amp;c., he takes pains to shew that prophecy was in reality the greater gift.</p>
