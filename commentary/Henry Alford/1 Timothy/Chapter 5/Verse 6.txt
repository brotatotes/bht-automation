<b> 6.</b>] Contrast to the character just described: and that certainly with a view to point out that this kind of widow is no object for the charity of the Church, as not being at all a partaker of the life unto God.<br><br><b> is given to dissipation</b>] The Greek word which I have thus rendered signifies to live riotously or retchlessly.<br><br><b> is dead while she liveth</b>] while alive in the flesh, has no real life in the Spirit: see ref.—and Matt. viii. 22: Eph. v. 14, I cannot help regarding the idea as in the background,— ‘and, if devoid of spiritual life, then not to be taken into account by the Church.’
