<p><strong> 7</strong> .] <strong> ταῦτα</strong> most naturally applies to the characters just given of widows, not more generally: and in that case ἵνα ἀνεπίλημπτοι (see reff.) ὦσιν must refer to the widows also, not to the τέκνα and ἔκγονα , or to these and the widows together, as Heydenr., or more widely still, as Grot., al. This narrower reference is confirmed by the next verse, which takes up the duty of the relations, being connected not by γάρ , but by δέ .</p>
