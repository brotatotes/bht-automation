<b> 7.] Knowing</b> refers back to<i> “we be comforted:”</i>—<b>we are comforted with the assurance that, &c.</b><br><br><b> so are ye of the comfort also]</b> not,<i> “so shall ye be,”</i> as A.V.: he is speaking generally, of the community of consolation<i> subsisting</i> mutually between himself and the Corinthians; and it was this thought which helped to console him.
