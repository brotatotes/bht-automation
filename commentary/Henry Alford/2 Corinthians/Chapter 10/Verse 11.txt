<p><strong> 11.</strong> ] <strong> λογιζέσθω</strong> , as in <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="2co+10:7">2 Corinthians 10:7</span> .
	</p>
<p><strong> ὁ τοιοῦτος</strong> , viz. who thus speaks. The introduction of the verse without any connecting particle gives force and emphasis.
	</p>
<p>After <strong> παρόντες</strong> supply ἐσμεν , not ἐσόμεθα Not only the conduct of the Apostle on his next visit, but his <em> general character</em> , is in question.</p>
