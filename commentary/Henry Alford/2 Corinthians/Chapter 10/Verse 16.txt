<b> 16.] so as</b> (with a view)<b> to preach the gospel in the regions beyond you, not</b> (with a view)<b> to boast ourselves within another man’s line</b> (measuring<i> line</i>: according to the metaphor so common among us, ‘in his line’—i. e. ‘within the line which ‘Providence has marked out for him’)<b> with regard to</b> (or, ‘to the extent of: ‘to extend our boasting to’)<b> things ready made to our hand.</b>
