<b> 18.]</b> The reason of this being,<i> that not the self-commender, but he whom the Lord commends</i> by selecting him as His instrument, as He had the Apostle, and giving him the<i> epistle of commendation,</i> to be known and read by all men, of souls converted and churches founded, is<b> approved</b>, i.e. really and in the end abiding the test of trial.
