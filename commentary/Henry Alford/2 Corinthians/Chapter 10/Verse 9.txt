<b> 9.]</b> follows on ver. 8, but requires some clause to be supplied, such as ‘And I say this,’ or the like:<b> I say this, because I wish not to seem, &c.</b><br><br><b> by my letters]</b> He had written<i> two</i> before this, see 1 Cor. v. 9; but this is not necessarily here implied: for he may reckon this which he is now writing. Still less can we infer hence that a<i> third</i> had been written before this.
