<b> 18.] Since many,</b> viz. the false teachers, but not only they:— ‘since it is a common habit,’ for he is here speaking as “<i>one of the foolish ones</i>” (see Job ii. 10)<b> boast according to the flesh</b> (i. e. ‘in a spirit of regard’— ‘having regard to their extraction, achievements, &c.’ as below vv. 22 ff),<b> I also will boast</b> (according to the flesh).
