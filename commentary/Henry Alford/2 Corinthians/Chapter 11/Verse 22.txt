<b> 22.] “</b>The three honourable appellations with which the adversaries magnified themselves,—resting on their Jewish extraction, are arranged so as to form a<i> climax</i>: so that<b> Hebrews</b> refers to the<i> nationality</i>,—<b>Israelites</b> to the<i> theocracy</i> (tom, ix. 4 ff.), and<b> seed of Abraham</b> to the<i> claim to a part in the Messiah</i> (Rom. ix. 7: xi. 1, al.).” Meyer.
