<b> 16.] But be it so</b> ( ‘but let us suppose the former matter dismissed’),<b> let the fact be granted, that I myself</b> (emphatic)<b> did not burden you.</b> Then the sense breaks off, and the force of the concession goes no further, the following words making a new hypothesis.<b> Nevertheless, being</b> (by habit and standing)<b> crafty</b> (unprincipled, and versatile in devices),<b> I caught you with guile</b> (with some subtle way.<i> Caught</i> you, in order to practise upon you for my own ends).
