<b> 18.]</b> This journey ot Titus cannot, of course, be the one spoken of ch, viii. 6, 17, 22; but some previous mission to them before this Epistle was written: probably that from which he returned with the report of their penitence to Paul in Macedonia, ch. vii. 6 ff. We certainly have not elsewhere any hint of “<i>the brother</i>” having accompanied him on this journey: but this is no reason why it should not have been so.
