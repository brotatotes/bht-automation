<b> 8.] In respect of this</b> (<i>angel of Satan,</i> not<i> thorn</i>, see below)<b> I thrice</b> (Meyer well observes, ‘At his first and second request, no answer was given to him: on the third occasion, it came; and his faithful resignation to the Lord’s will prevented his asking again’)<b> besought the Lord</b> (Christ, see ver. 9),<b> that he might depart from me</b> (the angel of Satan: in Luke iv. 13, the same Greek word is used of the devil<i> departing</i> from our Lord: as also in Acts xxii. 29, “they<i> departed</i> from him which should have examined him”).
