<b> 7.]</b> Yet he prays God rather that they may require no such demonstration of his apostolic power, even though he lose in<br><br> reputation by it.<br><br><b> not that we should appear....]</b> ‘And the purpose of this my prayer is not to gain any repute by your Christian graces, but that you may be highly endowed with them, and (if it so happen) we may be as of no repute in the judgment of men, by your good conduct tending to the non-exercise and so to the depreciation of our Apostolic power.’
