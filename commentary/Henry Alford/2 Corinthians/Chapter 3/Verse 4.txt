<b> 4.]</b> The connexion with the foregoing is immediate: he had just spoken of his consciousness of apostolic success among them (which assertion would be true also of other churches which he had founded) being his worldwide recommendation. It is<i> this confidence</i> of which he here speaks.<b> Such confidence, however, we possess through Christ toward God:</b> i.e. ‘it is no vain boast, but. rests_on power imparted to us through Christ in regard to God, in reference to God’s work and our own account to be given to Him.’
