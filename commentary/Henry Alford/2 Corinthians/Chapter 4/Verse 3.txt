<p><strong> 3.</strong> ] <strong> But if</strong> (‘which I concede;’ see note, 1Co 4:7 ) <strong> it is even so, that our gospel</strong> (the gospel preached by us) <strong> is vailed, it is among</strong> (in the estimation of) <strong> the perishing that it is vailed.</strong> The allegory of ch. 3 is continued, the hiding of the gospel by the vail placed before the understanding.</p>
