<b> 5.] For we preach not</b> (the subject of our preaching is not)<b> ourselves</b> (Meyer understands ‘as lords;’ but as De Wette observes, this would anticipate the development of thought which follows, the contrast between Christ Jesus as<i> lord</i>, and ourselves as your<i> servants</i>, not being yet raised),<b> but Christ Jesus as Lord; and ourselves as your servants for Jesus’ sake</b> (on account of Him and His work).
