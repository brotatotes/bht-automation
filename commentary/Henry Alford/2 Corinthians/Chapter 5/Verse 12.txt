<p><strong> 12.</strong> ] <strong> We are not recommending ourselves again to you</strong> (see ch. 2Co 3:1 ), <strong> but [are] giving you an occasion for matter of boasting</strong> ( καύχημα , not = καύχησις as De W., ‘a source, whence matter of boasting may be derived’) <strong> on our behalf</strong> (of us, as your teachers, and to the upholding of our ministry), <strong> that ye may have it</strong> (viz. καύχημα , matter of boasting) <strong> against those who boast in face (fair outward appearance), and not in heart</strong> (i.e. in those things which they <em> exhibit</em> , and <em> are outwardly</em> = κατὰ τὴν σάρκα , ch. <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="2co+11:18">2 Corinthians 11:18</span> , not in matters which are <em> in their hearts</em> : implying that their hearts are indifferent about the matters of which they boast).</p>
