<b> 12.]</b><b> We are not recommending ourselves again unto you</b> (see ch. iii. 1),<b> but are giving you occasion of glorying on our behalf</b> (of us, as your teachers, and to the upholding of our ministry),<b> that ye may have it</b> (viz. occasion of boasting)<b> against those who glory in face</b> (fair outward appearance)<b> and not in heart</b> (i. e. in those things which they<i> exhibit</i>, and are<i> outwardly</i>, see ch. xi. 18, not in matters which are<i> in their hearts</i> implying that their hearts: are indifferent about the matters of which they boast).
