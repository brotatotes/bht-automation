<b> 8.]</b> Perhaps the<i> instrumental</i> signification of<b> through</b> need not be strictly retained. The preposition, once adopted, is kept for the sake of parallelism, though with various shades of meaning. Here it points out the<i> medium through which.</i> Thus understood, these two pairs in ver. 8 will form an easy transition from instrumental, through medial, to the passive characteristics which follow.
