<b> 10.]</b><i> How</i> ‘grief according to God’<i> produces such an effect.</i><b> For</b><b> grief according to God</b> (so literally)<b> works</b> (brings about, promotes, see reff.)<b> repentance unto salvation never to be regretted.</b> The epithet ‘<i>never to be regretted</i>’ belongs not to “<i>repentance</i>,” but to “<i>salvation:</i>” ‘<i>salvation which none will ever regret</i>’ having attained, however difficult it may have been to reach, however dearly it may have been bought.
