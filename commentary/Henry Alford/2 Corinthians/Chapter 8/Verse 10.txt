<b> 10.]</b> Ver. 9 was parenthetic: he now resumes the proposition of ver. 8, that he did not speak by way of command....<b> And I give my opinion in this matter,</b> the stress being on<b> opinion</b>, as distinguished from<i> command. “I give my judgment,”</i> as rendered in the version of the Five Clergymen, is objectionable here, as conveying the very idea which the Apostle wishes to negative, that of an authoritative decision.
