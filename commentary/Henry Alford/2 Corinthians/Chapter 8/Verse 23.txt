 23. ]  General recommendation of the three .
	 εἴτε ὑπ  .   Τίτου ]  Whether concerning Titus (we may supply λέγω or γράφω , or as in E. V., ‘any enquire:’ or we need not supply any thing),  he is my partner and (especially)  my fellow-worker towards you: whether our brethren (be in question: viz. the two mentioned but generalized by the absence of the article ‘  whether [  any ]  brethren of ours ’),  they are Apostles (in the more general sense of Acts 14:14 ; 1 Thessalonians 2:6 ; Php 2:25 )  of the churches (i.e. ‘are of the churches, what we are of the Lord’ persons sent out with authority),  the glory of Christ (i.e. men whose work tends to Christ’s glory).
