<b> 15. in those days</b>] In the days between the Ascension and Pentecost: during which it appears that the number of the assembly had increased, not probably by fresh conversions, but by the gathering round the Apostles of those who had previously been disciples.<br><br><b> the number of names</b>] that is, of persons: but the term would hardly be used except where the number is small. See Rev. iii. 4, and note.<br><br><b> an hundred and twenty</b>] De Wette asks, ‘where were the 500 brethren of 1 Cor. xv. 6?’ We surely may answer, ‘not in Jerusalem.’
