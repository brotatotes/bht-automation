<b> 37. the matter</b>] Not the<i> thing</i>, but the<i> thing said</i>, the ‘<i>material</i>’ of the proclamation, in this case equivalent to ‘<i>the history.</i>’<br><br><b> began from Galilee</b>] It was from Galilee first that the fame of Jesus went abroad, as Luke himself relates, Luke iv. 14, 37, 44; vii. 17; ix. 6. (xxiii. 5.) Galilee also was the nearest to Cæsarea, and may have been for this reason expressly mentioned.<br><br><b> after the baptism which John preached</b>] So also St. Peter dates the ministry of our Lord in ch. i. 22.
