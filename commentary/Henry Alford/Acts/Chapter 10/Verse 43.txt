<b> 43.</b>] All the prophets, generically: not that<i> every one</i> positively asserted this, but that the whole bulk of prophetic testimony announced it. To press such expressions to literal exactness is mere trifling. See ch. iii. 21, 24.<br><br><b> shall receive remission of sins</b>] The<i> legal sacrifices</i>, as well as the declarations of the prophets, all pointed to the remission of sins by faith in Him. And the<i> universality</i> of this proclamation, applying to<b> whosoever believeth in him,</b> is set forth by the prophets in many places, and was recognized even by the Jews themselves in their expositions of Scripture, though not in their practice.
