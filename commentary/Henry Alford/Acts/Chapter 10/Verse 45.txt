<b> 45.</b>] We do not read that Peter himself was astonished. He had been specially prepared by the vision:<i> they had not.</i>—The<b> speaking with tongues</b> here is identified with the<b> speaking with other tongues</b> of ch. ii. 4, by the assertion of ch. xi. 15, just cited;—and this again with the<b> speaking with tongues</b> of ch. xix. 6: so that the gift was<i> one and the same throughout</i>. On the whole subject, see note, ch. ii. 4.
