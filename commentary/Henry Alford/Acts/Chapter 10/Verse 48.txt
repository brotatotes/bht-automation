 48.   προσέταξεν ] As the Lord Himself when on earth  did not baptize ( Joh 4:2 ), so did not ordinarily the Apostles (see 1 Corinthians 1:13-17 , and note). Perhaps the same reason may have operated in both cases, lest those baptized by our Lord, or by the chief Apostles, should arrogate to themselves pre-eminence on that account.
