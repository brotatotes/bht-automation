<p><strong> 7. </strong> <strong> ἀπῆλθεν</strong> ] So in <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="lu+1:38">Luke 1:38</span> : another token of the objective reality of the vision: εἰσελθόντα ( Act 10:3 ) and ἀπῆλθ . denoting the <em> real acts</em> of the angel, not the mere deemings of Cornelius.
	</p>
<p><strong> λαλῶν</strong> must be regarded as the imperfect participle, as in <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="joh+9:8">John 9:8</span> .</p>
