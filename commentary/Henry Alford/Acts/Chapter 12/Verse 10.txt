<b> 10.</b>] The<i> first</i> and<i> second</i> watch or guard cannot mean the two soldiers to whom he was chained, on account of the mention of his<i> going out</i> above: but are probably the other two, one at the door of the chamber, the other at the outer door of the building. Then ‘the iron gate leading into the city’ was that outside the prison buildings, forming the exit from the premises. The situation of the prison is uncertain, but seems to have been<i> in</i> the city. The additional clause in the margin is remarkable, and can hardly be other than genuine.
