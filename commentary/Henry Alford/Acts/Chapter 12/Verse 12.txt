<b> 12.</b>] And now<b> having become aware of it</b>,—for this, and not “<i>when he had considered the thing,</i>” is the meaning,—he proceeds at once to action.<br><br><b> John, whose surname was Mark</b>] It is uncertain whether this John Mark was the same as the Evangelist Mark: but they have been generally believed to be the same. For a full account of him, see Introduction to Mark. His mother Mary was not sister, but aunt of Barnabas: see Col. iv. 10, note.
