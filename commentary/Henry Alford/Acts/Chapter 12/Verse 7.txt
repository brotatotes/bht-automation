<p><strong> 7</strong> .] <strong> οἰκήματι</strong> <strong> , the chamber</strong> . It is in St. Luke’s manner to relate simultaneously the angelic appearance and the shining of a light around: cf. <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="lu+2:9">Luke 2:9</span> ; <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="lu+24:4">Luke 24:4</span> ; ch. <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="ac+10:30">Acts 10:30</span> . The light accompanied, or perhaps, as suggested here in syr-marg, <em> shone from</em> , the angel.</p>
