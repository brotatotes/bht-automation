<b> 18. he bore them as a nurse beareth a child</b>] The adoption of this rendering, instead of that of the A.V., depends on the change of one letter in the Greek. ‘The word is “<i>etrop</i> (or,<i> ph</i>)<i> ophoresen</i>:” the former being the reading rendered in the A.V.
