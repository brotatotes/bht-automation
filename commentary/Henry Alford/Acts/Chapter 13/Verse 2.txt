<b> 2. As they ministered</b>] The word in the original is that generally used to express the priestly service among the Jews, to which now had succeeded that of the<i> prophets and teachers</i> in the Christian church:<b> ministering</b> is therefore the only word adequate to render it, as A.V. More closely to define it is not only impracticable, but is narrowing an expression purposely left general.
