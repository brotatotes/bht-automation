<b> 22. he removed him</b>] i.e.<b> deposed him:</b> in this case, by his<i> death</i>, for David was not made king till then. Or perhaps the word may refer to the sentence pronounced against Saul, 1 Sam. xiii. 14, or xv. 23, 28, and the following verb,<b> raised up</b>, to the whole process of the exaltation of David to be king. But I prefer the former.
