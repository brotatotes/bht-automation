<b> 3. when they had fasted and prayed</b>] not, “<i>when they had done fasting and praying:</i>” this was a<i> new</i> fasting and<i> special</i> prayer for Barnabas and Saul. Fasting and prayer have ever been connected with the solemn times of ordination by the Christian church; but the fasts four times a year, or ‘ember days at the four seasons,’ for the special purpose of ordinations, were probably not introduced till the fourth or even fifth century.
