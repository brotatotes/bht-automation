<b> 42.</b>] The insertions in the text followed by the A.V. have been made partly perhaps to remove the ambiguity in the “<i>they</i>” and “<i>them</i>,” and to shew who they were that besought, &c. But they confuse the sense.<b> As they</b> (the congregation)<b> were going out, they</b> (the same)<b> besought</b>.
