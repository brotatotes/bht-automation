<b> 50. devout... women</b>] Women had a strong religious influence both for and against Christianity: see for the former ch. xvi. 14; xvii. 4; Phil. iv. 3; 1 Cor. vii. 16: for the latter, we have Josephus’s statement, that the majority of the wives of the Damascenes were proselytes: which may be compared with ch. ix. 22–25. These were proselytes of the gate, or at least inclined to Judaism.
