<b> 17.</b>] Compare Rom. i. 19, 20. The words<b> gave us rain from heaven</b> had a remarkable applicability in a country where we have seen from Strabo (on ver. 6) that there was<i> great scarcity of water</i>. He relates that in one city of Lycaonia, where water was reached by digging the wells very deep, it was sold for money.
