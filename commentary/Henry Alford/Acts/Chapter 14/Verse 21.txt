<p><strong> 21. </strong> <strong> ὑπέστρ</strong> <strong> .</strong> ] They were not far from the famous pass, called the ‘Cilician gates,’ which leads direct into that province: but, notwithstanding all that had befallen him, Paul prefers returning by the churches which he had founded, to a short and easy journey to the coast by his own home.</p>
