<b> 36. some days after</b>]<i> How long</i>, we are not informed but perhaps (?) during this time took place that visit of Peter to Antioch mentioned Gal. ii. 11 ff., when he sacrificed his Christian consistency and better persuasions to please some Judaizers, and even Barnabas was led away with the dissimulation. On this occasion Paul boldly rebuked him. See, on the whole occurrence, notes to Gal. as above.
