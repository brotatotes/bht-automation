<b> 19.</b>] Her masters (a partnership of persons.—They may have been the heirs of some one to whom she had belonged) perceived that the hope of their gain had gone out (<i>with the dæmon</i>).<br><br><b> caught...</b> and<b> drew</b> give the idea of force having been used. Paul and Silas only are apprehended as having been the principal persons in the company.<br><br><b> unto the rulers</b> is said<i> generally:</i> they dragged them to the forum to the authorities,—afterwards specified as<i> magistrates</i> (or generals, see below).
