<p><strong> 39. </strong> <strong> παρεκάλεσαν</strong> ] Not ‘ <em> comforted:</em> ’ but, as E. V., <strong> besought them</strong> : viz. not to make their treatment matter of legal complaint. In the request to depart from the city, the prætors seem to shew fear of a change in the temper of the mob. See the curious addition in the var. readd.</p>
