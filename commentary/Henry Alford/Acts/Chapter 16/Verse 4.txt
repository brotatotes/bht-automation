<p><strong> 4. </strong> <strong> τὰς πόλ</strong> <strong> .</strong> ] Iconium, and perhaps Antioch in Pisidia. He might at Iconium see the elders of the church of Antioch, as he did afterwards those of Ephesus at Miletus. If he went to Antioch, he might regain his route into Phrygia and Galatia by crossing the hills east of that city.</p>
