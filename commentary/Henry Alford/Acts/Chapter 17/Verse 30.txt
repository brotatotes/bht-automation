<b> 30. God overlooked</b>] The rendering of the A.V. hears the same meaning, but is to our ears in these days objectionable. In this assurance lie treasures of mercy for those who lived in the times of ignorance. God<b> overlooked</b> them: i.e. corrected not this<i> ignorance itself</i> as a sin, but<i> the abuses even of this</i>, by which the heathen sunk into deeper degradation. The same argument is treated more at length in Rom. i. ii.
