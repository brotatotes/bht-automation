 9.   λαβόντες τὸ ἱκανόν ] ‘Satisdatione accepta;’ either by  sureties , or by a  sum of money , or both. They bound over Jason and the rest ( τινας ἀδελφούς , Act 17:6 ) to take care that no more trouble was given by these men: in accordance with which security they sent them away; and by night, to avoid the notice of the ὄχλος .
