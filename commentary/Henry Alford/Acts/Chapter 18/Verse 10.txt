<b> 10. I have much people</b>] See John x. 16. As our Lord<i> forewarned</i> Paul in Jerusalem that<i> they would not</i> receive his testimony concerning Him, so here He<i> encourages</i> him, by a promise of much success in Corinth. The word<b> people</b>, the express title beforetime<i> of the Jews</i>, is still used now, notwithstanding their<i> secession</i>.
