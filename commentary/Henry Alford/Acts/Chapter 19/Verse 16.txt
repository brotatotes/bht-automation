<b> 16. them both</b>] The weight of MSS. evidence for this reading is even surpassed by its internal probability. There would be every reason, as<i> seven</i> have been before mentioned, for altering it: but no imaginable one for substituting it for that of the common text.<i> Two only</i>, it would seem, were thus employed on this particular occasion: and St. Luke has retained the word as it stood in the record furnished to him. Whether any similar occurrence happened to the rest, we are not informed: this one is selected as most notorious.
