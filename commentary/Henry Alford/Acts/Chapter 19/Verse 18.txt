<p><strong> 18.</strong> ] The natural effect of such an occurrence was to induce a horror of magical arts, &amp;c., which some were still continuing to countenance or practise secretly, together with a profession of Christianity. Such persons now came forward and confessed their error. The πράξεις of this verse denotes the <em> association with</em> such practices: the next verse treats of the <em> magicians themselves</em> .</p>
