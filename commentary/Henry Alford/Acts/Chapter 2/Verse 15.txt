<p><strong> ὥρα τρίτη</strong> ] the <em> first hour of prayer</em> : before which no pious Jew might eat or drink: “Non licet homini gustare quidquam, antequam oraverit orationem suam.” Berachoth. f. 28. 2; Lightf., Wetst.
	</p>
<p>But perhaps we need not look further than the ordinary intent of such a defence the improbability of intoxication at that hour of the morning. See <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="ec+10:16">Ecclesiastes 10:16</span> ; Isa 5:11 ; <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="1th+5:7">1 Thessalonians 5:7</span> .</p>
