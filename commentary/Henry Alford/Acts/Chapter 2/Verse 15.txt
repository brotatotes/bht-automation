	 ὥρα τρίτη ] the  first hour of prayer : before which no pious Jew might eat or drink: “Non licet homini gustare quidquam, antequam oraverit orationem suam.” Berachoth. f. 28. 2; Lightf., Wetst.
	But perhaps we need not look further than the ordinary intent of such a defence the improbability of intoxication at that hour of the morning. See Ecclesiastes 10:16 ; Isa 5:11 ; 1 Thessalonians 5:7 .
