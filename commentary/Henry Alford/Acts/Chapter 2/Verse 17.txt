<b> 17.</b>]<b> in the last days</b> is an<i> exposition</i> of the words “<i>after these things</i>” in the LXX and Hebrew, referring it to the days of the Messiah, as Isa. ii. 2; Micah iv. 1, al. See also 2 Tim. iii. 1; Heb. i. 1.<b> saith God</b> does not occur in the verse of Joel, but at the beginning of the whole passage, ver. 12, and is supplied by Peter here.
