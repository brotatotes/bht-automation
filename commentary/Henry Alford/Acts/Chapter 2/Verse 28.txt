<b> 28.</b>]<b> Thou hast made known</b>: in the Hebrew, and English Bible, ‘<i>Thou wilt make known</i>.”<br><br><b> thou shalt make me full of joy with thy countenance</b> is in the Hebrew, and English Bible, “<i>In</i> (Heb.<i> with</i>)<i> thy presence is fulness of joy</i>.’ These two last clauses refer to the Resurrection and the Ascension respectively.
