<b> 34.</b>] This<i> exaltation</i> of Christ is also proved from prophecy—and from the same passage with which Jesus Himself had silenced His enemies. See notes, Matt. xxii. 41 ff. The connexion is,<b> For David himself is not ascended into the heavens</b>,—<i>as he would be, if the former prophecy applied to him</i>: BUT<b> he himself says</b>, removing all doubt on the subject, &c.
