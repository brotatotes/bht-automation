<b> 36.</b>] THE CONCLUSION FROM ALL THAT HAS BEEN SAID. The Apostle says,<b> let all the house of Israel know</b>, because all hitherto said has gone upon proofs and sayings belonging to<i> Israel</i>, and to<i> all</i> Israel. In the words<b> God hath made</b>, we have as before, the ground-tone of the whole discourse.
