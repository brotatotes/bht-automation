<b> 39.</b>]<b> your children</b>, viz. as included in the prophecy cited ver. 17,<b> your little ones</b>: not, as in ch. xiii, 32, ‘<i>your descendants</i>,’ which would be understood by any Jew to be<i> necessarily implied</i>.
