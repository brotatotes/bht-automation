<b> 19. serving the Lord</b>] With the sole exception of the assertion of our Lord, ‘Ye cannot serve God and mammon,’ Matt. vi. 24; Luke xvi. 13, this peculiar verb (<i> “to be a bond-servant to”</i>), for ‘serving God,’ is used by<i> Paul only</i>, and by him seven times, viz. Rom. vii. 6, 25; xii. 11; xiv. 18; xvi. 18; Phil. ii, 22; Col. iii. 24; 1 Thess. i. 9.
