<b> 32. which is able</b>] Clearly spoken of<i> God</i>, not of the<i> word of His grace</i>, which cannot be said<i> to give an inheritance</i>, however it might<i> build up</i>.—The expression “<i>an in-heritance among all them that are sanctified</i>” is strikingly similar to “<i>his inheritance in the saints</i>” Eph. i. 18, addressed to this same church. See also ch. xxvi. 18.
