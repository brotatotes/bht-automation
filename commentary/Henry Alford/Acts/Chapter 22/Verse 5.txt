<b> 5. the high priest</b>] ‘<i>of that day, who is still living:’</i> i.e. Theophilus, see on ch. ix. 1. Similarly,<b> the whole Sanhedrim</b> are ‘<i>those who were then members, and now survive</i>.”<br><br><b> unto the</b> (<b>Jewish</b>: or, their)<b> brethren</b>] The rendering, ‘<i>against the</i> (Christian)<i> brethren</i>,’ is altogether inadmissible. If ever Paul spoke to the Jews<i> as a Jew</i>, it was on this occasion.
