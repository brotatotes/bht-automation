<b> 18.</b>] The construction in the original is peculiar, and can hardly be represented in a faithful English version, The nominative case to the verb<b> found</b> has to be supplied, somehow thus:<b> amidst which they found me purified in the temple, none who detected me in the act of raising a tumult.... but certain Asiatic Jews....</b> This would leave it to be inferred that no legal officers had apprehended him, but certain private individuals, illegally; who besides had not come forward to substantiate any charge him.
