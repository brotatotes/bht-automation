<p><strong> 20.</strong> ] <strong> Or let these persons themselves say, what fault they found in me while I stood before the Sanhedrim, other than in the matter of this one saying …</strong> τί serves for τί ἄλλο . So in English: <strong> What fault but this</strong> : i.e. ‘What <em> other</em> fault but this.’</p>
