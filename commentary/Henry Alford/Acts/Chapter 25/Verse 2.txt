<b> 2. the high priest</b>] The High Priest now was Ishmael the son of Phabi. See chronological table in the Introduction.<br><br> The term<b> chief of the Jews</b> is more general than “<i>elders</i>,” though most of the chief<i> men</i> must have been members of the Sanhedrim. Festus, relating this application, ver. 15, calls them “<i>elders</i>.”
