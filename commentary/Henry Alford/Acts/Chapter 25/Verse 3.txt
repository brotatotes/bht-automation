<p><strong> 3.</strong> ] <strong> χάριν</strong> = καταδίκην , <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="ac+25:15">Acts 25:15</span> .
	</p>
<p><strong> ποιοῦντες</strong> , not for ποιήσοντες : they were <em> making, contriving</em> , the ambush already. The country was at this time, as may be seen abundantly in Jos. Antt. xx., full of sicarii; who were hired by the various parties to take off their adversaries.</p>
