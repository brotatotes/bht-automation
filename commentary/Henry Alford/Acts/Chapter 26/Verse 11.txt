Matt. x. 17.<b> I compelled them to blaspheme</b> does not imply that any<i> did</i> blaspheme (Christ: so Pliny, in his celebrated Epistle, speaks of ordering the Bithynian Christians<i> to curse Christ</i>, and adds, that he hears none can be compelled to do this who are really Christians): the verb only relates the<i> attempt</i>. The persecuting the Christians even to foreign cities, forms the transition to the narrative following.
