<b> 17. delivering thee from</b>] This, and not ‘<i>choosing thee out of</i>,’ is the right meaning.<br><br><b> the people</b>] as elsewhere,<b> the Jewish people</b>. “Thus,” says Calvin, “the Lord armed him against all fears which awaited him, and at the same time prepared him to bear the cross.”<br><br><b> unto whom</b>] to<i> both</i>, the people, and the Gentiles; not the Gentiles only.
