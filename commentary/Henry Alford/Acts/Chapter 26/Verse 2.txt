<p><strong> 2</strong> .] There is no force in Meyer’s observation, that by the omission of the art. before Ἰουδαίων , Paul wishes to express that the charges were made by <em> some, not by all</em> of the Jews. That omission is the one so often overlooked by the German critics (e.g. Stier also here), <em> after a preposition</em> . See Middl. ch. vi. § 1, and compare κατὰ Ἰουδαίους in the next verse, of which the above cannot be said.
