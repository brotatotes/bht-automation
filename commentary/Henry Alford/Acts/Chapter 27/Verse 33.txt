<b> 33.</b>] This precaution on the part of Paul was another means taken of providing for their safety. All would, on the approaching day, have their strength fully taxed: which therefore needed recruiting by food.....<b> until it began to be day</b>: i.e. in the interval between the last-mentioned occurrence and daybreak, Paul employed the time, &c.<br><br> The words<b> having taken nothing</b> are spoken hyperbolically, and cannot mean literally that they had abstained entirely from food<i> during the whole fortnight</i>.
