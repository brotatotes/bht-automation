	</p>
<p><strong> πείθων</strong> ] <strong> persuading</strong> : not ‘ <em> teaching</em> ,’ as Kuin., nor ‘ <em> trying to persuade</em> .’ Meyer well remarks, Paul, on his part, subjectively, performed that indicated by πείθειν ; that this did not produce its objective effect in all his hearers, does not alter the meaning of the word.</p>
