<p><strong> 23</strong> . <strong> ἐξολεθρ</strong> .] LXX ἐγὼ ἐκδικήσω ἐξ αὐτοῦ . This word, only known to later Greek, is often found in the LXX. See besides reff., <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="ge+17:14">Genesis 17:14</span> ; <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="de+9:3">Deuteronomy 9:3</span> ; Ps. 17:40; 72:27. In most places where it occurs, the readings vary between - ολοθρ - and - ολεθρ -; see var. readd.</p>
