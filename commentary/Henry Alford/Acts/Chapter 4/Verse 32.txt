<b> 32.]</b> “Where faith reigns, it conciliates men’s minds so that all will the same thing. For hence comes discord, because we are not ruled by this divine spirit of Christ.” Calvin. On the community of goods, see note at ch. ii. 45. We have the view there taken strikingly confirmed here by the expressions used<b> No one called</b> (reckoned)<b> any thing of his goods</b> (which were still “<i>his goods</i>,” not alienated) [<b>to be</b>]<b> his own</b>, No one<i> called</i> his possessions his own: this shews, says Bengel, that he had not altogether in reality renounced the possession of them.
