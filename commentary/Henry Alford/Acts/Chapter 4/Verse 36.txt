<p><strong> 36</strong> .] Barnabas, בַּר נְבוּאָה , is υἱὸς προφητείας and the interpretation has been generally made good by taking παράκλησις as included in προφητεία , and as in the sense of exhortation: see ch. <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="ac+11:23">Acts 11:23</span> .
	</p>
<p><strong> Λευεΐτης</strong> ] The Levites might possess land at all times within the precincts of the Levitical cities: such was the case, e.g., in <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="jer+32:7">Jeremiah 32:7</span> . At the division of the kingdoms, the priests and Levites all resorted to Rehoboam in Judah (and Benjamin), <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="2ch+11:13">2 Chronicles 11:13</span> ; from that time probably, but certainly after the captivity, when the Mosaic division of the land was no longer accurately observed, the possession of land by Levites seems to have been allowed. The whole subject is involved in some uncertainty: cf. <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="le+25:32">Leviticus 25:32</span> ff.; <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="nu+35:1-8">Numbers 35:1-8</span> ; <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="de+12:12">Deuteronomy 12:12</span> ; <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="de+18:8">Deuteronomy 18:8</span> , al.
	</p>
