<p><strong> 7.</strong> ] <strong> ἐν ποίᾳ δυνάμει</strong> not = ἐν π . ἐξουσίᾳ , ‘in what <em> authority</em> ,’ but <strong> in what</strong> (manner of) <strong> power</strong> ; of what kind was the enabling cause, the element in which, as its condition, the deed was wrought? <strong> ἐν ποίῳ ὀνόματι</strong> not ‘ <em> in what name</em> ,’ i.e. ‘ <em> by whose authority</em> ,’ <strong> but by</strong> (‘ <em> in</em> ,’ see above) <strong> what</strong> (manner of) <strong> name</strong> , spoken: see ch. <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="ac+3:6">Acts 3:6</span> ; <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="ac+3:16">Acts 3:16</span> ; Jos. Antt. viii. 2. 5.
	</p>
<p><strong> τοῦτο</strong> , not the <em> teaching</em> (Olshaus., &amp;c.), nor <em> both the miracle and the teaching</em> (Heinr.), but the <em> miracle</em> : and that only.</p>
