<b> 1.] Ananias</b> signifies in Hebrew,—<i>The cloud of God</i>, or<i> The mercy of God</i>.<br><br><b> Sapphira</b> is probably a Greek name for the precious stone<i> sapphire</i>.—The crime of these two is well described by Meyer: ‘By the sale of their field, and the bringing in of the money, they in fact professed to give the<i> whole price</i> as a gift of brotherly love to the common stock: but their aim was to get for themselves the credit of holy love and zeal by<i> one portion</i> of the price, whereas they had selfishly kept back the other portion for themselves. They wished to serve<i> two</i> masters, but to appear to serve only One.’
