<b> 23.]</b> “They ought to have enquired first, How did ye escape? But as if nothing had happened, they ask them, saying&c.” Chrysostom. “The same shyness of open allusion to the names or facts connected with Jesus and the spread of his doctrine may be traced in the words “<i>this name</i>,” and “<i>this man’s blood</i>,” and is a strong mark of truth and circumstantiality— “The High Priest will not name Jesus: Peter names and celebrates Him.” Bengel.
