<p><strong> 14.</strong> ] We may either take the words thus, ὅτι Ἰησοῦς ὁ Ναζωραῖος , οὗτος κατ ., “ <em> that Jesus of N., he it is who shall destroy</em> ’ … (see ch. <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="ac+7:35">Acts 7:35</span> ; 1Co 6:4 ), or ὅτι Ἰησοῦς , ὁ Ναζωραῖος οὗτος , κατ ., ‘ <em> that Jesus, this Nazarene, shall destroy</em> …,’ or, which seems by far the best, take the whole together, <strong> that this Jesus of N. shall destroy</strong> , as in E. V. Compare ὁ Παῦλος οὗτος , ch. <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="ac+19:26">Acts 19:26</span> .</p>
