<p><strong> 8</strong> .] This is the first instance of any, <em> not an Apostle</em> , working signs and wonders. The power was perhaps conferred by the laying on of the Apostles’ hands; though, that having been for a special purpose merely, and the working miracles being a fulfilment of the promise, <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="mr+16:17-18">Mark 16:17-18</span> , to <em> all believers</em> , I should rather refer the power to the <em> eminence of Stephen’s faith</em> .
	</p>
<p><strong> χάριτος</strong> , <strong> divine grace</strong> (not ‘favour with the people’): the effects of which, the miracles, were called <strong> χαρίσματα</strong> .</p>
