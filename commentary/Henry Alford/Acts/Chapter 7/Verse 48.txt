<p><strong> 48.</strong> ] But, though Solomon built Him an house, <em> we are not to suppose, for all that, that He is confined to earthly spots</em> .
	</p>
<p><strong> καθὼς ὁ πρ</strong> <strong> . </strong> <strong> λ</strong> .] We have in substance the same declaration by Solomon himself at the dedication of his temple, <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="1ki+8:27">1 Kings 8:27</span> ; see also the beautiful prayer of David, <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="1ch+29:10-19">1 Chronicles 29:10-19</span> . The citation is freely from the LXX.
	</p>
<p>The student will not fail to be interested in observing the apparent reference to this declaration in Stephen’s apology, by St. Paul, ch. <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="ac+17:24">Acts 17:24</span> .</p>
