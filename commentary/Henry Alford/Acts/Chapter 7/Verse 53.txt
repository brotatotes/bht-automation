<b> 53. at the injunction of angels</b>] Many explanations have been given of these words. An enumeration and description of them may be seen in my Greek Testament. The key to the right understanding of them seems to be the similar expression in Gal. iii. 19,<i> “the law,... ordained by</i> [means of]<i> angels.”</i> The law was<i> given</i> by God, but<i> announced</i> by angels. The people received God’s law then,<b> at the in-junction of angels.</b>
