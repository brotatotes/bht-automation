<p><strong> 56.</strong> ] This is the only time that our Lord is by human lips called the SON OF MAN after His ascension (<span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="re+1:13">Revelation 1:13</span> ; <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="re+14:14">Revelation 14:14</span> , are not instances). And <em> why here</em> ? I believe, for this reason. Stephen, full of the Holy Ghost, speaking <em> now</em> not of himself at all ( Act 7:55 ), but entirely by the utterance of the Spirit, repeats the <em> very words</em> , <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="mt+26:64">Matthew 26:64</span> , in which Jesus Himself, <em> before this council</em> , had foretold His glorification; and assures them that that exaltation of the SON of MAN, which they should hereafter witness to their dismay, was <em> already begun and actual</em> .</p>
