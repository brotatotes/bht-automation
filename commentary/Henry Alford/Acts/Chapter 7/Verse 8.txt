<p><strong> 8</strong> .] On the institution of circumcision, it is called a διαθήκη , <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="ge+17:10">Genesis 17:10</span> , and the immediate promise of that covenant was δώσω σοι κ . τῷ σπέρματί σου μετά σε τὴν γῆν ἣν παροικεῖς , πᾶσαν τὴν γῆν Χαναὰν εἰς κατάσχεσιν αἰώνιον · καὶ ἔσομαι αὐτοῖς εἰς θεόν , id. <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="ac+7:8">Acts 7:8</span> .
	</p>
<p><strong> οὕτως</strong> , <strong> thus</strong> , ‘in this new covenant state;’ or, ‘in fulfilment of the promise of <em> seed</em> implied in the above words.’ In this word <strong> οὕτως</strong> lies hid the germ of the subsequent teaching of the Holy Spirit by St. Paul, <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="ga+3:0">Galatians 3:0</span> .</p>
