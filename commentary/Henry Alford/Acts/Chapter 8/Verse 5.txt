<b> 5. Philip</b>]<i> The deacon:</i> not<i> one of the Twelve:</i> this is precluded by vv. 1 and 14. And it is probable, that the persecution should have been directed especially against the colleagues of Stephen. Philip is mentioned again as<i> the Evangelist</i>,—probably from his having been the first recorded who preached (<i>evangelized</i>) the word,—in ch. xxi. 8,—as married, and having four daughters, virgins, who prophesied.
