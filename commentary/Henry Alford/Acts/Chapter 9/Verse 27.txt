	 τοὺς ἀποστ .] Only Peter, and James the Lord’s brother, Galatians 1:18-19 . Probably there were no other Apostles there at the time: if there were, it is hardly conceivable that Saul should not have seen them. On his second visit, he saw John also ( Gal 2:9 ). Perhaps he never saw in the flesh any other of the Apostles after his conversion.
