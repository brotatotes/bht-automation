<b> 8.</b>]<b> When his eyes were opened</b> (it would seem that he had closed them on the first disappearance of the vision)<b>, he saw no one.</b> He explains it, ch. xxii. 11, “<i>when I could not see for the glory of that light.</i>” He had seen, what those with him had not seen, the glorious Person of the Lord Jesus.
