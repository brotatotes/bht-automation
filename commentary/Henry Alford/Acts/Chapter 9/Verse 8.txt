<p><strong> 8.</strong> ] <strong> On his eyes being opened</strong> (it would seem that he had closed them on the first disappearance of the vision), <strong> he saw no one</strong> . He explains it, ch. <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="ac+22:11">Acts 22:11</span> , ὡς δὲ οὐκ ἐνέβλεπον ἀπὸ τῆς δόξης τοῦ φωτὸς ἐκείνου . He had seen, what those with him had not seen, the glorious Person of the Lord Jesus. See below on <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="ac+9:18">Acts 9:18</span> .</p>
