<b> 21</b>.] See on Eph. vi. 4. In the words “<i>that they be not disheartened</i>” it is assumed that the result of such irritation will be to cause repeated punishment, and so eventual desperation, on the part of the child. It would be well if all who have to educate children took to heart Bengel’s remark here; “A broken spirit is the pest of youth.”
