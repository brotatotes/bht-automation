 7.]<b> in which</b> (vices)<b> ye also once walked when ye lived</b> (before your death with Christ to the world)<b> in these things</b> (the assertion is not tautological: see Gal. v. 25, “<i>If we live by the Spirit, by the Spirit let us also walk</i>.” When ye were alive to these things, ye regulated your course by them, walked in them. “Living and walking differ, as do the power, and the act: living precedes, walking follows.” Calvin).
