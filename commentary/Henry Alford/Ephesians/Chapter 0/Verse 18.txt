<b> 18.]</b> THE APOSTOLIC BLESSING. No special intention need be suspected in the words<b> with your spirit</b> (as Chrysostom does, saying, “He thus dissuades them from fleshly reliance”), seeing that the same expression occurs at the end of other Epistles: see Phil. iv. 28; Philem. 25; 2 Tim. iv. 22.
