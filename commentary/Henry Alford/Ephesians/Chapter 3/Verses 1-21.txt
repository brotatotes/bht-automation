<p><strong> 1 21</strong> .] AIM AND END OF THE CHURCH IN THE SPIRIT. And herein, <em> the revelation to it of the mystery of Christ, through those ministers who wrought in the Spirit: primarily, as regarded the Ephesians, through</em> himself. <em> Thus first, of</em> HIS OFFICE AS APOSTLE OF THE GENTILES (1 13): <em> secondly</em> , under the form of a prayer for them, THE AIM AND END OF THAT OFFICE AS RESPECTED THE CHURCH: <em> its becoming strong in the power of the Spirit</em> (14 19). Then (20, 21) <em> doxology</em> , concluding this first division of the Epistle.</p>
