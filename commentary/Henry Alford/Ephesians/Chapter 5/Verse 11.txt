<b> 11.] And have no fellowship with the unfruitful works of darkness</b> (see Gal. v. 19, 22; on which Jerome says, “Vices finish with themselves and perish; virtues put forth fruit and abound.” See also the distinction in John iii. 20, 21; v. 29)<b>, but rather even reprove them</b> (<i>in words:</i> not only abstain from fellowship with them, but attack them and put them to shame).
