<b> 12.] For</b> (the connexion seems to be, ‘reprove them—this they want, and this is more befitting you—for to have the least part in them, even in speaking of them, is shameful’)<b> the things done in secret by them it is shameful even to speak of</b> (the connexion being— ‘I mention not, and you need not<i> speak</i> of, these deeds of darkness, much less have any fellowship with them—your connexion with them must be only that which the act of<i> reproof</i> necessitates’).
