<b> 11.] Put on the entire armour</b> (emphatic: repeated again ver. 18: offensive, as well as defensive. It is probable that the Apostle was daily familiarized in his imprisonment with the Roman method of arming)<b> of God</b> (i. e. as supplied, ministered by God)<b>, that ye may be able to stand against the schemes</b> (the original word here also occurs in ch. iv. 14)<b> of the devil.</b>
