<b> 9.]</b> As we have said before (referring, not to ver. 8; for the word more naturally, as in 2 Cor. xiii. 2 [so too 1 Thess. iv. 6], relates to something said on a former occasion,—but to what he had said during his presence with them: see a similar reference, ch. v. 3, 21),<b> 1 also now say again,—If any one is</b> (no longer now a supposition, but an assumption of the fact)<b> preaching to you</b> (evangelizing you),<b> other</b> (with another gospel)<b> than that which ye received</b> (from us),<b> let him be accursed</b> (see above).
