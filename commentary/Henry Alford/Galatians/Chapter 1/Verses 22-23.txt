	 τῆς Ἰουδαίας excludes Jerusalem, where he  was known . Jowett doubts this: but it seems to be required by Acts 9:26-29 . Chrys. seems to mistake the Apostle’s purpose, when he says, ἵνα μάθη ̣ ς , ὅτι τοσοῦτον ἀπεῖχε τοῦ κηρύξαι αὐτοῖς περιτομήν , ὅτι οὐδὲ ἀπὸ ὄψεως γνώριμος ἦν αὐτοῖς : and Olshausen, in supposing him to be refuting the idea that he had learned the Gospel from other Christians in Palestine.
