<b> 8.]</b> Parenthetic explanation of this word “<i>intrusted.</i>” The word<b> wrought</b> applies to the<i> signs following</i> with which the Lord accompanied His word spoken by them, and to the power with which they spoke that word. The agent in this<i> working</i> is GOD,—the Father: see 1 Cor. xii. 6; Phil. ii. 13; Rom. xv. 15, 16.
