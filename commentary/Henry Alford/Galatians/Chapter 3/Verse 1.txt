<b> CH. III. 1—V. 12.]</b> SECOND, OR POLEMICAL PART OF THE EPISTLE.<br><br><b> 1.]</b> The Apostle exclaims indignantly, moved by the fervour and truth of his rebuke of Peter, against the folly of the Galatians, for suffering themselves to be bewitched out of their former vivid apprehension of Christ’s work and Person.
