 29 .] Christ is ‘  Abraham’s seed ’ ( Gal 3:16 ): ye are one in and with Christ, have put on Christ; therefore  ye are Abraham’s seed; consequently heirs by promise; for to Abraham and his seed were the promises made. The stress is on ὑμεῖς , τοῦ Ἀβραάμ , and κατ ʼ ἐπαγγελίαν , especially on the latter, carrying the conclusion of the argument, as against inheritance  by the law . See on this verse, the note on Gal 3:16 above. “The declaration of Gal 3:7 is now substantiated by 22 verses of the deepest, the most varied, and most comprehensive reasoning that exists in the whole compass of the great Apostle’s writings.” Ellicott.
