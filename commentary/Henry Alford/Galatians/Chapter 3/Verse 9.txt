<p><strong> 9</strong> .] Consequence of <strong> ἐν</strong> ευλογηθήσονται above, substantiated by Gal 3:10 below. A share in Abraham’s <em> blessing</em> must be the accompaniment of faith, not of works of the law <strong> πίστεως</strong> has the emphasis.
	</p>
<p><strong> σύν</strong> , to shew their community with him in the blessing: <strong> τῷ πιστῷ</strong> , to shew wherein the community consists, viz. FAITH.</p>
