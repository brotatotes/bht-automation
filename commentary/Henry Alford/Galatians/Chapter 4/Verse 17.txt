<b> 17.]</b> ‘My telling you the truth may have made me seem your enemy: but I warn you that these men who court you so zealously have no honourable purpose in so doing: it is only in order to get you away from the community as a separate clique, that you may court them.’ Thus the verse seems to fit best into the context. As regards particular words, their<i> shutting out</i> or<i> excluding</i> must bear the meaning of exclusion from a larger and attraction to a smaller, viz. their own, party. Our very word ‘exclusive’ conveys the same idea.
