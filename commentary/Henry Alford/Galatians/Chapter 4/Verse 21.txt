<p><strong> 21</strong> . <strong> θέλοντες</strong> ] καλῶς εἶπεν · <strong> οἱ θέλοντες</strong> , οὐ γὰρ τῆς τῶν πραγμάτων ἀκολουθίας , ἀλλὰ τῆς ἐκείνων ἀκαίρου φιλονεικίας τὸ πρᾶγμα ἦν . Chrys.
	</p>
<p><strong> τ</strong> <strong> . </strong> <strong> νόμον οὐκ ἀκούετε</strong> ] <strong> do ye not hear</strong> (heed) <strong> the law</strong> , listen to that which the law imparts and impresses on its hearers? Meyer would understand, ‘do ye not hear the law read?’ viz. in the synagogues, &amp;c. But the other seems to me more natural.</p>
