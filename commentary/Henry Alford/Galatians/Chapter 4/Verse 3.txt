<b> 3.] we</b>—are Jews only here included, or Jews and Gentiles? Clearly,<i> both</i>: for “<i>that we might receive the adoption of sons,</i>” ver. 5, is spoken of all believers in Christ. He regards the Jews as, for this purpose, including all mankind (see note on ch. iii. 23), God’s only positive dealings by revelation being with them—and the Gentiles as partakers both in their infant-discipline, and in their emancipation in Christ.<b></b>
