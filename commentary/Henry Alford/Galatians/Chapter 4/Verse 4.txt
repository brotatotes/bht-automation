<b> 4.] the fulness of the time</b> ( ‘that whereby the time was filled up’): answers to the<i> time appointed by the Father,</i> ver. 2. The Apostle uses this term with regard not only to the absolute will of God, but to the preparations which were made for the Redeemer on this earth, The manifestation of mau’s guilt was complete:—and the way of the Lord was prepared, by various courses of action which He had brought about by men as his instruments.
