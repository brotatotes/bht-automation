<b> 1.] With liberty did Christ make us free</b> (i. e.<i> free men</i> is our rightful name and ought to be our estimation of ourselves, seeing that<i> freedom</i> is our inheritance by virtue of Christ’s redemption of us).<b> Stand fast, therefore, and be not again</b> (see note on ch. iv. 9: in fact, the whole world was under the law in the sense of its being God’s only revelation to man)<b> involved in the yoke of bondage.</b>
