So that our verse requires expansion, both in the direction of Romans 7:15-20 , and in the other direction, οὐ γὰρ ὃ θέλω (after the natural man) ποιῶ κακόν · ἀλλ ʼ ὃ οὐ θέλω ἀγαθόν , τοῦτο ποιῶ , to make it logically complete.
