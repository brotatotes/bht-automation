<b> 23.] meekness,</b>—again, towards God and man: and<b> temperance</b>,—the<i> holding-in</i> of the lusts and desires. This verse (see above on ver. 18) substantiates “<i>ye are not under the law</i>”—for if you are led by the Spirit, these are its fruits in you, and against these the law has nothing to say: see 1 Tim. i. 9, 10.
