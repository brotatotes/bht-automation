<b> 4.]</b><i> The test applied:</i> emphasis on<b> work</b>, which is the complex, the whole practical result of his life.<br><br><b> prove]</b> i.e.<b> put to the trial. and then</b> (after he has done this)<b> he will have his matter of boasting, that whereof to boast</b> (not without a slight irony,—whatever matter of boasting he finds, after such a testing, will be)<b> in reference to himself alone, and not in reference to the other</b> (or, his neighbour—the man with whom he was comparing himself: general in its meaning, but particular in each case of comparison).
