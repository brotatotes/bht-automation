<b> 34.</b>]<i> Illustration</i>, in reverse order, of the two particulars mentioned in ver. 33.<b> For ye both sympathized with</b> (see on ch. iv. 15)<b> them who were in bonds, and ye took with joy the plundering of your goods, knowing that ye have for yourselves a better possession and abiding</b> (that cannot be plundered: compare Matt. vi. 20).
