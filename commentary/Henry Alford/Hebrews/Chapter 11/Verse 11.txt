<b> 11.</b>] Example of SARAH,<i> whose faith worked with that of Abraham to produce Isaac.</i><b> By faith Sara herself also</b> (the words<b> herself also</b> merely indicate transition from one personal subject to another, the new subject being thus thrown ont into prominence)<b> received power for the deposition of seed</b> (power, to fructify seed deposed),<b> and that beyond</b> (in inconsistency with, contrary to the law of)<b> the time of age</b> (proper for such fructification),<b> seeing that she esteemed Him faithful who had promised.</b>
