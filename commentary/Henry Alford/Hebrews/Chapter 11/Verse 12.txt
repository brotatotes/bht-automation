<b> 12.</b>]<i> Wonderful result of this faith of Abraham and Sarah.</i><b> Wherefore also from one sprung there, and that one deadened</b> (past that vital power which nature requires),<b> even as the stars of the heaven in multitude, and as the sand which is by the lip</b> (margin)<b> of the sea which is innumerable</b> (so ran the promises to Abraham, Gen. xiii. 16, and more fully Gen. xxii. 17. The comparison th the sand as indicating great number is frequently found in the Old Test., e.g., Gen. xli, 49; Josh. xi. 4; 1 Sam. xiii. 5; 2 Sam. xvi. 11; 1 Kings iv. 29; Isa. x. 22).
