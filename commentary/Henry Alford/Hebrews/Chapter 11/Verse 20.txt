<p><strong> 20</strong> .] <strong> By faith, Isaac blessed Jacob and Esau concerning things future also</strong> (the καί belongs, not to πίστει , πίστει καὶ περὶ μελλ ., by faith and that respecting things future, as Lünem., al. (Syr. joins πίστει περὶ μελλ .), for πίστις περί , though good Greek, is not N. T. language, but to περὶ μελλ ., blessed them concerning not only things present, but things future also. Jacob is named before Esau, as the worthier and more important in the theocratic sense; perhaps also as having gained the greater portion of the blessing).</p>
