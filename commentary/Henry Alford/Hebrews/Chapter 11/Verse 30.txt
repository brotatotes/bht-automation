<b> 30.</b>]<i> A second example of the strength of faith in Israel generally.</i><b> By faith</b> (of Israel, who obeyed the command of Joshua through all the days, which to the unbeliever would seem irrational. Chrysostom, says, “For there is no power in the sound of a trumpet to throw down stones, even if a man go on blowing for a thousand years: but faith can do all things”)<b> the Walls of Jericho fell</b> (see Josh. vi. 5, 20),<b> having been compassed about</b> (see the narrative in Josh. vi.)<b> during seven days.</b>
