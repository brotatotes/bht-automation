<b> 4.] And</b> (couples to ver. 1, of which the subsequent verses have been explanatory)<b> none taketh the office to</b><b> himself</b> (carrying the stress of the sentence);<b> but (only when) called by God, as indeed was Aaron</b> (see Exod. xxviii. 1, xxix. 4; Levit. viii. 1; Num. iii. 10; but especially Num. xvi.–xviii Schöttgen quotes from the Rabbinical book “Moses said to Korah and his fellows, If Aaron my brother had<i> taken to himself</i> the priesthood, ye did rightly in rising against him: but now God has given it to him.”
