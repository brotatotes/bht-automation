<p><strong> 16</strong> .] <em> Justification of the foregoing assertion</em> . <strong> For where is emulation</strong> (in a bad sense) <strong> and rivalry</strong> (see above), <strong> there is confusion</strong> (ref. 1 Cor.: anarchy, restless disturbance. Cf. ref. Prov., στόμα ἄστεγον ποιεῖ ἀκαταστασίας ), <strong> and every evil</strong> (reff.) <strong> thing</strong> (or, deed).</p>
