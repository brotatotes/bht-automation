<p><strong> 2</strong> .] <em> The effect of the coming judgment is depicted as already present</em> , and its material as already stored up against them. What is meant by the figure used, we learn in <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="jas+5:4">James 5:4</span> . <strong> Your riches are corrupted</strong> (see besides reff., <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="job+33:21">Job 33:21</span> ; <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="job+40:7">Job 40:7</span> . <strong> σήπω</strong> is transitive σῆψον δὲ ἀσεβεῖς παραχρῆμα , <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="job+40:7">Job 40:7</span> (12), but <strong> σέσηπα</strong> the perf. middle. The expression is figurative, and <strong> πλοῦτος</strong> to be understood of <em> all</em> riches: ‘your possessions’) <strong> and your garments</strong> (the general term πλοῦτος is now split into its component parts. clothing and treasure) <strong> are become motheaten</strong> (ref.: see also <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="isa+51:8">Isaiah 51:8</span> ; <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="ac+12:23">Acts 12:23</span> .
