<b> 22.]</b> Notice—they ever ask about his<i> person</i>: he ever refers them to his<i> office</i>. He is no one—a<i> voice</i> merely: it is the work of God, the testimony to Christ, which is every thing. So the formalist ever in the church asks concerning any one who appears,<i> Who is he</i>? while the witness for Christ only exalts, only cares for Christ’s work.
