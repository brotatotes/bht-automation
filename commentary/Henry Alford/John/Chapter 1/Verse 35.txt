I can hardly suppose with De Wette, that these two had been absent on the preceding day. Rather, what they then heard seems to have made a powerful impression on their minds, so that the repetition of the notice is now the signal for them to follow Jesus. (On the second disciple, see below on <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="joh+1:41">John 1:41</span> .)</p>
