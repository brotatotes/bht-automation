<b> 9.]</b> The word<b> true</b> in this connexion imports original, ‘archetypal,’ and is used of the true genuine sources and patterns of those things which we find here below only in fragmentary imitations and derivations. Such an<i> original</i> was the light here spoken of;—but John was only a derived light,—not a light<i> lighting</i>, but a light<i> lighted</i>.
