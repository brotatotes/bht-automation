<p><strong> 18.</strong> ] The truth of this voluntary rendering up was shewn by His whole sufferings, from the falling of His enemies to the ground in the garden (ch. Joh 18:6 ) to His last words, <strong> παρατίθεμαι</strong> τὸ πν . μου , <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="lu+23:46">Luke 23:46</span> (see note there). His resurrection also was eminently His own work, by virtue of the Spirit of the Father dwelling in and filling Him: the ἐξουσία in both these cases being the ἐντολή , appointment, ordinance of the Father, from the counsel of whose will the whole mediatorial office of Christ sprung: see ch. <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="joh+12:49">John 12:49</span> .</p>
