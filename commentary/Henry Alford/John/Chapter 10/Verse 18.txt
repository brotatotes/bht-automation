 18. ] The truth of this voluntary rendering up was shewn by His whole sufferings, from the falling of His enemies to the ground in the garden (ch. Joh 18:6 ) to His last words,  παρατίθεμαι τὸ πν . μου , Luke 23:46 (see note there). His resurrection also was eminently His own work, by virtue of the Spirit of the Father dwelling in and filling Him: the ἐξουσία in both these cases being the ἐντολή , appointment, ordinance of the Father, from the counsel of whose will the whole mediatorial office of Christ sprung: see ch. John 12:49 .
