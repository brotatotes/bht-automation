<b> 18.]</b> The truth of this voluntary rendering up was shewn by His whole sufferings, from the falling of His enemies to the ground in the garden (ch. xviii. 6) to His last words,<i> I</i><b> commend</b> (render up)<i> my Spirit,</i> Luke xxiii. 46 (see note there). His resurrection also was eminently His own work, by virtue of the Spirit of the Father dwelling in and filling Him: the<i> power</i> in both these cases being the<i> commandment,</i> appointment, ordinance of the Father, from the counsel of whose will the whole mediatorial office of Christ sprung: see ch. xii, 49.<b></b>
