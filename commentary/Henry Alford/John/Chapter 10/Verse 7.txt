<b> 7.]</b> What follows is not so much an exposition, as an expansion of the allegory.<br><br> The key to this verse is the right understanding of what went before. Bear in mind, that verses 1–5 were spoken of<i> shepherds in general.</i> But these shepherds themselves go into and out of the fold<i> by the same door as the sheep:</i> and Christ<i> is that door;</i> THE DOOR OF THE SHEEP: the<i> one</i> door both for sheep and shepherds, into the fold, into God’s Church, to the Father.
