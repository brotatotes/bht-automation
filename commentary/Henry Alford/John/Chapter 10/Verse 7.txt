<p><strong> 7.</strong> ] What follows is not so much an exposition, as an expansion of the allegory.
	</p>
<p>The key to this verse is the right understanding of what went before. Bear in mind, that Joh 10:1-5 were of <em> shepherds in general</em> . But these shepherds themselves go into and out of the fold <em> by the same door as the sheep:</em> and Christ <em> is that door;</em> THE DOOR OF THE SHEEP: the <em> one</em> door both for sheep and shepherds, into the fold (see <strong> ἡ θύρα</strong> <strong> ,</strong> absol. Joh 10:9 ), into God’s Church, to the Father.</p>
