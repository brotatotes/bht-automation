<b> 9.]</b> expands and fixes ver. 7. “There is no entrance for salvation into the church but by Me, whether it be for shepherd, or for sheep.” Erasmus. See Numb. xxvii. 16, 17. The sequel of the verse shaws that this combined meaning is the true one. Meyer, who understands it all of<i> shepherds alone,</i> finds great difficulty in the interpretation of the latter words: “shall go in and out<i> before the sheep,</i> and find pasture for them,” is certainly a forced meaning.
