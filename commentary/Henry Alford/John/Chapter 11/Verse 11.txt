<b> 11.]</b> The<i> special reason</i> for going, which the disciples appear not to have borne in mind, having probably supposed from ver. 4 that Lazarus would recover.<br><br><b> Our friend]</b> Bengel notices, with what condescension our Lord shares the friendship with His disciples. And the word<b> our</b> gives a reason why<i> they</i> should go too.<br><br> This term,<b> is fallen asleep,</b> might have recalled to<i> three at least</i> of the disciples that other saying, Matt. ix. 24. But the former expression, “<i>is not unto death,</i>” had not been understood,—and that error ruled in their minds.
