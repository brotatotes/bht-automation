<p><strong> 2.</strong> ] Another reference ιο a fact which, as our Lord prophesied, was known wherever the gospel was preached. This reference containing, as it does, the expression τὸν κύριον (= <strong> our Lord</strong> ), q. d. ‘as we all well know,’ is a striking illustration of that prophecy. John himself relates the occurrence, ch. <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="joh+12:3">John 12:3</span> , being necessary for the course of his narrative.</p>
