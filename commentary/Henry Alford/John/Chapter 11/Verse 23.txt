<p><strong> 23.</strong> ] I believe these words of our Lord to contain no allusion to the <em> immediate</em> restoration of Lazarus; but to be pædagogically used, to lead on to the requisite faith in her mind. I have to learn whether ἀναστήσεται in this direct absolute sense could be used of his <em> recall into human life</em> .</p>
