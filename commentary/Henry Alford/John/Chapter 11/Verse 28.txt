<p><strong> φωνεῖ σε</strong> ] This is not recorded. Stier thinks that the <strong> Lord</strong> had not actually asked for her, but that Martha sees such an especial fitness for her hearing in the words of <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="joh+11:25-26">John 11:25-26</span> , that she uses this expression. But is it not somewhat too plainly asserted, to mean only <em> calling by inference</em> ? Meyer regards the <strong> φωνεῖ σε</strong> as proving it to have been a fact.</p>
