The caves were generally horizontal, natural or artificial,—with recesses in the sides, where the bodies were laid. There is no necessity here for supposing the entrance to have been otherwise than horizontal, as the word<i> cave</i> would lead us to believe. Graves were of both kinds: we have the vertically sunk mentioned Luke xi. 44. Compare Isa. xxii. 16; 2 Chron. xvi. 14; 2 Kings xxiii. 16.<br><br>Probably, from this circumstance, as from ‘the Jews’ coming to condole,—and the costly ointment (ch. xii. 3),—the family was wealthy.
