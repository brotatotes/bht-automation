 40. ] I can hardly think she supposed merely that Jesus desired to  look on the face of the dead; she expected  something was about to be done, but in her anxiety for decorum ( Luk 10:40 ) she was willing to avoid the consequence of opening the cave. This feeling Jesus here rebukes, by referring her to the plain duty of simple faith, insisted on by Him before ( Joh 11:25-26 ? or in some other teaching?) as the condition of beholding the glory of God (not merely in the event about to follow, for that was seen by many who did not believe, but in a deeper sense, that of the unfolding of the ἀνάστασις κ . ζωή in the personal being).
