<b> 54.]</b> Observe the word<b> Jews</b> here, used as designating the official body. He was still among<i> Jews</i> at Ephraim. This city is mentioned 2 Chron. xiii. 19 in connexion with Bethel, as also by Josephus. It was near to the wilderness, i.e. to the desert of Judah. Its situation is at present unknown. Robinson supposes it to be the same with Ophrah (Josh. xviii. 23: 1 Sam. xiii. 17: not Judg. vi. 11, 24; viii. 27) and Ephron of the O.T. (2 Chron. xiii. 19), and the modern et-Taiyibeh, twenty Roman miles from Jerusalem.
