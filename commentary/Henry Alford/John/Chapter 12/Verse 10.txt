<b> 10.] consulted,</b> not, ‘<i>came to a</i> (formal)<i> resolution,</i>’ but<b> were in the mind,—had an intention:</b> see Acts v. 33; xv. 37.<br><br> The chief priests, named here and in ch. xi. 57, were of the sect of the Sadducees; and therefore disbelieved the<i> fact</i> of the raising of Lazarus; only viewing him as one whom it would be desirable to put out of the way, as an object of popular attention in connexion with Jesus.
