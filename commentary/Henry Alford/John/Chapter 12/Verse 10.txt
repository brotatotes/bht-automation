<p><strong> 10.</strong> ] <strong> ἐβουλ</strong> <strong> .,</strong> not, ‘ <em> came to a</em> (formal) <em> resolution</em> ,’ but <strong> were in the mind,</strong> <strong> had an intention:</strong> see <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="ac+5:33">Acts 5:33</span> ; <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="ac+15:37">Acts 15:37</span> .
	</p>
<p>The chief priests, named here and in ch. <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="joh+11:57">John 11:57</span> , were of the sect of the Sadducees; and therefore disbelieved the <em> fact</em> of the raising of Lazarus; only viewing him as one whom it would be desirable to put out of the way, as an object of popular attention in connexion with Jesus.</p>
