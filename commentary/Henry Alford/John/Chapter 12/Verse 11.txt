<b> 11. were going away</b> (to Bethany)<b>].</b> The word contains in it the sense of<i> mere falling away,</i> viz. from under the hand and power of the chief priests.<br><br><b> when they heard]</b> From the multitude who had returned from Bethany, ver. 9.
