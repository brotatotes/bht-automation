<p><strong> 20.</strong> ] These <strong> Ἕλληνες</strong> were not <em> Grecian Jews</em> , who would not have been so called: but <em> Gentiles</em> , ‘proselytes of the gate,’ who were in the habit (implied by the pres. part. <strong> ἀναβαινόντων</strong> ) of coming up to the feast: see ch. Joh 7:35 reff. and note; also <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="ac+8:27">Acts 8:27</span> .</p>
