<p><strong> 21.</strong> ] For what reason <em> Philip</em> was selected, it is impossible to say. The Greek form of his name may imply some connexion with Hellenistic Jews, who may have been friends or relatives of these Greeks. If they were <em> from the neighbourhood of Bethsaida</em> , they would indeed have been familiar with the person of Jesus: but what they here requested was evidently a private interview.</p>
