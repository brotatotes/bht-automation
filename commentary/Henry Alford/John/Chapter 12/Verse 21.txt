<b> 21.]</b> For what reason<i> Philip</i> was selected, it is impossible to say. ‘The form of his name is Greek, and may imply some connexion with Grecian Jews, who muy have been friends or relatives of these Greeks. If they were from the neighbourhood of Bethsaida, they would indeed have been familiar with the person of Jesus:—but what they here requested was evidently a private interview.
