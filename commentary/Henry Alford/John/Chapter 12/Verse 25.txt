<p><strong> ψυχή</strong> is not <em> really</em> in a double sense: as the wheat-corn retains its identity, though it die, so the ψυχή : so that the two senses are, in their depth, but one. ψυχή is <em> the life</em> in both cases; not the <em> soul</em> , in the present acceptation of that term.</p>
