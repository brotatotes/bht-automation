<b> 26.]</b> Connexion:—The ministering to, or intimate union with, Christ (the position of Philip and Andrew and the rest, and that into which these Greeks seemed desirous to enter) implies<i> following</i> Him,—and that, through tribulation to glory.<br><br><b> where I am]</b> The word refers, not to the place of our Lord at that moment, but to His essential, true place, i.e. (ch. xvii. 24) in the glory of the Father.<br><br><b> him will the Father honour]</b> By glorifying him in My glorification, ch. xvii. 24.
