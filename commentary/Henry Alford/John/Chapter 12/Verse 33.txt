<b> 33. by what manner of death]</b> The words here can hardly point to more than the external circumstances of His death. Some have found in the expression the whole<i> consequences</i> and<i> character</i> of His Death; but see ch. xviii. 32.<br><br> St. John does not say that this was<i> all</i> that the “<i>lifting up</i>” meant, but that it was its first and obvious reference.
