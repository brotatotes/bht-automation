<p><strong> 40.</strong> ] The prophecy is freely cited, after neither the Heb. nor the LXX, which is followed in <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="mt+13:14">Matthew 13:14</span> f. What God <em> bids</em> the Prophet <em> do</em> , is here described as <em> done</em> , and by Himself: which is obviously <em> implied</em> in the Heb. text. The reading αὑτῶν (Morus) supplying ὁ λαὸς οὗτος as the subject of τετύφ . and πεπώρ ., is out of the question, as ungrammatical, and inconsistent with the context, which will only allow of ὁ κύριος (i.e. Jehovah) as the <em> subject</em> .</p>
