<b> 15.]</b> Notice that our Lord commands us to do, not “<i>that which</i><b> I</b> have done to you,” but “<b>as,</b><i> in like manner as,</i> I have done to you.” Our Lord’s action was symbolical, and is best imitated in His followers by endeavouring, “if a man be overtaken in a fault, to restore such an one in the spirit of meekness:” Gal. vi. 1.
