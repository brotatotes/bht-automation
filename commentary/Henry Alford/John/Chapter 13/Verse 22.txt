We seem called on here to decide a much-controverted question,  where in John’s narrative the institution of the Lord’s supper is to be inserted? I believe certainly  before this announcement, as in Luke: and if before it,  perhaps before the washing of the disciples’ feet: for I see no break which would admit it between our Joh 13:1 and John 13:21 .
