<p><strong> 25.</strong> ] <strong> ἀναπεσών</strong> <strong> , leaning back on the bosom of Jesus.
	</strong></p>
<p><strong> οὕτως</strong> <strong> ,</strong> as in ref. I understand it, that John, who was before lying <em> close to</em> the bosom ( ἐν τῷ κόλπῳ ) of Jesus, now leaned his head absolutely <em> upon</em> His breast, to ask the question. This escaped the notice of the rest at the table: see on Matt. as above.</p>
