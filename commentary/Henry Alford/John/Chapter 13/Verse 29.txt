<b> 29.]</b> The first supposition agrees with ver. 1,—that it was “<i>before the feast of the passover</i>.” Had it been the night of the passover, the next day being hallowed as a sabbath, nothing<i> could have been bought</i>. On the whole question see notes on Matt. xxvi. 17, and cy xviii. 28. On the<i> second</i> supposition, see ch. xii. 5. The gift to the poor might be, to<i> help them to procure their paschal lamb.</i>
