<b> 34.]</b> The<i> newness</i> of this commandment, consists in its<i> simplicity</i>, and (so to speak)<i> unicity</i>. The same<i> kind</i> of love was prescribed in the Old Test. (see Rom. xiii. 8):— ‘as thyself’ is the<i> highest</i> measure of love, and it is therefore not in degree that the new commandment differs from the old, nor in<i> extent</i>, but in being<i> the</i> commandment of the new covenant,—the firstfruit of the Spirit in the new dispensation (Gal. v. 22); see 1 John ii. 7, 8 (and note), where the word<b> new</b> is commented on by the Apostle himself.
