<p><strong> 4.</strong> ] <strong> τὰ ἱμ</strong> <strong> .,</strong> “eas, quæ lotionem impedirent.” Bengel. He put Himself into the ordinary dress of a servant. Or, which is far more probable, on the deepest grounds, did He not humble Himself so far as <em> literally</em> to divest Himself, and gird Himself merely, as the basest of slaves?</p>
