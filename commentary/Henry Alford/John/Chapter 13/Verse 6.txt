<b> 6.]</b> And<b> so</b> (the<b> so</b> taking up the narrative again after the word<b> began</b>, as if it were said, ‘in pursuance of this intention’) He comes to Simon Peter; not<i> first,</i> as some have maintained, both with and without reference to the primacy of Peter:—for that. would be hardly consistent (see on the preceding verse) with the context, which seems to require that the washing should have begun and been going on, before He came to Peter.
