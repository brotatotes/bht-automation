<b> 22.]</b> Judas,<b> not Iscariot</b> is the same person as “<i>Judas</i><i> the [brother] of James,</i>” in Luke vi. 16: see note on Matt. x. 3. Meyer remarks that the words<b> not Iscariot</b> are in reality superfluous, after ch. xiii. 30, but are added by St. John from his deep horror of the Traitor who bore the same name.
