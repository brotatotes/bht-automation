<b> 25.</b>]<b> have I spoken</b> is anticipatory, referring, as “<i>I said</i>” (ver. 26), to the futures, “<i>shall teach,</i>” and “<i>shall bring to remembrance.</i>” Meyer supposes that a<i> pause</i> took place here, and the Lord looks back on what He had said to them. But this does not seem so natural.
