<b> 29.</b>]<b> I have told you</b>—viz. ‘the prophecies of My Resurrection and Ascension,’&c.<br><br><b> ye may believe</b>] See ch. xiii. 19, where the words “<i>that I am He</i>” are supplied.<b> That ye may believe</b>, in the fullest sense of the word. “Not that they did not previously believe Him to be the Son of God: but then, when that was fulfilled in Him, which He had before predieted,—this their faith, which now, when he was speaking to them, was small, and, when He died, was almost extinguished, revived and flourished.” Augustine. See 1 John v. 13.
