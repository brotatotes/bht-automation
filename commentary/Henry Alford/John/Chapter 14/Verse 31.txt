<b> 31.</b>] ‘But My Death is an act of voluntary obedience, that it may be known that I love and obey the Father—that the glory of the Father in and by Me may be manifested.’ The construction is elliptic: supply, ‘But (his power over Me for death will be permitted by Me) that,’&c. And the period should be at<b> so I do</b>, as in the text.
