<b> 5.]</b> Thomas is slow of belief and apprehension. The answer to “<i>whither goest thou?</i>” ch. xiii. 37, which Peter seems to have apprehended, was not sufficient for him; see ch. xx. 25: “for he thought,” says Euthymius, “that it was some material place to which the Lord was going, and that the road thither was of the same kind.”
