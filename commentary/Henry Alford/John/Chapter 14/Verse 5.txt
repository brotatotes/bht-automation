<p><strong> 5.</strong> ] Thomas is slow of belief and apprehension. The answer to ποῦ ὑπάγεις ; ch. <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="joh+13:37">John 13:37</span> , which Peter seems to have apprehended, was not sufficient for him: see ch. <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="joh+20:25">John 20:25</span> : ᾤετο γάρ , says Euthym [192] , αἰσθητὸν εἶναί τινα τόπον ὅπου ὑπάγει , καὶ ὁδὸν ὁμοίως τοιαύτην .
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:3em;"> [192] Euthymius Zigabenus, 1116</p>
