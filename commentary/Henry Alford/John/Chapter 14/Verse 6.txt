<b> 6.]</b> Our Lord inverts the order of Thomas’s question, and in answering it practically,<i> for them</i>, speaks of ‘the Way’ first.<i> He</i> is THE WAY; not merely the Forerunner; which would imply on our part only an outward connexion with Him as His<i> followers</i>: but<i> the way</i>, in and on which we must go, having an inner union with and in Him (see Heb. x. 20).
