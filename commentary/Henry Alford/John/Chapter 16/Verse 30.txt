<b> 30.]</b> ‘Thou hast spoken so clearly of our feeling towards Thee, and of Thyself, that we have no occasion to ask Thee any thing;—and this was what Thou didst announce would be;—we know therefore, by its being so, that Thou knowest the secrets of our hearts (<b>all things</b> by inference),—and hence believe that Thou camest forth from God.’ The whole being a understanding of what had gone before, vv. 23, 25.
