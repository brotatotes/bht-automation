<b> 8.</b>]<b> I have given unto them the words...</b>, and the similar sayings ch. xv. 15 al., seem to be a reference to Deut. xviii. 18, 19, where it is said that the Prophet ‘shall speak unto them all that I shall command Him.’ The imparting to them of these “<i>words</i>” was the efficient cause of their faith:—see their confession ch. vi. 68, 69, where “<i>we have believed and know</i>” are connected as here.
