<b> 22.</b>] See Acts xxiii. 2.<br><br><b> one of the officers which stood by</b>] This was probably one of the band who took Jesus, and had brought Him hither. It is not quite certain whether the word here used implies a blow with the hand, or with a staff. They had staves, and perhaps thus used them: see note on Matt. xxvi. 67. This blow was a signal for the indignities which followed.
