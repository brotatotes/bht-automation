<b> 8.</b>] Bengel strikingly says of this reply of our Lord, “He will say it once again hereafter.” And Augustine, “What will He do when He cometh to judge, who did this when He was to be judged? What will be His power when about to reign, who could do this when about to die?”<br><br><b> let these go their way</b>] The band of soldiers, in their ignorance, appear to have been laying hands on the Apostles. This saying was sufficient to shew Peter and the rest what was the appointed course for them;—the command to<i> let them</i> go addressed to the band, is a command for<i> them to go</i>, when interpreted by the Apostles.
