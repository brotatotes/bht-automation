<b> 14. the preparation of the passover</b>] The signification, ‘Friday in the Passover week,’ has found many, and some recent, defenders. But this is not the natural meaning of the words, nor would it ever have been thought of in this place, but for the difficulty arising from the whole Passover question, which I have discussed on Matt. xxvi. 17–19, and on ch. xviii. 28.
