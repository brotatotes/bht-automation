<p><strong> 36.</strong> ] ‘For’ i.e. as connected with the true Messiahship of Christ, ‘these things were a fulfilment of Scripture.’ It is possible that <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="ps+33:20">Psalms 33:20</span> (LXX) may be also referred to; but no doubt the primary reference is to the Paschal Lamb of Exod., as in reff.: see <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="1co+5:7">1 Corinthians 5:7</span> .</p>
