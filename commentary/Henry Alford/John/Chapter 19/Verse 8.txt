<p><strong> 8.</strong> ] This charge served to increase the fear which Pilate had before: see note on ch. <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="joh+18:37">John 18:37</span> . The name <strong> υἱὸς θεοῦ</strong> served also to confirm the omen already furnished by the dream of his wife. That this fear was not a fear <em> of the Jews</em> , nor <em> of acting unjustly</em> , but of the Person of Jesus, is evident from what follows.</p>
