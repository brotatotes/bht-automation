<p><strong> 2, 3. </strong> <strong> κ</strong> <strong> . </strong> <strong> ἤρχοντο πρ</strong> <strong> . </strong> <strong> αὐτ</strong> <strong> .</strong> ] This has been perhaps erased as not being understood. It was their mock-reverential approach, as to a crowned king: coming probably with obeisances and pretended homage. In the χαῖρε ὁ β . τ . Ἰουδαίων , “non tam Christum derident, quam Judæis insultant:” Lampe. See notes on <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="mt+27:27-30">Matthew 27:27-30</span> ; and on πορφύραν , <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="mr+15:17">Mark 15:17</span> .</p>
