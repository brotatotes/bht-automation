 13. ] No data are given to determine whether the reason of the short stay at Capernaum was the near approach of the Passover.
	Nothing is said of those who accompanied Jesus: but at all events,  His already called disciples would be with Him (see John 2:22 , and ch. Joh 3:22 ), and among them in all probability the Evangelist himself: but  not the rest of the Twelve , who were not yet called. Of this visit, the synoptic narrative records nothing.
