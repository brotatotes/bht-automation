<b> 17.] his disciples remembered,</b><i> at the time</i>, not afterwards, which would have been expressed, as in ver. 22. But the very remembrance itself was prophetic. The “<i>eating up</i>” spoken of in that passion-Psalm, was the marring and wasting of the Saviour’s frame by His zeal for God and God’s Church, which resulted in the buffeting, the scourging, the Cross.
