<p><strong> 18.</strong> ] On the demand of the Jews, see <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="de+13:1-3">Deuteronomy 13:1-3</span> . It was not only to justify His having driven out the abomination; this any one might have done; but to justify the mission and the whole course of action which the words τοῦ πατρός μου implied. They used the same expression at the end of His ministry, <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="mt+21:23">Matthew 21:23</span> .</p>
