 13. ] Here again the finest psychological truth underlies the narrative. The other women (Mark 16:5 ; Luk 24:5 )  were afraid at the vision; but now Mary, having but one thought or desire, to recover the lost Body of her Lord ( τὸν κύριόν  μου ),  feels no fear .
	The angels doubtless are proceeding further to assure her as they did the women before: but this is broken off by the appearance of the Lord Himself, or perhaps by Mary’s turning away.
