<b> 30.</b>]<b> Yea, and</b>,—or,<b> moreover</b>: meaning, ‘This book must not be supposed to be a complete account.’<br><br><b> signs</b>] not, as many interpret the word, ‘proofs of His resurrection,’—but, as ch. xii. 37 and elsewhere in this Gospel,<b> miracles</b>, in the most general sense—these after the Resurrection included:—for St. John is here reviewing his whole narrative,<b> this book</b>.
