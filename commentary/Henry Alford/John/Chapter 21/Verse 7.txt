<b> 7.</b>] The<b> therefore</b> here seems distinctly to allude to that former occasion in Luke v. 1 ff.—the similarity of the incident having led the beloved Apostle to scrutinize more closely the person of Him who spoke to them. “John is the more keen-sighted, Peter the more ardent. So John recognizes Him before Peter; but Peter goes forth to Him before John.” Euthymius.
