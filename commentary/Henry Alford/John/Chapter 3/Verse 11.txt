<b> 11.]</b> Henceforward the discourse is an answer to the<i> unbelief</i>, and in answering that, to the<i> question</i> (<b>How can these things be?</b>) of Nicodemus: by shewing him the appointed means of this. new birth, and of being upheld in the life to which it is the entrance, viz.<i> faith in the Son of God</i>.
