<b> 13.]</b> The whole verse seems to have intimate connexion with and reference to Prov. xxx. 4, “Who hath ascended up to heaven, or descended?” and as spoken to a learned doctor of the law, would recall that verse,—especially as the further question is there asked, ‘Who hath gathered<i> the wind</i> in His fists?’ and ‘What is His name, and what His Son’s name?’ See also Deut. xxx. 12, and the citation, Rom. x. 6–8.
