<b> 22.] After these things</b>: the sequence is not<i> immediate</i>; for this, St. John uses “<i>after this</i>” or “<i>that</i>,” see ch. xi. 7, 11; xix. 28.<br><br><b> the land of Judæa</b>] The rural districts of Judæa, in distinction from the metropolis.<br><br><b> baptized</b>, viz. by means of His disciples;—see ch. iv. 2, and note. The place is not named: perhaps He did not remain in one fixed spot.
