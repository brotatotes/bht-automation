<b> 28.]</b> “Not only so, but I have always given the same consistent testimony; that I was only the forerunner of One greater than myself.’ The word<b> him</b> in the original does not refer to “<i>the Christ</i>:” but<i> to Jesus</i>, as the subject of ver. 26; and thus is not merely a<i> general</i> testimony with regard to the Messiah, but a<i> personal</i> one to Jesus. In reading this verse therefore, strong emphasis should be laid on the word<b> him</b>.
