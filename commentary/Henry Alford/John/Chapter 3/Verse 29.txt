<b> 29.]</b> Here first, (and here only in our Gospel,) comes, from the mouth of the Forerunner, this great symbolical reference, which is so common in the other Gospels and in the Epistles. It is remarkable that our Lord brings it forward in His answer<i> to the disciples of John</i> respecting fasting, Matt. ix. 15: where see note on the further import of the terms used.
