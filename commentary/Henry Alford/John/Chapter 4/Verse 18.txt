<b> 18.]</b> There was<i> literal</i> truth, but no more, in the woman’s answer: and the Lord, by His divine knowledge, detects the hidden falsehood of it. Notice it is<b> true</b> (a fact—bare truth), not<b> truly</b>: this<i> one word</i> was<i> true</i>: further shewn by the emphatic position of the word<b> husband</b> in our Lord’s answer,—which was not so placed in hers.
