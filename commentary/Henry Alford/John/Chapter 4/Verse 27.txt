<b> 27.]</b> The ground of their wonder, as given in the original, was the circumstance, that our Lord was talking with<b> a woman</b>. None of them said either—<i>to the woman</i><b>—What seekest thou?</b> or to the Lord,<b> Why disputest thou,</b> or<b> Why talkest thou with her?</b>—or perhaps both questions<i> to Him. Why talkest thou with her</i>?—I rather prefer the former interpretation.
