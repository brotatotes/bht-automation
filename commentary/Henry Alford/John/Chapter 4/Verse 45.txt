<b> 45.]</b> They received Him, but in accordance with the proverbial saying just recorded;—not for any honour in which they themselves held Him, or value which they had for His teaching; but<i> on account of His fame in Jerusalem</i>, the metropolis,—which set them the fashion in their estimate of men and things.<br><br><b> for they also went unto the feast</b> is inserted for those readers who might not be aware of the practice of the Galileans to frequent the feasts at Jerusalem.
