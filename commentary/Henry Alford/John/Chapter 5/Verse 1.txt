<b> 1. After these things]</b> Lücke remarks that when John wishes to indicate immediate succession, he uses “<i>after this</i>” (or “<i>that</i>”), ch. ii. 12; xi. 7, 11; xix. 28; when mediate, after an interval, “<i>after these things</i>,” ch. iii. 22; v. 14; vi. 1; vii. 1; xix. 38. So that apart from other considerations which would lead us to the same conclusion, we may infer that some interval has elapsed since the last verse of ch. iv.
