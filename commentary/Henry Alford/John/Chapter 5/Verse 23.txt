<b> 23.]</b> This being so, the end of all is,<i> the honour of the Father in and by the Son</i>. He (the Son) is the Lord of life, and the Judge of the world;—all must honour Him with equal honour to that which they pay to the Father:—and whosoever does not, however he may imagine that he honours or approaches God, does not honour Him at all;—because<i> He can only be known or honoured by us as</i> ‘THE FATHER wHO SENT HIS SON.’
