<b> 25.]</b> This verse continues to refer to<i> spiritual</i> awakening from the dead. The words<b> The hour is coming, and now is</b> are an expression used of those things which are to characterize the spiritual Kingdom of Christ, which was even now begun among men, but not yet brought (until the day of Pentecost, Acts ii.) to its completion. ‘Thus<b> it cometh</b>, in its fulness,—and<b> even now is begun</b>.
