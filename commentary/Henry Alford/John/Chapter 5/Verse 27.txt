Then again the Father hath given Him power to pass judgment,<i> because He is</i> THE SON OF MAN; man is to be judged by Man,—by that Man whom God hath appointed, who is the inclusive Head of humanity, and to whom mankind, and man’s world, pertain by right of covenant-purchase. This<i> executing judgment</i> leads the thought to the<i> great occasion</i> when judgment shall be executed; which accordingly is treated of in the next verse.<b> 28.] Marvel not at this</b>, as in ch. iii, 7, introduces a matter of even<i> greater</i> wonder to them;—the astounding proof which shall be given in the face of the universe that this is so.
