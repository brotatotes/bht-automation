<p><strong> 41.</strong> ] <strong> οὐ λαμβ</strong> <strong> .,</strong> not merely, ‘ <em> I do not desire,’ ‘non capto;</em> ’ but, ‘ <strong> I do not</strong> <em> receive;</em> ’ ‘no such praise nor testimony accrues to Me, nor has in Me that on which it can lay hold.’ ‘My glory is altogether from another source.’</p>
