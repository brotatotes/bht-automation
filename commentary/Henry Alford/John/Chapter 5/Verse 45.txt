 45. ] The work of Christ is not  κατηγορεῖν  , even as He is Judge; but  κρίνειν  , by the appointment of the Father. And therefore though He has said so much of the unbelief of the Jews, and charged them in the last verse with breach of the central law of God  He will not accuse them; nay, it is not needful; for Moses, whom they disbelieved, while vainly hoping in him (see above on Joh 5:39 ), ἐπαναπαυόμενοι νόμῳ , Romans 2:17 ,  already accused them: see Deuteronomy 31:21 ; Deuteronomy 31:26 , and ch. John 7:19 .
