<b> 11.]</b> On the process of the miracle, see notes on Matthew. St. John describes the distribution as being the act of the Lord Himself, and leaves the intervention of the disciples to be understood.<br><br> The<i> giving thanks</i> here answers to<i> blessing</i> in the other Gospels. It was the ‘<i>grace</i>’ of the father of the family; perhaps the ordinary one in use among the Jews. St. John seems to connect with it the idea brought out by St. Luke, “<i>He blessed</i><b> them</b>,” i.e.<i> the loaves</i>: see ver. 23.
