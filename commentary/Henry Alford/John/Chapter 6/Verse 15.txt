<b> 15.]</b> After such a recognition, nothing was wanting but that the multitudes who were journeying to the Passover should take Jesus with them, and proclaim Him king of the Jews in the holy City itself.<br><br> The other three Evangelists, while they do not give any intimation of this reason of our Lord’s withdrawal, relate the<i> fact</i>, and St. Luke preserves in the very next verse<i> a trace of its motive</i>,—by the question<i> ‘Whom do the people say that I am?’</i> and the answer, expressing the very confession of the people here.
