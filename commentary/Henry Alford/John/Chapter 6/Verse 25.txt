<p><strong> 25.</strong> ] <strong> πέραν τ</strong> <strong> . </strong> <strong> θαλ</strong> <strong> .</strong> is <em> now</em> the <em> west</em> bank; we have been crossing the sea with the multitude.

<p><strong> πότε</strong> <strong> ,</strong> as Stier remarks, includes πῶς in its meaning. Our Lord leaves the question unanswered, because it was not for <em> a sign to these people</em> that He had miraculously crossed the lake.</p>
