<b> 28.]</b> The people understand His<b> working</b><i> literally</i>, and dwell upon it. They quite seem to think that the food which is to endure for ever is to be spiritually interpreted; and they therefore ask this question,—referring the “<i>working</i>” to the works of the law.<br><br><b> the works of God</b> must not be taken to mean ‘<i>the works which God works</i>,’ but, as in Jer. xlviii. 10; 1 Cor. xv. 58,<b> the works well pleasing to God</b>.
