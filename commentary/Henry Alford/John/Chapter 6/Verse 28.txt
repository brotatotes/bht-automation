<p><strong> 28.</strong> ] The people understand His ἐργάζεσθε <em> literally</em> , and dwell upon it. They quite seem to think that the food which is to endure for ever is to be spiritually interpreted; and they therefore ask this question, referring the ἐργάζ . to the works of the law.
	</p>
<p><strong> τὰ ἔργα τοῦ θεοῦ</strong> must not be taken to mean ‘ <em> the works which God works</em> ,’ but, as in <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="jer+48:10">Jeremiah 48:10</span> ( Jer 31:10 LXX): <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="1co+15:58">1 Corinthians 15:58</span> , <strong> the works well pleasing to God</strong> .</p>
