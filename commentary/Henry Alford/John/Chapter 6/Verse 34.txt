<p><strong> 34.</strong> ] Ch. Joh 4:15 is exactly parallel. The Jews understand this bread, as the Samaritan woman understood the water, to be some miraculous kind of sustenance which would bestow life everlasting: perhaps they thought of the heavenly manna, which the Rabbis speak of as prepared for the just in the future world: see quotations in Lücke, 2:132, also <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="re+2:17">Revelation 2:17</span> .
