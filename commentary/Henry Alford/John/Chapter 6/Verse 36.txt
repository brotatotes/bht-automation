<b> 36. I said unto you]</b> “When did He say this to them? perhaps it was said, but has not been recorded.” Euthymius. But perhaps the reference may be to ch. v. 37–44, and<b> unto you</b> may be said generally. Stier and others think that ver. 26 is referred to: but this is far-fetched.<br><br> We have instances of reference to sayings not recorded, in ch. x. 26; xii. 34.<br><br><b> have even seen]</b> ‘Ye have seen the true Bread from heaven, the<i> sign</i> greater than the manna,<i> even Me Myself</i>: and yet have not believed.’
