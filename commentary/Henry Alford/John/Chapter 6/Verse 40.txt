The looketh on</b> here is a different thing from the mere<i> seeing</i> of ver. 36. It is the awakening of the attention preparatory to faith, answering to the looking on the serpent of brass:<i> with the eyes of the soul</i>, as Euthymius says; but we must not make the<i> looking</i> equivalent to<i> believing</i>, to which it is only preparatory.
