<b> 46.]</b> The connexion is: the mention of<b> hearing from the Father</b> might lead them to think of a personal communication from the Father to each man, and thus the necessity of the mission of the Son might be invalidated. This was the only way in which a Jew could misunderstand ver. 45; he could not dream of a seeing of the Father with bodily eyes.<br><br><b> he which is of God, is Jesus Himself</b>; see ch. vii. 29. His knowledge of the Father is<i> complete</i> and immediate; ours,<i> partial</i>, and derived through Him only.
