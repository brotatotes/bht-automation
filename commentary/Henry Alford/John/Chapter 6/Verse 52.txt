<b> 52.]</b> The inference conveyed in the word<b> eat</b>, which word, be it noted,<i> first comes from the Jews themselves</i>, is yet a right one. If He is the Bread, and that Bread is His Flesh, we must<i> eat His Flesh</i>, though not in the sense here meant by them. They contended against one another, probably some having more insight into the possibility of a spiritual meaning than others.
