 56. ]  He who thus lives upon Me, abides in Me (see ch. Joh 15:5 and note);  and I (that living power and nourishment conveyed by the ἄρτος τῆς ζωῆς which = ἐγώ )  abide in him. Beware of imagining, as Bp. Wordsw. again (see note on Mat 16:18 ), that there is any especial emphasis on  μου  because of its position .
