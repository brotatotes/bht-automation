<p><strong> 65.</strong> ] These unbelievers had not that <em> drawing to Christ</em> , which leads ( Joh 6:44 ) to true coming to Him. Observe the parallelism between ᾖ δεδομένον αὐτῷ here, and ὃ δίδωσίν μοι , <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="joh+6:37">John 6:37</span> . Both these gifts are in the Father’s power.</p>
