<p><strong> 22 24.</strong> ] These verses are involved and parenthetical in construction, but very characteristic of the minute care with which the Evangelist will account for every circumstance which is essential to his purpose in the narrative.
	</p>
<p><strong> ὁ ὄχλος</strong> ] We are not to understand <em> the whole multitude who were fed</em> , but that portion of them which had remained on the coast over the night. Many had probably dispersed to the villages about, or perhaps taken up their night quarters more inland.
	</p>
