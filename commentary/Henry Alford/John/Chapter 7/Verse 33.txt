The words were spoken, not to the officers only, but to all the people.<br><br><b> Yet a little while....]</b> This appears to be said in reference to ver. 30, to shew them the uselessness of their attempting to lay hands on Him till His hour was come, which it soon would do.<br><br><b> unto him that sent me]</b> It has been asked, ‘If Jesus thus specified where He was going, how could the Jews ask the question in ver. 35?’ but De Wette answers well, that the Jews knew not “<i>Him that sent Him</i>,” and therefore the saying was a dark one to them.
