<b> 50</b>.] The Jews had, since the sabbath-healing, condemned Jesus, and were seeking to kill him. But in Exod. xxiii. 1, 2; Deut. 1. 16, 17, justice is commanded to be done in the way here insisted on by Nicodemus. Observe the consistency, and development, of the character of Nicodemus; and see more on ch. xix. 39.
