<p><strong> 24.</strong> ] Since this ( Joh 8:23 ) is the case, <strong> if ye do not believe that I am He,</strong> the Deliverer, and be renewed by Faith, <strong> ye shall die in your sins</strong> (plural here, as struck nearer home to their consciences, and implying individual acts of sin, the results of the carnal state).
