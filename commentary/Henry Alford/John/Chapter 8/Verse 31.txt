<p><strong> 31.</strong> ] <strong> ἐν τῷ λ</strong> <strong> . </strong> <strong> τῷ ἐμῷ</strong> = ἐν ἐμοί , ch. <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="joh+15:7">John 15:7</span> , though that perhaps is spoken of a deeper entrance into the state of union with Christ. <em> Remaining in His word</em> is not merely <em> obeying His teaching</em> , but is the inner conviction of the truth of that revelation of Himself, which is his λαλιά or λόγος .
	</p>
<p><strong> ἐστέ</strong> <strong> ,</strong> for probably they had given some outward token of believing on Him, e.g. that of ranging themselves among His disciples.</p>
