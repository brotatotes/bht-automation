<b> 34.] doeth sin</b>, not merely “<i>sinneth</i>,” for that all do; but in the same sense as “<i>work iniquity</i>” is said, Matt. vii. 23. It implies<b> living in the practice of sin, doing sin</b>, as a habit: see reff. ‘he mere moral sentiment, of which this is the spiritual expression, was common among the Greek and Roman philosophers.
