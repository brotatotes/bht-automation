<b> 42.</b>] ‘If you were the children of God, the<i> moral proof</i> of such descent would be, that you would love Me, who am specially the Son of God, and who am come by the mission, and bearing the character of God.’<br><br><b> am come</b> conveys the result of<b> proceeded forth</b>, as Meyer; who also remarks that mere<i> sending</i> will not exhaust the term<b> proceeded forth</b>, which must be taken in its deeper theological meaning, of the proceeding forth of the Eternal Son from the essence of the Father.
