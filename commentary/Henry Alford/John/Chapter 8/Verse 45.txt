<p><strong> 45.</strong> ] ‘And the very reason why ye do not believe <em> Me</em> (as contrasted with <em> him</em> ) is, <em> because I speak the truth</em> ; you <em> not being of the truth</em> , but of him who is falsehood itself.’ This implies a charge of wilful striving against known and recognized truth. Euthymius fills up the context εἰ μὲν ἔλεγον ψεῦδος , ἐπιστεύσατέ μοι ἄν , ὡς τὸ ἴδιον τοῦ πατρὸς ὑμῶν λέγοντι : see ch. <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="joh+5:43">John 5:43</span> .</p>
