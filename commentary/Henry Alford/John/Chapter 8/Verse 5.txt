<b> 5</b>.] I will just remark that the very fact of their questioning thus, ‘Moses commanded,... but what sayest<i> Thou</i>?’ belongs to the<i> last days</i> of the Lord’s ministry, and cannot: well be introduced chronologically where it here stands: nor does St. John any where introduce these questions between the law of Moses and Jesus; but the other Gospels often do.
