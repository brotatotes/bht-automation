<b> 51.</b>] There is no pause (De Wette) between ver. 50 and this. This is the direct carrying on of the discourse, arising out of<b> judgeth</b> in the last verse, and forming a new attempt of grace to plead with them, as Lampe calls it. Ye are<i> now</i> children of the devil, but if ye keep My word, ye shall be rescued from that<i> murderer.</i>
