<p><strong> 15.</strong> ] <strong> πάλιν</strong> refers to <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="joh+9:10">John 9:10</span> . The enquiry was official, as addressed to the chief witness in the matter. We cannot hence infer with Lücke that no one else was present at the healing but Jesus and His disciples.</p>
