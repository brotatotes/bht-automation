<b> 24. Give glory to God]</b> not, ‘Give God the praise’ (A. V.) i.e. ‘the glory of thy healing:’ for the Pharisees want to overawe the man by their authority, and make him deny the miracle altogether. The words are a form of<i> adjuration</i> (see Josh. vii. 19),<i> to tell the truth,</i> q.d. ‘Remember that you are in God’s presence, and speak as unto Him.’
