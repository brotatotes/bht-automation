<b> 37.]</b> These words,<b> Thou hast both seen him,</b>&c. serve to remind the man of the benefit he has received, and to awaken in him the liveliest gratitude: compare Luke ii. 30. They do not refer to a<i> former seeing,</i> when he was healed: this was the first time that he had<i> seen</i> his Benefactor.
