<b> 32. his father David]</b> This announcement makes it almost certain (still see note above) that<i> Mary also</i> was house of David. No astonishment is expressed by her at this part of the statement and yet, from the nature of her question, it is clear that<i> she did not explain it supposing Joseph to be the destined of her child.</i> See 2 Sam. vii. 13: Ps. lxxxix, 3, 4: Isa. ix. 7: Jer. xxxiii. 15.
