<b> 41</b>.] The salutation uttered by Elisabeth is certainly implied to have been<i> an inspiration of the Holy Spirit.</i> No intimation had been made to her of the situation of Mary. The movement of the babe in her womb (possibly for the first time) was part of the effect of the same spiritual influence. The<i> known</i> mysterious effects of sympathy in such cases, at least lead us to believe that there may be corresponding effects where the causes are of a kind<i> beyond our common experience</i>.
