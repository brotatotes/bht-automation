<b> 46.] My soul... My spirit</b> the<i> whole inner being:</i> see on 1 Thess. v. 23.<br><br><b> my Savior]</b> not merely ‘<i>Deliverer from degradation</i>,<i> as a daughter of David’</i>—but, in a higher sense,<b> author of that salvation which God’s people expected:</b> among whom the Holy Virgin reckons herself.
