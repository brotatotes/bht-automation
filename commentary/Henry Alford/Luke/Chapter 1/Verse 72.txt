<p><strong> 72. </strong> <strong> ποιῆσαι</strong> <strong> …</strong> ] For a similar use of the infinitive, see <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="lu+1:54">Luke 1:54</span> . We may take it here either as of the purpose, “ <em> to perform</em> …,” which is recommended by the ὅρκον ὃν κ . τ . λ ., below, or with Euthym [14] , Bleek, alli [15] ., as epexegetic, and equivalent to ἐν τῷ ποιῆσαι , or in English to a participial clause, ‘performing,’ &amp;c.
	</p>

	</p>
