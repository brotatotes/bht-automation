<p><strong> 76.</strong> ] It is not <em> necessary</em> to interpret <strong> κυρίου</strong> of the Messiah: it may be said of God, whose people ( Luk 1:77 ) Israel was. But the believing Christian will find it far more natural thus to apply it, especially in connexion with <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="mt+1:21">Matthew 1:21</span> .</p>
