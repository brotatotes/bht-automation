<p><strong> 24, 25.</strong> ] <strong> περιέκρυβεν</strong> either, <em> to avoid defilement:</em> see <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="jdg+13:13-14">Judges 13:13-14</span> , to <em> hide her pregnancy</em> from her neighbours till it was certain and apparent, or, from the precaution which the first months of pregnancy require.
	</p>
<p>Kuinoel suggests, that the reason may have been, that she might devote herself more uninterruptedly to exercises of devotion and thankfulness, and that this is expressed by the words following.
	</p>
<p>If so, <strong> ὅτι</strong> must mean ‘ <em> because</em> ,’ as indeed is the usage of these first chapters, see below on <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="lu+1:45">Luke 1:45</span> ; but it seems here to be only the usual particle by which a speech is introduced: see <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="ge+29:33">Genesis 29:33</span> . And indeed <strong> λέγουσα</strong> really carries the reason of her hiding herself “seeing that she said (within herself).…”
	</p>
