<b> 19.</b>] Our Lord here,—including all the evil and poison in nature in the<b> power of the enemy</b>,—from the power given Him over that enemy, asserts the gift to them, extended afterwards to all believers (Mark xvi. 18), of authority to ‘bruise the head of the serpent’ (Gen. iii. 15). There is an evident allusion to Ps. xci. 13.
