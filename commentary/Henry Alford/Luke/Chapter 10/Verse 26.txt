<p><strong> 26. </strong> <strong> πῶς ἀν</strong> <strong> .;</strong> ] A common Rabbinical formula for eliciting a text of Scripture.
	</p>
<p><strong> πῶς</strong> is not merely = τί , but implies <em> how?</em> i.e. to what purport; so that the answer should contain a summary of his reading in the Law.</p>
