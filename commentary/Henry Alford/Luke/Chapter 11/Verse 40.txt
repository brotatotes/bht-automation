<b> 40.</b>] seems clearly to me to be a<i> question</i>, and to mean, as A.V.,<b> Did not He, who made the outside, make the inside also?</b>—i. e. if His works have become unclean and polluted through sin, what is the use of<i> only partially</i> purging them,—not<i> accomplishing</i> the purgation?—must not the cleansing, to be good for any thing,<i> extend to the whole?</i> See, on a proposal otherwise to understand it, the note in my Greek Test.
