<p><strong> 44.</strong> ] See <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="mt+23:27">Matthew 23:27</span> ; but here the point of comparison is different. <em> There</em> (see note) <em> the sepulchres are whited, that men may not pass over them unawares:</em> and the comparison is to the outside fairness, and inside abomination. <em> Here</em> , the graves <em> are not seen</em> , and men thinking they are walking on clean ground <em> are defiled</em> by passing over them. Perhaps the difference of expression may have been occasioned by the greater wealth and splendour and display of the Pharisees <em> in the metropolis</em> , where <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="mt+23:0">Matthew 23:0</span> was spoken.
