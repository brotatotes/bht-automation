<b> 16.]</b> Our Lord in this parable sets before us one arrived at the very height of worldly prosperity, and that by no unfair means; but, as Augustine says, “not by removing landmarks, not by rob-bing the poor, not by overreaching the simple.” It was<i> by God’s blessing</i> that he me thus rich, which might have been a<i> real</i> blessing, if he had known how to use it.
