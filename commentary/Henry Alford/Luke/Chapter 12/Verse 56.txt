<b> 56.</b>]<b> the face of the earth</b>—perhaps referring to other signs of rain or heat from the appearance of the hills, &c.<br><br><b> this time</b>] The signs of this time were very plain;—the sceptre had departed from Judah;—the general expectation of the coming of the Messiah is testified even by profane authors;—the prophets had all spoken of Him, and the greatest of them, the Baptist, had announced His arrival.
