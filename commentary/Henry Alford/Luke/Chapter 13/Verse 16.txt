<b> 16.]</b> The contrast is strongly drawn—between a<i> dumb animal</i>, and (not merely a human creature, but) a<i> daughter of Abraham</i>—one of the chosen people (I cannot see any necessity for a<i> spiritual daughtership</i> [Gal. iii. 7] being here implied),—between a<i> few hours</i>, since the last watering, and ‘lo these<i> eighteen years</i>’ (compare ver. 7, “<i>Behold these three years</i>”....).
