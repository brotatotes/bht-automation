<b> 27. workmen of iniquity]</b> This unusual expression seems to mean,<i> persons engaged in the hire and receiving the wages of unrighteousness</i>:—see Matt. vii. 23, where<i> “ye that work lawlessness”</i> (20 literally) answers to it.<br><br> the question of ver. 23 given:— ‘<i>they shall be</i> MANY<i>—but what is that to you, if you be not among them?’</i>
