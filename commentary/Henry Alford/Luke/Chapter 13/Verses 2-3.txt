<p><strong> 2, 3.</strong> ] Our Lord perceives this to be their reasoning they did not <em> express</em> it, as is plain by the <strong> δοκεῖτε ὅτι</strong> <strong> …</strong> He does not deny that all the Galilæans were sinners, and deserved God’s judgments, but <em> that these were pre-eminently so</em> . The <strong> ὁμοίως</strong> (the force of which is lost in the E. V., ‘ <em> likewise</em> ’) should be rendered <strong> in like manner,</strong> as indeed the Jewish people did perish by the sword of the Romans.</p>
