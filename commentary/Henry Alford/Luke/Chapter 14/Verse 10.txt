<p><strong> 10.</strong> ] <strong> ἵνα</strong> <strong> ,</strong> not expressing the view with which <em> thou</em> art to do it (Meyer, <em> bezeichnet die Abficht des</em> ἀνάπεσε ), but a consequence which may follow: the view with which the act, as an objective fact, happens: the effect, of which it is (however the actor may be unaware of this) the cause; as the μήποτε in <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="lu+14:8">Luke 14:8</span> .</p>
