<b> 17.</b>] The<b> servant</b> represents one<i> spirit,</i> one<i> message:</i> but is not necessarily, in the three cases, one and the same<i> person.</i> The three messages were delivered (1) by John the Baptist and our Lord: (2) by our Lord and the Apostles; (3) by the Apostles and those who came after. The elder prophets cannot be meant, for [<b>all</b>]<b> things are now ready</b> was the message, representing the proclamation of John the Baptist and our Lord,<i> “The kingdom of heaven is at hand.”</i>
