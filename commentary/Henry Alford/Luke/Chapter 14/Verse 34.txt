The<b> therefore</b> and<b> even,</b> here restored to the text are both valuable; the former as importing the recurrence of a saying known before, the latter as giving force to the supposition.. The<i> salt,</i> in Scripture symbolism, is the whole life-retaining antiseptic influence of the Spirit of God:—this, working in the<i> being My disciple,</i> is good: but if even this be corrupted—if the mere appearance of this, and not the veritable salt (which is the<i> savour</i>), be in you—wherewith, &c.? Such a disciple is to be<b> cast out.</b> Salt was not used for<i> land,</i> Ps. vii. 34, nor for<i> mingling with manure;</i>
