<p><strong> 6.</strong> ] In this return to His house, must be understood the whole course of seeking and finding which the good Shepherd, either by Himself or His agents, now pursues in each individual case, even until He brings the lost sheep home into heaven to himself not in <em> reality</em> , so that it should not take place till the <em> death</em> of the penitent but <em> proleptically</em> , till the <em> name is written in heaven;</em> till the sinner is penitent. This is clear from the interpretation in <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="lu+15:7">Luke 15:7</span> . The <strong> φίλοι καὶ γείτονες</strong> = the angels (and spirits of just men made perfect?).
	</p>
<p><strong> τὸ πρόβ</strong> <strong> . </strong> <strong> τὸ ἀπολωλός</strong> breathes a totally different thought from τ . δραχμὴν ἣν ἀπώλεσα . There is pity and love in it, which, from the nature of the case, the other does not admit of.</p>
