<p><strong> 13.</strong> ] See note on <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="mt+6:24">Matthew 6:24</span> . The connexion here is, that we must, while put in trust with the ἄδικος μαμωνᾶς , be serving <em> not it, but God</em> . The saying here applies (as Olshausen remarks) admirably to the Pharisees and Publicans: the former were, to outward appearance, the servants of God, but inwardly served Mammon; the latter, <em> by profession</em> in the service of Mammon, were, by coming to Jesus, shewing that they inwardly served God.</p>
