<p><strong> 20.</strong> ] The significant name Lazarus (= Eleazarus = אֶלְעָזָר , <em> Deus auxilium</em> ) should have prevented the expositors from imagining this to be <em> a true history</em> .
	</p>
<p>Perhaps by this name our Lord may have intended <em> to fill in the character of the poor man</em> , which indeed must otherwise be understood to be that of one who feared God.
	</p>
<p><strong> ἐβέβ</strong> <strong> ., was,</strong> or <strong> had been, cast down,</strong> i.e. was placed there on purpose to get what he could of alms.
	</p>
<p><strong> πυλῶνα</strong> <strong> ,</strong> see on ref. Matt.: it was the portal, which led out of the προαύλιον into the αὐλή .</p>
