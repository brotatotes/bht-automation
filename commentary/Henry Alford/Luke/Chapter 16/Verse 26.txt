<b> 26.]</b><i> Even if it were not so,</i>—however, and for whatsoever reason, God’s decree hath placed thee there,—thy wish is<i> impossible.</i><br><br><b> a great gulf]</b> In the interpretation,—the irresistible decree—<i>then</i> truly so, but<i> no such on earth</i>—by which the Almighty Hand hath separated us and you,<b> in order that,</b> not merely<i> so that</i>, none may pass it. In the graphic description, a yawning chasm impassable.<br><br><b> is fixed]</b> for ever. This expression precludes all idea that the following verse indicates the beginning of a better mind in the rich man.
