<b> 27.]</b> This is the<i> believing and trembling</i> of James ii. 19. His eyes are now opened to the truth; and no wonder that his natural sympathies are awakened for his brethren.<br><br> That a<i> lost spirit</i> should feel and express such sympathy, is not to be wondered at; the misery of such will be very much heightened by the awakened and active state of those higher faculties and feelings which selfishness and the body kept down here.
