<b>Nay</b>—not, ‘<i>they will not hear them:</i>’ he could not tell that, and besides, it would have taken away much of the ground of the answer of Abraham:—the word deprecates leaving their salvation in<i> such uncertainty,</i> as the chance of their hearing Moses and the prophets seems to him to imply.— ‘<i>Leave it not so, when it might be at once and for ever done by sending</i><i> them one from the dead.</i>’
