<p><strong> 4.</strong> ] <strong> ἔγνων</strong> not = ἔγνωκα , which would be, ‘ <em> I know</em> , as part of my stock of knowledge, I am well aware,’ but implying, <strong> I have just arrived at the knowledge, an idea has just struck me I have a plan.
	</strong></p>
<p><strong> δέξωνται</strong> viz. those who are about to be spoken of, the χρεοφειλέται . He has them in his mind.
	</p>
<p>Observe, the aim of his scheme is that they may receive him into their houses, <em> give him shelter</em> . This is made use of afterwards in the interpretation, for which see on <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="lu+16:9">Luke 16:9</span> .</p>
