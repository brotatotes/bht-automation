<p><strong> 10 12.</strong> ] Closely connected with the foregoing (against De Wette and Strauss): the ‘faithfulness in the least’ is the same as the prudence and shrewdness just spoken of; in the case of the children of light they run up into one τίς ἐστιν ὁ <strong> πιστὸς</strong> οἰκονόμος <strong> καὶ φρόνιμος</strong> , ch. <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="lu+12:42">Luke 12:42</span> ; the <strong> ἐλάχιστον</strong> = ὁ ἄδικος (see above: not “ <em> fallacious</em> ,” as Wordsw.) μαμωνᾶς = <strong> τὸ ἀλλότριον</strong> <em> the wealth of this present world</em> , which is not the Christian’s own, nor his proper inheritance. The <strong> πολύ</strong> <strong> = </strong> <strong> τὸ ἀληθινόν</strong> <strong> = </strong> <strong> το ὑμέτερον</strong> = <em> the true riches of God’s inheritance:</em> of which the earth (see Mat 5:5 ) forms a part, which ὁ θεός (implied in the τίς for there will be none to give it you if you be untrue during this state of probation; He will not be your God) shall give to you. The wealth of this world is ἀλλότριον forfeited by sin only put into our hands to try us, and to be rendered an account of.</p>
