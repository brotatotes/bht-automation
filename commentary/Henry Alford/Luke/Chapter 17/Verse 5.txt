<b> 5.]</b> ‘<i>Increase our faith,</i>’ of the A.V., is not exact: Add unto us, i.e. give us more faith, is more literal and simpler.<br><br> This is the only example in the Gospels in which<i> the Apostles</i> are marked out as requesting or saying any thing to Lord. They are amazed at the greatness of the faith which is to overcome<i> offences</i> and for-give<i> sins</i> as in vv. 8, 4:—and pray that<i> more faith</i> may be added to them.
