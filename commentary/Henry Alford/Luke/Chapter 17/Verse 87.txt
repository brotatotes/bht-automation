<b> 87.]</b><b> Where, Lord? i.e. where shall this happen?</b> The disciples know not the universality of this which our Lord is announcing to them, and which His dark and awful saying proclaims; see note on it, Matt. xxiv. 28.<br><br> Observe, there is<i> not a word,</i> except so far as the greater coming includes the lesser, in all this, of the destruction of Jerusalem. The future coming of the Lord is the<i> only subject:</i> and thus it is an entirely distinct discourse from that in Matt. xxiv., or in our ch. xxi.
