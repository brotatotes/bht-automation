<p><strong> 34 36.</strong> ] See on <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="mt+24:40-41">Matthew 24:40-41</span> . Here, there are two references: (1) to the servants of the Lord in the midst of the world out of which they shall be separated: (2) to the separation of the faithful and unfaithful among <em> themselves</em> .
	</p>
<p>Luk 17:34 indicates a closer relationship than that of mere fellow-workmen, and sets forth the division of even families in that day.</p>
