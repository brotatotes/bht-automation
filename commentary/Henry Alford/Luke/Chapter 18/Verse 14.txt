<b> 14.]</b> The sense is, One returned home in the sight of God with his prayer answered, and that prayer had grasped the true object of prayer,—the forgiveness of sins (so that<b> justified</b> is in the usual sense of the Epistles of St. Paul,<b> justified before God</b>—see reff.), the other prayed not for it, and obtained it not. Therefore he who would seek justification before God must seek it by humility and not by self-righteousness.
