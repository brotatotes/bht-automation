 31 34. ] FULLER DECLARATION OF HIS SUFFERINGS AND DEATH. Matthew 20:17-19 . Mark 10:32-34 . The narrative of the journey now passes to  the last section of it , the going up to Jerusalem, properly so called; that which in Matt. and Mark forms  the whole journey . We know from Joh 11:54 that this journey took place from Ephraim, a city near the desert.
