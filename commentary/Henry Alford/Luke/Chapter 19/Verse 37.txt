<b> 37.</b><b> the whole multitude of the disciples</b>] In the widest sense; it is equivalent to<i> “the multitudes,”</i>” Mat thew. The<i> “mighty work,”</i> In the which dwelt, mostly on their minds,<b> was the raising</b> of Lazarus, John xii. 17, 18:—but as this perhaps was not known to St. Luke, we must understand him to mean,<i> all that they had seen during their journey with Him.</i>
