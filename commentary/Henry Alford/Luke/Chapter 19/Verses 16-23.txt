It is observable here, however, how exactly and minutely in keeping is every circumstance. <em> Thy pound</em> <strong> hath gained ten pounds;</strong> the humility with which this is stated, where no account of ἡ ἰδία δύναμις is taken as in Matt., and then the proportion of the reward, <strong> δέκα πόλεις</strong> so according with the nature of what the Prince went to receive, and the occasion of his return.</p>
