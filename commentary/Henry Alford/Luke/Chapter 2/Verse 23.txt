<p><strong> 23.</strong> ] God had taken <em> the tribe of Levi instead of the firstborn that openeth the womb</em> , <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="nu+3:12">Numbers 3:12</span> , and required only the excess in number of the first-born over the Levites to be redeemed (ib. Num 3:44-51 ). This arrangement appears afterwards to have been superseded by a general command to redeem <em> all the first-born</em> at five shekels of the sanctuary ( Num 18:15-16 ).</p>
