 23. ] God had taken  the tribe of Levi instead of the firstborn that openeth the womb , Numbers 3:12 , and required only the excess in number of the first-born over the Levites to be redeemed (ib. Num 3:44-51 ). This arrangement appears afterwards to have been superseded by a general command to redeem  all the first-born at five shekels of the sanctuary ( Num 18:15-16 ).
