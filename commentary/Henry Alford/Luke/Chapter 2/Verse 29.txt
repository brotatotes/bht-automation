<p><strong> 29.</strong> ] <strong> ἀπολύεις</strong> <strong> ,</strong> not τοῦ ζῆν , or ἐκ τῆς γῆς , but as being τὸν δοῦλόν σου , he thinks of his death as the termination of, and so dismissal from, <em> his servitude</em> . Meyer. Bleek thinks that there is no such allusion, but that the word is used absolutely, as in <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="ge+15:2">Genesis 15:2</span> ; <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="nu+20:29">Numbers 20:29</span> .</p>
