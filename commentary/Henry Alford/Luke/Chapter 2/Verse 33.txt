 33.   ὁ πατ  .   αὐτοῦ ] In Luk 2:48 we have Joseph again called by this name.  Our Lord Himself would not speak of him thus , see Luke 2:49 ; but in the simplicity of the narrative we may read οἱ γονεῖς αὐτοῦ and such expressions, without any danger of forgetting the momentous history of the Conception and Nativity.
