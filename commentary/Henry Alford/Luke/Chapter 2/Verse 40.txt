<p><strong> 40.</strong> ] <strong> ηὔξανεν</strong> <em> in body</em> . <strong> ἐκρ</strong> <strong> .,</strong> <em> in spirit:</em> πνεύματι is a correct gloss. “The body advances in stature, and the soul in wisdom … the divine nature revealed its own wisdom in proportion to the measure of the bodily growth.” Cyril. Oxf. transl. p. 30.
