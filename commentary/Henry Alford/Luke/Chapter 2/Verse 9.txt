<p><strong> 9.</strong> ] <strong> δόξα</strong> <strong> the brightness of God’s presence</strong> the <em> Shechinah</em> (see reff.) which also accompanied His angels when they appeared to men. It is agreeable at least to the analogy of the divine dealings, to suppose with Olshausen, that these shepherds, like Symeon, were <em> waiting for the consolation of Israel</em> .</p>
