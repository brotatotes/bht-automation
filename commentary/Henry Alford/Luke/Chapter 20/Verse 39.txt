Peculiar to Luke;—implied however in Matthew ver. 34, and Mark ver. 28.<b> 41–44.</b>] QUESTION RESPECTING CHRIST AND DAVID. Matt. xxii. 41–46; Mark xii. 85–87, where see notes. St. Luke omits the question of the lawyer, which occurred<i> immediately</i> on the gathering together of the Pharisees after the last incident. This question of our Lord seems to have followed close on that, which (and not that in vv. 27 ff. here) was their<i> last to Him,</i> Mark xii. 34.
