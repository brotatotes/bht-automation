<b> 19.</b>]<b> In your Patience</b> (i. e. by your endurance of all these things)<b> ye shall acquire</b> (not,<i> possess,</i> which is an ungrammatical rendering)<b> your souls:</b> this endurance being God’s appointed way, in and by which your salvation is to be put in your possession.<br><br><b> acquire, as “find,”</b> Matt. xvi. 25—<b> “save,”</b> ch. ix. 24.
