<b> 16.</b>] The full meaning of this declaration is to be sought in the words this<i> passover.</i> It was<i> that particular</i> Passover, not<i> merely</i> the Passover generally,—though of course that also,—that was to receive its fulfilment in the kingdom of God. And to this fulfilment our Lord alludes again in ver. 30. It is to this marriage supper of the Lamb, that the parable Matt. xxi. 1–14 in its ultimate application refers: nor can we help thinking on the faithless Apostle at this very supper, in verses 11–13 there:—see notes on that Lace.
