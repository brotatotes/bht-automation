<p><strong> 20. </strong> <strong> τὸ ὑπὲρ ὑμῶν ἐκχυννόμενον</strong> ] These words cannot be said of ποτήριον , ‘ <em> nam poculum plenum non effunditur, sed bibitur</em> ’ (Bengel), but are said πρὸς τὸ σημαινόμενον , which is the wine poured out from the grapes ( τὸ γέννημα τῆς ἀμπέλου ) and represents the Blood poured out from the Lord’s Body.
	</p>
<p>Here follows, in <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="mt+26:29">Matthew 26:29</span> , <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="mr+14:25">Mark 14:25</span> , a second declaration, respecting <em> not drinking any more of this fruit of the vine</em> .</p>
