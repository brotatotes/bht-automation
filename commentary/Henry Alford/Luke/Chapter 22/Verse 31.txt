<b> 31.</b>] not only<i> ‘hath desired to have you,’</i> A.V., but<b> hath obtained you;</b>— ‘his desire is granted.’<br><br><b> you,</b> all of you: not Simon alone, as sometimes understood, even by preachers, from the A.V. only. This must include Judas, though it does not follow that he was present; the sifting separated the chaff from the wheat, which chaff he was, see Amos ix. 9.
