<b> 53.</b>] There is an important addition here to the other reports of our Lord’s speech;—<b>but this is your hour, and the power of darkness.</b> It stands here instead of the declaration that<i> this was done that the Scriptures might be fulfilled</i> (Matthew, ver. 66: Mark, ver. 49).
