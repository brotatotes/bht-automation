<b> 67.</b>] First, before this enquiry, took place the<i> “witness”</i> referred to in ver. 71; and the person who said this<b> was</b> the high priest, and with an adjuration, Matthew, ver. 63. The rendering in the margin is the most natural and correct:<b> If thou art</b> (not if thou<i> be</i>)<b> the Christ, tell us.</b> The others,<i> ‘Tell us whether thou be the Christ;’</i> and,<i> ‘Art thou the Christ? tell us,’</i> are forced and unusual renderings of the original.
