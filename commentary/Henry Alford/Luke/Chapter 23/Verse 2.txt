<b> 2.</b>] This charge was intended to represent the result of their previous judgment,<b> we found;</b>—whereas, in fact,<i> no such matter had been before them:</i> but they falsely allege it before Pilate, knowing that it was the point on which his judgment was likely to be most severe. The words themselves which they use are not so false, as the spirit, and impression which they convey.
