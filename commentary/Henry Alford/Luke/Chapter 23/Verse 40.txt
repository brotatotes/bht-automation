<p><strong> 40.</strong> ] Bengel supports the notion that this penitent thief was a <em> Gentile</em> . But surely this is an unwarranted assumption. What should a Gentile know of Paradise, or of the kingdom of the Messiah as about to come? The silence of the penitent is broken by the <strong> ἡμᾶς</strong> of the other compromising him in the scoff.
