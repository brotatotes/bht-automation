<p><strong> 41. </strong> <strong> ἡμεῖς</strong> ] He classes himself with the other in condemnation, but not in his prayer afterwards.
	</p>
<p><strong> ἄτοπον</strong> <strong> , unseemly.</strong> This is a remarkable testimony to the innocence of Jesus from one who was probably executed for his share in those very tumults which He was accused of having excited.</p>
