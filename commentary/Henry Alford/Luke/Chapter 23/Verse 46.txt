<b> 46.</b>] The use of<b> with a loud voice</b> shews that<i> this was the cry</i> to which St. Matthew and St. Mark allude. The words uttered are from the LXX, varying however from the common reading<i> “I will commend,”</i> and giving the verb in the<i> present,</i> which is also the rendering of the Hebrew.
