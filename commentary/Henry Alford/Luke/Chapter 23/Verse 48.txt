<p><strong> 48.</strong> ] Peculiar to Luke.
	</p>
<p><strong> τὰ γενόμενα</strong> are the darkness and other prodigies, after which we have no more <em> raillery:</em> men’s tempers are changed, and we here see the result.
	</p>
<p><strong> τύπτοντες</strong> <strong> …</strong> a sign of self-accusation, at least for the time, which is renewed on the preaching of Peter, <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="ac+2:37">Acts 2:37</span> .</p>
