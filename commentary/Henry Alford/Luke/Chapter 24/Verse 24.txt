<b> 24. certain</b>] See ver. 12 and note. It is natural, even in accordance with ver. 12, that the antithesis to<i> “certain women”</i> before, and the loose way of speaking to a stranger, who (they believed) was not acquainted with any among them, might cause them here to use this word without any reference to Peter being<i> accompanied.</i> But what wonder, if the reports of such a day of anxiety and confusion were themselves disjointed and confused?
