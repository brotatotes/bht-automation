<p><strong> 37.</strong> ] On account of His sudden appearance, and the likeness to one whom they knew to have been dead.
	</p>
<p><strong> πνεῦμα</strong> is a <em> ghost</em> or <em> spectre</em> an appearance of the dead to the living; not exactly as φάντασμα , <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="mt+14:26">Matthew 14:26</span> , which might have been <em> any</em> appearance of a supernatural kind.</p>
