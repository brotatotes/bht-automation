<p><strong> 7 9.</strong> ] <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="mt+3:7-10">Matthew 3:7-10</span> . John’s speech is verbatim as Matt., except that καρπ . ἀξ . is singular, and δόξητε Matt. = ἄρξησθε Luke. This indicates a common origin of this portion, which however is still thus slightly deflected; and let it be borne in mind that the slighter the deflection, the more striking the independence of the Evangelists.
	</p>
