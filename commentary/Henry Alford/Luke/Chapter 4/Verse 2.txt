<p><strong> 2.</strong> ] The literal rendering of the present text will be: <strong> Jesus … was led by</strong> ( <em> in, in the power of</em> , the ἐν of instrumentality by the conditioning element) <strong> the Spirit in the wilderness, being tempted</strong> (the pres. part. carries a slight ratiocinative force, as usual) <strong> during forty days by the devil.</strong> So that St. Luke, as also St. Mark, implies that the temptation continued <em> the whole forty days</em> .
