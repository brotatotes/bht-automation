<b> 20. sat down</b>] It was the custom in the synagogues to<i> stand</i> while reading the law,<i> sit down</i> to explain it. Our Lord on other occasions taught<i> sitting,</i> e.g. Matt. v. 1: Mark iv. 1; xiii. 3.<b> The minister was</b> the officer whose duty it was to keep the<b> sacred books.</b>
