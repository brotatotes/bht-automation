<p><strong> 20. </strong> <strong> ἐκάθισεν</strong> ] It was the custom in the synagogues to <em> stand</em> while reading the law, and <em> sit down</em> to explain it. Our Lord on other occasions taught <em> sitting</em> , e.g. <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="mt+5:1">Matthew 5:1</span> ; <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="mr+4:1">Mark 4:1</span> ; <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="mr+13:3">Mark 13:3</span> .
	</p>
<p>The <strong> ὑπηρέτης</strong> was the חָזָן whose duty it was to keep the sacred books.</p>
