<b> 42.</b>]<b> the multitudes are</b><i> “Simon and they that were with him”</i> in Mark. The great number of sick which were brought to the Lord on the evening before, and this morning, is accounted for by some from His departure having been fixed on and known beforehand; but it is perhaps more simple to view it us the natural result of the effect of the healing of the dæmoniac in the synagogue, on the popular mind.
