 10.   ἔσῃ ζωγρῶν ] Compare, and indeed throughout this miracle, the striking parallel, and yet contrast, in John 21:0 with its injunction,  ‘feed My lambs,’ ‘shepherd My sheep,’ given to the same Peter; its net which  did not burst: and the minute and beautiful appropriateness of each will be seen: this, at, or near, the commencement of the Apostolic course; that, at how different, and how fitting a time!
	It is perhaps too subtle, and hardly accordant with the rules of emphasis, to find (with Me [48] . and Stier) a fitness in  ζωγρῶν as expressing the ethical catching of men. I prefer taking it as the word common to both acts merely as  catch .
