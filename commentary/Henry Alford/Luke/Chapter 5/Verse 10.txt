<b> 10.</b>]<b> thou shalt be a catcher of men:</b>—compare, and indeed throughout this miracle, the striking parallel, and yet contrast, in John xxi.—with its injunction,<i> ‘Feed my lambs,’</i><i> ‘Shepherd My sheep,’</i> given to the same Peter; its net which<i> did not burst:</i> and the minute and beautiful appropriateness of each will be seen: this, at, or near, the commencement of the apostolic course; that, at how different, and how fitting a time!
