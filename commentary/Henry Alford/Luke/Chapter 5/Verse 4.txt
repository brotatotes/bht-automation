<b> 4.</b>]<b> Launch out is,</b> in the original, singular, as addressed to Peter alone, who was the steersman of his ship;<b> let down</b> is plural, as addressed to the fishermen in the ship collectively. So below also,<b> I will let down,</b> of the director,—<b>when they had this done,</b> of the doers,—of the act.
