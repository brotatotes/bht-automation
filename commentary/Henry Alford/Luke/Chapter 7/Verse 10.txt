<b> 10.]</b> Here Matthew simply states the fact of the healing, apparently not knowing of any having been sent.<br><br> This is one of the three greatest recorded miracles of our Lord: of which it has been observed, that He raised one (Jairus’s daughter) when<i> just dead</i>,—one<i> on the way to burial</i>,—and one (Lazarus)<i> who had been buried four days</i>.
