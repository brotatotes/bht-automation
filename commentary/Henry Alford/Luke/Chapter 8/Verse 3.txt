<b> 3.]</b> Prof. Blunt has observed in his Coincidences, that we find a reason here why Herod should say to his<i> servants</i> (Matt. xiv. 2), ‘This is John the Baptist,’&., viz.—because his<i> steward’s wife</i> was a disciple of Jesus, and so there would be frequent mention of Him among the servants in Herod’s court.
