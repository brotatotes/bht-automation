<b> 3.]</b> Prof. Blunt has observed in his Coincidences, that we find a reason here why Herod should say to his<i> servants</i> (Matt. xiv. 2), ‘This is John the Baptist,’&., viz.—because his<i> steward’s wife</i> was a disciple of Jesus, and so there would be frequent mention of Him among the servants in Herod’s court.<br><br> This is Herod Antipas.<br><br> Joanna is mentioned again ch. xxiv. 10, and again in company with Mary Magdalene and others. Susanna is not again mentioned.<br><br><b> ministered</b>, providing food, and giving other necessary attentions.<br><br><b> unto them</b>, viz. the Lord and His Apostles.
