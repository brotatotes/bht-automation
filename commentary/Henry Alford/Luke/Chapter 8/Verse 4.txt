<b> 4.]</b> The present participle<b> gathering,</b> is overlooked by the A.V.: as is also<b> coming to him</b>; literally<b> coming up one after another</b>. It was the desire of those who had been impressed by His discourses and miracles to be further taught, that brought them together to Him now. He spoke this parable sitting in a boat, and the multitude on the shore.
