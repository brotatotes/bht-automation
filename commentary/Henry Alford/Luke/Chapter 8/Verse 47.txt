<b> 47.</b>] It is not necessary (though perhaps probably), from the<b> when all denied</b>, ver. 45, that<i> the woman</i> should also have denied with them. She may have hidden herself among the crowd. Our Lord (Mark, ver. 32) looked around to see “<i>her that had done this thing,</i>”—a wonderful precision of expression, by which His absolute knowledge of the whole matter is set before us.
