<p><strong> 22 25.</strong> ] JESUS, CROSSING THE LAKE, STILLS THE STORM. <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="mt+8:18">Matthew 8:18</span> ; <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="mt+8:23-27">Matthew 8:23-27</span> . <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="mr+4:35-41">Mark 4:35-41</span> . The chronology of this occurrence would be wholly uncertain, were it not for the precision of Mark, who has introduced it by ἐν ἐκείνῃ τῇ ἡμέρᾳ ὀψίας οὔσης , i.e. <em> on the same day in which the preceding parables were delivered</em> . How it has come to be misplaced in Matthew, must ever be matter of obscurity. The fact <em> that it is so</em> , is no less unquestionable than the proof that it furnishes of the independence of the two other Evangelists.</p>
