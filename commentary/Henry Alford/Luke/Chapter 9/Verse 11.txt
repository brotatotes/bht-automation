<p><strong> ἀποδεξάμ</strong> <strong> .</strong> ] This word includes what Mark tells us of His going forth from His solitude, or perhaps landing from the ship, and seeing a great multitude, and having compassion on them; <strong> having received them,</strong> i.e. not sent them away.</p>
