<p><strong> 19. </strong> <strong> ὅτι πρ. τις τ. ἀρχ. ἀν.</strong> ] See <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="lu+9:8">Luke 9:8</span> . There is no improbability, nor contradiction to John’s account that the multitudes sought to make Him a king, in our Lord’s asking this question. We must remember that such enquiries were not made by Him <em> for information</em> , but as a means of drawing out the confession of others, as here.</p>
