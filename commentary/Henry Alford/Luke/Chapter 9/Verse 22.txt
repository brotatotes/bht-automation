<b> 22.</b>]<b> As far as slain</b> is nearly verbatim with Mark: the last clause nearly so with Matthew. And yet, according to the Commentators, St. Mark has<i> compiled his account from St. Matthew and St. Luke</i>. The<i> almost</i> verbal agreement of the three in so solemn and sad an announcement, is what we might expect. Such words would not be easily forgotten.
