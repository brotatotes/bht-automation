<b> 54.</b>] The disciples whom He named ‘sons of thunder,’ Mark iii. 17. They<i> saw</i> some insult of manner, or actual refusal to allow the Lord to enter their village. That a collision of this kind did take place, is plain from the last verse, and implied from the occasion alluded to by the two Apostles, where the fire was invoked in the presence of the offending persons.<i> It</i> happened also in Samaria.
