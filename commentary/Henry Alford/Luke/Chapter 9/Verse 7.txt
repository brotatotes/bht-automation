<b> 7.</b>] “<i>by him,</i>” though not genuine, and an explanatory gloss, points to the right account of the matter. Herod (see Mark) heard the account of the miracles wrought by the Twelve; but even then it was HIS<i> name</i> which was spread abroad. These works were done in their Master’s Name, and in popular rumour<i> passed for His</i>.
