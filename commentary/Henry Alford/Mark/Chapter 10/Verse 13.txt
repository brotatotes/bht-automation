<b> 13. young children]</b> Not only so, but as in Luke,<i> infants</i>: and our Lord was not to<i> teach</i> them, but only to touch, and<b> pray</b> over them. This simple, seemingly superstitious application of those that brought them (perhaps not the mothers only) the disciples, interrupted in their converse on high and important subjects, despise and reprove.
