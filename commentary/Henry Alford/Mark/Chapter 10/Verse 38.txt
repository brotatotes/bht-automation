<b> 38.]</b> Observe the present tenses,<b> drink of</b>, and<b> am baptised with</b>. They may mean that the Lord had already the cup of His suffering at His lips; was already, so to speak, sprinkled with the first drops of spray of His baptism of blood: or they may be merely official: “<i>that I am to drink of, and to be baptized with.</i>”
