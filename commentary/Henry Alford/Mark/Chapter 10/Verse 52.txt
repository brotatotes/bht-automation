<b> 52.]</b> In Matthew only, Jesus<i> touches</i> him. The account here and in Luke seems to correspond more closely with the wonderful strength of his faith. Our Lord healed<i> by a word</i> in such cases, see Matt. viii 10–13, ch. vii. 29, and other places. St. Luke adds, “<i>glorifying God</i>,”—and that<i> all the people seeing him gave glory to God</i>; see also Luke xix. 37.
