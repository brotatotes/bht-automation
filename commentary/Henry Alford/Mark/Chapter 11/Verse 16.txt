<p><strong> 16. </strong> <strong> οὐκ ἤφιεν ἵνα</strong> ] “Observa, ἵνα et ὄφρα a recentioribus poëtis frequentari post verba jubendi.” Herm. ad Viger., p. 849. See note on <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="1co+14:13">1 Corinthians 14:13</span> .
	</p>
<p>This was the <em> court of the Gentiles</em> , which was used as a thoroughfare; which desecration our Lord forbade.
	</p>
<p><strong> σκεῦος</strong> is <em> any vessel</em> , e.g. a pail or basket, used for common life.</p>
