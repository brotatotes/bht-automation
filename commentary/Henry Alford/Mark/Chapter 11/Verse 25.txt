<p>In <strong> ὅταν στήκετε</strong> <strong> ,</strong> the <strong> ἄν</strong> connects, not with the verb, but with the <strong> ὅτε</strong> <strong> ,</strong> giving indefiniteness to the occasion, not to the act. See Klotz, Devar. p. 470, 475. He gives an example from Lycurgus contra Leocratem, p. 162 (§ 107), ὅταν ἐν τοῖς ὅπλοις ἐκστρατευόμενοί εἰσι .</p>
