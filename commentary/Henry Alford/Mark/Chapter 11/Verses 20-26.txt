<p><strong> 20 26.</strong> ] The answers are very similar to those in Matt., but with one important addition here, viz. <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="mr+11:25-26">Mark 11:25-26</span> : see <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="mt+6:14">Matthew 6:14</span> , and <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="1ti+2:8">1 Timothy 2:8</span> . The connexion here seems to be, ‘Though you should aim at strength of faith, yet <em> your faith</em> should not work in all respects as you have seen me do, in judicial anger condemning the unfruitful and evil; but you must <em> forgive</em> .’</p>
