<p><strong> 31.</strong> ] Our Lord adds this second, as an application or bringing home of the first.
	</p>
<p>The first is the <em> Sun</em> , so to speak, of the spiritual life: this the <em> lesser light</em> , which reflects the shining of that other. It is <em> like</em> to it, inasmuch as both are <em> laws of love:</em> both deduced from the great and highest love: both dependent on ‘I am the Lord thy God,’ <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="le+19:18">Leviticus 19:18</span> .
	</p>
<p>Stier sets forth beautifully the strong contrast between the <em> requirements of these two commands</em> , and the <em> then state of the Jewish Church:</em> see <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="joh+7:19">John 7:19</span> .</p>
