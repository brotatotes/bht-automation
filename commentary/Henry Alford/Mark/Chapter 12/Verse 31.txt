<b> 31.]</b> Our Lord adds this second as an application or bringing home of the first.<br><br> The first is the<i> Sun</i>, so to speak, of the spiritual life:—this the<i> lesser light</i>, which reflects the shining of that other. It is<i> like</i> to it, inasmuch as both are<i> laws of love:</i> both deduced from the great and highest love: both dependent on “I am the Lord thy God,” Lev. xix. 18.<br><br> Stier sets forth beautifully the strong contrast between the<i> requirements of these two commands, and the then state of the Jewish Church:</i> see John vii. 19.
