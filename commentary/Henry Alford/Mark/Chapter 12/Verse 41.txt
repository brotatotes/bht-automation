 41.   τοῦ γαζ  . ] This is usually understood of  thirteen chests , which stood in the court of the women, into which were thrown contributions for the temple, or the tribute (of Mat 17:24 ). But it is hardly likely that  they would be called  τὸ γαζ ., and we hear of a building by this name in Jos. Antt. xix. 6. 1. Lücke, on John 8:20 , believes  some part of the court of the women to be intended, perhaps a chamber in connexion with these chests.
	Our Lord had at this time  taken his leave of the temple , and was going out of it between Matthew 23:0 end, and 24.
