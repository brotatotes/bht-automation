Peculiar to Mark, and containing the condensed matter of Matthew, vv. 43–47, and perhaps an allusion to the parable of the talents in Matt. xxv.<br><br> The<b> porter</b> is the<b> door-porter</b>, whose office it would be to look out for approaching travellers,—answering especially to the ministers of the word, who are (Ezek. xxxiii.)<i> watchmen</i> to God’s church.
