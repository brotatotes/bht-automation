<p><strong> 15.</strong> ] In the midst of a verbal accordance with Luke we have here inserted <strong> ἕτοιμον</strong> <strong> ,</strong> indicating that the guest-chamber was <em> already prepared</em> for the celebration of the Passover, as would indeed be probable at this time in Jerusalem. The disciples had therefore only to get ready <em> the Passover itself</em> .</p>
