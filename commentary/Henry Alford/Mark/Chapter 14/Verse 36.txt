<b> 36.] Abba</b> is the familiar and endearing appellation for Father. It is an Aramaic form, and after St. Mark’s manner inserted as ‘Ephphatha,’ ch. vii. 34,— ‘Talitha cumi,’ ch. v. 41.<br><br><b> Father</b> is not given as the interpretation of<b> Abba</b>, but came to be attached to it in one phrase as a form of address: see the references.
