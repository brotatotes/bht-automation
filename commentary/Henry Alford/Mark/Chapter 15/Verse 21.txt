<b> 21. Alexander and Rufus]</b> It is quite uncertain whether Alexander be identical with either of the persons of that name mentioned Acts xix. 33, 1 Tim. i. 20, 2 Tim. iv. 14, or whether those, or any two of them, it one and the same person. There is a Rufus saluted Rom. xvi. 13. The words<b> coming out of the country</b> determine nothing as to its being a working day or otherwise, any more than “<i>that passed by</i>” Matthew, ver. 39: nothing is said as to the<i> distance</i> from whence he came.
