<b> 42. the preparation, that is, the day before the sabbath]</b> The Friday afternoon (the preparation—Parascevé, “the name by which Friday is now generally known in Asia and Greece.” Wordsw.) before<i> sunset</i>, at which time the Sabbath would begin, and the taking down, &c.<i> would be unlawful</i>. The three Evangelists do not imply that this “preparation” had any thing<i> especial</i> in it, as St. John does, ver. 31.
