<p><strong> 10.</strong> ] <strong> ἐκεῖνος</strong> is <em> no where found used absolutely</em> by Mark, but <em> always emphatically</em> (see ch. <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="mr+4:11">Mark 4:11</span> ; <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="mr+7:15">Mark 7:15</span> ; <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="mr+7:20">Mark 7:20</span> ; Mar 14:21 ); whereas here and Mar 16:11 it is absolutely used (not in <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="mr+16:13">Mark 16:13</span> b and 20, where it is emphatical).
	</p>

	</p>
