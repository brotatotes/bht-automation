<b> 13.] they also</b>—as Mary Magdalene had done before.<br><br><b> the residue</b>—supply, of those that had been with Him.<br><br><b> neither believed they them</b>—not consistent with Luke xxiv. 33, 34. Here again the Harmonists have used every kind of distortion of the plain meaning of words to reconcile the two accounts; assuming that some believed and some doubted, that they first doubted and then believed; or, according to Bengel, first believed and then doubted.
