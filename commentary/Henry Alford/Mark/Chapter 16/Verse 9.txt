	 ἀφ  ʼ   ἧς ἐκβ  .… ] This notice, coming so late,  after the mention of Mary Magdalene in Mark 16:1 , is remarkable. The instances quoted by De Wette to shew that the unexpected introduction of notices contained in the other Gospels is in Mark’s manner, do not seem to me to apply here.
