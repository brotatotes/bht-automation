<b> 22.]</b><b> the scribes which came down from Jerusalem...</b> peculiar to Mark: see note on Matt. ver. 24. Here Matthew has “<i>the Pharisees</i>”—Luke “<i>some of them</i>,” i.e. “<i>the people</i>.”<br><br><b> He hath Beelzebub]</b> This addition is most important. If He was<i> possessed</i> by Beelzebub, the<i> prince of mons</i>, He would thus have authority over the inferior evil spirits.
