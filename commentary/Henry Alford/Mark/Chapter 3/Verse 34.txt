<p><strong> 34.</strong> ] Matt. here has some remarkable and graphic details also: ἐκτείνας τὴν χεῖρα αὐτ . ἐπὶ τ . μαθητὰς αὐτ … Both accounts were from eye-witnesses, <em> the one</em> noticing the outstretched hand; <em> the other</em> , the look cast round. Deeply interesting are such particulars, the more so, as shewing the way in which the records arose, and their united strength, derived from their independence and variety.</p>
