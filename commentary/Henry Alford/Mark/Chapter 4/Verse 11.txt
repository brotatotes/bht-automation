<p><strong> 11.</strong> ] <strong> τὸ μυστήριον</strong> = τὰ μυστήρια Matt. and Luke.
	</p>
<p><strong> τοῖς ἔξω</strong> added here (= τοῖς λοιποῖς , Luke) means <em> the multitudes</em> those out of the circle of his followers. In the Epistles, <em> all who are not Christians</em> , the corresponding meaning for those days, are designated by it.
	</p>
<p><strong> τὰ πάντα γίνεται</strong> <strong> ] the whole matter is transacted.</strong> Herod. ix. 46, ἡμῖν οἱ λόγοι γεγόνασι .</p>
