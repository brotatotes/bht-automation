<b> 11.] the mystery;</b><i> “the mysteries”</i> Matthew and Luke.<b> them that are without</b> added here ( “<i>the rest,</i>” Luke) means<i> the multitudes</i>—those out of the circle of his followers. In the Epistles,<i> all who are not Christians,</i>—the corresponding meaning for those days,—are designated by it.
