<b> 12.]</b> We must keep the<b> that</b> strictly to its full meaning—<b>in order that.</b> When God transacts a matter, it is idle to say that the result is not the purpose. He doeth all things after the counsel of His own will. St Matthew, as usual, quotes a prophecy; St. Mark hardly ever—except at the beginning of his Gospel: St. Luke, very seldom.
