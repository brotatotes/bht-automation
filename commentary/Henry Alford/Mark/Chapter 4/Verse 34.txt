<b> 34.] when they were alone...</b> We have<i> three</i> such instances—the<i> sower</i>, the<i> tares</i>, Matt. xiii. 36 ff., and the<i> saying concerning defilement</i>, Matt. xv. 16 ff. To these we may add the<i> two parables in John</i>—ch. x. 1–18, which however was<i> publicly</i> explained,—and ch. xv. 1–12;—and perhaps Luke xvi. 9; xviii. 6–8.
