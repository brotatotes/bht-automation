<b> 20.] Gadara</b> (see on Matt. viii. 28) was one of the cities of Decapolis (see also on Matt. iv. 25). “Our Lord, in His humility, ascribed the work to His Father: but the healed man, in his gratitude, attributed it to Christ.” Euthymius. He commands the man to tell this, for He was little known in Perea where it happened, and so would have no consequences to fear, as in Galilee, &c.
