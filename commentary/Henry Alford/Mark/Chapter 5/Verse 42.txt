<p><strong> 42.] </strong> <strong> καὶ περιεπ</strong> <strong> .,</strong> peculiar to Mark.
	</p>
<p>The whole account is probably derived from the testimony of Peter, who was present. The <strong> ἦν γὰρ ἐτῶν δώδεκα</strong> is added, as Bengel, to shew that she “rediit ad statum ætati congruentem.”
	</p>
<p>Mar 5:43 betokens an eye-witness, who relates what passed <em> within</em> . Matt. says nothing of this, but tells what took place <em> without</em> , viz. the spreading abroad of the report. Notice in the last words, that her <em> further</em> recovery of strength is left to natural causes.</p>
