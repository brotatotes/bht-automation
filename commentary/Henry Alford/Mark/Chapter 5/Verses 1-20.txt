 1 20. ] HEALING OF A DÆMONIAC AT GERGESA. Matthew 8:28-34 .Luke 8:26-39; Luke 8:26-39 . The accounts of Mark and Luke are strictly cognate, and bear traces of having been originally given by two eye-witnesses, or perhaps even by one and the same, and having passed through others who had learnt one or two minute additional particulars. Matt.’s account is evidently not from an eye-witness. Some of the most striking circumstances are there omitted. See throughout notes on Matt.,  wherever the narrative is in common .
