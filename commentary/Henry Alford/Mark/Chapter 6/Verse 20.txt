<b> 20.] kept him safe</b>, or<b> preserved him</b>; not, as in A.V.<i> observed him</i>, or<i> ‘esteemed him highly</i>’:—kept him in safety that he should not be killed by Herodias. Whether Herod heard him only at such times as he happened to be at Machaerus, or took him also to his residence at Tiberias, is uncertain.
