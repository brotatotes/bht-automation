<p><strong> 23.</strong> ] The contracted ἡμίσους belongs to later Greek, as does also <strong> ἀθετέω</strong> <strong> ,</strong> <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="mr+6:26">Mark 6:26</span> . Webst. and Wilk. quote a parallel from Cic. de Senectute, c. xii.: “Flaminius, cum esset consul in Gallia, exoratus in convivio a scorto est, ut securi feriret aliquem eorum, qui in vinculis essent, damnati rei capitalis.”</p>
