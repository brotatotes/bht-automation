<b> 19. purging]</b> The participle refers to<b> the draught</b> (sewer). There need not be any difficulty in this additional clause: what is stated is<i> physically</i> true. The<i> sewer</i> is that which, by the removal of the part carried off, purifies the meat; the portion available for nourishment being in its passage converted into chyle, and the remainder being cast out.
