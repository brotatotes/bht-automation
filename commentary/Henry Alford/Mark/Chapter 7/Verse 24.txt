<p><strong> 24.</strong> ] <strong> ἐκεῖθεν</strong> is not, <em> from the land of Gennesaret</em> (Meyer), for ch. <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="mr+6:55-56">Mark 6:55-56</span> , has completely removed definiteness from the locality; but refers to the (unspecified) place of the last discourse.
	</p>
<p><strong> μεθόρια</strong> ] The place must have been the <em> neighbourhood of Tyre</em> . The word is used in Xen. Cyr. i. 4. 16, ἐν τοῖς μεθορίοις τοῖς τε αὐτῶν καὶ τοῖς Μήδων , in a sense approaching that in our text: the repetition of the τοῖς assigning μεθόρια to <em> both</em> countries.
	</p>
<p><strong> οὐδ</strong> <strong> . </strong> <strong> ἤθ</strong> <strong> . </strong> <strong> γν</strong> <strong> .</strong> ] Not (Fritz.), ‘ <em> wished to know no man</em> :’ but <strong> would have no man know it.</strong></p>
