<b> 3.]</b> The word<b> oft</b> thus rendered has perplexed all the Commentators. Of the various renderings which have been given of it, two only seem to be admissible: (1) that given in the text,<b> oft</b>; and (2)<b> diligently</b>, which is adopted by the ancient Syriac version, and seems agreeable to Hebrew usage. Between these two it is not easy to decide.
