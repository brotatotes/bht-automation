<b> 31.]</b> He went first northward (perhaps for the same reason, of privacy, as before) through Sidon, then crossed the Jordan, and so approached the lake on its E. side. On<i> Decapolis</i>, see Matt. iv, 25. We have the same journey related Matt. xv. 29; and “<i>the dumb speaking</i>” mentioned among the miracles, for which the people glorified the God of Israel.
