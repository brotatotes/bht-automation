<b> 10.]</b> Matthew mentions<i> Magadan</i>, ver. 39. Dalmanutha was probably a village in the neighbourhood,—see note on Matthew, and “The Land and the Book,” p. 393;—a striking instance of the independence of Mark: called by the Harmonists “an addition to St. Matthew’s narrative, to shew his independent knowledge of the fact.” What very anomalous writers the Evangelists must have been!
