<p><strong> 10.</strong> ] Matt. mentions <em> Magadan</em> , <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="mt+15:39">Matthew 15:39</span> . Dalmanutha was probably a village in the neighbourhood, see note on Matt., and The Land and the Book, p. 393; a striking instance of the independence of Mark: called by the Harmonists “an addition to St. Matthew’s narrative, to shew his independent knowledge of the fact.” Wordsw. What very anomalous writers the Evangelists must have been!</p>
