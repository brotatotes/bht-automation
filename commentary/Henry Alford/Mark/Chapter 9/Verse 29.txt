<p><strong> τοῦτο τὸ γένος</strong> ] That there are <em> kinds</em> , more and less malicious, of evil spirits, we find from Mat 12:45 and the pertinacity and cruelty of this one shewed him to belong to the worst kind. The Lord’s saying here (if the doubtful words are to stand) is rather for their <em> after</em> guidance, than their present; for <em> they could not fast while He was with them</em> , ch. <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="mr+2:19">Mark 2:19</span> .</p>
