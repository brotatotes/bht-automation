<b> 38.]</b> Only found besides in Luke, vv. 49, 50.<br><br> Notice the<i> repetition</i> of<b> he followeth not us as</b> characteristic of Mark. The connexion of this remark with what goes before, is: ‘If the receiving any one, even a little child,<i> in thy Name</i>, be receiving Thee; were we doing right when we forbade one who used thy Name, but did not follow us?’ “Let those observe this,” says Bengel, “who bind on spiritual gifts to canonical succession.” This man actually did what the very Apostles themselves were specially appointed to do: and our Lord, so far from prohibiting, encourages him; see Num. xi. 26–29.
