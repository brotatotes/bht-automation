<p><strong> 39.</strong> ] See <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="1co+12:3">1 Corinthians 12:3</span> . The very success of the miracle will awe him, and prevent him from soon or lightly speaking evil of me.
	</p>
<p>We must beware of supposing that the application of this saying is to be confined to the <em> working of a miracle</em> Mar 9:40 shews that it is general a weighty maxim of Christian toleration and charity, and caution to men how they presume to limit the work of the Spirit of God to any sect, or succession, or outward form of Church: cf. <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="php+1:16-18">Philippians 1:16-18</span> . See the way in which the nearly opposite inference is extracted from the words, in the very curious note of Bp. Wordsw. here.</p>
