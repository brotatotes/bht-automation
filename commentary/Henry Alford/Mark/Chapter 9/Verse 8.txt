<b> 8. no man any more]</b> i.e.<b> none of those who appeared</b>, but ( ‘<i>nay on the contrary</i>’)<b> Jesus alone</b>.<br><br><b> what the rising from the dead should mean</b> does not refer to the<i> Resurrection generally</i>, for it was an article of Jewish belief, and connected with the times of the Messiah; but to<i> His Resurrection as connected with his Death; the whole</i> was enigmatical to them.
