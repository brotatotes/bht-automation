	Mark 9:44  ; Mark 9:46  ;  Mar 9:48 are only in Mark; they are cited from Isaiah (see reff.), where the prophecy is of the  carcases of those who have transgressed against the Lord . This triple repetition gives sublimity, and leaves no doubt of the discourse having been  verbatim thus uttered. See note on Matthew 5:22 .
