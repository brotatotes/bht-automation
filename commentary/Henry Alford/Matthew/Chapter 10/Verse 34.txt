<b> 34.</b>] In Luke xii. 51–53 this announcement, as here, is closely connected with the mention of our Lord’s own sufferings (ver. 38). As He won His way to victory thro the contradiction of sinners and strife, so must those who come after Him. The immediate reference is to the divisions in families owing to conversions to Christianity. Ver. 35 is quoted nearly literally from Micah vii. 6. When we read in Commentators that these divisions were not the purpose, but the inevitable results only, of the Lord’s coming, we must remember that with God,<i> results</i> are all<i> purposed</i>.
