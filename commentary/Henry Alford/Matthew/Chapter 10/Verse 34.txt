 34. ] In Luk 12:51-53 this announcement, as here, is closely connected with the mention of our Lord’s own sufferings ( Mat 10:38 ). As He won His way to victory through the contradiction of sinners and strife, so must those who come after Him. The immediate reference is to the divisions in families owing to conversions to Christianity. Mat 10:35 is quoted nearly literally from Micah 7:6 . When we read in Commentators, e.g. De Wette, that these divisions were not the purpose, but the inevitable results only, of the Lord’s coming, we must remember that with God,  results are all  purposed .
