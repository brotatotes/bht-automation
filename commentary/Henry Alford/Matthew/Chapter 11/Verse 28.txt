<b> 28.</b>] This is the great and final answer to the question,<i> Art thou He that should come, or do we look for another</i>? As before, we may observe the closest connexion between this and the preceding. As the Son is<i> the great Revealer</i>, and as the<i> to whomsoever He will</i> is by His grace extended to<i> all the weary</i>—all who feel their need—so He here<i> invites them to receive this revelation</i>,<b> learn of Me</b>. But the way to this heavenly wisdom is by quietness and confidence, rest unto the soul, the reception of the divine grace for the pardon of sin, and the breaking of the yoke of the corruption of our nature.
