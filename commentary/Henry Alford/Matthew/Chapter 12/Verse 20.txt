<b> 20.</b>] A proverbial expression for, ‘He will not crush the contrite heart, nor extinguish the slightest spark of repentant feeling in the sinner.’<br><br> Until He shall have brought out the conflict, the cause, the judgment, unto victory,—caused it, i.e. to<i> issue in victory</i>: i.e. such shall be his behaviour and such his gracious tenderness, during the day of grace: while the conflict is yet going on,—the judgment not yet decided.
