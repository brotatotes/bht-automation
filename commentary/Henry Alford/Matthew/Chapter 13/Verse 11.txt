<b> 11.</b>] The Kingdom of Heaven, like other kingdoms, has its secrets (<b>mysteries</b>,—see a definition by St. Paul in Rom. xvi. 25 f.,—viz. “Something kept secret since the world began, but now made manifest”) and inner counsels, which strangers must not know. These are only revealed to the humble diligent hearers,<b> to you</b>: to those who were immediately around the Lord with the twelve; not<b> to them</b> = “<i>the rest</i>” Luke, = “<i>them that are without</i>” Mark. (1 Cor. v. 12, 13.)
