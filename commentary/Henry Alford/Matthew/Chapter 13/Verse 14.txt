This prophecy is quoted with a similar reference John xii. 40: Acts xxviii. 26, 27; see also Rom. xi. 8.<br><br><b> is fulfilled</b>]<b> is being fulfilled</b>, ‘finds one of the stages of its fulfilment:’ a partial one having taken place in the contemporaries of the prophet. The prophecy is cited verbatim from the LXX, which changes the imperative of the Hebrew ( ‘Make the heart of this people fat,’&c., E.V.) into the indicative, as bearing the same meaning.<br><br><b> in them</b> properly signifies relation, ‘with regard to them.’
