<b> 23.</b>] The reason alleged by the disciples must be coupled with our Lord's unwillingness to be known, stated by St. Mark (vii. 24), and means, ‘<i>she will draw the attention of all upon us.</i>’<b> Send her away</b> does not necessarily imply<i> granting</i> her request, nor the contrary; but simply<b> dismiss her</b>, leaving the method to our Lord Himself.
