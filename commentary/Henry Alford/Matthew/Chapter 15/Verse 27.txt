<b> 27.</b>] The sense of the original is not given by ‘<i>yet</i>’ in the E.V. The woman, in her humility,<i> accepts</i> the appellation which our Lord gives her, and<i> grounds her plea upon an inference from it</i>. Her words also have a reference to “<i>let the children first be filled,</i>” expressed by Mark vii. 27. It is,<b> Yea, Lord: for even the dogs eat:</b> or,<b> for the dogs too eat</b>.
