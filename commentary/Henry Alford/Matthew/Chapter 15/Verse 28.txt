<p><strong> 28.</strong> ] In Mark, διὰ τοῦτον τὸν λόγον , ὕπαγε . The greatness of the woman’s faith consisted in this, that in spite of all discouragements she continued her plea; and not only so, but accepting and laying to her account all adverse circumstances, she out of them made reasons for urging her request. St. Mark gives the additional circumstance, that on returning to her house she found the devil gone out, and her daughter lying on the bed.</p>
