<b> 28.</b>] In Mark, “<i>For this saying, go thy way.</i>” The greatness of the woman’s faith consisted in this, that in spite of all discouragements she continued her plea; and not only so, but accepting and laying to her account all adverse circumstances, she ont of them made reasons for urging her request.<br><br> St. Mark gives the additional circumstance, that on returning to her house she found the devil gone out, and her daughter lying on the bed.
