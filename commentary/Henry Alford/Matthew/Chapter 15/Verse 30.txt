<p><strong> 30.</strong> ] <strong> κυλλοί</strong> are persons <em> maimed in the hands;</em> see Jerome in var. readd. (But it is also used of the feet, τί δεῦρο πόδα σὺ <strong> κυλλὸν</strong> ἀνὰ κύκλον κυκλεῖς ; Aristoph. Av. 1379.) The meaning need not be, that a <em> wanting member was supplied</em> to these persons; but that a debility, such as that arising from paralysis or wound, was healed.
	</p>
