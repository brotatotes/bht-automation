<b> 5.</b>] This crossing of the lake was not the voyage to Magadan mentioned in ch. xv. 39, for after the dialogue with the Pharisees, Mark adds (viii. 13), “<i>entering into the ship again he departed to the other side.</i>”<br><br><b> they forgot to take bread</b>; viz.<i> on their land journey further</i>. This is also to be understood in Mark (viii. 14), who states their having only one loaf in the ship, not to shew that they<i> had</i> forgotten to take bread before starting, but as a reason why they should have provided some on landing.
