 21 28. ] OUR LORD ANNOUNCES HIS APPROACHING DEATH AND RESURRECTION. REBUKE OF PETER. Mark 8:31-38 ; Mark 9:1 .Luke 9:22-27; Luke 9:22-27 . See note on Matthew 16:13 . Obscure intimations had before been given of our Lord’s future sufferings, see ch. Matthew 10:38 : John 3:14 , and of His resurrection, John 2:19 ( Mat 10:17-18 ?), but never yet plainly, as now. With Mark’s usual precise note of circumstances, he adds, καὶ παῤῥησίᾳ τὸν λόγον ἐλάλει .
