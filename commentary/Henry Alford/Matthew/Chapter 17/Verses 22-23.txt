 22, 23. ] OUR LORD’S SECOND ANNOUNCEMENT OF HIS DEATH AND RESURRECTION. Mark 9:30-32 .Luke 9:43-45; Luke 9:43-45 . This followed immediately after the miracle ( Mar 9:30 ); our Lord went privately through Galilee; ἐδίδασκεν  γὰρ κ . τ . λ .: the imparting of this knowledge more accurately to His disciples, which He had begun to do in the last chapter, was the reason for His privacy. For more particulars, see Luke 9:45 ; Mark 9:32 .
