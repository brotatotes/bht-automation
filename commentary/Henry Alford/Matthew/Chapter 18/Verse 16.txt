<p><strong> 16. </strong> <strong> παρ</strong> <strong> … </strong> <strong> ἔτι</strong> ] <strong> Go again, and take</strong> … The <em> first</em> attempt of brotherly love is to heal the wound, to remove the offence, <em> in secrecy:</em> to <em> cover</em> the sin: but if this cannot be done, the <em> next</em> step is, to take two or three, still, in case of an adjustment, <em> preventing publicity;</em> but in the other event, <em> providing sufficient legal witness</em> . See reff. and <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="joh+8:17">John 8:17</span> .
	</p>
<p><strong> ῥῆμα</strong> <strong> ,</strong> not <em> thing</em> , but <strong> word</strong> , as always. Cf. St. Paul’s apparent reference to these words of our Lord, <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="2co+13:1">2 Corinthians 13:1</span> .</p>
