<b> 23. Therefore]</b> ‘because this is so,’ because unlimited forgiveness is the law of the Kingdom of Heaven. The servants here are not<i> slaves,</i> but ministers or stewards. By the<i> commanding to be sold</i> of ver. 25 they could not be slaves in the literal sense. But in Oriental language all the subjects of the king, even the great ministers of state, are<i> called slaves.</i> The individual example is one in<i> high trust,</i> or his debt could never have reached the enormous sum mentioned. See Isa. i. 18.
