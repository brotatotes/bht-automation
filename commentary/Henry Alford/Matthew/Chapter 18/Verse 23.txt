<p><strong> 23. </strong> <strong> διὰ τοῦτο</strong> ] ‘because this is so,’ because unlimited forgiveness is the law of the Kingdom of Heaven. The δοῦλοι here are not <em> slaves</em> , but ministers or stewards. By the πραθῆναι of Mat 18:25 they could not be slaves in the literal sense.
	</p>
<p>But in Oriental language (see Herodotus passim) all the subjects of the king, even the great ministers of state, are called δοῦλοι . The individual example is one in <em> high trust</em> , or his debt could never have reached the enormous sum mentioned. See <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="isa+1:18">Isaiah 1:18</span> .</p>
