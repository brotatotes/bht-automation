<p><strong> 5.</strong> ] Having shewn the child as the pattern of humility, the Lord proceeds to shew the honour in which children are held in His heavenly kingdom; and not only actual, but <em> spiritual</em> children for both are understood in the expression ἓν παιδίον τοιοῦτον .
	</p>
<p>The <strong> receiving in My name</strong> is the serving ( ἔσται πάντων διάκονος Mar 9:35 ) with Christian love, and as belonging to Christ (see also ch. Mat 25:40 ).</p>
