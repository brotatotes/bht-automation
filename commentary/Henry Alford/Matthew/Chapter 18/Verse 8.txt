<p><strong> 8.</strong> ] The connexion is ‘ <em> Wilt thou avoid being the man on whom this woe is pronounced? then cut off all occasion of offence in thyself first</em> .’ The cautions following are used in a wider sense than in ch. <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="mt+5:29-30">Matthew 5:29-30</span> . In Mark, the ‘ <em> foot</em> ’ is expanded into a separate iteration of the command.
