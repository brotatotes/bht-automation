<b> 10.] the case,</b> not the cause of divorce just mentioned; nor, the condition of the man with his wife: but<b> the account to be given,</b><i> ‘the original ground and principle,’</i> of the relationship of man and wife. The disciples apprehend that the trials and temptations of marriage would prove sources of sin and misery. This question and its answer are peculiar to Matthew.
