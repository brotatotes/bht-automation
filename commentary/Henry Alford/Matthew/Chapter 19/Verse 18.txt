<b> 18.]</b> De Wette observes well, that our Lord gives this enumeration of the commandments to<i> bring out the self-righteous spirit of the young man,</i> which He before saw. He only mentions those of the second table, having in ver. 17, in His declaration respecting<i> “good,”</i> included those of the first. Mark has the addition of<i> “Defraud not,”</i> representing probably the tenth commandment.
