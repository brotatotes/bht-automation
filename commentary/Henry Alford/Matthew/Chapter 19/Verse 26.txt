<b> 26. beheld them]</b> Probably to give force to and impress what was about to be said, especially as it was a saying reaching into the spiritual doctrines of the Gospel, which they could not yet apprehend.<br><br><b> this,</b> salvation in general, and even of those least likely to be saved.<br><br><b> with,</b> in both cases, means, ‘in the estimation of.’
