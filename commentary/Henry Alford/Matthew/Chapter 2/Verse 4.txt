<b> 4. when he had gathered</b>] i.e. says Lightfoot, he<i> assembled the Sanhedrim</i>. For the Sanhedrim consisting of seventy-one members, and comprising Priests, Levites, and Israelites, under the term “<i>chief priests</i>” are contained the two first of these, and under “<i>scribes of the people</i>” the third.
