<b> 16.]</b> The last were first,<i> as equal to the first;</i> first,<i> in order of payment;</i> first,<i> as superior to the first</i> (no others being brought into comparison), in that their reward was<i> more</i> in proportion to their work, and<i> not married</i> by a murmuring spirit. The first were last in these same respects.
