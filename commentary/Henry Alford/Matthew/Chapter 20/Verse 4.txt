No positive stipulation is made with these second, but they are to depend on the justice of the house-holder. They might expect 3/4ths of a denarius. From the same dialogue being implied at the sixth and ninth hour (<i> “he did likewise”</i>) the<i> “whatsoever is right”</i> is probably in each case the corresponding part of the denarius, at least<i> in their expectation;</i> so that<i> it cannot be said that no covenant was made.</i>
