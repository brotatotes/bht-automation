 8. ] By the Mosaic law ( Deu 24:15 ) the wages of an hired servant were to be paid him  before night . This was at the twelfth hour, or sunset: see Matthew 20:12 . I do not think the  ἐπίτροπος must be pressed as having a spiritual meaning. If it has, it represents  Christ (see Hebrews 3:6 , and ch. Mat 11:27 ).
	 ἀρξάμενος is not merely expletive, but definite, as in Luke 23:5 .
