<p><strong> 17.</strong> ] If this is to be literally understood of the <em> village</em> (and not of a district round it, including part of the Mount of Olives; see Luk 21:37 ), this will be the <em> second night spent at Bethany</em> . I would rather of the two understand it <em> literally</em> , and that the spending the nights <em> on the Mount of Olives</em> did not begin till the <em> next night</em> (Tuesday).</p>
