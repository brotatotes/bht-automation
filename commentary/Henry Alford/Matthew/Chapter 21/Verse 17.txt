<b> 17.]</b> If this is to be literally understood of the<i> village</i> (and not of a district round it, including part of the Mount of Olives; see Luke xxi. 37), this will be the<i> second night spent at Bethany.</i> I would rather of the two understand it<i> literally,</i> and that the spending the nights<i> on the Mount of Olives</i> did not begin till the<i> next night</i> (Tuesday).
