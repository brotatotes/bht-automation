This is]</b> So Nicodemus, John iii. 2,<b> “we know</b><i> that thou art a teacher come from God,”</i> even at the beginning of His ministry; how much more then after three years spent in His divine working. The latent consciousness that Jesus<i> was the Messiah,</i> expressed in the prophecy of Caiaphas (John xi. 49–62, compare the<b> Thou hast said</b> of our ch. xxvi. 64), added no doubt to the guilt of the Jewish rulers in rejecting and crucifying Him, however this consciousness may have been accompanied with ignorance of one kind or other in all of them,—see Acts iii. 17 and note.
