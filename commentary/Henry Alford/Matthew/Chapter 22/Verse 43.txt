<b> 43. in spirit</b>] i.e.<b> by the inspiration of the Holy Spirit:</b> “<i>by</i> (<i>in</i>)<i> the Holy Ghost,</i>” Mark. This is a weighty declaration by our Lord of the inspiration of the prophetic Scriptures. St. Mark (ver. 37) adds to this “the common people heard him gladly.” Here then end the endeavours of His adversaries to entrap Him by questions: they now betake themselves to other means. “A new scene, as it were, henceforth opens.” Bengel.
