 18.] The primary meaning of this seems to be, that whosoever remained faithful till the destruction of Jerusalem, should be preserved from it. No Christian, that we know of, perished in the siege or after it: see below. But it has<i> ulterior</i> meanings, according to which the end will signify to an individual, the<i> day of his death</i> (see Rev. ii. 10),—his martyrdom, as in the case of some of those here addressed,—to the Church, endurance<i> in the faith to the end of all things.</i> See Luke xxi. 19, and note.
