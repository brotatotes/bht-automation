<p><strong> 31.</strong> ] In 1 Thess., as above, the voice of the Archangel and the trump of God are distinguished from one another, which seems to favour the reading which inserts <strong> καί</strong> here. This <em> is not the great Trumpet of the general Resurrection</em> (ref. 1 Cor.), except in so far as that may be spoken of as including also the first resurrection: see on this verse the remarkable opening of <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="ps+50:1-23">Psalms 50:1-23</span> ., which is itself a prophecy of these same times.</p>
