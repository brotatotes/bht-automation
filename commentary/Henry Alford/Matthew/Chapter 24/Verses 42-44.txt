 42 44. ] Our Lord here resumes the tone of direct exhortation with which He commenced. To the secure and careless He will come as a thief in the night: to His own, as their Lord. See Obadiah 1:5 ; Revelation 3:3 ; Rev 16:15 ; 1 Thessalonians 5:1-10 , where the idea is expanded at length. Compare Mat 24:7 there with our Matthew 24:49 , and on the distinction between those who are of the day, and those who are of the night, see notes, there.
