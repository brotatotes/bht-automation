<b> 20.]</b> The faithful servant does not take the praise to himself—<b>thou deliveredst unto me</b> is his confession—and<b> beside them</b> the enabling cause of his gain;— ‘without Me, can do nothing,’ John xv. 5. This is plainer in Luke (xix. 16), “<i>Thy pound hath gained ten pounds.”</i> See 1 Cor. xv. 10:—and on the joy and alacrity of these faithful servants in the day of reckoning, 1 Thess. ii. 19: 2 Cor. i. 14: Phil. iv. 1.
