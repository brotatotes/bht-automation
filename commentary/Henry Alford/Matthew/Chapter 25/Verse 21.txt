<b> 21.]</b> See the corresponding sentence in Luke xix. 17, and note. The joy here is not a<i> feast</i>, as sometimes interpreted, but that joy spoken of Heb. xii. 2, and Isa. liii. 11—that joy of the Lord arising from the completion of His work and labour of love, of which the first Sabbatical rest of the Creator was typical— Gen. i. 31; ii. 2,—and of which His faithful ones shall in the end<b> partake:</b> see Heb. iv. 3–11: Rev. iii. 21.
