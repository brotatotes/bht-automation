<b> 32.]</b> The expression<b> all the nations</b> implies<b> all the nations of the world</b>, as distinguished from the<i> elect already gathered to Him</i>, just as the Gentiles were by that name distinguished from his chosen people the Jews. Among these are “the other sheep which He has, not of this fold,” John x. 16.<br><br><b> he shall separate]</b> See Ezek. xxxiv. 17.<b> The sheep</b> are those referred to in Rom. ii. 7, 10;<b> the goats</b> in ib. vv. 8, 9, where this same<i> judgment according to works</i> is spoken of.
