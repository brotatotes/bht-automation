<b> 40. my brethren]</b> Not necessarily the saints with Him in glory—though primarily those—but also any of the great family of man. Many of those here judged may never have had an opportunity of doing these things to the saints of Christ properly so called.<br><br> In this is fulfilled the covenant of God to Abraham, “in thy seed shall all the nations of the earth (so in LXX) be blessed.” Gen. xxii. 18.
