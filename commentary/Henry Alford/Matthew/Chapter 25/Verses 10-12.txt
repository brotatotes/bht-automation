For it is  not the final coming of the Lord to judgment, when the day of grace will be past , that is spoken of, except in so far as it is hinted at in the background, and in the individual application of the parable (virtually, not actually) coincides, to each man, with the day of his death.  This feast is the  marriage supper of Revelation 19:7-9 (see also ib. Rev 19:2 );  after which these improvident ones gone to buy their oil shall be  judged in common with the rest of the dead , ibid. Matthew 20:12-13 .
