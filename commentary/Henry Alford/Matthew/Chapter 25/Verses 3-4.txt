The lamps being the hearts lit with the flame of heavenly love and patience, supplied with the oil of the Spirit, now comes in the difference between the wise and foolish: the one  made no provision for the supply of this the others  did . How so? The wise ones  gave all diligence to make their calling and election sure (2 Peter 1:10 ; 2Pe 1:5-8 ), making their bodies, souls, and spirits (their  vessels , 2Co 4:7 ) a means of supplying spiritual food for the light within, by seeking, in the appointed means of grace, more and more of Godâ€™s Holy Spirit. The others  did not this but trusting that the light, once burning, would ever burn, made no provision for the strengthening of the inner man by watchfulness and prayer.
