<p><strong> οἱ πωλοῦντες</strong> are the ordinary dispensers of the means of grace <em> ultimately</em> of course God Himself, who alone can give his Spirit. The counsel was good, and well followed but the time was past. (Observe that those who sell are a <em> particular class</em> of persons no mean argument for a <em> set and appointed ministry</em> and moreover for a <em> paid</em> ministry.
