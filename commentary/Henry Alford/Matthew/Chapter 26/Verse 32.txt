<b> 32.]</b> In this announcement our Lord seems to have in mind the remainder of the verse in Zechariah: “and I will turn mine hand upon the little ones.” As this could not be<i> cited</i> in any intelligible connexion with present circumstances, our Lord gives the announcement of its fulfilment, in a promise to<i> precede</i> them (a pastoral office, see John x. 4) into Galilee, whither they should naturally return after the feast was over: see ch. xxviii. 7, 10, 16.
