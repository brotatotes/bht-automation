<b> 34.]</b> The very words<i> in their order</i> are, I doubt not, reported by St. Mark,— “<i>This day, even in this night, before the cock crow twice, thou shalt deny me thrice.</i>” The contrast to Peter’s boast, and the climax, is in these words the strongest; and the inference also comes out most clearly, that they likewise were not now said for the first time.
