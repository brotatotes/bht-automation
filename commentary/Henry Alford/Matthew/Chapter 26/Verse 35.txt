<b> 35.]</b> This<b> though I must</b> again appears to have the precision of a repeated asseveration. St. Mark has the stronger expression<i> “he spake the more vehemently,”</i> which even more clearly indicates that the<i> die with thee</i> not now first said. The rest said it, but not so earnestly perhaps;—at all events, Peter's confidence cast theirs into the shade.
