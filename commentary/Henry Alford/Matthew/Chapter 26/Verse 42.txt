 42.] St. Mark merely says of this second prayer, “<i>he... spake the same words</i>.” St. Luke gives it as “<i>prayed more earnestly</i>”—and relates in addition, that His sweat was like the fall of drops of blood on the ground: see notes on Luke xxii. 44. At what precise time the angel appeared to Him is uncertain: I should be inclined to think,<i> after the first prayer</i>, before He came to His disciples.
