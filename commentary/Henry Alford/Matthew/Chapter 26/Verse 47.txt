<b> 47.]</b> Judas is specified as “<i>one of the Twelve</i>,” probably because the appellation, as connected with this part of his history, had become the usual one—thus we have in Luke “<i>he that was called Judas, one of the Twelve</i>”—fuller still. To the<i> reader</i>, this specification is not without meaning, though that meaning may not have been intended.
