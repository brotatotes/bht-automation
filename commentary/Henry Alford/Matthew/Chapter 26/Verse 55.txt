<b> 55.]</b> St. Mark begins this with an<i> answered</i>—it was an answer to their<i> actions</i>, not to their words. St. Luke, here minutely accurate, informs us that it was<i> to the chief priests and captains of the temple elders</i>, that our Lord said this.<br><br> In his submission to be reckoned among the transgressors, our Lord yet protests against any suspicion that He could<i> act</i> as such.<br><br><b> sat</b> (Matt. only) to indicate complete quiet and freedom from attack.<br><br><b> daily</b>—during the week past, and perhaps at other similar times.<br><br><b> sat teaching</b> is the greatest possible contrast to a thief and robber.
