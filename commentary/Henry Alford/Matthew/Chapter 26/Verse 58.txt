<p>We have not here the more complete detail of <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="joh+18:15-19">John 18:15-19</span> . The <strong> αὐλή</strong> is one and the same great building, in which both Annas and Caiaphas lived. This is evident from a comparison of the narratives of Peter’s denial: see below. The circumstance of a fire being lighted and the servants sitting round it, mentioned by the other three Evangelists, is here omitted.</p>
