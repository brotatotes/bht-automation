<b> 61.]</b> See ch. xxvii. 40: the<i> false witness</i> consisted in<i> giving that sense to His words,</i> which it appears by ch. xxvii. 63 they<i> knew they did not bear.</i> There is perhaps a trace, in the<i> different reports</i> of Matt. and Mark, of the<i> discrepancy between the witnesses.</i> There is considerable difference between the words attributed to Him here, and there.
