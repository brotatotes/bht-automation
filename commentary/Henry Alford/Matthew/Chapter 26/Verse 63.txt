The junction of<i> the Son of God</i> with<i> the Christ</i> must not be pressed beyond the meaning which Caiaphas probably assigned to it—viz. the title given to the Messiah from the purport of the prophecies respecting Him. it is however a very different thing when our Lord by His answer<i> affirms this</i>, and invests the words with their fullest meaning and dignity.
