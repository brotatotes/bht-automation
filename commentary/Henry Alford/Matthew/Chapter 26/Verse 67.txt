<p><strong> 67.</strong> ] Luke gives these indignities, and in the same place as here, adding, what indeed might have been suspected, that it was not the members of the Sanhedrim, but <em> the men who held Jesus in custody</em> , who inflicted them on Him.
	</p>
<p><strong> κολαφίζω</strong> is <em> to strike with the fist</em> ; <strong> ῥαπίζω</strong> <strong> ,</strong> generally, <em> to strike a flat blow with the back of the hand</em> but also, and probably here, since another set of persons are described as doing it, <em> to strike with a staff</em> .</p>
