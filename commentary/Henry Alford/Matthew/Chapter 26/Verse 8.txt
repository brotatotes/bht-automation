<p><strong> 8. </strong> <strong> οἱ μαθηταί</strong> ] <em> Judas alone</em> is mentioned, <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="joh+12:4">John 12:4</span> . It may have been that some were found ready to second his remark, but that John, from his peculiar position at the table, if, as is probable, the same as in <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="joh+13:23">John 13:23</span> , may not have observed it. If so, the independent origin of the two accounts is even more strikingly shewn.
	</p>
