<p><strong> 1, 2.</strong> ] FINAL ANNOUNCEMENT OF HIS SUFFERINGS, NOW CLOSE AT HAND. (<span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="mr+14:1">Mark 14:1</span> .<span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="lu+22:1">Luke 22:1</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="lu+22:1">Luke 22:1</span> .) The public office of our Lord as a <em> Teacher</em> having been now fulfilled, His <em> priestly office</em> begins to be entered upon. He had not completed <em> all</em> his discourses, for He delivered, after this, those contained in <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="joh+14:1-31">John 14:1-31</span> ; <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="joh+15:1-27">John 15:1-27</span> ; <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="joh+16:1-33">John 16:1-33</span> ; Joh 17:1-26 but <em> not in public</em> ; only to the inner circle of his disciples. From this point commences THE NARRATIVE OF HIS PASSION.</p>
