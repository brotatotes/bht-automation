 30 35. ] DECLARATION THAT ALL SHOULD FORSAKE HIM. CONFIDENCE OF PETER. Mark 14:26-31 . See Luke 22:31-38 ; John 13:36-38 . Here, accurately speaking perhaps between ὑμνήσαντες and ἐξῆλθον , come in the discourses and prayer of our Lord in John 14-17, spoken (see note on Joh 14:31 ) without change of place, in the supper-chamber.
