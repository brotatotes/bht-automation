<b> 11.]</b> Before this Pilate had come out and demanded the cause of his being delivered up; the Jews not entering the Pretorium.<br><br> The primary accusation against Him seems to have been that He said that He was Christ a King. This is presupposed in the enquiry of this verse.<br><br><b> Thou sayest</b> is not to be rendered as a<i> doubtful</i> answer—much less with Theophylact, as meaning, ‘<i>Thou sayest it, not I:</i>’ but as a<i> strong affirmative</i>.
