 50. ] It has been doubted whether the τετέλεσται of John ( Joh 19:30 ) and πάτερ , εἰς χ . σου παρατίθεμαι τ . πν . μου of Luke ( Luk 19:46 ) are to be  identified with this crying out , or to be taken as  distinct from it . But a nearer examination of the case will set the doubt at rest. The παρέδωκεν of John (ib.)  implies the speech in Luke ; which accordingly was that uttered in this φωνὴ μεγάλη . The τετέλεσται was said before: see notes on John.
