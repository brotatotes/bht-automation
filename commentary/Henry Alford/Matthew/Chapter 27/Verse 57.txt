<b> 57.</b>]<i> Before sunset,</i> at which time the sabbath, and that an high day, began: see Deut. xxi. 23. The<i> Roman custom</i> was for the bodies to remain on the crosses till devoured by birds of prey. On the other hand Josephus says that the Jews were so careful about burying that they took down even those who had been crucified, and buried them before sunset.
