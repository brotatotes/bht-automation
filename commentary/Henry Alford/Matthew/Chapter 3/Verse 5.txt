<b> 5.</b>]<b> all the region round about Jordan</b> means all the neighbourhood of Jordan not included in “Jerusalem and Judæa” before mentioned. Parts of Peræa, Samaria, Galilee, and Gaulonitis come under this denomination.—There need be no surprise at such multitudes going out to John. The nature of his announcement, coupled with the prevalent expectation of the time, was enough to produce this effect. See, as strictly consistent with this account, chap. xi. 7–15.
