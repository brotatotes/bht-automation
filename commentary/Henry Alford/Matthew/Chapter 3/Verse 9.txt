<b> 9. think not to say</b>] Not merely equivalent to “<i>say not</i>:” but,<b> Do not fancy you may say</b>, &c. The expression<b> to say within yourselves</b>, as similar expressions in Scripture (e. g. Ps. x. 6, 11; xiv. 1: Eccl. i. 16; ii. 15, al. fr.), is used to signify the act by which outward circumstances are turned into thoughts of the mind.
