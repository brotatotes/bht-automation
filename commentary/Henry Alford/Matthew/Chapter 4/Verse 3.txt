<b> 3. when the tempter came</b>] From the words of both St. Mark and St. Luke, it appears that our Lord was tempted<i> also during the forty days</i>. Whether the words of St. Mark, “<i>he was with the wild beasts,</i>” allude to<i> one kind</i> of temptation, is uncertain: see note on Mark i. 13.—The words “<i>came to him</i>” need not be understood of the<i> first</i> approach, but the first<i> recorded</i>— ‘at a certain time the tempter approaching, &c’
