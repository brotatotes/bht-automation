<p><strong> 10.</strong> ] ‘Martyres non facit pœna, sed causa. Nam si pœna martyres faceret, omnia metalla martyribus plena essent, omnes catenæ martyres traherent: omnes qui gladio feriuntur, coronarentur. Nemo ergo dicat, Quia patior Justus sum. Quia ipse qui primo passus est, pro justitia passus est, ideo magnam exceptionem addidit. Beati qui persecutionem patiuntur <em> propter justitiam</em> .’ (Aug [39] Enarr. in <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="ps+34:13">Psalms 34:13</span> , vol. iv.) See <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="1pe+3:14">1 Peter 3:14</span> ; <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="1pe+4:14">1 Peter 4:14</span> , which probably refers to this verse. The repetition of the promise in Mat 5:3 is a close of the string of promises as it began. See the remarkable variation in the var. readd.
