<p><strong> ὑπηρέτης</strong> = πράκτωρ in <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="lu+12:58">Luke 12:58</span> , and is the officer of the court who saw the sentences executed. If we are called on to assign a meaning to ὑπηρέτης in the interpretation, it must represent the chief of those who in ch. <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="mt+18:34">Matthew 18:34</span> , are hinted at by βασανισταί , viz. the great enemy, the minister of the divine wrath.
