<p><strong> 19 34.</strong> ] From cautions against the hypocrisy of formalists, the discourse naturally passes to the <em> entire dedication of the heart to God</em> , from which all duties of the Christian should be performed. In this section this is enjoined, 1. ( Mat 6:19-24 ) with regard to earthly <em> treasures</em> , from the impossibility of serving God and Mammon: 2. ( Mat 6:25-34 ) with regard to earthly <em> cares</em> , from the assurance that our Father careth for us.</p>
