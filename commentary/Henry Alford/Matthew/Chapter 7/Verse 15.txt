<b> 15</b>.] The connexion is,—<i>strive to enter&c.: but be not misled by persons who pretend to guide you into it, but will not do so in reality</i>. These<b> false prophets</b>, directly, refer to those who were soon to arise, to deceive, if possible, even the very elect, ch. xxiv. 24; and indirectly, to all such false teachers in all ages<br><br><b> in sheep’s clothing</b>] There may be allusion to the prophetic dress, ch. iii. 4; but most probably it only means that, in order to deceive, they put on the garb and manners of the sheep themselves.
