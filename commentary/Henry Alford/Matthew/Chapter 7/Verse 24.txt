<b> 24. these sayings of mine</b>] more probably,<b> these sayings from me</b>: see Acts i. 4,<i> ye have heard of</i> (from)<i> me</i>. The expression,<b> these sayings</b>, seems to bind together the Sermon, and preclude,<b> as</b> indeed does the whole structure of the Sermon, the sup-position that these last chapters are merely a collection of sayings uttered at different times.
