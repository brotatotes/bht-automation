From St. Mark we learn that  the whole city was collected at the door; from St. Luke, that  the dæmons cried out and said, ‘Thou art Christ the Son of God .’ And from both, that  our Lord permitted them not to speak, for they knew Him . They brought the sick in the evening, either because it was cool, or because the day’s work was over, and men could be found to carry them, or perhaps because it was the sabbath (see Mark 1:21 ; Mark 1:29 ; Mar 1:32 ), which ended at sunset.
