<b> 16</b>.]<i> at sunset</i>, Mark ver. 82: Luke ver. 40. From St. Mark we learn that<i> the whole city was</i><i> collected at the door</i>; from St. Luke, that<i> the daemons cried out and said, ‘Thow art Christ the Son of God</i>.’ And from both, that<i> our Lord permitted them not to speak, for they knew Him</i>. They brought sick in the evening, either because it was cool,—or because the day’s work was over, and men could be found to carry them,—or perhaps because it was the sabbath (see Mark i. 21, 29, 82), which ended at sunset.
