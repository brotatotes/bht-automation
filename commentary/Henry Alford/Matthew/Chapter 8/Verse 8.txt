<p><strong> 8.</strong> ] The centurion heard that the Lord was coming, <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="lu+7:6">Luke 7:6</span> , and sent friends to Him with this second and still humbler message. He knew and felt himself, as a heathen, to be out of the fold of God, a stranger to the commonwealth of Israel; and therefore unworthy to receive under his roof the Redeemer of Israel.</p>
