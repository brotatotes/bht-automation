<b> 9</b>.] The meaning is, ‘I know how to obey, being<i> myself</i> under authority: and in turn know how others obey, having soldiers under me:’ inferring, ‘if then<b> I</b>, in my subordinate station of command, am obeyed, how much more Thou, who art over all, and whom diseases serve as their Master!” That this is the right interpretation, is shewn by our Lord’s special commendation of his faith, ver. 10.
