<p><strong> 11. </strong> <strong> ἰδόντες</strong> ] <strong> having observed this,</strong> see <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="mt+9:4">Matthew 9:4</span> . These Pharisees appear to have been the Pharisees <em> of the place:</em> Luke adds αὐτῶν : οἱ Φ . καὶ οἱ γραμ . αὐτῶν . The very circumstances related shew that this remonstrance cannot have taken place <em> at</em> the feast. The Pharisees say the words to the disciples: our Lord hears it. This denotes an occasion when our Lord and the disciples were present, but not surely intermixed with the ὄχλος τελωνῶν πολύς .</p>
