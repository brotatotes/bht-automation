<b> 11.</b>] These Pharisees appear to have been the Pharisees<i> of the place</i>: Luke has “<i>their Scribes and Pharisees.</i>” The very circumstances related shew that this remonstrance cannot have taken place<i> at</i> the feast. The Pharisees say the words to the disciples: our Lord hears it. This denotes an occasion when our Lord and the disciples were present, but not surely intermixed with the<i> great company of publicans.</i>
