<p><strong> 12. </strong> <strong> ἰσχύοντες</strong> <strong> .… </strong> <strong> κακῶς ἔχ</strong> <strong> .</strong> ] Both words, in the application of the saying, must be understood <em> subjectively</em> (‘ironica concessio,’ Calvin, Meyer): as referring to their respective opinions of themselves; as also δικαιους and ἁμαρτωλούς , <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="mt+9:13">Matthew 9:13</span> : not as though the Pharisees were <em> objectively</em> either ἰσχύοντες or δίκαιοι , however much objective truth κακῶς ἔχοντες and ἁμαρτωλοί may have had as applied to the publicans and sinners.</p>
