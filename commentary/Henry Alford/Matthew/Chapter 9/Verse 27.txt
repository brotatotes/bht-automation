<b> 27.</b>]<b> departed thence</b> is too vague to be taken as a fixed note of sequence; for “<i>thence</i>” may mean the house of Jaeirus, or the town itself, or even that part of the country,—as ver. 26 has generalized the locality, and implied some pause of time.<br><br><b> son of David</b>] a title of honour, and of recognition as the Messiah. It is remarkable that, in all the three narratives of giving sight to the blind in this Gospel, the title<b> Son of David</b> appears.
