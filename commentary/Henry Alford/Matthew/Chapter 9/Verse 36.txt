<b> 36. the multitudes</b>] Wherever He went, in all the cities.<br><br><b> harassed</b>] plagued,—viz. literally, with weariness in following Him; or spiritually, with the tyranny of the Scribes and Pharisees, their<i> heavy burdens</i>, ch. xxii. 4,<br><br><b> scattered abroad</b>] neglected, cast hither and thither, as sheep would be who had wandered from their pasture.
