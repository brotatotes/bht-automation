<b> 4. knowing</b>] lit.,<b> seeing</b>: viz. by the spiritual power indwelling in Him: See John ii. 24, 25. No other interpretation of such passages is admissible. St. Mark’s expression, “<i>perceived in his spirit,</i>” is more precise and conclusive. From<b> wherefore</b> to<b> thine house</b> is common (nearly verbatim) to the three Evangelists.
