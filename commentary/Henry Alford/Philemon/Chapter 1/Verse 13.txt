<b> 13.</b>]<b> I</b>, emphatic,<b> I, for my part. in thy stead</b>|For, wert thou here, thou wouldst. minister to me: I was minded therefore to retain him in thy place.<b> in the bonds of the gospel</b>] Explained well by Theodoret, “Thou owest me service as a disciple to a master, and a master who preaches divine things:” not without allusion also to the fetters which the Gospel had laid on himself.
