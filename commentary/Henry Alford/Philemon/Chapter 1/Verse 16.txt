<p><strong> 16</strong> .] And that, in a different relation from the one before subsisting. But οὐκέτι ὡς δοῦλον does not imply his manumission; rather the contrary: the stress is on <strong> ὡς</strong> and <strong> ὑπέρ</strong> ‘no longer <em> as</em> a slave (though he be one), but <em> above</em> a slave.’
	</p>
<p><strong> μάλιστα</strong> , ‘of all other men,’ of all those without thy house, with whom he has been connected: but πόσῳ μᾶλλον σοί , with whom he stands in so near and lasting a relation.</p>
