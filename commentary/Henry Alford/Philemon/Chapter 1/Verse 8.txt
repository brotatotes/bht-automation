<p><strong> 8</strong> .] <strong> διό</strong> relates to διὰ τ . ἀγάπ . below, and refers back to the last verse; it is not to be joined to the participial clause as Chrys., al.: it was not on account of Phm 1:7 that St. Paul had confidence to <em> command</em> him, but that he <em> preferred beseeching</em> him.
	</p>

	</p>
