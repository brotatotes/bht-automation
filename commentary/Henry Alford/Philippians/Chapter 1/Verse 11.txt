<p><strong> 11</strong> . <strong> πεπληρωμένοι καρπὸν δικαιοσ</strong> <strong> .</strong> ] <strong> filled with</strong> (the accusative of reference or secondary government, reff.) <strong> the fruit of righteousness</strong> (that result of work for God’s glory which is the product of a holy life: <strong> δικαιοσ</strong> <strong> .</strong> being here, the whole purified moral habit of the regenerate and justified man. Cf. καρπ . τοῦ πνεύματος , <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="ga+5:22">Galatians 5:22</span> , τ . φωτός , <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="eph+5:9">Ephesians 5:9</span> , δικαιοσύνης , Jam 3:18 ) <strong> which is</strong> (specifies the καρπός that it is not of nor by man, but) <strong> through Jesus Christ</strong> (by the working of the Spirit which He sends from the Father: “Silvestres sumus oleastri et inutiles, donee in Christum sumus insiti, qui viva sua radice frugiferas arbores nos reddit.” Calvin) <strong> unto the glory and praise of God</strong> (belongs to <strong> πεπληρωμένοι</strong> ).</p>
