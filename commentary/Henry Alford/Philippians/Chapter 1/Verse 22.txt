<b> 22.] But if</b> (not the hypothetical ‘if:’ but, assuming that it is so)<b> the continuing to live in the flesh</b> (expansion of “<i>to live</i>”),<b> this very thing</b> (this very<i> life</i> which I am undervaluing)<b> is to me the fruit of my work</b> (i. e. that in which the fruit of my apostolic ministry will be involved,—the condition of that fruit being brought forth),<b> then what</b> (i. e. which of the two)<b> I shall choose</b> (for myself)<b> I know not.</b>
