<b> 26.] that your matter of boasting</b> (not, as commonly rendered, ‘<i>your boasting</i>’ where the two can be distinguished they should be. Their Christian matter of boasting in him was, the possession of the Gospel, which they had received from him, which would abound, be assured and increased, by his presence among them)<b> may abound in Christ Jesus</b> (its field, element of increase, it being a Christian matter of glorying)<b> in me</b> (its field, element, of<i> abounding in Christ Jesus,</i> I being the worker of that which furnishes this material),<b> by means of my presence again with you.</b>
