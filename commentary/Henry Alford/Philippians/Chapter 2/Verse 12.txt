<b> 12. So then]</b> i.e. as a consequence on this pattern set you by Christ. The<b> more obedient</b> answers to “<i>becoming obedient</i>,” ver. 8, and<b> salvation</b> to the exaltation of Christ. It is therefore better to refer “<i>so then</i>” to that which has just preceded, than to all the foregoing exhortations, ch. i. 27 ff.
