<b> 14 ff.]</b><i> More detailed exhortations,</i> as to the manner of their Christian energizing.<br><br><b> murmuring,</b> in every other place in the N.T., as also in Exod. xvi. 7, 9, signifies murmuring against men, not against God. And the context here makes it best to keep the same sense: such murmurings arising from selfishness, which is especially discommended to us by the example of Christ.<br><br><b> disputings]</b> by the same rule, we should rather understand disputings with men, than doubts respecting God or duty.
