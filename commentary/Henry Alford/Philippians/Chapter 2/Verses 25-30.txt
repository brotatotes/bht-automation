<p><strong> 25 30</strong> .] <em> Of Epaphroditus: his mission: and recommendation of him</em> . Epaphroditus is not elsewhere mentioned. The name was a common one: see Wetst. h. l., and Tacit. Ann. xv. 55; Suet. Domit. 14. There is perhaps no reason for supposing him identical with Epaphras (<span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="col+1:7">Colossians 1:7</span> ; <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="col+4:12">Colossians 4:12</span> . Phm 1:23 ), who was a minister of the Colossian church.
	</p>
<p>We must not attempt to give a strict official meaning to each of the words predicated of Epaphroditus. The accumulation of them serves to give him greater recommendation in the eyes of the Philippians.</p>
