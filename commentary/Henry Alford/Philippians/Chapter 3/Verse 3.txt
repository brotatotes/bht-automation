<b> 3.</b>]<b> For we are the</b><i> real</i><b> CIRCUMcision</b> (whether bodily circumcised, or not—there would be among them some of both sorts: see Rom. ii. 25, 29; Col. ii. 11),<b> who worship</b> (pay religious service and obedience)<b> by the Spirit of God</b> (see John iv. 23, 24. ‘The Spirit of God is the agent, whereby our service is rendered: see Rom. v. 5; vili. 14; xii. 1; Heb. ix. 14. The emphasis is on it: for<i> both</i> profess a<i> worship</i>.<b> Of God is</b> expressed for solemnity),<b> and glory in</b> (stress on<i> This</i>,—are not ashamed of Him and seek our boast in circumcision, or the law, but make our<i> boast</i> in Him)<b> Christ Jesus, and trust not in the flesh</b> ( ‘but in the Spirit—in our union with Christ’).
