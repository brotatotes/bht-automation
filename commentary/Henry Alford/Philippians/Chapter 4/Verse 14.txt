<p><strong> συγκοινωνήσαντές μου τῇ θλίψει</strong> ] ὅρα σοφίαν , πῶς ἐπαίρει τὸ πρᾶγμα , Thl.: <strong> in that ye made yourselves partakers with my present tribulation</strong> (not <em> poverty</em> : by their <em> sympathy</em> for him they suffered with him; and their gift was a <em> proof</em> of this sympathy).</p>
