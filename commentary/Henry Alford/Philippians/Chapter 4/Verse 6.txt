<b> 6.</b>]<b> in every thing by your prayer and your supplication</b>: or even better,<b> by the prayer and the supplication</b> appropriate to each thing. On the difference between<i> prayer</i> and<i> supplication</i> see on Eph. vi. 18, 1 Tim. ii. 1.—Not “with<i> your</i> thanksgiving,” because the matters themselves may not be recognized as grounds of<i> thanksgiving</i>, but<i> it</i> should<i> accompany</i> every request.
