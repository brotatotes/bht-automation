<b>And it worketh all the power</b> (performs all the acts of authority)<b> of the first beast in his presence</b> (while the first beast is subsisting and beholding; and as the expression seems to shew, being in a relation to it of serving and upholding),<b> and maketh the earth and those that dwell in it to worship the first beast, whose wound of death was healed</b> (this was formerly, ver. 4, described as the reason why the world wondered after the former beast)
