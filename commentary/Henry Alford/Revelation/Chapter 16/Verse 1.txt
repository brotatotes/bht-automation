<b> 1.</b>]<i> Introductory</i>.<b> And I heard a great voice out of the temple</b><i><b></b></i> (from the fact, ch. xv. 8, that the divine Presence is filling the temple, and that none might enter into it, this voice can be no other than the diving voice)<b> saying to the seven angels, Go and pour out the seven vials of the wrath of God into the earth</b> (so, previous to the series of trumpets, the angel casts the fire from the altar into the earth, ch. viii. 5).
