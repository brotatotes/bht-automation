<b> 3</b>.]<b> And the second poured out his vial into the sea: and it</b> (the sea, compare ch. viii. 8, 11)<b> became blood as of a dead man</b> (blood as when a dead corpse lies in its Blood: loathsome and corrupting)<b> and every soul of life</b> (so literally:<b> soul</b> being used in its physical sense of animal soul)<b> died</b>, [all)<b> the things in the sea.</b>
