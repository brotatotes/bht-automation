<b>And I heard the altar saying</b> (certainly the simplest understanding of these words is, that they involve personification of the altar. On the altar are the prayers of the saints, offered before God: beneath the altar are the souls of the martyrs crying for vengeance: when therefore the altar speaks, it is the concentrated testimony of these which speaks by it),<b> Even so, Lord God Almighty, true and just are Thy judgments.</b>
