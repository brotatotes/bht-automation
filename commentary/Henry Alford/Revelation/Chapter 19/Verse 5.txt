<b>And a voice came forth from the throne</b> (from perhaps gives more the direction than the actual source of the voice. It is useless to conjecture whose voice it is; but we may say that [on account of the expression our God] it is not that of the Lamb. Our Lord never spoke thus: compare John xx. 17, note)<b> saying, Give praise to our God, all His</b><b> servants</b> (see Ps. cxxxiv. 1), and ye that fear Him, the small and the great (sec Ps. cxv. 18).
