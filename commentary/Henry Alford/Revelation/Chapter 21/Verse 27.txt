<b>And there shall never enter into her every thing unclean, and working abomination</b><b> and falsehood, but only</b> (literally,<b> except</b>)<b> they that are written in the book of life of the Lamb</b> (if then the kings of the earth, and the nations, bring their glory und their treasures into her, and if none shall ever enter into her that is not written in the book of life, it follows, that those kings, and these nations, are written in the book of life. And so perhaps some light may he thrown on one of the darkest, mysteries of redemption. There may be,—I say with all diffidence–those who have been saved by Christ without ever forming a part of his visible organized Church).
