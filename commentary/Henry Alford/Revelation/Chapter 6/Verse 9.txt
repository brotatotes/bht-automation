<b> 9.] And when he opened the fifth seal, I saw under the altar</b> (it is an altar of<i> sacrifice</i> which is here meant; the peculiar form of the word<i> slain</i>, which follows, seems plainly to imply this: see below)<b> the souls</b> (i. e. departed spirits. It is manifestly idle to enquire, seeing that the Apostle was in a state of spiritual and supernatural vision,<i> how</i> these disembodied spirits became visible to him, That they were not clothed with bodies is manifest)<b> of those that have been slain on account of the word of God and on account of the testimony which they had</b> (so literally: i. e, which was committed to them to bear, and which they bore: see ch. xii. 17.
