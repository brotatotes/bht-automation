<b> 28.</b>] There is, in the original, a play on words in this place, which can hardly be expressed in any other language.<b> Because they reprobated the knowledge of God, God gave them over to a reprobate mind</b>, is indeed a very inadequate, but as far as the<i> form</i> of the two words is concerned, an<i> accurate</i> representation of it. Mr. Conybeare gives it— ‘ ‘As they thought fit to cast out the acknowledgment of God, God gave them over to an outcast mind.”
