<p><strong> 8 17.</strong> ] OPENING OF THE EPISTLE. <em> His thankfulness for the faith of the Romans: remembrance of them in his prayers: wish to visit them: hindrances hitherto, but still earnest intention of doing so, that he may further ground them in that Gospel, of which he is not ashamed, inasmuch as it is</em> THE POWER OF GOD TO ALL WHO BELIEVE. <em> This leads to the announcement</em> ( <em> in a citation from the Scripture</em> ) <em> of one great subject of the Epistle</em> , viz.: JUSTIFICATION BY FAITH.</p>
