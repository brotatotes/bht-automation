 11 .]  For (proof of the former part of Rom 10:10 )  the Scripture saith, Every one who believeth on Him shall not be ashamed . πᾶς is neither in the LXX nor the Heb., but is implied in the indefinite participle. The Apostle seems to use it here as taking up παντὶ τῷ πιστεύοντι , Romans 10:4 . See ch. Romans 9:33 .
