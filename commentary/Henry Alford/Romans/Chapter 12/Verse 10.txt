<b> 10.</b>] The word rendered<b> affectionate</b> is properly used of the love of near relatives to one another, and agrees therefore exactly with<b> brotherly love.</b><br><br><b> out-doing one another</b>] Not, as A.V., “<i>preferring one another:</i>” but as Chrysostom explains it, “do not wait to be loved by another, but thyself spring forward to the act, and make the beginning.”
