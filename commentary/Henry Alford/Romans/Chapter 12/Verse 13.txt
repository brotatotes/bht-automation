<b> 13. given to hospitality</b>] This is but a feeble rendering of the original, which is,<b> pursuing</b>, making earnestly a point of,<b> hospitality</b>; and on which Chrysostom remarks, “He does not say,<i> practising</i>, but<i> pursuing</i>, teaching us not to wait for those that are in need, but rather to run after them and track them out.”
