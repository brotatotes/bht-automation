<b> 7. ministry</b>]<i> any subordinate ministration in the Church.</i> In Acts vi. 1 and 4, we have the word applied both to the lower ministration, that of alms and food, and to the higher, the<i> ministry of the word</i>, which belonged to the Apostles. But here it seems to be used in a more restricted sense, from its position as distinct from prophecy, teaching, exhortation, &c.
