<b> 3.</b>] And the<i> tendency of</i> these powers is<i> salutary:</i> to encourage good works, and discourage evil. Tholuck observes, that this verse is a token that the Apostle wrote the Epistle<i> before the commencement of the Neronian persecution.</i> Had this been<i> otherwise</i>, the<i> principle</i> stated by him would have been<i> the same:</i> but he could hardly have passed so apparent an exception to it without remark.
