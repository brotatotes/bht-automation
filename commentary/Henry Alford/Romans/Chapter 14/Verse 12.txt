<b> 12.</b>] The stress is on<b> concerning himself:</b> and the next verse refers back to it, laying the emphasis on<b> one another.</b> ‘Seeing that our account to God will be of<i> each man’s own self</i>, let us take heed lest by judging<i> one another</i> (<b>judging</b> here in the general sense of ‘pass judgment on,’ including both the despising of the strong and the<i> judging</i> of the weak)<i> we incur the guilt of destroying one another.</i>’
