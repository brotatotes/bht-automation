<p><strong> 7.</strong> ] This verse illustrates the κυρίῳ of the former, and at the same time sets in a still plainer light than before, that <em> both parties</em> , the eater and the abstainer, are servants of another, even Christ.
	</p>
<p><strong> ἑαυτῷ</strong> and <strong> κυρίῳ</strong> are datives commodi: <strong> ζῇν</strong> and <strong> ἀποθνήσκειν</strong> represent the whole sum of our course on earth.</p>
