In the latter a more direct announcement is given of the<i> share which the Gentiles were to have</i> in the root of Jesse. The version is that of the Septuagint, which here differs considerably from the Hebrew. The latter is nearly literally rendered in A.V.: “And in that day there shall be a root (Hebrew, ‘and it shall happen in that day, the branch’) of Jesse, which shall stand for an ensign of the people: to it shall the Gentiles seek.”
