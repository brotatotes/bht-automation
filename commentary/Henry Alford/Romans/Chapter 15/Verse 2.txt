<p><strong> 2.</strong> ] The qualification, εἰς τὸ ἀγ . πρὸς οἰκ ., excludes all <em> mere</em> pleasing of men from the Christian’s motives of action. The Apostle repudiates it in his own case, <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="ga+1:10">Galatians 1:10</span> . Bengel remarks, ‘ <em> bonum</em> , genus, <em> ædificatio</em> , species:’ to a good end, and that good end his edification.</p>
