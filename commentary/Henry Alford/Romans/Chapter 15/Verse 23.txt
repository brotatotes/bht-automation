<b> 23.</b>]<b> I have no more</b><b> place</b>, or<b> occasion</b> (viz. of apostolic work.—On the construction of this sentence, see note in my Greek Test.).—Respecting the question whether this journey into Spain was ever taken, the views of Commentators have differed, according to their conclusion respecting the liberation of the Apostle from his imprisonment at Rome. I have discussed this in the Introduction to the Pastoral Epistles,§ii.
