<p><strong> 27.</strong> ] The <em> fact</em> is re-stated, with a view to an inference from it, viz. that the εὐδόκησαν was not merely a matter of benevolence, but of <em> repayment</em> : the Gentiles being debtors to the Jews for spiritual blessings. This general principle is very similarly enounced in <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="1co+9:11">1 Corinthians 9:11</span> . It is suggested by Grot., al., that by this Paul wished to hint to the Romans the duty of a similar contribution.</p>
