<b> 4.</b>] The Apostle both justifies the above citation, and prepares the way for the subject to be next introduced, viz. the<i> duty of unanimity,</i> grounded on the testimony of these Scriptures to Christ. The expression,<b> whatsoever things were written aforetime,</b> applies to the<i> whole ancient Scriptures,</i> not to the prophetic parts only.
