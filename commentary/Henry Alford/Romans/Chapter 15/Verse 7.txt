 7. ]  Wherefore (on which account, viz. that the wish of the last verse may be accomplished)  receive (see ch. Rom 14:1 )  one another, as Christ also received you, with a view to God’s glory (that this is the meaning of εἰς δόξαν τοῦ θεοῦ , appears by Romans 15:9 , τὰ δὲ ἔθνη ὑπὲρ ἐλέους  δοξάσαι τὸν θεόν ).
	The Apostle does not expressly name  Jewish and Gentile converts as those to whom he addresses this exhortation, but it is evident from the next verse that it is so.
