<b> 13.</b>] Rufus may have been the son of Simon of Cyrene, mentioned Mark xv, 21: but the name was very common,<b> the elect,</b>—not to be softened to merely<i> excellent</i>, a sense unknown to our Apostle;—<b>elect</b>, i.e. one of the eleet of the Lord.<b> and</b> mine the Apostle adds from affectionate regard towards the mother of Rufus: ‘my mother,’ in my reverence and affection for her. Jowett compares our Lord’s words to St. John, John xix. 27.
