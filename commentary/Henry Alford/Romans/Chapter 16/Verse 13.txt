<p><strong> 13.</strong> ] Rufus may have been the son of Simon of Cyrene, mentioned <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="mr+15:21">Mark 15:21</span> ; but the name was very common.
	</p>
<p><strong> ἐκλεκτόν</strong> not to be softened, as De W., al., to merely ‘eximium,’ a sense unknown to our Apostle; <strong> elect</strong> , i.e. one of the elect of the Lord.
	</p>
<p><strong> καὶ ἐμοῦ</strong> the Apostle adds from affectionate regard towards the mother of Rufus: ‘my mother,’ in my reverence and affection for her. Jowett compares our Lord’s words to St. John, <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="joh+19:27">John 19:27</span> .</p>
