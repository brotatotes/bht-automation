<p><strong> 18.</strong> ] <strong> χρηστολογία</strong> , κολακεία , Theophyl. Wetstein cites from Julius Capitolinus, in Pertinace, 13, “omnes, qui libere conferebant, male Pertinacem loquebantur, <em> chrestologum</em> eum appellantes, qui bene loqueretur et male faceret.”
