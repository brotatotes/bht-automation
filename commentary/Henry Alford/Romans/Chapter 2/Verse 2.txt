<p><strong> 2.</strong> ] <strong> οἴδ</strong> <strong> . </strong> <strong> δέ</strong> , ‘ <em> atqui scimus</em> ’ <strong> now we know.
	</strong></p>
<p><strong> κατὰ ἀλ</strong> <strong> .</strong> ] <strong> according to truth</strong> , as E. V., De Wette: not, ‘ <em> truly,’ ‘revera</em> ’ (as Raphel, &amp;c.) for οἴδαμεν , on which the emphasis is, implies certain knowledge. Nor does κατὰ ἀλ . belong to κρῖμα , ‘ <em> judgment according to truth</em> ’ (as Olsh.), but to ἐστίν , <strong> is</strong> , (proceeds) <strong> according to justice</strong> ( Joh 8:16 ).</p>
