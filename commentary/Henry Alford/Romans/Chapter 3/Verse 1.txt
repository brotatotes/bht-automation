<p><strong> 1.</strong> ] <strong> οὖν</strong> , ‘quæ cum ita sint.’ If true Judaism and true circumcision be merely spiritual, what is the profit of external Judaism and ceremonial circumcision?
	</p>
<p><strong> περισσόν</strong> ] <strong> advantage, profit, pre-eminence</strong> , see reff. It is best to take the question, not as <em> coming from an objector</em> , which supposition has obscured several parts of this Epistle, but as <em> asked by the Apostle himself</em> , anticipating the thoughts of his reader.</p>
