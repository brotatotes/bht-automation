<b> 27–IV. 25.</b>] JEWISH BOASTING ALTOGETHER REMOVED<i> by this truth</i>, NOT<i> however</i> BY MAKING VOID THE LAW,<i> nor</i> BY DEGRADING ABRAHAM FROM HIS PRE-EMINENCE,<i> but</i> BY ESTABLISHING THE LAW,<i> and shewing that Abraham was really</i> JUSTIFIED BY FAITH,<i> and is the</i> FATHER OF THE FAITHFUL.<br><br><b> 27.</b>]<b> our</b> (or,<b> the</b>)<b> boasting</b>, viz.<i> of the Jews</i>, of which he had spoken before, ch. ti.:—not ‘<i>boasting</i>’ in general, which will not suit ver, 29.
