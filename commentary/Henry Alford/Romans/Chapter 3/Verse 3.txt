The word does not import ‘did<i> not believe</i>,’ which certainly would be out of place here, where the Apostle is not speaking of faith or want of faith as yet, but of<i> unrighteousness</i> (ver. 5), and moral guilt. The word seems tobe used in the sense of<i> were unfaithful</i><i> to the covenant</i>, the very condition of which was to walk in the ways of the Lord and observe his statutes.
