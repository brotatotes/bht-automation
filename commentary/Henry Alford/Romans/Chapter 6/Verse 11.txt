<b> 11.</b>]<i> An exhortation to realize this state of death unto sin and life unto God with Christ</i>.<b> Even so</b> (after the same manner as Christ)<b> reckon ye yourselves</b> (better than ‘<i>infer yourselves to be</i>’)<b> dead</b> (indeed)<b> unto sin</b> (as ver. 2 and following),<b> but alive unto God in Christ Jesus</b> (i. e. ‘<i>by virtue of your union with Him</i>: not<i> through</i> Christ Jesus; in this chapter it is not Christ’s<i> Mediatorship</i>, but His<i> Headship</i>, which is prominent).
