<b> 1.</b>] Connect with vi. 14, which is in fact the sentence immediately preceding. The steps of the proof are these:<i> The law binds a man only so long as he lives</i> (ver. 1): for example,—a married woman is only bound to her husband so long as he lives (vv. 2, 3):—so also the Christian<i> being dead</i> with Christ and alive to Him<i> is freed from the law</i> (ver. 4).
