<b> 11.</b>]<b> For</b> (explanatory how ver. 10 happened)<b> sin</b> (the sinful principle within me),<b> having found an occasion</b> (connect as in ver. 8, where see note),—<b>through</b> (by means of)<b> the commandment deceived me</b> (there is a plain reference to the Tempter deceiving Eve, which was accomplished by means of the commandment, exciting doubt of and objection to it, and lust after the forbidden thing: see 2 Cor. xi. 3; 1 Tim. ii. 14),<b> and through</b> (by means of)<b> it slew me</b> (i. e. brought me into the state of misery and death mentioned in ver. 10;—but there is an allusion again to the effect of the fall as the act of the Tempter).
