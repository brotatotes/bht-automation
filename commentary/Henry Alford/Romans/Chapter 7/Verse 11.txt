<p><strong> 11.</strong> ] <strong> For</strong> (explanatory how Rom 7:10 happened) <strong> sin</strong> (the sinful principle within me) <strong> having found occasion</strong> (absol. as in <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="ro+7:8">Romans 7:8</span> , where see note), <strong> by means of the commandment deceived me</strong> (there is a plain reference to the Tempter deceiving Eve, which was accomplished by means of the commandment, exciting doubt of and objection to it, and lust after the forbidden thing: see reff. 2 Cor., 1 Tim.), <strong> and by it slew me</strong> (i.e. brought me into the state of misery and death, mentioned in <span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="ro+7:10">Romans 7:10</span> ; but there is an allusion again to the effect of the fall as the act of the Tempter).</p>
