 11. ]  For (explanatory how Rom 7:10 happened)  sin (the sinful principle within me)  having found occasion (absol. as in Romans 7:8 , where see note),  by means of the commandment deceived me (there is a plain reference to the Tempter deceiving Eve, which was accomplished by means of the commandment, exciting doubt of and objection to it, and lust after the forbidden thing: see reff. 2 Cor., 1 Tim.),  and by it slew me (i.e. brought me into the state of misery and death, mentioned in Romans 7:10 ; but there is an allusion again to the effect of the fall as the act of the Tempter).
