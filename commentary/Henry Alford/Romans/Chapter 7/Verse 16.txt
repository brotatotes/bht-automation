<p><strong> 16.</strong> ] <strong> But if</strong> (= ‘ <em> now seeing that</em> ;’ takes up the foregoing and draws an inference from it) <strong> what I wish not, that I do, I agree with</strong> (bear witness to) <strong> the law that it is good</strong> (viz. ‘in that the <em> law</em> prohibits what <em> I also</em> dislike, the law and I are as one in proscribing the thing, the <em> law</em> , and <em> my wish</em> , tend the same way’).</p>
