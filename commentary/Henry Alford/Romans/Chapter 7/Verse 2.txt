<b> 2.</b>]<b> For</b> (not merely an example, but the example is itself the proof)<b> the woman which hath an husband</b> (literally, the woman under a husband, in subjection to a husband)<b> is bound by the law to her husband while he liveth</b> (literally,<b> to her living husband</b>):<b> but if her husband die, she is loosed from</b> (literally, annulled from)<b> the law of</b> ( ‘regarding,’ compare “<i>the law of the leper</i>,” Levit. xiv. 2)<b> the husband.</b>
