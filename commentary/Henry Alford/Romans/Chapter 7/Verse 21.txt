This is the view of Calv., Beza, Grot., Estius, Wolf, Winer, Meyer (<span class="scriptRef" com="hac" lang="eng" ref="ex+1:0">Exodus 1:0</span> , but in subsequent editions he has altered his view more than once), De Wette, al. It cannot well be referred to the <em> Mosaic law</em> , as, with various forced arrangements and constructions, Chrys., Theophyl., Theodoret, Tholuck, Olsh., Fritz., Köllner; the great objection being, that all these <em> do violence to the context</em> .
