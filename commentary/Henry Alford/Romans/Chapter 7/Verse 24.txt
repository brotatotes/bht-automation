<b> 24.]</b><i> The division of the man against himself,—his inward conflict, and miserable state of captivity to sin in the flesh, while with the mind he loves and serves the law of God. From this wretched condition, which is a very death in life, who shall deliver him?</i> The expression<b> body</b> cannot, well be figurative, meaning,<i> “the totality of sins,”</i> or<i> “the deadly mass of sin,”</i> but must, on account of the part which the<i> flesh</i> and the<i> members</i> have hitherto borne, be<i> literal</i>.
