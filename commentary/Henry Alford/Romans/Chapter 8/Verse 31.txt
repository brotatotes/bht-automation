<b> 31.</b>]<b> What then shall we say to these things</b> (what answer can the hesitating or discouraged find to this array of the merciful acts of God’s love on behalf of the believer)?<b> If God is for us</b> (and this He has been proved to be, vv. 28–30,—in having foreknown, predestinated, called, justified, glorified us),<b> who shall be</b> (or, is: there is no verb expressed in the original)<b> against us?</b>
