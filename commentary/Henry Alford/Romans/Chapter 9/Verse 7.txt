<b> 7.</b>]<b> Nor, because they are</b> (physically)<b> the seed of Abraham, are they all children</b> (so as to inherit the promise):<b> but</b> (we read) “<b>In Isaac shall thy seed be called</b>” (i. e. those only shall be called truly and properly, for the purposes of the covenant, thy seed, who are descended from Isaac, not those from Ishmael or any other son).
