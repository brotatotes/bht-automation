The datives are of the element or condition: the same was expressed with ἐν , ch. Titus 1:13 ; ἵνα ὑγιαίνωσιν ἐν τῇ πίστει . The articles should not be overlooked. The occurrence of τῇ ἀγάπῃ and τῇ ὑπομονῇ prevents us from rendering τῇ πίστει objective as in ch. Titus 1:13 , and compels us to take the subjective and reflective meaning).
