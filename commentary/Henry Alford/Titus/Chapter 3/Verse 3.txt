<b> 3</b>.]<b> For</b> (reason why we should shew all meekness, &c.: “Because we were once, as the thief said to his fellow, in the same condemnation.” Theophylact)<b> we</b> (Christians)<b> also</b> (as well as they)<b> were</b> (emphatically prefixed)<b> once without understanding</b> (of spiritual things; see Eph. iv. 18),<b> disobedient</b> (to God, ch. i. 16: he is no longer speaking of<i> authorities</i>, but has passed into a new train of thought),<b> led astray, slaves to divers lusts and pleasures, passing our lives in malice and envy, hateful, hating one another.</b>
