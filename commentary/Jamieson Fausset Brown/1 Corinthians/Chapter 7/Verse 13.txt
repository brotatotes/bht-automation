13. the woman—a believer. 
	let her not leave him—"her
	husband," instead of "him," is the reading of the
	oldest manuscripts The Greek for "leave" is the same
	as in 1 Corinthians 7:12, "put
	away"; translate, "Let her not put away [that is,
	part with] her husband." The wife had the power of effecting a
	divorce by Greek and Roman law.
