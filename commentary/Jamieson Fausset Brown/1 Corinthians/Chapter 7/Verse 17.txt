17. But—Greek, "If
	not." "Only." Caution that believers should not make
	this direction (1 Corinthians 7:16; as
	ALFORD explains it) a
	ground for separating "of themselves" (1 Corinthians 7:16- :). Or, But if there be no hope of gaining
	over the unbeliever, still let the general principle be maintained,
	"As the Lord hath allotted to each, as God hath
	called each, so let him walk" (so the Greek in the oldest
	reading); let him walk in the path allotted to him and wherein he was
	called. The heavenly calling does not set aside our earthly callings.
	so ordain I in all
	churches—Ye also therefore should obey.
