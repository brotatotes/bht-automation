17. in the last days—meaning,
	the days of the Messiah (Isaiah 2:2);
	as closing all preparatory arrangements, and constituting the final
	dispensation of God's kingdom on earth. 
	pour out of my Spirit—in
	contrast with the mere drops of all preceding time. 
	upon all flesh—hitherto
	confined to the seed of Abraham. 
	sons . . . daughters . . .
	young men . . . old men . . . servants . . . handmaidens—without
	distinction of sex, age, or rank. 
	see visions . . . dream
	dreams—This is a mere accommodation to the ways in which the
	Spirit operated under the ancient economy, when the prediction was
	delivered; for in the New Testament, visions and dreams are rather
	the exception than the rule.
