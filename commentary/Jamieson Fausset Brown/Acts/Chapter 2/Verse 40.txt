40. with many other words did he
	testify and exhort—Thus we have here but a summary of Peter's
	discourse; though from the next words it would seem that only the
	more practical parts, the home appeals, are omitted. 
	Save yourselves from this
	untoward generation—as if Peter already foresaw the hopeless
	impenitence of the nation at large, and would have his hearers hasten
	in for themselves and secure their own salvation.
