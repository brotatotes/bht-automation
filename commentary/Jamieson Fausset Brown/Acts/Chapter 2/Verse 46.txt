46. daily . . . in the
	temple—observing the hours of Jewish worship. 
	and breaking bread from house
	to house—rather, "at home" (Margin), that is,
	in private, as contrasted with their temple-worship,
	but in some stated place or places of meeting. 
	eat their meat with
	gladness—"exultation." 
	and singleness of heart.
