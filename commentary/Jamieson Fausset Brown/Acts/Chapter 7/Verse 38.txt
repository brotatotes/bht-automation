38. in the church—the
	collective body of God's chosen people; hence used to denote the
	whole body of the faithful under the Gospel, or particular sections
	of them. 
	This is he that was in the
	church in the wilderness, with the angel . . . and with our
	fathers—alike near to the Angel of the Covenant, from whom he
	received all the institutions of the ancient economy, and to the
	people, to whom he faithfully reported the living oracles and among
	whom he set up the prescribed institutions. By this high testimony
	to Moses, Stephen rebuts the main charge for which he was on trial.
