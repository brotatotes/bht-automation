9. by me if any man enter in—whether
	shepherd or sheep. 
	shall be saved—the
	great object of the pastoral office, as of all the divine
	arrangements towards mankind. 
	and shall go in and out and
	find pasture—in, as to a place of safety and
	repose; out, as to "green pastures and still waters"
	(Psalms 23:2) for nourishment and
	refreshing, and all this only transferred to another clime, and
	enjoyed in another manner, at the close of this earthly scene (Psalms 23:2- :).
