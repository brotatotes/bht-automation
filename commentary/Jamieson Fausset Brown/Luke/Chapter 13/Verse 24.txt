24. Strive—The word signifies
	to "contend" as for the mastery, to "struggle,"
	expressive of the difficulty of being saved, as if one would
	have to force his way in.
strait gate—another
	figure of the same. (See on  :-). 
	for many . . . will
	seek—"desire," that is, with a mere wish or slothful
	endeavor. 
	and shall not be able—because
	it must be made a life-and-death struggle.
