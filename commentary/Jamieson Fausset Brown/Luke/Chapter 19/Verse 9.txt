9. Jesus said unto him—but
	also before all. 
	This day, c.—memorable
	saying! Salvation already come, but not a day old. 
	to this house—so
	expressed probably to meet the taunt, "He is gone to be guest,"
	&c. The house is no longer polluted it is now fit to receive Me.
	But salvation to a house is an exceedingly precious idea,
	expressing the new air that would henceforth breathe in it, and the
	new impulses from its head which would reach its members (Psalms 118:15;
	Acts 16:15; Acts 16:16;
	Acts 16:31). 
	son of Abraham—He was
	that by birth, but here it means a partaker of his faith,
	being mentioned as the sufficient explanation of salvation
	having come to him.
