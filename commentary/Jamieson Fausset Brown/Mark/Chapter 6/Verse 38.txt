38. He saith unto them, How many
	loaves have ye? go and see. And when they knew, they say, Five, and
	two fishes—John is more precise and full: "One of His
	disciples, Andrew, Simon Peter's brother, saith unto Him, There is a
	lad here which hath five barley loaves and two small fishes: but what
	are they among so many?" (John 6:8;
	John 6:9). Probably this was the
	whole stock of provisions then at the command of the disciples—no
	more than enough for one meal to them—and entrusted for the time to
	this lad. "He said, Bring them hither to me" (John 6:9- :).
