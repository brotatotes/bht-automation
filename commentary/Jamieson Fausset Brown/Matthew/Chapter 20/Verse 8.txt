8. So when even was come—that
	is, the reckoning time between masters and laborers (see  :-); pointing to the day of final account. 
	the lord of the vineyard
	saith unto his steward—answering to Christ Himself, represented
	"as a Son over His own house" ( :-; see Matthew 11:27; John 3:35;
	John 5:27). 
	Call the labourers and give
	them their hire, beginning from the last unto the first—Remarkable
	direction this—last hired, first paid.
