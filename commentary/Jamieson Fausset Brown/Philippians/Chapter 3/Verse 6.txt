6. Concerning—Translate as
	before and after, "As touching Zeal" (compare
	Acts 22:3; Acts 26:9).
	blameless—Greek,
	"having become blameless" as to ceremonial
	righteousness: having attained in the eyes of man blameless
	legal perfection. As to the holiness before God, which is the
	inner and truest spirit of the law, and which flows from "the
	righteousness of God by faith," he on the contrary declares (Acts 26:9- :) that he has not attained perfection.
