 CHAPTER 9 
	
 :-. THE BEARING
	OF THE FOREGOING
	TRUTHS UPON THE CONDITION
	AND DESTINY OF THE
	CHOSEN PEOPLE—ELECTION—THE
	CALLING OF THE GENTILES.
	 
	Too well aware that he was
	regarded as a traitor to the dearest interests of his people (Acts 21:33;
	Acts 22:22; Acts 25:24),
	the apostle opens this division of his subject by giving vent to his
	real feelings with extraordinary vehemence of protestation.
