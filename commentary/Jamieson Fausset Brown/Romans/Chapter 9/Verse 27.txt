27-29. Esaias also crieth—"But
	Isaiah crieth"—an expression denoting a solemn testimony
	openly borne (John 1:15; John 7:28;
	John 7:37; John 12:44;
	Acts 23:6; Acts 24:21).
	concerning Israel, Though the
	number of the children—"sons" 
	of Israel be as the sand of
	the sea, a—"the" 
	remnant—that is, the
	elect remnant only shall be saved.
