31. But Israel, which
	followed—"following" 
	after the law of
	righteousness, hath not attained—"attained not" 
	unto the law of
	righteousness—The word "law" is used here, we think,
	in the same sense as in Romans 7:23,
	to denote "a principle of action"; that is, "Israel,
	though sincerely and steadily aiming at acceptance with God,
	nevertheless missed it."
