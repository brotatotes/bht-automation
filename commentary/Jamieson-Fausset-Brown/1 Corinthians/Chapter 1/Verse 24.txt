<p><b>24. called</b>—(compare <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>). The same class as the "us which are (being) saved"
	(<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1co+1:18">1 Corinthians 1:18</span>); the elect, who have
	obeyed the call; called effectually (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="ro+8:28">Romans 8:28</span>;
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="ro+8:30">Romans 8:30</span>). 
	</p>
<p><b>Christ</b>—"Crucified"
	is not here added, because when the offense of the cross is overcome,
	"Christ" is received in all His relations, not only in His
	cross, but in His life and His future kingdom. 
	</p>
<p><b>power</b>—so meeting all
	the reasonable requirements of the Jews who sought "a sign."
	The cross (the death of a slave), which to the Jews (looking for a
	temporal Messiah) was a "stumbling-block," is really "the
	power of God" to the salvation of all who believe. 
	</p>
<p><b>wisdom of God</b>—so really
	exhibiting, and in the highest degree (if they would but see it),
	that which the Greeks sought after—<i>wisdom</i> (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="ro+8:30- :">Romans 8:30- :</span>).</p>
