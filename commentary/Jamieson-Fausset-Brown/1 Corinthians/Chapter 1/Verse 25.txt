<p><b>25. foolishness of God</b>—that
	is, God's plan of salvation which men deem "foolishness." 
	</p>
<p><b>weakness of God</b>—Christ
	"crucified through weakness" (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>, the great <i>stumbling-block</i> of the Jews), yet "living
	by the <i>power</i> of God." So He <i>perfects strength</i> out
	of the <i>weakness</i> of His servants (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1co+2:3">1 Corinthians 2:3</span>;
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="2co+12:9">2 Corinthians 12:9</span>).</p>
