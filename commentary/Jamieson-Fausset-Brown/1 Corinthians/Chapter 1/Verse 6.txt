<p><b>6.</b> According as the testimony of
	(of, and concerning) Christ (who is both the object and author of

	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1co+2:1">1 Corinthians 2:1</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1ti+2:6">1 Timothy 2:6</span>;

	(<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1co+12:3">1 Corinthians 12:3</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="mr+16:20">Mark 16:20</span>;
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="2co+1:21">2 Corinthians 1:21</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="2co+1:22">2 Corinthians 1:22</span>;

	(compare <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="php+1:7">Philippians 1:7</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="heb+2:3">Hebrews 2:3</span>),

	setting their seal to its truth, through the inward power of His
	Spirit, and the outward gifts and miracles accompanying it [CALVIN].</p>
