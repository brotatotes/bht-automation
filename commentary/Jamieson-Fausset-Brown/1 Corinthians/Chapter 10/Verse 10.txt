<p><b>10. some of them . . . murmured</b>—upon
	the death of Korah and his company, who themselves were murmurers
	(<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="nu+16:41">Numbers 16:41</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="nu+16:49">Numbers 16:49</span>).
	Their murmurs against Moses and Aaron were virtually murmurs against
	God (compare <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="ex+16:8">Exodus 16:8</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="ex+16:10">Exodus 16:10</span>).
	Paul herein glances at the Corinthian murmurs against himself, the
	apostle of Christ. 
	</p>
<p><b>destroyed</b>—fourteen
	thousand seven hundred perished. 
	</p>
<p><b>the destroyer</b>—THE same
	destroying angel sent by God as in <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="ex+12:23">Exodus 12:23</span>;
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="2sa+24:16">2 Samuel 24:16</span>.</p>
