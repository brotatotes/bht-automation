<p><b>12. thinketh he standeth</b>—stands
	and thinks that he stands [BENGEL];
	that is, stands "by faith . . . well pleasing" to God; in
	contrast to <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1co+10:5">1 Corinthians 10:5</span>, "with
	many of them God was not well pleased" (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1co+10:5- :">1 Corinthians 10:5- :</span>). 
	</p>
<p><b>fall</b>—from his place in
	the Church of God (compare <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1co+10:5- :">1 Corinthians 10:5- :</span>, "fell"). Both temporally and spiritually (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1co+10:5- :">1 Corinthians 10:5- :</span>). Our security, so far as relates to God, consists in faith;
	so far as relates to ourselves, it consists in fear.</p>
