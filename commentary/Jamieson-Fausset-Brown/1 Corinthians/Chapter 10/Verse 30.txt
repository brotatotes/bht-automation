<p><b>30. For</b>—The oldest manuscripts
	omit "For." 
	</p>
<p><b>by grace</b>—rather,
	"thankfully" [ALFORD].
	</p>
<p><b>I . . . be partaker</b>—I
	partake of the food set before me. 
	</p>
<p><b>evil spoken of</b>—by him
	who does not use his liberty, but will eat nothing without
	scrupulosity and questioning whence the meat comes. 
	</p>
<p><b>give thanks</b>—which
	consecrates all the Christian's acts (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="ro+14:6">Romans 14:6</span>;
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1ti+4:3">1 Timothy 4:3</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1ti+4:4">1 Timothy 4:4</span>).</p>
