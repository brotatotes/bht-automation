<p><b>1.</b> Rather belonging to the end
	of the tenth chapter, than to this chapter. 
	</p>
<p><b>followers</b>—<i>Greek,</i>
	"imitators." 
	</p>
<p><b>of Christ</b>—who did not
	please Himself (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="ro+15:3">Romans 15:3</span>); but
	gave Himself, at the cost of laying aside His divine glory, and dying
	as man, for us (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="eph+5:2">Ephesians 5:2</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="php+2:4">Philippians 2:4</span>;
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="php+2:5">Philippians 2:5</span>). We are to follow Christ
	first, and earthly teachers only so far as they follow Christ.</p>
