<p><b>23. less honourable</b>—"We
	think" the feet and the belly "less honorable," though
	not really so in the nature of things. 
	</p>
<p><b>bestow . . . honour</b>—<i>putting</i>
	shoes <i>on</i> (<i>Margin</i>) the feet, and clothes to cover the
	belly. 
	</p>
<p><b>uncomely parts</b>—the
	secret parts: the poorest, though unclad in the rest of the body,
	cover these.</p>
