<p><b>7. But</b>—Though all the gifts
	flow from the <i>one</i> God, Lord, and Spirit, the "manifestation"
	by which the Spirit acts (as He is hidden in Himself), varies in each
	individual. 
	</p>
<p><b>to every man</b>—<i>to each</i>
	of the members of the Church <i>severally.</i>
</p>
<p><b>to profit withal</b>—<i>with
	a view to the profit</i> of the whole body.</p>
