<p><b>11. When . . . a child</b>—
	(<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1co+3:1">1 Corinthians 3:1</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1co+14:20">1 Corinthians 14:20</span>).
	</p>
<p><b>I spake</b>—alluding to
	"tongues." 
	</p>
<p><b>understood</b>—or, "had
	the sentiments of." Alluding to "prophecy." 
	</p>
<p><b>I thought</b>—<i>Greek</i>
	"reasoned" or "judged"; alluding to "knowledge."
	</p>
<p><b>when I became . . . I put
	away</b>—rather, "now that I am become a man, I have done away
	with the things of the child."</p>
