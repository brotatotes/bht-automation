10. it may be—that is,
	perhaps, speaking by conjecture. "It may chance" ( :-). 
	so many—as may be
	enumerated by investigators of such matters. Compare "so much,"
	used generally for a definite number left undefined ( :-; also 2 Samuel 12:8). 
	kinds of voices—kinds
	of articulate speech. 
	without signification—without
	articulate voice (that is, distinct meaning). None is without
	its own voice, or mode of speech, distinct from the rest.
