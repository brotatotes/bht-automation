<p><b>10. it may be</b>—that is,
	perhaps, speaking by conjecture. "It may chance" (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>). 
	</p>
<p><b>so many</b>—as may be
	enumerated by investigators of such matters. Compare "so much,"
	used generally for a definite number left undefined (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>; also <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="2sa+12:8">2 Samuel 12:8</span>). 
	</p>
<p><b>kinds of voices</b>—kinds
	of articulate speech. 
	</p>
<p><b>without signification</b>—<i>without
	articulate voice</i> (that is, distinct meaning). <i>None is without
	its own voice,</i> or mode of speech, <i>distinct</i> from the rest.</p>
