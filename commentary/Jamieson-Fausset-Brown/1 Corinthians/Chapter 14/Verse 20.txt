20. Brethren—an appellation
	calculated to conciliate their favorable reception of his
	exhortation. 
	children in understanding—as
	preference of gifts abused to nonedification would make you (compare
	1 Corinthians 3:1; Matthew 10:16;
	Romans 16:19; Ephesians 4:14).
	The Greek for "understanding" expresses the will of
	one's spirit, Romans 8:6 (it
	is not found elsewhere); as the "heart" is the will of the
	"soul." The same Greek is used for "minded"
	in Romans 8:6. 
	men—full-grown. Be
	childlike, not childish.
