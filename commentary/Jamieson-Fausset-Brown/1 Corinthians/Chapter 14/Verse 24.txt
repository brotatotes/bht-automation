<p><b>24. all</b>—one by one (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>). 
	</p>
<p><b>prophesy</b>—speak the
	truth by the Spirit intelligibly, and not in unintelligible tongues. 
	</p>
<p><b>one</b>—"anyone."
	Here <i>singular;</i> implying that this effect, namely, <i>conviction
	by all,</i> would be produced on <i>anyone,</i> who might happen to
	enter. In <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1co+14:23">1 Corinthians 14:23</span> the <i>plural</i>
	is used; "unlearned or unbelievers"; implying that however
	many there might be, not one would profit by the tongues; yea, their
	being many would confirm them in rejecting the sign, as many
	unbelieving men together strengthen one another in unbelief;
	individuals are more easily won [BENGEL].
	</p>
<p><b>convinced</b>—convicted in
	conscience; said of the "one that believeth not" (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="joh+16:8">John 16:8</span>;
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="joh+16:9">John 16:9</span>). 
	</p>
<p><b>judged</b>—His secret
	character is opened out. "Is searched into" [ALFORD].
	Said of the "one unlearned" (compare <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="joh+16:9- :">John 16:9- :</span>).</p>
