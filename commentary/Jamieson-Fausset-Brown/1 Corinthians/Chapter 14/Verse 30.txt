<p><b>30. If any thing</b>—Translate,
	"<i>But</i> if any thing." 
	</p>
<p><b>another that sitteth by</b>—a
	hearer. 
	</p>
<p><b>let the first hold his
	peace</b>—Let him who heretofore spoke, and who came to the
	assembly furnished with a previous ordinary (in those times)
	revelation from God (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1co+14:26">1 Corinthians 14:26</span>),
	give place to him who at the assembly is moved to prophesy by a
	sudden revelation from the Spirit.</p>
