<p><b>9. So . . . ye</b>—who have life;
	as opposed to "things without life" (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>). 
	</p>
<p><b>by the tongue</b>—the
	language which ye speak in. 
	</p>
<p><b>ye shall speak</b>—Ye will
	be speaking into the air, that is, <i>in vain</i> (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>).</p>
