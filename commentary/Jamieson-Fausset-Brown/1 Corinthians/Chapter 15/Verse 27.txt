<p><b>27. all things</b>—including death
	(compare <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="eph+1:22">Ephesians 1:22</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="php+3:21">Philippians 3:21</span>;
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="heb+2:8">Hebrews 2:8</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1pe+3:22">1 Peter 3:22</span>).
	It is said, "<i>hath</i> put," for what God has said is the
	same as if it were already done, so sure is it. Paul here quotes <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1pe+3:22- :">1 Peter 3:22- :</span> in proof of his previous declaration, "For (it is
	written), '<i>He hath put all things under His feet.</i>'" 
	</p>
<p><b>under his feet</b>—as His
	footstool (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="ps+110:1">Psalms 110:1</span>). In
	perfect and lasting subjection. 
	</p>
<p><b>when he</b>—namely, God,
	who by His Spirit inspired the Psalmist.</p>
