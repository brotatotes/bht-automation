<p><b>in corruption</b>—<i>liable
	to corruption: corruptible:</i> not merely a prey <i>when dead</i> to
	corruption; as the contrast shows, "raised in incorruption,"
	that is, <i>not liable to corruption: incorruptible.</i></p>
