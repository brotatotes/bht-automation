<p><b>43. in dishonour</b>—answering to
	"our <i>vile</i> body" (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>); literally, "our body of humiliation": liable to
	various humiliations of disease, injury, and decay at last. 
	</p>
<p><b>in glory</b>—the garment of
	incorruption (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1co+15:42">1 Corinthians 15:42</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1co+15:43">1 Corinthians 15:43</span>)
	like His glorious body (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="php+4:21">Philippians 4:21</span>),
	which we shall put on (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1co+15:49">1 Corinthians 15:49</span>;
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1co+15:53">1 Corinthians 15:53</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="2co+5:2-4">2 Corinthians 5:2-4</span>).
	</p>
<p><b>in weakness</b>—liable to
	infirmities (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="2co+13:4">2 Corinthians 13:4</span>). 
	</p>
<p><b>in power</b>—answering to a
	"spiritual body" (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="2co+13:4- :">2 Corinthians 13:4- :</span>; compare <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="lu+1:17">Luke 1:17</span>,
	"Spirit and power"). Not liable to the weaknesses of our
	present frail bodies (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="isa+33:24">Isaiah 33:24</span>;
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="re+21:4">Revelation 21:4</span>).</p>
