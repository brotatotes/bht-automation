<p><b>48. As is the earthy</b>—namely,
	Adam. 
	</p>
<p><b>they . . . that are
	earthy</b>—All Adam's posterity in their <i>natural</i> state
	(<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="joh+3:6">John 3:6</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="joh+3:7">John 3:7</span>).
	</p>
<p><b>the heavenly</b>—Christ. 
	</p>
<p><b>they . . . that are
	heavenly</b>—His people in their regenerate state (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="php+3:20">Philippians 3:20</span>;
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="php+3:21">Philippians 3:21</span>). As the former precedes
	the latter state, so the <i>natural bodies</i> precede the <i>spiritual
	bodies.</i></p>
