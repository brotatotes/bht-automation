<p><b>7. seen of James</b>—the Less, the
	brother of our Lord (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="ga+1:19">Galatians 1:19</span>).
	The Gospel according to the Hebrews, quoted by JEROME
	[<i>On Illustrious Men,</i> p. 170 D.], records that "James
	swore he would not eat bread from the hour that he drank the cup of
	the Lord, till he should see Him rising again from the dead." 
	</p>
<p><b>all the apostles</b>—The
	term here includes many others besides "the Twelve" already
	enumerated (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1co+15:5">1 Corinthians 15:5</span>): perhaps
	the seventy disciples (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="lu+10:1">Luke 10:1</span>)
	[CHRYSOSTOM].</p>
