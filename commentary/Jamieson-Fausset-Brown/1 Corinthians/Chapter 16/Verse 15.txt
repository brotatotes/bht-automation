<p><b>15. first-fruits of Achaia</b>—the
	first Achæan converts (compare <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>). The image is from the <i>first-fruits</i> offered to the
	Lord (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="le+23:10">Leviticus 23:10</span>; compare <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="le+23:10- :">Leviticus 23:10- :</span>). The members of this family had been baptized by Paul
	himself (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1co+1:16">1 Corinthians 1:16</span>). 
	</p>
<p><b>addicted themselves to the
	ministry of the saints</b>—Translate, "Set themselves, (that
	is, voluntarily) to minister unto the saints" (compare <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1co+1:16- :">1 Corinthians 1:16- :</span>).</p>
