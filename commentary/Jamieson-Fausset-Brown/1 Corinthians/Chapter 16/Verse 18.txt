<p><b>18. refreshed my spirit and
	yours</b>—"yours" will be refreshed on receiving this
	letter, by knowing that "my spirit is refreshed" by their
	having come to me from you; and (perhaps) by the good report they
	gave of many of you (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>); <i>my refreshment of spirit</i> redounds to <i>yours,</i>
	as being my disciples (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="2co+7:13">2 Corinthians 7:13</span>;
	compare <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="zec+6:8">Zechariah 6:8</span>). 
	</p>
<p><b>acknowledge</b>—render them
	due acknowledgments by a kind reception of them: <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="zec+6:8- :">Zechariah 6:8- :</span>, "know" them in their true worth and treat them
	accordingly.</p>
