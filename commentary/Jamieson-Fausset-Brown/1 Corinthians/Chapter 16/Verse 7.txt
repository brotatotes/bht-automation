<p><b>7. I will not see you now by the
	way</b>—literally, "I do not wish to see you this time in
	passing"; that is, to pay you now what would have to be a merely
	passing visit as I did in the second visit (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>). In contrast to "a while," that is, <i>some
	time,</i> as the <i>Greek</i> might better be translated. 
	</p>
<p><b>but</b>—The oldest
	manuscripts read "for."</p>
