<p><b>18. seemeth</b>—that is, <i>is,
	and is regarded by</i> himself and others. 
	</p>
<p><b>wise in this world</b>—wise
	in mere worldly wisdom (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1co+1:20">1 Corinthians 1:20</span>).
	</p>
<p><b>let him become a fool</b>—by
	receiving the Gospel in its unworldly simplicity, and so <i>becoming
	a fool in the world's sight</i> [ALFORD].
	Let him <i>no longer think</i> himself wise, but seek the true wisdom
	from God, bringing his understanding into captivity to the obedience
	of faith [ESTIUS].</p>
