<p><b>19. with God</b>—<i>in the
	judgment of God.</i>
</p>
<p><b>it is written</b>—in <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>. The formula of quoting SCRIPTURE
	used here, establishes the canonicity of Job. 
	</p>
<p><b>He taketh . . . wise in . . .
	own craftiness</b>—proving the "foolishness" of the
	world's wisdom, since it is made by God the very snare to catch those
	who think themselves so wise. Literally, "He who taketh . . .
	the whole of the sentence not being quoted, but only the part which
	suited Paul's purpose.</p>
