<p><span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>. TRUE VIEW
	OF MINISTERS: THE
	JUDGMENT IS
	NOT TO BE
	FORESTALLED; MEANWHILE
	THE APOSTLES' LOW
	STATE CONTRASTS
	WITH THE CORINTHIANS'
	PARTY PRIDE,
	NOT THAT
	PAUL WOULD
	SHAME THEM,
	BUT AS A FATHER
	WARN THEM;
	FOR WHICH END
	HE SENT
	TIMOTHY, AND WILL
	SOON COME
	HIMSELF.</p>
