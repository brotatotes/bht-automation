<p><b>naked</b>—that is,
	insufficiently clad (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="ro+8:35">Romans 8:35</span>). 
	</p>
<p><b>buffeted</b>—as a <i>slave</i>
	(<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1pe+2:20">1 Peter 2:20</span>), the reverse of the
	state of the Corinthians, "reigning as <i>kings</i>" (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1pe+2:20- :">1 Peter 2:20- :</span>). So Paul's master before him was "buffeted" as a
	slave, when about to die a slave's death (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1pe+2:20- :">1 Peter 2:20- :</span>).</p>
