<p><b>4. judgments</b>—that is, cases
	for judgment. 
	</p>
<p><b>least esteemed</b>—literally,
	"those of no esteem." Any, however low in the Church,
	rather than the heathen (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1co+1:28">1 Corinthians 1:28</span>).
	Questions of earthly property are of secondary consequence in the
	eyes of true Christians, and are therefore delegated to those in a
	secondary position in the Church.</p>
