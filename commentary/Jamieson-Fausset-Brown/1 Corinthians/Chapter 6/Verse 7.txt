<p><b>7. utterly a fault</b>—literally,
	"a shortcoming" (not so strong as <i>sin</i>). Your going
	to law at all is a falling short of your high privileges, not to say
	your doing so <i>before unbelievers,</i> which aggravates it. 
	</p>
<p><b>rather take wrong</b>—
	(<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="pr+20:22">Proverbs 20:22</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="mt+5:39">Matthew 5:39</span>;
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="mt+5:40">Matthew 5:40</span>); that is, "suffer
	yourselves to be wronged."</p>
