<p><b>11. But and if she depart</b>—or
	"be separated." If the sin of separation has been
	committed, that of a new marriage is not to be added (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>). 
	</p>
<p><b>be reconciled</b>—by
	appeasing her husband's displeasure, and recovering his good will. 
	</p>
<p><b>let not . . . husband put
	away . . . wife</b>—In <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="mt+5:32">Matthew 5:32</span>
	the only exception allowed is, "saving for the cause of
	fornication."</p>
