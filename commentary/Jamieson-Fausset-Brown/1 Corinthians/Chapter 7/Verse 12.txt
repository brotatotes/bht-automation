<p><b>12. to the rest</b>—the other
	classes (besides "the married," <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>, where both husband and wife are believers) about whom the
	Corinthians had inquired, namely, those involved in mixed marriages
	with unbelievers. 
	</p>
<p><b>not the Lord</b>—by any
	direct command spoken by Him. 
	</p>
<p><b>she be pleased</b>—<i>Greek,</i>
	"consents": implying his wish in the first instance, with
	which hers <i>concurs.</i></p>
