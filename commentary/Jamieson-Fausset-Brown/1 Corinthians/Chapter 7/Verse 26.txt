<p><b>26. I suppose</b>—"I
	consider." 
	</p>
<p><b>this</b>—namely, "for
	a man so to be," that is, in the same state in which he is (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>). 
	</p>
<p><b>for</b>—by reason of. 
	</p>
<p><b>the present distress</b>—the
	distresses to which believers were then beginning to be subjected,
	making the married state less desirable than the single; and which
	would prevail throughout the world before the destruction of
	Jerusalem, according to Christ's prophecy (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>; compare <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="ac+11:28">Acts 11:28</span>).</p>
