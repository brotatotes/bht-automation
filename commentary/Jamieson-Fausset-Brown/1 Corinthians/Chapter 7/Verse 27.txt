<p><b>27.</b> Illustrating the meaning of
	"so to be," <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1co+7:26">1 Corinthians 7:26</span>.
	Neither the married (those "bound to a wife") nor the
	unmarried (those "loosed from a wife") are to "seek"
	a change of state (compare <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1co+7:20">1 Corinthians 7:20</span>;
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1co+7:24">1 Corinthians 7:24</span>).</p>
