<p><b>30. they that weep . . . wept
	not</b>—(Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="2co+6:10">2 Corinthians 6:10</span>). 
	</p>
<p><b>they that buy . . . possessed
	not</b>—(Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="isa+24:1">Isaiah 24:1</span>;
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="isa+24:2">Isaiah 24:2</span>). Christ specifies as
	the condemning sin of the men of Sodom not merely their open
	profligacy, but that "they bought, they sold," c., as men
	whose all was in this world (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="isa+24:2- :">Isaiah 24:2- :</span>). "Possessed" in the <i>Greek</i> implies a
	<i>holding fast of a possession</i> this the Christian will not do,
	for his "enduring substance" is elsewhere (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="isa+24:2- :">Isaiah 24:2- :</span>).</p>
