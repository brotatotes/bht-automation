<p><b>37. steadfast</b>—not to be turned
	from his purpose by the obloquy of the world. 
	</p>
<p><b>having no necessity</b>—arising
	from the natural inclinations of the daughter. 
	</p>
<p><b>power over his . . .
	will</b>—when, owing to his daughter's will not opposing his will,
	he has power to carry into effect his will or wish. 
	</p>
<p><b>decreed</b>—determined.</p>
