<p><b>I think also</b>—"I
	also think"; just as you Corinthians and your teachers <i>think</i>
	much of your opinions, <i>so I also give my opinion</i> by
	inspiration; so in <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1co+7:25">1 Corinthians 7:25</span>,
	"my judgment" or opinion. <i>Think</i> does not imply
	doubt, but often a matter of well-grounded assurance (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1co+7:25- :">1 Corinthians 7:25- :</span>).</p>
