<p><b>9. if they cannot contain</b>—that
	is, "have not continency." 
	</p>
<p><b>burn</b>—with the secret
	flame of lust, which lays waste the whole inner man. (Compare
	AUGUSTINE [<i>Holy
	Virginity</i>]). The dew of God's grace is needed to stifle the
	flame, which otherwise would thrust men at last into hell-fire.</p>
