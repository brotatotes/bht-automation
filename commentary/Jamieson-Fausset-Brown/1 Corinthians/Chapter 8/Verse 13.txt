<p><b>13. meat</b>—<i>Old</i> English
	for "food" in general. 
	</p>
<p><b>make . . . to offend</b>—<i>Greek,</i>
	"is a stumbling-block to." 
	</p>
<p><b>no flesh</b>—In order to
	ensure my avoiding flesh offered to idols, I would abstain from <i>all
	kinds of flesh,</i> in order not <i>to be a stumbling-block</i> to my
	brother.</p>
