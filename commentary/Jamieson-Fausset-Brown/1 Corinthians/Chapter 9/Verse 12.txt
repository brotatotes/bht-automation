<p><b>12. others</b>—whether true
	apostles (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1co+9:5">1 Corinthians 9:5</span>) or false ones
	(<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="2co+11:20">2 Corinthians 11:20</span>). 
	</p>
<p><b>we rather</b>—considering
	our greater labors for you (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="2co+11:20- :">2 Corinthians 11:20- :</span>). 
	</p>
<p><b>suffer all things</b>—without
	complaining of it. We desire to <i>conceal</i> (literally, "hold
	as a water-tight vessel") any distress we suffer from straitened
	circumstances. The same <i>Greek</i> is in <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="2co+11:20- :">2 Corinthians 11:20- :</span>. 
	</p>
<p><b>lest we . . . hinder . . .
	gospel</b>—not to <i>cause a hindrance</i> to its progress by
	giving a handle for the imputation of self-seeking, if we received
	support from our flock. The less of incumbrance and expense caused to
	the Church, and the more of work done, the better for the cause of
	the Gospel (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="2ti+2:4">2 Timothy 2:4</span>).</p>
