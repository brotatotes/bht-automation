<p><b>13. minister about holy things</b>—the
	Jewish priests and Levites. The <i>Greek</i> especially applies to
	the former, the priests <i>offering sacrifices.</i>
</p>
<p><b>partakers with the altar</b>—a
	part of the victims going to the service of the altar, and the rest
	being shared by the priests (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="le+7:6">Leviticus 7:6</span>;
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="nu+18:6">Numbers 18:6</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="de+18:1">Deuteronomy 18:1</span>,
	&amp;c.).</p>
