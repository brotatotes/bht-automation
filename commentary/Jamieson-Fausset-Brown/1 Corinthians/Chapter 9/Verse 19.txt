<p><b>19. free from all men</b>—that is,
	from the power of all men. 
	</p>
<p><b>gain the more</b>—that is,
	<i>as many of them</i> ("all men") <i>as possible.</i>
	"Gain" is an appropriate expression in relation to a
	"reward" (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1th+2:19">1 Thessalonians 2:19</span>;
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1th+2:20">1 Thessalonians 2:20</span>); he therefore repeats
	it frequently (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1th+2:20- :">1 Thessalonians 2:20- :</span>).</p>
