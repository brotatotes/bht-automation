<p><b>6. say</b>—profess. 
	</p>
<p><b>have fellowship with him</b>—
	(<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1jo+1:3">1 John 1:3</span>). The essence of the
	Christian life. 
	</p>
<p><b>walk</b>—in inward and
	outward action, whithersoever we turn ourselves [BENGEL].
	</p>
<p><b>in darkness</b>—<i>Greek,</i>
	"in <i>the</i> darkness"; opposed to "the light"
	(compare <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1jo+2:8">1 John 2:8</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1jo+2:11">1 John 2:11</span>).
	</p>
<p><b>lie</b>— (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1jo+2:11- :">1 John 2:11- :</span>). 
	</p>
<p><b>do not</b>—in <i>practice,</i>
	whatever we <i>say.</i>
</p>
<p><b>the truth</b>— (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="eph+4:21">Ephesians 4:21</span>;
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="joh+3:21">John 3:21</span>).</p>
