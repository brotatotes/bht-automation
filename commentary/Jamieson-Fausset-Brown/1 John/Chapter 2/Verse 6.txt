<p><b>6. abideth</b>—implying a
	condition lasting, without intermission, and without end. 
	</p>
<p><b>He that saith . . . ought</b>—so
	that his deeds may be consistent with his words. 
	</p>
<p><b>even as he</b>—Believers
	readily supply the name, their hearts being full of Him (compare <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>). "Even as He walked" when on earth, especially
	in respect to <i>love.</i> John delights in referring to Christ as
	the model man, with the words, "Even as He," &amp;c. "It
	is not Christ's walking on the sea, but His ordinary walk, that we
	are called on to imitate" [LUTHER].</p>
