<p><b>10. children of the devil</b>—(See
	on <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1jo+3:8">1 John 3:8</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1jo+3:8- :">1 John 3:8- :</span>). There is no middle class between the children of God and
	the children of the devil. 
	</p>
<p><b>doeth not
	righteousness</b>—Contrast <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1jo+3:8- :">1 John 3:8- :</span>. 
	</p>
<p><b>he that loveth not his
	brother</b>— (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1jo+4:8">1 John 4:8</span>); a
	particular instance of that <i>love</i> which is the sum and
	fulfilment of all righteousness, and the token (not loud professions,
	or even seemingly good works) that distinguishes God's children from
	the devil's.</p>
