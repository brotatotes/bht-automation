</p>
<p><b>of that wicked one</b>—Translate,
	"<i>evil</i> one," to accord with "Because his own
	works were <i>evil.</i>" Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>, "of the devil," in contrast to "of God,"
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1jo+3:10">1 John 3:10</span>. 
	</p>
<p><b>slew he him? Because his own
	works were evil, and his brother's righteous</b>—through envy and
	hatred of his brother's piety, owing to which God accepted Abel's,
	but rejected Cain's offering. Enmity from the first existed between
	the seed of the woman and the seed of the serpent.</p>
