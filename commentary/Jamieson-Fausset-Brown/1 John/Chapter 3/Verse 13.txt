<p><b>13. Marvel not</b>—The marvel
	would be if the world loved you. 
	</p>
<p><b>the world</b>—of whom Cain
	is the representative (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1jo+3:12">1 John 3:12</span>).
	</p>
<p><b>hate you</b>—as Cain hated
	even his own brother, and that to the extent of murdering him. The
	world feels its bad works tacitly reproved by your good works.</p>
