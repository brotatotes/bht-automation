<p><b>3. this hope</b>—of being
	hereafter "like Him." <i>Faith</i> and <i>love,</i> as well
	as <i>hope,</i> occur in <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1jo+3:11">1 John 3:11</span>;
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1jo+3:23">1 John 3:23</span>. 
	</p>
<p><b>in</b>—rather, "(resting)
	<i>upon</i> Him"; grounded on His promises. 
	</p>
<p><b>purifieth himself</b>—by
	Christ's Spirit in him (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="joh+15:5">John 15:5</span>,
	end). "Thou purifiest thyself, not of thyself, but of Him who
	comes that He may dwell in thee" [AUGUSTINE].
	One's justification through faith is presupposed. 
	</p>
<p><b>as he is pure</b>—unsullied
	with any uncleanness. The Second Person, by whom both the Law and
	Gospel were given.</p>
