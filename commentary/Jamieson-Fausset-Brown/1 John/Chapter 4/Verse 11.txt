<p><b>11.</b> God's love to us is the
	grand motive for our love to one another (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>). 
	</p>
<p><b>if</b>—as we all admit as a
	fact. 
	</p>
<p><b>we . . . also</b>—as being
	<i>born of God,</i> and therefore resembling our Father who is love.
	In proportion as we appreciate God's love to us, we love Him and also
	<i>the brethren,</i> the children (by regeneration) of the same God,
	the representatives of the unseen God.</p>
