<p><b>16. And we</b>—<i>John and his
	readers</i> (not as <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1jo+4:14">1 John 4:14</span>,
	<i>the apostles</i> only). 
	</p>
<p><b>known and believed</b>—True
	<i>faith,</i> according to John, is a faith of <i>knowledge</i> and
	experience: true <i>knowledge</i> is a knowledge of <i>faith</i>
	[LUECKE]. 
	</p>
<p><b>to us</b>—<i>Greek,</i> "in
	our case" (see on <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1jo+4:9">1 John 4:9</span>).
	</p>
<p><b>dwelleth</b>—<i>Greek,</i>
	"abideth." Compare with this verse, <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1jo+4:9- :">1 John 4:9- :</span>.</p>
