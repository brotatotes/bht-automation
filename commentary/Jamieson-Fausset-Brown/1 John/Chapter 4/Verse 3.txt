<p><b>3. confesseth not that Jesus Christ
	is come in the flesh</b>—IRENÆUS
	[3.8], LUCIFER, ORIGEN,
	on <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="mt+25:14">Matthew 25:14</span>, and <i>Vulgate</i>
	read, "Every spirit which <i>destroys</i> (<i>sets aside,</i> or
	<i>does away with</i>) Jesus (Christ)." CYPRIAN
	and POLYCARP support
	<i>English Version</i> text. The oldest extant manuscripts, which
	are, however, centuries after POLYCARP,
	read, "Every spirit that confesseth not (that is, refuses to
	confess) Jesus" (in His person, and all His offices and
	divinity), omitting "is come in the flesh." 
	</p>
<p><b>ye have heard</b>—from your
	Christian teachers. 
	</p>
<p><b>already is it in the world</b>—in
	the person of <i>the false prophets</i> (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="mt+25:14- :">Matthew 25:14- :</span>).</p>
