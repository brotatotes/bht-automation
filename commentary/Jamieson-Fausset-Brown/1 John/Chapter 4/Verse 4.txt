<p><b>4. Ye</b>—emphatical: YE
	who confess Jesus: in contrast to "them," the false
	teachers. 
	</p>
<p><b>overcome them</b>— (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1jo+5:4">1 John 5:4</span>;
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1jo+5:5">1 John 5:5</span>); instead of being
	"overcome and brought into (spiritual) bondage" by them
	(<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="2pe+2:19">2 Peter 2:19</span>). <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="joh+10:8">John 10:8</span>;
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="joh+10:5">John 10:5</span>, "the sheep did <i>not
	hear them</i>": "a stranger will they not follow, but will
	flee from him: for they know not the voice of strangers." 
	</p>
<p><b>he that is in you</b>—<i>God,</i>
	of whom ye are. 
	</p>
<p><b>he that is in the word</b>—the
	spirit of Antichrist, the devil, "the prince of this world."</p>
