<p><b>5. of the world</b>—They derive
	their spirit and teaching from the world, "unregenerate human
	nature, ruled over and possessed by Satan, the prince of this world"
	[ALFORD]. 
	</p>
<p><b>speak they of the word</b>—They
	draw the matter of their conversation from the life, opinions, and
	feelings of the world. 
	</p>
<p><b>the world heareth them</b>—
	(<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="joh+15:18">John 15:18</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="joh+15:19">John 15:19</span>).
	<i>The world loves its own.</i></p>
