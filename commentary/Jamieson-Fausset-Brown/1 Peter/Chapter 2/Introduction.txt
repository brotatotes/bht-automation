<p><span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>. EXHORTATIONS. 
	</p>
<p>To guileless feeding on the word
	by the sense of their privileges as new-born babes, living stones in
	the spiritual temple built on Christ the chief corner-stone, and
	royal priests, in contrast to their former state: also to abstinence
	from fleshly lusts, and to walk worthily in all relations of life, so
	that the world without which opposes them may be constrained to
	glorify God in seeing their good works. Christ, the grand pattern to
	follow in patience under suffering for well-doing.</p>
