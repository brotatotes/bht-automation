<p><b>20. what</b>—<i>Greek,</i> "what
	kind of." 
	</p>
<p><b>glory</b>—what peculiar
	<i>merit.</i>
</p>
<p><b>buffeted</b>—the punishment
	of slaves, and suddenly inflicted [BENGEL].
	</p>
<p><b>this is</b>—Some oldest
	manuscripts read, "for." Then the translation is, "But
	if when . . . ye take it patiently (it is a glory), <i>for</i> this
	is acceptable." 
	</p>
<p><b>acceptable</b>—<i>Greek,</i>
	"thankworthy," as in <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>.</p>
