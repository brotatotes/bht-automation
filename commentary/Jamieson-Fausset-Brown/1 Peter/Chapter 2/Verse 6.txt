<p><b>6. Wherefore also</b>—The oldest
	manuscripts read, "Because that." The statement above is so
	"<i>because</i> it is contained in Scripture." 
	</p>
<p><b>Behold</b>—calling
	attention to the glorious announcement of His eternal counsel. 
	</p>
<p><b>elect</b>—so also believers
	(<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1pe+2:9">1 Peter 2:9</span>, "chosen,"
	<i>Greek,</i> "<i>elect</i> generation"). 
	</p>
<p><b>precious</b>—in <i>Hebrew,</i>
<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="isa+28:16">Isaiah 28:16</span>, "a
	corner-stone of preciousness." See on <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="isa+28:16- :">Isaiah 28:16- :</span>. So in <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1pe+2:7">1 Peter 2:7</span>, Christ
	is said to be, to believers, "precious," <i>Greek,</i>
	"preciousness." 
	</p>
<p><b>confounded</b>—same <i>Greek</i>
	as in <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="ro+9:33">Romans 9:33</span> (Peter here as
	elsewhere confirming Paul's teaching. See <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="ro+9:33- :">Romans 9:33- :</span>
	also <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="ro+10:11">Romans 10:11</span>), "ashamed."
	In <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="isa+28:16">Isaiah 28:16</span>, "make
	haste," that is, flee in sudden panic, covered with the <i>shame</i>
	of confounded hopes.</p>
