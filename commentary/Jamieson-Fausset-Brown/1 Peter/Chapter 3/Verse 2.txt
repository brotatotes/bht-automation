<p><b>2. behold</b>—on narrowly looking
	into it, literally, "having closely observed." 
	</p>
<p><b>chaste</b>—pure, spotless,
	free from all impurity. 
	</p>
<p><b>fear</b>—<i>reverential,</i>
	towards your husbands. Scrupulously pure, as opposed to the noisy,
	ambitious character of worldly women.</p>
