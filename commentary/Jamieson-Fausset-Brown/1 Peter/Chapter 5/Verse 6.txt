<p><b>6. under the mighty hand</b>—afflicting
	you (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1pe+3:15">1 Peter 3:15</span>): "accept"
	His chastisements, and turn to Him that smiteth you. He depresses the
	proud and exalts the humble. 
	</p>
<p><b>in due time</b>—Wait humbly
	and patiently for His own fit time. One oldest manuscript and <i>Vulgate</i>
	read, "In the season of visitation," namely, His visitation
	in mercy.</p>
