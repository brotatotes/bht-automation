<p><b>4. as</b>—according as; even as. 
	</p>
<p><b>allowed</b>—<i>Greek,</i>
	"We have been approved on trial," "deemed fit."
	This word corresponds to "God which <i>trieth</i> our hearts"
	below. This approval as to sincerity depends solely on the grace and
	mercy of God (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="ac+9:15">Acts 9:15</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1co+7:25">1 Corinthians 7:25</span>;
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="2co+3:5">2 Corinthians 3:5</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1ti+1:11">1 Timothy 1:11</span>;
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1ti+1:12">1 Timothy 1:12</span>). 
	</p>
<p><b>not as pleasing</b>—not as
	persons who seek to please men; characteristic of false teachers (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1ti+1:12- :">1 Timothy 1:12- :</span>).</p>
