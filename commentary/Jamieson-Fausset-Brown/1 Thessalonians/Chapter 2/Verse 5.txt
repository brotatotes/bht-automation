<p><b>5. used we flattering
	words</b>—literally, "become (that is, have we been found) in
	(the use of) language of flattery"; the resource of those who
	try to "please men." 
	</p>
<p><b>as ye know</b>—"Ye
	know" as to whether I <i>flattered</i> you; as to
	"covetousness," GOD,
	the Judge of the heart, alone can be "my witness." 
	</p>
<p><b>cloak of</b>—that is, any
	specious guise under which I might cloak "covetousness."</p>
