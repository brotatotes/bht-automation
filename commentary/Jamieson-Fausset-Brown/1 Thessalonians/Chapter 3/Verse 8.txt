<p><b>8. now</b>—as the case is; seeing
	ye stand fast. 
	</p>
<p><b>we live</b>—we flourish. It
	<i>revives us</i> in our affliction to hear of your steadfastness
	(<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="ps+22:26">Psalms 22:26</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="2jo+1:3">2 John 1:3</span>).
	</p>
<p><b>if</b>—implying that the
	vivid joy which the missionaries "now" feel, <i>will
	continue</i> if the Thessalonians continue steadfast. They still
	needed exhortation, <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1th+3:10">1 Thessalonians 3:10</span>;
	therefore he subjoins the conditional clause, "if ye," &amp;c.
	(<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="php+4:1">Philippians 4:1</span>).</p>
