<p><b>3. For</b>—enforcing the assertion
	that his "commandments" were "by (the authority of)
	the Lord Jesus" (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1th+4:2">1 Thessalonians 4:2</span>).
	Since "this is the will of God," let it be your will also. 
	</p>
<p><b>fornication</b>—not
	regarded as a sin at all among the heathen, and so needing the more
	to be denounced (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="ac+15:20">Acts 15:20</span>).</p>
