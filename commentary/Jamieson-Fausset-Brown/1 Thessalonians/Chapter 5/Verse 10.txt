<p><b>10. died for us</b>—<i>Greek,</i>
	"in our behalf." 
	</p>
<p><b>whether we wake or
	sleep</b>—whether we be found at Christ's coming awake, that is,
	alive, or asleep, that is, in our graves. 
	</p>
<p><b>together</b>—<i>all</i> of
	us <i>together;</i> the living not preceding the dead in their
	glorification "with Him" at His coming (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>).</p>
