<p><b>6. others</b>—<i>Greek,</i> "the
	rest" of the world: the unconverted (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>). "Sleep" here is worldly apathy to spiritual
	things (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="ro+13:11">Romans 13:11</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="eph+5:14">Ephesians 5:14</span>);
	in <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1th+5:7">1 Thessalonians 5:7</span>, ordinary <i>sleep;</i>
	in <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1th+5:10">1 Thessalonians 5:10</span>, death. 
	</p>
<p><b>watch</b>—for Christ's
	coming; literally, "be wakeful." The same <i>Greek</i>
	occurs in <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1co+15:34">1 Corinthians 15:34</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="2ti+2:26">2 Timothy 2:26</span>.
	</p>
<p><b>be sober</b>—refraining
	from carnal indulgence, mental or sensual (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="2ti+2:26- :">2 Timothy 2:26- :</span>).</p>
