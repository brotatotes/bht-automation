<p><b>11. learn</b>—not "teach"
	(<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1ti+2:12">1 Timothy 2:12</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1co+14:34">1 Corinthians 14:34</span>).
	She should not even put questions in the public assembly (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1co+14:34- :">1 Corinthians 14:34- :</span>). 
	</p>
<p><b>with all subjection</b>—not
	"usurping authority" (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1co+14:34- :">1 Corinthians 14:34- :</span>). She might teach, but not in public (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1co+14:34- :">1 Corinthians 14:34- :</span>). Paul probably wrote this Epistle from Corinth, where the
	precept (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1co+14:34">1 Corinthians 14:34</span>) was in
	force.</p>
