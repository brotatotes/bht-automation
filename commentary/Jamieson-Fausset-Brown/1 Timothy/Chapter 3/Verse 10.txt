<p><b>10.</b> "And moreover," c.
	[ALFORD]. 
	</p>
<p><b>be proved</b>—not by a
	period of probation, but by a searching inquiry, conducted by
	Timothy, the ordaining president (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>), whether they be "blameless" then when found so,
	"let them act as deacons." 
	</p>
<p><b>blameless</b>—<i>Greek,</i>
	"unexceptionable"; as the result of public investigation
	unaccused [TITTMANN].</p>
