<p><b>3. Not given to wine</b>—The <i>Greek</i>
	includes <i>besides</i> this, not indulging in <i>the brawling,
	violent conduct towards others,</i> which proceeds from being given
	to wine. The opposite of "patient" or (<i>Greek</i>)
	"forbearing," reasonable to others (see on <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>). 
	</p>
<p><b>no striker</b>—with either
	hand or tongue: not as some teachers pretending a holy zeal (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>), answering to "not a brawler" or fighter
	(compare <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1ki+22:24">1 Kings 22:24</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="ne+13:25">Nehemiah 13:25</span>;
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="isa+58:4">Isaiah 58:4</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="ac+23:2">Acts 23:2</span>;
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="2ti+2:24">2 Timothy 2:24</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="2ti+2:25">2 Timothy 2:25</span>).
	</p>
<p><b>not covetous</b>—<i>Greek,</i>
	"not a lover of money," whether he have much or little (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="2ti+2:25- :">2 Timothy 2:25- :</span>).</p>
