<p><b>11. younger</b>—than sixty years
	old (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1ti+5:9">1 Timothy 5:9</span>). 
	</p>
<p><b>refuse</b>—to take on the
	roll of presbyteress widows. 
	</p>
<p><b>wax wanton</b>—literally,
	"over-strong" (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1ti+5:9- :">1 Timothy 5:9- :</span>). 
	</p>
<p><b>against Christ</b>—rebelling
	against Christ, their proper Bridegroom [JEROME].
	</p>
<p><b>they will</b>—<i>Greek,</i>
	"they wish"; their <i>desire</i> is to marry again.</p>
