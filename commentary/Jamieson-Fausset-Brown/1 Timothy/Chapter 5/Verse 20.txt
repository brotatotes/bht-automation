<p><b>20. Them that sin</b>—whether
	presbyters or laymen. 
	</p>
<p><b>rebuke before all</b>—publicly
	before the Church (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="mt+18:15-17">Matthew 18:15-17</span>;
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1co+5:9-13">1 Corinthians 5:9-13</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="eph+5:11">Ephesians 5:11</span>).
	Not until this "rebuke" was disregarded was the offender to
	be excommunicated. 
	</p>
<p><b>others . . . fear</b>—that
	other members of the Church may have a wholesome fear of offending
	(<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="de+13:11">Deuteronomy 13:11</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="ac+5:11">Acts 5:11</span>).</p>
