<p><b>12. Fight the good fight</b>—BIRKS
	thinks this Epistle was written from Corinth, where contests in the
	national games recurred at stated seasons, which will account for the
	allusion here as in <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>. Contrast "strifes of words" (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>). Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1ti+1:18">1 Timothy 1:18</span>;
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="2ti+4:7">2 Timothy 4:7</span>. The "good
	profession" is connected with the <i>good fight</i> (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="2ti+4:7- :">2 Timothy 4:7- :</span>). 
	</p>
<p><b>lay hold on eternal life</b>—the
	crown, or garland, the prize of victory, laid hold of by the winner
	in the "good fight" (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="2ti+4:7">2 Timothy 4:7</span>;
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="2ti+4:8">2 Timothy 4:8</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="php+3:12-14">Philippians 3:12-14</span>).
	"<i>Fight</i> (literally, 'strive') with such striving
	earnestness as to <i>lay hold on</i> the prize, <i>eternal life.</i>"
	</p>
<p><b>also</b>—not in the oldest
	manuscripts. 
	</p>
<p><b>professed a good
	profession</b>—<i>Greek,</i> "didst confess THE
	good <i>confession,</i>" namely, <i>the Christian</i> confession
	(as the <i>Greek</i> word is the same in this verse as that for
	"confession" in <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1ti+6:13">1 Timothy 6:13</span>,
	probably the <i>profession</i> here is the confession <i>that
	Christ's kingdom is the kingdom of the truth,</i> <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="joh+18:36">John 18:36</span>;
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="joh+18:37">John 18:37</span>), at thy being set
	apart to thy ministerial function (whether in general, or as overseer
	at Ephesus): the same occasion as is referred to in <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1ti+1:18">1 Timothy 1:18</span>;
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1ti+4:14">1 Timothy 4:14</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="2ti+1:4">2 Timothy 1:4</span>.
	</p>
<p><b>before many witnesses</b>—who
	would testify against thee if thou shouldest fall away [BENGEL].</p>
