<p><b>8. And</b>—<i>Greek,</i> "But."
	In contrast to the greedy gain-seekers (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>). 
	</p>
<p><b>having</b>—so long as we
	have food. (The <i>Greek</i> expresses "food sufficient in each
	case for our continually recurring wants" [ALFORD]).
	It is implied that we, as believers, shall <i>have</i> this (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>). 
	</p>
<p><b>raiment</b>—<i>Greek,</i>
	"covering"; according to some including a <i>roof to cover
	us,</i> that is, a dwelling, as well as clothing. 
	</p>
<p><b>let us be therewith
	content</b>—literally, "we shall be sufficiently provided";
	"we shall be sufficed" [ALFORD].</p>
