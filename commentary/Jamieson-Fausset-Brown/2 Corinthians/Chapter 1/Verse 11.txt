<p><b>11. helping together by prayer for
	us</b>—rather, "helping together on our behalf by your
	<i>supplication</i>"; the words "for us" in the <i>Greek</i>
	following "helping together," not "prayer." 
	</p>
<p><b>that for the gift,</b>
	c.—literally, "That on the part of many persons the gift
	(literally, 'gift of grace' the mercy) bestowed <i>upon us</i> by
	means of (that is, through the prayers of) many may be offered thanks
	for (may have thanks offered for it) on our behalf."</p>
