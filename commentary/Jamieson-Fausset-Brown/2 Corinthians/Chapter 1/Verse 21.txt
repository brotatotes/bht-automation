<p><b>21. stablisheth us . . . in
	Christ</b>—that is, in the faith of Christ—in believing in
	Christ. 
	</p>
<p><b>anointed us</b>—As "Christ"
	is the "Anointed" (which His name means), so "He hath
	<i>anointed</i> (<i>Greek,</i> "<i>chrisas</i>") us,"
	ministers and believing people alike, with the Spirit (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="2co+1:22">2 Corinthians 1:22</span>;
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1jo+2:20">1 John 2:20</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1jo+2:27">1 John 2:27</span>).
	Hence we become "a sweet savor of Christ" (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1jo+2:27- :">1 John 2:27- :</span>).</p>
