<p><b>15. no great thing</b>—no
	difficult matter. 
	</p>
<p><b>if his ministers also</b>—as
	well as himself. 
	</p>
<p><b>righteousness</b>—answering
	to "light" (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="2co+11:14">2 Corinthians 11:14</span>);
	the manifestation wherewith God reveals Himself in Christ (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="mt+6:33">Matthew 6:33</span>;
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="ro+1:17">Romans 1:17</span>). 
	</p>
<p><b>end</b>—The test of things
	is <i>the</i> end which strips off every specious <i>form</i> into
	which Satan's agents may now "transform" themselves
	(compare <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="php+3:19">Philippians 3:19</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="php+3:21">Philippians 3:21</span>).
	</p>
<p><b>according to their works</b>—not
	according to their pretensions.</p>
