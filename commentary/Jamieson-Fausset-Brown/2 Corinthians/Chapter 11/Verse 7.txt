<p><b>7. Have I</b>—literally, "OR
	have I?" Connected with <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>, "Or will any of you make it an objection that I have
	preached to you gratuitously?" He leaves their good feeling to
	give the answer, that this, so far from being an objection, was a
	decided superiority in him above the false apostles (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>). 
	</p>
<p><b>abasing myself</b>—in my
	mode of living, waiving my right of maintenance, and earning it by
	manual labor; perhaps with slaves as his fellow laborers (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="ac+18:3">Acts 18:3</span>;
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="php+4:12">Philippians 4:12</span>). 
	</p>
<p><b>ye . . . exalted</b>—spiritually,
	by your admission to Gospel privileges. 
	</p>
<p><b>because</b>—"in that."
	</p>
<p><b>gospel of God</b>—"of
	God" implies its divine glory to which they were admitted. 
	</p>
<p><b>freely</b>—"without
	charge."</p>
