<p><span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>. REVELATIONS IN
	WHICH HE
	MIGHT GLORY:
	BUT HE
	RATHER GLORIES
	IN INFIRMITIES, AS
	CALLING FORTH
	CHRIST'S POWER:
	SIGNS OF HIS
	APOSTLESHIP: HIS
	DISINTERESTEDNESS: NOT
	THAT HE
	IS EXCUSING
	HIMSELF TO THEM;
	BUT HE DOES
	ALL FOR THEIR
	GOOD, LEST HE
	SHOULD FIND
	THEM NOT
	SUCH AS HE
	DESIRED, AND SO
	SHOULD HAVE
	TO BE SEVERE
	AT HIS COMING.</p>
