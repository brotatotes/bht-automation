<p><b>10. Therefore</b>—because I wish
	the "sharpness" to be in my <i>letters</i> rather than in
	<i>deeds</i> [CHRYSOSTOM].
	</p>
<p><b>edification . . . not to
	destruction</b>—<i>for building up . . . not for casting down.</i>
	To "use sharpness" would seem to be <i>casting down,</i>
	rather than <i>building up;</i> therefore he prefers not to have to
	use it.</p>
