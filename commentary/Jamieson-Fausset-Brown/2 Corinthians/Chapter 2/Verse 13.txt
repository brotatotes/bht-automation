<p><b>13. no rest in my spirit</b>—rather,
	"no rest <i>for</i> my spirit" (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>). As here his "spirit" had no rest; so in <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>, his "flesh." His "spirit" under the Holy
	Spirit, hence, concluded that it was not necessary to avail himself
	of the "door" of usefulness at Troas any longer. 
	</p>
<p><b>taking . . . leave of
	them</b>—the disciples at Troas.</p>
