<p><b>10.</b> <i>For even</i> the
	ministration of condemnation, the law, <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span> (<i>which has been glorified</i> at Sinai in Moses' person),
	<i>has</i> now (<i>English Version</i> translates less fitly, "<i>was
	made</i> . . . had") <i>lost its glory in this respect by reason
	of the surpassing glory</i> (of the Gospel): as the light of the
	stars and moon fades in the presence of the sun.</p>
