<p><b>17. the Lord</b>—Christ (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="2co+3:14">2 Corinthians 3:14</span>;
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="2co+3:16">2 Corinthians 3:16</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="2co+4:5">2 Corinthians 4:5</span>).
	</p>
<p><b>is that Spirit</b>—is THE
	Spirit, namely, <i>that Spirit</i> spoken of in <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="2co+4:5- :">2 Corinthians 4:5- :</span>, and here resumed after the parenthesis (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="2co+4:5- :">2 Corinthians 4:5- :</span>): Christ is the Spirit and "end" of the Old
	Testament, who giveth life to it, whereas "the letter killeth"
	(<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1co+15:45">1 Corinthians 15:45</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="re+19:10">Revelation 19:10</span>,
	end). 
	</p>
<p><b>where the Spirit of the Lord
	is</b>—in a man's "heart" (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="2co+3:15">2 Corinthians 3:15</span>;
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="ro+8:9">Romans 8:9</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="ro+8:10">Romans 8:10</span>).
	</p>
<p><b>there is liberty</b>— (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="ro+8:10- :">Romans 8:10- :</span>). "There," and <i>there only.</i> Such cease to be
	slaves to the letter, which they were while the veil was on their
	heart. They are free to serve God in the Spirit, and rejoice in
	Christ Jesus (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="php+3:3">Philippians 3:3</span>): they
	have no longer the spirit of bondage, but of free sonship (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="ro+8:15">Romans 8:15</span>;
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="ga+4:7">Galatians 4:7</span>). "Liberty" is
	opposed to the letter (of the legal ordinances), and to the veil, the
	badge of slavery: also to the <i>fear</i> which the Israelites felt
	in beholding Moses' <i>glory unveiled</i> (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="ex+34:30">Exodus 34:30</span>;
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1jo+4:18">1 John 4:18</span>).</p>
