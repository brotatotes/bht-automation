<p align="CENTER"> CHAPTER 4 
	</p>
<p>
</p>
<p><span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>. HIS PREACHING
	IS OPEN
	AND SINCERE, THOUGH TO
	MANY THE GOSPEL
	IS HIDDEN.
	 
	</p>
<p>For he preaches Christ, not
	himself: the human vessel is frail that God may have the glory; yet,
	though frail, faith and the hope of future glory sustain him amidst
	the decay of the outward man.</p>
