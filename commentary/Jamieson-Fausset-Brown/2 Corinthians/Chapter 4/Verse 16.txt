<p><b>16. we faint not</b>—notwithstanding
	our sufferings. Resuming <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="2co+4:1">2 Corinthians 4:1</span>.
	</p>
<p><b>outward man</b>—the body,
	the flesh. 
	</p>
<p><b>perish</b>—"is wearing
	away"; "is wasted away" by afflictions. 
	</p>
<p><b>inward man</b>—our
	spiritual and true being, the "life" which even in our
	mortal bodies (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="2co+4:11">2 Corinthians 4:11</span>)
	"manifests the life of Jesus." 
	</p>
<p><b>is renewed</b>—"is
	being renewed," namely, with fresh "grace" (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="2co+4:11- :">2 Corinthians 4:11- :</span>), and "faith" (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="2co+4:11- :">2 Corinthians 4:11- :</span>), and hope (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="2co+4:17">2 Corinthians 4:17</span>;
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="2co+4:18">2 Corinthians 4:18</span>).</p>
