<p><b>5. For</b>—Their blindness is not
	our fault, as if we had self-seeking aims in our preaching. 
	</p>
<p><b>preach . . . Christ . . . the
	Lord</b>—rather, "Christ <i>as Lord,</i>" and ourselves
	as your servants, &amp;c. "<i>Lord,</i>" or "<i>Master,</i>"
	is the correlative term to "servants."</p>
