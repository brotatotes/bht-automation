<p><b>5. wrought us</b>—framed us by
	redemption, justification, and sanctification. 
	</p>
<p><b>for the selfsame thing</b>—"unto"
	it; namely, unto what is mortal of us being swallowed up in life (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>). 
	</p>
<p><b>who also</b>—The oldest
	manuscripts omit "also." 
	</p>
<p><b>earnest of the Spirit</b>—(See
	on <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="2co+1:22">2 Corinthians 1:22</span>). It is the Spirit
	(as "the first-fruits") who creates in us the groaning
	desire for our coming deliverance and glory (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="2co+1:22- :">2 Corinthians 1:22- :</span>).</p>
