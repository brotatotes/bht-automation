<p><b>9. Wherefore</b>—with such a sure
	"confidence" of being blessed, whether we die before, or be
	found alive at Christ's coming. 
	</p>
<p><b>we labour</b>—literally,
	"make it our ambition"; the only lawful ambition. 
	</p>
<p><b>whether present or
	absent</b>—whether we be found at His coming present in the body,
	or absent from it. 
	</p>
<p><b>accepted</b>—<i>Greek,</i>
	"well-pleasing."</p>
