<p><b>16. agreement</b>—accordance of
	sentiments (compare <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1ki+18:21">1 Kings 18:21</span>;
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="eph+5:7">Ephesians 5:7</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="eph+5:11">Ephesians 5:11</span>).
	</p>
<p><b>the temple of God</b>—that
	is, you believers (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1co+3:16">1 Corinthians 3:16</span>;
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1co+6:19">1 Corinthians 6:19</span>). 
	</p>
<p><b>with idols</b>—Compare
	Dagon before the ark (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1co+6:19- :">1 Corinthians 6:19- :</span>). 
	</p>
<p><b>as</b>—"<i>even as</i>
	God said." Quotation from <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="le+26:12">Leviticus 26:12</span>;
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="jer+31:33">Jeremiah 31:33</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="jer+32:38">Jeremiah 32:38</span>;
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="eze+37:26">Ezekiel 37:26</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="eze+37:27">Ezekiel 37:27</span>;
	compare <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="mt+28:20">Matthew 28:20</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="joh+14:23">John 14:23</span>.
	</p>
<p><b>walk in them</b>—rather,
	"<i>among</i> them." As "dwell" implies the
	divine <i>presence,</i> so "walk," the divine <i>operation.</i>
	God's dwelling in the body and soul of saints may be illustrated by
	its opposite, demoniacal possession of body and soul. 
	</p>
<p><b>my people</b>—rather, "they
	shall be <i>to me</i> a people."</p>
