<p><b>6. By . . . by,</b> c.—rather, as
	<i>Greek,</i> "In . . . in," implying not the instrument,
	but the sphere or element in which his ministry moved. 
	</p>
<p><b>knowledge</b>—spiritual: in
	Gospel mysteries, unattainable by mere reason (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1co+2:6-16">1 Corinthians 2:6-16</span>
<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="2co+3:6">2 Corinthians 3:6</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="2co+3:17">2 Corinthians 3:17</span>;
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="2co+3:18">2 Corinthians 3:18</span>). 
	</p>
<p><b>long-suffering . . .
	kindness</b>—associated with "charity" or "<i>love</i>"
	(<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1co+13:4">1 Corinthians 13:4</span>), as here. 
	</p>
<p><b>by the Holy Ghost</b>—in
	virtue of His influences which produce these graces, and other gifts,
	"love unfeigned" being the foremost of them.</p>
