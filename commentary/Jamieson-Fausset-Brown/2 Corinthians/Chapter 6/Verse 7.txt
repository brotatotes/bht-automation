<p><b>7. By the word of truth, by the
	power of God</b>—rather, "IN
	. . . in," c. As to "the word of truth" (compare
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="2co+4:2">2 Corinthians 4:2</span> <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="col+1:5">Colossians 1:5</span>),
	and "the (miraculous) power of God" (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="col+1:5- :">Colossians 1:5- :</span>); <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1co+2:4">1 Corinthians 2:4</span>, "in
	demonstration of the Spirit and of power." 
	</p>
<p><b>by the armour</b>—<i>Greek,</i>
	"through" or "by means of the armor."
	"Righteousness," which is the <i>breastplate</i> alone in
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="eph+6:13-17">Ephesians 6:13-17</span>, here is
	made the <i>whole</i> Christian panoply (compare <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="eph+6:13-49.6.17- :">Ephesians 6:13-49.6.17- :</span>). 
	</p>
<p><b>on . . . right . . . and . .
	. left</b>—that is, guarding on every side.</p>
