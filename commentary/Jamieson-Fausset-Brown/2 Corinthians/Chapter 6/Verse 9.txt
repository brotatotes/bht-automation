	known</b>—"unknown" in our true character to those who
	"evil report" of us, "well known" to those who
	hold us in "good report" (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>). CONYBEARE
	explains, "Unknown by men, yet <i>acknowledged by God</i>"

	God and men</i> (believers) are intended as knowing him (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="2co+5:11">2 Corinthians 5:11</span>;
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="2co+11:6">2 Corinthians 11:6</span>). 
	</p>
