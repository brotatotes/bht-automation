<p><b>13.</b> The oldest manuscripts read
	thus, "Therefore (<i>Greek,</i> 'for this <i>cause,</i>' namely,
	because our aim has been attained) we have been (<i>English Version,</i>
	'were,' is not so accurate) comforted; yea (<i>Greek,</i> 'but'), in
	OUR comfort we exceedingly the more joyed for the joy of Titus,"
	&amp;c. (compare <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="2co+7:7">2 Corinthians 7:7</span>).</p>
