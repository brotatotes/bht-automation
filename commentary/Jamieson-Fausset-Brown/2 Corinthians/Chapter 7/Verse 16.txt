<p><b>16. therefore</b>—omitted in the
	oldest manuscripts. The conclusion is more emphatical without it. 
	</p>
<p><b>that I have confidence in you
	in all things</b>—rather, as <i>Greek,</i> "that in everything
	I <i>am of good courage concerning</i> (literally, 'in the case of')
	you," as contrasted with my former doubts concerning you.</p>
