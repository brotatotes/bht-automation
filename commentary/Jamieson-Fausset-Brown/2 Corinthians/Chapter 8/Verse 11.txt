<p><b>11. perform</b>—"complete the
	doing also" (see on <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="2co+8:10">2 Corinthians 8:10</span>).
	</p>
<p><b>a readiness to will</b>—<i>Greek,</i>
	"<i>the</i> readiness <i>of</i> will"; referring to <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="2co+8:10- :">2 Corinthians 8:10- :</span>, where the <i>Greek</i> for "to be forward," ought
	to be translated as here, "to will." 
	</p>
<p><b>performance</b>—"completion"
	[ALFORD], The godly should
	show the same zeal to finish, as well as to begin well, which the
	worldly exhibit in their undertakings (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="2co+8:10- :">2 Corinthians 8:10- :</span>).</p>
