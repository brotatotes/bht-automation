<p><b>3-5. they were willing</b>—rather,
	supply from <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="2co+8:5">2 Corinthians 8:5</span>, the
	ellipsis thus, "According to their power . . . yea, and beyond
	their power, THEY GAVE."
	</p>
<p><b>of themselves</b>—not only
	not being besought, but themselves beseeching us.</p>
