<p><b>10.</b> Translate, as in <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>, "He that ministereth (supplieth) seed to the sower
	and bread for food" (literally, "bread for <i>eating</i>").
	</p>
<p><b>minister</b>—rather future,
	as the oldest manuscripts, "<i>Shall</i> minister (supply) and
	multiply." 
	</p>
<p><b>your seed</b>—your means
	for liberality. 
	</p>
<p><b>the fruits of your
	righteousness</b>—the heavenly rewards for your Christian charity
	(<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="mt+10:42">Matthew 10:42</span>). Righteousness
	shall be itself the reward, even as it is the thing rewarded (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="ho+10:12">Hosea 10:12</span>;
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="mt+5:6">Matthew 5:6</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="mt+6:33">Matthew 6:33</span>).</p>
