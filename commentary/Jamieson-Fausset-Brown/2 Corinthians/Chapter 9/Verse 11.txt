<p><b>bountifulness</b>—<i>Greek,</i>
	"single-minded liberality." Translated "simplicity,"
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="ro+12:8">Romans 12:8</span>. 
	</p>
<p><b>causeth through us</b>—literally,
	"worketh through us"; that is, through our instrumentality
	as the distributors. 
	</p>
<p><b>thanksgiving</b>—on the
	part of the recipients.</p>
