<p><b>14.</b> Translate, "Themselves
	also with prayer for you, longing after you on account of the
	exceeding grace of God (resting) upon you." <i>English Version</i>
	is, however, good sense: They glorify God (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>) by the experimental proof, &amp;c., "and by their
	prayer for you." But the <i>Greek</i> favors the former.</p>
