<p><b>2. ready a year ago</b>—to send
	off the money, owing to the apostle's former exhortation (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1co+16:1">1 Corinthians 16:1</span>;
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1co+16:2">1 Corinthians 16:2</span>). 
	</p>
<p><b>your zeal</b>—<i>Greek,</i>
	"the zeal from you," that is, on your part; propagated from
	you to others. 
	</p>
<p><b>provoked</b>—that is,
	stimulated. 
	</p>
<p><b>very many</b>—<i>Greek,</i>
	"the greater number," namely, of the Macedonians.</p>
