<p><b>4. if they of Macedonia</b>—rather
	as <i>Greek,</i> "if Macedonians." 
	</p>
<p><b>unprepared</b>—with your
	collection; see <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="2co+9:2">2 Corinthians 9:2</span>,
	"ready," <i>Greek,</i> "prepared." 
	</p>
<p><b>we, not to say ye</b>—<i>Ye</i>
	would naturally feel more ashamed for yourselves, than we (who
	boasted of you) would for you. 
	</p>
<p><b>confident boasting</b>—The
	oldest manuscripts read simply "confidence," namely, in
	your liberality.</p>
