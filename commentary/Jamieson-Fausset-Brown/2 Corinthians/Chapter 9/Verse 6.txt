<p><b>6. </b><i><b>I say</b></i><b>
</b>—ELLICOTT and others
	supply the ellipsis thus: "But <i>remember</i> this." 
	</p>
<p><b>bountifully</b>—literally,
	"<i>with,</i>" or "<i>in blessings.</i>" The word
	itself implies a <i>beneficent spirit in the giver</i> (compare <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>, end), and the <i>plural</i> implies the <i>abundance</i> and
	liberality of the gifts. "The reaping shall correspond to the
	proportions and spirit of the sowing" [BENGEL].
	Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="eze+34:26">Ezekiel 34:26</span>, "Showers
	of blessing."</p>
