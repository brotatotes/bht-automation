<p><b>8. all grace</b>—even in external
	goods, and even while ye bestow on others [BENGEL].
	</p>
<p><b>that</b>—"in order
	that." God's gifts are bestowed on us, not that we may have them
	to ourselves, but that we may the more "abound in good works"
	to others. 
	</p>
<p><b>sufficiency</b>—so as not
	to need the help of others, having yourselves from God "bread
	for your food" (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="2co+9:10">2 Corinthians 9:10</span>).
	</p>
<p><b>in all </b><i><b>things</b></i><b>
</b>—<i>Greek,</i> "in everything." 
	</p>
<p><b>every good work</b>—of
	charity to others, which will be "your seed sown" (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="2co+9:10- :">2 Corinthians 9:10- :</span>).</p>
