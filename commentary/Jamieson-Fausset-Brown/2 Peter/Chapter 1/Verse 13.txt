<p><b>13. Yea</b>—<i>Greek,</i> "But";
	though "you know" the truth (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>). 
	</p>
<p><b>this tabernacle</b>—soon to
	be taken down (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="2co+5:1">2 Corinthians 5:1</span>): I
	therefore need <i>to make the most of my short time</i> for the good
	of Christ's Church. The zeal of Satan against it, the more intense <i>as
	his time is short,</i> ought to stimulate Christians on the same
	ground. 
	</p>
<p><b>by</b>—<i>Greek,</i> "in"
	(compare <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="2pe+3:1">2 Peter 3:1</span>).</p>
