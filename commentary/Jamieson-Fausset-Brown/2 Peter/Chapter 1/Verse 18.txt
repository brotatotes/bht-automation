<p><b>18. which came</b>—rather as
	<i>Greek,</i> "we heard borne from heaven." 
	</p>
<p><b>holy mount</b>—as the
	Transfiguration mount came to be regarded, on account of the
	manifestation of Christ's divine glory there. 
	</p>
<p><b>we</b>—emphatical: we,
	James and John, as well as myself.</p>
