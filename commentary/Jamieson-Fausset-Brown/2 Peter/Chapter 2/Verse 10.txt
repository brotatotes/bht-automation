<p><b>10. chiefly</b>—They <i>especially</i>
	will be punished (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="jdg+1:8">Judges 1:8</span>). 
	</p>
<p><b>after</b>—following after. 
	</p>
<p><b>lust of
	uncleanness</b>—<i>defilement:</i> "hankering after polluting
	and unlawful use of the flesh" [ALFORD].
	</p>
<p><b>government</b>—<i>Greek,</i>
	"lordship," "dominion" (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="jdg+1:8- :">Judges 1:8- :</span>). 
	</p>
<p><b>Presumptuous</b>—<i>Greek,</i>
	"Darers." <i>Self-will</i> begets <i>presumption.</i>
	Presumptuously daring. 
	</p>
<p><b>are not afraid</b>—though
	they are so insignificant in <i>might; Greek,</i> "tremble not"
	(<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="jdg+1:8">Judges 1:8</span>, end). 
	</p>
<p><b>speak evil of</b>—<i>Greek,</i>
	"blaspheme." 
	</p>
<p><b>dignities</b>—<i>Greek,</i>
	"glories."</p>
