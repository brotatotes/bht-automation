<p><b>21. the way of righteousness</b>—"the
	way of truth" (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="2pe+2:2">2 Peter 2:2</span>).
	Christian doctrine, and "the knowledge of the Lord and Saviour."
	</p>
<p><b>turn</b>—back again; so the
	<i>Greek.</i>
</p>
<p><b>from the holy commandment</b>—the
	Gospel which enjoins <i>holiness;</i> in opposition to their
	<i>corruption.</i> "Holy," not that it makes holy, but
	because it ought to be kept <i>inviolate</i> [TITTMANN].
	</p>
<p><b>delivered</b>—once for all;
	admitting no turning back.</p>
