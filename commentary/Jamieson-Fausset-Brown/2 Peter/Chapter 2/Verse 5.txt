<p><b>5. eighth</b>—that is, Noah, and
	seven others. Contrasted with the densely peopled "world of the
	ungodly." 
	</p>
<p><b>preacher</b>—not only
	"righteous" himself (compare <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>), but also "a preacher of righteousness": adduced
	by Peter against the <i>licentiousness</i> of the false teachers (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>) who have no prospect before them but destruction, even as it
	overtook the ungodly world in Noah's days.</p>
