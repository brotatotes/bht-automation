<p><b>7. just</b>—righteous. 
	</p>
<p><b>filthy
	conversation</b>—literally, "behavior in licentiousness"
	(<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="ge+19:5">Genesis 19:5</span>). 
	</p>
<p><b>the wicked</b>—<i>Greek,</i>
	"lawless": who set at defiance the <i>laws</i> of nature,
	as well as man and God. The Lord reminds us of Lot's faithfulness,
	but not of his sin in the cave: so in Rahab's case.</p>
