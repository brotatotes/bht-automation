<p><b>14. that ye . . . be found of
	him</b>—"in His sight" [ALFORD],
	at His coming; plainly implying a <i>personal</i> coming. 
	</p>
<p><b>without spot</b>—at the
	coming marriage feast of the Lamb, in contrast to <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>, "Spots they are and blemishes while they feast,"
	not having on the King's pure wedding garment. 
	</p>
<p><b>blameless</b>— (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1co+1:8">1 Corinthians 1:8</span>;
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="php+1:10">Philippians 1:10</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1th+3:13">1 Thessalonians 3:13</span>;
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1th+5:23">1 Thessalonians 5:23</span>). 
	</p>
<p><b>in peace</b>—in all its
	aspects, towards God, your own consciences, and your fellow men, and
	as its consequence eternal <i>blessedness:</i> "the God of
	peace" will effect this for you.</p>
