<p><b>18. grow</b>—Not only do not "fall
	from" (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="2pe+3:17">2 Peter 3:17</span>), but <i>grow
	onward:</i> the true secret of not going backward. <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="2pe+3:17- :">2 Peter 3:17- :</span>, "Grow up into Him, the Head, Christ." 
	</p>
<p><b>grace and . . . knowledge of
	. . . Christ</b>—"the grace and knowledge of Christ"
	[ALFORD rightly]: <i>the
	grace</i> of which <i>Christ</i> is the author, and <i>the knowledge</i>
	of which <i>Christ</i> is the object. 
	</p>
<p><b>for ever</b>—<i>Greek,</i>
	"to the day of eternity": the day that has no end: "the
	day of the Lord," beginning with the Lord's coming.</p>
