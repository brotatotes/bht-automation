<p><b>3. Knowing this first</b>—from the
	word of the apostles. 
	</p>
<p><b>shall come</b>—Their very
	<i>scoffing</i> shall confirm the truth of the prediction. 
	</p>
<p><b>scoffers</b>—The oldest
	manuscripts and <i>Vulgate</i> add, "(scoffers) <i>in</i> (that
	is, 'with') <i>scoffing.</i>" As <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>, "harping with harps." 
	</p>
<p><b>walking after their own
	lusts</b>— (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="2pe+2:10">2 Peter 2:10</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="jdg+1:16">Judges 1:16</span>;
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="jdg+1:18">Judges 1:18</span>). Their own pleasure is
	their sole law, unrestrained by reverence for God.</p>
