<p><b>7.</b> (Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>, end). 
	</p>
<p><b>which are now</b>—"the
	postdiluvian visible world." In contrast to "that <i>then
	was,</i>" <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="2pe+3:6">2 Peter 3:6</span>. 
	</p>
<p><b>the same</b>—Other oldest
	manuscripts read, "His" (God's). 
	</p>
<p><b>kept in store</b>—<i>Greek,</i>
	"treasured up." 
	</p>
<p><b>reserved</b>—"kept."
	It is only God's constantly watchful providence which holds together
	the present state of things till His time for ending it.</p>
