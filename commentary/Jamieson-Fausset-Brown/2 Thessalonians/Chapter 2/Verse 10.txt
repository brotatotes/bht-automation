<p><b>10. deceivableness</b>—rather as
	<i>Greek,</i> "deceit of (to promote) unrighteousness" (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>). 
	</p>
<p><b>in</b>—The oldest
	manuscripts and versions omit "in." Translate, "<i>unto</i>
	them that <i>are perishing</i>" (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="2co+2:15">2 Corinthians 2:15</span>;
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="2co+2:16">2 Corinthians 2:16</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="2co+4:3">2 Corinthians 4:3</span>):
	the victims of him whose very name describes his <i>perishing</i>
	nature, "the son of perdition"; in contrast to <i>you</i>
	whom (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="2th+2:13">2 Thessalonians 2:13</span>) "God hath
	from the beginning chosen to <i>salvation</i> through <i>sanctification</i>
	of the Spirit and belief of the truth." 
	</p>
<p><b>because</b>—literally, "in
	requital for"; in just retribution for their having no <i>love</i>
	for the truth which was within their reach (on account of its putting
	a check on their bad passions), and for their having "pleasure
	in unrighteousness" (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="2th+2:12">2 Thessalonians 2:12</span>;
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="ro+1:18">Romans 1:18</span>); they are <i>lost</i>
	because they loved not, but rejected, the truth which would have
	<i>saved</i> them. 
	</p>
<p><b>received not</b>—<i>Greek,</i>
	"welcomed not"; admitted it not cordially. 
	</p>
<p><b>love of the truth</b>—not
	merely love of <i>truth,</i> but love of THE
	<i>truth</i> (and of, Jesus who is <i>the Truth,</i> in opposition to
	Satan's "lie," <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="2th+2:9">2 Thessalonians 2:9</span>;
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="2th+2:11">2 Thessalonians 2:11</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="joh+8:42-44">John 8:42-44</span>),
	can <i>save</i> (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="eph+4:21">Ephesians 4:21</span>). We
	are required not merely to assent to, but to <i>love</i> the truth
	(<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="ps+119:97">Psalms 119:97</span>). The Jews
	rejected Him who came in His divine Father's name; they will receive
	Antichrist coming in <i>his own</i> name (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="ps+119:97- :">Psalms 119:97- :</span>). Their pleasant sin shall prove their terrible scourge.</p>
