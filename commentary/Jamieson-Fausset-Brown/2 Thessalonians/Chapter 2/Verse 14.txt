<p><b>14. you</b>—The oldest manuscripts
	read, "us." 
	</p>
<p><b>by our gospel</b>—"<i>through</i>"
	the Gospel which we preach. 
	</p>
<p><b>to . . . glory</b>—In <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span> it was "salvation," that is, deliverance from all
	evil, of body and soul (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1th+5:9">1 Thessalonians 5:9</span>);
	here it is positive good, even "glory," and that "the
	glory of our Lord Jesus" Himself, which believers are privileged
	to share with Him (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="joh+17:22">John 17:22</span>;
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="joh+17:24">John 17:24</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="ro+8:17">Romans 8:17</span>;
