<p><b>14. note that man</b>—mark him in
	your own mind as one to be avoided (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>). 
	</p>
<p><b>that he may be ashamed</b>—<i>Greek,</i>
	"made to turn and look into himself, and so be put to shame."
	Feeling himself shunned by godly brethren, he may become ashamed of
	his course.</p>
