<p><b>4. we have confidence in the Lord</b>—as
	"faithful" (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="2th+3:3">2 Thessalonians 3:3</span>).
	Have confidence in no man when left to himself [BENGEL].
	</p>
<p><b>that ye both do</b>—Some of
	the oldest manuscripts insert a clause, "that ye both have done"
	before, "and are doing, and will do." He means the <i>majority</i>
	by "ye," not <i>all</i> of them (compare <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="2th+3:11">2 Thessalonians 3:11</span>;
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="2th+1:3">2 Thessalonians 1:3</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1th+3:6">1 Thessalonians 3:6</span>).</p>
