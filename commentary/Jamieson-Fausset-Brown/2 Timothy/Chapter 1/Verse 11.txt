<p><b>11. Whereunto</b>—For the
	publication of which Gospel. 
	</p>
<p><b>I am appointed</b>—<i>Greek,</i>
	"I was appointed." 
	</p>
<p><b>preacher</b>—<i>Greek,</i>
	"herald." 
	</p>
<p><b>teacher of the Gentiles</b>—
	(<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1ti+2:7">1 Timothy 2:7</span>). He brings forward his
	own example in this verse and <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1ti+2:7- :">1 Timothy 2:7- :</span>, as a pattern for Timothy, as a <i>public</i> "preacher,"
	an "apostle," or <i>missionary</i> from place to place, and
	a "teacher" <i>in private</i> instructing His flock with
	patient perseverance.</p>
