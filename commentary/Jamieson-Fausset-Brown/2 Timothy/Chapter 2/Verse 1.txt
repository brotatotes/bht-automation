<p><b>1. Thou therefore</b>—following my
	example (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="2ti+1:8">2 Timothy 1:8</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="2ti+1:12">2 Timothy 1:12</span>),
	and that of ONESIPHORUS
	(<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="2ti+1:16-18">2 Timothy 1:16-18</span>), and
	shunning that of those who forsook me (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="2ti+1:16-55.1.18- :">2 Timothy 1:16-55.1.18- :</span>). 
	</p>
<p><b>my son</b>—<i>Children</i>
	ought to imitate their father. 
	</p>
<p><b>be strong</b>—literally,
	"be invested with <i>power.</i>" Have power, and show
	thyself to have it; implying an abiding state of power. 
	</p>
<p><b>in the grace</b>—the
	<i>element</i> IN which
	the believer's strength has place. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="2ti+1:16-55.1.18- :">2 Timothy 1:16-55.1.18- :</span>, "God hath given us the spirit of <i>power.</i>"</p>
