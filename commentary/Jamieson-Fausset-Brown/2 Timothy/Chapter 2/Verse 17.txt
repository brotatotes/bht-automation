<p><b>17. will eat</b>—literally, "will
	have pasture." The consuming progress of mortification is the
	image. They pretend to give rich spiritual <i>pasture</i> to their
	disciples: the only <i>pasture</i> is that of a spiritual cancer
	feeding on their vitals. 
	</p>
<p><b>canker</b>—a "cancer"
	or "gangrene." 
	</p>
<p><b>Hymenaeus</b>—(See on <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>). After his excommunication he seems to have been readmitted
	into the Church and again to have troubled it.</p>
