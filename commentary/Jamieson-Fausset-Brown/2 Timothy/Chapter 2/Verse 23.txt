<p><b>23.</b> (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>.) 
	</p>
<p><b>unlearned</b>—<i>Greek,</i>
	"undisciplined"; not tending to promote the discipline of
	faith and morals (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="pr+5:23">Proverbs 5:23</span>).
	"Uninstructive"; in contrast with "instructing"
	(<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="2ti+2:25">2 Timothy 2:25</span>), and "wise unto
	salvation" (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="2ti+3:15">2 Timothy 3:15</span>). 
	</p>
<p><b>avoid</b>—"decline."</p>
