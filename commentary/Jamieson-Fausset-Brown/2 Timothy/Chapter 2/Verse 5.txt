<p><b>5. And</b>—"Moreover." 
	</p>
<p><b>strive for masteries</b>—"strive
	in the games" [ALFORD];
	namely, the great national games of Greece. 
	</p>
<p><b>yet is he not crowned,
	except</b>—even though he gain the victory. 
	</p>
<p><b>strive lawfully</b>—observing
	all the conditions of both the contest (keeping within the bounds of
	the course and stript of his clothes) and the preparation for it,
	namely, as to self-denying diet, anointing, exercise, self-restraint,
	chastity, decorum, &amp;c. (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>).</p>
