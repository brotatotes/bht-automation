<p><b>perfect, throughly
	furnished</b>—<i>Greek,</i> "thoroughly perfected," and
	so "perfect." The man of God is perfectly accoutred out of
	Scripture for his work, whether he be a minister (compare <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="2ti+4:2">2 Timothy 4:2</span>;
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="2ti+3:16">2 Timothy 3:16</span>) or a spiritual layman.
	No oral tradition is needed to be added.</p>
