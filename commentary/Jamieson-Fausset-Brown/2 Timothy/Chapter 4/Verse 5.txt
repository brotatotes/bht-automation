<p><b>5.</b> I am no longer here to
	withstand these things; be thou a worthy successor of me, no longer
	depending on me for counsel, but thine own master, and swimming
	without the corks [CALVIN];
	follow my steps, inherit their result, and the honor of their end
	[ALFORD]. 
	</p>
<p><b>watch thou</b>—literally,
	"with the wakefulness of one <i>sober.</i>" 
	</p>
<p><b>in all things</b>—on all
	occasions and under all circumstances (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>). 
	</p>
<p><b>endure affliction</b>—suffer
	hardships [ALFORD]. 
	</p>
<p><b>evangelist</b>—a missionary
	bishop preacher, and teacher. 
	</p>
<p><b>make full proof of</b>—fulfil
	in all its requirements, leaving nothing undone (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="ac+12:25">Acts 12:25</span>;
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="ro+15:19">Romans 15:19</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="col+4:17">Colossians 4:17</span>).</p>
