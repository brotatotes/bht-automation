<p style=" "><b>of
	all men</b>
	— who have had opportunity of knowing his character.</p>
<p style=" "><b>of
	the truth itself</b>
	— The Gospel standard of <i>truth</i>
	bears witness to him that he walks conformably to it, in acts of real
	love, hospitality to the brethren (in contrast to Diotrephes), etc.
	Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>
	“He that doeth truth cometh to the light, that his deeds may be
	made manifest that they are wrought in God.”</p>
<p style=" "><b>we
	also</b> —
	besides the testimony of “all men,” and “of the truth itself.”</p>
<p style=" "><b>ye
	know</b> —
	The oldest manuscripts read, “thou knowest.”</p>
