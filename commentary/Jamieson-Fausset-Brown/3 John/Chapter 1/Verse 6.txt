<p style=" "><b>borne
	witness of thy charity before the church</b>
	— to stimulate others by the good example. The brethren so
	entertained by Gaius were missionary evangelists (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>);
	and, probably, in the course of narrating their missionary labors for
	the edification of the Church where John then was, incidentally
	mentioned the loving hospitality shown them by Gaius.</p>
<p style=" "><b>bring
	forward on their journey</b>
	— “If thou (<i>continue
	to</i>)
	forward on their journey” by giving them provisions for the way.</p>
<p style=" "><b>after
	a godly sort</b>
	— <i>Greek,</i>
	“in a manner worthy of God,” whose ambassadors they are, and
	whose servant thou art. He who honors God’s missionary servants
	(<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>),
	honors God.</p>
