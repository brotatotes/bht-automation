<p style=" "><b>We</b>
	— in contradistinction to “the Gentiles” or “heathen”
	referred to, <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>.</p>
<p style=" "><b>therefore</b>
	— as they take nothing from the Gentiles or heathen.</p>
<p style=" "><b>receive</b>
	— The oldest manuscripts read, “take up.” As they “take”
	nothing from the Gentiles, we ought to <i>take</i>
	them <i>up</i>
	so as to support them.</p>
<p style=" "><b>fellow
	helpers</b>
	— with them.</p>
<p style=" "><b>to
	the truth</b>
	— that is, <i>to
	promote</i>
	the truth.</p>
