<p><b>14. Not so, Lord</b>—See <i>Marginal</i>
	reference. 
	</p>
<p><b>I have never eaten anything
	that is common</b>—that is, <i>not sanctified</i> by divine
	permission to eat of it, and so "unclean." "The
	distinction of meats was a sacrament of national distinction,
	separation, and consecration" [WEBSTER
	and WILKINSON].</p>
