<p><b>36-38. the word . . . sent unto the
	children of Israel</b>—for to them (he would have them distinctly
	know) the Gospel was first preached, even as the facts of it took
	place on the special theater of the ancient economy. 
	</p>
<p><b>preaching peace by Jesus
	Christ</b>—the glorious sum of all Gospel truth (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>). 
	</p>
<p><b>he is Lord of all</b>—exalted
	to embrace under the canopy of His peace, Jew and Gentile alike, whom
	the blood of His Cross had cemented into one reconciled and accepted
	family of God (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>).</p>
