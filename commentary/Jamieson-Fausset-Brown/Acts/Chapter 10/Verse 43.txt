<p><b>43. To him give all the prophets
	witness</b>—that is, This is the burden, generally of the prophetic
	testimony. It was fitter thus to give the spirit of their testimony,
	than to quote them in detail on such an occasion. But let this
	apostolic statement of the evangelical import of the Old Testament
	writings be devoutly weighed by those who are disposed to rationalize
	away this element in the Old Testament. 
	</p>
<p><b>whosoever believeth in
	him</b>—This was evidently said with special reference to the
	Gentile audience then before him, and formed a noble practical
	conclusion to the whole discourse.</p>
