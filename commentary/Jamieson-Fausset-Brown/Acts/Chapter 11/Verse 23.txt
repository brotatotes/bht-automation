<p><b>23. when he . . . had seen the grace
	of God</b>—in the new converts. 
	</p>
<p><b>was glad</b>—owned and
	rejoiced in it at once as divine, though they were uncircumcised. 
	</p>
<p><b>exhorted them all that with
	purpose of heart</b>—as opposed to a hasty and fickle discipleship.
	</p>
<p><b>they would cleave unto the
	Lord</b>—the Lord Jesus.</p>
