<p><b>28. that there should be great
	dearth throughout all the world</b>—the whole Roman empire. 
	</p>
<p><b>which came to pass in the
	days of Claudius Cæsar</b>—Four famines occurred during his reign.
	This one in Judea and the adjacent countries took place, A.D.
	41 [JOSEPHUS, <i>Antiquities,</i>
	20.2,5]. <i>An important date for tracing out the chronology of the
	Acts.</i> (But this subject is too difficult and extensive to admit
	of being handled here).</p>
