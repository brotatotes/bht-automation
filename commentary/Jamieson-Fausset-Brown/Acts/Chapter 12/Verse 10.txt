<p><b>10. first and the second ward . . .
	the iron gate that leadeth unto the city</b>—We can only conjecture
	the precise meaning of all this, not knowing the position of the
	prison. 
	</p>
<p><b>passed on through one street;
	and forthwith the angel departed from him</b>—when he had placed
	him beyond pursuit. Thus "He disappointeth the devices of the
	crafty, so that their heads cannot perform their enterprise"
	(<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="job+5:12">Job 5:12</span>).</p>
