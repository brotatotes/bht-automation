<p><b>11. when Peter was come to
	himself</b>—recovered from his bewilderment, and had time to look
	back upon all the steps that had followed each other in such rapid
	succession. 
	</p>
<p><b>Now I know of a surety, that
	the Lord hath sent his angel, and hath delivered me,</b> &amp;c.—another
	evidence that Peter expected nothing but to seal his testimony with
	his blood on this occasion.</p>
