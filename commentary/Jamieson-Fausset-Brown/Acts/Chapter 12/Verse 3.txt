<p><b>3. because he saw it pleased the
	Jews</b>—Popularity was the ruling passion of this Herod, not
	naturally so cruel as some of the family [JOSEPHUS,
	<i>Antiquities,</i> 19.7.3]. 
	</p>
<p><b>to take Peter also</b>—whose
	loss, at this stage of the Church, would have been, so far as we can
	see, irreparable. 
	</p>
