<p><b>5, 6. prayer was made without
	ceasing</b>—rather, "instant," "earnest,"
	"urgent" (<i>Margin</i>); as in <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="lu+22:44">Luke 22:44</span>;
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="ac+26:7">Acts 26:7</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1pe+4:8">1 Peter 4:8</span>
	(see <i>Greek</i>). 
	</p>
<p><b>of the church unto God for
	him</b>—not in public assembly, for it was evidently not safe to
	meet thus; but in little groups in private houses, one of which was
	Mary's (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="ac+12:12">Acts 12:12</span>). And this
	was kept up during all the days of unleavened bread.</p>
