<p><b>15-17. Then Paul stood up, and
	beckoning with his hand</b>—as was his manner on such occasions (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>; and see <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="ac+26:1">Acts 26:1</span>). 
	</p>
<p><b>Men of Israel, and ye that
	fear God</b>—by the latter expression meaning religious proselytes,
	who united with the Jews in all acts of ordinary worship. 
	</p>
<p><b>and exalted the people when
	they dwelt as strangers in Egypt</b>—by marvellous interpositions
	for them in their deepest depression.</p>
