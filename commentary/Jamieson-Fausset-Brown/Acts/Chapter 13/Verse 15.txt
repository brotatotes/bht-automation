15-17. Then Paul stood up, and
	beckoning with his hand—as was his manner on such occasions ( :-; and see Acts 26:1). 
	Men of Israel, and ye that
	fear God—by the latter expression meaning religious proselytes,
	who united with the Jews in all acts of ordinary worship. 
	and exalted the people when
	they dwelt as strangers in Egypt—by marvellous interpositions
	for them in their deepest depression.
