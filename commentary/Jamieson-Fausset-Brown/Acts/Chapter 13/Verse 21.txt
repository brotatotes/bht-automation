<p><b>21. God gave . . . them Saul . . .
	of the tribe of Benjamin</b>—That the speaker was himself of the
	same name and of the same tribe, has often been noticed as in all
	likelihood present to the apostle's mind while speaking. 
	</p>
<p><b>forty years</b>—With this
	length of Saul's reign (not mentioned in the Old Testament), JOSEPHUS
	coincides [<i>Antiquities,</i> 6.14.9].</p>
