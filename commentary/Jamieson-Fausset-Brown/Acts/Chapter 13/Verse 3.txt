<p><b>3. laid their hands on them</b>—(See
	on <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="ac+6:6">Acts 6:6</span>) —"recommending
	them to the grace of God for the work which they had to fulfil"
	(<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="ac+14:26">Acts 14:26</span>). 
	</p>
<p><b>sent them away</b>—with the
	double call—of <i>the Spirit</i> first, and next of <i>the Church.</i>
	So clothed, their mission is thus described: "They being sent
	forth by the Holy Ghost." Have we not here for all time the true
	principle of appointment to sacred offices? 
	</p>
