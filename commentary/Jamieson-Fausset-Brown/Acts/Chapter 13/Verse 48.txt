48. when the Gentiles heard this,
	they were glad—to perceive that their accession to Christ was a
	matter of divine arrangement as well as apostolic effort. 
	and glorified the word of the
	Lord—by a cordial reception of it. 
	and as many as were ordained
	to eternal life believed—a very remarkable statement, which
	cannot, without force, be interpreted of anything lower than this,
	that a divine ordination to eternal life is the cause, not the
	effect, of any man's believing.
