<p><b>48. when the Gentiles heard this,
	they were glad</b>—to perceive that their accession to Christ was a
	matter of divine arrangement as well as apostolic effort. 
	</p>
<p><b>and glorified the word of the
	Lord</b>—by a cordial reception of it. 
	</p>
<p><b>and as many as were ordained
	to eternal life believed</b>—a very remarkable statement, which
	cannot, without force, be interpreted of anything lower than this,
	that <i>a divine ordination to eternal life is the cause,</i> not the
	effect, <i>of any man's believing.</i></p>
