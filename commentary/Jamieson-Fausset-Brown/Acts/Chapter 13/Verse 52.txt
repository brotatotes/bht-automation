<p><b>52. the disciples</b>—who, though
	not themselves expelled, had to endure sufferings for the Gospel, as
	we learn from <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="ac+14:22">Acts 14:22</span>. 
	</p>
<p><b>were filled with joy and with
	the Holy Ghost</b>—who not only raised them above shame and fear,
	as professed disciples of the Lord Jesus, but filled them with holy
	and elevated emotions.</p>
