<p><b>6. when they had gone through the
	isle unto Paphos</b>—on the opposite or west side of the island,
	about one hundred miles by land, along the south coast; the Roman
	capital, where the governor resided. 
	</p>
<p><b>they found a . . .
	sorcerer</b>—one of a numerous class of impostors who, at this time
	of general unbelief, were encouraged even by cultivated Romans.</p>
