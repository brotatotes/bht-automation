9. Then Saul . . . also . . . called
	Paul—and henceforward Paul only; a softening of his former
	name, in accommodation to Roman ears, and (as the word signifies
	"little") probably with allusion as elsewhere to his
	insignificance of stature and appearance (2 Corinthians 10:1;
	2 Corinthians 10:10) [WEBSTER
	and WILKINSON]. 
	filled with the Holy
	Ghost—the Spirit coming mightily upon him. 
	set his eyes on him and
	said—Henceforward Barnabas sinks into the background. The whole
	soul of his great colleague, now drawn out, as never before, shoots,
	by the lightning gaze of his eye, through the dark and tortuous
	spirit of the sorcerer. What a picture!
