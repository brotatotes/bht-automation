<p>"After this detailed account
	of Paul's labors at Pisidian Antioch, Luke subjoins only brief
	notices of his further labors, partly because from the nature of the
	case his discourses must have embraced nearly the same topics, and
	partly because the consequences that resulted assumed quite a similar
	shape" [OLSHAUSEN].</p>
