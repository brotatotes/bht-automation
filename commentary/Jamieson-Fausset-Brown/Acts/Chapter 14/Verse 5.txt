<p><b>5. an assault made . . . to stone
	them</b>—rather here, "an impetuous movement" with a view
	to stoning them: for in <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="2co+11:25">2 Corinthians 11:25</span>,
	Paul says, "<i>Once</i> I was stoned," and that was at
	Lystra, as expressly related in <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="2co+11:25- :">2 Corinthians 11:25- :</span>. (PALEY'S
	remarks—<i>Horæ Paulinæ</i>—on this singular coincidence
	between the Epistle and the history are very striking).
