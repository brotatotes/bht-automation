<p><b>14-17. Simeon</b>—a <i>Hebrew</i>
	variation of Simon, as in <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="2pe+1:1">2 Peter 1:1</span>;
	(<i>Greek</i>), the Jewish and family name of Peter. 
	</p>
<p><b>hath declared how God at the
	first</b>—answering to Peter's own expression "a good while
	ago" (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="ac+15:7">Acts 15:7</span>). 
	</p>
<p><b>did visit the Gentiles to
	take out of them</b>—in the exercise of His adorable sovereignty. 
	</p>
<p><b>a people for his name</b>—the
	honor of his name, or for His glory.</p>
