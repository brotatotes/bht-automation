<p><b>38. But Paul thought not good to
	take him with them who departed from them</b>—that is, who <i>had</i>
	departed; but the word is stronger than this—"who stood aloof"
	or "turned away" from them. 
	</p>
<p><b>from Pamphylia, and went not
	with them to the work</b>—the work yet before them. The allusion is
	to what is recorded in <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="ac+13:13">Acts 13:13</span>
	(see on <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="ac+15:1">Acts 15:1</span>).</p>
