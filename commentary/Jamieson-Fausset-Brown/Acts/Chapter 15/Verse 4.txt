<p><b>4. And when they were come to
	Jerusalem</b>—This was Paul's THIRD
	VISIT TO JERUSALEM
	after his conversion, and <i>on this occasion took place what is
	related in</i> <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="ga+2:1-10">Galatians 2:1-10</span>.
	(See there). 
	</p>
<p><b>were received of the church,
	and the apostles and elders</b>—evidently at a meeting formally
	convened for this purpose: the deputation being one so influential,
	and from a church of such note. 
	</p>
