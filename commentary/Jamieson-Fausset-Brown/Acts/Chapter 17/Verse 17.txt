<p><b>17. Therefore disputed</b>—or,
	discussed. 
	</p>
<p><b>he in the synagogue with the
	Jews</b>—The sense is not, "Therefore went he to the Jews,"
	because the Gentile Athenians were steeped in idolatry; but,
	"Therefore set he himself to lift up his voice to the idol city,
	but, as his manner was, he began with the Jews." 
	</p>
<p><b>and with the devout
	persons</b>—Gentile proselytes. After that, 
	</p>
<p><b>in the market</b>—the
	<i>Agora</i>, or place of public concourse. 
	</p>
<p><b>daily with them that met with
	him</b>—or "came in his way."</p>
