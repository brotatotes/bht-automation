</p>
<p><span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>. PAUL'S
	ARRIVAL AND LABORS
	AT CORINTH, WHERE
	HE IS
	REJOINED BY SILAS
	AND TIMOTHY, AND, UNDER
	DIVINE ENCOURAGEMENT,
	MAKES A LONG
	STAY—AT
	LENGTH, RETRACING
	HIS STEPS,
	BY EPHESUS,
	CÆSAREA, AND JERUSALEM,
	HE RETURNS
	FOR THE LAST TIME
	TO ANTIOCH, THUS
	COMPLETING HIS
	SECOND MISSIONARY
	JOURNEY.</p>
