<p><b>5-7. When they heard this</b>—not
	the mere words reported in <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="ac+19:4">Acts 19:4</span>,
	but <i>the subject expounded</i> according to the tenor of those
	words. 
	</p>
<p><b>they were baptized</b>—not
	however by Paul himself (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1co+1:14">1 Corinthians 1:14</span>).
	</p>
<p><b>in the name of the Lord
	Jesus</b>—into the whole fulness of the new economy, as now opened
	up to their believing minds.</p>
