<p><b>9. when divers</b>—"some."
	</p>
<p><b>were hardened,</b>
	&amp;c.—implying that others, probably a large number, believed. 
	</p>
<p><b>spake evil of that way before
	the multitude, he departed</b>—from the synagogue, as at Corinth
	(<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="ac+18:7">Acts 18:7</span>). 
	</p>
<p><b>and separated the
	disciples</b>—withdrawing to a separate place of meeting, for the
	sake both of the converts already made, and the unsophisticated
	multitude. 
	</p>
<p><b>disputing</b>—"discoursing"
	or "discussing." 
	</p>
<p><b>daily in the school</b>—or
	lecture hall. 
	</p>
<p><b>of one Tyrannus</b>—probably
	a converted teacher of rhetoric or philosophy.</p>
