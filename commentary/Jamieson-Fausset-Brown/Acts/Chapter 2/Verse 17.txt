<p><b>17. in the last days</b>—meaning,
	the days of the Messiah (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="isa+2:2">Isaiah 2:2</span>);
	as closing all preparatory arrangements, and constituting the final
	dispensation of God's kingdom on earth. 
	</p>
<p><b>pour out of my Spirit</b>—in
	contrast with the mere drops of all preceding time. 
	</p>
<p><b>upon all flesh</b>—hitherto
	confined to the seed of Abraham. 
	</p>
<p><b>sons . . . daughters . . .
	young men . . . old men . . . servants . . . handmaidens</b>—without
	distinction of sex, age, or rank. 
	</p>
<p><b>see visions . . . dream
	dreams</b>—This is a mere accommodation to the ways in which the
	Spirit operated under the ancient economy, when the prediction was
	delivered; for in the New Testament, visions and dreams are rather
	the exception than the rule.</p>
