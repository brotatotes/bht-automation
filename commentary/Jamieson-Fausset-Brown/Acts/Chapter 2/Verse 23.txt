<p><b>23. determinate counsel and
	foreknowledge</b>—God's fixed plan and perfect foresight of all the
	steps involved in it. 
	</p>
<p><b>ye have taken, and by wicked
	hands have crucified and slain</b>—How strikingly is the
	criminality of Christ's murderers here presented in harmony with the
	eternal purpose to surrender Him into their hands!</p>
