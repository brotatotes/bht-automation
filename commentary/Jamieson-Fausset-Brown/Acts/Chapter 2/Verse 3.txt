<p><b>3. cloven tongues, like as of fire,</b>
	&amp;c.—"disparted tongues," that is, tongue-shaped,
	flame-like appearances, rising from a common center or root, and
	resting upon each of that large company:—beautiful visible symbol
	of the burning energy of the Spirit now descending in all His
	plenitude upon the Church, and about to pour itself through every
	tongue, and over every tribe of men under heaven!</p>
