<p><b>37-40. pricked in their hearts</b>—the
	begun fulfilment of <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="zec+12:10">Zechariah 12:10</span>,
	whose full accomplishment is reserved for the day when "all
	Israel shall be saved" (see on <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="zec+12:10- :">Zechariah 12:10- :</span>). 
	</p>
<p><b>what shall we do?</b>—This
	is that beautiful spirit of genuine compunction and childlike
	docility, which, discovering its whole past career to have been one
	frightful mistake, seeks only to be set right for the future, be the
	change involved and the sacrifices required what they may. So Saul of
	Tarsus (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="ac+9:6">Acts 9:6</span>).</p>
