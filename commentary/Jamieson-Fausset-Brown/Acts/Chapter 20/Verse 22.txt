<p><b>22, 23. And now, behold, I</b>—"I"
	is emphatic here. 
	</p>
<p><b>bound in the spirit</b>—compare
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="ac+19:21">Acts 19:21</span>. This internal
	pressure, unattended with any knowledge of "what was to befall
	him there," was the result of that higher guidance which shaped
	all his movements.</p>
