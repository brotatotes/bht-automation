<p><b>7. upon the first day of the week,
	when the disciples came together</b>—This, compared with <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>, and other similar allusions, plainly indicates that the
	Christian observance of the day afterwards distinctly called "the
	Lord's Day," was already a fixed practice of the churches. 
	</p>
<p><b>Paul preached</b>—discoursed.
	The <i>tense</i> implies continued action—"kept discoursing."</p>
