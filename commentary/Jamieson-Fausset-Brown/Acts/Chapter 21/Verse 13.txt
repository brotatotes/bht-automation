<p><b>13. Then Paul answered, What mean ye
	to weep and to break mine heart</b>—Beautiful union of manly
	resoluteness and womanly tenderness, alike removed from mawkishness
	and stoicism! 
	</p>
<p><b>I am ready not to be bound
	only</b>—"If that is all, let it come." 
	</p>
<p><b>but to die,</b> &amp;c.—It
	was well he could add this, for he had that also to do.</p>
