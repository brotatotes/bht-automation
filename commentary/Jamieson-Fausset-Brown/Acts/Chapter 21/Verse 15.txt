<p><b>15, 16. we took up our
	carriages</b>—"our baggage." 
	</p>
<p><b>and went up to Jerusalem</b>—for
	the <i>fifth</i> time after his conversion, thus concluding <i>his
	third missionary tour,</i> which proved his <i>last,</i> so far as
	recorded; for though he accomplished the fourth and last part of the
	missionary plan sketched out (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>) —"After I have been at Jerusalem, I must also see
	Rome"—it was as "a prisoner of Jesus Christ."</p>
