<p><b>7. when we had finished our
	course</b>—completing the voyage 
	</p>
<p><b>from Tyre, we came</b>—which
	they would do the same day. 
	</p>
<p><b>to Ptolemais</b>—anciently
	called Accho (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="jdg+1:31">Judges 1:31</span>), now St.
	Jean d'Acre, or Acre. 
	</p>
<p><b>and saluted the brethren, and
	abode,</b> &amp;c.—disciples gathered probably as at Tyre, on the
	occasion mentioned (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="ac+11:19">Acts 11:19</span>).</p>
