<p><b>35. I will hear thee</b>—The word
	means, "give thee a full hearing." 
	</p>
<p><b>to be kept in Herod's
	judgment hall</b>—"prætorium," the palace built at
	Cæsarea by Herod, and now occupied by the Roman procurators; in one
	of the buildings attached to which Paul was ordered to be kept.</p>
