<p><b>1. after five days</b>—or, on the
	fifth day from their departure from Jerusalem. 
	</p>
<p><b>Ananias . . . with the
	elders</b>—a deputation of the Sanhedrim. 
	</p>
<p><b>a certain orator</b>—one of
	those Roman advocates who trained themselves for the higher practice
	of the metropolis by practicing in the provinces, where the <i>Latin</i>
	language, employed in the courts, was but imperfectly understood and
	Roman forms were not familiar. 
	</p>
<p><b>informed . . . against
	Paul</b>—"laid information," that is, put in the charges.</p>
