<p><b>16. And herein</b>—On this
	account, accordingly; that is, looking forward to that awful day
	(compare <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="2co+5:10">2 Corinthians 5:10</span>). 
	</p>
<p><b>I exercise myself</b>—The
	"I" here is emphatic; "Whatever they do, this is my
	study." 
	</p>
<p><b>to have always a conscience
	void of offence,</b> c.—See <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="ac+23:1">Acts 23:1</span>
<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="2co+1:12">2 Corinthians 1:12</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="2co+2:17">2 Corinthians 2:17</span>,
	c. that is, "These are the great principles of my life and
	conduct—how different from turbulence and sectarianism!"</p>
