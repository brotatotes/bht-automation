<p><b>17. Now after many</b>—several 
	</p>
<p><b>years absence from
	Jerusalem</b>—I came to bring alms to my of Macedonia and Greece,
	which he had taken such pains to gather. This only allusion in the
	Acts to what is dwelt upon so frequently in his own Epistles (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="ro+15:25">Romans 15:25</span>;
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="ro+15:26">Romans 15:26</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1co+16:1-4">1 Corinthians 16:1-4</span>;
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="2co+8:1-4">2 Corinthians 8:1-4</span>), throws a
	beautiful light on the truth of this History. (See PALEY'S
	<i>Horæ Paulinæ</i>). 
	</p>
<p><b>and offerings</b>—connected
	with his Jewish vow: see <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="ac+24:18">Acts 24:18</span>.</p>
