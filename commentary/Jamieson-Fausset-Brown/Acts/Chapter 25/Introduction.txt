 CHAPTER 25 
	
 :-. FESTUS,
	COMING TO JERUSALEM,
	DECLINES TO HAVE
	PAUL BROUGHT
	THITHER FOR JUDGMENT,
	BUT GIVES THE
	PARTIES A HEARING
	ON HIS RETURN
	TO CÆSAREA—ON
	FESTUS ASKING
	THE APOSTLE IF HE
	WOULD GO
	TO JERUSALEM FOR
	ANOTHER HEARING
	BEFORE HIM, HE
	IS CONSTRAINED
	IN JUSTICE TO HIS
	CAUSE TO APPEAL
	TO THE EMPEROR.
