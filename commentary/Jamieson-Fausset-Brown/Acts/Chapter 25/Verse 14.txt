<p><b>14, 15. when there many</b>—"several"
	</p>
<p><b>days, Festus declared Paul's
	cause</b>—taking advantage of the presence of one who might be
	presumed to know such matters better than himself; though the lapse
	of "several days" ere the subject was touched on shows that
	it gave Festus little trouble.</p>
