<p><b>23. when Agrippa was come, and
	Bernice, with great pomp</b>—in the same city in which their
	father, on account of his pride, had perished, eaten up by worms
	[WETST]. 
	</p>
<p><b>with the chief captains</b>—(See
	on <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="ac+25:2">Acts 25:2</span>). JOSEPHUS
	[<i>Wars of the Jews,</i> 3.4.2] says that five cohorts, whose full
	complement was one thousand men, were stationed at Cæsarea. 
	</p>
