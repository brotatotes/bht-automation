<p><b>the man myself</b>—No doubt
	Paul was fight when he said, "The king knoweth of these things .
	. . for I am persuaded that none of these things are hidden from him;
	for this thing was not done in a corner" (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>). Hence his curiosity to see and hear the man who had
	raised such commotion and was remodelling to such an extent the whole
	Jewish life.</p>
