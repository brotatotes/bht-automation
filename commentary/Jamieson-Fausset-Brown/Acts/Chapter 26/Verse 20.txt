<p><b>20. showed . . . to them of
	Damascus, and at Jerusalem</b>—omitting Arabia; because, beginning
	with the Jews, his object was to mention first the places where his
	former hatred of the name of Christ was best known: the mention of
	the Gentiles, so unpalatable to his audience, is reserved to the
	last. 
	</p>
<p><b>repent and return to God, and
	do works meet for repentance</b>—a brief description of conversion
	and its proper fruits, suggested, probably, by the Baptist's teaching
	(<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="lu+3:7">Luke 3:7</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="lu+3:8">Luke 3:8</span>).</p>
