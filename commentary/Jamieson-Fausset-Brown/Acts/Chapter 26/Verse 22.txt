<p><b>22, 23. having obtained
	help</b>—"succor." 
	</p>
<p><b>from God</b>—"that
	[which cometh] from God." 
	</p>
<p><b>I continue</b>—"stand,"
	"hold my ground." 
	</p>
<p><b>unto this day, witnessing,</b>
	c.—that is, This life of mine, so marvellously preserved, in spite
	of all the plots against it, is upheld for the Gospel's sake
	therefore I "witnessed," &amp;c.</p>
