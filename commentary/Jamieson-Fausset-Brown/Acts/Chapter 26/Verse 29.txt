<p><b>29. I would to God,</b> &amp;c.—What
	unequalled magnanimity does this speech breathe! Only his Master ever
	towered above this. 
	</p>
<p><b>not only . . . almost . . .
	but altogether</b>—or, "whether soon or late," or "with
	little or much difficulty." 
	</p>
<p><b>except these bonds</b>—doubtless
	holding up his two chained hands (see on <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>): which in closing such a noble utterance must have had an
	electrical effect.</p>
