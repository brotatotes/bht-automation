<p><b>3. </b><i><b>I know</b></i><b> thee
	to be expert,</b> &amp;c.—His father was zealous for the law, and
	he himself had the office of president of the temple and its
	treasures, and the appointment of the high priest [JOSEPHUS,
	<i>Antiquities,</i> 20.1.3]. 
	</p>
<p><b>hear me patiently</b>—The
	idea of "indulgently" is also conveyed.</p>
