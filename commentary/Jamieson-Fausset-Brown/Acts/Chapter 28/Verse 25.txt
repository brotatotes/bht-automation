<p><b>25-29. when they</b>—the Jews. 
	</p>
<p><b>agreed not among
	themselves</b>—the discussion having passed into one between the
	two parties into which the visitors were now divided, respecting the
	arguments and conclusions of the apostle. 
	</p>
<p><b>they departed</b>—the
	material of discussion being felt by both parties to be exhausted. 
	</p>
<p><b>after Paul had spoken one
	word</b>—one solemn parting testimony, from those Scriptures
	regarded by both alike as "the Holy Ghost speaking" to
	Israel.</p>
