<p><b>11. the lame man . . . held,</b>
	&amp;c.—This is human nature. 
	</p>
<p><b>all the people ran together
	unto them in the porch,</b> &amp;c.—How vividly do these graphic
	details bring the whole scene before us! Thus was Peter again
	furnished with a vast audience, whose wonder at the spectacle of the
	healed beggar clinging to his benefactors prepared them to listen
	with reverence to his words.</p>
