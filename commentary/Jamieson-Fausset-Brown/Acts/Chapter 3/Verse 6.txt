<p><b>6. Silver and gold have I none, but
	such as I have give I thee</b>—What a lofty superiority breathes in
	these words! 
	</p>
<p><b>In the name of Jesus Christ
	of Nazareth rise up and walk</b>—These words, uttered with
	supernatural power, doubtless begat in this poor man the faith that
	sent healing virtue through his diseased members.</p>
