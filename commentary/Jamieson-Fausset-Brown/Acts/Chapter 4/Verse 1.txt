<p><b>1-12. the captain</b>—of the
	Levitical guard. 
	</p>
<p><b>of the temple</b>—annoyed
	at the disturbance created around it. 
	</p>
<p><b>and the Sadducees</b>—who
	"say that there is no resurrection" (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>), irritated at the apostles "preaching through (rather,
	'in') Jesus the resurrection from the dead"; for the
	resurrection of Christ, if a fact, effectually overthrew the
	Sadducean doctrine.</p>
