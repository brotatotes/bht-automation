<p><b>24. they lifted up their voice</b>—the
	assembled disciples, on hearing Peter's report. 
	</p>
<p><b>with one accord</b>—the
	breasts of all present echoing every word of this sublime prayer. 
	</p>
<p><b>Lord</b>—(See on <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>). Applied to God, the term expresses absolute authority. 
	</p>
<p><b>God which hast made heaven
	and earth</b>—against whom, therefore, all creatures are powerless.</p>
