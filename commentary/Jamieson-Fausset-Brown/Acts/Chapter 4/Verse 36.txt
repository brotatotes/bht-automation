<p><b>36. Joses,</b> &amp;c.—This is
	specified merely as an eminent example of that spirit of generous
	sacrifice which pervaded all. 
	</p>
<p><b>son of consolation</b>—no
	doubt so surnamed from the character of his ministry. 
	</p>
<p><b>a Levite</b>—who, though as
	a tribe having no inheritance, might and did acquire property as
	individuals (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="de+18:8">Deuteronomy 18:8</span>). 
	</p>
