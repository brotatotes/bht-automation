<p><b>15. into the streets</b>—"in
	every street." 
	</p>
<p><b>on beds and couches</b>—The
	words denote the softer couches of the rich and the meaner cribs of
	the poor [BENGEL]. 
	</p>
<p><b>shadow of Peter . . . might
	overshadow some of them</b>—Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="ac+19:12">Acts 19:12</span>;
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="lu+8:46">Luke 8:46</span>. So Elisha. Now the
	predicted greatness of Peter (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="lu+8:46- :">Luke 8:46- :</span>), as the directing spirit of the early Church, was at its
	height.</p>
