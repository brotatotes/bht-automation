<p><b>4. While it remained, was it not
	thine own? and after it was sold, was it not in thine own power?</b>—from
	which we see how purely voluntary were all these sacrifices for the
	support of the infant community. 
	</p>
<p><b>not lied to men but God</b>—to
	men so entirely the instruments of the directing Spirit that the lie
	was rather told to Him: language clearly implying both the distinct
	<i>personality</i> and the proper <i>divinity</i> of the Holy Ghost.</p>
