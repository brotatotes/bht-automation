<p><b>3. look ye out among you</b>—that
	is, ye, "the multitude," from among yourselves. 
	</p>
<p><b>seven men of honest
	report</b>—good reputation (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="ac+10:22">Acts 10:22</span>;
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1ti+3:7">1 Timothy 3:7</span>). 
	</p>
<p><b>full of the Holy Ghost</b>—not
	full of miraculous gifts, which would have been no qualification for
	the duties required, but <i>spiritually gifted</i> (although on two
	of them miraculous power did rest). 
	</p>
<p><b>and wisdom</b>—discretion,
	aptitude for practical business. 
	</p>
<p><b>whom we may appoint</b>—for
	while the <i>election</i> was vested in the Christian people, the
	<i>appointment</i> lay with the apostles, as spiritual rulers.</p>
