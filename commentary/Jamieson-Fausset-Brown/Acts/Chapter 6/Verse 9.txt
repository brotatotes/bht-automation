<p><b>9, 10. synagogue of the
	Libertines</b>—Jewish freedmen; manumitted Roman captives, or the
	children of such, expelled from Rome (as appears from JOSEPHUS
	and TACITUS), and now
	residing at Jerusalem. 
	</p>
<p><b>Cyrenians</b>—Jews of
	Cyrene, in Libya, on the coast of Africa. 
	</p>
