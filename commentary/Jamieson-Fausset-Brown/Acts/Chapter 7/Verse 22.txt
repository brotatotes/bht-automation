<p><b>22. mighty in words</b>—Though
	defective in utterance (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="ex+4:10">Exodus 4:10</span>);
	his recorded speeches fully bear out what is here said. 
	</p>
<p><b>and deeds</b>—referring
	probably to unrecorded circumstances in his early life. If we are to
	believe JOSEPHUS, his
	ability was acknowledged ere he left Egypt.</p>
