<p><b>39. To whom our fathers would not
	obey,</b> &amp;c.—Here he shows that <i>the deepest dishonor done
	to Moses came from the nation that now professed the greatest
	jealousy for his honor.</i>
</p>
<p><b>in their hearts turned back .
	. . into Egypt</b>—"In this Stephen would have his hearers
