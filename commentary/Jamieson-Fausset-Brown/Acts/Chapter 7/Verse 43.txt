<p><b>43. Yea, ye took up the tabernacle
	of Molech,</b> c.—Two kinds of idolatry are charged upon the
	Israelites: that of the golden calf and that of the heavenly bodies
	Molech and Remphan being deities, representing apparently the divine
	powers ascribed to nature, under different aspects. 
	</p>
<p><b>carry you beyond Babylon</b>—the
	well-known region of the captivity of Judah; while "Damascus"
	is used by the prophet (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="am+5:27">Amos 5:27</span>),
	whither the ten tribes were carried.</p>
