<p><b>13. Then Simon himself believed
	also</b>—Left without followers, he thinks it best to join the man
	who had fairly outstripped him, not without a touch of real
	conviction. 
	</p>
<p><b>and . . . was baptized</b>—What
	a light does this throw on what is called <i>Baptismal Regeneration!</i>
</p>
<p><b>he continued with Philip</b>—"was
	in constant attendance upon" him.</p>
