<p><b>27. a man of Ethiopia</b>—Upper
	Egypt, Meroe. 
	</p>
<p><b>an eunuch of great
	authority</b>—Eunuchs were generally employed for confidential
	offices in the East, and to some extent are still. 
	</p>
<p><b>Candace</b>—the family name
	of the queens of Upper Egypt, like Pharaoh, Cæsar, c. (as appears
	from classic authors). 
	</p>
<p><b>had come to Jerusalem to
	worship</b>—that is, to keep the recent feast of Pentecost, as a
	Gentile proselyte to the Jewish faith. (See <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="isa+56:3-8">Isaiah 56:3-8</span>
<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="joh+12:20">John 12:20</span>).</p>
