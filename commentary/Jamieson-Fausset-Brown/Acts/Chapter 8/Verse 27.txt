27. a man of Ethiopia—Upper
	Egypt, Meroe. 
	an eunuch of great
	authority—Eunuchs were generally employed for confidential
	offices in the East, and to some extent are still. 
	Candace—the family name
	of the queens of Upper Egypt, like Pharaoh, Cæsar, c. (as appears
	from classic authors). 
	had come to Jerusalem to
	worship—that is, to keep the recent feast of Pentecost, as a
	Gentile proselyte to the Jewish faith. (See Isaiah 56:3-8
John 12:20).
