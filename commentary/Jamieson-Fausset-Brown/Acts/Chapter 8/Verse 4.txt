<p><b>4. they that were scattered abroad
	went everywhere preaching</b>—Though solemnly enjoined to do this
	(<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="lu+24:47">Luke 24:47</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="ac+1:8">Acts 1:8</span>),

	persecution which swept them out. How often has the rage of Christ's
	enemies thus "turned out rather unto the furtherance of the
	Gospel" (see <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="php+1:12">Philippians 1:12</span>;
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="php+1:13">Philippians 1:13</span>).
