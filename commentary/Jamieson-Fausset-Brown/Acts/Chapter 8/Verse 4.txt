4. they that were scattered abroad
	went everywhere preaching—Though solemnly enjoined to do this
	(Luke 24:47; Acts 1:8),
	they would probably have lingered at Jerusalem, but for this besom of
	persecution which swept them out. How often has the rage of Christ's
	enemies thus "turned out rather unto the furtherance of the
	Gospel" (see Philippians 1:12;
	Philippians 1:13).
