<p><b>14.</b> (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>.) 
	</p>
<p><b>redemption</b>—rather as
	<i>Greek,</i> "<i>our</i> redemption." 
	</p>
<p><b>through his blood</b>—omitted
	in the oldest manuscripts; probably inserted from <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>. 
	</p>
<p><b>sins</b>—Translate as
	<i>Greek,</i> "<i>our</i> sins." The more general term: for
	which <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="eph+1:7">Ephesians 1:7</span>, <i>Greek,</i>
	has, "our <i>transgressions,</i>" the more special term.</p>
