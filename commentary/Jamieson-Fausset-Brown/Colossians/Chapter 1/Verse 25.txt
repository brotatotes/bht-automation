<p><b>25. am</b>—<i>Greek,</i> "I
	<i>was</i> made a minister": resuming <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>, "whereof I Paul was made a minister." 
	</p>
<p><b>dispensation</b>—the
	<i>stewardship</i> committed to me to dispense in the house of God,
	the Church, to the whole family of believers, the goods of my Master
	(<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="lu+12:42">Luke 12:42</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1co+4:1">1 Corinthians 4:1</span>;
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1co+4:2">1 Corinthians 4:2</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1co+9:17">1 Corinthians 9:17</span>;
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="eph+3:2">Ephesians 3:2</span>). 
	</p>
<p><b>which is given</b>—<i>Greek,</i>
	"which <i>was</i> given." 
	</p>
<p><b>for you</b>—with a view to
	you, Gentiles (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="col+1:27">Colossians 1:27</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="ro+15:16">Romans 15:16</span>).
	</p>
<p><b>to fulfil</b>—to bring it
	fully to all: the end of his stewardship: "fully preached"
	(<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="ro+15:19">Romans 15:19</span>). "The <i>fulness</i>
	of Christ (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="col+1:19">Colossians 1:19</span>), and of
	the times (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="eph+1:10">Ephesians 1:10</span>) required
	him so to do" [BENGEL].</p>
