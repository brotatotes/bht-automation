<p><b>4. Since we heard</b>—literally,
	"Having heard." The language implies that he had only heard
	of, and not <i>seen,</i> them (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>). Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="ro+1:8">Romans 1:8</span>, where
	like language is used of a Church which he had not at the time
	visited. 
	</p>
<p><b>love . . . to all</b>—the
	absent, as well as those present [BENGEL].</p>
