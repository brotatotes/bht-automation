<p><b>17. things to come</b>—the
	blessings of the Christian covenant, the substance of which Jewish
	ordinances were but the type. Compare "ages to come," that
	is, the Gospel dispensation (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>). <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="heb+2:5">Hebrews 2:5</span>, "the
	world to come." 
	</p>
<p><b>the body is of Christ</b>—The
	<i>real substance</i> (of the blessings typified by the law) belongs
	to Christ (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="heb+8:5">Hebrews 8:5</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="heb+10:1">Hebrews 10:1</span>).</p>
