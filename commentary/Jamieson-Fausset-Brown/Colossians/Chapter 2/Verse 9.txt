<p><b>of the Godhead</b>—The
	<i>Greek</i> (<i>theotes</i>) means the ESSENCE
	and NATURE <i>of the
	Godhead,</i> not merely the <i>divine perfections</i> and attributes
	of Divinity (<i>Greek,</i> "<i>theiotes</i>"). He, as man,
	was not merely God-like, but in the fullest sense, God. 
	</p>
