<p><b>2. Continue</b>—<i>Greek,</i>
	"Continue perseveringly," "persevere" (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>), "watching <i>thereunto</i>"; here, "watch
	<i>in the same,</i>" or "<i>in it,</i>" that is, in
	prayer: watching against the indolence as to prayer, and in prayer,
	of our corrupt wills. 
	</p>
<p><b>with thanksgiving</b>—for
	everything, whether joyful, or sorrowful, mercies temporal and
	spiritual, national, family, and individual (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1co+14:17">1 Corinthians 14:17</span>;
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="php+4:6">Philippians 4:6</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1th+5:18">1 Thessalonians 5:18</span>).</p>
