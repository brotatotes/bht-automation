<p><b>4.</b> ALFORD
	thinks that Paul asks their prayers for his release as if it were the
	"only" way by which he could "make it (the Gospel)
	manifest" as he ought. But while this is <i>included</i> in
	their subject of prayer, <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="php+1:12">Philippians 1:12</span>;
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="php+1:13">Philippians 1:13</span>, written somewhat later
	in his imprisonment, clearly shows that "a door for the word"
	could be opened, and was opened, for its <i>manifestation,</i> even
	while he remained imprisoned (compare <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="php+1:13- :">Philippians 1:13- :</span>).</p>
