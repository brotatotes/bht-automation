<p><b>14. earnest</b>—the first
	instalment paid as a pledge that the rest will follow (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="ro+8:23">Romans 8:23</span>;
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="2co+1:22">2 Corinthians 1:22</span>). 
	</p>
<p><b>until</b>—rather, "<i>Unto</i>
	the redemption," c. joined thus, "ye were sealed (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="2co+1:22- :">2 Corinthians 1:22- :</span>) unto," that is, <i>for the purpose of and against,</i>
	the accomplishment of "the redemption," namely, not the
	<i>redemption</i> in its first stage, made by the blood of Christ,
	which secures our <i>title,</i> but, in its final completion, when
	the actual <i>possession</i> shall be ours, the full "redemption
	of the body" (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="ro+8:23">Romans 8:23</span>), as
	well as of the soul, from every infirmity (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="ro+8:23- :">Romans 8:23- :</span>). The deliverance of the creature (the body, and the whole
	visible creation) from the bondage of corruption, and from the
	usurping prince of this world, into the glorious liberty of the
	children of God (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="ro+8:21-23">Romans 8:21-23</span>;
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="2pe+3:13">2 Peter 3:13</span>). 
	</p>
<p><b>of the purchased
	possession</b>—God's people <i>purchased</i> ("acquired,"
	<i>Greek</i>) as His <i>peculiar</i> (<i>Greek</i>) possession by the
	blood of Christ (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="ac+20:28">Acts 20:28</span>). We
	value highly that which we pay a high price for; so God, His Church
	(<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="eph+5:25">Ephesians 5:25</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="eph+5:26">Ephesians 5:26</span>;
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1pe+1:18">1 Peter 1:18</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1pe+2:9">1 Peter 2:9</span>;
	"my <i>special</i> treasure," <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1pe+2:9- :">1 Peter 2:9- :</span>, <i>Margin</i>).</p>
