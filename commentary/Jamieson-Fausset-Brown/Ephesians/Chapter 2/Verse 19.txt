<p><b>19. Now, therefore</b>—rather, "So
	then" [ALFORD]. 
	</p>
<p><b>foreigners</b>—rather,
	"sojourners"; opposed to "members of the household,"
	as "strangers" is to "fellow citizens." <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="php+3:19">Philippians 3:19</span>;
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="php+3:20">Philippians 3:20</span>, "conversation,"
	<i>Greek,</i> "citizenship." 
	</p>
<p><b>but</b>—The oldest
	manuscripts add, "are." 
	</p>
<p><b>with the saints</b>—"the
	commonwealth of (spiritual) Israel" (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="php+3:20- :">Philippians 3:20- :</span>). 
	</p>
<p><b>of God</b>—THE
	FATHER; as JESUS
	CHRIST appears in <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="php+3:20- :">Philippians 3:20- :</span>, and THE SPIRIT
	in <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="eph+2:22">Ephesians 2:22</span>.</p>
