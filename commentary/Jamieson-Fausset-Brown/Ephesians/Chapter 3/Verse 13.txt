<p><b>13.</b> "I entreat you not to
	be dispirited." 
	</p>
<p><b>for you</b>—in your behalf.
	</p>
<p><b>which is</b>—rather, "which
	<i>are</i> your glory," namely, inasmuch as showing that God
	loved you so much, as both to give His Son for you, and to permit His
	apostles to suffer "tribulations" for you [CHRYSOSTOM]
	in preaching the Gospel to the Gentiles. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>, "prisoner for you Gentiles." My tribulations are
	your spiritual "glory," as your faith is furthered thereby
	(<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1co+4:10">1 Corinthians 4:10</span>).</p>
