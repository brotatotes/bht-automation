<p><b>21.</b> Translate, "Unto Him be
	<i>the</i> glory (that is, the whole glory of the gracious
	dispensation of salvation just spoken of) in the Church (as the
	theater for the manifestation of the glory, <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>) in Christ Jesus (as in Him all the glory centers, <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>) to all the generations of eternal ages," literally,
	"of the age of the ages." Eternity is conceived as
	consisting of "ages" (these again consisting of
	"generations") endlessly succeeding one another.</p>
