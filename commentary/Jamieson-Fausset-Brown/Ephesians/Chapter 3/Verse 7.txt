<p><b>7. Whereof</b>—"of which"
	Gospel. 
	</p>
<p><b>according to</b>—in
	consequence of, and in accordance with, "the gift of the grace
	of God." 
	</p>
<p><b>given</b>—"which (gift
	of grace) was given to me by (<i>Greek,</i> 'according to,' as in
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="eph+3:20">Ephesians 3:20</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="eph+1:19">Ephesians 1:19</span>:
	as the result of, and in proportion to) the effectual working (<i>Greek,</i>
	'energy,' or 'in-working') of His power."</p>
