<p><b>8. am</b>—Not merely <i>was</i> I
	in times past, but I still am the least worthy of so high an office
	(compare <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1ti+1:15">1 Timothy 1:15</span>, end). 
	</p>
<p><b>least of all saints</b>—not
	merely "of all <i>apostles</i>" (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1co+15:9">1 Corinthians 15:9</span>;
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1co+15:10">1 Corinthians 15:10</span>). 
	</p>
<p><b>is</b>—<i>Greek,</i> "has
	been given." 
	</p>
<p><b>among</b>—omitted in the
	oldest manuscripts Translate, "to <i>announce</i> to the
	Gentiles <i>the glad tidings</i> of the unsearchable (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1co+15:10- :">1 Corinthians 15:10- :</span>) riches," namely, of Christ's <i>grace</i> (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="eph+1:7">Ephesians 1:7</span>;
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="eph+2:7">Ephesians 2:7</span>). <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="eph+2:7- :">Ephesians 2:7- :</span>, "unsearchable" as a mine inexhaustible, whose
	treasures can never be fully explored (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="eph+3:18">Ephesians 3:18</span>;
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="eph+3:19">Ephesians 3:19</span>).</p>
