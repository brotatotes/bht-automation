<p><b>13. come in</b>—rather, "attain
	unto." ALFORD
	expresses the <i>Greek order,</i> "Until we arrive all of us at
	the unity," c. 
	</p>
<p><b>faith and . . .
	knowledge</b>—Full unity of <i>faith</i> is then found, when all
	alike thoroughly <i>know Christ,</i> the object of faith, and that in
	His highest dignity as "the Son of God" [DE
	WETTE] (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="eph+3:17">Ephesians 3:17</span>
<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="eph+3:19">Ephesians 3:19</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="2pe+1:5">2 Peter 1:5</span>).
	Not even Paul counted himself to have fully "attained" (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="2pe+1:5- :">2 Peter 1:5- :</span>). Amidst the variety of the gifts and the multitude of
	the Church's members, its "faith" is to be ONE:
	as contrasted with the state of "children carried about with
	EVERY WIND OF DOCTRINE."
	(<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="eph+4:14">Ephesians 4:14</span>). 
	</p>
<p><b>perfect man</b>—unto the
	<i>full-grown man</i> (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1co+2:6">1 Corinthians 2:6</span>;
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="php+3:15">Philippians 3:15</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="heb+5:14">Hebrews 5:14</span>);
	the <i>maturity</i> of an <i>adult;</i> contrasted with children (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="heb+5:14- :">Hebrews 5:14- :</span>). Not "perfect <i>men</i>"; for the many members
	constitute but <i>one</i> Church joined to the one Christ. 
	</p>
<p><b>stature,</b> c.—The
	standard of spiritual "stature" is "the fulness of
	Christ," that is, which Christ has (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="eph+1:23">Ephesians 1:23</span>
<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="eph+3:19">Ephesians 3:19</span>; compare <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="eph+3:19- :">Ephesians 3:19- :</span>); that the body should be worthy of the Head, the perfect
	Christ.</p>
