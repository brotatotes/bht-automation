<p><b>15. speaking the truth</b>—Translate,
	"holding the truth"; "following the truth";
	opposed to "error" or "deceit" (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>). 
	</p>
<p><b>in love</b>—"Truth"
	is never to be sacrificed to so-called "charity"; yet it is
	to be maintained in charity. Truth in word and act, love in manner
	and spirit, are the Christian's rule (compare <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="eph+4:21">Ephesians 4:21</span>;
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="eph+4:24">Ephesians 4:24</span>). 
	</p>
<p><b>grow up</b>—from the state
	of "children" to that of "full-grown men." There
	is growth only in the spiritually alive, not in the dead. 
	</p>
<p><b>into him</b>—so as to be
	more and more incorporated with Him, and become one with Him. 
	</p>
<p><b>the head</b>— (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="eph+4:24- :">Ephesians 4:24- :</span>).</p>
