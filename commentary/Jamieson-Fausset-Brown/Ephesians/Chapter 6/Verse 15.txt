<p><b>15.</b> Translate, "Having shod
	your feet" (referring to the sandals, or to the military shoes
	then used). 
	</p>
<p><b>the preparation</b>—rather,
	"the preparedness," or "readiness of," that is,
	arising from the "Gospel" (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>). Preparedness to do and suffer all that God wills;
	readiness for march, as a Christian soldier. 
	</p>
<p><b>gospel of peace</b>—(compare
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="lu+1:79">Luke 1:79</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="ro+10:15">Romans 10:15</span>).
	The "peace" within forms a beautiful contrast to the raging
	of the outward conflict (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="isa+26:3">Isaiah 26:3</span>;
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="php+4:7">Philippians 4:7</span>).</p>
