<p><b>13. heard</b>—even before I came
	among you. 
	</p>
<p><b>conversation</b>—"my
	former way of life." 
	</p>
<p><b>Jews' religion</b>—The
	term, "<i>Hebrew,</i>" expresses the <i>language;</i>
	"Jew," the <i>nationality,</i> as distinguished from the
	Gentiles; "Israelite," the highest title, the religious
	privileges, as a member of the theocracy. 
	</p>
<p><b>the church</b>—Here
	singular, marking its unity, though constituted of many particular
	churches, under the one Head, Christ. 
	</p>
<p><b>of God</b>—added to mark
	the greatness of his sinful alienation from God (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>). 
	</p>
<p><b>wasted</b>—laid it waste:
	the opposite of "building it up."</p>
