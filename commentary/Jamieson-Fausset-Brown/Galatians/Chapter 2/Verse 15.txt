<p><b>15, 16.</b> Connect these verses
	together, and read with most of the oldest manuscripts "But"
	in the beginning of <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="ga+2:16">Galatians 2:16</span>:
	"We (I and thou, Peter) by nature (not by proselytism), Jews,
	and not sinners as (Jewish language termed the Gentiles) from among
	the Gentiles, YET
	(literally, 'BUT') knowing
	that . . . even we (resuming the 'we' of <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="ga+2:16- :">Galatians 2:16- :</span>, 'we also,' as well as the Gentile sinners; casting away
	trust in the law), have believed," &amp;c.</p>
