<p><b>10.</b> Confirmation of <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>. They who depend on the works of the law cannot share the
	blessing, for they are under the curse "written," <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>, <i>Septuagint.</i> PERFECT
	<i>obedience</i> is required by the words, "in all things."
	CONTINUAL <i>obedience</i>
	by the word, "continueth." No man renders this obedience
	(compare <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="ro+3:19">Romans 3:19</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="ro+3:20">Romans 3:20</span>).
	It is observable, Paul quotes Scripture to the Jews who were
	conversant with it, as in Epistle to the Hebrews, as <i>said</i> or
	<i>spoken;</i> but to the Gentiles, as <i>written.</i> So Matthew,
	writing for Jews, quotes it as "said," or "spoken";
	Mark and Luke, writing for Gentiles, as "written" (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="mt+1:22">Matthew 1:22</span>;
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="mr+1:2">Mark 1:2</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="lu+2:22">Luke 2:22</span>;
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="lu+2:23">Luke 2:23</span>) [TOWNSON].</p>
