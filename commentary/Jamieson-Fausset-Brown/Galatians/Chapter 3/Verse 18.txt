<p><b>18. the inheritance</b>—all the
	blessings to be inherited by Abraham's literal and spiritual
	children, according to the promise made to him and to his Seed,
	Christ, justification and glorification (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="ga+4:7">Galatians 4:7</span>;
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="ro+8:17">Romans 8:17</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1co+6:9">1 Corinthians 6:9</span>).
	</p>
<p><b>but God,</b> &amp;c.—The
	<i>Greek</i> order requires rather, "But to Abraham it was by
	promise that God hath given it." The conclusion is, <i>Therefore
	the inheritance is not of,</i> or <i>from the law</i> (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1co+6:9- :">1 Corinthians 6:9- :</span>).</p>
