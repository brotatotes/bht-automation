<p><b>9. they</b>—and they alone. 
	</p>
<p><b>of faith</b>—(See on <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>, beginning). 
	</p>
<p><b>with</b>—together with. 
	</p>
<p><b>faithful</b>—implying what
	it is in which they are "blessed together with him,"
	namely, faith, the prominent feature of his character, and of which
	the result to all who like him have it, is justification.</p>
