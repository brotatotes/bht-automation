<p><b>19. My little children</b>—
	(<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1ti+1:18">1 Timothy 1:18</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="2ti+2:1">2 Timothy 2:1</span>;
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1jo+2:1">1 John 2:1</span>). My relation to you is
	not merely that of one <i>zealously courting</i> you (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="ga+4:17">Galatians 4:17</span>;
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="ga+4:18">Galatians 4:18</span>), but that of a <i>father</i>
	to his <i>children</i> (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1co+4:15">1 Corinthians 4:15</span>).
	</p>
<p><b>I travail in birth</b>—that
	is, like a mother in pain till the birth of her child. 
	</p>
<p><b>again</b>—a second time.
	The former time was when I was "present with you" (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1co+4:15- :">1 Corinthians 4:15- :</span>; compare <i>Note,</i> see on <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1co+4:15- :">1 Corinthians 4:15- :</span>). 
	</p>
<p><b>Christ be formed in you</b>—that
	you may live nothing but Christ, and think nothing but Christ (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1co+4:15- :">1 Corinthians 4:15- :</span>), and glory in nothing but Him, and His death, resurrection,
	and righteousness (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="php+3:8-10">Philippians 3:8-10</span>;
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="col+1:27">Colossians 1:27</span>).</p>
