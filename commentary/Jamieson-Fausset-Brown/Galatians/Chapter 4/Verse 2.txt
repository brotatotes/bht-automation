<p><b>2. tutors and governors</b>—rather,
	"guardians (of the person) and stewards (of the property)."
	Answering to "the law was our schoolmaster" or "tutor"
	(<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="ga+3:24">Galatians 3:24</span>). 
	</p>
<p><b>until the time appointed of
	the father</b>—in His eternal purposes (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="ga+3:24- :">Galatians 3:24- :</span>). The <i>Greek</i> is a legal term, expressing <i>a time
	defined</i> by law, or testamentary disposition.</p>
