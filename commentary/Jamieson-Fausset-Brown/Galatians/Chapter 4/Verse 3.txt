<p><b>3. we</b>—the Jews primarily, and
	inclusively the Gentiles also. For the "we" in <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span> plainly refers to <i>both</i> Jew and Gentile believers. The
	Jews in their bondage to the law of Moses, as the representative
	people of the world, include all mankind virtually amenable to God's
	law (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="ro+2:14">Romans 2:14</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="ro+2:15">Romans 2:15</span>;
	compare <i>Note,</i> see on <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="ro+2:15- :">Romans 2:15- :</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="ga+3:23">Galatians 3:23</span>). Even the
	Gentiles were under "bondage," and in a state of discipline
	suitable to nonage, till Christ came as the Emancipator. 
	</p>
<p><b>were in bondage</b>—as
	"servants" (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="ga+4:1">Galatians 4:1</span>). 
	</p>
<p><b>under the elements</b>—or
	"rudiments"; rudimentary religion teaching of a
	non-Christian character: <i>the elementary lessons of outward things</i>
	(literally, "of the [outward] world"); such as the legal
	ordinances mentioned, <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="ga+4:10">Galatians 4:10</span>
	(<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="col+2:8">Colossians 2:8</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="col+2:20">Colossians 2:20</span>).
	Our childhood's lessons [CONYBEARE
	and HOWSON]. Literally,
	The <i>letters</i> of the alphabet (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="col+2:20- :">Colossians 2:20- :</span>).</p>
