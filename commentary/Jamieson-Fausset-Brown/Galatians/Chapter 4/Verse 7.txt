<p><b>7. Wherefore</b>—Conclusion
	inferred from <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="ga+4:4-6">Galatians 4:4-6</span>. 
	</p>
<p><b>thou</b>—individualizing
	and applying the truth to each. Such an individual appropriation of
	this comforting truth God grants in answer to them who cry, "Abba,
	Father." 
	</p>
<p><b>heir of God through
	Christ</b>—The oldest manuscripts read, "an heir through God."
	This combines on behalf of man, the whole before-mentioned agency, of
	THE TRINITY:
	the Father sent His Son and the Spirit; the Son has freed us from the
	law; the Spirit has completed our sonship. Thus the redeemed are
	heirs THROUGH the Triune
	GOD, not through the law,
	nor through fleshly descent [WINDISCHMANN
	in ALFORD]; (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="ga+4:4-48.4.6- :">Galatians 4:4-48.4.6- :</span> confirms this). 
	</p>
<p><b>heir</b>—confirming <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="ga+4:4-48.4.6- :">Galatians 4:4-48.4.6- :</span>; compare <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="ro+8:17">Romans 8:17</span>.</p>
