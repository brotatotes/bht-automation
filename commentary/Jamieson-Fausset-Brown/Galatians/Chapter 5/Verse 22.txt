<p><b>22. love</b>—the leader of the
	band of graces (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>). 
	</p>
<p><b>gentleness</b>—<i>Greek,</i>
	"benignity," conciliatory to others; whereas "goodness,"
	though ready to do good, has not such <i>suavity</i> of manner
	[JEROME]. ALFORD
	translates, "kindness." 
	</p>
<p><b>faith</b>—"faithfulness";
	opposed to "heresies" [BENGEL].
	ALFORD refers to <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>, "Believeth all things": <i>faith</i> in the
	widest sense, toward God and man. "Trustfulness" [CONYBEARE
	and HOWSON].</p>
