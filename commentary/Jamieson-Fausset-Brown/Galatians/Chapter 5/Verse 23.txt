<p><b>23. temperance</b>—The <i>Greek</i>
	root implies <i>self-restraint</i> as to one's desires and lusts. 
	</p>
<p><b>against such</b>—not
	<i>persons,</i> but things, as in <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>. 
	</p>
<p><b>no law</b>—confirming <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>, "Not under the law" (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1ti+1:9">1 Timothy 1:9</span>;
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1ti+1:10">1 Timothy 1:10</span>). The law itself
	commands love (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="ga+5:14">Galatians 5:14</span>); so far
	is it from being "against such."</p>
