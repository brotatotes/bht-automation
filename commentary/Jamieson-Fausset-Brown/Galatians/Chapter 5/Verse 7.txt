<p><b>7.</b> Translate, "Ye were
	running well" in the Gospel race (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1co+9:24-26">1 Corinthians 9:24-26</span>;
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="php+3:13">Philippians 3:13</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="php+3:14">Philippians 3:14</span>).
	</p>
<p><b>who,</b> &amp;c.—none whom
	you ought to have listened to [BENGEL]:
	alluding to the Judaizers (compare <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="php+3:14- :">Philippians 3:14- :</span>). 
	</p>
<p><b>hinder</b>—The <i>Greek</i>
	means, literally, "hinder by breaking up a road." 
	</p>
<p><b>not obey the truth</b>—not
	submit yourselves to the true Gospel way of justification.</p>
