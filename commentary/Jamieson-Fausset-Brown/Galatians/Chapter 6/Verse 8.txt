<p><b>8.</b> Translate, "He that
	soweth <i>unto his own</i> flesh," with a view to fulfilling its
	desires. He does not say, "<i>His</i> spirit," as he does
	say, "His flesh." For in ourselves we are not spiritual,
	but carnal. The flesh is devoted to <i>selfishness.</i>
