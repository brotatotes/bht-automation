<p><b>9.</b> (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>). And when we do good, let us also persevere in it without
	fainting. 
	</p>
<p><b>in due season</b>—in its
	own proper season, God's own time (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>). 
	</p>
<p><b>faint not</b>—literally,
	"be relaxed." Stronger than "be not weary." <i>Weary
	of well-doing</i> refers to the will; "faint not" to
	relaxation of the powers [BENGEL].
	No one should faint, as in an earthly harvest sometimes happens.</p>
