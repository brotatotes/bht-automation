<p><b>23.</b> (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="heb+3:6">Hebrews 3:6</span>;
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="heb+3:14">Hebrews 3:14</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="heb+4:14">Hebrews 4:14</span>.)
	</p>
<p><b>profession</b>—<i>Greek,</i>
	"confession." 
	</p>
<p><b>our faith</b>—rather as
	<i>Greek,</i> "our hope"; which is indeed <i>faith</i>
	exercised as to the future inheritance. <i>Hope</i> rests on faith,
	and at the same time quickens <i>faith,</i> and is the ground of our
	bold <i>confession</i> (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1pe+3:15">1 Peter 3:15</span>).
	Hope is similarly (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="heb+10:22">Hebrews 10:22</span>)
	connected with <i>purification</i> (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="heb+10:22- :">Hebrews 10:22- :</span>). 
	</p>
<p><b>without wavering</b>—without
	declension (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="heb+3:14">Hebrews 3:14</span>),
	"steadfast unto the end." 
	</p>
<p><b>he</b>—God is faithful to
	His promises (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="heb+6:17">Hebrews 6:17</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="heb+6:18">Hebrews 6:18</span>;
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="heb+11:11">Hebrews 11:11</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="heb+12:26">Hebrews 12:26</span>;
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="heb+12:28">Hebrews 12:28</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1co+1:9">1 Corinthians 1:9</span>;
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1co+10:13">1 Corinthians 10:13</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1th+5:24">1 Thessalonians 5:24</span>;
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="2th+3:3">2 Thessalonians 3:3</span>; see also Christ's
	promise, <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="joh+12:26">John 12:26</span>); but man
	is too often unfaithful to his duties.</p>
