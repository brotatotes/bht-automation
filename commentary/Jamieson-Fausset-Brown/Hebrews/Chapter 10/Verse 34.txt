<p><b>34. ye had compassion on me in my
	bonds</b>—The oldest manuscripts and versions omit "me,"
	and read, "Ye both sympathized with <i>those in bonds</i>
	(answering to the last clause of <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>; compare <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="heb+13:3">Hebrews 13:3</span>;
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="heb+13:23">Hebrews 13:23</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="heb+6:10">Hebrews 6:10</span>),
	and accepted (so the <i>Greek</i> is translated in <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="heb+6:10- :">Hebrews 6:10- :</span>) with joy (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="jas+1:2">James 1:2</span>;
	<i>joy</i> in tribulations, as exercising faith and other graces, <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="jas+1:2- :">James 1:2- :</span>; and the pledge of the coming glory, <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="jas+1:2- :">James 1:2- :</span>) the plundering of your (own) goods (answering to the first
	clause of <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="heb+10:33">Hebrews 10:33</span>)." 
	</p>
<p><b>in yourselves</b>—The
	oldest manuscripts omit "in": translate, "knowing that
	ye have <i>for</i> (or '<i>to</i>') <i>yourselves.</i>" 
	</p>
<p><b>better</b>—a heavenly (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="heb+10:33- :">Hebrews 10:33- :</span>). 
	</p>
<p><b>enduring</b>—not liable to
	<i>spoiling.</i>
</p>
<p><b>substance</b>—possession:
	peculiarly our own, if we will not <i>cast away</i> our birthright.</p>
