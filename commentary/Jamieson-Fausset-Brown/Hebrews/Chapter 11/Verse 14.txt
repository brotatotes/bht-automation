<p><b>14. For</b>—proof that "faith"
	(<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="heb+11:13">Hebrews 11:13</span>) was their
	actuating principle. 
	</p>
<p><b>declare plainly</b>—make it
	plainly evident. 
	</p>
<p><b>seek</b>—<i>Greek,</i>
	"seek <i>after</i>"; implying the direction towards which
	their desires ever tend. 
	</p>
<p><b>a country</b>—rather as
	<i>Greek,</i> "a fatherland." In confessing themselves
	<i>strangers</i> here, they evidently imply that they regard not this
	as their home or fatherland, but seek after another and a better.</p>
