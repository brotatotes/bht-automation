<p><b>28. kept</b>—<i>Greek,</i> "<i>hath</i>
	kept," the Passover being, in Paul's day, still observed. His
	<i>faith</i> here was his belief in the invisible God's promise that
	the destroying angel should <i>pass over,</i> and not <i>touch</i>
	the inmates of the blood-sprinkled houses (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>). "He acquiesced in the bare word of God where the
	thing itself was not apparent" [CALVIN].
	</p>
<p><b>the first-born</b>—<i>Greek</i>
	neuter; <i>both of man and beast.</i></p>
