<p><b>31.</b> Rahab showed her "faith"
	in her confession, <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="jos+2:9">Joshua 2:9</span>;
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="jos+2:11">Joshua 2:11</span>, "I know that
	Jehovah hath given you the land; Jehovah your God, is God in heaven
	above, and in earth beneath." 
	</p>
<p><b>the harlot</b>—Her former
	life adds to the marvel of her repentance, faith, and preservation
	(<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="mt+21:31">Matthew 21:31</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="mt+21:32">Matthew 21:32</span>).
	</p>
<p><b>believed not</b>—<i>Greek,</i>
	"were disobedient," namely, to the will of God manifested
	by the miracles wrought in behalf of Israel (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="mt+21:32- :">Matthew 21:32- :</span>). 
	</p>
<p><b>received</b>—in her house
	(<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="jos+2:1">Joshua 2:1</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="jos+2:4">Joshua 2:4</span>;
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="jos+2:6">Joshua 2:6</span>). 
	</p>
<p><b>with peace</b>—peaceably;
	so that they had nothing to fear in her house. Thus Paul, quoting the
	same examples (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="heb+11:17">Hebrews 11:17</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="heb+11:31">Hebrews 11:31</span>)
	for the power of <i>faith,</i> as James (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="jas+2:21">James 2:21</span>;
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="jas+2:25">James 2:25</span>; see on <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="jas+2:25- :">James 2:25- :</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="jas+2:25- :">James 2:25- :</span>) does for
	justification by <i>works</i> evidentially, shows that in maintaining
	justification by faith alone, he means not a dead faith, but "faith
	which <i>worketh</i> by love" (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="jas+2:25- :">James 2:25- :</span>).</p>
