<p><span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>. EXHORTATION TO
	FOLLOW THE WITNESSES
	OF FAITH JUST
	MENTIONED: NOT
	TO FAINT IN TRIALS:
	TO REMOVE
	ALL BITTER
	ROOTS OF SIN:
	FOR WE
	ARE UNDER, NOT
	A LAW OF TERROR,
	BUT THE GOSPEL OF
	GRACE, TO DESPISE
	WHICH WILL
	BRING THE HEAVIER
	PENALTIES, IN PROPORTION
	TO OUR GREATER
	PRIVILEGES.</p>
