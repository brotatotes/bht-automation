<p><b>16. But</b>—But the sacrifice of
	praise with the lips (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="heb+13:15">Hebrews 13:15</span>)
	is not enough; there must be also <i>doing good</i> (beneficence) and
	communicating (that is, imparting a share of your means, <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="heb+13:15- :">Hebrews 13:15- :</span>) to the needy. 
	</p>
<p><b>with such</b>—and not mere
	ritualistic sacrifices.</p>
