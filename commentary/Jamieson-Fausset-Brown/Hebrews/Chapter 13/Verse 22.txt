<p><b>22. suffer the word</b>—The
	Hebrews not being the section of the Church assigned to Paul (but the
	Gentiles), he uses gentle entreaty, rather than authoritative
	command. 
	</p>
<p><b>few words</b>—compared with
	what might be said on so important a subject. <i>Few,</i> in an
	Epistle which is more of a <i>treatise</i> than an Epistle (compare
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1pe+5:12">1 Peter 5:12</span>). On the seeming
	inconsistency with <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="ga+6:11">Galatians 6:11</span>,
	compare <i>Note,</i> see on <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="ga+6:11- :">Galatians 6:11- :</span>.</p>
