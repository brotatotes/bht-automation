<p><b>24. all</b>—The Scriptures are
	intended for <i>all,</i> young and old, not merely for ministers.
	Compare the different classes addressed, "wives," <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>; little children, <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>; "all," <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1pe+3:8">1 Peter 3:8</span>;
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1pe+5:5">1 Peter 5:5</span>. He says here "all,"
	for the Hebrews whom he addresses were not all in one place, though
	the Jerusalem Hebrews are chiefly addressed. 
	</p>
<p><b>They of Italy</b>—not
	merely the brethren at Rome, but of other places in Italy.</p>
