<p><span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>. DANGER OF
	NEGLECTING SO
	GREAT SALVATION,
	FIRST SPOKEN
	BY CHRIST; TO WHOM,
	NOT TO ANGELS,
	THE NEW
	DISPENSATION WAS
	SUBJECTED; THOUGH HE
	WAS FOR A TIME
	HUMBLED BELOW THE ANGELS:

	TOOK PLACE
