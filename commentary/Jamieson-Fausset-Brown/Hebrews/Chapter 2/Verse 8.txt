<p><b>8.</b> (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>.) 
	</p>
<p><b>For in that</b>—that is,
	"For in that" <i>God saith in the eighth Psalm,</i> "He
	put the all things (so the <i>Greek, the</i> all things just
	mentioned) in subjection under him (man), He left nothing . . . As no
	limitation occurs in the sacred writing, the "all things"
	must include heavenly, as well as earthly things (compare <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1co+3:21">1 Corinthians 3:21</span>;
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1co+3:22">1 Corinthians 3:22</span>). 
	</p>
<p><b>But now</b>—As things now
	are, we see not yet <i>the</i> all things put under man.</p>
