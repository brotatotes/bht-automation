<p><b>14. For,</b> c.—enforcing the
	warning, <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="heb+3:12">Hebrews 3:12</span>. 
	</p>
<p><b>partakers of Christ</b>—(Compare
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="heb+3:1">Hebrews 3:1</span> <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="heb+3:6">Hebrews 3:6</span>).
	So "partakers of the Holy Ghost" (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="heb+3:6- :">Hebrews 3:6- :</span>). 
	</p>
<p><b>hold</b>—<i>Greek,</i>
	"hold fast." 
	</p>
<p><b>the beginning of our
	confidence</b>—that is, the confidence (literally, <i>substantial,
	solid confidence</i>) of faith which we have begun (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="heb+6:11">Hebrews 6:11</span>;
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="heb+12:2">Hebrews 12:2</span>). A Christian so long as
	he is not <i>made perfect,</i> considers himself as a <i>beginner</i>
	[BENGEL]. 
	</p>
<p><b>unto the end</b>—unto the
	coming of Christ (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="heb+12:2">Hebrews 12:2</span>).</p>
