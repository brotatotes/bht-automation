<p><b>5. faithful in all his house</b>—that
	is in all GOD'S house (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>). 
	</p>
<p><b>servant</b>—not here the
	<i>Greek</i> for "slave," but "a ministering
	attendant"; marking the high office of Moses towards God, though
	inferior to Christ, a kind of <i>steward.</i>
</p>
<p><b>for a testimony of,</b> c.—in
	order that he might in his typical institutions give "testimony"
	to Israel "of the things" of the Gospel "which were to
	be spoken afterwards" by Christ (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="heb+8:5">Hebrews 8:5</span>
<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="heb+9:8">Hebrews 9:8</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="heb+9:23">Hebrews 9:23</span>;
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="heb+10:1">Hebrews 10:1</span>).</p>
