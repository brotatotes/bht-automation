<p><b>8.</b> Answer to the objection which
	might be made to his reasoning, namely, that those brought into
	Canaan by Joshua (so "Jesus" here means, as in <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>) did enter the <i>rest</i> of God. If the rest of God meant
	Canaan, God would not after their entrance into that land, have
	spoken (or speak [ALFORD])
	of another (future) day of entering the rest.</p>
