<p><b>10.</b> <i>Greek,</i> rather,
	"<i>Addressed</i> by God (by the appellation) High Priest."
	Being formally recognized by God as High Priest at the time of His
	being "made perfect" (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>). He was High Priest already <i>in the purpose of God</i>
	before His passion; but after it, when perfected, He was formally
	addressed so.</p>
