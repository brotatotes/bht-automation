<p><b>9. made perfect</b>—completed,
	brought to His goal of learning and suffering through death (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>) [ALFORD],
	namely, at His glorious resurrection and ascension. 
	</p>
<p><b>author</b>—<i>Greek,</i>
	"cause." 
	</p>
<p><b>eternal salvation</b>—obtained
	for us in the <i>short</i> "days of Jesus' flesh" (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>; compare <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="heb+5:6">Hebrews 5:6</span>, "for
	ever," <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="isa+45:17">Isaiah 45:17</span>). 
	</p>
<p><b>unto all . . . that obey
	him</b>—As Christ <i>obeyed</i> the Father, so must we <i>obey</i>
	Him by faith.</p>
