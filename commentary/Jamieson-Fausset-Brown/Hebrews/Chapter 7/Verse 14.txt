<p><b>14. evident</b>—literally,
	"manifest before the eyes" as a thing indisputable; a proof
	that whatever difficulties may now appear, <i>then</i> Jesus Christ's
	genealogy labored under none. 
	</p>
<p><b>our Lord</b>—the only place
	where this now common title occurs without "Jesus," or
	"Christ," except <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>. 
	</p>
<p><b>sprang</b>—as a plant, and
	a branch. 
	</p>
