<p><b>18. there is</b>—<i>Greek,</i>
	"there takes place," according to <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>. 
	</p>
<p><b>disannuling</b>—a
	repealing. 
	</p>
<p><b>of the commandment</b>—ordaining
	the Levitical priesthood. And, as the Levitical priesthood and the
	law are inseparably joined, since the former is repealed, the latter
	is so also (see on <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="heb+7:11">Hebrews 7:11</span>). 
	</p>
<p><b>going before</b>—the legal
	ordinance introducing and giving place to the Christian, the
	antitypical and permanent end of the former. 
	</p>
<p><b>weakness and
	unprofitableness</b>—The opposite of "power" (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="heb+7:11- :">Hebrews 7:11- :</span>).</p>
