<p><b>21.</b> Translate in the <i>Greek</i>
	order, "For they indeed (the existing legal priests) without the
	(solemn) <i>promise</i> on oath (so the <i>Greek</i> [TITTMANN])
	are made priests." 
	</p>
<p><b>by him</b>—God. 
	</p>
<p><b>unto him</b>—the Lord, the
	Son of God (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="ps+110:1">Psalms 110:1</span>). 
	</p>
<p><b>not repent</b>—never change
	His purpose. 
	</p>
<p><b>after the order of
	Melchisedec</b>—omitted in some oldest manuscripts, contained in
	others.</p>
