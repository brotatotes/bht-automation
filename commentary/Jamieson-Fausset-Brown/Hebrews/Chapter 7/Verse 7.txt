<p><b>7.</b> The principle that the
	blesser is superior to him whom he blesses, holds good only in a
	blessing given with divine authority; not merely a prayerful wish,
	but one that is divinely efficient in working its purport, as that of
	the patriarchs on their children: so Christ's blessing, <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="lu+24:51">Luke 24:51</span>;
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="ac+3:26">Acts 3:26</span>.</p>
