<p><b>10. make with</b>—<i>Greek,</i>
	"make <i>unto.</i>" 
	</p>
<p><b>Israel</b>—comprising the
	before disunited (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="heb+8:8">Hebrews 8:8</span>) ten
	tribes' kingdom, and that of Judah. They are united in the spiritual
	Israel, the elect Church, now: they shall be so in the literal
	restored kingdom of Israel to come. 
	</p>
<p><b>I will put</b>—literally,
	"(I) giving." This is the first of the "better
	promises" (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="heb+8:6">Hebrews 8:6</span>). 
	</p>
<p><b>mind</b>—their intelligent
	faculty. 
	</p>
<p><b>in,</b> c.—rather, "
	ON their hearts." Not
	on tables of stone as the law (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="heb+8:6- :">Hebrews 8:6- :</span>). 
	</p>
<p><b>write</b>—<i>Greek,</i>
	"inscribe." 
	</p>
<p><b>and I will be to them a God,</b>
	&amp;c.—fulfilled first in the outward kingdom of God. Next, in the
	inward Gospel kingdom. Thirdly, in the kingdom at once outward and
	inward, the spiritual being manifested outwardly (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="heb+8:6- :">Hebrews 8:6- :</span>). Compare a similar progression as to the priesthood (1) <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="heb+8:6- :">Hebrews 8:6- :</span> (2) <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1pe+2:5">1 Peter 2:5</span>; (3)
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="isa+61:6">Isaiah 61:6</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="re+1:6">Revelation 1:6</span>.
	This progressive advance of the significance of the Old Testament
	institutions, &amp;c., says THOLUCK,
	shows the <i>transparency</i> and prophetic character which runs
	throughout the whole.</p>
