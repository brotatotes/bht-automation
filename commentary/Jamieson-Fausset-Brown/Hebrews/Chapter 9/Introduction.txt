<p><span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>. INFERIORITY OF THE
	OLD TO THE NEW
	COVENANT IN THE MEANS
	OF ACCESS TO GOD:
	THE BLOOD
	OF BULLS AND GOATS
	OF NO REAL
	AVAIL: THE
	BLOOD OF CHRIST
	ALL-SUFFICIENT TO PURGE
	AWAY SIN,
	WHENCE FLOWS
	OUR HOPE
	OF HIS APPEARING
	AGAIN FOR OUR
	PERFECT SALVATION.</p>
