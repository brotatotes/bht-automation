<p><b>15.</b> The <i>Greek</i> is, "<i>But</i>
	if," &amp;c.: the "But" taking up the argument against
	such a one as "said he had faith, and yet had not works,"
	which are its fruits. 
	</p>
<p><b>a brother,</b> &amp;c.—a
	<i>fellow Christian,</i> to whom we are specially bound to give help,
	independent of our general obligation to help all our fellow
	creatures. 
	</p>
<p><b>be</b>—The <i>Greek</i>
	implies, "<i>be found,</i> on your access to them."</p>
