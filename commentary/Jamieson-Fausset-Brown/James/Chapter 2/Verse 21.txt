21. Abraham . . . justified by
	works—evidentially, and before men (see on  :-). In James 2:23, James,
	like Paul, recognizes the Scripture truth, that it was his faith
	that was counted to Abraham for righteousness in his justification
	before God. 
	when he had offered—rather,
	"when he offered" [ALFORD],
	that is, brought as an offering at the altar; not implying that he
	actually offered him.
