<p><b>9.</b> <i>Respect of persons</i>
	violates the command to <i>love all alike</i> "as thyself."
	</p>
<p><b>ye commit sin</b>—literally,
	"ye work sin," <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="mt+7:23">Matthew 7:23</span>,
	to which the reference here is probably, as in <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="mt+7:23- :">Matthew 7:23- :</span>. Your <i>works</i> are sin, whatever boast of the law ye
	make in words (see on <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="jas+2:8">James 2:8</span>). 
	</p>
<p><b>convinced</b>—<i>Old</i>
	English for "convicted." 
	</p>
<p><b>as transgressors</b>—not
	merely of this or that particular command, but of the whole
	absolutely.</p>
