13. Who—(Compare Psalms 34:12;
	Psalms 34:13). All wish to appear
	"wise": few are so. 
	show—"by works,"
	and not merely by profession, referring to Psalms 34:13- :. 
	out of a good conversation
	his works—by general "good conduct" manifested
	in particular "works." "Wisdom" and
	"knowledge," without these being "shown," are as
	dead as faith would be without works [ALFORD].
	with meekness of wisdom—with
	the meekness inseparable from true "wisdom."
