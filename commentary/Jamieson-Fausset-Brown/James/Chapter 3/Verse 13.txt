<p><b>13. Who</b>—(Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="ps+34:12">Psalms 34:12</span>;
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="ps+34:13">Psalms 34:13</span>). All wish to appear
	"wise": few are so. 
	</p>
<p><b>show</b>—"by works,"
	and not merely by profession, referring to <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="ps+34:13- :">Psalms 34:13- :</span>. 
	</p>
<p><b>out of a good conversation
	his works</b>—by <i>general</i> "good conduct" manifested
	in <i>particular</i> "works." "Wisdom" and
	"knowledge," without these being "shown," are as
	dead as faith would be without works [ALFORD].
	</p>
<p><b>with meekness of wisdom</b>—with
	the meekness inseparable from true "wisdom."</p>
