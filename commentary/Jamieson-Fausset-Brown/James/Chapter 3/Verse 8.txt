<p><b>8. no man</b>—literally, "no
	one of men": neither can a man control his neighbor's, nor even
	his own tongue. Hence the truth of <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span> appears. 
	</p>
<p><b>unruly evil</b>—The <i>Greek,</i>
	implies that it is at once <i>restless</i> and <i>incapable of
	restraint.</i> Nay, though nature has hedged it in with a double
	barrier of the lips and teeth, it bursts from its barriers to assail
	and ruin men [ESTIUS]. 
	</p>
<p><b>deadly</b>—literally,
	"death-bearing."</p>
