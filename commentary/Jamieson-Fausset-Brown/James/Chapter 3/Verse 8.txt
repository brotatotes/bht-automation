	unruly evil—The Greek,
	implies that it is at once restless and incapable of
	restraint. Nay, though nature has hedged it in with a double
	barrier of the lips and teeth, it bursts from its barriers to assail
	and ruin men [ESTIUS]. 
	deadly—literally,
