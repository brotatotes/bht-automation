<p><b>13. afflicted</b>—referring to the
	"suffering affliction" (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>). 
	</p>
<p><b>let him pray</b>—not
	"swear" in rash impatience. 
	</p>
<p><b>merry</b>—joyous in mind. 
	</p>
<p><b>sing psalms</b>—of praise.
	Paul and Silas sang psalms even in affliction.</p>
