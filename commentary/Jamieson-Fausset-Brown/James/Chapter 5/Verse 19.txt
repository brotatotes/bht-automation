<p><b>19.</b> The blessing of reclaiming
	an erring sinner by the mutual consent and intercessory prayer just
	recommended. 
	</p>
<p><b>do err</b>—more literally,
	"be led astray." 
	</p>
<p><b>the truth</b>—the Gospel
	doctrine and precepts. 
	</p>
<p><b>one</b>—literally, "any";
	as "<i>any</i>" before. <i>Everyone</i> ought to seek the
	salvation of <i>everyone</i> [BENGEL].</p>
