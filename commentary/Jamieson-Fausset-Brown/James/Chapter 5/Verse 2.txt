<p><b>2. corrupted</b>—<i>about to be
	destroyed</i> through God's curse on your oppression, whereby your
	riches are accumulated (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="jas+5:4">James 5:4</span>).
	CALVIN thinks the sense
	is, Your riches perish without being of any use either to others or
	even to yourselves, for instance, your garments which are moth-eaten
	in your chests. 
	</p>
<p><b>garments . . .
	moth-eaten</b>—referring to <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="mt+6:19">Matthew 6:19</span>;
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="mt+6:20">Matthew 6:20</span>.</p>
