<p><b>39. Come and see</b>—His <i>second
	utterance,</i> more winning still. 
	</p>
<p><b>tenth hour</b>—not ten A.M.
	(as some), according to <i>Roman,</i> but four P.M.,
	according to <i>Jewish</i> reckoning, which John follows. The hour is
	mentioned to show why they stayed out the day with him—because
	little of it remained.</p>
