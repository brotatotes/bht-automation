<p><b>46. any good out of
	Nazareth</b>—remembering Bethlehem, perhaps, as Messiah's predicted
	birthplace, and Nazareth having no <i>express</i> prophetic place at
	all, besides being in no repute. The question sprang from mere dread
	of mistake in a matter so vital.
