<p><b>10. I am come that they might have
	life, and . . . more abundantly</b>—not merely to <i>preserve</i>
	but <i>impart</i> LIFE,
	and communicate it in rich and unfailing exuberance. What a claim!
	Yet it is only an echo of all His teaching; and He who uttered these
	and like words must be either a blasphemer, all worthy of the death
	He died, or "God with us"—there can be no middle course.</p>
