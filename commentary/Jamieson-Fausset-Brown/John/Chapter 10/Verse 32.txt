32. Many good works have I showed
	you—that is, works of pure benevolence (as in  :-, "Who went about doing good," c. see  :-). 
	from my Father—not so
	much by His power, but as directly commissioned by Him to do them.
	This He says to meet the imputation of unwarrantable assumption of
	the divine prerogatives [LUTHARDT].
	for which of those works do
	ye stone me?—"are ye stoning (that is, going to stone)
	me?"
