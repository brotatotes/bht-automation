<p><b>9. by me if any man enter in</b>—whether
	shepherd or sheep. 
	</p>
<p><b>shall be saved</b>—the
	great object of the pastoral office, as of all the divine
	arrangements towards mankind. 
	</p>
<p><b>and shall go in and out and
	find pasture</b>—<i>in,</i> as to a place of <i>safety</i> and
	<i>repose; out,</i> as to "green pastures and still waters"
	(<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="ps+23:2">Psalms 23:2</span>) for nourishment and
	refreshing, and all this only transferred to another clime, and
	enjoyed in another manner, at the close of this earthly scene (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="ps+23:2- :">Psalms 23:2- :</span>).</p>
