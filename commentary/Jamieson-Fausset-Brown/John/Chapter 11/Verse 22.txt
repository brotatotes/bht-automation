<p><b>22. But I know that even now,</b>
	&amp;c.—Energetic characters are usually sanguine, the rainbow of
	hope peering through the drenching cloud. 
	</p>
<p><b>whatsoever thou wilt ask of
	God, God will give it thee</b>—that is "even to the
	restoration of my dead brother to life," for that plainly is her
	meaning, as the sequel shows.</p>
