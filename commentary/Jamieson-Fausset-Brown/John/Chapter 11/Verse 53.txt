<p><b>53. they took council together to
	put him to death</b>—Caiaphas but expressed what the party was
	secretly wishing, but afraid to propose. 
	</p>
<p><b>Jesus . . . walked no more
	openly among the Jews</b>—How could He, unless He had wished to die
	before His time? 
	</p>
<p><b>near to the wilderness</b>—of
	Judea. 
	</p>
<p><b>a city called Ephraim</b>—between
	Jerusalem and Jericho.</p>
