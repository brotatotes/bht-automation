<p><b>44-50. Jesus cried</b>—in a loud
	tone, and with peculiar solemnity. (Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>). 
	</p>
<p><b>and said, He that believeth
	on me,</b> &amp;c.—This seems to be a supplementary record of some
	weighty proclamations, for which there had been found no natural
	place before, and introduced here as a sort of <i>summary and winding
	up</i> of His whole testimony.</p>
