<p><b>23-26. there was leaning on Jesus'
	bosom one of his disciples, whom Jesus loved</b>—Thus modestly does
	our Evangelist denote himself, as reclining next to Jesus at the
	table. 
	</p>
<p><b>Peter . . . beckoned to him
	to ask who it should be of whom he spake</b>—reclining probably at
	the corresponding place on the other side of Jesus.</p>
