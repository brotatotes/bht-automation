<p><b>35. By this shall all men know that
	ye are my disciples</b>—the disciples of Him who laid down His life
	for those He loved. 
	</p>
<p><b>if ye have love one to
	another</b>—for My sake, and as one in Me; for to <i>such</i> love
	men outside the circle of believers know right well they are entire
	strangers. Alas, how little of it there is even within this circle!</p>
