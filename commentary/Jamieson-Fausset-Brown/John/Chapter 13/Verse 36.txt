<p><b>36-38. Peter said</b>—seeing
	plainly in these directions how to behave themselves, that He was
	indeed going from them. 
	</p>
<p><b>Lord, whither guest
	thou?</b>—having hardly a glimmer of the real truth. 
	</p>
<p><b>Jesus answered, . . . thou
	canst not follow me now, but thou shalt follow me afterwards</b>—How
	different from what He said to the Jews: "Whither I go <i>ye
	cannot come</i>" (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="joh+8:21">John 8:21</span>).</p>
