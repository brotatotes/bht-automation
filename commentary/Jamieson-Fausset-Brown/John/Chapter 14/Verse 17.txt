<p><b>17. whom the world cannot receive,</b>

<p><b>he dwelleth with you, and
	shall be in you</b>—Though the proper fulness of both these was yet
	future, our Lord, by using both the present and the future, seems
	plainly to say that they <i>already</i> had the germ of this great
	blessing.</p>
