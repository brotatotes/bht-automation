<p><b>6. If a man abide not in me, he is
	cast forth as a branch . . . withered . . . cast into the fire . . .
	burned</b>—The one proper use of the vine is to <i>bear fruit;</i>
	failing this, it is good for one other thing—<i>fuel.</i> (See <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>). How awfully striking the figure, in this view of it!</p>
