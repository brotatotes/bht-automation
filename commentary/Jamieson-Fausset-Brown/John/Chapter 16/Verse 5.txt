<p><b>5. But now I go my way to him that
	sent me</b>—While He was with them, the world's hatred was directed
	chiefly against Himself; but His departure would bring it down upon
	them as His representatives. 
	</p>
<p><b>and none of you asketh me,
	Whither goest thou?</b>—They <i>had</i> done so in a sort (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="joh+13:36">John 13:36</span>;
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="joh+14:5">John 14:5</span>); but He wished more
	intelligent and eager inquiry on the subject.</p>
