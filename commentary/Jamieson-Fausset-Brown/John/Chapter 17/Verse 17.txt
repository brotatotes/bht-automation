<p><b>17. Sanctify them</b>—As the
	former prayer, "<i>Keep</i> them," was "negative,"
	asking <i>protection</i> for them from the poisonous element which
	surrounded and pressed upon their renewed nature, so this prayer,
	"<i>Sanctify</i> them," is positive, asking the <i>advancement
	and completion</i> of their begun sanctification. 
	</p>
<p><b>through</b>—in. 
	</p>
<p><b>thy truth</b>—God's
	revealed truth, as the medium or element of sanctification; a
	statement this of immense importance. 
	</p>

	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="eph+1:13">Ephesians 1:13</span>).</p>
