<p><b>18. As thou hast sent</b>—sentest.
	</p>
<p><b>me into the world, even so
	have I also sent them</b>—sent I also them. 
	</p>
<p><b>into the world</b>—As their
	mission was to carry into effect the purposes of their Master's
	mission, so our Lord speaks of the <i>authority</i> in both cases as
	<i>co-ordinate.</i></p>
