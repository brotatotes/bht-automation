<p><b>15-18. Simon Peter followed
	Jesus</b>—Natural though this was, and safe enough, had he only
	"watched and prayed that he enter not into temptation," as
	his Master bade him (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="mt+26:41">Matthew 26:41</span>),
	it was, in his case, a fatal step. 
	</p>
<p><b>and . . . another
	disciple</b>—Rather, "the other disciple"—our
	Evangelist himself, no doubt. 
	</p>
<p><b>known unto the high
	priest</b>—(See on <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="joh+18:1">John 18:1</span>).
	</p>
<p><b>went in with Jesus into the
	palace of the high priest.</b></p>
