<p><b>7. Then asked he them again, Whom
	seek ye?</b>—Giving them a door of escape from the guilt of a deed
	which <i>now</i> they were able in some measure to understand. 
	</p>
<p><b>Jesus of Nazareth</b>—The
	stunning effect of His first answer wearing off, they think only of
	the necessity of executing their orders.</p>
