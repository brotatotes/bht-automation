<p><b>17. And he bearing his cross</b>—(See
	on <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>). 
	</p>
<p><b>went forth</b>—Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>, "without the camp"; "without the gate."
	On arriving at the place, "they gave Him vinegar to drink
	mingled with gall [wine mingled with myrrh, <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>], and when He had tasted thereof, He would not drink"
	(<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="mt+27:34">Matthew 27:34</span>). This potion was
	stupefying, and given to criminals just before execution, to deaden
	the sense of pain. 
	</p>
<p align="CENTER"> Fill high the bowl,
	and spice it well, and pour 
	</p>
<p align="CENTER"> The dews oblivious: for
	the Cross is sharp, 
	</p>
<p align="CENTER"> The Cross is sharp, and
	He 
	</p>
<p align="CENTER"> Is tenderer than a lamb. 
	</p>
