<p><b>2. the soldiers platted a crown of
	thorns, and put it on his head</b>—in mockery of a regal <i>crown.</i>
</p>
<p><b>and they put on him a purple
	robe</b>—in mockery of the <i>imperial purple;</i> first "stripping
	him" (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="mt+27:28">Matthew 27:28</span>) of His
	own outer garment. The robe may have been the "gorgeous"
	one in which Herod arrayed and sent Him back to Pilate (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="mt+27:28- :">Matthew 27:28- :</span>). "And they put a reed into His right hand" (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="mt+27:28- :">Matthew 27:28- :</span>) —in mockery of the regal <i>scepter.</i> "And they
	bowed the knee before Him" (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="mt+27:28- :">Matthew 27:28- :</span>).</p>
