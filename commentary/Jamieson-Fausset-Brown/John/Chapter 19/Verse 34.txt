<p><b>34. But one of the soldiers</b>—to
	make assurance of the fact doubly sure. 
	</p>
<p><b>with a spear pierced his
	side</b>—making a wound deep and wide, as indeed is plain from
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="joh+20:27">John 20:27</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="joh+20:29">John 20:29</span>.
	Had life still remained, it must have fled now. 
	</p>
<p><b>and forthwith came thereout
	blood and water</b>—"It is now well known that the effect of
	long-continued and intense agony is frequently to produce a secretion
	of a colorless lymph within the pericardium (the membrane enveloping
	the heart), amounting in many cases to a very considerable quantity"
	[WEBSTER and WILKINSON].</p>
