<p><b>4, 5. Woman</b>—no term of
	disrespect in the language of that day (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>). 
	</p>
<p><b>what . . . to do with
	thee</b>—that is, "In my Father's business I have to do with
	Him only." It was a gentle rebuke for <i>officious interference,</i>
	entering a region from which all creatures were excluded (compare
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="ac+4:19">Acts 4:19</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="ac+4:20">Acts 4:20</span>).
	</p>
<p><b>mine hour,</b> c.—hinting
	that He <i>would</i> do something, but at His own time and so she
	understood it (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="joh+2:5">John 2:5</span>).</p>
