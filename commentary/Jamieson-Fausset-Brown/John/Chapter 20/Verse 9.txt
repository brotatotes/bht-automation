<p><b>9. For as yet they knew</b>—that
	is, understood. 
	</p>
<p><b>not the scripture that he
	must rise again from the dead</b>—In other words, they believed in
	His resurrection at first, not because they were prepared by
	Scripture to expect it; but <i>facts</i> carried resistless
	conviction of it in the first instance to their minds, and furnished
	a key to the Scripture predictions of it.</p>
