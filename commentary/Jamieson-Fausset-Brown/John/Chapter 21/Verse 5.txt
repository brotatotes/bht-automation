<p><b>5. Children</b>—This term would
	not necessarily identify Him, being not unusual from any superior;
	but when they did recognize Him, they would feel it sweetly like
	Himself. 
	</p>
<p><b>have ye any meat?</b>—provisions,
	supplies, meaning <i>fish.</i>
</p>
<p><b>They answered . . . No</b>—This
	was in His wonted style, making them <i>tell</i> their case, and so
	the better prepare them for what was coming.</p>
