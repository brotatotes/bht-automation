<p><b>12. earthly things</b>—such as
	<i>regeneration,</i> the gate of entrance to the kingdom of God <i>on
	earth,</i> and which Nicodemus should have understood better, as a
	truth even of that more <i>earthly</i> economy to which he belonged. 
	</p>
<p><b>heavenly things</b>—the
	things of the new and more heavenly evangelical economy, only to be
	fully understood after the effusion of the Spirit from heaven through
	the exalted Saviour.</p>
