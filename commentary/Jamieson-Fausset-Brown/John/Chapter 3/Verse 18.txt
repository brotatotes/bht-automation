<p><b>18. is not condemned</b>—Having,
	immediately on his believing, "passed from death unto life"
	(<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="joh+5:24">John 5:24</span>). 
	</p>
<p><b>condemned already</b>—Rejecting
	the one way of deliverance from that "condemnation" which
	God gave His Son to <i>remove,</i> and so wilfully <i>remaining</i>
	condemned.</p>
