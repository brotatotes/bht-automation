<p><b>7. Marvel not,</b> c.—If a
	spiritual nature only can see and enter the kingdom of God if all we
	bring into the world with us be the reverse of spiritual; and if this
	spirituality be solely of the Holy Ghost, no wonder a new birth is
	indispensable. 
	</p>
<p><b>Ye must</b>—"<i>Ye,</i>
	says Jesus, not <i>we</i>" [BENGEL].
	After those universal propositions, about what "<i>a man</i>"
	must be, to "enter the kingdom of God" (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>) —this is remarkable, showing that our Lord meant to hold
	Himself forth as "<i>separate from sinners.</i>"</p>
