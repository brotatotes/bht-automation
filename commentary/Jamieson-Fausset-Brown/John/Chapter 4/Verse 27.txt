<p><b>27. marvelled that he talked with
	the woman</b>—It never probably occurred to them to marvel that He
	talked with <i>themselves;</i> yet in His eye, as the sequel shows,
	He was quite as nobly employed. How poor, if not false, are many of
	our most plausible estimates! 
	</p>
<p><b>no man said . . . What? . . .
	Why?</b>—awed by the spectacle, and thinking there must be
	something under it.</p>
