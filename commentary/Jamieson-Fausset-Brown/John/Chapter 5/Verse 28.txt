<p><b>28. Marvel not at this</b>—this
	committal of all judgment to <i>the Son of man.</i>
</p>
<p><b>for the hour is coming</b>—He
	adds not in this case (as in <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>), "and now is," because this was not to be till
	the close of the whole dispensation of mercy.</p>
