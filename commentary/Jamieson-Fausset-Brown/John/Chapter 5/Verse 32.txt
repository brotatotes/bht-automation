<p><b>32. There is another</b>—that is,
	<i>the Father,</i> as is plain from the connection. How brightly the
	distinction of the Persons shines out here! 
	</p>
<p><b>and I know that the witness,</b>
	c.—"This is the Son's testimony to the Father's truth (see
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="joh+7:28">John 7:28</span> <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="joh+8:26">John 8:26</span>;
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="joh+8:55">John 8:55</span>). It testifies to the
	full consciousness on the part of the Son, even in the days of His
	humiliation, of the righteousness of the Father" [ALFORD].
	And thus He cheered His spirit under the cloud of human opposition
	which was already gathering over His head.</p>
