<p><b>36-38. I have greater
	witness</b>—rather, "The witness which I have is greater."
	</p>
<p><b>the works . . . bear witness
	of me</b>—not simply as <i>miracles</i> nor even as a miracle of
	<i>mercy,</i> but these miracles, <i>as He did them,</i> with a <i>will</i>
	and a <i>power,</i> a <i>majesty</i> and a <i>grace</i> manifestly
	<i>His own.</i></p>
