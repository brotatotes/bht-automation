45. Do not think I will accuse you
	to the Father—"My errand hither is not to collect evidence
	to condemn you at God's bar." 
	one that
	accuseth you, even Moses, &c.—"Alas!
	that will be too well done by another, and him the object of all your
	religious boastings—Moses," here put for "the Law,"
	the basis of the Old Testament Scriptures.
