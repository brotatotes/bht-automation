<p><b>45. Do not think I will accuse you
	to the Father</b>—"My errand hither is not to collect evidence
	to condemn you at God's bar." 
	</p>
<p><i><b>one</b></i><b> that
	accuseth you, </b><i><b>even</b></i><b> Moses,</b> &amp;c.—"Alas!
	that will be too well done by another, and him the object of all your
	religious boastings—Moses," here put for "<i>the Law,</i>"
	the basis of the Old Testament Scriptures.</p>
