<p><b>27. which the Son of man</b>—taking
	that title of Himself which denoted His incarnate life. 
	</p>
<p><b>shall give unto you</b>—in
	the sense of <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="joh+6:51">John 6:51</span>. 
	</p>
<p><b>him hath God the Father
	sealed</b>—marked out and authenticated for that transcendent
	office, to impart to the world the bread of an everlasting life, and
	this in the character of "the Son of <i>man.</i>"</p>
