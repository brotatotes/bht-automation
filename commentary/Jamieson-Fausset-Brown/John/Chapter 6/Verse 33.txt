<p><b>33. For the bread of God is he,</b>
	c.—This verse is perhaps best left in its own transparent
	grandeur—holding up the Bread Itself as <i>divine, spiritual,</i>
	and <i>eternal</i> its ordained Fountain and essential Substance,
	"<i>Him who came down from heaven to give it</i>" (that
	Eternal Life which was with the Father and was manifested unto us,
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1jo+1:2">1 John 1:2</span>); and its designed
	objects, "<i>the world.</i>"</p>
