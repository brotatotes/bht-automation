<p><b>44. can come to me</b>—in the
	sense of <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="joh+6:35">John 6:35</span>. 
	</p>
<p><b>except the Father which hath
	sent me</b>—that is, the Father <i>as the Sender of Me</i> and <i>to
	carry out the design of My mission.</i>
</p>

	</p>
