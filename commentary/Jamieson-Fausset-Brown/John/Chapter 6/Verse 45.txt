<p><b>45. written in the prophets</b>—in
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="isa+54:13">Isaiah 54:13</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="jer+31:33">Jeremiah 31:33</span>;
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="jer+31:34">Jeremiah 31:34</span>; other similar passages
	may also have been in view. Our Lord thus falls back upon Scripture
	authority for this seemingly hard saying. 
	</p>
<p><b>all taught of God</b>—not
	by <i>external</i> revelation merely, but by <i>internal
	illumination,</i> corresponding to the "drawing" of <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="jer+31:34- :">Jeremiah 31:34- :</span>. 
	</p>
<p><b>Every man therefore,</b>
	c.—that is, who hath been thus efficaciously taught of Him. 
	</p>
<p><b>cometh unto me</b>—<i>with
	absolute certainty,</i> yet in the sense above given of "drawing"
	that is, "As none can come to Me but as divinely drawn, so none
	thus drawn shall fail to come."</p>
