<p><b>65. Therefore said I,</b> &amp;c.—that
	is, "That was why I spoke to you of the necessity of divine
	teaching which some of you are strangers to." 
	</p>
<p><b>except it were given
	him</b>—plainly showing that by the Father's "drawing"
	(<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="joh+6:44">John 6:44</span>) was meant an
	<i>internal</i> and <i>efficacious</i> operation, for in recalling
	the statement here He says, it must be "<i>given</i> to a man to
	come" to Christ.</p>
