<p><b>1, 2. After these things</b>—that
	is, <i>all that is recorded after</i> <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>. 
	</p>
<p><b>walked in Galilee</b>—continuing
	His labors there, instead of going to Judea, as might have been
	expected. 
	</p>
<p><b>sought to kill him</b>—referring
	back to <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="joh+5:18">John 5:18</span>. <i>Hence it
	appears that our Lord did not attend the Passover mentioned in</i>
<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="joh+6:4">John 6:4</span> —being the <i>third</i>
	since His ministry began, if the feast mentioned in <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="joh+6:4- :">John 6:4- :</span> was a Passover.</p>
