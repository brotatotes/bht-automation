<p><b>12. much murmuring</b>—buzzing. 
	</p>
<p><b>among the people</b>—the
	multitudes; the natural expression of a Jewish writer, indicating
	without design the crowded state of Jerusalem at this festival
	[WEBSTER and WILKINSON].
	</p>
<p><b>a good man . . . Nay . . .
	deceiveth the people</b>—the two opposite views of His claims, that
	they were <i>honest,</i> and that they were an <i>imposture.</i></p>
