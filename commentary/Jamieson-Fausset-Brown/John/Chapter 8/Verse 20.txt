<p><b>20. These words spake Jesus in the
	treasury</b>—a division, so called, of the fore court of the
	temple, part of the court of the women [JOSEPHUS,
	<i>Antiquities,</i> 19.6.2, &amp;c.], which may confirm the
	genuineness of <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="joh+8:2-11">John 8:2-11</span>,
	as the place where the woman was brought. 
	</p>
<p><b>no man laid hands on him,</b>
	&amp;c.—(See on <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="joh+7:30">John 7:30</span>). In
	the dialogue that follows, the conflict waxes sharper on both sides,
	till rising to its climax, they take up stones to stone him.</p>
