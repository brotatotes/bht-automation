<p><b>57-59. Then said the Jews unto him,
	Thou art not yet fifty years old</b>—"No inference can be
	drawn from this as to the age of our Lord at the time as man. Fifty
	years was with the Jews the completion of manhood" [ALFORD].
	</p>
<p><b>and hast thou seen
	Abraham?</b>—He had said Abraham saw <i>Him,</i> as being his
	peculiar privilege. They give the opposite turn to it—"Hast
	Thou seen <i>Abraham?</i>" as an honor too great for Him to
	pretend to.</p>
