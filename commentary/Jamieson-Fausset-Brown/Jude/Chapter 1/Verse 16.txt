<p style=" "><b>murmurers</b>
	— in secret: <i>muttering
	murmurs</i>
	against God’s ordinances and ministers in Church and state. Compare
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>,
	“speak evil of dignities”; <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>,

<p style=" "><b>complainers</b>
	— never satisfied with their lot (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>;
	compare the penalty, <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>,
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>).</p>
<p style=" "><b>walking
	after their own lusts</b>
	— (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>).
	The secret of their <i>murmuring
	and complaining</i>
	is the restless insatiability of their desires.</p>
<p style=" "><b>great
	swelling words</b>
	— (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>).</p>
<p style=" "><b>men’s
	persons</b>
	— their mere outward appearance and rank.</p>
<p style=" "><b>because
	of advantage</b>
	— for the sake of what they may gain from them. While they <i>talk
	great swelling words,</i>
	they are really mean and fawning towards those of wealth and rank.</p>
