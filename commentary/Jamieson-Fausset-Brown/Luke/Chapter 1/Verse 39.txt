<p><b>39. hill country</b>—the
	mountainous tract running along the middle of Judea, from north to
	south [WEBSTER and
	WILKINSON]. 
	</p>
<p><b>with haste</b>—transported
	with the announcement to herself and with the tidings, now first made
	known to her, of Elisabeth's condition. 
	</p>
<p><b>a city of Juda</b>—probably
	Hebron (see <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="jos+20:7">Joshua 20:7</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="jos+21:11">Joshua 21:11</span>).</p>
