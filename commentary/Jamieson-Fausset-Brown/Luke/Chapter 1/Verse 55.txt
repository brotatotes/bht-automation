<p><b>55. As he spake to our fathers</b>—<i>The
	sense requires this clause to be read as a parenthesis.</i> (Compare
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="mic+7:20">Micah 7:20</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="ps+98:3">Psalms 98:3</span>).
	</p>
<p><b>for ever</b>—the perpetuity
