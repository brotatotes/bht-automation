<p><b>6. commandments and ordinances</b>—The
	one expressing their <i>moral</i>—the other their
	<i>ceremonial</i>—obedience [CALVIN
	and BENGEL], (Compare
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="eze+11:20">Ezekiel 11:20</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="heb+9:1">Hebrews 9:1</span>).
	It has been denied that any such distinction was known to the Jews
	and New Testament writers. But <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="heb+9:1- :">Hebrews 9:1- :</span>, and other passages, put this beyond all reasonable doubt.</p>
