<p><b>69. horn of salvation</b>—that is
	"strength of salvation," or "mighty Salvation,"
	meaning the Saviour Himself, whom Simeon calls "Thy Salvation"
	(<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="lu+2:30">Luke 2:30</span>). The metaphor is
	taken from those animals whose <i>strength</i> is in their horns
	(<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="ps+18:2">Psalms 18:2</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="ps+75:10">Psalms 75:10</span>;
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="ps+132:17">Psalms 132:17</span>). 
	</p>
<p><b>house of . . . David</b>—<i>This
	shows that Mary must have been known to be of the royal line,</i>
	independent of Joseph; of whom Zacharias, if he knew anything, could
	not know that after this he would recognize Mary.</p>
