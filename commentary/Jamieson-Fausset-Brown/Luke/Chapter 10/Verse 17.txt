<p><b>17. returned</b>—evidently not
	long away. 
	</p>
<p><b>Lord,</b> &amp;c.—"Thou
	hast exceeded Thy promise, for '<i>even the devils,</i>'" &amp;c.
	The possession of such power, not being expressly in their
	commission, as in that to the Twelve (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>), filled them with more astonishment and joy than all else. 
	</p>
<p><b>through thy name</b>—taking
	no credit to themselves, but feeling lifted into a region of
	unimagined superiority to the powers of evil simply through their
	connection with Christ.</p>
