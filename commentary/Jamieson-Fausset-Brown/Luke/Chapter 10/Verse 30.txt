<p><b>30. A certain man</b>—a Jew. 
	</p>
<p><b>from Jerusalem to Jericho</b>—a
	distance of nineteen miles northeast, a deep and very fertile
	hollow—"the <i>Temple</i> of Judea" [TRENCH].
	</p>
<p><b>thieves</b>—"robbers."
	The road, being rocky and desolate, was a notorious haunt of robbers,
	then and for ages after, and even to this day.</p>
