<p><b>1. one,</b> &amp;c.—struck with
	either the matter or the manner of our Lord's prayers. 
	</p>
<p><b>as John,</b> &amp;c.—From
	this reference to John, it is possible that disciple had not heard
	the Sermon on the Mount. Nothing of John's <i>inner</i> teaching (to
	his own disciples) has been preserved to us, but we may be sure he
	never taught his disciples to say, "Our Father."</p>
