<p><b>21, 22. strong man</b>—meaning
	<i>Satan.</i>
</p>
<p><b>armed</b>—pointing to all
	the subtle and varied methods by which he wields his dark power over
	men. 
	</p>
<p><b>keepeth</b>—"guardeth."
	</p>
<p><b>his palace</b>—<i>man</i>
	whether viewed more largely or in individual souls—how significant
	of what men are to Satan! 
	</p>
<p><b>in peace</b>—undisturbed,
	secure in his possession.</p>
