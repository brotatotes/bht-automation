15. unto them—the multitude
	around Him (Luke 12:1). 
	of covetousness—The
	best copies have "all," that is, "every kind of
	covetousness"; because as this was one of the more plausible
	forms of it, so He would strike at once at the root of the
	evil. 
	a man's life, &c.—a
	singularly weighty maxim, and not less so because its meaning and its
	truth are equally evident.
