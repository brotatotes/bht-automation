<p><b>15. unto them</b>—the multitude
	around Him (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="lu+12:1">Luke 12:1</span>). 
	</p>
<p><b>of covetousness</b>—The
	best copies have "all," that is, "every kind of
	covetousness"; because as this was one of the more plausible
	forms of it, so He would strike at once at the <i>root</i> of the
	evil. 
	</p>
<p><b>a man's life,</b> &amp;c.—a
	singularly weighty maxim, and not less so because its meaning and its
	truth are equally evident.</p>
