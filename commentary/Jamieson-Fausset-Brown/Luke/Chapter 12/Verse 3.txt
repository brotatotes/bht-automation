<p><b>1-3. meantime</b>—in close
	connection, probably, with the foregoing scene. Our Lord had been
	<i>speaking out</i> more plainly than ever before, as matters were
	coming to a head between Him and His enemies, and this seems to have
	suggested to His own mind the warning here. He had just Himself
	illustriously exemplified His own precepts. 
	</p>

	</p>
