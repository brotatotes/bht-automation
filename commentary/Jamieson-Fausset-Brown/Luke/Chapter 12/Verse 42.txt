<p><b>42. Who then,</b> &amp;c.—answering
	the question indirectly by another question, from which they were
	left to gather what it would be:—To you certainly in the first
	instance, representing the "stewards" of the "household"
	I am about to collect, but generally to all "servants" in
	My house. 
	</p>
<p><b>faithful and wise</b>—<i>Fidelity</i>
	is the first requisite in a servant, <i>wisdom</i> (discretion and
	judgment in the exercise of his functions), the next. 
	</p>
<p><b>steward</b>—house steward,
	whose it was to distribute to the servants their allotted portion of
	food. 
	</p>
<p><b>shall make</b>—will deem
	fit to be made.</p>
