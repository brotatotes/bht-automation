<p><b>24. Strive</b>—The word signifies
	to "contend" as for the mastery, to "struggle,"
	expressive of the <i>difficulty</i> of being saved, as if one would
	have to <i>force his way in.</i>
</p>
<p><b>strait gate</b>—another
	figure of the same. (See on <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>). 
	</p>
<p><b>for many . . . will
	seek</b>—"desire," that is, with a mere wish or slothful
	endeavor. 
	</p>
<p><b>and shall not be able</b>—because
	it must be made a <i>life-and-death struggle.</i></p>
