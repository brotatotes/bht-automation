<p><b>33. it cannot be that a prophet,</b>
	&amp;c.—"<i>It would never do</i> that," &amp;c.—awful
	severity of satire this upon "the bloody city!" "He
	seeks to kill me, does he? Ah! I must be out of Herod's jurisdiction
	for that. Go tell him I neither fly from him nor fear him, but
	Jerusalem is the prophets' slaughter-house."</p>
