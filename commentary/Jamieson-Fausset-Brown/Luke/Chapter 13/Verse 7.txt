<p><b>7. three years</b>—a long enough
	trial for a fig tree, and so denoting probably just a <i>sufficient</i>
	period of culture for spiritual fruit. The supposed allusion to the
	duration of our Lord's ministry is precarious. 
	</p>
<p><b>cut it down</b>—indignant
	language. 
	</p>
<p><b>cumbereth</b>—not only
	doing no good, but wasting ground.</p>
