<p><b>12-14. call not thy friends</b>—Jesus
	certainly did not mean us to dispense with the duties of ordinary
	fellowship, but, remitting these to their proper place, inculcates
	what is better [BENGEL]. 
	</p>
<p><b>lest . . . a recompense be
	given thee</b>—a fear the world is not afflicted with [BENGEL].
	The meaning, however, is that no <i>exercise of principle</i> is
	involved in it, as selfishness itself will suffice to prompt to it
	(<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="mt+5:46">Matthew 5:46</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="mt+5:47">Matthew 5:47</span>).</p>
