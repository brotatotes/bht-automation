<p><b>7-11. a parable</b>—showing that
	His design was not so much to inculcate mere politeness or good
	manners, as <i>underneath</i> this to teach something <i>deeper</i>
	(<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="lu+14:11">Luke 14:11</span>). 
	</p>
<p><b>chief rooms</b>—principal
	seats, in the middle part of the couch on which they reclined at
	meals, esteemed the most honorable.</p>
