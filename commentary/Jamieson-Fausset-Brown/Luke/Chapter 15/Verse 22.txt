<p><b>22. But the Father said,</b> c.—The
	son has not said all he purposed, not so much, because the father's
	demonstrations had rekindled the filial, and swallowed up all servile
	feeling [TRENCH] (on the
	word "Father," see on <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>), but because the father's heart is made to appear too full
	to listen, at that moment, to more in this strain. 
	</p>
<p><b>the best robe</b>—Compare
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="zec+3:4">Zechariah 3:4</span> <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="zec+3:5">Zechariah 3:5</span>,
	"Take away the filthy garments from him; behold I have clothed
	thee with change of raiment; and they clothed him with garments"
	(<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="isa+61:10">Isaiah 61:10</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="re+3:18">Revelation 3:18</span>).
	</p>
<p><b>a ring</b>—(Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="ge+41:42">Genesis 41:42</span>;
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="jas+2:2">James 2:2</span>). 
	</p>
<p><b>shoes</b>—Slaves went
	barefoot. Thus, we have here a threefold symbol of <i>freedom</i> and
	<i>honor,</i> restored, as the fruit of <i>perfect reconciliation.</i></p>
