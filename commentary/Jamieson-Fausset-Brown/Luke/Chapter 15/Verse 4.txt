<p><b>4. leave the ninety and nine</b>—bend
	all His attention and care, as it were, to the one object of
	recovering the lost sheep; not saying. "It is but one; let it
	go; enough remain." 
	</p>
<p><b>go after . . . until,</b>
	&amp;c.—pointing to all the diversified means which God sets in
	operation for recovering sinners.</p>
