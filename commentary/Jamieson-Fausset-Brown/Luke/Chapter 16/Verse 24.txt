24. Father Abraham—a
	well-founded, but unavailing, claim of natural descent (Luke 3:8;
	John 8:37). 
	mercy on me—who never
	showed any (James 2:3). 
	send Lazarus—the pining
	victim of his merciless neglect. 
	that he may—take me
	hence? No; that he dares not to ask. 
	dip . . . tongue—that
	is the least conceivable and the most momentary
	abatement of his torment; that is all. But even this he is told is
	(1) unreasonable.
