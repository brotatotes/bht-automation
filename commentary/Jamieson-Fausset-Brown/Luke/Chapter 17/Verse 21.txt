<p><b>21. Lo here! . . . lo there!</b>—shut
	up within <i>this</i> or <i>that</i> sharply defined and visible
	geographical or ecclesiastical limit. 
	</p>
<p><b>within you</b>—is of an
	internal and <i>spiritual</i> character (as contrasted with their
	<i>outside</i> views of it). But it has its <i>external</i> side too.</p>
