<p><b>29. There is no man,</b>
	c.—graciously acknowledging at once the completeness and the
	acceptableness of the surrender as a thing already made. 
	</p>
<p><b>house,</b> &amp;c.—The
	specification is still more minute in Matthew and Mark, (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="mt+19:27">Matthew 19:27</span>
<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="mr+10:29">Mark 10:29</span>) to take in <i>every</i>
	form of self-sacrifice. 
	</p>
<p><b>for the kingdom of God's
	sake</b>—in Mark (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="mr+10:29">Mark 10:29</span>),
	"for MY sake and the
	Gospel's." See on <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="lu+6:22">Luke 6:22</span>.</p>
