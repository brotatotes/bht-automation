<p><b>25. just</b>—upright in his moral
	character. 
	</p>
<p><b>devout</b>—of a religious
	frame of spirit. 
	</p>
<p><b>waiting for the consolation
	of Israel</b>—a beautiful title of the coming Messiah, here
	intended. 
	</p>
<p><b>the Holy Ghost
	was</b>—supernaturally. 
	</p>
<p><b>upon him</b>—Thus was the
	Spirit, after a dreary absence of nearly four hundred years,
	returning to the Church, to quicken expectation, and prepare for
	coming events.</p>
