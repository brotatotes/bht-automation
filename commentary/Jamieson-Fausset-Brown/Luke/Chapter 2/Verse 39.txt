39. Nothing is more difficult
	than to fix the precise order in which the visit of the Magi, with
	the flight into and return from Egypt ( :-), are to be taken, in relation to the circumcision and
	presentation of Christ in the temple, here recorded. It is perhaps
	best to leave this in the obscurity in which we find it, as the
	result of two independent, though if we knew all, easily reconcilable
	narratives.
