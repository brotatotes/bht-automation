<p><b>14. reasoned among
	themselves</b>—(Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="ge+37:18-20">Genesis 37:18-20</span>;
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="joh+11:47-53">John 11:47-53</span>). 
	</p>
<p><b>the heir</b>—sublime
	expression of the great truth, that God's inheritance was destined
	for, and in due time to come into the possession of, His Son <i>in
	our nature</i> (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="heb+1:2">Hebrews 1:2</span>). 
	</p>
<p><b>inheritance . . . ours</b>—and
	so from mere <i>servants</i> we may become <i>lords;</i> the deep aim
	of the depraved heart, and literally "<i>the root of all evil.</i>"</p>
