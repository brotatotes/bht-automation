<p><b>desired </b><i><b>to have</b></i><b>
</b>—rather, "hath obtained you," properly "asked
	and obtained"; alluding to Job (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="job+1:6-12">Job 1:6-12</span>;
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="job+2:1-6">Job 2:1-6</span>), whom he
	solicited and obtained that he might sift him as wheat, insinuating
	as "the accuser of the brethren" (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="job+2:1-18.2.6- :">Job 2:1-18.2.6- :</span>), that he would find chaff enough in his religion, if
	indeed there was any wheat at all. 
	</p>
