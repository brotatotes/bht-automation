40. Dost not thou—"thou"
	is emphatic: "Let others jeer, but dost thou?" 
	fear God—Hast thou no
	fear of meeting Him so soon as thy righteous Judge? Thou art within
	an hour or two of eternity, and dost thou spend it in reckless
	disregard of coming judgment? 
	in the same condemnation—He
	has been condemned to die, but is it better with thee? Doth even a
	common lot kindle no sympathy in thy breast?
