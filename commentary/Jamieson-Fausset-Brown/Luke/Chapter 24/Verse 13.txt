<p><b>13. two of them</b>—One was
	<i>Cleopas</i> (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="lu+24:18">Luke 24:18</span>); who
	the other was is mere conjecture. 
	</p>
<p><b>Emmaus</b>—about seven and
	a half miles from Jerusalem. They probably lived there and were going
	home after the Passover.</p>
