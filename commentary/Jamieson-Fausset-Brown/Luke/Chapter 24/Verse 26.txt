<p><b>26. Ought not Christ</b>—"the
	Christ," "the Messiah." 
	</p>
<p><b>to suffer . . . and
	enter</b>—that is, through the gate of suffering (and suffering
	"<i>these things,</i>" or <i>such a death</i>) to enter
	into His glory. "Ye believe in the glory; but these very
	sufferings are the predicted gate of entrance into it."</p>
