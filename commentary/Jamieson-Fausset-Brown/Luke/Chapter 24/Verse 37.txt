<p><b>37, 38. a spirit</b>—the ghost of
	their dead Lord, but not Himself in the body (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="ac+12:15">Acts 12:15</span>;
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="mt+14:26">Matthew 14:26</span>). 
	</p>
<p><b>thoughts</b>—rather,
	"reasonings"; that is, whether He were risen or no, and
	whether this was His very self.</p>
