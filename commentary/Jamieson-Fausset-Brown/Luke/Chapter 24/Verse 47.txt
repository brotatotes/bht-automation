<p><b>47. beginning at Jerusalem</b>—(1)
	As the metropolis and heart of the then existing kingdom of God:—"to
	the Jew first" (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="ro+1:16">Romans 1:16</span>;
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="ac+13:46">Acts 13:46</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="isa+2:3">Isaiah 2:3</span>,
	see on <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="isa+2:3- :">Isaiah 2:3- :</span>). (2) As the great
	reservoir and laboratory of all the sin and crime of the nation, thus
	proclaiming for all time that there is mercy in Christ for the chief
	of sinners. (See on <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="isa+2:3- :">Isaiah 2:3- :</span>).</p>
