<p><b>and for all the evils which
	Herod had done</b>—important fact here only mentioned, showing how
	<i>thoroughgoing</i> was the fidelity of the Baptist to his royal
	hearer, and how strong must have been the workings of conscience in
	that slave of passion when, notwithstanding such plainness, he "did
	many things and heard John gladly" (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="mr+6:20">Mark 6:20</span>
<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="mr+6:26">Mark 6:26</span>).</p>
