<p><b>5. Master</b>—betokening not
	surely a first acquaintance, but a relationship already formed. 
	</p>
<p><b>all night</b>—the usual
	time of fishing then (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="joh+21:3">John 21:3</span>),
	and even now Peter, as a fisherman, knew how hopeless it was to "let
	down his net" again, save as a mere act of faith, "at His
	word" of command, which carried in it, as it ever does,
	assurance of success. (This shows he must have been already and for
	some time a follower of Christ.)</p>
