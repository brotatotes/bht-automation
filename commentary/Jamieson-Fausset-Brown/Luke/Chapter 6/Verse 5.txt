<p><b>5. Lord also</b>—rather "even"
	(as in <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="mt+12:8">Matthew 12:8</span>). 
	</p>
<p><b>of the sabbath</b>—as naked
	a claim to <i>all the authority of Him who gave the law at Mount
	Sinai</i> as could possibly be made; that is, "I have said
	enough to vindicate the men ye carp at on My account: <i>but in this
	place is the Lord of the law, and they have</i> His sanction."
	(See <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="mr+2:28">Mark 2:28</span>.) 
	</p>
