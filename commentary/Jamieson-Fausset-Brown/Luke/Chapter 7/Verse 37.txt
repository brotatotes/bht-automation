<p><b>37, 38. a sinner</b>—one who had
	led a profligate life. <i>Note.—There is no ground whatever for the
	popular notion that this woman was Mary Magdalene,</i> nor do we know
	what her name was. (See on <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="lu+8:2">Luke 8:2</span>.)
	</p>
<p><b>an alabaster box of
	ointment</b>—a perfume vessel, in some cases very costly (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="lu+8:2- :">Luke 8:2- :</span>). "The ointment has here a peculiar interest, as the
	offering by a penitent of what had been an accessory in her
	unhallowed work of sin" [ALFORD].</p>
