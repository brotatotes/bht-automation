<p><b>18. how ye</b>—in <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>, "<i>what ye</i> hear." The one implies the other.
	The precept is very weighty. 
	</p>
<p><b>seemeth to have</b>—or,
	"thinketh that he hath" (<i>Margin</i>). The "having"
	of <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="mt+13:12">Matthew 13:12</span> (on which see),
	and this "thinking he hath," are not different. Hanging
	loosely on him, and not appropriated, it <i>is</i> and <i>is not</i>
	his. 
	</p>
