<p><b>42. But Jesus called them to him,
	and saith unto them, Ye know that they which are accounted to
	rule</b>—are recognized or acknowledged as rulers. 
	</p>
<p><b>over the Gentiles exercise
	lordship over them: and their great ones exercise authority upon
	them</b>—as superiors exercising an acknowledged authority over
	inferiors.</p>
