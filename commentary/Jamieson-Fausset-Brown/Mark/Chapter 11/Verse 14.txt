<p><b>14. And Jesus answered and said unto
	it, No man eat fruit of thee hereafter for ever</b>—That word did
	not <i>make</i> the tree barren, but sealed it up in its own
	barrenness. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>. 
	</p>
<p><b>And his disciples heard
	it</b>—and marked the saying. This is introduced as a connecting
	link, to explain what was afterwards to be said on the subject, as
	the narrative has to proceed to the other transactions of this day.
