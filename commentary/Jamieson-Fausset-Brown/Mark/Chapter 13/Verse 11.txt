<p><b>11. But when they shall lead you,
	and deliver you up, take no thought beforehand</b>—"Be not
	anxious beforehand." 
	</p>
<p><b>what ye shall speak, neither
	do ye premeditate</b>—"Be not filled with apprehension, in the
	prospect of such public appearances for Me, lest ye should bring
	discredit upon My name, nor think it necessary to prepare beforehand
	what ye are to say." 
	</p>
<p><b>but whatsoever shall be given
	you in that hour, that speak ye: for it is not ye that speak, but the
	Holy Ghost</b>—(See on <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>.)</p>
