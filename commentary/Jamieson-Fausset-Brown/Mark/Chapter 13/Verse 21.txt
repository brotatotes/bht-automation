<p><b>21. And then, if any man shall say
	to you, Lo, here is Christ; or, lo he is there; believe him not</b>—So
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="lu+17:23">Luke 17:23</span>. 
	</p>
<p><b>22. For false Christs and
	false prophets shall rise, and shall show signs and wonders.</b> No
	one can read JOSEPHUS'
	account of what took place before the destruction of Jerusalem
	without seeing how strikingly this was fulfilled. 
	</p>
<p><b>to seduce, if it were
	possible, even the elect</b>—implying that this, though all <i>but</i>
	done, will prove impossible. What a precious assurance! (Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="lu+17:23- :">Luke 17:23- :</span>).</p>
