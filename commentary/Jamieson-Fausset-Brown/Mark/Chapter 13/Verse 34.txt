<p><b>34. For the Son of man is as a man
	taking a far journey,</b> c.—The idea thus far is similar to that
	in the opening part of the parable of the talents (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="mt+25:14">Matthew 25:14</span>
<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="mt+25:15">Matthew 25:15</span>).

	gatekeeper. 
	</p>
<p><b>to watch</b>—pointing to
