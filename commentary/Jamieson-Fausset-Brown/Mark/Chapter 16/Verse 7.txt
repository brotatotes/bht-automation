<p><b>7. But go your way, tell his
	disciples and Peter</b>—This Second Gospel, being drawn up—as all
	the earliest tradition states—<i>under the eye of Peter,</i> or
	from materials chiefly furnished by him, there is something deeply
	affecting in the preservation of this little clause by Mark alone.
