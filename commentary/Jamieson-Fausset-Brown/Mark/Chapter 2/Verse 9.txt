<p><b>9. Whether is it easier to say to
	the sick of the palsy, Thy sins be forgiven thee</b>—or "are
	forgiven thee"; 
	</p>
<p><b>or to say, Arise, and take up
	thy bed and walk?</b>—"Is it easier to command away disease
	than to bid away sin? If, then, I do the one which you can see, know
	thus that I have done the other, which you cannot see."</p>
