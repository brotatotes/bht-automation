<p><b>24. And he saith unto them, Take
	heed what ye hear</b>—In Luke (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>) it is, "Take heed how ye hear." The one implies
	the other, but both precepts are very weighty. 
	</p>
<p><b>with what measure ye mete, it
	shall be measured to you</b>—See on <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>. 
	</p>
<p><b>and unto you that hear</b>—that
	is, thankfully, teachably, profitably. 
	</p>
<p><b>shall more be given.</b></p>
