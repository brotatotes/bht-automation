<p><b>29. But when the fruit is brought
	forth</b>—to maturity 
	</p>
<p><b>immediately he putteth in the
	sickle, because the harvest is come</b>—This charmingly points to
	the transition from the earthly to the heavenly condition of the
	Christian and the Church. 
	</p>
