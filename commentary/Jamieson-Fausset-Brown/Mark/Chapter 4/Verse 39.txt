<p><b>39. And he arose, and rebuked the
	wind</b>—"and the raging of the water" (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>). 
	</p>
<p><b>and said unto the sea, Peace,
	be still</b>—two sublime words of command, from a Master to His
	servants, the elements. 
	</p>
<p><b>And the wind ceased, and
	there was a great calm</b>—The sudden hushing of the wind would not
	at once have calmed the sea, whose commotion would have settled only
	after a considerable time. But the word of command was given to both
	elements at once.</p>
