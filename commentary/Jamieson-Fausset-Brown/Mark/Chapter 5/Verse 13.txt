13. And forthwith Jesus gave them
	leave—In Matthew (Matthew 8:32)
	this is given with majestic brevity—"Go!" The owners, if
	Jews, drove an illegal trade; if heathens, they insulted the national
	religion: in either case the permission was just. 
	And the unclean spirits went
	out—of the man. 
	and entered into the swine:
	and the herd ran violently—rushed. 
	down a steep place—down
	the hanging cliff. 
	into the sea (they were about
	two thousand)—The number of them is given by this graphic
	Evangelist alone. 
	and were choked in the
	sea—"perished in the waters" (Matthew 8:32- :).
