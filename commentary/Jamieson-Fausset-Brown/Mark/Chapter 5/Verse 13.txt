<p><b>13. And forthwith Jesus gave them
	leave</b>—In Matthew (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="mt+8:32">Matthew 8:32</span>)
	this is given with majestic brevity—"Go!" The owners, if
	Jews, drove an illegal trade; if heathens, they insulted the national
	religion: in either case the permission was just. 
	</p>
<p><b>And the unclean spirits went
	out</b>—of the man. 
	</p>
<p><b>and entered into the swine:
	and the herd ran violently</b>—rushed. 
	</p>
<p><b>down a steep place</b>—down
	the hanging cliff. 
	</p>
<p><b>into the sea (they were about
	two thousand)</b>—The number of them is given by this graphic
	Evangelist alone. 
	</p>
<p><b>and were choked in the
	sea</b>—"perished in the waters" (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="mt+8:32- :">Matthew 8:32- :</span>).</p>
