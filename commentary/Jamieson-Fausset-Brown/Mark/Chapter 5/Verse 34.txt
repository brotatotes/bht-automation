<p><b>34. And he said unto her,
	Daughter</b>—"be of good comfort" (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>). 
	</p>
<p><b>thy faith hath made thee
	whole; go in peace, and be whole of thy plague</b>—Though healed as
	soon as she believed, it seemed to her a stolen cure—she feared to
	acknowledge it. Jesus therefore sets His royal seal upon it. But what
	a glorious dismissal from the lips of Him who is "our Peace"
	is that, "Go in peace!" 
	</p>
