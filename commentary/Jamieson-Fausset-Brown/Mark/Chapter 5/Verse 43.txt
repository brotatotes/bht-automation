<p><b>43. And he charged them
	straitly</b>—strictly. 
	</p>
<p><b>that no man should know
	it</b>—The only reason we can assign for this is His desire not to
	let the public feeling regarding Him come too precipitately to a
	crisis. 
	</p>
<p><b>and commanded that something
	should be given her to eat</b>—in token of perfect restoration.</p>
