<p><b>8. For he said unto him</b>—that
	is, before the unclean spirit cried out. 
	</p>
<p><b>Come out of the man, unclean
	spirit!</b>—Ordinarily, obedience to a command of this nature was
	immediate. But here, a certain delay is permitted, the more signally
	to manifest the power of Christ and accomplish His purposes.</p>
