<p><b>15. And he charged them, saying,
	Take heed, beware of the leaven of the Pharisees</b>—"and of
	the Sadducees" (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="mt+16:6">Matthew 16:6</span>). 
	</p>
<p><b>and of the leaven of
	Herod</b>—The teaching or "doctrine" (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="mt+16:6- :">Matthew 16:6- :</span>) of the Pharisees and of the Sadducees was quite different,
	but both were equally pernicious; and the Herodians, though rather a
	political party, were equally envenomed against our Lord's spiritual
	teaching. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="mt+16:6- :">Matthew 16:6- :</span>. The
	<i>penetrating</i> and <i>diffusive</i> quality of leaven, for good
	or bad, is the ground of the comparison.</p>
