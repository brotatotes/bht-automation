<p><b>23. And he took the blind man by the
	hand, and led him out of the town</b>—Of the deaf and dumb man it
	is merely said that "He took him aside" (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>); but this blind man He <i>led by the hand</i> out of the
	town, doing it Himself rather than employing another—great
	humility, exclaims BENGEL—that
	He might gain his confidence and raise his expectation. 
	</p>
<p><b>and when he had spit on his
	eyes</b>—the organ affected—See on <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>. 
	</p>
