<p><b>17. And one of the multitude
	answered, and said, Master, I have brought unto thee my son</b>—"mine
	only child" (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="lu+9:38">Luke 9:38</span>). 
	</p>
<p><b>which hath a dumb spirit</b>—a
	spirit whose operation had the effect of rendering his victim
	speechless, and deaf also (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="lu+9:38- :">Luke 9:38- :</span>). In Matthew's report of the speech (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="lu+9:38- :">Luke 9:38- :</span>), the father says "he is lunatic"; this being
	another and most distressing effect of the possession.</p>
