<p><b>23. Behold, a virgin</b>—It should
	be "<i>the</i> virgin" meaning that particular virgin
	destined to this unparalleled distinction. 
	</p>
<p><b>shall be with child, and
	shall bring forth a son, and they shall call his name Emmanuel,
	which, being interpreted, is, God with us</b>—Not that He was to
	have this for a proper name (like "Jesus"), but that He
	should come to be known <i>in this character,</i> as God manifested
	in the flesh, and the living bond of holy and most intimate
	fellowship between God and men from henceforth and for ever.</p>
