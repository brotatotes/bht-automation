<p><b>1. And when he had called unto him
	his twelve disciples, he gave them power</b>—The word signifies
	both "power," and "authority" or "right."
	Even if it were not evident that here both ideas are included, we
	find both words expressly used in the parallel passage of Luke (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>) —"He gave them power and authority"—in other
	words, He both <i>qualified</i> and <i>authorized</i> them. 
	</p>
