<p><b>11. And into whatsoever city or
	town</b>—town or village. 
	</p>
<p><b>ye shall enter
	inquire</b>—carefully. 
	</p>
<p><b>who in it is worthy</b>—or
	"meet" to entertain such messengers; not in point of rank,
	of course, but of congenial disposition. 
	</p>
<p><b>and there abide till ye go
	thence</b>—not shifting about, as if discontented, but returning
	the welcome given with a courteous, contented, accommodating
	disposition.</p>
