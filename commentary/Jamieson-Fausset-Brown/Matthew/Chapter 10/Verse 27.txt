<p><b>27. What I tell you in darkness</b>—in
	the privacy of a teaching for which men are not yet ripe. 
	</p>
<p><b>that speak ye in the
	light</b>—for when ye go forth all will be ready. 
	</p>
<p><b>and what ye hear in the ear,
	that preach ye upon the housetops</b>—Give free and fearless
	utterance to all that I have taught you while yet with you.
	<i>Objection:</i> But this may cost us our life? <i>Answer:</i> It
	may, but there their power ends:</p>
