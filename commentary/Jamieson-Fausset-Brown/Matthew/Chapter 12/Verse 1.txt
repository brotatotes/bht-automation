<p><b>1. At that time Jesus went on the
	sabbath day through the corn</b>—"the cornfields"
	(<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="mr+2:23">Mark 2:23</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="lu+6:1">Luke 6:1</span>).
	</p>
<p><b>and his disciples were an
	hungered</b>—not as one may be before his regular meals; but
	evidently from shortness of provisions: for Jesus defends their
	plucking the corn-ears and eating them on the plea of <i>necessity.</i>
</p>
<p><b>and began to pluck the ears
	of corn, and to eat</b>—"rubbing them in their hands" (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="lu+6:1- :">Luke 6:1- :</span>).</p>
