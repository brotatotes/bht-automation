<p><b>34. O generation of vipers</b>—(See
	on <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="mt+3:7">Matthew 3:7</span>). 
	</p>
<p><b>how can ye, being evil, speak
	good things? for out of the abundance of the heart the mouth
	speaketh</b>—a principle obvious enough, yet of deepest
	significance and vast application. In <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="mt+3:7- :">Matthew 3:7- :</span> we find it uttered as part of the discourse delivered after
	the choice of the apostles.</p>
