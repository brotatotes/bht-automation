<p><b>39. But he answered and said unto
	them</b>—"when the people were gathered thick together"
	(<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="lu+11:29">Luke 11:29</span>). 
	</p>
<p><b>an evil and adulterous
	generation</b>—This latter expression is best explained by <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="lu+11:29- :">Luke 11:29- :</span>, "Surely as a wife treacherously departeth from her
	husband, so have ye dealt treacherously with Me, O house of Israel,
	saith the Lord." For this was the relationship in which He stood
	to the covenant-people—"I am married unto you" (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="lu+11:29- :">Luke 11:29- :</span>). 
	</p>
<p><b>seeketh after a sign</b>—In
	the eye of Jesus this class were but the spokesmen of their
	generation, the exponents of the reigning spirit of unbelief. 
	</p>
<p><b>and there shall no sign be
	given to it, but the sign of the prophet Jonas.</b></p>
