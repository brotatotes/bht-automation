<p><b>5. Or have ye not read in the law,
	how that on the sabbath days the priests in the temple profane the
	sabbath</b>—by doing "servile work." 
	</p>
<p><b>and are blameless?</b>—The
	double offerings required on the sabbath day (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>) could not be presented, and the new-baked showbread
	(<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="le+24:5">Leviticus 24:5</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1ch+9:32">1 Chronicles 9:32</span>)
	could not be prepared and presented every sabbath morning, without a
	good deal of servile work on the part of the priests; not to speak of
	circumcision, which, when the child's eighth day happened to fall on
	a sabbath, had to be performed by the priests on that day. (See on
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1ch+9:32- :">1 Chronicles 9:32- :</span>).</p>
