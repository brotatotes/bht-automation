<p><b>6. But I say unto you, That in this
	place is one greater than the temple</b>—or rather, according to
	the reading which is best supported, "something greater."
	The argument stands thus: "The ordinary rules for the observance
	of the sabbath give way before the requirements of the temple; but
	there are rights here before which the temple itself must give way."
	Thus indirectly, but not the less decidedly, does our Lord put in His
	own claims to consideration in this question—claims to be presently
	put in even more nakedly.</p>
