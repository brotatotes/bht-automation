<p><b>10. And the disciples came, and said
	unto him</b>—"they that were with Him, when they were alone"
	(<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="mr+4:10">Mark 4:10</span>). 
	</p>
<p><b>Why speakest thou to them in
	parables?</b>—Though before this He had couched some things in the
	parabolic form, for more vivid illustration, it would appear that He
	now, for the first time, formally employed this method of teaching.</p>
