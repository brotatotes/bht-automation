<p><b>14. And in them is fulfilled</b>—rather,
	"is fulfilling," or "is receiving its fulfilment."
	</p>
<p><b>the prophecy of Esaias, which
	saith</b>— (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="isa+6:9">Isaiah 6:9</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="isa+6:10">Isaiah 6:10</span>

	</p>
<p><b>By hearing ye shall hear, and
	shall not understand,</b> &amp;c.—They were thus judicially sealed
	up under the darkness and obduracy which they deliberately preferred
	to the light and healing which Jesus brought nigh to them.</p>
