<p><b>21. From that time forth began Jesus
	to show unto his disciples</b>—that is, with an <i>explicitness and
	frequency</i> He had never observed before. 
	</p>
<p><b>how that he must go unto
	Jerusalem and suffer many things</b>—"and be rejected,"
	(<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="mr+8:31">Mark 8:31</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="lu+9:22">Luke 9:22</span>).
	</p>
<p><b>of the elders and chief
	priests and scribes</b>—not as before, merely by not receiving Him,
	but by formal deeds. 
	</p>
<p><b>and be killed, and be raised
	again the third day</b>—Mark (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="lu+9:22- :">Luke 9:22- :</span>) adds, that "He spake that saying openly"—"explicitly,"
	or "without disguise."</p>
