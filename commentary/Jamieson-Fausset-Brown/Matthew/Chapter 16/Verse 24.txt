<p><b>24. Then said Jesus unto his
	disciples</b>—Mark (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="mr+8:34">Mark 8:34</span>)
	says, "When He had called the people unto Him, with His
	disciples also, He said unto them"—turning the rebuke of one
	into a warning to all. 
	</p>
<p><b>If any man will come after
	me, let him deny himself, and take up his cross, and follow me.</b></p>
