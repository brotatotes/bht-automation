<p><b>12. And being warned of God in a
	dream that they should not return to Herod, they departed</b>—or,
	"withdrew." 
	</p>
<p><b>to their own country another
	way</b>—What a surprise would this vision be to the sages, just as
	they were preparing to carry the glad news of what they had seen to
	the <i>pious</i> king! But the Lord knew the bloody old tyrant better
	than to let him see their face again. 
	</p>
