<p><b>10. But when the first came, they
	supposed that they should have received more</b>—This is that
	calculating, mercenary spirit which had peeped out—though perhaps
	very slightly—in Peter's question (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>), and which this parable was designed once for all to put
	down among the servants of Christ.</p>
