6. And about the eleventh hour—but
	one hour before the close of the working day; a most unusual hour
	both for offering and engaging 
	and found others standing
	idle, and saith, Why stand ye here all the day idle?—Of course
	they had not been there, or not been disposed to offer themselves at
	the proper time; but as they were now willing, and the day was not
	over, and "yet there was room," they also are engaged, and
	on similar terms with all the rest.
