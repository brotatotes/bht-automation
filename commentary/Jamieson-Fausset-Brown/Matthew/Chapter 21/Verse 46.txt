<p><b>46. But when they sought to lay
	hands on him</b>—which Luke (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>) says they did "the same hour," hardly able to
	restrain their rage. 
	</p>
<p><b>they feared the
	multitude</b>—rather, "the multitudes." 
	</p>
<p><b>because they took him for a
	prophet</b>—just as they feared to say John's baptism was of men,
	because the masses took him for a prophet (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>). Miserable creatures! So, for this time, "they left
	Him and went their way" (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>).</p>
