<p><b>3. and sent forth his
	servants</b>—representing all preachers of the Gospel. 
	</p>
<p><b>to call them that were
	bidden</b>—here meaning the Jews, who were "bidden," from
	the first choice of them onwards through every summons addressed to
	them by the prophets to hold themselves in readiness for the
	appearing of their King. 
	</p>
<p><b>to the wedding</b>—or the
	marriage festivities, when the preparations were all concluded. 
	</p>
<p><b>and they would not come</b>—as
	the issue of the whole ministry of the Baptist, our Lord Himself, and
	His apostles thereafter, too sadly showed.</p>
