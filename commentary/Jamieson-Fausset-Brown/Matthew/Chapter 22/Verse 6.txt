<p><b>6. And the remnant took his
	servants, and entreated them spitefully</b>—insulted them. 
	</p>
<p><b>and slew them</b>—These are
	two different classes of unbelievers: the one simply <i>indifferent;</i>
	the other absolutely <i>hostile</i>—the one, contemptuous <i>scorners;</i>
	the other, bitter <i>persecutors.</i></p>
