<p><b>15. Woe unto you, scribes and
	Pharisees, hypocrites! for ye compass sea and land to make one
	proselyte</b>—from heathenism. We have evidence of this in
	JOSEPHUS. 
	</p>
<p><b>and when he is made, ye make
	him twofold more the child of hell than yourselves</b>—condemned,
	for the hypocrisy he would learn to practice, both by the religion he
	left and that he embraced.</p>
