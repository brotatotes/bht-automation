<p><b>2. Saying, The scribes and the
	Pharisees sit</b>—The Jewish teachers <i>stood</i> to read, but <i>sat</i>
	to expound the Scriptures, as will be seen by comparing <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="lu+4:16">Luke 4:16</span>;
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="lu+4:20">Luke 4:20</span>. 
	</p>
<p><b>in Moses' seat</b>—that is,
	as interpreters of the law given by Moses.</p>
