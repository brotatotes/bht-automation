<p><b>5. But all their works they do for
	to be seen of men</b>—Whatever good they do, or zeal they show, has
	but one motive—human applause. 
	</p>
<p><b>they make broad their
	phylacteries</b>—strips of parchment with Scripture-texts on them,
	worn on the forehead, arm, and side, in time of prayer. 
	</p>
<p><b>and enlarge the borders of
	their garments</b>—fringes of their upper garments (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>).</p>
