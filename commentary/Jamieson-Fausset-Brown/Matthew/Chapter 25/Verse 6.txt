<p><b>6. And at midnight</b>—that is,
	the time when the Bridegroom will be least expected; for "the
	day of the Lord so cometh as a thief in the night" (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>). 
	</p>
<p><b>there was a cry made, Behold,
	the Bridegroom cometh; go ye out to meet him</b>—that is, Be ready
	to welcome Him.</p>
