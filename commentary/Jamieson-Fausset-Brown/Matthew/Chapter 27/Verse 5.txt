<p><b>5. And he cast down the pieces of
	silver</b>—The sarcastic, diabolical reply which he had got, in
	place of the sympathy which perhaps he expected, would deepen his
	remorse into an agony. 
	</p>
<p><b>in the temple</b>—the
	temple proper, commonly called "the sanctuary," or "the
	holy place," into which only the priests might enter. How is
	this to be explained? Perhaps he flung the money in after them. But
	thus were fulfilled the words of the prophet—"I cast them to
	the potter in the house of the Lord" (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>). 
	</p>
<p><b>and departed, and went and
	hanged himself</b>—For the details, see on <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>.</p>
