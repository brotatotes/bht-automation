<p><b>19. Go ye therefore, and teach all
	nations</b>—rather, "make disciples of all nations"; for
	"teaching," in the more usual sense of that word, comes in
	afterwards, and is expressed by a different term. 
	</p>
<p><b>baptizing them in the name of
	the Father, and of the Son, and of the Holy Ghost</b>—It should be,
	"into the name"; as in <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>, "And were all baptized unto (or rather '<i>into</i>')
	Moses"; and <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="ga+3:27">Galatians 3:27</span>, "For
	as many of you as have been baptized <i>into</i> Christ."</p>
