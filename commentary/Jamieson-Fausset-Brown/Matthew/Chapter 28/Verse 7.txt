<p><b>7. And go quickly, and tell his
	disciples</b>—For a precious addition to this, see on <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>. 
	</p>
<p><b>that he is risen from the
	dead; and, behold, he goeth before you into Galilee</b>—to which
	those women belonged (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="mt+27:55">Matthew 27:55</span>).
	</p>
<p><b>there shall ye see him</b>—This
	must refer to those more public manifestations of Himself to large
	numbers of disciples at once, which He vouchsafed only in Galilee;
	for individually He was seen of some of those very women almost
	immediately after this (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="mt+28:9">Matthew 28:9</span>;
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="mt+28:10">Matthew 28:10</span>). 
	</p>
<p><b>Lo, I have told you</b>—Behold,
	ye have this word from the world of light!</p>
