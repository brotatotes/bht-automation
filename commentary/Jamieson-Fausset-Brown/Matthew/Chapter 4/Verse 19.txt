<p><b>19. And he saith unto them, Follow
	me</b>—rather, as the same expression is rendered in Mark, "Come
	ye after Me" (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="mr+1:17">Mark 1:17</span>). 
	</p>
<p><b>and I will make you fishers
	of men</b>—raising them from a lower to a higher <i>fishing,</i> as
