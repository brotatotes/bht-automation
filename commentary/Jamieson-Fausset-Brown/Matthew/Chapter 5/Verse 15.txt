<p><b>15. Neither do men light a
	candle</b>—or, lamp. 
	</p>
<p><b>and put it under a bushel</b>—a
	dry measure. 
	</p>
<p><b>but on a candlestick</b>—rather,
	"under the bushel, but on the lampstand." The article is
	inserted in both cases to express the familiarity of everyone with
	those household utensils. 
	</p>
<p><b>and it giveth light</b>—shineth
	"unto all that are in the house."</p>
