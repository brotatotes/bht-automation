<p><b>40. And if any man will sue thee at
	the law, and take away thy coat</b>—the inner garment; in pledge
	for a debt (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="ex+22:26">Exodus 22:26</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="ex+22:27">Exodus 22:27</span>).
	</p>
<p><b>let him have thy cloak
	also</b>—the outer and more costly garment. This overcoat was not
	allowed to be retained over night as a pledge from the poor because
	they used it for a bed covering.</p>
