<p><b>5. And when thou prayest, thou
	shalt</b>—or, preferably, "when ye pray ye shall." 
	</p>
<p><b>not be as the hypocrites are:
	for they love to pray standing in the synagogues and in the corners
	of the streets</b>—(See on <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>). 
	</p>
<p><b>that they may be seen of men.
	Verily I say unto you, They have,</b> &amp;c.—The <i>standing</i>
	posture in prayer was the ancient practice, alike in the Jewish and
	in the early Christian Church. But of course this conspicuous posture
	opened the way for the ostentatious.</p>
