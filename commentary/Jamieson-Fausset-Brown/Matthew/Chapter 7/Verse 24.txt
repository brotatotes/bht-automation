<p><b>24. Therefore</b>—to bring this
	discourse to a close. 
	</p>
<p><b>whosoever heareth these
	sayings of mine, and doeth them</b>—see <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>, which seems a plain allusion to these words; also <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="lu+11:28">Luke 11:28</span>;
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="ro+2:13">Romans 2:13</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1jo+3:7">1 John 3:7</span>.
	</p>
<p><b>I will liken him unto a wise
	man</b>—a shrewd, prudent, provident man. 
	</p>
<p><b>which built his house upon a
	rock</b>—the rock of true discipleship, or genuine subjection to
	Christ.</p>
