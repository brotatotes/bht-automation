<p><b>37. Then saith he unto his
	disciples, The harvest truly is plenteous</b>—His eye doubtless
	rested immediately on the Jewish field, but this he saw widening into
	the vast field of "the world" (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>), teeming with souls having to be gathered to Him. 
	</p>
<p><b>but the labourers</b>—men
	divinely qualified and called to gather them in—"are few."</p>
