<p style=" "><b>Which
	... was ... unprofitable</b>
	— belying his name Onesimus, which means “profitable.” Not only
	was he “unprofitable,” but positively injurious, having “wronged”
	his master. Paul uses a mild expression.</p>
<p style=" "><b>now
	profitable</b>
	— Without godliness a man has no station. <i>Profitable</i>
	in spiritual, as well as in <i>temporal</i>
	things.</p>
