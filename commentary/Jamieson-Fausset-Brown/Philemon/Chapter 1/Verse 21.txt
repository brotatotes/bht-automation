<p style=" "><b>Having
	confidence in thy obedience</b>
	— to my apostolic authority, if I were to “enjoin” it (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>),
	which I do not, preferring to beseech thee for it as a favor (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>).</p>
<p style=" "><b>thou
	will also do more</b>
	— towards Onesimus: hinting at his possible manumission by
	Philemon, <i>besides,</i>
	being kindly received.</p>
