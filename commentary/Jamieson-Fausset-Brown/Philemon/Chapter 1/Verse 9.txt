<p style=" "><b>for
	love’s sake</b>
	— mine to thee, and (what ought to be) thine to Onesimus. Or, that
	Christian love of which thou showest so bright an example (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>).</p>
<p style=" "><b>being
	such an one</b>
	— Explain, <i>Being
	such a one</i>
	as thou knowest me to be, namely,</p>
<p style=" "><b>Paul</b>
	— the founder of so many churches, and an apostle of Christ, and
	thy father in the faith.</p>
<p style=" "><b>the
	aged</b> —
	a circumstance calculated to secure thy respect for anything I
	request.</p>
<p style=" "><b>and
	now also a prisoner of Jesus Christ</b>
	— the strongest claim I have on thy regard: if for no other reason,
	at least in consideration of this, through commiseration gratify me.</p>
