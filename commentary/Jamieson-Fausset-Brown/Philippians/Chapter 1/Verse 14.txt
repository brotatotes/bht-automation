<p><b>14.</b> Translate as <i>Greek,</i>
	"And <i>that</i> (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="php+1:13">Philippians 1:13</span>)
	<i>most</i> of the brethren in the Lord," &amp;c. "In the
	Lord," distinguishes them from "brethren after the flesh,"
	Jewish fellow countrymen. ELLICOTT
	translates, "<i>Trusting in the Lord.</i>" 
	</p>
<p><b>by my bonds</b>—encouraged
	by my patience in bearing my bonds. 
	</p>
<p><b>much more bold</b>—Translate
	as <i>Greek,</i> "are more abundantly bold."</p>
