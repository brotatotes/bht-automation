<p><b>29. For</b>—rather, a proof that
	this is an evident token from God of your salvation, "<i>Because,</i>"
	c. 
	</p>
<p><b>it is given</b>—<i>Greek,</i>
	"it <i>has been</i> granted <i>as a favor,</i>" or "gift
	of grace." Faith is the gift of God (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>), not wrought in the soul by the will of man, but by the Holy
	Ghost (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="joh+1:12">John 1:12</span> <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="joh+1:13">John 1:13</span>).
	</p>
<p><b>believe on him</b>—"To
	believe <i>Him,</i>" would merely mean to believe He speaks the
	truth. "To believe <i>on Him,</i>" is to believe in, and
	trust through, Him to obtain eternal salvation. <i>Suffering for
	Christ</i> is not only not a mark of God's anger, but <i>a gift of
	His grace.</i></p>
