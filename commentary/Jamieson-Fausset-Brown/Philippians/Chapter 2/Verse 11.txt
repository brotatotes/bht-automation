<p><b>11. every tongue</b>—Compare
	"every knee" (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="php+2:10">Philippians 2:10</span>).
	<i>In every way</i> He shall be acknowledged as Lord (no longer as
	"servant," <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="php+2:7">Philippians 2:7</span>).
	As none can fully do so "but by the Holy Ghost" (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="php+2:7- :">Philippians 2:7- :</span>), the spirits of good men who are dead, must be the class
	<i>directly</i> meant, <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="php+2:10">Philippians 2:10</span>,
	"under the earth." 
	</p>
