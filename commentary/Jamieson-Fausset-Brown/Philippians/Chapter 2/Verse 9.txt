<p><b>9. Wherefore</b>—as the just
	consequence of His self-humiliation and obedience (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="ps+8:5">Psalms 8:5</span>;
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="ps+8:6">Psalms 8:6</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="ps+110:1">Psalms 110:1</span>;
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="ps+110:7">Psalms 110:7</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="mt+28:18">Matthew 28:18</span>;
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="lu+24:26">Luke 24:26</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="joh+5:27">John 5:27</span>;
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="joh+10:17">John 10:17</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="ro+14:9">Romans 14:9</span>;
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="eph+1:20-22">Ephesians 1:20-22</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="heb+2:9">Hebrews 2:9</span>).
	An intimation, that if we would hereafter be exalted, we too must,
	after His example, now humble ourselves (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="php+2:3">Philippians 2:3</span>;
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="php+2:5">Philippians 2:5</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="php+3:21">Philippians 3:21</span>;
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1pe+5:5">1 Peter 5:5</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1pe+5:6">1 Peter 5:6</span>).
	Christ emptied Christ; God exalted Christ as man to equality with God
	[BENGEL]. 
	</p>
<p><b>highly exalted</b>—<i>Greek,</i>
	"<i>super-eminently</i> exalted" (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1pe+5:6- :">1 Peter 5:6- :</span>). 
	</p>
<p><b>given him</b>—<i>Greek,</i>
	"bestowed on Him." 
	</p>
<p><b>a name</b>—along with the
	corresponding <i>reality,</i> glory and majesty. 
	</p>
<p><b>which</b>—Translate,
	namely, "that which is above every name." The name "JESUS"
	(<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="php+2:10">Philippians 2:10</span>), which is even now
	in glory His name of honor (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="ac+9:5">Acts 9:5</span>).
	"Above" not only men, but angels (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="ac+9:5- :">Acts 9:5- :</span>).</p>
