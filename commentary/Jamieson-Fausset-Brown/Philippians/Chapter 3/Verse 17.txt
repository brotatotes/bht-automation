<p><b>17. followers</b>—<i>Greek,</i>
	"imitators together." 
	</p>
<p><b>of me</b>—as I am an
	<i>imitator of Christ</i> (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>): Imitate me no farther than as I imitate Christ. Or as
	BENGEL "My fellow
	imitators of God" or "Christ"; "imitators of
	Christ together with me" (see on <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="eph+5:1">Ephesians 5:1</span>). 
	</p>
<p><b>mark</b>—for imitation. 
	</p>
<p><b>which walk so as ye have us
	for an ensample</b>—In <i>English Version</i> of the former clause,
	the translation of this clause is, "those who are walking so as
	ye have an example in us." But in BENGEL'S
	translation, "inasmuch as," or "since," instead
	of "as."</p>
