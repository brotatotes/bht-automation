<p><b>7. gain</b>—rather as <i>Greek,</i>
	"gains"; including all possible advantages of outward
	status, which he had heretofore enjoyed. 
	</p>
<p><b>I counted</b>—<i>Greek,</i>
	"I <i>have</i> counted for Christ's sake loss." He no
	longer uses the plural as in "gains"; for he counts them
	all but one great "loss" (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="mt+16:26">Matthew 16:26</span>;
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="lu+9:25">Luke 9:25</span>).</p>
