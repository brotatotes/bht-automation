<p>This eleventh chapter is a
	compendious summary of, and introduction to, the more detailed
	prophecies of the same events to come in the twelfth through
	twentieth chapters. Hence we find <i>anticipatory</i> allusions to
	the subsequent prophecies; compare <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>, "the beast that ascendeth out of the bottomless pit"
	(not mentioned before), with the detailed accounts, <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="re+13:1">Revelation 13:1</span>;
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="re+13:11">Revelation 13:11</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="re+17:8">Revelation 17:8</span>;
	also <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="re+11:8">Revelation 11:8</span>, "the great
	city," with <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="re+14:8">Revelation 14:8</span>;
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="re+17:1">Revelation 17:1</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="re+17:5">Revelation 17:5</span>;
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="re+18:10">Revelation 18:10</span>.</p>
