5. will hurt—Greek,
	"wishes," or "desires to hurt them." 
	fire . . . devoureth—(Compare
	Jeremiah 5:14; Jeremiah 23:29).
	out of their mouth—not
	literally, but God makes their inspired denunciations of judgment to
	come to pass and devour their enemies. 
	if any man will hurt
	them—twice repeated, to mark the immediate certainty of
	the accomplishment. 
	in this manner—so in
	like manner as he tries to hurt them (compare Jeremiah 23:29- :). Retribution in kind.
