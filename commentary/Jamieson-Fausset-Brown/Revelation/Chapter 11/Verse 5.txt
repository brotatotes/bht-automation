<p><b>5. will hurt</b>—<i>Greek,</i>
	"wishes," or "desires to hurt them." 
	</p>
<p><b>fire . . . devoureth</b>—(Compare
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="jer+5:14">Jeremiah 5:14</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="jer+23:29">Jeremiah 23:29</span>).
	</p>
<p><b>out of their mouth</b>—not
	literally, but God makes their inspired denunciations of judgment to
	come to pass and <i>devour</i> their enemies. 
	</p>
<p><b>if any man will hurt
	them</b>—twice repeated, to mark the <i>immediate certainty</i> of
	the accomplishment. 
	</p>
<p><b>in this manner</b>—so in
	like manner as he tries to hurt them (compare <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="jer+23:29- :">Jeremiah 23:29- :</span>). Retribution in kind.</p>
