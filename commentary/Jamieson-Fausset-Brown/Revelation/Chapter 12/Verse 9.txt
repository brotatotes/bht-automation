<p><b>9. that old serpent</b>—alluding
	to <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="ge+3:1">Genesis 3:1</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="ge+3:4">Genesis 3:4</span>.
	</p>
<p><b>Devil</b>—the <i>Greek,</i>
	for "accuser," or "slanderer." 
	</p>
<p><b>Satan</b>—the <i>Hebrew</i>
	for "adversary," especially in a court of justice. The
	twofold designation, <i>Greek</i> and <i>Hebrew,</i> marks the
	twofold objects of his accusations and temptations, the elect
	Gentiles and the elect Jews. 
	</p>
<p><b>world</b>—<i>Greek,</i>
	"habitable world."</p>
