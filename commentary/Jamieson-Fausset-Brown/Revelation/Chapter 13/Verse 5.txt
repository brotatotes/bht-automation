<p><b>5. blasphemies</b>—So ANDREAS
	reads. B reads "blasphemy." A, "blasphemous things"
	(compare <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="da+7:8">Daniel 7:8</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="da+11:25">Daniel 11:25</span>).
	</p>
<p><b>power</b>—"authority";
	<i>legitimate power</i> (<i>Greek,</i> "<i>exousia</i>"). 
	</p>
<p><b>to continue</b>—<i>Greek,</i>
	"<i>poiesai,</i>" "to act," or "work."
	B reads, "to make <i>war</i>" (compare <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="da+11:25- :">Daniel 11:25- :</span>). But A, C, <i>Vulgate, Syriac,</i> and ANDREAS
	omit "war." 
	</p>
