<p><b>12. Here,</b> c.—resumed from <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span> see on <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="re+14:1">Revelation 14:1</span>. In
	the fiery ordeal of persecution which awaits all who will not worship
	the beast, the <i>faith</i> and <i>patience</i> of the followers of
	<i>God and Jesus</i> shall be put to the test, and proved. 
	</p>
<p><b>patience</b>—<i>Greek,</i>
	"<i>hupomene,</i>" "patient, persevering endurance."
	The second "here" is omitted in A, B, C, <i>Vulgate,
	Syriac, Coptic,</i> and PRIMASIUS.
	Translate, "Here is the endurance of the saints, who keep,"
	&amp;c. 
	</p>
<p><b>the faith of Jesus</b>—the
	faith which has Jesus for its object.</p>
