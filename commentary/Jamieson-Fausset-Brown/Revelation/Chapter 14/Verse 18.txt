<p><b>18. from the altar</b>—upon which
	were offered the incense-accompanied prayers of all saints, which
	bring down in answer God's fiery judgment on the Church's foes, the
	<i>fire</i> being <i>taken from the altar and cast upon the earth.</i>
</p>
<p><b>fully ripe</b>—<i>Greek,</i>
	"come to their acme"; ripe for punishment.</p>
