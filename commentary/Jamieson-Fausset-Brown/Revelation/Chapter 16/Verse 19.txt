<p><b>19. the great city</b>—the capital
	and seat of the apostate Church, spiritual Babylon (of which Rome is
	the representative, if one literal city be meant). The city in <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span> (see on <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="re+16:1">Revelation 16:1</span>), is
	probably distinct, namely, Jerusalem under Antichrist (<i>the beast,</i>
	who is distinct from <i>the harlot</i> or apostate Church). In <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="re+16:1- :">Revelation 16:1- :</span> only a <i>tenth</i> of Jerusalem falls whereas here the
	city (Babylon) "became (<i>Greek</i>) into three parts" by
	the earthquake. 
	</p>
<p><b>cities of the nations</b>—other
	great cities in league with spiritual Babylon. 
	</p>
<p><b>great . . . came in
	remembrance</b>—<i>Greek,</i> "Babylon the great was
	remembered" (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="re+18:5">Revelation 18:5</span>). It
	is now that the last call to escape from Babylon is given to God's
	people in her (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="re+18:4">Revelation 18:4</span>). 
	</p>
<p><b>fierceness</b>—the <i>boiling
	over</i> outburst of His wrath (<i>Greek,</i> "<i>thumou
	orgees</i>"), compare <i>Note,</i> see on <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="re+18:4- :">Revelation 18:4- :</span>.</p>
