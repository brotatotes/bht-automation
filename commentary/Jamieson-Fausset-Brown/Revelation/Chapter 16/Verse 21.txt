<p><b>21. fell</b>—<i>Greek,</i>
	"descends." 
	</p>
<p><b>upon men</b>—<i>Greek,</i>
	"<i>the</i> men." 
	</p>
<p><b>and men blasphemed God</b>—not
	those struck who died, but the rest. Unlike the result in the case of
	Jerusalem (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="re+11:13">Revelation 11:13</span>), where
	"the remnant . . . affrighted . . . gave glory to the God of
	heaven." 
	</p>
<p><b>was</b>—<i>Greek,</i> "is."</p>
