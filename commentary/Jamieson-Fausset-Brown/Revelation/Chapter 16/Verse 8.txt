<p><b>8. angel</b>—so <i>Coptic</i> and
	ANDREAS. But A, B, C,
	<i>Vulgate,</i> and <i>Syriac</i> omit it. 
	</p>
<p><b>upon</b>—not as in <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="re+16:2">Revelation 16:2</span>;
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="re+16:3">Revelation 16:3</span>, "into." 
	</p>
<p><b>sun</b>—Whereas by the
	fourth trumpet the sun is darkened (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="re+16:3- :">Revelation 16:3- :</span>) in a third part, here by the fourth vial the sun's bright
	scorching power is intensified. 
	</p>
<p><b>power was given unto
	him</b>—rather, "unto <i>it,</i>" the sun. 
	</p>
<p><b>men</b>—<i>Greek,</i> "the
	men," namely, those who had the mark of the beast (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="re+16:3- :">Revelation 16:3- :</span>).</p>
