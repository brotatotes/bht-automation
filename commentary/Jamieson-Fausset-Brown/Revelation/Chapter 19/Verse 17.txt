<p><b>17. an</b>—<i>Greek,</i> "one."
	</p>
<p><b>in the sun</b>—so as to be
	conspicuous in sight of the whole world. 
	</p>
<p><b>to all the fowls</b>— (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>). 
	</p>
<p><b>and gather yourselves</b>—A,
	B, <i>Vulgate, Syriac, Coptic,</i> and ANDREAS
	read, "be gathered," omitting "and." 
	</p>
<p><b>of the great God</b>—A, B,
	<i>Vulgate, Syriac, Coptic,</i> and ANDREAS
	read, "the great supper (that is, banquet) of God."</p>
