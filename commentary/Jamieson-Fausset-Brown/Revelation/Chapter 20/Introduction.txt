</p>
<p><span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>. SATAN BOUND,
	AND THE FIRST-RISEN
	SAINTS REIGN
	WITH CHRIST, A
	THOUSAND YEARS;
	SATAN LOOSED,
	GATHERS THE NATIONS,
	GOG AND MAGOG,
	ROUND THE CAMP
	OF THE SAINTS, AND
	IS FINALLY
	CONSIGNED TO THE LAKE
	OF FIRE; THE
	GENERAL RESURRECTION
	AND LAST JUDGMENT.</p>
