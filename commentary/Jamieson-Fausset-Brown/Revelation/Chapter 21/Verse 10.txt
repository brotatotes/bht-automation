<p><b>10.</b> The words correspond to <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>, to heighten the contrast of the bride and harlot. 
	</p>
<p><b>mountain</b>—Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>, where a similar vision is given from a <i>high mountain.</i>
</p>
<p><b>that great</b>—omitted in
	A, B, <i>Vulgate, Syriac, Coptic,</i> and CYPRIAN.
	Translate then, "the holy city Jerusalem." 
	</p>
<p><b>descending</b>—Even in the
	millennium the earth will not be a suitable abode for transfigured
	saints, who therefore shall then reign in heaven over the earth. But
	after the renewal of the earth at the close of the millennium and
	judgment, they shall <i>descend</i> from heaven to dwell on an earth
	assimilated to heaven itself. "From God" implies that "we
	(the city) are God's workmanship."</p>
