<p><b>17. hundred . . . forty . . . four
	cubits</b>—twelve times twelve: the Church-number squared. The wall
	is far beneath the height of the city. 
	</p>
<p><b>measure of a man, that is, of
	the angel</b>—The ordinary measure used by <i>men</i> is the
	measure here used by the <i>angel,</i> distinct from "the
	measure of the sanctuary." Men shall then be <i>equal to the
	angels.</i></p>
