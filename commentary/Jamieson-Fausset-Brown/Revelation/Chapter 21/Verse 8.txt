<p><b>8. the fearful</b>—<i>Greek,</i>
	"the cowardly," who do not <i>quit themselves like men</i>
	so as to "overcome" in the good fight; who have the spirit
	of slavish "fear," not love, towards God; and who through
	fear of man are not bold for God, or "draw back." Compare
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="re+21:27">Revelation 21:27</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="re+22:15">Revelation 22:15</span>.
	</p>
<p><b>unbelieving</b>—<i>Greek,</i>
	"faithless." 
	</p>
<p><b>abominable</b>—who have
	drank of the harlot's "cup of abominations." 
	</p>
<p><b>sorcerers</b>—one of the
	characteristics of Antichrist's time. 
	</p>
<p><b>all liars</b>—<i>Greek,</i>
	"all <i>the</i> liars": or else "all <i>who are</i>
	liars"; compare <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1ti+4:1">1 Timothy 4:1</span>;
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1ti+4:2">1 Timothy 4:2</span>, where similarly <i>lying</i>
	and dealings with <i>spirits</i> and <i>demons,</i> are joined
	together as features of "the latter times." 
	</p>
<p><b>second death</b>— <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1ti+4:2- :">1 Timothy 4:2- :</span>: "<i>everlasting</i> destruction," <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="2th+1:9">2 Thessalonians 1:9</span>;
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="mr+9:44">Mark 9:44</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="mr+9:46">Mark 9:46</span>;
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="mr+9:48">Mark 9:48</span>, "Where THEIR
	worm dieth not, and the fire is not quenched."</p>
