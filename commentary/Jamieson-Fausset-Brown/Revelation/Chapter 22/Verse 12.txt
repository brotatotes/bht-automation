<p><b>12. And</b>—in none of our
	manuscripts. But A, B, <i>Vulgate, Syriac, Coptic,</i> and CYPRIAN
	omit it. 
	</p>
<p><b>behold, I come
	quickly</b>—(Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="re+22:7">Revelation 22:7</span>).
	</p>
<p><b>my reward is with me</b>—
	(<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="isa+40:10">Isaiah 40:10</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="isa+62:11">Isaiah 62:11</span>).
	</p>
<p><b>to give</b>—<i>Greek,</i>
	"to render." 
	</p>
<p><b>every man</b>—<i>Greek,</i>
	"to <i>each.</i>" 
	</p>
<p><b>shall be</b>—so B in MAI.
	But B in TISCHENDORF, and
	A, <i>Syriac,</i> read, "is."</p>
