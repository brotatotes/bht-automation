	earnest shall be given in the millennium (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>). God can only dwell where the curse and its cause, the

	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="ga+3:13">Galatians 3:13</span>) shall be in it."
	Compare in the millennium, <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="ga+3:13- :">Galatians 3:13- :</span>.
