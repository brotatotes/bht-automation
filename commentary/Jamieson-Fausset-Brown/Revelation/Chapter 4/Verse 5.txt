<p><b>5. proceeded</b>-<i>Greek,</i>
	"proceed." 
	</p>
<p><b>thunderings and voices</b>—The
	two oldest manuscripts transpose, "voices and thunderings."
	Compare at the giving of the law on Sinai, <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>. "The <i>thunderings</i> express God's threats against
	the ungodly: there are voices in the thunders (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>), that is, not only does He threaten generally, but also
	predicts <i>special</i> judgments" [GROTIUS].
	</p>
<p><b>seven lamps . . . seven
	Spirits</b>—The Holy Spirit in His sevenfold operation, as the
	light-and-life Giver (compare <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>, <i>seven eyes . . . the seven Spirits of God;</i> <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="re+1:4">Revelation 1:4</span>;
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="re+21:23">Revelation 21:23</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="ps+119:105">Psalms 119:105</span>)
	and fiery purifier of the godly, and consumer of the ungodly (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="ps+119:105- :">Psalms 119:105- :</span>).</p>
