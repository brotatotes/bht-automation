<p><b>3. no man</b>—<i>Greek,</i> "no
	<i>one.</i>" Not merely <i>no man,</i> but also <i>no one</i> of
	any order of beings. 
	</p>
<p><b>in earth</b>—<i>Greek,</i>
	"upon the earth." 
	</p>
<p><b>under the earth</b>—namely,
	in Hades. 
	</p>
<p><b>look thereon</b>—to look
	upon the contents, so as to read them.</p>
