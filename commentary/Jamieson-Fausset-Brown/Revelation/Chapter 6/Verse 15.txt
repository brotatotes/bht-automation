<p><b>15. kings . . . hid themselves</b>—Where
	was now the spirit of those whom the world has so greatly feared?
	[BENGEL]. 
	</p>
<p><b>great men</b>—statesmen and
	high civil officers. 
	</p>
<p><b>rich men . . . chief
	captains</b>—The three oldest manuscripts, A, B, C, transpose thus,
	"chief captains . . . rich men." 
	</p>
<p><b>mighty</b>—The three oldest
	manuscripts, A, B, and C read, "strong" physically (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>). 
	</p>
<p><b>in</b>—literally "into";
	ran <i>into,</i> so as to <i>hide themselves</i> in. 
	</p>
<p><b>dens</b>—"caves."</p>
