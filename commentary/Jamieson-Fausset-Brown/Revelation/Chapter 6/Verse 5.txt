<p><b>5. Come and see</b>—The two oldest
	manuscripts, A, C, and <i>Vulgate</i> omit "and see." B
	retains the words. 
	</p>
<p><b>black</b>—implying <i>sadness</i>
	and <i>want.</i>
</p>
<p><b>had</b>—<i>Greek,</i>
	"having." 
	</p>
<p><b>a pair of balances</b>—the
	symbol of scarcity of provisions, the bread being doled out by
	weight.</p>
