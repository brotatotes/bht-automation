<p><b>12. third part</b>—not a <i>total</i>
	obscuration as in the sixth seal (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="re+6:12">Revelation 6:12</span>;
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="re+6:13">Revelation 6:13</span>). This <i>partial</i>
	obscuration, therefore, comes between the prayers of the martyrs
	under the fifth seal, and the last overwhelming judgments on the
	ungodly under the sixth seal, at the eve of Christ's coming. 
	</p>
<p><b>the night likewise</b>—withdrew
	a third part of the light which the bright Eastern moon and stars
	ordinarily afford.</p>
