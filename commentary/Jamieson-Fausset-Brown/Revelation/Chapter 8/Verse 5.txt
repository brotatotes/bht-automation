5. cast it into the earth—that
	is, unto the earth: the hot coals off the altar cast on the
	earth, symbolize God's fiery judgments about to descend on the
	Church's foes in answer to the saints' incense-perfumed prayers which
	have just ascended before God, and those of the martyrs. How
	marvellous the power of the saints' prayers! 
	there were—"there
	took place," or "ensued."
