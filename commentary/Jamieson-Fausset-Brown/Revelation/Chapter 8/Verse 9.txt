<p><b>9.</b> The symbolical interpreters
	take <i>the ships</i> here to be <i>churches.</i> For the <i>Greek</i>
	here for ships is not the common one, but that used in the Gospels of
	the apostolic vessel in which Christ taught: and the first churches
	were in the shape of an inverted ship: and the <i>Greek</i> for
	<i>destroyed</i> is also used of heretical <i>corruptings</i> (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>).</p>
