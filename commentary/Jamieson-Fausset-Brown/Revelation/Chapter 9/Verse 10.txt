<p><b>10. tails like unto scorpions</b>—like
	unto <i>the tails of</i> scorpions. 
	</p>
<p><b>and there were stings</b>—There
	is no oldest manuscript for this reading. A, B, <i>Aleph, Syriac,</i>
	and <i>Coptic</i> read, "and (they have) stings: and in their
	tails (is) their power (literally, 'authority': authorized power) to
	hurt."</p>
