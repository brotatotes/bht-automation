<p><b>14. in,</b> c.—<i>Greek,</i> "<i>epi
	to potamo</i>" "on," or "at the great river."
	</p>
<p><b>Euphrates</b>—(Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>). The river whereat Babylon, the ancient foe of God's
	people was situated. Again, whether from the literal region of the
	Euphrates, or from the spiritual Babylon (<i>the apostate Church,</i>
	especially ROME), four
	angelic ministers of God's judgments shall go forth, assembling an
	army of horsemen throughout the four quarters of the earth, to slay a
	third of men, the brunt of the visitation shall be on Palestine.</p>
