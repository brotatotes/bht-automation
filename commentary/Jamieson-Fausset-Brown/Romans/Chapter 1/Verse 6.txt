<p><b>6. Among whom are ye also</b>—that
	is, along with others; for the apostle ascribes nothing special to
	the Church of Rome (compare <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>) [BENGEL]. 
	</p>
<p><b>the called</b>—(See on <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>). 
	</p>
<p><b>of Christ Jesus</b>—that
	is, either called "<i>by</i> Him" (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>), or the called "<i>belonging</i> to Him";
	"Christ's called ones." Perhaps this latter sense is best
	supported, but one hardly knows which to prefer.</p>
