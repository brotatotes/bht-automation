<p><b>4. For Christ is the end</b>—the
	object or aim. 
	</p>
<p><b>of the law for</b>—justifying
	</p>
<p><b>righteousness to every one
	that believeth</b>—that is, contains within Himself all that the
	law demands for the justification of such as embrace Him, whether Jew
	or Gentile (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="ga+3:24">Galatians 3:24</span>).</p>
