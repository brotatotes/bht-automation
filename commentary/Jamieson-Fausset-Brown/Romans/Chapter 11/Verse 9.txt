<p><b>9. And David saith</b>— (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>), which in such a Messianic psalm must be meant of the
	rejecters of Christ. 
	</p>
<p><b>Let their table,</b> &amp;c.—that
	is, Let their very blessings prove a curse to them, and their
	enjoyments only sting and take vengeance on them.</p>
