<p><b>14. Bless</b>—that is, Call down
	by prayer a blessing on. 
	</p>
<p><b>them which persecute you,</b>
	&amp;c.—This is taken from the Sermon on the Mount (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>), which, from the allusions made to it, seems to have been
	the storehouse of Christian morality among the churches.</p>
