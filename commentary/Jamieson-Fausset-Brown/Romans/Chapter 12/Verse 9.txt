<p><b>9. Let love be without
	dissimulation</b>—"Let your love be unfeigned" (as in
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="2co+6:6">2 Corinthians 6:6</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1pe+2:22">1 Peter 2:22</span>;
	and see <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1jo+3:18">1 John 3:18</span>). 
	</p>
<p><b>Abhor that which is evil;
	cleave to that which is good</b>—What a lofty tone of moral
	principle and feeling is here inculcated! It is not, Abstain from the
	one, and do the other; nor, Turn away from the one, and draw to the
	other; but, Abhor the one, and cling, with deepest sympathy, to the
	other.</p>
