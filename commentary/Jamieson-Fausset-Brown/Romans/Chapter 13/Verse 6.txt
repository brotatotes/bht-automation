<p><b>6, 7. For, for this cause pay
	ye</b>—rather, "ye pay" 
	</p>
<p><b>tribute also</b>—that is,
	"This is the reason why ye pay the contributions requisite for
	maintaining the civil government." 
	</p>
<p><b>for they are God's ministers,
	attending continually upon this very thing</b>—"to this very
