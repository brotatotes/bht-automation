<p><b>7. Render therefore to all their
	dues</b>—From magistrates the apostle now comes to other officials,
	and from them to men related to us by whatever tie. 
	</p>
<p><b>tribute</b>—land tax. 
	</p>
<p><b>custom</b>—mercantile tax. 
	</p>
<p><b>fear</b>—reverence for
	superiors. 
	</p>
<p><b>honour</b>—the respect due
	to persons of distinction.</p>
