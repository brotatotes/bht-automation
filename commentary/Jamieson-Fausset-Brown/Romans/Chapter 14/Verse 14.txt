<p><b>14, 15. I know, and am persuaded
	by</b>—or rather, "in" 
	</p>
<p><b>the Lord Jesus</b>—as
	"having the mind of Christ" (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>). 
	</p>
<p><b>that there is nothing unclean
	of itself</b>—Hence it is that he calls those "the strong"
	who believed in the abolition of all ritual distinctions under the
	Gospel. (See <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="ac+10:15">Acts 10:15</span>). 
	</p>
<p><b>but</b>—"save that"
	</p>
<p><b>to him that esteemeth
	anything to be unclean, to him it is unclean</b>—"and
	therefore, though <i>you</i> can eat of it with out sin, <i>he
	cannot.</i>"</p>
