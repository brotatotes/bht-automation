<p><b>18. For he that in these things</b>—"in
	this," meaning this threefold life. 
	</p>
<p><b>serveth Christ</b>—Here
	again observe how, though we do these three things as a "kingdom
	of <i>God,</i>" yet it is "<i>Christ</i>" that we
	serve in so doing; the apostle passing here from God to Christ as
	naturally as before from Christ to God—in a way to us
	inconceivable, if Christ had been viewed as a mere creature (compare
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="2co+8:21">2 Corinthians 8:21</span>). 
	</p>
<p><b>is acceptable to God, and
	approved of men</b>—these being the things which God delights in,
	and men are constrained to approve. (Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="pr+3:4">Proverbs 3:4</span>;
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="lu+2:52">Luke 2:52</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="ac+2:47">Acts 2:47</span>;
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="ac+19:20">Acts 19:20</span>).</p>
