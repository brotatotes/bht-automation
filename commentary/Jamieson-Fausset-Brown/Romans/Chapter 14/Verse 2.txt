<p><b>2. one believeth that he may eat all
	things</b>—See <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="ac+10:16">Acts 10:16</span>. 
	</p>
<p><b>another, who is weak, eateth
	herbs</b>—restricting himself probably to a vegetable diet, for
	fear of eating what might have been offered to idols, and so would be
	unclean. (See <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1co+8:1-13">1 Corinthians 8:1-13</span>).</p>
