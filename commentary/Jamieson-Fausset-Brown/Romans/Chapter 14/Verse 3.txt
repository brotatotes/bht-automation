<p><b>3. Let not him that eateth
	despise</b>—look down superciliously upon "him that eateth
	not." 
	</p>
<p><b>and let not him that eateth
	not judge</b>—sit in judgment censoriously upon "him that
	eateth." 
	</p>
<p><b>for God hath received him</b>—as
	one of His dear children, who in this matter acts not from laxity,
	but religious principle.</p>
