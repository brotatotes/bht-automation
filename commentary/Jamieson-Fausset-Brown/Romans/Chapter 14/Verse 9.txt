<p><b>9. For to this end Christ both,</b>
	&amp;c.—The true reading here is, To this end Christ died and lived
	("again"). 
	</p>
<p><b>that he might be Lord both of
	the dead and</b>—"and of the" 
	</p>
<p><b>living</b>—The grand object
	of His death was to <i>acquire</i> this absolute Lordship over His
	redeemed, both in their living and in their dying, as His of right.</p>
