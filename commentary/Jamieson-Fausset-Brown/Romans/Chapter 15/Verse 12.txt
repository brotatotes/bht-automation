<p><b>12. And again, Esaias saith</b>—
	(<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="isa+11:10">Isaiah 11:10</span>). 
	</p>
<p><b>There shall be a</b>—"the"
	</p>
<p><b>root of Jesse</b>—meaning,
	not "He from whom Jesse sprang," but "He that is
	sprung from Jesse" (that is, Jesse's son David)—see <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="isa+11:10- :">Isaiah 11:10- :</span>. 
	</p>
<p><b>and he that shall rise,</b>
	&amp;c.—So the <i>Septuagint</i> in substantial, though not verbal,
	agreement with the original.</p>
