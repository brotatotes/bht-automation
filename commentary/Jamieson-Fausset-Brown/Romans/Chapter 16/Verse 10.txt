<p><b>10. Salute Apelles approved</b>—"the
	approved" 
	</p>
<p><b>in Christ</b>—or, as we
	should say, "that tried Christian"; a noble commendation. 
	</p>
<p><b>Salute them which are of
	Aristobulus' </b><i><b>household</b></i><b> </b>—It would seem,
	from what is said of Narcissus in <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>, that this Aristobulus himself had not been a Christian;
	but that the Christians of his household simply were meant; very
	possibly some of his slaves.</p>
