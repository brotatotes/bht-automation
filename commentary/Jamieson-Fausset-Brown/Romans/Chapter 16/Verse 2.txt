<p><b>2. Receive her in the Lord</b>—that
	is, as a genuine disciple of the Lord Jesus. 
	</p>
<p><b>as</b>—"so as" 
	</p>
<p><b>becometh saints</b>—so as
	saints should receive saints. 
	</p>
<p><b>assist her in whatsoever
	business she hath</b>—"may have" 
	</p>
<p><b>need of you</b>—some
	private business of her own. 
	</p>
<p><b>for she hath been a succourer
	of many, and of myself also</b>—(See <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="ps+41:1-3">Psalms 41:1-3</span>;
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="2ti+1:16-18">2 Timothy 1:16-18</span>).</p>
