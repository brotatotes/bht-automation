<p><b>3-5. Salute Priscilla</b>—The true
	reading here is "Prisca" (as in <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>), a contracted form of Priscilla, as "Silas" of
	"Silvanus." 
	</p>
<p><b>and Aquila my helpers</b>—The
	wife is here named before the husband (as in <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="ac+18:18">Acts 18:18</span>;
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="ro+16:26">Romans 16:26</span>, according to the true
	reading; also in <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="2ti+4:19">2 Timothy 4:19</span>),
	probably as being the more prominent and helpful to the Church.</p>
