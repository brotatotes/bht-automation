<p><b>16. In the day,</b> c.—Here the
	unfinished statement of <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="ro+2:12">Romans 2:12</span>
	is resumed and closed. 
	</p>
<p><b>shall judge the secrets of
	men</b>—here specially referring to the unfathomed depths of
	hypocrisy in the self-righteous whom the apostle had to deal with.
	(See <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="ec+12:14">Ecclesiastes 12:14</span> <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1co+4:5">1 Corinthians 4:5</span>).
	</p>
<p><b>according to my gospel</b>—to
	my teaching as a preacher of the Gospel.</p>
