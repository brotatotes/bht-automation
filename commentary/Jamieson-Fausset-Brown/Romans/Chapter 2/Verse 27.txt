<p><b>25-29. For circumcision</b>—that
	is, One's being within the covenant of which circumcision was the
	outward sign and seal. 
	</p>
<p><b>verily profiteth, if thou
	keep the law</b>—if the inward reality correspond to the outward
	sign. 
	</p>
<p><b>but if,</b> &amp;c.—that
	is, "Otherwise, thou art no better than the uncircumcised
	heathen."</p>
