7-10. To them who, c.—The
	substance of these verses is that the final judgment will turn upon
	character alone. 
	by patient continuance in
	well-doing, &c.—Compare  :-: "That on the good ground are they, which in an honest
	and good heart, having heard the word, keep it, and bring forth fruit
	with patience" denoting the enduring and
	progressive character of the new life.
