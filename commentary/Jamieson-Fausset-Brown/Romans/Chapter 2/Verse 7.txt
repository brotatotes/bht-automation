<p><b>7-10. To them who,</b> c.—The
	substance of these verses is that the final judgment will turn upon
	<i>character</i> alone. 
	</p>
<p><b>by patient continuance in
	well-doing,</b> &amp;c.—Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>: "That on the good ground are they, which in an honest
	and good heart, having heard the word, keep it, and bring forth fruit
	<i>with patience</i>" denoting the <i>enduring</i> and
	<i>progressive</i> character of the new life.</p>
