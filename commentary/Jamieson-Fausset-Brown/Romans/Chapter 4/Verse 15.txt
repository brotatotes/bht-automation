<p><b>15. Because the law worketh
	wrath</b>—has nothing to give to those who break is but
	condemnation and vengeance. 
	</p>
<p><b>for where there is no law,
	there is no transgression</b>—It is just the law that makes
	transgression, in the case of those who break it; nor can the one
	exist without the other.</p>
