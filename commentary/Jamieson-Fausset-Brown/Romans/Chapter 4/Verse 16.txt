<p><b>16, 17. Therefore,</b> &amp;c.—A
	general summary: "Thus justification is by <i>faith,</i> in
	order that its purely <i>gracious</i> character may be seen, and that
	all who follow in the steps of Abraham's faith—whether of his
	natural seed or no—may be assured of the like justification with
	the parent believer."</p>
