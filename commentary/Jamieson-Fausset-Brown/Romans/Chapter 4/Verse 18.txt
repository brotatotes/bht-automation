<p><b>18-22. Who against hope</b>—when
	no ground for hope appeared. 
	</p>
<p><b>believed in hope</b>—that
	is, cherished the believing expectation. 
	</p>
<p><b>that he might become the
	father of many nations, according to that which was spoken, So shall
	thy seed be</b>—that is, Such "as the stars of heaven,"
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="ge+15:5">Genesis 15:5</span>.</p>
