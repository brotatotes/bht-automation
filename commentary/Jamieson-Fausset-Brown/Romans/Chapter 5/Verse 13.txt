<p><b>13, 14. For until the law sin was in
	the world</b>—that is during all the period from Adam "until
	the law" of Moses was given, God continued to treat men as
	sinners. 
	</p>
<p><b>but sin is not imputed where
	there is no law</b>—"There must therefore have been a law
	during that period, because sin <i>was</i> then imputed"; as is
	now to be shown.</p>
