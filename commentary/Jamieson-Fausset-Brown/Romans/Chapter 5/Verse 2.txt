<p><b>2. By whom also we have</b>—"have
	had" 
	</p>
<p><b>access by faith into this
	grace</b>—favor with God. 
	</p>
<p><b>wherein we stand</b>—that
	is "To that same faith which <i>first</i> gave us 'peace with
	God' we owe our introduction into that <i>permanent standing</i> in
	the favor of God which the justified enjoy." As it is difficult
	to distinguish this from the peace first mentioned, we regard it as
	merely an additional phase of the same [MEYER,
	PHILIPPI, MEHRING],
	rather than something new [BEZA,
	THOLUCK, HODGE].
	</p>
<p><b>and rejoice</b>—"glory,"
	"boast," "triumph"—"rejoice" is not
	strong enough. 
	</p>

	God</b>—On "hope," see on <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>.</p>
