<p><b>8. But God commendeth</b>—"setteth
	off," "displayeth"—in glorious contrast with all
	that men will do for each other. 
	</p>
<p><b>his love toward us, in that,
	while we were yet sinners</b>—that is, in a state not of positive
	"goodness," nor even of negative "righteousness,"
	but on the contrary, "sinners," a state which His soul
	hateth. 
	</p>
<p><b>Christ died for us</b>—Now
	comes the overpowering inference, emphatically redoubled.</p>
