<p><b>16. that to whom ye yield yourselves
	servants to obey</b>—with the view of obeying him. 
	</p>
<p><b>his servants ye are to whom
	ye obey</b>—to whom ye yield that obedience. 
	</p>
<p><b>whether of Sin unto
	death</b>—that is, "issuing in death," in the awful sense
	of <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="ro+8:6">Romans 8:6</span>, as the sinner's final
	condition. 
	</p>
<p><b>or of Obedience unto
	righteousness</b>—that is, obedience resulting in a righteous
	character, as the enduring condition of the servant of new Obedience
	(<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1jo+2:17">1 John 2:17</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="joh+8:34">John 8:34</span>;
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="2pe+2:19">2 Peter 2:19</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="mt+6:24">Matthew 6:24</span>).</p>
