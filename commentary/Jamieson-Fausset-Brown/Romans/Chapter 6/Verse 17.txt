17. But God be thanked, that ye were
	the servants of Sin—that is, that this is a state of things now
	past and gone. 
	but ye have obeyed from the
	heart that form of doctrine which was delivered you—rather,
	"whereunto ye were delivered" (Margin), or cast, as
	in a mould. The idea is, that the teaching to which they had heartily
	yielded themselves had stamped its own impress upon them.
