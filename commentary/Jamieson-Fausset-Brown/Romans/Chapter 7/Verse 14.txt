<p><b>14. For we know that the law is
	spiritual</b>—in its demands. 
	</p>
<p><b>but I am carnal</b>—fleshly
	(see on <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>), and as such,
	incapable of yielding spiritual obedience. 
	</p>
<p><b>sold under sin</b>—enslaved
	to it. The "I" here, though of course not the <i>regenerate,</i>
	is neither the <i>unregenerate,</i> but the sinful principle of the
	renewed man, as is expressly stated in <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>.</p>
