<p><b>15, 16. For,</b> c.—better, "For
	that which I do I know not" that is, "In obeying the
	impulses of my carnal nature I act the slave of another will than my
	own as a renewed man?" 
	</p>
<p><b>for,</b> &amp;c.—rather,
	"for not what I would (wish, desire) that do I, but what I hate
	that I do."</p>
