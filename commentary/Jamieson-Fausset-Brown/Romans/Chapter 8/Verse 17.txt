<p><b>17. And if children, then
	heirs</b>—"heirs also." 
	</p>
<p><b>heirs of God</b>—of our
	Father's kingdom. 
	</p>
<p><b>and joint-heirs with
	Christ</b>—as the "First-born among many brethren" (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>), and as "Heir of all things" (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>). 
	</p>
<p><b>if so be that we
	suffer</b>—"provided we be suffering with Him." 
	</p>
<p><b>that we may be also glorified
	together</b>—with Him. This necessity of conformity to Christ in
	suffering in order to participate in His glory, is taught alike by
	Christ Himself and by His apostles (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="joh+12:24-26">John 12:24-26</span>;
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="mt+16:24">Matthew 16:24</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="mt+16:25">Matthew 16:25</span>;
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="2ti+2:12">2 Timothy 2:12</span>).</p>
