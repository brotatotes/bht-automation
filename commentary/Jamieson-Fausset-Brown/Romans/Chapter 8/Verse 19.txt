<p><b>19-22. For,</b> c.—"The
	apostle, fired with the thought of the future glory of the saints,
	pours forth this splendid passage, in which he represents the whole
	creation groaning under its present degradation, and looking and
	longing for the revelation of this glory as the end and consummation
	of its existence" [HODGE].
	</p>
<p><b>the earnest
	expectation</b>—(compare <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>). 
	</p>
<p><b>of the creature</b>—rather,
	"the creation." 
	</p>
<p><b>waiteth for the
	manifestation</b>—"is waiting for the revelation" 
	</p>
<p><b>of the sons of God</b>—that
	is, "for the redemption of their bodies" from the grave (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>), which will reveal their sonship, now hidden (compare
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="lu+20:36">Luke 20:36</span> <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="re+21:7">Revelation 21:7</span>).</p>
