<p><span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>. THE BEARING
	OF THE FOREGOING
	TRUTHS UPON THE CONDITION
	AND DESTINY OF THE
	CHOSEN PEOPLE—ELECTION—THE
	CALLING OF THE GENTILES.
	 
	</p>
<p>Too well aware that he was
	regarded as a traitor to the dearest interests of his people (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="ac+21:33">Acts 21:33</span>;
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="ac+22:22">Acts 22:22</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="ac+25:24">Acts 25:24</span>),
	the apostle opens this division of his subject by giving vent to his
	real feelings with extraordinary vehemence of protestation.</p>
