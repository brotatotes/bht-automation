1, 2. I say the truth in Christ—as
	if steeped in the spirit of Him who wept over impenitent and doomed
	Jerusalem (compare Romans 1:9;
	2 Corinthians 12:19; Philippians 1:8).
	my conscience bearing me
	witness in the Holy Ghost—"my conscience as quickened,
	illuminated, and even now under the direct operation of the Holy
	Ghost."
