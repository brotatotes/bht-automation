<p><b>13. This witness</b>—"This
	testimony (though coming from a Cretan) is true." 
	</p>
<p><b>sharply</b>—Gentleness
	would not reclaim so perverse offenders. 
	</p>
<p><b>that they</b>—that <i>those
	seduced</i> by the false teachers may be brought back to <i>soundness</i>
	in the faith. Their malady is strifes about words and questions
	(<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="tit+3:9">Titus 3:9</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1ti+6:4">1 Timothy 6:4</span>).</p>
