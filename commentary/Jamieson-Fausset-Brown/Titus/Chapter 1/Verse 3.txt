<p><b>3. in due times</b>—<i>Greek,</i>
	"in <i>its own seasons,</i>" the seasons appropriate to it,
	and fixed by God for it (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="ac+1:7">Acts 1:7</span>).
	</p>
<p><b>manifested</b>—implying
	that the "promise," <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="ac+1:7- :">Acts 1:7- :</span>, had lain hidden in His eternal purpose heretofore (compare
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="col+1:26">Colossians 1:26</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="2ti+1:9">2 Timothy 1:9</span>;
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="2ti+1:10">2 Timothy 1:10</span>). 
	</p>
<p><b>his word</b>—equivalent to
	"eternal life" (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="tit+1:2">Titus 1:2</span>;
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="joh+5:24">John 5:24</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="joh+6:63">John 6:63</span>;
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="joh+17:3">John 17:3</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="joh+17:17">John 17:17</span>).
	</p>
<p><b>through preaching</b>—<i>Greek,</i>
	"<i>in</i> preaching," of rather as ALFORD
	(see on <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="joh+17:17- :">John 17:17- :</span>), "in the
	(Gospel) <i>proclamation</i> (the thing preached, the Gospel) with
	which I was entrusted." 
	</p>
<p><b>according to</b>—in
	pursuance of (compare <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1ti+1:1">1 Timothy 1:1</span>). 
	</p>
<p><b>of God our Saviour</b>—rather
	as <i>Greek,</i> "of <i>our Saviour God.</i>" <i>God</i> is
	predicated of <i>our Saviour</i> (compare <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="jdg+1:25">Judges 1:25</span>;
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="lu+1:47">Luke 1:47</span>). Also <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="ps+24:5">Psalms 24:5</span>;
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="isa+12:2">Isaiah 12:2</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="isa+45:15">Isaiah 45:15</span>;
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="isa+45:21">Isaiah 45:21</span>, <i>Septuagint.</i>
	Applied to Jesus, <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="tit+1:4">Titus 1:4</span>;
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="tit+2:13">Titus 2:13</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="tit+3:6">Titus 3:6</span>;
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="2ti+1:10">2 Timothy 1:10</span>.</p>
