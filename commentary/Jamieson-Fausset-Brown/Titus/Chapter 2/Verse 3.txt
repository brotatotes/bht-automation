<p><b>3. behaviour</b>—"deportment."
	</p>
<p><b>as becometh holiness</b>—"as
	becometh women consecrated to God" [WAHL]:
	being by our Christian calling priestesses unto God (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="eph+5:3">Ephesians 5:3</span>;
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1ti+2:10">1 Timothy 2:10</span>). "Observant of
	sacred decorum" [BENGEL].
	</p>
<p><b>not false accusers</b>—not
	slanderers: a besetting sin of some elderly women. 
	</p>
<p><b>given to much wine</b>—the
	besetting sin of the Cretans (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1ti+2:10- :">1 Timothy 2:10- :</span>). Literally, "enslaved to much wine." Addiction to
	wine is <i>slavery</i> (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="ro+6:16">Romans 6:16</span>;
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="2pe+2:19">2 Peter 2:19</span>). 
	</p>
<p><b>teachers</b>—in private:
	not in public (<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1co+14:34">1 Corinthians 14:34</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1ti+2:11">1 Timothy 2:11</span>;
	<span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="1ti+2:12">1 Timothy 2:12</span>); influencing for good
	the younger women by precept and example.</p>
