<p><b>14. And . . . also</b>—<i>Greek,</i>
	"But . . . also." Not only <i>thou,</i> but let others also
	of "our" fellow believers (or "whom we have gained
	over at Crete") with thee. 
	</p>
<p><b>for necessary uses</b>—<i>to
	supply the necessary wants</i> of Christian missionaries and
	brethren, according as they stand in need in their journeys for the
	Lord's cause. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="jfb" lang="eng" ref="tit+1:8">Titus 1:8</span>,
	"a lover of hospitality."</p>
