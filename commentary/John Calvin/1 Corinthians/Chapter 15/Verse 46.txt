<p><b>46.</b><i>But this is not first, which is spiritual.</i> “It is necessary,” says he, “that before we are restored in Christ, we derive our origin from Adam, and resemble him. Let us, therefore, not wonder, if we begin with the <i>living soul,</i> for as <i>being born</i> precedes in order <i>being born again,</i> so <i>living</i> precedes <i>rising again.”</i> </p>
