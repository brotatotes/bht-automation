<p><b>26</b><i>Salute all the brethren with an holy kiss</i>. As to the <i>kiss</i>, it was a customary token of salutation, as has been stated elsewhere. <span style="color:#800000;font-weight:bold;"><sup>(623)</sup></span> In these words, however, he declares his affection towards all the saints. </p>
<p><span style="color:#800000;font-weight:bold;">(623)</span> See Calvin on the Corinthians, vol. 2, p. 78. </p>
