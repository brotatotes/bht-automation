<p><b>16</b><i>But avoid profane and unmeaning noises</i> My opinion as to the import of these words has been stated in my commentary on the last chapter of the First Epistle to Timothy; and my readers will find it there. <span style="color:#800000;font-weight:bold;"><sup>(176)</sup></span> </p>
<p><i>For they will grow to greater ungodliness.</i> That he may more effectually deter Timothy from that profane and noisy talkativeness, he states that it is a sort of labyrinth, or rather a deep whirlpool, from which they cannot go out, but into which men plunge themselves more and more. </p>
