<p><b>29.</b>We must also note that which he addeth, that <i>he came without gainsaying</i>, For this is the holy silence of faith, when as without murmuring against God we receive that meekly which he commandeth, abandoning <span style="color:#800000;font-weight:bold;"><sup>(686)</sup></span> all contrary reasons which intrude themselves. </p>
<p><span style="color:#800000;font-weight:bold;">(686)</span> “<blu>Procul abigendo</blu>,” driving far away. </p>
