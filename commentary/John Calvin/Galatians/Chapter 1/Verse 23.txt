<p><b>23.</b><i>Which once he destroyed</i>. This does not mean that <i>faith</i> <span style="color:#800000;font-weight:bold;"><sup>(36)</sup></span> may actually be destroyed, but that he lessened its influence on the minds of weak men. Besides, it is the will, rather than the deed, that is here expressed. </p>
<p><span style="color:#800000;font-weight:bold;">(36)</span> ”The word <grk><blu><span lang="el-GR" style="">πίστις</span></blu></grk> denotes not only the act of believing, but that which is believed.” — Beza. </p>
