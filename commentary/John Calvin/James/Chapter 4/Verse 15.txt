<p><b>15</b><i>If the Lord will</i>. A twofold condition is laid down, “If we shall live so long,” and, “If the Lord will;” because many things may intervene to upset what we may have determined; for we are blind as to all future events. <span style="color:#800000;font-weight:bold;"><sup>(135)</sup></span> By will he means not that which is expressed in the law, but God’s counsel by which he governs all things. </p>
<p><span style="color:#800000;font-weight:bold;">(135)</span> The words may be rendered thus, “If the Lord will, we shall both live and do this or that.” So that living and doing are both dependent on God’s will. </p>
