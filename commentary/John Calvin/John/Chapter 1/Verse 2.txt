<p><b>2.</b><i>He was in the beginning</i>. In order to impress more deeply on our minds what had been already said, the Evangelist condenses the two preceding clauses into a brief summary, that <i>the Speech</i> always <i>was</i>, and that <i>he was with God</i>; so that it may be understood that <i>the beginning</i> was before all time. </p>
