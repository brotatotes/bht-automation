<p><b>29.</b><i>And, having filled a sponge with vinegar, they fixed it on hyssop.</i> When he says that they <i>fixed the sponge on hyssop,</i> the meaning is, that they fastened it to the end of a bunch of <i>hyssop,</i> that it might be raised to Christ’s mouth; for, in that country, <i>hyssops</i> grow as large as small shrubs, <span style="color:#800000;font-weight:bold;"><sup>(180)</sup></span> </p>
<p><span style="color:#800000;font-weight:bold;">(180)</span> “<blu><span lang="fr-FR" style="">Car l’a les hyssopes sont grans comme petits arbnsseaux</span></blu>.” </p>
