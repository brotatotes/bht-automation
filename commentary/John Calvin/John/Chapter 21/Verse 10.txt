<p><b>10.</b><i>Bring some of the fishes which you have now caught.</i> Though the net was filled in a moment, without any great labor on their part yet the taking of them is not ascribed by Christ to the disciples, thus, we call the bread which we daily eat, OUR <i>bread,</i> and yet, by asking that it may be <i>given</i> to us, we acknowledge that it proceeds from the blessing of God, (<span class="scriptRef" com="cal" lang="eng" ref="mt+6:11">Matthew 6:11</span>.) </p>
