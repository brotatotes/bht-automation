<p><b>27.</b><i>They did not know that he spoke to them about the Father.</i> Hence we see how stupid those men are whose understandings are possessed by Satan. Nothing could be more plain than that they were summoned to the judgment-seat of God. But what then? They are altogether blind. This happens daily to other enemies of the Gospel; and such blindness ought to instruct us to walk with fear. <span style="color:#800000;font-weight:bold;"><sup>(229)</sup></span> </p>
