<p><b>17.</b><i>But, beloved</i>. To a most ancient prophecy he now adds the admonitions of the apostles, the memory of whom was recent. As to the verb <grk><blu><span lang="el-GR" style="">μνήσθητε</span></blu></grk>, it makes no great difference, whether you read it as declarative or as an exhortation; for the meaning remains the same, that being fortified by the prediction he quotes, they ought to be terrified. </p>
