<p><b><span class="scriptRef" com="cal" lang="eng" ref="lu+4:15">Luke 4:15</span></b><b>.</b><i>He was glorified by all.</i> This is stated by Luke for the express purpose of informing us, that, from the very commencement, a divine power shone in Christ, and compelled even those, who cherished a malignant spirit of contradiction, to join in admiring him. </p>
