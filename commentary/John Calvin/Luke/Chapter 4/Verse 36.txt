<p><b><span class="scriptRef" com="cal" lang="eng" ref="lu+4:36">Luke 4:36</span></b><b>.</b><i>And trembling came upon all</i> This is the result of the miracle. They are compelled to acknowledge that there is in Christ something more than man, and justly trace the glory and power of the miracle to his doctrine. <i>What speech is this,</i> they say, which even the devils themselves are forced to obey? </p>
