<p><b>3.</b><i>Unless you are converted.</i> To the example of <i>little children</i> must be referred the <i>conversion</i> of which he now speaks. Hitherto they had been too much habituated to the ordinary customs of men; and if they would gain their object, they must pursue a totally different course. <span style="color:#800000;font-weight:bold;"><sup>(499)</sup></span> Every one wished for himself the first or the second rank; but Christ does not allot even the lowest place to any man who does not lose sight of distinctions and <i>humble himself</i> On the contrary, he says, </p>
<p><span style="color:#800000;font-weight:bold;">(499)</span> “<blu><span lang="fr-FR" style="">Il leur est besoin de tourner bride, et de s’accoustumer a tout cela</span></blu>;” — “they must wheel round, and get accustomed to all this.” </p>
