<p><b><span class="scriptRef" com="cal" lang="eng" ref="mt+20:34">Matthew 20:34</span></b><b>.</b><i>And followed him.</i> This was an expression of gratitude, <span style="color:#800000;font-weight:bold;"><sup>(673)</sup></span> when the blind men became <i>followers</i> of Christ; for, though it is uncertain how long they discharged this duty, yet it showed a grateful mind, that they presented themselves to many, in that journey, as mirrors of the grace of Christ. Luke adds, that <i>the people gave praise to God,</i> which tends to prove the certainty of the miracle. </p>
<p><span style="color:#800000;font-weight:bold;">(673)</span> “<blu><span lang="fr-FR" style="">Ceci a este un signe de recognoissance du bien receu de Christ</span></blu>;” — “this was an expression of gratitude for the favor received from Christ.” </p>
