<p><b>48.</b><i>And immediately one ran.</i> As Christ had once refused to drink, it may be conjectured with probability, that it was repeatedly offered to him for the sake of annoyance; though it is also not improbable that <i>the vinegar</i> was held out to him in a cup before he was raised aloft, and that <i>a sponge</i> was afterwards applied to his mouth, while he was hanging on the cross. </p>
