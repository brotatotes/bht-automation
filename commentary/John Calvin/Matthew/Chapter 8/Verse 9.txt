<p><b><span class="scriptRef" com="cal" lang="eng" ref="mt+8:9">Matthew 8:9</span></b><b>.</b><i>For I am a man subject to the power of another</i> This comparison does not imply equality between the two cases, but is taken from the less to the greater. He forms a higher conception of the divine power, which is manifested in Christ, than of the authority which was possessed by himself over servants and soldiers. </p>
