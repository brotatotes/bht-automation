<p><b>6.</b>He again testifies his gratitude, in recording the kindness of Mary to him. Nor is there any doubt but that he commemorates these praises, in order to recommend those whom he praised to the Romans. <span style="color:#800000;font-weight:bold;"><sup>(475)</sup></span> </p>
<p><span style="color:#800000;font-weight:bold;">(475)</span> It is said of Mary, that she “labored much<grk><blu><span lang="el-GR" style="">,” εἰς ἡμᾶς,</span></blu></grk> towards us, or among us; “<blu>inter nos</blu> — among us,” [<u>Beza</u> ]; “<blu>pro nobis</blu> — for us,” [<u>Grotius</u> ]. The reading<grk><blu><span lang="el-GR" style="">εἰς ὑμᾶς,</span></blu></grk> towards you, has many MSS. in its favor, and also<grk><blu><span lang="el-GR" style="">ἐν ὑμῖν,</span></blu></grk> among you. — <i>Ed.</i> </p>
