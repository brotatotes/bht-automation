<p><b>11.</b>The first effect is, that <i>there is none that understands:</i> and then this ignorance is immediately proved, for <i>they seek not God;</i> for empty is the man in whom there is not the knowledge of God, whatever other learning he may possess; yea, the sciences and the arts, which in themselves are good, are empty things, when they are without this groundwork. </p>
