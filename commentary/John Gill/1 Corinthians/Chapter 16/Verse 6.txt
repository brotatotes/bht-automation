<p><strong>And it may be that I will abide</strong>,.... He could not be certain what calls in Providence he might have to some other places, where his presence would be necessary:</p>
<p><strong>yea, and winter with you</strong>; stay all winter with them, for the space of three months, as we read he did in Greece, <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="ac+20:1">Acts 20:1</span>.</p>
<p><strong>That ye may bring me on my journey whithersoever I go</strong>; both by accompanying him in person themselves, in token of honour and respect, and by providing things necessary for his journey; see</p>
<p><span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="ac+15:3">Acts 15:3</span>.</p>
