And it may be that I will abide,.... He could not be certain what calls in Providence he might have to some other places, where his presence would be necessary:yea, and winter with you; stay all winter with them, for the space of three months, as we read he did in Greece, Acts 20:1.That ye may bring me on my journey whithersoever I go; both by accompanying him in person themselves, in token of honour and respect, and by providing things necessary for his journey; seeActs 15:3.
