<p><strong>For it is better, if the will of God be so</strong>,.... For all things are ordered by the will of God, even all the sufferings and afflictions of the saints; and which is a reason why they ought to be patiently submitted to, and bore: and "better" it is, more honourable and profitable,</p>
<p><strong>that ye suffer for well doing</strong>; for believing in Christ, professing him and his Gospel, giving a free and open reason for so doing, and for exercising a good conscience, and living godly in Christ Jesus:</p>
<p><strong>than for evil doing</strong>; as a murderer, a thief, an evildoer, or a busy body in other men's matters, <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="1pe+4:15">1 Peter 4:15</span>.</p>
