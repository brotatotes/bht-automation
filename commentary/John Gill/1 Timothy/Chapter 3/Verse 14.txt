<p><strong>These things write I unto thee</strong>,.... Concerning the offices of bishops and deacons, their several qualifications, and the rules of judging of persons fit for such service:</p>
<p><strong>hoping to come unto thee shortly</strong>; at Ephesus. He could not tell whether he could come or not, and therefore makes no promise, but hoped he should; and since it was uncertain, he thought fit to write the above things for his instruction and use.</p>
