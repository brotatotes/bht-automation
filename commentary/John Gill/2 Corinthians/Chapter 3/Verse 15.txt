<p><strong>But even unto this day, when Moses is read</strong>,.... These words are an explanation of the former, and show that by the Old Testament is designed, more especially, Moses, or the writings of Moses; which were frequently read, and preached upon in the Jewish synagogues; see <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="ac+13:15">Acts 13:15</span> and that by "the veil untaken away", is meant,</p>
<p><strong>the veil upon their heart</strong>; that is, the veil of blindness, ignorance of Christ, and the Gospel; of the prophecies of the Old Testament, and even of the law itself, its nature, use, and end; preferring the traditions of their fathers, before the written law of Moses.</p>
