<p><strong>And Tychicus have I sent to Ephesus.</strong> To supply the place of Timothy, while he came to Rome, and continued there: so careful was the apostle of the church there, that they might not be without the ministry of the word during his absence; see <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="eph+6:20">Ephesians 6:20</span>.</p>
