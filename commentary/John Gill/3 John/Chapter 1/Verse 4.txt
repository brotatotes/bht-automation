<p><strong>I have no greater joy</strong>,.... Nothing that causes greater joy. The Vulgate Latin version reads "grace" or "thanks"; and then the sense is, that he had nothing to be more thankful for:</p>
<p><strong>than to hear that my children walk in truth</strong>; meaning his spiritual children, those whose conversion he had been the instrument of; and among these it seems Gaius was one.</p>
