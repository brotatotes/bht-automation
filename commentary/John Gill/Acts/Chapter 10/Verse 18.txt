And called, and asked,.... That is, they called to the people of the house, Simon's family; and in a civil and courteous manner asked them,whether Simon, which was surnamed Peter; which they said to distinguish him from Simon the master of the house, and as Cornelius was directed, and they were instructed by him: concerning him they inquired, whether hewere lodged there; the Ethiopic version adds, without any foundation for it, "and the place where they called at was Peter's lodging room".
