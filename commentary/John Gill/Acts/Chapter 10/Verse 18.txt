<p><strong>And called, and asked</strong>,.... That is, they called to the people of the house, Simon's family; and in a civil and courteous manner asked them,</p>
<p><strong>whether Simon, which was surnamed Peter</strong>; which they said to distinguish him from Simon the master of the house, and as Cornelius was directed, and they were instructed by him: concerning him they inquired, whether he</p>
<p><strong>were lodged there</strong>; the Ethiopic version adds, without any foundation for it, "and the place where they called at was Peter's lodging room".</p>
