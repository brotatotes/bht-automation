<p><strong>Arise, therefore, and get thee down</strong>,.... From the top of the house where he was:</p>
<p><strong>and go with them</strong>; the three men, to Caesarea</p>
<p><strong>doubting nothing</strong>; whether it is right or wrong, lawful or unlawful, to go with them, because not Jews, but uncircumcised Gentiles, laying aside all such Jewish scruples:</p>
<p><strong>for I have sent them</strong>: the Spirit of God is said to do what Cornelius did at his instigation and direction, signified by an angel he sent to him, <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="ac+10:5">Acts 10:5</span>.</p>
