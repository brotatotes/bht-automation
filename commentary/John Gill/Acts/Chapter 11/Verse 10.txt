<p><strong>And this was done three times</strong>,.... That is, the voice from heaven answered three times, and encouraged Peter to kill and eat; and so the Ethiopic version renders it, "and he likewise said unto me the third time"; the words as before:</p>
<p><strong>and all were drawn up again into heaven</strong>; the sheet with all the creatures upon it, by the same hands that let it down; the Syriac version renders it, "and all betook themselves to heaven"; as if it all went up to heaven of itself; and the Ethiopic version, "and the whole returned again to heaven"; <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>.</p>
