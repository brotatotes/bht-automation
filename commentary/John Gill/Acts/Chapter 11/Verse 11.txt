<p><strong>And behold, immediately</strong>,.... The moment the vision was over;</p>
<p><strong>there were three men already come unto the house where I was</strong>; they stood before the gate, as in <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="ac+10:17">Acts 10:17</span> and as the Syriac and Ethiopic here have it, and were inquiring for Peter, whether he lodged there; and these men were</p>
<p><strong>sent from Caesarea unto me</strong>; the Syriac version adds, "by Cornelius": now this incident being just at the close of the above vision, served greatly to explain it to the apostle, and encouraged him to do what he had done; and besides which, he had also the express order of the Spirit, for his going with them to Cornelius's house, as follows.</p>
