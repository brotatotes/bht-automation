<p><strong>Then remembered I the word of the Lord, how that he said</strong>,.... Just before his ascension, <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="ac+1:5">Acts 1:5</span>.</p>
<p><strong>John indeed baptized with water, but ye shall be baptized with the Holy Ghost</strong>; as they were on the day of Pentecost, and as now Cornelius and his family were; and since they had the greater baptism, the baptism of the Spirit, the lesser baptism of water could not be denied them.</p>
