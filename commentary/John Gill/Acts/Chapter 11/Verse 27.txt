<p><strong>And in these days came prophets from Jerusalem to Antioch.</strong>, That is, some time in that year that Saul and Barnabas were at Antioch, there came from Jerusalem thither some Christian prophets; for such there were in the Christian church, who had not only a gift of expounding the more mysterious prophecies of the Old Testament, but also of foretelling things to come; see</p>
<p><span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="ac+13:1">Acts 13:1</span>.</p>
