<p><strong>Saying, thou wentest into men uncircumcised</strong>,.... Into the houses of such, and lodged with them, and familiarly conversed with them:</p>
<p><strong>and didst eat with them</strong>; which, according to the traditions of the Jews, were unlawful; <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span> they say nothing about his preaching to them, and baptizing them, because these were so manifestly agreeable to the commission of Christ, in <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="mt+28:19">Matthew 28:19</span> and yet how these could be without the other, is not easy to say.</p>
