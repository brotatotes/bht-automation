<p><strong>Which they also did</strong>,.... They not only determined, but they put their resolutions into execution, and acted according to a rule which the apostle recommends, <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="2co+8:11">2 Corinthians 8:11</span></p>
<p><strong>and sent it to the elders</strong>; to the apostles; for though there were deacons there, yet they chose to send it to them, that they might put it into proper hands to distribute to the necessitous: and this collection they sent</p>
<p><strong>by the hands of Saul and Barnabas</strong>; of this journey to Jerusalem, Paul makes no mention in <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="ga+1:17">Galatians 1:17</span>.</p>
