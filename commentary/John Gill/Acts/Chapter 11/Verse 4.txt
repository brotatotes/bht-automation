<p><strong>But Peter rehearsed the matter from the beginning</strong>,.... Of the vision at Joppa;</p>
<p><strong>and expounded it by order unto them</strong>; or related every particular, in a very methodical and orderly manner: he did not insist upon his authority as an apostle, and much less pretended to a primacy to the rest of the apostles; but submitted to have his case heard, examined, and judged of by the body of the brethren.</p>
