<p><strong>And I heard a voice saying unto me</strong>,.... As he apprehended in his mind; and this voice he heard in the same sense and manner, as the Apostle Paul heard when in a trance, <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="ac+22:17">Acts 22:17</span>.</p>
<p><strong>arise, Peter, slay and eat</strong>; that is, get up and slay some of these creatures upon the sheet, and dress them and eat them.</p>
