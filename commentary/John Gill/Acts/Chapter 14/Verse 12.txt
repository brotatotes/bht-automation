<p><strong>And they called Barnabas Jupiter</strong>,.... The supreme God; it may be because that Barnabas was the oldest man, of the tallest stature, and largest bulk, and made the best figure; whereas Paul was younger, of a low stature, and mean appearance:</p>
<p><strong>and Paul Mercurius</strong>, because he was the chief speaker; Mercury was the god of eloquence, and the messenger of the gods, and the interpreter of their will r; Paul being chiefly concerned in preaching and speaking to the people, they called him by the name of this God: the Jews had a doctor in their schools, whom they called</p>
<p>דאש המדברים, "the chief of the speakers" s.</p>
<p>r Vid Macrob. Saturnal. l. 1. c. 17, 19. s Juchasin, fol. 45. 2. &amp; 46. 1.</p>
