<p><strong>And when there was an assault made</strong>,.... Or a strong bias and inclination were in the minds,</p>
<p><strong>both of the Gentiles, and also of the Jews, with their rulers</strong>; who encouraged them in it:</p>
<p><strong>to use them despitefully, and to stone them</strong>; not only to give them reproachful language, but to smite, buffet, and scourge them, and to stone them for blasphemy, which was a punishment among the Jews for such causes.</p>
