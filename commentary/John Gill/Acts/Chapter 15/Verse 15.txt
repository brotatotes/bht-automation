<p><strong>And to this agree the words of the prophets</strong>,.... In several of the prophecies of the Old Testament, which plainly speak of the calling of the Gentiles; though James thought it sufficient to produce only one instead of many:</p>
<p><strong>as it is written</strong>; in <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="am+9:11">Amos 9:11</span> and this is agreeably to the Jewish way of citing Scriptures, as Surenhusius observes; so it is said q, in twenty four places the priests are called Levites, and this is one of them, <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="eze+44:15">Ezekiel 44:15</span> where only one place is mentioned, instead of the twenty four.</p>
<p>q T. Bab. Yebamot, fol. 86, 2. &amp; Beracot, fol. 4. 1, 2.</p>
