<p><strong>Men that have hazarded their lives</strong>,.... As at Antioch, in Pisidia, where a persecution was raised against them, and they were expelled out of the coasts of that place; and at Lystra, where Paul was stoned, and left for dead, <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="ac+13:50">Acts 13:50</span> and this they did, not for any sinister and selfish ends, for any worldly gain, or vain glory, and popular applause, but</p>
<p><strong>for the name of our Lord Jesus Christ</strong>; for the sake of his Gospel, and the enlargement of his interest, and kingdom in the world; and therefore were worthy of esteem, and ought to be regarded and attended to, and greatly to be preferred to false teachers; who only sought themselves, their worldly honour, and ease, and would sacrifice nothing, nor hazard anything, for the sake of Christ.</p>
