<p><strong>Paul also and Barnabas continued in Antioch</strong>,.... As well as Silas: teaching and preaching the word of the Lord; the Gospel of Christ; not the word of men, but the word of the Lord, of which he is both the author and subject: this they preached in season, and out of season, with power, purity, plainness, and faithfulness.</p>
<p><strong>And many others also</strong>; who either came along with them from Jerusalem, when they brought the letter from thence, or were here before; who came hither upon the persecution raised at the death of Stephen, <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="ac+11:19">Acts 11:19</span> or they were the prophets which afterwards came from Jerusalem thither, <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="ac+11:27">Acts 11:27</span> such as Simeon called Niger, Lucius of Cyrene, and Manaen, <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="ac+13:1">Acts 13:1</span>.</p>
