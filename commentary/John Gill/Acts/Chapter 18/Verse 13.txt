<p><strong>Saying, this fellow persuadeth men to worship God contrary to the law.</strong> Meaning either to the law of the Romans, which forbad the bringing in of any new gods, without the leave of the senate;</p>
<p><span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>; or rather to the law of Moses: the Arabic version reads, "our law"; though this was false, for Moses in his law wrote of Christ, and ordered the children of Israel to hearken to him.</p>
