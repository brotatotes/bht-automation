<p><strong>When they desired him to tarry longer time with them</strong>,.... Either Aquila and Priscilla, whom he left here, for here they were, as is certain from <strong>Ac 18:24,26</strong> or rather the Jews with whom he reasoned, who might be desirous of further conference with him, upon the subject they had disputed about; either in order to gain more knowledge, or in hopes of baffling and confounding him:</p>
<p><strong>he consented not</strong>; for a reason afterwards given.</p>
