<p><strong>But if ye inquire anything concerning other matters</strong>,.... Than what belongs to the craft and business of Demetrius, and the artificers:</p>
<p><strong>it shall be determined in a lawful assembly</strong>; that is, called together according to law, and who have a right to hear, try, and judge causes, which such a confused lawless assembly as this in the theatre had not.</p>
