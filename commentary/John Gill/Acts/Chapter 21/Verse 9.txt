<p><strong>And the same man had four daughters</strong>,.... So that he was a married man, which may be observed against the Papists, who forbid marriage to ecclesiastics: and they were,</p>
<p><strong>virgins</strong>: not under any vow of virginity, but they had not as yet changed their state of life, and were pure and incorrupt:</p>
<p><strong>which did prophesy</strong>; not explain and interpret Scripture, or preach in public assemblies; for these were not allowed women, neither in the Jewish synagogues, nor in Christian assemblies; but they were endowed with a gift of foretelling future events, as was promised such should have in Gospel times, <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="joe+2:28">Joel 2:28</span>.</p>
