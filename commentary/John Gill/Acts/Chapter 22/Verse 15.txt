<p><strong>For thou shalt be his witness unto all men</strong>,.... Gentiles as well as Jews, an eye and an ear witness to them;</p>
<p><strong>of what thou hast seen and heard</strong>; as that he saw him personally and alive, and so could witness to the truth of his resurrection; for after he had been seen by all the apostles, he was last of all seen of Paul; and also, that he heard him and received from him the Gospel, and a mission and commission to preach it; for what he preached he did not receive of man, nor was he taught it by any, but he had it by revelation from Jesus Christ.</p>
