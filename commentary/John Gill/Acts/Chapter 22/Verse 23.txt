<p><strong>And as they cried out</strong>,.... In this furious manner:</p>
<p><strong>and cast off their clothes</strong>; either like madmen, that knew not what they did, or in order to stone him; see <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="ac+7:57">Acts 7:57</span>.</p>
<p><strong>and threw dust into the air</strong> either with their hands, or by striking the earth, and scraping it with their feet, through indignation and wrath, like persons possessed, or mad.</p>
