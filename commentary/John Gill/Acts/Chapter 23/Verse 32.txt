<p><strong>On the morrow they left the horsemen to go with him</strong>,.... That is, the two hundred soldiers, and the two hundred spearmen, who were all on foot, left the seventy horsemen to conduct Paul to Caesarea; for being come to Antipatris, all danger from the Jews was over:</p>
<p><strong>and returned to the castle</strong>; the castle Antonia in Jerusalem, from whence they set out.</p>
