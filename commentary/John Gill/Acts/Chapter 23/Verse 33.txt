<p><strong>Who, when they came to Caesarea</strong>,.... The seventy horsemen:</p>
<p><strong>and delivered the epistle to the governor</strong>; to Felix, governor of Judea, who was now at Caesarea; namely, the letter which Claudius Lysias, the chief captain, sent to him; the form and contents of which are before given:</p>
<p><strong>these presented Paul also before him</strong>; concerning whom, and whose affairs, the letter was.</p>
