<p><strong>Except it be for this one voice</strong>,.... Sentence or expression following, which declares his faith about the resurrection; not that he thought or owned that this was an evil in him, but that it was the only one, that any of them, the Jews, a part among them, namely, the Sadducees, could condemn, or judge to be evil in him:</p>
<p><strong>that I cried, standing among them</strong>; whereby they were thrown into confusion and division among themselves: and it was this,</p>
<p><strong>touching the resurrection of the dead I am called in question by you this day</strong>; see <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="ac+23:6">Acts 23:6</span>.</p>
