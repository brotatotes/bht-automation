<p><strong>But the chief captain Lysias came upon us</strong>,.... Suddenly, and at unawares, immediately, and with great haste, before they could execute their designs; which were not to judge Paul according to law, but to kill him, in the manner the zealots did:</p>
<p><strong>and with great violence took him away out of our hands</strong>; for he came with an army, and rescued him, <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="ac+23:27">Acts 23:27</span> Some copies add, "and sent him to thee"; and so the Syriac version reads.</p>
