<p><strong>And the Jews also assented</strong>,.... That is, Ananias the high priest, and the elders that were with him, agreed to what Tertullus said, and confirmed the same.</p>
<p><strong>Saying, that these things were so</strong>; that Paul was such a person, and was guilty of the crimes he had set forth; and that the chief captain had taken the steps, and done the things he had related.</p>
