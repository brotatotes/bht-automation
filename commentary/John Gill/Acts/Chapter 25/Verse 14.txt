<p><strong>And when they had been there many days</strong>,.... Indulging themselves in pleasure, and spending their time in conversing on various subjects; and in order to carry on the conversation, and pass away time,</p>
<p><strong>Festus declared Paul's case unto the king</strong>; in the following manner:</p>
<p><strong>saying, there is a certain man left in bonds by Felix</strong>; the former governor in Caesarea, meaning Paul.</p>
