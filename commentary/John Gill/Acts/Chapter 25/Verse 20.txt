<p><strong>And because I doubted of such manner of questions</strong>,.... Or was ignorant of them, and knew not what to make of them, or to say to them, and was at an entire loss what to do in this affair:</p>
<p><strong>I asked him whether he would go to Jerusalem, and there be judged of these matters</strong>; before the Jewish sanhedrim, who best understood them.</p>
