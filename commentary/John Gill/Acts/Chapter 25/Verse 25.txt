<p><strong>But when I found that he had committed nothing worthy of death</strong>,.... Which was a public testimony of the apostle's innocence, to the great mortification of his enemies, some of whom might be present; a like testimony was given of him by Lysias, <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="ac+23:29">Acts 23:29</span>.</p>
<p><strong>And that he himself hath appealed unto Augustus</strong>; the Emperor Nero; see <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="ac+25:21">Acts 25:21</span>.</p>
<p><strong>I have determined to send him</strong>; having had the opinion of his council upon it.</p>
