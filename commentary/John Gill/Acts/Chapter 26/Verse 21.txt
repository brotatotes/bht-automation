<p><strong>For these causes the Jews caught me in the temple</strong>,.... He means the Asiatic Jews, who finding him in the temple, laid hold on him, and dragged him out of it: and, as he says, went about to kill me; for no other reason, but for preaching to the Gentiles, and for preaching the above doctrines to them: what he refers to is in</p>
<p><span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="ac+21:27">Acts 21:27</span>.</p>
