<p><strong>And when he had thus spoken</strong>,.... These words are omitted in the Alexandrian copy, the Vulgate Latin, Syriac, and Ethiopic versions:</p>
<p><strong>the king rose up</strong>; from the judgment seat; that is, King Agrippa:</p>
<p><strong>and the governor</strong>; the Roman governor, Festus:</p>
<p><strong>and Bernice</strong>: the sister of King Agrippa:</p>
<p><strong>and they that sat with them</strong>; either in council, or to hear; the chief captains, and principal inhabitants of Caesarea.</p>
