<p><strong>Then the soldiers cut off the ropes of the boat</strong>,.... With which it had been fastened to the sides of the ship, and by which the mariners were letting it down, in order to get into it, and go off:</p>
<p><strong>and let her fall off</strong>; from the sides of the ship into the sea, and so prevented the shipmen quitting the ship; for now they gave more credit to Paul than to them.</p>
