<p><strong>For the man was above forty years old</strong>,.... So that the miracle was the greater, that a man born lame, and who had been so above forty years, should have a cure; and he was the more known to the people, and his testimony met with more credit:</p>
<p><strong>on whom this miracle of healing was showed</strong>; both for the good of men, for the glory of God, and for the confirmation of the Gospel of Christ.</p>
