<p><strong>And kept back part of the price</strong>,.... At which the possession was sold; he reserved it for his own use, after he had given out that he sold it for the service of the church:</p>
<p><strong>his wife also being privy to it</strong>; to this private reserve:</p>
<p><strong>and brought a certain part</strong>; whether the greater part, or an equal part, half of it, or a lesser part; some little part of it, so the phrase seems to signify, is not certain:</p>
<p><strong>and laid it at the apostles' feet</strong>; as the rest did, thereby to make a show of charity, and cover the deceit.</p>
