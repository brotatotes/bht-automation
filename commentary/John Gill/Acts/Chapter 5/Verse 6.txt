And the young men arose,.... The younger brethren of the church, who were robust, and strong, and fit for the following service: these rose up from their seats at once, not willing that such an awful spectacle should lie long before them:and wound him up; in linen clothes, as was the manner of the Jews:and carried him out: of the house where they were, and out of the city; for the burying places of the Jews were without the city:and buried him; which was all done in a very short time, as appears by what follows.
