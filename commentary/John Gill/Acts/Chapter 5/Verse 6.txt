<p><strong>And the young men arose</strong>,.... The younger brethren of the church, who were robust, and strong, and fit for the following service: these rose up from their seats at once, not willing that such an awful spectacle should lie long before them:</p>
<p><strong>and wound him up</strong>; in linen clothes, as was the manner of the Jews:</p>
<p><strong>and carried him out</strong>: of the house where they were, and out of the city; for the burying places of the Jews were without the city:</p>
<p><strong>and buried him</strong>; which was all done in a very short time, as appears by what follows.</p>
