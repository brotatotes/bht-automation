<p><strong>Then they suborned men</strong>,.... Hired false witnesses, which seems to have been commonly done by the Jews; so they did in the case of Christ:</p>
<p><strong>which said, we have heard him speak blasphemous words against Moses, and against God</strong>; that is, against the law of Moses, and so against God, who gave the law to Moses, as appears from <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="ac+6:13">Acts 6:13</span> the blasphemous words seem to be, with respect to the ceremonial law, and the abrogation of it, which Stephen might insist upon, and they charged with blasphemy; see <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="ac+6:14">Acts 6:14</span>.</p>
