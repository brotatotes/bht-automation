<p><strong>And at the second time Joseph was made known to his brethren</strong>,.... That is, when the brethren of Joseph went a second time down to Egypt for corn, Joseph made himself known unto them, <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="ge+45:1">Genesis 45:1</span>.</p>
<p><strong>And Joseph's kindred was made known unto Pharaoh</strong>; for though it was known before that he was an Hebrew, see <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="ge+39:17">Genesis 39:17</span> yet it was not known of what family he was, who was his father, or his brethren, but now it was known, <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="ge+45:16">Genesis 45:16</span>.</p>
