<p><strong>So Jacob went down into Egypt</strong>,.... At the invitation of his son Joseph:</p>
<p><strong>and died, he, and our fathers</strong>; both Jacob and his twelve sons died in Egypt, though we have no account of the death of any of them, but Jacob and Joseph, particularly; only in general, that Joseph died, and all his brethren, and all that generation, <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="ge+49:33">Genesis 49:33</span> <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="ex+1:6">Exodus 1:6</span> the Syriac version adds "there", that is, in Egypt.</p>
