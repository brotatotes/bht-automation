<p><strong>Then said the Lord to him</strong>,.... To Moses, who through curiosity had made too near an approach:</p>
<p><strong>put off thy shoes from thy feet</strong>; in token of humility, obedience, and reverence:</p>
<p><strong>for the place where thou standest is holy ground</strong>; not really, but relatively, on account of the divine presence in it, and only so long as that continued.</p>
