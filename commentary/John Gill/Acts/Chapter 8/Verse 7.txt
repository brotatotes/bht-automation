<p><strong>For unclean spirits</strong>,.... Devils, so called because they were unclean themselves, defiled others, add delighted in impure persons and places; <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span></p>
<p><strong>crying with loud voice</strong>; showing their unwillingness to remove, and the irresistibleness of divine power they could not withstand:</p>
<p><strong>came out of many that were possessed</strong> with them; who had for a long time dwelt in them, and had greatly afflicted them:</p>
<p><strong>and many taken with palsies, and that were lame, were healed</strong>; by Philip, in the name of Christ, through a word speaking, or by touching them, without making use of any means or medicines.</p>
