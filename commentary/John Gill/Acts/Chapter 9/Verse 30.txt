<p><strong>Which when the brethren knew</strong>,.... When the members of the church heard of it, by some means or another:</p>
<p><strong>they brought him down to Caesarea</strong>; not that which was before called Strato's tower, the same as in <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="ac+8:40">Acts 8:40</span> but Caesarea Philippi, mentioned in <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="mt+16:13">Matthew 16:13</span> the Syriac version adds, "by night", and some copies, "and sent him forth to Tarsus"; a city of Cilicia, his own native place, where he might be more safe, and also useful among his friends and acquaintance; <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="ga+1:21">Galatians 1:21</span>.</p>
