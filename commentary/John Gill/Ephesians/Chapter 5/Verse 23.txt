<p><strong>For the husband is the head of the wife</strong>,....</p>
<p><span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>.</p>
<p><strong>Even as Christ is the head of the church</strong>; all the elect;</p>
<p><span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>. And he is the Saviour of the body; not "of our body", as the Ethiopic version reads, of that part of man, which is called the body; though that indeed is redeemed and saved by Christ, as well as the soul; but "of his body", as the Vulgate Latin version reads; that is, of the church, which is his body; see <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="eph+1:23">Ephesians 1:23</span>; of which he is the Saviour; he provides everything for it, preserves and protects it, and has wrought out salvation for it, which every member of it partakes of.</p>
