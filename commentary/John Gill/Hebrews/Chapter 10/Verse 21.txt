<p><strong>And [having an] high priest over the house of God.</strong> The church of God, over which Christ is as prophet, priest, and King, and as the Son and owner of it; <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>;</p>
<p><span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>. In the Greek text it is, "a great priest"; so the Messiah is called by the Targum on <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="zec+6:12">Zechariah 6:12</span> כהן רב, "a great priest", as he is; even a great high priest, as in <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="heb+4:14">Hebrews 4:14</span>, and greater than Aaron, and any of his sons.</p>
