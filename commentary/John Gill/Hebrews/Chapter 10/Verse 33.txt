<p><strong>Partly whilst ye were made a gazing stock</strong>,.... Brought upon the stage or theatre, and made a spectacle to the world, angels, and men, <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="1co+4:9">1 Corinthians 4:9</span></p>
<p><strong>both by reproaches and afflictions</strong>; suffering both in their characters and reputations, and in their persons and substance:</p>
<p><strong>and partly whilst ye became companions of them that were so used</strong>; they maintained their communion with them, relieved them in distress, and sympathized with them.</p>
