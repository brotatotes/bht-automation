Ver. 12 Therefore sprang there even of one,.... That is, Abraham: the Arabic version has here a strange interpolation;"this faith Isaac and Rebecca conceived in mind, and so there were born of one, Esau and Jacob.''And him as good as dead; being an hundred years of age; :-. The Ethiopic version reads, "the bodies of both were like a dead carcass"; both of Abraham and Sarah:so many as the stars of the sky in multitude, and as the sand which is by the sea shore innumerable; as was promised to Abraham,Genesis 15:5 which has been fulfilled, Isaiah 10:22 and will still have a further fulfilment, Hosea 1:10.
