<p>Ver. 12 <strong>Therefore sprang there even of one</strong>,.... That is, Abraham: the Arabic version has here a strange interpolation;</p>
<p>"this faith Isaac and Rebecca conceived in mind, and so there were born of one, Esau and Jacob.''</p>
<p><strong>And him as good as dead</strong>; being an hundred years of age;</p>
<p><span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>. The Ethiopic version reads, "the bodies of both were like a dead carcass"; both of Abraham and Sarah:</p>
<p><strong>so many as the stars of the sky in multitude, and as the sand which is by the sea shore innumerable</strong>; as was promised to Abraham,</p>
<p><span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="ge+15:5">Genesis 15:5</span> which has been fulfilled, <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="isa+10:22">Isaiah 10:22</span> and will still have a further fulfilment, <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="ho+1:10">Hosea 1:10</span>.</p>
