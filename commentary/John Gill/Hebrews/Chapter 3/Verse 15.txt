<p><strong>While it is said today</strong>,.... Exhort one another, and hold fast Christ and his Gospel, and faith and confidence therein; what follows is a repetition of the citation in <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="heb+3:7">Hebrews 3:7</span> in order to make a further improvement of it; which shows, that the words belong to the present times of the Gospel, and contain in them matter of moment, and great concern; and that Scripture instructions and exhortations are of perpetual use.</p>
