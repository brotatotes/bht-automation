<p><strong>Seeing therefore it remaineth</strong>,.... It follows by just consequence,</p>
<p><strong>that some must enter therein</strong>; for God's swearing concerning some, that they should not enter into his rest, supposes that others should: and</p>
<p><strong>they to whom it was first preached</strong>; to whom the Gospel was first preached, namely, the Israelites in the wilderness: entered not in because of unbelief; See <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="heb+3:19">Hebrews 3:19</span>.</p>
