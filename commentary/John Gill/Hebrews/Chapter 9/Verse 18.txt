<p><strong>Whereupon neither the first testament</strong>,.... Or the first administration of the covenant of grace under the law:</p>
<p><strong>was dedicated without blood</strong>; or "confirmed" without it, that dispensation being a typical one; and that blood was typical of the blood of Christ, by which the new covenant or testament is ratified; see <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="ex+24:7">Exodus 24:7</span>.</p>
