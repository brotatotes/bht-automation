<p><strong>Out of the same mouth proceedeth blessing and cursing</strong>,.... Which is not only a contradiction, but unnatural, as well as wicked and sinful:</p>
<p><strong>my brethren, these things ought not so to be</strong>: in any, and much less in professors of religion: such things are unbecoming men, are a scandal upon human nature, and exceeding unworthy of the Christian name; see <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="ps+50:16">Psalms 50:16</span>.</p>
