<p>Ver. 16 <strong>For where envying and strife is</strong>,.... Where these are cherished in the heart, and especially where they break out into action, in families, neighbourhoods, states, or churches:</p>
<p><strong>there is confusion and every evil work</strong>; these occasion disturbances, raise uneasiness, make disquietude, and cause tumults whenever they appear; and put persons upon doing everything that is wicked, to gratify such insatiable lusts.</p>
