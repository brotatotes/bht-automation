<p><strong>And looking upon Jesus as he walked</strong>,.... Either by them; or as he was going from them to his lodgings; it being toward the close of the day, when John had finished his work for that day, and the people were departing home: John fixed his eyes intently on Christ, with great pleasure and delight, and pointing at him,</p>
<p><strong>he saith, behold the Lamb of God</strong>; as in <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="joh+1:29">John 1:29</span>, where it is added, "which taketh away the sin of the world"; and which the Ethiopic version subjoins here.</p>
