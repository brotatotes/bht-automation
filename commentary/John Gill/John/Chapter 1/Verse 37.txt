<p><strong>And the two disciples heard him speak</strong>,.... The above words, and took notice of them; faith in Christ came by hearing them; they reached their hearts, and they found their affections, and the desires of their souls, to be after Christ:</p>
<p><strong>and they followed Jesus</strong>; left their master, and went after him, in order to get some acquaintance with him, and receive some instructions from him.</p>
