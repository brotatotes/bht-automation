<p><strong>One of the two which heard John [speak]</strong>,.... The above things, concerning Jesus being the Lamb of God:</p>
<p><strong>and followed him</strong>; that is, Jesus, as the Syriac and Arabic versions read; and the Persic version, Christ: and the Ethiopic version, "the Lord Jesus"; for not John, but Jesus they followed:</p>
<p><strong>was Andrew, Simon Peter's brother</strong>: see <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="mt+4:18">Matthew 4:18</span> the other, as before observed, might be the writer of this Gospel.</p>
