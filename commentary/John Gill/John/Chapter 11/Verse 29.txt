<p><strong>As soon as she heard that</strong>,.... That Christ was come, and inquired for her, and wanted to see her:</p>
<p><strong>she arose quickly, and came unto him</strong>; having an equal affection for him as her sister Martha; and which she showed, by leaving her comforters at once, and by making the haste she did, to another and better comforter: both Martha and Mary, out of their great love to Christ, break through the rule for mourners mentioned in note,</p>
<p><span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>, of not going out of the door of the house the first week of mourning.</p>
