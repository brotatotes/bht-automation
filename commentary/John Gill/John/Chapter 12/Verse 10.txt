<p><strong>But the chief priests</strong>,.... With the rest of the sanhedrim:</p>
<p><strong>consulted that they might put Lazarus also to death</strong>; as well as Jesus, and that for no other crime, but because he was raised from the dead by him; which shows what consciences these men had, and how horribly wicked they were; that they stopped at nothing, whereby they might satisfy their malice and envy, and secure their worldly interests and advantages.</p>
