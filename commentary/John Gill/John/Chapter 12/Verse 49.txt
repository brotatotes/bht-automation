<p><strong>For I have not spoken of myself</strong>,.... As man, or as separate from his Father; his doctrine was not human, but divine, and therefore a rejection of it cannot escape notice at the future judgment:</p>
<p><strong>but the Father which sent me, he gave me a commandment what I should say, and what I should speak</strong>; Christ, as man, had his mission, and commission, and his instructions from his Father to preach the Gospel unto men; he was anointed for it by the Holy Ghost; he was enjoined the preaching of it by his Father, and the several doctrines he published were delivered him by him; see <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="joh+8:28">John 8:28</span>.</p>
