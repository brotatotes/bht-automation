<p><strong>Why was not this ointment sold for three hundred pence</strong>,.... Meaning Roman pence, one of which is, of the value of our money, seven pence halfpenny; so that three hundred pence amount to nine pounds seven shillings and six pence:</p>
<p><strong>and given to the poor</strong>? this was his pretence, and with which he covered himself; his uneasiness was, because it was not sold, and the money put into his hands, as appears by what follows.</p>
