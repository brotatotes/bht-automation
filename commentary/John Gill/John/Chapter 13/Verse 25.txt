<p><strong>He then lying on Jesus' breast</strong>,.... Being on the couch just before Jesus, with his back to him, he bends backwards, and falling on Jesus' breast, whispers in his ear:</p>
<p><strong>and saith unto him, Lord, who is it</strong>? using his interest in Christ, and making thus free with him, in compliance with Peter's request; and was no doubt desirous himself of knowing who the person was.</p>
