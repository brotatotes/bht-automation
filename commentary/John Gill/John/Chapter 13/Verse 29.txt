<p><strong>For some of them thought</strong>,.... This was the thought of some of the disciples, it may be of all of them but John;</p>
<p><strong>because Judas had the bag</strong>: which was for the common supply of Christ and his disciples, and for the relief of the poor;</p>
<p><span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>.</p>
<p><strong>That Jesus had said unto him, buy those things that we have need of against the feast</strong>; the feast of the passover, which was to be two days after; and shows, that this was not the passover which Christ now ate with his disciples:</p>
<p><strong>or that he should give something to the poor</strong>; for whom Christ cared, and had a hearty concern, and for whom Judas had very little, notwithstanding his high pretensions.</p>
