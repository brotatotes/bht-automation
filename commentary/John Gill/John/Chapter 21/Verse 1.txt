<p><strong>After these things</strong>,.... The resurrection of Christ from the dead, his appearance to Mary Magdalene, and twice to his disciples; once when Thomas was absent, and at another time when he was present:</p>
<p><strong>Jesus showed himself again to the disciples</strong>, a third time, as in</p>
<p><span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="joh+21:14">John 21:14</span> though not to them all; seven are only mentioned, as together, when he appeared to them:</p>
<p><strong>at the sea of Tiberias</strong>; the same with the sea of Galilee; see <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="joh+6:1">John 6:1</span> for after the second appearance of Christ to his disciples, they went from Jerusalem to Galilee, by the order of Christ, who appointed to meet them there, <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="mt+28:10">Matthew 28:10</span></p>
<p><strong>and on this way showed he himself</strong>; the manner in which he made his appearance, and the persons to whom, are as follow.</p>
