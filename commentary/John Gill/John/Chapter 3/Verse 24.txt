<p><strong>For John was not yet cast into prison.</strong> As he afterwards was by Herod, for the sake of Herodias, because he reproved Herod for taking her to be his wife, when she was wife to his brother Philip; see <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="mt+14:3">Matthew 14:3</span>; and this circumstance shows, that these things were done before that journey of Christ into Galilee, mentioned in <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="mt+4:12">Matthew 4:12</span>.</p>
