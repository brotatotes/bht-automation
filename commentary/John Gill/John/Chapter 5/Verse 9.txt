<p><strong>And immediately the man was made whole</strong>,.... As soon as ever the words were spoken by Christ, such power went with them, as restored the man to perfect health; and he finding himself to be quite well, rose up directly:</p>
<p><strong>and took up his bed and walked</strong>; which may be expressive of a sinner's rising from the bed of sin, and taking up the cross, or carrying the body of sin and death with him; and walking by faith in Christ, as he has received him:</p>
<p><strong>and on the same day was the sabbath</strong>; which is remarked, for the sake of what follows.</p>
