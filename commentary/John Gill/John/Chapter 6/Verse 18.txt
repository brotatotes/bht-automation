<p><strong>And the sea arose</strong>,.... Swelled, and was tumultuous and raging; the waves mounted up, and tossed the ship to and fro:</p>
<p><strong>by reason of a great wind that blew</strong>; which agitated the waters of the sea, and lifted up the waves; which storm seems to have arose after they had set sail, and were got into the midst of the sea.</p>
