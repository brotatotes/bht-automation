<p><strong>And a great multitude followed him</strong>,.... From several cities and towns in Galilee, where he had been preaching and working miracles:</p>
<p><strong>because they saw his miracles which he did on them that were diseased</strong>; so that it was not for the sake of his doctrine, or for the good of their souls, they followed him; but either to gratify their curiosity in seeing his miracles, or to be healed in their bodies, as others had been.</p>
