<p><strong>Then they willingly received him into the ship</strong>,.... When they knew who he was; and especially he was the more welcome, as they were in distress; and he able, as they well knew, to help them:</p>
<p><strong>and immediately the ship was at the land whither they went</strong>; which was done, as Nonnus observes, by a divine motion; for not only the wind ceased, but another miracle was wrought; the ship was in an instant at the place whither they intended to go.</p>
