<p><strong>These things said he in the synagogue</strong>,.... Openly and publicly, in the place of divine worship, where the Jews resorted for that purpose:</p>
<p><strong>as he taught in Capernaum</strong>; his own city, and where there was a synagogue, into which he often went and taught his doctrines, and wrought miracles; see <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="mt+3:13">Matthew 3:13</span>.</p>
