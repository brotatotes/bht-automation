<p><strong>He spake of Judas Iscariot, [the son] of Simon</strong>,.... These are the words of the evangelist, pointing out the person Christ intended, lest any other should be suspected:</p>
<p><strong>for he it was that should betray him</strong>: as it was determined and foretold, and which Christ knew full well, and therefore said the above words:</p>
<p><strong>being one of the twelve</strong>; apostles, whom Christ had chosen, and which was an aggravation of his crime.</p>
