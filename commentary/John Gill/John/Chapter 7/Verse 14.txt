<p><strong>Now about the midst of the feast</strong>,.... About the fourth day of it, for it lasted eight days; this might be on the sabbath day, which sometimes was בתוך החג, "in the middle of the feast" n; and the rather, since it follows,</p>
<p><strong>Jesus went up into the temple</strong>; as the Lord and proprietor of it, and as was his usual method; he had for some reasons kept himself retired till now, and now he appeared publicly:</p>
<p><strong>and taught</strong> the people his doctrine; he expounded the Scriptures, gave the true sense of them, and instructed the people out of them.</p>
<p>n Misa. Succa, c. 5. sect. 5.</p>
