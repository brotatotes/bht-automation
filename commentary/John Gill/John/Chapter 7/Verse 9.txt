<p><strong>When he had said these words unto them</strong>,.... Had exhorted them to go up to the feast, and told them that he should not go yet, and the reason of it:</p>
<p><strong>he abode [still] in Galilee</strong>; and went not up with his brethren, nor at all at present; showing hereby a firmness and resolution of mind, not using lightness of speech; and his words being not yea, and nay, but all of a piece, and by which he abode.</p>
