<p><strong>And because I tell you the truth</strong>,.... And no lie, the whole truth of the Gospel, and particularly the truth of his divine sonship:</p>
<p><strong>ye believe me not</strong>; to such an infatuation and judicial blindness were they give up, to disbelieve him, because he told the truth, and to believe a lie, that they might be damned; which showed them to be the children of the devil, and under his power and influence.</p>
