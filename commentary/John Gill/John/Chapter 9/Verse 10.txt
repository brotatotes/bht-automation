<p><strong>Therefore said they unto him</strong>,.... When the case was clear, and it was out of question that he was the man:</p>
<p><strong>how were thine eyes opened</strong>? or made to see: they might well ask this question, since such a thing was never known before, that one born blind received his sight; and as great a miracle it is in grace, and as great a mystery to a natural man, how one should be born again, or be spiritually enlightened.</p>
