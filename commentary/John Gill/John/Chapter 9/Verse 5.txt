<p><strong>As long as I am in the world</strong>,.... Which had been now two or three and thirty years; but was not to be much longer.</p>
<p><strong>I am the light of the world</strong>; <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>. Though doubtless he said this with some view to the cure he was about to perform, it being agreeable to his character and work, while he was in the world.</p>
