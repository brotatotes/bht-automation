<p><strong>And when Zacharias saw him</strong>,.... The angel; he was troubled, and fear fell upon him; for such appearances of angels were not now so common as formerly: and when they were more usual, generally had such effects on the minds, even of good men; see <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="jdg+6:22">Judges 6:22</span>.</p>
