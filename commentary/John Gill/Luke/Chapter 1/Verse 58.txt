<p><strong>And her neighbours, and her cousins</strong>,.... That lived in Hebron, and the parts adjacent, whether of the house of Aaron, or of the tribe of Judah; to both which she was related, and who dwelt near her, the priests in the city of Hebron, and the children of Judah in the places about it:</p>
<p><strong>heard how the Lord had showed great mercy upon her</strong>; or "had magnified his mercy with her"; see <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="ge+19:19">Genesis 19:19</span> in removing her barrenness, and so taking away her reproach from among men; in giving her strength to conceive, and bring forth a son, that was to be so great, as the prophet of the Highest; and more than a prophet, and greater than any born of women:</p>
<p><strong>and they rejoiced with her</strong>: as the angel had foretold they should, <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="lu+1:14">Luke 1:14</span> The Persic version reads, "with him, Zacharias"; having rendered the other clause thus, though wrongly, "hearing that God had poured out his mercy on the house of Zacharias"; see <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="ro+12:15">Romans 12:15</span>.</p>
