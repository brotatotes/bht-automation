<p><strong>And his mother answered and said</strong>,.... That is, Elisabeth:</p>
<p><strong>not so, but he shall be called John</strong>; knowing that this was the name wherewith the angel said he should be called; either by divine revelation, she being filled with the Holy Ghost, <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="lu+1:41">Luke 1:41</span> or by information of her husband, who, doubtless, in writing, gave her an account of all that the angel had said unto him.</p>
