<p><strong>The oath which he swore to our father Abraham.</strong> When he swore by himself, because he could swear by no greater, that in blessing he would bless him; that his seed should possess the gates of his enemies, and in it all the nations of the earth should be blessed: all which have been fulfilled in Jesus the Messiah; see <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="ge+22:16">Genesis 22:16</span>.</p>
