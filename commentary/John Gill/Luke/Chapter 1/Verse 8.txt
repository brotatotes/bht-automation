<p><strong>And it came to pass, that while he executed the priest's office</strong>,.... To which he was called and ordained, even to offer gifts and sacrifices for men; whilst he was in the way of his duty, when oftentimes God appears to, and in favour of his people; whilst he was performing it,</p>
<p><strong>before God</strong>; in the temple, where was the symbol of the divine presence, before the altar of the Lord; and as having the fear of God before his eyes; considering himself as in the sight of God, and doing his work faithfully and sincerely:</p>
<p><strong>in the order of his course</strong>; taking his turn in the order of the course of Abia, to which he belonged; <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>.</p>
