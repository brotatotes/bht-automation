<p><strong>But into whatsoever city ye enter</strong>,.... On the other hand Christ here directs how to behave towards other cities they should come to, and be rejected:</p>
<p><strong>and they receive you not</strong>; refuse to admit them into their houses, or provide in any shape for them, nor hear their message:</p>
<p><strong>go your ways out into the streets of the same</strong>. The Ethiopic version reads, "cast the dust off your feet into the streets of it: and say"; aloud, in the most public manner, in the ears of all the people, as follows.</p>
