<p><strong>And he turned him unto his disciples</strong>,.... Both to the twelve apostles, and seventy disciples;</p>
<p><strong>and said privately</strong>; or to them apart: the phrase "privately" is wanting in the Vulgate Latin version:</p>
<p><strong>blessed are the eyes that see the things that ye see</strong>; the person of the Messiah, his kingdom setting up in the world, miracles wrought by him, and Satan falling before him; <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>.</p>
