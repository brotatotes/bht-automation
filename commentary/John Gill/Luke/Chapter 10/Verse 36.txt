<p><strong>Which now of these three</strong>,.... The priest, the Levite, and the Samaritan,</p>
<p><strong>thinkest thou, was neighbour to him that fell among the thieves</strong>? the priest and Levite that passed by, and took no notice of him, and gave him no relief, neither by words nor actions; or the Samaritan, that did all the above kind and generous things to him?</p>
