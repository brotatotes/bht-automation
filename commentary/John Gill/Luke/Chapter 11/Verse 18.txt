<p><strong>And if Satan also be divided against himself, how shall his kingdom stand</strong>?.... This is the accommodation of the above parables, or proverbial sentences; suggesting, that Satan must be against himself, if what the Pharisees said was true; and consequently, his kingdom and government, could not long subsist:</p>
