<p><strong>But if I with the, finger of God</strong>,.... The power of God, referring to <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="ex+8:19">Exodus 8:19</span> and so the Cabalistic Jews r explain it,</p>
<p>"the finger is one of the five in the hand, and is that finger which works by the power of Elohim;''</p>
<p>it is the same with the Spirit of God; <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="ex+8:19- :">Exodus 8:19- :</span> which is often called the hand of the Lord, <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="eze+1:3">Ezekiel 1:3</span>.</p>
<p>r R. Mosch in Sepher Hashem, apud Cabal. Denudata. T. I. par. l. p. 146.</p>
