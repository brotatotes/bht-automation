<p><strong>And he said, woe unto you also, ye lawyers</strong>,.... Christ was so far from calling back what he had said or suggested, that he repeats and confirms it, and more particularly names them, and enlarges on their evil practices:</p>
<p><strong>for ye lade men with burdens grievous to be borne, and ye yourselves touch not the burdens with one of your fingers; <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span></strong>.</p>
