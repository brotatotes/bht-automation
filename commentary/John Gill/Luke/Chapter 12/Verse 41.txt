<p><strong>Then Peter said unto him, Lord</strong>,.... The Syriac, Persic, and Ethiopic versions read, "our Lord":</p>
<p><strong>speakest thou this parable</strong>: of the master at the wedding, and his servants waiting for him, or of the housekeeper watching that his house be not broken up, or both:</p>
<p><strong>unto us, or unto all</strong>? Peter was in doubt whether the above discourse was peculiarly directed to them, the apostles, as containing special instructions to them in the discharge of the ministerial work; or whether it was designed for all his disciples and followers, both in the present age, and in time to come, to the end of the world.</p>
