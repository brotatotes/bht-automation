<p><strong>Of a truth I say unto you</strong>,.... This, with Christ, was a strong way of asserting the truth of any thing; and the sense is, you may assure yourselves of it, this will certainly be the happy case of such servant:</p>
<p><strong>that he will make him ruler over all that he hath</strong>; as Joseph was made by Pharaoh. Such an one shall reign with Christ on earth a thousand years, and then in heaven with him to all eternity, where he shall enjoy all things; <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span></p>
