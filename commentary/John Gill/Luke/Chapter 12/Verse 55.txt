<p><strong>And when ye see the south wind blow</strong>,.... From the hot countries of Egypt, Ethiopia, and Arabia, which lay south of Judea:</p>
<p><strong>ye say there will be heat</strong>; or hot sultry weather, scorching heat, which such a wind brings with it:</p>
<p><strong>and it cometh to pass</strong>; generally speaking, as is asserted.</p>
