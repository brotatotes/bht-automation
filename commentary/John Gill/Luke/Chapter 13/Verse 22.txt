<p><strong>And he went through the cities and villages</strong>,.... Either of Galilee, or of Judea, or both; since he was upon his journey from Galilee, through Judea, to Jerusalem, as it follows:</p>
<p><strong>teaching and journeying toward Jerusalem</strong>; as he was journeying he taught in every place he came, where he could have an opportunity; his delight was to do good both to the bodies and souls of men; and he was constant and assiduous in it.</p>
