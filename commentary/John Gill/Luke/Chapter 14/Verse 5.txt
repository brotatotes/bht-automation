<p><strong>And answered them, saying</strong>,.... Murmuring secretly at what he had done:</p>
<p><strong>which of you shall have an ass, or an ox fallen into a pit, and will not straightway pull him out on the sabbath day</strong>? being just ready to be drowned there; and therefore it must be much more right and necessary to cure a man, a reasonable creature, just drowning with a dropsy, as this man was.
