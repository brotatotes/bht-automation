<p><strong>And he called him</strong>,.... By the prophets, sent one after another; by John the Baptist, by Christ himself, and by his apostles:</p>
<p><strong>and said unto him, how is it that I hear this of thee</strong>? of thy corrupting the word; of thy covetousness, rapine, and theft; of thy adultery and idolatry, and sad violation of the law; see <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="ro+2:21">Romans 2:21</span></p>
<p><strong>give an account of thy stewardship</strong>: what improvement is made of thy gifts; what care has been taken of my vineyard, the Jewish church; and where are the fruits that might be expected to have been received at your hands:</p>
<p><strong>for thou mayest be no longer steward</strong>. This was foretold by the prophets, that God would write a "Loammi" upon the people of the Jews; that he would cut off three shepherds in one month, and particularly lay aside the idol shepherd, by whom the Pharisees may be meant, <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="zec+11:8">Zechariah 11:8</span> and by John the Baptist, who declared the axe was laid to the root of the tree, and it was just ready to be cut down, <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="mt+3:10">Matthew 3:10</span> and by Christ, that the kingdom of God should be taken from them, <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="mt+21:43">Matthew 21:43</span> and by the apostles, who turned from them to the Gentiles, <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="ac+13:46">Acts 13:46</span>.</p>
