And Jesus said unto him,.... In answer to his question, beginning with the character he gave him:why callest thou me good? it being unusual to address men, even their Rabbins, under such a title:none is good, save one, [that is], God: or "but God alone"; as the Vulgate Latin and Arabic versions render it; or, "but the one God", as read the Syriac, Persic, and Ethiopic versions; :-.
