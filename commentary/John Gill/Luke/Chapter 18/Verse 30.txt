<p><strong>Who shall not receive manifold more in this present time</strong>,.... Not more houses, parents, brethren, c. but that which is abundantly preferable to them such comfort, peace, satisfaction, and pleasure, as are not to be found in such enjoyments:</p>
<p><strong>and in the world to come life everlasting</strong>; which was what the young man was desirous of inheriting, <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="lu+18:18">Luke 18:18</span>.</p>
