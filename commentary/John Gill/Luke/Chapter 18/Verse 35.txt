<p><strong>And it came to pass that as he was come nigh unto Jericho</strong>,.... Which lay in his way to Jerusalem;</p>
<p><strong>a certain blind man sat by the way side begging</strong>: this was not blind Bartimaeus, nor his companion, for they were cured by Christ as he went out of Jericho; but this man before he came to it; for we afterwards read of his entrance into, and passing through Jericho,</p>
