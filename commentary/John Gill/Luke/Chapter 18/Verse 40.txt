<p><strong>And Jesus stood</strong>,.... He stood still; he made a full stop, and went no further, when, it is very likely, he was just over against him:</p>
<p><strong>and commanded him to be brought unto him</strong>; either by the disciples, or by some of the multitude:</p>
<p><strong>and when he was come near he asked him</strong>; the following question.</p>
