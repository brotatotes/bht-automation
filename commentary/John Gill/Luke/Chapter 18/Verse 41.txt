<p><strong>Saying, what wilt thou that I shall do unto thee</strong>?.... Is it alms thou askest? or is it thy sight thou wouldst have restored?</p>
<p><strong>and he said, Lord, that I may receive my sight</strong>; this he chose, this was his request, and what he cried so vehemently for; and which he believed Christ, the son of David, was able to do for him.</p>
