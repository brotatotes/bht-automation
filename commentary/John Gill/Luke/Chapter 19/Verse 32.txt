<p><strong>And they that were sent went their way</strong>,.... The two disciples that were sent by Christ, were obedient to him, and went, as he directed them, and did as he ordered them;</p>
<p><strong>and found, even as he had said unto them</strong>. The Vulgate Latin and Ethiopic versions, and so some copies, add, "the colt standing"; that is, in the place, and manner which he had described to them;</p>
