<p><strong>And they brought him to Jesus</strong>,.... That is, the colt:</p>
<p><strong>and they cast their garments upon the colt</strong>; that is, the disciples, who brought him to Jesus; the Persic version here, as in</p>
<p><span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="mt+21:7">Matthew 21:7</span> renders it very wrongly, "Jesus put his own garment on its back, and sat on it"; it follows,</p>
<p><strong>and they set Jesus thereon</strong>; in order to ride upon him, as he did to Jerusalem.</p>
