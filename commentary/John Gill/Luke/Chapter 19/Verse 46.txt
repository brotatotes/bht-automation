Saying unto them, it is written,.... In Isaiah 56:7my house is the house of prayer; built and devoted for that service:but ye have made it a den of thieves; which clause is not written in the above prophecy, but are the words of Christ referring to Jeremiah 7:11. The Ethiopic version adds, "and robbers"; and the Persic version adds, "and a place of cut-purses";Jeremiah 7:11- :.
