<p><strong>And when they found him not</strong>,.... In the company that came from Jerusalem with them, nor among any of their relations and friends, with whom they supposed he was:</p>
<p><strong>they turned back again to Jerusalem</strong>, that is, the next morning, for it can hardly be thought they would set out that night, after they had travelled all day, without taking some repose:</p>
<p><strong>seeking him</strong>; at Jerusalem, in the streets and broad places of it; a figure of the church and ordinances, where souls look for, and inquire after their beloved, when they have lost him, <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="so+3:1">Song of Solomon 3:1</span>.</p>
