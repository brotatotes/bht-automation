<p><strong>And all that heard him were astonished</strong>,.... All in the sanhedrim, both the doctors, and their disciples, were amazed,</p>
<p><strong>at his understanding</strong>; in the knowledge of the law, and of the Scriptures:</p>
<p><strong>and his answers</strong>; which he returned to the questions the doctor's put to him, which were made with so much wisdom and judgment, that it was surprising in one of his years.</p>
