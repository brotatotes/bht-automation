<p><strong>Which devour widows' houses</strong>,.... As the characters of them, in the preceding verse, expose their pride, this shows their avarice; they were very voracious and cruel; they did not spare widows, but devoured their substance:</p>
<p><strong>and for a show make long prayers</strong>; to cover their wickedness, pretending great devotion and religion;</p>
<p><strong>the same shall receive greater damnation</strong>: than openly profane sinners; doing such wickedness under a cloak of religion, will aggravate their condemnation; <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>.</p>
