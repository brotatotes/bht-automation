<p><strong>And Jesus said unto them</strong>,.... Since they would not give him a direct answer to his question:</p>
<p><strong>neither tell I you by what authority I do these things</strong>; nor was there any need of it; they might easily perceive by what he had said, from whence he professed to have received his authority, from God, and not men; <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>.</p>
