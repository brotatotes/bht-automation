<p><strong>And ye shall be betrayed both by parents, and brethren</strong>,.... <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span></p>
<p><strong>and kinsfolks, and friends</strong>. The Syriac, Persic, and Ethiopic versions add, "your", to each of these relations, as your parents, c.</p>
<p><strong>and some of you shall they cause to be put to death</strong> as Stephen was stoned to death, and James, the brother of John, Herod killed with the sword, <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="ac+7:58">Acts 7:58</span> and indeed all of them were put to death, except John, before the destruction of Jerusalem.</p>
