And he saw also a certain poor widow,.... Whom he took particular notice of above all the rest: the poor, and the widow, are regarded by him, and are his care; nor are their mean services, done in faith, and from a principle of love, despised by him, but preferred to the greater services of others, where faith and love are wanting:casting in thither two mites; the value of a farthing. The Persic version renders it, "two bottoms of yarn"; :-:
