<p><strong>And he saw also a certain poor widow</strong>,.... Whom he took particular notice of above all the rest: the poor, and the widow, are regarded by him, and are his care; nor are their mean services, done in faith, and from a principle of love, despised by him, but preferred to the greater services of others, where faith and love are wanting:</p>
<p><strong>casting in thither two mites</strong>; the value of a farthing. The Persic version renders it, "two bottoms of yarn";</p>
