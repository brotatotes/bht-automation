<p><strong>And they asked him</strong>,.... That is, his disciples, when they were come to the Mount of Olives, and as he sat upon that,</p>
<p><span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="mt+24:3">Matthew 24:3</span></p>
<p><strong>saying, master, but when shall these things be</strong>? when the temple shall be destroyed; and one stone shall not be left upon another;</p>
<p><strong>and what sign [will there be] when these things shall come to pass</strong>? which shows that this refers to the destruction of the temple, and so the signs following; <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="mt+24:3- :">Matthew 24:3- :</span>.</p>
