<p><strong>And he shall show you a large upper room furnished</strong>,.... With all things necessary, to eat and drink out of, at the passover;</p>
<p><strong>there make ready</strong>. The Ethiopic version adds, "for us", as in</p>
<p><span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="mr+14:15">Mark 14:15</span>. The Persic version renders it, "there prepare a place"; but this was prepared already; the words design the preparation of the passover, and every thing proper for it.</p>
