<p><strong>And they went</strong>,.... From Bethany to Jerusalem:</p>
<p><strong>and found as he had said unto them</strong>; they met the man with the pitcher of water, and by following him, found the house Christ meant to keep the passover at; they told the master of it, what Christ ordered them, who immediately showed them a very convenient room, as he had described to them:</p>
<p><strong>and they made ready the passover</strong>; provided a lamb, and got it killed and dressed, and prepared every thing necessary for the keeping of the feast, according to divine appointment; <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>.</p>
