<p><strong>And when he was at the place</strong>,.... In the garden, at Gethsemane, which was at the foot of the Mount of Olives;</p>
<p><strong>he said unto them</strong>; to the disciples, as the Persic version reads;</p>
<p><strong>pray that ye enter not into temptation</strong>. This, according to the Evangelists Matthew and Mark, was said to them after he had prayed the first time, and returned to the disciples, and found them sleeping; <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>.</p>
