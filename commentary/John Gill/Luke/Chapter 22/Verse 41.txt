<p><strong>And he was withdrawn from them</strong>,.... That is, from the three disciples, Peter, James, and John, whom he took along with him, leaving the rest at some further distance; and from these he removed,</p>
<p><strong>about a stone's cast</strong>; fifty, or sixty feet from the place where they were:</p>
<p><strong>and kneeled down and prayed</strong>; the following prayer.</p>
