<p><strong>And when they had blindfolded him</strong>,.... Or put a veil, or linen cloth over his eyes, as children do at play, to make themselves some sport with him:</p>
<p><strong>they struck him on the face</strong>; on that part of it which was not covered, either with their hands, or with rods:</p>
<p><strong>and asked him, saying, prophesy, who is it that smote thee</strong>? hereby deriding his prophetic office, and using such language as children do at blindman's buff; <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>.</p>
