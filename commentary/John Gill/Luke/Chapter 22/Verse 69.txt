<p><strong>Hereafter shall the son of man</strong>,.... Meaning himself, who was truly man, and then in a low and mean form, and thought by the Jews to be a mere man; though this character was known by them to belong to the Messiah, especially what follows; as that he should</p>
<p><strong>sit on the right hand of the power of God</strong>: as he did after his resurrection, and ascension, and which was manifest by the destruction of their nation, city, and temple; <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>.</p>
