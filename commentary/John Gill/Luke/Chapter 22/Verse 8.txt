<p><strong>And he sent Peter and John</strong>,.... That is, Jesus sent them, as the Syriac, Persic, and Ethiopic versions express it; these were two favourite disciples of Christ, and were now sent by him from Bethany to Jerusalem:</p>
<p><strong>saying, go and prepare us the passover, that we may eat</strong>; it together; so servants used to be sent, to go and prepare the passover for their masters; <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>.</p>
