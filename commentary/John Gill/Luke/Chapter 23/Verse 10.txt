<p><strong>And the chief priests and Scribes</strong>,.... The sanhedrim that followed him from Pilate's hall, to Herod's palace; fearing, lest Herod should be disposed to let him go, should he gratify him by working a miracle:</p>
<p><strong>stood</strong>; before Herod; so witnesses, and accusers, used to do;</p>
<p><span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span></p>
<p><strong>and vehemently accused him</strong>; of the same things they had accused him before Pilate, with great bitterness and constancy, increasing, and aggravating the charges against him.</p>
