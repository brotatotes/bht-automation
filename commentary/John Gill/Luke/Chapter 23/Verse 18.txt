And they cried out all at once,.... The chief priests, rulers, and people, not bearing to hear of a release of him, now they had got him in their hands; and enraged at the proposal, in a most clamorous way, cried out, as one man, immediately:Saying, away with this man; to the cross; to Calvary, the place of execution; away with him out of the world; he is not fit to live:and release unto us Barabbas; whose character is given in the next verse;  :-.
