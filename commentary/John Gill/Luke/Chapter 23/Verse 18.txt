<p><strong>And they cried out all at once</strong>,.... The chief priests, rulers, and people, not bearing to hear of a release of him, now they had got him in their hands; and enraged at the proposal, in a most clamorous way, cried out, as one man, immediately:</p>
<p><strong>Saying, away with this man</strong>; to the cross; to Calvary, the place of execution; away with him out of the world; he is not fit to live:</p>
<p><strong>and release unto us Barabbas</strong>; whose character is given in the next verse; <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>.</p>
