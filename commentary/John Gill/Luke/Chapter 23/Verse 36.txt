<p><strong>And the soldiers also mocked him</strong>,.... The Roman soldiers, to whom the execution was committed, who crucified him, and parted his garments, and stood at his cross watching; these joined in the insult, which is not to be wondered at. Coming to him and offering him vinegar; which was what was a part of their allowance, and was their drink; <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>.</p>
