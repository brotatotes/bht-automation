<p><strong>And all his acquaintance</strong>,.... That were related to him in a natural, or in a spiritual sense, or both, as his own mother, and beloved disciple John, who were both present, <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="joh+19:26">John 19:26</span> or those that were known unto him, and familiar with him, who attended on his ministry, and often conversed, and were intimately acquainted with him:</p>
<p><strong>and the women that followed him from Galilee</strong>; among whom were Mary Magdalene, and Mary the mother of James, and Joses, and Salome the mother of Zebedee's children:</p>
<p><strong>stood afar off</strong>; from the cross:</p>
<p><strong>beholding these things</strong>; with wonder, as well as looking upon their dear suffering Lord, with aching hearts, and flowing eyes.</p>
