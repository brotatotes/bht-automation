<p><strong>And behold, there was a man named Joseph</strong>,....</p>
<p><span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>.</p>
<p><strong>a counsellor</strong>; Mark says, he was an "honourable" one; he was either one of the council of the high priest, or a member of the great sanhedrim; <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>.</p>
<p><strong>and he was a good man, and a just</strong>; he was kind and beneficent in his temper, and just, and righteous in his life and actions; a like character is given of Joseph the husband of Mary, the mother of our Lord, <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="mt+1:19">Matthew 1:19</span>.</p>
