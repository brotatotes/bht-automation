And the women also which came with him from Galilee,.... :-.followed after; Joseph; and those that were with him, when they carried the body of Jesus, in order to inter it:and beheld, the sepulchre; took notice of it, what an one it was, whereabout it stood in the garden, that they might know, and find it again:and how his body was laid; in what position, form, and order; and observed that it was only wrapped in linen, and not anointed, or embalmed.
