<p><strong>And the women also which came with him from Galilee</strong>,....</p>
<p><span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>.</p>
<p><strong>followed after</strong>; Joseph; and those that were with him, when they carried the body of Jesus, in order to inter it:</p>
<p><strong>and beheld, the sepulchre</strong>; took notice of it, what an one it was, whereabout it stood in the garden, that they might know, and find it again:</p>
<p><strong>and how his body was laid</strong>; in what position, form, and order; and observed that it was only wrapped in linen, and not anointed, or embalmed.</p>
