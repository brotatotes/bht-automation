<p><strong>It was Mary Magdalene</strong>,.... Out of whom Christ had cast seven devils, who was a sincere penitent, a true believer in Christ, and an affectionate lover of him, and to whom he first appeared:</p>
<p><strong>and Joanna</strong>; the wife of Chuza, Herod's steward, one that had been a follower of Christ, and had ministered to him of her substance; see <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="lu+8:2">Luke 8:2</span></p>
<p><strong>and Mary the mother of James</strong>; called the less; and also of Joses, and Simon, and Judas, the brethren, or kinsmen of Christ; this Mary, being the wife of Cleophas, or Alphaeus, said k to be the brother of Joseph, the husband of Mary, the mother of our Lord:</p>
<p><strong>and other women that were with them</strong>; as Salome, the mother of Zebedee's children, and others, that came from Galilee;</p>
<p><span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="mr+15:40">Mark 15:40</span>.</p>
<p><strong>which told these things unto the apostles</strong>; before observed.</p>
<p>k Euseb. Eccl. Hist. l. 3. c. 11. 32. &amp; 4. 22.</p>
