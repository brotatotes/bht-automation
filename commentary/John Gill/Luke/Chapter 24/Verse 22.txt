<p><strong>Yea, and certain women also of our company</strong>,.... Who came with them from Galilee, who were of the same religion, professed the same faith, and belonged to the same society and community; who they were, and their names, see <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="lu+24:10">Luke 24:10</span>.</p>
<p><strong>made us astonished</strong>; surprised us, with an account they brought, so that we could not, nor can we now tell, what to think or say of it; it is such an one, we know not how to believe, nor to disprove; it is we fear too good to be true, and should it be as they report, it is amazing indeed:</p>
<p><strong>which were early at the sepulchre</strong>; of the person now mentioned, Jesus of Nazareth; even this very morning, by break of day, at least at sunrise, whither they went to anoint his body, thinking nothing at all about the resurrection of him.</p>
