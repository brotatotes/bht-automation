<p><strong>And they entered in</strong>,.... To the sepulchre, being invited, encouraged, and led on by the angel that sat upon the stone; for the Jews' sepulchres were built large enough for persons to go into;</p>
<p><span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span></p>
<p><strong>and found not the body of the Lord Jesus</strong>; as they expected, having seen him put there, and had observed in what cave in the sepulchre, and in what form he was laid.</p>
