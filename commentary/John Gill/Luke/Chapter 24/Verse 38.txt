<p><strong>And he said unto them, why are ye troubled</strong>,.... Who had more reason to rejoice, and be glad, as they were when they knew that it was Jesus:</p>
<p><strong>and why do thoughts arise in your hearts</strong>? whether what they saw was Jesus, or an apparition, which gave them a great deal of trouble and uneasiness, and filled them with fright and terror; as it was, and is usual with persons when they fancy they see a spirit, or an apparition; see <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="mt+14:26">Matthew 14:26</span>.</p>
