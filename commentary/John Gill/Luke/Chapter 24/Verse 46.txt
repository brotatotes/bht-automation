<p><strong>And he said unto them, thus it is written</strong>,.... In the above cited books of the Law, the Prophets, and the Psalms; not what after follows, in so many words, but the matter and substance thereof:</p>
<p><strong>and thus it behoved Christ to suffer, and to rise from the dead the third day</strong>; it was necessary, both because of the decree and appointment of God, and because of the prophecies and predictions of the Old Testament; see <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="lu+24:25">Luke 24:25</span>.</p>
