<p><strong>Which was the son of Maath</strong>,.... Surnamed Aser, who governed nine years:</p>
<p><strong>which was the son of Mattathias</strong>; called Eli Matathias, who governed twelve years:</p>
<p><strong>which was the son of Semei</strong>; and named Abner Semei, who governed eleven years:</p>
<p><strong>which was the son of Joseph</strong>; called Joseph the first, who governed seven years:</p>
<p><strong>which was the son of Juda</strong>; who, according to the same writer, must be Judas, surnamed Hyrcanus the first, who governed fourteen years.</p>
