<p><strong>And now also the axe is laid unto the root of the trees</strong>,.... Not only to Jesse's family, which as a root in a dry ground, and to Jerusalem, the metropolis of the nation; but to the root of the vain boasting of every Jew; their descent from Abraham, the covenant made with him, their ecclesiastical state and civil polity, all which would quickly be at an end: the Romans were now among them, the axe in God's hand; by means of whom, utter ruin and destruction would be brought upon their nation, city, and temple:</p>
<p><strong>every tree therefore which bringeth not forth good fruit is hewn down, and cast into the fire; <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span></strong>.</p>
