<p><strong>And when they had brought their ships to land</strong>,.... Both Simon Peter's, and the other in which his partners were, and which were laden with fish:</p>
<p><strong>they forsook all</strong>; even all their fish, which they doubtless might have made much money of, and their nets, and their ships, and their servants, and their relations, and friends:</p>
<p><strong>and followed him</strong>; Christ; and became his disciples, even all four of them, Peter, Andrew, James, and John.</p>
