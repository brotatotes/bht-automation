<p><strong>But so much the more went there a fame abroad of him</strong>,.... For the more he charged the man to keep silence, the more he blazed it abroad, being elated with the cure he received, and filled with gratitude to his benefactor; <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="mr+1:45">Mark 1:45</span>.</p>
<p><strong>And great multitudes came together to hear</strong>: him, or from him, as the Syriac, Arabic, Persic, and Ethiopic versions add; to hear the doctrines of the Gospel preached by him: "and to be healed by him of their infirmities"; their bodily weaknesses and disorders.</p>
