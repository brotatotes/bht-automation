<p><strong>And behold, men brought in a bed a man which was taken with a palsy</strong>,.... Four men brought him, as Mark says, <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="mr+2:3">Mark 2:3</span> and which the Ethiopic version expresses here: "and they sought means to bring him in": into the house where Jesus was:</p>
<p><strong>and to lay him before him</strong>; at his feet, in hope of moving his compassion, and to obtain a cure of him: of the nature of this disease, and of the sort which this man's seems to be, <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="mr+2:3- :">Mark 2:3- :</span>.</p>
