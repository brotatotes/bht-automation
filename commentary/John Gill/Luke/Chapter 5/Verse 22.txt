<p><strong>But when Jesus perceived their thoughts</strong>,.... Being God omniscient;</p>
<p><strong>he answering said unto them, what reason ye in your hearts</strong>? This he said, not as being ignorant what their reasonings were, for it is before said he perceived their thoughts, but to expose the wickedness of them; in one exemplar of Beza's it is added, "evil things", as in <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="mt+9:4">Matthew 9:4</span>.</p>
