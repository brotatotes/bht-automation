<p><strong>But the days will come</strong>,.... And that in a very little time, as they did:</p>
<p><strong>when the bridegroom shall be taken away from them</strong>: as their master, John, was taken away from them, and now in prison, and therefore it was no wonder they mourned and fasted; signifying, that in a short time he, the bridegroom of his church and people, should be taken away by death:</p>
<p><strong>and then they shall fast in those days</strong>; mourn, and be humbled, of which fasting was, a sign, for the death of their Lord, and on account of the many afflictions and persecutions they should endure for his sake; <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>.</p>
