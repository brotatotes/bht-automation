<p><strong>Simon, whom he also named Peter</strong>,.... Which signifies a rock, or stone, as Cephas also does, see <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="joh+1:42">John 1:42</span> from his constancy, steadfastness, and solidity:</p>
<p><strong>and Andrew his brother</strong>; who was called at the same time with him, and were brethren, both in nature and grace:</p>
<p><strong>James and John</strong>: the two sons of Zebedee, who were called next:</p>
<p><strong>Philip and Bartholomew</strong>; the latter of these is by some thought to be Nathanael.</p>
