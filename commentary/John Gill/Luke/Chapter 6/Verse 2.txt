And certain of the Pharisees said unto them,.... Unto the disciples. The Evangelists Matthew and Mark say, that they said this to Jesus: no doubt but they said it to both, first to one, and then to the other; probably last of all to Christ, who returned an answer to it:why do ye that which is not lawful on the sabbath day? as to pluck ears of corn, and rub them, and eat them;  :-
