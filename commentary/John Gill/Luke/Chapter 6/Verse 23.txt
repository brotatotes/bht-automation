<p><strong>Rejoice ye in that day</strong>,.... When they should be hated, discarded, reproached, and anathematized: and leap for joy; as if the greatest honour and happiness imaginable had been conferred on them; and as persons do, when in the greatest rapture:</p>
<p><strong>for behold, your reward is great in heaven, for in the like manner did their fathers unto the prophets</strong>;</p>
