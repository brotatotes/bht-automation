<p><strong>Whosoever cometh to me</strong>,.... To be a disciple and follower:</p>
<p><strong>and heareth my sayings, and doth them</strong>;</p>
<p><span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>.</p>
<p><strong>I will show you to whom he is like</strong>; or "to what thing he is like"; so the Syriac and Arabic versions; though what follows seems better to agree with person than thing.</p>
