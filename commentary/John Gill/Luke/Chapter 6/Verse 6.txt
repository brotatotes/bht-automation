<p><strong>And it came to pass also on another sabbath</strong>,.... Whether the following sabbath, or some time after, is not certain,</p>
<p><strong>that he entered into the synagogue</strong>. The Arabic version reads, "into their synagogue", as in <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="mt+12:9">Matthew 12:9</span> the synagogue of the Jews; in what place, whether at Capernaum, or some other city of Galilee, is not so clear:</p>
<p><strong>and taught</strong>; explained the Scriptures to the people, and instructed them in the doctrines of the Gospel:</p>
<p><strong>and there was a man whose right hand was withered</strong>; who was in the synagogue, and one of his hearers; <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="mt+12:9- :">Matthew 12:9- :</span></p>
