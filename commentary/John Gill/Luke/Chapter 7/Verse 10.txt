<p><strong>And they that were sent</strong>,.... Both the elders of the Jews, and the friends of the centurion:</p>
<p><strong>returning to the house</strong>; of the centurion, where his servant lay, and from whence they came:</p>
<p><strong>found the servant whole that had been sick</strong>; for he was healed directly, as soon as the centurion had expressed his faith, and Christ had declared that it should be according to it, <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="mt+8:13">Matthew 8:13</span>.</p>
