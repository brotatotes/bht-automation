<p><strong>And John calling unto him two of his disciples</strong>,.... Which were a sufficient number to be sent on an errand, to ask a question, and report the answer, or bear witness to any fact they should see, or hear done.</p>
<p><strong>Sent them unto Jesus, saying, art thou he that should come, or look we for another?</strong> not that he doubted that Jesus was the Messiah; nor was it for his own satisfaction so much that he sent these disciples of his with this question, but for theirs; and to remove all doubt and hesitation from them about Christ.</p>
