<p><strong>And when the messengers of John were departed</strong>,.... The Syriac and Persic versions read, "the disciples of John"; and the Arabic version, "the two disciples of John"; the two that he sent, when they were gone back with the answer of Christ;</p>
<p><strong>he,</strong> "Jesus", as the Persic version expresses it,</p>
<p><strong>began to speak unto the people concerning John;</strong> not caring to say any thing about him to the messengers, or whilst they were present, lest he should be charged with flattery; <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span></p>
<p><strong>What went ye into the wilderness for to see? a reed shaken with the wind</strong>? an inconsistent, wavering, and unstable man? if so, they were greatly mistaken; or the motions and gesture of the man?</p>
<p><span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>.</p>
