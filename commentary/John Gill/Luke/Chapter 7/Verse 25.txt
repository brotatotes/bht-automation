But what went you out for to see?.... If not his air and action, what was it? was it his apparel and dress? was it to seea man clothed in soft raiment? If this was the case, their labour was in vain, and they had their walk for nothing; for John was clothed with camels' hair, rough and undressed, and was girt with a leathern girdle; there was nothing in his person, mien, and garb, that was attractive:they which are gorgeously, apparelled; or richly clothed, as John was not:and live delicately; in the most elegant manner, and on the richest dainties, as John did not, his food being locusts and wild honey:are in kings' courts; and not in a wilderness, where John; came preaching.
