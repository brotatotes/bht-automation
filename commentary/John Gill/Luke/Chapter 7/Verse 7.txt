<p><strong>Wherefore neither thought I myself worthy to come unto thee</strong>,.... In person; therefore he sent the elders of the Jews to him first, and now some of his friends, who delivered these words in his name:</p>
<p><strong>but say in a word, and my servant shall be healed</strong>; speak but the word only, rebuke the distemper, command it off, and it will be gone; so great was his faith in the power of Christ.</p>
