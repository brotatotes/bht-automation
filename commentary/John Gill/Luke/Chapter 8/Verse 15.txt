<p><strong>But that on the good ground are they</strong>,.... The seed that fell on good ground design such hearers,</p>
<p><strong>which in an honest and good heart, having heard the word, keep it</strong>: who hear with an honest and good intention, and faithfully keep it, and hold it fast:</p>
<p><strong>and bring forth fruit with patience</strong>; with great constancy, suffering much for the sake of it; and the more they suffer, the more fruitful they are. See this explanation of the parable more largely insisted on in the following notes.</p>
