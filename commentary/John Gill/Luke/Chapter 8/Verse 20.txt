<p><strong>And it was told him by certain, which said</strong>,.... The phrase, "which said", is omitted in the Vulgate Latin version, and in Beza's most ancient copy. The Syriac, Arabic, and Ethiopic versions only read, "and they said unto him"; and the Persic version renders it, "a certain person said"; some one person, as in <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="mt+12:47">Matthew 12:47</span></p>
<p><strong>thy mother, and thy brethren, stand without, desiring to see thee;</strong> and to speak with thee, as in <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="mt+12:47">Matthew 12:47</span>.</p>
