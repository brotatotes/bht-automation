And they arrived at the country of the Gadarenes,.... In Matthew 8:28 it is called the country of the Gergesenes,Matthew 8:28- : as it is here, in the Arabic and Ethiopic versions; and "of the Gerasenes", in the Vulgate Latin; but the Syriac and Persic versions read, "of the Gadarenes", as in Mark 5:1.Mark 5:1- :.which is over against Galilee: from whence the ship launched, and Christ and his disciples came.
