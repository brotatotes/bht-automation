When he saw Jesus,.... Even afar off, at some considerable distance, he ran towards him, Mark 5:6.He cried out, and fell down before him; that is, the man possessed with the devil did so, under his impulse, and through his agitation of him:and with a loud voice said; which was the unclean spirit in the man:what have I to do with thee, Jesus, thou Son of God, most high? I beseech thee torment me not; i.e. before the time;Mark 5:6- :.
