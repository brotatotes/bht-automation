<p><strong>When he saw Jesus</strong>,.... Even afar off, at some considerable distance, he ran towards him, <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="mr+5:6">Mark 5:6</span>.</p>
<p><strong>He cried out, and fell down before him</strong>; that is, the man possessed with the devil did so, under his impulse, and through his agitation of him:</p>
<p><strong>and with a loud voice said</strong>; which was the unclean spirit in the man:</p>
<p><strong>what have I to do with thee, Jesus, thou Son of God, most high? I beseech thee torment me not</strong>; i.e. before the time;</p>
<p><span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="mr+5:6- :">Mark 5:6- :</span>.</p>
