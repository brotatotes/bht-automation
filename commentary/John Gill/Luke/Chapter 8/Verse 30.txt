<p><strong>And Jesus asked him, saying, what is thy name</strong>?.... This question was put, not out of ignorance in Christ, but for the sake of those that were with him; and partly, that the miserable condition of this man might be the more known; and partly, that his own power might be the more manifest in the dispossession:</p>
<p><strong>and he said, legion, because many devils were entered into him</strong>;</p>
