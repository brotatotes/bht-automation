<p><strong>And behold, there came a man named Jairus</strong>,....</p>
<p><span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>.</p>
<p><strong>and he was a ruler of the synagogue</strong>; at Capernaum; and it was the more remarkable, that such an one should come to Christ, and express any regard to his person, or faith in his power, and therefore a "behold" is prefixed to this account; <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>.</p>
<p><strong>And he fell down at Jesus' feet</strong>; showing great reverence and humility, and as Matthew says, "worshipped him"; if not in a religious, yet in a civil way:</p>
<p><strong>and besought him that he would come into his house</strong>; which was at some distance from thence, as appears by what follows.</p>
