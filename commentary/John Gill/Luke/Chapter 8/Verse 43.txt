<p><strong>And a woman having an issue of blood twelve years</strong>,.... The Persic version reads, "there was a woman in that city", c. in the city of Capernaum <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>.</p>
<p><strong>Which had spent all her living upon physicians</strong>; she had applied to one physician and another, and had consumed all her substance in this way:</p>
<p><strong>neither could be healed of any</strong>; though she had followed the directions and prescriptions of many, who pretended they were able to cure her; <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>.</p>
