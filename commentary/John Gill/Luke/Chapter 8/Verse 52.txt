<p>Ver. 52 <strong>And all wept and bewailed her</strong>,.... Not only her relations and friends, and the servants of the house, but the mourning women, that were hired on this occasion, and employed for this purpose:</p>
<p><strong>but he said, weep not</strong>; neither in show, as the mourning women did, nor in reality, as the friends of the deceased:</p>
<p><strong>she is not dead, but sleepeth</strong>;</p>
