<p><strong>While he thus spake there came a cloud</strong>,.... While Peter was making the above request, before an answer was returned, a cloud appeared, a very uncommon one, as a symbol of the divine presence: "and overshadowed them"; Jesus, Moses, Elias, and the disciples:</p>
<p><strong>and they feared as they entered into the cloud</strong>; either as they themselves entered into it, that coming gradually over them, because of the glory of it, and the solemnity that attended it; or as Moses and Elias entered into it; and so the Syriac and Persic versions read, "they feared when they saw Moses and Elias enter into the cloud"; which took them out of their sight: just as the cloud received Jesus out of the sight of his disciples, when he ascended to heaven, <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="ac+1:9">Acts 1:9</span>.</p>
