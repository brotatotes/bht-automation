And Jesus said unto him, forbid him not,.... "Or forbid not" him, or any other so doing:for he that is not against us, is for us: in two exemplars of Beza's it is read, "for he is not against you": the Vulgate Latin, and Syriac versions, instead of "us", in both clauses read "you", and so likewise the Persic and Ethiopic versions;  :-, :-.
