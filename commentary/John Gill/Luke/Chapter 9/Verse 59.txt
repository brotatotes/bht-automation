<p><strong>And he said unto another, follow me</strong>,.... According to Matthew, one of his disciples, who had attended him some time, and whom he now called to the ministerial work; <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>. The Ethiopic version reads, "another said to him, shall I not follow thee?" but without any foundation: they are certainly the words of Christ, directed to another person, at the same time he met with the former:</p>
<p><strong>but he said, Lord, suffer me first to go and bury my father; <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span></strong>.</p>
