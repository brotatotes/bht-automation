<p><strong>And they departed</strong>,.... That is, the apostles, as the Syriac and Persic versions express it: they went from Christ, and the place where he was, from Capernaum, at least from some place in Galilee:</p>
<p><strong>and went through the towns</strong>; the Syriac, Persic, and Ethiopic versions add, "and cities"; that is, of Judea, as well as Galilee, even the whole land of Israel:</p>
<p><strong>preaching the Gospel</strong>; which explains what is meant by the kingdom of God, <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="lu+9:2">Luke 9:2</span> and healing everywhere; all sorts of bodily diseases, wherever they came.</p>
