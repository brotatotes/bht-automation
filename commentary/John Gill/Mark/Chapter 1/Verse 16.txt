<p><strong>Now as he walked by the sea of Galilee</strong>,.... The same with the sea of Tiberias, <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="joh+6:1">John 6:1</span>,</p>
<p><strong>he saw Simon</strong>: whose surname was Peter, the son of Jonas:</p>
<p><strong>and Andrew his brother</strong>; the brother of Simon,</p>
<p><strong>casting a net into the sea</strong>; of Galilee, in order to catch fish:</p>
<p><strong>for they were fishers</strong>: by occupation, this was their trade and business, by which they got their livelihood; <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="joh+6:1- :">John 6:1- :</span>.</p>
