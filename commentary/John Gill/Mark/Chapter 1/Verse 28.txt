<p><strong>And immediately his fame spread abroad</strong>,.... Not only in the city of Capernaum, where these things were done, and where his fame was first spread, but also</p>
<p><strong>throughout all the region round about Galilee</strong>: and not only throughout Galilee, but throughout all the country that was bordering upon it, and adjacent to it; see <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="mt+4:23">Matthew 4:23</span>. The Persic version reads, "through all the provinces".</p>
