<p><strong>And Simon, and they that were with him</strong>,.... Peter, and his brother Andrew, together with James and John,</p>
<p><strong>followed after him</strong>; some time after he was gone; for he privately withdrew from them, so that they might not be aware when he went, nor apprized of his departure, for some considerable time; which when they were, they set out, in diligent search, and eager pursuit after him, until they found him.</p>
