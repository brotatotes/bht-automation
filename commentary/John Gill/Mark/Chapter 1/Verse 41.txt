<p><strong>And Jesus, moved with compassion</strong>,.... At the sad and deplorable case the poor man was in, being a merciful high priest, and not with a desire of popular applause, and vain glory:</p>
<p><strong>put forth his hand and touched him</strong>; though the leprosy was spread all over him, and there was no place clean, and touching him was forbidden by the law:</p>
<p><strong>and saith unto him, I will be thou clean</strong>; <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>.</p>
