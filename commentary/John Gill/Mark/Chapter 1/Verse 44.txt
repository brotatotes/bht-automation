<p><strong>And saith unto him, see thou say nothing to any man</strong>,.... By the way, till he came to the priest;</p>
<p><strong>but go thy way, show thyself to the priest</strong>: the Syriac and Persic versions read, "to the priests"; and the Vulgate Latin renders it, "to the chief priest"; but any priest might judge of the cleansing of a leper;</p>
