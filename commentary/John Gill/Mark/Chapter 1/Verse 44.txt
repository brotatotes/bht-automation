And saith unto him, see thou say nothing to any man,.... By the way, till he came to the priest;but go thy way, show thyself to the priest: the Syriac and Persic versions read, "to the priests"; and the Vulgate Latin renders it, "to the chief priest"; but any priest might judge of the cleansing of a leper;and offer for thy cleansing those things which Moses commanded for a testimony unto them;  :-.
