<p><strong>And there went out unto him all the land of Judea</strong>,.... The people of the land, a great number of them:</p>
<p><strong>and they of Jerusalem</strong>; the inhabitants of Jerusalem, hearing of this new preacher, the new doctrine that he taught, and the new ordinance that he administered:</p>
<p><strong>and were all baptized of him in the river Jordan, confessing their sins</strong>; that is, as many of them as were brought to a sight and sense of their sins, and made a confession of them, these he baptized, or immersed, in the river Jordan; for certain it is, that there were many of the Pharisees and Sadducees who came, whom he rejected;</p>
