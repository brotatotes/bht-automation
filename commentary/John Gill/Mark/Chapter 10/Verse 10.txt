<p><strong>And in the house</strong>,.... To which Christ retired, after he had put the Pharisees to silence, and dismissed the multitude:</p>
<p><strong>his disciples asked him again of the same matter</strong>; concerning the affair of divorces, be bad been discoursing with the Pharisees about; some things being said, they had not been used to, and which they did not thoroughly understand; and therefore chose privately to converse with him on this subject, for their further information.</p>
