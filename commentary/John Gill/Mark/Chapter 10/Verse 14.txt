<p><strong>But when Jesus saw it</strong>,.... Observed that his disciples reproved those that brought their children to, him,</p>
<p><strong>he was much displeased</strong>; with his disciples, who took too much upon them; for they ought first, to have known their master's will; whether it was his pleasure to grant the favour desired for these children, and not to have forbid them of themselves:</p>
<p><strong>and said unto them</strong>; the disciples, as the Persic version reads:</p>
<p><strong>suffer the little children to come unto me, and forbid them not, for of such is the kingdom of God</strong>; or "of them who" are, איך הלין, "as these", as the Syriac version, renders the words; or, as the Arabic, who "are like to these"; and the Persic, who are "like to these little children"; in innocence and humility; <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>.</p>
