<p><strong>And Jesus, looking upon them</strong>,.... And by their countenances saw the surprise and anxiety of mind they were in, as well as by his omniscience, knew their private reasonings among themselves:</p>
<p><strong>saith, with men it is impossible, but not with God; for with God all things are possible</strong>; even to reduce a camel to so small a size, as to go through the eye of a needle; and to work upon a rich man's heart, so as to take him off of his trust and confidence in his worldly riches, and bring him to a compliance with his will, and into his kingdom; and also to protect, and save his poor and mean followers, notwithstanding all the difficulties, dangers, and oppositions they meet with; <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>.</p>
