<p><strong>And he answered and said unto them</strong>,.... Very prudently and wisely,</p>
<p><strong>what did Moses command you</strong>? according to Matthew, he put another question to them; see <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="mt+19:4">Matthew 19:4</span>; no doubt but both were put, and this after they had urged the authority and law of Moses: and therefore be very pertinently asks them, what Moses had said about divorces, what law he had left; and puts them upon producing and repeating it, that the sense of it might be examined, and it be considered, upon what account it was given.</p>
