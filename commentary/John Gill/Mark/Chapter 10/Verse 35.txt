<p><strong>And James, and John, the sons of Zebedee, came unto him</strong>,.... Along with their mother, who was their mouth, and spoke for them, and they by her:</p>
<p><strong>saying, master, we would that thou shouldst do for us whatsoever we shall desire</strong>: that is, we earnestly desire, that whereas we have a favour to ask of thee, that thou wouldst not deny us it, be it what it will; <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>.</p>
