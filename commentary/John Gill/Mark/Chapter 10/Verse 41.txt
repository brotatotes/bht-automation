<p><strong>And when the ten heard it</strong>,.... The request made by the sons of Zebedee or their mother for them:</p>
<p><strong>they began to be much displeased with James and John</strong>; Matthew says "they were moved with indignation against them", <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="mt+20:24">Matthew 20:24</span>; they were filled with, wrath and were very angry with them; which they showed in their countenances and by their behaviour towards them as well as by words: the Syriac and Arabic versions, render it, "they began to murmur against them"; they highly resented it and were ready to break out into an open quarrel upon it;</p>
