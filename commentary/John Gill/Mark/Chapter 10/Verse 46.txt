<p><strong>And they came to Jericho</strong>,.... Christ and his disciples, from the coasts of Judea, beyond Jordan, in their way to Jerusalem; where Christ met with Zaccheus and converted him, and after some short stay at his house, departed thence;</p>
<p><strong>and as he went out of Jericho with his disciples, and a great number of people</strong>: which followed him out of that city, to go with him to Jerusalem, being but ten miles off:</p>
<p><strong>blind Bartimeus, the son of Timaeus, sat by the highway side begging</strong>; who was one of the two blind men Matthew makes mention of it,</p>
