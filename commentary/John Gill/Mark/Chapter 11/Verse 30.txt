<p><strong>The baptism of John</strong>,.... The doctrine of which he was the first preacher, and the ordinance of which he was the first administrator:</p>
<p><strong>was it from heaven, or of men</strong>? was it of divine or of human institution?</p>
<p><strong>answer me</strong>; directly and plainly, without any shuffling, or evasion: it is a fair question, and may be answered; and the answer to it our Lord suggests would naturally lead to a proper one to their question; <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>.</p>
