<p><strong>And they said unto them</strong>,.... The very express words,</p>
<p><strong>even as Jesus had commanded</strong>: not that these were the words they said, but "the Lord hath need of him": upon which they said no more, were satisfied and contented, that they should untie the colt, and take it with them:</p>
<p><strong>and they let them go</strong>; and the colt with them, very freely;</p>
