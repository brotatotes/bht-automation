And they brought it,.... The penny, which was a Roman one, and worth seven pence halfpenny of our money:and he saith unto them, whose is this image, and superscription; for it had the head of an emperor upon it, very likely the image of the then reigning emperor Tiberius, and a superscription on it, expressing his name, and perhaps a motto along with it:and they said unto him, Caesar's; one of the Roman emperors, Augustus, or Tiberius; most probably the latter; :-, :-.
