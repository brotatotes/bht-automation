<p><strong>And they brought it</strong>,.... The penny, which was a Roman one, and worth seven pence halfpenny of our money:</p>
<p><strong>and he saith unto them, whose is this image, and superscription</strong>; for it had the head of an emperor upon it, very likely the image of the then reigning emperor Tiberius, and a superscription on it, expressing his name, and perhaps a motto along with it:</p>
<p><strong>and they said unto him, Caesar's</strong>; one of the Roman emperors, Augustus, or Tiberius; most probably the latter;</p>
