<p><strong>And as touching the dead, that they rise</strong>,.... For the proof of the doctrine of the resurrection of the dead,</p>
<p><strong>have ye not read in the book of Moses</strong>; that is, in the law of Moses; for though it was divided into five parts, it was but one book; just as the Psalms are called the Book of Psalms, <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="ac+1:20">Acts 1:20</span>, and the Prophets, the Book of the Prophets, <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="ac+7:42">Acts 7:42</span>.
