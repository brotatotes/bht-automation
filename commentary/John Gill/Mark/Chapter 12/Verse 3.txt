<p><strong>And they caught him</strong>,.... This clause is left out in the Syriac and Persic versions, though it seems proper to be retained; and denotes the rudeness and violence with which the prophets of the Lord were used by the Jewish nation:</p>
<p><strong>and beat him</strong>: either with their fists, or with rods, and scourges, till the skin was flayed off:</p>
<p><strong>and sent him away empty</strong>; without any fruit to carry with him, or give an account of, to the owner of the vineyard.</p>
