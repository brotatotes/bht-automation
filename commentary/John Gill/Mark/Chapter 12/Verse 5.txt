<p><strong>And again he sent another</strong>,.... That is, another servant, or set of men, it may be in the times of the Maccabees, who were used in a very inhuman manner; see <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="heb+11:37">Hebrews 11:37</span>;</p>
<p><strong>and him they killed</strong>; either with the sword, or by inflicting some capital punishment, as stoning, strangling, c.</p>
<p><strong>and many others</strong> that is, either the owner of the vineyard sent many other servants, or the husbandmen ill used many others that were sent to them:</p>
<p><strong>beating some</strong>; with their hands, or with scourges;</p>
<p><strong>and killing some</strong>; in one or other of the above ways.</p>
