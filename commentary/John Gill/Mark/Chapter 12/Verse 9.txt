<p><strong>What shall therefore the Lord of the vineyard do</strong>?.... The Arabic and Ethiopic versions add, to them; that is, to the husbandmen, as is expressed in <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="mt+21:40">Matthew 21:40</span>,</p>
<p><span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="mt+21:40- :">Matthew 21:40- :</span>:</p>
<p><strong>he will come and destroy the husbandmen, and will give the vineyard unto others</strong>. As the former clause contains a question put by Christ upon his having finished the parable, this is an answer to it, given by the chief priests, Scribes, and elders, in whose presence, and for whose sake it was delivered; <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="mt+21:40- :">Matthew 21:40- :</span>.</p>
