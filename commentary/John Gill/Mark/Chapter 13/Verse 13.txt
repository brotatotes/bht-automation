And ye shall be hated of all men,.... Not only of your friends and relations of your countrymen the Jews; but of all men, the generality of men, in, all nations of the world, wherever they came:for my name's sake; for the sake of Christ and his Gospel, they professed and preached:but he that shall endure; reproaches, afflictions, and persecutions, patiently; or persevere in the faith of Christ, in the profession of his name, and in preaching his Gospel:to the end; of such troubles, and of life:the same shall be saved; if not with a temporal, yet with an everlasting salvation;  :-, :-.
