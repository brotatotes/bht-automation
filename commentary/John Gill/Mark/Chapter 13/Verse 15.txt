<p><strong>And let him that is on the house top</strong>,.... On the battlements of the house, either for diversion or devotion:</p>
<p><strong>not go down into the house</strong>; in the inner way by the stairs, or ladder within doors:</p>
<p><strong>neither enter therein</strong>; being come down from the top of the house, by stairs, or a ladder without, which was usual:</p>
<p><strong>to take any thing out of his house</strong>; to take care of his goods, or take any thing along with him, that might be useful in his flight, and journey, and stay abroad; <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>.</p>
