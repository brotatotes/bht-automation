And let him that is on the house top,.... On the battlements of the house, either for diversion or devotion:not go down into the house; in the inner way by the stairs, or ladder within doors:neither enter therein; being come down from the top of the house, by stairs, or a ladder without, which was usual:to take any thing out of his house; to take care of his goods, or take any thing along with him, that might be useful in his flight, and journey, and stay abroad;  :-.
