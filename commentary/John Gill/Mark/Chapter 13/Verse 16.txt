<p><strong>And let him that is in the field</strong>,.... At work, in any sort of business there,</p>
<p><strong>not turn back again</strong>: either to his own house, or rather to that part of the field where he laid down his clothes:</p>
<p><strong>for to take up his garment</strong>; but let him flee without it, or otherwise he would be in great danger; <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>.</p>
