<p><strong>For false Christs and false prophets shall rise</strong>,.... As there did, both before and after the destruction of Jerusalem:</p>
<p><strong>and shall show signs and wonders</strong>; as they pretended to do, and did, at least to the appearance of people:</p>
<p><strong>to seduce, if it were possible, even the elect</strong>; who were chosen in Christ, unto eternal life; in consequence of which they truly believed in him, and were constant followers of him; but this was not possible: though such was the force of their deception, that there was apparent danger of it, were it not for the power and grace of God; <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>.</p>
