<p><strong>But in those days, after that tribulation</strong>,.... That is, after the destruction of the city and temple of Jerusalem, and the miseries and calamities attending it, in the times immediately following it; see <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="mt+24:29">Matthew 24:29</span>;</p>
<p><strong>the sun shall be darkened</strong>: so the Shekinah, or glory of the divine majesty was withdrawn, and all the symbols of the divine presence were gone, when the temple was destroyed:</p>
<p><strong>and the moon shall not give her light</strong>; the ceremonial law, which though abolished by the death of Christ, was observed by the Jews as long as the temple stood; but now ceased, particularly that principal branch of it, the daily sacrifice; <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="mt+24:29- :">Matthew 24:29- :</span>.</p>
