<p><strong>For many shall come in my name</strong>,.... Taking upon them the name of the Messiah: saying,</p>
<p><strong>I am [Christ]</strong>; the word "Christ", is rightly supplied from</p>
<p><span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="mt+24:5">Matthew 24:5</span>; otherwise in the original it is only, "I am"; which the Persic version doubles, reading it, "I am indeed, I am": he that was promised and expected, the true Messiah; he that was to come:</p>
<p><strong>and shall deceive many</strong>; <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="mt+24:5- :">Matthew 24:5- :</span>.</p>
