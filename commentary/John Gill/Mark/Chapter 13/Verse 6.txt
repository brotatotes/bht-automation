For many shall come in my name,.... Taking upon them the name of the Messiah: saying,I am [Christ]; the word "Christ", is rightly supplied fromMatthew 24:5; otherwise in the original it is only, "I am"; which the Persic version doubles, reading it, "I am indeed, I am": he that was promised and expected, the true Messiah; he that was to come:and shall deceive many; Matthew 24:5- :.
