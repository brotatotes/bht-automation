And when ye shall hear of wars, and rumours of wars,.... Among the Jews themselves, and with the Romans:be not troubled; keep your place, abide by your work, go on preaching the Gospel, without distressing yourselves about the event of things:for [such things] must needs be: being decreed by God, foretold by Christ, and made necessary by the sins of the people:but the end shall not be yet; of the temple, of Jerusalem, and of the Jewish state and nation;  :-.
