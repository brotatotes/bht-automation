<p><strong>And he will show you a large upper room</strong>,.... A room in the highest part of the house, large enough for such a company, for thirteen persons, which was the number of Christ and his disciples:</p>
<p><strong>furnished and prepared</strong>; with a table, and a sufficient number of couches to sit, or lie upon, and with all proper vessels necessary on such an occasion:</p>
<p><strong>there make ready for us</strong>; the passover.</p>
