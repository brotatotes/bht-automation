<p><strong>And his disciples went forth</strong>,.... The two disciples, as the Arabic version has it, Peter and John, set out from Bethany to Jerusalem directly:</p>
<p><strong>and came into the city</strong>; the city of Jerusalem:</p>
<p><strong>and found as he had said unto them</strong>; a man bearing a pitcher of water, whom they followed to the house he went into, and addressed the master of the house, as Jesus had bid them; when he showed them an upper room, very commodious and fit for the purpose, as Christ had said; and which is a considerable proof of the prescience of Christ:</p>
<p><strong>and they made ready the passover</strong>; they bought a lamb; they had it killed in the temple, according to rule; and they brought it to the house, where they were to sup, and got it roasted; and provided unleavened bread, and wine, and bitter herbs, and every thing that was proper for the feast; <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>.</p>
