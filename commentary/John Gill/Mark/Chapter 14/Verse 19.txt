And they began to be sorrowful,.... And were so, all but Judas, at this saying of Christ's:and to say unto him, one by one; even till it came to Judas himself,is it I? that shall betray thee;and another said, is it I? This clause is wanting in the Vulgate Latin, Syriac, Arabic, Persic, and Ethiopic versions, and in two of Beza's copies; and indeed seems to be redundant, since the disciples are said before to express themselves in this manner, one by one; :-.
