<p><strong>And as they did eat</strong>,.... The paschal lamb, and the unleavened bread, just at the conclusion of that feast:</p>
<p><strong>Jesus took bread, and blessed, and brake it</strong>; beginning and instituting a new feast, to be kept in aftertimes, in commemoration of his sufferings and death, now near at hand;</p>
<p><strong>and gave to them</strong>, the disciples,</p>
<p><strong>and said, take, eat</strong>: the word eat is not in the Vulgate Latin, Syriac, Arabic, Persic, and Ethiopic versions, and is wanting in some copies:</p>
<p><strong>this is body</strong>; a figure and representation of it;</p>
