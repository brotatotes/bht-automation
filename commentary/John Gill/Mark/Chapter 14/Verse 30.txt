<p><strong>And Jesus saith unto him, verily I say unto thee</strong>,.... As confident as thou art of standing by me, and abiding with me;</p>
<p><strong>that this day</strong>, which was then begun; for the Jews reckoned their days from evening, as in <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="ge+1:5">Genesis 1:5</span>;</p>
<p><strong>even in this night</strong>; this night to be observed, this night of the passover, before it is past:</p>
<p><strong>before the cock crow twice</strong>; for there was a first and second cock crowing, the one at midnight, and the other near break of day, and which last is properly the cock crowing: the word "twice" is left out in the Ethiopic version:</p>
<p><strong>thou shalt deny me thrice</strong>; as he did; <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="ge+1:5- :">Genesis 1:5- :</span>.</p>
