<p><strong>But he spake the more vehemently</strong>,.... With a louder voice; with more spirit and eagerness; in a more peremptory and self-confident way.</p>
<p><strong>If I should die with thee, I will not deny thee in any wise</strong>. The Syriac version adds, "O my Lord", my dear Lord, I will never deny thee upon any consideration whatever; and the Persic version, O Lord:</p>
<p><strong>likewise also said they all</strong>; as he said, so said "all the disciples", as the Syriac version reads it; <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>.</p>
