<p><strong>And they came to a place which is named Gethsemane</strong>,.... At the foot of the Mount of Olives, where the olives, which grew in great plenty on the mount, were pressed: and where our Lord began to be bruised, for our sins:</p>
<p><strong>and be saith to his disciples</strong>: to eight of them:</p>
<p><strong>sit ye here while I shall pray</strong>; at some distance from hence;</p>
