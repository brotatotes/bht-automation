<p><strong>And again he went away</strong>,.... To the same place, or at much such a distance from them, as before:</p>
<p><strong>and prayed and spake the same words</strong>; or word, that is, the same matter; for λογος, here, answers to דבר, which signifies a thing, or matter, as well as word: Christ prayed to the same effect, for matter and substance the same as before, though not in the same express words, as is clear from <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="mt+26:39">Matthew 26:39</span>.</p>
