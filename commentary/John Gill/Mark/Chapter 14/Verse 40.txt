<p><strong>And when he returned, he found them asleep again</strong>,.... Notwithstanding the expostulation he had used with them, the exhortation he had given them, and the danger he had suggested to them:</p>
<p><strong>for their eyes were heavy</strong>: with sleep and sorrow:</p>
<p><strong>neither wist they what to answer him</strong>; partly through confusion and shame, not knowing how to excuse themselves; and partly, through their being stupefied with sleep and grief.</p>
