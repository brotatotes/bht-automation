And he cometh the third time,.... After he had prayed a third time, to the same purport as before:and saith unto them, sleep on now, and take your rest; which words are spoken ironically:it is enough; or "the end is come"; as the Syriac and Arabic versions render it, of watching and praying:the hour is come, behold the son of man is betrayed into the hands of sinners; both Jews and Gentiles, by one of his own disciples; :-.
