<p><strong>I was daily with you in the temple</strong>,.... That is, for several days past; ever since he made his public entry into Jerusalem:</p>
<p><strong>teaching</strong>; the people, in a public manner:</p>
<p><strong>and ye took me not</strong>; did not attempt to lay hands on him, seize him, and carry him away; which he signifies might have been easily done; <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>.</p>
<p><strong>But the Scriptures must be fulfilled</strong>: which spoke of the betraying him by Judas; and of their taking him in this private and secret manner; and of the flight of the disciples from him next mentioned;</p>
<p><span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>.</p>
