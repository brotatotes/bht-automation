<p><strong>And Jesus said, I am</strong>,.... That is, the Son of God; in proof of which he adds,</p>
<p><strong>and ye shall see the son of man sitting on the right hand of power</strong>; that is, of God, who is all power, the Lord God Almighty:</p>
<p><strong>and coming in the clouds of heaven</strong>; either at the destruction of Jerusalem, or at the last day, referring to the prophecy in <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="da+7:13">Daniel 7:13</span>;</p>
