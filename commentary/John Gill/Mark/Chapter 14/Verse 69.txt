<p><strong>And a maid saw him again</strong>,.... Either the same maid, so the Syriac and Persic versions read, "that maid": that selfsame maid, as before, or another, as in <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="mt+26:71">Matthew 26:71</span>, and so the Arabic version reads it here; but the Ethiopic as before "a daughter"; that is, of the high priest:</p>
<p><strong>and began to say to them that stood by</strong>; the fire, along with Peter, warming themselves:</p>
<p><strong>this is one of them</strong>; this man is one of the disciples and followers of Jesus of Nazareth; he is of that sect, he certainly belongs to them, and is come here only as a spy.</p>
