<p><strong>For ye have the poor with you always</strong>,....</p>
<p><span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>;</p>
<p><strong>and whensoever ye will ye may do them good</strong>; by feeding them when hungry, clothing them when naked, and supplying them with the necessaries of life:</p>
<p><strong>but me ye have not always</strong>; meaning, with respect to his bodily presence, which, in a short time, would be removed from them, and they would have no opportunity of showing him any such outward respect personally; <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>.</p>
