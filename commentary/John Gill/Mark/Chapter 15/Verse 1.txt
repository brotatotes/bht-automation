<p><strong>And straightway in the morning</strong>,.... As soon as it was break of day, or daylight appeared:</p>
<p><strong>the chief priests held a consultation with the elders and Scribes</strong>; who were the principal men in the sanhedrim:</p>
<p><strong>and the whole council</strong>; which, on this extraordinary occasion, was convened; the result of which was, to bind Jesus, and deliver him up to the Roman governor, to be put to death by him, as a seditious person, and an enemy to Caesar, and accordingly they did so:</p>
<p><strong>and bound Jesus, and carried him away, and delivered him to Pilate</strong>. The Syriac and Persic versions add, "the governor";</p>
