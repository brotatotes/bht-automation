<p><strong>And Pilate answered and said again unto them</strong>,.... Being astonished that they should ask the release of such an infamous person; and being very desirous of saving Jesus:</p>
<p><strong>what will ye then that I shall do unto him, whom ye call the king of the Jews</strong>? at least many of you; would you have me put him to death? surely this can never be desired; or would you have me inflict some slight punishment on him, as scourging him, and so dismiss him?</p>
