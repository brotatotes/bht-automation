<p><strong>And they bring him unto the place, Golgotha</strong>,.... A famous, or rather an infamous one, well known, and much noted for the many executions there:</p>
<p><strong>which is, being interpreted, the place of a skull</strong>; because the skulls of men that had been executed and buried there, being dug up again, lay scattered about; <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>.</p>
