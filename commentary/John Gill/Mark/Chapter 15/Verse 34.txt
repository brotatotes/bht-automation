And at the ninth hour Jesus cried with a loud voice,.... :-;saying, Eloi, Eloi, lama sabachthani? in Matthew it is, "Eli, Eli", Both "Eli" and "Eloi", are Hebrew words, and signify the same; and are both used in Psalms 22:1, from whence the whole is taken:which is, being interpreted, my God, my God, why hast thou forsaken me?
