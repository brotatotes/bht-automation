<p><strong>And at the ninth hour Jesus cried with a loud voice</strong>,....</p>
<p><span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>;</p>
<p><strong>saying, Eloi, Eloi, lama sabachthani</strong>? in Matthew it is, "Eli, Eli", Both "Eli" and "Eloi", are Hebrew words, and signify the same; and are both used in <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="ps+22:1">Psalms 22:1</span>, from whence the whole is taken:</p>
<p><strong>which is, being interpreted, my God, my God, why hast thou forsaken me</strong>? <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="ps+22:1- :">Psalms 22:1- :</span>.</p>
