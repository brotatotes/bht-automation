<p><strong>And Pilate marvelled if he were already dead</strong>,.... For death, by crucifixion, was a slow lingering death; persons that were in their full strength hung a great while before they expired; and the two thieves, which were crucified with Christ, were not dead when he was:</p>
<p><strong>and calling unto him the centurion</strong>; who was set to watch him:</p>
<p><strong>he asked him, whether he had been any while dead</strong>; he inquired of him, whether he was dead, and how long he had been dead.</p>
