<p><strong>And Mary Magdalene, and Mary the mother of Joses</strong>,.... Or Joseph, as the Vulgate Latin and Ethiopic versions read:</p>
<p><strong>beheld where he was laid</strong>: very likely they saw Joseph, and his men, take him down from the cross, and they followed him, and observed where he laid him; or, as the Ethiopic version reads, "where they buried him"; placing themselves, as Matthew suggests, right "over against the sepulchre", <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="mt+27:61">Matthew 27:61</span>; so that they were witnesses of his death, and of his burial, as they afterwards were of his resurrection from the dead.</p>
