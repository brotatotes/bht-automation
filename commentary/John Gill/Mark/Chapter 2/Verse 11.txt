<p><strong>I say unto thee, arise, and take up thy bed</strong>,.... He bid him, in an authoritative way to arise from his bed, in which he was brought, and on which he lay before him, and take it up upon his shoulders, directly, and in the face of all the people, carry it away:</p>
<p><strong>and go thy way into thine house</strong>; to show himself whole to his family and friends, and go about his business; <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>,</p>
