<p><strong>And when the Scribes and Pharisees saw him eat</strong>,.... They were offended at his eating and drinking, though it was in moderation; because he did not fast as they, and their disciples did; and especially, that he eat</p>
<p><strong>with publicans and sinners</strong>; men of very infamous characters, and bad lives, with whom the Pharisees disdained to keep company:</p>
<p><strong>they said unto his disciples, how is it that he eateth and drinketh with publicans and sinners</strong>? The Vulgate Latin, Arabic, Persic, and Ethiopic versions read, "your master", <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>; so some Greek copies here.</p>
