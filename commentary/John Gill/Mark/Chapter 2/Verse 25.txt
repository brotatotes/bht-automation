<p><strong>And he said unto them</strong>,.... By way of answer to their question, and which was a full one, and enough to silence them:</p>
<p><strong>have ye never read what David did</strong>; referring to the history in</p>
<p><span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="1sa+21:1">1 Samuel 21:1</span>.</p>
<p><strong>when he had need</strong>: of bread, was in great necessity, and in the utmost distress:</p>
<p><strong>and was an hungered, he, and they that were with him</strong>? which was a justifiable reason for what he and his company did; as it was for the action of the disciples; being in a like case, and therefore very appropriate to the purpose; <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="1sa+21:1- :">1 Samuel 21:1- :</span>.</p>
