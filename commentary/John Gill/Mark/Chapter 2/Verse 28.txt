<p><strong>Therefore the son of man is Lord also of the sabbath.</strong> Meaning himself, who had a power not only to dispense with it, but to abrogate it as he did, with the rest of the rituals of the ceremonial law; <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>. So that it did not become them to find fault with what his disciples did, with his leave and approbation.</p>
