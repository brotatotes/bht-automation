<p><strong>And to have power to heal sicknesses</strong>,.... All manner of corporeal diseases that attend men and women:</p>
<p><strong>and to cast out devils</strong>; from such who were possessed with them: that is, he chose and appointed them to be his apostles, with a view of conferring such powers upon them hereafter; for as yet, they were not vested with them, nor sent out to exercise them; no, not till near twelve months after.</p>
