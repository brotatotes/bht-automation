<p><strong>There came then his brethren and his mother</strong>,.... At the same time he was speaking to the Scribes, who seem to be different persons from his friends and kinsmen, <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="mr+3:21">Mark 3:21</span>,</p>
<p><strong>and standing without</strong>; for Christ was within, in the house, talking with the Scribes and Pharisees, and preaching to the people; and the crowd being so great, that they could not get into the house; they</p>
<p><strong>sent unto him, calling him</strong>: they not only sent one in to let him know who they were, and that they were without doors, desirous to speak with him; but also, with a voice as loud as they could, called to him themselves; <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="mr+3:21- :">Mark 3:21- :</span>.</p>
