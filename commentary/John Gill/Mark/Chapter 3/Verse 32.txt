<p><strong>And the multitude sat about him</strong>,.... In a circle, all around him, to hear him preach; so that there was no such thing as the messenger coming near him; but the message being whispered from one to another, it came to those who sat nearest him:</p>
<p><strong>and they said unto him, behold, thy mother and thy brethren without seek for thee</strong>: in five of Beza's ancient copies, and in his most ancient one, are added, and thy sisters: agreeably, Christ hereafter makes mention of sister, as well as mother and brother;</p>
<p><span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>, and so it is read in the Alexandrian copy.</p>
