<p><strong>And he answered them</strong>,.... The multitude that sat about him, and informed him of his mother and brethren being without, and desirous of speaking with him:</p>
<p><strong>saying, who is my mother, or my brethren</strong>? which is said not through ignorance or contempt, but either, as displeased with the interruption given him; or with a view to take an opportunity from hence of expressing his superior value to his spiritual relations; which looks with no favourable aspect on the superstitious notions, and veneration of the virgin Mary among the papists;</p>
