<p><strong>And he looked round about on them which sat about him</strong>,.... To find out his disciples among them, and point them out particularly, by stretching forth his hand towards them: and said,</p>
<p><strong>behold my mother and my brethren</strong>: not in a natural, but in a spiritual sense; his mother, because, in regeneration, he was formed in them; his brethren, because, by adoption, his God was their God, his Father their Father; <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>.</p>
