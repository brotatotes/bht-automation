<p><strong>And he saith unto them, know ye not this parable</strong>?.... So easy to be understood, taken from things common, and which fall under every one's observation:</p>
<p><strong>and how then will you know all parables</strong>? if not this single one, and which is so plain, how will ye be able to understand the numerous parables hereafter to be related, and which will be much more difficult?</p>
