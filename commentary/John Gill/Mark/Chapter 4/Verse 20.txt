<p><strong>And these are they which are sown on good ground</strong>,.... Such hearers who are intended by the good ground on which other seed fell, are those who are made good men by the grace of God; for there is none good naturally, nor that doeth good, no not one; these are</p>
<p><strong>such who hear the word, and receive it</strong>; as the word of God, in whose hearts it works effectually; who receive it not into their heads only, but into their hearts; and having received it, hold it fast, and abide by it in the worst of times:</p>
<p><strong>and bring forth fruit, some thirty fold, some sixty, and some an hundred</strong>; all bring forth good fruit of the same quality, though not of the same quantity: for a larger exposition of this explanation of the parable, see the notes on <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="mt+13:19">Matthew 13:19</span>.</p>
