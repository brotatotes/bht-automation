<p><strong>And when they were alone, he expounded all things to his disciples</strong>: after they returned with him from the sea side, to the house in Capernaum, where he usually was when there; see <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="mt+13:36">Matthew 13:36</span>. The multitude being dismissed, he unfolded and explained all these parables to his disciples, and led them into a large knowledge of himself, and the mysteries of the kingdom of heaven; whereby they were furnished for the work he had called them to, and designed them for.</p>
