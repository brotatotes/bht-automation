<p><strong>And the same day, when the even was come</strong>,.... After he had finished his parables among the multitude, and had explained them to his disciples:</p>
<p><strong>he saith unto them</strong>; his disciples,</p>
<p><strong>let us pass over unto the other side</strong>: that is, of the sea of Galilee, or lake of Gennesaret, to the country of the Gadarenes, and Gergesenes; with a view for retirement and rest, after the fatigue of the day; and for the trial of the faith of his disciples, by a storm which he knew would arise, whilst they were on the sea; and for the sake of a miracle he was to work on the other side, after related.</p>
