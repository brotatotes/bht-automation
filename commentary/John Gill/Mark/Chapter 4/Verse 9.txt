<p><strong>And he said unto them</strong>,.... To the multitude of hearers that were on the sea shore attending to the word preached, and among whom, doubtless, there were all those sorts of hearers mentioned in this parable:</p>
<p><strong>he that hath ears to hear, let him hear</strong>: observe, and take notice of what has been said, as being of the greatest moment and importance: for a larger explanation and illustration of this parable, see the notes on <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="mt+13:3">Matthew 13:3</span>.</p>
