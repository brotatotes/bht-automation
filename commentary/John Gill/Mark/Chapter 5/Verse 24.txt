<p><strong>And Jesus went with him</strong>,.... Immediately without objecting to the weakness of his faith:</p>
<p><strong>and much people followed him</strong>; to see the miracle performed, which they were exceedingly fond of:</p>
<p><strong>and thronged him</strong>; so that it was with some difficulty and trouble that he passed along the streets to: the ruler's house.</p>
