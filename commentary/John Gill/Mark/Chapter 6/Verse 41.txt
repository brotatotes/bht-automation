<p><strong>And when he had taken the five loaves and two fishes</strong>,.... Out of the hands of those that brought them into his own:</p>
<p><strong>he looked up to heaven, and blessed, and brake the loaves</strong>:</p>
<p><span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>,</p>
<p><strong>and gave them to his disciples to set before them</strong>; the multitude, in order to eat of them:</p>
<p><strong>and the two fishes divided he among them all</strong>; that every one might have a part. The Syriac and Persic versions read, they divided; that is, the apostles.</p>
