<p><strong>And they that did eat of the loaves</strong>,.... And also of the fishes; for they all ate of both;</p>
<p><strong>were about five thousand men</strong>; the word ωσει, "about", is omitted in the Vulgate Latin, Syriac, Arabic, Persic and Ethiopic versions, reading five thousand men certain. The Evangelist Matthew adds, "beside children and women", <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="mt+14:21">Matthew 14:21</span>.</p>
