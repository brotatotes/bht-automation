<p><strong>And when they were come out of the ship</strong>,.... Christ and his disciples, and were ashore,</p>
<p><strong>straightway they knew him</strong>; that is, the men of that place, as in Mt. 14:35, <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>; and so the Syriac, Arabic, and Persic versions read here, "the inhabitants of that country", the country of Gennesaret; they knew him, having seen and heard him before.</p>
