<p><strong>And whithersoever he entered, into villages, or cities, or country</strong>,.... Whether in smaller towns, or larger cities, or the fields, where were houses, here and there one:</p>
<p><strong>they laid the sick in the streets</strong>; or "markets", in any public places:</p>
<p><strong>and besought him that they but might touch the border of his garment</strong>; if they might not be admitted to touch his person, or he did not choose to lay his hands on them:</p>
<p><strong>and as many as touched him</strong>; or "it", the border of his garment, as they desired:</p>
<p><strong>were made whole</strong>; of whatsoever sickness, or disease, they were afflicted with; <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>.</p>
