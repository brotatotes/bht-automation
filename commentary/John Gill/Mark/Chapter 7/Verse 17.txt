<p><strong>And when he was entered into the house</strong>,.... Very probably at Capernaum, and it may be the house of Simon and Andrew, where he used to be when there:</p>
<p><strong>from the people</strong>; being separated from them, having dismissed and left them, when he and his disciples were by themselves alone:</p>
<p><strong>his disciples asked him concerning the parable</strong>; that saying of his to the people, which was somewhat dark and intricate to them; that nothing without a man going into him defiled him, but what comes out of him: this was asked by Peter, in the name of the rest;</p>
