<p><strong>And he saith unto them</strong>,.... With some warmth of spirit and resentment, at their stupidity:</p>
<p><strong>are ye so without understanding also</strong>? As well as others, and to such a degree; and "yet", as Matthew expresses it, <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="mt+15:16">Matthew 15:16</span>, so wretchedly stupid, and so long, and as much, as others:</p>
<p><strong>do ye not perceive</strong>? common sense will tell you,</p>
<p><strong>that whatsoever thing from, without entereth into the man, it cannot defile him</strong>; <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="mt+15:16- :">Matthew 15:16- :</span>.</p>
