<p><strong>And he said</strong>,.... Continued to say in his discourse; though this is left but in the Syriac version;</p>
<p><strong>that which cometh out of the man, that defileth the man</strong>; meaning, not his excrements, which were unclean by the law, <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="de+23:13">Deuteronomy 23:13</span> but what comes out of his heart, by his mouth; or is expressed in action, as appears by what follows; <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="de+23:13- :">Deuteronomy 23:13- :</span>.</p>
