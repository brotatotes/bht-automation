And he said,.... Continued to say in his discourse; though this is left but in the Syriac version;that which cometh out of the man, that defileth the man; meaning, not his excrements, which were unclean by the law, Deuteronomy 23:13 but what comes out of his heart, by his mouth; or is expressed in action, as appears by what follows; Deuteronomy 23:13- :.
