<p><strong>And they reasoned among themselves</strong>,.... Upon Christ's giving this caution, and recollecting with themselves, that they had forgot to buy any provisions, and take with them:</p>
<p><strong>saying, it is because we have no bread</strong>; that he says these words; tacitly chiding and reproving us, for our want of thought and care;</p>
