<p><strong>And when the seven among four thousand</strong>,.... That is, when seven loaves were broken among four thousand men,</p>
<p><strong>how many baskets full of fragments took ye up</strong>? this surely you must remember, it being so recent an action, but just done, as it were:</p>
<p><strong>and they said, seven</strong>; for this, as yet, could not have slipped their memories; though they had, been reasoning among themselves because of their straitness of provisions, as if these things had never been done.</p>
