<p><strong>He answereth him</strong>,.... The father of the child, and who is included in the reproof afterwards given, for his unbelief, and taking part with the Scribes against his disciples; though the Vulgate Latin, Arabic, Persic, and Ethiopic versions, read, "them"; meaning not his disciples, but the Scribes and Pharisees, with the father of the child: and saith,</p>
<p><strong>O faithless generation, how long shall I be with you? how long shall I suffer you? bring him unto me</strong>; <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>.</p>
