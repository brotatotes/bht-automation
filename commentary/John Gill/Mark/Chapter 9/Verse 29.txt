<p><strong>And he said unto them</strong>,.... Matthew, besides the following reason, assigns another, as given by our Lord, why they could not cast out the foul spirit, which was their unbelief; of which they were guilty in some sort, as well as the Jews, and the father of the child; but Mark omits it, and only relates this as the reason;</p>
<p><strong>this kind can come forth by nothing, but by prayer and fasting</strong>; which they had not observed; <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>.</p>
