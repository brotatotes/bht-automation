<p><strong>And suddenly, when they had looked round about</strong>,.... Upon hearing the voice, to see if they could observe any other object, by whom it was pronounced, and whether the same they had seen continued:</p>
<p><strong>they saw no man any more</strong>; neither Moses, nor Elias, not at that time, nor ever after;</p>
