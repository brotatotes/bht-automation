<p><strong>And Ezekias begat Manasses</strong>,.... Or Manasseh, <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="2ki+20:21">2 Kings 20:21</span>. Him Hezekiah begat of Hephzibah, <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="2ki+21:1">2 Kings 21:1</span>. He was very remarkable both for his sins, and for his humiliation on account of them.</p>
<p><strong>And Manasses begat Amon</strong>, of Meshullemeth, the daughter of Haruz of Jotbah, <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="2ki+21:19">2 Kings 21:19</span>. He was a very wicked prince.</p>
<p><strong>And Amon begat Josias</strong>, or Josiah of Jedidah, the daughter of Adaiah of Boscath, <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="2ki+22:1">2 Kings 22:1</span>. He was a very pious king, and was prophesied of by name some hundreds of years before he was born,</p>
<p><span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="1ki+13:2">1 Kings 13:2</span>.</p>
