<p><strong>And Ozias begat Joatham</strong>,.... Called Jotham, <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="2ki+15:7">2 Kings 15:7</span> him Ozias begat of Jerushah, the daughter of Zadok, <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="2ki+15:33">2 Kings 15:33</span>.</p>
<p><strong>And Joatham begat Achaz</strong>, or Ahaz, <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="2ki+15:38">2 Kings 15:38</span> to him the sign was given, and the famous prophecy of the Messiah, <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="isa+7:14">Isaiah 7:14</span>.</p>
<p><strong>And Achaz begat Ezekias</strong>, or Hezekiah, <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="2ki+16:20">2 Kings 16:20</span> him Ahaz begat of Abi, the daughter of Zachariah, <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="2ki+18:2">2 Kings 18:2</span>. He was a very religious king, and had that singular favour from God to have fifteen years added to his days, <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="isa+38:5">Isaiah 38:5</span>.</p>
