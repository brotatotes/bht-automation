<p><strong>Neither the seven loaves of the four thousand</strong>,.... Have you forgot the other miracle done but a very little while ago, when I fed four thousand men, beside women and children, with seven loaves and a few small fishes;</p>
<p><strong>and how many baskets ye took up</strong>? no less than seven large baskets; and am I not able to provide for you? distress not yourselves about this matter; give not way to unbelief, which must argue great stupidity and insensibility.</p>
