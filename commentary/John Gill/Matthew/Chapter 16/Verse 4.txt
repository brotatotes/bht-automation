<p><strong>A wicked and adulterous generation</strong>,.... He says the same things here, as he did to the Pharisees on a like occasion, in <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="mt+12:39">Matthew 12:39</span>.</p>
<p><strong>And he left them</strong>; as persons hardened, perverse, and incurable, and as unworthy to be conversed with:</p>
<p><strong>and departed</strong>: to the ship which brought him thither, and went in it to the other side of the sea of Galilee; see <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="mr+8:13">Mark 8:13</span>.</p>
