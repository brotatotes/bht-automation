A wicked and adulterous generation,.... He says the same things here, as he did to the Pharisees on a like occasion, in Matthew 12:39.And he left them; as persons hardened, perverse, and incurable, and as unworthy to be conversed with:and departed: to the ship which brought him thither, and went in it to the other side of the sea of Galilee; see Mark 8:13.
