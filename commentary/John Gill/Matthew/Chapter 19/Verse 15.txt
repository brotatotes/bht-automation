<p><strong>And he laid his hands on them</strong>,.... "And blessed them", as Mark says; he put his hands upon them, according to the custom of the country, and wished all kind of prosperity to them:</p>
<p><strong>and departed thence</strong>, out of the house where he had been, and his disciples with him: the Ethiopic version renders it, "and they went from thence", from those parts, towards Jerusalem.</p>
