<p><strong>Take that thine is</strong>,.... By agreement, and go thy way; out of my sight, give me no more trouble on this head; which looks like a dismissal from his service, and after privileges; and was true of many among the Jews, who were only nominal professors, and from whom the Gospel and ordinances of it were taken:</p>
<p><strong>I will give unto this last</strong> man that was called, and sent into the vineyard,</p>
<p><strong>even as unto thee</strong>; the same outward privileges, besides special grace, and eternal glory, which it looks as if the other had not.</p>
