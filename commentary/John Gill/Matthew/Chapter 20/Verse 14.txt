Take that thine is,.... By agreement, and go thy way; out of my sight, give me no more trouble on this head; which looks like a dismissal from his service, and after privileges; and was true of many among the Jews, who were only nominal professors, and from whom the Gospel and ordinances of it were taken:I will give unto this last man that was called, and sent into the vineyard,even as unto thee; the same outward privileges, besides special grace, and eternal glory, which it looks as if the other had not.
