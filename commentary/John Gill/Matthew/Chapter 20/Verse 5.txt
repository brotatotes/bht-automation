<p><strong>Again he went out about the sixth and ninth hour</strong>,.... About twelve o'clock, or at noon, and three o'clock in the afternoon. These three last mentioned seasons of the day, were the hours of prayer; see <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="ac+2:15">Acts 2:15</span> and did likewise: seeing others in the same place, and posture, he called them, and sent them into his vineyard, to labour there, giving them the same promise he did to others.</p>
