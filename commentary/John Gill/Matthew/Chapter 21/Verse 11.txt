<p><strong>And the multitude said</strong>,.... Or the people, as the Vulgate Latin, and Munster's Hebrew Gospel read; the common people, that went before, and followed after him: these knew Christ better than the inhabitants of Jerusalem, the Scribes and Pharisees, and rulers of the people.</p>
<p><strong>This is Jesus the prophet</strong>: that prophet Moses spoke of, in</p>
<p><span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="de+18:15">Deuteronomy 18:15</span> and the nation of the Jews in general expected:</p>
<p><strong>of Nazareth of Galilee</strong>; who, though he was not born there, yet being educated, and having lived much in that place, is said to be of it; and which was the common opinion of the people.</p>
