<p><strong>Again he sent other servants</strong>,.... Meaning, perhaps, such as suffered in the times of the Maccabees:</p>
<p><strong>more than the first</strong>; their number was greater, though their office was the same, at least not higher:</p>
<p><strong>and they did unto them likewise</strong>; they beat them with rods, they killed them with the sword, and stoned them, <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="heb+11:36">Hebrews 11:36</span>.</p>
