<p><strong>He also that had received two talents</strong>,.... A lesser degree of ministerial gifts; and who as he received next to the other, and was the next, who in proportion to what he had received, had traded and gained, he is mentioned in the next: place, as giving in his account; who</p>
<p><strong>came and said, Lord, thou deliveredst unto me two talents, behold I have gained two other talents besides them</strong>: his account, abating the sum and gains, is given in, in the same form as the other.</p>
