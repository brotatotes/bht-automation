<p><strong>The governor answered and said unto them</strong>,.... A second time, after some time had been allowed and taken up to consider of the matter, and which the chief priests and elders improved among the people against Jesus.</p>
<p><strong>Whether of the twain will ye that I release unto you</strong>? for as these two were proposed, one of them must be released; and it lay in the breast of the people to choose which they would:</p>
<p><strong>they said, Barabbas</strong>; so that Christ was not only numbered among, and reckoned with transgressors, but he was accounted worse than the worst of them; a seditious person, a robber, and a murderer was preferred before him: see <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="ac+3:14">Acts 3:14</span>.</p>
