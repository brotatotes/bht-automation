<p><strong>And they straightway left their nets</strong>,.... That is, as soon as he had called them, they left their worldly employment, and followed him; they gave up themselves to his service, and became his disciples; they not only left their "nets", but their fishing boats, and fishing trade, and all that belonged to it, even all their substance; and also their relations, friends, and acquaintance, see</p>
<p><span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="mt+19:27">Matthew 19:27</span> which shows what a mighty power went along with the words and call of Christ; and what a ready, cheerful, and voluntary subjection this produces, wherever it takes place.</p>
