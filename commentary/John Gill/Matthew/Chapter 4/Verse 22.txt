<p><strong>And they immediately left the ship</strong>,.... More is expressed here than before, for they not only left their nets, but their ship too; which was of much more value; nay, even "their father" also, "with the hired servants", <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="mr+1:20">Mark 1:20</span> and so complied with the call of Christ to his people, <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="ps+45:10">Psalms 45:10</span> and thereby proved, that they were sincerely his followers, <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="lu+14:26">Luke 14:26</span> and might expect the gracious promise of Christ to be made good unto them,</p>
