<p><strong>And he opened his mouth</strong>,.... He spoke with a clear and strong voice, that all the people might hear him; and with great freedom, utterance, and cheerfulness, and things of the greatest moment and importance;</p>
<p><strong>and taught them</strong>; not his disciples only, but the whole multitude, who heard him with astonishment; see <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="mt+7:28">Matthew 7:28</span>. Some things in the following discourse are directed to the disciples in particular, and others regard the multitude in general.</p>
