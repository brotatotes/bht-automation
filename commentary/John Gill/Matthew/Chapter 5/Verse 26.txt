<p><strong>Verily, I say unto thee</strong>,.... This may be depended upon, you may assure yourself of it, that</p>
<p><strong>thou shalt by no means come out thence</strong>, from prison,</p>
<p><strong>till thou hast paid the uttermost farthing</strong>, or "last farthing"; or as the Ethiopic version reads it, "till thou hast exactly paid all"; which seems to express the inexorableness of the creditor, and the impossibility of the debtor's release.</p>
