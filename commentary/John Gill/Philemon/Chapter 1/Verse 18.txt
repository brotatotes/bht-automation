<p><strong>If he hath wronged thee</strong>,.... By squandering away his time, spoiling his work, or corrupting his fellow servants:</p>
<p><strong>or oweth thee ought</strong>; by embezzling his master's goods, robbing him of his money, and running away from his service:</p>
<p><strong>put that on mine account</strong>; Signifying that he would be answerable for all, and make good all debts and damages.</p>
