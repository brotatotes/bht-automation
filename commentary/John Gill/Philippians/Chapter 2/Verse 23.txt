<p><strong>Him therefore I hope to send presently</strong>,.... For the reasons now given:</p>
<p><strong>so soon as I shall see how it will go with me</strong>; whether he should be released from his bonds or not; whether he should live or die; whether he should be set free, or be called to suffer martyrdom for the sake of Christ; for he expected, that the matter would be determined in a very short time, when, be it at it would, Timothy would be spared.</p>
