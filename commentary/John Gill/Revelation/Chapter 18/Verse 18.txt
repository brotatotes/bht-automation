<p><strong>And cried when they saw the smoke of her burning</strong>,....</p>
<p><span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span></p>
<p><strong>saying, what city is like unto this great city</strong>? as before for magnificence and grandeur, so now for sorrow, desolation, and ruin; nor was any city like it for power and authority, for pride and luxury, for idolatry and superstition, blasphemy and impenitence; the like the sailors say of Tyre, <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="eze+27:30">Ezekiel 27:30</span> from whence this and other expressions are borrowed in this lamentation.</p>
