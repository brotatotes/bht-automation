<p><strong>And he came</strong>,.... He drew nigh to the throne of God, he engaged his heart to approach unto him, and came up even to his seat, which a mere creature, without a Mediator, cannot do:</p>
<p><strong>and took the book out of the right hand of him that sat upon the throne</strong>; <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>; it being given unto him, as in <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="re+1:1">Revelation 1:1</span>; and a commission and authority to open it, and make it manifest to others, and to accomplish the several events, in the several periods of time, it points unto.</p>
