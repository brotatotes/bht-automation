<p><strong>Making request, if by any means now at length</strong>,.... A principal thing, which be incessantly and importunately requested at the throne of grace, was, that he might have an opportunity of coming to them; that God in his providence would open a way for him; and that he might have a safe and comfortable journey in a very little time; all which he submits to the will of God, as a good man ought to do; and which he thus expresses,</p>
<p><strong>I might have a prosperous journey by the will of God, to come unto you</strong>; see <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="jas+4:13">James 4:13</span>.</p>
