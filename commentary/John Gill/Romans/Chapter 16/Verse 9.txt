<p><strong>Salute Urbane, our helper in Christ</strong>,.... This also was a Roman name, and which many of the popes of Rome have since taken to themselves; he is said to be one of the seventy disciples, and to be a bishop in Macedonia; <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>; which is not very probable: others have conjectured him to be one of the pastors of the church of Rome, which is more likely; and if he was, but few of his successors have deserved the character given of him, an "helper in Christ"; in spreading the Gospel, and enlarging the kingdom and interest of Christ:</p>
<p><strong>and Stachys my beloved</strong>; this is a Greek name, he is said to be one of the seventy disciples, and bishop of Byzantium;</p>
<p><span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="+:-"> :-</span>. According to the Roman martyrology, he was ordained bishop of the Byzantine church, by Andrew the apostle, but this is not to be depended on; he was, however, because of his faith in Christ, and love to him, or on such like spiritual accounts, very dear to the apostle.</p>
