<p><strong>Tribulation and anguish</strong>,.... These, with the foregoing words, are expressive of the second death, the torments of hell, the worm that never dies, and the fire that is not quenched: which will fall</p>
<p><strong>upon every soul of man that doth evil</strong>; whose course of life and conversation is evil; for "the soul that sins shall die",</p>
<p><span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="eze+18:4">Ezekiel 18:4</span>, unless satisfaction is made for his sins by the blood of Christ:</p>
<p><strong>of the Jew first, and also of the Gentile</strong>; regard being had to what nation they belong.</p>
