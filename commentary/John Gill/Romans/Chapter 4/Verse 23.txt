<p><strong>Now it was not written for his sake alone</strong>,.... This was not left on the sacred records, <span class="scriptRef" com="geb" lang="eng" ref="ge+15:6">Genesis 15:6</span>,</p>
<p><strong>that it was imputed to him</strong>; purely on his account, merely for his sake, as an encomium of his faith, and an honourable testimony to it, and for the encouragement of it; though this was doing him a very great honour, and was one design of it.</p>
