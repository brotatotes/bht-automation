
	
	
	 1:21 world (d-9)  Kosmos , the usual word for world. see chs. 3.19; 6.2; Hebrews 9:1 . preaching (e-25) '  The preaching' gives a different sense from 'preaching.' Without the article it is the instrument, the foolishness of such a means. Here 'the preaching' is the actual thing preached; and such is the force of the Greek.
