	 1:21 world (d-9) <em> Kosmos</em> , the usual word for world. see chs. 3.19; 6.2; <span class="scriptRef" com="dsn" lang="eng" ref="heb+9:1">Hebrews 9:1</span> . preaching (e-25) ' <em> The</em> preaching' gives a different sense from 'preaching.' Without the article it is the instrument, the foolishness of such a means. Here 'the preaching' is the actual thing preached; and such is the force of the Greek.</p>
