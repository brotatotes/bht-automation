	 10:29 liberty (e-17) 'Are lawful' is the verb, of which <em> exousia</em> , the word translated 'right' or 'liberty' in ch. 8.9, and 'power' in <span class="scriptRef" com="dsn" lang="eng" ref="mt+10:1">Matthew 10:1</span> , is the noun. The word translated 'liberty' in v. 29 is different, and is opposed to 'bondage,' as in <span class="scriptRef" com="dsn" lang="eng" ref="joh+8:32">John 8:32</span> ,<span class="scriptRef" com="dsn" lang="eng" ref="joh+8:36">John 8:36</span> ; <span class="scriptRef" com="dsn" lang="eng" ref="2co+3:17">2 Corinthians 3:17</span> ; <span class="scriptRef" com="dsn" lang="eng" ref="ga+5:13">Galatians 5:13</span> .</p>
