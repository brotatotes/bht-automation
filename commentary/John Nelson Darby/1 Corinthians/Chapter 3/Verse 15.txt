<p>
	
	
	 3:15 fire. (b-24) The addition of the article gives the right sense. 'saved by fire' may mean that the fire was a means of safety, whereas 'through the fire' is in spite of it, or going through the danger and difficulty of it. <em> Dia</em> has two distinct uses as 'through' in English. see <span class="scriptRef" com="dsn" lang="eng" ref="1pe+3:20">1 Peter 3:20</span> for the other sense.</p>
