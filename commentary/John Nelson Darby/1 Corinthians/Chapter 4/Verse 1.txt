
	
	
	 4:1 servants (i-9) The appointed servant. Three words are translated 'servant'.  doulos , a slave, bondman;  diakonos , a person who acts or waits in service; and  huperetes , as here, which is always used in the New Testament for an official servant, or apparitor. see Luke 1:2 ; Acts 26:16 . For  latreuo , serve, see Matthew 4:10 .
