<p>
	
	
	 4:1 servants (i-9) The appointed servant. Three words are translated 'servant'. <em> doulos</em> , a slave, bondman; <em> diakonos</em> , a person who acts or waits in service; and <em> huperetes</em> , as here, which is always used in the New Testament for an official servant, or apparitor. see <span class="scriptRef" com="dsn" lang="eng" ref="lu+1:2">Luke 1:2</span> ; <span class="scriptRef" com="dsn" lang="eng" ref="ac+26:16">Acts 26:16</span> . For <em> latreuo</em> , serve, see <span class="scriptRef" com="dsn" lang="eng" ref="mt+4:10">Matthew 4:10</span> .</p>
