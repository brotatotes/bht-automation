	 7:28 have (b-17) Or 'she has.' I say 'they' to embrace both sexes, which the 'such' ( <em> toioutoi</em> , plural), and what follows, distinctly implies. The Greek for 'virgin' is feminine from its primary natural reference, but see <span class="scriptRef" com="dsn" lang="eng" ref="re+14:4">Revelation 14:4</span> .</p>
