<p>
	
	
	 1:13 (a-1) Or 'hold fast.' Timothy had heard no <em> form</em> from Paul, but <em> words</em> or <em> doctrines</em> . Hence, he was to have a summary or outline, so as to state clearly and definitely what he did hold. I have added '[words],' because in English 'which' might be thought to refer to outline. The Greek means a systematic <em> expose</em> , in outline, of any system of doctrine or philosophy, as <span class="scriptRef" com="dsn" lang="eng" ref="1ti+1:16">1 Timothy 1:16</span> , 'delineation.'</p>
