	 1:23 the (g-6) Or 'in faith,' that is, full assurance of heart in the gospel. creation (h-33) The word is used either of 'the creation,' or abstractly of 'the creature' as such; once for 'institution,' <span class="scriptRef" com="dsn" lang="eng" ref="1pe+2:13">1 Peter 2:13</span> . Thus, in ver. 15, it is not of each individual as such, but of everything called 'creation' in its nature. see <span class="scriptRef" com="dsn" lang="eng" ref="heb+9:11">Hebrews 9:11</span> .</p>
