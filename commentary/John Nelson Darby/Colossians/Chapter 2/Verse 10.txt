<p>
	
	
	 2:10 complete (d-4) <em> Pleroo</em> , as ch. 1.9. Or 'filled full,' referring to all fulness being in him. The fulness or completeness of the Godhead is in Christ, as towards us; and we, as towards God, are complete in him. 'Godhead' here is <em> Theotes</em> -- Godhead in the absolute sense -- not <em> Theiotes</em> , merely divine in character.</p>
