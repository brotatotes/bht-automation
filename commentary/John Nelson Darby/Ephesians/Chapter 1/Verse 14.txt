<p>
	
	
	 1:14 to (g-8) Or 'up to,' <em> eis</em> . It is more than 'till,' because it has the inheritance in view. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="dsn" lang="eng" ref="ga+3:24">Galatians 3:24</span> . It may be translated 'for the redemption,' connecting it with 'sealed,' instead of with 'earnest;' thus, 'Ye have been sealed with the Holy Spirit of promise, (who is the earnest of our inheritance,) for,' &amp;c.</p>
