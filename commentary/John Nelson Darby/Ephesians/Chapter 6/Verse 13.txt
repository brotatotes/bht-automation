	 6:13 take (g-4) 'Take,' 'taken,' vers, 13 and 16, are aorists. The sense is 'taking up,' as arms or clothes to put on. taking a person with you, <span class="scriptRef" com="dsn" lang="eng" ref="2ti+4:11">2 Timothy 4:11</span> . things, (h-26) Or 'having overcome all things.' It is to 'carry through and put in execution all that is purposed and called for, in spite of opposition.'</p>
