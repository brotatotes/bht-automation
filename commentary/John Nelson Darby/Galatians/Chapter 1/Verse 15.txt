
	
	
	 1:15 God, (g-3) Or 'the God who,' c. [even] (h-8) I have inserted 'even' here that it may be quite clear that 'from my mother's womb' is a point of time. There can be no doubt of it. see Matthew 19:12 , Luke 1:15 Acts 3:2 ; Acts 14:8 . The Greek has the force of 'taking out,' as distinguished or appropriated to something, though doubtless meaning 'to separate.' I have therefore said 'set apart.'
