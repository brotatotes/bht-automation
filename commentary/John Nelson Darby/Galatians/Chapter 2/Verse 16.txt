	 2:16 [nor] (a-16) The Greek has the sense of 'but,' with an exclusive force, which I have endeavoured to render by 'nor' in brackets. see <span class="scriptRef" com="dsn" lang="eng" ref="ro+7:7">Romans 7:7</span> ; <span class="scriptRef" com="dsn" lang="eng" ref="ro+14:14">Romans 14:14</span> ; <span class="scriptRef" com="dsn" lang="eng" ref="1co+7:17">1 Corinthians 7:17</span> ; <span class="scriptRef" com="dsn" lang="eng" ref="ga+1:7">Galatians 1:7</span> . We may translate perhaps, 'but only.'</p>
