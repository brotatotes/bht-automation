	 5:25 by (h-4) by (h-11) The expression 'by the Spirit' occurs in vers. 18 and 25; but in ver. 16 'walk' refers to the general manner of life, as <span class="scriptRef" com="dsn" lang="eng" ref="ro+8:4">Romans 8:4</span> , so I have left 'in.' In vers. 18 and 25 'walk' refers to the rule or line followed, as ch. 6.16; <span class="scriptRef" com="dsn" lang="eng" ref="ro+4:12">Romans 4:12</span> ; <span class="scriptRef" com="dsn" lang="eng" ref="php+3:16">Philippians 3:16</span> . It is characteristic of the walk, leading, and life, the Spirit being the instrument and power.</p>
