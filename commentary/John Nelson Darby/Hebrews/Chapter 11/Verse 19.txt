	 11:19 received (h-17) The force of the Greek, thus applied, is to get back what one had, or what belonged to one, when it might have seemed lost for ever, as <span class="scriptRef" com="dsn" lang="eng" ref="mt+25:27">Matthew 25:27</span> . The sense I think quite certain in its application to Isaac's sacrifice. The aorist is constantly used in this chapter historically.</p>
