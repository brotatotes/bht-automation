	 12:24 new (c-7) <em> Neos</em> ; not the usual word for new covenant, which is <em> kainos</em> . <em> Neos</em> is 'new' in the sense of fresh, youthful. <em> Kainos</em> is new as wholly different. 'The new ( <em> kainos</em> ) man,' <span class="scriptRef" com="dsn" lang="eng" ref="eph+4:24">Ephesians 4:24</span> , i.e. it is not the old; <em> neos</em> , <span class="scriptRef" com="dsn" lang="eng" ref="col+3:10">Colossians 3:10</span> , it is not grown old.</p>
