	 4:6 word, (b-29) <em> Apeitheo</em> . see Note e, <span class="scriptRef" com="dsn" lang="eng" ref="joh+3:36">John 3:36</span> . 'Who had not hearkened to the word' is, I apprehend, the true force here. It is taken from the LXX, <span class="scriptRef" com="dsn" lang="eng" ref="nu+14:43">Numbers 14:43</span> and <span class="scriptRef" com="dsn" lang="eng" ref="de+1:26">Deuteronomy 1:26</span> , which refer to the occasion on which God declared their carcases should fall in the wilderness.</p>
