	 7:14 sprung (d-9) Or 'arisen.' It is a question whether the word alludes to arising, as the sun, or springing up, as a plant. 'the branch.' For 'the branch' was translated 'dayspring' by the LXX, and the verb is used for both in Greek. see <span class="scriptRef" com="dsn" lang="eng" ref="jer+23:5">Jeremiah 23:5</span> ; <span class="scriptRef" com="dsn" lang="eng" ref="zec+3:8">Zechariah 3:8</span> .</p>
