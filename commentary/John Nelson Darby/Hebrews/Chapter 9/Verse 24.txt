	 9:24 figures (g-13) <em> Antitupos</em> , meaning the impression made by a die or stamp ( <em> tupos</em> ). The heavenly things were the original, 'the pattern,' copied (ch. 8.5), and so it was, as said to Moses. Hence they were the <em> tupos</em> ; the tabernacle <em> antitupos</em> , here rendered 'figure,' what answered to it. before (h-24) Lit. 'to the face.'</p>
