
	
	
	 1:4 beforehand (e-15) The article is used here with the perfect participle. The word signifies to appoint by notice beforehand, to fix by proclamation. They are  the ones who of old were so noticed and marked out. sentence, (f-18)  Krima . not the act of condemnation, but the subject-matter on which they are condemned. see Note, 1 Corinthians 11:29 . Christ. (g-37) Or 'denying the only Master and our Lord Jesus Christ.' 'Master' is  despotes . see Acts 4:24 ; 2 Peter 2:1 .
