	 1:4 beforehand (e-15) The article is used here with the perfect participle. The word signifies to appoint by notice beforehand, to fix by proclamation. Christ. (g-37) Or 'denying the only Master and our Lord Jesus Christ.' 'Master' is <em> despotes</em> . see <span class="scriptRef" com="dsn" lang="eng" ref="ac+4:24">Acts 4:24</span> ; <span class="scriptRef" com="dsn" lang="eng" ref="2pe+2:1">2 Peter 2:1</span> .</p>
