<p>
	
	
	 1:24 Eh! (e-2) The imperative of the verb 'to let alone,' but used as an interjection, as a cry of dissatisfaction. Nazarene? (f-11) The force of 'Nazarene' here is simply, I apprehend, 'of Nazareth.' The word is different from that translated 'Nazaraean' in <span class="scriptRef" com="dsn" lang="eng" ref="mt+2:23">Matthew 2:23</span> and elsewhere.</p>
