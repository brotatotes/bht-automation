	 4:20 trawl-nets, (b-6) Lit. 'the trawl-nets.' The word is supposed to derive from the verb 'to cast.' so <span class="scriptRef" com="dsn" lang="eng" ref="mr+1:18">Mark 1:18</span> ; <span class="scriptRef" com="dsn" lang="eng" ref="lu+5:2-6">Luke 5:2-6</span> ; <span class="scriptRef" com="dsn" lang="eng" ref="joh+21:6-11">John 21:6-11</span> ; 'seine,' in <span class="scriptRef" com="dsn" lang="eng" ref="mt+13:47">Matthew 13:47</span> , is a net drawn round from the shore.</p>
