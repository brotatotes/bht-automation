	 1:12 lamps, (c-20) Or 'lampstands' ( <em> luchnia</em> ) but simply here, I think, 'lamps' as compared with <span class="scriptRef" com="dsn" lang="eng" ref="heb+9:2">Hebrews 9:2</span> , where it is clearly the 'candlestick' or 'lampstand.' If <em> luchnos</em> and <em> luchnia</em> are distinguished, <em> luchnia</em> is the stand. The words are so distinguished in <span class="scriptRef" com="dsn" lang="eng" ref="mt+5:15">Matthew 5:15</span> , <span class="scriptRef" com="dsn" lang="eng" ref="lu+8:16">Luke 8:16</span> ; <span class="scriptRef" com="dsn" lang="eng" ref="lu+11:33">Luke 11:33</span> .</p>
