	 12:11 spirit (b-8) Or 'by the Spirit.' See Note at ch. 8.9. serving (c-10) The word means 'to serve as a bondman' <em> douleuo</em> . It is different from the words used for service in ver. 1 (which is from <em> latreuo</em> ), and for service in ver. 7 (which is from <em> diakoneo</em> ). See <span class="scriptRef" com="dsn" lang="eng" ref="1co+4:1">1 Corinthians 4:1</span> .</p>
