	 5:9 of] (a-11) Or 'by his blood,' 'by his life.' when <em> en</em> is used morally it has the force of 'in the power of,' 'in the intrinsic character of;' thus 'in flesh,' 'in spirit.' The article and pronoun here give it a somewhat more instrumental character. 'Through' in ver. 10 is <em> dia</em> .</p>
