<p><em>It hath been declared to me by them of the family of Chloe</em> — Whom some suppose to have been the wife of Stephanas, and the mother of Fortunatus and Achaicus. By these three the Corinthians had sent their letter to St. Paul, <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="1co+16:17">1 Corinthians 16:17</span>.</p>
<p><em>That there are contentions</em> — A word equivalent with schisms in the preceding verse.</p>
