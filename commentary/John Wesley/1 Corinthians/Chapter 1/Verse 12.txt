<p><em>Now this I say</em> — That is, what I mean is this: there are various parties among you, who set themselves, one against an other, in behalf of the several teachers they admire.</p>
<p><em>And I of Christ</em> — They spoke well, if they had not on this pretence despised their teachers, <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="1co+4:8">1 Corinthians 4:8</span> Perhaps they valued themselves on having heard Christ preach in his own person.</p>
