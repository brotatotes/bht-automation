<p><em>Of him</em> — Out of his free grace and mercy. Are ye Engrafted into Christ Jesus, who is made unto us that believe wisdom, who were before utterly foolish and ignorant.</p>
<p><em>Righteousness</em> — The sole ground of our justification, who were before under the wrath and curse of God.</p>
<p><em>Sanctification</em> — A principle of universal holiness, whereas before we were altogether dead in sin.</p>
<p><em>And redemption</em> — That is, complete deliverance from all evil, and eternal bliss both of soul and body.</p>
