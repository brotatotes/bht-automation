<p><em>The cup which we bless</em> — By setting it apart to a sacred use, and solemnly invoking the blessing of God upon it.</p>
<p><em>Is it not the communion of the blood of Christ</em> — The means of our partaking of those invaluable benefits, which are the purchase of the blood of Christ. The communion of the body of Christ - The means of our partaking of those benefits which were purchased by the body of Christ - offered for us.</p>
