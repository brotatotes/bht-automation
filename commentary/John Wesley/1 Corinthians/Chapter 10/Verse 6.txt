<p><em>Now these things were our examples</em> — Showing what we are to expect if, enjoying the like benefits, we commit the like sins. The benefits are set down in the same order as by Moses in Exodus; the sins and punishments in a different order; evil desire first, as being the foundation of all; next, idolatry, <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="1co+10:7,14">1 Corinthians 10:7,14</span>; then fornication, which usually accompanied it, <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="1co+10:8">1 Corinthians 10:8</span>; the tempting and murmuring against God, in the following verses.</p>
<p><em>As they desired</em> — Flesh, in contempt of manna. <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="nu+11:4">Numbers 11:4</span></p>
