<p><em>First apostles</em> — Who plant the gospel in the heathen nations.</p>
<p><em>Secondly prophets</em> — Who either foretel things to come, or speak by extra-ordinary inspiration, for the edification of the church.</p>
<p><em>Thirdly teachers</em> — Who precede even those that work miracles. Under prophets and teachers are comprised evangelists and pastors, <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="eph+4:11">Ephesians 4:11</span>.</p>
<p><em>Helps, governments</em> — It does not appear that these mean distinct offices: rather, any persons might be called helps, from a peculiar dexterity in helping the distressed; and governments, from a peculiar talent for governing or presiding in assemblies.</p>
