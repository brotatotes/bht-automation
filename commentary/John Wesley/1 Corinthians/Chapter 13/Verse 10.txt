<p><em>But when that which is perfect is come</em> — At death and in the last day.</p>
<p><em>That which is in part shall vanish away</em> — Both that poor, low, imperfect, glimmering light, which is all the knowledge we now can attain to; and these slow and unsatisfactory methods of attaining, as well as of imparting it to others.</p>
