<p><em>Follow after love</em> — With zeal, vigour, courage, patience; else you can neither attain nor keep it.</p>
<p><em>And</em> — In their place, as subservient to this.</p>
<p><em>Desire spiritual gifts; but especially that ye may prophesy</em> — The word here does not mean foretelling things to come; but rather opening and applying the scripture.</p>
