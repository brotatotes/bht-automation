<p><em>Be not children in understanding</em> — This is an admirable stroke of true oratory! to bring down the height of their spirits, by representing that wherein they prided themselves most, as mere folly and childishness.</p>
<p><em>In wickedness be ye infants</em> — Have all the innocence of that tender age.</p>
<p><em>But in understanding be ye grown men</em> — Knowing religion was not designed to destroy any of our natural faculties, but to exalt and improve them, our reason in particular.</p>
