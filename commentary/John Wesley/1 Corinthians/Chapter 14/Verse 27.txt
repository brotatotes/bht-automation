<p><em>By two or three at most</em> — Let not above two or three speak at one meeting.</p>
<p><em>And that by course</em> — That is, one after another.</p>
<p><em>And let one interpret</em> — Either himself, <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="1co+14:13">1 Corinthians 14:13</span>; or, if he have not the gift, some other, into the vulgar tongue. It seems, the gift of tongues was an instantaneous knowledge of a tongue till then unknown, which he that received it could afterwards speak when he thought fit, without any new miracle.</p>
