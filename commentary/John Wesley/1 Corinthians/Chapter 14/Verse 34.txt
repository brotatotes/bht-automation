<p><em>Let your women be silent in the churches</em> — Unless they are under an extraordinary impulse of the Spirit. For, in other cases, it is not permitted them to speak - By way of teaching in public assemblies.</p>
<p><em>But to be in subjection</em> — To the man whose proper office it is to lead and to instruct the congregation. <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="ge+3:16">Genesis 3:16</span>.</p>
