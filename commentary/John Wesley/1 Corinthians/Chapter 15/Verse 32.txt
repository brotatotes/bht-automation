<p>If to speak after the manner of men - That is, to use a proverbial phrase, expressive of the most imminent danger I have fought with wild beasts at Ephesus - With the savage fury of a lawless multitude, <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="ac+19:29">Acts 19:29</span>, etc. This seems to have been but just before.</p>
<p><em>Let as eat, …</em> — We might, on that supposition, as well say, with the Epicureans, Let us make the best of this short life, seeing we have no other portion.</p>
