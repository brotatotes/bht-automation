<p><em>It is sown in dishonour</em> — Shocking to those who loved it best, human nature in disgrace! It is raised in glory - Clothed with robes of light, fit for those whom the King of heaven delights to honour.</p>
<p><em>It is sown in weakness</em> — Deprived even of that feeble strength which it once enjoyed.</p>
<p><em>It is raised in power</em> — Endued with vigour, strength, and activity, such as we cannot now conceive.</p>
