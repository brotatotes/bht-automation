<p><em>And I, brethren</em> — He spoke before, <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="1co+2:1">1 Corinthians 2:1</span>, of his entrance, now of his progress, among them.</p>
<p><em>Could not speak to you as unto spiritual</em> — Adult, experienced Christians. But as unto men who were still in great measure carnal, as unto babes in Christ - Still weak in grace, though eminent in gifts, <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="1co+1:5">1 Corinthians 1:5</span>.</p>
