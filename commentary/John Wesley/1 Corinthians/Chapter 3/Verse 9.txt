<p><em>For we are all fellowlabourers</em> — God's labourers, and fellowlabourers with each other.</p>
<p><em>Ye are God's husbandry</em> — This is the sum of what went before: it is a comprehensive word, taking in both a field, a garden, and a vineyard.</p>
<p><em>Ye are God's building</em> — This is the sum of what follows.</p>
