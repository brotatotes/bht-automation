<p>But I did not mean that you should altogether refrain from conversing with heathens, though they are guilty in some of these respects.</p>
<p><em>Covetous, rapacious, idolaters</em> — Sinners against themselves, their neighbour, God.</p>
<p><em>For then ye must go out of the world</em> — Then all civil commerce must cease. So that going out of the world, which some account a perfection, St. Paul accounts an utter absurdity.</p>
