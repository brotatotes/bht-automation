<p><em>Therefore let us keep the feast</em> — Let us feed on him by faith. Here is a plain allusion to the Lord's supper, which was instituted in the room of the passover.</p>
<p><em>Not with the old leaven</em> — Of heathenism or Judaism. Malignity is stubbornness in evil. Sincerity and truth seem to be put here for the whole of true, inward religion.</p>
