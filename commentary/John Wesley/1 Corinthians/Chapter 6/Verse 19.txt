<p>And even your body is not, strictly speaking, your own even this is the temple of the Holy Ghost - Dedicated to him, and inhabited by him. What the apostle calls elsewhere "the temple of God," <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="1co+3:16,17">1 Corinthians 3:16,17</span>, and "the temple of the living God," <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="2co+6:16">2 Corinthians 6:16</span>, he here styles the temple of the Holy Ghost; plainly showing that the Holy Ghost is the living God.</p>
