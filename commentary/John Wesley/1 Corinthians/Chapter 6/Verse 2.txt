<p><em>Know ye not</em> — This expression occurs six times in this single chapter, and that with a peculiar force; for the Corinthians knew and gloried in it, but they did not practise.</p>
<p><em>That the saints</em> — After having been judged themselves.</p>
<p><em>Shall judge the world</em> — Shall be assessors with Christ in the judgment wherein he shall condemn all the wicked, as well angels as men, <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="mt+19:28">Matthew 19:28</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="re+20:4">Revelation 20:4</span>.</p>
