<p><em>To the rest</em> — Who are married to unbelievers.</p>
<p><em>Speak I</em> — By revelation from God, though our Lord hath not left any commandment concerning it.</p>
<p><em>Let him not put her away</em> — The Jews, indeed, were obliged of old to put away their idolatrous wives, <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="ezr+10:3">Ezra 10:3</span>; but their case was quite different. They were absolutely forbid to marry idolatrous women; but the persons here spoken of were married while they were both in a state of heathenism.</p>
