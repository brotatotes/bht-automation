<p><em>There is a difference also between a wife and a virgin</em> — Whether the church be under persecution or not.</p>
<p><em>The unmarried woman</em> — If she know and use her privilege.</p>
<p><em>Careth only for the things of the Lord</em> — All her time, care, and thoughts centre in this, how she may be holy both in body and spirit. This is the standing advantage of a single life, in all ages and nations. But who makes a suitable use of it?</p>
