<p><em>Not that I may cast a snare upon you</em> — Who are not able to receive this saying.</p>
<p><em>But for your profit</em> — Who are able. That ye may resolutely and perseveringly wait upon the Lord - The word translated wait signifies sitting close by a person, in a good posture to hear. So Mary sat at the feet of Jesus, <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="lu+10:39">Luke 10:39</span>.</p>
<p><em>Without distraction</em> — Without having the mind drawn any way from its centre; from its close attention to God; by any person, or thing, or care, or incumbrance whatsoever.</p>
