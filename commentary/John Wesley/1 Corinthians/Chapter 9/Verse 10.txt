<p><em>He who ploweth ought to plow in hope</em> — Of reaping. This seems to be a proverbial expression.</p>
<p><em>And he that thresheth in hope</em> — Ought not to be disappointed, ought to eat the fruit of his labours. And ought they who labour in God's husbandry. <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="de+25:4">Deuteronomy 25:4</span></p>
