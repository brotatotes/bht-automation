<p><em>That your joy may be full</em> — So our Lord also, <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="joh+15:11">John 15:11</span>; 16:22. There is a joy of hope, a joy of faith, and a joy of love. Here the joy of faith is directly intended. It is a concise expression.</p>
<p><em>Your joy</em> — That is, your faith and the joy arising from it: but it likewise implies the joy of hope and love.</p>
