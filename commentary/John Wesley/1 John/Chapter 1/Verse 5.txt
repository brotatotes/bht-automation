<p>And this is the sum of the message which we have heard of him - The Son of God.</p>
<p><em>That God is light</em> — The light of wisdom, love, holiness, glory. What light is to the natural eye, that God is to the spiritual eye.</p>
<p><em>And in him is no darkness at all</em> — No contrary principle. He is pure, unmixed light.</p>
