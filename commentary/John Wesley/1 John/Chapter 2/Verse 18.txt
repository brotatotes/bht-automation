<p><em>My little children, it is the last time</em> — The last dispensation of grace, that which is to continue to the end of time, is begun.</p>
<p><em>Ye have heard that antichrist cometh</em> — Under the term antichrist, or the spirit of antichrist, he includes all false teachers and enemies to the truth; yea, whatever doctrines or men are contrary to Christ. It seems to have been long after this that the name of antichrist was appropriated to that grand adversary of Christ, "the man of sin," <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="2th+2:3">2 Thessalonians 2:3</span> Antichrist, in St. John's sense, that is, antichristianism, has been spreading from his time till now; and will do so, till that great adversary arises, and is destroyed by Christ's coming.</p>
