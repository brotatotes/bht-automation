<p><em>I have written</em> — Namely, <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="1jo+2:13">1 John 2:13</span>.</p>
<p><em>To you because ye know the truth</em> — That is, to confirm you in the knowledge ye have already.</p>
<p><em>Ye know that no lie is of the truth</em> — That all the doctrines of these antichrists are irreconcilable to it.</p>
