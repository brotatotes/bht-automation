<p><em>Who is that liar</em> — Who is guilty of that lying, but he who denies that truth which is the sum of all Christianity? That Jesus is the Christ; that he is the Son of God; that he came in the flesh, is one undivided truth. and he that denies any part of this, in effect denies the whole.</p>
<p><em>He is antichrist</em> — And the spirit of antichrist, who in denying the Son denies the Father also.</p>
