<p><em>These things</em> — From1John2:21.</p>
<p><em>I have written to you</em> — St. John, according to his custom, begins and ends with the same form, and having finished a kind of parenthesis, <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="1jo+2:20-26">1 John 2:20-26</span>, continues, 2:27, what he said in the twentieth verse, concerning them that would seduce you.</p>
