<p><em>And now, beloved children</em> — Having finished his address to each, he now returns to all in general.</p>
<p><em>Abide in him, that we</em> — A modest expression.</p>
<p><em>May not be ashamed before him at his coming</em> — O how will ye, Jews, Socinians, nominal Christians, be ashamed in that day!</p>
