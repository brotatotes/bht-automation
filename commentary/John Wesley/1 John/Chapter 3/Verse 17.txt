<p><em>But whoso hath this world's good</em> — Worldly substance, far less valuable than life.</p>
<p><em>And seeth his brother have need</em> — The very sight of want knocks at the door of the spectator's heart.</p>
<p><em>And shutteth up</em> — Whether asked or not.</p>
<p><em>His bowels of compassion from him, how dwelleth the love of God in him</em> — Certainly not at all, however he may talk, <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="1jo+3:18">1 John 3:18</span>, of loving God.</p>
