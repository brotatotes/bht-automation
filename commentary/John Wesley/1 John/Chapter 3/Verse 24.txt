<p><em>And he that keepeth his commandments</em> — That thus believes and loves.</p>
<p><em>Abideth in him, and God in him: and hereby we know that he abideth in us, by the Spirit which he hath given us</em> — Which witnesses with our spirits that we are his children, and brings forth his fruits of peace, love, holiness. This is the transition to the treating of the Holy Spirit which immediately follows.</p>
