<p>This extends to things of the greatest importance.</p>
<p><em>If any one see his brother</em> — That is. any man.</p>
<p><em>Sin a sin which is not unto death</em> — That is, any sin but total apostasy from both the power and form of godliness.</p>
<p><em>Let him ask, and God will give him life</em> — Pardon and spiritual life, for that sinner.</p>
<p><em>There is a sin unto death: I do not say that he shall pray for that</em> — That is, let him not pray for it. A sin unto death may likewise mean, one which God has determined to punish with death.</p>
