<p><em>For whatsoever</em> — This expression implies the most unlimited universality.</p>
<p><em>Is born of God overcometh the world</em> — Conquers whatever it can lay in the way, either to allure or fright the children of God from keeping his commandments.</p>
<p><em>And this is the victory</em> — The grand means of overcoming.</p>
<p><em>Even our faith</em> — Seeing all things are possible to him that believeth.</p>
