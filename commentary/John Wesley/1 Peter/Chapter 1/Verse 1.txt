<p><em>To the sojourners</em> — Upon earth, the Christians, chiefly those of Jewish extraction.</p>
<p><em>Scattered</em> — Long ago driven out of their own land. Those scattered by the persecution mentioned <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="ac+8:1">Acts 8:1</span>, were scattered only through Judea and Samaria, though afterwards some of them travelled to Phenice, Cyprus, and Antioch.</p>
<p><em>Through Pontus, Galatia, Cappadocia, Asia, and Bithynia</em> — He names these five provinces in the order wherein they occurred to him, writing from the east. All these countries lie in the Lesser Asia. The Asia here distinguished from the other provinces is that which was usually called the Proconsular Asia being a Roman province.</p>
