<p><em>Of which salvation</em> — So far beyond all that was experienced under the Jewish dispensation. The very prophets who prophesied long ago of the grace of God toward you - Of his abundant, overflowing grace to be bestowed on believers under the Christian dispensation.</p>
<p><em>Inquired</em> — Were earnestly inquisitive.</p>
<p><em>And searched diligently</em> — Like miners searching after precious ore, after the meaning of the prophecies which they delivered.</p>
