<p><em>Searching what time</em> — What particular period.</p>
<p><em>And what manner of time</em> — By what marks to be distinguished.</p>
<p><em>The glories that were to follow</em> — His sufferings; namely, the glory of his resurrection, ascension, exaltation, and the effusion of his Spirit; the glory of the last judgment, and of his eternal kingdom; and also the glories of his grace in the hearts and lives of Christians.</p>
