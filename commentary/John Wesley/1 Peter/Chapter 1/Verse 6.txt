<p><em>Wherein</em> — That is, in being so kept. Ye even now greatly rejoice, though now for a little while - Such is our whole life, compared to eternity.</p>
<p><em>If need be</em> — For it is not always needful. If God sees it to be the best means for your spiritual profit.</p>
<p><em>Ye are in heaviness</em> — Or sorrow; but not in darkness; for they still retained both faith, <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="1pe+1:5">1 Peter 1:5</span>, hope, and love; yea, at this very time were rejoicing with joy unspeakable, <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="1pe+1:8">1 Peter 1:8</span>.</p>
