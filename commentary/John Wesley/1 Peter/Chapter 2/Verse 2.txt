<p><em>Desire</em> — Always, as earnestly as new born babes do, <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="1pe+1:3">1 Peter 1:3</span>.</p>
<p><em>The milk of the word</em> — That word of God which nourishes the soul as milk does the body, and which is sincere, pure from all guile, so that none are deceived who cleave to it.</p>
<p><em>That you may grow thereby</em> — In faith, love, holiness, unto the full stature of Christ.</p>
