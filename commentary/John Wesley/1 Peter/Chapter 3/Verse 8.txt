<p><em>Finally</em> — This part of the epistle reaches to1Peter4:11. The apostle seems to have added the rest afterwards.</p>
<p><em>Sympathizing</em> — Rejoicing and sorrowing together. Love all believers as brethren. Be pitiful - Toward the afflicted.</p>
<p><em>Be courteous</em> — To all men. Courtesy is such a behaviour toward equals and inferiors as shows respect mixed with love.</p>
