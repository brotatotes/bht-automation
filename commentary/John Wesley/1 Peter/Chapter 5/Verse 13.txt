<p><em>The church that is at Babylon</em> — Near which St. Peter probably was, when he wrote this epistle.</p>
<p><em>Elected together with you</em> — Partaking of the same faith with you.</p>
<p><em>Mark</em> — It seems the evangelist.</p>
<p><em>My son</em> — Probably converted by St. Peter. And he had occasionally served him, "as a son in the gospel."</p>
