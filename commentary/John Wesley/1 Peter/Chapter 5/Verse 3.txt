<p><em>Neither as lording over the heritage</em> — Behaving in a haughty, domineering manner, as though you had dominion over their conscience. The word translated heritage, is, literally, the portions. There is one flock under the one chief Shepherd; but many portions of this, under many pastors.</p>
<p><em>But being examples to the flock</em> — This procures the most ready and free obedience.</p>
