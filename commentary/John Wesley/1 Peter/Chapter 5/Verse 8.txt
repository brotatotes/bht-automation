<p>But in the mean time watch. There is a close connexion between this, and the duly casting our care upon him. How deeply had St. Peter himself suffered for want of watching! Be vigilant - As if he had said, Awake, and keep awake. Sleep no more: be this your care.</p>
<p><em>As a roaring lion</em> — Full of rage.</p>
<p><em>Seeking</em> — With all subtilty likewise.</p>
<p><em>Whom he may devour or swallow up</em> — Both soul and body.</p>
