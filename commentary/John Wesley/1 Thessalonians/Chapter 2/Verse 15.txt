<p><em>Us</em> — Apostles and preachers of the gospel.</p>
<p><em>They please not God</em> — Nor are they even careful to please him, notwithstanding their fair professions.</p>
<p><em>And are contrary to all men</em> — Are common enemies of mankind; not only by their continual seditions and insurrections, and by their utter contempt of all other nations; but in particular, by their endeavouring to hinder their hearing or receiving the gospel.</p>
