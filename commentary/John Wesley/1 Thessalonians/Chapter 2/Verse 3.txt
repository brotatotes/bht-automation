<p><em>For our exhortation</em> — That is, our preaching. A part is put for the whole.</p>
<p><em>Is not, at any time, of deceit</em> — We preach not a lie, but the truth of God.</p>
<p><em>Nor of uncleanness</em> — With any unholy or selfish view. This expression is not always appropriated to lust, although it is sometimes emphatically applied thereto.</p>
<p><em>Nor in guile</em> — But with great plainness of speech.</p>
