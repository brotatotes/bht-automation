<p><em>Flattering words</em> — This ye know.</p>
<p><em>Nor a cloak of covetousness</em> — Of this God is witness. He calls men to witness an open fact; God, the secret intentions of the heart. In a point of a mixed nature, <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="1th+2:10">1 Thessalonians 2:10</span>, he appeals both to God and man.</p>
