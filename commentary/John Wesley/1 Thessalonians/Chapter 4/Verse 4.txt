<p><em>That every one know</em> — For this requires knowledge, as well as chastity.</p>
<p><em>To possess his vessel</em> — His wife.</p>
<p><em>In sanctification and honour</em> — So as neither to dishonour God or himself, nor to obstruct, but further, holiness; remembering, marriage is not designed to inflame, but to conquer, natural desires.</p>
