<p><em>Esteem them very highly</em> — Literally, more than abundantly, in love - The inexpressible sympathy that is between true pastors and their flock is intimated, not only here, but also in divers other places of this epistle. See1Thessalonians2:7,8.</p>
<p><em>For their work's sake</em> — The principal ground of their vast regard for them. But how are we to esteem them who do not work at all?</p>
