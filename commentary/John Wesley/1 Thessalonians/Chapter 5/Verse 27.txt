<p><em>I charge you by the Lord</em> — Christ, to whom proper divine worship is here paid.</p>
<p><em>That this epistle</em> — The first he wrote.</p>
<p><em>Be read to all the brethren</em> — That is, in all the churches. They might have concealed it out of modesty, had not this been so solemnly enjoined: but what Paul commands under so strong an adjuration, Rome forbids under pain of excommunication.</p>
