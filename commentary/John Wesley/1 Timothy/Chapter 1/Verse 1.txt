<p>Paul an apostle-Familiarity is to be set aside where the things of God are concerned.</p>
<p><em>According to the commandment of God</em> — The authoritative appointment of God the Father.</p>
<p><em>Our Saviour</em> — So styled in many other places likewise, as being the grand orderer of the whole scheme of our salvation.</p>
<p><em>And Christ our hope</em> — That is, the author, object, and ground, of all our hope.</p>
