<p><em>This is a faithful saying</em> — A most solemn preface.</p>
<p><em>And worthy of all acceptation</em> — Well deserving to be accepted, received, embraced, with all the faculties of our whole soul.</p>
<p><em>That Christ</em> — Promised.</p>
<p><em>Jesus</em> — Exhibited.</p>
<p><em>Came into the world to save sinners</em> — All sinners, without exception.</p>
