<p><em>This charge I commit to thee</em> — That thou mayest deliver it to the church.</p>
<p><em>According to the prophecies concerning thee</em> — Uttered when thou wast received as an evangelist, <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="1ti+4:14">1 Timothy 4:14</span>; probably by many persons, <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="1ti+6:12">1 Timothy 6:12</span>; that, being encouraged by them, thou mightest war the good warfare.</p>
