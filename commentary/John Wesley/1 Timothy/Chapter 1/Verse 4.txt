<p><em>Neither give heed</em> — So as either to teach or regard them.</p>
<p><em>To fables</em> — Fabulous Jewish traditions.</p>
<p><em>And endless genealogies</em> — Nor those delivered in scripture, but the long intricate pedigrees whereby they strove to prove their descent from such or such a person.</p>
<p><em>Which afford questions</em> — Which lead only to useless and endless controversies.</p>
