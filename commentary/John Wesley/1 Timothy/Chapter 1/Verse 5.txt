<p><em>Whereas the end of the commandment</em> — of the whole Christian institution.</p>
<p><em>Is love</em> — And this was particularly the end of the commandment which Timotheus was to enforce at Ephesus, <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="1ti+1:3,18">1 Timothy 1:3,18</span>. The foundation is faith; the end, love. But this can only subsist in an heart purified by faith, and is always attended with a good conscience.</p>
