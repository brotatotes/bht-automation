<p><em>And Adam was not deceived</em> — The serpent deceived Eve: Eve did not deceive Adam, but persuaded him. "Thou hast hearkened unto the voice of thy wife," <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="ge+3:17">Genesis 3:17</span>. The preceding verse showed why a woman should not "usurp authority over the man." this shows why she ought not "to teach." She is more easily deceived, and more easily deceives.</p>
<p><em>The woman being deceived transgressed</em> — "The serpent deceived" her, <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="ge+3:13">Genesis 3:13</span>, and she transgressed.</p>
