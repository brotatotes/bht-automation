<p><em>Yet she</em> — That is, women in general, who were all involved with Eve in the sentence pronounced, <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="ge+3:16">Genesis 3:16</span>.</p>
<p><em>Shall be saved in childbearing</em> — Carried safe through the pain and danger which that sentence entails upon them for the transgression; yea, and finally saved, if they continue in loving faith and holy wisdom.</p>
