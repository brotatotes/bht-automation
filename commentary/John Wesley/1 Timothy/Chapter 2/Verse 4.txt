<p>Who willeth seriously all men - Not a part only, much less the smallest part.</p>
<p><em>To be saved</em> — Eternally. This is treated of, <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="1ti+2:5,6">1 Timothy 2:5,6</span>. And, in order thereto, to come - They are not compelled.</p>
<p><em>To the knowledge of the truth</em> — Which brings salvation. This is treated of, <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="1ti+2:6,7">1 Timothy 2:6,7</span>.</p>
