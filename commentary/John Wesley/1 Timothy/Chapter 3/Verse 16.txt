<p><em>The mystery of godliness</em> — Afterwards specified in six articles, which sum up the whole economy of Christ upon earth.</p>
<p><em>Is the pillar and ground</em> — The foundation and support of all the truth taught in his church.</p>
<p><em>God was manifest in the flesh</em> — In the form of a servant, the fashion of a man, for three and thirty years.</p>
<p><em>Justified by the Spirit</em> — Publicly "declared to be the Son of God," by his resurrection from the dead.</p>
<p><em>Seen</em> — Chiefly after his resurrection.</p>
<p><em>By angels</em> — Both good and bad.</p>
<p><em>Preached among the gentiles</em> — This elegantly follows. The angels were the least, the gentiles the farthest, removed from him; and the foundation both of this preaching and of their faith was laid before his assumption.</p>
<p><em>Was believed on in the world</em> — Opposed to heaven, into which he was taken up. The first point is, He was manifested in the flesh; the last, He was taken up into glory.</p>
