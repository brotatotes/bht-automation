<p><em>Neglect not</em> — They neglect it who do not exercise it to the full.</p>
<p><em>The gift</em> — Of feeding the flock, of power, and love, and sobriety.</p>
<p><em>Which was given thee by prophecy</em> — By immediate direction from God.</p>
<p><em>By the laying on of my hands</em> — <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="2ti+1:6">2 Timothy 1:6</span>; while the elders joined also in the solemnity. This presbytery probably consisted of some others, together with Paul and Silas.</p>
