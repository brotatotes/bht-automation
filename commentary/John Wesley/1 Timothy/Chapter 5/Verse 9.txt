<p><em>Let not a widow be chosen</em> — Into the number of deaconesses, who attended sick women or travelling preachers.</p>
<p><em>Under threescore</em> — Afterwards they were admitted at forty, if they were eminent for holiness.</p>
<p><em>Having been the wife of one husband</em> — That is, having lived in lawful marriage, whether with one or more persons successively.</p>
