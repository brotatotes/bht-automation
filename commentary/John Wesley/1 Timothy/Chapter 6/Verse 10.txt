<p><em>Love of money</em> — Commonly called "prudent care" of what a man has.</p>
<p><em>Is the root</em> — The parent of all manner of evils.</p>
<p><em>Which some coveting have erred</em> — Literally, missed the mark. They aimed not at faith, but at something else.</p>
<p><em>And pierced themselves with many sorrows</em> — From a guilty conscience, tormenting passions, desires contrary to reason, religion, and one another. How cruel are worldly men to themselves!</p>
