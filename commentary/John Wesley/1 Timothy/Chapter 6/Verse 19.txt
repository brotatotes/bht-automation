<p><em>Treasuring up for themselves a good foundation</em> — Of an abundant reward, by the free mercy of God.</p>
<p><em>That they may lay hold on eternal life</em> — This cannot be done by alms-deeds; yet they "come up for a memorial before God," <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="ac+10:4">Acts 10:4</span>. And the lack even of this may be the cause why God will withhold grace and salvation from us.</p>
