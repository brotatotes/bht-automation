<p><em>If any teach otherwise</em> — Than strict practical holiness in all Its branches.</p>
<p><em>And consent not to sound words</em> — Literally, healthful words; words that have no taint of falsehood, or tendency to encourage sin.</p>
<p><em>And the doctrine which is after godliness</em> — Exquisitely contrived to answer all the ends, and secure every interest, of real piety.</p>
