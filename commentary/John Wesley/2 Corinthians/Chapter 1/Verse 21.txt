<p>I say, to the glory of God - For it is God alone that is able to fulfil these promises.</p>
<p><em>That establisheth us</em> — Apostles and teachers.</p>
<p><em>With you</em> — All true believers. In the faith of Christ; and hath anointed us - With the oil of gladness, with joy in the Holy Ghost, thereby giving us strength both to do and suffer his will.</p>
