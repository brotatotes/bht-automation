<p><em>Who also hath sealed us</em> — Stamping his image on our hearts, thus marking and sealing us as his own property.</p>
<p><em>And given us the earnest of his Spirit</em> — There is a difference between an earnest and a pledge. A pledge is to be restored when the debt is paid; but an earnest is not taken away, but completed. Such an earnest is the Spirit. The first fruits of it we have <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="ro+8:23">Romans 8:23</span>; and we wait for all the fulness.</p>
