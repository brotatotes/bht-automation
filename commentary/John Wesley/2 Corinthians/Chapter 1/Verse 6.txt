<p><em>And whether we are afflicted, it is for your comfort and salvation</em> — For your present comfort, your present and future salvation.</p>
<p><em>Or whether we are comforted, it is for your comfort</em> — That we may be the better able to comfort you.</p>
<p><em>Which is effectual in the patient enduring the same sufferings which we also suffer</em> — Through the efficacy of which you patiently endure the same kind of sufferings with us.</p>
