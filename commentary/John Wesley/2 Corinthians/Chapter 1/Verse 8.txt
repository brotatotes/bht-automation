<p><em>We would not have you ignorant, brethren, of the trouble which befell us in Asia</em> — Probably the same which is described in the nineteenth chapter of the Acts. <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="ac+19:1">Acts 19:1</span> The Corinthians knew before that he had been in trouble: he now declares the greatness and the fruit of it.</p>
<p><em>We were exceedingly pressed, above our strength</em> — Above the ordinary strength even of an apostle.</p>
