<p><em>For the weapons of our warfare</em> — Those we use in this war.</p>
<p><em>Are not carnal</em> — But spiritual, and therefore mighty to the throwing down of strong holds - Of all the difficulties which men or devils can raise in our way. Though faith and prayer belong also to the Christian armour, <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="eph+6:15">Ephesians 6:15</span>, etc., yet the word of God seems to be here chiefly intended.</p>
