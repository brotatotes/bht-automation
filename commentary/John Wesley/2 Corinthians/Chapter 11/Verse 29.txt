<p><em>Who</em> — So he had not only the care of the churches, but of every person therein.</p>
<p><em>Is weak, and I am not weak</em> — By sympathy, as well as by condescension.</p>
<p><em>Who is offended</em> — Hindered in, or turned out of, the good way.</p>
<p><em>And I burn not</em> — Being pained as though I had fire in my bosom.</p>
