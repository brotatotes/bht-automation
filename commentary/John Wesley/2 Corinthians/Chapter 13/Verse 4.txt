<p><em>He was crucified through weakness</em> — Through the impotence of human nature.</p>
<p><em>We also are weak with him</em> — We appear weak and despicable by partaking of the same sufferings for his sake.</p>
<p><em>But we shall live with him</em> — Being raised from the dead.</p>
<p><em>By the power of God in you</em> — By that divine energy which is now in every believer, <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="2co+13:5">2 Corinthians 13:5</span>.</p>
