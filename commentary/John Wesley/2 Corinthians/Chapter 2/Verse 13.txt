<p><em>I had no rest in my spirit</em> — From an earnest desire to know how my letter had been received.</p>
<p><em>Because I did not find Titus</em> — In his return from you.</p>
<p><em>So I went forth into Macedonia</em> — Where being much nearer, I might more easily be informed concerning you. The apostle resumes the thread of his discourse, <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="2co+7:2">2 Corinthians 7:2</span>, interposing an admirable digression concerning what he had done and suffered elsewhere, the profit of which he by this means derives to the Corinthians also; and as a prelude to his apology against the false apostles.</p>
