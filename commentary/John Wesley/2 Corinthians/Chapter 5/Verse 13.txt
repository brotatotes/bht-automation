<p><em>For if we are transported beyond ourselves</em> — Or at least, appear so to others, treated of, <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="2co+5:15-21">2 Corinthians 5:15-21</span>, speaking or writing with uncommon vehemence.</p>
<p><em>It is to God</em> — He understands (if men do not) the emotion which himself inspires.</p>
<p><em>If we be sober</em> — Treated of, <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="2co+6:1-10">2 Corinthians 6:1-10</span>. If I proceed in a more calm, sedate manner.</p>
<p><em>It is for your sakes</em> — Even good men bear this, rather than the other method, in their teachers. But these must obey God, whoever is offended by it.</p>
