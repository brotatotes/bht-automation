<p><em>Namely</em> — The sum of which is, God - The whole Godhead, but more eminently God the Father.</p>
<p><em>Was in Christ, reconciling the world</em> — Which was before at enmity with God.</p>
<p><em>To himself</em> — So taking away that enmity, which could no otherwise be removed than by the blood of the Son of God.</p>
