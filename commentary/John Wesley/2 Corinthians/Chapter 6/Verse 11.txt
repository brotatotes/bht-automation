<p>From the praise of the Christian ministry, which he began2Corinthians2:14, he now draws his affectionate exhortation.</p>
<p><em>O ye Corinthians</em> — He seldom uses this appellation. But it has here a peculiar force.</p>
<p><em>Our mouth is opened toward you</em> — With uncommon freedom, because our heart is enlarged - In tenderness.</p>
