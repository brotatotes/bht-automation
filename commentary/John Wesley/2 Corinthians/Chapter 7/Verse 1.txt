<p><em>Let us cleanse ourselves</em> — This is the latter part of the exhortation, which was proposed, <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="2co+6:1">2 Corinthians 6:1</span>, and resumed, <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="2co+6:14">2 Corinthians 6:14</span>.</p>
<p><em>From all pollution of the flesh</em> — All outward sin.</p>
<p><em>And of the spirit</em> — All inward. Yet let us not rest in negative religion, but perfect holiness - Carrying it to the height in all its branches, and enduring to the end in the loving fear of God, the sure foundation of all holiness.</p>
