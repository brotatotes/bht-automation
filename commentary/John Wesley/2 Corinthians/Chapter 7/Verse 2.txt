<p><em>Receive us</em> — The sum of what is said in this, as well as in the tenth and following chapters.</p>
<p><em>We have hurt no man</em> — In his person.</p>
<p><em>We have corrupted no man</em> — In his principles.</p>
<p><em>We have defrauded no man</em> — Of his property. In this he intimates likewise the good he had done them, but with the utmost modesty, as it were not looking upon it.</p>
