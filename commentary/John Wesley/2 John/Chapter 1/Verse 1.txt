The elder — An appellation suited to a familiar letter, but upon a weighty subject.To the elect — That is, Christian. Kuria is undoubtedly a proper name, both here and in2John1:5; for it was not then usual to apply the title of lady to any but the Roman empress; neither would such a manner of speaking have been suitable to the simplicity and dignity of the apostle.Whom — Both her and her children.I love in the truth — With unfeigned and holy love.
