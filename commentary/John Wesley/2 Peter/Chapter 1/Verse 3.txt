<p><em>As his divine power has given us all things</em> — There is a wonderful cheerfulness in this exordium, which begins with the exhortation itself.</p>
<p><em>That pertain to life and godliness</em> — To the present, natural life, and to the continuance and increase of spiritual life. Through that divine knowledge of him - Of Christ.</p>
<p><em>Who hath called us by</em> — His own glorious power, to eternal glory, as the end; by Christian virtue or fortitude, as the means.</p>
