<p><em>For if after they</em> — Who are thus ensnared.</p>
<p><em>Have escaped the pollutions of the world</em> — The sins which pollute all who know not God.</p>
<p><em>Through the knowledge of Christ</em> — That is, through faith in him, <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="2pe+1:3">2 Peter 1:3</span>.</p>
<p><em>They are again entangled therein, and overcome, their last state is worse than the first</em> — More inexcusable, and causing a greater damnation.</p>
