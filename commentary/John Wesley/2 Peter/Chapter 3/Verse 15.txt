<p><em>And account the longsuffering of the Lord salvation</em> — Not only designed to lead men to repentance, but actually conducing thereto: a precious means of saving many more souls.</p>
<p><em>As our beloved brother Paul also hath written to you</em> — This refers not only to the single sentence preceding, but to all that went before. St. Paul had written to the same effect concerning the end of the world, in several parts of his epistles, and particularly in his Epistle to the Hebrews. <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="ro+2:4">Romans 2:4</span>.</p>
