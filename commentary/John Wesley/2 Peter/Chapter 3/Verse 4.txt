<p><em>Saying, Where is the promise of his coming</em> — To judgment (They do not even deign to name him.) We see no sign of any such thing.</p>
<p><em>For ever since the fathers</em> — Our first ancestors.</p>
<p><em>Fell asleep, all things</em> — Heaven. water, earth.</p>
<p><em>Continue as they were from the beginning of the creation</em> — Without any such material change as might make us believe they will ever end.</p>
