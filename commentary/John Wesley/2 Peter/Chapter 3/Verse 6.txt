<p><em>Through which</em> — Heaven and earth, the windows of heaven being opened, and the fountains of the great deep broken up.</p>
<p><em>The world that then was</em> — The whole antediluvian race.</p>
<p><em>Being overflowed with water, perished</em> — And the heavens and earth themselves, though they did not perish, yet underwent a great change. So little ground have these scoffers for saying that all things continue as they were from the creation.</p>
