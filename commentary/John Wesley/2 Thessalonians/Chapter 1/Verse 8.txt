<p><em>Taking vengeance</em> — Does God barely permit this, or (as "the Lord" once "rained brimstone and fire from the Lord out of heaven," <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="ge+19:24">Genesis 19:24</span>) does a fiery stream go forth from him for ever? Who know not God - (The root of all wickedness and misery) who remain in heathen ignorance.</p>
<p><em>And who obey not</em> — This refers chiefly to the Jews, who had heard the gospel.</p>
