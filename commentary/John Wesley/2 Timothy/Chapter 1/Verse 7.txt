<p>And let nothing discourage thee, for God hath not given us - That is, the spirit which God hath given us Christians, is not the spirit of fear - Or cowardice.</p>
<p><em>But of power</em> — Banishing fear.</p>
<p><em>And love and sobriety</em> — These animate us in our duties to God, our brethren, and ourselves. Power and sobriety are two good extremes. Love is between, the tie and temperament of both; preventing the two bad extremes of fearfulness and rashness. More is said concerning power, <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="1ti+1:8">1 Timothy 1:8</span>; concerning love, <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="2ti+2:14">2 Timothy 2:14</span>, etc.; concerning sobriety, <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="2ti+3:1">2 Timothy 3:1</span>, etc.</p>
