<p><em>The cloak</em> — Either the toga, which belonged to him as a Roman citizen, or an upper garment, which might be needful as winter came on.</p>
<p><em>Which I left at Troas with Carpus</em> — Who was probably his host there.</p>
<p><em>Especially the parchments</em> — The books written on parchment.</p>
