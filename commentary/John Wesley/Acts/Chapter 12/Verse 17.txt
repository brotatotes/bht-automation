<p><em>Beckoning to them</em> — Many of whom being amazed, were talking together.</p>
<p><em>And he said, Show these things to James</em> — The brother or kinsman of our Lord, and author of the epistle which bears his name. He appears to have been a person of considerable weight and importance, probably the chief overseer of that province, and of the Church in Jerusalem in particular.</p>
<p><em>He went into another place</em> — Where he might be better concealed till the storm was over.</p>
