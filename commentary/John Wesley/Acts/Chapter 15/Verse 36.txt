<p><em>Let us go and visit the brethren in every city where we have preached</em> — This was all that St. Paul designed at first; but it was not all that God designed by his journey, whose providence carried him much farther than he intended.</p>
<p><em>And see how they do</em> — How their souls prosper: how they grow in faith, hope love: what else ought to be the grand and constant inquiry in every ecclesiastical visitation? Reader, how dost thou do?</p>
