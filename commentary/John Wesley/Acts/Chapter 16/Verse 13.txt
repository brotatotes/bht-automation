<p><em>We went out of the gate</em> — The Jews usually held their religious assemblies (either by choice or constraint) at a distance from the heathens: by a river side - Which was also convenient for purifying themselves.</p>
<p><em>Where prayer was wont to be made</em> — Though it does not appear there was any house built there.</p>
<p><em>We spake</em> — At first in a familiar manner. Paul did not immediately begin to preach.</p>
