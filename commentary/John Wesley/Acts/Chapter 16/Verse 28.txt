<p><em>But Paul cried</em> — As they were all then in the dark, it is not easy to say, how Paul knew of the jailer's purpose; unless it were by some immediate notice from God, which is by no means incredible.</p>
<p><em>With a loud voice</em> — Through earnestness, and because he was at some distance.</p>
<p><em>Do thyself no harm</em> — Although the Christian faith opens the prospect into another life, yet it absolutely forbids and effectually prevents a man's discharging himself from this.</p>
