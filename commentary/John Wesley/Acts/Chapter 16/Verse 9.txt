<p><em>A vision appeared to Paul by night</em> — It was not a dream, though it was by night. No other dream is mentioned in the New Testament than that of Joseph and of Pilate's wife.</p>
<p><em>A man of Macedonia</em> — Probably an angel clothed in the Macedonian habit, or using the language of the country, and representing the inhabitants of it.</p>
<p><em>Help us</em> — Against Satan, ignorance, and sin.</p>
