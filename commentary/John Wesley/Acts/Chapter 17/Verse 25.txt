<p><em>Neither is he served as though he needed any thing</em> — or person - The Greek word equally takes in both.</p>
<p><em>To all</em> — That live and breathe;-in him we live; and breathe - In him we move. By breathing life is continued. I breathe this moment: the next is not in my power: and all things - For in him we are. So exactly do the parts of this discourse answer each other.</p>
