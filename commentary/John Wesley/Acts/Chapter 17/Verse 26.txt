<p><em>He hath made of one blood the whole nation of men</em> — By this expression the apostle showed them in the most unaffected manner, that though he was a Jew, be was not enslaved to any narrow views, but looked on all mankind as his brethren: having determined the times - That it is God who gave men the earth to inhabit, Paul proves from the order of times and places, showing the highest wisdom of the Disposer, superior to all human counsels.</p>
<p><em>And the bounds of their habitation</em> — By mountains, seas, rivers, and the like.</p>
