<p><em>He spake</em> — Privately; and taught publicly. Probably he returned to live at Alexandria, soon after he had been baptized by John; and so had no opportunity of being fully acquainted with the doctrines of the Gospel, as delivered by Christ and his apostles.</p>
<p><em>And explained to him the way of God more perfectly</em> — He who knows Christ, is able to instruct even those that are mighty in the Scriptures.</p>
