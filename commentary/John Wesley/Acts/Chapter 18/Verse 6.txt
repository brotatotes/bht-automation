<p><em>He shook his raiment</em> — To signify he would from that time refrain from them: and to intimate, that God would soon shake them off as unworthy to be numbered among his people.</p>
<p><em>I am pure</em> — None can say this but he that has borne a full testimony against sin.</p>
<p><em>From henceforth I will go to the Gentiles</em> — But not to them altogether. He did not break off all intercourse with the Jews even at Corinth. Only he preached no more in their synagogue.</p>
