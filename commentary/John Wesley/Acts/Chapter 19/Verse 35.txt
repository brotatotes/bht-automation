<p><em>The register</em> — Probably the chief governor of the public games.</p>
<p><em>The image which fell down from Jupiter</em> — They believed that very image of Diana, which stood in her temple, fell down from Jupiter in heaven. Perhaps he designed to insinuate, as if falling down from Jupiter, it was not made with hands, and so was not that sort of idols which Paul had said were no gods.</p>
