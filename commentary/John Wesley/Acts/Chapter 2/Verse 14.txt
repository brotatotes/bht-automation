<p><em>Then Peter standing up</em> — All the gestures, all the words of Peter, show the utmost sobriety; lifted up his voice - With cheerfulness and boldness; and said to them - This discourse has three parts; each of which, <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="ac+2:14,22,29">Acts 2:14,22,29</span>, begins with the same appellation, men: only to the last part he prefixes with more familiarity the additional word brethren.</p>
<p><em>Men of Judea</em> — That is, ye that are born in Judea. St. Peter spoke in Hebrew, which they all understood.</p>
