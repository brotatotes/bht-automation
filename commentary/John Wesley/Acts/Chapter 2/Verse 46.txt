<p><em>Continuing daily</em> — breaking the bread - in the Lord's Supper, as did many Churches for some ages.</p>
<p><em>They partook of their food with gladness and singleness of heart</em> — They carried the same happy and holy temper through all their common actions: eating and working with the same spirit wherewith they prayed and received the Lord's Supper.</p>
