<p><em>Mitylene</em> — Was a city and part of the isle of Lesbos, about seven miles distant from the Asiatic coast. `16.</p>
<p><em>For Paul had determined to sail by Ephesus</em> — Which lay on the other side of the bay.</p>
<p><em>He hasted to be at Jerusalem on the day of pentecost</em> — Because then was the greatest concourse of people.</p>
