<p><em>Binding his own feet and hands</em> — In the manner that malefactors were wont to be bound when apprehended.</p>
<p><em>So shall the Jews bind the man whose girdle this is</em> — St. Paul's bonds were first particularly foretold at Cesarea, to which he afterward came in bonds, <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="ac+23:33">Acts 23:33</span>.</p>
