<p><em>Then Paul took the men</em> — Yielding his own judgment to their advice, which seemed to flow not out of spiritual but carnal wisdom; seeming to be what he really was not: making as if he believed the law still in force.</p>
<p><em>Declaring</em> — Giving notice to the priests in waiting, that he designed to accomplish the days of purification, till all the sacrifice should be offered, as the Mosaic law required, <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="nu+6:13">Numbers 6:13</span>.</p>
