<p><em>I am standing at Cesar's judgment seat</em> — For all the courts of the Roman governors were held in the name of the emperor, and by commission from him.</p>
<p><em>No man can give me up</em> — He expresses it modestly: the meaning is, Thou canst not.</p>
<p><em>I appeal to Cesar</em> — Which any Roman citizen might do before sentence was passed.</p>
