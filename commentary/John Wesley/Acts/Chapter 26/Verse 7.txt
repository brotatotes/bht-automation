<p><em>Our twelve tribes</em> — For a great part of the ten tribes also had at various times returned from the east to their own country, <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="jas+1:1">James 1:1</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="1pe+1:1">1 Peter 1:1</span>.</p>
<p><em>Worshipping continually night and day</em> — That is, this is what they aim at in all their public and private worship.</p>
