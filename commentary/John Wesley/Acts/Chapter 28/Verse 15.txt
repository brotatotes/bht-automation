<p><em>The brethren</em> — That is, the Christians, came out thence to meet us - It is remarkable that there is no certain account by whom Christianity was planted at Rome. Probably some inhabitants of that city were at Jerusalem on the day of pentecost, <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="ac+2:10">Acts 2:10</span>; and being then converted themselves, carried the Gospel thither at their return. Appii-Forum was a town fifty-one miles from Rome; the Three Taverns about thirty.</p>
<p><em>He took courage</em> — He saw Christ was at Rome also, and now forgot all the troubles of his journey.</p>
