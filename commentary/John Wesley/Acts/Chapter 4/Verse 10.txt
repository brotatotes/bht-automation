<p><em>Be it known to you all</em> — Probably the herald of God proclaimed this with a loud voice.</p>
<p><em>Whom God hath raised from the dead</em> — They knew in their own consciences that it was so. And though they had hired the soldiers to tell a most senseless and incredible tale to the contrary, <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="mt+28:12,15">Matthew 28:12,15</span>, yet it is observable, they did not, so far as we can learn, dare to plead it before Peter and John.</p>
