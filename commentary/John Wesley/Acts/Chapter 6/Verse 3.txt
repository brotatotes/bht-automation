<p><em>Of good report</em> — That there may be no room to suspect them of partiality or injustice.</p>
<p><em>Full of the Holy Ghost and wisdom</em> — For it is not a light matter to dispense even the temporal goods of the Church. To do even this well, a large measure both of the gifts and grace of God is requisite.</p>
<p><em>Whom we will set over this business</em> — It would have been happy for the Church, had its ordinary ministers in every age taken the same care to act in concert with the people committed to their charge, which the apostles themselves, extraordinary as their office was, did on this and other occasions.</p>
