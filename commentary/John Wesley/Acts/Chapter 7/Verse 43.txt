<p><em>Ye took up</em> — Probably not long after the golden calf: but secretly; else Moses would have mentioned it.</p>
<p><em>The shrine</em> — A small, portable chapel, in which was the image of their god. Moloch was the planet Mars, which they worshipped under a human shape. Remphan, that is, Saturn, they represented by a star.</p>
<p><em>And I will carry you beyond Babylon</em> — That is, beyond Damascus (which is the word in Amos) and Babylon. This was fulfilled by the king of Assyria, <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="2ki+17:6">2 Kings 17:6</span>.</p>
