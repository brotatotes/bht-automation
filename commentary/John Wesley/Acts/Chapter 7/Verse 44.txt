<p><em>Our fathers had the tabernacle of the testimony</em> — The testimony was properly the two tables of stone, on which the ten commandments were written. Hence the ark which contained them is frequently called the ark of the testimony; and the whole tabernacle in this place.</p>
<p><em>The tabernacle of the testimony</em> — according to the model which he had seen - When he was caught up in the visions of God on the mount.</p>
