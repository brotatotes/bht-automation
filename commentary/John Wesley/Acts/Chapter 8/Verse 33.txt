<p><em>In his humiliation his judgment was taken away</em> — That is, when he was a man, he had no justice shown him. To take away a person's judgment, is a proverbial phrase for oppressing him.</p>
<p><em>And who shall declare, or count his generation</em> — That is, who can number his seed, <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="isa+53:10">Isaiah 53:10</span>; which he hath purchased by laying down his life?</p>
