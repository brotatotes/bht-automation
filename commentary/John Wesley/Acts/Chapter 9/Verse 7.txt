<p><em>The men</em> — stood - Having risen before Saul; for they also fell to the ground, <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="ac+26:14">Acts 26:14</span>. It is probable they all journeyed on foot.</p>
<p><em>Hearing the noise</em> — But not an articulate voice. And seeing the light, but not Jesus himself, <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="ac+26:13">Acts 26:13</span>, etc.</p>
