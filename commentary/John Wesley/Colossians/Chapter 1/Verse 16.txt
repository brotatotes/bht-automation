<p><em>For</em> — This explains the latter part of the preceding verse. Through implies something prior to the particles by and for; so denoting the beginning, the progress, and the end.</p>
<p><em>Him</em> — This word, frequently repeated, signifies his supreme majesty, and excludes every creature.</p>
<p><em>Were created all things that are in heaven</em> — And heaven itself. But the inhabitants are named, because more noble than the house.</p>
<p><em>Invisible</em> — The several species of which are subjoined. Thrones are superior to dominions; principalities, to powers. Perhaps the two latter may express their office with regard to other creatures: the two former may refer to God, who maketh them his chariots, and, as it were, rideth upon their wings.</p>
