<p><em>And</em> — From the whole he now descends to the most eminent part, the church.</p>
<p><em>He is the head of the church</em> — Universal; the supreme and only head both of influence and of government to the whole body of believers.</p>
<p><em>Who is</em> — The repetition of the expression { <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="col+1:15">Colossians 1:15</span>} points out the entrance on a new paragraph.</p>
<p><em>The beginning</em> — Absolutely, the Eternal.</p>
<p><em>The first begotten from the dead</em> — From whose resurrection flows all the life, spiritual and eternal, of all his brethren.</p>
<p><em>That in all things</em> — Whether of nature or grace.</p>
<p><em>He might have the pre-eminence</em> — Who can sound this depth?</p>
