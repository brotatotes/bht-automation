<p><em>And ye</em> — Who believe.</p>
<p><em>Are filled with him</em> — <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="joh+1:16">John 1:16</span>. Christ is filled with God, and ye are filled with Christ. And ye are filled by him. The fulness of Christ overflows his church, <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="ps+133:3">Psalm 133:3</span>. He is originally full. We are filled by him with wisdom and holiness.</p>
<p><em>Who is the head of all principality and power</em> — Of angels as well as men Not from angels therefore, but from their head, are we to ask whatever we stand in need of.</p>
