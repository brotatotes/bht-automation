<p><em>And having spoiled the principalities and powers</em> — The evil angels, of their usurped dominion.</p>
<p><em>He</em> — God the Father.</p>
<p><em>Exposed them openly</em> — Before all the hosts of hell and heaven.</p>
<p><em>Triumphing over them in or by him</em> — By Christ. Thus the paragraph begins with Christ, goes on with him, and ends with him.</p>
