<p>Out of pretended humility, they worshipped angels, as not daring to apply immediately to God. Yet this really sprung from their being puffed up: (the constant forerunner of a fall, <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="pr+16:18">Proverbs 16:18</span>) so far was it from being an instance of true humility.</p>
