<p><em>Therefore</em> — The inference begun, <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="col+2:16">Colossians 2:16</span>; is continued. A new inference follows, <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="col+3:1">Colossians 3:1</span>.</p>
<p><em>If ye are dead with Christ from the rudiments of the world</em> — That is, If ye are dead with Christ, and so freed from them, why receive ye ordinances - Which Christ hath not enjoined, from which he hath made you free.</p>
