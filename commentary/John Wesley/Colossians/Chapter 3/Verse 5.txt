<p><em>Mortify therefore</em> — Put to death, slay with a continued stroke.</p>
<p><em>Your members</em> — Which together make up the body of sin.</p>
<p><em>Which are upon the earth</em> — Where they find their nourishment.</p>
<p><em>Uncleanness</em> — In act, word, or thought.</p>
<p><em>Inordinate affection</em> — Every passion which does not flow from and lead to the love of God.</p>
<p><em>Evil desire</em> — The desire of the flesh, the desire of the eye, and the pride of life.</p>
<p><em>Covetousness</em> — According to the derivation of the word, means the desire of having more, or of any thing independent on God.</p>
<p><em>Which is idolatry</em> — Properly and directly; for it is giving the heart to a creature.</p>
