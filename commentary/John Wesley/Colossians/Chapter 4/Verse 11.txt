<p><em>These</em> — Three, Aristarchus, Marcus, and Justus. Of all the circumcision - That is, of all my Jewish fellowlabourers.</p>
<p><em>Are the only fellowworkers unto the kingdom of God</em> — That is, in preaching the gospel.</p>
<p><em>Who have been a comfort to me</em> — What, then, can we expect? that all our fellowworkers should be a comfort to us?</p>
