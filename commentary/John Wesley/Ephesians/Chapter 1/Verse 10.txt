<p><em>That in the dispensation of the fullness of the times</em> — In this last administration of God's fullest grace, which took place when the time appointed was fully come.</p>
<p><em>He might gather together into one in Christ</em> — Might recapitulate, re-unite, and place in order again under Christ, their common Head.</p>
<p><em>All things which are in heaven, and on earth</em> — All angels and men, whether living or dead, in the Lord.</p>
