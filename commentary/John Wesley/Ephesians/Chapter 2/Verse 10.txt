<p><em>For we are his workmanship</em> — Which proves both that salvation is by faith, and that faith is the gift of God.</p>
<p><em>Created unto good works</em> — That afterwards we might give ourselves to them.</p>
<p><em>Which God had before preprepared</em> — The occasions of them: so we must still ascribe the whole to God.</p>
<p><em>That we might walk in them</em> — Though not be justified by them.</p>
