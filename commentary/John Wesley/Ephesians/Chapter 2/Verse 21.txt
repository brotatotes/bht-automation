<p><em>On whom all the building fitly framed together</em> — The whole fabric of the universal church rises up like a great pile of living materials.</p>
<p><em>Into an holy temple in the Lord</em> — Dedicated to Christ, and inhabited by him, in which he displays his presence, and is worshipped and glorified. What is the temple of Diana of the Ephesians, whom ye formerly worshipped, to this?</p>
