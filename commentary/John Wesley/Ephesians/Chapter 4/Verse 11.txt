<p>And, among other his free gifts, he gave some apostles - His chief ministers and special witnesses, as having seen him after his resurrection, and received their commission immediately from him.</p>
<p><em>And same prophets, and some evangelists</em> — A prophet testifies of things to come; an evangelist of things past: and that chiefly by preaching the gospel before or after any of the apostles. All these were extraordinary officers. The ordinary were.</p>
<p><em>Some pastors</em> — Watching over their several flocks.</p>
<p><em>And some teachers</em> — Whether of the same or a lower order, to assist them, as occasion might require.</p>
