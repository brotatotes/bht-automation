In this verse is noted the office of ministers; in the next, the aim of the saints; in the14th, 15th, 16th, the way of growing in grace. And each of these has three parts, standing in the same order.For the perfecting the saints — The completing them both in number and their various gifts and graces.To the work of the ministry — The serving God and his church in their various ministrations.To the edifying of the body of Christ — The building up this his mystical body in faith, love, holiness.
