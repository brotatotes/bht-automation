<p>In this verse is noted the office of ministers; in the next, the aim of the saints; in the14th, 15th, 16th, the way of growing in grace. And each of these has three parts, standing in the same order.</p>
<p><em>For the perfecting the saints</em> — The completing them both in number and their various gifts and graces.</p>
<p><em>To the work of the ministry</em> — The serving God and his church in their various ministrations.</p>
<p><em>To the edifying of the body of Christ</em> — The building up this his mystical body in faith, love, holiness.</p>
