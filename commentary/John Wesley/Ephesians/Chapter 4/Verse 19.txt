<p><em>Who being past feeling</em> — The original word is peculiarly significant. It properly means, past feeling pain. Pain urges the sick to seek a remedy, which, where there is no pain, is little thought of.</p>
<p><em>Have given themselves up</em> — Freely, of their own accord. Lasciviousness is but one branch of uncleanness, which implies impurity of every kind.</p>
