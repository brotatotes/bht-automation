<p><em>Now this expression, He ascended, what is it, but that he descended</em> — That is, does it not imply, that he descended first? Certainly it does, on the supposition of his being God. Otherwise it would not: since all the saints will ascend to heaven, though none of them descended thence.</p>
<p><em>Into the lower parts of the earth</em> — So the womb is called, <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="ps+139:15">Psalm 139:15</span>; the grave, <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="ps+63:9">Psalm 63:9</span>.</p>
