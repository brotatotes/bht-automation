<p><em>Brethren</em> — This is the only place in this epistle where he uses this compellation. Soldiers frequently use it to each other in the field.</p>
<p><em>Be strong</em> — Nothing less will suffice for such a fight: to be weak, and remain so, is the way to perish.</p>
<p><em>In the power of his might</em> — A very uncommon expression, plainly denoting what great assistance we need as if his might would not do, it must be the powerful exertion of his might.</p>
