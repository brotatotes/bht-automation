<p><em>Honour</em> — That is, love, reverence, obey, assist, in all things. The mother is particularly mentioned, as being more liable to be slighted than the father.</p>
<p><em>Which is the first commandment with a promise</em> — For the promise implied in the second commandment does not belong to the keeping that command in particular, but the whole law. <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="ex+20:12">Exodus 20:12</span></p>
