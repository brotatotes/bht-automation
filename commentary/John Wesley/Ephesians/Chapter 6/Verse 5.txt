<p><em>Your masters according to the flesh</em> — According to the present state of things: afterward the servant is free from his master.</p>
<p><em>With fear and trembling</em> — A proverbial expression, implying the utmost care and diligence.</p>
<p><em>In singleness of heart</em> — With a single eye to the providence and will of God.</p>
