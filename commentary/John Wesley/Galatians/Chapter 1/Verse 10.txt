<p><em>For</em> — He adds the reason why he speaks so confidently.</p>
<p><em>Do I now satisfy men</em> — Is this what I aim at in preaching or writing? If I still - Since I was an apostle.</p>
<p><em>Pleased men</em> — Studied to please them; if this were my motive of action; nay, if I did in fact please the men who know not God.</p>
<p><em>I should not be the servant of Christ</em> — Hear this, all ye who vainly hope to keep in favour both with God and with the world!</p>
