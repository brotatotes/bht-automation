<p><em>But when it pleased God</em> — He ascribes nothing to his own merits, endeavours, or sincerity.</p>
<p><em>Who separated me from my mother's womb</em> — Set me apart for an apostle, as he did Jeremiah for a prophet. <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="jer+1:5">Jeremiah 1:5</span>. Such an unconditional predestination as this may consist, both with God's justice and mercy.</p>
<p><em>And called me by his grace</em> — By his free and almighty love, to be both a Christian and an apostle.</p>
