<p><em>To reveal his Son in me</em> — By the powerful operation of his Spirit, <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="2co+4:6">2 Corinthians 4:6</span>; as well as to me, by the heavenly vision.</p>
<p><em>That I might preach him to others</em> — Which I should have been ill qualified to do, had I not first known him myself.</p>
<p><em>I did not confer with flesh and blood</em> — Being fully satisfied of the divine will, and determined to obey, I took no counsel with any man, neither with my own reason or inclinations, which might have raised numberless objections.</p>
