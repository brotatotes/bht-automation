<p><em>As</em> — He speaks upon mature deliberation; after pausing, it seems, between the two verses.</p>
<p><em>We</em> — I and the brethren who are with me.</p>
<p><em>Have said before</em> — Many times, in effect, if not in terms.</p>
<p><em>So I say</em> — All those brethren knew the truth of the gospel. St. Paul knew the Galatians had received the true gospel.</p>
