<p><em>Then fourteen years after</em> — My first journey thither.</p>
<p><em>I went up again to Jerusalem</em> — This seems to be the journey mentioned <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="ac+15:2">Acts 15:2</span>; several passages here referring to that great council, wherein all the apostles showed that they were of the same judgment with him.</p>
