<p><em>We</em> — St. Paul, to spare St. Peter, drops the first person singular, and speaks in the plural number. <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="ga+2:18">Galatians 2:18</span>, he speaks in the first person singular again by a figure; and without a figure, <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="ga+2:19">Galatians 2:19</span>, etc.</p>
<p><em>Who are Jews by nature</em> — By birth, not proselytes only.</p>
<p><em>And not sinners of the gentiles</em> — That is, not sinful Gentiles; not such gross, enormous, abandoned sinners, as the heathens generally were.</p>
