<p><em>For I through the law</em> — Applied by the Spirit to my heart, and deeply convincing me of my utter sinfulness and helplessness.</p>
<p><em>Am dead to the law</em> — To all hope of justification from it.</p>
<p><em>That I may live to God</em> — Not continue in sin. For this very end am I, in this sense, freed from the law, that I may be freed from sin.</p>
