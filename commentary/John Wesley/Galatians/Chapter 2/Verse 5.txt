<p><em>To whom we did not yield by submission</em> — Although in love he would have yielded to any. With such wonderful prudence did the apostle use his Christian liberty ! circumcising Timothy, <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="ac+16:3">Acts 16:3</span>, because of weak brethren, but not Titus, because of false brethren.</p>
<p><em>That the truth of the gospel</em> — That is, the true genuine gospel.</p>
<p><em>Might continue with you</em> — With you gentiles. So we defend, for your sakes, the privilege which you would give up.</p>
