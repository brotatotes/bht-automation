<p><em>And they who undoubtedly were something</em> — Above all others.</p>
<p><em>What they were</em> — How eminent soever.</p>
<p><em>It is no difference to me</em> — So that I should alter either my doctrine or my practice.</p>
<p><em>God accepteth no man's person</em> — For any eminence in gifts or outward prerogatives. In that conference added nothing to me - Neither as to doctrine nor mission.</p>
