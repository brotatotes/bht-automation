<p><em>And when James</em> — Probably named first because he was bishop of the church in Jerusalem.</p>
<p><em>And Cephas</em> — Speaking of him at Jerusalem he calls him by his Hebrew name.</p>
<p><em>And John</em> — Hence it appears that he also was at the council, though he is not particularly named in the Acts.</p>
<p><em>Who undoubtedly were pillars</em> — The principal supporters and defenders of the gospel.</p>
<p><em>Knew</em> — After they had heard the account I gave them.</p>
<p><em>The grace</em> — Of apostleship.</p>
<p><em>Which was given me, they</em> — In the name of all.</p>
<p><em>Gave to me and Barnabas</em> — My fellow-labourer.</p>
<p><em>The right hands of fellowship</em> — They gave us their hands in token of receiving us as their fellow - labourers, mutually agreeing that we - I and those in union with me.</p>
<p><em>Should go to the gentiles</em> — Chiefly.</p>
<p><em>And they</em> — With those that were in union with them, chiefly to the circumcision - The Jews.</p>
