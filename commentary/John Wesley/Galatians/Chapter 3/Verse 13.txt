<p><em>Christ</em> — Christ alone. The abruptness of the sentence shows an holy indignation at those who reject so great a blessing.</p>
<p><em>Hath redeemed us</em> — Whether Jews or gentiles, at an high price.</p>
<p><em>From the curse of the law</em> — The curse of God, which the law denounces against all transgressors of it.</p>
<p><em>Being made a curse for us</em> — Taking the curse upon himself, that we might be delivered from it, willingly submitting to that death which the law pronounces peculiarly accursed. <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="de+21:23">Deuteronomy 21:23</span>.</p>
