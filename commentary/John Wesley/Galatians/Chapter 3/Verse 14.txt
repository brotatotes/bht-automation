<p><em>That the blessing of Abraham</em> — The blessing promised to him.</p>
<p><em>Might come on the gentiles</em> — Also.</p>
<p><em>That we</em> — Who believe, whether Jews or gentiles.</p>
<p><em>Might receive the promise of the Spirit</em> — Which includes all the other promises.</p>
<p><em>Through faith</em> — Not by works; for faith looks wholly to the promise.</p>
