<p><em>I speak after the manner of men</em> — I illustrate this by a familiar instance, taken from the practice of men. Though it be but a man's covenant, yet, if it be once legally confirmed, none - No, not the covenanter himself, unless something unforeseen occur, which cannot be the case with God.</p>
<p><em>Disannulleth, or addeth thereto</em> — Any new conditions.</p>
