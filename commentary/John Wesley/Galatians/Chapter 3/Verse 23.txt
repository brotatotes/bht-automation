<p><em>But before faith</em> — That is, the gospel dispensation.</p>
<p><em>Came, we were kept</em> — As in close custody.</p>
<p><em>Under the law</em> — The Mosaic dispensation.</p>
<p><em>Shut up unto the faith which was to be revealed</em> — Reserved and prepared for the gospel dispensation.</p>
