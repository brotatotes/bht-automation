<p><em>Now</em> — To illustrate by a plain similitude the preeminence of the Christian, over the legal, dispensation.</p>
<p><em>The heir, as long as he is a child</em> — As he is under age.</p>
<p><em>Differeth nothing from a servant</em> — Not being at liberty either to use or enjoy his estate.</p>
<p><em>Though he be lord</em> — Proprietor of it all.</p>
