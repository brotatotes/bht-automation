<p><em>Ye observe days</em> — Jewish sabbaths.</p>
<p><em>And months</em> — New moons.</p>
<p><em>And times</em> — As that of the passover, pentecost, and the feast of tabernacles.</p>
<p><em>And years</em> — Annual solemnities. it does not mean sabbatic years. These were not to be observed out of the land of Canaan.</p>
