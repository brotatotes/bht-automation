<p><em>My little children</em> — He speaks as a parent, both with authority, and the most tender sympathy, toward weak and sickly children.</p>
<p><em>Of whom I travail in birth again</em> — As I did before, <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="ga+4:13">Galatians 4:13</span>, in vehement pain, sorrow, desire, prayer.</p>
<p><em>Till Christ be formed in you</em> — Till there be in you all the mind that was in him.</p>
