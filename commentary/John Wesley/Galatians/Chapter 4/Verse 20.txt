<p><em>I could wish to be present with you now</em> — Particularly in this exigence.</p>
<p><em>And to change</em> — Variously to attemper.</p>
<p><em>My voice</em> — He writes with much softness; but he would speak with more. The voice may more easily be varied according to the occasion than a letter can.</p>
<p><em>For I stand in doubt of you</em> — So that I am at a loss how to speak at this distance.</p>
