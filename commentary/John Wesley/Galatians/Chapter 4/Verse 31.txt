<p><em>So then</em> — To sum up all.</p>
<p><em>We</em> — Who believe.</p>
<p><em>Are not children of the bondwoman</em> — Have nothing to do with the servile Mosaic dispensation.</p>
<p><em>But of the free</em> — Being free from the curse and the bond of that law, and from the power of sin and Satan.</p>
