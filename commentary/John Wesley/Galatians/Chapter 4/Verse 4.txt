<p><em>But when the fulness of the time</em> — Appointed by the Father, <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="ga+4:2">Galatians 4:2</span>.</p>
<p><em>Was come, God sent forth</em> — From his own bosom. His Son, miraculously made of the substance of a woman - A virgin, without the concurrence of a man.</p>
<p><em>Made under the law</em> — Both under the precept, and under the curse, of it.</p>
