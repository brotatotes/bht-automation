<p>Yet I have confidence that - After ye have read this.</p>
<p><em>Ye will be no otherwise minded</em> — Than I am, and ye were.</p>
<p><em>But he that troubleth you</em> — It seems to have been one person chiefly who endeavoured to seduce them.</p>
<p><em>Shall bear his judgment</em> — A heavy burden, already hanging over his head.</p>
