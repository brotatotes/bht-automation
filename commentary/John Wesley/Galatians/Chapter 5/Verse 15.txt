<p><em>But if</em> — On the contrary, in consequence of the divisions which those troublers have occasioned among you, ye bite one another by evil speaking.</p>
<p><em>And devour one another</em> — By railing and clamour.</p>
<p><em>Take heed ye be not consumed one of another</em> — By bitterness, strife, and contention, our health and strength, both of body and soul, are consumed, as well as our substance and reputation.</p>
