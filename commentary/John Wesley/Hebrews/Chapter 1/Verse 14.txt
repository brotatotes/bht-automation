<p><em>Are they not all</em> — Though of various orders.</p>
<p><em>Ministering spirits, sent forth</em> — Ministering before God, sent forth to men.</p>
<p><em>To attend on them</em> — In numerous offices of protection, care, and kindness.</p>
<p><em>Who</em> — Having patiently continued in welldoing, shall inherit everlasting salvation.</p>
