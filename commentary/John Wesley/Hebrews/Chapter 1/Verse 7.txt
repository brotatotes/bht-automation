<p><em>Who maketh his angels</em> — This implies, they are only creatures, whereas the Son is eternal, <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="heb+1:8">Hebrews 1:8</span>; and the Creator himself, <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="heb+1:10">Hebrews 1:10</span>.</p>
<p><em>Spirits and a flame of fire</em> — Which intimates not only their office, but also their nature; which is excellent indeed, the metaphor being taken from the most swift, subtle, and efficacious things on earth; but nevertheless infinitely below the majesty of the Son. <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="ps+104:4">Psalm 104:4</span>.</p>
