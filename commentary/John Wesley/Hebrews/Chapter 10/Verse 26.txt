<p><em>For when we</em> — Any of us Christians.</p>
<p><em>Sin wilfully</em> — By total apostasy from God, termed "drawing back," <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="heb+10:38">Hebrews 10:38</span>. After having received the experimental knowledge of the gospel truth, there remaineth no more sacrifice for sins - None but that which we obstinately reject.</p>
