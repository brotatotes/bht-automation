<p><em>By faith Abraham</em> — When God made that glorious trial of him.</p>
<p><em>Offered up Isaac</em> — The will being accepted as if he had actually done it.</p>
<p><em>Yea, he that had received the promises</em> — Particularly that grand promise, "In Isaac shall thy seed be called." Offered up - This very son; the only one he had by Sarah. <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="ge+22:1">Genesis 22:1</span>,etc.</p>
