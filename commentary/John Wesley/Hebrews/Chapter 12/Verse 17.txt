<p><em>He was rejected</em> — He could not obtain it.</p>
<p><em>For he found no place for repentance</em> — There was no room for any such repentance as would regain what he had lost.</p>
<p><em>Though he sought it</em> — The blessing of the birth-right.</p>
<p><em>Diligently with tears</em> — He sought too late. Let us use the present time.</p>
