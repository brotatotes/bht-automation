<p><em>The things which are shaken</em> — Namely, heaven and earth.</p>
<p><em>As being made</em> — And consequently liable to change.</p>
<p><em>That the things which are not shaken may remain</em> — Even "the new heavens and the new earth," <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="re+21:1">Revelation 21:1</span>.</p>
