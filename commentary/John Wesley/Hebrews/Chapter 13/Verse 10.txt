<p>On the former part of this verse, the fifteenth and sixteenth depend; on the latter, the intermediate verses.</p>
<p><em>We have an altar</em> — The cross of Christ.</p>
<p><em>Whereof they have no right to eat</em> — To partake of the benefits which we receive therefrom.</p>
<p><em>Who serve the tabernacle</em> — Who adhere to the Mosaic law.</p>
