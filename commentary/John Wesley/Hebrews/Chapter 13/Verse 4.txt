<p>Marriage is honourable in, or for all sorts of men, clergy as well as laity: though the Romanists teach otherwise.</p>
<p><em>And the bed undefiled</em> — Consistent with the highest purity; though many spiritual writers, so called, say it is only licensed whoredom.</p>
<p><em>But whoremongers and adulterers God will judge</em> — Though they frequently escape the sentence of men.</p>
