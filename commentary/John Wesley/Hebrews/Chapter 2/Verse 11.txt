<p><em>For</em> — They are nearly related to each other.</p>
<p><em>He that sanctifieth</em> — Christ, <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="heb+13:12">Hebrews 13:12</span>.</p>
<p><em>And all they that are sanctified</em> — That are brought to God; that draw near or come to him, which are synonymous terms.</p>
<p><em>Are all of one</em> — Partakers of one nature, from one parent, Adam.</p>
