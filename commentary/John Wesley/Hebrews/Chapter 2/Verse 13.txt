<p><em>And again</em> — As one that has communion with his brethren in sufferings, as well as in nature, he says, I will put my trust in him - To carry me through them all.</p>
<p><em>And again</em> — With a like acknowledgment of his near relation to them, as younger brethren, who were yet but in their childhood, he presents all believers to God, saying, Behold I and the children whom thou hast given me. <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="isa+8:17,18">Isaiah 8:17,18</span></p>
