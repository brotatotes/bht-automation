<p><em>Wherefore it behoved him</em> — It was highly fit and proper, yea, necessary, in order to his design of redeeming them.</p>
<p><em>To be made in all things</em> — That essentially pertain to human nature, and in all sufferings and temptations.</p>
<p><em>Like his brethren</em> — This is a recapitulation of all that goes before: the sum of all that follows is added immediately. That he might be a merciful and faithful High Priest-Merciful toward sinners; faithful toward God. A priest or high priest is one who has a right of approaching God, and of bringing others to him. Faithful is treated of, <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="heb+3:2">Hebrews 3:2</span>, etc., with its use; merciful, <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="heb+4:14">Hebrews 4:14</span>, etc., with the use also; High Priest, <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="heb+5:4">Hebrews 5:4</span>, etc., <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="heb+7:1">Hebrews 7:1</span>, etc. The use is added from <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="heb+10:19">Hebrews 10:19</span>.</p>
<p><em>In things pertaining to God, to expiate the sins of the people</em> — Offering up their sacrifices and prayers to God; deriving God's grace, peace, and blessings upon them.</p>
