<p>It is done only with regard to Jesus, God-Man, who is now crowned with glory and honour - As a reward for his having suffered death.</p>
<p><em>He was made a little lower than the angels</em> — Who cannot either suffer or die.</p>
<p><em>That by the grace of God, he might taste death</em> — An expression denoting both the reality of his death, and the shortness of its continuance.</p>
<p><em>For every man</em> — That ever was or will be born into the world.</p>
