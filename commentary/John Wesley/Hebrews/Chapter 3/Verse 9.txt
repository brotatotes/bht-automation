<p><em>Where your fathers</em> — That hard-hearted and stiff-necked generation. So little cause had their descendants to glory in them.</p>
<p><em>Tempted me</em> — Whether I could and would help them.</p>
<p><em>Proved me</em> — Put my patience to the proof, even while they saw my glorious works both of judgment and mercy, and that for forty years.</p>
