<p><em>Concerning whom</em> — The apostle here begins an important digression, wherein he reproves, admonishes, and exhorts the Hebrews.</p>
<p><em>We</em> — Preachers of the gospel.</p>
<p><em>Have many things to say, and hard to be explained</em> — Though not so much from the subject-matter, as from your slothfulness in considering, and dulness in apprehending, the things of God.</p>
