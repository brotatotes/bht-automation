<p><em>Every one that useth milk</em> — That neither desires, nor can digest, anything else: otherwise strong men use milk; but not milk chiefly, and much less that only.</p>
<p><em>Is unexperienced in the word of righteousness</em> — The sublimer truths of the gospel. Such are all who desire and can digest nothing but the doctrine of justification and imputed righteousness.</p>
