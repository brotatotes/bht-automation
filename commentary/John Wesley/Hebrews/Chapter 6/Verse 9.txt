<p><em>But, beloved</em> — in this one place he calls them so. he never uses this appellation, but in exhorting.</p>
<p><em>We are persuaded of you things that accompany salvation</em> — We are persuaded you are now saved from your sins; and that ye have that faith, love, and holiness, which lead to final salvation.</p>
<p><em>Though we thus speak</em> — To warn you, lest you should fall from your present steadfastness.</p>
