<p><em>Without father, without mother, without pedigree</em> — Recorded, without any account of his descent from any ancestors of the priestly order.</p>
<p><em>Having neither beginning of days, nor end of life</em> — Mentioned by Moses.</p>
<p><em>But being</em> — In all these respects.</p>
<p><em>Made like the Son of God</em> — Who is really without father, as to his human nature; without mother, as to his divine; and in this also, without pedigree - Neither descended from any ancestors of the priestly order.</p>
<p><em>Remaineth a priest continually</em> — Nothing is recorded of the death or successor of Melchisedec. But Christ alone does really remain without death, and without successor.</p>
