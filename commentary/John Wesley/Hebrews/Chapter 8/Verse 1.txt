<p><em>We have such an high priest</em> — Having finished his description of the type in Melchisedec, the apostle begins to treat directly of the excellency of Christ's priesthood, beyond the Levitical.</p>
<p><em>Who is set down</em> — Having finished his oblation.</p>
<p><em>At the right hand of the Majesty</em> — Of God.</p>
