<p><em>If the ashes of an heifer</em> — Consumed by fire as a sin-offering, being sprinkled on them who were legally unclean.</p>
<p><em>Purified the flesh</em> — Removed that legal uncleanness, and re-admitted them to the temple and the congregation. <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="nu+19:17,18,19">Numbers 19:17,18,19</span>.</p>
