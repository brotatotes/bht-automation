<p><em>He took the blood of calves</em> — Or heifers.</p>
<p><em>And of goats, with water, and scarlet wool, and hyssop</em> — All these circumstances are not particularly mentioned in that chapter of Exodus, but are supposed to be already known from other passages of Moses.</p>
<p><em>And the book itself</em> — Which contained all he had said.</p>
<p><em>And sprinkled all the people</em> — Who were near him. The blood was mixed with water to prevent its growing too stiff for sprinkling; perhaps also to typify that blood and water, <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="joh+19:34">John 19:34</span>. <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="ex+24:7,8">Exodus 24:7,8</span></p>
