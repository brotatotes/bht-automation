<p><em>And almost all things</em> — For some were purified by water or fire.</p>
<p><em>Are according to the law purified with blood</em> — Offered or sprinkled. And according to the law, there is no forgiveness of sins without shedding of blood - All this pointed to the blood of Christ effectually cleansing from all sin, and intimated, there can be no purification from it by any other means.</p>
