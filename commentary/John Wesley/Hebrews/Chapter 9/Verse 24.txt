<p><em>For Christ did not enter into the holy place made with hands</em> — He never went into the holy of holies at Jerusalem, the figure of the true tabernacle in heaven, <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="heb+8:2">Hebrews 8:2</span>.</p>
<p><em>But into heaven itself, to appear in the presence of God for us</em> — As our glorious high priest and powerful intercessor.</p>
