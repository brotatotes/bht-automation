<p><em>Having the golden censer</em> — Used by the high priest only, on the great day of atonement. And the ark, or chest, of the covenant - So called from the tables of the covenant contained therein.</p>
<p><em>Wherein was the manna</em> — The monument of God's care over Israel.</p>
<p><em>And Aaron's rod</em> — The monument of the regular priesthood.</p>
<p><em>And the tables of the covenant</em> — The two tables of stone, on which the ten commandments were written by the finger of God the most venerable monument of all.</p>
