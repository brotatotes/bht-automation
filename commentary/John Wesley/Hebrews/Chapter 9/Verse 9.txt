<p><em>Which</em> — Tabernacle, with all its furniture and services.</p>
<p><em>Is a figure</em> — Or type, of good things to come Which cannot perfect the worshipper - Neither the priest nor him who brought the offering.</p>
<p><em>As to his conscience</em> — So that he should be no longer conscious of the guilt or power of sin. Observe, the temple was as yet standing.</p>
