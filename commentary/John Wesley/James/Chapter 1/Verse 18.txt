<p><em>Of his own will</em> — Most loving, most free, most pure, just opposite to our evil desire, <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="jas+1:15">James 1:15</span>.</p>
<p><em>Begat he us</em> — Who believe.</p>
<p><em>By the word of truth</em> — The true word, emphatically so termed; the gospel.</p>
<p><em>That we might be a kind of first-fruits of his creatures</em> — Christians are the chief and most excellent of his visible creatures; and sanctify the rest. Yet he says, A kind of - For Christ alone is absolutely the first - fruits.</p>
