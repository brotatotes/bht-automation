<p><em>Let every man be swift to hear</em> — This is treated of from <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="jas+1:21">James 1:21</span> to the end of the next chapter.</p>
<p><em>Slow to speak</em> — Which is treated of in he third chapter.</p>
<p><em>Slow to wrath</em> — Neither murmuring at God, nor angry at his neighbour. This is treated of in the third, and throughout the fourth and fifth chapters.</p>
