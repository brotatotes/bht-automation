<p><em>But let him ask in faith</em> — A firm confidence in God. St. James also both begins and ends with faith, <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="jas+5:15">James 5:15</span>; the hinderances of which he removes in the middle part of his epistle.</p>
<p><em>He that doubteth is like a wave of the sea</em> — Yea, such are all who have not asked and obtained wisdom.</p>
<p><em>Driven with the wind</em> — From without.</p>
<p><em>And tossed</em> — From within, by his own unstableness.</p>
