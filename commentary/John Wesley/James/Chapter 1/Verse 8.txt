<p><em>A doubleminded man</em> — Who has, as it were, two souls; whose heart is not simply given up to God.</p>
<p><em>Is unstable</em> — Being without the true wisdom; perpetually disagrees both with himself and others, <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="jas+3:16">James 3:16</span>.</p>
