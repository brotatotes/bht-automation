<p><em>And the scripture</em> — Which was afterwards written. Was hereby eminently fulfilled, Abraham believed God, and it was imputed to him for righteousness - This was twice fulfilled, - when Abraham first believed, and when he offered up Isaac. St. Paul speaks of the former fulfilling; St. James, of the latter.</p>
<p><em>And he was called the Friend of God</em> — Both by his posterity, <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="2ch+20:7">2 Chronicles 20:7</span>; and by God himself, <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="isa+41:8">Isaiah 41:8</span> so pleasing to God were the works be wrought in faith. <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="ge+15:6">Genesis 15:6</span></p>
