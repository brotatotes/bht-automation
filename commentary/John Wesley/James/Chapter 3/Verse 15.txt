<p><em>This wisdom</em> — Which is consistent with such zeal.</p>
<p><em>Is earthly</em> — Not heavenly; not from the Father of Lights.</p>
<p><em>Animal</em> — Not spiritual; not from the Spirit of God.</p>
<p><em>Devilish</em> — Not the gift of Christ, but such as Satan breathes into the soul.</p>
