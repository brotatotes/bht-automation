<p><em>From whence come wars and fightings</em> — Quarrels and wars among you, quite opposite to this peace? Is it not from your pleasures - Your desires of earthly pleasures.</p>
<p><em>Which war</em> — Against your souls.</p>
<p><em>In your members</em> — Here is the first seat of the war. Hence proceeds the war of man with man, king with king, nation with nation.</p>
