<p><em>Speak not evil one of another</em> — This is a grand hinderance of peace. O who is sufficiently aware of it! He that speaketh evil of another does in effect speak evil of the law, which so strongly prohibits it.</p>
<p><em>Thou art not a doer of the law, but a judge</em> — Of it; thou settest thyself above, and as it were condemnest, it.</p>
