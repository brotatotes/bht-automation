<p><em>Ye have killed the just</em> — Many just men; in particular, "that Just One," <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="ac+3:14">Acts 3:14</span>. They afterwards killed James, surnamed the Just, the writer of this epistle.</p>
<p><em>He doth not resist you</em> — And therefore you are secure. But the Lord cometh quickly, <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="jas+5:8">James 5:8</span>.</p>
