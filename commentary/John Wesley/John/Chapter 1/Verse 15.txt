<p><em>John cried</em> — With joy and confidence; This is he of whom I said - John had said this before our Lord's baptism, although he then knew him not in person: he knew him first at his baptism, and afterward cried, This is he of whom I said. etc.</p>
<p><em>He is preferred before me</em> — in his office: for he was before me - in his nature.</p>
