<p><em>In him was life</em> — He was the foundation of life to every living thing, as well as of being to all that is.</p>
<p><em>And the life was the light of men</em> — He who is essential life, and the giver of life to all that liveth, was also the light of men; the fountain of wisdom, holiness, and happiness, to man in his original state.</p>
