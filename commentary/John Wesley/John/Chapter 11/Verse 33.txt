<p><em>He groaned</em> — So he restrained his tears. So he stopped them soon after, <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="joh+11:38">John 11:38</span>.</p>
<p><em>He troubled himself</em> — An expression amazingly elegant, and full of the highest propriety. For the affections of Jesus were not properly passions, but voluntary emotions, which were wholly in his own power. And this tender trouble which he now voluntarily sustained, was full of the highest order and reason.</p>
