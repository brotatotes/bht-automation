<p><em>These came to Philip of Bethsaida in Galilee</em> — Perhaps they used to lodge there, in their journey to Jerusalem. Or they might believe, a Galilean would be more ready to serve them herein, than a Jew.</p>
<p><em>Sir</em> — They spake to him, as to one they were little acquainted with.</p>
<p><em>We would see Jesus</em> — A modest request. They could scarce expect that he would now have time to talk with them.</p>
