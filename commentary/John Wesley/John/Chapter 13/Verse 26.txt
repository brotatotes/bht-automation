<p><em>Jesus answered</em> — In his ear. So careful was he not to offend (if it had been possible) even Judas himself.</p>
<p><em>The sop</em> — Which he took up while he was speaking.</p>
<p><em>He giveth it to Judas</em> — And probably the other disciples thought Judas peculiarly happy! But when even this instance of our Lord's tenderness could not move him, then Satan took full possession.</p>
