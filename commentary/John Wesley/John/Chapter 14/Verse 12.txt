<p><em>Greater works than these shall he do</em> — So one apostle wrought miracles merely by his shadow, <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="ac+5:15">Acts 5:15</span>; another by handkerchiefs carried from his body, <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="ac+19:12">Acts 19:12</span>; and all spake with various tongues. But the converting one sinner is a greater work than all these.</p>
<p><em>Because I go to my Father</em> — To send you the Holy Ghost.</p>
