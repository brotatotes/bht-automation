<p><em>And I will ask the Father</em> — The21st verse, <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="joh+14:21">John 14:21</span>, shows the connection between this and the preceding verses.</p>
<p><em>And he will give you another Comforter</em> — The Greek word signifies also an advocate, instructer, or encourager.</p>
<p><em>Another</em> — For Christ himself was one.</p>
<p><em>To remain with you for ever</em> — With you, and your followers in faith, to the end of the world.</p>
