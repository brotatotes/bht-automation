<p><em>Thou knowest all things</em> — Even our hearts. Although no question is asked thee, yet thou answerest the thoughts of every one.</p>
<p><em>By this we believe that thou camest forth from God</em> — They, as it were, echo back the words which he had spoken in <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="joh+16:27">John 16:27</span>, implying, We believe in God; we believe also in thee.</p>
