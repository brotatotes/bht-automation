<p><em>I have manifested thy name</em> — All thy attributes; and in particular thy paternal relation to believers; to the men whom thou hast given me - The apostles, and so <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="joh+17:12">John 17:12</span>.</p>
<p><em>They were thine</em> — By creation, and by descent from Abraham.</p>
<p><em>And thou hast given them me</em> — By giving them faith in what I have spoken. So <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="joh+17:9">John 17:9</span>.</p>
