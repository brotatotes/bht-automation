<p><em>Thou sayest</em> — The truth.</p>
<p><em>To this end was I born</em> — Speaking of his human origin: his Divine was above Pilate's comprehension. Yet it is intimated in the following words, I came into the world, that I might witness to the truth - Which was both declared to the Jews, and in the process of his passion to the princes of the Gentiles also.</p>
<p><em>Every one that is of the truth</em> — That is, a lover of it, heareth my voice - A universal maxim. Every sincere lover of truth will hear him, so as to understand and practise what he saith.</p>
