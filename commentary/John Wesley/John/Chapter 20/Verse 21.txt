<p><em>Peace be unto you</em> — This is the foundation of the mission of a true Gospel minister, peace in his own soul, <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="2co+4:1">2 Corinthians 4:1</span>.</p>
<p><em>As the Father hath sent me, so send I you</em> — Christ was the apostle of the Father, <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="heb+3:1">Hebrews 3:1</span>. Peter and the rest, the apostles of Christ.</p>
