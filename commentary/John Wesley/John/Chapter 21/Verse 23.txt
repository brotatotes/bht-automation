<p><em>The brethren</em> — That is, the Christians. Our Lord himself taught them that appellation, <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="joh+20:17">John 20:17</span>.</p>
<p><em>Yet Jesus did not say to him, that he should not die</em> — Not expressly. And St. John himself, at the time of writing his Gospel, seems not to have known clearly, whether he should die or not.</p>
