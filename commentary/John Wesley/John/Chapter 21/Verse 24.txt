<p><em>This is the disciple who testifieth</em> — Being still alive after he had wrote.</p>
<p><em>And we know that his testimony is true</em> — The Church added these words to St. John's, Gospel, as Tertius did those to St. Paul's Epistle to the Romans, <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="ro+16:22">Romans 16:22</span>.</p>
