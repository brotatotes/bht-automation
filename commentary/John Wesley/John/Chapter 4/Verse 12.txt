<p><em>Our father Jacob</em> — So they fancied he was; whereas they were, in truth, a mixture of many nations, placed there by the king of Assyria, in the room of the Israelites whom he had carried away captive, <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="2ki+17:24">2 Kings 17:24</span>.</p>
<p><em>Who gave us the well</em> — In Joseph their supposed forefather: and drank thereof - So even he had no better water than this.</p>
