<p><em>His disciples marvelled that he talked with a woman</em> — Which the Jewish rabbis reckoned scandalous for a man of distinction to do. They marvelled likewise at his talking with a woman of that nation, which was so peculiarly hateful to the Jews.</p>
<p><em>Yet none said</em> — To the woman, What seekest thou? - Or to Christ, Why talkest thou with her?</p>
