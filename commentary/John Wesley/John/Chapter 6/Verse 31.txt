<p><em>Our fathers ate manna</em> — This sign Moses gave them.</p>
<p><em>He gave them bread from heaven</em> — From the lower sublunary heaven; to which Jesus opposes the highest heaven: in which sense he says seven times, <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="joh+6:32,33,38,50,58,62">John 6:32,33,38,50,58,62</span>, that he himself came down from heaven.</p>
