<p><em>It is the Spirit</em> — The spiritual meaning of these words, by which God giveth life.</p>
<p><em>The flesh</em> — The bare, carnal, literal meaning, profiteth nothing.</p>
<p><em>The words which I have spoken, they are spirit</em> — Are to be taken in a spiritual sense and, when they are so understood, they are life - That is, a means of spiritual life to the hearers.</p>
