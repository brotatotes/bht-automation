<p><em>Jesus answered the</em> — And yet even ye have not all acted suitable to this knowledge.</p>
<p><em>Have I not chosen or elected you twelve?</em> — But they might fall even from that election.</p>
<p><em>Yet one of you</em> — On this gracious warning, Judas ought to have repented; is a devil - Is now influenced by one.</p>
