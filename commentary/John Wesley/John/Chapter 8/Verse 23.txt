<p><em>Ye are</em> — Again he passes over their interruption, and proves what he advanced, <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="joh+8:21">John 8:21</span>.</p>
<p><em>Of them that are beneath</em> — From the earth.</p>
<p><em>I am of them that are above</em> — Here he directly shows whence he came, even from heaven, and whither he goes.</p>
