<p><em>If a man keep my word</em> — So will my Father consult my glory. We keep his doctrine by believing, his promises by hoping, his command by obeying.</p>
<p><em>He shall never see death</em> — That is, death eternal. He shall live for ever. Hereby he proves that he was no Samaritan; for the Samaritans in general were Sadducees.</p>
