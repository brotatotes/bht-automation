<p><em>The man answered</em> — Utterly illiterate as he was. And with what strength and clearness of reason! So had God opened the eyes of his understanding, as well as his bodily eyes.</p>
<p><em>Why, herein is a marvellous thing, that ye</em> — The teachers and guides of the people, should not know, that a man who has wrought a miracle, the like of which was never heard of before, must be from heaven, sent by God.</p>
