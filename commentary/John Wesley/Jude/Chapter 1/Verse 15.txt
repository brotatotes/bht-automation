<p><em>To execute judgment</em> — Enoch herein looked beyond the flood.</p>
<p><em>Upon all</em> — Sinners, in general. And to convict all the ungodly, in particular, of all the grievous things which ungodly sinners (a sinner is bad; but the ungodly who sin without fear are worse) have spoken against him, <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="jude+1:8,10">Jude 1:8,10</span>, though they might not think, all those speeches were against him.</p>
