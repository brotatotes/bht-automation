<p><em>These are murmurers</em> — Against men.</p>
<p><em>Complainers</em> — Literally, complainers of their fate, against God.</p>
<p><em>Walking</em> — With regard to themselves. After their own foolish and mischievous desires. Having men's persons in admiration for the sake of gain - Admiring and commending them only for what they can get.</p>
