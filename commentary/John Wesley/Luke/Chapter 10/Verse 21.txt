<p><em>Lord of heaven and earth</em> — In both of which thy kingdom stands, and that of Satan is destroyed.</p>
<p><em>That thou hast hid these things</em> — He rejoiced not in the destruction of the wise and prudent, but in the display of the riches of God's grace to others, in such a manner as reserves to Him the entire glory of our salvation, and hides pride from man. <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="mt+11:25">Matthew 11:25</span>.</p>
