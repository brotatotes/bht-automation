<p><em>And he said to the people also</em> — In the preceding verses he speaks only to his disciples.</p>
<p><em>From the west</em> — In Judea, the west wind, blowing from the sea, usually brought rain: the south wind, blowing from the deserts of Arabia, occasioned sultry heat. <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="mt+16:2">Matthew 16:2</span>.</p>
