<p><em>They all began to make excuse</em> — One of them pleads only his own will, I go: another, a pretended necessity, I must needs go: the third, impossibility, I cannot come: all of them want the holy hatred mentioned <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="lu+14:26">Luke 14:26</span>. All of them perish by things in themselves lawful.</p>
<p><em>I must needs go</em> — The most urgent worldly affairs frequently fall out just at the time when God makes the freest offers of salvation.</p>
