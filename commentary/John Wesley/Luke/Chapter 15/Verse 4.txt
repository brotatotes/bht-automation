<p><em>Leave the ninety and nine in the wilderness</em> — Where they used to feed: all uncultivated ground, like our commons, was by the Jews termed wilderness or desert.</p>
<p><em>And go after</em> — In recovering a lost soul, God as it were labours. May we not learn hence, that to let them alone who are in sin, is both unchristian and inhuman! <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="mt+18:12">Matthew 18:12</span>.</p>
