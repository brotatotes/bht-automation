<p><em>Fourscore and four years</em> — These were the years of her life, not her widowhood only.</p>
<p><em>Who departed not from the temple</em> — Who attended there at all the stated hours of prayer.</p>
<p><em>But served God with fastings and prayers</em> — Even at that age.</p>
<p><em>Night and day</em> — That is, spending therein a considerable part of the night, as well as of the day.</p>
