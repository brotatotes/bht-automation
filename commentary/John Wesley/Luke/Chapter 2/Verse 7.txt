<p><em>She laid him in the manger</em> — Perhaps it might rather be translated in the stall. They were lodged in the ox stall, fitted up on occasion of the great concourse, for poor guests.</p>
<p><em>There was no room for them in the inn</em> — Now also, there is seldom room for Christ in an inn. <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="mt+1:25">Matthew 1:25</span></p>
