<p><em>And he said</em> — On the day of his ascension.</p>
<p><em>In the law, and the prophets, and the psalms</em> — The prophecies as well as types, relating to the Messiah, are contained either in the books of Moses (usually called the law) in the Psalm, or in the writings of the prophets; little being said directly concerning him in the historical books.</p>
