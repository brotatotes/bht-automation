<p><em>To him that smiteth thee on the cheek</em> — Taketh away thy cloak - These seem to be proverbial expressions, to signify an invasion of the tenderest points of honour and property.</p>
<p><em>Offer the other</em> — Forbid not thy coat - That is, rather yield to his repeating the affront or injury, than gratify resentment in righting your self; in any method not becoming Christian love. <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="mt+5:39">Matthew 5:39</span>.</p>
