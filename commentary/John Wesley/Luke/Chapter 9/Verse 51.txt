<p><em>The days are fulfilled that he should be received up</em> — That is, the time of his passion was now at hand. St. Luke looks through this, to the glory which was to follow.</p>
<p><em>He steadfastly set his face</em> — Without fear of his enemies, or shame of the cross, <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="heb+12:2">Hebrews 12:2</span>.</p>
