<p><em>And he was there forty days, tempted by Satan</em> — Invisibly. After this followed the temptation by him in a visible shape, related by St. Matthew.</p>
<p><em>And he was with the wild beasts</em> — Though they had no power to hurt him. St. Mark not only gives us a compendium of St. Matthew's Gospel, but likewise several valuable particulars, which the other evangelists have omitted.</p>
