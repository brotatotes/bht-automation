<p><em>A young man</em> — It does not appear, that he was one of Christ's disciples. Probably hearing an unusual noise, he started up out of his bed, not far from the garden, and ran out with only the sheet about him, to see what was the matter.</p>
<p><em>And the young men laid hold on him</em> — Who was only suspected to be Christ's disciple: but could not touch them who really were so.</p>
