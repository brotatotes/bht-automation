<p><em>Take heed what ye hear</em> — That is, attend to what you hear, that it may have its due influence upon you.</p>
<p><em>With what measure you mete</em> — That is, according to the improvement you make of what you have heard, still farther assistance shall be given.</p>
<p><em>And to you that hear</em> — That is, with improvement.</p>
