<p><em>And preserved him</em> — Against all the malice and contrivances of Herodias.</p>
<p><em>And when he heard him</em> — Probably sending for him, at times, during his imprisonment, which continued a year and a half.</p>
<p><em>He heard him gladly</em> — Delusive joy! While Herodias lay in his bosom.</p>
