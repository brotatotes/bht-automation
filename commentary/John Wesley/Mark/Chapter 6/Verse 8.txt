<p><em>He commanded them to take nothing for their journey</em> — That they might be always unincumbered, free, ready for motion.</p>
<p><em>Save a staff only</em> — He that had one might take it; but he that had not was not to provide one, <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="mt+10:9">Matthew 10:9</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="lu+9:3">Luke 9:3</span>.</p>
