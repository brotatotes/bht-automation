<p><em>And when he called the people</em> — To hear a truth of the last importance, and one that equally concerned them all.</p>
<p><em>Let him deny himself</em> — His own will, in all things small and great, however pleasing, and that continually: And take up his cross - Embrace the will of God, however painful, daily, hourly, continually. Thus only can he follow me in holiness to glory.</p>
