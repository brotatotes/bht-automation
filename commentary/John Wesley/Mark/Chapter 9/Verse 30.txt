<p><em>They passed through Galilee</em> — Though not through the cities, but by them, in the most private ways.</p>
<p><em>He was not willing that any should know it: for he taught his disciples</em> — He wanted to be alone with them some time, in order to instruct them fully concerning his sufferings.</p>
<p><em>The Son of man is delivered</em> — It is as sure as if it were done already. <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="mt+17:22">Matthew 17:22</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="lu+9:44">Luke 9:44</span>.</p>
