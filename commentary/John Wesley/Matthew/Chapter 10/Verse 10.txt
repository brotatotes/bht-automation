<p><em>Neither scrip</em> — That is, a wallet, or bag to hold provisions: Nor yet a staff - We read, <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="mr+6:8">Mark 6:8</span>, Take nothing, save a staff only. He that had one might take it; they that had none, might not provide any.</p>
<p><em>For the workman is worthy of his maintenance</em> — The word includes all that is mentioned in the9th and10th verses; <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="mt+10:9,10">Matthew 10:9,10</span> all that they were forbidden to provide for themselves, so far as it was needful for them. <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="lu+10:7">Luke 10:7</span>.</p>
