<p><em>These twelve Jesus sent forth</em> — Herein exercising his supreme authority, as God over all. None but God can give men authority to preach his word.</p>
<p><em>Go not</em> — Their commission was thus confined now, because the calling of the Gentiles was deferred till after the more plentiful effusion of the Holy Ghost on the day of pentecost.</p>
<p><em>Enter not</em> — Not to preach; but they might to buy what they wanted, <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="joh+4:9">John 4:9</span>.</p>
