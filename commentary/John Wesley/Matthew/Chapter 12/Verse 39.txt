<p><em>An adulterous generation</em> — Whose heart wanders from God, though they profess him to be their husband. Such adulterers are all those who love the world, and all who seek the friendship of it.</p>
<p><em>Seeketh a sign</em> — After all they have had already, which were abundantly sufficient to convince them, had not their hearts been estranged from God, and consequently averse to the truth.</p>
<p><em>The sign of Jonah</em> — Who was herein a type of Christ.</p>
