<p><em>He proposed to them another parable</em> — The former parables relate chiefly to unfruitful hearers; these that follow, to those who bear good fruit.</p>
<p><em>The kingdom of heaven</em> — Both the Gospel dispensation, and the inward kingdom. <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="mr+4:30">Mark 4:30</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="lu+13:18">Luke 13:18</span>.</p>
