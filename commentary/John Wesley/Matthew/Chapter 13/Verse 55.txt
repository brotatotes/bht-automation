<p><em>The carpenter's son</em> — The Greek, word means, one that works either in wood, iron, or stone.</p>
<p><em>His brethren</em> — Our kinsmen. They were the sons of Mary, sister to the virgin, and wife of Cleophas or Alpheus.</p>
<p><em>James</em> — Styled by St. Paul also, the Lord's brother, <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="ga+1:19">Galatians 1:19</span>. Simon - Surnamed the Canaanite.</p>
