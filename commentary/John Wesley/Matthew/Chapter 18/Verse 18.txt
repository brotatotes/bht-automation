<p><em>Whatsoever ye shall bind on earth</em> — By excommunication, pronounced in the spirit and power of Christ.</p>
<p><em>Whatsoever ye shall loose</em> — By absolution from that sentence. In the primitive Church, absolution meant no more than a discharge from Church censure.</p>
