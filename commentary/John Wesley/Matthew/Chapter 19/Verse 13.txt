<p><em>That he should lay his hands on them</em> — This was a rite which was very early used, in praying for a blessing on young persons. See <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="ge+48:14,20">Genesis 48:14,20</span>.</p>
<p><em>The disciples rebuked them</em> — That is, them that brought them: probably thinking such an employ beneath the dignity of their Master. <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="mr+10:13">Mark 10:13</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="lu+18:15">Luke 18:15</span>.</p>
