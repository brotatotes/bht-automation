<p><em>He cast out all that sold and bought</em> — Doves and oxen for sacrifice. He had cast them out three years before, <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="joh+2:14">John 2:14</span>; bidding them not make that house a house of merchandise. Upon the repetition of the offence, he used sharper words.</p>
<p><em>In the temple</em> — That is, in the outer court of it, where the Gentiles used to worship.</p>
<p><em>The money changers</em> — The exchangers of foreign money into current coin, which those who came from distant parts might want to offer for the service of the temple. <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="mr+11:11,15">Mark 11:11,15</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="lu+19:45">Luke 19:45</span>.</p>
