<p><em>The builders</em> — The scribes and priests, whose office it was to build up the Church.</p>
<p><em>Is become the head of the corner</em> — Or the chief corner stone: he is become the foundation of the Church, on which the whole building rests, and is the principal corner stone, for uniting the Gentiles to it, as the chief corner stone of a house supports and links its two sides together. <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="ps+118:22">Psalm 118:22</span>.</p>
