<p><em>The Lord said to my Lord</em> — This his dominion, to which David himself was subject, shows both the heavenly majesty of the king, and the nature of his kingdom.</p>
<p><em>Sit thou on my right hand</em> — That is, remain in the highest authority and power. <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="ps+110:1">Psalm 110:1</span>.</p>
