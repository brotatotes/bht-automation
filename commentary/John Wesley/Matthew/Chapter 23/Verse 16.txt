<p><em>Wo to you, ye blind guides</em> — Before he had styled them hypocrites, from their personal character: now he gives them another title, respecting their influence upon others. Both these appellations are severely put together in the23d and <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="mt+23:23,25">Matthew 23:23,25</span>25th verses; and this severity rises to the height in the33d verse.</p>
<p><em>The gold of the temple</em> — The treasure kept there.</p>
<p><em>He is bound</em> — To keep his oath.</p>
