<p><em>When ye see the abomination of desolation</em> — Daniel's term is, The abomination that maketh desolate, <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="da+11:31">Daniel 11:31</span>; that is, the standards of the desolating legions, on which they bear the abominable images of their idols: Standing in the holy place - Not only the temple and the mountain on which it stood, but the whole city of Jerusalem, and several furlongs of land round about it, were accounted holy; particularly the mount on which our Lord now sat, and on which the Romans afterward planted their ensigns.</p>
<p><em>He that readeth let him understand</em> — Whoever reads that prophecy of Daniel, let him deeply consider it. <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="mr+13:14">Mark 13:14</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="lu+21:20">Luke 21:20</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="da+9:27">Daniel 9:27</span>.</p>
