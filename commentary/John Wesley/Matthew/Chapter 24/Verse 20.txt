<p><em>Pray ye that your flight be not in the winter</em> — They did so; and their flight was in the spring.</p>
<p><em>Neither on the Sabbath</em> — Being on many accounts inconvenient; beside that many would have scrupled to travel far on that day. For the Jews thought it unlawful to walk above two thousand paces (two miles) on the Sabbath day.</p>
