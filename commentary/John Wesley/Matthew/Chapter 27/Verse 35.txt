<p><em>They parted his garments</em> — This was the custom of the Romans. The soldiers performed the office of executioners, and divided among them the spoils of the criminals.</p>
<p><em>My vesture</em> — That is, my inner garment. <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="ps+22:18">Psalm 22:18</span>.</p>
