<p><em>John had his raiment of camels' hair</em> — Coarse and rough, suiting his character and doctrine.</p>
<p><em>A leathern girdle</em> — Like Elijah, in whose spirit and power he came.</p>
<p><em>His food was locusts and wild honey</em> — Locusts are ranked among clean meats, <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="le+11:22">Leviticus 11:22</span>. But these were not always to be had. So in default of those, he fed on wild honey.</p>
