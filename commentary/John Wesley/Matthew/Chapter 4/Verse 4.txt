<p><em>It is written</em> — Thus Christ answered, and thus we may answer all the suggestions of the devil.</p>
<p><em>By every word that proceedeth out of the mouth of God</em> — That is, by whatever God commands to sustain him. Therefore it is not needful I should work a miracle to procure bread, without any intimation of my Father's will. <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="de+8:3">Deuteronomy 8:3</span>.</p>
