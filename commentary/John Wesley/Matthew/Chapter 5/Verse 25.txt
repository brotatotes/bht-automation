<p><em>Agree with thine adversary</em> — With any against whom thou hast thus offended: while thou art in the way - Instantly, on the spot; before you part.</p>
<p><em>Lest the adversary deliver thee to the judge</em> — Lest he commit his cause to God. <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="lu+12:58">Luke 12:58</span>.</p>
