<p>Our Lord here refers to the promise made to the pure in heart of seeing God in all things, and points out a false doctrine of the scribes, which arose from their not thus seeing God. What he forbids is, the swearing at all, 1, by any creature, 2, in our ordinary conversation: both of which the scribes and Pharisees taught to be perfectly innocent. <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="ex+20:7">Exodus 20:7</span>.</p>
