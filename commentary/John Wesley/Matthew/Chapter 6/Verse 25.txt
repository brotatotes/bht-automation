<p>And if you serve God, you need be careful for nothing.</p>
<p><em>Therefore take not thought</em> — That is, be not anxiously careful. Beware of worldly cares; for these are as inconsistent with the true service of God as worldly desires.</p>
<p><em>Is not the life more than meat?</em> — And if God give the greater gift, will he deny the smaller? <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="lu+12:22">Luke 12:22</span>.</p>
