<p><em>The strait gate</em> — The holiness described in the foregoing chapters. And this is the narrow way.</p>
<p><em>Wide is the gate, and many there are that go in through it</em> — They need not seek for this; they come to it of course.</p>
<p><em>Many go in through it, because strait is the other gate</em> — Therefore they do not care for it; they like a wider gate. <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="lu+13:24">Luke 13:24</span>.</p>
