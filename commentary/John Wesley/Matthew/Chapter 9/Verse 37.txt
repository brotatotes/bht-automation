<p><em>The harvest truly is great</em> — When Christ came into the world, it was properly the time of harvest; till then it was the seed time only.</p>
<p><em>But the labourers are few</em> — Those whom God sends; who are holy, and convert sinners. Of others there are many. <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="lu+10:2">Luke 10:2</span>.</p>
