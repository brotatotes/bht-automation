<p><em>He saw a man named Matthew</em> — Modestly so called by himself. The other evangelists call him by his more honourable name, Levi.</p>
<p><em>Sitting</em> — In the very height of his business, at the receipt of custom - The custom house, or place where the customs were received. <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="mr+2:14">Mark 2:14</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="lu+5:27">Luke 5:27</span>.</p>
