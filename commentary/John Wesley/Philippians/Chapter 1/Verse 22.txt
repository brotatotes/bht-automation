<p>Here he begins to treat of the former clause of the preceding verse. Of the latter he treats, <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="php+2:17">Philippians 2:17</span>.</p>
<p><em>But if I am to live is the flesh, this is the fruit of my labour</em> — This is the fruit of my living longer, that I can labour more. Glorious labour! desirable fruit! in this view, long life is indeed a blessing.</p>
<p><em>And what I should choose I know not</em> — That is, if it were left to my choice.</p>
