<p><em>Some indeed preach Christ out of contention</em> — Envying St. Paul's success, and striving to hurt him thereby.</p>
<p><em>Not sincerely</em> — From a real desire to glorify God.</p>
<p><em>But supposing</em> — Though they were disappointed. To add more affliction to my bonds - By enraging the Romans against me.</p>
