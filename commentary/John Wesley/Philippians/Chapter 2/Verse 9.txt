<p><em>Wherefore</em> — Because of his voluntary humiliation and obedience. He humbled himself; but God hath exalted him - So recompensing his humiliation.</p>
<p><em>And hath given him</em> — So recompensing his emptying himself.</p>
<p><em>A name which is above every name</em> — Dignity and majesty superior to every creature.</p>
