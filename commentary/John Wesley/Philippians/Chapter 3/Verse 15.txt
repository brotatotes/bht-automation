<p><em>Let us, as many as are perfect</em> — Fit for the race, strong in faith; so it means here.</p>
<p><em>Be thus minded</em> — Apply wholly to this one thing.</p>
<p><em>And if in anything ye</em> — Who are not perfect, who are weak in faith.</p>
<p><em>Be otherwise minded</em> — Pursuing other things. God, if ye desire it, shall reveal even this unto you - Will convince you of it.</p>
