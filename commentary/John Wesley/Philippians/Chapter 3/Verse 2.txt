<p><em>Beware of dogs</em> — Unclean, unholy, rapacious men. The title which the Jews usually gave the gentiles, he returns upon themselves.</p>
<p><em>The concision</em> — Circumcision being now ceased, the apostle will not call them the circumcision, but coins a term on purpose, taken from a Greek word used by the LXX, <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="le+21:5">Leviticus 21:5</span>, for such a cutting as God had forbidden.</p>
