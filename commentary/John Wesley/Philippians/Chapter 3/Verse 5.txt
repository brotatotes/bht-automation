<p><em>Circumcised the eighth day</em> — Not at ripe age, as a proselyte.</p>
<p><em>Of the tribe of Benjamin</em> — Sprung from the wife, not the handmaid.</p>
<p><em>An Hebrew of Hebrews</em> — By both my parents; in everything, nation, religion, language.</p>
<p><em>Touching the law, a pharisee</em> — One of that sect who most accurately observe it.</p>
