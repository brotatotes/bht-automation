<p><em>I know how to be abased</em> — Having scarce what is needful for my body.</p>
<p><em>And to abound</em> — Having wherewith to relieve others also. Presently after, the order of the words is inverted, to intimate his frequent transition from scarcity to plenty, and from plenty to scarcity.</p>
<p><em>I am instructed</em> — Literally, I am initiated in that mystery, unknown to all but Christians.</p>
<p><em>Both to be full and to be hungry</em> — For one day.</p>
<p><em>Both to abound and to want</em> — For a longer season.</p>
