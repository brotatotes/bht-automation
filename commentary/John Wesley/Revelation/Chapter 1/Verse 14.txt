<p>His head and his hair - That is, the hair of his head, not his whole head.</p>
<p><em>Were white as white wool</em> — Like the Ancient of Days, represented in Daniel's vision, <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="da+7:9">Daniel 7:9</span>. Wool is commonly supposed to be an emblem of eternity.</p>
<p><em>As snow</em> — Betokening his spotless purity.</p>
<p><em>And his eyes as a flame of fire</em> — Piercing through all things; a token of his omniscience.</p>
