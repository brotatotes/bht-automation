<p><em>Who hath testified</em> — In the following book.</p>
<p><em>The word of God</em> — Given directly by God.</p>
<p><em>And the testimony of Jesus</em> — Which he hath left us, as the faithful and true witness.</p>
<p><em>Whatsoever things he saw</em> — In such a manner as was a full confirmation of the divine original of this book.</p>
