<p><em>And he cried</em> — Uttering the words set down, verse6. <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="re+10:6">Revelation 10:6</span> And while he cried, or was crying - At the same instant.</p>
<p><em>Seven thunders uttered their voices</em> — In distinct words, each after the other. Those who spoke these words were glorious, heavenly powers, whose voice was as the loudest thunder.</p>
