<p><em>Eat it up</em> — The like was commanded to Ezekiel. This was an emblem of thoroughly considering and digesting it.</p>
<p><em>And it will make thy belly bitter, but it will be sweet as honey in thy mouth</em> — The sweetness betokens the many good things which follow, <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="re+11:1,15">Revelation 11:1,15</span>, etc.; the bitterness, the evils which succeed under the third woe.</p>
