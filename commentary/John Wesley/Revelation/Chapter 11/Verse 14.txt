<p><em>The second woe is past</em> — The butchery made by the Saracens ceased about the year847, when their power was so broken by Charles the Great that they never recovered it.</p>
<p><em>Behold, the third woe cometh quickly</em> — Its prelude came while the Roman see took all opportunities of laying claim to its beloved universality, and enlarging its power and grandeur. And in the year755 the bishop of Rome became a secular prince, by king Pepin's giving him the exarchate of Lombardy. The beginning of the third woe itself stands, <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="re+12:12">Revelation 12:12</span>.</p>
