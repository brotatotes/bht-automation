<p><em>And he prevailed not</em> — The dragon himself is principally mentioned; but his angels, likewise, are to be understood.</p>
<p><em>Neither was this place found any more in heaven</em> — So till now he had a place in heaven. How deep a mystery is this! One may compare this with <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="lu+10:18">Luke 10:18</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="eph+2:2">Ephesians 2:2</span>; 4:8; 6:12.</p>
