<p><em>And the winepress was trodden</em> — By the Son of God, <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="re+19:15">Revelation 19:15</span>.</p>
<p><em>Without the city</em> — Jerusalem. They to whom St. John writes, when a man said, "The city," immediately understood this.</p>
<p><em>And blood came out of the winepress, even to the horses' bridles</em> — So deep at its first flowing from the winepress! One thousand six hundred furlongs - So far! at least two hundred miles, through the whole land of Palestine.</p>
