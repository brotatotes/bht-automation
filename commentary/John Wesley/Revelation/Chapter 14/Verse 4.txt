<p><em>These are they who had not been defiled with women</em> — It seems that the deepest defilement, and the most alluring temptation, is put for every other.</p>
<p><em>They are virgins</em> — Unspotted souls; such as have preserved universal purity.</p>
<p><em>These are they who follow the Lamb</em> — Who are nearest to him. This is not their character, but their reward Firstfruits - Of the glorified spirits. Who is ambitious to be of this number?</p>
