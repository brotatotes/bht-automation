<p><em>And one of the four living creatures gave the seven angels</em> — After they were come out of the temple.</p>
<p><em>Seven golden phials</em> — Or bowls. The Greek word signifies vessels broader at the top than at the bottom.</p>
<p><em>Full of the wrath of God, who liveth for ever and ever</em> — A circumstance which adds greatly to the dreadfulness of his wrath.</p>
