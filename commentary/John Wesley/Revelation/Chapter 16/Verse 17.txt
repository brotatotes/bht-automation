<p><em>And the seventh poured out his phial upon the air</em> — Which encompasses the whole earth. This is the most weighty phial of all, and seems to take up more time than any of the preceding.</p>
<p><em>It is done</em> — What was commanded, verse1. <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="re+16:1">Revelation 16:1</span> The phials are poured out.</p>
