<p><em>And the great city</em> — Namely, Jerusalem, here opposed to the heathen cities in general, and in particular to Rome.</p>
<p><em>And the cities of the nations fell</em> — Were utterly overthrown.</p>
<p><em>And Babylon was remembered before God</em> — He did not forget the vengeance which was due to her, though the execution of it was delayed.</p>
