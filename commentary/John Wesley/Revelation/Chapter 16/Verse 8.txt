<p><em>The fourth poured out his phial upon the sun</em> — Which was likewise affected by the fourth trumpet. There is also a plain resemblance between the first, second, and third phials, and the first, second, and third trumpet.</p>
<p><em>And it was given him</em> — The angel.</p>
<p><em>To scorch the men</em> — Who had the mark of the beast.</p>
<p><em>With fire</em> — As well as with the beams of the sun. So these four phials affected earth, water, fire, and air.</p>
