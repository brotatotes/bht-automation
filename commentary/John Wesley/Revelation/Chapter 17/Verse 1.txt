<p><em>And there came one of the seven angels, saying, Come hither</em> — This relation concerning the great whore, and that concerning the wife of the Lamb, <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="re+21:9,10">Revelation 21:9,10</span>, have the same introduction, in token of the exact opposition between them.</p>
<p><em>I will show thee the judgment of the great whore</em> — Which is now circumstantially described.</p>
<p><em>That sitteth as a queen</em> — In pomp, power, ease, and luxury.</p>
<p><em>Upon many waters</em> — Many people and nations, verse15. <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="re+17:15">Revelation 17:15</span></p>
