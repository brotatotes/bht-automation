<p><em>With whom the kings of the earth</em> — Both ancient and modern, for many ages.</p>
<p><em>Have committed fornication</em> — By partaking of her idolatry and various wickedness.</p>
<p><em>And the inhabitants of the earth</em> — The common people.</p>
<p><em>Have been made drunk with the wine of her fornication</em> — No wine can more thoroughly intoxicate those who drink it, than false zeal does the followers of the great whore.</p>
