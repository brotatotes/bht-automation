<p><em>And the woman was arrayed</em> — With the utmost pomp and magnificence.</p>
<p><em>In purple and scarlet</em> — These were the colours of the imperial habit: the purple, in times of peace; and the scarlet, in times of war.</p>
<p><em>Having in her hand a golden cup</em> — Like the ancient Babylon, <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="jer+51:7">Jeremiah 51:7</span>.</p>
<p><em>Full of abominations</em> — The most abominable doctrines as well as practices.</p>
