<p><em>And his eyes are a flame of fire</em> — They were said to be as or like a flame of fire, before, <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="re+1:14">Revelation 1:14</span>; an emblem of his omniscience.</p>
<p><em>And upon his head are many diadems</em> — For he is king of all nations.</p>
<p><em>And he hath a name written, which none knoweth but himself</em> — As God he is incomprehensible to every creature.</p>
