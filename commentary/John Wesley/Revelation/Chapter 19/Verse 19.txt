<p><em>And I saw the kings of the earth</em> — The ten kings mentioned <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="re+17:12">Revelation 17:12</span>; who had now drawn the other kings of the earth to them, whether Popish, Mahometan, or pagan.</p>
<p><em>Gathered together to make war with him that sat upon the horse</em> — All beings, good and evil, visible and invisible, will be concerned in this grand contest. See <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="zec+14:1">Zechariah 14:1</span>, etc.</p>
