<p><em>But thou hast there</em> — Whom thou oughtest to have immediately cast out from the flock.</p>
<p><em>Them that hold the doctrine of Balaam</em> — Doctrine nearly resembling his.</p>
<p><em>Who taught Balak</em> — And the rest of the Moabites.</p>
<p><em>To cast a stumblingblock before the sons of Israel</em> — They are generally termed, the children, but here, the sons, of Israel, in opposition to the daughters of Moab, by whom Balaam enticed them to fornication and idolatry.</p>
<p><em>To eat things sacrificed to idols</em> — Which, in so idolatrous a city as Pergamos, was in the highest degree hurtful to Christianity.</p>
<p><em>And to commit fornication</em> — Which was constantly joined with the idol-worship of the heathens.</p>
