<p><em>And to the angel of the church at Thyatira</em> — Where the faithful were but a little flock.</p>
<p><em>These things saith the Son of God</em> — See how great he is, who appeared "like a son of man!" <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="re+1:13">Revelation 1:13</span>.</p>
<p><em>Who hath eyes as a flame of fire</em> — "Searching the reins and the heart," verse23. <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="re+2:23">Revelation 2:23</span> And feet like fine brass - Denoting his immense strength. Job comprises both these, his wisdom to discern whatever is amiss, and his power to avenge it, in one sentence, <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="job+42:2">Job 42:2</span>, "No thought is hidden from him, and he can do all things."</p>
