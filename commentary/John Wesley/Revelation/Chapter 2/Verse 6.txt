<p><em>But thou hast this</em> — Divine grace seeks whatever may help him that is fallen to recover his standing.</p>
<p><em>That thou hatest the works of the Nicolaitans</em> — Probably so called from Nicolas, one of the seven deacons, <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="ac+6:5">Acts 6:5</span>. Their doctrines and lives were equally corrupt. They allowed the most abominable lewdness and adulteries, as well as sacrificing to idols; all which they placed among things indifferent, and pleaded for as branches of Christian liberty.</p>
