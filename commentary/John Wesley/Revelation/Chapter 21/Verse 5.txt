<p><em>And he that sat upon the throne said</em> — Not to St. John only. From the first mention of "him that sat upon the throne," <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="re+4:2">Revelation 4:2</span>, this is the first speech which is expressly ascribed to him.</p>
<p><em>And he</em> — The angel.</p>
<p><em>Saith to me Write</em> — As follows.</p>
<p><em>These sayings are faithful and true</em> — This includes all that went before. The apostle seems again to have ceased writing, being overcome with ecstasy at the voice of him that spake.</p>
