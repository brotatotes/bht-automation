<p><em>And there came one of the seven angels that had the seven phials</em> — Whereby room had been made for the kingdom of God.</p>
<p><em>Saying, Come, I will show thee the bride</em> — The same angel had before showed him Babylon, <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="re+17:1">Revelation 17:1</span>, which is directly opposed to the new Jerusalem.</p>
