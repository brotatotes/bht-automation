<p><em>And he showed me a river of the water of life</em> — The ever fresh and fruitful effluence of the Holy Ghost. See <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="eze+47:1-12">Ezekiel 47:1-12</span>; where also the trees are mentioned which "bear fruit every month," that is, perpetually.</p>
<p><em>Proceeding out of the throne of God, and of the Lamb</em> — "All that the Father hath," saith the Son of God, "is mine;" even the throne of his glory.</p>
