<p><em>To the angel of the church at Laodicea</em> — For these St. Paul had had a great concern, <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="col+2:1">Colossians 2:1</span>.</p>
<p><em>These things saith the Amen</em> — That is, the True One, the God of truth.</p>
<p><em>The beginning</em> — The Author, Prince, and Ruler.</p>
<p><em>Of the creation of God</em> — Of all creatures; the beginning, or Author, by whom God made them all.</p>
