<p><em>I counsel thee</em> — who art poor, and blind, and naked.</p>
<p><em>To buy of me</em> — Without money or price.</p>
<p><em>Gold purified in the fire</em> — True, living faith, which is purified in the furnace of affliction.</p>
<p><em>And white raiment</em> — True holiness.</p>
<p><em>And eyesalve</em> — Spiritual illumination; the "unction of the Holy One," which teacheth all things.</p>
