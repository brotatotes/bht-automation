<p><em>Behold, I</em> — who have all power; and they must then comply.</p>
<p><em>I will make them come and bow down before thy feet</em> — Pay thee the lowest homage.</p>
<p><em>And know</em> — At length, that all depends on my love, and that thou hast a place therein. O how often does the judgment of the people turn quite round, when the Lord looketh upon them! <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="job+42:7">Job 42:7</span>, etc.</p>
