<p><em>Worthy art thou to receive</em> — This he receives not only when he is thus praised, but also when he destroys his enemies and glorifies himself anew.</p>
<p><em>The glory and the honour and the power</em> — Answering the thrice-holy of the living creatures, verse9. <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="re+4:9">Revelation 4:9</span> For thou hast created all things - Creation is the ground of all the works of God: therefore, for this, as well as for his other works, will he be praised to all eternity.</p>
<p><em>And through thy will they were</em> — They began to be. It is to the free, gracious and powerfully-working will of Him who cannot possibly need anything that all things owe their first existence.</p>
<p><em>And are created</em> — That is, continue in being ever since they were created.</p>
