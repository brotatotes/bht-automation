<p><em>And I saw</em> — The many angels.</p>
<p><em>And heard</em> — The voice and the number of them.</p>
<p><em>Round about the elders</em> — So forming the third circle. It is remarkable, that men are represented through this whole vision as nearer to God than any of the angels.</p>
<p><em>And the number of them was</em> — At least two hundred millions, and two millions over. And yet these were but a part of the holy angels. Afterward, <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="re+7:11">Revelation 7:11</span>, St. John heard them all.</p>
