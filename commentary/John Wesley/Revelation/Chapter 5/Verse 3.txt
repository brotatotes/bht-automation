<p><em>And none</em> — No creature; no, not Mary herself.</p>
<p><em>In heaven, or in earth, neither under the earth</em> — That is, none in the universe. For these are the three great regions into which the whole creation is divided.</p>
<p><em>Was able to open the book</em> — To declare the counsels of God.</p>
<p><em>Nor to look thereon</em> — So as to understand any part of it.</p>
