<p><em>And he came</em> — Here was "Ask of me," <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="ps+2:8">Psalm 2:8</span>, fulfilled in the most glorious manner.</p>
<p><em>And took</em> — it is one state of exaltation that reaches from our Lord's ascension to his coming in glory. Yet this state admits of various degrees. At his ascension, "angels, and principalities, and powers were subjected to him." Ten days after, he received from the Father and sent the Holy Ghost. And now he took the book out of the right hand of him that sat upon the throne - who gave it him as a signal of his delivering to him all power in heaven and earth. He received it, in token of his being both able and willing to fulfil all that was written therein.</p>
