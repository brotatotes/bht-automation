<p><em>And all the angels stood</em> — In waiting.</p>
<p><em>Round about the throne, and the elders and the four living creatures</em> — That is, the living creatures, next the throne; the elders, round these; and the angels, round them both.</p>
<p><em>And they fell on their faces</em> — So do the elders, once only, <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="re+11:16">Revelation 11:16</span>. The heavenly ceremonial has its fixed order and measure.</p>
