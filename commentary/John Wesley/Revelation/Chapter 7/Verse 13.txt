<p><em>And one of the elders</em> — What stands, verses13-17, <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="re+7:13-17">Revelation 7:13-17</span> might have immediately followed the tenth verse; but that the praise of the angels, which was at the same time with that of the "great multitude," came in between.</p>
<p><em>Answered</em> — He answered St. John's desire to know, not any words that he spoke.</p>
