<p><em>And the name of the star is called Wormwood</em> — The unparalleled bitterness both of Arius himself and of his followers show the exact propriety of his title.</p>
<p><em>And the third part of the waters became wormwood</em> — A very considerable part of Afric was infected with the same bitter doctrine and Spirit. And many men (though not a third part of them) died - By the cruelty of the Arians.</p>
