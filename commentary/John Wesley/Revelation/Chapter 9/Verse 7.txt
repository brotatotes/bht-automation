<p><em>And the appearances</em> — This description suits a people neither throughly civilized, nor entirely savage; and such were the Persians of that age.</p>
<p><em>Of the locusts are like horses</em> — With their riders. The Persians excelled in horsemanship.</p>
<p><em>And on their heads are as it were crowns</em> — Turbans.</p>
<p><em>And their faces are as the faces of men</em> — Friendly and agreeable.</p>
