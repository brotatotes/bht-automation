<p><em>To the Greeks and the barbarians</em> — He includes the Romans under the Greeks; so that this division comprises all nations.</p>
<p><em>Both to the wise, and the unwise</em> — For there were unwise even among the Greeks, and wise even among the barbarians.</p>
<p><em>I am a debtor to all</em> — I am bound by my divine mission to preach the gospel to them.</p>
