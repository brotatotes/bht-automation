<p><em>And changed</em> — With the utmost folly. Here are three degrees of ungodliness and of punishment: the first is described, <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="ro+1:21-24">Romans 1:21-24</span>; the second, <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="ro+1:25-27">Romans 1:25-27</span>; the third, in <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="ro+1:28">Romans 1:28</span>, and following verses. The punishment in each case is expressed by God gave them up. If a man will not worship God as God, he is so left to himself that he throws away his very manhood.</p>
<p><em>Reptiles</em> — Or creeping things; as beetles, and various kinds of serpents.</p>
