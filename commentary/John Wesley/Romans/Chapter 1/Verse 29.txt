<p><em>Filled with all injustice</em> — This stands in the first place; unmercifulness, in the last.</p>
<p><em>Fornication</em> — Includes here every species of uncleanness.</p>
<p><em>Maliciousness</em> — The Greek word properly implies a temper which delights in hurting another, even without any advantage to itself.</p>
