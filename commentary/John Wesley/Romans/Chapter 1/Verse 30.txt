<p><em>Whisperers</em> — Such as secretly defame others.</p>
<p><em>Backbiters</em> — Such as speak against others behind their back.</p>
<p><em>Haters of God</em> — That is, rebels against him, deniers of his providence, or accusers of his justice in their adversities; yea, having an inward heart-enmity to his justice and holiness.</p>
<p><em>Inventors of evil things</em> — Of new pleasures, new ways of gain, new arts of hurting, particularly in war.</p>
