<p><em>By whom we have received</em> — I and the other apostles.</p>
<p><em>Grace and apostleship</em> — The favour to be an apostle, and qualifications for it.</p>
<p><em>For obedience to the faith in all nations</em> — That is, that all nations may embrace the faith of Christ.</p>
<p><em>For his name</em> — For his sake; out of regard to him.</p>
