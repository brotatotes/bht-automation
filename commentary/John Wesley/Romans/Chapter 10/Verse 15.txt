<p><em>But how shall they preach, unless they be sent</em> — Thus by a chain of reasoning, from God's will that the gentiles also should "call upon him," St. Paul infers that the apostles were sent by God to preach to the gentiles also.</p>
<p><em>The feet</em> — Their very footsteps; their coming. <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="isa+52:7">Isaiah 52:7</span>.</p>
