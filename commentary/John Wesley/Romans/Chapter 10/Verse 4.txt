<p><em>For Christ is the end of the law</em> — The scope and aim of it. It is the very design of the law, to bring men to believe in Christ for justification and salvation. And he alone gives that pardon and life which the law shows the want of, but cannot give.</p>
<p><em>To every one</em> — Whether Jew or gentile, treated of, <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="ro+10:11">Romans 10:11</span>, etc.</p>
<p><em>That believeth</em> — Treated of, <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="ro+10:5">Romans 10:5</span>.</p>
