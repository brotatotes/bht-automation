<p>And this will surely come to pass.</p>
<p><em>For if the first fruits be holy, so is the lump</em> — The consecration of them was esteemed the consecration of all and so the conversion of a few Jews is an earnest of the conversion of all the rest.</p>
<p><em>And if the root be holy</em> — The patriarchs from whom they spring, surely God will at length make their descendants also holy.</p>
