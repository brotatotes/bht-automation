<p><em>And so all Israel shall be saved</em> — Being convinced by the coming of the gentiles. But there will be a still larger harvest among the gentiles, when all Israel is come in.</p>
<p><em>The deliverer shall come</em> — Yea, the deliverer is come; but not the full fruit of his coming. <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="isa+59:20">Isaiah 59:20</span></p>
