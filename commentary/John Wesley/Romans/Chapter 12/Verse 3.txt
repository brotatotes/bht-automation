<p><em>And I say</em> — He now proceeds to show what that will of God is.</p>
<p><em>Through the grace which is given to me</em> — He modestly adds this, lest he should seem to forget his own direction.</p>
<p><em>To every one that is among you</em> — Believers at Rome. Happy, had they always remembered this! The measure of faith - Treated of in the first and following chapters, from which all other gifts and graces flow.</p>
