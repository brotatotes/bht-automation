<p>From our duty to magistrates he passes on to general duties.</p>
<p><em>To love one another</em> — An eternal debt, which can never be sufficiently discharged; but yet if this be rightly performed, it discharges all the rest.</p>
<p><em>For he that loveth another</em> — As he ought.</p>
<p><em>Hath fulfilled the whole law</em> — Toward his neighbour.</p>
