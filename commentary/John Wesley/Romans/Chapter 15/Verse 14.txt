<p>There are several conclusions of this Epistle. The first begins at this verse; the second, <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="ro+16:1">Romans 16:1</span>; the third, <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="ro+16:17">Romans 16:17</span>; the fourth, <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="ro+16:21">Romans 16:21</span>; and the fifth, <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="ro+16:25">Romans 16:25</span>; Ye are full of goodness - By being created anew. And filled with all knowledge - By long experience of the things of God. To admonish - To instruct and confirm.</p>
