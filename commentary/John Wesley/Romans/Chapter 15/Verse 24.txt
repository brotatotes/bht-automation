<p><em>Into Spain</em> — Where the gospel had not yet been preached.</p>
<p><em>If first I may be somewhat satisfied with your company</em> — How remarkable is the modesty with which he speaks! They might rather desire to be satisfied with his.</p>
<p><em>Somewhat satisfied</em> — Intimating the shortness of his stay; or, perhaps, that Christ alone can throughly satisfy the soul.</p>
