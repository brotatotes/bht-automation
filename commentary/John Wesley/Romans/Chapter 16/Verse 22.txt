<p><em>I Tertius, who wrote this epistle, salute you</em> — Tertius, who wrote what the apostle dictated, inserted this, either by St. Paul's exhortation or ready permission.</p>
<p><em>Caius</em> — The Corinthian, <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="1co+1:14">1 Corinthians 1:14</span>.</p>
<p><em>My host, and of the whole church</em> — Who probably met for some time in his house.</p>
