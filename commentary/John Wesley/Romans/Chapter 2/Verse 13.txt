<p>For not the hearers of the law are, even now, just before God, but the doers of the law shall be justified - Finally acquitted and rewarded a most sure and important truth, which respects the gentiles also, though principally the Jews. St. Paul speaks of the former, <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="ro+2:14">Romans 2:14</span>, etc.; of the latter, <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="ro+2:17">Romans 2:17</span>, etc. Here is therefore no parenthesis; for the sixteenth verse also depends on the fifteenth, not on the twelfth. <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="ro+2:16,15,12">Romans 2:16,15,12</span>.</p>
