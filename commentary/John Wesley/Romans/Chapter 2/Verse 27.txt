<p><em>Yea, the uncircumcision that is by nature</em> — Those who are, literally speaking, uncircumcised.</p>
<p><em>Fulfilling the law</em> — As to the substance of it.</p>
<p><em>Shall judge thee</em> — Shall condemn thee in that day.</p>
<p><em>Who by the letter and circumcision</em> — Who having the bare, literal, external circumcision, transgressest the law.</p>
