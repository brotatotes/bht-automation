<p><em>But he is a Jew</em> — That is, one of God's people.</p>
<p><em>Who is one inwardly</em> — In the secret recesses of his soul. And the acceptable circumcision is that of the heart - Referring to <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="de+30:6">Deuteronomy 30:6</span>; the putting away all inward impurity. This is seated in the spirit, the inmost soul, renewed by the Spirit of God.</p>
<p><em>And not in the letter</em> — Not in the external ceremony.</p>
<p><em>Whose praise is not from men, but from God</em> — The only searcher of the heart.</p>
