<p><em>Or despisest thou</em> — Dost thou go farther still, - from hoping to escape his wrath, to the abuse of his love?.</p>
<p><em>The riches</em> — The abundance.</p>
<p><em>Of his goodness, forbearance, and longsuffering</em> — Seeing thou both hast sinned, dost sin, and wilt sin. All these are afterwards comprised in the single word goodness. Leadeth thee - That is, is designed of God to lead or encourage thee to it.</p>
