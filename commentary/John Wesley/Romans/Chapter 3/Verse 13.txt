<p><em>Their throat</em> — Is noisome and dangerous as an open sepulchre. Observe the progress of evil discourse, proceeding out of the heart, through the throat, tongue, lips, till the whole mouth is filled therewith.</p>
<p><em>The poison of asps</em> — Infectious, deadly backbiting, tale-bearing, evil-speaking, is under (for honey is on) their lips. An asp is a venomous kind of serpent. <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="ps+5:9">Psalm 5:9</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="ps+140:3">Psalm 140:3</span>.</p>
