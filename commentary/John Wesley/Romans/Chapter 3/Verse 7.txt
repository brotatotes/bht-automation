<p>But, may the objector reply, if the truth of God hath abounded - Has been more abundantly shown.</p>
<p><em>Through my lie</em> — If my lie, that is, practice contrary to truth, conduces to the glory of God, by making his truth shine with superior advantage.</p>
<p><em>Why am I still judged as a sinner</em> — Can this be said to be any sin at all? Ought I not to do what would otherwise be evil, that so much "good may come?" To this the apostle does not deign to give a direct answer, but cuts the objector short with a severe reproof.</p>
