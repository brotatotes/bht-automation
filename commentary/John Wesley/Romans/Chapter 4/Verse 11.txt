<p><em>And</em> — After he was justified.</p>
<p><em>He received the sign of circumcision</em> — Circumcision, which was a sign or token of his being in covenant with God.</p>
<p><em>A seal</em> — An assurance on God's part, that he accounted him righteous, upon his believing, before he was circumcised.</p>
<p><em>Who believe in uncircumcision</em> — That is, though they are not circumcised.</p>
