<p><em>Abraham believed God</em> — That promise of God concerning the numerousness of his seed, <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="ge+15:5,7">Genesis 15:5,7</span>; but especially the promise concerning Christ, <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="ge+12:3">Genesis 12:3</span>, through whom all nations should be blessed.</p>
<p><em>And it was imputed to him for righteousness</em> — God accepted him as if he had been altogether righteous. <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="ge+15:6">Genesis 15:6</span>.</p>
