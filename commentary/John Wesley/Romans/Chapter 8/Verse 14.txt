<p><em>For as many as are led by the Spirit of God</em> — In all the ways of righteousness.</p>
<p><em>They are the sons of God</em> — Here St. Paul enters upon the description of those blessings which he comprises, <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="ro+8:30">Romans 8:30</span>, in the word glorified; though, indeed, he does not describe mere glory, but that which is still mingled with the cross. The sum is, through sufferings to glory.</p>
