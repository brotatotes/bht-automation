<p><em>The creation was made subject to vanity</em> — Abuse, misery, and corruption.</p>
<p><em>By him who subjected it</em> — Namely, God, <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="ge+3:17">Genesis 3:17</span>; 5:29. Adam only made it liable to the sentence which God pronounced; yet not without hope.</p>
