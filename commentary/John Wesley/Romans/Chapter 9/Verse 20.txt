<p><em>Nay, but who art thou, O man</em> — Little, impotent, ignorant man.</p>
<p><em>That repliest against God</em> — That accusest God of injustice, for himself fixing the terms on which he will show mercy? Shall the thing formed say to him that formed it, Why hast thou made me thus - Why hast thou made me capable of honour and immortality, only by believing?</p>
