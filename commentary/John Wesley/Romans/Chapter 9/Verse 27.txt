<p>But Isaiah testifies, that (as many gentiles will be accepted, so) many Jews will be rejected; that out of all the thousands of Israel, a remnant only shall be saved. This was spoken originally of the few that were saved from the ravage of Sennacherib's army. <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="isa+10:22,23">Isaiah 10:22,23</span></p>
