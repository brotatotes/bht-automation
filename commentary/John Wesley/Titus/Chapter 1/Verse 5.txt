<p><em>The things which are wanting</em> — Which I had not time to settle myself.</p>
<p><em>Ordain elders</em> — Appoint the most faithful, zealous men to watch over the rest. Their character follows, <span class="scriptRef" com="wen" lang="eng" ref="tit+1:6-9">Titus 1:6-9</span>. These were the elders, or bishops, that Paul approved of;-men that had living faith, a pure conscience, a blameless life.</p>
