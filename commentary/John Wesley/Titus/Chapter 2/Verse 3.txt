<p><em>In behaviour</em> — The particulars whereof follow.</p>
<p><em>As becometh holiness</em> — Literally, observing an holy decorum. Not slanderers - Or evil-speakers.</p>
<p><em>Not given to much wine</em> — If they use a little for their often infirmities.</p>
<p><em>Teachers</em> — Age and experience call them so to be. Let them teach good only.</p>
