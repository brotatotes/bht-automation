<p><em>Discreet</em> — Particularly in the love of their children.</p>
<p><em>Chaste</em> — Particularly in the love of their husbands.</p>
<p><em>Keepers at home</em> — Whenever they are not called out by works of necessity, piety, and mercy.</p>
<p><em>Good</em> — Well tempered, sweet, soft, obliging.</p>
<p><em>Obedient to their husbands</em> — Whose will, in all things lawful, is a rule to the wife.</p>
<p><em>That the word of God be not blasphemed</em> — Or evil spoken of; particularly by unbelieving husbands, who lay all the blame on the religion of their wives.</p>
