<p>l had baptized [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εβαπτισα] </span></span>. The correct reading is ejbaptisqhte ye were baptized. So Rev. Paul 's commission contains no mention of baptism. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+9:15">Acts 9:15</span>, with <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+28:15">Matthew 28:15</span>. From his peculiar position as the inaugurator of a second epoch of Christianity, many would be tempted to regard him as the real founder of the Church, and to boast of having been baptized into his name. "No outward initiation of converts entered into his ministry" (Edwards).</p>
