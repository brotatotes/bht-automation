<p>Should be made of none effect [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κενωθη] </span></span>. Lit., emptied. Rev., made void. Compare is made void, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ro+4:14">Romans 4:14</span>, and the kindred adjective kenon, kenh vain, ch. 14 14. The nucleus of the apostolic preaching was a fact - Christ crucified. To preach it as a philosophic system would be to empty it of its saving power, a truth which finds abundant and lamentable illustration in the history of the Church.</p>
