<p>The word of the cross [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ο λογος ο του σταυρου] </span></span>. Lit., the word, that, namely, of the cross. The second article is definitive and emphatic. The word of which the substance and purport is the cross. </p>
<p>To them that perish [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τοις απολλυμενοις] </span></span>. Lit., that are perishing. So Rev. The present participle denotes process : they who are on the way to destruction. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="2co+2:15">2 Corinthians 2:15</span>. </p>
<p>Foolishness [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μωρια] </span></span>. Only in this epistle. See on have lost his savor, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+5:13">Matthew 5:13</span>. </p>
<p>Which are saved [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τοις σωζομενοις] </span></span>. Rev., being saved : in process of salvation.</p>
