I thank [ευχαριστω] . Found in the Gospels, Acts, and Revelation, but most frequently in Paul. My God. Some very high authorities omit. The pronoun implies close personal relationship. Compare Acts 27:23; Philippians 1:3; Philippians 3:8. By Christ Jesus [εν] . Better, as Rev., in; in fellowship with. The element or sphere in which the grace is manifested.
