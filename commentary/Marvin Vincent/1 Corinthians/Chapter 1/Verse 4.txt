<p>I thank [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ευχαριστω] </span></span>. Found in the Gospels, Acts, and Revelation, but most frequently in Paul. </p>
<p>My God. Some very high authorities omit. The pronoun implies close personal relationship. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+27:23">Acts 27:23</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="php+1:3">Philippians 1:3</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="php+3:8">Philippians 3:8</span>. By Christ Jesus [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εν] </span></span>. Better, as Rev., in; in fellowship with. The element or sphere in which the grace is manifested.</p>
