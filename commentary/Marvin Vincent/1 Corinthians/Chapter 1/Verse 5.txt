<p>Ye are enriched [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επλουτισθητε] </span></span>. Rev. more literally, "were enriched." Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="col+3:16">Colossians 3:16</span>; and see on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ro+2:4">Romans 2:4</span>. </p>
<p>Utterance - knowledge [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">λογω - γνωσει] </span></span>. The two words are found together, ch. 12 8; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="2co+11:6">2 Corinthians 11:6</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="2co+8:7">2 Corinthians 8:7</span>. For knowledge, see on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ro+11:33">Romans 11:33</span>. Utterance, aptitude in speech. Paul gives thanks for speech as a means of testifying for Christ. "The saints have never been silent" (Pascal).</p>
