Ye are enriched [επλουτισθητε] . Rev. more literally, "were enriched." Compare Colossians 3:16; and see on Romans 2:4. Utterance - knowledge [λογω - γνωσει] . The two words are found together, ch. 12 8; 2 Corinthians 11:6; 2 Corinthians 8:7. For knowledge, see on Romans 11:33. Utterance, aptitude in speech. Paul gives thanks for speech as a means of testifying for Christ. "The saints have never been silent" (Pascal).
