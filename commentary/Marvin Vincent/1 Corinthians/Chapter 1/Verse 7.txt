<p>Come behind [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">υστερεισθαι] </span></span>. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="lu+14:14">Luke 14:14</span>, and compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ro+3:23">Romans 3:23</span>. Contrast with were enriched. </p>
<p>Gift [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">χαρισματι] </span></span>. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ro+1:11">Romans 1:11</span>. Its prevailing sense in this epistle is that of special spiritual endowments, such as tongues, prophecy, etc. Here of spiritual blessings generally. </p>
<p>Waiting [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">απεκδεχομενους] </span></span>. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ro+8:19">Romans 8:19</span>. Denoting assiduous waiting.
