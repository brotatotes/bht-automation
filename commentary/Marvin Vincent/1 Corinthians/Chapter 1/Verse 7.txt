Come behind [υστερεισθαι] . See on Luke 14:14, and compare Romans 3:23. Contrast with were enriched. Gift [χαρισματι] . See on Romans 1:11. Its prevailing sense in this epistle is that of special spiritual endowments, such as tongues, prophecy, etc. Here of spiritual blessings generally. Waiting [απεκδεχομενους] . See on Romans 8:19. Denoting assiduous waiting. Dr. Thayer compares the phrase wait it out [εκ] . Revelation [αποκαλυψιν] . See on Revelation 1:1.
