Confirm. Compare ver. 6. Unto the end. Of the present aeon or period. See on end of the world, Matthew 28:20. Blameless [ανεγκλητους] . Used by Paul only. In apposition with you. Rev., unreprovable. The kindred verb ejgkalew occurs only in Acts and Romans. See on Romans 8:33. It means to accuse publicly, but not necessarily before a tribunal. See Acts 23:28, Acts 23:29; Acts 26:2, Acts 26:7. Hence the word here points to appearance at God 's bar.
