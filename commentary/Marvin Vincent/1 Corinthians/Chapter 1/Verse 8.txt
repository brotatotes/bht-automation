<p>Confirm. Compare ver. 6. </p>
<p>Unto the end. Of the present aeon or period. See on end of the world, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+28:20">Matthew 28:20</span>. </p>
<p>Blameless [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ανεγκλητους] </span></span>. Used by Paul only. In apposition with you. Rev., unreprovable. The kindred verb ejgkalew occurs only in Acts and Romans. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ro+8:33">Romans 8:33</span>. It means to accuse publicly, but not necessarily before a tribunal. See <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+23:28">Acts 23:28</span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+23:29">Acts 23:29</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+26:2">Acts 26:2</span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+26:7">Acts 26:7</span>. Hence the word here points to appearance at God 's bar.</p>
