<p>Moreover [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δε] </span></span>. But the correct reading is gar for, introducing an illustration of rejection by God, and thus connecting what follows with the close of the last chapter. It is possible that I may be rejected, for the Israelites were. </p>
<p>All. Strongly emphasized in contrast with most of them (A. V., many) in ver. 5. All enjoyed the privileges, but few improved them. The word is repeated five times. </p>
<p>Under the cloud. The cloudy pillar which guided the Israelites. It is sometimes spoken of as covering the host. See <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ps+105:39">Psalms 105:39</span>; Wisdom 10 17; 19 7; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="nu+14:14">Numbers 14:14</span>.</p>
