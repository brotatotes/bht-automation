<p>Baptized unto Moses [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εις] </span></span>. Rev., margin, into. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+28:19">Matthew 28:19</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ro+6:3">Romans 6:3</span>. They were introduced into a spiritual union with Moses, and constituted his disciples. </p>
<p>Cloud - sea. The two together forming the type of the water of baptism. Bengel says : "The cloud and the sea are in their nature water." The cloud was diffused and suspended water.</p>
