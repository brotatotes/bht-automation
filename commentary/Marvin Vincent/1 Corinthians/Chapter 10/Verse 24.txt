<p>Another's wealth [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">το του ετερου] </span></span>. Lit., that which is the other's. Wealth, inserted by A. V. is used in the older English sense of well - being. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+19:25">Acts 19:25</span>. The A. V. also ignores the force of the article, the other. Rev., much better, his neighbor 's good.</p>
