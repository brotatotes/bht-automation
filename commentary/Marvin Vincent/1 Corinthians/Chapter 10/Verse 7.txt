<p>Idolaters. Referring to the danger of partaking of the idol feasts. To play [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">παιζειν] </span></span>. The merrymaking generally which followed the feast, not specially referring to the dancing at the worship of the golden calf. See <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ex+32:19">Exodus 32:19</span>. </p>
<p>Commit fornication. Lasciviousness was habitually associated with idol - worship. The two are combined, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+14:29">Acts 14:29</span>. A thousand priests ministered at the licentious rites of the temple of Venus at Corinth. 109 Three and twenty thousand. A plain discrepancy between this statement and <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="nu+25:9">Numbers 25:9</span>, where the number is twenty - four thousand. It may have been a lapse of memory.</p>
