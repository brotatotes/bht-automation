<p>In the church [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εν εκκλησια] </span></span>. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+16:18">Matthew 16:18</span>. Not the church edifice, a meaning which the word never has in the New Testament, and which appears first in patristic writings. The marginal rendering of the Rev. is better : in congregation.</p>
