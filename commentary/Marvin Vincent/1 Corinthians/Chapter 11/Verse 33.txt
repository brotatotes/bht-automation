<p>Tarry [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εκδεχεσθε] </span></span>. In the usual New - Testament sense, as <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="joh+5:3">John 5:3</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+17:16">Acts 17:16</span>; though in some cases the idea of expectancy is emphasized, as <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="heb+10:13">Hebrews 10:13</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="heb+11:10">Hebrews 11:10</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="jas+5:7">James 5:7</span>. Some render receive ye one another, in contrast with despising the poorer guests; but this is not according to New - Testament usage.</p>
