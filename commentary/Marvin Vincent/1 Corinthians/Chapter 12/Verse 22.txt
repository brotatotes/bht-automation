<p>Seem to be [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δοκουντα - υπαρχειν] </span></span>. The allusion is probably to those which seem to be weaker in their original structure, naturally. This may be indicated by the use of uJparcein to be (see on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="jas+2:15">James 2:15</span>); compare einai to be, in ver. 23. Others explain of those which on occasion seem to be weaker, as when a member is diseased.</p>
