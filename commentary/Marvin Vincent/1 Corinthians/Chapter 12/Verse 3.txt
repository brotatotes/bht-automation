<p>Calleth Jesus accursed [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">λεγει αναθεμα ιησους] </span></span>. Lit., saith Anathema Jesus. Rev., preserving the formula, saith Jesus is Anathema. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+18:6">Acts 18:6</span>, and see on offerings, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="lu+21:5">Luke 21:5</span>. Paul uses only the form ajnaqema, and always in the sense of accursed.</p>
