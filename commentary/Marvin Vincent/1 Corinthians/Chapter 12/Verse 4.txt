<p>Diversities [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">διαιρεσεις] </span></span>. Only here in the New Testament. It may also be rendered distributions. There is no objection to combining both meanings, a distribution of gifts implying a diversity. Ver. 11, however, seems to favor distributions. </p>
<p>Gifts [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">χαρισματων] </span></span>. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ro+1:11">Romans 1:11</span>. </p>
<p>Administrations [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">διακονιων] </span></span>. Rev., better, ministrations. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="eph+4:12">Ephesians 4:12</span>. In the New Testament commonly of spiritual service of an official character. See <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+1:25">Acts 1:25</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+6:4">Acts 6:4</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+20:24">Acts 20:24</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ro+11:13">Romans 11:13</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1ti+1:12">1 Timothy 1:12</span>; and on minister, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+20:26">Matthew 20:26</span>.</p>
