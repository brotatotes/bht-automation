Operations [ενεργηματα] . Rev., workings. Outward manifestations and results of spiritual gifts. The kindred word ejnergeia energy is used only by Paul : and only of superhuman good or evil. Compare Ephesians 1:19; Ephesians 3:7; Colossians 2:12. See on Mark 6:14. Worketh [ενεργων] . Etymologically akin to operations. See on Mark 6:14; James 5:16. All [τα παντα] . Or them all. The article shows that they are regarded collectively.
