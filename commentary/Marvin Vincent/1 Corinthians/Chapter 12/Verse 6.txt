<p>Operations [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ενεργηματα] </span></span>. Rev., workings. Outward manifestations and results of spiritual gifts. The kindred word ejnergeia energy is used only by Paul : and only of superhuman good or evil. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="eph+1:19">Ephesians 1:19</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="eph+3:7">Ephesians 3:7</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="col+2:12">Colossians 2:12</span>. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mr+6:14">Mark 6:14</span>. </p>
<p>Worketh [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ενεργων] </span></span>. Etymologically akin to operations. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mr+6:14">Mark 6:14</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="jas+5:16">James 5:16</span>. </p>
<p>All [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τα παντα] </span></span>. Or them all. The article shows that they are regarded collectively.</p>
