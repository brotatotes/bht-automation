<p>And now [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">νυνι δε] </span></span>. Rev., but; better than and, bringing out the contrast with the transient gifts. Now is logical and not temporal. Thus, as it is. </p>
<p>Abideth. Not merely in this life. The essential permanence of the three graces is asserted. In their nature they are eternal. </p>
