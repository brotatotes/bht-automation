Convinced [ελεγχεται] . Of his sins. See on tell him his fault, Matthew 18:15; convinced, James 2:9; reproved, John 3:20. Rev., reproved : convicted in margin. Judged [ανακρινεται] . Examined and judged. The word implies inquiry rather than sentence. Each inspired speaker, in his heart - searching utterances, shall start questions which shall reveal the hearer to himself. See on discerned, ch. 2 14. On the compounds of krinw, see on ch. 1 Corinthians 11:29, 1 Corinthians 11:31, 1 Corinthians 11:32.
