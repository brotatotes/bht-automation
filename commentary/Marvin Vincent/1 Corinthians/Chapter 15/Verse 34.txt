<p>Awake [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εκνηψατε] </span></span>. Only here in the New Testament. It means to awake from a drunken stupor. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="joe+1:5">Joel 1:5</span>, Sept. The kindred verb ajnanhfw return to soberness (A. V. and Rev., recover), occurs at <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="2ti+2:26">2 Timothy 2:26</span>. </p>
<p>Have not the knowledge (ajgnwsian ecousin). Lit., have an ignorance. Stronger than ajgnoein to be ignorant. They have and hold it. For the form of expression, see on have sorrow, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="joh+16:29">John 16:29</span>. The word for ignorance is found only here and <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1pe+2:15">1 Peter 2:15</span> (see note).</p>
