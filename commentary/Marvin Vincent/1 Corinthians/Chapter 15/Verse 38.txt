<p>As it hath pleased [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">καθως ηθελησεν] </span></span>. Lit., even as He willed; at the creation, when He fixed the different types of grain, so that each should permanently assume a form according to its distinctive type - a body of its own : that wheat should always be wheat, barley barley, etc. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ge+1:11">Genesis 1:11</span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ge+1:12">Genesis 1:12</span>.</p>
