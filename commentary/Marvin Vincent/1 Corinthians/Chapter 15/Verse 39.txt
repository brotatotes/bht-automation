<p>All flesh is not the same flesh. Still arguing that it is conceivable that the resurrection - body should be organized differently from the earthly body, and in a way which cannot be inferred from the shape of the earthly body. There is a great variety of organization among bodies which we know : it may fairly be inferred that there may be a new and different organization in those which we do not know. Flesh is the body of the earthly, living being, including the bodily form. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ro+7:5">Romans 7:5</span>, sec. 3.</p>
