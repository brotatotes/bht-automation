<p>Put on [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ενδυσασθαι</span></span>] The metaphor of clothing. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="2co+5:2-4">2 Corinthians 5:2-4</span>. Incorruption and immortality are to invest the spiritually - embodied personality like a garment.</p>
