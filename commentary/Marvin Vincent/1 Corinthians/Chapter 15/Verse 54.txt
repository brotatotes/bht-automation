Is swallowed up [κατεποθη] . From Isaiah 25:8. The quotation agrees with the Hebrew : He shall swallow up death forever, rather than with the Septuagint, Death has prevailed and swallowed men up, which reverses the meaning of the Hebrew. Compare 2 Corinthians 5:4. In victory [εις νικος] . Lit., unto victory, so that victory is to be established.
