<p>Spirit [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πνευμα] </span></span>. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ro+8:4">Romans 8:4</span>. The things of God can be recognized only by the highest element of the human personality. They have not entered into the heart (kardia, see on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ro+1:21">Romans 1:21</span>), but into the spirit, which is the highest and principal point of contact with the Spirit of God.</p>
