The spirit of the world [το πνευμα του κοσμου] . For this use of pneuma, see on Romans 8:4, under 7. Kosmov world, is used with the ethical sense. See on John 1:9, under 4, e, The phrase means the principle of evil which animates the unregenerate world; not the personal spirit of evil or Satan, since Paul does not use pneuma spirit, elsewhere in the personal sense of an evil spirit. See note on Ephesians 2:2. Of God [εκ του θεου] . Lit., from God : proceeding forth from Him. "God in us reveals God in our nature" (Edwards).
