<p>The spirit of the world [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">το πνευμα του κοσμου] </span></span>. For this use of pneuma, see on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ro+8:4">Romans 8:4</span>, under 7. Kosmov world, is used with the ethical sense. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="joh+1:9">John 1:9</span>, under 4, e, The phrase means the principle of evil which animates the unregenerate world; not the personal spirit of evil or Satan, since Paul does not use pneuma spirit, elsewhere in the personal sense of an evil spirit. See note on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="eph+2:2">Ephesians 2:2</span>. </p>
<p>Of God [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εκ του θεου] </span></span>. Lit., from God : proceeding forth from Him. "God in us reveals God in our nature" (Edwards).</p>
