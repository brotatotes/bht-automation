<p>I was with you [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εγενομην προς υμας] </span></span>. I was is rather I became. I fell into a state of weakness, etc., after I had come among you. With you, i e., in intercourse with. See on with God, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="joh+1:1">John 1:1</span>. The implication is that his condition grew out of the circumstances in which he found himself in Corinth.</p>
