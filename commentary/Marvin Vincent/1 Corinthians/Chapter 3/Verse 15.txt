<p>Shall suffer loss [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ζημιωθησεται] </span></span>. He shall be mulcted, not punished. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+16:26">Matthew 16:26</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="lu+9:25">Luke 9:25</span>. </p>
<p>He himself shall be saved. Compare Dante of Constantine : "The next who follows, with the laws and me, Under the good intent that bore bad fruit Became a Greek by ceding to the pastor; Now knoweth he how all the ill deduced From his good action is not harmful to him, Although the world thereby may be destroyed." " Paradiso, " 20 55 - 60. </p>
<p>By fire [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δια πυρος] </span></span>. Better, Rev., through fire. He will escape as through the fire that consumes his work, as one does through the flames which destroy his house.</p>
