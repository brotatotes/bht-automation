Another [ετερος] . See on Matthew 6:24. Not merely another, numerically, but another of different affinities and prepossessions. Carnal. The best texts read anqrwpoi men. Are ye not mere men? But ministers. Omit but, and place the interrogations after Paul and Apollos, respectively, as Rev. For ministers see on Matthew 20:26; Mark 9:35. Servants, not heads of parties.
