<p>Another [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ετερος] </span></span>. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+6:24">Matthew 6:24</span>. Not merely another, numerically, but another of different affinities and prepossessions. </p>
<p>Carnal. The best texts read anqrwpoi men. Are ye not mere men? But ministers. Omit but, and place the interrogations after Paul and Apollos, respectively, as Rev. For ministers see on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+20:26">Matthew 20:26</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mr+9:35">Mark 9:35</span>. Servants, not heads of parties.</p>
