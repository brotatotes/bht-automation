<p>For Christ 's sake - in Christ [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δια χριστον - εν χριστω] </span></span>. We apostles are fools in the world 's eyes on account of [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δια</span></span>] Christ, because we know and preach nothing but Christ : You are wise in Christ, as Christians, making your Christianity a means to your worldly greatness - union with Christ the basis of worldly wisdom. "Wise men are ye in your connection with Christ ! Sagacious, enlightened Christians !" (Meyer). Honorable [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ενδοξοι] </span></span>. With a suggestion of display and splendor. Right honorable are ye!</p>
