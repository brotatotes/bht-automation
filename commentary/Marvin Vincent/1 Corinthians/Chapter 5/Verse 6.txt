<p>Glorying [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">καυχημα] </span></span>. Not the act, but the subject of boasting; namely, the condition of the Corinthian church. </p>
<p>Lump [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">φυραμα] </span></span>. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ro+12:21">Romans 12:21</span>. A significant term, suggesting the oneness of the Church, and the consequent danger from evil - doers.</p>
