Let us keep the feast [εορταζωμεν] . Only here in the New Testament. The epistle was probably written a short time before the Passover. See ch. 1 Corinthians 16:8. Sincerity [ειλικρινειας] . See on pure minds, 2 Peter 3:1. Truth. Bengel observes : "Sincerity takes care not to admit evil with the good; truth, not to admit evil instead of good."
