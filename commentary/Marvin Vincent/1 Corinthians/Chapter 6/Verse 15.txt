<p>Members of Christ. The body is not only for the Lord (ver. 13), adapted for Him : it is also united with Him. See <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="eph+4:16">Ephesians 4:16</span>. </p>
<p>Members of a harlot. The union of man and woman, whether lawful or unlawful, confers a double personality. Fornication effects this result in an immoral way.</p>
