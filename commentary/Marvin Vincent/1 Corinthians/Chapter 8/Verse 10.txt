<p>Idol 's temple [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ειδωλειω] </span></span>. Only here in the New Testament. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="re+2:14">Revelation 2:14</span>. </p>
<p>Be emboldened [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">οικοδομηθησεται] </span></span>. Lit., be built up. The A. V. misses the irony of the expression. His apparent advance is really detrimental. Calvin remarks : "a ruinous upbuilding."</p>
