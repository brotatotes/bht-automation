<p>That he knoweth anything [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εγνωκεναι τι] </span></span>. Or, literally, has come to know. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="joh+2:24">John 2:24</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="joh+3:10">John 3:10</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="joh+17:3">John 17:3</span>. Showing in what sense knowledge was used in the previous clause : fancied knowledge; knowledge of divine things without love.</p>
