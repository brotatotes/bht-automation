Altogether [παντως] . Better, as Rev., in margin, as He doubtless doth, or, as American Rev., assuredly. In hope [επ ελπιδι] . See on Romans 8:21. Resting on hope. Compare Aeschylus : "When hope has raised me up on strength [επ αλκας] ; i e. elated me with confidence" (" Choephoroe, " 407). He that thresheth in hope should be partaker of his hope. The text is in error here. The true reading is oJ ajlown ejp' ejlpidi tou metecein and; he that thresheth to thresh in hope of partaking.
