<p>Altogether [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">παντως] </span></span>. Better, as Rev., in margin, as He doubtless doth, or, as American Rev., assuredly. </p>
<p>In hope [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επ ελπιδι] </span></span>. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ro+8:21">Romans 8:21</span>. Resting on hope. Compare Aeschylus : "When hope has raised me up on strength [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επ αλκας] </span></span>; i e. elated me with confidence" (" Choephoroe, " 407). </p>
<p>He that thresheth in hope should be partaker of his hope. The text is in error here. The true reading is oJ ajlown ejp' ejlpidi tou metecein and; he that thresheth to thresh in hope of partaking.</p>
