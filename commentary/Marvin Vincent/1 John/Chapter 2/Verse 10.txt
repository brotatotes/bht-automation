Abideth [μενει] . See on ver. 6. Compare ver. 9, is in. Occasion of stumbling [σκανδαλον] . See on offend, Matthew 5:29. For the image in John, see John 6:61; John 11:9; John 16:1; Revelation 2:14. The meaning is not that he gives no occasion of stumbling to others, but that there is none in his own way. See John 11:9, John 11:10.
