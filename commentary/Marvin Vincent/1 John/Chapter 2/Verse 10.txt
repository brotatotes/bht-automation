<p>Occasion of stumbling [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">σκανδαλον] </span></span>. See on offend, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+5:29">Matthew 5:29</span>. For the image in John, see <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="joh+6:61">John 6:61</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="joh+11:9">John 11:9</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="joh+16:1">John 16:1</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="re+2:14">Revelation 2:14</span>.
