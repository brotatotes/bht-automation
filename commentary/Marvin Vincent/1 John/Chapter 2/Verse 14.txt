<p>Him that is from the beginning. The eternal, pre - existent Christ, who was from the beginning (<span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="joh+1:1">John 1:1</span>). The eternal Son, through whom men are brought into the relation of children of God, and learn to know the Father. The knowledge of God involves, on the part of both fathers and children, the knowledge of Christ. </p>
<p>Strong [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ισχυροι] </span></span>. See on was not able, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="lu+14:30">Luke 14:30</span>; I cannot, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="lu+16:3">Luke 16:3</span>.</p>
