Him that is from the beginning. The eternal, pre - existent Christ, who was from the beginning (John 1:1). The eternal Son, through whom men are brought into the relation of children of God, and learn to know the Father. The knowledge of God involves, on the part of both fathers and children, the knowledge of Christ. Strong [ισχυροι] . See on was not able, Luke 14:30; I cannot, Luke 16:3.
