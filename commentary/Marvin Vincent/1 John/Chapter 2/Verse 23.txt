<p>Hath not the Father [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ουδε τον πατερα εχει] </span></span>. Properly, "hath not even the Father," though he professes to reverence the Father while rejecting the Son. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="joh+8:42">John 8:42</span>.</p>
