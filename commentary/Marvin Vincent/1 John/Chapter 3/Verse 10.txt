Here the article is wanting, compare ver. 7. Righteousness is regarded, not in its completeness, but as bearing a particular character. It will be interesting to follow out the same distinction between the following words with and without the article : ajmartia sin; ajgaph love; zwh life; ajlhqeia truth.
