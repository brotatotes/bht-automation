<p>This world 's good [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τον βιον του κοσμου] </span></span>. Rev., the worlds goods. Biov means that by which life is sustained, resources, wealth. </p>
<p>Seeth [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">θεωρη] </span></span>. Deliberately contemplates. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="joh+1:18">John 1:18</span>. Rev., beholdeth. The only occurrence of the verb in John's Epistles. </p>
<p>Have need [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">χρειαν εχοντα] </span></span>. Lit., having need. Rev., in need. </p>
<p>Bowels of compassion [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τα σπλαγχνα] </span></span>. See on pitiful, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1pe+3:8">1 Peter 3:8</span>. Rev., much better, his compassion. The word only here in John.</p>
