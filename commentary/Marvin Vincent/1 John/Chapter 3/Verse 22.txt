We ask [αιτωμεν] . See on Luke 11:9. We receive of Him [λαμβανομεν απ αυτου] On the form of expression, see on 1 5. For the thought, compare John 14:7. We keep [τηρουμεν] . See on 1 Peter 1:5. Note the combination of keep and do. Watchful discernment and habitual practice. Compare Psalms 123:2. The same combination occurs 1 John 5:2, 1 John 5:3, where instead of the first thrwmen keep, read poiwmen do. Pleasing [αρεστα] . See John 8:29. In His sight [ενωπιον αυτου] . Compare emprosqen aujtou before Him, or in His presence (ver. 19). In His sight "accentuates the thought of the divine regard. Compare John 7:37 and John 20:30" (Westcott).
