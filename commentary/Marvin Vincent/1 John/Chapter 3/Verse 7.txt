<p>Little children. See on 2 1. </p>
<p>Deceive [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πλανατω] </span></span>. Rev., better, lead astray. See on 1 8. </p>
<p>Doeth righteousness. See on ver. 4, and compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1jo+2:29">1 John 2:29</span>. Note the article thn, the righteousness, in its completeness and unity. Not merely doing righteous acts. "In his relation to other men he will do what is just; and in his relation to the gods he will do what is holy; and he who does what is just and holy cannot be other than just and holy" (Plato, "Gorgias," 507).</p>
