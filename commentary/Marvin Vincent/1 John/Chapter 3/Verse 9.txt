Whosoever is born [πας ο γεγεννημενος] . On the form of expression, see on ver. 4. Rev., begotten. The perfect participle indicates a condition remaining from the first : he who hath been begotten and remains God 's child. His seed. The divine principle of life. Cannot. See on ver. 6. Conceived as a perfect ideal, life in God excludes the possibility of sin. Compare Romans 4:0 throughout.
