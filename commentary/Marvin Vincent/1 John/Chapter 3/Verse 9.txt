<p>Whosoever is born [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πας ο γεγεννημενος] </span></span>. On the form of expression, see on ver. 4. Rev., begotten. The perfect participle indicates a condition remaining from the first : he who hath been begotten and remains God 's child. </p>
<p>His seed. The divine principle of life. </p>
<p>Cannot. See on ver. 6. Conceived as a perfect ideal, life in God excludes the possibility of sin. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ro+4:0">Romans 4:0</span> throughout.</p>
