<p>God. Beginning the sentence emphatically, and without the article : God as God. "God hath no man ever yet seen." Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="joh+1:18">John 1:18</span>. </p>
<p>His love. Not our love to Him, nor His love to us, but the love which is peculiarly His; which answers to His nature.</p>
