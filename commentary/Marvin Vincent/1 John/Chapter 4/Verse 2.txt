Hereby [εν τουτω] . See on 2 3. Know ye [γινωσκετε] . Perceive. See on John 2:24. Confesseth [ομολογει] . See on Matthew 7:23; Matthew 10:32. That Jesus Christ is come in the flesh [ιησουν χριστον εν σαρκι εληλυθοτα] . Lit., Jesus Christ having come, etc. The whole phrase forms the direct object of the verb confesseth. Of God. Compare 1 Corinthians 12:3.
