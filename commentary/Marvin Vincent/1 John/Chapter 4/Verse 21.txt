<p>That [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ινα] </span></span>. Not defining the contents of the commandment, but expressing intent. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="joh+13:34">John 13:34</span>, and see on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="joh+14:13">John 14:13</span>. His brother. "To the persecutor Saul, Christ said, 'Saul, Saul, why persecutest thou Me? I have ascended into heaven, yet still I lie upon earth. Here I sit at the right hand of the Father; there I still hunger, thirst, and am a stranger '" (Augustine). </p>
