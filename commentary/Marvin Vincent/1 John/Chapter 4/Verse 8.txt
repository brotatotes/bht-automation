Knoweth not [ουκ εγνω] . The aroist tense : did not know, from the beginning. He never knew. Is love [αγαπη εστι. ν] . See on God is light (i. 5), and the truth (i. 6); also God is spirit (John 4:24). Spirit and light are expressions of God 's essential nature. Love is the expression of His personality corresponding to His nature. See on love of God (ii. 5). Truth and love stand related to each other. Loving is the condition of knowing.
