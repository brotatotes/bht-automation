<p>Knoweth not [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ουκ εγνω] </span></span>. The aroist tense : did not know, from the beginning. He never knew. </p>
<p>Is love [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αγαπη εστι. ν] </span></span>. See on God is light (i. 5), and the truth (i. 6); also God is spirit (<span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="joh+4:24">John 4:24</span>). Spirit and light are expressions of God 's essential nature. Love is the expression of His personality corresponding to His nature. See on love of God (ii. 5). Truth and love stand related to each other. Loving is the condition of knowing.</p>
