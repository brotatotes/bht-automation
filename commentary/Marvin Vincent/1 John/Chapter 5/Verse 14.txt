Confidence [παρρησια] . Rev., boldness. See on 2 28; John 7:13. On have boldness, see on John 16:22. We ask [αιτωμεθα] . With a possible reference in the middle voice to asking for ourselves. According to His will [κατα το θελημα αυτου] . For the phrase compare 1 Peter 4:19; Galatians 1:4; Ephesians 1:5, Ephesians 1:11. He heareth us [ακουει ημων] . Compare John 9:31; John 11:41, John 11:42. Hear is used in this sense by John only.
