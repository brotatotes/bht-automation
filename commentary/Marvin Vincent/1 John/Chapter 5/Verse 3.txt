<p>Grievous [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">βαρειαι] </span></span>. Lit., heavy. The word occurs six times in the New Testament. <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+20:29">Acts 20:29</span>, violent, rapacious; "grievous wolves" : <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="2co+10:10">2 Corinthians 10:10</span>, weighty, impressive, of Paul 's letters : <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+23:23">Matthew 23:23</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+25:7">Acts 25:7</span>, important, serious; the weightier matters of the law; serious charges against Paul.</p>
