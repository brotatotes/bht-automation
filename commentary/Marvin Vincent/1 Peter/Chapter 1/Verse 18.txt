Ye were redeemed [ελυτρωθητε] . The verb occurs only in two other passages, Luke 24:21; Titus 2:14. It carries the idea of a ransom - price (lutron, from luw, to loose). With silver or gold [αργυριω η χρυσιω] . Lit., with silver or gold money; the words meaning, respectively, a small coin of silver or of gold. Conversation. Rev., manner of life. See on ver. 15. Received by tradition from your fathers [πατροπαραδοτου] . A clumsy translation; improved by Rev., handed down from your fathers. The word is peculiar to Peter.
