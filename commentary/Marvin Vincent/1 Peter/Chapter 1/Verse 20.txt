<p>Foreordained [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">προεγνωσμενου] </span></span>. Lit., and better, foreknown, as Rev. Manifested [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">φανερωθεντος] </span></span>. Observe the difference in tense. </p>
<p>Foreknown is the perfect participle, has been known from all eternity down to the present : "in reference to the place held and continuing to be held by Christ in the divine mind" (Salmond). Manifested is the aorist participle, pointing to a definite act at a given time. </p>
<p>In these last times [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επ εσχατου των χρονων] </span></span>. Lit., as Rev., at the end of the times.</p>
