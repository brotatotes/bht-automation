<p>Using [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εχοντες] </span></span>. Lit., having or holding. </p>
<p>Cloke [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επικαλυμμα] </span></span>. Only here in New Testament. Lit., a veil. The idea is that of using Christian freedom as a mask for ungodly license. Paul uses the kindred verb (<span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ro+4:7">Romans 4:7</span>) of the covering of sins. On the sentiment, compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ga+5:13">Galatians 5:13</span>.</p>
