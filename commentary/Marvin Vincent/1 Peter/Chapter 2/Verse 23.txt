<p>Reviled - again [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αντελοιδορει] </span></span>. Only here in the New Testament. Committed himself [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">παρεδιδου] </span></span>. But his gives a reflexive force to the verb which has no parallel. Commentators are divided, some supplying his cause, as Rev., in margin; others, his judgment; other, his revilers. Better, the subject of the contest - his insults and injuries. Salmond renders, but left it to him, etc. </p>
<p>Judgeth righteously. Compare without respect of persons, ch. 1 17.</p>
