<p>Ye have tasted [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εγευσασθε] </span></span>. Aorist tense. More literally, ye tasted. "A taste excites the appetite" (Bengel). Compare long for, ver. 2, and <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ps+34:8">Psalms 34:8</span>. </p>
<p>Gracious [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">χρηστος] </span></span>. Actively benignant, "as distinguished from other adjectives which describe goodness on the side of its sterling worth and its gentleness" (Salmond). See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+11:30">Matthew 11:30</span>.</p>
