<p>He is precious [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">η τιμη] </span></span>. Wrong. Render, as Rev., For you therefore which believe is the preciousness (honor, in margin). </p>
<p>Is made the head of the corner [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εγενηθη εις κεφαλην γωνιας] </span></span>. Rev., correctly, "was made." The preposition eijv, unto, carrying the idea of coming unto the place of honor, is not rendered in A. V. or Rev. Lit., it would be, was made or became unto the head, etc.</p>
