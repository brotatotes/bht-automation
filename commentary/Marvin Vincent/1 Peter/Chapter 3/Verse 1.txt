Likewise [ομοιως] . Rev., in like manner; better, because likewise in popular speech has, wrongly, the sense of also. Peter means in like manner with servants (ch. 2 18). Be in subjection [υποτασσομεναι] . Lit., being in subjection, or submitting yourselves; the same word which is used of the submission of servants (ch. 2 18). Be won [κερδηθησονται] . Rev., be gained. The word used by Christ, Matthew 18:15 : "gained thy brother."
