<p>Sanctify the Lord God. The A. V. follows the Tex. Rec., reading ton Qeon, God, instead of ton Criston, Christ, which is the reading of the best texts. The article with Christ shows that kurion, Lord, is to be taken predicatively. Render, therefore, as Rev., sanctify Christ (the Christ) as Lord. </p>
<p>Ready to give an answer [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ετοιμοι προς απολογιαν] </span></span>. Lit., ready for an answer. Answer is our word apology, not in the popular sense of excuse, but in the more radical sense of defence. So it is translated <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+22:1">Acts 22:1</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="php+1:7">Philippians 1:7</span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="php+1:16">Philippians 1:16</span>. Clearing of yourselves, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="2co+7:11">2 Corinthians 7:11</span>. Meekness. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+5:5">Matthew 5:5</span>.</p>
