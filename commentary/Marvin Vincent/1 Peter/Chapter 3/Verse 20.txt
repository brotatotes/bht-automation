<p>In which [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εις ην] </span></span>. Lit., into which. A pregnant construction; into which they were gathered, and in which they were saved. </p>
<p>By water [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δια] </span></span>. Rev., through. Some take this as instrumental, by means of water; other as local, by passing through the water, or being brought safely through the water into the ark. Rev., in margin, were brought safely through water.</p>
