<p>Amazement [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πτοησιν] </span></span>. Rev., terror. Compare the kindred verb ptohqhte, be terrified, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="lu+21:9">Luke 21:9</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="lu+24:37">Luke 24:37</span>; on which, see note.
