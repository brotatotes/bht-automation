<p>Oracles [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">λογια] </span></span>. In classical Greek, of the oracular responses of heathen deities. Here, divine utterances or revelations. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+7:38">Acts 7:38</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ro+3:2">Romans 3:2</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="heb+5:12">Hebrews 5:12</span>. </p>
<p>Giveth [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">χορηγει] </span></span>. Only here and <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="2co+9:10">2 Corinthians 9:10</span>. Peter uses the compound ejpicorhgew, furnish, in <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="2pe+1:5">2 Peter 1:5</span>; which see.</p>
