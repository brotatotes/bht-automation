<p>A busybody in other men's matters [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αλλοτριοεπισκοπος] </span></span>. Only here in New Testament. Lit., the overseer of another's matters. One who usurps authority in matters not within his province. Rev., meddler. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="lu+12:13">Luke 12:13</span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="lu+12:14">Luke 12:14</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1th+4:11">1 Thessalonians 4:11</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="2th+3:11">2 Thessalonians 3:11</span>. It may refer to the officious interference of Christians in the affairs of their Gentile neighbors, through excess of zeal to conform them to the Christian standard.</p>
