<p>A Christian. Only three times in the New Testament, and never as a name used by Christians themselves, but as a nickname or a term of reproach. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+11:26">Acts 11:26</span>. Hence Peter's idea is, if any man suffer from the contumely of those who contemptuously style him Christian.</p>
