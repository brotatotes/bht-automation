<p>Commit [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">παρατιθεσθωσαν] </span></span>. Give in charge as a deposit. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="lu+12:48">Luke 12:48</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+20:32">Acts 20:32</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1ti+1:18">1 Timothy 1:18</span>. The word is used by Christ in commending his soul to God (<span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="lu+23:46">Luke 23:46</span>). </p>
<p>Well - doing [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αγαθοποιια] </span></span>. Only here in New Testament. Compare ch. 2 14. The surrender to God is to be coupled with the active practice of good. </p>
