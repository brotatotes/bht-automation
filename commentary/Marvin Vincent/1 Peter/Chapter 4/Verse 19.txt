Commit [παρατιθεσθωσαν] . Give in charge as a deposit. Compare Luke 12:48; Acts 20:32; 1 Timothy 1:18. The word is used by Christ in commending his soul to God (Luke 23:46). Well - doing [αγαθοποιια] . Only here in New Testament. Compare ch. 2 14. The surrender to God is to be coupled with the active practice of good.
