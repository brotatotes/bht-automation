<p>Mighty hand [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κραταιαν χειρα] </span></span>. A phrase found nowhere else in the New Testament, but occurring in the Septuagint, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ex+3:19">Exodus 3:19</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="de+3:24">Deuteronomy 3:24</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="job+30:21">Job 30:21</span>. The adjective kratrian, mighty, is, moreover, used only here. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="lu+1:51">Luke 1:51</span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="lu+1:52">Luke 1:52</span>.</p>
