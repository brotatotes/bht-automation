<p>Casting [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επιρριψαντες] </span></span>. The aorist participle denoting an act once for all; throwing the whole life with its care on him. </p>
<p>All your care [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πασαν την μεριμναν] </span></span>. The whole of your care. "Not every anxiety as it arises, for none will arise if this transference has been effectually made." Care. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+6:25">Matthew 6:25</span>, take no thought. Rev., rightly, anxiety. </p>
<p>He careth [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μελει] </span></span>. Meaning the watchful care of interest and affection. The sixth and seventh verses should be taken together : Humble yourselves and cast all your anxiety. Pride is at the root of most of our anxiety. To human pride it is humiliating to cast everything upon another and be cared for. See <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="jas+4:6">James 4:6</span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="jas+4:7">James 4:7</span>.</p>
