<p>For [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">οτι] </span></span>. Incorrect. Rend. how that. It is explanatory of your election. For similar usage see <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1co+1:26">1 Corinthians 1:26</span>. </p>
<p>Our gospel. The gospel as preached by Paul and his colleagues. Comp. <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ro+2:16">Romans 2:16</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ro+16:25">Romans 16:25</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ga+1:11">Galatians 1:11</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ga+2:2">Galatians 2:2</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1th+2:4">1 Thessalonians 2:4</span>. My gospel is sometimes used in connection with an emphasis upon some particular feature of the gospel, as in <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ro+2:16">Romans 2:16</span>, where Paul is speaking of the judgment of the world by Christ; or in <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ro+16:25">Romans 16:25</span>, where he is referring to the extension of the messianic kingdom to the Gentiles. </p>
<p>In word [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εν λογω] </span></span>. The gospel did not appeal to them as mere eloquent and learned discourse. </p>
<p>In power [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εν δυναμει] </span></span>. Power of spiritual persuasion and conviction : not power as displayed in miracles, at least not principally, although miraculous demonstrations may be included. Paul rarely alluded to his power of working miracles. </p>
<p>Assurance [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πληροφορια] </span></span>. Assured persuasion of the preacher that the message was divine. The word not in pre - Christian Greek writers, nor in LXX Only in one other passage in Paul, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="col+2:2">Colossians 2:2</span>. See <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="heb+6:11">Hebrews 6:11</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="heb+10:22">Hebrews 10:22</span>. </p>
<p>We were [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εγενηθημεν] </span></span>. More correctly, we shewed or proved ourselves.</p>
