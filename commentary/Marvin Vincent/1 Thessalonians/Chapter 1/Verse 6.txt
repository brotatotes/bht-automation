Followers [μιμηται] . More literally and better, imitators. Only once outside of Paul 's writings, Hebrews 6:12. Comp. 1 Thessalonians 3:9; 1 Thessalonians 2:0 Thessalonians 7; 1 Corinthians 4:16; 1 Corinthians 11:1; Galatians 4:12; Philippians 3:17; Philippians 4:9. And of the Lord. Guarding against any possible imputation of self - assertion or conceit. Comp. 1 Corinthians 11:1. Tribulation [θλιψει] . See on Matthew 13:21. Referring especially to persecutions at the hands of the Jews (Acts 17:5 ff.), which probably continued after Paul 's departure from Thessalonica.
