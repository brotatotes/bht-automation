<p>An ensample [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τυπον] </span></span>. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1pe+5:3">1 Peter 5:3</span>. </p>
<p>Macedonia and Achaia. Shortly after 146 B. C., all Greece south of Macedonia and Epirus was formed into a Roman province under the name of Achaia, and Macedonia with Epirus into another province called Macedonia.</p>
