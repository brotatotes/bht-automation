<p>Hath sounded forth [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εξηχηται] </span></span>. N. T. o. LXX <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="joe+3:14">Joel 3:14</span>; Sir. 40 13, of thunder; 3 Macc. 3 2, of a report. It means a loud, unmistakable proclamation. </p>
<p>The word of the Lord [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ο λογος του κυριου] </span></span>. The phrase in Paul only in these Epistles. Comp. <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="2th+3:1">2 Thessalonians 3:1</span>; 2Th 4:15. Comparatively frequent in Acts. Paul has logov Qeou or tou Qeou word of God, eight times, and logov tou cristou word of the Christ, once, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="col+3:16">Colossians 3:16</span>. The meaning here is the gospel, regarded either as the message proceeding from the Lord, or concerning him. It is the eujaggelion qeou the gospel of God : see ch. <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1th+2:8">1 Thessalonians 2:8</span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1th+2:9">1 Thessalonians 2:9</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ro+1:1">Romans 1:1</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ro+14:16">Romans 14:16</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="2co+11:7">2 Corinthians 11:7</span>; As Professor Sanday remarks on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ro+1:1">Romans 1:1</span>, "it is probably a mistake in these cases to restrict the force of the genitive to one particular aspect : all aspects are included in which the gospel is in any way related to God and Christ." In every place. A rhetorical exaggeration, signifying the whole known world. It is explained by the extensive commercial relations of Thessalonica. Comp. <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ro+1:8">Romans 1:8</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="col+1:6">Colossians 1:6</span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="col+1:23">Colossians 1:23</span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="2co+2:14">2 Corinthians 2:14</span>. Is spread abroad [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εξεληλυθεν] </span></span>. Lit. and better, has gone forth. 12</p>
