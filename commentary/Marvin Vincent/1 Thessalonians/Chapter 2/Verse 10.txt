<p>Holily - justly - unblameably [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">οσιως - δικαιως - αμεμπτως] </span></span>. For dikaiwv holily, see on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="lu+1:75">Luke 1:75</span>; for dikaiwv justly or righteously, on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ro+1:17">Romans 1:17</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ro+5:7">Romans 5:7</span>. Amemptwv unblameably, only in these Epistles. See ch. <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1th+3:13">1 Thessalonians 3:13</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1th+5:23">1 Thessalonians 5:23</span>. For the distinction between osiov and dikaiov see Plato, Gorg. 507.</p>
