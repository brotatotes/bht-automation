Holily - justly - unblameably [οσιως - δικαιως - αμεμπτως] . For dikaiwv holily, see on Luke 1:75; for dikaiwv justly or righteously, on Romans 1:17; Romans 5:7. Amemptwv unblameably, only in these Epistles. See ch. 1 Thessalonians 3:13; 1 Thessalonians 5:23. For the distinction between osiov and dikaiov see Plato, Gorg. 507.
