<p>Persecuted [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εκδιωξαντων] </span></span>. Rev. more literally and correctly, drave out. The word only here, though it occurs as an alternative reading, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="lu+11:49">Luke 11:49</span>. Probably with special reference to his own expulsion from Thessalonica. <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+17:5-10">Acts 17:5-10</span>. </p>
<p>Contrary to all men. Tacitus (Hist. 5 5) describes the Jews as stubborn in their faith, prompt in kindly offices to each other, but bitterly hostile toward everybody else : Juvenal (Sat. 14 102 f.) says that they observe and respect whatever Moses has taught in his mystical volume; not to show the way except to one who practices the same rites, and to show the well only to the circumcised.</p>
