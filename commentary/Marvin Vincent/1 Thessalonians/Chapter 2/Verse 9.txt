<p>Labor - travail [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κοπον - μοχθον] </span></span>. The two words are associated in <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="2co+11:27">2 Corinthians 11:27</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="2th+3:8">2 Thessalonians 3:8</span>. Mocqov travail, P o. Frequent in LXX Kopov emphasises fatigue, mocqov hardship. </p>
