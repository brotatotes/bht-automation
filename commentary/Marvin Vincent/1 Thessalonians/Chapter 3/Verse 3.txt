<p>Moved [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">σαινεσθαι] </span></span>. N. T. o o LXX In Class., as early as Homer, of dogs; to wag the tail, fawn (Hom. <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ob+1:10">Obadiah 1:10</span> <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ob+1:217">Obadiah 1:217</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ob+1:16">Obadiah 1:16</span> <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ob+1:6">Obadiah 1:6</span>). Hence of persons, to fawn or cringe. The word is apparently used here in the original sense, to be shaken. 25 We are appointed [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κειμεθα] </span></span>.
