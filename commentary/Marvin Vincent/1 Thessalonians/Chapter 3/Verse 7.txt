<p>Affliction [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αναγκη] </span></span>. Rev. distress. The derivation from agxein to press tightly, to choke (Lightfoot, Ellicott) is doubtful. In the sense of urgency, distress, seldom in Class. See <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1co+7:26">1 Corinthians 7:26</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="2co+6:4">2 Corinthians 6:4</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="2co+12:10">2 Corinthians 12:10</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="lu+21:23">Luke 21:23</span>.</p>
