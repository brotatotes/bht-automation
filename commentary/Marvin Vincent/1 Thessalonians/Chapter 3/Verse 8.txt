<p>Stand fast [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">στηκετε] </span></span>. The sense of firm standing is derived from the context, and does not inhere in the word. In <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mr+3:31">Mark 3:31</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mr+11:25">Mark 11:25</span>, it means simply to stand. Comp. <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="php+4:1">Philippians 4:1</span>. It does not occur earlier than N. T.</p>
