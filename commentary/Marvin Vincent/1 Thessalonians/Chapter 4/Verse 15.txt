<p>By the word of the Lord [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εν λογω κυριου] </span></span>. Or in the word. Logov of a concrete saying, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ro+9:9">Romans 9:9</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ro+13:9">Romans 13:9</span>. We do not say this on our own authority. Comp. <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1co+7:10">1 Corinthians 7:10</span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1co+7:12">1 Corinthians 7:12</span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1co+7:25">1 Corinthians 7:25</span>. No recorded saying of the Lord answers to this reference. It may refer to a saying transmitted orally, or to a direct revelation to Paul. Comp. <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ga+1:12">Galatians 1:12</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ga+2:2">Galatians 2:2</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="eph+3:3">Ephesians 3:3</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="2co+12:1">2 Corinthians 12:1</span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="2co+12:9">2 Corinthians 12:9</span>. </p>
<p>Remain [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">περιλειπομενοι] </span></span>. P o. and only in this Epistle. The plural we indicates that Paul himself expected to be alive at the parousia. 26 Shall not prevent [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ου μη φθασωμεν] </span></span>. The A. V. misses the force of the double negative - shall in no wise prevent. Prevent in the older sense of anticipate, be beforehand with. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+17:25">Matthew 17:25</span>, and <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1th+2:16">1 Thessalonians 2:16</span>. The living shall not share the blessings of the advent sooner than the dead in Christ.</p>
