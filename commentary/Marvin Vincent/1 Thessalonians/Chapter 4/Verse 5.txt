<p>Not in the lust of concupiscence [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μη εν παθει επιθυμιας] </span></span>. Lit. in passion of desire. Not with avaricious greed. For ejpiqumia see on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mr+4:19">Mark 4:19</span>. Its meaning is by no means limited to sensual lust; see, for instance, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="lu+22:15">Luke 22:15</span>. It is used as including all kinds of worldly desires, as <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ga+5:16">Galatians 5:16</span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ga+5:24">Galatians 5:24</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1jo+2:17">1 John 2:17</span>. In <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ro+7:7">Romans 7:7</span>, especially of covetousness.</p>
