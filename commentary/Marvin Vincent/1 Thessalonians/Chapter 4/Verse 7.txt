<p>Unto uncleanness [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επι ακαθαρσια] </span></span>. Better, for uncleanness; ejpi denoting aim or intention. The intention is viewed as the basis of the act [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επι υπον] </span></span>. Comp. <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ga+5:13">Galatians 5:13</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="eph+2:10">Ephesians 2:10</span>. </p>
<p>In sanctification [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εν] </span></span>. Note the change of preposition. Sanctification is the characteristic life - element of the Christian, in which he is to live. Comp. in peace, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1co+7:15">1 Corinthians 7:15</span>; in hope, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="eph+4:4">Ephesians 4:4</span>.</p>
