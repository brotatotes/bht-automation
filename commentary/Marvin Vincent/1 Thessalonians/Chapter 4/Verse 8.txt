Despiseth [αθετων] . Better, rejecteth. Setteth aside. Comp. Galatians 2:21; Galatians 3:15; 1 Corinthians 1:19. Used in N. T. both of persons and things. His Holy Spirit [το πνευμα αυτου το αγιον] . Solemn and emphatic : His Spirit, the holy. Similarly, Acts 14:8, Acts 14:28; Acts 19:6; Acts 20:23; Ephesians 1:13; Ephesians 4:30.
