<p>Know [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ειδεναι] </span></span>. See on ch. <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1th+4:4">1 Thessalonians 4:4</span>. Recognize them for what they are, and as entitled to respect because of their office. Comp. ejpiginwskete acknowledge, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1co+16:18">1 Corinthians 16:18</span>; and ejgnwsqhv takest knowledge, LXX, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ps+143:3">Psalms 143:3</span>. Ignatius, Smyrn. 9, has ejpiskopon eiJudenai to know the bishop, to appreciate and honor him. </p>
<p>Are over [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">προισταμενους] </span></span>. Lit. who are placed before you. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ro+12:8">Romans 12:8</span>. Used of superintendents of households, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1ti+3:4">1 Timothy 3:4</span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1ti+3:5">1 Timothy 3:5</span>, xii. of the ruling of elders of the church, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1ti+5:17">1 Timothy 5:17</span>. It does not indicate a particular ecclesiastical office, but is used functionally. The ecclesiastical nomenclature of the Pauline Epistles is unsettled, corresponding with the fact that the primitive church was not a homogeneous body throughout christendom. The primitive Pauline church consisted of a number of separate fraternities which were self - governing. The recognition of those who ministered to the congregations depended on the free choice of their members. See for instance <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1co+16:15">1 Corinthians 16:15</span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1co+16:16">1 Corinthians 16:16</span>. The congregation exercised discipline and gave judgment : <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1co+5:3-5">1 Corinthians 5:3-5</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="2co+2:6">2 Corinthians 2:6</span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="2co+2:7">2 Corinthians 2:7</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="2co+7:11">2 Corinthians 7:11</span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="2co+7:12">2 Corinthians 7:12</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ga+6:1">Galatians 6:1</span>. </p>
