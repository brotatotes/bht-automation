<p>Quench not the Spirit. Since he is the inspirer of prayer, and the bestower of all gifts of grace on the Church. Comp. <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="eph+4:30">Ephesians 4:30</span>. The operation of the Spirit is set forth under the image of fire in <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+3:11">Matthew 3:11</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="lu+12:49">Luke 12:49</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+2:3">Acts 2:3</span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+2:4">Acts 2:4</span>. The reference here is to the work of the Spirit generally, and not specially to his inspiration of prayer or prophecy.</p>
