Faithful [πιστος] . Comp. 2 Timothy 2:13, and see on 1 John 1:9; Revelation 1:5; Revelation 3:14. That calleth [ο καλων] . = the caller. The emphasis is on the person rather than on the act. Comp. Romans 9:11; Galatians 1:6, Galatians 1:15; Galatians 5:8; 1 Thessalonians 2:12; 1 Peter 5:10; James 1:5.
