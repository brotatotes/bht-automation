I charge [ενορκιζω] . N. T. o. Rev. stronger and more literal, I adjure. Class. This strong appeal may perhaps be explained by a suspicion on Paul 's part that a wrong use might be made of his name and authority (see 2 Thessalonians 2:2), so that it was important that his views should be made known to all.
