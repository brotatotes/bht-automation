<p>Be drunken [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μεθυσκομενοι] </span></span>. Lit. who are made drunk or get drunk. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="joh+2:10">John 2:10</span>. In N. T. always of intoxication. In LXX, the Hebrews shekar strong drink is several times rendered by mequsma; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="jude+1:13">Jude 1:13</span>:4, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="jude+1:7">Jude 1:7</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1sa+1:11">1 Samuel 1:11</span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1sa+1:15">1 Samuel 1:15</span>.</p>
