<p>My own son in the faith [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">γνησιω τεκνω εν πιστει] </span></span>. More correctly, "my true child in faith." Comp. <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="tit+1:4">Titus 1:4</span>. With these two exceptions, teknon or uiJov ejn pistei does not occur in N. T. En pistei or th pistei is not come on Paul; see <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1co+16:13">1 Corinthians 16:13</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="2co+8:7">2 Corinthians 8:7</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="2co+13:5">2 Corinthians 13:5</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ga+2:20">Galatians 2:20</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="2th+2:13">2 Thessalonians 2:13</span>. In the Pastorals, nine times. In Paul joined with zhn to live, einai to be, sthkein to stand, bebaiousqai to be established. For gnhsiov true, see <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="2co+8:8">2 Corinthians 8:8</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="php+2:20">Philippians 2:20</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="php+4:3">Philippians 4:3</span>. It means natural by birth - relation, therefore true or genuine. Mercy [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ελεος] </span></span>. This addition to the usual form of salutation is peculiar to the Pastorals.</p>
