<p>Learn [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μανθανετω] </span></span>. Comp. <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1co+14:35">1 Corinthians 14:35</span>. </p>
<p>In silence [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εν ησυχια] </span></span>. See on peaceable, ver. 2. Rev, renders quietness; but the admonition concerns the behavior of women in religious assemblies. Comp. <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1co+14:34">1 Corinthians 14:34</span>. The word is used in the sense of silence, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+22:2">Acts 22:2</span> : with the broader meaning quietness in <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="2th+3:12">2 Thessalonians 3:12</span>.</p>
