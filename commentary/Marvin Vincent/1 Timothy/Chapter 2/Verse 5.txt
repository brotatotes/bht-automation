<p>For [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">γαρ] </span></span>. The universality of the grace is grounded in the unity of God. Comp. <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ro+3:30">Romans 3:30</span>. One divine purpose for all implies one God who purposes. </p>
