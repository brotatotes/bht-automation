Therefore [εις τουτο] . More correctly, to this end; or with a view to this. We labor and strive [κοπιωμεν και αγωνιζομεθα] . Both Pauline words. See on Colossians 1:29, where the two are found together as here. Also on kopou labor, 1 Thessalonians 1:3, and kopiwntav, and laboring 1 Thessalonians 5:12. Comp. ch. 1 Timothy 5:17, and 2 Timothy 2:6. Both words denote strenuous and painful effort. 111 The kai; has an ascensive force : "we labor, yea struggle." We trust in [ηλπικαμεν επι] . Better, have set our hope on. The verb with ejpi in Pastorals, in Paul, Romans 14:12, a citation, and in 1 Peter 1:13.
