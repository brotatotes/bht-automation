To reading [αναγνωσει] . Three times in N. T. See Acts 13:15; 2 Corinthians 3:14. The verb ajnaginwskein usually of public reading. See on Luke 4:16. So in LXX In post - classical Greek, sometimes of reading aloud with comments. See Epictetus, Diss. 3, 23, 20. Dr. Hatch says : "It is probable that this practice of reading with comments... may account for the coordination of 'reading' with 'exhortation' and 'teaching' in 1 Timothy 4:13." Exhortation [τη παρακλησει] . Often in Paul. See on consolation, Luke 6:24, comfort, Acts 9:31, and comforter, John 14:16.
