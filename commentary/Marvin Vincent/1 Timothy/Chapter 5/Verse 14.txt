<p>That the younger women marry [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">νεωτερας γαμειν] </span></span>. Better, the younger widows. This seems to be required by oun therefore, connecting the subject of the verb with the class just described. They are enjoined to marry, rather than to assume a position in the church which they might disgrace by the conduct described in vv. 11 - 13. Comp. <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1co+7:8">1 Corinthians 7:8</span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1co+7:9">1 Corinthians 7:9</span>. </p>
<p>Bear children [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τεκνογονειν] </span></span>. N. T. o. o LXX, o Class. Comp. teknogonia childbearing, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1ti+2:15">1 Timothy 2:15</span>. </p>
<p>Guide the house [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">οικοδεσποτειν] </span></span>. Better, rule the house. N. T. o. o LXX, o Class. Oikodespothv master of the house is quite common in the Synoptic Gospels. </p>
<p>Occasion [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αφορμην] </span></span>. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ro+7:8">Romans 7:8</span>. </p>
<p>To the adversary [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τω αντικειμενω] </span></span>. The one who is set over against. Not Satan, but the human enemy of Christianity. Comp. <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="php+1:28">Philippians 1:28</span>, and oJ ejx ejnantiav he that is of the contrary part, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="tit+2:8">Titus 2:8</span>. </p>
<p>To speak reproachfully [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">λοιδοριας χαριν] </span></span>. Lit. in the interest of reviling. Const. with give on occasion. Loidoria reviling only here and <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1pe+3:9">1 Peter 3:9</span>. For the verb loidorein to revile see <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="joh+9:28">John 9:28</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+23:4">Acts 23:4</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1co+4:12">1 Corinthians 4:12</span>; and note on Jas 9:28.</p>
