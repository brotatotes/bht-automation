Receive not an accusation [κατηγοριαν μη παραδεχου] . Neither word in Paul. For accusation see on. John 5:45. It means a formal accusation before a tribunal. The compound verb para emphasises the giver or transmitter of the thing received : to receive from another. But [εκτος ει μη] . Except. A pleonastic formula, except in case. The formula in 1 Corinthians 14:5; 1 Corinthians 14:9. Before [επι] . Or on the authority of. On condition that two witnesses testify. The O. T. law on this point in Deuteronomy 19:15. Comp. Matthew 18:16; John 8:17; John 9:0 1 Corinthians 13:1.
