<p>Others [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">οι λοιποι] </span></span>. More correctly, the rest. His fellow Elders. May fear [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">φοβον εχωσιν] </span></span>. May have fear, which is stronger than A. V.</p>
