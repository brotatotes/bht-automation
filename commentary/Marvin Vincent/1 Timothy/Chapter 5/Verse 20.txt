Them that sin [τους αμαρτανοντας] . Referring to Elders, who, by reason of their public position [προεστωτες] , should receive public rebuke. Rebuke [ελεγχε] . Comp. 2 Timothy 4:2; Titus 1:9, Titus 1:13; Titus 2:15. See on reproved, John 3:20. Others [οι λοιποι] . More correctly, the rest. His fellow Elders. May fear [φοβον εχωσιν] . May have fear, which is stronger than A. V.
