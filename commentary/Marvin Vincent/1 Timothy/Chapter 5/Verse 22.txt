<p>Lay hands on. Probably with reference to that rite in the formal restoration of those who had been expelled from the church for gross sins. Suddenly [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ταχεως] </span></span>. Better, hastily. </p>
<p>Neither be partaker of other men's sins [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μηδε κοινωνει αμαρτιαις αλλοτριαις] </span></span>. Letter, make common cause with. See on communicating, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ro+12:13">Romans 12:13</span>. Comp. <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ro+14:27">Romans 14:27</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1pe+4:13">1 Peter 4:13</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="eph+5:11">Ephesians 5:11</span>. By a too hasty and inconsiderate restoration, he would condone the sins of the offenders, and would thus make common cause with them. </p>
<p>Keep thyself pure [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">σεαυτον αγνον τηρει] </span></span>. Comp. ch. <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1ti+6:14">1 Timothy 6:14</span>. Enjoining positively what was enjoined negatively in the preceding clause. For pure see on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1jo+3:3">1 John 3:3</span>. For keep see on reserved, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1pe+1:4">1 Peter 1:4</span>. The phrase eJauton threin to keep one's self, in <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="jas+1:27">James 1:27</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="2co+11:9">2 Corinthians 11:9</span>.</p>
