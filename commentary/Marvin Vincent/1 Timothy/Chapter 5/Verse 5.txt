<p>And desolate [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">και μεμονωμενη] </span></span>. N. T. o. From monov alone. </p>
<p>Explanatory of a widow indeed. One entirely bereaved. </p>
<p>Trusteth in God [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ηλπικεν επι τον θεον] </span></span>. Strictly hath directed her hope at God. Rev. hath her hope set on God implies ejpi with the dative, as <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1jo+3:3">1 John 3:3</span>.</p>
