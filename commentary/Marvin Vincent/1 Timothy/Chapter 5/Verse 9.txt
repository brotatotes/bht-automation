<p>Be taken into the number [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">καταλεγεσθω] </span></span>. Better, enrolled (as a widow). N. T. o. Very, rare in LXX Common in Class. Originally, to pick out, as soldiers. Hence, to enroll, enlist. Here, to be enrolled in the body of widows who are to receive church support. See on ver. 3.</p>
