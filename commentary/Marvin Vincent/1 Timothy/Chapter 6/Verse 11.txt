Man of God [ανθρωπε θεου] . The phrase only in Pastorals. Comp 2 Timothy 3:17. Not an official designation. Righteousness [δικαιοσυνην] . See on Romans 1:17. Not in the Pauline dogmatic sense, but as Ephesians 5:9, moral rectitude according to God 's law. Meekness [πραυπαθιαν] . N. T. o. o LXX Meekness of feeling [παθος] . The usual word is prauthv, often in Paul. See on meek, Matthew 5:5. With the whole verse comp. Titus 3:12.
