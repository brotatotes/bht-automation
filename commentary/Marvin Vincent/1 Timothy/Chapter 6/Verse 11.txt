<p>Man of God [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ανθρωπε θεου] </span></span>. The phrase only in Pastorals. Comp <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="2ti+3:17">2 Timothy 3:17</span>. Not an official designation. </p>
<p>Righteousness [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δικαιοσυνην] </span></span>. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ro+1:17">Romans 1:17</span>. Not in the Pauline dogmatic sense, but as <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="eph+5:9">Ephesians 5:9</span>, moral rectitude according to God 's law. </p>
