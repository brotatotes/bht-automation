<p>Witnessed a good confession [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μαρτυρησαντος την καλην ομολογιαν] </span></span>. Letter, the or his good confession. The phrase is unique. The good confession is the historical confession of Jesus before Pilate, which is the warrant for the truthfulness of Timothy's confession. Christ is called is the faithful and true witeness " [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μαρτυς] </span></span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="re+1:5">Revelation 1:5</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="re+3:14">Revelation 3:14</span>.
