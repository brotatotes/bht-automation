<p>Partakers of the benefit [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">οι της ευεργεσιας αντιλαμβανομενοι] </span></span>. The verb means to take hold of; hence, to take hold for the purpose of helping; to take up for, as <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="lu+1:54">Luke 1:54</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+20:35">Acts 20:35</span>. o P. Euergesia, benefit only here and <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+4:9">Acts 4:9</span>. Better, kindly service. Rend. they that busy themselves in the kindly service. 126 The reference is to the kindly acts which the masters do to their slaves; not to the benefits received by the slaves. Comp. <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ga+5:13">Galatians 5:13</span>.</p>
