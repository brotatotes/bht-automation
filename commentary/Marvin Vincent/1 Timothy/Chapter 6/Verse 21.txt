Epaggelia, primarily announcement, but habitually promise in N. T. In Pastorals only here and 1 Timothy 4:8. With the promise of the life in Christ goes the provision for its proclamation. Hence the apostle, in proclaiming "ye shall live; through Christ," is an apostle according to the promise. Of life which is in Christ Jesus.
