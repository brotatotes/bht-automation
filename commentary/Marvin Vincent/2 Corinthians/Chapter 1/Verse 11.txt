<p>Persons [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">προσωπων] </span></span>. Face is the usual rendering of the word in the New Testament. Even when rendered person the usage is Hebraistic for face. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="jas+2:1">James 2:1</span> There is no reason for abandoning that sense here. The expression is pictorial; that thanksgiving may be given from many faces; the cheerful countenances being an offering of thanks to God.</p>
