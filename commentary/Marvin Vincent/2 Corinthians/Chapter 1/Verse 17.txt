<p>Did I use lightness [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τη ελαφρια εχρησαμην] </span></span>. Rev., shew fickleness. Elafria, lightness, only here in the New Testament. </p>
<p>Compare ejlafrov light, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+11:30">Matthew 11:30</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="2co+4:17">2 Corinthians 4:17</span>. His change of plan had given rise to the charge of fickleness. </p>
<p>The yea, yea, and the nay, nay. That I should say "yes" at one time and "no" at another; promising to come and breaking my promise.</p>
