Did I use lightness [τη ελαφρια εχρησαμην] . Rev., shew fickleness. Elafria, lightness, only here in the New Testament. Compare ejlafrov light, Matthew 11:30; 2 Corinthians 4:17. His change of plan had given rise to the charge of fickleness. The yea, yea, and the nay, nay. That I should say "yes" at one time and "no" at another; promising to come and breaking my promise.
