<p>Was not [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ουκ εγενετο] </span></span>. Rather, did not prove to be, in the result. In Him was yea [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ναι εν αυτω γεγονεν] </span></span>. Lit., yea has come to pass in Him. He has shown Himself absolutely the truth. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="joh+14:6">John 14:6</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="re+3:7">Revelation 3:7</span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="re+3:14">Revelation 3:14</span>.</p>
