Sealed [σφραγισαμενος] . See on John 3:33; Revelation 22:10. Earnest [αρραβωνα] . Only here, ch. 5 5, and Ephesians 1:14. It means caution - money, deposited by a purchaser in pledge of full payment. Of the Spirit. Not the foretaste or pledge of the Spirit, but the Spirit Himself in pledge of the fulfillment of the promises. By a common Greek usage the words are in apposition : the earnest which is the Spirit.
