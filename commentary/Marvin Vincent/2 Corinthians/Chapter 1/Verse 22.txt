<p>Sealed [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">σφραγισαμενος] </span></span>. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="joh+3:33">John 3:33</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="re+22:10">Revelation 22:10</span>. Earnest [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αρραβωνα] </span></span>. Only here, ch. 5 5, and <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="eph+1:14">Ephesians 1:14</span>. It means caution - money, deposited by a purchaser in pledge of full payment. </p>
<p>Of the Spirit. Not the foretaste or pledge of the Spirit, but the Spirit Himself in pledge of the fulfillment of the promises. By a common Greek usage the words are in apposition : the earnest which is the Spirit.</p>
