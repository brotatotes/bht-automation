<p>Sufferings of Christ. Not things suffered for Christ 's sake, but Christ 's own sufferings as they are shared by His disciples. See <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+20:22">Matthew 20:22</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="php+3:10">Philippians 3:10</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="col+1:24">Colossians 1:24</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1pe+4:13">1 Peter 4:13</span>. Note the peculiar phrase abound [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">περισσευει</span></span>] in us, by which Christ 's sufferings are represented as overflowing upon His followers. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="col+1:24">Colossians 1:24</span>.</p>
