<p>Through a window [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δια θυριδος] </span></span>. Only here and <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+20:9">Acts 20:9</span>. </p>
<p>Diminutive of qura a door. The same expression is used in Sept., <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="jos+2:15">Joshua 2:15</span>, of the escape of the spies from Jericho, and <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1sa+19:12">1 Samuel 19:12</span>, of David 's escape from Saul by the aid of Michal. </p>
<p>Basket [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">σαργανη] </span></span>. Lit., braided work; a rope - basket or hamper. Luke, in his narrative of the incident, uses spuriv, for which see on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+14:20">Matthew 14:20</span>. </p>
