<p>Signs [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">σημεια] </span></span>. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+24:24">Matthew 24:24</span>. Stanley observes that the passage is remarkable as containing (what is rare in the history of miracles) a direct claim to miraculous powers by the person to whom they were ascribed. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1co+2:4">1 Corinthians 2:4</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ro+14:19">Romans 14:19</span>. </p>
<p>Were wrought [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κατειργασθη] </span></span>. The testimony was decisive. They were fully wrought out.</p>
