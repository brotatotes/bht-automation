<p>Strifes [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εριθειαι] </span></span>. Rev., better, factions. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="jas+3:14">James 3:14</span>. Wraths [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">θυμοι</span></span>] For the plural, compare deaths, ch. 11 33; drunkennesses, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ga+5:21">Galatians 5:21</span>; bloods, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="joh+1:13">John 1:13</span> (see note); the willings of the flesh, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="eph+2:3">Ephesians 2:3</span>; mercies, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="php+2:1">Philippians 2:1</span>. Excitements or outbursts of wrath. </p>
<p>Whisperings [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ψιθυρισμοι] </span></span>. Psithurismoi, the sound adapted to the sense. Only here in the New Testament. Secret slanders. In Sept., <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ec+10:11">Ecclesiastes 10:11</span>, it is used of the murmuring of a snake - charmer. 161 Yiquristhv whisperer, occurs <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ro+1:29">Romans 1:29</span>. </p>
<p>Swellings [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">φυσιωσεις] </span></span>. Only here in the New Testament. Conceited inflation. For the kindred verb fusiaw to puff up, see on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1co+4:6">1 Corinthians 4:6</span>. </p>
<p>Tumults [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ακαταστασιαι] </span></span>. See on ch. <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="2co+6:5">2 Corinthians 6:5</span>.</p>
