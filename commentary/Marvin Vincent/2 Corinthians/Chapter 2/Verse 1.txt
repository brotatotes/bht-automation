<p>With myself [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εμαυτω] </span></span>. Rev., better, for myself. Paul, with affectionate tact, puts it as if he had taken this resolution for his own pleasure. In heaviness [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εν λυπη] </span></span>. Meaning, apparently, the apostle 's own sorrowful state of mind. This is wrong. He refers to the sorrow which his coming would bring to the Church. Compare to spare, ch. 1 23. Rev., with sorrow. </p>
