<p>Forgive [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">χαρισασθαι] </span></span>. The idea of freeness (cariv, see on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="lu+1:30">Luke 1:30</span>) lies in the word forgive, which is forth - give. </p>
<p>Overmuch sorrow [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τη περισσοτερα λυπη] </span></span>. Rev. gives the force of the article, his sorrow. Overmuch, excessive, through the refusal of pardon.</p>
