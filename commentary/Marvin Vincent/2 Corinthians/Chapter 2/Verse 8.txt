<p>Confirm your love [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κυρωσαι αγαπην] </span></span>. The verb is found only here and <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ga+3:15">Galatians 3:15</span>. From kurov supreme power, authority. Hence to take judicial resolution to treat the offender with brotherly love.</p>
