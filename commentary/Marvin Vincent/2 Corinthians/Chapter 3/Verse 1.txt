<p>Do we begin again. Rev., are we beginning. As if anticipating, the taunt so often repeated, that he had no commendatory letters, and therefore was forced to commend himself by self - laudation and by dishonest means. See ch. <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="2co+4:2">2 Corinthians 4:2</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="2co+10:12">2 Corinthians 10:12</span>. You will say, "You are beginning again the old strain of self - commendation as in the first epistle." See <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1co+9:15-21">1 Corinthians 9:15-21</span>. </p>
<p>To commend [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">συνισταναι] </span></span>. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ro+3:5">Romans 3:5</span>. </p>
