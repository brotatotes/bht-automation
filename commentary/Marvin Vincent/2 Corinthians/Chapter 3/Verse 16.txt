<p>It shall turn. The heart of Israel. </p>
<p>Shall be taken away [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">περιαιρειται] </span></span>. Rev., correctly, is taken away. The verb occurs twice in Acts (xxvii. 20, 40) of the taking away of hope, and of the unfastening of the anchors in Paul 's shipwreck; and in <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="heb+10:11">Hebrews 10:11</span>, of the taking away of sins. There is an allusion here to the removal of the veil from Moses ' face whenever he returned to commune with God. See <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ex+34:34">Exodus 34:34</span>.</p>
