It shall turn. The heart of Israel. Shall be taken away [περιαιρειται] . Rev., correctly, is taken away. The verb occurs twice in Acts (xxvii. 20, 40) of the taking away of hope, and of the unfastening of the anchors in Paul 's shipwreck; and in Hebrews 10:11, of the taking away of sins. There is an allusion here to the removal of the veil from Moses ' face whenever he returned to commune with God. See Exodus 34:34.
