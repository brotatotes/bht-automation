<p>Now the Lord is that Spirit. Kuriov the Lord is used in <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ex+34:34">Exodus 34:34</span> for Jehovah. The Lord Christ of ver. 16 is the Spirit who pervades and animates the new covenant of which we are ministers (ver. 6), and the ministration of which is with glory (ver. 8). Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ro+8:9-11">Romans 8:9-11</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="joh+14:16">John 14:16</span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="joh+14:18">John 14:18</span>. </p>
<p>Liberty. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ro+8:15">Romans 8:15</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ga+4:7">Galatians 4:7</span>.</p>
