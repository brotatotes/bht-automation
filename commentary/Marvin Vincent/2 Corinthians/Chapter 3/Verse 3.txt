An epistle of Christ ministered by us [επιστολη χριστου διακονηθεισα υφ ημων] . An epistle written by Christ through our ministry; that is, you, as the converted subjects of our ministry, are an epistle of Christ. Others explain : an epistle of which Christ forms the contents, thus making the apostles the writers. For the expression ministered by us, compare ch. 8 19, 20; 1 Peter 1:12. Ink [μελανι] . From melav black. Only here, 2 John 1:12 (see note), and 3 John 1:13. The Spirit. Instead of ink. Fleshy tables of the heart [πλαξιν καρδιας σαρκιναις] . The best texts read kardiaiv the dative case in apposition with tables. Render, as Rev., tables which are hearts of flesh. Compare Ezekiel 11:19; Jeremiah 17:1; Jeremiah 31:33. For of flesh, see on Romans 7:14.
