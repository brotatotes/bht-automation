<p>Confidence. In the fact that he may appeal to them, notwithstanding their weaknesses and errors. </p>
<p>Through Christ to God - ward [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δια του χριστου προς τον θεον] </span></span>. Through Christ who engenders the confidence, toward God, with reference to God who gives us success, and to whom we must account for our work.</p>
