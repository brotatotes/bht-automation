<p>Outward man - inward man. The material and spiritual natures. </p>
<p>Perish [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">διαφθειρεται] </span></span>. Rev., much better, is decaying. Perish implies destruction : the idea is that of progressive decay. </p>
<p>Is renewed [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ανακαινουται] </span></span>. Better, is being renewed, the process of renewal going on along with the process of decay. Stanley cites a line attributed to Michael Angelo : "The more the marble wastes the more the statue grows." Compare Euripides : "Time does not depress your spirit, but it grows young again : your body, however, is weak" (" Heraclidae, " 702, 703) </p>
<p>Day by day [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ημερα και ημερα] </span></span>. Lit., by day and day. A Hebrew form of expression.</p>
