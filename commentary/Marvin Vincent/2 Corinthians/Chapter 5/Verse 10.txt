Appear [φανερωθηναι] . Rev., better, be made manifest. Appear is not strong enough, since it implies only presence at the judgment - seat. The important fact is our being revealed as we are. Judgment seat [βηματος] . See on Acts 7:5. May receive [κομισηται] . See on 1 Peter 1:8. Compare Ephesians 6:8; Colossians 3:25. In the body [δια] . Lit., through the body as a medium. Bad [φαυλον] . See on James 3:16.
