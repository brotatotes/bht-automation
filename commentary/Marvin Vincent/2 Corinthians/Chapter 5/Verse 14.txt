<p>The love of Christ. Christ 's love to men. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1jo+2:5">1 John 2:5</span>. </p>
<p>Constraineth [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">συνεχει] </span></span>. See on taken, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="lu+4:38">Luke 4:38</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+18:5">Acts 18:5</span>. It is the word rendered I am in a strait, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="php+1:23">Philippians 1:23</span>. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="lu+12:50">Luke 12:50</span>. The idea is not urging or driving, but shutting up to one line and purpose, as in a narrow, walled road.</p>
