<p>Wrought [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κατεργασαμενος] </span></span>. The compound is significant, indicating an accomplished fact. Through the various operations of His Spirit and the processes of His discipline, God has worked us out (Stanley, worked up) for this change. The process includes the dissolution of what is mortal no less than the renewal. The one is a step to the other. See <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1co+14:36">1 Corinthians 14:36</span>. </p>
<p>Earnest of the Spirit. See on ch. <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="2co+1:22">2 Corinthians 1:22</span>, and compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ro+8:11">Romans 8:11</span>. Of the Spirit is appositional, the Spirit as the earnest.</p>
