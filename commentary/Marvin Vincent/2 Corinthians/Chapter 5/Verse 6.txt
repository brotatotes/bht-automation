At home [ενδημουντες] . En in, dhmov people. Only in this chapter. To be among one's own people, and not to travel abroad. We are absent [εκδημουμεν] . Lit., we live abroad. Only in this chapter. Compare Philippians 1:23; Philippians 3:20; Hebrews 11:13; Hebrews 13:14. There is a play upon the words which might be expressed by at home, from home.
