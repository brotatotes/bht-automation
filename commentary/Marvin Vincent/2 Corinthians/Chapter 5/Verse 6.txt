<p>At home [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ενδημουντες] </span></span>. En in, dhmov people. Only in this chapter. To be among one's own people, and not to travel abroad. </p>
<p>We are absent [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εκδημουμεν] </span></span>. Lit., we live abroad. Only in this chapter. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="php+1:23">Philippians 1:23</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="php+3:20">Philippians 3:20</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="heb+11:13">Hebrews 11:13</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="heb+13:14">Hebrews 13:14</span>. There is a play upon the words which might be expressed by at home, from home.</p>
