<p>We labor [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">φιλοτιμουμεθα] </span></span>. Used by Paul only, here, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ro+14:20">Romans 14:20</span> (note), <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1th+4:11">1 Thessalonians 4:11</span>. Labor is a feeble translation, not bringing out the idea of the end contemplated, as the motive of the toil. Rev., we make it our aim.</p>
