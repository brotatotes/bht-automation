<p>Having - possessing [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εχοντες - κατεχοντες] </span></span>. The contrast is twofold : between having and not having, and between temporary and permanent having, or having and keeping. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="lu+8:15">Luke 8:15</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1co+14:2">1 Corinthians 14:2</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1th+5:21">1 Thessalonians 5:21</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="heb+3:6">Hebrews 3:6</span>.</p>
