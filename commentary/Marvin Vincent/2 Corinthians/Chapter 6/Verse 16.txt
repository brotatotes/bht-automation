<p>Agreement [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">συγκαταθεσις] </span></span>. Only here in the New Testament. </p>
<p>Compare the kindred verb sugkatatiqemai to consent, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="lu+23:51">Luke 23:51</span>. Lit., a putting down or depositing along with one. Hence of voting the same way with another, and so agreeing. </p>
<p>Ye are. Read, as Rev., we are. </p>
<p>God hath said, etc. The quotation is combined and condensed from <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="le+27:11">Leviticus 27:11</span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="le+27:12">Leviticus 27:12</span>; and <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="eze+37:27">Ezekiel 37:27</span>, after the Septuagint. Paul treats it as if directly affirmed of the christian Church, thus regarding that Church as spiritually identical with the true church of Israel.</p>
