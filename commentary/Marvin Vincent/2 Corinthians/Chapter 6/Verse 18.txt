<p>I will be to you, etc. From <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="2sa+7:14">2 Samuel 7:14</span>, where the Septuagint and Hebrew agree. Paul says sons and daughters for son. </p>
<p>Almighty [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">παντοκρατωρ] </span></span>. The word is peculiar to Revelation, occurring nowhere else in the New Testament. Here it is a quotation. Frequent in the Septuagint. </p>
