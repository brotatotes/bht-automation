<p>Were comforted in your comfort [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">παρακεκλημεθα επι τη παρακλησει υμων] </span></span>. The best texts place a period after were comforted, transfer the de and (yea) from after ejpi exceedingly the more to directly after ejpi in (your comfort), and instead of your read our. The whole, as Rev., therefore we have been comforted. And in our comfort we joyed the more exceedingly, etc. </p>
<p>In our comfort [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επι] </span></span>. In addition to. Stanley, with this comfort before me, I was still more rejoiced, etc.</p>
