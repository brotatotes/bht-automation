<p>Receive [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">χωρησατε] </span></span>. From cwrov place or space. Primarily, to leave a space, make room for. See on containing, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="joh+2:6">John 2:6</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="joh+8:37">John 8:37</span>. The meaning here is make room for us. Rev., open your hearts to us, which is felicitous in view of the reference to ch. 6 12. It is equivalent to saying enlarge your hearts to take us in, as our heart is enlarged (ch. 6 11). </p>
<p>Defrauded [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επλεονεκτησαμεν] </span></span>. Used by Paul only. It adds the idea of wrong for the sake of gain, which is not necessarily implied in either of the other verbs.</p>
