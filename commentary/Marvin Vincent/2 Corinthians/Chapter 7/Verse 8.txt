<p>Repent [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μεταμελομαι] </span></span>. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+21:29">Matthew 21:29</span>. Rev., regret it. </p>
<p>Though I did repent. Punctuate as Am. Rev., I do not regret it : though (even if) I did regret it (for I see that that epistle made you sorry, though but for a season) I now rejoice.</p>
