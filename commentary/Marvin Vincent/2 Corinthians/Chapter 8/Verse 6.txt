<p>Had begun [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">προενηρξατο] </span></span>. Only here and ver. 10. Rev., giving the force of pro before, had made a beginning before : on his first visit to Corinth. </p>
<p>Complete - this grace also [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επιτελεση και την χαριν ταυτην] </span></span>. Should complete among you the act of love [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">χαριν] </span></span>, the contribution already begun, in addition to whatever else He has yet to complete among you [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">και αλσο] </span></span>.</p>
