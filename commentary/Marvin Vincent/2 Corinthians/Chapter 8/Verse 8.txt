<p>Sincerity (gnhsion). Used by Paul only. Contracted from genhsiov legitimately born : hence genuine. Paul calls Timothy his lawful son in the faith (<span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1ti+1:2">1 Timothy 1:2</span>). The kindred adverb gnhsiwv sincerely (A. V. naturally), occurs once, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="php+2:20">Philippians 2:20</span>. See note.</p>
