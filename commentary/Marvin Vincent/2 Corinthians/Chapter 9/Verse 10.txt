<p>Ministereth [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επιχορηγων] </span></span>. Rev., supplieth. See on add, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="2pe+1:5">2 Peter 1:5</span>. Both minister bread, etc. Construe bread with supplieth, as Rev., supplieth seed to the sow and bread for food. </p>
<p>Minister and multiply [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">χορηγησαι και πληθυναι] </span></span>. The correct reading is the future, corhghsei kai plhqunei shall supply and multiply. The fruits [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τα γενηματα] </span></span>. Lit., what has been begotten or born. Used of men, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+3:7">Matthew 3:7</span>, A. V., generation, Rev., offspring. Elsewhere of fruits, as fruit of the vine, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mr+14:25">Mark 14:25</span>.</p>
