<p>Service [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">λειτουργιας] </span></span>. Also rendered ministry or ministration (A. V. and Rev.), as <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="lu+1:23">Luke 1:23</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="heb+8:6">Hebrews 8:6</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="heb+9:21">Hebrews 9:21</span>. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="lu+1:23">Luke 1:23</span>. The word is used of this same contribution, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ro+14:7">Romans 14:7</span>. </p>
<p>Supplieth [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εστιν προσαναπληρουσα] </span></span>. Lit., fills up by adding to. Only here and ch. 11 9. Supplementing what the saints lack. </p>
<p>Through many thanksgivings. The need of the poor is filled, like an empty vessel, to the brim, and the supply overflows in the thanksgiving which it calls out. Thus christian beneficence does a double work, in giving relief and in generating thankfulness.</p>
