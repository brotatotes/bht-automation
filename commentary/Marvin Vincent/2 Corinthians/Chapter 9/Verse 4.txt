<p>Confident boasting [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">υποστασει] </span></span>. Primarily something put under, foundation, ground; so substance (sub, stans, standing under), substantial quality : thence steadiness, confidence. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="heb+3:14">Hebrews 3:14</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="heb+11:1">Hebrews 11:1</span>. In the Septuagint the word represents fifteen different Hebrew words.</p>
