<p>Go before. Notice the thrice repeated before, emphasizing the injunction to have everything ready before Paul 's arrival. </p>
<p>Make up beforehand [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">προκαταρτισωσιν] </span></span>. Adjust. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+4:21">Matthew 4:21</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+21:16">Matthew 21:16</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="lu+6:40">Luke 6:40</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1pe+5:10">1 Peter 5:10</span>. </p>
<p>Bounty [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ευλογιαν] </span></span>. Lit., blessing. In this sense only here in the New Testament. In the Septuagint indifferently of gift or blessing. See <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ge+33:11">Genesis 33:11</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="jude+1:15">Jude 1:15</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="eze+34:26">Ezekiel 34:26</span>. In <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="pr+11:25">Proverbs 11:25</span>, liberal soul is rendered by Sept., eujlogoumenh blessed. </p>
<p>Whereof ye had notice before [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">προεπηγγελημενην] </span></span>. Rev., better, your afore - promised bounty. The bounty promised by you, or by me on your behalf.</p>
