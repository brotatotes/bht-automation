<p>Which dwelleth [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">την μενουσαν] </span></span>. Rev., abideth. Enlarging on the idea of the truth : that which abideth. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1jo+4:9">1 John 4:9</span>, on the phrase to abide in, see on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1jo+2:6">1 John 2:6</span>. </p>
<p>Shall be with us [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μεθ ημων εσται] </span></span>. With us has the emphatic position in the sentence : and with us it shall be. Note the change from abideth in to shall be with, and see on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="joh+14:16">John 14:16</span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="joh+14:17">John 14:17</span>.</p>
