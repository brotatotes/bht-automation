Which dwelleth [την μενουσαν] . Rev., abideth. Enlarging on the idea of the truth : that which abideth. See on 1 John 4:9, on the phrase to abide in, see on 1 John 2:6. Shall be with us [μεθ ημων εσται] . With us has the emphatic position in the sentence : and with us it shall be. Note the change from abideth in to shall be with, and see on John 14:16, John 14:17.
