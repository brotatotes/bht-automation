I rejoiced. Expressions of thankful joy are common in the Pauline salutations. See Romans, 1 Corinthians, Ephesians, Philippians, Colossians, 1 Thessalonians, 2 Thessalonians, Philemon. Greatly [λιαν] . The word is found in John's writings only here and 3 John 1:3. I found [ευρηκα] . See on John 1:41. Rev., I have found. Of thy children [εκ των τεκνων] . The rendering is obscure. Rev., rightly, supplies certain. Compare John 16:17. In truth [εν αληθεια] . Compare 3 John 1:3. See on 1 John 1:8.
