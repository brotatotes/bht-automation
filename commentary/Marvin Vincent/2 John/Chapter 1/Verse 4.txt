<p>I rejoiced. Expressions of thankful joy are common in the Pauline salutations. See Romans, 1 Corinthians, Ephesians, Philippians, Colossians, 1 Thessalonians, 2 Thessalonians, Philemon. </p>
<p>Greatly [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">λιαν] </span></span>. The word is found in John's writings only here and <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="3jo+1:3">3 John 1:3</span>. </p>
<p>I found [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ευρηκα] </span></span>. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="joh+1:41">John 1:41</span>. Rev., I have found. </p>
<p>Of thy children [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εκ των τεκνων] </span></span>. The rendering is obscure. Rev., rightly, supplies certain. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="joh+16:17">John 16:17</span>. </p>
<p>In truth [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εν αληθεια] </span></span>. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="3jo+1:3">3 John 1:3</span>. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1jo+1:8">1 John 1:8</span>.</p>
