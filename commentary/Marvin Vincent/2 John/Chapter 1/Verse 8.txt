<p>We lose [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">απολεσωμεν] </span></span>. The best texts read ajpoleshte, ye lose. So Rev, with destroy in margin. The best texts read ajpolabhte ye receive. The compounded preposition ajpo, has the force of back : receive back from God. </p>
