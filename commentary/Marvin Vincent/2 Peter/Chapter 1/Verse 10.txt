<p>The rather [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μαλλον] </span></span>. The adverb belongs rather with the verb give diligence. Render, as Rev., give the more diligence. </p>
<p>Brethren [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αδελφοι] </span></span>. The only instance of this form of address in Peter, who commonly uses beloved. </p>
<p>Fall [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πταισητε] </span></span>. Lit., stumble, and so Rev. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="jas+3:2">James 3:2</span>.</p>
