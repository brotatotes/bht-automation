<p>In the knowledge [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εν επιγνωσει] </span></span>. The compound expressing full knowledge, and so common in Paul 's writings. </p>
<p>Our Lord [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κυριου ημων] </span></span>. The word Lord in the second epistle is always used of God, unless Christ or Savior is added.</p>
