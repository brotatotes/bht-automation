Is [γινεται] . More literally, arises or originates. Private [ιδιας] . See on ver. 3. His own. Rev., special, in margin. Interpretation [επιλυσεως] . Only here in New Testament. Compare the cognate verb expounded (Mark 4:34) and determined (Acts 19:39). The usual word is eJrmhneia (1 Corinthians 12:10; 1 Corinthians 14:26). Literally, it means loosening, untying, as of hard knots of scripture.
