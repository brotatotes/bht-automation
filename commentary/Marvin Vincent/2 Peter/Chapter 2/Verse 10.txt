<p>Go after the flesh. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="jude+1:7">Jude 1:7</span>. </p>
<p>Of uncleanness [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μιασμου] </span></span>. Only here in New Testament. See on defilements, ver. 20. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="jude+1:8">Jude 1:8</span>. </p>
<p>Despise government. Rev., dominion. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="jude+1:8">Jude 1:8</span>. </p>
<p>Presumptuous [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τολμηται] </span></span>. Only here in New Testament. Lit., darers. Rev., daring. </p>
<p>Self - willed [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αυθαδεις] </span></span>. Only here and <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="tit+1:7">Titus 1:7</span>. From aujtov, self, and hdomai, to delight in. Therefore a self - loving spirit. </p>
<p>They tremble [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τρεμουσιν] </span></span>. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mr+5:33">Mark 5:33</span>. An uncommon word in the New Testament. <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="lu+8:47">Luke 8:47</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+9:6">Acts 9:6</span>. </p>
<p>Dignities [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δοξας] </span></span>. Lit., glories. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="jude+1:8">Jude 1:8</span>. Probably angelic powers : note the reference to the angels immediately following, as in <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="jude+1:9">Jude 1:9</span> to Michael. They defy the spiritual powers though knowing their might.</p>
