Pollutions [μιασματα] . Only here in New Testament. Compare ver. 2 Peter 2:10The word is transcribed in miasma. Entangled [εμπλακεντες] . Only here and 2 Timothy 2:4. The same metaphor occurs in Aeschylus (" Prometheus ") : "For not on a sudden or in ignorance will ye be entangled [εμπλεχθησεσθε] by your folly in an impervious net of Ate (destruction)."
