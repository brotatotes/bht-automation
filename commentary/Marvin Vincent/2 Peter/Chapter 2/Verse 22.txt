According to the true proverb [το της αληθους παριοιμιας] . Lit., that of the true proverb, or the matter of the proverb. For a similar construction see Matthew 21:21, that of the fig - tree; Matthew 8:33, the things of those possessed. On proverb, see notes on Matthew 13:3. Vomit [εξεραμα] . Only here in New Testament. Wallowing [κυλισμον] . Only here in New Testament. Mire [βορβορου] . Only here in New Testament. This use of dogs and swine together recalls Matthew 7:6.
