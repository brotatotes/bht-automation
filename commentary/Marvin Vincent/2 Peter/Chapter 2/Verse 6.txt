<p>Turning into ashes [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τεφρωσας] </span></span>. Only here in New Testament. </p>
<p>Having made them an example [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">υποδειγμα τεθεικως] </span></span>. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1pe+2:21">1 Peter 2:21</span>. The word for example is condemned as unclassical by the Attic grammarians, and paradeigma is substituted, which means, properly, a sculptor's or a painter's model, or an architect's plan.</p>
