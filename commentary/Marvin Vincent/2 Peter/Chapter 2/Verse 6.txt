Turning into ashes [τεφρωσας] . Only here in New Testament. Having made them an example [υποδειγμα τεθεικως] . Compare 1 Peter 2:21. The word for example is condemned as unclassical by the Attic grammarians, and paradeigma is substituted, which means, properly, a sculptor's or a painter's model, or an architect's plan.
