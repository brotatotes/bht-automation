<p>Godly [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ευσεβεις] </span></span>. Used by Peter only. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+10:2">Acts 10:2</span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+10:7">Acts 10:7</span>. See on ch. <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="2pe+1:3">2 Peter 1:3</span>. Temptation [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πειρασμου] </span></span>. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1pe+1:6">1 Peter 1:6</span>. </p>
<p>To reserve [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τηρειν] </span></span>. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1pe+1:4">1 Peter 1:4</span>. Rev., keep, is not an improvement. </p>
<p>To be punished [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κολαζομενους] </span></span>. Only here and <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+4:21">Acts 4:21</span>, where the narrative probably came from Peter. The participle here is, lit., being punished, and therefore the A. V. is wrong. Rev., rightly, under punishment. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+25:46">Matthew 25:46</span>.</p>
