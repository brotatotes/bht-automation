<p>The world that then was [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ο τοτε κοσμος] </span></span>. Lit., the then world. The word for world is literally order, and denotes the perfect system of the material universe. </p>
<p>Being overflowed [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κατακλυσθεις] </span></span>. Only here in New Testament. Cataclysm is derived from it.</p>
