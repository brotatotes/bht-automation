<p><span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="2co+1:8">2 Corinthians 1:8</span>. Uper never in N. T. in a formula of swearing. </p>
<p>Gathering together [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επισυναγωγης] </span></span>. Only here and <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="heb+10:25">Hebrews 10:25</span>. See <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+24:31">Matthew 24:31</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mr+13:27">Mark 13:27</span>; comp. 2 Macc. ii. 7.</p>
