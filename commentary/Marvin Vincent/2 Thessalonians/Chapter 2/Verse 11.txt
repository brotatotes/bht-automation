<p>Strong delusion [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ενεργειαν πλανης] </span></span>. Rev., literally and correctly, a working of error. See on working ver. 9. The phrase is unique in N. T. It means an active power of misleading. For planh error which shows itself in action, see on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1th+2:3">1 Thessalonians 2:3</span>. </p>
<p>A lie [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τω ψευδει] </span></span>. Properly, the lie. The article gives the generic sense, falsehood in all its forms. Comp. <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="joh+8:44">John 8:44</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ro+1:25">Romans 1:25</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="eph+4:25">Ephesians 4:25</span>. Comp. the contrast of truth and unrighteousness in ver. 12. All wrongdoing has an element of falsity.</p>
