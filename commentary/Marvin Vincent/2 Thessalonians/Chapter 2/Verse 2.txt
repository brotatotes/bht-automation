<p>Shaken [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">σαλευθηναι] </span></span>. From salov the tossing or swell of the sea. See <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="lu+21:25">Luke 21:25</span>. Comp. <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+11:7">Matthew 11:7</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+24:29">Matthew 24:29</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+4:31">Acts 4:31</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="heb+12:26">Hebrews 12:26</span>. In mind [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">απο του νοος] </span></span>. More correctly, from your mind. Nouv signifies the judgment, sober sense. Comp. <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1co+14:15">1 Corinthians 14:15</span>, and see on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ro+7:23">Romans 7:23</span>. They are to "keep their heads" under the temptation to fanatical extravagances concerning the Lord 's appearing. </p>
<p>Be troubled [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">θρεισθαι] </span></span>. From qroov clamor, tumult. The meaning is be unsettled or thrown into confusion. </p>
<p>By spirit [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δια πνευματος] </span></span>. By prophetic utterances of individuals in Christian assemblies, claiming the authority of divine revelations. </p>
<p>By word [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δια λογου] </span></span>. Oral expressions falsely imputed to Paul. </p>
<p>By letter as from us [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δι επιστολης ως δι ημων] </span></span>. Const. as from us with word and letter. The reference is to a letter or letters forged in Paul 's name; not to the first Thessalonian Epistle, as misunderstood by the readers. </p>
<p>As that [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ως οτι] </span></span>. Indicating the contents of such communications. </p>
<p>Is at hand [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ενεστηκεν] </span></span>. Better than Rev. is now present. Lightfoot, happily, is imminent.</p>
