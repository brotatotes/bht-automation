<p>That is called God [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">λεγομενον θεον] </span></span>. Above the true God and the false gods. The opposer claims divine honors for himself. </p>
<p>That is worshipped [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">σεβασμα] </span></span>. An object of adoration, including things as well as persons. Only here and <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+17:23">Acts 17:23</span> on which see note under devotions. </p>
<p>Temple of God. According to some, a figure of the Christian Church. Others, the temple of Jerusalem. </p>
<p>Shewing [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αποδεικνυντα] </span></span>. Publicly asserting divine dignity. Rev. setting himself forth as God.</p>
