<p>What withholdeth [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">το κατεχον] </span></span>. Better restraineth. The verb means to hold fast, as <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="lu+8:15">Luke 8:15</span> : to hold back, as <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="lu+4:42">Luke 4:42</span>. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ro+1:18">Romans 1:18</span>. He refers to some power which hinders the revelation of the man of sin or Antichrist. </p>
<p>In his time [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εν τω αυτου καιρω] </span></span>. Better, in his own season, Not before his appointed season.</p>
