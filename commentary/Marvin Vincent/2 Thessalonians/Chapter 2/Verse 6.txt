What withholdeth [το κατεχον] . Better restraineth. The verb means to hold fast, as Luke 8:15 : to hold back, as Luke 4:42. See on Romans 1:18. He refers to some power which hinders the revelation of the man of sin or Antichrist. In his time [εν τω αυτου καιρω] . Better, in his own season, Not before his appointed season.
