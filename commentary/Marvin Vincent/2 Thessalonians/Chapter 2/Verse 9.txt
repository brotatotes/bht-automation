<p>After the working of Satan. The sense is that the coming of Antichrist proclaims itself to be according to the working of Satan by means of power, signs, etc. 'Energeia P o. power in exercise, used only of superhuman power. See <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="col+1:29">Colossians 1:29</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="col+2:12">Colossians 2:12</span>. </p>
<p>Signs and lying wonders [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">σημειοις και τερασιν ψευδους] </span></span>. Lit. signs and wonders of a lie. Of a lie characterizes the three words, power, signs, wonders. All bear the stamp of fraud. For signs and wonders see on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+24:24">Matthew 24:24</span>, and mighty works, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+11:20">Matthew 11:20</span>.</p>
