Finally [το λοιπον] . See on 1 Thessalonians 4:1. May have free course [τρεχη] . More literally, simply, and better, may run. Have swift progress through the world. An O. T. idea. See Psalms 147:15, and comp. Isaiah 55:11 and Acts 12:24. Be glorified [δοξαζηται] . Acknowledged in its true power and glory. Comp. John 12:28. The phrase the word of the Lord - be glorified, only here.
