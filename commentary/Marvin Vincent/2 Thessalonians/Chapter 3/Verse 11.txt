<p>Working not at all - busybodies [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μηδεν εργαζομενους - περιεργαζομενους] </span></span>. One of Paul 's frequent wordplays. See on reprobate mind, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ro+1:28">Romans 1:28</span>. Not busy, but busybodies. Periergazesqai (N. T. o.) is to bustle about a thing : here, to be officious in others' affairs. See on ta perierga curious arts, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+19:19">Acts 19:19</span>, and <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1ti+5:13">1 Timothy 5:13</span>.</p>
