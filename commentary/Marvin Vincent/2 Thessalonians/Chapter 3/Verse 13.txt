<p>Be not weary [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εντραπη] </span></span>. With one exception, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="lu+13:1">Luke 13:1</span>, only in Paul. To faint or lose heart. </p>
<p>Well doing [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">καλοποιουντες] </span></span>. N. T. o. According to the Greek idiom, doing well, be not weary. Not limited to works of charity, but including Christian conduct generally, as, for instance, steadily attending to their own business, ver. 12.</p>
