<p>The salutation of Paul with mine own hand [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ασπασμος τη εμη χειρι παυλου] </span></span>. Rev. properly, "the salutation of me Paul." The genitive of me is contained, according to a familiar Greek idiom, in the possessive pronoun my. Paul had apparently been employing an amanuensis. </p>
<p>In every epistle. Comp. <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1co+16:21">1 Corinthians 16:21</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="col+4:18">Colossians 4:18</span>. </p>
