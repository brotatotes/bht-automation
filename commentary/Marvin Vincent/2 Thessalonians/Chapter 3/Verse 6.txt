<p>Withdraw yourselves from [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">στελλεσθαι υμας απο] </span></span>. Stellesqai, P o. In the active voice, to place, arrange, equip : in the middle voice, to provide for, take care. See <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="2co+8:20">2 Corinthians 8:20</span>. Here with ajpo from, to place one's self away from. </p>
<p>Disorderly [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ατακτως] </span></span>. This adverb, the verb ajtaktew, and the adjective ataktov are found only in Paul, and only in the Thessalonian Epistles. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1th+5:14">1 Thessalonians 5:14</span>.</p>
