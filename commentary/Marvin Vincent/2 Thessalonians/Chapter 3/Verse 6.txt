Withdraw yourselves from [στελλεσθαι υμας απο] . Stellesqai, P o. In the active voice, to place, arrange, equip : in the middle voice, to provide for, take care. See 2 Corinthians 8:20. Here with ajpo from, to place one's self away from. Disorderly [ατακτως] . This adverb, the verb ajtaktew, and the adjective ataktov are found only in Paul, and only in the Thessalonian Epistles. See on 1 Thessalonians 5:14.
