<p>Therefore [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ουν] </span></span>. In view of what has been said in the previous chapter. Be strong [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ενδυναμου] </span></span>. In Paul, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ro+4:20">Romans 4:20</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="eph+6:10">Ephesians 6:10</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="php+4:13">Philippians 4:13</span>. Lit. be strengthened inwardly. </p>
<p>In the grace [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εν τη χαριτι] </span></span>. Grace is the inward source of strength. Comp. the association of grace and strength in <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="2co+12:9">2 Corinthians 12:9</span>.</p>
