<p>If we suffer we shall also reign with him [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ει υπομενομεν, και συνβασιλευσομεν] </span></span>. For suffer, rend. endure. Sunbasileuein to reign with, only here and <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1co+4:8">1 Corinthians 4:8</span>. Comp. <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="lu+19:17">Luke 19:17</span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="lu+19:19">Luke 19:19</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="lu+22:29">Luke 22:29</span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="lu+22:30">Luke 22:30</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ro+5:17">Romans 5:17</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="re+4:4">Revelation 4:4</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="re+5:10">Revelation 5:10</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="re+22:5">Revelation 22:5</span>. </p>
<p>If we deny him he also will deny us [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ει αρνησομεθα. κακεινος αρνησεται ημας] </span></span>. The verb P o. Him must be supplied. The meaning of the last clause is, will not acknowledge us as his own. Comp. <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="lu+9:26">Luke 9:26</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+10:33">Matthew 10:33</span>.</p>
