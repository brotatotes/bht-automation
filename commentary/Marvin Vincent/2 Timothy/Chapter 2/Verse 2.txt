Among many witnesses [δια πολλων μαρτυρων] . Dia through the medium of, and therefore in the presence of. Commit [παραθου] . As a trust or deposit [παραθηκη] . See on chapter 2 Timothy 1:12, 2 Timothy 1:14. In Paul only 1 Corinthians 10:27. Faithful [πιστοις] . Not believing, but trusty, as appears from the context. See on 1 John 1:9; Revelation 1:5; Revelation 3:14. Able [ικανοι] . In Pastorals only here. Very common in Luke and Acts : a few times in Paul. See on many, Romans 14:23.
