<p>Youthful lusts [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">νεωτερικας επιθυμιας] </span></span>. Newterikov youthful, N. T. o. For ejpiqumia desire, lust, see on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mr+4:19">Mark 4:19</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1th+4:5">1 Thessalonians 4:5</span>. Such counsel from Paul to Timothy seems strange. </p>
<p>Follow [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">διωκε] </span></span>. Pursue. Stronger than follow. A favorite word with Paul to denote the pursuit of moral and spiritual ends. See <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ro+9:30">Romans 9:30</span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ro+9:31">Romans 9:31</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ro+12:13">Romans 12:13</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ro+1:0">Romans 1:0</span> Cor. xiv.. 1; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="php+3:12">Philippians 3:12</span>. </p>
<p>Peace [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ειρηνην] </span></span>. Not a distinct virtue in the list, but a consequence of the pursuit of the virtues enumerated. Const. with with them that call, etc. For peace with diwkein pursue, see <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ro+14:19">Romans 14:19</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="heb+12:14">Hebrews 12:14</span>, and <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ps+34:14">Psalms 34:14</span>, cit. <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1pe+3:11">1 Peter 3:11</span>. </p>
<p>Call on the Lord [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επικαλουμενων τον κυριον] </span></span>. A Pauline phrase, only here in Pastorals. See <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ro+10:12">Romans 10:12</span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ro+10:13">Romans 10:13</span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ro+10:14">Romans 10:14</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1co+1:2">1 Corinthians 1:2</span>. See also <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+2:21">Acts 2:21</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+9:14">Acts 9:14</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+22:16">Acts 22:16</span>. </p>
<p>Out of a pure heart [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εκ καθαρας καρδιας] </span></span>. Const. with call on the Lord. The phrase, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1ti+1:5">1 Timothy 1:5</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1pe+1:22">1 Peter 1:22</span>. Comp. <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+5:8">Matthew 5:8</span>.</p>
