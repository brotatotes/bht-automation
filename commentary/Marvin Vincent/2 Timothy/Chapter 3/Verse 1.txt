<p>This know [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τουτο γινωσκε] </span></span>. The phrase N. T. o. Comp. Paul 's ginwskein uJmav boulomai I would have you to know, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="php+1:12">Philippians 1:12</span>; and qelw de uJmav eiJudenai I would you should know, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1co+11:3">1 Corinthians 11:3</span>. </p>
<p>In the last days [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επ εσχαταις ημεραις] </span></span>. The phrase only here in Pastorals, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+2:17">Acts 2:17</span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="jas+5:3">James 5:3</span>. Similar expressions are ejn kairw ejscatw in the last season, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1pe+1:5">1 Peter 1:5</span> : ejp' ejscatou twn cronwn at the last of the times, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1pe+1:20">1 Peter 1:20</span> : ejp' ejscatou cronou at the last time, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="jude+1:18">Jude 1:18</span> ejp' ejscatwn twn hJmerwn at the last of the days, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="2pe+3:3">2 Peter 3:3</span> : ejn uJsteroiv kairoiv in the latter seasons, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1ti+4:1">1 Timothy 4:1</span>. The times immediately preceding Christ 's second appearing are meant. Comp. <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="heb+1:2">Hebrews 1:2</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="jas+5:3">James 5:3</span>. </p>
<p>Perilous times [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">καιροι χαλεποι] </span></span>. Only here and <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+8:28">Matthew 8:28</span>. Lit. hard times : schwere Zeiten. Kairov. denotes a definite, specific season. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+12:1">Matthew 12:1</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+1:17">Acts 1:17</span>. </p>
<p>Shall come [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ενστησονται] </span></span>. Or will set in. Mostly in Paul. Only here in Pastorals. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ga+1:4">Galatians 1:4</span>.</p>
