Who will live [οι θελοντες ζην] . Whose will is to live, or who are bent on living. Godly [ευσεβως] . Only here and Titus 2:12. Comp. kat' eujsebeian according to godliness, 1 Timothy 6:3; Titus 1:1; and ejn pash eujsebeia in all godliness, 1 Timothy 2:2. See also 1 Timothy 4:7; 1 Timothy 6:11, and on godliness, 1 Timothy 2:2. Shall suffer persecution [διωχθησονται] . In this sense only here in Pastorals.
