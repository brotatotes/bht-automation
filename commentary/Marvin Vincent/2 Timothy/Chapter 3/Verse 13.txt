<p>Seducers [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">γοντες] </span></span>. N. T. o. Better, impostors or deceivers. From goan to howl. Originally, one who chants spells; a wizard, sorcerer. Hence, a cheat. </p>
<p>Shall wax worse and worse [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">προκοψουσιν επι το χειρον] </span></span>. Lit. shall proceed to the worse. The formula, Past o. Comp. verse 9 and chapter <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="2ti+2:16">2 Timothy 2:16</span>. Deceiving [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πλανωντες] </span></span>. Properly, leading astray. See on planoiv seducing, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1ti+4:1">1 Timothy 4:1</span>.</p>
