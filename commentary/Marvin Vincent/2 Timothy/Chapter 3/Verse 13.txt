Seducers [γοντες] . N. T. o. Better, impostors or deceivers. From goan to howl. Originally, one who chants spells; a wizard, sorcerer. Hence, a cheat. Shall wax worse and worse [προκοψουσιν επι το χειρον] . Lit. shall proceed to the worse. The formula, Past o. Comp. verse 9 and chapter 2 Timothy 2:16. Deceiving [πλανωντες] . Properly, leading astray. See on planoiv seducing, 1 Timothy 4:1.
