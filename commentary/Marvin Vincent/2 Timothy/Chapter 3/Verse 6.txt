<p>Of this sort [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εκ τουτων] </span></span>. Lit. of these. The formula often in Paul. Which creep [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">οι ενδυνοντες] </span></span>. N. T. o. Thrust themselves into. Comp. <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="jude+1:4">Jude 1:4</span>, pareiseduhsan crept in privily (see note); <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="2pe+2:1">2 Peter 2:1</span> (note), pareisaxousin shall privily bring in; and <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ga+2:4">Galatians 2:4</span>, pareisaktouv brought in by stealth. </p>
<p>Lead captive [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αιχμαλωτιζοντες] </span></span>. Only here in Pastorals. See on captives, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="lu+4:18">Luke 4:18</span>; and <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="2co+10:5">2 Corinthians 10:5</span>. </p>
<p>Silly women [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">γυναικαρια] </span></span>. N. T. o. o LXX Silly is expressed by the contemptuous diminutive. Comp. Vulg. mulierculas. </p>
<p>Laden [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">σεσωρευμενα] </span></span>. Only here and <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ro+12:20">Romans 12:20</span>, citation. In LXX, see Judith 14 11, of loading a wagon with the property of Holofernes. It implies heaped up; heavily laden. </p>
<p>Led away [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αγομενα] </span></span>. Away is superfluous. It is only an inference. The meaning is under the direction of. Comp. <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ro+8:14">Romans 8:14</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ga+5:18">Galatians 5:18</span>. Divers [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ποικιλαις] </span></span>. In Pastorals only here and <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="tit+3:3">Titus 3:3</span>. Lit. variegated, of different tints. See on manifold wisdom, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="eph+3:10">Ephesians 3:10</span>. <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="eph+3:141">Ephesians 3:141</span></p>
