<p>Ever learning. From any one who will teach them. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1ti+5:13">1 Timothy 5:13</span>. It is a graphic picture of a large class, by no means extinct, who are caught and led by the instructions of itinerant religious quacks. Never able [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μηδεποτε δυναμενα] </span></span>. Because they have not the right motive, and because they apply to false teachers. Ellicott thinks that there is in dunamena a hint of an unsuccessful endeavor, in better moments, to attain to the truth.</p>
