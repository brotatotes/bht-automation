<p>Greatly withstood [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">λιαν αντεστη] </span></span>. Comp. chapter <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="2ti+3:8">2 Timothy 3:8</span>, and <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ga+2:11">Galatians 2:11</span>. This may refer to the occurrences at Ephesus (<span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+19:33">Acts 19:33</span>), or to Alexander 's attitude during Paul 's trial. The former is more probable. Lian greatly, not in Paul, except in the compound uJperlian, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="2co+11:5">2 Corinthians 11:5</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="2co+12:11">2 Corinthians 12:11</span>. Only here in Pastorals. Mostly in Synoptic Gospels.</p>
