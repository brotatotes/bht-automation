<p>Strengthened [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ενεδυναμωσεν] </span></span>. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1ti+1:12">1 Timothy 1:12</span>. </p>
<p>The preaching [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">το κηρυγμα] </span></span>. Better, the message (par excellence), the gospel message. Usually with a defining word, as of Jonah; of Jesus Christ; my preaching; our preaching. Absolutely, as here, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1co+1:21">1 Corinthians 1:21</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="tit+1:3">Titus 1:3</span>. </p>
<p>Might be fully known [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πληροφορηθη] </span></span>. See on verse 5. Lit. might be fulfilled; fully carried out by being proclaimed before rulers in the capital of the world. Comp. <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ro+14:19">Romans 14:19</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+23:11">Acts 23:11</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+28:31">Acts 28:31</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="php+1:12-14">Philippians 1:12-14</span>. </p>
<p>Out of the mouth of the lion [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εκ στοματος λεοντος] </span></span>. Figurative expression for danger of death. Comp. <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1co+14:32">1 Corinthians 14:32</span>. As usual, all manner of special references have been imagined : the lions of the amphitheatre; Nero; the chief accuser; the Jews; the Devil.</p>
