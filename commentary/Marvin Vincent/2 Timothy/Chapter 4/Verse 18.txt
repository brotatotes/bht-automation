Every evil work [εκ εργου πονηρου] . Every design and attempt against him and his work. Ponhrov evil cannot be limited to evil on its active side. See on 1 Corinthians 5:13. The word is connected at the root with penesqai to be needy, and ponein to toil; and this connection opens a glimpse of that sentiment which associated badness with a poor and toiling condition. The word means originally full of or oppressed by labors; thence, that which brings annoyance or toil.
