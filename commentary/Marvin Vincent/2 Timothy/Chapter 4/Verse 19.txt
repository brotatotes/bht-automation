Salute [ασπασαι] . Very often in Paul. The singular only here and Titus 3:15. Prisca and Aquila. They appear in Corinth, Acts 18:2, Acts 18:3; in Ephesus, Acts 18:18, Acts 18:26; 1 Corinthians 16:19. Onesiphorus. Profit - bringer. Comp. chapter 2 Timothy 1:16. One of the punning names so common among slaves. Comp. Chresimus, Chrestus, Onesimus, Symphorus, all of which signify useful or helpful.
