Erastus. In Acts 19:22, sent by Paul with Timothy to Macedonia from Ephesus. Romans 16:23, the city - treasurer who sends salutations. He cannot be certainly identified with the one mentioned here. The writer merely selects names of well - known companions of Paul. Trophimus. See Acts 22:4; Acts 21:9. Sick [ασθενουντα] . By Paul mostly in a moral sense, as weak in the faith, Romans 4:19; the law was weak, Romans 8:3; the weak brother, 1 Corinthians 8:11. Of bodily sickness, Philippians 2:26, Philippians 2:27.
