The elder. See on 2 John 1:0. Gaius. The name occurs several times in the New Testament, as Acts 19:29; Acts 20:4; Romans 16:23; 1 Corinthians 1:14. The person addressed here cannot be identified. The well - beloved. Rev., the beloved. In the Greek order the name comes first. Gaius the beloved. In the truth [εν αληθεια] . Rev., properly, omitting the article, in truth. See on 2 John 1:4.
