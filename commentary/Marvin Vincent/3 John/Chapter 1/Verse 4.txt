<p>Joy [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">χαραν] </span></span>. The texts vary; some reading carin grace or favor from God, on which see <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="2jo+1:3">2 John 1:3</span>. Note the Greek order : greater joy than this have I not. </p>
<p>My children [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τα εμα τεκνα] </span></span>. Lit., mine own children. </p>
<p>Walk [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">περιπατουντα] </span></span>. Rev., rightly, walking. The participle expresses something habitual.</p>
