<p>The Church [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εκκλησιας] </span></span>. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+16:18">Matthew 16:18</span>. </p>
<p>If thou bring forward on their journey [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">προπεμψας] </span></span>. Lit., having sent forward. The aorist tense represents the act as accomplished. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+14:3">Acts 14:3</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="tit+3:13">Titus 3:13</span>. Rev., set forward. </p>
<p>After a godly sort [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αξιως του θεου] </span></span>. Lit., worthily of God. So Rev. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1th+2:12">1 Thessalonians 2:12</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="col+1:10">Colossians 1:10</span>. </p>
<p>Thou shalt do well [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">καλως ποιησεις] </span></span>. For the phrase, see <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+10:33">Acts 10:33</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="php+4:14">Philippians 4:14</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="jas+2:8">James 2:8</span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="jas+2:19">James 2:19</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="2pe+1:19">2 Peter 1:19</span>. Rev., renders the whole : whom thou wilt do well to set forward on their journey worthily of God.</p>
