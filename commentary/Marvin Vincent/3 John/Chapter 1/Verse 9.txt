<p>I wrote unto the Church. The best texts insert ti somewhat, which indicates that the apostle did not regard the communication as specially important. </p>
<p>Diotrephes [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">διοτρεφης] </span></span>. The name is from Diov of Zeus (Jove), and trefw to nourish, and means Jove - nursed. </p>
<p>Who loveth to have the pre - eminence [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ο φιλοπρωτευων] </span></span>. From the adjective filoprwtov fond of being first. The word occurs here only.</p>
