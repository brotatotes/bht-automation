<p>A Sabbath - day's journey [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">σαββατου εχον οδον] </span></span>. Lit., having a Sabbath 's way. The way conceived as belonging to the mountain; connected with it in reference to the neighborhood of Jerusalem. A Sabbath - day's journey, according to Jewish tradition, was about three - quarters of a mile. It was the supposed distance between the camp and the tabernacle in the wilderness (<span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="jos+3:4">Joshua 3:4</span>.)</p>
