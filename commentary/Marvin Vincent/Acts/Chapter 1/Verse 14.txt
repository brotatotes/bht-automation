<p>Continued [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ησαν προσκαρτερουντες] </span></span>. Participle and finite verb as above. The verb is from karterov, strong, stanch, and means originally to persist obstinately in. In this sense here, and in <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ro+12:12">Romans 12:12</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ro+13:6">Romans 13:6</span>. Hence to adhere firmly to. So in <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mr+3:9">Mark 3:9</span>, "that a small ship should wait on him;" i e., keep near at hand. The idea of steady persistence is supplied by the Rev., steadfastly. </p>
