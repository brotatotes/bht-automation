Numbered [κατηριθμημενος] . Only here in New Testament. With [συν] . The best texts read ejn, among. So Rev. Obtained [ελαχε] . Strictly, "received by lot." Rev., better, received. Compare Luke 1:9. In classical Greek, of receiving public magistracies. Part [τον κληρον] . The A. V. does not give the force of the article, the lot which was his. So Rev., "his portion :" lit., lot.. Ministry. See on minister, Matthew 20:26. Compare bishopric, ver. 20.
