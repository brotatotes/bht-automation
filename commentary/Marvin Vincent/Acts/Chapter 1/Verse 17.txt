<p>Numbered [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κατηριθμημενος] </span></span>. Only here in New Testament. </p>
<p>With [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">συν] </span></span>. The best texts read ejn, among. So Rev. </p>
<p>Obtained [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ελαχε] </span></span>. Strictly, "received by lot." Rev., better, received. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="lu+1:9">Luke 1:9</span>. In classical Greek, of receiving public magistracies. Part [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τον κληρον] </span></span>. The A. V. does not give the force of the article, the lot which was his. So Rev., "his portion :" lit., lot.. </p>
<p>Ministry. See on minister, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+20:26">Matthew 20:26</span>. Compare bishopric, ver. 20.</p>
