<p>Purchased [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εκτησατο] </span></span>. See on possess, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="lu+18:12">Luke 18:12</span>. Better, as Rev., obtained. Judas did not purchase the field, but the priests did with the money which he returned to them (<span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+27:7">Matthew 27:7</span>). The expression means merely that the field was purchased with the money of Judas. </p>
<p>Falling headlong [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πρηνης γενομενος] </span></span>. Lit., having become headlong. </p>
<p>He burst asunder [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ελακησε] </span></span>. Only here in New Testament. Lit., to crack, to burst with a noise. So Homer, of the bones cracking beneath a blow (" Iliad, "13, 616). Compare Aristophanes," Clouds, " 410.</p>
