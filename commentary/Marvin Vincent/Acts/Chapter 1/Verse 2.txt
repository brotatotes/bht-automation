<p>Had given commandment [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εντειλαμενος] </span></span>. Special injunctions or charges. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+4:6">Matthew 4:6</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mr+13:34">Mark 13:34</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="heb+11:22">Hebrews 11:22</span>. </p>
<p>Through the Holy Ghost. Construe with had given commandment : by means of the Holy Spirit, which inspired him. Not, as some interpreters, with whom he had chosen.</p>
