<p>Habitation [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επαυλις] </span></span>. Only here in New Testament. The word is used in classical Greek of a place for cattle. So Herodotus (i., 111) : "The herdsman took the child in his arms, and went back the way he had come, till he reached the fold" [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επαυλιν] </span></span>. Also of a farm - building, a country - house.. </p>
<p>Bishopric [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επισκοπην] </span></span>. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1pe+2:12">1 Peter 2:12</span>. Rev., better, office, with overseership in margin. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="lu+19:44">Luke 19:44</span>. </p>
<p>Another [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ετερος] </span></span>. And different person. See on ch. <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+2:4">Acts 2:4</span>.</p>
