Habitation [επαυλις] . Only here in New Testament. The word is used in classical Greek of a place for cattle. So Herodotus (i., 111) : "The herdsman took the child in his arms, and went back the way he had come, till he reached the fold" [επαυλιν] . Also of a farm - building, a country - house.. Bishopric [επισκοπην] . See on 1 Peter 2:12. Rev., better, office, with overseership in margin. Compare Luke 19:44. Another [ετερος] . And different person. See on ch. Acts 2:4.
