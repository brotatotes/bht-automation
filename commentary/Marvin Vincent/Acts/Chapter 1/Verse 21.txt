<p>Went in and went out. An expression for constant intercourse. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="de+18:19">Deuteronomy 18:19</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ps+121:8">Psalms 121:8</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="joh+10:9">John 10:9</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+9:28">Acts 9:28</span>. Among us [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εφ ημας] </span></span>. The margin of Rev., over us, i e., as our head, is a sound rendering, and supported by <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+25:21">Matthew 25:21</span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+25:23">Matthew 25:23</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="lu+9:1">Luke 9:1</span>. The rendering before, in the presence of, occurs <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+10:18">Matthew 10:18</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="lu+21:12">Luke 21:12</span>.</p>
