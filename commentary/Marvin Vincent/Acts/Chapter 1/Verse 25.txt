<p>That he may take part [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">λαβειν τον κληρον] </span></span>. Lit., to take the lot. But the best texts read ton topon, the place. Rev., to take the place. By transgression fell [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">παρεβη] </span></span>. See on trespasses, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+6:14">Matthew 6:14</span>. The rendering of the A. V. is explanatory. Rev., better, fell away. </p>
<p>His own place. Compare "the place in this ministry." Ton idion, his own, is stronger than the simple possessive pronoun. It is the place which was peculiarly his, as befitting his awful sin - Gehenna.</p>
