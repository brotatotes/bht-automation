<p>Not so [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μηδαμως] </span></span>. Stronger : by no means. "With that simple and audacious self - confidence which in his (Peter's) character was so singularly mingled with fits of timidity and depression, he boldly corrects the voice which orders him, and reminds the divine Interlocutor that he must, so to speak, have made an oversight" (Farrar, " Life and Works of Paul "). Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+16:22">Matthew 16:22</span>. </p>
<p>Common [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κοινον] </span></span>. Unholy.</p>
