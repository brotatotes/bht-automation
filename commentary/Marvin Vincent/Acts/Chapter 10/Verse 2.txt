Devout [ευσεβης] . See on godliness, 2 Peter 1:3. Prayed [δεομενος] . See on prayers, Luke 5:33. " Unheard by all but angel ears The good Cornelius knelt alone, Nor dream 'd his prayers and tears Would help a world undone. "The while upon his terrac'd roof The lov'd apostle to his Lord, In silent thought aloof For heavenly vision soared." Keble, Christian Year.
