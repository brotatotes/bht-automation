<p>Four days ago [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">απο τεταρτης ημερας</span></span>] Lit., from the fourth day; reckoning backward from the day on which he was speaking. </p>
<p>I was fasting, and. The best texts omit. </p>
<p>At the ninth hour I prayed [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">την εννατην προσευχομενος] </span></span>. Lit., praying during the ninth hour. With the omission of I was fasting, and, the rendering is as Rev., Four days ago, until this hour, I was keeping the ninth hour of prayer. 17</p>
