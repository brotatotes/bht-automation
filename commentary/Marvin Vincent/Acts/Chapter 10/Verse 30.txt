Four days ago [απο τεταρτης ημερας] Lit., from the fourth day; reckoning backward from the day on which he was speaking. I was fasting, and. The best texts omit. At the ninth hour I prayed [την εννατην προσευχομενος] . Lit., praying during the ninth hour. With the omission of I was fasting, and, the rendering is as Rev., Four days ago, until this hour, I was keeping the ninth hour of prayer. 17
