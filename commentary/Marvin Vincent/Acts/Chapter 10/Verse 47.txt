<p>Water [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">το υδωρ] </span></span>. Note the article : the water; co - ordinating the water with the Spirit (see <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1jo+5:8">1 John 5:8</span>), and designating water as the recognized and customary element of baptism. </p>
