<p>Purpose [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">προθεσει] </span></span>. Originally, placing in public; setting before. Hence of the shew - bread, the loaves set forth before the Lord (see on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mr+2:26">Mark 2:26</span>). Something set before one as an object of attainment : a purpose.</p>
