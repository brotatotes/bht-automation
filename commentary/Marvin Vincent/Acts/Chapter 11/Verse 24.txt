<p>Good [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αγαθος] </span></span>. More than strictly upright. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ro+5:7">Romans 5:7</span>, where it is distinguished from dikaiov, just or righteous. "His benevolence effectually prevented him censuring anything that might be new or strange in these preachers to the Gentiles, and caused him to rejoice in their success" (Gloag).</p>
