That time [εκεινον τον καιρον] . More correctly, that juncture. See on ch. Acts 1:7. The date is A. D. 44. Herod the king. Called also Agrippa, and commonly known as Herod Agrippa I, the grandson of Herod the Great. Stretched forth his hands [επεβαλεν τας χειρας] . Lit. laid on his hands. The A. V. is wrong, and so is the Rev. Render, laid hands on certain of the church to afflict them. Vex [κακωσαι] . Vex is used in the older and stronger sense of torment or oppress. See Exodus 22:21; Numbers 25:17; Matthew 14:22. Its modern usage relates rather to petty annoyances. Rev., better, afflict.
