<p>Constantly affirmed [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">διισχυριζετο] </span></span>. Better, confidently affirmed; constant is used in its older sense of consistent. The verb contains two ideas : strong assertion [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ισχυς] </span></span>, and holding to the assertion through all contradiction [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δια] </span></span>; hence, she strongly and consistently asserted. </p>
<p>Angel. Guardian angel, according to the popular belief among the Jews that every individual has his guardian angel, who may, on occasion, assume a visible appearance resembling that of the person whose destiny is committed to him.</p>
