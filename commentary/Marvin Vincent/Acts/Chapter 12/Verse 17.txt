<p>Beckoning [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κατασεισας] </span></span>. Lit., having shaken downward with his hand, in order to bespeak silence and attention. It was a familiar gesture of Paul. See ch. <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+21:40">Acts 21:40</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+26:1">Acts 26:1</span>.</p>
