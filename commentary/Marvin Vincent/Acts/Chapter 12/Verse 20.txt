<p>Highly displeased [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">θυμομαχων] </span></span>. Originally, to fight desperately : but as there is no record of any war of Herod with the Tyrians and Sidonians, the word is to be taken in the sense of the A. V. Only here in New Testament. </p>
<p>Chamberlain [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τον επι του κοιτωνος] </span></span>. Lit., the one over the bedchamber.</p>
