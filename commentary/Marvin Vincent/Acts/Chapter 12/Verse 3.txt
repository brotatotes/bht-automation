<p>He proceeded to take [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">προσεθετο συλλαβειν] </span></span>. Rev., seize. Lit., he added to take. A Hebrew form of expression. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="lu+19:11">Luke 19:11</span>, he added and spake; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="lu+20:12">Luke 20:12</span>, again he sent a third; lit., he added to send.</p>
