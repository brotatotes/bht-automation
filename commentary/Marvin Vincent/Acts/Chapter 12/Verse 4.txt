<p>Quaternions. A quaternion was a body of four soldiers; so that there were sixteen guards, four for each of the four night - watches. </p>
<p>The passover. The whole seven days of the feast. </p>
<p>Bring him forth [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αναγαγαγειν αυτον] </span></span>. Lit., lead him up; i e., to the elevated place where the tribunal stood, to pronounce sentence of death before the people. See <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="joh+19:13">John 19:13</span>.</p>
