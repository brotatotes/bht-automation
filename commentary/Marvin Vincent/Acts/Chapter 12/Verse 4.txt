Quaternions. A quaternion was a body of four soldiers; so that there were sixteen guards, four for each of the four night - watches. The passover. The whole seven days of the feast. Bring him forth [αναγαγαγειν αυτον] . Lit., lead him up; i e., to the elevated place where the tribunal stood, to pronounce sentence of death before the people. See John 19:13.
