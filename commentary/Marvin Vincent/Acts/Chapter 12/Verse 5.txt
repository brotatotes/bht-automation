<p>Without ceasing [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εκτενης] </span></span>. Wrong. The word means earnest. See on fervently, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1pe+1:22">1 Peter 1:22</span>; and compare instantly, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+26:7">Acts 26:7</span>; more earnestly, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="lu+22:44">Luke 22:44</span>; fervent, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1pe+4:8">1 Peter 4:8</span>. The idea of continuance is, however, expressed here by the finite verb with the participle. Very literally, prayer was arising earnest.</p>
