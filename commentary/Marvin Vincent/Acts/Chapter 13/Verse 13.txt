Loosed [αναχθεντες] . See on Luke 8:22. Paul and his company [οι περι τον παυλον] . Lit., those around Paul. In later writers, used to denote the principal person alone, as John 11:19, came to Mary and Martha; where the Greek literally reads, came to the women around Mary and Martha. Paul, and not Barnabas, now appears as the principal person.
