<p>Loosed [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αναχθεντες] </span></span>. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="lu+8:22">Luke 8:22</span>. </p>
<p>Paul and his company [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">οι περι τον παυλον] </span></span>. Lit., those around Paul. In later writers, used to denote the principal person alone, as <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="joh+11:19">John 11:19</span>, came to Mary and Martha; where the Greek literally reads, came to the women around Mary and Martha. Paul, and not Barnabas, now appears as the principal person.</p>
