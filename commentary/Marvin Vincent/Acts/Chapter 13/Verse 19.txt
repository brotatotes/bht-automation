<p>Divided by lot [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κατεκληρονομησεν] </span></span>. The A. V. gives the literal rendering. The Rev., gave them their land for an inheritance, is correct, so far as the meaning inheritance is concerned (see on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1pe+1:4">1 Peter 1:4</span>), but does not give the sense of distribution which is contained in the word.</p>
