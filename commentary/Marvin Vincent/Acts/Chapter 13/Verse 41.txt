<p>Perish [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αφανισθητε] </span></span>. Lit., vanish. </p>
<p>Declare [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εκδιηγηται] </span></span>. Only here and ch. 14 3. See on shew, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="lu+8:39">Luke 8:39</span>. The word is a very strong expression for the fullest and clearest declaration : declare throughout.</p>
