<p>Religious [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">σεβομενων] </span></span>. Lit., worshipping. Compare ver. 50 and ch. 16 14. </p>
<p>Proselytes [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">προσηλυτων] </span></span>. Originally, one who arrives at a place; a stranger; thence of one who comes over to another faith.</p>
