<p>Honorable [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ευσχημονας] </span></span>. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mr+14:43">Mark 14:43</span>. Women of rank, or, as Rev., of honorable state. </p>
<p>Coasts [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">οριων] </span></span>. Not a good rendering, because it implies merely a sea - coast; whereas the word is a general one for boundaries.</p>
