Sorcerer [μαγον] . That the man was an impostor is told us in the next word, but not in this term. It is the word used of the wise men who came to the Savior 's cradle. See Matthew 2:1, Matthew 2:7, Matthew 2:16. Elymas was a magian; of what kind is shown by false prophet. See on Matthew 2:1. Bar - Jesus. Son of Jesus or Joshua.
