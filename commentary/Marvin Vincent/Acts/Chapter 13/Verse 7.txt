<p>The deputy [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ανθυπατω] </span></span>. Better, Rev., proconsul. See Introduction to Luke, on Luke's accuracy in designating public officers. </p>
<p>Sergius Paulus. Di Cesnola relates the discovery at Soli, which, next to Salamis, was the most important city in the island, of a slab with a Greek inscription containing the name of Paulus, proconsul. </p>
<p>Prudent [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">συνετω] </span></span>. Better, as Rev., a man of understanding. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+11:25">Matthew 11:25</span>.</p>
