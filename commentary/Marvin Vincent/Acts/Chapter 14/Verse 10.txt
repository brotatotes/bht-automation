<p>Upright [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ορθος] </span></span>. Only here and <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="heb+12:13">Hebrews 12:13</span>. Compare made straight, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="lu+13:13">Luke 13:13</span>, and see note there. </p>
<p>Leaped [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ηλατο] </span></span>. Better, as Rev., leaped up. Note the aorist tense, indicating a single act, while the imperfect, walked, denotes continuous action.</p>
