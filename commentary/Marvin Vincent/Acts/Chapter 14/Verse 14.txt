<p>Ran in [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εισεπηδησαν] </span></span>. A feeble translation, even if this reading is retained. The verb means to leap or spring. The best texts read ejxephdhsan, sprang forth, probably from the gate of their house, or from the city gate, if the sacrifice was prepared in front of it. </p>
<p>Crying out [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κραζοντες] </span></span>. Inarticulate shouts to attract attention.</p>
