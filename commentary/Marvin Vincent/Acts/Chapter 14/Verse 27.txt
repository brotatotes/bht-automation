<p>With them [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μετ αυτων] </span></span>. In connection with them; assisting them. And how [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">και οτι] </span></span>. Better, that. The and has an incressive and particularizing force : "and in particular, above all." </p>
