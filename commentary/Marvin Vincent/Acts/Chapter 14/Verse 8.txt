<p>Impotent [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αδυνατος] </span></span>. The almost universal meaning of the word in the New Testament is impossible (see <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+19:26">Matthew 19:26</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="heb+6:4">Hebrews 6:4</span>, etc.). The sense of weak or impotent occurs only here and <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ro+14:1">Romans 14:1</span>.</p>
