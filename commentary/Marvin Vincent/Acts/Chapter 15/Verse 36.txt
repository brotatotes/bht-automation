<p>Let us go again and visit [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επιστρεψαντες δη επισκεψωμεθα] </span></span>. Lit., Having returned, let us now visit. The A. V. omits now. See on ch. <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+13:2">Acts 13:2</span>. </p>
<p>In every city [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κατα πασαν πολιν] </span></span>. Kata has the force of city by city.</p>
