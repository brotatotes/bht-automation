<p>Him [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τουτον] </span></span>. Lit., that one. It marks him very strongly, and is an emphatic position at the end of the sentence. </p>
<p>Departed [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αποσταντα] </span></span>. Rev., withdrew. It furnishes the derivation of our word apostatize.</p>
