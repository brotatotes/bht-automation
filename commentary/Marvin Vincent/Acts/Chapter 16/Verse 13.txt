<p>Out of the city [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εξω της πολεως] </span></span>. The best texts read pulhv, the gate. </p>
<p>River. Probably the Gangas or Gangites. </p>
<p>Where prayer was wont to be made [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ου ενονιζετο προσευχη ειναι] </span></span>. The best texts read ejnomizomen proseuchn, where we supposed there was a place of prayer. The number of Jews in Philippi was small, since it was a military and not a mercantile city; consequently there was no synagogue, but only a proseucha, or praying - place, a slight structure, and often open to the sky. It was outside the gate, for the sake of retirement, and near a stream, because of the ablutions connected with the worship.</p>
