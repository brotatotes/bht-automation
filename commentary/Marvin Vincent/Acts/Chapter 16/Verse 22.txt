<p>Rent off their clothes [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">περιρρηξαντες] </span></span>. Only here in New Testament. By the usual formula of command to the lictors : Go, lictors; strip off their garments; let them be scourged ! </p>
<p>To beat [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ραβδιζειν] </span></span>. From rJabdov, a rod. Rev. properly adds, with rods.</p>
