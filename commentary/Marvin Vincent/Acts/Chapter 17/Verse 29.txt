The Godhead [το θειον] . Lit., that which is divine. Like to gold, etc. These words must have impressed his hearers profoundly, as they looked at the multitude of statues of divinities which surrounded them. Graven [χαραγματι] . Not a participle, as A. V., but a noun, in apposition with gold, silver, and stone : "a graving or carved work of art," etc.
