<p>The Godhead [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">το θειον] </span></span>. Lit., that which is divine. </p>
<p>Like to gold, etc. These words must have impressed his hearers profoundly, as they looked at the multitude of statues of divinities which surrounded them. </p>
<p>Graven [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">χαραγματι] </span></span>. Not a participle, as A. V., but a noun, in apposition with gold, silver, and stone : "a graving or carved work of art," etc.</p>
