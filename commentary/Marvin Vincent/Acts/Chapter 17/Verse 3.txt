<p>Opening and alleging. The latter word is rather propounding, or setting forth [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">παρατιθεμενος] </span></span>. See on set before, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="lu+9:16">Luke 9:16</span>; and commit, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1pe+4:19">1 Peter 4:19</span>. Bengel remarks, "Two steps, as if one, having broken the rind, were to disclose and exhibit the kernel."</p>
