<p>Gallio. Brother of the philosopher Seneca (Nero's tutor), and uncle of the poet Lucan, the author of the "Pharsalia." Seneca speaks of him as amiable and greatly beloved. </p>
<p>Deputy. See on ch. <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+13:7">Acts 13:7</span>. The verb, to be deputy, occurs only here. </p>
<p>Judgment - seat. See on ch. <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+7:5">Acts 7:5</span>.</p>
