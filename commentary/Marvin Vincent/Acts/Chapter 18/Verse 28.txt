<p>Mightily [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ευτονως] </span></span>. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="lu+23:10">Luke 23:10</span>. </p>
<p>Convinced [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">διακατηλεγχετο] </span></span>. Only here in New Testament. See on tell him his fault, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+18:15">Matthew 18:15</span>. The compound here is a very strong expression for thorough confutation. Confute (Rev.) is better than convince. Note the prepositions. He confuted them thoroughly [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δια] </span></span>, against [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κατα</span></span>] all their arguments. </p>
