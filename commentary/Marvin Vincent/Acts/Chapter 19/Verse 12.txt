Body [χρωτος] . Properly, the surface of the body, the skin; but, in medical language, of the body. Handkerchiefs [σουδαρια] . See on Luke 19:20. Aprons [σιμικινθια] . Only here in New Testament. A Latin word, semicinctia. Lit., something passing half - way round the body : an apron or waistband. Perhaps garments worn by Paul when engaged at his trade.
