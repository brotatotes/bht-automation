<p>Body [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">χρωτος] </span></span>. Properly, the surface of the body, the skin; but, in medical language, of the body. </p>
<p>Handkerchiefs [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">σουδαρια] </span></span>. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="lu+19:20">Luke 19:20</span>. </p>
<p>Aprons [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">σιμικινθια] </span></span>. Only here in New Testament. A Latin word, semicinctia. Lit., something passing half - way round the body : an apron or waistband. Perhaps garments worn by Paul when engaged at his trade.</p>
