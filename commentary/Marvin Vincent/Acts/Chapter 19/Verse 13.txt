<p>Vagabond [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">περιερχομενων] </span></span>. Lit., going about. Rev., strolling. </p>
<p>Exorcists [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εξορκιστων] </span></span>. Only here in New Testament. The kindred verb, adjure, occurs <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+26:63">Matthew 26:63</span>, and means, originally, to administer an oath. These Jewish exorcists pretended to the power of casting out evil spirits by magical arts derived from Solomon.</p>
