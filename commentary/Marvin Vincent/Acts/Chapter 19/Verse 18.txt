<p>Confessed and shewed [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εξομολογουμενοι και αναγγελλοντες] </span></span>. The two words denote the fullest and most open confession. They openly [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εξ</span></span>] confessed, and declared thoroughly (ajna, from top to bottom) their deeds. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+3:6">Matthew 3:6</span>.</p>
