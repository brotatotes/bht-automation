<p>The theater. The site of which can still be traced. It is said to have been capable of seating fifty - six thousand persons. </p>
<p>Having seized [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">συναρπασαντες] </span></span>. Lit., "having seized along with [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">συν</span></span>] :" carried them along with the rush. </p>
<p>Companions in travel [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">συνεκδημους] </span></span>. Only here and <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="2co+8:19">2 Corinthians 8:19</span>. The word is compounded of sun, along with, ejk, forth, and dhmov, country or land, and means, therefore, one who has gone forth with another from his country.</p>
