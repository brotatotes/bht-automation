The theater. The site of which can still be traced. It is said to have been capable of seating fifty - six thousand persons. Having seized [συναρπασαντες] . Lit., "having seized along with [συν] :" carried them along with the rush. Companions in travel [συνεκδημους] . Only here and 2 Corinthians 8:19. The word is compounded of sun, along with, ejk, forth, and dhmov, country or land, and means, therefore, one who has gone forth with another from his country.
