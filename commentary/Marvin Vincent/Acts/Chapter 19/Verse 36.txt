<p>Quiet [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κατεσταλμενους] </span></span>. Compare quieted (ver. 35). The verb means to let down or lower; and so is applied, metaphorically, to keeping one's self in check; repressing. </p>
<p>Rash [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">προπετες] </span></span>. Lit., headlong.</p>
