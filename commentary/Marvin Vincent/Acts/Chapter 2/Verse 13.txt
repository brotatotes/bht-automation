Others [ετεροι] . Of a different class. The first who commented on the wonder did so curiously, but with no prejudice. Those who now spoke did so in a hostile spirit. See on ver. 4. Mocking (diacleuazontev; so the best texts). From cleuh, a joke. Only here in New Testament. New wine [γλευκους] . Lit., "sweet wine." Of course intoxicating.
