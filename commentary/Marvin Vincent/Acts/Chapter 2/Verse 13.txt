<p>Others [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ετεροι] </span></span>. Of a different class. The first who commented on the wonder did so curiously, but with no prejudice. Those who now spoke did so in a hostile spirit. See on ver. 4. </p>
<p>Mocking (diacleuazontev; so the best texts). From cleuh, a joke. Only here in New Testament. </p>
<p>New wine [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">γλευκους] </span></span>. Lit., "sweet wine." Of course intoxicating.</p>
