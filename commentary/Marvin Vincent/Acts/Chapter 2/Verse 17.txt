<p>All flesh. Without distinction of age, sex, or condition. </p>
<p>Visions [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ορασεις] </span></span>. Waking visions. </p>
<p>Dream dreams [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ενυπνια ενυπνιασθησονται] </span></span>. The best texts read ejnupnioiv, with dreams. The verb occurs only here and <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="jude+1:8">Jude 1:8</span>. The reference is to visions in sleep.</p>
