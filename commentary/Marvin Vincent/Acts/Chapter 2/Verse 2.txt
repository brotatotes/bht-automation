<p>A sound [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ηχος] </span></span>. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="lu+4:37">Luke 4:37</span>. </p>
<p>Of a rushing mighty wind [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">φερομενης πνοης βιαιας] </span></span>. Lit., of a mighty wind born along. Pnoh is a blowing, a blast. Only here and ch. 27 25. Rev., as of the rushing of a mighty wind. </p>
<p>The house. Not merely the room. Compare ch. 1 13. </p>
<p>Were sitting. Awaiting the hour of prayer. See ver. 15.</p>
