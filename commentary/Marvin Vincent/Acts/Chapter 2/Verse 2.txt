A sound [ηχος] . See on Luke 4:37. Of a rushing mighty wind [φερομενης πνοης βιαιας] . Lit., of a mighty wind born along. Pnoh is a blowing, a blast. Only here and ch. 27 25. Rev., as of the rushing of a mighty wind. The house. Not merely the room. Compare ch. 1 13. Were sitting. Awaiting the hour of prayer. See ver. 15.
