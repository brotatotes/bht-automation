<p>That great and notable day of the Lord come. The Rev. heightens the emphasis by following the Greek order, the day of the Lord, that great and notable day. Notable [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επιφανη</span></span>] only here in New Testament. The kindred noun ejpifaneia, appearing (compare our word Epiphany), is often used of the second coming of the Lord. See <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1ti+6:14">1 Timothy 6:14</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="2ti+4:1">2 Timothy 4:1</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="tit+2:13">Titus 2:13</span>.</p>
