<p>Being delivered [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εκδοτον] </span></span>. An adjective : given forth, betrayed. </p>
<p>Ye have taken. The best texts omit. </p>
<p>Wicked hands. The best texts read by the hand of lawless men. </p>
<p>Crucified [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">προσπηζαντες] </span></span>. Only here in New Testament. The verb simply means to affix to or on anything. The idea of the cross is left to be supplied. </p>
<p>Have slain [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ανειλετε] </span></span>. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="lu+23:32">Luke 23:32</span>. Rev., rendering the aorist more closely, did slay.</p>
