Being delivered [εκδοτον] . An adjective : given forth, betrayed. Ye have taken. The best texts omit. Wicked hands. The best texts read by the hand of lawless men. Crucified [προσπηζαντες] . Only here in New Testament. The verb simply means to affix to or on anything. The idea of the cross is left to be supplied. Have slain [ανειλετε] . See on Luke 23:32. Rev., rendering the aorist more closely, did slay.
