<p>Rejoiced [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ηγαλλιασατο] </span></span>. Rev., was glad. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1pe+1:6">1 Peter 1:6</span>. </p>
<p>Shall rest [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κατασκηνωσει] </span></span>. See on nests, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+8:20">Matthew 8:20</span>. Better, as Rev., dwell. Lit., dwell in a tent or tabernacle. Rendered lodge, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+8:32">Matthew 8:32</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mr+4:32">Mark 4:32</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="lu+8:19">Luke 8:19</span>. It is a beautiful metaphor. My flesh shall encamp on hope; pitch its tent there to rest through the night of death, until the morning of resurrection. </p>
<p>In hope [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επ ελπισι] </span></span>. Lit., on hope : resting on the hope of resurrection; his body being poetically conceived as hoping.</p>
