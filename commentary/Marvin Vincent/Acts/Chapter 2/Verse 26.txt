Rejoiced [ηγαλλιασατο] . Rev., was glad. See on 1 Peter 1:6. Shall rest [κατασκηνωσει] . See on nests, Matthew 8:20. Better, as Rev., dwell. Lit., dwell in a tent or tabernacle. Rendered lodge, Matthew 8:32; Mark 4:32; Luke 8:19. It is a beautiful metaphor. My flesh shall encamp on hope; pitch its tent there to rest through the night of death, until the morning of resurrection. In hope [επ ελπισι] . Lit., on hope : resting on the hope of resurrection; his body being poetically conceived as hoping.
