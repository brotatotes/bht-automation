<p>With one accord [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ομοθυμαδον] </span></span>. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+18:19">Matthew 18:19</span>. </p>
<p>From house to house [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κατ οικον] </span></span>. Better, as Rev., at home, contrasted with in the temple. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="php+1:2">Philippians 1:2</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="col+4:15">Colossians 4:15</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1co+16:19">1 Corinthians 16:19</span>. </p>
<p>Did eat their meat [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μετελαμβανον τροφης] </span></span>. Rev., take their food. Partake would be better, giving the force of meta, with. Note the imperfect : "continued to partake." </p>
<p>Singleness [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αφελοτητι] </span></span>. Only here in New Testament. Derived from aj, not, and felleuv, stony ground. Hence of something simple or plain.</p>
