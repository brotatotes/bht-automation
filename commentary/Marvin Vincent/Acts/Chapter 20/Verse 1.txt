<p>Embraced [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ασπασαμενος] </span></span>. Better, as Rev., took leave. The word is used for a salutation either at meeting or parting. See ch. <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+21:6">Acts 21:6</span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+21:7">Acts 21:7</span>.</p>
