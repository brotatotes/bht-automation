<p>Arrived [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">παρεβαλομεν] </span></span>. Only here and <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mr+4:30">Mark 4:30</span>, where it is used more nearly according to its original sense, to throw beside; to bring one thing beside another in comparison. Here, of bringing the vessel alongside the island. The narrative implies that they only touched (Rev.) there, but not necessarily the word.</p>
