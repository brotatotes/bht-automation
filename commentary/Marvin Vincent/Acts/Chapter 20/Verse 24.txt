<p>But none of these things move me, neither count I, etc. The best texts omit neither count I, and render, I esteem my life of no account, as if it were precious to myself. </p>
<p>Dear [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τιμιαν] </span></span>. Of value; precious. </p>
<p>Course [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δρομον] </span></span>. A favorite metaphor of Paul, from the race - course.
