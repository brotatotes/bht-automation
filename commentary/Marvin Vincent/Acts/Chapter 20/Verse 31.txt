<p>Watch [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">γρηγορειτε] </span></span>. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mr+8:35">Mark 8:35</span>. </p>
<p>To warn [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">νουθετων] </span></span>. From nouv, the mind, and tiqhmi, to put. Lit., to put in mind; admonish (so Rev., better than warn). "It's fundamental idea is the well - intentioned seriousness with which one would influence the mind and disposition of another by advice, admonition, warning, putting right, according to circumstances" [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">χρεμερ] </span></span>.</p>
