Watch [γρηγορειτε] . See on Mark 8:35. To warn [νουθετων] . From nouv, the mind, and tiqhmi, to put. Lit., to put in mind; admonish (so Rev., better than warn). "It's fundamental idea is the well - intentioned seriousness with which one would influence the mind and disposition of another by advice, admonition, warning, putting right, according to circumstances" [χρεμερ] .
