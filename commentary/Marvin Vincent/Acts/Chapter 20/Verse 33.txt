<p>Raiment. Mentioned along with gold and silver because it formed a large part of the wealth of orientals. They traded in costly garments, or kept them stored up for future use. See on purple, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="lu+16:19">Luke 16:19</span>; and compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ezr+2:69">Ezra 2:69</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ne+7:70">Nehemiah 7:70</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="job+27:16">Job 27:16</span>. This fact accounts for the allusions to the destructive power of the moth (<span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+6:19">Matthew 6:19</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="jas+5:2">James 5:2</span>).</p>
