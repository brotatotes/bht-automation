<p>They are informed [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κατηχηθησαν] </span></span>. More than informed. They had been carefully instructed, probably by the Judaizing teachers. See on instructed, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="lu+1:4">Luke 1:4</span>. </p>
<p>To forsake Moses [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αποστασιαν απο, μωσεως] </span></span>. Lit., apostasy from Moses. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="2th+2:3">2 Thessalonians 2:3</span>.</p>
