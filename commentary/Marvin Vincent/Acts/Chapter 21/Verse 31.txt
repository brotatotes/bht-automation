Chief captain [χιλιαρχω] . A commander of a thousand men. See on Mark 6:21; and on centurion, Luke 7:2. Band [σπειρης] . Or cohort. See on Mark 14:16. These troops were quartered in the tower of Antonia, which was at the northwestern corner of the temple - area, and communicated with the temple - cloisters by staircases.
