<p>Chief captain [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">χιλιαρχω] </span></span>. A commander of a thousand men. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mr+6:21">Mark 6:21</span>; and on centurion, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="lu+7:2">Luke 7:2</span>. </p>
<p>Band [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">σπειρης] </span></span>. Or cohort. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mr+14:16">Mark 14:16</span>. These troops were quartered in the tower of Antonia, which was at the northwestern corner of the temple - area, and communicated with the temple - cloisters by staircases.</p>
