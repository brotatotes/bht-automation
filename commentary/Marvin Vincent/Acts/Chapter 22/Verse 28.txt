<p>Sum [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κεφαλαιου] </span></span>. Lit., capital. The purchase of Roman citizenship was an investment. Under the first Roman emperors it was obtained only at large cost and with great difficulty; later, it was sold for a trifle. I was free born [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εγω και γεγεννημαι] </span></span>. Lit., I am even so born, leaving the mind to supply free or a Roman. Better, as Rev., I am a Roman born.</p>
