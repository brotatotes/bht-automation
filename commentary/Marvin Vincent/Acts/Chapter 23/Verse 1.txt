<p>Earnestly beholding. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="lu+4:20">Luke 4:20</span>. Some, who hold that Paul 's eyesight was defective, explain this steadfast look in connection with his imperfect vision. </p>
<p>Men and brethren. He addresses the Sanhedrim as an equal. </p>
<p>I have lived [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πεπολιτευμαι] </span></span>. Lit., have lived as a citizen, with special reference to the charge against him that he taught men against the law and the temple. He means that he has lived as a true and loyal Jew. </p>
<p>Conscience [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">συνειδησει] </span></span>. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1pe+3:16">1 Peter 3:16</span>.</p>
