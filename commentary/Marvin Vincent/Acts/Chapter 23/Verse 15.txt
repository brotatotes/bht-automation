<p>Enquire [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">διαγινωσκειν] </span></span>. Only here and ch. 24 22. Originally, to distinguish or discern; hence, to decide, as a suit. Rev., more correctly, therefore, judge. </p>
<p>More perfectly [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ακριβεστερον] </span></span>. Rev., better, more exactly. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="lu+1:3">Luke 1:3</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+18:25">Acts 18:25</span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+18:26">Acts 18:26</span>. </p>
<p>Concerning him [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τα περι αυτου] </span></span>. Lit., the things about him. Rev., better, his case.</p>
