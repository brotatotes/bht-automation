Enquire [διαγινωσκειν] . Only here and ch. 24 22. Originally, to distinguish or discern; hence, to decide, as a suit. Rev., more correctly, therefore, judge. More perfectly [ακριβεστερον] . Rev., better, more exactly. See on Luke 1:3; Acts 18:25, Acts 18:26. Concerning him [τα περι αυτου] . Lit., the things about him. Rev., better, his case.
