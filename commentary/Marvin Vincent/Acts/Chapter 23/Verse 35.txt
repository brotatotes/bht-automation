I will hear thee [διακουσομαι] . Better, as Rev., will hear thy cause; the word meaning "to hear fully [δια] in a judicial sense." The present questioning was merely preliminary. Herod 's palace. Built by Herod the Great. Judaea being now a Roman province, the palace of its former kings had become the governor 's official residence. It thus appears that Paul was leniently dealt with, and not cast into the common prison.
