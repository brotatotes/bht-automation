<p>Liberty [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ανεσιν] </span></span>. From ajnihmi, to send up; thence, to loosen, release. It is almost exactly expressed by our vulgarism, to let up. The noun here is more correctly rendered by Rev., indulgence. In all the other New Testament passages it is rendered rest, ease, or relief. See <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="2co+2:13">2 Corinthians 2:13</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="2co+7:5">2 Corinthians 7:5</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="2co+8:13">2 Corinthians 8:13</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="2th+1:7">2 Thessalonians 1:7</span>. </p>
<p>To minister [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">υπηρετειν] </span></span>. See on officer, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+5:25">Matthew 5:25</span>.</p>
