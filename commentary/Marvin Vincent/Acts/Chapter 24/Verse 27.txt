Porcius Festus came into Felix 's room [ελαβε διαδοχον ο φηλιξ πορκιον φηστον] . Rev., better, Felix was succeeded by Porcius Festus. The Greek idiom is, Felix received Porcius Festus as a successor. To shew the Jews a pleasure [χαριτας καταθεσθαι τοις ιουδαιοις] . Lit., to lay up thanks for himself with the Jews. Rev., correctly, to gain favor with the Jews.
