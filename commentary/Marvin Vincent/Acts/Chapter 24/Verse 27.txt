<p>Porcius Festus came into Felix 's room [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ελαβε διαδοχον ο φηλιξ πορκιον φηστον] </span></span>. Rev., better, Felix was succeeded by Porcius Festus. The Greek idiom is, Felix received Porcius Festus as a successor. </p>
<p>To shew the Jews a pleasure [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">χαριτας καταθεσθαι τοις ιουδαιοις] </span></span>. Lit., to lay up thanks for himself with the Jews. Rev., correctly, to gain favor with the Jews. </p>
