Pestilent fellow [λοιμον] . Lit., a plague or pest. Ringleader [πρωτοστατην] . Originally, one who stands first on the right of a line; a file - leader. Thus Thucydides says that all armies when engaging are apt to thrust outward their right wing; and adds, "The first man in the front rank [ο πρωτοστατης] of the right wing is originally responsible for the deflection" (v., 71). Here, of course, metaphorically, as A. V. and Rev. Only here in New Testament. Sect [αιρεσεως] . See on heresies, 2 Peter 2:1. Nazarenes. The only passage in scripture where this term is used to denote the Christians. See on Matthew 2:23.
