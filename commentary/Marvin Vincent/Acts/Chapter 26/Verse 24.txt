<p>Much learning doth make thee mad [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τα πολλα σε γραμματα εις μανιαν περιτρεπει] </span></span>. The A. V. omits the article with much learning : "the much knowledge" with which thou art busied. Rev., "thy much learning." Doth make thee mad : literally, is turning thee to madness.</p>
