<p>Almost and altogether [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εν ολιγω και εν μεγαλω] </span></span>. 30 Lit., in little and in great; i e., with little or with great pains. </p>
<p>Were [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">γενεσθαι] </span></span>. Better, as Rev., might become. Agrippa 's word, "to become a Christian," is repeated. </p>
