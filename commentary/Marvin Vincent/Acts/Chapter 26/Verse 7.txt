<p>Twelve tribes [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δωδεκαφυλον] </span></span>. Only here in New Testament. A collective term, embracing the tribes as a whole. Meyer renders our twelve - tribe - stock. </p>
<p>Instantly [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εν εκτενεια] </span></span>. Only here in New Testament. Lit., in intensity. See on fervently, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1pe+1:22">1 Peter 1:22</span>. Compare more earnestly, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="lu+22:44">Luke 22:44</span>; without ceasing, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+12:5">Acts 12:5</span>; fervent, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1pe+4:8">1 Peter 4:8</span>. See, also, on instantly and instant, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="lu+7:4">Luke 7:4</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="lu+23:23">Luke 23:23</span>. </p>
<p>Serving. Compare ch. 24 14; and see on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="lu+1:74">Luke 1:74</span>. </p>
<p>Come [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">καταντησαι] </span></span>. Lit., to arrive at, as if at a goal. Compare ch. <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+16:1">Acts 16:1</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+18:19">Acts 18:19</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+25:13">Acts 25:13</span>, etc. Rev. attain.</p>
