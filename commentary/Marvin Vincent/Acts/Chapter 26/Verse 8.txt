<p>That God should raise the dead [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ει ο θεος νεκρους εγειρει] </span></span>. Much better, as Rev., if God raises the dead. He does not put it as a supposition, but as a fact : if God raises the dead, as you admit that he has the power to do, and as your own writings tell you that he has done.</p>
