<p>Bear up [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αντοφθαλμειν] </span></span>. Only here in New Testament. From ajnti opposite, and ojfqalmov, the eye. Lit, to look the wind : in the eye. The ancient ships often had an eye painted on each side of the bow. To sail "into the eye of the wind" is a modern nautical phrase. </p>
<p>We let her drive [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επιδοντες εφερομεθα] </span></span>. Lit., having given up to it, we were born along.</p>
