<p>We had much work to come by the boat [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μολις ισχυσαμεν περικρατεις γενεσθαι της σκαφης] </span></span>. Lit., we were with difficulty able to become masters of the boat : i e., to secure on deck the small boat which, in calm weather, was attached by a rope to the vessel 's stern. Rev., we were able with difficulty to secure the boat. On with difficulty, see note on scarce, ver. 7.</p>
