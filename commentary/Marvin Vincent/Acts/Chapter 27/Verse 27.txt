<p>Adria. The Adriatic Sea : embracing all that part of the Mediterranean lying south of Italy, east of Sicily, and west of Greece. </p>
<p>Deemed [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">υπενοουν] </span></span>. Better, as Rev., suspected or surmised. </p>
<p>That they drew near to some country. Lit., that some land is drawing near to them.</p>
