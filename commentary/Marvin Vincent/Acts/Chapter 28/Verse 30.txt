Hired house [μισθωματι] . Probably different from the xenia, or lodging - place, where he resided for the first few days, perhaps as the guest of friends, though under custody, and where he received the Jews (ver. 23). ===Romans 1:0 CHAPTER I Superscription (vers. 1, 2). Dr. Morison observes that the superscription is peerless for its wealth of theological idea.
