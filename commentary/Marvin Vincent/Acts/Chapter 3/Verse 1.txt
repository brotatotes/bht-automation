<p>Went up [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ανεβαινον] </span></span>. The imperfect : were going up. So Rev., ascending the terraces, on the highest of which the temple stood. </p>
<p>Ninth hour. The time of the evening sacrifice; or, as the words of prayer indicate, half an hour later, for the prayer which accompanied the offering of incense.</p>
