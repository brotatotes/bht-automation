<p>Which before was preached [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τον προκεκηρυγμενον] </span></span>. But the best texts read prokeceirismenon, appointed. Compare ch. 22 14. Used by Luke only, ch. <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+22:14">Acts 22:14</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+26:16">Acts 26:16</span>. The verb originally means to take in hand.</p>
