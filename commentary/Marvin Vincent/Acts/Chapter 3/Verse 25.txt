<p>Covenant [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">διαθηκης] </span></span>. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+26:28">Matthew 26:28</span>. </p>
<p>Made (die qeto). The Rev. gives covenanted in margin. The noun covenant is derived from the verb diatiqnmi, originally to distribute or arrange. Hence to arrange or settle mutually; to make a covenant with.</p>
