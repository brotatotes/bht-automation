<p>It spread [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">διανεμηθη] </span></span>. Only here in New Testament. Lit., be distributed. In <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="2ti+2:17">2 Timothy 2:17</span>, "their word will eat as canker," is, literally, will have distribution or spreading [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">νομην εξει] </span></span>. Bengel, however, goes too far when he represents the members of the council as speaking in the figure of a canker. "They regard the whole as a canker."</p>
