<p>Kept back [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ενοσφισατο] </span></span>. Only here, ver. 3, and <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="tit+2:10">Titus 2:10</span>, where it is rendered purloining. From nosfi, aloof, apart. The verb means to set apart for one's self; hence to appropriate wrongfully.</p>
