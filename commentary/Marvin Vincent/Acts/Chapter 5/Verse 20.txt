<p>Of this life. The eternal life which Christ revealed. It is a peculiar use of the phrase, which is commonly employed in contrast with the life to come, as <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1co+14:19">1 Corinthians 14:19</span>. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="joh+6:63">John 6:63</span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="joh+6:68">John 6:68</span>. Not equivalent to these words of life.</p>
