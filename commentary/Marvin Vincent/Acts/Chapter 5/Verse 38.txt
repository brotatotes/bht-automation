<p>Refrain [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αποστητε] </span></span>. Lit., stand off. </p>
<p>Of men [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εξ ανθρωπων] </span></span>. Out of men, proceeding out of their devices. It will come to naught [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">καταλυθησεται] </span></span>. Lit., be loosened down. Used of the dilapidation of the temple (<span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="lu+21:6">Luke 21:6</span>), and of the dissolution of the body under the figure of striking a tent (<span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="2co+5:1">2 Corinthians 5:1</span>). See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mr+13:2">Mark 13:2</span>.</p>
