Refrain [αποστητε] . Lit., stand off. Of men [εξ ανθρωπων] . Out of men, proceeding out of their devices. It will come to naught [καταλυθησεται] . Lit., be loosened down. Used of the dilapidation of the temple (Luke 21:6), and of the dissolution of the body under the figure of striking a tent (2 Corinthians 5:1). See on Mark 13:2.
