<p>Whiles it remained, was it not thine own [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ουχι μενον σοι εμενε] </span></span>. A play on the words. Lit., remaining, did it not remain to thee? Rev., very happily, whiles it remained, did it not remain thine own? </p>
<p>Conceived [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εθου] </span></span>. Lit., put or fixed. Wherefore didst thou fix this deed in thy heart ? - ie, resolve upon it.</p>
