Whiles it remained, was it not thine own [ουχι μενον σοι εμενε] . A play on the words. Lit., remaining, did it not remain to thee? Rev., very happily, whiles it remained, did it not remain thine own? Conceived [εθου] . Lit., put or fixed. Wherefore didst thou fix this deed in thy heart ? - ie, resolve upon it.
