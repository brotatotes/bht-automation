<p>They were counted worthy to suffer shame [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κατηξιωθησαν ατιμασθηναι] </span></span>. This is an instance of what rhetoricians style an oxymoron, from ojxuv, sharp, and mwrov, foolish; a pointedly foolish saying, which is witty or impressive through sheer contradiction or paradox, as laborious idleness, sublime indifference. In this case the apostles are described as dignified by indignity. </p>
