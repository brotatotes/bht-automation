<p>Gave up the ghost [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εξεψυξε] </span></span>. Used by Luke only. A rare word, occurring in the Septuagint, and in medical writers. See <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="eze+21:7">Ezekiel 21:7</span>, "Every spirit shall faint." See, also, on failing, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="lu+21:26">Luke 21:26</span>.</p>
