They stirred up the people [συνεκινησαν τον λαον] . The verb occurs only here in the New Testament. It implies to stir up as a mass, to move them together [συν] . This is the first record of the hostility of the people toward the disciples. See ch. Acts 2:47. Caught [συνηρπασαν] . Used by Luke only. Better as Rev., seized. See on Luke 8:29.
