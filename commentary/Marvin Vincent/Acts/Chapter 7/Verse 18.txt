<p>Another [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ετερος] </span></span>. Not merely a successor, but a monarch of a different character. </p>
<p>Knew not. As sixty years had elapsed since Joseph 's death, and a new dynasty was coming to the throne, this may be taken literally : did not know his history and services. Some explain, did not recognize his merits.</p>
