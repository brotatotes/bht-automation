Dealt subtilely [κατασοφισαμενος] . Only here in New Testament. Lit., to employ cunning against. See on sofov, wise, James 3:13. So that they cast out [του ποιειν εκθετα] . Lit., make exposed. The verb ejktiqhmi, to set out, or place outside, is not uncommon in classical Greek for the exposure of a new - born child. Thus Herodotus, of Cyrus, exposed in infancy : "The herdsman's wife entreated him not to expose [εκθειναι] the babe" (1, 112). The rendering of the A. V., "so that they cast out," is correct, expressing the result, and not Pharaoh 's design. Young children [βρεφη] . Incorrect. See on 1 Peter 2:2. Rev., rightly babes. Live [ζωογονεισθαι] Or, be preserved alive. See on Luke 17:33.
