<p>Deliverer [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">λυτρωτη, ν] </span></span>. Strictly, a ransomer or redeemer. Only here in New Testament, See on ransom, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+20:28">Matthew 20:28</span>; and redeemed, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1pe+1:18">1 Peter 1:18</span>. </p>
<p>By the hand [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εν χειρι</span></span>] The best texts read sun ceiri, "with the hand;" i e., in association with the protecting and helping power of the angel.</p>
