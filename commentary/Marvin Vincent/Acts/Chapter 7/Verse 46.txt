<p>Desired [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ητησατο] </span></span>. More correctly, asked : through Nathan. See <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="2sa+7:2">2 Samuel 7:2</span>. </p>
<p>Tabernacle [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">σκηνωμα] </span></span>. It was not a tabernacle or tent which David proposed to build, but a house. See <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="2sa+7:2">2 Samuel 7:2</span>. Rev., rightly, habitation. Compare oikon, a house, ver. 47 and <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="2ch+6:18">2 Chronicles 6:18</span>.</p>
