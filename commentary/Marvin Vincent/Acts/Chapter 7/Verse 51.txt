<p>Stiff - necked and uncircumcised [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">σκληροτραχηλοι και απεριτμητοι] </span></span>. Both only here in New Testament. </p>
<p>Resist [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αντιπιπτετε] </span></span>. It is a very strong expression, implying active resistance. Lit., to fall against or upon. Used of falling upon an enemy. Only here in New Testament. </p>
<p>Ye have been [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">γεγενησθε] </span></span>. More correctly, as Rev., ye have become.</p>
