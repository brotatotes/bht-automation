<p>They were cut. See on ch. <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+5:33">Acts 5:33</span>. In both instances, of anger. A different word is used to express remorse, ch. 2 37. </p>
<p>Gnashed [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εβρυχον] </span></span>. Originally to eat greedily, with a noise, as wild beasts : hence to gnash or grind the teeth.</p>
