<p>Carried to his burial [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">συνεκομισαν] </span></span>. Only here in New Testament. Lit., to carry together; hence, either to assist in burying or, better, to bring the dead to the company [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">συν</span></span>] of the other dead. The word is used of bringing in harvest. </p>
<p>Stephen [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">στεφανον] </span></span>. Meaning crown. He was the first who received the martyr's crown. </p>
<p>Lamentation [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κοπετον] </span></span>. Lit., beating (of the breast). Only here in New Testament.</p>
