Devout. See on Luke 2:25. Carried to his burial [συνεκομισαν] . Only here in New Testament. Lit., to carry together; hence, either to assist in burying or, better, to bring the dead to the company [συν] of the other dead. The word is used of bringing in harvest. Stephen [στεφανον] . Meaning crown. He was the first who received the martyr's crown. Lamentation [κοπετον] . Lit., beating (of the breast). Only here in New Testament.
