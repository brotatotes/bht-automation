In the gall [εις χολην] . Lit., into. Thou hast fallen into and continuest in. Gall, only here and Matthew 27:34. Gall of bitterness is bitter enmity against the Gospel. Bond of iniquity [συνδεσμον αδικιας] . Thou hast fallen into iniquity as into fetters. The word sundesmon denotes a close, firm bond [συν, τογετηερ] . It is used of the bond of Christian peace (Ephesians 4:3); of the close compacting of the church represented as a body (Colossians 2:19); and of love as the bond of perfectness (Colossians 3:14). See Isaiah 58:6.
