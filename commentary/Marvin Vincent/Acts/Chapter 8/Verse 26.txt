<p>The south [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μεσημβριαν] </span></span>. A contracted form of meshmeria, midday, noon, which is the rendering at <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+22:6">Acts 22:6</span> the only other passage where it occurs. Rev. gives at noon in margin. </p>
<p>Desert. Referring to the route. On desert, see on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="lu+14:4">Luke 14:4</span>. There were several roads from Jerusalem to Gaza. One is mentioned by the way of Bethlehem to Hebron, and thence through a region actually called a desert.</p>
