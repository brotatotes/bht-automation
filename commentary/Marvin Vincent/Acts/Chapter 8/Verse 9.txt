<p>Used sorcery [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μαγευων] </span></span>. Only here in New Testament. One of the wizards so numerous throughout the East at that time, and multiplied by the general expectation of a great deliverer and the spread of the Messianic notions of the Jews, who practiced upon the credulity of the people by conjuring and juggling and soothsaying. </p>
<p>Bewitched [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εξιστων] </span></span>. Better as Rev., amazed. See on ch. <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+2:7">Acts 2:7</span>.</p>
