<p>Breathing out [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εμπνεων] </span></span>. Lit., breathing upon or at, and so corresponding to against the disciples. </p>
<p>Threatenings and slaughter [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">απειλης και φονου] </span></span>. Lit., threatening; so Rev. In the Greek construction, the case in which these words are marks them as the cause or source of the "breathing;" breathing hard out of threatening, and murderous desire.</p>
