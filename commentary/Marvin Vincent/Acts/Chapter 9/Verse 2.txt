<p>Of this way [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">της οδου] </span></span>. Rev., more correctly, "the way." A common expression in the Acts for the Christian religion : "the characteristic direction of life as determined by faith on Jesus Christ" (Meyer). See ch. <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+19:9">Acts 19:9</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+22:4">Acts 22:4</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+24:22">Acts 24:22</span>. For the fuller expression of the idea, see ch. <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+16:17">Acts 16:17</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+18:25">Acts 18:25</span>. </p>
<p>Women. Paul three times alludes to his persecution of women as an aggravation of his cruelty (ch. <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+8:3">Acts 8:3</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+9:2">Acts 9:2</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+22:4">Acts 22:4</span>).</p>
