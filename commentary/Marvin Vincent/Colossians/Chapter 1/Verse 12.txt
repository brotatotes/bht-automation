<p>Made us meet [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ικανωσαντι] </span></span>. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="2co+3:6">2 Corinthians 3:6</span>. </p>
<p>To be partakers of the inheritance [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εις την μεριδα του κληρου] </span></span>. Lit., for the portion of the lot; that is, the portion which is the lot. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+8:21">Acts 8:21</span>, where the two words are coordinated. </p>
<p>In light [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εν τω φωτι] </span></span>. Connect with inheritance : the inheritance which is in light. This need not be limited to future glory. The children of God walk in light on earth. See <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="joh+3:21">John 3:21</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="joh+11:9">John 11:9</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="joh+12:36">John 12:36</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="eph+5:8">Ephesians 5:8</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1th+5:5">1 Thessalonians 5:5</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1jo+1:7">1 John 1:7</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1jo+2:10">1 John 2:10</span>.</p>
