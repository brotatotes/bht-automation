Ye are complete in Him [εστε εν αυτω πεπληρωμενοι] . Rev., made full. Compare John 1:16; Ephesians 1:23; Ephesians 3:19; Ephesians 4:13. Not, ye are made full in Him, but ye are in Him, made full. In Him dwells the fullness; being in Him, ye are filled. Compare John 17:21; Acts 17:28. Acts 17:19 Acts 17:7
