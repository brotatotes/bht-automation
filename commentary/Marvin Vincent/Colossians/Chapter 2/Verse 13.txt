<p>Dead [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">νεκρους] </span></span>. Morally, as <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="eph+2:1">Ephesians 2:1</span> <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="eph+1:5">Ephesians 1:5</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ro+6:11">Romans 6:11</span>. In your sins [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εν τοις παραπτωμασιν] </span></span>. The best texts omit ejn in, and the dative is instrumental, through or by. Rev., through your trespasses. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+6:14">Matthew 6:14</span>. </p>
<p>The uncircumcision of your flesh. That sinful, carnal nature of which uncircumcision was the sign, and which was the source of the trespasses. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="eph+2:11">Ephesians 2:11</span>. </p>
<p>He quickened together [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">συνεζωοποιησεν] </span></span>. Only here and <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="eph+2:5">Ephesians 2:5</span>. Endowed with a new spiritual life, as ver. 12. This issues in immortal life. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="eph+2:6">Ephesians 2:6</span>. </p>
<p>Having forgiven us [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">χαρισαμενος ημιν] </span></span>. Freely (cariv grace, free gift), as <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="lu+7:42">Luke 7:42</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="2co+2:7">2 Corinthians 2:7</span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="2co+2:10">2 Corinthians 2:10</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="col+3:13">Colossians 3:13</span>. Note the change of pronoun from you to us, believers generally, embracing himself. This change from the second to the first person, or, vice versa, is common in Paul 's writings. See ch. <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="col+1:10-13">Colossians 1:10-13</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="col+3:3">Colossians 3:3</span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="col+3:4">Colossians 3:4</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="eph+2:2">Ephesians 2:2</span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="eph+2:3">Ephesians 2:3</span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="eph+2:13">Ephesians 2:13</span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="eph+2:14">Ephesians 2:14</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="eph+4:31">Ephesians 4:31</span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="eph+4:32">Ephesians 4:32</span>.</p>
