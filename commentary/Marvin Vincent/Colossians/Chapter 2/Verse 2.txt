<p>Comforted (paraklhqwsin). Not so much tranquilized as braced. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="joh+14:16">John 14:16</span>. </p>
<p>Knit together [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">συμβιβασθεντες] </span></span>. See on proving, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+9:22">Acts 9:22</span>. In the Septuagint it means to instruct, as <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ex+18:16">Exodus 18:16</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="de+4:9">Deuteronomy 4:9</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="isa+40:13">Isaiah 40:13</span> (compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1co+2:16">1 Corinthians 2:16</span>); <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ps+31:8">Psalms 31:8</span>. Used of putting together in one's mind, and so to conclude by comparison. Thus <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+16:10">Acts 16:10</span>, assuredly gathering, Rev., concluding. </p>
<p>Full assurance [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πληροφοριας] </span></span>. Or fullness. See <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="heb+6:11">Hebrews 6:11</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="heb+10:22">Hebrews 10:22</span>. Of understanding [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">συνεσεως] </span></span>. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mr+12:33">Mark 12:33</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="lu+2:47">Luke 2:47</span>. </p>
<p>To the acknowledgment [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εις επιγνωσιν] </span></span>. Wrong. Epignwsiv is the full knowledge, as ch. 1 9 (note). Rev., that they may know. </p>
<p>Of God. The best textual authorities add Cristou of Christ. So Rev., of God, even Christ. Christ is in apposition with mystery. Compare ch. 1 27.</p>
