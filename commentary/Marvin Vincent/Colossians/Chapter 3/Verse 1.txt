<p>Be risen [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">συνηγερθητε] </span></span>. Rev., correctly, were raised. See ch. <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="col+2:12">Colossians 2:12</span>. In their baptism in which they died (ch. 2 20). Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ro+6:2">Romans 6:2</span> sqq. Sitteth [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εστιν καθημενος] </span></span>. According to the A. V. the literal rendering would be is sitting. Is, however, must be taken separately; where Christ is, seated. Seated is a secondary predicate, as hidden in ch. 2 3. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="eph+2:4-6">Ephesians 2:4-6</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="re+3:21">Revelation 3:21</span>.</p>
