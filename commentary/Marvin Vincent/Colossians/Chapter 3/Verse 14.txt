<p>Above all (ejpi pasin). According to the metaphor of the garment. Over all, like an upper garment, put on, etc. </p>
<p>Charity. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1co+13:1">1 Corinthians 13:1</span>. </p>
<p>Bond of perfectness [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">συνδεσμος της τελειοτητος] </span></span>. Love embraces and knits together all the virtues. Teleiothv perfectness is a collective idea, a result of combination, to which bond is appropriate. Compare Plato : "But two things cannot be held together without a third; they must have some bond of union. And the fairest bond is that which most completely fuses and is fused into the things which are bound" (" Timaeus, " 31).</p>
