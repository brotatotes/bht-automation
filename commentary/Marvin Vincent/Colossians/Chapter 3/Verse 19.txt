<p>Be not bitter [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μη πικραινεσθε] </span></span>. Lit., be not embittered. Used only here by Paul. Elsewhere only in Revelation. The compounds parapikrainw to exasperate, and parapikrasmov provocation, occur only in <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="heb+3:16">Hebrews 3:16</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="heb+3:8">Hebrews 3:8</span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="heb+3:15">Hebrews 3:15</span>. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="eph+4:31">Ephesians 4:31</span>.</p>
