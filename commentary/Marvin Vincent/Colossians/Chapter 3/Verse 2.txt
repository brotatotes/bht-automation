<p>Set your affection [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">φρονειτε] </span></span>. Lit., be minded, think. As Rev., set your mind. Seek marks the practical striving; set your mind, the inward impulse and disposition. Both must be directed at things above. "You must not only seek heaven, you must think heaven" (Lightfoot). Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="php+3:19">Philippians 3:19</span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="php+3:20">Philippians 3:20</span>.</p>
