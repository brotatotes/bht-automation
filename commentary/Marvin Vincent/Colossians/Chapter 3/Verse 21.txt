<p>Provoke to anger [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ερεθιζετε] </span></span>. Only here and <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="2co+9:2">2 Corinthians 9:2</span>, where it is used of stirring up to good works. To anger is added by A. V. Be discouraged (ajqumwsin). Only here in the New Testament. Lose heart, or become dispirited.</p>
