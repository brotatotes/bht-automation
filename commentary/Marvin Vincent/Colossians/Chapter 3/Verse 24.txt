Of the inheritance. Which consists or is in the inheritance. Compare the similar construction, ch Colossians 1:12. See Matthew 21:35-38, where the doulov bond - servant and the klhronomov heir are contrasted; and Romans 8:15-17; Galatians 4:1-7. For ye serve [γαρ δουλευετε] . Omit for. Some take the verb as imperative, serve ye; but the indicative is better as explaining from the Lord.
