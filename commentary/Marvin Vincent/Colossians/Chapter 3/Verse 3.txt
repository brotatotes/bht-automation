<p>Ye are dead [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">απεθανετε] </span></span>. Rev., correctly, ye died, as ch. 2 20. Is hid [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κεκρυπται] </span></span>. Your new spiritual life is no longer in the sphere of the earthly and sensual, but is with the life of the risen Christ, who is unseen with God. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="php+3:20">Philippians 3:20</span>.</p>
