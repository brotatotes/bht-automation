Who is our life [ζωη] . See on John 1:4. The life is not only with Christ, it is Christ. Compare John 14:6; 2 Corinthians 4:10, 2 Corinthians 4:11; 1 John 5:11, 1 John 5:12. For the change of person, our for your, see on ch. Colossians 2:13. Shall appear [φανερωθη] . Rev., correctly, shall be manifested. Compare 1 John 3:2, note. See on Romans 3:21. In glory. Compare Romans 8:17.
