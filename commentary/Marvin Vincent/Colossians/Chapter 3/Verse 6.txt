<p>Wrath - cometh. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ro+1:18">Romans 1:18</span>. The present tense denotes the certainty of the future event, as <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+17:11">Matthew 17:11</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="joh+4:21">John 4:21</span>. The best texts omit upon the children of disobedience.</p>
