<p>In the which [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εν οις] </span></span>. The omission of upon the children, etc., necessitates the reference to which things (ver. 6) Otherwise we might render among whom. </p>
<p>Walked - lived. Walked, referring to their practice, lived, to their condition. Their conduct and their condition agreed. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ga+5:25">Galatians 5:25</span>.</p>
