<p>Masters, etc. The best texts attach this verse to the preceding chapter. Render [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">παρεχεσθε] </span></span>. The Greek implies on your part. </p>
<p>Equal [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">την ισοτητα] </span></span>. Lit., the equality. Not equality of condition, but the brotherly equality growing out of the Christian relation in which there is neither bond nor free. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="php+1:16">Philippians 1:16</span>.</p>
