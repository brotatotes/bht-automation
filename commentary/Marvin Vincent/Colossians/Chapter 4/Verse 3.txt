See also entering in, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1th+1:9">1 Thessalonians 1:9</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1th+2:1">1 Thessalonians 2:1</span>. And the parallel passage, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="eph+6:19">Ephesians 6:19</span>. There may be an allusion to a release from imprisonment.</p>
