<p>Your faith [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">την καθ υμας πιστιν] </span></span>. The Greek phrase is nowhere else used by Paul. Lit., as Rev., the faith which is among you. Expositors endeavor to make a distinction between this and Paul 's common phrase hJ pistiv uJmwn your faith, but they differ widely, and the distinction is at best doubtful. </p>
<p>Love. Omit. </p>
<p>Unto all the saints [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">την εις παντας τους αγιους] </span></span>. Lit., that which is toward all, etc. Love being omitted, this refers to faith : faith which displays its work and fruits toward fellow Christians.
