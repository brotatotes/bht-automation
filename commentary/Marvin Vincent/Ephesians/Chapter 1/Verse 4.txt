<p>Even as [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">καθως] </span></span>. Explaining blessed us, in ver. 3. His blessing is in conformity with the fact that He chose. </p>
<p>Chose [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εξελεξατο] </span></span>. Middle voice, for himself. </p>
<p>In Him. As the head and representative of our spiritual humanity. </p>
<p>Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1co+14:22">1 Corinthians 14:22</span>. Divine election is in Christ the Redeemer. The crown of divine sovereignty is redemption. God rules the world to save it. </p>
<p>Holy and without blame [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αγιους και αμωμους] </span></span>. The positive and negative aspects of christian life. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="col+1:22">Colossians 1:22</span>. Rev., without blemish. The reference is to moral rather than to forensic righteousness. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1th+4:7">1 Thessalonians 4:7</span>. </p>
<p>In love. Join with foreordained, ver. 5. Having in love foreordained.</p>
