<p>Course [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αιωνα] </span></span>. Lit., age. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="joh+1:9">John 1:9</span>. </p>
<p>Power [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εξουσιας] </span></span>. Collective, the whole empire of evil spirits. </p>
<p>The air. According to Paul 's usage, in the simple physical sense. See <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+22:23">Acts 22:23</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1co+9:26">1 Corinthians 9:26</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1th+4:17">1 Thessalonians 4:17</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="re+16:17">Revelation 16:17</span>. The air is regarded as the region of the demons ' might. </p>
<p>The spirit. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1co+2:12">1 Corinthians 2:12</span>. The term designates the power over which Satan rules, on the side of its operation in men's hearts. </p>
<p>Now. With an implied reference to its former working in his readers. Compare once, ver. 3 Children of disobedience [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">υιοις της απειθειας] </span></span>. Compare ch. 5 6. A Hebraistic expression. Compare son of perdition, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="joh+17:12">John 17:12</span>; children of obedience, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1pe+1:14">1 Peter 1:14</span>; children of cursing, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="2pe+2:14">2 Peter 2:14</span>. Rev., correctly, sons of disobedience : belonging to disobedience as sons to a parent.</p>
