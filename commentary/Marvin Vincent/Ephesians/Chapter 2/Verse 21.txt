<p>All the building [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πασα οικοδομη] </span></span>. Lit., every building. Rev., each several building. But the reference is evidently to one building, and the rendering of A. V. should be retained though the article is wanting. Fitly framed together [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">συναρμολογουμενη] </span></span>. The present participle indicates the framing as in progress. </p>
<p>Temple [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ναον] </span></span>. Sanctuary. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+4:5">Matthew 4:5</span>. The more sacred portion of the structure is chosen for the figure.</p>
