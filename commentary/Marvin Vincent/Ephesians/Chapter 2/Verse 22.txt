Are builded together [συνοικοδομεισθε] . As component parts of the one building. The reference is to individual Christians, not to communities. Habitation [κατοικητηριον] . Answering to temple. Only here and Revelation 18:2. Indicating a permanent dwelling. See on dwell, Luke 11:26; Acts 2:5; Mark 5:3. In marked contrast with sojourners, ver. 19. Through the Spirit [εν] . Better, as Rev., in. In the fellowship of the indwelling Spirit.
