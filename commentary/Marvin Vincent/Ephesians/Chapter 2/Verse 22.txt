<p>Are builded together [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">συνοικοδομεισθε] </span></span>. As component parts of the one building. The reference is to individual Christians, not to communities. </p>
<p>Habitation [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κατοικητηριον] </span></span>. Answering to temple. Only here and <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="re+18:2">Revelation 18:2</span>. Indicating a permanent dwelling. See on dwell, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="lu+11:26">Luke 11:26</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+2:5">Acts 2:5</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mr+5:3">Mark 5:3</span>. In marked contrast with sojourners, ver. 19. Through the Spirit [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εν] </span></span>. Better, as Rev., in. In the fellowship of the indwelling Spirit. </p>
