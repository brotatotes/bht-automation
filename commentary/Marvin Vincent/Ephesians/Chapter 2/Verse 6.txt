<p>Raised us up. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ro+6:5">Romans 6:5</span>. </p>
<p>Made us sit together [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">συνεκαθισεν] </span></span>. Compare set Him, ch. 1 20. </p>
<p>Together is ambiguous. Render with Him, as Rev. "Even now we sit there in Him, and shall sit with Him in the end" (Andrews, cited by Ellicott). Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ro+8:30">Romans 8:30</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="re+3:21">Revelation 3:21</span>. Meyer renders hath given us joint seat. </p>
<p>In Christ Jesus. Connect with raised up, made us sit, and in heavenly places. Resurrection, enthronement, heaven, all are in Christ.</p>
