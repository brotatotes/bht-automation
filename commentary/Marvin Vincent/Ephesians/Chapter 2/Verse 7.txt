<p>The ages to come (toiv aijwsin toiv ejpercomenoiv). Lit., the ages, those which are coming on. Which are successively arriving until Christ 's second coming. </p>
<p>He might show [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ενδειξηται] </span></span>. The middle voice denotes for His own glory. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="col+1:6">Colossians 1:6</span>. </p>
<p>In kindness [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εν χρηστοτητι] </span></span>. See on easy, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+11:30">Matthew 11:30</span>. The grace of God is to be displayed in His actual benefits.</p>
