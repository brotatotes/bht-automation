The ages to come (toiv aijwsin toiv ejpercomenoiv). Lit., the ages, those which are coming on. Which are successively arriving until Christ 's second coming. He might show [ενδειξηται] . The middle voice denotes for His own glory. See on Colossians 1:6. In kindness [εν χρηστοτητι] . See on easy, Matthew 11:30. The grace of God is to be displayed in His actual benefits.
