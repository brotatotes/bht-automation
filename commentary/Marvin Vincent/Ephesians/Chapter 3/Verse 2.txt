<p>If ye have heard [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ειγε ηκουσατε] </span></span>. Here begins a long digression extending to ver. 14. If, Rev., if so be, means upon the supposition that; not implying the certainty of the assumption, though this shade of meaning is given by the context. The words are a reminder of his preaching among them. </p>
<p>Dispensation [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">οικονομιαν] </span></span>. See on ch. <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="eph+1:10">Ephesians 1:10</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="col+1:25">Colossians 1:25</span>. The divine arrangement or disposition.</p>
