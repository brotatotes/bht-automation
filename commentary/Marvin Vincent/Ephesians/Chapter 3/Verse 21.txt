Glory. Properly, the glory, which is His due. In the Church. Through which His many - tinted wisdom is to be displayed, and which is His fullness. The variety of the divine wisdom is again hinted at in all that we ask or think. By Christ Jesus [εν] . Rev., better, in. As the Church is the outward domain in which God is to be praised, so Christ is the spiritual sphere of this praise. Throughout all ages, world without end [εις πασας τας γενεας του αιωνος των αιωνων] . Lit., unto all the generations of the age of the ages. Eternity is made up of ages, and ages of generations.
