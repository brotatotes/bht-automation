<p>Glory. Properly, the glory, which is His due. </p>
<p>In the Church. Through which His many - tinted wisdom is to be displayed, and which is His fullness. The variety of the divine wisdom is again hinted at in all that we ask or think. </p>
<p>By Christ Jesus [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εν] </span></span>. Rev., better, in. As the Church is the outward domain in which God is to be praised, so Christ is the spiritual sphere of this praise. </p>
<p>Throughout all ages, world without end [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εις πασας τας γενεας του αιωνος των αιωνων] </span></span>. Lit., unto all the generations of the age of the ages. Eternity is made up of ages, and ages of generations. </p>
