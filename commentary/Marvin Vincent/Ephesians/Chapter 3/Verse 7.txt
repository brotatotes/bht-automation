<p>Gift of the grace. The gift in which the grace of God consisted, the apostleship to the Gentiles. </p>
<p>By the effectual working of His power [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κατα την ενεργειαν της δυναμεως αυτου] </span></span>. Rev., better, according to the working, etc. The gift was bestowed in accordance with that efficiency which could transform Saul the persecutor into Paul the apostle to the Gentiles.</p>
