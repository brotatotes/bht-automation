Gift of the grace. The gift in which the grace of God consisted, the apostleship to the Gentiles. By the effectual working of His power [κατα την ενεργειαν της δυναμεως αυτου] . Rev., better, according to the working, etc. The gift was bestowed in accordance with that efficiency which could transform Saul the persecutor into Paul the apostle to the Gentiles.
