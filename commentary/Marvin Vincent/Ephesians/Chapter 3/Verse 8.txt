Less than the least [τω ελαχιστοτερω] . Only here in the New Testament, and very characteristic. A comparative is formed upon a superlative : more least than all the saints. Compare 1 Corinthians 14:8. 1 Corinthians 14:1 1 Corinthians 14:68 Unsearchable [ανεξιχνιαστον] . Only here and Romans 11:33 (note). Which cannot be tracked out.
