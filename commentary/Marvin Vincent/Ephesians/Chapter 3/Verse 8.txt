<p>Less than the least [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τω ελαχιστοτερω] </span></span>. Only here in the New Testament, and very characteristic. A comparative is formed upon a superlative : more least than all the saints. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1co+14:8">1 Corinthians 14:8</span>. <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1co+14:1">1 Corinthians 14:1</span> <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1co+14:68">1 Corinthians 14:68</span> Unsearchable [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ανεξιχνιαστον] </span></span>. Only here and <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ro+11:33">Romans 11:33</span> (note). </p>
