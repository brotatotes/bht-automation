<p>Till [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μεχρι] </span></span>. Specifying the time up to which this ministry and impartation of gifts are to last. </p>
<p>Come [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">καταντησωμεν] </span></span>. Arrive at, as a goal. See <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+16:1">Acts 16:1</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+18:19">Acts 18:19</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+25:13">Acts 25:13</span>. Rev., attain. </p>
<p>In the unity [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εις] </span></span>. Rev., correctly, unto. Compare one faith, ver. 5. Knowledge [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">της επιγνωσεως] </span></span>. The full knowledge. Not identical with faith, since the article puts it as a distinct conception; but related to faith. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="php+3:9">Philippians 3:9</span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="php+3:10">Philippians 3:10</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1jo+4:16">1 John 4:16</span>. "Christians are not to be informed merely on different sections of truth and erring through defective information on other points, but they are to be characterized by the completeness and harmony of their ideas of the power, work, history, and glory of the Son of God" (Eadie). </p>
<p>Of the Son of God. Belongs to both faith and knowledge. Faith in Him, knowledge of Him. </p>
<p>Perfect [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τελειον] </span></span>. Rev., full grown. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1co+2:6">1 Corinthians 2:6</span>. </p>
<p>Measure of the stature [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μετρον ηλικιας] </span></span>. Defining perfect man. For stature, see on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="lu+12:25">Luke 12:25</span>. The word is rendered age, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="joh+9:21">John 9:21</span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="joh+9:23">John 9:23</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="heb+11:11">Hebrews 11:11</span>. So here, by some, the age when the fullness of Christ is received. But fullness and grow up (ver. 15) suggest rather the idea of magnitude. </p>
<p>Fullness of Christ. Which belongs to Christ and is imparted by Him. See <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="joh+1:16">John 1:16</span>, and compare ch. 3 19.</p>
