<p>Speaking the truth [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αληθευοντες] </span></span>. Only here and <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ga+4:16">Galatians 4:16</span>. In classical Greek it means to be true, to arrive at truth, and to speak truth. Here the idea is rather that of being or walking in truth. Rev., in margin, dealing truly. </p>
<p>In love. Some connect with grow up. The parallel construction, tossed and carried about in the sleight, in craftiness, speaking truth in love, favors the A. V. and Rev., as does the awkwardness of speaking truth standing alone. Moreover, Paul 's habit is to subjoin, and not to prefix, his qualifying clauses.</p>
