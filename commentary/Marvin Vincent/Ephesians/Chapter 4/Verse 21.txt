<p>If so be that ye heard Him [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ει γε αυτον ηκουσατε] </span></span>. The indicative mood implies the truth of the supposition : if ye heard as ye did. Him is emphatic. If it was Him that ye heard. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="joh+10:27">John 10:27</span>. </p>
<p>By Him [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εν αυτω</span></span>] . Rev., correctly, in Him. In fellowship with. As the truth is in Jesus [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">καθως εστιν αληθεια εν τω ιησου] </span></span>. As corresponds with not so. Ye did not in such a manner learn Christ if ye were taught in such a manner as is truth, etc. Render, as Rev., as truth is in Jesus. Schaff paraphrases : "If you were taught so that what you received is true as embodied in the personal Savior." " Taught in the lines of eternal fact and spiritual reality which meet in him " [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μουλε] </span></span>. Jesus is used rather than Christ : the historical rather than the official name. The life of Christianity consists in believing fellowship with the historic Jesus, who is the Christ of prophecy.</p>
