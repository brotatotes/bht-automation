New man [καινον] . See on Matthew 26:29. Created [κτισθεντα] . See on ch. Ephesians 2:10. In righteousness and true holiness [εν δικιαιοσυνη και οσιοτητι της αληθειας] . Rev., correctly, in righteousness and holiness of truth. See on Luke 1:75. Truth. Opposed to deceit, ver. 22, and likewise personified. Righteousness and holiness are attributes of truth.
