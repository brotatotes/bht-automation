<p>Be ye angry and sin not [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">οργιζεσθε και μη αμαρτανετε] </span></span>. Cited from <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ps+4:5">Psalms 4:5</span>, after the Septuagint. Hebrew, stand in awe and sin not. Righteous anger is commanded, not merely permitted. </p>
<p>Wrath [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">παροργισμω</span></span>] Irritation, exasperation; something not so enduring as ojrgh anger, which denotes a deep - seated sentiment. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="joh+3:36">John 3:36</span>.</p>
