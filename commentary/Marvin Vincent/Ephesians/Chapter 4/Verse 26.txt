Be ye angry and sin not [οργιζεσθε και μη αμαρτανετε] . Cited from Psalms 4:5, after the Septuagint. Hebrew, stand in awe and sin not. Righteous anger is commanded, not merely permitted. Wrath [παροργισμω] Irritation, exasperation; something not so enduring as ojrgh anger, which denotes a deep - seated sentiment. See on John 3:36.
