Corrupt [σαπρος] . See on Luke 6:43, and Colossians 4:6. That which is good [ει τις αγαθος] . Lit., if any is good. Discourse that is good, whatever it be. To the use of edifying [προς οικοδομην της χρειας] . Lit., for the building up of the need. Rev., edifying as the need may be. Compare 1 Thessalonians 5:11, 1 Thessalonians 5:14.
