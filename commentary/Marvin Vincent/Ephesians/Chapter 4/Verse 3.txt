<p>Endeavoring [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">σπουδαζοντες] </span></span>. Not strong enough. Originally the verb means to make haste. So the kindred noun spoudh haste, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mr+6:25">Mark 6:25</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="lu+1:39">Luke 1:39</span>. Hence diligence. Rev., here, giving diligence. </p>
<p>To keep [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τηρειν] </span></span>. See on reserved, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1pe+1:4">1 Peter 1:4</span>. </p>
<p>Unity of the Spirit. Wrought by the Holy Spirit. </p>
<p>Bond of peace. The bond which is peace. Compare ch. 2 14, our peace - made both one. Christ, our peace, is thus a bond of peace. Others, however, treat in the bond as parallel with in love of ver. 2, and cite <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="col+3:14">Colossians 3:14</span>, "love the bond of perfectness."</p>
