Endeavoring [σπουδαζοντες] . Not strong enough. Originally the verb means to make haste. So the kindred noun spoudh haste, Mark 6:25; Luke 1:39. Hence diligence. Rev., here, giving diligence. To keep [τηρειν] . See on reserved, 1 Peter 1:4. Unity of the Spirit. Wrought by the Holy Spirit. Bond of peace. The bond which is peace. Compare ch. 2 14, our peace - made both one. Christ, our peace, is thus a bond of peace. Others, however, treat in the bond as parallel with in love of ver. 2, and cite Colossians 3:14, "love the bond of perfectness."
