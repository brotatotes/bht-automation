<p>One God and Father. The fundamental ground of unity. Note the climax : One Church, one Christ, one God. </p>
<p>Above all [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επι παντων] </span></span>. Rev, over : as ruler. </p>
<p>Through - in [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δια - εν] </span></span>. Through, pervading : in, indwelling. Compare ch. <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="eph+2:22">Ephesians 2:22</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="eph+3:17">Ephesians 3:17</span>.</p>
