<p>He saith. God. This use of the personal pronoun is frequent in Paul 's writings. See <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ga+3:16">Galatians 3:16</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="eph+4:8">Ephesians 4:8</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1co+6:16">1 Corinthians 6:16</span>. Awake. etc. The quotation is probably a combination and free rendering of <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="isa+60:1">Isaiah 60:1</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="isa+26:19">Isaiah 26:19</span>. For similar combinations see on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ro+3:10">Romans 3:10</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ro+9:33">Romans 9:33</span>. By some the words are regarded as the fragment of a hymn. </p>
<p>Shall give thee light. Rev., correctly, shall shine upon thee.</p>
