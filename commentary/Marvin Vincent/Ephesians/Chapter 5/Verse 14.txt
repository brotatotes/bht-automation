He saith. God. This use of the personal pronoun is frequent in Paul 's writings. See Galatians 3:16; Ephesians 4:8; 1 Corinthians 6:16. Awake. etc. The quotation is probably a combination and free rendering of Isaiah 60:1; Isaiah 26:19. For similar combinations see on Romans 3:10; Romans 9:33. By some the words are regarded as the fragment of a hymn. Shall give thee light. Rev., correctly, shall shine upon thee.
