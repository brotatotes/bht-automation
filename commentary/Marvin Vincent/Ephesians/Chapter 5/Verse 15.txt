<p>See that ye walk circumspectly [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">βλεπετε πως ακριβως περιπατειτε] </span></span>. Lit., look how exactly ye walk. The best texts place twv how after ajkribwv exactly. So Rev., look carefully how ye walk. </p>
<p>Circumspectly is better rendered carefully. It means exactly, accurately, from akrov the farthest point. See on inquired diligently, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+2:6">Matthew 2:6</span>; and compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="lu+1:3">Luke 1:3</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+18:25">Acts 18:25</span>, notes. </p>
<p>Not as unwise, but as wise. Explanatory of carefully.</p>
