<p>Speaking to yourselves [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">λαλουντες εαυτοις] </span></span>. Rev., one to another. The A. V. is literally correct, but is open to the misinterpretation each one communing with himself. The meaning is as in <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="col+3:13">Colossians 3:13</span>, and Rev. is better. </p>
