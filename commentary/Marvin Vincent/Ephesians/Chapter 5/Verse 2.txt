Walk in love. As imitators of God who is love. Loved us [ημας] The correct reading is uJmav you. Gave [παρεδωκεν] . To death Compare Romans 4:25, where the same verb was delivered is followed by was raised. See also Romans 8:32; Galatians 2:20. Offering - sacrifice [προσφοραν - θυσιαν] . Offering, general, including the life as well as the death of Christ : sacrifice, special : on the cross. Properly, a slain offering. A sweet smelling savor [οσμην ευωδιας] . Rev., correctly, odor of a sweet smell. See on 2 Corinthians 2:14, 2 Corinthians 2:15, 2 Corinthians 2:16. The Septuagint, in Leviticus 1:9, uses this phrase to render the Hebrew, a savor of quietness. For [εις] expresses design, that it might become, or result : so that it became.
