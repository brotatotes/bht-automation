He might present it to Himself [παραστηση αυτος εαυτω] . As a bride. Compare 2 Corinthians 11:2. Notice the two pronouns in conjunction, He, to Himself. Christ Himself presents the bride. Spot [σπιλον] . Only here and 2 Peter 2:13; The kindred verb spilow to defile, occurs James 3:6; Jude 1:23. Wrinkle [ρυτιδα] . Only here in the New Testament.
