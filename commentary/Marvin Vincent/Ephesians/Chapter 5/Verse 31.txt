<p>Shall be joined [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">προσκολληθησεται] </span></span>. Only here; and <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mr+10:7">Mark 10:7</span>. </p>
<p>See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="lu+14:15">Luke 14:15</span>. The compound verb denotes most intimate union. </p>
<p>Shall be one flesh [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εσονται εις σαρκα μιαν] </span></span>. The A. V. overlooks the force of eijv unto. Lit., shall be unto one flesh. Rev., shall become.</p>
