<p>Ye know [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ιστε γινωσκοντες] </span></span>. The A. V. fails to give the whole force of the expression, which is, ye know recognizing. Rev., ye know of a surety. </p>
<p>Idolater. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="col+3:5">Colossians 3:5</span>, and see on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1co+5:10">1 Corinthians 5:10</span>.</p>
