<p>We wrestle [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εστιν ημιν η παλη] </span></span>. Rev., more literally and correctly, our wrestling is. Palh wrestling, only here. </p>
<p>Flesh and blood. The Greek reverses the order. </p>
<p>Principalities and powers. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="col+1:16">Colossians 1:16</span>. </p>
<p>Rulers of the darkness of this world [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κοσμοκρατορας του σκοτους τουτου] </span></span>. Rev., more correctly, world - rulers of this darkness. </p>
<p>World - Rulers only here. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="joh+14:30">John 14:30</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="joh+16:11">John 16:11</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1jo+5:19">1 John 5:19</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="2co+4:4">2 Corinthians 4:4</span>. </p>
<p>Spiritual wickedness [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τα πνευματικα της πονηριας</span></span>] Lit., the spiritual things of wickedness. Rev., spiritual hosts of wickedness. The phrase is collective, of the evil powers viewed as a body. Wickedness is active evil, mischief. Hence Satan is called oJ ponhrov the wicked one. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="lu+3:19">Luke 3:19</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="lu+7:21">Luke 7:21</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1jo+2:13">1 John 2:13</span>. </p>
<p>In high places [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εν τοις επουρανιοις] </span></span>. Rev., more literally, in the heavenly places. Used in the general sense of the sky or air. See on ch. <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="eph+2:2">Ephesians 2:2</span>.</p>
