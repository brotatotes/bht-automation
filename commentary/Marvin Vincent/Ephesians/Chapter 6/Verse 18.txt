<p>Always [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εν παντι καιρω] </span></span>. Incorrect. It means on every occasion. Rev., at all seasons. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="lu+21:36">Luke 21:36</span>. </p>
<p>With all prayer and supplication [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δια πασης προσευχης και δεησεως] </span></span>. Prayer is general, supplication special. Dia with is literally through; that is, through the medium of. All, lit., every. Prayer is of various kinds, formal, silent, vocal, secret, public, petitionary, ejaculatory - shot upward like a dart (jaculum) on a sudden emergency. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ps+5:1">Psalms 5:1</span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ps+5:2">Psalms 5:2</span>. </p>
<p>Watching thereunto [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εις αυτο αγρυπνουντες] </span></span>. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="col+4:2">Colossians 4:2</span>. For watching, see on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mr+13:33">Mark 13:33</span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mr+13:35">Mark 13:35</span>. Thereunto, unto prayer, for occasions of prayer, and to maintain the spirit of prayer. One must watch before prayer, in prayer, after prayer. </p>
<p>Perseverance [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">προσκαρτερησει] </span></span>. Only here. The kindred verb proskarterew to continue, occurs often. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+1:14">Acts 1:14</span>.</p>
