<p>I am an ambassador in bonds [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πρεσβευω εν αλυσει] </span></span>. The verb to be an ambassador occurs only here and <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="2co+5:20">2 Corinthians 5:20</span>. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="php+1:9">Philippians 1:9</span>. In bonds, lit., in a chain : the particular word for the coupling - chain by which he was bound to the hand of his guard.</p>
