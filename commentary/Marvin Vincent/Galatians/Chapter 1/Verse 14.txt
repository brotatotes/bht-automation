<p>Profited [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">προεκοπτον] </span></span>. Better, advanced. See on is far spent, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ro+13:12">Romans 13:12</span>. Paul means that he outstripped his Jewish contemporaries in distinctively Jewish culture, zeal, and activity. Comp. <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="php+3:4-6">Philippians 3:4-6</span>. </p>
<p>Equals [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">συνηλικιωτας] </span></span>. N. T. o. The A. V. is indefinite. The meaning is equals in age. So Rev., of mine own age. </p>
<p>Nation [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">γενει] </span></span>. Race. Not sect of the Pharisees. Comp. <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="php+3:5">Philippians 3:5</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="2co+11:26">2 Corinthians 11:26</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ro+9:3">Romans 9:3</span>. </p>
<p>Zealous [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ζηλωτης] </span></span>. Lit. a zealot. The extreme party of the Pharisees called themselves "zealots of the law ";" zealots of God. " See on Simon the Canaanite, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mr+3:18">Mark 3:18</span>. Paul describes himself under this name in his speech on the stairs, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+22:3">Acts 22:3</span>. Comp. <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="php+3:5">Philippians 3:5</span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="php+3:6">Philippians 3:6</span>. </p>
<p>Traditions [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">παραδοσεων] </span></span>. The Pharisaic traditions which had been engrafted on the law. See <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+14:2">Matthew 14:2</span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+14:6">Matthew 14:6</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mr+7:3">Mark 7:3</span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mr+7:13">Mark 7:13</span>, and on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="2th+2:15">2 Thessalonians 2:15</span>.</p>
