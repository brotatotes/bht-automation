To see [ιστορησαι] . N. T. o. 1. To inquire into : 2. to find out by inquiring : 3. to gain knowledge by visiting; to become personally acquainted with. In LXX, only 1 Ezra 1:33, Ezra 1:42, to relate, to record. Often in Class. The word here indicates that Paul went, not to obtain instruction, but to form acquaintance with Peter. Cephas. See on Matthew 16:18; John 1:42; 1 Corinthians 1:12.
