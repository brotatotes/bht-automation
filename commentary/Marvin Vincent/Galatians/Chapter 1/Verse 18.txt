<p>To see [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ιστορησαι] </span></span>. N. T. o. </p>
<p>1. To inquire into : </p>
<p>2. to find out by inquiring : </p>
<p>3. to gain knowledge by visiting; to become personally acquainted with. In LXX, only 1 <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ezr+1:33">Ezra 1:33</span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ezr+1:42">Ezra 1:42</span>, to relate, to record. Often in Class. The word here indicates that Paul went, not to obtain instruction, but to form acquaintance with Peter. </p>
<p>Cephas. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+16:18">Matthew 16:18</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="joh+1:42">John 1:42</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1co+1:12">1 Corinthians 1:12</span>.</p>
