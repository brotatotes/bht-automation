<p>Brethren - with me. The circle of Paul 's colleagues or more intimate friends. Comp. <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="php+4:21">Philippians 4:21</span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="php+4:22">Philippians 4:22</span>, where the brethren with me are distinguished from all the saints - the church members generally. </p>
<p>Unto the churches of Galatia. See Introduction. This is a circular letter to several congregations. Note the omission of the commendatory words added to the addresses in the two Thessalonian and first Corinthian letters.</p>
