<p>Was unknown [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ημην αγνοουμενος] </span></span>. Better, was still unknown, the imperfect denoting that he remained unknown during his stay in Syria and Cilicia. </p>
<p>Of Judaea. The province, as distinguished from Jerusalem, where he must have been known as the persecutor of the church. See <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+9:1">Acts 9:1</span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+9:2">Acts 9:2</span>. </p>
<p>Which were in Christ. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1th+2:14">1 Thessalonians 2:14</span>.</p>
