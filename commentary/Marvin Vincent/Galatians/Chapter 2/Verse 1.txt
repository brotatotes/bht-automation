Fourteen years after [δια δεκατεσσαρων ετων] . Rev. after the space of fourteen years. Comp. dij ejtwn pleionwn after several years, Acts 24:17; dij hJmerwn after (some) days, Mark 2:1. Dia means after, that is, a given number of years being interposed between two points of time. Not, in the course of (Rev. marg.).
