<p>Fourteen years after [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δια δεκατεσσαρων ετων] </span></span>. Rev. after the space of fourteen years. Comp. dij ejtwn pleionwn after several years, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+24:17">Acts 24:17</span>; dij hJmerwn after (some) days, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mr+2:1">Mark 2:1</span>. Dia means after, that is, a given number of years being interposed between two points of time. Not, in the course of (Rev. marg.).</p>
