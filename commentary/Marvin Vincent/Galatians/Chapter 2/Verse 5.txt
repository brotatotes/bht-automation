<p>We gave place by subjection [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ειξαμεν τη υποταγη] </span></span>. We, Paul and Barnabas. Gave place or yielded, N. T. o By the subjection which was demanded of us. The noun only in Paul and the Pastorals, and always in the sense of self - subjection. Comp. <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="2co+9:13">2 Corinthians 9:13</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1ti+2:11">1 Timothy 2:11</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1ti+3:4">1 Timothy 3:4</span>.</p>
