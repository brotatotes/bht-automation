<p>But [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δε] </span></span>. Better, now. The de continues the argument, adding the scripture testimony. </p>
<p>By the law [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εν νομω] </span></span>. Rather, in the sphere of the law; thus corresponding with continueth in, verse 10. </p>
<p>The just shall live by faith [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ο δικαιος εκ πιστεως ζησεται] </span></span>. Better, the righteous. Quoted from <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="hab+2:4">Habakkuk 2:4</span>, and appears in <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ro+1:17">Romans 1:17</span>, and <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="heb+10:28">Hebrews 10:28</span>. The LXX has mou my, either after dikaiov, "my righteous one shall live, etc.," or after pistewv, "by my faith or faithfulness." 60</p>
