But [δε] . Better, now. The de continues the argument, adding the scripture testimony. By the law [εν νομω] . Rather, in the sphere of the law; thus corresponding with continueth in, verse 10. The just shall live by faith [ο δικαιος εκ πιστεως ζησεται] . Better, the righteous. Quoted from Habakkuk 2:4, and appears in Romans 1:17, and Hebrews 10:28. The LXX has mou my, either after dikaiov, "my righteous one shall live, etc.," or after pistewv, "by my faith or faithfulness." 60
