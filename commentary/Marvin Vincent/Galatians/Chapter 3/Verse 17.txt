<p>And this I say [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τουτο δε λεγω] </span></span>. Now I mean this. Not strictly the conclusion from vv. 15, 16, since Paul does not use this phrase in drawing a conclusion (comp. <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1co+1:12">1 Corinthians 1:12</span>, and touto de fhmi, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1co+7:29">1 Corinthians 7:29</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1co+14:50">1 Corinthians 14:50</span>). It is rather the application, for which the way was prepared in verse 16, of the analogy of verse 15 to the inviolable stability of God 's covenant. </p>
