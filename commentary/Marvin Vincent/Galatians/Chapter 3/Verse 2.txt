<p>This only. I will convince you of your error by this one point. Do you owe the gifts of the Spirit to the works of the law, or to the message of faith ? </p>
<p>Received ye, etc. The answer lies in the question. You cannot deny that you received the gifts of the Spirit by the message of faith. </p>
