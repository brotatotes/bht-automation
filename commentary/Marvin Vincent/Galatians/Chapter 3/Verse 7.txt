<p>Know ye [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">γινωσκετε] </span></span>. Imperative. It may also be rendered as indicative, ye know, but the imperative is livelier, and the statement in the verse is one of the points which the writer is trying to prove. </p>
<p>They which are of faith [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">οι εκ πιστεως] </span></span>. Ek pistewv from or out of faith, is found with the verb to justify (<span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ro+3:26">Romans 3:26</span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ro+3:30">Romans 3:30</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ro+5:1">Romans 5:1</span>) : with other verbs, as live (<span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ro+1:17">Romans 1:17</span>); eat (<span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ro+14:23">Romans 14:23</span>) : with the noun dikaiosunh righteousness (<span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ro+1:17">Romans 1:17</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ro+9:30">Romans 9:30</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ro+10:6">Romans 10:6</span>) : with other nouns, as promise (<span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ga+3:22">Galatians 3:22</span>), law (<span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ga+3:12">Galatians 3:12</span>). For parallels to the phrase oiJ ejk pistewv, see <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ro+3:26">Romans 3:26</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ro+4:16">Romans 4:16</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ro+14:23">Romans 14:23</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ga+3:9">Galatians 3:9</span>. It denotes believers as sprung from, or receiving their spiritual condition from that which specially characterizes them. Comp. oiJ ejx ejriqiav they who are of faction, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ro+2:8">Romans 2:8</span>; oiJ ejk nomou they who are of the law, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ro+4:14">Romans 4:14</span>; oJ ejk thv ajlhqeiav he who is of the truth, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="joh+18:37">John 18:37</span>.</p>
