<p>With [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">συν] </span></span>. Not = like or as, but in fellowship with. Believers are regarded as homogeneous with Abraham, and as thus sharing the blessing which began in him. </p>
<p>Faithful [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πιστω] </span></span>. Or believing, as <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+16:1">Acts 16:1</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="2co+11:15">2 Corinthians 11:15</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1ti+5:16">1 Timothy 5:16</span>. Those who are of the faith are one in blessing with him whose characteristic was faith.</p>
