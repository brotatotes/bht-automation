The metaphorical use of the word is frequent in O. T. See Psalms 7:14; Sir. 19 11; 31 5; 63 17; Micah 4:10; Isaiah 26:18; Isaiah 66:8. Paul means that he is for the second time laboring and distressed for the Galatian converts, with the same anguish which attended his first efforts for their conversion. The metaphor of begetting children in the gospel is found in 1 Corinthians 4:15; Philippians 1:10. It was a Jewish saying : "If one teaches the son of his neighbor the law, the Scripture reckons this the same as though he had begotten him."
