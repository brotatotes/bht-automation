<p>A bondmaid [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">της παιδισκης] </span></span>. The bondmaid, indicating a well known character, Hagar, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ge+16:3">Genesis 16:3</span>. The word in Class. means also a free maiden; but in N. T. always a slave. So almost always in LXX; but see <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ru+4:12">Ruth 4:12</span>; Judith 12 13.</p>
