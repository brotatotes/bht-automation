For [γαρ] . Your determination to be under the law is opposed by Scripture, if you will understand it, for it is written, etc. A bondmaid [της παιδισκης] . The bondmaid, indicating a well known character, Hagar, Genesis 16:3. The word in Class. means also a free maiden; but in N. T. always a slave. So almost always in LXX; but see Ruth 4:12; Judith 12 13.
