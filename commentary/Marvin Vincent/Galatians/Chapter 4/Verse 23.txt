Was born [γεγεννηται] . Has been born, or is born : perfect tense, treating the historical fact as if present. After the flesh [κατα σαρκα] . According to the regular course of nature. Very common in Paul. By promise [δι επαγγελιας] . Most editors retain the article, the promise of Genesis 17:16, Genesis 17:19; Genesis 18:10. Comp. Romans 9:9. In virtue of the promise; for according to natural conditions he would not have been born.
