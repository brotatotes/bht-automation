<p>Are an allegory [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εστιν αλληγορουμενα] </span></span>. N. T. o. Lit. are allegorised. From allo another, ajgoreuein to speak. Hence, things which are so spoken as to give a different meaning from that which the words express. For parable, allegory, fable, and proverb, see on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+13:3">Matthew 13:3</span>. </p>
