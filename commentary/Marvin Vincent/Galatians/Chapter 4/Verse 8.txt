<p>Over against their filial freedom in Christ, Paul sets their lapse into subjection to the elements of the world (verse 3). </p>
<p>Knew not God. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="2th+1:8">2 Thessalonians 1:8</span>. </p>
<p>Ye did service [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εδουλευσατε] </span></span>. Better, were in bondage or were slaves. By nature [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">φυσει] </span></span>. Not denying their existence (comp. <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1co+8:5">1 Corinthians 8:5</span>) but their deity. Emphasis on by nature.
