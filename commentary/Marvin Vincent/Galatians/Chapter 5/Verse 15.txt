<p>Bite and devour [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δακνετε και κατεσθιετε] </span></span>. Strong expressions of partisan hatred exerting itself for mutual injury. Daknein to bite, N. T. o. In LXX metaphorically, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mic+3:5">Micah 3:5</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="hab+2:7">Habakkuk 2:7</span>. For katesqiein devour, comp. <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+23:13">Matthew 23:13</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="2co+11:20">2 Corinthians 11:20</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="re+11:5">Revelation 11:5</span>. </p>
<p>Be consumed [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αναλωθητε] </span></span>. Rare in N. T. See <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="lu+9:54">Luke 9:54</span>. Partisan strife will be fatal to the Christian community as a whole. The organic life of the body will be destroyed by its own members.</p>
