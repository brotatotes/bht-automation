Contrast of Paul 's own boasting and its ground with those of the false apostles. By whom [δι ου] . The relative may refer either to the cross, by which, or to Christ, by whom. The cross was a stumbling - block to the Jews (ch. 3 13), and it is the crucified Christ that Paul is emphasizing. Comp. ch. Galatians 2:20; Galatians 5:24. The world [κοσμος] .
