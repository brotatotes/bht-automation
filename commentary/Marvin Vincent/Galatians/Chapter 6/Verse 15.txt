A new creature [καινη κτισις] . Comp. 2 Corinthians 5:17. For kainh new see on Matthew 26:29. For ktisiv on Romans 8:19; 2 Corinthians 5:17. Here of the thing created, not of the act of creating. The phrase was common in Jewish writers for one brought to the knowledge of the true God. Comp. Ephesians 2:10, Ephesians 2:15.
