<p>Be not deceived [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μη πλανασθε] </span></span>. For the phrase see <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1co+6:9">1 Corinthians 6:9</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1co+14:33">1 Corinthians 14:33</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="jas+1:16">James 1:16</span>. Deceive is a secondary sense; the primary meaning being lead astray. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mr+12:24">Mark 12:24</span>. The connection of the exhortation may be with the entire section from ver. 1 (Eadie and Sieffert), but is more probably with ver. 6. The Galatians are not to think that it is a matter of no consequence whether their fellowship be with their Christian teachers who preach the word of truth, or with the Judaising innovators who would bring them under bondage to the law. </p>
<p>Is not mocked [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ου μυκτηριζεται] </span></span>. N. T. o. Quite often in LXX See <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1ki+18:27">1 Kings 18:27</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="2ki+19:21">2 Kings 19:21</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="job+22:19">Job 22:19</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="pr+1:30">Proverbs 1:30</span>. Also the noun mukthrismov mockery, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="job+34:7">Job 34:7</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ps+34:16">Psalms 34:16</span>. See Ps. of <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="so+4:8">Song of Solomon 4:8</span>. The verb, literally, to turn up the nose at. Comp. Horace, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="jas+1:6">James 1:6</span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="jas+1:5">James 1:5</span>, naso suspendis adunco, 2 8, 64; Epist. 1 19, 45. </p>
<p>That [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τουτο] </span></span>. Most emphatic. That and nothing else. Comp. <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+7:16">Matthew 7:16</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="2co+9:6">2 Corinthians 9:6</span>.</p>
