Be weary [ενκακωμεν] . Lit. faint or lose heart. Comp. 2 Thessalonians 3:13. In due season [καιρω ιδιω] . In the season which is peculiarly the harvest - time of each form of well - doing. See on ver. 5. Faint [εκλυομενοι] . Only here in Paul. See Matthew 14:32; Mark 8:3; Hebrews 12:3, Hebrews 12:5. Lit. to be loosened or relaxed, like the limbs of the weary.
