<p>Sixth quotation (10 - 12), exhibiting the superior dignity of the Son as creator in contrast with the creature. Psalm ci. 26 - 28. The Psalm declares the eternity of Jahveh. </p>

T. o. Often in Class., LXX only <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ps+18:152">Psalms 18:152</span>. The more usual formula is ejn ajrch or ajp' ajrchv. </p>
