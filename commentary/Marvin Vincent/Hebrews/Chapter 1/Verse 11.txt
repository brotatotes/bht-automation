<p>They [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αυτοι] </span></span>. The heavens : not heaven and earth. </p>
<p>Remainest [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">διαμενεις] </span></span>. Note the present tense : not shalt remain. Permanency is the characteristic of God in the absolute and eternal present.</p>
