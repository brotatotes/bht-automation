<p>Vesture [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">περιβολαιον] </span></span>. Only here and <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1co+11:5">1 Corinthians 11:5</span>. From periballein to throw around : a wrapper, mantle. </p>
<p>Shalt thou fold them up [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ελιξεις αυτους] </span></span>. Rather, roll them up. A scribal error for ajllaxeiv shalt change. After these words the LXX repeats wJv iJmation as a garment from ver. 11. </p>
<p>Shall not fail [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ουκ εκλειψουσιν] </span></span>. Shall not be ended. With this exception the verb only in Luke's Gospel. See <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="lu+16:9">Luke 16:9</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="lu+22:32">Luke 22:32</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="lu+23:45">Luke 23:45</span>. Very frequent in LXX</p>
