<p>Seventh quotation, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ps+109:0">Psalms 109:0</span>. No one of the angels was ever enthroned at God 's right hand. </p>
<p>Sit [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">καθου] </span></span>. Or be sitting, as distinguished from ejkaqisen, ver. 3, which marked the act of assuming the place. </p>
<p>On my right hand [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εκ δεξιων μου] </span></span>. Lit. "from my right hand." The usual formula is ejn dexia. The genitive indicates moving from the right hand and taking the seat. The meaning is, "be associated with me in my royal dignity." Comp. <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="da+7:13">Daniel 7:13</span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="da+7:14">Daniel 7:14</span>, and the combination of the Psalm and Dan. in Christ 's words, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mr+14:62">Mark 14:62</span>. Comp. also <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+24:30">Matthew 24:30</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+2:34">Acts 2:34</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1co+14:25">1 Corinthians 14:25</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1pe+3:22">1 Peter 3:22</span>.</p>
