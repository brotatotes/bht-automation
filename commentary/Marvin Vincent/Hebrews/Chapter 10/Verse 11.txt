<p>Every priest [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πας] </span></span>. Suggesting many priests. Comp. ch. <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="heb+7:23">Hebrews 7:23</span>. Standeth [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εστηκεν] </span></span>. Servile attitude, contrasted with that of the exalted Savior, ch. 1 3. </p>
<p>Daily - often - the same. The wearisome round of daily offerings, always the same, contrasted with the one offering, once for all. Take away [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">περιελειν] </span></span>. Only here in connection with sin. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="2co+3:16">2 Corinthians 3:16</span>. The verb literally means to strip off all round.
