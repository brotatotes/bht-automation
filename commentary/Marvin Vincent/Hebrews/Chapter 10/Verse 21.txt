A high priest [ιερεα μεγαν] . Lit. a great priest. Comp. Leviticus 21:10, LXX Not merely = ajrciereuv high priest, but emphasizing Christ 's superior greatness as high priest. House of God [οικον του θεου] . In the Gospels always of the temple. Not found in Paul. Once in the Pastorals, of the church, 1 Timothy 3:15, and so 1 Peter 4:17. Here the whole Christian family. Comp. 1 Corinthians 3:16, 1 Corinthians 3:17; 2 Corinthians 6:16; Ephesians 2:22.
