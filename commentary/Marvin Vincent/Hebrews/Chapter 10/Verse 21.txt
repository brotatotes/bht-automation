<p>A high priest [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ιερεα μεγαν] </span></span>. Lit. a great priest. Comp. <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="le+21:10">Leviticus 21:10</span>, LXX Not merely = ajrciereuv high priest, but emphasizing Christ 's superior greatness as high priest. </p>
<p>House of God [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">οικον του θεου] </span></span>. In the Gospels always of the temple. Not found in Paul. Once in the Pastorals, of the church, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1ti+3:15">1 Timothy 3:15</span>, and so <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1pe+4:17">1 Peter 4:17</span>. Here the whole Christian family. Comp. <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1co+3:16">1 Corinthians 3:16</span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1co+3:17">1 Corinthians 3:17</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="2co+6:16">2 Corinthians 6:16</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="eph+2:22">Ephesians 2:22</span>.</p>
