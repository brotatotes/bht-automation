<p>Profession of our faith [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">την ομολογιαν της ελπιδος] </span></span>. Rend. "confession of our hope." Faith does not appear among Ms. readings. It is an innovation of the translators. Hope is the rendering of Tyndale, Coverdale, the Great Bible, the Geneva, the Bishops ', and Rheims. On confession see on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="2co+9:13">2 Corinthians 9:13</span>, and comp. notes on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1ti+6:12">1 Timothy 6:12</span>, </p>
