<p>A remembrance of sins [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αναμνησις αμαρτιων] </span></span>. Each successive sacrifice was a fresh reminder of sins to be atoned for; so far were the sacrifices from satisfying the conscience of the worshipper. jAnamnhsiv, lit. a calling to mind. Comp. ver. 17, and see LXX, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="nu+5:15">Numbers 5:15</span>.</p>
