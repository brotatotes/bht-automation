<p>Ye might receive the promise [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κομισησθε την επαγγελιαν] </span></span>. Comp. ch. <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="heb+11:13">Hebrews 11:13</span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="heb+11:39">Hebrews 11:39</span>, and see on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1pe+1:8">1 Peter 1:8</span>. The verb implies, not mere obtaining, but receiving and carrying away for use and enjoyment.</p>
