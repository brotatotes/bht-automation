As good as dead [νενεκρωμενου] . Comp. Romans 4:19. As good as is an addition of A. V. The Greek reads and that a dead man. Comp. nekrwsin deadness applied to Sarah, Romans 4:19. Stars - sand. See Genesis 22:17; Genesis 32:12. By the seashore [παρα το χειλος της θαλασσης] . Lit. by the lip of the sea. The phrase N. T. o. Very often in LXX, as Genesis 22:17; Exodus 14:30 : lip of a river, Genesis 41:17; Exodus 7:15 : of a brook, Deuteronomy 2:36; Deuteronomy 3:12 : of Jordan, 2 Kings 2:13. So in Class. The vigor thus supernaturally imparted to Abraham does not appear to have exhausted itself in the generation of Isaac; since, according to Genesis 25:2, Abraham became by Keturah the father of six sons after the death of Sarah.
