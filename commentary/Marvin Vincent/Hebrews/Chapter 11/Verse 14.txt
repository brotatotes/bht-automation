<p>Declare plainly [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εμφαμιζουσιν] </span></span>. o P. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="joh+14:21">John 14:21</span>. Occasionally in LXX Rend. "make it manifest." </p>
<p>They seek a country (patrida ejpizhtousin). The verb is found in LXX, chiefly in the sense of seeking after God or another deity. See <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="2ki+1:3">2 Kings 1:3</span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="2ki+1:6">2 Kings 1:6</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="2ki+3:11">2 Kings 3:11</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="2ki+8:8">2 Kings 8:8</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="2ki+22:18">2 Kings 22:18</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="2ch+18:6">2 Chronicles 18:6</span>. Comp. ejpizhtoumenh poliv a city sought after (Zion), <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="isa+61:12">Isaiah 61:12</span>. Patriv is a native country; a fatherland. Only here and in Gospels and Acts. Quite often in LXX</p>
