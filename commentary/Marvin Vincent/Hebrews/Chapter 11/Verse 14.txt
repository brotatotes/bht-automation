Declare plainly [εμφαμιζουσιν] . o P. See on John 14:21. Occasionally in LXX Rend. "make it manifest." They seek a country (patrida ejpizhtousin). The verb is found in LXX, chiefly in the sense of seeking after God or another deity. See 2 Kings 1:3, 2 Kings 1:6; 2 Kings 3:11; 2 Kings 8:8; 2 Kings 22:18; 2 Chronicles 18:6. Comp. ejpizhtoumenh poliv a city sought after (Zion), Isaiah 61:12. Patriv is a native country; a fatherland. Only here and in Gospels and Acts. Quite often in LXX
