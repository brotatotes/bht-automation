<p>Now they desire [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">νυν ορεγονται] </span></span>. Nun now is logical : as the case now stands. For ojregontai desire, see on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1ti+3:1">1 Timothy 3:1</span>. </p>
<p>Is not ashamed [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ουκ επαισχυνεται] </span></span>. Because they have commended themselves to God by their faith, so that he acknowledges them as his own. Comp. ch. <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="heb+2:11">Hebrews 2:11</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mr+8:28">Mark 8:28</span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mr+8:38">Mark 8:38</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ro+1:16">Romans 1:16</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="2ti+1:8">2 Timothy 1:8</span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="2ti+1:16">2 Timothy 1:16</span>. To be called their God [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">θεος επικαλεισθαι αυτων] </span></span>. Lit. to be surnamed. Comp. <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+4:36">Acts 4:36</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+10:5">Acts 10:5</span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+10:18">Acts 10:18</span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+10:32">Acts 10:32</span>. God was called the God of Abraham, of Isaac, and of Jacob. See <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ex+3:6">Exodus 3:6</span>. </p>
<p>For he hath prepared for them a city [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ητοιμασιν γαρ αυτοις πολιν] </span></span>. Comp. <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+25:34">Matthew 25:34</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="joh+14:2">John 14:2</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="re+21:2">Revelation 21:2</span>. City is significant, as showing that the fulfillment of God 's promise lies in introducing them into the perfection of social life. Comp. <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="re+3:12">Revelation 3:12</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="re+21:2">Revelation 21:2</span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="re+21:10">Revelation 21:10</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="re+22:19">Revelation 22:19</span>.</p>
