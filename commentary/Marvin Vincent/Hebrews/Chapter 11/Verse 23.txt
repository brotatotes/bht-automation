Of his parents [υπο των πατερων αυτου] . Lit. by his fathers. Comp. Exodus 2:2. Paterev fathers, according to a late Greek usage, is employed like goneiv parents. Similarly the Lat. patres and soceri, including both parents, or father and mother in law. Proper [αστειον] . Only here and Acts 7:20, on which see note. Rend. "comely." Commandment [διαταγμα] . N. T. o. Rend. "mandate."
