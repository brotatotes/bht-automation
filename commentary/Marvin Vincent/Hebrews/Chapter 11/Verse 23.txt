<p>Of his parents [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">υπο των πατερων αυτου] </span></span>. Lit. by his fathers. Comp. <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ex+2:2">Exodus 2:2</span>. Paterev fathers, according to a late Greek usage, is employed like goneiv parents. Similarly the Lat. patres and soceri, including both parents, or father and mother in law. </p>
<p>Proper [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αστειον] </span></span>. Only here and <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+7:20">Acts 7:20</span>, on which see note. Rend. "comely." </p>
<p>Commandment [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">διαταγμα] </span></span>. N. T. o. Rend. "mandate."</p>
