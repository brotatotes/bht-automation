To suffer affliction with [συνκακουχεισθαι] . N. T. o, o LXX, o Class. The verb kakoucein to treat ill, ver. 37; ch. 13 3; LXX, 1 Kings 2:26; 1 Kings 11:39. Rend. "to be evil entreated." Than to enjoy the pleasures of sin for a season [η προσκαιρον εχειν αμαρτιας απολαυσιν] . Lit. than to have temporary enjoyment of sin. The emphasis is first on temporary and then on sin. For ajpolausiv enjoyment, see on 1 Timothy 6:17. Proskairov for a season, temporary, rare in N. T. o LXX Once in Paul, see 2 Corinthians 4:18.
