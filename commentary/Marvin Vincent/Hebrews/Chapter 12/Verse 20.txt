That which was commanded [το διαστελλομενον] . See on Mark 7:36; Acts 14:24. Touch [θιγη] . Elsewhere in N. T. only ch. 11 28 and Colossians 2:21. LXX only Exodus 19:12. It implies a touching or grasping which affects the object (comp. ver. 18 on yhlafan). In Class. often of touching or handling some sacred object which may be desecrated by the one who lays hands on it. See Soph. Philoct. 667; Oed. Tyr. 891, 899. So here, the touch of the mountain was profanation. Shall be stoned [λιθοβολησεται] . Found in Matthew, Luke, and Acts. In LXX see Exodus 19:13. Comp. ejliqasqhsan, ch. 11 37. The correct text omits or thrust through with a dart.
