The sight [το φανταζομενον] . N. T. o. LXX, Wisd. 6 16; Sir. 31 5. Rend. "the appearance" : that which was made to appear. I exceedingly fear and quake [εκφοβος ειμι και εντρομος] . Lit. I am frightened away (or out) and trembling. Ekfobov only here and Mark 9:6. Comp. LXX, Deuteronomy 9:19. Entromov, only Acts 7:32; Acts 16:29. Rare in LXX
