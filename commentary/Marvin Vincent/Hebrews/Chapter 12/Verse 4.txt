<p>Unto blood [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μεχρις αιματος] </span></span>. Your strife against sin has not entailed the shedding of your blood, as did that of many of the O. T. worthies, and of Jesus himself. See ch. <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="heb+11:35">Hebrews 11:35</span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="heb+11:37">Hebrews 11:37</span>. Of Jesus it is said, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="php+2:8">Philippians 2:8</span>, " he became obedient to the extent of death [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μεχρι θανατου] </span></span>. Comp. 2 Macc. 13 14. </p>
<p>Striving against sin [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">προς την αμαρτιαν ανταγωνιζομενοι] </span></span>. The verb N. T. o. LXX, 4 Macc. 17 14. Sin is personified.</p>
