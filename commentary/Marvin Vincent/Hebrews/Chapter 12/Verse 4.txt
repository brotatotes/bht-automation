Unto blood [μεχρις αιματος] . Your strife against sin has not entailed the shedding of your blood, as did that of many of the O. T. worthies, and of Jesus himself. See ch. Hebrews 11:35, Hebrews 11:37. Of Jesus it is said, Philippians 2:8, " he became obedient to the extent of death [μεχρι θανατου] . Comp. 2 Macc. 13 14. Striving against sin [προς την αμαρτιαν ανταγωνιζομενοι] . The verb N. T. o. LXX, 4 Macc. 17 14. Sin is personified.
