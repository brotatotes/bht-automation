He chasteneth [παιδευει] . See on Luke 23:16. Scourgeth [μαστιγοι] . Not very common, but found in all the four Gospels. Hebrews only here. Quite often in LXX Receiveth [παραδεχεται] . Admits to filial privileges : acknowledges as his own. Of receiving the word of God, Mark 4:20 : of receiving delegates from a body, Acts 14:4 : of adopting or approving customs, Acts 16:21.
