<p>He chasteneth [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">παιδευει] </span></span>. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="lu+23:16">Luke 23:16</span>. </p>
<p>Scourgeth [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μαστιγοι] </span></span>. Not very common, but found in all the four Gospels. Hebrews only here. Quite often in LXX </p>
<p>Receiveth [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">παραδεχεται] </span></span>. Admits to filial privileges : acknowledges as his own. Of receiving the word of God, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mr+4:20">Mark 4:20</span> : of receiving delegates from a body, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+14:4">Acts 14:4</span> : of adopting or approving customs, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+16:21">Acts 16:21</span>.</p>
