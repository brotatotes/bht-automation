<p>Of which all are partakers [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ης μετοχοι γεγονασι παντες] </span></span>. Rend. "of which all have been made partakers." For metocoi partakers see on ch. <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="heb+3:14">Hebrews 3:14</span>. All, that is, all sons of God. </p>
<p>Bastards [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">νοθοι] </span></span>. N. T. o. See Wisd. 4 3. They might think that they would not suffer if they were really God 's sons; whereas the reverse is the case. If they did not suffer, they would not be God 's sons.</p>
