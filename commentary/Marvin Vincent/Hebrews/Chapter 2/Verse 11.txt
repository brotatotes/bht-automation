<p>In order to bring many sons unto glory, Christ assumes to them the relation of brother. </p>
<p>He that sanctifieth [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ο αγιαζων] </span></span>. Sanctification is the path to glorification. Comp. <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="heb+10:14">Hebrews 10:14</span>. </p>
<p>Of one [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εξ ενος] </span></span>. Probably God, although the phrase may signify of one piece, or of one whole. Jesus and his people alike have God for their father. Therefore they are brethren, and Christ, notwithstanding his superior dignity, is not ashamed to call them by that name.</p>
