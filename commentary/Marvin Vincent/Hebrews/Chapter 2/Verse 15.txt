<p>Deliver [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">απαλλαξη] </span></span>. Only here in Hebrews, and besides, only <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="lu+12:58">Luke 12:58</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+19:12">Acts 19:12</span>. Tolerably often in LXX Very common in Class. Used here absolutely, not with douleiav bondage, reading deliver from bondage. </p>
<p>Subject to bondage [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ενοχοι δουλειας] </span></span>. Enocoi from ejn in and ecein to hold. Lit. holden of bondage. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="jas+2:10">James 2:10</span>. Comp. the verb ejvexein, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mr+6:19">Mark 6:19</span> (note), and <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ga+5:1">Galatians 5:1</span>. Douleia bondage only in Hebrews and Paul.</p>
