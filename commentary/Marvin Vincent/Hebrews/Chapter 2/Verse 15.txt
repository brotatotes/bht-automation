Deliver [απαλλαξη] . Only here in Hebrews, and besides, only Luke 12:58; Acts 19:12. Tolerably often in LXX Very common in Class. Used here absolutely, not with douleiav bondage, reading deliver from bondage. Subject to bondage [ενοχοι δουλειας] . Enocoi from ejn in and ecein to hold. Lit. holden of bondage. See on James 2:10. Comp. the verb ejvexein, Mark 6:19 (note), and Galatians 5:1. Douleia bondage only in Hebrews and Paul.
