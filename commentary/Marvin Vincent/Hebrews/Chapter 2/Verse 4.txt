<p>God also bearing them witness [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">συνεπιμαρτυρουντος του θεου] </span></span>. The verb N. T. o : sun along with other witnesses : ejpi giving additional testimony : marturein to bear witness. </p>
<p>With signs and wonders [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">σημειοις τε και τερασιν] </span></span>. A very common combination in N. T. See <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+24:24">Matthew 24:24</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mr+13:22">Mark 13:22</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="joh+4:48">John 4:48</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+2:43">Acts 2:43</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="2co+12:11">2 Corinthians 12:11</span>, etc. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+24:24">Matthew 24:24</span>. Divers miracles [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ποικιλαις δυναμεσιν] </span></span>. Rend. powers. No doubt these include miracles, see <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+2:22">Acts 2:22</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="2co+12:12">2 Corinthians 12:12</span>; but powers signifies, not the miraculous manifestations, as signs and wonders, but the miraculous energies of God as displayed in his various forms of witness. Gifts [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μερισμοις] </span></span>. Rend. distributions or impartations. </p>
<p>Of the Holy Ghost. The genitive is objective : distributions of the one gift of the Holy Spirit in different measure and in different ways. Comp. <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1co+12:4-11">1 Corinthians 12:4-11</span>. </p>
<p>According to his will [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κατα την αυτου θελησιν] </span></span>. Qelhsiv willing : his act of will. N. T. o. Const. with distributions. The Spirit was imparted and distributed as God willed. The hortatory digression ends here. The subject of the Son's superiority to the angels is resumed.</p>
