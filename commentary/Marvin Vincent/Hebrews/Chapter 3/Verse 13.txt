<p>While it is called today [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αρχις ου το σημερον καλειται] </span></span>. Lit. so long as the today is being named. The article points to the former expression - the "today" of ver. 7. It is the day of grace, while salvation through Christ is still attainable. </p>
<p>Through the deceitfulness of sin [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">απατη της αμαριας] </span></span>. jApath is rather a trick, stratagem, deceit, than the quality of deceitfulness. The warning is against being hardened by a trick which their sin may play them. Note the article, the or his sin - the sin of departing from the living God. The particular deceit in this case would be the illusion of faithfulness to the past.</p>
