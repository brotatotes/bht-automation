<p>While it is said [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εν τω λεγεσθαι] </span></span>. The formula by which the writer reverts to the previous citation. Connect with if we hold fast. The exhortation of ver. 12 answered to <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ps+95:0">Psalms 95:0</span>; so the condition of fulfillment in ver. 14 is declared to rest on the same Scripture. Only on the ground of what is said in that Psalm does the holding fast come to pass. Rend. therefore, "We are fellows of Christ if we hold the beginning of our confidence steadfast unto the end, seeing it is said," etc.</p>
