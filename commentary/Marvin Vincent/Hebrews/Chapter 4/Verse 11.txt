<p>This promise of rest carries with it a special responsibility for the people of God. </p>
<p>Let us labor therefore [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">σπουδασωμεν ουν] </span></span>. For the verb, see on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="eph+4:3">Ephesians 4:3</span>. Give diligence, not hasten, which is the primary meaning. That rest [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εκεινην την καταπαυσιν] </span></span>. The Sabbath - rest of God, instituted at creation, promised to the fathers, forfeited by their unbelief, remaining to us on the condition of faith. </p>
<p>Lest any man fall after the same example of unbelief [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ινα μη εν τω αυτω τις υποδειγματι πεση της απειθειας] </span></span>. Pesh fall is to be taken absolutely; not, fall into the same example. jUpodeigma example, mostly in Hebrews. Rejected as unclassical by the Attic rhetoricians. Originally a sign which suggests something : a partial suggestion as distinct from a complete expression. See ch. <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="heb+8:5">Hebrews 8:5</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="heb+9:23">Hebrews 9:23</span>. Thus Christ 's washing of the disciples ' feet (<span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="joh+13:15">John 13:15</span>) was a typical suggestion of the whole field and duty of ministry. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1pe+2:6">1 Peter 2:6</span>. It is not easy to give the exact force of ejn in. Strictly speaking, the "example of disobedience" is conceived as that in which the falling takes place. The fall is viewed in the sphere of example. Comp. 2 Macc. 4 30; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1co+2:7">1 Corinthians 2:7</span>. Rend. that no man fall in the same example of disobedience : the same as that in which they fell.</p>
