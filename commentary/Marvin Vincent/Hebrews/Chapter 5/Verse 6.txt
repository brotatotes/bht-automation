<p>Thou art a priest forever, etc. According to this verse Christ is prophetically pointed out in <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ps+110:0">Psalms 110:0</span> as an eternal priest, independent of fleshly descent, a king, and superior in dignity to the Levitical priests. According to the order [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κατα την ταξιν] </span></span>. According to the rank which Melchisedec held. Almost = like. For Melchisedec see ch. 7.</p>
