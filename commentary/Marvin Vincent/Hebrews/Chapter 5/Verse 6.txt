Thou art a priest forever, etc. According to this verse Christ is prophetically pointed out in Psalms 110:0 as an eternal priest, independent of fleshly descent, a king, and superior in dignity to the Levitical priests. According to the order [κατα την ταξιν] . According to the rank which Melchisedec held. Almost = like. For Melchisedec see ch. 7.
