V. follows T. R. tou kopou. Rend. your work and the love which ye shewed, etc. Which ye have shewed toward his name [ης ενεδειξασθε εις το ονομα αυτου] . The verb means, strictly, to show something in one's self; or to show one's self in something. similar praise is bestowed in ch. 10 32. They have shown both love and work toward God 's name.
