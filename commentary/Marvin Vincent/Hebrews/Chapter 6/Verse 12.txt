Slothful [νωθροι] . See on ch. Hebrews 5:11. Or sluggish, as you will become if you lose hope. Followers [μιμηται] . Rend. imitators. Faith and patience [πιστεως και μακροθυμιας] . For patience rend. long - suffering, and see on James 5:7. Faith and long - suffering go together. Faith does not win its inheritance without persevering endurance; hence long - suffering is not only presented as an independent quality, but is predicated of faith. Inherit [κληρονομουντων] . Notice the present participle, are inheriting. Their present faith and perseverance are now making for their final inheritance. Comp. Ephesians 1:14.
