<p>Slothful [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">νωθροι] </span></span>. See on ch. <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="heb+5:11">Hebrews 5:11</span>. Or sluggish, as you will become if you lose hope. </p>
<p>Followers [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μιμηται] </span></span>. Rend. imitators. </p>
<p>Faith and patience [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πιστεως και μακροθυμιας] </span></span>. For patience rend. long - suffering, and see on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="jas+5:7">James 5:7</span>. Faith and long - suffering go together. Faith does not win its inheritance without persevering endurance; hence long - suffering is not only presented as an independent quality, but is predicated of faith. </p>
<p>Inherit [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κληρονομουντων] </span></span>. Notice the present participle, are inheriting. Their present faith and perseverance are now making for their final inheritance. Comp. <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="eph+1:14">Ephesians 1:14</span>.</p>
