<p>And an oath for confirmation is to them an end of all strife [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">και πασης αυτοις αντιλογιαςπερας εις βεβαιων ο ορκος] </span></span>. FOR "an oath," rend. "the oath" : generic. Const. for confirmation with end. "The oath is final for confirmation." Perav is the outermost point; the point beyond which one cannot go. With this exception always in N. T. in the plural, of the ends of the earth. See <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+12:42">Matthew 12:42</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ro+10:18">Romans 10:18</span>. So often in LXX jAntilogia, strictly contradiction, only in Hebrews and <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="jude+1:11">Jude 1:11</span>, on which see note.</p>
