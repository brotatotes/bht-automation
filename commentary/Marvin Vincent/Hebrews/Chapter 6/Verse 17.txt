Wherein [εν ω] . Referring to the whole previous clause. In accordance with this universal human custom. Willing [βουλομενος] . Rend. being minded. See on Matthew 1:19. The immutability [το αμεταθετον] . The adjective used substantively. Only here and ver. 18. Confirmed [εμεσττευσεν] . Rend. interposed or mediated. Comp. mesithv mediator. From mesov midst.
