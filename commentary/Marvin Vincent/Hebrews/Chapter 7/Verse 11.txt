<p>In Christ, as the Melchisedec - priest, the ideal of the priesthood is realized. </p>
<p>Perfection [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τελειωσις] </span></span>. Only here and <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="lu+1:45">Luke 1:45</span>. See ch. <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="heb+9:9">Hebrews 9:9</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="heb+10:1">Hebrews 10:1</span>. </p>
