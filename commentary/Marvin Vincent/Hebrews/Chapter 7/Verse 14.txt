Evident [προδηλ on]. Obvious. See on 1 Timothy 5:24. Sprang [ανατεταλκεν] . Rend. hath sprung. In N. T. always of the rising of a heavenly body, sun or star, except Luke 12:54, of a cloud, and here. See LXX, Genesis 32:31; Exodus 22:3; Numbers 24:17; Jude 1:9:33; Isaiah 14:12; Isaiah 40:1; Malachi 4:2. Also of the springing up of plants, Genesis 2:5; Genesis 3:18; Deuteronomy 29:23; of the growing of the beard, 2 Samuel 10:5.
