<p>Evident [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">καταδηλον] </span></span>. N. T. o. Thoroughly evident. Not referring to that which is declared to be prodhlon evident in ver. 14, viz., that Christ sprang out of Judah, but to the general proposition - the unsatisfactory character of the Levitical priesthood. </p>
<p>Similitude [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ομιοτητα] </span></span>. Better, likeness : answering to made like, ver. 3, and emphasizing the personal resemblance to Melchisedec.</p>
