Were many priests [πλειονες εισιν γεγονοτες ιερεις] . Comp. ver. 21 for the construction. Rend. have been made priests many in number. Because they were not suffered to continue [δια το κωλυεσθαι παραμενειν] . Rend. because they are hindered from continuing. Paramenein "to abide by their ministration."
