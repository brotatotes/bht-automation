<p>Holy [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">οσιος] </span></span>. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="lu+1:75">Luke 1:75</span>. Of Christ, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+2:27">Acts 2:27</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+13:35">Acts 13:35</span> : of a bishop, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="tit+1:8">Titus 1:8</span>. </p>
