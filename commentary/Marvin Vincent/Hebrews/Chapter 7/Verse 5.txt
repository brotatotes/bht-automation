<p>If Melchisedec was greater than Abraham, he was greater than Abraham 's descendants, including the tribe of Levi. </p>
<p>They that are of the sons of Levi who receive, etc. [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">οι εκ των υιων λευει λαμβανοντες] </span></span>. Those out of the sons of Levi who become priests. Not those who receive the priesthood from the sons of Levi. Not all Levites were priests, but only those of the house of Aaron. </p>
<p>The office of the priesthood [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">την ιερατιαν] </span></span>. Only here and <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="lu+1:9">Luke 1:9</span>. A commandment [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εντολην] </span></span>. A special injunction, See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="jas+2:8">James 2:8</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="eph+2:15">Ephesians 2:15</span>. </p>
<p>To take tithes [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αποδεκατοιν] </span></span>. See on ver. 4. </p>
<p>That is of their brethren, though they come out of the loins of Abraham. The people, the brethren of the Levites, are descended from their common ancestor, Abraham, yet the Levites exact tithes from them.</p>
