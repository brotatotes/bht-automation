<p>Of the things which we have spoken [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επι τοις λεγομενοις] </span></span>. The A. V. is wrong. jEpi is in the case of, or in the consideration of : not of, nor in addition to. Toiv legomenoiv "the things which are being spoken" : the matters now under discussion. </p>
<p>The sum [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κεφαλαιον] </span></span>. Rend. the chief point. It is not the sum of what precedes, but the main point of the present discussion. This point is that Christ is the minister of a better sanctuary, connected with a better covenant. </p>
<p>Such an high priest [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τοιουτον] </span></span>. Taken up from ch. 7 26. </p>
<p>Is set [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εκαθισεν] </span></span>. Repeating ch. 1 3. Rend. sat down. </p>
<p>The throne of the majesty [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">του θρονου της μεγαλωσυνης] </span></span>. See on ch. <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="heb+1:3">Hebrews 1:3</span>. The phrase N. T. o. </p>
<p>In the heavens [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εν τοις ουρανοις] </span></span>. Const. with sat down, not with majesty, which is complete in itself and needs no qualifying epithet.</p>
