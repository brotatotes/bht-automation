<p>His neighbor [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τον πολιτην] </span></span>. Lit. his citizen : his fellow - citizen. 205 Know the Lord [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">γνωθι τον κυριον] </span></span>. As if commending God to the knowledge of one who is ignorant of him. </p>
<p>All shall know [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">παντες ειδησουσιν] </span></span>. Observe the two words for know : gnwqi of the recognition of a stranger; eijdhsousin of an absolute acquaintance as of one born under God 's covenant. </p>
<p>From the least to the greatest [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">απο μικρου εως μεγαλου αυτων] </span></span>. Lit. from the little unto the great of them. This knowledge of God will be without distinction of age or station.</p>
