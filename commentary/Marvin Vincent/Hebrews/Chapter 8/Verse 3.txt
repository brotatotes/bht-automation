A priest is appointed to offer gifts and sacrifices. Therefore Christ, a high priest, must have gifts and sacrifices to offer, and a sanctuary in which to offer them. Wherefore it is of necessity [οεν αναγκαιον] . Rend. wherefore it is necessary. Somewhat to offer [ο προσενεγκη] . Lit. what he may offer. The construction is unusual. Comp. Acts 21:16. The statement is a truism, unless it be assumed that the Hebrew Christians were ignorant of the doctrine of Christ 's priesthood.
