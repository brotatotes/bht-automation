<p>A priest is appointed to offer gifts and sacrifices. Therefore Christ, a high priest, must have gifts and sacrifices to offer, and a sanctuary in which to offer them. </p>
<p>Wherefore it is of necessity [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">οεν αναγκαιον] </span></span>. Rend. wherefore it is necessary. </p>
<p>Somewhat to offer [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ο προσενεγκη] </span></span>. Lit. what he may offer. The construction is unusual. Comp. <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+21:16">Acts 21:16</span>. The statement is a truism, unless it be assumed that the Hebrew Christians were ignorant of the doctrine of Christ 's priesthood.</p>
