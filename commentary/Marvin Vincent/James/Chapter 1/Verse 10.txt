<p>In that he is made low [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εν τη ταπεινωσει αυτου] </span></span>. A form of expression similar to the preceding. Lit., in his humiliation. Both the A. V. and Rev. preserve the kinship between tapeinov and tapeinwsei, by the word low. </p>
<p>Flower [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ανθος] </span></span>. Only here, ver. 11, and <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1pe+1:24">1 Peter 1:24</span>.</p>
