Drawn away [εξελκομενος] . Only here in New Testament. This and the following word are metaphors from hunting and fishing. Drawn away, as beasts are enticed from a safe covert into a place beset with snares. Note the present participle, as indicating the progress of the temptation : "is being drawn away." Enticed [δελεαζομενος] . As a fish with bait. Also the present participle. See on 2 Peter 2:14.
