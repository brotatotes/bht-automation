<p>Drawn away [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εξελκομενος] </span></span>. Only here in New Testament. This and the following word are metaphors from hunting and fishing. Drawn away, as beasts are enticed from a safe covert into a place beset with snares. Note the present participle, as indicating the progress of the temptation : "is being drawn away." </p>
<p>Enticed [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δελεαζομενος] </span></span>. As a fish with bait. Also the present participle. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="2pe+2:14">2 Peter 2:14</span>.</p>
