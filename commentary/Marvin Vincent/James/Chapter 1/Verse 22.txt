<p>Hearers [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ακροαται] </span></span>. Used by James only. </p>
<p>Deceiving [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">παραλογιζομενοι] </span></span>. From para, beside, contrary to, and logizomai, to reckon, and hence to conclude by reasoning. The deception referred to is, therefore, that into which one betrays himself by false reasoning - reasoning beside the truth.</p>
