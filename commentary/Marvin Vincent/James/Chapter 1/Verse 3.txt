<p>Trying [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δοκιμιον] </span></span>. Rev., proof; but the American Revisers insist on proving, and rightly. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1pe+1:7">1 Peter 1:7</span>. </p>
<p>Worketh [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κατεργαζεται] </span></span>. The compound verb with kata, down through, indicates accomplishment. The proving will work successfully and thoroughly. This harmonizes with a perfect work, ver. 4. </p>
<p>Patience [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">υπομονην] </span></span>. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="2pe+1:6">2 Peter 1:6</span>, and <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="jas+5:7">James 5:7</span>.</p>
