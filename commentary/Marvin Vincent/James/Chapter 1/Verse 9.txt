<p>But. Omitted in A. V. Introducing a contrast with the double - minded. The brother of low degree [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ο αδελφος ο ταπεινος] </span></span>. Lit., the brother, the lowly one. Not in the higher Christian sense of tapeinov (see on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+11:29">Matthew 11:29</span>), but, rather, poor and afflicted, as contrasted with rich. Rejoice [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ανθος] </span></span>. Not strong enough. It is, rather, boast. So Rev., glory. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ro+5:3">Romans 5:3</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="php+3:3">Philippians 3:3</span>. </p>
<p>In that he is exalted [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εν τω υψει αυτου] </span></span>. Lit., in his exaltation. Rev., in his high estate.</p>
