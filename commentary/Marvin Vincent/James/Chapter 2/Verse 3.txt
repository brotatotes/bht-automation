Ye have respect [επιβλεψητε] . Lit., ye look upon, with the idea of respectful consideration; ye regard. Compare Luke 1:48; Luke 9:38. In a good place [καλως] . Lit., honorably; in a seat of honor. Under. Not literally underneath, but down on the ground beside. Compare Matthew 23:6, on the fondness of the Jews for the chief places in the synagogue.
