<p>Ye have respect [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επιβλεψητε] </span></span>. Lit., ye look upon, with the idea of respectful consideration; ye regard. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="lu+1:48">Luke 1:48</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="lu+9:38">Luke 9:38</span>. </p>
<p>In a good place [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">καλως] </span></span>. Lit., honorably; in a seat of honor. </p>
<p>Under. Not literally underneath, but down on the ground beside. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+23:6">Matthew 23:6</span>, on the fondness of the Jews for the chief places in the synagogue.</p>
