<p>They [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αυτοι] </span></span>. Emphatic. "Is it not they who blaspheme ?" Worthy [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">καλον] </span></span>. Rev., better, because stronger, honorable. By this epithet the disgracefulness of the blasphemy is emphasized. </p>
<p>By the which ye are called [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">το επικληθεν εφ υμας] </span></span>. Lit., which is called upon you; the name of Christ, invoked in baptism. The phrase is an Old - Testament one. See <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="de+28:10">Deuteronomy 28:10</span>, where the Septuagint reads that the name of the Lord has been called upon thee. Also, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="2ch+7:14">2 Chronicles 7:14</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="isa+4:1">Isaiah 4:1</span>. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+14:17">Acts 14:17</span>.</p>
