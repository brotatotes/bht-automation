They [αυτοι] . Emphatic. "Is it not they who blaspheme ?" Worthy [καλον] . Rev., better, because stronger, honorable. By this epithet the disgracefulness of the blasphemy is emphasized. By the which ye are called [το επικληθεν εφ υμας] . Lit., which is called upon you; the name of Christ, invoked in baptism. The phrase is an Old - Testament one. See Deuteronomy 28:10, where the Septuagint reads that the name of the Lord has been called upon thee. Also, 2 Chronicles 7:14; Isaiah 4:1. Compare Acts 14:17.
