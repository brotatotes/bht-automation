<p>Behold. Following the old reading, ide. All the best texts read eij de, now if. So Rev. </p>
<p>Bits [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">χαλινους] </span></span>. Only here and <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="re+14:20">Revelation 14:20</span>. It may be rendered either bit, as A. V., or bridle, as Rev., but bridle is preferable because it corresponds with the verb to bridle (ver. 2) which is compounded with this noun. </p>
<p>Horses. The position in the sentence is emphatic. </p>
