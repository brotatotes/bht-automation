No man [ουδεις ανθρωπων] . A strong expression. Lit., no one of men. Unruly [ακατασχετον] . Lit., not to be held back. The proper reading, however, is ajkatastaton, unsettled. See on kaqistatai, hath its place, ver. 6. Rev., correctly, restless. Deadly [θανατηφορου] . Lit., death - bearing, or bringing. Only here in New Testament. Poison [ιου] . Rendered rust at ch. 5 3; and found only in these two passages and in Romans 3:13, in the citation of Psalms 140:3.
