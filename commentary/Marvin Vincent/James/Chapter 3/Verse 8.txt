<p>No man [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ουδεις ανθρωπων] </span></span>. A strong expression. Lit., no one of men. </p>
<p>Unruly [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ακατασχετον] </span></span>. Lit., not to be held back. The proper reading, however, is ajkatastaton, unsettled. See on kaqistatai, hath its place, ver. 6. Rev., correctly, restless. </p>
<p>Deadly [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">θανατηφορου] </span></span>. Lit., death - bearing, or bringing. Only here in New Testament. </p>
<p>Poison [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ιου] </span></span>. Rendered rust at ch. 5 3; and found only in these two passages and in <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ro+3:13">Romans 3:13</span>, in the citation of <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ps+140:3">Psalms 140:3</span>.</p>
