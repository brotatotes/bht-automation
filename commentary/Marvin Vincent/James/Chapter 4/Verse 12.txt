<p>There is one lawgiver [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εις εστιν ο νομοθετης] </span></span>. The A. V. fails to note the emphatic position of one. Better, Rev., one only is the lawgiver. Nomoqethv, lawgiver, only here in New Testament. </p>
<p>But who art thou? [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">συ γε τις ει] </span></span>. According to the Greek order : but thou, who art tho?</p>
