<p>For that ye ought to say [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αντι του λεγειν υμας] </span></span>. Ver. 14 was parenthetical, so that at this point the thought is taken up from ver 13 Ye who say we will go, etc. - for that ye ought to say. The rendering in margin of Rev. is simpler : instead of your saying.</p>
