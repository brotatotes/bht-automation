Be afflicted [ταλαιπωρησατε] . Only here in New Testament. The kindred noun talaipwria, misery, occurs ch. 5 1. Mourn [πενθησατε] . Used of grief that is manifested. So mostly in New Testament, and very commonly joined, as here, with weep. So Mark 16:10; Luke 6:25, etc. In the next sentence occurs the kindred noun penqov mourning, into which laughter, also something manifest, is to be changed. Heaviness [κατηφειαν] . Properly, a casting down of the eyes. Compare Luke 18:13. Only here in New Testament.
