Go to. See on ch. James 4:13. Weep and howl [κλαυσατε ολολυζοντες] . Lit., weep, howling. The latter is a descriptive word, ol - ol - uz - o. Only here in New Testament, and denoting a more demonstrative and passionate expression of grief than weeping. Miseries [ταλαιπωριαις] . Only here and Romans 3:16. See on be afflicted, ch. 4 9. That shall come upon [επερχομεναις] . Present participle. More correctly, as Rev., that are coming.
