<p>Is cankered [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κατιωται] </span></span>. Only here in New Testament, from ijov, rust, as in the following sentence. Also poison, as ch. 3 8. The preposition kata indicates thoroughness, completely rusted. </p>
<p>Flesh [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τας σαρκας] </span></span>. The noun is plural : the fleshy parts of the body. So Sept. (<span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="2ki+9:36">2 Kings 9:36</span>) : " the flesh [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τας σαρκας</span></span>] of Jezebel. So <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="re+19:18">Revelation 19:18</span>.</p>
