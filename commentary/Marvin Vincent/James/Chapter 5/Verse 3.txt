Is cankered [κατιωται] . Only here in New Testament, from ijov, rust, as in the following sentence. Also poison, as ch. 3 8. The preposition kata indicates thoroughness, completely rusted. Flesh [τας σαρκας] . The noun is plural : the fleshy parts of the body. So Sept. (2 Kings 9:36) : " the flesh [τας σαρκας] of Jezebel. So Revelation 19:18.
