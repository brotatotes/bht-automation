<p>The thief [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ο κλεπτης] </span></span>. Christ puts Himself in contrast with the meaner criminal. </p>
<p>I am come [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ηλθον] </span></span>. More correctly, I came. I am come would be the perfect tense. </p>
<p>More abundantly [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">περισσον] </span></span>. Literally, may have abundance.</p>
