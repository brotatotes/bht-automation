<p>The hireling fleeth. The best texts omit. Read, as Rev., supplying he fleeth. </p>
<p>Careth not [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ου μελει αυτω] </span></span>. Literally, the sheep are not a care to him. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1pe+5:7">1 Peter 5:7</span>. The contrast is suggestive.</p>
