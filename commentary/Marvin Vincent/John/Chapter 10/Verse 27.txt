Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="joh+3:29">John 3:29</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="joh+5:30">John 5:30</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="joh+14:15">John 14:15</span>, etc.</p>
