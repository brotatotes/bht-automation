<p>I give [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">διδωμι] </span></span>. Not, I will give. The gift is present and continuous. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="joh+3:36">John 3:36</span>. </p>
<p>Shall pluck [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αρπασει] </span></span>. See on ver. 12. Compare can pluck, ver. 29. Here Jesus speaks of the fact; there of the possibility. Rev., snatch. Wyc., ravish.</p>
