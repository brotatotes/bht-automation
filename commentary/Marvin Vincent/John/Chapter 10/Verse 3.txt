<p>Porter [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">θυρωρος] </span></span>. From qura, door, and wra, care. An under - shepherd, to whose charge the sheep are committed after they have been folded for the night, and who opens the door on the arrival of the shepherd in the morning. </p>
<p>Calleth [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">καλει] </span></span>. But the best texts read fwnei, expressing personal address.</p>
