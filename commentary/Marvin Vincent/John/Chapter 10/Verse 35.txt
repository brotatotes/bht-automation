<p>The Scripture [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">η γραφη] </span></span>. The passage of scripture. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="joh+2:22">John 2:22</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="joh+5:47">John 5:47</span>. </p>
<p>Broken [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">λυθηναι] </span></span>. Literally, loosened. Wyc., undone. The word is characteristic of John. He uses it of the destruction of the temple (ii. 19); the breaking of the Sabbath (v. 18); the violation of the law (vii. 23); the destruction of Satan 's works (<span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1jo+3:8">1 John 3:8</span>), besides elsewhere in the physical sense.</p>
