Didymus [διδυμος] . Not a surname of Thomas, but the Greek equivalent of the Aramaic name, twin. See on Mark 3:18. The word occurs only in John's Gospel. Fellow - disciples [συμμαθηταις] . Only here in the New Testament. We may die. "He will die for the love which he has, but he will not affect the faith which he has not" (Westcott).
