<p>Didymus [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">διδυμος] </span></span>. Not a surname of Thomas, but the Greek equivalent of the Aramaic name, twin. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mr+3:18">Mark 3:18</span>. The word occurs only in John's Gospel. </p>
<p>Fellow - disciples [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">συμμαθηταις] </span></span>. Only here in the New Testament. </p>
