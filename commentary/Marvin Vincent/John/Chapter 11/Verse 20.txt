<p>That Jesus was coming [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">οτι ο ιησους ερχεται] </span></span>. Literally, is coming. The exact words of the message : Jesus is coming. </p>
<p>Went and met [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">υπηντησεν] </span></span>. The verb means to go to meet.</p>
