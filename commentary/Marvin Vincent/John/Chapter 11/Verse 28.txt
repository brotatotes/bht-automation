<p>The Master [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ο διδασκαλος] </span></span>. Literally, the teacher. Westcott remarks that this title opens a glimpse into the private intercourse of the Lord and the disciples : so they spoke of Him. </p>
<p>Is come [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">παρεστιν] </span></span>. Literally, is present. Rev., is here.</p>
