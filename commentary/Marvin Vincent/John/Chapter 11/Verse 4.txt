<p>Unto death. Not to have death as its final issue. </p>
<p>For the glory [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">υπερ] </span></span>. Here, as elsewhere in John, in behalf of. Canon Westcott remarks : "The sickness is regarded in a triple relation; unto, in respect of the actual result; in behalf of, in respect of the suffering born; in order that, in respect of the divine purpose."</p>
