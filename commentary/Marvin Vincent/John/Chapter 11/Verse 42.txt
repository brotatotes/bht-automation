<p>The people [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τον οχλον] </span></span>. In view of the distinction which John habitually makes between the Jews and the multitude, the use of the latter term here is noticeable, since Jews occurs at vv. 19, 31, 36. It would seem to indicate that a miscellaneous crowd had gathered. Rev., the multitude. See on 1 19.</p>
