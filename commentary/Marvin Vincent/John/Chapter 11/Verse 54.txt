<p>Wilderness. The wild hill - country, northeast of Jerusalem. </p>
<p>Ephraim. The site is uncertain. Commonly taken as Ophrah (<span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1sa+13:17">1 Samuel 13:17</span>), or Ephraim (<span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="2ch+13:19">2 Chronicles 13:19</span>), and identified with el - Taiyibeh, sixteen miles from Jerusalem, and situated on a hill which commands the Jordan valley. </p>
