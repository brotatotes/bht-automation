Wilderness. The wild hill - country, northeast of Jerusalem. Ephraim. The site is uncertain. Commonly taken as Ophrah (1 Samuel 13:17), or Ephraim (2 Chronicles 13:19), and identified with el - Taiyibeh, sixteen miles from Jerusalem, and situated on a hill which commands the Jordan valley. 55 - 57. John 12:1-11. Compare Matthew 26:6-13; Mark 14:3-9.
