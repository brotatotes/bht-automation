<p>Answered [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">απεκρινατο] </span></span>. The best texts read ajpokrinetai, answereth. </p>
<p>The hour is come, that [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εληλυθεν η ωρα ινα] </span></span>. This is not equivalent to "the hour is come in which." The hour is used absolutely : the critical hour is come in order that the Son, etc.</p>
