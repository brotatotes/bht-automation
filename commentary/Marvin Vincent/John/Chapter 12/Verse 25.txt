Life [ψυχην] . See on Mark 12:30; Luke 1:46. Shall lose [απολεσει] . The best texts read ajpolluei, loseth. See on Luke 9:25. In this world. This earthly economy, regarded as alien and hostile to God. The words are added in order to explain the strong phrase, hateth his life or soul. Shall keep [φυλαξει] . See on 1 Peter 1:4.
