A pound [λιτραν] . Only here and John 19:39. Matthew and Mark, ajlabastron, a flask. Of spikenard [ναρδου πιστικης] . So Mark. See on Mark 14:3. Very precious [πολυτιμου] . Literally, of much value. Matthew has barutimou, of weighty value. Anointed. See on 11 2. Feet. The Synoptists mention only the pouring on the head.
