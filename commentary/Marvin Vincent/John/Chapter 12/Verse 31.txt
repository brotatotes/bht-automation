The prince of this world [ο αρχων ρου κοσμου τουτου] . The phrase occurs only in the Gospel; here, John 14:30; John 16:11. Shall be cast out [εκβληθησεται εξω] . In every case but one where the word ejkballw occurs in John, it is used of casting out from a holy place or society. See John 2:15; John 9:34, John 9:3; 3 John 1:10; Revelation 12:2. Compare John 10:4.
