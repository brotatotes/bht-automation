<p>Be lifted up [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">υψωθω] </span></span>. See on 3 14. The primary reference is to the cross, but there is included a reference to the resurrection and ascension. Bengel says : "In the very cross there was already something tending towards glory." Wyc., enhanced. </p>
<p>From the earth [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εκ της γης] </span></span>. Literally, out of the earth. </p>
<p>Will draw [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ελκυσω] </span></span>. See on 6 44. </p>
<p>All men [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">παντας] </span></span>. Some high authorities read panta, all things. </p>
<p>Unto Me [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">προς εμαυτον] </span></span>. Rev., rightly, unto myself : in contrast with the prince of this world.</p>
