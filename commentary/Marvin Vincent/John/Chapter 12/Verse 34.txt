<p>35 With you [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μεθ υμων] </span></span>. The best texts read panta, among you. </p>
<p>While ye have [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εως] </span></span>. The best texts read wJv, as : walk in conformity with the fact that you have the Light among you. </p>
<p>Lest darkness come upon you [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ινα μη σξοτια υμας καταλαβη] </span></span>. Rev., better, that darkness overtake you not. On overtake see on taketh, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mr+9:18">Mark 9:18</span>; and perceived, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+4:13">Acts 4:13</span>.</p>
