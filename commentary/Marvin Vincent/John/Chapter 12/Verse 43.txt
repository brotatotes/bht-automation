Praise [δοξαν] . Much better, Rev., glory, because suggesting a contrast with the vision of divine glory referred to in ver. 41. Compare John 5:44. Than [ηπερ] The word cannot be rendered by a corresponding word in English The force is, "more than the glory of God, though He is so much more glorious." The word is found nowhere else in the New Testament. Some authorities read uper, above.
