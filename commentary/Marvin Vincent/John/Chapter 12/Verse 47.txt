Believe not [μη πιστευση] . The best texts read fulaxh, keep (them). Came [ηλθον] . The aorist tense, pointing to the purpose of the coming, as I am come (ver. 46) to the result. Compare John 8:14; John 9:39; John 10:10; John 12:27, John 12:47; John 14:22. Both tenses are found in John 8:42; John 16:28.
