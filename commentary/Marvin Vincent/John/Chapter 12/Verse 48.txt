Rejecteth [αθετων] . See on Luke 7:30. The word [ο λογος] . Comprehending all the sayings [ρηματα] . The same [εκεινος] . That. The pronoun of remote reference Westcott finely remarks : "The resumptive, isolating pronoun places in emphatic prominence the teaching which is regarded as past, and separated from those to whom it was addressed. It stands, as it were, in the distance, as a witness and an accuser." The last day. Peculiar to John. See John 6:39.
