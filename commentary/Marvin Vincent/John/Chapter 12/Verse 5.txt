<p>Three hundred pence [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τριακοσιων δηναριων] </span></span>. Or three hundred denarii. On the denarius, see on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+20:2">Matthew 20:2</span>. Mark says more than three hundred pence. Three hundred denarii would be about fifty dollars, or twice that amount if we reckon according to the purchasing power. The poor [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πτωχοις] </span></span>. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+5:3">Matthew 5:3</span>. No article : to poor people.</p>
