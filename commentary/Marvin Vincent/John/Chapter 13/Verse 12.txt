<p>Was set down [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αναπεσων] </span></span>. Literally, having reclined. The guests reclined on couches, lying on the left side and leaning on the left hand. The table was in the hollow square or oblong formed on three sides by the couches, the fourth side being open, and the table extending beyond the ends of the couches. </p>
<p>Know ye [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">γινωσκετε] </span></span>? Perceive or understand ye?</p>
