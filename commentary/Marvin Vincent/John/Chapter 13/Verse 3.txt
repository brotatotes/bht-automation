<p>Had given [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δεδωκεν] </span></span>. The best texts read edwken, gave, the aorist marking Jesus ' commission as given once for all. </p>
<p>Was come [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εξηλθε] </span></span>. This rendering would require the perfect tense. The aorist points to His coming as a historic fact, not as related to its result. See on 12 47. Rev., rightly, came forth. </p>
<p>Went [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">υπαγει] </span></span>. Present tense : goeth; withdrawing from the scenes of earth. Note the original order : that it was from God He came forth, and unto God He is going.</p>
