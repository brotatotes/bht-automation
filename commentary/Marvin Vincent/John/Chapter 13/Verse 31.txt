Now. Marking a crisis, at which Jesus is relieved of the presence of the traitor, and accepts the consequences of his treachery. Is - glorified [εδοξασθη] . Literally, was glorified. The aorist points to the withdrawal of Judas. Jesus was glorified through death, and His fate was sealed (humanly speaking) by Judas ' going out. He speaks of the death and consequent glorification as already accomplished.
