<p>Now. Marking a crisis, at which Jesus is relieved of the presence of the traitor, and accepts the consequences of his treachery. </p>
<p>Is - glorified [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εδοξασθη] </span></span>. Literally, was glorified. The aorist points to the withdrawal of Judas. Jesus was glorified through death, and His fate was sealed (humanly speaking) by Judas ' going out. He speaks of the death and consequent glorification as already accomplished.</p>
