<p>If God be glorified in Him. The most ancient authorities omit. In Himself [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εν εαυτω] </span></span>. His glory will be contained in and identified with the divine glory. Compare "the glory which I had with thee," para soi (xvii. 5). En in, indicates unity of being; para with, unity of position.</p>
