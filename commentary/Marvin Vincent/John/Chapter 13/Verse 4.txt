<p>From the supper [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εκ του δειπνου] </span></span>. Out of the group gathered at the table. </p>
<p>Laid aside [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τιθησι] </span></span>. Present tense : layeth aside. </p>
<p>Garments [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ιματια] </span></span>. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+5:40">Matthew 5:40</span>. Upper garments. </p>
<p>Towel [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">λεντιον] </span></span>. A Latin word, linteum. A linen cloth. Only here and ver. 5. </p>
<p>Girded (die zwsen). Only in this chapter and <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="joh+21:7">John 21:7</span>. The compound verb means to bind or gird all round.</p>
