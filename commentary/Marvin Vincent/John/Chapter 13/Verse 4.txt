From the supper [εκ του δειπνου] . Out of the group gathered at the table. Laid aside [τιθησι] . Present tense : layeth aside. Garments [ιματια] . See on Matthew 5:40. Upper garments. Towel [λεντιον] . A Latin word, linteum. A linen cloth. Only here and ver. 5. Girded (die zwsen). Only in this chapter and John 21:7. The compound verb means to bind or gird all round.
