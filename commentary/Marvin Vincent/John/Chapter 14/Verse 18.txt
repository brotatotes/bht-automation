<p>Leave [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αφησω] </span></span>. See on 4 3. </p>
<p>Comfortless [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ορφανους] </span></span>. Literally, bereft or orphans. Only here and <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="jas+1:27">James 1:27</span>, where it is rendered fatherless. Compare my little children (xiii. 33). "He hath not left us without a rule (xiii. 34); nor without an example (xiii. 15); nor without a motive (xiv. 15); nor without a strength (xv. 5); nor without a warning (xv. 2, 6); nor without a Comforter (xiv. 18); nor without a reward (xiv. 2) (James Ford," The Gospel of St. John Illustrated "). </p>
<p>I will come [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ερχομαι] </span></span>. Present tense, I come. See on ver. 3.</p>
