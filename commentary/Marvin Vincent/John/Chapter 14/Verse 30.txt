<p>Hereafter I will not talk [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ουκ επι λαλησω] </span></span>. Rev., more correctly, I will no more speak. </p>
<p>The prince of this world. The best texts read, "of the world." </p>
<p>Hath nothing in me. No right nor power over Christ which sin in Him could give. The Greek order is, in me he hath nothing.</p>
