<p>The true vine [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">η αμπελος η αληθινη] </span></span>. Literally, the vine, the true (vine). True, genuine, answering to the perfect ideal. See on 1 9. The vine was a symbol of the ancient church. See the passages cited above, and <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ho+10:1">Hosea 10:1</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+21:33">Matthew 21:33</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="lu+13:6">Luke 13:6</span>. </p>
