<p>Henceforth - not [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ουκετι] </span></span>. Rev., better, no longer. No longer servants, as you were under the dispensation of the law. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ga+4:7">Galatians 4:7</span>. </p>
<p>Servants [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δουλους] </span></span>. Strictly, bond - servants. </p>
<p>Knoweth not [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ουκ οιδε] </span></span>. Has no instinctive perception. See on 2 24. You. The position of the pronoun in the Greek is emphatic : "You I have called friends."</p>
