Henceforth - not [ουκετι] . Rev., better, no longer. No longer servants, as you were under the dispensation of the law. Compare Galatians 4:7. Servants [δουλους] . Strictly, bond - servants. Knoweth not [ουκ οιδε] . Has no instinctive perception. See on 2 24. You. The position of the pronoun in the Greek is emphatic : "You I have called friends."
