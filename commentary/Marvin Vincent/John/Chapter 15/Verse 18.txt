<p>If the world hate [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ει μισει] </span></span>. Literally, hates. The indicative mood with the conditional particle assumes the fact as existing : If the world hates you, as it does. </p>
<p>Ye know [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">γινωσκετε] </span></span>. This may also be rendered as imperative : Know ye. </p>
<p>It hated [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μεμισηκεν] </span></span>. The perfect tense, hath hated. The hatred continues to the present time. </p>
<p>Before it hated you [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πρωτον υμων] </span></span>. Literally, first in regard of you. See on 1 15.</p>
