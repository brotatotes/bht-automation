If the world hate [ει μισει] . Literally, hates. The indicative mood with the conditional particle assumes the fact as existing : If the world hates you, as it does. Ye know [γινωσκετε] . This may also be rendered as imperative : Know ye. It hated [μεμισηκεν] . The perfect tense, hath hated. The hatred continues to the present time. Before it hated you [πρωτον υμων] . Literally, first in regard of you. See on 1 15.
