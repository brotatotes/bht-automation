<p>Branch [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κλημα] </span></span>. Occurring only in this chapter. Both this and kladov, branch (see on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+24:32">Matthew 24:32</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mr+11:8">Mark 11:8</span>) are derived from klaw, to break. The word emphasizes the ideas of tenderness and flexibility. </p>
<p>Purgeth [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">καθαιρει] </span></span>. Cleanseth, Rev.</p>
