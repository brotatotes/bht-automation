<p>He is cast forth [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εβληθη εξω] </span></span>. The aorist tense. Literally, was cast forth. The aorist, denoting a momentary act, indicates that it was cast forth at the moment it ceased to abide in the vine. Forth signifies from the vineyard; exw, outside. </p>
<p>As a branch [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ως το κλημα] </span></span>. Strictly, the branch : the unfruitful branch. </p>
<p>Is withered [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εξηρανθη] </span></span>. The aorist, as in was cast forth. Wyc, shall wax dry. </p>
<p>Men gather. Or, as Rev., they gather. Indefinite. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="isa+27:11">Isaiah 27:11</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="eze+14:5">Ezekiel 14:5</span>.</p>
