<p>In my love [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εν τη αγαπη τη εμη] </span></span>. Literally, in the love, that which is mine. Not only the love of the disciple for Christ, nor the love of Christ for the disciple, but the Christ - principle of love which includes both. See the same form of expression in the joy that is mine, ver. 11; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="joh+3:29">John 3:29</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="joh+17:13">John 17:13</span>; the judgment (v. 30; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="joh+8:16">John 8:16</span>); the commandments (xiv. 15); peace (xiv. 27).</p>
