In my love [εν τη αγαπη τη εμη] . Literally, in the love, that which is mine. Not only the love of the disciple for Christ, nor the love of Christ for the disciple, but the Christ - principle of love which includes both. See the same form of expression in the joy that is mine, ver. 11; John 3:29; John 17:13; the judgment (v. 30; John 8:16); the commandments (xiv. 15); peace (xiv. 27).
