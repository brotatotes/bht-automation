<p>All things that [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">παντα οσα] </span></span>. Literally, all things as many as. Rev., all things whatsoever. </p>
<p>Shall take [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ληψεται] </span></span>. The best texts read lambanei, taketh. The relation between the Son and the Spirit is put by Jesus as present and constant.</p>
