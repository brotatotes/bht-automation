He saith [λαλει] . Emphasizing the purport of the saying. A little while [το μικρον] . In vv. 16, 17, without the article. Here the article the or this little while defines the special point of their difficulty; this "little while" of which He speaks. We cannot tell [ουκ οιδαμεν] . Rev., more simply and literally, we know not. He saith [λαλει] . Emphasizing the form of the saying.
