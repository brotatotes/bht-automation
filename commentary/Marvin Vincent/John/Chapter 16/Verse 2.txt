<p>They shall put you out of the synagogues. See on 9 22. </p>
<p>Yea [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αλλ] </span></span>. Literally, but. They shall excommunicate you, but worse than this, the hour cometh, etc. </p>
<p>The hour cometh that [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ινα] </span></span>. Literally, "there cometh an hour in order that." The hour is ordained with that end in view : it comes fraught with the fulfillment of a divine purpose. </p>
<p>Whosoever [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πας ο] </span></span>. Literally, everyone who. </p>
<p>Doeth service [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">λατρειαν προσφερειν] </span></span>. Literally, bringeth or offereth service. Latreia means, strictly, service for hire, but is used of any service, and frequently of the service of God.</p>
