<p>A woman [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">η γυνη] </span></span>. Literally, the woman. The generic article marking the woman as representing her sex : woman as such. </p>
<p>She is in travail. A common Old Testament image of sorrow issuing in joy. See <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="isa+21:3">Isaiah 21:3</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="isa+26:17">Isaiah 26:17</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="isa+66:7">Isaiah 66:7</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ho+13:13">Hosea 13:13</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mic+4:9">Micah 4:9</span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mic+4:10">Micah 4:10</span>. The anguish [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">της θλιψεως] </span></span>. Commonly rendered affliction or tribulation in A. V. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+13:21">Matthew 13:21</span>. </p>
<p>Joy [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">την χαραν] </span></span>. Properly, the joy which answers to the anguish. </p>
<p>A man [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ανθρωπος] </span></span>. See on 1 30.</p>
