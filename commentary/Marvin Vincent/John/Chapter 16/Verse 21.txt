A woman [η γυνη] . Literally, the woman. The generic article marking the woman as representing her sex : woman as such. She is in travail. A common Old Testament image of sorrow issuing in joy. See Isaiah 21:3; Isaiah 26:17; Isaiah 66:7; Hosea 13:13; Micah 4:9, Micah 4:10. The anguish [της θλιψεως] . Commonly rendered affliction or tribulation in A. V. See on Matthew 13:21. Joy [την χαραν] . Properly, the joy which answers to the anguish. A man [ανθρωπος] . See on 1 30.
