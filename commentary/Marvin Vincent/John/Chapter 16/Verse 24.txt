<p>Ask [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αιτειτε] </span></span>. The present imperative, implying continuous asking. Be asking. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mr+6:22">Mark 6:22</span>, aithson, the aorist imperative, marking a single, definite petition. </p>
<p>May be full [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">η πεπληρωμενη] </span></span>. Very literally, may be having been fulfilled. Rev., more correctly, fulfilled. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="joh+14:11">John 14:11</span>.</p>
