<p>But [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αλλα] </span></span>. Marks a breaking off of the enumeration of fearful details; but (to say no more of these things), I have spoken these, etc. </p>
<p>At the beginning [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εξ αρχης] </span></span>. Properly, from the beginning. So Rev. The phrase only here and <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="joh+6:64">John 6:64</span>.</p>
