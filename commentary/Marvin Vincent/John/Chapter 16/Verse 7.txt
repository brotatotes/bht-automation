<p>It is expedient [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">συμφερει] </span></span>. From son together, and ferw to bear or bring. The underlying idea of the word is concurrence of circumstances. Go away [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">απελθω] </span></span>. The different words for go should be noted in this verse, and ver. 10. Here, expressing simple departure from a point. </p>
<p>Depart [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πορευθω] </span></span>. Rev., go. With the notion of going for a purpose, which is expressed in I will send him.</p>
