<p>These words [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ταυτα] </span></span>. Literally, these things. So Rev. </p>
<p>Said. John nowhere says that Jesus prayed, as the other Evangelists do. Thy Son - thy Son [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">σου τον υιον - ο υιος] </span></span>. Properly, thy Son - the Son. The second phrase marks a change from the thought of personal relationship to that of the relation in which Jesus manifests the Father 's glory.</p>
