In the world. Omit. I kept [ετηρουν] . Imperfect tense. I continued to keep. The I is emphatic : I kept them, now do Thou keep them. I kept [ετηρουν] . Rev., rightly, I guarded. The A. V. overlooks the distinction between the two words for keeping. The former word means, I preserved them; the latter, I guarded them as a means to their preservation. See on reserved, 1 Peter 1:4.
