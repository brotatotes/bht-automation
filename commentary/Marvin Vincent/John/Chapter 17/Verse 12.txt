<p>In the world. Omit. </p>
<p>I kept [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ετηρουν] </span></span>. Imperfect tense. I continued to keep. The I is emphatic : I kept them, now do Thou keep them. </p>
<p>I kept [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ετηρουν] </span></span>. Rev., rightly, I guarded. The A. V. overlooks the distinction between the two words for keeping. The former word means, I preserved them; the latter, I guarded them as a means to their preservation. See on reserved, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1pe+1:4">1 Peter 1:4</span>. </p>
<p>Is lost - perdition [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">απωλετο - απωλειας] </span></span>. A play of words : "None of them perished, but the son of perishing" (Westcott). </p>
<p>The scripture [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">η γραφη] </span></span>. See close of note on 5 47, and on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mr+12:10">Mark 12:10</span>.</p>
