<p>Power [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εξουσιαν] </span></span>. Rev., rightly, authority. See on 1 12. </p>
<p>All flesh. A Hebrew phrase, denoting the whole of humanity in its imperfection. See <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ge+6:12">Genesis 6:12</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ps+65:2">Psalms 65:2</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="isa+40:5">Isaiah 40:5</span>, etc. That He should give eternal life to as many as Thou hast given Him [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ινα παν ο δεδωκας αυτω, δωση αυτοις ζωην αιωνιον] </span></span>. Literally, that all that Thou hast given Him, to them He should give eternal life. All [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">παν] </span></span>, singular number, regards the body of Christian disciples collectively : to them, individually.</p>
