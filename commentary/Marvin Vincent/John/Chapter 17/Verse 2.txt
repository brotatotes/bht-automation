Power [εξουσιαν] . Rev., rightly, authority. See on 1 12. All flesh. A Hebrew phrase, denoting the whole of humanity in its imperfection. See Genesis 6:12; Psalms 65:2; Isaiah 40:5, etc. That He should give eternal life to as many as Thou hast given Him [ινα παν ο δεδωκας αυτω, δωση αυτοις ζωην αιωνιον] . Literally, that all that Thou hast given Him, to them He should give eternal life. All [παν] , singular number, regards the body of Christian disciples collectively : to them, individually.
