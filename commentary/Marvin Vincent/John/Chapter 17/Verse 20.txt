<p>Shall believe [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πιστευσοντων] </span></span>. The best texts read pisteuontwn, the present participle, that believe. The future body of believers is conceived as actually existing. </p>
<p>On me through their word. The Greek order is, believe through their word on me. "Believe through their word" forms a compound idea.</p>
