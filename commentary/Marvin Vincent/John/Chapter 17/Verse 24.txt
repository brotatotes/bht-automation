<p>They - whom [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ους] </span></span>. The best texts read o, that which. The construction is similar to that in ver. 2, "that He should give eternal life," etc. Like pan, all, in that passage, that which here refers to the body of believers taken collectively. </p>
