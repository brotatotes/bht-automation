<p>I have glorified - I have finished [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εδοξασα - ετελειωσα] </span></span>. The best texts read, teleiwsav, having finished; the participle defining the manner in which He had glorified the Father upon earth. So Rev. </p>
<p>To do [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ινα ποιησω] </span></span>. Literally, in order that I should do (it).</p>
