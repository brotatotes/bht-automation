<p>Simon Peter. The names of Simon Peter and Malchus are mentioned only by John in connection with this incident. The incident itself is related by all the Evangelists. </p>
<p>A sword. Contrary to the rule which forbade the carrying of weapons on a feast - day. </p>
<p>The high priest 's servant. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+26:51">Matthew 26:51</span>. </p>
<p>Right ear. Luke and John. The others do not specify which ear. For ear John and Mark have wjtarion, a diminutive; Luke, oujv, and Matthew, wjtion, a diminutive in form, but not in force. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+26:51">Matthew 26:51</span>.</p>
