<p>Thy sword. Omit thy, and read, the sword. </p>
<p>Sheath [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">θηκην] </span></span>. Only here in the New Testament. From tiqhmi, to put. That into which the sword is put. </p>
<p>The cup. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+26:39">Matthew 26:39</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mr+14:36">Mark 14:36</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="lu+22:42">Luke 22:42</span>. Peculiar to John.</p>
