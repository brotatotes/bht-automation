The captain [χιλιαρχος] . See on Mark 6:21, and on centurion, Luke 7:2. Took [συνελαβον] . Rev., better, seized. It is the technical word for arresting. Literally, took with them, of which there is a suggestion in the modern policeman's phrase, go along with me. Compare Luke 22:54. 13 - 18. Compare Matthew 26:57, Matthew 26:58; 69 - 75; Mark 14:53, Mark 14:54; 66 - 72; Luke 22:54-62.
