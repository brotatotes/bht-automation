<p>The captain [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">χιλιαρχος] </span></span>. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mr+6:21">Mark 6:21</span>, and on centurion, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="lu+7:2">Luke 7:2</span>. </p>
<p>Took [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">συνελαβον] </span></span>. Rev., better, seized. It is the technical word for arresting. Literally, took with them, of which there is a suggestion in the modern policeman's phrase, go along with me. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="lu+22:54">Luke 22:54</span>. </p>
