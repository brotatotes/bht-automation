<p>Stood. It is discouraging to see how the A. V. habitually ignores the imperfect tense, and thus detracts from the liveliness of the narrative. Render, as Rev., were standing. </p>
<p>Fire of coals [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ανθρακιαν] </span></span>. Only here and <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="joh+21:9">John 21:9</span>. Matthew does not mention the fire. Mark has to fwv, strictly, the light of the fire. Luke says they had kindled a fire [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πυρ] </span></span>. </p>
<p>Warmed. Rev., correctly, were warming. So, ver. 25, was standing and was warming, for stood and warmed. </p>
