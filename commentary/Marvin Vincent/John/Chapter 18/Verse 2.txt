<p>Which betrayed [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ο παραδιδους] </span></span>. The present participle, marking the betrayal as in progress. Literally, who is betraying. </p>
<p>Resorted [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">συνηχθη] </span></span>. Literally, assembled. The items of this verse are peculiar to John.</p>
