<p>Art thou [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">συ ει] </span></span>. Thou is emphatic. Thou, the despised malefactor. King of the Jews. The civil title. The theocratic title, king of Israel (i. 49; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="joh+12:13">John 12:13</span>) is addressed to Jesus on the cross (<span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+27:42">Matthew 27:42</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mr+14:32">Mark 14:32</span>) in mockery.</p>
