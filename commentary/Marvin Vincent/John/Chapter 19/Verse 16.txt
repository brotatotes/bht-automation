<p>Delivered. Luke says, delivered to their will (xxiii. 25). Pilate pronounced no sentence, but disclaimed all responsibility for the act, and delivered Christ up to them [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αυτοις] </span></span>, they having invoked the responsibility upon themselves. See <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+27:24">Matthew 27:24</span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+27:25">Matthew 27:25</span>. </p>
<p>And led Him away. The best texts omit.</p>
