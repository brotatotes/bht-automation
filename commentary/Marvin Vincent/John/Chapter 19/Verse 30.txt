<p>Gave up the ghost [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">παρεδωκε το πνευμα] </span></span>. Rev., his spirit. Matthew, ajfhken dismissed. Mark, ejxepneusen, breathed forth (his life). So Luke, who adds, "Father, into thy hands I commend (paratiqemai, see on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="lu+9:16">Luke 9:16</span>) my spirit." </p>
<p>31 - 42. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+27:57-61">Matthew 27:57-61</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mr+14:42-47">Mark 14:42-47</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="lu+23:50-60">Luke 23:50-60</span>.</p>
