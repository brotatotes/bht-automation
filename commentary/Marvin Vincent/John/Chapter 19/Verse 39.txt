<p>Came Nicodemus - came by night. The contrast is marked between his first and his second coming. </p>
<p>Mixture [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μιγμα] </span></span>. Only here in the New Testament. Some authorities read eligma, a roll. </p>
<p>Pounds. Roman pounds, of nearly twelve ounces. The large quantity may be explained by the intention of covering the entire body with the preparation, and by the fact that a portion was designed for the couch of the body in the grave. Compare the account of the burial of Asa, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="2ch+16:14">2 Chronicles 16:14</span>. "Extraordinary reverence in its sorrowful excitement does not easily satisfy itself" (Meyer).</p>
