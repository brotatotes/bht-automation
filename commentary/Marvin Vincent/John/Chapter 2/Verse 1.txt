<p>The third day. Reckoning from the last day mentioned (i. 43). </p>
<p>A marriage [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">γαμος] </span></span>. Or marriage festival, including a series of entertainments, and therefore often found in the plural. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+22:2">Matthew 22:2</span>. </p>
<p>Cana of Galilee. To distinguish it from Cana in Coelo - Syria. </p>
<p>Mother of Jesus. Her name is never mentioned by John. </p>
<p>Was there. When Jesus arrived. Probably as an intimate friend of the family, assisting in the preparations.</p>
