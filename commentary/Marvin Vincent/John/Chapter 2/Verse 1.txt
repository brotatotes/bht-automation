The third day. Reckoning from the last day mentioned (i. 43). A marriage [γαμος] . Or marriage festival, including a series of entertainments, and therefore often found in the plural. See on Matthew 22:2. Cana of Galilee. To distinguish it from Cana in Coelo - Syria. Mother of Jesus. Her name is never mentioned by John. Was there. When Jesus arrived. Probably as an intimate friend of the family, assisting in the preparations.
