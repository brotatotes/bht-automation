<p>Forty and six years was this temple in building [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τεσσαρακοντα και εξ ετεσιν ωκοδομηθη ο ναος ουτος] </span></span>. Literally, In forty and six years was this temple built. It was spoken of as completed, although not finished until thirty - six years later. </p>
<p>Thou. The position of the Greek pronoun makes it emphatic.</p>
