<p>Breathed on them [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ενεφυσησεν] </span></span>. Only here in the New Testament. The act was symbolic, after the manner of the Hebrew prophets. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="eze+37:5">Ezekiel 37:5</span>. </p>
<p>The Holy Ghost. The article is wanting. The gift bestowed was not that of the personal Holy Spirit, but rather an earnest of that gift; an effusion of the Spirit.</p>
