<p>Be not [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μη γινου] </span></span>. Literally, become not. Thomas was in a fair way to become unbelieving, through his doubt of the resurrection. </p>
<p>Faithless - believing [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">απιστος - πιστος] </span></span>. There is a correspondence of the words here, to which, perhaps, the nearest approach in English is unbelieving, believing.</p>
