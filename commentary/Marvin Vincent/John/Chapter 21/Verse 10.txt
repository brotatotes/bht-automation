<p>Of the fish [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">των οψαριων] </span></span>. As in ver. 9. Emphasizing the fish as food. </p>
<p>Ye hate caught [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επιασατε] </span></span>. See on ver. 3. Bengel says : "By the Lord 's gift they had caught them : and yet, He courteously says, that they have caught them."</p>
