Lovest [αγαπας] . Again the colder word, but more than these is omitted. I love [φιλω] . Peter reiterates his former word expressive of personal affection. Feed [ποιμαινε] . A different word : tend, as Rev. See on 1 Peter 5:2. Sheep [προβατα] . Some of the best texts read probatia, diminutive, little sheep.
