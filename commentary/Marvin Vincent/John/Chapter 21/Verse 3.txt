<p>A ship [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">το πλοιον] </span></span>. Rev., the boat; restoring the article, which indicates a familiar implement. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="lu+5:2">Luke 5:2</span>. </p>
<p>Immediately. Omit. </p>
<p>That night. The emphatic pronoun that [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εκεινη</span></span>] may indicate that their ill success was unusual. </p>
<p>Caught [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επιασαν] </span></span>. So ver. 10. The verb means to lay hold of, and is nowhere else used in the New Testament of taking fish.
