A ship [το πλοιον] . Rev., the boat; restoring the article, which indicates a familiar implement. See on Luke 5:2. Immediately. Omit. That night. The emphatic pronoun that [εκεινη] may indicate that their ill success was unusual. Caught [επιασαν] . So ver. 10. The verb means to lay hold of, and is nowhere else used in the New Testament of taking fish. Elsewhere in this Gospel always of the seizure of Christ by the authorities (vii. 30, 39, 44; John 8:20; John 10:39; John 11:57). Of apprehending Peter and Paul (Acts 12:4; 2 Corinthians 11:32). Of the taking of the beast (Revelation 19:20). Of taking by the hand (Acts 3:7).
