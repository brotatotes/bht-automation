Children [παιδια] . Or, little children. Used also by John, in address, twice in the First Epistle (ii. 13, 18), where, however, the more common word is teknia, little children. Have ye any meat [μη τι προσφαγιον εχετε] ? The interrogative mh ti indicates that a negative answer is expected : you have not, I suppose, anything.
