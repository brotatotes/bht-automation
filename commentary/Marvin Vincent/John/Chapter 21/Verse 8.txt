A little ship [τω πλοιαριω] . The noun is diminutive. Rev., the little boat. It is hardly probable that this refers to a smaller boat accompanying the vessel. Compare the alternation of ploion and ploiarion in John 6:17, John 6:19, John 6:21, John 6:22, John 6:24. Two hundred cubits. A little over a hundred yards. With fishes [των ιχθυων] . Or, the net of the fishes. So Wyc, Rev., full of fishes.
