<p>A fire of coals. Charcoal. See <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="joh+18:18">John 18:18</span>. </p>
<p>Fish [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">οψαριον] </span></span>. See on 6 9. </p>
<p>Bread [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αρτον] </span></span>. Or, a loaf. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+4:1">Matthew 4:1</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+7:9">Matthew 7:9</span>.</p>
