They were come to land [απεβησαν εις την γην] . Not of the arrival of the boat, but of the going ashore of the boatmen. Rev., therefore, correctly, they got out upon the land. A fire of coals. Charcoal. See John 18:18. Fish [οψαριον] . See on 6 9. Bread [αρτον] . Or, a loaf. See on Matthew 4:1; Matthew 7:9.
