<p>And [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">και] </span></span>. Note the simple connective particle, with nothing to indicate the logical sequence of the thought. </p>
<p>Hath ascended. Equivalent to hath been in. Jesus says that no one has been in heaven except the Son of man who came down out of heaven; because no man could be in heaven without having ascended thither. </p>
<p>Which is in heaven. Many authorities omit.</p>
