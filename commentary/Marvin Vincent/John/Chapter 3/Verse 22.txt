The land of Judaea [την ιουδαιαν γην] . Literally, the Judaean land. The phrase occurs only here in the New Testament. Tarried (die triben). The verb originally means to rub, hence to wear away, consume; and so of spending or passing time. Baptized [εβαπτιζεν] . The imperfect tense agrees with the idea of tarrying. He continued baptizing during His stay.
