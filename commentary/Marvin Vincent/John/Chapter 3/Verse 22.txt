<p>The land of Judaea [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">την ιουδαιαν γην] </span></span>. Literally, the Judaean land. The phrase occurs only here in the New Testament. </p>
<p>Tarried (die triben). The verb originally means to rub, hence to wear away, consume; and so of spending or passing time. </p>
<p>Baptized [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εβαπτιζεν] </span></span>. The imperfect tense agrees with the idea of tarrying. He continued baptizing during His stay.</p>
