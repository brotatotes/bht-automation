<p>Was baptizing [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ην βαπτιζων] </span></span>. The substantive verb with the participle also indicating continuous or habitual action; was engaged in baptizing. </p>
<p>Aenon, near to Salim. The situation is a matter of conjecture. The word, Aenon is probably akin to the Hebrew ayin, an eye, a spring. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="jas+3:11">James 3:11</span>. </p>
<p>Much water [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">υδατα πολλα] </span></span>. Literally, many waters. Probably referring to a number of pools or springs. </p>
<p>Came - were baptized. Imperfects. They kept coming.</p>
