The bride. A common figure in the Old testament prophecies, of the relation between Jehovah and His people (Ezekiel 16:0; Hosea 2:19; Malachi 2:11). See also on Matthew 1:21, concerning Hosea. Friend of the bridegroom. Or groomsman. The term is appropriate to Judaea, the groomsmen not being customary in Galilee. See Matthew 9:15, where the phrase children of the bridechamber is used.
