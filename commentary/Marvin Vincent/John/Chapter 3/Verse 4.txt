<p>When he is old [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">γερων ων] </span></span>. Literally, being an old man. </p>
<p>Can he [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μη δυναται] </span></span>. The interrogative particle anticipates a negative answer. Surely he cannot. </p>
<p>Second time. Nicodemus looks at the subject merely from the physical side. His second time is not the same as Jesus ' anew. As Godet remarks, "he does not understand the difference between a second beginning and a different beginning."</p>
