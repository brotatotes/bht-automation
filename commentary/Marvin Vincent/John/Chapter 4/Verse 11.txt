To draw with [αντλημα] . The noun means what is drawn, the act of drawing, and the thing to draw with. Here the bucket, of skin, with three cross sticks at the mouth to keep it open, and let down by a goat 's - hair rope. Not to be confounded with the water - pot [υδρια] of ver. 28. The word is found only here in the New Testament. Well [φρεαρ] . See on ver. 6. It may have been fed by living springs [πηγαι] . That living water [το υδωρ το ζων] . Literally, the water the living.
