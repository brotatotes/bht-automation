<p>To draw with [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αντλημα] </span></span>. The noun means what is drawn, the act of drawing, and the thing to draw with. Here the bucket, of skin, with three cross sticks at the mouth to keep it open, and let down by a goat 's - hair rope. Not to be confounded with the water - pot [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">υδρια</span></span>] of ver. 28. The word is found only here in the New Testament. </p>
<p>Well [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">φρεαρ] </span></span>. See on ver. 6. It may have been fed by living springs [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πηγαι] </span></span>. </p>
<p>That living water [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">το υδωρ το ζων] </span></span>. Literally, the water the living.</p>
