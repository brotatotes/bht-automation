He left [αφηκε] . The verb means literally to send away, dismiss. It is used of forgiving offenses (Matthew 6:43; James 5:15); of yielding up (Matthew 27:50); of letting alone (Matthew 19:14); of allowing or permitting (Luke 6:12). See on these passages. Its employment here is peculiar. Compare John 16:28, of Christ 's leaving the world. Again. See John 1:44.
