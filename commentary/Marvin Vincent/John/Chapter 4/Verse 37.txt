Herein [εν τουτω] . Literally, in this. In this relation between sower and reaper. Is that saying true [ο λογος εστιν ο αληθινος] . Rev., properly, the saying; the common proverb. True : not only says the truth, but the saying is completely fulfilled according to the ideal in the sowing and reaping of which Jesus speaks. The literal rendering of the Greek, as given above, is, "the saying is the true (saying);" but several high authorities omit the article before true.
