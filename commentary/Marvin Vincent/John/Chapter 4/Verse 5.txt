<p>Then [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ουν] </span></span>. Not a particle of time, but of logical connection. Therefore, going by this route, He must needs, etc. </p>
<p>City. Not implying a place of great size or importance. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="joh+11:54">John 11:54</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+2:23">Matthew 2:23</span>. </p>
<p>Sychar. Commonly identified with Schechem, the modern Nablous, and regarded as a corruption of Sichem. Some modern authorities, however, argue that a place so famous as Schechem would not be referred to under another name, and identify the site with Askar, about two miles east of Nablous. The name Sychar means drunken - town or lying - town. </p>
<p>Parcel of ground [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">χωριου] </span></span>. A diminutive from cwra a region.</p>
