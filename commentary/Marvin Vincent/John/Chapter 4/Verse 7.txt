<p>A woman. Held in low esteem by the popular teachers; a Samaritan, and therefore despised by the Jews; poor, for drawing water was not, as in earlier times, performed by women of station (<span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ge+24:15">Genesis 24:15</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ge+29:9">Genesis 29:9</span>). Of Samaria. Literally, out of Samaria [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εκ] </span></span>. Not of the city of Samaria, which was some six miles distant, but the country. A Samaritan by race and religion. </p>
<p>To draw. See on 2 8.</p>
