He that was healed [ιαθεις] . Compare ver. 10, and note the different word for healing. See references there. Who it was [τις εστιν] . The present tense, who it is. Had conveyed Himself away [εξενευσεν] . The verb means, literally, to turn the head aside, in order to avoid something. Hence, generally, to retire or withdraw. Only here in the New Testament.
