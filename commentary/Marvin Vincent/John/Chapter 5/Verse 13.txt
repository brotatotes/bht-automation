<p>He that was healed [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ιαθεις] </span></span>. Compare ver. 10, and note the different word for healing. See references there. </p>
<p>Who it was [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τις εστιν] </span></span>. The present tense, who it is. </p>
<p>Had conveyed Himself away [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εξενευσεν] </span></span>. The verb means, literally, to turn the head aside, in order to avoid something. Hence, generally, to retire or withdraw. Only here in the New Testament.</p>
