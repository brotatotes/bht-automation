<p>Findeth - said. Note the lively interchange of the tenses, as in ver. 13. </p>
<p>Sin no more [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μηκετι αμαρτανε] </span></span>. No longer continue to sin. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+1:21">Matthew 1:21</span>. Jesus thus shows His knowledge that the sickness was the result of sin. </p>
<p>A worse thing. Than even those thirty - eight years of suffering. </p>
<p>Come unto thee [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">σοι γενηται] </span></span>. Rev., better, befall thee. Literally, come to pass.</p>
