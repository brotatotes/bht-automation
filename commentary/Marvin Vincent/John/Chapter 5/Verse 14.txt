Findeth - said. Note the lively interchange of the tenses, as in ver. 13. Sin no more [μηκετι αμαρτανε] . No longer continue to sin. See on Matthew 1:21. Jesus thus shows His knowledge that the sickness was the result of sin. A worse thing. Than even those thirty - eight years of suffering. Come unto thee [σοι γενηται] . Rev., better, befall thee. Literally, come to pass.
