<p>Had broken [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ελυε] </span></span>. Literally, was loosing : the imperfect tense. See on He did, ver. 16. Not, broke the Sabbath in any particular case, but was annulling the law and duty of Sabbath observance. </p>
<p>His Father [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πατερα ιδιον] </span></span>. Properly, His own Father. So Rev.</p>
