For the Father [ουδε γαρ ο πατηρ] . The A. V. misses the climax in ouJude; not even the Father, who might be expected to be judge. Hath committed [δεδωκεν] . Rev., given. The habitual word for the bestowment of the privileges and functions of the Son. See ver. John 5:36; John 3:35; John 6:37, John 6:39; John 10:29, etc. All judgment (thn krisin pasan). Literally, the judgment wholly.
