<p>For the Father [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ουδε γαρ ο πατηρ] </span></span>. The A. V. misses the climax in ouJude; not even the Father, who might be expected to be judge. </p>
<p>Hath committed [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δεδωκεν] </span></span>. Rev., given. The habitual word for the bestowment of the privileges and functions of the Son.
