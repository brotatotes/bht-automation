<p>The graves [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τοις μνημειοις] </span></span>. Rev., better; tombs. Two words are used in the New Testament for the place of burial, tafov, and mnhmeion or mnhma. The former emphasizes the idea of burial (qaptw, to bury); the latter of preserving the memory of the dead; from mimnhskw, to remind.</p>
