Greater witness [την μαρτυριαν μειζω] . The article, omitted in A. V., has the force of my, as in ver. 34. Rev., the witness which I have is greater. Hath given. See on ver. 22. To finish [ινα τελειωσω] . Literally, in order that I should accomplish. Rev., accomplish. See on 4 34. The same works [αυτα τα εργα] . Rev., more correctly, the very works.
