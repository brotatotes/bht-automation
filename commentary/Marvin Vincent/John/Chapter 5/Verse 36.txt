<p>Greater witness [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">την μαρτυριαν μειζω] </span></span>. The article, omitted in A. V., has the force of my, as in ver. 34. Rev., the witness which I have is greater. </p>
<p>Hath given. See on ver. 22. </p>
<p>To finish [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ινα τελειωσω] </span></span>. Literally, in order that I should accomplish. Rev., accomplish. See on 4 34. </p>
<p>The same works [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αυτα τα εργα] </span></span>. Rev., more correctly, the very works.</p>
