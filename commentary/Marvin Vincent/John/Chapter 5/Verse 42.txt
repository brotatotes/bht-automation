<p>I know [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εγνωκα] </span></span>. See on 2 24. </p>
<p>The love of God. Love toward God. This was the summary of their own law. The phrase occurs elsewhere in the Gospels only in <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="lu+11:42">Luke 11:42</span>. In you [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εν εαυτοις] </span></span>. Rev., rightly, in yourselves. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="joh+6:53">John 6:53</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1jo+5:10">1 John 5:10</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mr+4:17">Mark 4:17</span>.</p>
