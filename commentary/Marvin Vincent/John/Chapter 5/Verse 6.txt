Had been now a long time [πολυν ηδη χρονον εχει] . Literally, he hath already much time. Wilt thou [θελεις] . Not merely, do you wish, but are you in earnest? See on Matthew 1:19. Jesus appeals to the energy of his will. Not improbably he had fallen into apathy through his long sickness. Compare Acts 3:4; John 7:17. Whole [υγιης] . Sound.
