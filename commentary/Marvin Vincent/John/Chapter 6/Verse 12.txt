<p>Fragments [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κλασματα] </span></span>. From klaw, to break. Rev., broken pieces. That remain [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">περισσευσαντα] </span></span>. Rev., remain over. Literally, exceed the necessary supply. Only John gives the Lord 's command to collect the fragments, and the reason for it, that nothing be lost.</p>
