<p>Meat (brwsin). See on 4 32. In <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+6:19">Matthew 6:19</span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+6:20">Matthew 6:20</span>, and there only, it is used in the sense of rust, that which eats or corrodes. Similarly, corrode is from rodo, to gnaw. </p>
<p>Him hath God the Father sealed. The Rev. makes the sentence culminate properly in God : "for Him the Father, even God, hath sealed." According to the strict Greek order it is : for Him the Father sealed, even God. On sealed [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εσφραγισεν</span></span>] see on 3 33. Wyc., betokened Him.</p>
