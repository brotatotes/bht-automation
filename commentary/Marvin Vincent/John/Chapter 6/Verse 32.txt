Moses gave you not [ου μωσης δεδωκεν υμιν] . The antithesis is between Moses and my Father. So Rev., rightly, "it was not Moses that gave you," etc. - "but my Father giveth," etc. Some editors change the perfect tense, dedwken, hath given, to the aorist, edwken, gave. The true bread from heaven [τον αρτον εκ τουουρανου τον αληθινον] . The translation would gain by following the Greek order, "the bread out of heaven, the real bread."
