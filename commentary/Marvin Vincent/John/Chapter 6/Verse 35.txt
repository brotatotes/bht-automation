I am the bread of life. A form of expression peculiar to John. See vv. 41, 48, 51; John 8:12; John 10:7, John 10:9, John 10:11, John 10:14; John 11:25; John 14:6; John 14:1, John 14:5. Cometh - believeth. Faith in its active aspect and in its resting aspect. Never [ου μη] . Rather, in nowise, or by no means. Rev., shall not.
