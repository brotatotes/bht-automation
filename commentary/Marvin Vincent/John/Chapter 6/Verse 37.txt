<p>All that [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">παν ο] </span></span>. The neuter singular of the adjective and pronoun. All believers are regarded as one complete whole. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="joh+17:24">John 17:24</span>, according to the correct reading, "that which Thou hast given me." </p>
<p>Shall come [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ηξει] </span></span>. Emphasizing the idea of reaching or arriving. </p>
<p>Cometh [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ερχομενον] </span></span>. A different verb, emphasizing the process of coming.</p>
