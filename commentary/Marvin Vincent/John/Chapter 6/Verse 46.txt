Hath seen. As contrasted with hearing and learning. (ver. 45). The Father is not seen immediately, but through the Son. Compare John 1:18; John 14:9; 1 John 3:2, Matthew 11:27. Of God [παρα του θεου] . More correctly, as Rev., from, with an idea of association with : from with God. Para is used of procession from a personal object, indicating it generally as the starting - point.
