<p>Hath seen. As contrasted with hearing and learning. (ver. 45). The Father is not seen immediately, but through the Son. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="joh+1:18">John 1:18</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="joh+14:9">John 14:9</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1jo+3:2">1 John 3:2</span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+11:27">Matthew 11:27</span>. </p>
<p>Of God [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">παρα του θεου] </span></span>. More correctly, as Rev., from, with an idea of association with : from with God. Para is used of procession from a personal object, indicating it generally as the starting - point.</p>
