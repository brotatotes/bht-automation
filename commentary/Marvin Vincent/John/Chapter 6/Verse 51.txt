<p>The living bread [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ο αρτος ο ζων] </span></span>. Literally, the bread the living (bread). Wyc., quick bread. </p>
<p>I will give. The ejgw, I, is emphatic, in contrast with Moses (ver. 32). </p>
<p>Flesh. See on 1 14. </p>
<p>Which I will give. The best texts omit. Read, as Rev., my flesh for the life of the world.</p>
