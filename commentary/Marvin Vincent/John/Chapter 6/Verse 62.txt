What and if ye shall see [εαν ουν θεωρητε] . The question is marked by an aposiopesis, i e., a breaking off of the sentence and leaving the hearer to complete it for himself. Literally, if then ye should behold, etc. - the completion would be, would not this still more cause you to stumble ? Ascend [αναβαινοντα] . Rev., properly, renders the participle, ascending. I speak [λαλω] . But the correct reading is lelalhka, the perfect tense, I have spoken, or I have just spoken, referring to the preceding discourse.
