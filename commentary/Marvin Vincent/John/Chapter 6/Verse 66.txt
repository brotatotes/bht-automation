<p>From that time [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εκ τουτου] </span></span>. Render, as Rev., upon this. As a result proceeding out of [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εκ</span></span>] this. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="joh+19:12">John 19:12</span>. </p>
<p>Went back [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">απηλθον εις τα οπισω] </span></span>. The Greek expresses more than the English. They went away [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">απο</span></span>] from Christ, Literally, to the things behind, to what they had left in order to follow the Lord. </p>
<p>Walked [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">περιεπατουν] </span></span>. Literally, walked about, with Jesus in His wanderings here and there.</p>
