<p>Are sure [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εγνωκαμεν] </span></span>. Literally, have come to know. The order of the words believe and know is reversed in <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="joh+17:8">John 17:8</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1jo+4:16">1 John 4:16</span>. In the case of the first disciples, faith, produced by the overpowering impression of Jesus ' works and person, preceded intellectual conviction. </p>
<p>That Christ, the Son of the living God. The best texts substitute oJ agiov tou Qeou, the holy one of God. The other reading has resulted from the attempt to bring Peter's confession here into accord with that in <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+16:16">Matthew 16:16</span>. The two confessions differ in that "here the confession points to the inward character in which the Apostles found the assurance of life; there the confession was of the public office and theocratic person of the Lord" (Westcott).</p>
