<p>Judas Iscariot the son of Simon (Ioudan Simwnov Iskariwthn). The correct reading is Iskariwtou, Iscariot, agreeing in the genitive case with Simwnov, of Simon. Render, as Rev., Judas, the son of Simon Iscariot. Iscariot denotes the name of Simon 's town : a man of Kerioth. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+10:5">Matthew 10:5</span>. </p>
