<p>About the midst of the feast [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">της εορτης μεσουσης] </span></span>. A peculiar form of expression found only here. The midst is expressed by a participle from the verb mesow, to be in the middle. Literally, the feast being midway. Taught [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εδιδασκεν] </span></span>. Or began to teach. Imperfect tense.</p>
