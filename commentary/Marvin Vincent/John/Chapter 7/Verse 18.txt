His own glory [την δοξαν την ιδιαν] . Literally, the glow which is His own; the second article throwing His own into sharp contrast with His that sent Him. On His own, see on Acts 1:7; Matthew 22:5; Matthew 25:15. The same [ουτος] . Notice the characteristic use of the pronoun taking up and emphasizing the principal subject of the sentence. Unrighteousness [αδικια] . See on 2 Peter 2:13.
