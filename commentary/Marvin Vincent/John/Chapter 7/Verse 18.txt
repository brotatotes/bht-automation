<p>His own glory [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">την δοξαν την ιδιαν] </span></span>. Literally, the glow which is His own; the second article throwing His own into sharp contrast with His that sent Him. On His own, see on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+1:7">Acts 1:7</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+22:5">Matthew 22:5</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+25:15">Matthew 25:15</span>. The same [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ουτος] </span></span>. Notice the characteristic use of the pronoun taking up and emphasizing the principal subject of the sentence. </p>
<p>Unrighteousness [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αδικια] </span></span>. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="2pe+2:13">2 Peter 2:13</span>.</p>
