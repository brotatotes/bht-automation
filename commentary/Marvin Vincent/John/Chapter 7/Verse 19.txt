Did - give [δεδωκεν] . Some texts read the aorist tense edwken, in which case this rendering is correct. If with others we read the perfect, we should render hath not Moses given you the law, which you still profess to observe. Keepeth [ποιει] . Rev., rightly, doeth. Compare do in ver. 17. Go ye about [ζητειτε] . Properly, seek ye. So Rev.
