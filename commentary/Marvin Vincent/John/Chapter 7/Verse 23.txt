<p>Are ye angry [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">χολατε] </span></span>. Only here in the New Testament. From colh, gall. Strictly, the verb means to be full of bile, hence to be melancholy mad. </p>
<p>Every whit whole [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ολον υγιη] </span></span>. Strictly, I made a whole man sound, in contrast with the rite of circumcision which affects only a single member, but which, nevertheless, they practice on the Sabbath.</p>
