<p>Appearance [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">οψιν] </span></span>. Primarily, seeing or sight. In <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="joh+11:44">John 11:44</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="re+1:16">Revelation 1:16</span>, face, and hence external appearance. The word occurs only in the three passages cited. </p>
<p>Righteous judgment (thn dikaian krisin). Properly, the righteous judgment; that which is appropriate to the case in hand.</p>
