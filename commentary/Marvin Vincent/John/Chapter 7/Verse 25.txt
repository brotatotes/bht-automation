<p>Them of Jerusalem [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ιεροσολυμιτων] </span></span>. Literally, of the Jerusalemites, who knew better than the multitude the designs of the priesthood. The word occurs only here and <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mr+1:5">Mark 1:5</span>.</p>
