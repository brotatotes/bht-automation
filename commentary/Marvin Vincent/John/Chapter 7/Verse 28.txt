<p>Then [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ουν] </span></span>. Rev., rightly, therefore, giving the reason for the succeeding words in Jesus ' emotion awakened by the misconceptions of the people. </p>
<p>Cried [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εκραξεν] </span></span>. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mr+5:5">Mark 5:5</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="joh+9:24">John 9:24</span>. </p>
<p>As He taught [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">διδασκων] </span></span>. Better, Rev., teaching. The expression cried teaching implies speaking in a peculiarly solemn manner and with an elevation of voice. </p>
<p>Me - whence I am. Conceding the truth of the people's statement in ver. 27, we know this man whence he is, so far as His outward person and His earthly origin were concerned. He goes on to show that they are ignorant of His divine relationship. </p>
<p>True [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αληθινος] </span></span>. True to the ideal of a sender : a genuine sender in the highest sense of the term. See on 1 9.</p>
