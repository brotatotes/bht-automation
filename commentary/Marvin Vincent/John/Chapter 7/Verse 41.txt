<p>Shall Christ, etc. [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μη γαρ ο χριστος] </span></span>. The Rev. gives better the force of the interrogative particle with gar, for : What, doth the Christ come, etc. The idea in full is, "you cannot [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μη</span></span>] say that, for [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">γαρ</span></span>] doth the Christ, etc." </p>
<p>Shall - come [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ερχεται] </span></span>. The present tense. Rev., rightly, doth - come.</p>
