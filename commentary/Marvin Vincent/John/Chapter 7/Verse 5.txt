<p>Neither [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ουδε] </span></span>. Better, as Rev., not even. </p>
<p>Did believe [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επιστευον] </span></span>. The imperfect, were believing; referring not to a single act of faith, but to faith as habitual and controlling.</p>
