I am he [εγω ειμι] . He is inserted in the versions and is not in the text. By retaining it, we read, I am the Messiah. But the words are rather the solemn expression of His absolute divine being, as in ver 58 "If ye believe not that I am." See Deuteronomy 32:39; Isaiah 43:10; and compare vv. 28, 58 of this chapter, and John 13:19.
