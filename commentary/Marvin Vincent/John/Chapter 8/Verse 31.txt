<p>Believed on Him [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πεπιστευκοτας αυτω] </span></span>. Note the different phrase, distinguishing the Jews from the mixed company in ver. 30. Rev., rightly, believed Him. </p>
<p>If ye continue [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εαν υμεις μεινητε] </span></span>. The emphasis is on the ye, addressed to those whose faith was rudimentary; who believed Him, but did not yet believe on Him. Rev., abide. </p>
<p>In my word [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εν τω λογω τω εμω] </span></span>. Literally, in the word which is mine : peculiarly mine, characteristic of me. The expression is intentionally stronger than my word. Compare my love (xv. 9). </p>
<p>Indeed [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αληθως] </span></span>. Literally, truly; as Rev. As those who believe on me, not as those who are moved by temporary excitement to admit my claims.</p>
