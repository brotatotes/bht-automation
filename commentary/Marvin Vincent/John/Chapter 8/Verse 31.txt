Believed on Him [πεπιστευκοτας αυτω] . Note the different phrase, distinguishing the Jews from the mixed company in ver. 30. Rev., rightly, believed Him. If ye continue [εαν υμεις μεινητε] . The emphasis is on the ye, addressed to those whose faith was rudimentary; who believed Him, but did not yet believe on Him. Rev., abide. In my word [εν τω λογω τω εμω] . Literally, in the word which is mine : peculiarly mine, characteristic of me. The expression is intentionally stronger than my word. Compare my love (xv. 9). Indeed [αληθως] . Literally, truly; as Rev. As those who believe on me, not as those who are moved by temporary excitement to admit my claims.
