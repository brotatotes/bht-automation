<p>Whosoever committeth [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πας ο ποιων] </span></span>. Rev., more correctly, every one that committeth. </p>
<p>Sin [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">την αμαρτιαν] </span></span>. The definite article, the sin, shows that Jesus does not mean merely a simple act, but a life of sin. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1jo+3:4-8">1 John 3:4-8</span>, and doeth the truth (<span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="joh+3:21">John 3:21</span>); doeth the righteousness (<span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1jo+2:29">1 John 2:29</span>). The servant [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δουλος] </span></span>. Or, a servant. Properly, a bond - servant or slave. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+20:26">Matthew 20:26</span>. </p>
<p>Of sin. A few authorities omit, and read whosoever committeth sin is a bond - servant. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ro+6:17">Romans 6:17</span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ro+6:20">Romans 6:20</span>.</p>
