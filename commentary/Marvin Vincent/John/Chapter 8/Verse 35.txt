<p>Abideth not in the house forever. A slave has no permanent place in the house. He may be sold, exchanged, or cast out. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ge+21:10">Genesis 21:10</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ga+4:30">Galatians 4:30</span>. House. See <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="heb+3:6">Hebrews 3:6</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="joh+14:2">John 14:2</span>. The elder son in the parable of the prodigal (<span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="lu+14:29">Luke 14:29</span>), denies his sonship by the words, "These many years do I serve thee [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δουλευω] </span></span>."</p>
