<p>Speech - word [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">λαλιαν - λογον] </span></span>. The former word refers to the form, the latter to the substance of discourse. So <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+26:73">Matthew 26:73</span>, of Peter, "thy speech [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">λαλια</span></span>] bewrayeth thee;" thy mode of speaking. If they had understood the substance, they would have understood the form. </p>
