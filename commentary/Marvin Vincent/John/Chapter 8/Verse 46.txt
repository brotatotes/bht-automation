<p>Convinceth [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ελεγχει] </span></span>. See on 3 20. Rev., convicteth. </p>
<p>Sin [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αμαρτιας] </span></span>. Not fault or error, but sin in general, as everywhere in the New Testament. </p>
<p>The truth [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αληθειαν] </span></span>. Without the article, and therefore not the whole truth, but that which is true as to any part of divine revelation.</p>
