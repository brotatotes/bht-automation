<p>Rejoiced [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ηγαλλιασατο] </span></span>. With exultant joy. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1pe+1:6">1 Peter 1:6</span>. To see [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ινα ιδη] </span></span>. The Greek construction is peculiar. Literally, that he should see; i e., in the knowledge or anticipation that he should see. My day. The exact meaning of the expression is altogether uncertain.</p>
