<p>To the pool of Siloam. The best texts read simply, Go to Siloam. Received sight [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ανεβλεψα] </span></span>. Originally, to look up, as <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+14:19">Matthew 14:19</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mr+16:4">Mark 16:4</span>, and so some render it here; but better, I recovered sight.</p>
