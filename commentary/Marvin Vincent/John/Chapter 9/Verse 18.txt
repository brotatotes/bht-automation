<p>The Jews. Notice the change from the Pharisees. The Pharisees had already divided on this miracle (ver. 16). The Jews represent that section which was hostile to Jesus. </p>
<p>Of him that had received his sight [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αυτου του αναβλεψαντος] </span></span>. Properly, "of the very one who had received."</p>
