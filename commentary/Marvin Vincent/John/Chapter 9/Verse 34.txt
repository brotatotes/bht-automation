Altogether [ολος] . Literally, all of thee. In sins. Standing first in the Greek order, and emphatic, as is also su thou, in both instances. "In sins wast thou born, all of thee; and dost thou teach us?" Teach. Emphatic. Dost thou, thus born in sins, assume the office of teacher ? Cast him out. From the place where they were conversing. Not excommunicated, which this miscellaneous gathering could not do.
