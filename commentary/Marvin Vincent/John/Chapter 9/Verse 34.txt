<p>Altogether [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ολος] </span></span>. Literally, all of thee. </p>
<p>In sins. Standing first in the Greek order, and emphatic, as is also su thou, in both instances. "In sins wast thou born, all of thee; and dost thou teach us?" Teach. Emphatic. Dost thou, thus born in sins, assume the office of teacher ? </p>
<p>Cast him out. From the place where they were conversing. Not excommunicated, which this miscellaneous gathering could not do.</p>
