As long as [οταν] . More correctly, whensoever. Rev., when. Whether in my incarnation, or before my incarnation, or after it. Compare John 1:4, John 1:10. The light. See on 8 12. The article is wanting. Westcott says, "Christ is 'light to the world, ' as well as 'the one Light of the world. ' The character is unchangeable, but the display of the character varies with the occasion."
