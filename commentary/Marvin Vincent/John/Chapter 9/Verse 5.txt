<p>As long as [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">οταν] </span></span>. More correctly, whensoever. Rev., when. Whether in my incarnation, or before my incarnation, or after it. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="joh+1:4">John 1:4</span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="joh+1:10">John 1:10</span>. </p>
<p>The light. See on 8 12. The article is wanting. Westcott says, "Christ is 'light to the world, ' as well as 'the one Light of the world. ' The character is unchangeable, but the display of the character varies with the occasion."</p>
