And of some have compassion, making a difference. This follows the reading, kai ouv men ejleeite [ελεατε] diakrinomenoi. The best texts, however, read diakrinomenouv, which would require, "On some have mercy who are in doubt. So Rev. Others, again, for ejleeite, have mercy, read ejlegcete, reprove, and render diakrinomenouv, who are contentious :" Some who are contentious rebuke. " The Rev. rendering better suits what follows.
