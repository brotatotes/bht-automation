<p>Snatching them out of the fire. The writer has in mind <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="zec+3:2">Zechariah 3:2</span>, a brand plucked from the burning. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="am+4:11">Amos 4:11</span>. </p>
<p>With fear [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εν φοβω] </span></span>. Lit., in fear; i e., of the contagion of sin while we are rescuing them. </p>
<p>Spotted [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εσπιλωμενον] </span></span>. Only here and <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="jas+3:6">James 3:6</span>. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="2pe+2:13">2 Peter 2:13</span>.</p>
