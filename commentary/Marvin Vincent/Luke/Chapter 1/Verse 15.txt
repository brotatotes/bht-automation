<p>Strong drink [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">σικερα] </span></span>. A Hebrew word, meaning any kind of intoxicating liquor not made from grapes. Wyc., sydir. </p>
<p>Even from his mother's womb. Eti, yet, still, means while yet unborn. Tynd., even in his mother's womb. Compare verse 41.</p>
