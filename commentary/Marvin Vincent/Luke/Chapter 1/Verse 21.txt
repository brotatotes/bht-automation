<p>Waited [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ην προσδοκων] </span></span>. The finite verb and participle, denoting protracted waiting. Hence, better as Rev., were waiting. Wyc., was abiding. </p>
<p>Marvelled. According to the Talmud, the priests, especially the chief priests, were accustomed to spend only a short time in the sanctuary, otherwise it was feared that they had been slain by God for unworthiness or transgression.</p>
