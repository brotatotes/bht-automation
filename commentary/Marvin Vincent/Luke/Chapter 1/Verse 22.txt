<p>They perceived [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επεγνωσαν] </span></span>. Clearly perceived. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+7:16">Matthew 7:16</span>, and verse 4. </p>
