<p>Thou that art highly favored [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κεχαριτωμενη] </span></span>. Lit., as Rev. in margin, endued with grace. Only here and <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="eph+1:6">Ephesians 1:6</span>. The rendering full of grace, Vulgate, Wyc., and Tynd., is therefore wrong. </p>
<p>All the best texts omit blessed art thou among women. </p>
<p>Cast in her mind [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">διελογιζετο] </span></span>. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="jas+2:4">James 2:4</span>. The imperfect tense, "began to reason."</p>
