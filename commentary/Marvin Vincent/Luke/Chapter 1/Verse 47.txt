<p>God my Savior [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τω θεω τω σωτηρι μου] </span></span>. Note the two articles. "The God who is the or my Savior." The title Savior is often applied to God in the Old Testament. See Septuagint, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="de+32:15">Deuteronomy 32:15</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ps+24:5">Psalms 24:5</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ps+25:5">Psalms 25:5</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ps+95:1">Psalms 95:1</span>.</p>
