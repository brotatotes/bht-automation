<p>Writing table [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πινακιδιον] </span></span>. Table was formerly used in the sense of tablet. Thus Shakspeare : </p>
<p>"Yea, from the table of my memory, I'll wipe away all trivial fond records." Hamlet, 1, 5. </p>
<p>Tynd., writing tables. The meaning is a little writing tablet, probably covered with wax. Only here in the New Testament. Used by medical writers of a physician 's note book. Wyc., has a poyntel, i e., a style for writing. </p>
<p>Wrote, saying. A Hebrew form of expression. See <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="2ki+10:6">2 Kings 10:6</span>.</p>
