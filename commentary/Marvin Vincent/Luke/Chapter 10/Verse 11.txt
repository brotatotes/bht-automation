<p>Dust [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κονιορτον] </span></span>. From koniv, dust, and ornumi, to stir up. Strictly, dust that is raised by walking. </p>
<p>Cleaveth. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+19:5">Matthew 19:5</span>. Frequent in medical language of the uniting of wounds. </p>
<p>Wipe off [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">απομασσομεθα] </span></span>. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="lu+5:2">Luke 5:2</span>. Only here in New Testament.</p>
