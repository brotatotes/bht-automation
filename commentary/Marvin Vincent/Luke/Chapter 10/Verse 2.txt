<p>The harvest [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">θερισμος] </span></span>. From qerov, summer (compare qeromai, to become warm). Harvest, that which is gathered in summer. Wyc., much ripe corn is, but few workmen. </p>
<p>Pray. See on ch. <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="lu+8:38">Luke 8:38</span>. </p>
<p>Send forth [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εκβαλη] </span></span>. Lit., drive or thrust forth, implying the urgency of the mission. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mr+1:12">Mark 1:12</span>.</p>
