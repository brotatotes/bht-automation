<p>By chance [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κατα συγκυριαν] </span></span>. Only here in New Testament. The word means, literally, a coincidence. By coincidence of circumstances. There came down. Imperfect, was going down, as Rev. </p>

<p>Passed by on the other side [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αντιπαρηλθεν] </span></span>. The verb occurs only here and ver. 32.</p>
