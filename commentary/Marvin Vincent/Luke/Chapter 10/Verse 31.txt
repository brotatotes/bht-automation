By chance [κατα συγκυριαν] . Only here in New Testament. The word means, literally, a coincidence. By coincidence of circumstances. There came down. Imperfect, was going down, as Rev. Priest. The Talmudists said that there were almost as many priests at Jericho as at Jerusalem. Passed by on the other side [αντιπαρηλθεν] . The verb occurs only here and ver. 32.
