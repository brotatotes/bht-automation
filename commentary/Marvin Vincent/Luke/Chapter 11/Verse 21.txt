<p>A strong man [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ο ισχυρος] </span></span>. It has the article : the strong man. So Rev. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+12:29">Matthew 12:29</span>. </p>
<p>Armed [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">καθωπλισμενος] </span></span>. Fully armed : down [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κατα</span></span>] from head to heel. </p>
<p>His palace [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εαυτου αυλην] </span></span>. Lit., his own. jAulh is strictly the open court in front of a house : later, the court round which the house is built, and so applied to the house generally, as our door or roof. Rev., court; for there, in the open space, commanding the doors, he would mount guard.</p>
