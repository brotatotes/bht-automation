A strong man [ο ισχυρος] . It has the article : the strong man. So Rev. See on Matthew 12:29. Armed [καθωπλισμενος] . Fully armed : down [κατα] from head to heel. His palace [εαυτου αυλην] . Lit., his own. jAulh is strictly the open court in front of a house : later, the court round which the house is built, and so applied to the house generally, as our door or roof. Rev., court; for there, in the open space, commanding the doors, he would mount guard.
