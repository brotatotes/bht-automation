<p>Shall rise up [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αναστησονται] </span></span>. This verb is also used of rising from the dead, and that is implied here; but the meaning is, shall appear as witness. Hence Rev., stand up. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+12:41">Matthew 12:41</span>. </p>
<p>Preaching [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κηρυγμα] </span></span>. The proclamation. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="2pe+2:5">2 Peter 2:5</span>.</p>
