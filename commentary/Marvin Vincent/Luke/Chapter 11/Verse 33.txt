<p>Candle. Properly, lamp. </p>
<p>Secret place [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κρυπτην] </span></span>. Rather, a cellar or crypt. which latter is the Greek word transcribed. </p>
<p>The bushel. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+5:15">Matthew 5:15</span>. </p>
<p>Candlestick. Properly stand. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+5:15">Matthew 5:15</span>. </p>
<p>Which enter in [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εισπορευομενοι] </span></span>. Better with the continuous force of the present participle, are entering in from time to time. </p>
<p>Light [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">φεγγος] </span></span>. The word occurs in only two other places : <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+24:29">Matthew 24:29</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mr+13:24">Mark 13:24</span>, on which see notes.</p>
