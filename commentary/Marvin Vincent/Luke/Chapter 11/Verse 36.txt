<p>The bright shining of a candle [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ο λυχνος τη αστραπη] </span></span>. More correctly, as Rev., the lamp with its bright shining. jAstraph means lightning : see ch. <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="lu+10:18">Luke 10:18</span>; and that is the usual meaning in classical Greek, though it occurs, rarely, of the light of a lamp. It is used here to emphasize the idea of moral illumination.</p>
