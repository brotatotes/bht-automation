Ye tithe [αποδεκατουτε] . Tithe is tenth. See on Matthew 23:23. Rue [πηγανον] . Probably from phgnumi, to make fast; because of its thick, fleshy leaves. Matthew has anise. See on 23 23. Herb [λαχανον] . See on Mark 4:32. Wyc. has wort, originally the general term for a plant. Hence colewort, liverwort, and similar words. Compare the German wurz, root or herb.
