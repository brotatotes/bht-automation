<p>Ye tithe [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αποδεκατουτε] </span></span>. Tithe is tenth. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+23:23">Matthew 23:23</span>. </p>
<p>Rue [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πηγανον] </span></span>. Probably from phgnumi, to make fast; because of its thick, fleshy leaves. Matthew has anise. See on 23 23. </p>
<p>Herb [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">λαχανον] </span></span>. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mr+4:32">Mark 4:32</span>. Wyc. has wort, originally the general term for a plant. Hence colewort, liverwort, and similar words. Compare the German wurz, root or herb.</p>
