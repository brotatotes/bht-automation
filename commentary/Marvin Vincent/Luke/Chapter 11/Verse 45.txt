<p>Reproachest [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">υβριζεις] </span></span>. The lawyer converts Jesus ' reproach (see <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mr+16:14">Mark 16:14</span>, upbraided) into an insult; the word meaning to outrage or affront. </p>
<p>Us also [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">και ημας] </span></span>. Or perhaps better, even us, the learned.</p>
