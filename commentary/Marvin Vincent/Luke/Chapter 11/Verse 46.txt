Also [και] . Emphatic. "Even or also unto you lawyers, woe." Note the article as in the address to the Pharisees (ver. 43) : You, the lawyers. Ye lade. Compare heavy laden, Matthew 11:28. Grievous to be born [δυσβαστακτα] . Only here and Matthew 23:4. Touch [προσψαυετε] . Only here in New Testament. A technical term in medicine for feeling gently a sore part of the body, or the pulse. Matthew 23:4, has kinhsao, move.
