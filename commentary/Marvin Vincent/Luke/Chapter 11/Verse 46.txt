<p>Also [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">και] </span></span>. Emphatic. "Even or also unto you lawyers, woe." Note the article as in the address to the Pharisees (ver. 43) : You, the lawyers. Ye lade. Compare heavy laden, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+11:28">Matthew 11:28</span>. </p>
<p>Grievous to be born [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δυσβαστακτα] </span></span>. Only here and <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+23:4">Matthew 23:4</span>. </p>
<p>Touch [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">προσψαυετε] </span></span>. Only here in New Testament. A technical term in medicine for feeling gently a sore part of the body, or the pulse. <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+23:4">Matthew 23:4</span>, has kinhsao, move.</p>
