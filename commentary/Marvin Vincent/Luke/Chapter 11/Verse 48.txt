<p>Ye bear witness that ye allow [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μαρτυρες εστε και συνευδοκειτε] </span></span>. Rev., more correctly, ye are witnesses and consent. The compound verb means "give your full approval." Ye think [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δοκειτε] </span></span>; favorably [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ευ] </span></span>; along with them [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">συν] </span></span>.</p>
