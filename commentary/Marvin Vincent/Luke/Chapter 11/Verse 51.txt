<p>The altar and the temple. Oikou, temple, lit., house, is equivalent to naou, sanctuary (Rev.), in <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+23:35">Matthew 23:35</span>. The altar is the altar of burnt - offering. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+4:5">Matthew 4:5</span>; and compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="2ch+24:18-21">2 Chronicles 24:18-21</span>.</p>
