<p>To urge him vehemently [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δεινως ενεχειν] </span></span>. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mr+6:19">Mark 6:19</span>. </p>
<p>Provoke to speak [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αποστοματιζειν] </span></span>. Only here is New Testament. </p>
<p>From ajpo, from, and stoma, the mouth. Originally to dictate to a pupil what he is to learn by heart. Thus Plato : "When the grammar - master dictated [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αποστοματιζοι</span></span>] to you" (" Euthydemus, " 276). Hence to catechize, with the idea of putting words into Christ 's mouth, and making him say what they wanted him to say.</p>
