<p>Importunity [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αναιδειαν] </span></span>. Only here in New Testament. A very striking word to describe persistence. Lit., shamelessness. As related to prayer, it is illustrated in the case of Abraham 's intercession for Sodom (<span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ge+18:23-33">Genesis 18:23-33</span>); and of the Syro - Phoenician woman (<span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+14:22-28">Matthew 14:22-28</span>).</p>
