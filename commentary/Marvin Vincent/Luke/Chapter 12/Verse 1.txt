<p>An innumerable multitude [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">των μυριαδων του οχλου] </span></span>. The word muriav strictly means a number of ten thousand. It is our word myriad. Hence, generally, of any countless number. </p>
<p>First of all. Many connect this with what follows : "first of all beware," etc. </p>
<p>Leaven. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+13:33">Matthew 13:33</span>. </p>
<p>Which [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ητις] </span></span>. Classifying the leaven : which belongs to the category of hypocrisy. </p>
<p>Hypocrisy. See on hypocrites, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+23:13">Matthew 23:13</span>.</p>
