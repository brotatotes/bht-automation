An innumerable multitude [των μυριαδων του οχλου] . The word muriav strictly means a number of ten thousand. It is our word myriad. Hence, generally, of any countless number. First of all. Many connect this with what follows : "first of all beware," etc. Leaven. See on Matthew 13:33. Which [ητις] . Classifying the leaven : which belongs to the category of hypocrisy. Hypocrisy. See on hypocrites, Matthew 23:13.
