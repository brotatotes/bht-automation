<p>Closets [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ταμειοις] </span></span>. The word has the same root as temnw, to cut or divide, and means an apartment where supplies are divided and apportioned : a treasury, magazine, and therefore a secret and well - guarded place. There the steward [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ταμιας] </span></span>, the distributor, has his seat. </p>
