<p>Unto you, my friends [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">υμιν τοις φιλοις μου] </span></span>. See on Pharisees and lawyers, ch. 11 43, 46. Not an address, "O my friends," but, "unto you, the friends of me." </p>
<p>Be not afraid of [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μη φοβηθητε απο] </span></span>. Lit., "fear not from;" i e., from the hands of.</p>
