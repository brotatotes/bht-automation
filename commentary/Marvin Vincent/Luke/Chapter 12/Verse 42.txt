<p>That faithful and wise steward. Lit., that faithful steward, the wise man. </p>
<p>Household [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">θεραπειας] </span></span>. From its original meaning of waiting on, attendance (<span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="lu+9:11">Luke 9:11</span>), it comes to mean the retinue of attendants; the body of household servants. </p>
<p>Portion of meat [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">σιτομετριον] </span></span>. Lit., measure of food. </p>
<p>In due season. At the appointed time for distributing rations. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+24:45">Matthew 24:45</span>.</p>
