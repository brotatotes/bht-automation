That faithful and wise steward. Lit., that faithful steward, the wise man. Household [θεραπειας] . From its original meaning of waiting on, attendance (Luke 9:11), it comes to mean the retinue of attendants; the body of household servants. Portion of meat [σιτομετριον] . Lit., measure of food. In due season. At the appointed time for distributing rations. See on Matthew 24:45.
