<p>Discern [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δοκιμαζειν] </span></span>. See on trial and tried, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1pe+1:7">1 Peter 1:7</span>. It means here test or prove. You can test and prove the weather by your signs; but you cannot apply the proof which lies in the signs of the times. Rev., interpret, gives the idea. Wyc., prove.</p>
