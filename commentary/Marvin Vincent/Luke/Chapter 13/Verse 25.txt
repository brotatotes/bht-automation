<p>When once [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αφ ου] </span></span>. Lit., from the time that. Compare ver. 7. Some editors connect this with the previous sentence : "Shall not be able when once," etc. </p>
<p>Whence [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ποθεν] </span></span>. Of what family. Ye do not belong to my household. See <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="joh+7:27">John 7:27</span> : "We know whence he (Jesus) is;" i e., we know his birthplace and family.</p>
