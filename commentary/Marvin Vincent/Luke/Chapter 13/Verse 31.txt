<p>Day. The best texts read hour. </p>
<p>Will kill [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">θελει αποκτειναι] </span></span>. As in so many cases the A. V. renders as the future of the verb to kill; whereas there are two distinct verbs; to will or determine, and to kill. The meaning is, Herod willeth or is determined to kill thee. Rev., would fain, seems rather feeble.</p>
