<p>That fox. Herod. Describing his cunning and cowardice. </p>
<p>Cures [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ιασεις] </span></span>. Used by Luke only. </p>
<p>I shall be perfected [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τελειουμαι] </span></span>. The present tense : "the present of the certain future" (Meyer). The meaning is, I come to an end : I have done. Expositors differ greatly. Some interpret, "I end my career of healing," etc.; others, my life.</p>
