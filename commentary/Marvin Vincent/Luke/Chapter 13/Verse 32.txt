That fox. Herod. Describing his cunning and cowardice. Cures [ιασεις] . Used by Luke only. I shall be perfected [τελειουμαι] . The present tense : "the present of the certain future" (Meyer). The meaning is, I come to an end : I have done. Expositors differ greatly. Some interpret, "I end my career of healing," etc.; others, my life.
