Make excuse [παραιτεισθαι] . Also rendered in New Testament refuse, Hebrews 12:19, Hebrews 12:25, where both meanings occur. See also 2 Timothy 2:23, Rev. Our phrase, beg off, expresses the idea here. I must needs [εχω αναγκην] . Lit., I have necessity : a strong expression. Go [εξελθειν] . Go out [εξ] from the city.
