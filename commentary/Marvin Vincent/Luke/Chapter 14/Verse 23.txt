<p>Hedges [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">φραγμους] </span></span>. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+21:33">Matthew 21:33</span>. It may mean either a hedge, or a place enclosed with a hedge. Here the hedges beside which vagrants rest. </p>
<p>Compel. Compare constrained, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+14:22">Matthew 14:22</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+26:11">Acts 26:11</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ga+6:12">Galatians 6:12</span>. Not to use force, but to constrain them against the reluctance which such poor creatures would feel at accepting the invitation of a great Lord. May be filled [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">γεμισθη] </span></span>. A very strong word; properly of loading a ship. "Nature and grace alike abhor a vacuum" (Bengel).</p>
