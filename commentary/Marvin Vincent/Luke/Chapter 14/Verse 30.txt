<p>This man [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ουτος ο ανθρωπος] </span></span>. With sarcastic emphasis. </p>
<p>Was not able [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ουκ ισχυσεν] </span></span>. From ijscuv, strength. See on power, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="2pe+2:11">2 Peter 2:11</span>. To be strong in body or in resources, and so to be worth, as Lat., valere. "This man was not worth enough, or was not good for the completion." In this latter sense, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+5:13">Matthew 5:13</span>, "good for nothing."</p>
