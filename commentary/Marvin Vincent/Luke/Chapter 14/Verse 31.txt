<p>To make war against another king [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ετερω βασιλει συμβαλειν εις πολεμον] </span></span>. Lit., to come together with another king for war. So Rev., to encounter another king in war. </p>
<p>"Out he flashed, And into such a song, such fire for fame, Such trumpet - blowings in it, coming down To such a stern and iron - clashing close, That when he stopped we longed to hurl together." TENNYSON, Idyls of the King. </p>
<p>With ten thousand (ejn deka ciliasin). Lit., in ten thousands : i e., in the midst of; surrounded by. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="jude+1:14">Jude 1:14</span>.</p>
