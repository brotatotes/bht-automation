<p>They chose. Imperfect : were choosing. Something going on before his eyes. </p>
<p>The chief seats. Or couches. The Greek writers refer to the absurd contentions which sometimes arose for the chief seats at table. </p>
<p>Theophrastus designates one who thrusts himself into the place next the host as mikrofilotimov, one who seeks petty distinctions.</p>
