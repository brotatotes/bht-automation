<p>To his servants. Bond - servants. There is a fine touch in throwing in the bond - servants immediately after thy son (ver. 21). </p>
<p>Bring forth. Some texts add quickly [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ταχυ] </span></span>. So Rev. </p>
<p>The best robe [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">στολην την πρωτην] </span></span>. Lit., a robe, the first. Properly of a long, flowing robe, a festive garment. See <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mr+16:5">Mark 16:5</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="lu+20:46">Luke 20:46</span>. Ring. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="jas+2:2">James 2:2</span>. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ge+41:42">Genesis 41:42</span>. </p>
<p>Shoes. Both the ring and the shoes are marks of a free man. Slaves went barefoot.</p>
