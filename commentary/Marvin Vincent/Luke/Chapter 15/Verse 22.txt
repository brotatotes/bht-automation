To his servants. Bond - servants. There is a fine touch in throwing in the bond - servants immediately after thy son (ver. 21). Bring forth. Some texts add quickly [ταχυ] . So Rev. The best robe [στολην την πρωτην] . Lit., a robe, the first. Properly of a long, flowing robe, a festive garment. See Mark 16:5; Luke 20:46. Ring. See on James 2:2. Compare Genesis 41:42. Shoes. Both the ring and the shoes are marks of a free man. Slaves went barefoot.
