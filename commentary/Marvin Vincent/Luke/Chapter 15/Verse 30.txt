This thy son. Not my brother, but with the bitterest sarcasm. Was come [ηλθεν] . He says came, as of a stranger. Not returned. Devoured [καταφαγων] . We say "eat up;" the Greek said "eat down" [κατα] . The word is suggested, no doubt, by the mention of the calf, the kid, and the feasting. ===Luke 16:0 CHAPTER XVI THE PARABLE OF THE UNJUST STEWARD.
