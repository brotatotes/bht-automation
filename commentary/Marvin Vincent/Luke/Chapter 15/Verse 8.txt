<p>Pieces of silver [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δραχμας] </span></span>. Used by Luke only. A coin worth about eighteen cents, commonly with the image of an owl, a tortoise, or a head of Pallas. As a weight, 65. 5 grains. A common weight in dispensing medicines and writing prescriptions. Wyc., transcribing the Greek word, dragmes. Tynd., grotes.</p>
