<p>How is it that I hear this [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τι τουτο ακουω] </span></span>? Better as Rev., What is this that I hear? </p>
<p>Give an account [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αποδος τον λογον] </span></span>. Lit., "give back" [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">απο] </span></span>. Rev., render. The [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τον</span></span>] account which is due. Aristophanes has a striking parallel : "And now give back my signet; for thou shalt no longer be my steward" (" Knights, " 947). </p>
<p>Thou mayest [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δυνηση] </span></span>. More strictly, as Rev., thou canst.</p>
