Son [τεκνον] . Lit., child. Receivedst [απελαβες] . Received back [απο] as a reward or quittance. Compare ch. Luke 6:34; Luke 18:30; Luke 23:41. Gulf [χασμα] . From caskw, to yawn. Transcribed into the English chasm. In medical language, of the cavities in a wound or ulcer. Is fixed [εστηρικται] . Compare ch. 22 32; and see on 1 Peter 5:10.
