<p>It were better [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">λυσιτελει] </span></span>. Only here in New Testament. The verb means to pay what is due, and is equivalent to our phrase, it pays. </p>
<p>Millstone. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+18:6">Matthew 18:6</span>. The correct reading here is liqov mulikov, a millstone; not a great millstone as Matthew. </p>
<p>Thrown [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ερριπται] </span></span>. Hurled : with an underlying sense of violence, called out by so great an outrage.</p>
