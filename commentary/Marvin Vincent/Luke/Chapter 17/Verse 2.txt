It were better [λυσιτελει] . Only here in New Testament. The verb means to pay what is due, and is equivalent to our phrase, it pays. Millstone. Compare Matthew 18:6. The correct reading here is liqov mulikov, a millstone; not a great millstone as Matthew. Thrown [ερριπται] . Hurled : with an underlying sense of violence, called out by so great an outrage.
