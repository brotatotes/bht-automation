<p>Shall preserve [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ζωογονησει] </span></span>. Only here and <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+7:19">Acts 7:19</span>. Originally to engender; thence to produce alive or endue with life, and so to preserve alive. Wyc., shall quicken it.</p>
