Standing [εστως] . In a timid attitude : merely standing, not posturing as the Pharisee. See on ver. 11. Afar off. Some explain, from the sanctuary; others, from the Pharisee. Lift up his eyes. As worshippers ordinarily. Be merciful [ιλασθητι] . Lit., be propitiated. A sinner [τω αμαρτωλω] . With the definite article, "the sinner." " He thinks about no other man " (Bengel). 15 - 17. Compare Matthew 19:13-15; Mark 10:13-16.
