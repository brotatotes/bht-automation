<p>Standing [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εστως] </span></span>. In a timid attitude : merely standing, not posturing as the Pharisee. See on ver. 11. </p>
<p>Afar off. Some explain, from the sanctuary; others, from the Pharisee. Lift up his eyes. As worshippers ordinarily. </p>
<p>Be merciful [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ιλασθητι] </span></span>. Lit., be propitiated. </p>
<p>A sinner [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τω αμαρτωλω] </span></span>. With the definite article, "the sinner." " He thinks about no other man " (Bengel). </p>
<p>15 - 17. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+19:13-15">Matthew 19:13-15</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mr+10:13-16">Mark 10:13-16</span>.</p>
