<p>Despised [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εξουθενουντας] </span></span>. Lit., made nothing of. Rev., set at nought. Others [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τους λοιπους] </span></span>. The expression is stronger. Lit., the rest. They threw all others beside themselves into one class. Rev., correctly, all others.</p>
