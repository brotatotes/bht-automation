<p>Appear [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αναφαινεσθαι] </span></span>. Only here and <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+21:3">Acts 21:3</span>. It means to be brought to light; shown forth. The common phrase show up [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ανα</span></span>] represents it.</p>
