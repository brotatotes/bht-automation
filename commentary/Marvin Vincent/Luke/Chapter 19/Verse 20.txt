<p>I kept [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ειχον] </span></span>. The imperfect. I was keeping while thou wert absent. Napkin [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">σουδαριω] </span></span>. The Latin sudarium, from sudor, perspiration : a cloth for wiping off the sweat. Trench notes that the napkin which the idle servant does not need for its proper use (<span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ge+3:19">Genesis 3:19</span>) he uses for the wrapping up of his pound.</p>
