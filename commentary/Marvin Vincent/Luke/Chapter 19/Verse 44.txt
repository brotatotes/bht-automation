<p>Lay thee even with the ground (ejdafiousin). Only here in New Testament. Primarily, to beat level, like a threshing - floor or pavement. The Septuagint uses it in the sense of dashing down to the ground (<span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ps+137:9">Psalms 137:9</span>, and elsewhere). So Rev., from the succeeding reference to the children, and in allusion to the Psalm. </p>
