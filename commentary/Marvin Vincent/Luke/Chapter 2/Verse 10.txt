<p>I bring you good tidings of great joy [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ευαγγελιζομαι υμιν χαραν μεγαλην] </span></span>. Wyc. is strictly literal : I evangelize to you a great joy. </p>
<p>Which [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ητις] </span></span>. Of a class or character which, etc. </p>
<p>People (tw law). Rev., rightly, "the people;" the article pointing specially to the people of Israel.</p>
