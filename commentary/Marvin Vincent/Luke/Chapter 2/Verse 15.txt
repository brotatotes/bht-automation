<p>The shepherds. Some texts add oiJ anqrwpoi, the men; but the latter texts omit. </p>
<p>Let us go (die lqwmen). The preposition dia, through, implies through the intervening space. </p>
<p>Thing [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ρημα] </span></span>. See on chapter <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="lu+1:37">Luke 1:37</span>. The utterance of the shepherds contains a climax : "Let us go and see this saying, which has come to pass; which the Lord made known."</p>
