The shepherds. Some texts add oiJ anqrwpoi, the men; but the latter texts omit. Let us go (die lqwmen). The preposition dia, through, implies through the intervening space. Thing [ρημα] . See on chapter Luke 1:37. The utterance of the shepherds contains a climax : "Let us go and see this saying, which has come to pass; which the Lord made known."
