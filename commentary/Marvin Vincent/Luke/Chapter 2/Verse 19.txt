<p>Kept [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">συνετηρει] </span></span>. See on the simple verb threw, on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1pe+1:4">1 Peter 1:4</span>. The word signifies not merely to guard, but to keep, as the result of guarding. Hence the compound verb is very expressive : kept, sun, with or within herself : closely. Note the imperfect tense : was keeping all the while. Pondered [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">συμβαλλουσα] </span></span>. The present participle, pondering. Lit., bringing together : comparing and weighing facts. Wyc., bearing together in her heart. Vulg., conferens. Compare Sophocles, "Oedipus Coloneus," 1472 - 4. </p>
