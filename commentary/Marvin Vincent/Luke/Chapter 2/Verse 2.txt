<p>And this taxing was first made [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αυτη η απογραφη πρωτη εγενετο] </span></span>. Rather, this occurred as the first enrolment; or, as Rev., this was the first enrolment made; with reference to a second enrolment which took place about eleven years later, and is referred to in <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+5:37">Acts 5:37</span>.</p>
