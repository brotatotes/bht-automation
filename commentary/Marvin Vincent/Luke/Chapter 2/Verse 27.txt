<p>By the Spirit [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εν τω πνευματι] </span></span>. Lit., as Rev., "in the Spirit :" the Holy Spirit prompting him. Indicating rather his spiritual condition, as one who walked with God, than a special divine impulse. </p>
<p>After the custom [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κατα το ειθισμενον] </span></span>. Lit., according to that which was wont to be done. Only here in New Testament; and the kindred words, eqov, custom, and eqw, to be accustomed, occur more frequently in Luke than elsewhere. Very common in medical writings.</p>
