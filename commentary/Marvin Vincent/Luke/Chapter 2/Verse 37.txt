Of about fourscore and four years [ως ετων ογδοηκοντα τεσσαρων] . The A. V. might be supposed to be stating her age; but the best texts read ewv, until, instead of wv, about; and the statement refers to the time of her widowhood; a widow even for (or up to) fourscore and four years. So Rev. Served [λατρευουσα] . The present participle, serving. Rev., worshipping. See on chapter Luke 1:74.
