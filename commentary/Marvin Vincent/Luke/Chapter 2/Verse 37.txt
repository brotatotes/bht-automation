<p>Of about fourscore and four years [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ως ετων ογδοηκοντα τεσσαρων] </span></span>. The A. V. might be supposed to be stating her age; but the best texts read ewv, until, instead of wv, about; and the statement refers to the time of her widowhood; a widow even for (or up to) fourscore and four years. So Rev. </p>
<p>Served [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">λατρευουσα] </span></span>. The present participle, serving. Rev., worshipping. See on chapter <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="lu+1:74">Luke 1:74</span>.</p>
