<p>Understanding [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">συνεσει] </span></span>. From sunihmi, to bring together. Hence that quality of mind which combines : understanding not only of facts, but of facts in their mutual relations. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mr+12:33">Mark 12:33</span>; where there is meant "the love of a well pondered and duly considered resolution which determines the whole person; the love which clearly understands itself" [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">χρεμερ] </span></span>.</p>
