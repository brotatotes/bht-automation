To be taxed with Mary. We may read either, went up with Mary, denoting merely the fact of her accompanying him; or, to enroll himself with Mary, implying that both their names must be registered. Espoused. Not merely betrothed. See Matthew 1:20, Matthew 1:24, Matthew 1:25; also on Matthew 1:18. Great with child [εγκυω] . See on Chapter Luke 1:24. Only here in New Testament.
