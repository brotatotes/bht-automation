<p>Watched. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mr+3:2">Mark 3:2</span>. </p>
<p>Spies [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εγκαθετους] </span></span>. Only here in New Testament. From ejgkaqihmi, to send in, as a garrison into a city. Hence of persons sent in for the purpose of espionage. </p>
<p>Which should feign [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">υποκρινομενους] </span></span>. Lit., feigning. Rev., which feigned. Only here in New Testament. See on hypocrites, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+23:13">Matthew 23:13</span>. The power and authority [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τη αρχη και τη εξουσια] </span></span>. The former, the Roman power in general; the latter, the specific authority of the official.</p>
