<p>Shewed [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εμηνυσεν] </span></span>. Originally to disclose something secret. Hence, generally, to make known. </p>
<p>At the bush [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επι της βατου] </span></span>. Wrong. Render as Rev., in the place concerning the bush. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mr+12:26">Mark 12:26</span>. </p>
<p>41 - 44. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+22:41-46">Matthew 22:41-46</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mr+12:35-37">Mark 12:35-37</span>.</p>
