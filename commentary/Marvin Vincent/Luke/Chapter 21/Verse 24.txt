<p>Edge [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">στοματι] </span></span>. Lit., the mouth. So Wyc. Either in the sense of the foremost part, or picturing the sword as a devouring monster. In <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="heb+11:33">Hebrews 11:33</span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="heb+11:34">Hebrews 11:34</span>, the word is used in both senses : "the mouths of lions;" " the edge of the sword. " </p>
<p>Led away captive. See on captives, ch. 4 18. </p>
<p>Trodden down. Denoting the oppression and contempt which shall follow conquest.</p>
