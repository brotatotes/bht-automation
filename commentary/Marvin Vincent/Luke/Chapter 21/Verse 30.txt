<p>Ye see [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">βλεποντες] </span></span>. Lit., "looking, ye know," etc. Implying careful observation, with a view to determine the progress of the season. </p>
<p>Know [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">γινωσκετε] </span></span>. Perceive would be better.</p>
