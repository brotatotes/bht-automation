<p>Betrayeth [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">παραδιδοντος] </span></span>. The present participle : is now engaged in betraying. </p>
<p>With me. "He does not say with you : thus separating the traitor from the rest of the disciples, and showing that now he alone has to do with that wretch, as with an enemy" (Bengel).</p>
