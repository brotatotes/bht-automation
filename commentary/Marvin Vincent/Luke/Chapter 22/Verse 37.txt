Have an end [τελος εχει] . The phrase is synonymous with be accomplished telesqhnai, Rev., fulfilled). In classical Greek this latter word is often used of the fulfilment of an oracle : also of things which are settled beyond controversy. The two expressions here give the two meanings. The prophecy is fulfilled; the things concerning me are finally settled. 39 - 46. Compare Matthew 26:30, Matthew 26:36-46; Mark 14:26, Mark 14:32-42.
