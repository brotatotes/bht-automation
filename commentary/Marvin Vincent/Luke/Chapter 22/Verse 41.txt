<p>Was withdrawn [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">απεσπασθη] </span></span>. The Vulgate has avulsus est, "he was torn away," as by an inward urgency. Godet adopts this view, and so, apparently, Wyc., he was taken away. Meyer inclines to it; De Wette decidedly rejects it. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+21:1">Acts 21:1</span>. </p>
<p>Prayed. Imperfect, began to pray.</p>
