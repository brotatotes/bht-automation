There appeared [ωφθη] . The word most commonly used in the New Testament of seeing visions. See Matthew 17:3; Mark 9:4; Luke 1:11; Luke 22:43; Acts 2:17; Acts 7:35. The kindred noun ojptasia, wherever it occurs in the New Testament, means a vision. See Luke 1:22; Luke 24:23, etc. Strengthening [ενισχυων] . Only here and Acts 9:19. See on was not able, ch. 14 30; and cannot, ch. 16 3. Commonly intransitive; to prevail in or among. Used transitively only by Hippocrates and Luke.
