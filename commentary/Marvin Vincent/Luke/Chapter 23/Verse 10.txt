<p>Vehemently [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ευτονως] </span></span>. Only here and <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+18:28">Acts 18:28</span>, of the preaching of Apollos. Originally the word means well - strung; hence, in medical language, of a well - toned body.</p>
