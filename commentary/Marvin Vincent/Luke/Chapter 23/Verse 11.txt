<p>Gorgeous [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">λαμπραν] </span></span>. Lit., bright or brilliant. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+10:30">Acts 10:30</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="re+14:6">Revelation 14:6</span>. Wyc. and Tynd., white. Mark has purple (porfuran), and Matthew scarlet [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κοκκινην] </span></span>. </p>
<p>Apparel [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εδθητα] </span></span>. The general term for raiment. Matthew specifies the garment (xxvii. 28). Mark has simply purple (xv. 17). </p>
