<p>Perverteth [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αποστρεφοντα] </span></span>. Another compound of strefw, to turn; diastrefonta is rendered by the same word in ver. 2. Probably the words are used without any intentional distinction of meaning. </p>
<p>Diastrefonta implies more of the idea of distraction (compare Wyc., turning upside down); turning different ways; while ajpostrefonta emphasizes the turning away [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">απο</span></span>] of the people from their civil and religious allegiance. So Wyc., turning away. </p>
<p>Examined [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ανακρινας] </span></span>. Originally implying a thorough examination; ajna, up, from bottom to top. Technically, of a legal examination.</p>
