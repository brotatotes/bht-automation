<p>Were the more fierce [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επισχυον] </span></span>. Only here in New Testament. The verb means, literally, to grow strong. See on ch. <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="lu+14:30">Luke 14:30</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="lu+16:3">Luke 16:3</span>. Here the sense is, they were more energetic and emphatic. Rev., urgent. Wyc., waxed stronger. </p>
<p>Stirreth up [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ανασειει] </span></span>. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mr+14:11">Mark 14:11</span>. The increased urgency is shown by the use of a stronger word than perverteth (ver. 2).</p>
