<p>Consented [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">συγκατατεθειμενος] </span></span>. Only here in New Testament. </p>
<p>Another of Luke's numerous compounds. the Greek student will be struck with the array of compounds, from ver. 49 to 56, inclusive. The verb means to put [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τιθημι] </span></span>, down [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κατα] </span></span>, along with [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">συν] </span></span>. Hence to put down the same vote or opinion with another : to agree with or assent to.</p>
