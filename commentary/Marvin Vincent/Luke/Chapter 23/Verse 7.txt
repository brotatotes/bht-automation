<p>Sent [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ανεπεμψεν] </span></span>. Lit., sent him up [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ανα] </span></span>. Used of sending up to a higher court. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+25:21">Acts 25:21</span>, of sending Paul to Caesar. It also means to send back, as in ver. 11, and <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="php+1:11">Philippians 1:11</span>.</p>
