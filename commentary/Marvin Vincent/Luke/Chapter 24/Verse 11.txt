<p>To them [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ενωπιον αυτων] </span></span>. Rev., literally, in their sight. </p>
<p>Idle tales [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ληρος] </span></span>. Lit., silly talk; nonsense. Only here in New Testament. Used in medical language of the wild talk of delirium. Wyc., madness. Tynd., feigned things.</p>
