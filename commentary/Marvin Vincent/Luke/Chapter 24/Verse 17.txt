<p>Ye have [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αντιβαλλετε] </span></span>. Lit., throw back and forth; exchange. </p>
<p>"Discussed a doubt and tossed it to and fro" (Tennyson). </p>
<p>And are sad [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">σκυθρωποι] </span></span>. Only here and <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+6:16">Matthew 6:16</span>, on which see note. The best texts put the interrogation point after walk, add kai ejstaqhsan, and render, and they stood still, looking sad. So Rev.</p>
