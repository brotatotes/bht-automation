<p>Made us astonished [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εξεστησαν] </span></span>. Literally the verb means to put out of place; and so, to drive one out of his senses. Hence the A. V. is feeble. Rev., better, amazed us. </p>
<p>Early [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ορθριναι] </span></span>. Lit., early ones. Only here and <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="re+22:16">Revelation 22:16</span>. Compare orqrov, dawn, ver. 1.</p>
