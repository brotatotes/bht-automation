<p>Ought not [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ουχι εδει] </span></span>. The A. V. does not convey the precise meaning, which is, that, in the eternal order of things, and in fulfilment of the eternal counsel of God as expressed in the prophecies, it was essentially fitting that Christ should suffer. Rev. is clumsy but correct : beloved it not the Christ to suffer ?</p>
