<p>One mightier [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ο ισχυροτερος] </span></span>. The definite article points to an expected personage. Hence better as Rev., he that is mightier. </p>
<p>Unloose [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">λυσαι] </span></span>. So also Mark; but Matthew bastasai, to bear. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+3:11">Matthew 3:11</span>.</p>
