Being reproved [ελεγχομενος] . See on James 2:9. Evils [πονηρων] . Of several words in the New Testament denoting evil, this emphasizes evil in its activity. Hence Satan is oJ ponhrov, the evil one. An evil eye (Mark 7:22) is a mischief working eye. See on Mark 7:22. Added [προσεθηκεν] . Used by Luke twice as often as in all the rest of the New Testament. A very common medical word, used of the application of remedies to the body, as our apply, administer. So Hippocrates, "apply wet sponges to the head;" and Galen, "apply a decoction of acorns," etc. In prison. See on Matthew 14:3. 21 - 23. Compare Matthew 3:13-17; Mark 1:9-11.
