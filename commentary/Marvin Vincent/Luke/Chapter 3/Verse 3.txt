The country about Jordan. Which both Matthew and Mark call the wilderness. See on Matthew 3:1. Baptism of repentance. Wyc., penaunce. For [εις] . Better as Rev., unto, denoting the destination of the rite. Remission [αφεσιν] . See on James 5:15. The word occurs in Luke more frequently than in all other New Testament writers combined. Used in medical language of the relaxation of disease. Both Luke and John use the kindred verb ajfihmi, in the same sense. Luke 4:39; John 4:52.
