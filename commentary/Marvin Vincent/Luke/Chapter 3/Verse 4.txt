<p>Isaiah. In this prophetic citation Mark adds to Isaiah <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mal+3:1">Malachi 3:1</span>, which does not appear in either Matthew or Luke. Luke adds vv. 4, 5 of <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="isa+40:0">Isaiah 40:0</span>, which do not appear in the others. </p>
