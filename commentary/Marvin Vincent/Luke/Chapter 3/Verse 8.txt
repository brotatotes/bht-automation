<p>Fruits [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">καρπους] </span></span>. Matthew has the singular number, karpon, friut. Repentance [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">της μετανοιας] </span></span>. Note the article : the repentance which you profess in coming to my baptism. Rev., in margin, "your repentance." See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+3:2">Matthew 3:2</span>. </p>
<p>Begin. With the first accusing of your conscience. "He anticipates even attempt at excuse" (Bengel). Matthew has think not, indicating a delusive fancy. </p>
<p>Father. The word stands first in the sentence, "We have Abraham to our father," and is therefore emphatic, and with reason; for it was on their descent that the answer of these Jews to John's rebuke turned : " Our father is Abraham. </p>
<p>These stones. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+3:9">Matthew 3:9</span>.</p>
