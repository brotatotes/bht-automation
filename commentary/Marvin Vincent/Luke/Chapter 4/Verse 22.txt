Bare him witness. Compare verse 14. They confirmed the reports which had been circulated about him. Note the imperfect tense. There was a continuous stream of admiring comment. Similarly, were wondering. At the gracious words [λογοις της χαριτος] . Literally and correctly, as Rev., words of grace. See on chapter Luke 1:30. Is not [ουχι] . Expecting an affirmative answer.
