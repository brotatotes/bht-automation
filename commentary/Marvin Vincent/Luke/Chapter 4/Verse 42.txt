Sought after [επεζητουν] . Imperfect tense : were seeking. Came unto him [ηλθον εως αυτου] . Stronger than came to; for ewv is even up to, showing that they did not discontinue their search until they found him. Mark's narrative here is fuller and more graphic. ===Luke 5:0 CHAPTER V 1 - 11. Compare Matthew 4:18-22; Mark 1:16-20.
