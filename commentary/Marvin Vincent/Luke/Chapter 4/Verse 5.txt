<p>In a moment of time [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εν στιγμη χρονου] </span></span>. Peculiar to Luke. Stigmh is literally a mark made by a pointed instrument, a dot : hence a point of time. Only here in New Testament. Comapre stigmata, brand - marks, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ga+6:17">Galatians 6:17</span>. Tynd., in the twinkling of an eye.</p>
