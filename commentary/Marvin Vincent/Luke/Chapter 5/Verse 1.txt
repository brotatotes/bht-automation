<p>Pressed [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επικεισθαι] </span></span>. Lit., were laid upon. </p>
<p>To hear. The A. V. is correct according to the reading tou ajkouein, which it follows. The true reading is kai ajkouein, and heard. So Rev. </p>
<p>He stood [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αυτος ην εστως] </span></span>. The pronoun distinguishes him from the crowd which pressed upon him : he on his part stood. Render the participle and finite verb as Rev., was standing. </p>
<p>Lake [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">λιμνην] </span></span>. An illustration of the more classical style of Luke as compared with Matthew and Mark. They and John also use qalassa, sea. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+4:18">Matthew 4:18</span>.</p>
