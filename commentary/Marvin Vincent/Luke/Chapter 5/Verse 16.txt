<p>Withdrew [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ην υποχωρων] </span></span>. The participle with the imperfect of the finite verb denoting something in progress, and thus corresponding to the imperfect in verse 15. The multitudes were coming together, but he was engaged in retirement and prayer, so that he was inaccessible. The word occurs only in Luke, the usual New Testament word for withdraw being ajnacwrew. See <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+2:12">Matthew 2:12</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+12:15">Matthew 12:15</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mr+3:7">Mark 3:7</span>. </p>
