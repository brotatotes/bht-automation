They were all amazed [εκστασις ελαβεν απαντας] . Lit., amazement took hold on all, as Rev. On ekstasiv, amazement, see on Mark 5:42. Strange things [παραδοξα] . From para, contrary to, and doxa, opinion. Something contrary to received opinion, and hence strange. Compare the English paradox. Only here in New Testament. 27, 28. Compare Matthew 9:9; Mark 2:13, Mark 2:14.
