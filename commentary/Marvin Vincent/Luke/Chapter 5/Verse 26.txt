<p>They were all amazed [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εκστασις ελαβεν απαντας] </span></span>. Lit., amazement took hold on all, as Rev. On ekstasiv, amazement, see on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mr+5:42">Mark 5:42</span>. </p>
<p>Strange things [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">παραδοξα] </span></span>. From para, contrary to, and doxa, opinion. Something contrary to received opinion, and hence strange. </p>
<p>Compare the English paradox. Only here in New Testament. </p>
<p>27, 28. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+9:9">Matthew 9:9</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mr+2:13">Mark 2:13</span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mr+2:14">Mark 2:14</span>.</p>
