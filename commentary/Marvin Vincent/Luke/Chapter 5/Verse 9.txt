He was astonished [θαμβος περιεσχεν αυτον] . Lit., amazement encompassed him. See on 1 Peter 2:6. The draught [τη αγρα] . The word is used both of the act of catching and of that which is caught. In verse 4 it has the former sense : "let down your net for catching :" here, the latter, the catch or haul.
