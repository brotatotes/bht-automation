<p>He was astonished [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">θαμβος περιεσχεν αυτον] </span></span>. Lit., amazement encompassed him. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1pe+2:6">1 Peter 2:6</span>. </p>
<p>The draught [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τη αγρα] </span></span>. The word is used both of the act of catching and of that which is caught. In verse 4 it has the former sense : "let down your net for catching :" here, the latter, the catch or haul.</p>
