<p>They were filled with madness. Peculiar to Luke. </p>
<p>Anoia, madness, is, properly, want of understanding. The word thus implies senseless rage, as distinguished from intelligent indignation. </p>
<p>12 - 16. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+10:2-4">Matthew 10:2-4</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mr+3:13-19">Mark 3:13-19</span>.</p>
