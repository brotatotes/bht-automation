<p>A mountain [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">το ορος] </span></span>. The article denotes a familiar place. Rev., rightly, the mountain. </p>
<p>Continued all night [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ην διανυκτερευων] </span></span>. Only here in New Testament. Used in medical language. The all night prayer is peculiar to Luke's narrative.</p>
