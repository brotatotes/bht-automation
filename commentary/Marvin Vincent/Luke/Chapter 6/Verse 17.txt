<p>In the plain [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επι τοπου πεδινου] </span></span>. There is no article. More literally, and better, as Rev., in a plain or level place. There is a discrepancy in the two narratives. Matthew says he went up into the mountain and sat down. Vv. 17 - 19 are peculiar to Luke. </p>
