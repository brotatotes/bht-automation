<p>Cheek [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">σιαγονα] </span></span>. Lit., the jaw. The cheek is pareia. The blow intended is not, therefore, a mere slap, but a heavy blow; an act of violence rather than of contempt. </p>
<p>Taketh away [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αιροντος] </span></span>. Lit., taketh up, lifteth. </p>
