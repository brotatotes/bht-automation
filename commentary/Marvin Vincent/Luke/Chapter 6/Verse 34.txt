Lend [δανειζετε] . Properly, at interest. Sinners [οι αμαρτωλοι] . The article marks them as a class. So, often in New Testament, as when classed with publicans. Love. Not filousi, which implies an instinctive, affectionate attachment, but ajgapwsin, of a sentiment based on judgment and calculation, which selects its object for a reason. See, farther, on John 21:15-17. Tynd., the very sinners love their lovers.
