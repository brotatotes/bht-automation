<p>Lend [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δανειζετε] </span></span>. Properly, at interest. </p>
<p>Sinners [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">οι αμαρτωλοι] </span></span>. The article marks them as a class. So, often in New Testament, as when classed with publicans. </p>
<p>Love. Not filousi, which implies an instinctive, affectionate attachment, but ajgapwsin, of a sentiment based on judgment and calculation, which selects its object for a reason. See, farther, on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="joh+21:15-17">John 21:15-17</span>. Tynd., the very sinners love their lovers.</p>
