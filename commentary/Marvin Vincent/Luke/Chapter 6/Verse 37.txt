<p>Forgive [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">απολυετε] </span></span>. Lit., release. So Rev., Christ exhorts to the opposite of what he has just forbidden : "do not condemn, but release." Compare chapter <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="lu+22:68">Luke 22:68</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="lu+23:16">Luke 23:16</span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="lu+23:17">Luke 23:17</span>.</p>
