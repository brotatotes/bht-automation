<p>Can the blind [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μητι δυναται τυφλος] </span></span>? The interrogative particle expects a negative reply. Surely the blind cannot, etc. </p>
<p>Lead [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">οδηγειν] </span></span>. Better, guide, as Rev., since the word combines the ideas of leading and instructing. </p>
<p>Shall they not [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ουχι] </span></span>? Another interrogative paricle, this time expecting an affirmative answer.</p>
