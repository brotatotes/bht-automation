<p>They watched [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">παρετηρουντο] </span></span>. Imperfect. They kept watching. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mr+3:2">Mark 3:2</span>. </p>
<p>He would heal [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">θεραπευσει] </span></span>. So Rev. Some authorities, however, read qerapeuei, "whether he is healing." This may mean either "whether it is his habit to heal," which is far - fetched, or "whether he is actually healing." </p>
<p>Find. Peculiar to Luke, and emphasizing the eagerness of the Pharisees to discover a ground of accusation.</p>
