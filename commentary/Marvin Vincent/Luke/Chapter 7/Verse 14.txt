<p>Touched. Not fearing the ceremonial defilement of contact with the dead. </p>
<p>The bier [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">σορος] </span></span>. In classical Greek, originally, of a vessel for holding anything : sometimes of a cinerary urn. Here the open bier. Edersheim says "of wicker - work."</p>
