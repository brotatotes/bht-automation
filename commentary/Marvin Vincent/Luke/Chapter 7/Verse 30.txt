<p>Lawyers [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">νομικοι] </span></span>. Not legal practitioners, but interpreters and doctors of the Mosaic law. </p>
<p>Rejected [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ηθετησαν] </span></span>. Set aside, or annulled; made it vain through their disobedience. </p>
<p>Against themselves [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εισ εαυτους] </span></span>. More strictly, with reference to themselves.</p>
