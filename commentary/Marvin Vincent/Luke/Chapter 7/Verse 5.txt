<p>He hath built [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αυτος ωκοδομησεν] </span></span>. He is emphatic; himself, at his own expense. </p>
<p>A synagogue [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">την συναγωγην] </span></span>. The article, "the synagogue," marks the particular synagogue which these elders represented. Hence Rev., rightly, "our synagogue." " He did not merely avoid profaning the synagogue " (Bengel).</p>
