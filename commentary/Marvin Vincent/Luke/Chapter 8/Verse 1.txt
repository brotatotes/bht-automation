Afterward [εν τω καθεξης] . Rev., soon afterward. See on ch. Luke 7:11. Throughout every city and village [κατα πολιν και κωμην] . Lit., by city and village. See on ver. 4. Preaching [κηρυσσων] . Or proclaiming, as a herald. Compare ch. 4 18, and see on 1 Peter 2:5. And the twelve were with him. The were is supplied by the translators. Better, "he himself went about," etc., "and the twelve (went) with him;" or, as Rev., and with him the twelve.
