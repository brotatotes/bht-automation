<p>Afterward [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εν τω καθεξης] </span></span>. Rev., soon afterward. See on ch. <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="lu+7:11">Luke 7:11</span>. Throughout every city and village [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κατα πολιν και κωμην] </span></span>. Lit., by city and village. See on ver. 4. </p>
<p>Preaching [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κηρυσσων] </span></span>. Or proclaiming, as a herald. Compare ch. 4 18, and see on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1pe+2:5">1 Peter 2:5</span>. </p>
<p>And the twelve were with him. The were is supplied by the translators. Better, "he himself went about," etc., "and the twelve (went) with him;" or, as Rev., and with him the twelve.</p>
