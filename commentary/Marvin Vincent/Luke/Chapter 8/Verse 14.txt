<p>Go forth [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πορευομενοι] </span></span>. The present participle. Much better Rev., "they that have heard, and as they go on their way are choked," etc. </p>
<p>Choked with [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">υπο, υνδερ] </span></span>. Implying the impulse under which they pursue their course. </p>
<p>Bring (no fruit) to perfection (telesforousin). Only here in New Testament. Matthew and Mark have, it becometh unfruitful. The verb literally means to bring to an end or accomplishment.</p>
