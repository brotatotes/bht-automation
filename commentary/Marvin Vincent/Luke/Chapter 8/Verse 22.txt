Let us go over unto the other side of the lake. Wyc. has, pass we over the standing water. On lake, see on ch. Luke 5:1. Launched forth [ανηχθησαν] . See on ch. Luke 5:3. The verb literally means to lead up; hence to lead up to the high sea, or take to sea; put to sea. It is the word used of Jesus ' being led up into the wilderness and the mount of temptation (Matthew 4:1; Luke 2:22); also of bringing up a sacrifice to an idol - altar (Acts 7:41). Often in Acts in the accounts of Paul 's voyages.
