There met him out of the city. The words out of the city belong rather with a certain man. So Rev. Which had devils long time. The best texts insert kai, and, after devils (demons), and read "who had demons, and for a long time he had worn," etc. Long [ικανω] . See on ch. Luke 7:16. Tombs. See on Matthew 8:28. Compare Mark 5:4-6.
