<p>Steward [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επιτροπου] </span></span>. From ejpitrepw, to turn toward; thence to turn over to, transfer, and so commit or intrust to. The word thus literally means, one to whom the management of affairs is turned over. </p>
<p>4 - 18. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+13:1-23">Matthew 13:1-23</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mr+4:1-25">Mark 4:1-25</span>.</p>
