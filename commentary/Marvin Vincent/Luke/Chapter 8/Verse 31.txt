<p>Command them. The plural, referring to the legion. </p>
<p>The deep [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αβυσσον] </span></span>. Lit., the bottomless. Transcribed into our abyss, as Rev. Mark has a quite different request, that he would not send them out of the country (v. 10). In <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ro+10:7">Romans 10:7</span>, used of Hades, to which Christ descended; and in Revelation always of the bottomless pit. The demons refer to their place of abode and torment.</p>
