<p>Jairus. The name of one of the Israelite chiefs, Jair, who conquered and settled Bashan (<span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="nu+32:41">Numbers 32:41</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="jos+13:30">Joshua 13:30</span>). "His name lingered down to the time of the Christian era, when, in the same region as that which he conquered, we find a ruler of the synagogue named Jair" (Stanley, " Jewish Church ").</p>
