<p>Who touched [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τις ο αψαμενος] </span></span>. Lit., who is he that touched? Rev., who is it that. </p>
<p>Throng and press [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">συνεχουσιναποθλιβουσιν] </span></span>. On the former word, see ver. 37, and ch. 4 38. Rev. renders the latter, which occurs here only, more literally, crush. It means to squeeze out, as wine from grapes. See on tribulation, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+13:21">Matthew 13:21</span>.</p>
