<p>From the ruler of the synagogue 's house. A. V. and Rev. properly supply house, as the ruler himself is present with Jesus. </p>
<p>Dead. Placed first in the Greek order, for emphasis. "Dead is thy daughter." </p>
<p>Trouble. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+9:36">Matthew 9:36</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mr+5:35">Mark 5:35</span>. Tyndale renders dis - ease, in the old verbal sense of disturb.</p>
