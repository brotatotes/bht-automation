<p>In a company [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κλισιας] </span></span>. The plural, in companies. Lit., table - companies. The word is also used in classical Greek of a couch for reclining at table. Only here in New Testament. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mr+6:39">Mark 6:39</span>.</p>
