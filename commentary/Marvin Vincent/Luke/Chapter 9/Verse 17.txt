<p>Were filled. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+5:6">Matthew 5:6</span>. </p>
<p>There were taken up of fragments that remained to them twelve baskets [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">και ηρθη το περισσευσαν αυτπις κλασματων κοφινοι δωδεκα] </span></span>. The Rev. is more accurate, putting the comma after aujtoiv, to them, instead of after klasmatwn, fragments; and making the latter word depend on kofinoi, baskets. Render, therefore, And there was taken up that which remained over to them, of broken pieces, twelve baskets. </p>
