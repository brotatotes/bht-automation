<p>He straitly charged [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επιτιμησας] </span></span>. The word implies an emphatic, solemn charge; its meaning being, strictly, to lay a penalty upon one, and thence, to charge under penalty. </p>
<p>No man [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μηδενι] </span></span>. The conditional negative : no man, whoever he might be. </p>
<p>22 - 27. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+16:21-28">Matthew 16:21-28</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mr+8:31-38">Mark 8:31-38</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mr+9:1">Mark 9:1</span>.</p>
