He straitly charged [επιτιμησας] . The word implies an emphatic, solemn charge; its meaning being, strictly, to lay a penalty upon one, and thence, to charge under penalty. No man [μηδενι] . The conditional negative : no man, whoever he might be. 22 - 27. Compare Matthew 16:21-28; Mark 8:31-38; Mark 9:1.
