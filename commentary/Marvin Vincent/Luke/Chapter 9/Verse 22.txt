<p>Be rejected [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αποδοκιμασθηναι] </span></span>. The verb means to reject on scrutiny or trial, and therefore implies deliberate rejection. </p>
<p>Of the elders [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">απο] </span></span>. Lit., from the side of; on the part of.</p>
