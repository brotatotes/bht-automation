<p>Will come after [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">θελει] </span></span>. Not the future tense of the verb come, but the present of the verb to will : wills to come. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+1:19">Matthew 1:19</span>; and <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mr+8:34">Mark 8:34</span>. Rev., properly, would come. </p>
<p>Daily. Peculiar to Luke.</p>
