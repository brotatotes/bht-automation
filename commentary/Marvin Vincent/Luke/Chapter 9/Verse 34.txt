<p>A cloud. "A strange peculiarity has been noticed about Hermon, in the extreme rapidity of the formation of cloud on the summit. In a few minutes a thick cap forms over the top of the mountain, and as quickly disperses and entirely disappears" (Edersheim). </p>
<p>Overshadowed them [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επεσκιαζεν] </span></span>. A beautiful imperfect : "began to overshadow them;" thus harmonizing with the words, "as they entered into." Them [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αυτους</span></span>] must, I think, be confined to Moses, Elias, and Jesus. Grammatically, it might include all the six; but the disciples hear the voice out of the cloud, and the cloud, as a symbol of the divine presence, rests on these three as a sign to the disciples.
