Threw him down [ερρηξεν] . See on teareth, Mark 9:18. Tare [συνεσπαραξεν] . Only here in New Testament. Convulse, which is the exact Latin equivalent, would, perhaps, be the nearest rendering. Sparagmov, a kindred noun, is the word for a cramp. 43 - 45. Compare Matthew 17:22, Matthew 17:23; Mark 9:30-32.
