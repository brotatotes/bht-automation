Holes. See on Matthew 8:20. Birds [πετεινα] . Strictly, flying fowl. The common word for bird in the New Testament. Opniv occurs Matthew 23:37; Luke 13:34; but both times in the sense of hen. See on Matthew 23:37. Orneon is found in Revelation 18:2; Revelation 19:17, Revelation 19:21; and pthnon, another form for the word in this passage, occurs 1 Corinthians 14:30. Nests. See on Matthew 8:20.
