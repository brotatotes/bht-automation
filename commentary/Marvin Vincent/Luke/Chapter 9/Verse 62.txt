<p>Back [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εις τα οπισω] </span></span>. Lit., to things behind. "The figure is that of a man who, while engaged in labor, instead of keeping his eye on the furrow which he is drawing, looks behind at some object which attracts his interest. He is only half at work, and half - work only will be the result" (Godet). </p>
