Put his hand to [επιβαλων επι] . Lit., having laid his hand upon. Back [εις τα οπισω] . Lit., to things behind. "The figure is that of a man who, while engaged in labor, instead of keeping his eye on the furrow which he is drawing, looks behind at some object which attracts his interest. He is only half at work, and half - work only will be the result" (Godet). Fit [ευθετος] . Lit., well - placed : adjusted. ===Luke 10:0 CHAPTER X 1 - 16. Peculiar to Luke.
