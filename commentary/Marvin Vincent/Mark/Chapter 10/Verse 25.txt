<p>Needle [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ραφιδος] </span></span>. A word stigmatized by the grammarians as unclassical. One of them (Phrynichus) says, "As for rJafiv, nobody would know what it is." Matthew also uses it. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+19:24">Matthew 19:24</span>. Luke uses, belonhv, the surgical needle. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="lu+18:25">Luke 18:25</span>.</p>
