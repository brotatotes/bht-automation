<p>Bill [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">βιβλιον] </span></span>. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+19:7">Matthew 19:7</span>. Diminutive. Lit., a little book; Lat., libellus, from which comes our word libel, a written accusation. Accordingly Wyc. has a libel of forsaking, and Tynd. a testimonial of her divorcement.</p>
