<p>Afar off. Peculiar to Mark. </p>
<p>Having leaves. An unusual thing at that early season. If haply [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ει αρα] </span></span>. If, such being the case, i e., the tree having leaves - he might find fruit, which, in the fig, precedes the leaf. Mark alone adds, "for the time of figs was not yet."</p>
