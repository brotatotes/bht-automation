Vessel [σκευος] . See on Matthew 12:29; Mark 3:27. Temple [ιερου] . See on Matthew 4:5. The temple enclosure, not the naov, or sanctuary. People would be tempted to carry vessels, etc., through this, in order to save a long circuit. The court of the Gentiles, moreover, was not regarded by the Jews as entitled to the respect due to the other part of the enclosure. This our Lord rebukes.
