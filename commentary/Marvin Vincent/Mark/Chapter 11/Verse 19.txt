<p>When evening was come [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">οταν] </span></span>. Lit., whenever evening came on; not on the evening of the purging of the temple merely, but each day at evening. </p>
<p>20 - 24. All the details are peculiar to Mark. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+21:20-22">Matthew 21:20-22</span>.</p>
