<p>Catch [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αγρευσωσιν] </span></span>. From agra, hunting, the chase. Hence the picture in the word is that of hunting, while that in Matthew's word, pagideuswsin, is that of catching in a trap. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+22:15">Matthew 22:15</span>.</p>
