<p>Understanding [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">συνεσεως] </span></span>. A different word from that in verse 30. From sunihmi, to send or bring together. Hence sunesiv is a union or bringing together of the mind with an object, and so used to denote the faculty of quick comprehension, intelligence, sagacity. Compare on sunetwn, the prudent, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+11:25">Matthew 11:25</span></p>
