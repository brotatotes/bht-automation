A certain [μια] . Not a good translation. Lit., one as distinguished from the many rich. Better, simply the indefinite article, as Rev. Poor [πτωχη] . See on Matthew 5:3. Mites [λεπτα] . From leptov, peeled, husked; and thence thin or fine. Therefore of a very small or thin coin. Farthing [κοδραντης] . A Latin word, quadrans, or a quarter of a Roman as; quadrans meaning a forth, as farthing is fourthing.
