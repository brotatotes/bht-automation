<p>A certain [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μια] </span></span>. Not a good translation. Lit., one as distinguished from the many rich. Better, simply the indefinite article, as Rev. Poor [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πτωχη] </span></span>. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+5:3">Matthew 5:3</span>. </p>
<p>Mites [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">λεπτα] </span></span>. From leptov, peeled, husked; and thence thin or fine. Therefore of a very small or thin coin. </p>
<p>Farthing [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κοδραντης] </span></span>. A Latin word, quadrans, or a quarter of a Roman as; quadrans meaning a forth, as farthing is fourthing.</p>
