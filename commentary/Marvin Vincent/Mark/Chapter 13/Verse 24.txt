<p>Light [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">φεγγος] </span></span>. The word is used in the New Testament wherever the light of the moon is referred to. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+24:29">Matthew 24:29</span>, the only other instance. It occurs also in <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="lu+11:33">Luke 11:33</span>, but meaning the light - of a lamp.</p>
