<p>My guest - chamber [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">καταλυμα μου] </span></span>. <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="lu+22:11">Luke 22:11</span>. The word is not classical, and as used by an oriental signifies a khan or caravanserai. Hence inn at <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="lu+2:7">Luke 2:7</span>. My chamber. It was a common practice that more than one company partook of the paschal supper in the same apartment; but Christ will have his chamber for himself and his disciples alone.</p>
