<p>Token [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">συσσημον] </span></span>. A later Greek compound used only by Mark in this passage. Compare shmeion, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+26:48">Matthew 26:48</span>. The sun, with, gives the force of mutual token : a concerted signal.</p>
