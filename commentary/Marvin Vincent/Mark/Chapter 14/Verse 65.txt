<p>Buffet. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+26:67">Matthew 26:67</span>. Palms of their hands [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ραπισμασιν] </span></span>. An unclassical word, but used also by John (xix. 3). The word means blows. </p>
<p>Did strike. Following the old reading, eballon. The Correct reading is elabon, received. So Rev. Received him into custody.</p>
