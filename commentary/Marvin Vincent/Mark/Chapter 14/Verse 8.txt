<p>She hath done what she could [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ο εσχεν εποιησεν] </span></span>. Lit., what she had she did. Peculiar to Mark. </p>
<p>She is come aforehand to anoint [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">προελαβεν μυρισαι] </span></span>. Lit., she anticipated to anoint. Rev., hath anointed beforehand. The verb murizw is found only here.</p>
