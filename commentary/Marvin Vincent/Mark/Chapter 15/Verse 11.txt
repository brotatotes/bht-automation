<p>Moved [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ανεσεισαν] </span></span>. A feeble translation. Seiw is to shake. Hence seismov, an earthquake. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+13:8">Matthew 13:8</span>. Better as Rev., stirred up. Wyc., The bishops stirred the company of people.</p>
