<p>They gave [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εδιδουν] </span></span>. The imperfect tense is used in the same sense as in <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+3:14">Matthew 3:14</span> (Rev.), "John would have hindered." They were for giving; attempted to give. So Rev., excellently, offered. </p>
<p>Wine mingled with myrrh [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εσμυρνισμενον οινον] </span></span>. Lit., myrrhed wine. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+27:34">Matthew 27:34</span>.</p>
