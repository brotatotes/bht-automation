<p>Them that had made insurrection with him [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">συστασιαστων] </span></span>. </p>
<p>Fellow - rioters. But the better texts read stasiastwn, rioters, omitting the sun with (fellow) : and the Rev. accordingly omits with him. </p>
<p>Who [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">οιτινες] </span></span>. Denoting a class of criminals. </p>
<p>The insurrection. Note the article : the insurrection for which Barabbas and his fellows had been imprisoned.</p>
