She [εκεινη] . An absolute use of the pronoun unexampled in Mark. See also verses 11, 13. It would imply an emphasis which is not intended. Compare Mark 4:11; Mark 12:4, Mark 12:5, Mark 12:7; Mark 14:21. Went [πορευθεισα] . So in verses 12, 15. Went, go. This verb for to go occurs nowhere else in this Gospel except in compounds. Them that had been with him [τοις μετ αυτου γενομενοις] . A circumlocution foreign to the Gospels.
