<p>They that are whole [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">οι ισχυοντες] </span></span>. Lit., they that are strong. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="lu+14:30">Luke 14:30</span>, was not able; and <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="2pe+2:11">2 Peter 2:11</span>, power. </p>
<p>No need. The Greek order throws the emphasis on these words : No need have they that are strong of a physician. Wyc., Whole men have no need to a leech, but they that have evil.</p>
