His friends [οι παρ αυτου] . Lit., they who were from beside him : i e., by origin or birth. His mother and brethren. Compare verses 31, 32. Wyc., kinsmen. Tynd., they that belonged unto him. Not his disciples, since they were in the house with him. They said [ελεγον] . Imperfect tense. Very graphic, they kept saying.
