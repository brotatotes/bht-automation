<p>His friends [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">οι παρ αυτου] </span></span>. Lit., they who were from beside him : i e., by origin or birth. His mother and brethren. Compare verses 31, 32. Wyc., kinsmen. Tynd., they that belonged unto him. Not his disciples, since they were in the house with him. </p>
<p>They said [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ελεγον] </span></span>. Imperfect tense. Very graphic, they kept saying.</p>
