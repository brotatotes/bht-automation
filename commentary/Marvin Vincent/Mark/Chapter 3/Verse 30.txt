<p>They said [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ελεγον] </span></span>. Imperfect tense. They kept saying, or persisted in saying. An addition peculiar to Mark. </p>
<p>31, 32. They sent unto him calling him. and a multitude was sitting about him. Detail by Mark only; as also the words in verse 34, Looking round on them which sat round about him. </p>
