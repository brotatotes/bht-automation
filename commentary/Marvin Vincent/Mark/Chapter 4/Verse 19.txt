<p>The lusts of other things entering in [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αι περι τα λοιπα επιθυμιαι] </span></span>. Lusts not in the limited sense of mere sexual desire, but in the general sense of longing. The word is also used of desire for good and lawful things (<span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="lu+22:15">Luke 22:15</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="php+1:23">Philippians 1:23</span>).</p>
