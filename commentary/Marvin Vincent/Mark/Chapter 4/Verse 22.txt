<p>Which shall not be manifested [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εαν μη ινα φανερωθη] </span></span>. The A. V. makes Christ say that every hidden thing shall be revealed. This is wrong. He says that things are hidden in order that they may be manifested. Concealment is a means to revelation. </p>
