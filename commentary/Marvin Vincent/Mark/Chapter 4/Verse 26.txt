<p>Should cast [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">βαλη] </span></span>. Lit., should have cast, the aorist tense, followed by the presents sleep and rise [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">καθευδη ανδ εγειρηται] </span></span>. The whole, literally, "As if a man should have cast seed into the ground, and should be sleeping and rising night and day." The aorist tense indicates the single act of casting; the presents the repeated, continued sleeping and rising while the seed is growing. </p>
<p>Seed [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τον σπορον] </span></span>. The seed; that particular seed which he had to sow. Such is the force of the article.</p>
