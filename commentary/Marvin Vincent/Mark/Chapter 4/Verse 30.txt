<p>Peculiar to Mark. </p>
<p>With what comparison shall we compare it? [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εν τινι αυτην παραβολη θωμεν] </span></span>. Lit., In what parable might we put it? Rev., In what parable shall we set it forth? Note the we, taking the hearers, with a fine tact, into consultation.</p>
