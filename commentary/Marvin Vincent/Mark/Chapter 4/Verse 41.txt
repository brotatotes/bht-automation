<p>They feared exceedingly [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εφοβηθησαν φοβον μεγαν] </span></span>. Lit., they feared a great fear. </p>
<p>What manner of man is this? [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τις αρα ουτος εστιν] </span></span>. The A. V. is rather a rendering of Matthew's potapov, what manner of (viii. 27), than of Mark's tiv, who. The Rev. gives it rightly : Who then is this? The then [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αρα</span></span>] is argumentative. Since these things are so, who then is this? </p>
