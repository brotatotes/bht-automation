<p>That sprang up and increased [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αναβαινοντα και αυξανομενον] </span></span>. The Rev. literally renders the participles, growing up and increasing, thus describing the process more vividly. These two participles, moreover, explain the use of the imperfect tense ejdidou [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ψιελδεδ] </span></span>, denoting continuance. It began to yield and kept yielding as it increased. </p>
<p>Thirty [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εις τριακοντα] </span></span>. Lit., up to thirty.</p>
