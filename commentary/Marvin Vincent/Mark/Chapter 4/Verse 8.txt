That sprang up and increased [αναβαινοντα και αυξανομενον] . The Rev. literally renders the participles, growing up and increasing, thus describing the process more vividly. These two participles, moreover, explain the use of the imperfect tense ejdidou [ψιελδεδ] , denoting continuance. It began to yield and kept yielding as it increased. Thirty [εις τριακοντα] . Lit., up to thirty.
