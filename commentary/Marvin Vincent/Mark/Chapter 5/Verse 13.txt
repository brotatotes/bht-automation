<p>Ran [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ωρμησεν] </span></span>. The verb indicates hasty, headlong motion. Hence, as Rev., rushed. </p>
<p>Two Thousand. As usual, Mark alone gives the detail of number. </p>
<p>A steep place. But the noun has the definite article : tou krhmnou, the steep, as Rev.</p>
