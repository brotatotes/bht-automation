<p>See (qewrousin). Rev., rightly, behold. For it was more than simple seeing. The verb means looking steadfastly, as one who has an interest in the object, and with a view to search into and understand it : to look inquiringly and intently. </p>
<p>Clothed. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="lu+8:27">Luke 8:27</span>. For a long time he had worn no clothes.</p>
