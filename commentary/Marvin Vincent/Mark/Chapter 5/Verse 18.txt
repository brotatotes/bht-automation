When he was come [εμβαινοντος] . The participle is in the present tense. Not after he had embarked, but while he was in the act. Hence Rev., rightly, as he was entering. With this corresponds the graphic imperfect parekalei : While he was stepping into the boat the restored man was beseeching him. That [ινα] . In order that. Not the subject but the aim of the entreaty.
