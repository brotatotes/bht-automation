<p>Knowing [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επιγνους] </span></span>. Rev., perceiving. Lit., having fully known. </p>
<p>That virtue had gone out of him [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">την εξ αυτου δυναμιν εξελθουσαν] </span></span>. More correctly as Rev., that the power proceeding from him had gone forth. The object of the Savior 's knowledge was thus complex : 1st, his power; 2nd, that his power had gone forth, This and the following sentence are peculiar to Mark.</p>
