<p>Crying - he saith. The inarticulate cry (verse 5), and then the articulate speech. </p>
<p>What have I to do with thee? [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τι εμοι και σοι; ] </span></span>. Lit., what is there to me and thee? What have we in common? </p>
<p>I adjure thee by God. Stronger than Luke's I pray thee. The verb oJrkizw, I adjure, is condemned by the grammarians as inelegant.</p>
