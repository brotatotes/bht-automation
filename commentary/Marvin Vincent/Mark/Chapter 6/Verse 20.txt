<p>Observed him [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">συνετηρει] </span></span>. A mistranslation. Rev., kept him safe. Peculiar to Mark. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+9:17">Matthew 9:17</span>, are preserved; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="lu+2:19">Luke 2:19</span>, kept; sun, closely; threin, to preserve or keep, as the result of guarding. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="joh+17:12">John 17:12</span>, and reserved, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1pe+1:4">1 Peter 1:4</span>. </p>
<p>Did many things [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πολλα εποιει] </span></span>. The proper reading, however, is hjporei; from aj, not, and porov, a passage. Hence, strictly, to be in circumstances where one cannot find a way out. So Rev., rightly, he was much perplexed. The other reading is meaningless.</p>
