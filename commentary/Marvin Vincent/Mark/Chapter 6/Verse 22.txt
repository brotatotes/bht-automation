<p>The said Herodias [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αυτης της ηρωδιαδος] </span></span>. The A. V. misses the point of aujthv by the translation the said : the object being not to particularize the Herodias just referred to, but to emphasize the fact that Herodias' own daughter was put forward instead of a professional dancer. Hence Rev., correctly, "the daughter of Herodias herself." </p>
