The said Herodias [αυτης της ηρωδιαδος] . The A. V. misses the point of aujthv by the translation the said : the object being not to particularize the Herodias just referred to, but to emphasize the fact that Herodias' own daughter was put forward instead of a professional dancer. Hence Rev., correctly, "the daughter of Herodias herself." Damsel [κορασιω] . See on Mark 5:41.
