<p>When he had sent them away [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αποταξαμενος] </span></span>. Rev., more correctly, after he had taken leave. Unclassical, and used in this sense only in later Greek. So in <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="lu+9:61">Luke 9:61</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+18:18">Acts 18:18</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="2co+2:13">2 Corinthians 2:13</span>.</p>
