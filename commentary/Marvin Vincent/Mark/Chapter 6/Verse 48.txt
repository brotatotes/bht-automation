He saw [ιδων] . Participle. Rev., seeing. Better, however, the literal having seen. It was this which induced him to go to them. Toiling [βασανιζομενους] . Lit., tormented. Rev., distressed. See on Matthew 4:24. Wyc., travailing. Tynd., troubled. Fourth watch. Between 3 and 6 A. M. Would have passed by them. Peculiar to Mark.
