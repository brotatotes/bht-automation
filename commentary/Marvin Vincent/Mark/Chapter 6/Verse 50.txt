<p>They all saw him. Peculiar to Mark. </p>
<p>Spake with them [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ελαλησεν μετ αυτων] </span></span>. Both Matthew and John give the simple dative, aujtoiv to them. Mark's with them is more familiar, and gives the idea of a more friendly and encouraging address. It is significant, in view of Peter's relation to this gospel, that Mark omits the incident of Peter's walk on the waves (<span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+14:28-31">Matthew 14:28-31</span>).</p>
