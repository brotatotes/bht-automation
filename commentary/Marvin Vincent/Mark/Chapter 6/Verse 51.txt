<p>Sore amazed [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">λιαν εκ περισσου εξισταντο] </span></span>. Lit., exceedingly beyond measure. A strong expression peculiar to Mark. jExistanto, were amazed. Compare the cognate noun ekstasiv, and see on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mr+5:42">Mark 5:42</span>.</p>
