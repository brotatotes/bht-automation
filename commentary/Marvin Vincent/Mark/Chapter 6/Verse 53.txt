<p>Drew to the shore [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">προσωρμισθησαν] </span></span>. Peculiar to Mark. Rev., moored to the shore, though the meaning may be near the shore. jAnebh, he went up (verse 51), seems to indicate a vessel of considerable size, standing quite high out of the water. They may have anchored off shore.</p>
