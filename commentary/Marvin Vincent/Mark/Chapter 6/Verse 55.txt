<p>Ran round. From place to place where the sick were, to bring them to Jesus. Matthew has they sent. </p>
<p>Carry about [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">περιφερειν] </span></span>. peri, about; one hither and another thither, wherever Christ might be at the time. </p>
<p>Beds [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κραβαττοις] </span></span>. Condemned as bad Greek, but used by both Luke and John. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mr+2:4">Mark 2:4</span>.</p>
