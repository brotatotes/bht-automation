<p>Making of none effect. Rev., making void. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+14:6">Matthew 14:6</span>. Ye handed down. Note the past tense, identifying them for the moment with their forefathers. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+13:35">Matthew 13:35</span>, Ye slew. Christ views the Jewish persecutors and bigots, ancient and modern, as a whole, actuated by one spirit, and ascribes to one section what was done by another.</p>
