<p>There shall no sign be given [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ει δοθησεται σημειον] </span></span>. Lit., if a sign shall be given. The expression, is elliptical. It is a Hebrew idiom, and is really, at bottom, a form of imprecation. If I do not thus or so, may some judgment overtake me. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="heb+3:11">Hebrews 3:11</span>.</p>
