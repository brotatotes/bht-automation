<p>I send you forth [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εγω αποστελλω] </span></span>. Cognate to the word ajpostolov (apostle). The I is emphatic : "It is I that send you forth." </p>
<p>Wise [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">φρονιμοι] </span></span>. So A. V. and Rev. Denoting prudence with regard to their own safety. Wyc., wary. </p>
<p>Harmless [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ακεραιοι] </span></span>. Lit., unmixed, unadulterated. Used of wine without water, and of metal without alloy. Hence guileless. So Luther, without falsity. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ro+16:19">Romans 16:19</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="php+2:15">Philippians 2:15</span>. They were to imitate the serpent 's wariness, but not his wiliness. "The presence of the wolves demands they ye be wary; the fact that ye are my apostles (compare" I send you ") demands that ye be guileless " (Dr. Morison on Matthew).</p>
