<p>The Canaanite [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ο καναναιος] </span></span>. Rev., Cananaean. The word has nothing to do with Canaan. In <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="lu+6:15">Luke 6:15</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+1:13">Acts 1:13</span>, the same apostle is called Zelotes, Both terms indicate his connection with the Galilaean Zealot party, a sect which stood for the recovery of Jewish freedom and the maintenance of distinctive Jewish institutions. From the Hebrew kanna, zealous; compare the Chaldee kanan, by which this sect was denoted.</p>
