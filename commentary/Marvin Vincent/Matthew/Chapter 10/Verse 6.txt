<p>The lost sheep [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τα προβατα τα απολωλοτα] </span></span>. The Greek order throws the emphasis on lost; the sheep, the lost ones. Bengel observes that Jesus says lost oftener than led astray. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+18:12">Matthew 18:12</span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+18:14">Matthew 18:14</span>.</p>
