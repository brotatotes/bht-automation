<p>Are delivered [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">παρεδοθη] </span></span>. More lit., were delivered, as of a single act at a given time, as in this case, where the Son was sent forth by the Father, and clothed with authority. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+28:18">Matthew 28:18</span>. </p>
<p>Knoweth [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επιγινωσκει] </span></span>. The compound indicating full knowledge. Other behold only in part, "through a glass, darkly."</p>
