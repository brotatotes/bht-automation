<p>Labor and are heavy - laden [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κοπιωντες και πεφορτισμενοι] </span></span>. The first an active, the second a passive participle, exhibiting the active and passive sides of human misery. </p>
<p>Give rest [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αναπαυσω] </span></span>. Originally to make to cease; Tynd., ease; Wyc., refresh. The radical conception is that of relief.</p>
