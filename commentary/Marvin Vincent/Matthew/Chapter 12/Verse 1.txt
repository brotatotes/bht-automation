<p>Time [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">καιρω] </span></span>. Rev., season. The word implies a particular time; as related to some event, a convenient, appropriate time; absolutely, a particular point of time, or a particular season, like spring or winter. </p>
<p>Corn [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">σποριμων] </span></span>. From speirw, to sow. Properly, as Rev., corn - fields.</p>
