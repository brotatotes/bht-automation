<p>Shore [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αιγιαλον] </span></span>. Rev., beach, that over which the sea [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αλς</span></span>] rushes [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αισσει] </span></span>. The word for shore, ajkth, on which the sea breaks [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αγνυμι] </span></span>, is never used in the New Testament. Wyc., brink.</p>
