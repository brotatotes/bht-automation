<p>Put he forth [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">παρεθηκεν] </span></span>. But this would be rather the translation of proballw, from which problhma, a problem, is derived, while the word here used means rather to set before or offer. Often used of meals, to serve up. Hence, better, Rev., set he before them. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="lu+9:16">Luke 9:16</span>.</p>
