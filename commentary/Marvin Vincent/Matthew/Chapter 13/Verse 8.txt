<p>A hundred - fold. Mentioned as something extraordinary. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ge+26:12">Genesis 26:12</span>. Herodotus (i. 93) says of Babylonia, "In grain it is so fruitful as to yield commonly two - hundred - fold; and when the production is the greatest, even three - hundred - fold."</p>
