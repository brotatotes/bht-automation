<p>Desert [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ερημος] </span></span>. In the Greek order standing first as emphatic. The dominant thought of the disciples is remoteness from supplies of food. The first meaning of the word is solitary; from which develops the idea of void, bereft, barren. </p>
<p>Both meanings may well be included here. Note the two points of emphasis. The disciples say, Barren is the place. Christ answers, No need have they to go away. </p>
<p>Give [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δοτε] </span></span>. The disciples had said, "Send them away to buy for themselves." Christ replies, Give ye.</p>
