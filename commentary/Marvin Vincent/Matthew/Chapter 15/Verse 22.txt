<p>Out of the same coasts [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">απο των οριων εκεινων] </span></span>. Lit., as Rev., from those borders; i e., she crossed from Phoenicia into Galilee. </p>
<p>Cried [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εκραυγασεν] </span></span>. With a loud, importunate cry : from behind. Compare after, ver. 23. Making her daughter 's misery her own. </p>
<p>Grievously vexed with a devil [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κακως δαιμονιζεται] </span></span>. Lit., is badly demonized. Sir J. Cheke, very evil devilled.</p>
