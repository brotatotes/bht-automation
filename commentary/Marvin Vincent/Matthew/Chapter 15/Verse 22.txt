Out of the same coasts [απο των οριων εκεινων] . Lit., as Rev., from those borders; i e., she crossed from Phoenicia into Galilee. Cried [εκραυγασεν] . With a loud, importunate cry : from behind. Compare after, ver. 23. Making her daughter 's misery her own. Grievously vexed with a devil [κακως δαιμονιζεται] . Lit., is badly demonized. Sir J. Cheke, very evil devilled.
