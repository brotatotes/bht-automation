<p>Lowering [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">στυγναζων] </span></span>. The verb means to have a gloomy look. Dr. Morison compares the Scotch gloaming or glooming. Cranmer, the sky is glooming red. The word is used only here and at <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mr+10:22">Mark 10:22</span>, of the young ruler, turning from Christ with his face overshadowed with gloom. A. V., he was sad. Rev., his countenance fell. </p>
