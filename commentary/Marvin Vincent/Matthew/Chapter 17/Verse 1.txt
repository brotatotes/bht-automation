<p>Taketh [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">παραλαμβανει] </span></span>. Rev. gives the force of the preposition para, taketh with him. </p>
<p>Apart [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κατ ιδιαν] </span></span>. Not said of the mountain, as isolated, but of the disciples; so that they might be alone with him. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mr+9:2">Mark 9:2</span>, apart by themselves (kat ijdian monouv : lit., apart alone).</p>
