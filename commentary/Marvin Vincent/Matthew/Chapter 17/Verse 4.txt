<p>Let us make [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ποιησωμεν] </span></span>. But the best texts read, poihsw, I will make, which is more characteristic of Peter. He would erect the booths himself. </p>
<p>Three tabernacles [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">σκηνας] </span></span>. Tents or booths, out of the brushwood lying near. Peter realized that it was night, and was for preparing shelters into which the heavenly visitants might retire after their interview.</p>
