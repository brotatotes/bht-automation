<p>Told [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">διεσαφησαν] </span></span>. More than merely narrated. The verb is from dia, throughout, and safe w, to explain. They explained the circumstances throughout. </p>
<p>Their Lord [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τω κυριω εαυτων] </span></span>. Lit., "their own Lord;" as befitted their position, and as a mark of their confidence in him.</p>
