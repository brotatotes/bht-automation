<p>Have followed. "Peter had said together the words we have left, we have followed. Jesus replies to them separately; for the latter was peculiar to the apostles, the former common to them with others" (Bengel). </p>
<p>In the regeneration. The final restitution of all things. To be construed with ye shall sit. </p>
<p>Shall sit [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">καθιση] </span></span>. Or shall have taken his seat, which brings out more vividly the solemn inauguration of Christ 's judgment.</p>
