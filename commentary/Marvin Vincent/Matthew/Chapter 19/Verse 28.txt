Have followed. "Peter had said together the words we have left, we have followed. Jesus replies to them separately; for the latter was peculiar to the apostles, the former common to them with others" (Bengel). In the regeneration. The final restitution of all things. To be construed with ye shall sit. Shall sit [καθιση] . Or shall have taken his seat, which brings out more vividly the solemn inauguration of Christ 's judgment.
