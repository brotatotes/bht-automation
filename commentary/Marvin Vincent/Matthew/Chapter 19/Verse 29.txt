Every one [πας] . Compare 2 Timothy 4:8, "to all them that love his appearing." " Not only apostles, nor ought Peter to have inquired only concerning them " (Bengel). The promise hitherto restricted to the apostles now becomes general. A hundred - fold [εκατονταπλασιονα] . But many very high authorities read pollaplasiona, manifold. So Rev. in margin. Compare Mark 10:30, where there is added "houses and brethren," etc. Also the Arabic proverb : "Purchase the next world with this; so shalt thou win both."
