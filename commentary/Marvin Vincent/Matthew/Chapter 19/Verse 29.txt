<p>Every one [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πας] </span></span>. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="2ti+4:8">2 Timothy 4:8</span>, "to all them that love his appearing." " Not only apostles, nor ought Peter to have inquired only concerning them " (Bengel). The promise hitherto restricted to the apostles now becomes general. </p>
<p>A hundred - fold [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εκατονταπλασιονα] </span></span>. But many very high authorities read pollaplasiona, manifold. So Rev. in margin. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mr+10:30">Mark 10:30</span>, where there is added "houses and brethren," etc. Also the Arabic proverb : "Purchase the next world with this; so shalt thou win both." </p>
