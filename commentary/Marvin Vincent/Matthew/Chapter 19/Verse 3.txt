<p>Tempting. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+6:13">Matthew 6:13</span>. </p>
<p>For every cause. The temptation turned upon the dispute dividing the two great Rabbinical schools, the one of which (that of Hillel) held that a man might divorce his wife for any reason which rendered her distasteful to him; and the other (that of Shammai) that divorce was allowable only in case of unchastity. The querists would be anxious to know which side Jesus espoused.</p>
