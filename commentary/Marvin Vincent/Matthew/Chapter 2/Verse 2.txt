<p>The east [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ανατολη] </span></span>. Literally, the rising. Some commentators prefer to render at its rising, or when it rose. In <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="lu+1:78">Luke 1:78</span>, the word is translated dayspring, or dawn. The kindred verb occurs in <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+4:16">Matthew 4:16</span>, "light did spring up" [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ανετειλεν] </span></span>.</p>
