<p>The money - changers [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κολλυβιστων] </span></span>. From kollubov, the rate of exchange. These changers sat in the temple, in the court of the Gentiles, to change the foreign coins of pilgrims into the shekel of the sanctuary for payment of the annual tribute. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+17:24">Matthew 17:24</span>.</p>
