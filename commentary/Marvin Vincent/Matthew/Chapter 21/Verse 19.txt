<p>A fig - tree [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">συκην μιαν] </span></span>. Lit., one single fig - tree. Rev., in margin. </p>
<p>Presently [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">παραχρημα] </span></span>. Presently, in popular speech, has acquired something of a future force. I will do such a thing presently means, I will do it, not immediately, but soon. The rendering here was correct in the older English sense of instantly. So constantly in Shakspeare : </p>
<p>" PROSPERO. Go, bring the rabble, </p>
<p>O'er whom I gave thee pow'r, here, to this place. </p>
<p>ARIEL. Presently? </p>
<p>PROS. Ay, with a twink.</p>
