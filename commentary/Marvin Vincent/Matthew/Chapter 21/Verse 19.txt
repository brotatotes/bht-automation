A fig - tree [συκην μιαν] . Lit., one single fig - tree. Rev., in margin. Presently [παραχρημα] . Presently, in popular speech, has acquired something of a future force. I will do such a thing presently means, I will do it, not immediately, but soon. The rendering here was correct in the older English sense of instantly. So constantly in Shakspeare : " PROSPERO. Go, bring the rabble, O'er whom I gave thee pow'r, here, to this place. ARIEL. Presently? PROS. Ay, with a twink.
