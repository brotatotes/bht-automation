<p>Dinner [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αριστον] </span></span>. Not the principal meal of the day, but a noon - breakfast; luncheon. </p>
<p>Fatlings [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">σιτιστα] </span></span>. From sitov, corn, grain, or food generally. Properly animals especially fed up or fatted for a feast.</p>
