<p>Platter [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">παροψιδος] </span></span>. para, beside, oyon, meat. A side - dish, with the accompanying sense of something dainty; later, as here, the dish itself as distinguished from its contents. </p>
<p>Excess [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ακρασιας] </span></span>. aj, not, kratov, power. Hence conduct which shows a want of power over one's self : incontinence or intemperance..</p>
