<p>Went out and departed from the temple [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εξελθων απο του ιερου επορευετο] </span></span>. Rev., better : Went out from the temple and was going on his way. The temple, iJerou, not naou : the whole of the buildings connected with the temple, all of which, including the naov, or sanctuary, and the porches and courts, constituted the iJeron. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+4:5">Matthew 4:5</span>.</p>
