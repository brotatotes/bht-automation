Coming [παρουσιας] . Originally, presence, from pareinai, to be present. In this sense Philippians 2:12; 2 Corinthians 10:10. Also arrival, as in 1 Corinthians 16:17; 2 Corinthians 7:6, 2 Corinthians 7:7; 2 Thessalonians 2:9; 2 Peter 3:12. Of the second coming of Christ : James 5:8; 1 John 2:28; 2 Peter 3:4; 1 Thessalonians 4:15. Of the world [αιωνος] . Rather the existing, current age. They do not ask the signs of the Messiah 's coming at the end of all time, to judge the world.
