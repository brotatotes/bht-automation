<p>Straightway [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ευθεως] </span></span>. Connected with the beginning of this verse, instead of with the end of ver 14 Straightway he that had received, etc., indicating promptness on the servant 's part. </p>
<p>Traded with them [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ηργασατο εν αυτοις] </span></span>. Lit., wrought with them. The virgins wait, the servants work. </p>
<p>Made [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εποιησεν] </span></span>. Not made them, as A. V. The word is used in our sense of make money. Wyc. and Tynd., won. Geneva, gained. Some read ejkerdhsen, gained, as in ver. 17.</p>
