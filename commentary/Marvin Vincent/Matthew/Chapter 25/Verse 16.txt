Straightway [ευθεως] . Connected with the beginning of this verse, instead of with the end of ver 14 Straightway he that had received, etc., indicating promptness on the servant 's part. Traded with them [ηργασατο εν αυτοις] . Lit., wrought with them. The virgins wait, the servants work. Made [εποιησεν] . Not made them, as A. V. The word is used in our sense of make money. Wyc. and Tynd., won. Geneva, gained. Some read ejkerdhsen, gained, as in ver. 17.
