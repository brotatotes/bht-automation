<p>Goats [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εριφια] </span></span>. Diminutive. Lit., kidlings. The sheep and goats are represented as having previously pastured together. Compare the parables of the Tares and the Net. </p>
<p>On the right [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εκ δεξιων] </span></span>. Lit., from the right side or parts. The picture to the Greek reader is that of a row, beginning at the judge 's right hand.</p>
