<p>Slumbered and slept [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ενυσταξαν και εκαθευδον] </span></span>. Slumbered is, literally, nodded. Note the variation of tense. Nodded is aorist, denoting a transient act, the initial stage of slumber. They dropped their heads. Slept is imperfect, of continuous slumber.</p>
