<p>When Jesus understood it [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">γνους δε ο ιησους] </span></span>. The A. V. implies that some time elapsed before Jesus was aware of the disciples ' complaint. But the statement is that Jesus perceived it at once. Rev., rightly, Jesus perceiving it. </p>
<p>Good work [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">καλον] </span></span>. Lit., beautiful, but in a moral sense : an excellent, morally beautiful deed.</p>
