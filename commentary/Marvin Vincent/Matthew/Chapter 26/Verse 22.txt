<p>Began to say [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ηρξαντο] </span></span>. Denoting the commencement of a series of questions; one after the other (every one) saying, Is it I? </p>
<p>Is it I? [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μητι εγω ειμι] </span></span>. The form of the negative expects a negative answer. "Surely I am not the one."</p>
