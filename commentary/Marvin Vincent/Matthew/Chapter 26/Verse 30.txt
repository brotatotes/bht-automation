<p>Sung a hymn. Very probably the second part of the Jewish Hallel or Hallelujah, embracing <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ps+115:116">Psalms 115:116</span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ps+115:117">Psalms 115:117</span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ps+115:118">Psalms 115:118</span>. </p>
<p>They went out. In the original institution of the Passover it was enjoined that no one should go out of his house until morning (<span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ex+12:22">Exodus 12:22</span>). Evidently this had ceased to be regarded as obligatory.</p>
