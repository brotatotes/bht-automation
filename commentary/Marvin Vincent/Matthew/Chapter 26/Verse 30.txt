Sung a hymn. Very probably the second part of the Jewish Hallel or Hallelujah, embracing Psalms 115:116, Psalms 115:117, Psalms 115:118. They went out. In the original institution of the Passover it was enjoined that no one should go out of his house until morning (Exodus 12:22). Evidently this had ceased to be regarded as obligatory.
