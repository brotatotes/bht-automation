<p>A thief [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ληστην] </span></span>. Better Rev., a robber. See <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="joh+10:1">John 10:1</span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="joh+10:8">John 10:8</span>; and <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="lu+23:39-43">Luke 23:39-43</span>. It is more than a petty stealer; rather one with associates, who would require an armed band to apprehend him. Hence the propriety of the reference to swords and staves. </p>
<p>I sat [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εκαθεζομην] </span></span>. The imperfect tense, denoting something habitual. I was accustomed to sit.</p>
