<p>Buffet [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εκολαφισαν] </span></span>. With the fist. </p>
<p>Smote with the palms of their hands. All expressed by one word, ejrapisan, from rJapiv, a rod, and meaning to smite with rods, not with the palms. The same word is employed <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+5:39">Matthew 5:39</span>. It came to mean generally to strike.</p>
