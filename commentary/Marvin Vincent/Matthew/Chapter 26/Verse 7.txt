<p>An alabaster box [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αλαβαστρον] </span></span>. Rev., cruse; flask in margin. Lit., an alabaster, just as we call a drinking - vessel made of glass a glass. Luther renders glass. It was a kind of cruet, having a cylindrical form at the top. Pliny compares these vessels to a closed rosebud, and says that ointments are best preserved in them.</p>
