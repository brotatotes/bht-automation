<p>Vinegar [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">οξους] </span></span>. Sour wine; the posca or ordinary drink of the Roman soldiers. </p>
<p>Gave him to drink [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εποτιζεν] </span></span>. The imperfect tense implies was in the act of giving, or about to give. At this point the Jews standing near interposed, saying Let be [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αφες] </span></span>! "Stop! Do not give him the drink. Let us see if Elijah will come to his aid."</p>
