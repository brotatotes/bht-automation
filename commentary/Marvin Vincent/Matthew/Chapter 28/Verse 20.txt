<p>End of the word [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">συντελειας του αιωνος] </span></span>. Rev., in margin, and lit., consummation of the age. The current age is meant; and the consummation is coincident with the second coming of Christ, after the Gospel shall be been proclaimed throughout the world. "The Savior 's mind goes not farther; for after that, evangelizing work will cease. No man, after that will need to teach his neighbor, saying 'Know the Lord '" (<span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="jer+31:34">Jeremiah 31:34</span>) (Morison " On Matthew "). </p>
