<p>Is laid [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κειται] </span></span>. Not, is applied, as "She layeth her hands to the spindle" (<span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="pr+31:19">Proverbs 31:19</span>), but is lying. </p>
<p>Is hewn down and cast. The present tense is graphic, denoting what is to happen at once and certainly.</p>
