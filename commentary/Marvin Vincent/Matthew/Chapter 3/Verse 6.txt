<p>Were baptized [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εβαπτιζοντο] </span></span>. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mr+7:4">Mark 7:4</span>. Confessing their sins [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εξομολογουμενοι τας αμαρτιας αυτων] </span></span>. The words imply : </p>
<p>1. That confession was connected with baptism. They were baptized while in the act of confessing. </p>
<p>2. An open confession, not a private one to John (ejx, compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+19:18">Acts 19:18</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="jas+5:16">James 5:16</span>). </p>
<p>3. An individual confession; possibly a specific one. (See <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="lu+3:10-15">Luke 3:10-15</span>.)</p>
