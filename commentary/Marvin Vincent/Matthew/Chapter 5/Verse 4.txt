<p>They that mourn [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πενθουντες] </span></span>. Signifying grief manifested; too deep for concealment. Hence it is often joined with klaiein, to weep audibly (<span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mr+16:10">Mark 16:10</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="jas+4:9">James 4:9</span>). </p>
<p>Shall be comforted. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="joh+14:16">John 14:16</span>.</p>
