<p>Coat, cloke [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">χιτωνα, ιυατιον] </span></span>. The former, the shirt - like under - garment or tunic; the latter, the mantle, or ampler over - garment, which served as a covering for the night, and therefore was forbidden by the Levitical law to be retained in pledge over night (<span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ex+22:26">Exodus 22:26</span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ex+22:27">Exodus 22:27</span>). To yield up this without resistance therefore implies a higher degree of concession.</p>
