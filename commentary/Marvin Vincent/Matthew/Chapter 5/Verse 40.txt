Coat, cloke [χιτωνα, ιυατιον] . The former, the shirt - like under - garment or tunic; the latter, the mantle, or ampler over - garment, which served as a covering for the night, and therefore was forbidden by the Levitical law to be retained in pledge over night (Exodus 22:26, Exodus 22:27). To yield up this without resistance therefore implies a higher degree of concession.
