<p>Use vain repetitions [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">βατταλογησητε] </span></span>. A word formed in imitation of the sound, battalogein : properly, to stammer; then to babble or prate, to repeat the same formula many times, as the worshippers of Baal and of Diana of Ephesus (<span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1ki+18:26">1 Kings 18:26</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+19:34">Acts 19:34</span>) and the Romanists with their paternosters and aves.</p>
