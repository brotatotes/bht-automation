Heal [θεραπευσω] . So A. V. and Rev. The word, however, originally means to attend, and to treat medically. The centurion uses another and stronger word, shall be healed [ιαθησεται] . Luke, who as a physician is precise in the use of medical terms, uses both words in one verse (ix. 11). Jesus healed [ιατο] all who had need of treatment [θεραπειας] . Still, Luke himself does not always observe the distinction. See on Luke 5:15.
