<p>Minstrels [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αυλητας] </span></span>. More correctly, as Rev., flute - players, hired or volunteering as mourners. </p>
<p>Making a noise [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">θορυβουμενον] </span></span>. Rev., tumult. Representing the loud screaming and wailing by the women. It is the word used in <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+17:5">Acts 17:5</span> : "Set the city in an uproar."</p>
