<p>I would [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ηθελησα] </span></span>. Compare I would, ver. 13. Here the aorist tense and the verb meaning to will denote a single, decisive resolution. As it were of necessity [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ως κατα αναγκην] </span></span>. Wv as it were, Rev., as, marks the appearance of necessity. Philemon's kindly reception of Onesimus must not even seem to be constrained.</p>
