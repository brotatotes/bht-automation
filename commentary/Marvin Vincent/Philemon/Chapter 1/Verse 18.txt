<p>If he hath wronged [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ει ηδικδσεν] </span></span>. The indicative mood with the conditional particle may imply that what is put hypothetically is really a fact : if he wronged thee as he did. </p>
<p>Oweth. Perhaps indicating that Onesimus had been guilty of theft. Notice the general word wronged instead of the more exact specification of the crime. </p>
<p>Put that on my account [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τουτο εμοι ελλογα] </span></span>. For the verb, compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ro+5:13">Romans 5:13</span> (note).</p>
