Mark. Probably John Mark the evangelist. He appears as the companion of Paul, Acts 12:25; Colossians 4:10; 2 Timothy 4:11. Aristarchus. A Thessalonian. Alluded to Acts 19:29; Acts 20:4; Acts 27:2. He was Paul 's companion for a part of the way on the journey to Rome. Demas. See Colossians 4:14; 2 Timothy 4:10. Luke. The physician and evangelist. See Introduction to Luke's Gospel.
