<p>That [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">οπως] </span></span>. Connect with making mention. </p>
<p>The communication of thy faith [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">η κοινωνια της πιστεως σου] </span></span>. Koinwnia fellowship is often used in the active sense of impartation, as communication, contribution, almsgiving. So <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ro+14:26">Romans 14:26</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="2co+9:13">2 Corinthians 9:13</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="heb+13:16">Hebrews 13:16</span>. This is the sense here : the active sympathy and charity growing out of your faith. </p>
<p>May become effectual [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ενεργης] </span></span>. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="jas+5:16">James 5:16</span>. This adjective, and the kindred ejnergew to work, be effectual, ejnerghma working, operation, and ejnergeia energy, power in exercise, are used in the New Testament only of superhuman power, good or evil. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="eph+1:19">Ephesians 1:19</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+14:2">Matthew 14:2</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="php+2:13">Philippians 2:13</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1co+12:10">1 Corinthians 12:10</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="heb+4:12">Hebrews 4:12</span>. In the knowledge [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εν επιγνωσει] </span></span>. In denotes the sphere or element in which Philemon's charity will become effective. His liberality and love will result in perfect knowledge of God 's good gifts. In the sphere of christian charity he will be helped to a full experience and appropriation of these. He that gives for Christ 's sake becomes enriched in the knowledge of Christ. Knowledge is full, perfect knowledge; an element of Paul 's prayer for his readers in all the four epistles of the captivity. In you. Read in us. </p>
<p>In Christ Jesus [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εις χριστον ιησουν] </span></span>. Connect with may become effectual, and render, as Rev., unto Christ; that is, unto Christ 's glory.</p>
