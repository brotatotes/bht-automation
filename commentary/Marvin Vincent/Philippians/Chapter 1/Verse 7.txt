<p>Even as [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">καθως] </span></span>. The reason for being confident (ver. 6). </p>
<p>Defense [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">απολογια] </span></span>. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1pe+3:15">1 Peter 3:15</span>. </p>
<p>Confirmation [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">βεβαιωσει] </span></span>. Only here and <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="heb+6:16">Hebrews 6:16</span>. The kindred verb bebaiow to confirm, occurs frequently, as <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ro+14:8">Romans 14:8</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1co+1:8">1 Corinthians 1:8</span>, etc. </p>
<p>Partakers of my grace [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">συγκοινωνους μου της χαριτος] </span></span>. Better, as Rev., partakers with me of grace. Lit., the grace, either the divine endowment which enabled them both to suffer bonds, and to defend and establish the Gospel, or the loving favor of God, which confers suffering and activity alike as a boon. The two may be combined. Compare ver. 29.</p>
