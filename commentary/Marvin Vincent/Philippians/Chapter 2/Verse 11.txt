<p>Confess [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εξομολογησεται] </span></span>. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+3:6">Matthew 3:6</span>; thank, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+11:25">Matthew 11:25</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ro+14:11">Romans 14:11</span>. The verb may also be rendered thank, as <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+11:25">Matthew 11:25</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="lu+10:21">Luke 10:21</span>, that meaning growing out of the sense of open, joyful acknowledgment. The sense here is that of frank, open confession. 180 To the glory, etc. Connect with confess.</p>
