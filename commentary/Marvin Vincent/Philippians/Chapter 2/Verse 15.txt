<p>May be - harmless [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">γενησθε - ακεραιοι] </span></span>. May be is rather may prove or show yourselves to be. Harmless, lit., unmixed. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+10:16">Matthew 10:16</span>. Better, guileless. Blameless in the sight of others, guileless in your own hearts. </p>
<p>Sons of God [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τεκνα] </span></span>. Rev., better, children. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="joh+1:12">John 1:12</span>. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="de+32:5">Deuteronomy 32:5</span>. </p>
<p>Without rebuke [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αμωμα] </span></span>. Rev., correctly, without blemish. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="col+1:22">Colossians 1:22</span>. The word is epexegetical of the two preceding epithets, unblemished in reputation and in reality. </p>
<p>Crooked and perverse [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">σκολιας - διεστραμμενης] </span></span>. Crooked, see on untoward, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+2:40">Acts 2:40</span>; froward, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1pe+2:18">1 Peter 2:18</span>. Perverse, lit., warped, twisted. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+17:17">Matthew 17:17</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="lu+23:14">Luke 23:14</span>. </p>
<p>Ye shine [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">φαινεσθε] </span></span>. Rev., more correctly, ye are seen. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+24:27">Matthew 24:27</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="re+18:23">Revelation 18:23</span>, A. V., where the same error occurs. Shine would require the verb in the active voice, as <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="joh+1:5">John 1:5</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="joh+5:35">John 5:35</span>. Lights [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">φωστηρες] </span></span>. Only here and <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="re+21:11">Revelation 21:11</span>, see note. Properly, luminaries. So Rev., in margin. Generally of the heavenly bodies. See <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ge+1:14">Genesis 1:14</span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ge+1:16">Genesis 1:16</span>, Sept. </p>
<p>In the world. Connect with ye are seen, not with luminaries. The world, not only material, but moral. For the moral sense of kosmov world, see on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="joh+1:9">John 1:9</span>.</p>
