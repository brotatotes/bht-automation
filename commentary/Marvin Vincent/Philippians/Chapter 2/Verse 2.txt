Fulfill [πληρωσατε] . Or complete. Compare John 3:29. Be like - minded [το αυτο φρονητε] . Lit., think the same thing. The expression is a general one for concord, and is defined in the two following clauses : unity of affection, the same love; unity of sentiment, of one accord. The general expression is then repeated in a stronger form, thinking the one thing. A. V. and Rev., of one mind.
