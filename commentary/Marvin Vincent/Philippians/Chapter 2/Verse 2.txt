<p>Fulfill [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πληρωσατε] </span></span>. Or complete. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="joh+3:29">John 3:29</span>. </p>
<p>Be like - minded [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">το αυτο φρονητε] </span></span>. Lit., think the same thing. The expression is a general one for concord, and is defined in the two following clauses : unity of affection, the same love; unity of sentiment, of one accord. The general expression is then repeated in a stronger form, thinking the one thing. A. V. and Rev., of one mind.</p>
