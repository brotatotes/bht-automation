<p>Epaphroditus. Mentioned only in this epistle. See on Epaphras, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="php+1:23">Philippians 1:23</span>. The name is derived from Aphrodite (Venus), and means charming. </p>
<p>Messenger [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αποστολον] </span></span>. The same word as apostle, one sent with a commission. </p>
<p>He that ministered [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">λειτουργον] </span></span>. Kindred with leitourgia service, in ver. 17. Rev., minister.</p>
