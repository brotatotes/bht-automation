Epaphroditus. Mentioned only in this epistle. See on Epaphras, Philippians 1:23. The name is derived from Aphrodite (Venus), and means charming. Messenger [αποστολον] . The same word as apostle, one sent with a commission. He that ministered [λειτουργον] . Kindred with leitourgia service, in ver. 17. Rev., minister.
