Look [σκοπουντες] . Attentively : fixing the attention upon, with desire for or interest in. So Romans 16:17; Philippians 3:17; 2 Corinthians 4:18. Hence often to aim at; compare skopov the mark, ch. 3 14. The participles esteeming and looking are used with the force of imperatives. See on Colossians 3:16.
