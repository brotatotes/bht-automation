<p>Look [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">σκοπουντες] </span></span>. Attentively : fixing the attention upon, with desire for or interest in. So <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ro+16:17">Romans 16:17</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="php+3:17">Philippians 3:17</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="2co+4:18">2 Corinthians 4:18</span>. Hence often to aim at; compare skopov the mark, ch. 3 14. The participles esteeming and looking are used with the force of imperatives. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="col+3:16">Colossians 3:16</span>.</p>
