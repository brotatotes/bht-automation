One thing. I do is supplied. Some supply I count, which is less appropriate, since what follows is concerned with action rather than with thinking or reckoning. Reaching forth [επεκτεινομενος] . Only here in the New Testament. Epi direction, after; ejk forth; teinw to stretch. Rev., stretching forward. The metaphor is that of the footrace. Bengel says : "The eye outstrips and draws onward the hand, and the hand the foot."
