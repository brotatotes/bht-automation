<p>One thing. I do is supplied. Some supply I count, which is less appropriate, since what follows is concerned with action rather than with thinking or reckoning. </p>
<p>Reaching forth [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επεκτεινομενος] </span></span>. Only here in the New Testament. </p>
<p>Epi direction, after; ejk forth; teinw to stretch. Rev., stretching forward. The metaphor is that of the footrace. Bengel says : "The eye outstrips and draws onward the hand, and the hand the foot."</p>
