Followers together of me [συμμιμηται μου] . Only here in the New Testament. Rev., more correctly, imitators. Compare 1 Corinthians 11:1. Not imitators of Christ in common with me, but be together, jointly, imitators of me. Mark [σκοπειτε] . See on looking, ch. 2 4. So as [ουτως καθως] . Rev., "which so walk even as ye have," etc. The two words are correlative. Briefly, imitate me and those who follow my example.
