<p>Belly. <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ro+16:18">Romans 16:18</span>. So the Cyclops in Euripides : "My flocks which I sacrifice to no one but myself, and not to the gods, and to this my belly the greatest of the gods : for to eat and drink each day, and to give one's self no trouble, this is the God for wise men" (" Cyclops, " 334 - 338). Glory. That which they esteem glory. </p>
