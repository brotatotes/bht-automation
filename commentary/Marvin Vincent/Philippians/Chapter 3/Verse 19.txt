Belly. Romans 16:18. So the Cyclops in Euripides : "My flocks which I sacrifice to no one but myself, and not to the gods, and to this my belly the greatest of the gods : for to eat and drink each day, and to give one's self no trouble, this is the God for wise men" (" Cyclops, " 334 - 338). Glory. That which they esteem glory. Earthly things [τα επιγεια] . See on 2 Corinthians 5:1. Compare Colossians 3:2.
