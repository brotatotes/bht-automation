<p>What things [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ατινα] </span></span>. The double relative classifies; things which came under the category of gain. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ga+4:24">Galatians 4:24</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="col+2:23">Colossians 2:23</span>. Gain [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κερδη] </span></span>. Lit., gains. So Rev., in margin, and better. The various items of privilege are regarded separately. </p>
<p>I counted loss [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ηγημαι ζημιαν] </span></span>. Better, as Rev., have counted. The perfect tense implies that he still counts them as loss. See on ver. 8. Notice the singular number loss, and the plural gains. The various gains are all counted as one loss.</p>
