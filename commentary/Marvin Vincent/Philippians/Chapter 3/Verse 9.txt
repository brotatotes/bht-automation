<p>Be found [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ευρεθω] </span></span>. Discovered or proved to be. See on ch. <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="php+2:8">Philippians 2:8</span>. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ro+7:10">Romans 7:10</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ga+2:17">Galatians 2:17</span>. </p>
<p>Mine own righteousness [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εμην δικαιοσυνην] </span></span>. Rev., correctly, a righteousness of mine own. The A. V. would require the article with ejmhn mine, and assumes the existence of a personal righteousness; whereas Paul says, not having any righteousness which can be called mine. </p>
<p>Which is of the law [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">την εκ νομου] </span></span>. Rev., better, even that which is of the law; thus bringing out the force of the article which defines the character of that righteousness which alone could be personal, viz., righteousness consisting in the strict fulfillment of the law. </p>
<p>Through the faith of Christ [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δια πιστεως χριστου] </span></span>. Rev., better, through faith in Christ. Faith as opposed to the law. The change of prepositions, through [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δια</span></span>] faith, and of [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εκ</span></span>] the law, as turning on the distinction between faith represented as the medium, and the law as the source of justification, cannot be insisted upon as a rule, since both the prepositions are used with faith, as in <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ga+2:16">Galatians 2:16</span>. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ro+3:30">Romans 3:30</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ro+5:1">Romans 5:1</span>. </p>
<p>Of God. Contrasted with my own. </p>
<p>By faith [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επι] </span></span>. Resting upon faith, or on the condition of. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+3:16">Acts 3:16</span>.</p>
