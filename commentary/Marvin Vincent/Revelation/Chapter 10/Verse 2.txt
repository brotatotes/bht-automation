<p>In his hand [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εν] </span></span>. The roll of Ch. 5 was on the hand (ejpi, see ch. <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="re+5:1">Revelation 5:1</span>), being too large to be grasped within it. The roll was on the right hand; the little book is in the left. See on ver. 5.</p>
