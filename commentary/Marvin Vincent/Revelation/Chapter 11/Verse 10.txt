Shall rejoice (carousin). Read cairousin, present tense, rejoice. Shall make merry [ευφρανθησονται] . Read eujfrainontai, present tense, make merry; and for the word see note on fared sumptuously, Luke 16:19. Shall send gifts. As on a day of festival. See Nehemiah 8:10, Nehemiah 8:12. Tormented (ejbasanisan). See on vexed, 2 Peter 2:8, and on Matthew 4:23, Matthew 4:24.
