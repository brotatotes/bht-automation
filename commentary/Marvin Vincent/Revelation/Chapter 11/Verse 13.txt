<p>Of men [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ονοματα ανθρωπων] </span></span>. Lit., names of men See on ch. <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="re+3:4">Revelation 3:4</span>. Gave glory to the God of heaven. The phrase signifies not conversion, nor repentance, nor thanksgiving, but recognition, which is its usual sense in scripture. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="jos+7:19">Joshua 7:19</span> (Sept.). <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="joh+9:24">John 9:24</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+12:23">Acts 12:23</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ro+4:20">Romans 4:20</span>.</p>
