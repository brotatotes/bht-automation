Overcame [ενικησαν] . See on 1 John 2:13. By the blood of the Lamb [δια το αιμα του αρνιου] . The preposition dia with the accusative signifies on account of. Hence Rev., correctly, because of : in virtue of the shedding of that blood. Similarly in the succeeding clause, "because of the word of their testimony." For lamb, see on ch. Revelation 5:6. Testimony [μαρτυριας] . See on John 1:7. They loved not their life even unto death. Alford, correctly, "they carried their not - love of their life even unto death."
