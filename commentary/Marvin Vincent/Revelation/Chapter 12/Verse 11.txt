<p>Overcame [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ενικησαν] </span></span>. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1jo+2:13">1 John 2:13</span>. </p>
<p>By the blood of the Lamb [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δια το αιμα του αρνιου] </span></span>. The preposition dia with the accusative signifies on account of. Hence Rev., correctly, because of : in virtue of the shedding of that blood. Similarly in the succeeding clause, "because of the word of their testimony." For lamb, see on ch. <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="re+5:6">Revelation 5:6</span>. </p>
<p>Testimony [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μαρτυριας] </span></span>. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="joh+1:7">John 1:7</span>. </p>
<p>They loved not their life even unto death. Alford, correctly, "they carried their not - love of their life even unto death."</p>
