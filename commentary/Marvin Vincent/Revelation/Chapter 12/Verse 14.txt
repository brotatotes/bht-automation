<p>Two wings. The definite article aiJ the should be added : "the two wings." Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ex+19:4">Exodus 19:4</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="de+32:11">Deuteronomy 32:11</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ps+36:7">Psalms 36:7</span>. </p>
<p>The great eagle. The article does not point to the eagle of ch. 8 13, but is generic. </p>
<p>A time and times and half a time. Three years and a half. See on ch. <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="re+11:2">Revelation 11:2</span>.</p>
