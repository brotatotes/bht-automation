Red [πυρρος] . See on ch. Revelation 6:4. Dragon [δρακων] . Satan. See ver. 9. The word is found only in Revelation. In the Septuagint, of the serpent into which Moses ' rod was changed. In Isaiah 27:1; Ezekiel 29:3, of the crocodile or leviathan of Job 41:1. In Jeremiah 51:34, of a dragon. Crowns [διαδηματα] . The Kingly crown, not the chaplet [στεφανος] . See on ch. Revelation 2:10
