A leopard [παρδαλει] . The ancients do not seem to have distinguished between the leopard, the panther, and the ounce. The word stands for either. Leopard is leo - pard, the lion - pard, which was supposed to be a mongrel between a panther and a lioness. Compare Daniel 7:6. Bear. Compare Daniel 7:5. Lion. Compare Daniel 7:4.
