<p>To continue forty and two months [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ποιησαι μηνας τεσσερακοντα δυο] </span></span>. Lit., to make forty and two months. Similarly, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+14:33">Acts 14:33</span>, poihsantev cronon having tarried a space; lit., having made a time. See on continued there a year, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="jas+4:13">James 4:13</span>. The best expositors, however, render poihsai absolutely, to work, and the following accusative as the accusative of duration, "during forty and two months." Rev., margin to do his works during, etc. See <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="da+11:28">Daniel 11:28</span>.</p>
