To continue forty and two months [ποιησαι μηνας τεσσερακοντα δυο] . Lit., to make forty and two months. Similarly, Acts 14:33, poihsantev cronon having tarried a space; lit., having made a time. See on continued there a year, James 4:13. The best expositors, however, render poihsai absolutely, to work, and the following accusative as the accusative of duration, "during forty and two months." Rev., margin to do his works during, etc. See Daniel 11:28.
