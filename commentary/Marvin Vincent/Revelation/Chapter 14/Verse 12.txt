<p>Here are they. Omit here are, and read, are, Rev., the patience of the saints, they that keep. </p>
<p>The faith of Jesus. Which has Jesus for its object. </p>
<p><span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="re+14:1">Revelation 14:1</span>8Blessed [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μακαριοι] </span></span>. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+5:3">Matthew 5:3</span>. </p>
<p>From henceforth [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">απ αρτι] </span></span>. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="joh+13:33">John 13:33</span>. To be joined as in A. V. and Rev., with die in the preceding clause, and not with blessed, nor with the following clause. Not from henceforth saith the Spirit. The meaning is variously explained. Some, from the beginning of the Christian age and onward to the end; others, from the moment of death, connecting henceforth with blessed; others from the time when the harvest of the earth is about to be reaped. Sophocles says : "Show all religious reverence to the gods, for all other things Father Zeus counts secondary; for the reward of piety follows men in death. Whether they live or die it passeth not away" (" Philoctetes, " 1441 - 1444). </p>
<p>That they may rest [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ινα αναπαυσωνται] </span></span>. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+11:28">Matthew 11:28</span>. The ina that gives the ground of the blessed. </p>
<p>Labors [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κοπων] </span></span>. From koptw to strike. Hence to beat the breast in grief. Kopov is, therefore, primarily, a smiting as a sign of sorrow, and then sorrow itself. As labor, it is labor which involves weariness and sorrow. Follow them [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ακολουθει μετ αυτων] </span></span>. Rather, accompany. Rev., follow with them. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+4:25">Matthew 4:25</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mr+3:7">Mark 3:7</span>, etc. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="joh+1:43">John 1:43</span>. </p>
<p><span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="re+14:1">Revelation 14:1</span>5Thrust in [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πεμψον] </span></span>. Lit., send. Rev., send forth. </p>
<p>Harvest [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">θερισμος] </span></span>. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="lu+10:2">Luke 10:2</span>. </p>
<p>Is ripe [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εξηρανθη] </span></span>. Lit., was dried. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mr+11:20">Mark 11:20</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="joh+14:6">John 14:6</span>. Rev., is over - ripe.</p>
