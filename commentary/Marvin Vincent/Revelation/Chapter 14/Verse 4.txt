Were not defiled [ουκ εμολυνθησαν] . The verb means properly to besmear or besmirch, and is never used in a good sense, as miainein (John 18:28; Jude 1:8), which in classical Greek is sometimes applied to staining with color. See on 1 Peter 1:4. Virgins [παρθενοι] . Either celibate or living in chastity whether in married or single life. See 1 Corinthians 7:1-7, 1 Corinthians 7:29; 2 Corinthians 11:2. First - fruits [απαρχη] . See on James 1:18.
