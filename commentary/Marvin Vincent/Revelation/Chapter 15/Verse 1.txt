<p>The seven last plagues [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πληγας επτα τας εσχατας] </span></span>. Lit., seven plagues the last. Rev., "which are the last." See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mr+3:10">Mark 3:10</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="lu+10:30">Luke 10:30</span>. Is filled up [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ετελεσθη] </span></span>. More correctly, brought to an end [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τελος] </span></span>. Rev., finished. Lit., was finished, the prophetic aorist, which speaks of a thing foreseen and decided as if already done.</p>
