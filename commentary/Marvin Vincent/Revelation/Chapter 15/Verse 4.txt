Who shall not fear Thee? See Jeremiah 10:7. Omit thee. Holy [οσιος] . See on Luke 1:75. The term is applied to Christ in Acts 2:27, Acts 2:35; Hebrews 7:26. To God only here and ch. 16 5, where the correct reading is oJ osiov thou holy one, instead of oJ ejsomenov which shalt be. All nations shall come. Compare Psalms 86:9; Isaiah 2:2-4; Isaiah 66:9 Isaiah 66:3; Micah 4:2. Judgments [δικαιωματα] . Not merely divine decisions, but righteous acts generally. So Rev. Primarily, the word signifies that which has been deemed right so as to have the force of law. Hence an ordinance (Luke 1:6; Hebrews 9:1; Romans 1:32). A judicial decision for or against (Romans 5:16). A righteous deed. See ch. Revelation 19:8.
