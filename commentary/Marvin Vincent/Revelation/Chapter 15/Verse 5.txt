Behold. Omit. The temple of the tabernacle [ο ναος της σκηνης] . The sanctuary of the tabernacle. See on Matthew 4:5. Of the testimony. See Acts 7:44. The tabernacle was called "the Tabernacle of the Testimony" because it contained the ark with the law of God which testifies against sin. See Exodus 25:16, Exodus 25:21; Exodus 30:36; Exodus 34:29; Exodus 38:21. Compare ch. 11 19.
