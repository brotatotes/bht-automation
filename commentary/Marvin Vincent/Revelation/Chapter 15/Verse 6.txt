<p>Linen [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">λινον] </span></span>. The Rev. follows the reading liqon stone, after the analogy of <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="eze+28:13">Ezekiel 28:13</span>, "Every precious stone was thy covering." The idea is that of raiment studded with precious stones. See on ch. <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="re+2:17">Revelation 2:17</span>. </p>
<p>White [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">λαυπρον] </span></span>. Mostly applied in the New Testament to clothing, as <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="lu+23:11">Luke 23:11</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+10:30">Acts 10:30</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="jas+2:2">James 2:2</span>. Also to the water of life (ch. 22 1), and the morning - star (ch. 22 16). Rev., bright. </p>
