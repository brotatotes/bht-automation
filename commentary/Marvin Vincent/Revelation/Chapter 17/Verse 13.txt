Mind [γνωμην] . Meaning primarily the faculty of knowing, mind, reason; then that which is thought or known; opinion, purpose. See Acts 20:3; 1 Corinthians 7:25; Philippians 1:14. Shall give [διαδιδωσουσιν] . didoasin, the present tense, give. The force of dia is over; give over. Power and authority [δυναμιν και εξουσιαν] . For the distinction, see on 2 Peter 2:11.
