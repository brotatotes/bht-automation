<p>Hath put [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εδωκεν] </span></span>. Rev., with stricter rendering of the aorist, did put. Lit., did give. </p>
<p>To fulfill His will [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ποιησαι την γνωμην αυτου] </span></span>. See on ver. 13. Rev., more literally, to do his mind. </p>
<p>To agree [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ποιησαι μιαν γνωμην] </span></span>. Lit., to make one mind. Rev., come to one mind. </p>
<p>The words [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τα ρηματα] </span></span>. But read oiJ logoi the prophetic words. For the distinction, see on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="lu+1:37">Luke 1:37</span>.</p>
