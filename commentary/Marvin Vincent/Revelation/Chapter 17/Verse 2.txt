<p>Have committed fornication. The figure of a harlot committing fornication with kings and peoples occurs frequently in the prophets, representing the defection of God 's Church and its attachment to others. See <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="isa+1:21">Isaiah 1:21</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="jer+2:20">Jeremiah 2:20</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="jer+3:1">Jeremiah 3:1</span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="jer+3:6">Jeremiah 3:6</span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="jer+3:8">Jeremiah 3:8</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="eze+16:15">Ezekiel 16:15</span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="eze+16:16">Ezekiel 16:16</span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="eze+16:28">Ezekiel 16:28</span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="eze+16:31">Ezekiel 16:31</span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="eze+16:35">Ezekiel 16:35</span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="eze+16:41">Ezekiel 16:41</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="eze+23:5">Ezekiel 23:5</span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="eze+23:1">Ezekiel 23:1</span> <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="eze+23:9">Ezekiel 23:9</span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="eze+23:44">Ezekiel 23:44</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ho+2:5">Hosea 2:5</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ho+3:3">Hosea 3:3</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ho+4:1">Hosea 4:1</span> <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ho+4:4">Hosea 4:4</span>.
