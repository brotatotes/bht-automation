<p>Merchandise [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">γομον] </span></span>. Only here, ver. 12, and <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+21:3">Acts 21:3</span>. From gemw to be full. Hence, literally, lading or cargo. So Rev., in margin. The main features of the following description are taken from that of the destruction of Tyre, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="eze+26:27">Ezekiel 26:27</span>.</p>
