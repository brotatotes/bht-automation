Shipmaster [κυβερνητης] . From kubernaw to govern. Strictly, steersman. Only here and Acts 27:11. All the company in ships [πας επι των πλοιων ο ομιλος] . The best texts substitute oJ ejpi topon plewn, that saileth anywhere, lit., saileth to a place. So Rev. Trade by sea [την θαλασσαν εργαζονται] . Lit., work the sea, like the Latin mare exercent, live by seafaring. Rev., gain their living by sea.
