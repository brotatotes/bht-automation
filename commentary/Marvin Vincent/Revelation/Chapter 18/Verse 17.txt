<p>Shipmaster [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κυβερνητης] </span></span>. From kubernaw to govern. Strictly, steersman. Only here and <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+27:11">Acts 27:11</span>. </p>
<p>All the company in ships [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πας επι των πλοιων ο ομιλος] </span></span>. The best texts substitute oJ ejpi topon plewn, that saileth anywhere, lit., saileth to a place. So Rev. </p>
<p>Trade by sea [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">την θαλασσαν εργαζονται] </span></span>. Lit., work the sea, like the Latin mare exercent, live by seafaring. Rev., gain their living by sea.</p>
