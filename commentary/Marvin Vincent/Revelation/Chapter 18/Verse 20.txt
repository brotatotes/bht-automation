Hath avenged you on her [εκρινεν το κριμα υμων εξ αυτης] . Rev., more literally, hath judged your judgment on her or from her. The idea is that of exacting judgment from [εξ] . Compare the compound verb ejkdikeiv avenge, or exact vengeance from (ch. 6 10). The meaning is either, that judgment which is your due, or what she hath judged concerning you.
