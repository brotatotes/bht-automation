<p>Hath avenged you on her [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εκρινεν το κριμα υμων εξ αυτης] </span></span>. Rev., more literally, hath judged your judgment on her or from her. The idea is that of exacting judgment from [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εξ] </span></span>. Compare the compound verb ejkdikeiv avenge, or exact vengeance from (ch. 6 10). The meaning is either, that judgment which is your due, or what she hath judged concerning you.</p>
