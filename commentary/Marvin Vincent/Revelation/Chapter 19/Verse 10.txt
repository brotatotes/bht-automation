See thou do it not [ορα μη] . See not (to do it). The testimony of Jesus [η μαρτυρια του ιησου] . Some explain as the testimony which proceeds from Jesus. Jesus, by imparting this testimony to believers imparts to them the spirit of prophecy. Others, the witness which is born to Jesus. The way of bearing this witness, the substance and essence of this testimony is the Spirit of prophecy.
