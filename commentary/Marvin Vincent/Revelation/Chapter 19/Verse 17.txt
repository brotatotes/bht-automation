An angel [ενα αγγελον] . Lit., "one angel." Fowls [ορνεοις] . See on ch. Revelation 18:2. Rev., birds. Midst of heaven. See on ch. Revelation 8:13. Gather yourselves together [συναγεσθε] . The best texts read sunacqhte be gathered together, as Rev. Compare Ezekiel 39:17 sqq. The supper of the great God [το δειπνον του μεγαλου θεου] . Read to mega tou for tou megalou, and render the great supper of God.
