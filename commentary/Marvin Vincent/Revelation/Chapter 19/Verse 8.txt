Fine linen [βυσσινον] . See on Luke 16:19. The four vestments of the ordinary Jewish priest were made of linen or byssus. Their symbolic meaning depended in part on the whiteness and luster of their substance [καθαρον και λαμπρον πυρε ανδ βριγητ] . Righteousness [δικαιωματα] . More strictly, as Rev. righteous acts.
