<p>Fine linen [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">βυσσινον] </span></span>. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="lu+16:19">Luke 16:19</span>. The four vestments of the ordinary Jewish priest were made of linen or byssus. Their symbolic meaning depended in part on the whiteness and luster of their substance [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">καθαρον και λαμπρον πυρε ανδ βριγητ] </span></span>. </p>
<p>Righteousness [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δικαιωματα] </span></span>. More strictly, as Rev. righteous acts.</p>
