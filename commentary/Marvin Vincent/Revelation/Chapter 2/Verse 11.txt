<p>Be hurt [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αδικηθη] </span></span>. Strictly, wronged. </p>
<p>Second death. An expression peculiar to the Revelation. See <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="re+20:6">Revelation 20:6</span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="re+20:14">Revelation 20:14</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="re+21:8">Revelation 21:8</span>. In those two passages it is defined as the lake of fire. The death awaiting the wicked after judgment.</p>
