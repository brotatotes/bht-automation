He laid hold [εκρατησεν] . See on Mark 8:3; Acts 3:11.. The dragon [τον δρακοντα] . See on ch. Revelation 12:3. The word is commonly derived from edrakon, the second aorist tense of derkomai to see clearly, in allusion to the sharp sight of the fabled dragon. Old [αρχαιον] . See on 1 John 2:7. The Devil. Note the three epithets : the Old Serpent, the Devil, Satan. See on Matthew 4:1; Luke 10:18.
