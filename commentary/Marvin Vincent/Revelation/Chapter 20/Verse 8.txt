<p>Gog and Magog. See <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="eze+38:39">Ezekiel 38:39</span>. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ge+10:2">Genesis 10:2</span>. where Magog appears as a son of Japhet. Magog is a general name for the northern nations, and, according to Ezekiel, Gog is their prince. Josephus says that the descendants of Magog were the Scythians.</p>
