<p>Jasper. See on ch. <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="re+4:3">Revelation 4:3</span>. </p>
<p>Clear as crystal [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κρυσταλλιζοντι] </span></span>. Lit., shining like crystal.</p>
