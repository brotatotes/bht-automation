The building [ενδομησις] . Only here in the New Testament. From ejn in and dwmaw to build. Lit., that which is built in. Hence the building of the wall is the material built into the wall; of which the wall was composed. Glass [υαλω] . Only here and ver. 21. For the kindred adjective uJalinov of glass, see on ch. Revelation 4:6.
