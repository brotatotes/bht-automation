<p>It is done [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">γεγονεν] </span></span>. The correct reading is gegonan they are come to pass; i e., these words. </p>
<p>Alpha and Omega. Both have the article, "the alpha," etc. See on ch. <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="re+1:8">Revelation 1:8</span>. Unto him that is athirst. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="isa+55:1">Isaiah 55:1</span>. </p>
<p>Fountain [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πηγης] </span></span>. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="joh+4:6">John 4:6</span>. </p>
<p>Of the water of life. See <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="joh+4:10">John 4:10</span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="joh+4:14">John 4:14</span>. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="isa+12:3">Isaiah 12:3</span>.</p>
