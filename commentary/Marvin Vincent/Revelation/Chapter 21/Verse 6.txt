It is done [γεγονεν] . The correct reading is gegonan they are come to pass; i e., these words. Alpha and Omega. Both have the article, "the alpha," etc. See on ch. Revelation 1:8. Unto him that is athirst. Compare Isaiah 55:1. Fountain [πηγης] . See on John 4:6. Of the water of life. See John 4:10, John 4:14. Compare Isaiah 12:3.
