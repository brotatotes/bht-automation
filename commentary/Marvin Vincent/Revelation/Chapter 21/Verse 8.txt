The fearful [δειλοις] . The dative case. Hence, as Rev., for the fearful. Only here, Matthew 8:26, and Mark 4:40. Abominable [εβδελυγμενοις] . See on abomination, Matthew 24:15. Properly, defiled with abominations. Whoremongers [πορνοις] . Much better, as Rev., fornicators. Sorcerers. See on sorceries, ch. 9 21. Shall have their part [το μερος αυτων] . Lit., the whole passage reads : to the fearful, etc., their part. shall be is supplied.
