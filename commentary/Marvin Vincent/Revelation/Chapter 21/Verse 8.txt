<p>The fearful [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δειλοις] </span></span>. The dative case. Hence, as Rev., for the fearful. Only here, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+8:26">Matthew 8:26</span>, and <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mr+4:40">Mark 4:40</span>. </p>
<p>Abominable [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εβδελυγμενοις] </span></span>. See on abomination, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+24:15">Matthew 24:15</span>. Properly, defiled with abominations. </p>
<p>Whoremongers [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πορνοις] </span></span>. Much better, as Rev., fornicators. </p>
<p>Sorcerers. See on sorceries, ch. 9 21. </p>
<p>Shall have their part [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">το μερος αυτων] </span></span>. Lit., the whole passage reads : to the fearful, etc., their part. shall be is supplied.</p>
