My reward is with me [ο μισθος μου μετ εμου] . Misqov reward is strictly wages. Compare Isaiah 40:10; Isaiah 61:11. See on 2 Peter 2:13. To give [αποδουναι] . Lit., to give back or in return for, thus appropriate to misqov reward. Hence Rev., better, render. See on give an account, Luke 16:2; and gave, Acts 4:33. Shall be [εσται] . Read ejstin is.
