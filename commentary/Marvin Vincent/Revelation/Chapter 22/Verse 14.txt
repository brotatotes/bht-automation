That do His commandments [οι ποιουντες τας εντολας αυτου] . Read oiJ plunontev tav stolav aujtwn they that wash their robes. Compare ch. 7 14. That they may have right to the tree of life [ινα εσται η εξουσια αυτων επι το ξυλον της ζωης] . Lit., in order that theirs shall be authority over the tree of life. For ejxousia right, authority, see on John 1:12. Epi may be the preposition of direction : "may have right to come to" (so Rev.) or may be rendered over.
