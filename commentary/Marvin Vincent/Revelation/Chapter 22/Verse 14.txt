<p>That do His commandments [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">οι ποιουντες τας εντολας αυτου] </span></span>. Read oiJ plunontev tav stolav aujtwn they that wash their robes. Compare ch. 7 14. </p>
<p>That they may have right to the tree of life [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ινα εσται η εξουσια αυτων επι το ξυλον της ζωης] </span></span>. Lit., in order that theirs shall be authority over the tree of life. For ejxousia right, authority, see on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="joh+1:12">John 1:12</span>. Epi may be the preposition of direction : "may have right to come to" (so Rev.) or may be rendered over.</p>
