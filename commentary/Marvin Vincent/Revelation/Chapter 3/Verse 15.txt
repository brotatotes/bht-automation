<p>Cold [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ψυχρος] </span></span>. Attached to the world and actively opposed to the Church. "This," as Alford remarks, "as well as the opposite state of spiritual fervor, would be an intelligible and plainly - marked condition; at all events free from the danger of mixed motive and disregarded principle which belongs to the lukewarm state : inasmuch as a man in earnest, be he right or wrong, is ever a better man than one professing what he does not feel." </p>
<p>Hot [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ζεστος] </span></span>. From zew to boil or seethe. See on fervent, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+18:25">Acts 18:25</span>.</p>
