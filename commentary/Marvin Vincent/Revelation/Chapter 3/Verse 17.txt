Because thou sayest. Connect, as A. V. and Rev., with what follows, not with what precedes. Some interpret I will spue thee out of my mouth because thou sayest, etc. Increased with goods [πεπλουτηκα] . Rev., have gotten riches. The reference is to imagined spiritual riches, not to worldly possessions. Thou. Emphatic. Wretched [ο ταλαιπωρος] . Rev., better, giving the force of the article, the wretched one. From tlaw to endure, and peira a trial. Miserable [ελεεινος] . Only here and 1 Corinthians 5:19. An object of pity [ελεος] . Poor [πτωχος] . See on Matthew 5:3.
