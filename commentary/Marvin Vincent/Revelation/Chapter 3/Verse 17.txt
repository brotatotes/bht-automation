<p>Because thou sayest. Connect, as A. V. and Rev., with what follows, not with what precedes. Some interpret I will spue thee out of my mouth because thou sayest, etc. </p>
<p>Increased with goods [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πεπλουτηκα] </span></span>. Rev., have gotten riches. The reference is to imagined spiritual riches, not to worldly possessions. Thou. Emphatic. </p>
<p>Wretched [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ο ταλαιπωρος] </span></span>. Rev., better, giving the force of the article, the wretched one. From tlaw to endure, and peira a trial. </p>
<p>Miserable [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ελεεινος] </span></span>. Only here and <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1co+5:19">1 Corinthians 5:19</span>. An object of pity [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ελεος] </span></span>. </p>
<p>Poor [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πτωχος] </span></span>. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+5:3">Matthew 5:3</span>.</p>
