<p>I will make [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">διδωμι] </span></span>. Rev., rightly, I give. See on verse 8. The sense is broken off there and resumed here. </p>
<p>Of the synagogue [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εκ της συναγωγης] </span></span>. Certain ones of the synagogue. Most interpreters refer to the Jews. Others explain more generally, of the bowing down of the Church 's enemies at her feet. Trench refers to a passage in the Epistle of Ignatius to this Philadelphian church, implying the actual presence in the midst of it of converts from Judaism, who preached the faith which they once persecuted. </p>
<p>Of Satan. See on chapter <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="re+2:9">Revelation 2:9</span>. </p>
<p>I will make them to come [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ποιησω αυτους ινα ηξωσιν</span></span>] Lit., I will make them that they shall come. </p>
<p>Worship before thy feet. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="isa+60:14">Isaiah 60:14</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="isa+49:23">Isaiah 49:23</span>.</p>
