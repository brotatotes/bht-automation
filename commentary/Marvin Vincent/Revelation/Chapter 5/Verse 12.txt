<p>Power, etc. Rev., "the power." Compare the ascription in chapter <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="re+4:11">Revelation 4:11</span>, on which see note, and notice that each separate particular there has the article, while here it is attached only to the first, the power, the one article including all the particulars, as if they formed but one word. On the doxologies, see on chapter <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="re+1:6">Revelation 1:6</span>. </p>
<p>Riches [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πλουτον] </span></span>. Not limited to spiritual riches, but denoting the fulness of every gift of God. <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="jas+1:17">James 1:17</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+17:25">Acts 17:25</span>. Only here in a doxology. Blessing [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ευλογιαν] </span></span>. See on the kindred word eujloghtov blessed, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1pe+1:3">1 Peter 1:3</span>.</p>
