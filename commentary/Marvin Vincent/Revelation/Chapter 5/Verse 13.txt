Creature [κτισμα] . See 1 Timothy 4:4; James 1:18. From ktizw to found. A thing founded or created Rev., created thing. See on John 1:3. In the sea [επι της θαλασσης] . More accurately, "on the sea," as Rev. Not ships, but creatures of the sea which have come up from its depths to the surface. Blessing [η ευλογια] . Rev. rightly "the blessing." All the particulars of the following ascription have the article. Honor [τιμη] . Originally a valuing by which the price is fixed, hence the price itself, the thing priced, and so, generally, honor. See on Acts 28:10. Power [το κρατος] . Rev., the dominion. For the different words for power, see on 2 Peter 2:11.
