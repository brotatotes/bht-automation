<p>Creature [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κτισμα] </span></span>. See <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1ti+4:4">1 Timothy 4:4</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="jas+1:18">James 1:18</span>. From ktizw to found. A thing founded or created Rev., created thing. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="joh+1:3">John 1:3</span>. In the sea [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επι της θαλασσης] </span></span>. More accurately, "on the sea," as Rev. Not ships, but creatures of the sea which have come up from its depths to the surface. </p>
<p>Blessing [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">η ευλογια] </span></span>. Rev. rightly "the blessing." All the particulars of the following ascription have the article. </p>
<p>Honor [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τιμη] </span></span>. Originally a valuing by which the price is fixed, hence the price itself, the thing priced, and so, generally, honor. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+28:10">Acts 28:10</span>. Power [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">το κρατος] </span></span>. Rev., the dominion. For the different words for power, see on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="2pe+2:11">2 Peter 2:11</span>.</p>
