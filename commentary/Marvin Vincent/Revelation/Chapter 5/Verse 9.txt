They sing. Present tense, denoting the continuous, unceasing worship of heaven, or possibly, as describing their "office generally rather than the mere one particular case of its exercise" (Alford). Redeem [ηγορασας] . Lit., purchase, as Rev. See John 4:8; John 6:5. Us. Omit us and supply men, as Rev. With Thy blood [εν τω αιματι σου] . Lit., "in Thy blood." The preposition in is used Hebraistically of the price; the value of the thing purchased being contained in the price. Kindred [φυλης] . Rev., tribe. Often in the New Testament of the twelve tribes of Israel. People, nation [λαου, εθνους] . See on 1 Peter 2:9.
