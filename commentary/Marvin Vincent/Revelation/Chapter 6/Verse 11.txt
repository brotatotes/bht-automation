<p>White robes were given unto every one of them [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εδοθησαν εκαστοις στολαι λευκαι] </span></span>. The best texts read ejdoqh aujtoiv eJkastw stolh leukh there was given them to each one a white robe. So Rev. Stolh is properly a long, flowing robe; a festive garment. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mr+16:5">Mark 16:5</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="lu+14:22">Luke 14:22</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="lu+20:46">Luke 20:46</span>. </p>
<p>Should rest [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αναπαυσωνται] </span></span>. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+11:28">Matthew 11:28</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1pe+5:14">1 Peter 5:14</span>; compare chapter <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="re+14:13">Revelation 14:13</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="da+12:13">Daniel 12:13</span>. Not merely rest from their crying for vengeance, but rest in peace. </p>
<p>Fellow - servants. See Master in verse 10. </p>
<p>Should be fulfilled [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πληρωσονται] </span></span>. Completed in number. See <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="col+2:10">Colossians 2:10</span>. Some texts read plhrwswsin shall have fulfilled their course.</p>
