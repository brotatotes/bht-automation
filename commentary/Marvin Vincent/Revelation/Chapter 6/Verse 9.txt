Altar [θυσιαστηριου] . See on Acts 17:23. The altar of sacrifice, as is indicated by slain; not the altar of incense. The imagery is from the tabernacle. Exodus 39:39; Exodus 40:29. Souls [ψυχας] . Or lives. See on 3 John 1:2. He saw only blood, but blood and life were equivalent terms to the Hebrew. Slain [εσφαγμενων] . See on chapter Revelation 5:6. The law commanded that the blood of sacrificed animals should be poured out at the bottom of the altar of burnt - offering (Leviticus 4:7). They held [ειχον] . Not held fast, but bore the testimony which was committed to them.
