<p>Altar [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">θυσιαστηριου] </span></span>. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+17:23">Acts 17:23</span>. The altar of sacrifice, as is indicated by slain; not the altar of incense. The imagery is from the tabernacle. <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ex+39:39">Exodus 39:39</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ex+40:29">Exodus 40:29</span>. </p>
<p>Souls [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ψυχας] </span></span>. Or lives. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="3jo+1:2">3 John 1:2</span>. He saw only blood, but blood and life were equivalent terms to the Hebrew. </p>
<p>Slain [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εσφαγμενων] </span></span>. See on chapter <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="re+5:6">Revelation 5:6</span>. The law commanded that the blood of sacrificed animals should be poured out at the bottom of the altar of burnt - offering (<span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="le+4:7">Leviticus 4:7</span>). </p>
<p>They held [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ειχον] </span></span>. Not held fast, but bore the testimony which was committed to them.</p>
