<p>Answered. In the sense of taking up speech in connection with some given occasion, as <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+11:25">Matthew 11:25</span>. See also on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="joh+2:18">John 2:18</span>. </p>
<p>What are these, etc. The Rev., properly, follows the Greek order, which places first "These which are arrayed in the white robes, who are they?" emphatic and indicating the natural order of the thought as it presents itself to the inquirer. For what, render who, as Rev.</p>
