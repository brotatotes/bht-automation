Therefore. Because of this washing. Before the throne. Compare Ephesians 5:27. Serve [λατρευουσιν] . See on Luke 1:74. In scripture the verb never expresses any other service but that of the true God, or of the gods of heathenism. Temple [ναω] . Or sanctuary. See on Matthew 4:5. Dwell [σκηνωσει] . From skhnh a tent or tabernacle. Hence better, as Rev., shall spread His tabernacle. See on John 1:14, and compare Leviticus 26:11; Isaiah 4:5, Isaiah 4:6; Ezekiel 37:27.
