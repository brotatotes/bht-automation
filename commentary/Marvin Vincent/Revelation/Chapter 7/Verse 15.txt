<p>Therefore. Because of this washing. </p>
<p>Before the throne. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="eph+5:27">Ephesians 5:27</span>. </p>
<p>Serve [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">λατρευουσιν] </span></span>. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="lu+1:74">Luke 1:74</span>. In scripture the verb never expresses any other service but that of the true God, or of the gods of heathenism. </p>
<p>Temple [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ναω] </span></span>. Or sanctuary. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+4:5">Matthew 4:5</span>. </p>
<p>Dwell [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">σκηνωσει] </span></span>. From skhnh a tent or tabernacle. Hence better, as Rev., shall spread His tabernacle. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="joh+1:14">John 1:14</span>, and compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="le+26:11">Leviticus 26:11</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="isa+4:5">Isaiah 4:5</span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="isa+4:6">Isaiah 4:6</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="eze+37:27">Ezekiel 37:27</span>.</p>
