In the midst [ανα μεσον] . See on chapter Revelation 5:6. Shall feed [ποιμανει] . See on shall be shepherd of, Matthew 2:6; Acts 20:28; 1 Peter 5:2. Compare Psalms 23:1. Shall lead [οδηγησει] . See on Luke 6:39. Living fountains of waters [ζωσας πηγας υδατων] . For the participle living, read zwhv of life, and render as Rev., fountains of waters of life. Compare Psalms 23:2. In the Greek order, of life stands first as emphatic. All tears [παν δακρυον] . Rev., correctly, every tear. (Compare Isaiah 25:8.
