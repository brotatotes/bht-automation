When [οτε] . Read otan, the indefinite particle with the indicative mood. For a similar construction, see Mark 11:19 (correct reading). Alford observes that it occurs in the opening of this seal only, giving it an indefiniteness which does not belong to any of the rest. There was [εγενετο] . More literally, come to pass. Rev., there followed. About [ως] . A usual form of expression with John. See John 1:39; John 6:19; John 11:18.
