Of the horsemen [του ιππικου] . Singular number, like the English the horse or the cavalry. Two hundred thousand thousand [δυο μυριαδες μυριαδων] . Lit., two ten - thousands of ten - thousands. See on ch. Revelation 5:11. Rev., twice ten - thousand times ten - thousand. Compare Psalms 68:17; Daniel 7:10; Hebrews 12:22; Jude 1:14.
