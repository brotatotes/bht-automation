Their power [εξουσιαι αυτων] . Read ejxousia twn ippwn the power of the horses. Like unto serpents. "Long, smooth, subtle, clasping their victim in an embrace from which he cannot escape" (Milligan). As one of the innumerable fantasies of Apocalyptic exposition may be cited that of Elliott (" Horsae Apocalypticae ") who finds a reference to the horse tails, the symbols of authority of the Turkish pashas.
