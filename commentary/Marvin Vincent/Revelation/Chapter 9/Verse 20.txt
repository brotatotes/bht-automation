Repented not of the works [ουτε μετενοησαν εκ των εργων] . Lit., "Out of the works." The preposition ejk out of with repent, denotes a moral change involving an abandonment of evil works. See on Matthew 3:2; Matthew 21:29. Works of their hands. Not their course of life, but the idols which their hands had made. Compare Deuteronomy 4:28; Psalms 134:15; Acts 7:4. Devils [δαιμονια] . More properly, demons. See on Mark 1:34. Compare 1 Corinthians 10:20; 1 Timothy 4:1. See, hear, walk. Compare Daniel 5:23.
