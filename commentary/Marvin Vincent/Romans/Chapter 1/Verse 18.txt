<p>For. All men require this mode of justification, for all men are sinners, and therefore exposed to God 's wrath. </p>
<p>The wrath of God [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">οργη θεου] </span></span>. Not punishment, but the personal emotion. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="joh+3:36">John 3:36</span>. </p>
<p>Ungodliness and unrighteousness [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ασεβειαν και αδικιαν] </span></span>. </p>
<p>Irreligiousness and immorality. See on godliness, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="2pe+1:3">2 Peter 1:3</span>; also <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="2pe+2:13">2 Peter 2:13</span>. </p>
<p>Hold [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κατεχοντων] </span></span>. Not possess : compare ver. 21. Rev., correctly, hold down; i e., hinder or i. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="2th+2:6">2 Thessalonians 2:6</span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="2th+2:7">2 Thessalonians 2:7</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="lu+4:42">Luke 4:42</span>. </p>
<p>The truth. Divine truth generally, as apparent in all God 's self - revelations.</p>
