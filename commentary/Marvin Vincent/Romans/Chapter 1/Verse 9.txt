<p>I serve [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">λατρευω] </span></span>. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="lu+1:74">Luke 1:74</span>. The word was used in a special sense to denote the service rendered to Jehovah by the Israelites as His peculiar people. See <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ro+9:4">Romans 9:4</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+26:7">Acts 26:7</span>. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="heb+9:1">Hebrews 9:1</span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="heb+9:6">Hebrews 9:6</span>. As in his Philippian letter, Paul here appropriates the Jewish word for the spiritual Christian service. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="php+3:3">Philippians 3:3</span>.</p>
