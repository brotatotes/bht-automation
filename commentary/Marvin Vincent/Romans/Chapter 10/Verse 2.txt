<p>I bear them record [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μαρτυρω] </span></span>. Rev. witness. "He seems to be alluding to his conduct of former days, and to say, 'I know something of it, of that zeal '" (Godet). </p>
<p>Zeal of God [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ζηλον θεου] </span></span>. Rev., zeal for God. Like the phrase "faith of Christ" for "faith in Christ" (<span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="php+3:9">Philippians 3:9</span>); compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="col+2:12">Colossians 2:12</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="eph+3:12">Ephesians 3:12</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="joh+2:17">John 2:17</span>, "the zeal of thine house," i e., "for thy house." Knowledge [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επιγνωσιν] </span></span>. Full or correct and vital knowledge. See on ch. <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ro+1:28">Romans 1:28</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ro+3:20">Romans 3:20</span>.</p>
