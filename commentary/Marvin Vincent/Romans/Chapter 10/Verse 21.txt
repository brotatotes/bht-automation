<p>Disobedient - gainsaying [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">απειθουντα - αντιλεγοντα] </span></span>. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="joh+3:36">John 3:36</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="jude+1:11">Jude 1:11</span>. Disobedience is the manifestation of the refractoriness expressed in gainsaying. Some explain gainsaying as contradicting. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="lu+13:34">Luke 13:34</span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="lu+13:35">Luke 13:35</span>. </p>
