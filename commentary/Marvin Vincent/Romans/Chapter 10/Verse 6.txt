Paul recognizes a secondary meaning in Moses ' words, and thus changes the original expressions so as to apply them to the Christian faith - system. His object in the change is indicated by the explanatory words which he adds. He does not formally declare that Moses describes the righteousness of faith in these words, but appropriates the words of Moses, putting them into the mouth of the personified faith - righteousness.
