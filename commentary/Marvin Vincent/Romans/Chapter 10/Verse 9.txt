<p>That [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">οτι] </span></span>. So rendered as expressing the contents of the word of faith; but better because, giving a proof that the word is nigh. Confess and believe, correspond to mouth and heart. </p>
<p>The Lord Jesus [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κυριον ιησουν] </span></span>. Others, however, read to rJhma ejn tw stomati sou oti kuriov Ihsouv If thou shalt confess with thy mouth the word that Jesus is Lord. Rev., Jesus as Lord.</p>
