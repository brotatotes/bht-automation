<p>The casting away [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">η αποβολη] </span></span>. In contrast with receiving. Only here and <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+27:22">Acts 27:22</span>, where it means loss. Here exclusion from God 's people. Reconciling of the world [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">καταλλαγη κοσμου] </span></span>. See on ch. <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ro+5:10">Romans 5:10</span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ro+5:11">Romans 5:11</span>. Defining the phrase riches of the world in ver. 12. </p>
<p>Life from the dead. The exact meaning cannot be determined. Some refer it to the resurrection to follow the conversion of Israel, including the new life which the resurrection will inaugurate. Others, a new spiritual life. Others combine the two views.</p>
