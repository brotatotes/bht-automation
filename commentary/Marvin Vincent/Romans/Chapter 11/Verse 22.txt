<p>Goodness and severity [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">χρηστοτητα και αποτομιαν] </span></span>. For goodness, see on ch. <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ro+3:12">Romans 3:12</span>. Apotomia severity, only here in the New Testament. The kindred adverb, ajpotomwv sharply, occurs <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="2co+13:10">2 Corinthians 13:10</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="tit+1:13">Titus 1:13</span>. From ajpotemnw to cut off. Hence that which is abrupt, sharp. </p>
<p>Thou shalt be cut off [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εκκοπηση] </span></span>. Lit., cut out. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="lu+13:7">Luke 13:7</span>.</p>
