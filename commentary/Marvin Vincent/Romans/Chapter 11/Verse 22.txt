Goodness and severity [χρηστοτητα και αποτομιαν] . For goodness, see on ch. Romans 3:12. Apotomia severity, only here in the New Testament. The kindred adverb, ajpotomwv sharply, occurs 2 Corinthians 13:10; Titus 1:13. From ajpotemnw to cut off. Hence that which is abrupt, sharp. Thou shalt be cut off [εκκοπηση] . Lit., cut out. See on Luke 13:7.
