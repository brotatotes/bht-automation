<p>Who hath known, etc. From <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="isa+40:13">Isaiah 40:13</span>. Heb., Who hath measured the Spirit? Though measured may be rendered tried, proved, regulated. Compare the same citation in <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1co+2:16">1 Corinthians 2:16</span>. This is the only passage in the Septuagint where ruach spirit is translated by nouv mind. Known [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εγνω</span></span>] may refer to God 's gnwsiv knowledge and ways in ver. 33; counselor to His wisdom and judgments. No one has counseled with Him in forming His decisions.</p>
