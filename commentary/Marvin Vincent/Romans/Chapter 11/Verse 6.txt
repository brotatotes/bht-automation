<p>Otherwise [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επει] </span></span>. Lit., since. Since, in that case. </p>
<p>Grace is no more, etc. [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">γινεται] </span></span>. Lit., becomes. No longer comes into manifestation as what it really is. "It gives up its specific character" (Meyer). </p>
<p>But if of works, etc. The best texts omit to the end of the verse.</p>
