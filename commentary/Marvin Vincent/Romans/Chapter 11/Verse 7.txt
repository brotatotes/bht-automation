<p>Obtained [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επετυχεν] </span></span>. The simple verb tugcanw means originally to hit the mark; hence to fall in with, light upon, attain. </p>
<p>The election [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">η εκλογη] </span></span>. Abstract for concrete. Those elected; like hJ peritomh the circumcision for those uncircumcised (<span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="eph+2:11">Ephesians 2:11</span>. Compare thn katatomhn the concision, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="php+3:3">Philippians 3:3</span>). </p>
<p>Were blinded [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επωρωθησαν] </span></span>. Rev., correctly, hardened, though the word is used of blindness when applied to the eyes, as <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="job+17:7">Job 17:7</span>, Sept. See on hardness, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mr+3:5">Mark 3:5</span>. Compare sklhrunei hardeneth, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ro+9:18">Romans 9:18</span>.</p>
