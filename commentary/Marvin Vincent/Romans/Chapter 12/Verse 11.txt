Slothful [οκνηροι] . From ojknew to delay. In business [τη σπουδη] . Wrong. Render, as Rev., in diligence; see on ver. 8. Luther, "in regard to zeal be not lazy." Fervent [ζεοντες] . See on Acts 18:25. The Lord [τω κυριω] . Some texts read kairw the time or opportunity, but the best authorities give Lord.
