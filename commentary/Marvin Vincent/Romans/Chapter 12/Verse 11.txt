<p>Slothful [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">οκνηροι] </span></span>. From ojknew to delay. </p>
<p>In business [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τη σπουδη] </span></span>. Wrong. Render, as Rev., in diligence; see on ver. 8. Luther, "in regard to zeal be not lazy." </p>
<p>Fervent [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ζεοντες] </span></span>. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+18:25">Acts 18:25</span>. </p>
<p>The Lord [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τω κυριω] </span></span>. Some texts read kairw the time or opportunity, but the best authorities give Lord.</p>
