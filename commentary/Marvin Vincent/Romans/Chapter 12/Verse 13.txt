<p>Distributing [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κοινωνουντες] </span></span>. Rev., communicating to. The meaning is sharing in the necessities; taking part in them as one's own. So <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ro+14:27">Romans 14:27</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1ti+5:22">1 Timothy 5:22</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="2jo+1:11">2 John 1:11</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="heb+2:14">Hebrews 2:14</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1pe+4:13">1 Peter 4:13</span>. See on partners, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="lu+5:10">Luke 5:10</span>; fellowship, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+2:42">Acts 2:42</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1jo+1:3">1 John 1:3</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="2jo+1:11">2 John 1:11</span>. Given to hospitality [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">φιλοξενιαν διωκοντες] </span></span>. Lit., pursuing hospitality. For a similar use of the verb compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1co+14:1">1 Corinthians 14:1</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1th+5:15">1 Thessalonians 5:15</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="heb+12:14">Hebrews 12:14</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1pe+3:11">1 Peter 3:11</span>. A necessary injunction when so many Christians were banished and persecuted. The verb indicates not only that hospitality is to be furnished when sought, but that Christians are to seek opportunities of exercising it.</p>
