<p>Conformed - transformed [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">συσχηματιζεσθε - μεταμορφουσθε] </span></span>. </p>
<p>See on was transfigured, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+17:2">Matthew 17:2</span>. For conformed to, Rev., correctly, fashioned according to. </p>
<p>Mind [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">νοος] </span></span>. See on ch. <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ro+7:23">Romans 7:23</span>. Agreeing with reasonable service. That good and acceptable and perfect will. Better to render the three adjectives as appositional. "May prove what is the will of God, what is good," etc. The other rendering compels us to take well - pleasing in the sense of agreeable to men.</p>
