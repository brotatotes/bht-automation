<p>Being many [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">οι πολλοι] </span></span>. Lit., the many. Rev., better, who are many. Every one [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">το δε καθ εις] </span></span>. The literal phrase can only be rendered awkwardly : and as to what is true according to one; i e., individually, severally. Compare, for a similar phrase, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mr+14:19">Mark 14:19</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="joh+8:9">John 8:9</span>.</p>
