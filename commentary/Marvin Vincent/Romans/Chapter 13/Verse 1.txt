Every soul. Every man. See on ch. Romans 11:3. Higher powers [εξουσιαις υπερεχουσαις] . Lit., authorities which have themselves over. See on Mark 2:10; John 1:12. The powers that be [αι δε ουσαι] . Lit., the existing. Powers is not in the text, and is supplied from the preceding clause. Are ordained [τεταγμεναι εισιν] . Perfect tense : Have been ordained, and the ordinance remains in force. See on set under authority, Luke 7:8.
