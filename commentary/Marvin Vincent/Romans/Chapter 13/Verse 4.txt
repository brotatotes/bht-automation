<p>Beareth [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">φορει] </span></span>. Beareth and weareth. A frequentative form of ferw to bear. </p>
<p>Sword [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μαχαιραν] </span></span>. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="re+6:4">Revelation 6:4</span>. Borne as the symbol of the magistrate 's right to inflict capital punishment. Thus Ulpian : "They who rule whole provinces have the right of the sword (jus gladii)." The Emperor Trajan presented to a provincial governor, on starting for his province, a dagger, with the words, "For me. If I deserve it, in me."</p>
