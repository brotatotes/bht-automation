<p>Weak in the faith. Probably referring to a class of Jewish Christians with Essenic tendencies. 67 Better, as Rev., in faith, the reference being to faith in Christ, not to christian doctrine. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+6:7">Acts 6:7</span>. </p>
<p>Receive ye [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">προσλαμβανεσθε] </span></span>. Into fellowship. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+16:22">Matthew 16:22</span>. Doubtful disputations [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">διακρισεις διαλογισμων] </span></span>. Lit., judgings of thoughts. The primary meaning of dialogismov is a thinking - through or over. Hence of those speculations or reasonings in one's mind which take the form of scruples. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mr+7:21">Mark 7:21</span>. Diakrisiv has the same sense as in the other two passages where it occurs (<span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1co+12:10">1 Corinthians 12:10</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="heb+5:14">Hebrews 5:14</span>); discerning with a view to forming a judgment. Hence the meaning is, "receive these weak brethren, but not for the purpose of passing judgment upon their scruples."</p>
