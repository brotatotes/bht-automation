Why dost thou judge [συ τι κρινεις] . Thou emphatic, in contrast with the Lord. So Rev., "thou, why dost thou Judge ?" Referring to the weak brother. Compare judge as in ver. 4. The servant of another is here called brother. Judgment seat of Christ [τω βηματι του χριστου] . The best texts read Qeou of God So Rev. For judgment - seat, see on to set his foot on, Acts 7:5.
