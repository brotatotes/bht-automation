<p>Stumbling - block [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">προσκομμα] </span></span>. Compare ch. <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ro+9:32">Romans 9:32</span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ro+9:33">Romans 9:33</span>; 45 20. Skandalon occasion of falling is also rendered stumbling - block in other passages. Some regard the two as synonymous, others as related to different results in the case of the injured brother. So Godet, who refers stumbling - block to that which results in a wound, and cause of stumbling to that which causes a fall or sin.</p>
