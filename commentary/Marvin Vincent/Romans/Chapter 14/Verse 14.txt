<p>I know - am persuaded [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">οιδα - πεπεισμαι] </span></span>. "A rare conjunction of words, but fitted here to confirm against ignorance and doubt" (Bengel). For I know, see on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="joh+2:4">John 2:4</span>. The persuasion is not the result of his own reasoning, but of his fellowship in the Lord Jesus. So Rev, for by the Lord, etc. </p>
<p>Unclean [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κοινον] </span></span>. Lit., common. In the Levitical sense, as opposed to holy or pure. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mr+7:2">Mark 7:2</span>, "With defiled [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κοιναις χομμον] </span></span>, that is to say, with unwashen hands." See <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+10:14">Acts 10:14</span>.</p>
