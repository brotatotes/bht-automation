<p>Be grieved [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">λυπειται</span></span>] The close connection with destroy indicates that the meaning falls short of be destroyed, but is stronger than made to feel pain. It is a hurt to conscience, which, while not necessarily fatal, may lead to violation or hardening of conscience, and finally to fall. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1co+8:9-12">1 Corinthians 8:9-12</span>. </p>
<p>Meat [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">βρωμα] </span></span>. A general term for food. </p>
<p>Charitably [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κατα αγαπην] </span></span>. Lit., according to love. Rev. in love. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="2pe+1:6">2 Peter 1:6</span>. </p>
