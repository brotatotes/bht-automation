<p>Believeth that he may eat [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πιστευει φαγειν] </span></span>. The A. V. conveys the sense of having an opinion, thinking. But the point is the strength or weakness of the man's faith (see ver. 1) as it affects his eating. Hence Rev., correctly, hath faith to eat. </p>
<p>Herbs (lacana). From lacainw to dig. Herbs grown on land cultivated by digging : garden - herbs, vegetables. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mr+4:32">Mark 4:32</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="lu+12:42">Luke 12:42</span>.</p>
