<p>Hast thou faith [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">συ πιστιν εχεις] </span></span>. The best texts insert hn which. "The faith which thou hast have thou to thyself," etc. So Rev. </p>
<p>Condemneth not himself [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κρινων] </span></span>. Rev., better, judgeth. Who, in settled conviction of the rightness of his action, subjects himself to no self - judgment after it. </p>
<p>Alloweth [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δοκιμαζει] </span></span>. Rev., approveth. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1pe+1:7">1 Peter 1:7</span>. "Christian practice ought to be out of the sphere of morbid introspection."</p>
