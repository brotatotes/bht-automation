Hast thou faith [συ πιστιν εχεις] . The best texts insert hn which. "The faith which thou hast have thou to thyself," etc. So Rev. Condemneth not himself [κρινων] . Rev., better, judgeth. Who, in settled conviction of the rightness of his action, subjects himself to no self - judgment after it. Alloweth [δοκιμαζει] . Rev., approveth. See on 1 Peter 1:7. "Christian practice ought to be out of the sphere of morbid introspection."
