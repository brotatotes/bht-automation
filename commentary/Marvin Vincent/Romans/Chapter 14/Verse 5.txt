Esteemeth every day alike (krinei pasan hJmeran). Alike is inserted. Lit., judgeth every day; subjects every day to moral scrutiny. Be fully persuaded [πληροφορεισθω] . Better, Rev., assured. See on most surely believed, Luke 1:1. In his own mind. "As a boat may pursue its course uninjured either in a narrow canal or in a spacious lake" (Bengel).
