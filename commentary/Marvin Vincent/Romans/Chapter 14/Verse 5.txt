<p>Esteemeth every day alike (krinei pasan hJmeran). Alike is inserted. Lit., judgeth every day; subjects every day to moral scrutiny. </p>
<p>Be fully persuaded [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πληροφορεισθω] </span></span>. Better, Rev., assured. See on most surely believed, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="lu+1:1">Luke 1:1</span>. </p>
<p>In his own mind. "As a boat may pursue its course uninjured either in a narrow canal or in a spacious lake" (Bengel).</p>
