<p>Whereof I may glory [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">την καυχησιν] </span></span>. Rather, as Rev., my glorying, denoting the act. The ground of glorying would be kauchma as in ch. 4 2; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ga+6:4">Galatians 6:4</span>, etc. </p>
<p>Those things which pertain to God [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τα προς τον θεον] </span></span>. A technical phrase in Jewish liturgical language to denote the functions of worship (<span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="heb+2:17">Hebrews 2:17</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="heb+5:1">Hebrews 5:1</span>). According with the sacerdotal ideas of the previous verse.</p>
