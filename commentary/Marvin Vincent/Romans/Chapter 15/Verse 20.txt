<p>Have I strived [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">φιλοτιμουμενον] </span></span>. The verb means originally to be fond of honor, and hence, from a love of honor, to strive, be ambitious. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="2co+5:9">2 Corinthians 5:9</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1th+4:11">1 Thessalonians 4:11</span>. The correct sense is to prosecute as a point of honor. </p>
<p>Foundation (qemelion). See on settle, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1pe+5:10">1 Peter 5:10</span>.</p>
