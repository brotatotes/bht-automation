<p>Place [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τοπον] </span></span>. Scope, opportunity. So of Esau, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="heb+12:17">Hebrews 12:17</span>. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ro+12:19">Romans 12:19</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="eph+4:27">Ephesians 4:27</span>. </p>
<p>Many [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ικανων] </span></span>. See on worthy, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="lu+7:6">Luke 7:6</span>. The primary meaning is sufficient, and hence comes to be applied to number and quantity; many, enough, as <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mr+10:46">Mark 10:46</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="lu+8:32">Luke 8:32</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+9:23">Acts 9:23</span>, etc. So, long, of time (<span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+8:11">Acts 8:11</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+27:9">Acts 27:9</span>). Worthy, i e., sufficient for an honor or a place (<span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mr+1:7">Mark 1:7</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="lu+7:6">Luke 7:6</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1co+14:9">1 Corinthians 14:9</span>). Adequate (<span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="2co+2:16">2 Corinthians 2:16</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="2co+3:5">2 Corinthians 3:5</span>). Qualified (<span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="2ti+2:2">2 Timothy 2:2</span>). Here the sense might be expressed by for years enough.</p>
