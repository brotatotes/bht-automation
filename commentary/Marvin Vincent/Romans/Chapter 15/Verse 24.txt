Spain. The usual Greek name is Iberia. Paul adopts a modification of the Roman name, Hispania. In my journey [διαπορευομενος] . Lit., journeying through, or as I pass through. To be brought on my way [προπεμφθηναι] . Escorted. See on Acts 14:3. Filled [εμπλησθω] . Lit., filled full : satiated. Compare Acts 14:17; Luke 1:53. Rev., satisfied.
