<p>Spain. The usual Greek name is Iberia. Paul adopts a modification of the Roman name, Hispania. </p>
<p>In my journey [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">διαπορευομενος] </span></span>. Lit., journeying through, or as I pass through. </p>
<p>To be brought on my way [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">προπεμφθηναι] </span></span>. Escorted. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+14:3">Acts 14:3</span>. Filled [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εμπλησθω] </span></span>. Lit., filled full : satiated. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+14:17">Acts 14:17</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="lu+1:53">Luke 1:53</span>. Rev., satisfied.</p>
