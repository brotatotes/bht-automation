<p>To minister [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">λειτουργησαι] </span></span>. See on ch. <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ro+13:6">Romans 13:6</span>. By using this word for priestly service, Paul puts the ministry of almsgiving on the footing of a sacrificial service. It expresses the worship of giving.</p>
