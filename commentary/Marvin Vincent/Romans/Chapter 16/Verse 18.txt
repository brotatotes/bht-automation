<p>Good words [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">χρηστολογιας] </span></span>. Only here in the New Testament. Lit., good speaking. The compounded adjective crhs tov is used rather in its secondary sense of mild, pleasant So Rev., smooth speech. </p>
<p>Deceive (ejxapatwsin) Better, as Rev., beguile. It is not merely making a false impression, but practically leading astray Simple [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ακακων] </span></span>. Only here and <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="heb+7:26">Hebrews 7:26</span>. Lit., not evil. Rev., innocent. Bengel says : "An indifferent word. They are called so who are merely without positive wickedness, when they ought to abound also in prudence, and to guard against other men's wickedness."</p>
