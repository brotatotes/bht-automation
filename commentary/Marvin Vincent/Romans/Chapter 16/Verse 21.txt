Jason, possibly the Jason of <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+17:5">Acts 17:5</span>. Sosipater, possibly the Sopater of <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+20:4">Acts 20:4</span>. Both names were common.</p>
