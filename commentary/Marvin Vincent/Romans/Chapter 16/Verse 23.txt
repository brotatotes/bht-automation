Gaius. See Acts 19:29; Acts 20:4; 1 Corinthians 1:14. Possibly the same in all three references. Chamberlain [οικονομος] . See on Luke 16:1. The word appears in the New Testament in two senses : 1. The slave who was employed to give the other slaves their rations. So Luke 7:42. Luke 7:2. The land - steward, as Luke 16:1. Probably here the administrator of the city lands.
