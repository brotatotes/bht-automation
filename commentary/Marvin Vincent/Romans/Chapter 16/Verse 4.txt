<p>Who [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">οιτινες] </span></span>. The double relative, with an explanatory force : seeing that they. </p>
<p>Laid down their own necks [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τον εαυτων τραχηλον υπεθηκαν] </span></span>. Laid down is, literally, placed under (the axe). Whether the expression is literal or figurative, or if literal, when the incident occurred, cannot be determined.</p>
