<p>When [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">οταν] </span></span>. Lit., whenever, supposing a case which may occur at any time. </p>
<p>The Gentiles. Rev., properly, Gentiles. There is no article. Not the Gentiles collectively, but Gentiles among whom the supposed case occurs. Which have not the law [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τα μη νομον εχοντα] </span></span>. The mh not negatives the possession of the law. Rev., which have no law. </p>
<p>Having not the law [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">νομον μη εχοντες] </span></span>. Here mh not negatives the possession of the law. Rev., having no law. It is difficult to indicate the proper emphasis in the English text, since the use of italics is limited to words not in the original.</p>
