<p>Behold [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ιδε] </span></span>. But the correct reading is eij de but if. </p>
<p>Thou art called [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επονομαζη] </span></span>. Rev., much better, bearest the name of, bringing out the value which attached to the name Jew, the theocratic title of honor. See on Hebrews, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+6:1">Acts 6:1</span>. </p>
<p>Restest in [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επαναπαυη] </span></span>. Rev., better, upon, giving the force of ejpi in the verb. The radical conception of the verb ajnapauw is relief. See <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+11:28">Matthew 11:28</span>. Thou restest with a blind trust in God as thy Father and protector exclusively.</p>
