<p>Chiefly [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πρωτον] </span></span>. Rev., first of all; i e., first in order. Paul, however, does not enumerate further, being led away by another thought. </p>
<p>The oracles [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τα λογια] </span></span>. Diminutive. Strictly, brief utterances. Both in classical and biblical Greek, of divine utterances. In classical Greek, of prose oracles. See <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+7:38">Acts 7:38</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="heb+5:12">Hebrews 5:12</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1pe+4:11">1 Peter 4:11</span>. Not especially Messianic passages, but the Old Testament scriptures with the law and the promises.</p>
