The sign - a seal [σημειον - σφραγιδα] . Sign refers to the material token; seal to its religious import. Compare 1 Corinthians 9:2; Genesis 17:11. See on to seal, Revelation 22:10. That he might be [εις το ειναι αυτον] . Not so that he became, but expressing the divinely appointed aim of his receiving the sign.
