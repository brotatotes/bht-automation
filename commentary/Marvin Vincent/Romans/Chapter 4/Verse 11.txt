<p>The sign - a seal [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">σημειον - σφραγιδα] </span></span>. Sign refers to the material token; seal to its religious import. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1co+9:2">1 Corinthians 9:2</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ge+17:11">Genesis 17:11</span>. See on to seal, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="re+22:10">Revelation 22:10</span>. </p>
<p>That he might be [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εις το ειναι αυτον] </span></span>. Not so that he became, but expressing the divinely appointed aim of his receiving the sign.</p>
