<p>Heir of the world [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κληρονομον κοσμου] </span></span>. See on divided by lot, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+13:19">Acts 13:19</span>; and inheritance, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1pe+1:4">1 Peter 1:4</span>. "Paul here takes the Jewish conception of the universal dominion of the Messianic theocracy prefigured by the inheritance of Canaan, divests it of its Judaistic element, and raises it to a christological truth." Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+19:28">Matthew 19:28</span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+19:29">Matthew 19:29</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="lu+22:30">Luke 22:30</span>. The idea underlies the phrases kingdom of God, kingdom of Heaven.</p>
