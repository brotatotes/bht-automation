<p>For. Supply, Abraham found nothing according to the flesh; for, if he did. he has something to boast of. </p>
<p>By works [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εξ εργων] </span></span>. Lit., out of works. In speaking of the relation of works to justification, Paul never uses dia by or through, but ejk out of; works being regarded by the Jew as the meritorious source of salvation.</p>
