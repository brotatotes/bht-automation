<p>Being fully persuaded [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πληροφορηθεις] </span></span>. Rev., more accurately, fully assured. See on most surely believed, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="lu+1:1">Luke 1:1</span>. The primary idea is, being filled with a thought or conviction. </p>
<p>Able [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">δυνατος] </span></span>. The sense is stronger : mighty; compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="lu+1:49">Luke 1:49</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="lu+24:19">Luke 24:19</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+18:24">Acts 18:24</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="2co+10:4">2 Corinthians 10:4</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="re+6:15">Revelation 6:15</span>.</p>
