<p>The Scripture [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">η γραφη] </span></span>. The scripture passage. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="joh+2:22">John 2:22</span>; and foot - note on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="joh+5:47">John 5:47</span>. </p>
<p>It was counted for righteousness [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ελογισθη εις δικαιοσυνην] </span></span>. For the phrase logizesqai eijv to reckon unto, compare ch. <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ro+2:26">Romans 2:26</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ro+9:8">Romans 9:8</span>, where eijv is rendered for. The verb is also used with wJv as. So ch. 8 36; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1co+4:1">1 Corinthians 4:1</span>. So in Sept., eijv, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ps+56:31">Psalms 56:31</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="isa+29:17">Isaiah 29:17</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="isa+32:15">Isaiah 32:15</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="isa+40:17">Isaiah 40:17</span>; wJv. <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ge+31:15">Genesis 31:15</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="job+41:20">Job 41:20</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ps+43:22">Psalms 43:22</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="isa+5:28">Isaiah 5:28</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="isa+29:16">Isaiah 29:16</span>. The phrases ejlogisqh eijv and ejl. wJv are thus shown to be substantially equivalent. See further on ver. 5.</p>
