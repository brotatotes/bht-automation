<p>Describeth the blessedness [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">λεγει τον μακαρισμον] </span></span>. Makarismov does not mean blessedness, but the declaration of blessedness, the congratulation. So Plato : "The man of understanding will not suffer himself to be dazzled by the congratulation [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μακαρισμου</span></span>] of the multitude (" Republic," 9, 591). Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ga+4:15">Galatians 4:15</span> (Rev.), and see note there. Rev., correctly, pronounceth blessing.</p>
