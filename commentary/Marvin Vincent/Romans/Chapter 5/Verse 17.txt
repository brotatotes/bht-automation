<p>Reigned. The emphatic point of the comparison. The effect of the second Adam cannot fall behind that of the first. If death reigned, there must be a reign of life. </p>
<p>They which receive [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">οι λαμβανοντες] </span></span>. Not believingly accept, but simply the recipients. </p>
<p>Abundance of grace. Note the articles, the abundance of the grace.</p>
