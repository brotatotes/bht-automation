<p>Maketh not ashamed [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ου καταισχυνει] </span></span>. Mostly in Paul; elsewhere only in <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="lu+13:17">Luke 13:17</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1pe+2:6">1 Peter 2:6</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1pe+3:16">1 Peter 3:16</span>. Rev., putteth not to shame, thus giving better the strong sense of the word, to disgrace or dishonor. </p>
<p>Is shed abroad [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εκκεχυται] </span></span>. Rev. renders the perfect tense; hath been shed abroad. Lit., poured out. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="tit+3:6">Titus 3:6</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+2:33">Acts 2:33</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+10:45">Acts 10:45</span>. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="jude+1:11">Jude 1:11</span>.</p>
