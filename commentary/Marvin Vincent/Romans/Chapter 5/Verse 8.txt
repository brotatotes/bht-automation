<p>Commendeth. See on 3 5. Note the present tense. God continuously establishes His love in that the death of Christ remains as its most striking manifestation. </p>
<p>His love [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εαυτου] </span></span>. Rev., more literally, His own. Not in contrast with human love, but as demonstrated by Christ 's act of love.</p>
