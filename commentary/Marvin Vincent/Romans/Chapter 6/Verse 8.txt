<p>We be dead [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">απεθανομεν] </span></span>. The aorist. Rev., correctly, we died. The death is viewed as an event, not as a state. </p>
<p>We believe [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πιστευομεν] </span></span>. Dogmatic belief rather than trust, though the latter is not excluded. </p>
<p>Shall live with [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">συνζησομεν] </span></span>. Participation of the believer 's sanctified life with the life of Christ rather than participation in future glory, which is not the point emphasized. Compare ver. 11.</p>
