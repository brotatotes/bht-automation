<p>We know [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">οιδαμεν] </span></span>. Denoting something generally conceded. </p>
<p>Spiritual [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πνευματικος] </span></span>. The expression of the Holy Spirit. </p>
<p>Carnal [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">σαρκινος] </span></span>. Lit., made of flesh. A very strong expression. "This unspiritual, material, phenomenal nature" so dominates the unrenewed man that he is described as consisting of flesh. Others read sarkikov having the nature of flesh. </p>
<p>Sold under sin. As a slave. The preposition uJpo under, with the accusative, implies direction; so as to be under the power of.</p>
