We know [οιδαμεν] . Denoting something generally conceded. Spiritual [πνευματικος] . The expression of the Holy Spirit. Carnal [σαρκινος] . Lit., made of flesh. A very strong expression. "This unspiritual, material, phenomenal nature" so dominates the unrenewed man that he is described as consisting of flesh. Others read sarkikov having the nature of flesh. Sold under sin. As a slave. The preposition uJpo under, with the accusative, implies direction; so as to be under the power of.
