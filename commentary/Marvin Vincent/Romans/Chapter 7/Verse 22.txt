I delight in [συνηδομαι] . Lit., I rejoice with. Stronger than I consent unto (ver., 16). It is the agreement of moral sympathy. The inward man [τον εσω ανθρωπον] . The rational and moral I, the essence of the man which is conscious of itself as an ethical personality. Not to be confounded with the new man (Ephesians 4:24; Colossians 3:10). 41 It is substantially the same with the mind (ver. 23).
