<p>I had not known [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ουκ εγνων] </span></span>. Rev., correctly, I did not know. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="joh+2:24">John 2:24</span>. The I refers to Paul himself. He speaks in the first person, declaring concerning himself what is meant to apply to every man placed under the Mosaic law, as respects his relation to that law, before and after the revolution in his inner life brought about through his connection with that law. His personal experience is not excluded, but represents the universal experience. 39 Lust [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">επιθυμιαν] </span></span>. Rev., coveting. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mr+4:19">Mark 4:19</span>.</p>
