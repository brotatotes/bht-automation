<p>Ye shall die [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μελλετε αποθνησκειν] </span></span>. The expression is stronger than the simple future of the verb. It indicates a necessary consequence. So Rev., ye must. </p>
<p>Mortify [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">θανατουτε] </span></span>. Put to death. </p>
<p>Deeds [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πραξεις] </span></span>. Habitual practices. See on ch. <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ro+7:15">Romans 7:15</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="joh+3:21">John 3:21</span>.</p>
