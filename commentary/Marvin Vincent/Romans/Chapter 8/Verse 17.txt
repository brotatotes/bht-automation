<p>Joint - heirs. Roman law made all children, including adopted ones, equal heritors. Jewish law gave a double portion to the eldest son. The Roman law was naturally in Paul 's mind, and suits the context, where adoption is the basis of inheritance. </p>
<p>If so be that [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ειπερ] </span></span>. The conditional particle with the indicative mood assumes the fact. If so be, as is really the case. </p>
<p>Suffer with Him. Mere suffering does not fulfill the condition. It is suffering with Christ. Compare with Him - all things, ver. 32.</p>
