Joint - heirs. Roman law made all children, including adopted ones, equal heritors. Jewish law gave a double portion to the eldest son. The Roman law was naturally in Paul 's mind, and suits the context, where adoption is the basis of inheritance. If so be that [ειπερ] . The conditional particle with the indicative mood assumes the fact. If so be, as is really the case. Suffer with Him. Mere suffering does not fulfill the condition. It is suffering with Christ. Compare with Him - all things, ver. 32.
