The law of the Spirit of life [ο νομος του πνευματος της ζωης] . The law, the regulative principle; the Spirit, the divine Spirit who inspires the law (compare Romans 7:14). Of life, proceeding from the life of Jesus and producing and imparting life. Compare John 16:15. In Christ Jesus. Construe with hath made me free. Compare John 8:36.
