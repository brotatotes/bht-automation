<p>The law of the Spirit of life [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ο νομος του πνευματος της ζωης] </span></span>. The law, the regulative principle; the Spirit, the divine Spirit who inspires the law (compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ro+7:14">Romans 7:14</span>). Of life, proceeding from the life of Jesus and producing and imparting life. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="joh+16:15">John 16:15</span>. </p>
<p>In Christ Jesus. Construe with hath made me free. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="joh+8:36">John 8:36</span>.</p>
