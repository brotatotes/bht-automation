<p>By hope [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τη ελπιδι] </span></span>. Better in hope. We are saved by faith. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1pe+1:3">1 Peter 1:3</span>. </p>
