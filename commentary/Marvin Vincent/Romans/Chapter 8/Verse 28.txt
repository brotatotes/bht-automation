<p>Work together [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">συνεργει] </span></span>. Or, are working together, now, while the creation is in travail. Together refers to the common working of all the elements included in panta all things. </p>
<p>For good. Jacob cried, all these things are against me. Paul, all things are working together for good.</p>
