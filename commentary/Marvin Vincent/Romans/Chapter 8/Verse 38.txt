<p>Powers [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αρχαι] </span></span>. Angelic, higher than mere angels. </p>
<p>Things present [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ενεστωτα] </span></span>. Only in Paul and <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="heb+9:9">Hebrews 9:9</span>. The verb literally means to stand in sight. Hence to impend or threaten. So <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="2th+2:2">2 Thessalonians 2:2</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="2ti+3:1">2 Timothy 3:1</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1co+7:26">1 Corinthians 7:26</span>. Used of something that has set in or begun. So some render here. 48 Bengel says : "Things past are not mentioned, not even sins, for they have passed away." ===<span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ro+9:0">Romans 9:0</span> </p>
<p>CHAPTER IX </p>
<p>Luther says : "Who hath not known passion, cross, and travail of death, cannot treat of foreknowledge (election of grace) without injury and inward enmity toward God. Wherefore take heed that thou drink not wine while thou art yet a sucking babe. Each several doctrine hath its own reason and measure and age."</p>
