<p>It is not of him that willeth nor of him that runneth. It, the participation in God 's mercy. Of him, i e., dependent upon. Runneth, denoting strenuous effort. The metaphor from the foot - race is a favorite one with Paul. See <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1co+9:24">1 Corinthians 9:24</span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1co+9:26">1 Corinthians 9:26</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ga+2:2">Galatians 2:2</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ga+5:7">Galatians 5:7</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="php+2:16">Philippians 2:16</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="2th+3:1">2 Thessalonians 3:1</span>. God is laid under no obligation by a human will or a human work.</p>
