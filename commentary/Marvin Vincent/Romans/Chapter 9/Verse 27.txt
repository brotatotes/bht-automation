Crieth [κραζει] . An impassioned utterance. See on Luke 18:39; compare John 7:28, John 7:37; Acts 19:28; Acts 23:6. Mostly of an inarticulate cry. "The prophet in awful earnestness, and as with a scream of anguish, cries over Israel" (Morison). Concerning [υπερ] . Lit., over, as proclaiming a judgment which hangs over Israel.
