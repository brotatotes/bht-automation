<p>Crieth [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κραζει] </span></span>. An impassioned utterance. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="lu+18:39">Luke 18:39</span>; compare <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="joh+7:28">John 7:28</span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="joh+7:37">John 7:37</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+19:28">Acts 19:28</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+23:6">Acts 23:6</span>. Mostly of an inarticulate cry. "The prophet in awful earnestness, and as with a scream of anguish, cries over Israel" (Morison). </p>
<p>Concerning [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">υπερ] </span></span>. Lit., over, as proclaiming a judgment which hangs over Israel.</p>
