Not by faith [ουκ εκ πιστεως] . A. V. and Rev. supply the ellipsis, they sought it not. They stumbled [προσεκοψαν] . "In their foolish course Israel thought they were advancing on a clear path, and lo! all at once there was found in this way an obstacle upon which they were broken; and this obstacle was the very Messiah whom they had so long invoked in all their prayers" (Godet).
