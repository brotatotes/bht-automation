<p>This is the word of promise. The A. V. obscures the true sense. There is no article, and the emphasis is on promise. "I say 'a word of promise, ' for a word of promise is this which follows." Or, as Morison, "this word is one of promise." </p>
<p>At this time [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">κατα τον καιρον τουτον] </span></span>. Rev., according to this season. The reference is to <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ge+18:14">Genesis 18:14</span>, where the Hebrew is when the season is renewed or revives; i e., next year at this time. The season is represented as reviving periodically.</p>
