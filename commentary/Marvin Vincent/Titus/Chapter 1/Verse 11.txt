<p>Whose mouths must be stopped [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ους δει επιστομιζειν] </span></span>. Lit. whom it is necessary to silence. Epistomizein, N. T. o. o LXX Originally, to put something into the mouth, as a bit into a horse 's mouth. Epistomion is the stop of a water - pipe or of a hydraulic organ. Comp. fimoun <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1ti+5:18">1 Timothy 5:18</span>. </p>
<p>Who subvert [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">οιτινες ανατρεπουσιν] </span></span>. The double relative is explanatory of must; in as much as they, etc. For subvert rend. overthrow. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="2ti+2:18">2 Timothy 2:18</span>. </p>
