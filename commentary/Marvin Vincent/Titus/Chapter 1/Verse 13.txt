<p>Sharply [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αποτομως] </span></span>. Only here and <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="2co+13:10">2 Corinthians 13:10</span> (note). Paul has ajpotomia severity, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ro+11:22">Romans 11:22</span> (note). LXX, ajpotomwv severely, only Wisd. 5 22; ajpotomov severe (not in N. T.), Wisd. 5 20; 11 10; 12 9. From ajpotemnein to cut of. It signifies abrupt, harsh, summary dealing.</p>
