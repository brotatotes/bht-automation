Not giving heed [μη προσεχοντες] . Reprove sharply, that they may be sound in the faith, and may show their soundness by not giving heed, etc. See on 1 Timothy 1:4. To Jewish fables [ιουδαικοις μυθοις] . See on 1 Timothy 1:4. Note Jewish. The nature of these we do not know. Commandments of men [εντολαις ανθρωπων] . See on 1 Timothy 6:14. Comp. Colossians 2:22. Prescriptions concerning abstinence from meats, marriage, etc. The men are probably those of the circumcision, verse Titus 1:10What they teach theoretically, by means of the myths, they bring to bear practically, by means of their precepts. That turn from the truth [αποστρεφομενων την αληθειαν] . Comp. 2 Timothy 4:4, where the truth and fables appear in contrast.
