<p>Not giving heed [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μη προσεχοντες] </span></span>. Reprove sharply, that they may be sound in the faith, and may show their soundness by not giving heed, etc. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1ti+1:4">1 Timothy 1:4</span>. </p>
<p>To Jewish fables [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ιουδαικοις μυθοις] </span></span>. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1ti+1:4">1 Timothy 1:4</span>. Note Jewish. The nature of these we do not know. </p>
<p>Commandments of men [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εντολαις ανθρωπων] </span></span>. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1ti+6:14">1 Timothy 6:14</span>. Comp. <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="col+2:22">Colossians 2:22</span>. Prescriptions concerning abstinence from meats, marriage, etc. The men are probably those of the circumcision, verse </p>
<p><span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="tit+1:1">Titus 1:1</span>0What they teach theoretically, by means of the myths, they bring to bear practically, by means of their precepts. </p>
<p>That turn from the truth [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αποστρεφομενων την αληθειαν] </span></span>. Comp. <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="2ti+4:4">2 Timothy 4:4</span>, where the truth and fables appear in contrast.</p>
