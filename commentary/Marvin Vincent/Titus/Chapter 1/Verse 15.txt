<p>Unto the pure [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τοις καθαροις] </span></span>. The pure in heart and conscience. See <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="2ti+1:3">2 Timothy 1:3</span>. </p>
<p>All things are pure. Comp. <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1ti+4:4">1 Timothy 4:4</span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1ti+4:5">1 Timothy 4:5</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+10:15">Acts 10:15</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mr+7:15">Mark 7:15</span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mr+7:18">Mark 7:18</span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mr+7:19">Mark 7:19</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1co+10:26">1 Corinthians 10:26</span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1co+10:30">1 Corinthians 10:30</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ro+14:20">Romans 14:20</span>. The aphorism is suggested by the commandments of men, verse 14. </p>
<p>Unto them that are defiled [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τοις μεμιαμμενοις] </span></span>. Only here in Pastorals. See also <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="joh+18:28">John 18:28</span> (note); <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="heb+12:15">Hebrews 12:15</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="jude+1:8">Jude 1:8</span>. Only in <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="joh+18:28">John 18:28</span> in a ceremonial sense. Elsewhere of moral pollution. Nothing is pure. Their moral pollution taints everything with its own quality. The purest things become suggestors and ministers of impurity. Mind and conscience [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ο νους και η συνειδησις] </span></span>. For nouv see On <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ro+7:23">Romans 7:23</span> : for suneidhsiv, on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1pe+3:16">1 Peter 3:16</span>.</p>
