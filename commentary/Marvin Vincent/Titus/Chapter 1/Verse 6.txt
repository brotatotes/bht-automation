<p>Faithful children [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τεκνα πιστα] </span></span>. Better, believing children; or, as Rev., children that believe. Comp. <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1ti+3:4">1 Timothy 3:4</span>. </p>
<p>Not accused of riot [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">μη εν κατηγορια ασωτιας] </span></span>. Lit. not in accusation of profigacy. For kathgoria see on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1ti+5:19">1 Timothy 5:19</span>. Aswtia, lit. unsavingness; hence, dissoluteness, profigacy. Comp. <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="lu+14:13">Luke 14:13</span>, of the prodigal son, who lived unsavingly [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">ασωτως] </span></span>. Only here, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="eph+5:18">Ephesians 5:18</span>, and <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1pe+4:4">1 Peter 4:4</span> (note).</p>
