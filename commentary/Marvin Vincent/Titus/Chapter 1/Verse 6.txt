Faithful children [τεκνα πιστα] . Better, believing children; or, as Rev., children that believe. Comp. 1 Timothy 3:4. Not accused of riot [μη εν κατηγορια ασωτιας] . Lit. not in accusation of profigacy. For kathgoria see on 1 Timothy 5:19. Aswtia, lit. unsavingness; hence, dissoluteness, profigacy. Comp. Luke 14:13, of the prodigal son, who lived unsavingly [ασωτως] . Only here, Ephesians 5:18, and 1 Peter 4:4 (note).
