<p>A bishop [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">τον επισκοπον] </span></span>. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1ti+3:1">1 Timothy 3:1</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1ti+5:1">1 Timothy 5:1</span>. Rend. the bishop.
