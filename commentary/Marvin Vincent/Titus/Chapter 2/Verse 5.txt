<p>Keepers at home [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">οικουργους] </span></span>. Wrong. Rend. workers at home. N. T. o. o LXX, o Class. </p>
<p>Good [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αγαθας] </span></span>. Not attributive of workers at home, but independent. Rend. kindly. The mistress of the house is to add to her thrift, energy, and strict discipline, benign, gracious, heartily kind demeanor. Comp. <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mt+20:15">Matthew 20:15</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1pe+2:18">1 Peter 2:18</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+9:36">Acts 9:36</span>. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+11:24">Acts 11:24</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ro+5:7">Romans 5:7</span>. <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ro+5:1">Romans 5:1</span> <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ro+5:53">Romans 5:53</span> Obedient [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">υποτασσομενας] </span></span>. Better, subject or in subjection. Frequent in Paul, but not often in the active voice. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="jas+4:7">James 4:7</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ro+8:7">Romans 8:7</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="php+3:21">Philippians 3:21</span>; and comp. <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1co+14:34">1 Corinthians 14:34</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="eph+5:22">Ephesians 5:22</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="col+3:18">Colossians 3:18</span>.</p>
