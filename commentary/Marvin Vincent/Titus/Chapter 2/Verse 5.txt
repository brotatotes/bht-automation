Keepers at home [οικουργους] . Wrong. Rend. workers at home. N. T. o. o LXX, o Class. Good [αγαθας] . Not attributive of workers at home, but independent. Rend. kindly. The mistress of the house is to add to her thrift, energy, and strict discipline, benign, gracious, heartily kind demeanor. Comp. Matthew 20:15; 1 Peter 2:18; Acts 9:36. See on Acts 11:24; Romans 5:7. Romans 5:1 Romans 5:53 Obedient [υποτασσομενας] . Better, subject or in subjection. Frequent in Paul, but not often in the active voice. See on James 4:7; Romans 8:7; Philippians 3:21; and comp. 1 Corinthians 14:34; Ephesians 5:22; Colossians 3:18.
