<p>Put them in mind [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">υπομιμνησκε εξουσιαις] </span></span>. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="2ti+2:14">2 Timothy 2:14</span>, and on uJpomnhsin reminding, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="2ti+1:5">2 Timothy 1:5</span>. </p>
<p>Principalities and powers [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">αρχαις εξουσιαις] </span></span>. Omit and. Principalities which are authorities. Arch beginning = that which begins : the leader, principality. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="col+1:16">Colossians 1:16</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="jude+1:6">Jude 1:6</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+10:11">Acts 10:11</span>. Only here in Pastorals. Exousia right, authority. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="mr+2:10">Mark 2:10</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="joh+1:12">John 1:12</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="col+1:16">Colossians 1:16</span>. Only here in Pastorals. For the combination principalities and powers, see on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="lu+20:20">Luke 20:20</span>. </p>
<p>To obey magistrates [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">πειθαρχειν] </span></span>. Comp. <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+5:29">Acts 5:29</span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+5:32">Acts 5:32</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+27:21">Acts 27:21</span>. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+5:29">Acts 5:29</span>. The idea of magistrates is contained in the word itself; but it is quite proper to render as Rev. to be obedient. Rare in LXX </p>
<p>Ready to every good work [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">προς παν εργον αγαθον ετοιμους ειναι] </span></span>. The phrase N. T. o. Etoimov ready, only here in Pastorals. Comp. eJtoimasia readiness or preparation, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="eph+6:15">Ephesians 6:15</span> (note).</p>
