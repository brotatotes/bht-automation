<p>Shed [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">εξεχεεν] </span></span>. Or poured forth. Only here in Pastorals. Most frequent in Revelation. The pouring out of the Spirit is an 0. T. metaphor. See <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="joe+3:1">Joel 3:1</span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="joe+3:2">Joel 3:2</span>, cit. in <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+2:17">Acts 2:17</span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ac+2:18">Acts 2:18</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="zec+12:10">Zechariah 12:10</span>. In Paul the verb occurs but once, of shedding blood, <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="ro+3:15">Romans 3:15</span>, cit.</p>
