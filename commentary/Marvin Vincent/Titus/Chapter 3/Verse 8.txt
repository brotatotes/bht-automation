<p>Affirm constantly [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">διαβεβαιουσθαι] </span></span>. Past o. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="vnt" lang="eng" ref="1ti+1:7">1 Timothy 1:7</span>. Constantly, not continually, but uniformly and consistently. So Book of Common Prayer, "Collect for Saint John Baptist's Day," " and after his example constantly speak the truth. " Rend. affirm steadfastly. </p>
<p>Might be careful [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">φροντιζωσιν] </span></span>. N. T. o. Quite often in LXX Frequent in Class. To think or consider; hence to take careful thought, ponder, be anxious about. </p>
<p>To maintain [<span class="_800000"><span lang="el-GR">προιστασθαι] </span></span>.
