<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="1co+1:26">1 Corinthians 1:26</span>. <strong> For behold your calling, brethren,</strong> [1] <strong> how that not many wise men after the flesh, not many mighty, not many noble, are called.
	</strong></p>
<p style="margin-left:3.6em;"> [1] This <em> imperative </em> rendering is preferable to the <em> indicative</em> of our Authorised Version: ‘Ye see.’ It is the peculiar usage of the New Testament, and it is thrice so used in this very Epistle.
	</p>
