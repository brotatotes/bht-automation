<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="1co+1:27">1 Corinthians 1:27</span>. <strong> but God chose the foolish things of the world that he might</strong> put <strong> to shame them that are wise</strong>. There is here a significant transition from the <em> neuter </em> of the one class to the <em> masculine </em> of the other, to express a passage from the contemptible to the esteemed. <strong> and God chose the weak things of the world that he might put to shame the things that are strong.
	</strong></p>
