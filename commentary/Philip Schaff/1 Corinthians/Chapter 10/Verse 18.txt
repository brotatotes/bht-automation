<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="1co+10:18">1 Corinthians 10:18</span>. <strong> Behold Israel after the flesh: have not they which eat the sacrifices communion with the altar?</strong> Part of the animal was consumed on the altar, and the rest was divided between the priest and the offerer (<span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="le+7:15">Leviticus 7:15</span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="le+8:31">Leviticus 8:31</span>). Thus both “had communion with the altar,” that is, with the sacrifice laid on it, and, through it, with the glorious Object of all true worship.
	</p>
