<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="1co+10:6">1 Corinthians 10:6</span>. <strong> Now these things were our examples</strong> historical ‘types’ or ‘figures’ permitted to occur, as beacons divinely held forth for all time <strong> to the intent we should not lust after evil things, as they also lusted. </strong> Some of these “evil things,” fitted to come specially home to the Corinthian converts at that time, are now specified.
