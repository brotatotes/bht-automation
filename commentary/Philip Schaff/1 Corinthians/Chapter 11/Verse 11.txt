<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="1co+11:11">1 Corinthians 11:11</span>. <strong> Howbeit neither is the</strong> <strong> woman without the man,</strong> <strong> nor the man without the woman, in the Lord.</strong> [1] <strong> </strong> Each sex is dependent on the other, and made to be so. And when it is added “in the Lord,” the apostle would intimate that Christianity, so far from changing the original plan of Humanity, recognises, strengthens, and ennobles it in all its features.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:3.6em;"> [1] This is the genuine order of the text.
	</p>
