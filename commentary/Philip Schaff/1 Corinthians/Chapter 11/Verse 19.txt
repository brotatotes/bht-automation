<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="1co+11:19">1 Corinthians 11:19</span>. <strong> For there most be also heresies among you.</strong> The word signifies, first, a ‘taking’ or ‘choice;’ then, the thing chosen, and (in matters of judgment) an ‘opinion’ or set of opinions: here it is used in a bad sense, as in <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ga+5:20">Galatians 5:20</span>, for opinions tending to rend the Church.
	</p>
<p><strong> that they which are approved </strong> (of God) <strong> may be made manifest among you</strong> by contrast with the others.
	</p>
