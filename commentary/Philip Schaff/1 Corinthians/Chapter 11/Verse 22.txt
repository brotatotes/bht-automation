<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="1co+11:22">1 Corinthians 11:22</span>. <strong> Have ye not houses to eat and to drink in? or despise ye the church of God?</strong> emphatically so named here, as in <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="1co+11:16">1 Corinthians 11:16</span>, to express the affront put upon God Himself, regarded as present in their assemblies.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.06em;"><strong> and put them to shame that have not</strong> namely, the poor, by exposing their poverty and making them feel it. Since all these disorders sprang from their forgetting what they had been taught on this subject, the apostle now formally and at some length repeats it.
	</p>
