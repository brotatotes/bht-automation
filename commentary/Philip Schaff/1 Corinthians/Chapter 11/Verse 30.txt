<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="1co+11:30">1 Corinthians 11:30</span>. <strong> For this cause many among you are weak and sickly, and not a few fall asleep. </strong> Physical weakness, sickness, and death are undoubtedly meant here. Possibly some marked calamitous visitations of that church may be in view, the nature of which, however, it were in vain to conjecture.
	</p>
