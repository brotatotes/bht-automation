<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="1co+12:19-21">1 Corinthians 12:19-21</span> <strong> .</strong> <strong> And if they were all one member,</strong> etc. As in <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="1co+12:15-16">1 Corinthians 12:15-16</span>, the rebuke seems to be for <em> envying </em> the gifts of the more eminent, so here the rebuke is addressed to the more gifted for <em> despising </em> those of lesser gifts the “eye” as compared with the “hand,” the “head” with the “feet.”
