<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="1co+15:39">1 Corinthians 15:39</span>. <strong> All flesh is not the same flesh. </strong> ‘Take the members of the animal creation too; neither are they, any more than those of the vegetable world, all of one type,’ <strong> but there is one flesh of men, and another flesh of beasts, and another flesh of birds, and another of fishes.</strong> [1] <strong> </strong> In the next two verses the illustrations rise into a higher region.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:3.6em;"> [1] The two last clauses stand in this order in the true text. </p>
