<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="1co+16:14">1 Corinthians 16:14</span>. <strong> Let all that ye do be done in love. </strong> While the four preceding things express the sterner features of Christian duty, this pours suavity into them, and, being itself “the bond of perfectness,” encircles and beautifies the whole character.
	</p>
