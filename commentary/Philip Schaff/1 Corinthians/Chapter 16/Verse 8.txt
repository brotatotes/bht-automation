<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="1co+16:8">1 Corinthians 16:8</span>. <strong> But I will tarry at Ephesus until Pentecost. </strong> <em> N.B.
	</em></p>
<p>The allusion here to Pentecost fixes the <em> date </em> of this Epistle, while that to Ephesus shews <em> from whence it </em> was written.
