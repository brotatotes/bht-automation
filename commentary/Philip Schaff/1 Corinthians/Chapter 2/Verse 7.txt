In the same sense our Lord uses the word (<span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+13:11">Matthew 13:11</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+13:17">Matthew 13:17</span>). <strong> even the wisdom that hath been hidden, which God fore-ordained before the worlds unto our glory (see <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="2ti+2:10">2 Timothy 2:10</span></strong> <strong> ).
