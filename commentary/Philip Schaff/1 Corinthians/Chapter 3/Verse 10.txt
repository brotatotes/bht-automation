<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="1co+3:10">1 Corinthians 3:10</span>. <strong> According to the grace of God which was given unto me, as a wise master-builder, I laid a foundation</strong> alluding to our Lord’s parable of the “wise man who built his house upon the rock” (<span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+7:24-25">Matthew 7:24-25</span>). But he takes care to ascribe the “wisdom” shown in this to “the grace of God.”
	</p>
<p><strong> and another buildeth thereon. But let each man take heed how he buildeth thereon</strong> that is (as will presently appear), <em> with what materials </em> he builds.
	</p>
