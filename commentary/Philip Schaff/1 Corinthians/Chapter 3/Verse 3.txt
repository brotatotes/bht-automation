<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="1co+3:3">1 Corinthians 3:3</span>. <strong> for whereas there is among you Jealousy </strong> each party for its favourite preacher.
	</p>
<p><strong> and strife </strong> engendered by such jealousies (the next words in the received text, “and divisions,” are feebly attested, and indeed are out of place).
	</p>
<p><strong> are ye not carnal, and walk as men? </strong> unrenewed men.
