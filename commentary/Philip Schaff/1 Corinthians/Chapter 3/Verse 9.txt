<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="1co+3:9">1 Corinthians 3:9</span>. <strong> Far we are God’s fellow-workers: ye are God’s husbandry, God’s building.</strong> After sinking himself, with his fellow-workers, to the level of mere servants, he now lifts them up to the dignity of co-operators with God Himself in one field, to one end.
	</p>
<p>But the new figure of a “building” suggests a new set of ideas, fraught with new lessons lessons which the former figure of “husbandry” was not suited to express.
	</p>
