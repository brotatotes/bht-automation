1 Corinthians 4:12.  and we toil, working with our own hands  see Acts 18:3; Act 20:34 ; 1 Thessalonians 2:9; 2 Thessalonians 3:8. Indeed be had to vindicate his liberty to preach at Corinth without hire (when that was ascribed to want of manly openness), and in order to do this he had to work for his own support (see chap. 1 Corinthians 9:6).
