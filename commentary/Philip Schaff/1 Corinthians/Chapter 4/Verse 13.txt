<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="1co+4:13">1 Corinthians 4:13</span>. <strong> Being defamed, we intreat </strong> in the sense of returning soft words for calumnies. [1]
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:3.6em;"> [1] See a Mace. xiii. 23 for this tense of the word (Meyer).
	</p>
<p><strong> we are made as the</strong> <strong> filth of the world, the off scouring of all things, even until now. </strong> As these are the strongest conceivable figures, so the element of <em> duration, </em> as extending through his whole apostolic life, is added to intensify the statement.
	</p>
