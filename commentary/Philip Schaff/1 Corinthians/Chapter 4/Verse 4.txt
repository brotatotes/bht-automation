<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="1co+4:4">1 Corinthians 4:4</span>. <strong> For I know nothing against myself. </strong> As this is clearly the intended sense, so our translators probably meant to express the same, using the word “by” in a now obsolete sense.
	</p>
<p><strong> yet am I not hereby justified </strong> all human judgments being but provisional.
	</p>
<p><strong> but he that judgeth me is the Lord </strong> the Lord Christ (as will presently appear).
