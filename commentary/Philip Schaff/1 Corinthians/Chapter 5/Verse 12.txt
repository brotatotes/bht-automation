<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="1co+5:12">1 Corinthians 5:12</span>. <strong> For what have I to do with judging them that are without </strong> (the Christian pale)? As the Jews so described those outside the covenant, our Lord and the apostles borrowed the phrase from them (<span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+4:11">Mark 4:11</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="col+4:5">Colossians 4:5</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="1th+4:12">1 Thessalonians 4:12</span>).
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.18em;"><strong> do not ye judge them that are within?</strong> and that surely is responsibility enough.
