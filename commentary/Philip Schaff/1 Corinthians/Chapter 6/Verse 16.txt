<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="1co+6:16">1 Corinthians 6:16</span>. <strong> What, know ye not that he that is joined</strong> <strong> to a harlot is one body! for the twain, saith he, shall become one flesh. </strong> As the sexes, by marriage, become one natural life, an abhorred unity of nature is formed by the action here referred to.
