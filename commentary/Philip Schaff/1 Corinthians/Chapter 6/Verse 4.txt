<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="1co+6:4">1 Corinthians 6:4</span>. <strong> If then ye have to judge things pertaining to this life, set them to judge who are of no account in the church </strong> an ironical way of hinting that their differences were so petty that the poorest-witted among them were fit enough to deal with them. [1]
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:3.6em;"> [1] To view this as a question ‘Do ye set, <strong> </strong> etc.?’ <strong> </strong> as some do, seems to us very unnatural.
	</p>
