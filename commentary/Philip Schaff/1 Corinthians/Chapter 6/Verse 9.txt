<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="1co+6:9">1 Corinthians 6:9</span>. <strong> What, know ye not that the unrighteous </strong> (‘the wrong-doers’) <strong> shall not inherit the kingdom of God? Be not deceived: neither fornicator, nor idolaters, </strong> whose religion was itself voluptuous, particularly at Corinth, <strong> nor adulterers, nor effeminate </strong> given <strong> </strong> to voluptuous ease, encouraging their sensual inclinations, <strong> nor abusers of themselves with men </strong> practising the unnatural vice of <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ro+1:27">Romans 1:27</span>, but which ought not to be named among us as becometh saints. The five grosser forms of vice thus mentioned are next followed by five of a more familiar but not less soul-destroying kind.
