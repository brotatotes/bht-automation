<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="1co+8:10">1 Corinthians 8:10</span>. <strong> For if a man see thee who hast knowledge </strong> of the emptiness of idols and the lawfulness of all food <strong> sitting</strong> <strong> at meat in an idol’s</strong> <strong> temple.</strong> The word here used (in the Greek of the Old Testament and this one place of the New Testament) is used only for <em> heathen </em> temple, to mark its idolatrous character; the word employed for the temple of the living God being studiously avoided on such a subject.
