<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="1co+8:2">1 Corinthians 8:2</span>. <strong> If any man thinketh that he knoweth anything, he knoweth</strong> <strong> not yet as he ought to know</strong> <em> </em> has not got upon the right track in his search after knowledge, for what inflates its possessor cannot be true knowledge: all knowledge worthy of the name begets such a consciousness of remaining ignorance as effectually checks self-sufficiency.
