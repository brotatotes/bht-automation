<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="1co+9:15">1 Corinthians 9:15</span>. <strong> But I have used none of these things</strong> availed myself of none of these rights (the “ <em> I</em> ” here is emphatic)
	</p>
<p><strong> and I write</strong> [2] <strong> not these things that it may be so done in my case; for it were good for me rather to die than, </strong> etc. So thankful was he that he has been led to act at Corinth on this independent principle, that he feels a satisfaction in holding it up as an unanswerable refutation of those base insinuations against his motives.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:1.56em;"> [2] The epistolary aorist, expressing present action.
	</p>
