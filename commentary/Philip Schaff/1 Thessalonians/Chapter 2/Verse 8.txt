<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="1th+2:8">1 Thessalonians 2:8</span>. <strong> So,</strong> <em> i.e.</em> in like manner as the nursing mother.
	</p>
<p><strong> Not the gospel of God only, but our own souls also. </strong> So genuine and cordial was the love of Paul and his companions for the Thessalonians, that they did not merely deliver their message as officials seeking to discharge a responsibility laid upon them, but they were willing to sacrifice their lives for them, if need were. This willingness manifested itself in the self-denying and excessive toil of which Paul proceeds to speak.
	</p>
