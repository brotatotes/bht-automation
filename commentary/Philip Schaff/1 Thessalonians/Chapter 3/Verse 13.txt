<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="1th+3:13">1 Thessalonians 3:13</span>. <strong> The coming of our Lord Jesus Christ. </strong> This Paul looks forward to as the time when those who are holy shall be acknowledged and manifested as such; and beyond which there is no fear of falling away. His prayers for them reach to that point but not beyond.
	</p>
<p><strong> With all his saints.</strong> Christ is to appear with all His holy angels (<span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+25:31">Matthew 25:31</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="2th+1:7">2 Thessalonians 1:7</span>), and at His appearing His saints are at once to join Him (<span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+24:31">Matthew 24:31</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="1th+4:16-17">1 Thessalonians 4:16-17</span>). Probably both angels and men are included in the present expression.
	</p>
