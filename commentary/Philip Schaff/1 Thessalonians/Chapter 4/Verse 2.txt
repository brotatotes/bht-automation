<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="1th+4:2">1 Thessalonians 4:2</span>. <strong> For ye know. </strong> I give you no new code of morals, but beseech you to live up to the instructions I formerly gave you. I refer you to my original teaching, ‘for ye know,’ etc.
	</p>
<p><strong> By the Lord Jesus. </strong> It was the Lord Jesus who moved the apostle to deliver these commandments. Christ was the agent in the matter.
	</p>
