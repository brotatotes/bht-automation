<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="1th+5:11">1 Thessalonians 5:11</span>. <strong> Wherefore. </strong> There being such good grounds for hopefulness about the departed, comfort one another.
	</p>
<p><strong> Edify.</strong> ‘From the frequent application of the term <em> house </em> or <em> temple </em> to Christians collectively (as <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="1co+3:16">1 Corinthians 3:16</span>, <em> Know ye not that ye are the temple of God?) </em> and individually (as <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="1co+6:19">1 Corinthians 6:19</span>, <em> Know ye not that your body is the temple of the Holy Ghost?), </em> the figure of building is naturally used to express their improvement and advancement in the life of God’ (Vaughan).
	</p>
