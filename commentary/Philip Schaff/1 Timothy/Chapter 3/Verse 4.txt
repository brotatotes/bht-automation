<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="1ti+3:4">1 Timothy 3:4</span>. <strong> One that ruleth well his own house. </strong> Like the former condition, ‘the husband of one wife,’ the qualification seems to presuppose the experience of home life as practically the best, if not the necessary, preparation for the pastoral office.
