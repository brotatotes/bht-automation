<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="1ti+4:16">1 Timothy 4:16</span>. <strong> Take heed to thyself and to the doctrine. </strong> As before, ‘ <em> teaching’ </em> in its wider sense, rather than ‘doctrine.’ The condensed summary of <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="1ti+4:12-13">1 Timothy 4:12-13</span>, in their bearing on personal conduct and official work.
	</p>
<p><strong> Continue in them</strong>. See <em> </em> in all the ‘things’ dwelt on in the exhortation from <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="1ti+4:6">1 Timothy 4:6</span> onwards, and referred to in <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="1ti+4:15">1 Timothy 4:15</span>.
	</p>
<p><strong> Thou shalt save.</strong> Obviously in the highest sense, as implying the completed salvation from sin and from its penalty.
	</p>
