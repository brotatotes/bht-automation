<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="1ti+5:11">1 Timothy 5:11</span>. <strong> Refuse,</strong> <em> i.e. </em> decline to place them on the register of those entitled to special privileges.
	</p>
<p><strong> Wax wanton.</strong> Another of the vigorous colloquial phrases of the Epistle, implying partly wilful resistance, partly lascivious desire.
	</p>
<p><strong> They will marry.</strong> The Greek is more emphatic: ‘They <em> will </em> or <em> desire </em> to marry.’
	</p>
