<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="1ti+5:19">1 Timothy 5:19</span>. <strong> Against an elder.</strong> Here the context is obviously in favour of the official sense. The rule of ‘two or three witnesses,’ which in <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="de+19:15">Deuteronomy 19:15</span> is given as applicable to all judicial testimony, is here specialized as applying <em> à fortiori </em> to a case where there was a presumption in favour of the accused.
