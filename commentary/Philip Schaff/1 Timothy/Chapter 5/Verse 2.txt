<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="1ti+5:2">1 Timothy 5:2</span>. The exhortation is, of course, parallel to that in <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="1ti+5:1">1 Timothy 5:1</span>, but something more was needed to guard against suspicion and scandal. The free intercourse of a brother with brothers was not equally possible in this case, and therefore the limiting clause is added, ‘in all purity.’
