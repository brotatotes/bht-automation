<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="1ti+5:6">1 Timothy 5:6</span>. <strong> She that liveth in pleasure. </strong> The English words give the sense, but not the terseness or the vigour of the Greek verbs. ‘ <em> She that plays the wanton’ </em> comes somewhat nearer, but implies one form of evil too definitely.
	</p>
<p><strong> Is dead. </strong> Spiritually dead, and therefore to be treated as such for the purpose in band, and her name to be struck off the register of those entitled to support.
	</p>
