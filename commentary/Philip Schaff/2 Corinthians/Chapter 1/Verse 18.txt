<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="2co+1:18">2 Corinthians 1:18</span>. <strong> But as God is true</strong> a solemn asseveration, having the force of an appeal to God, or an oath, <strong> our word toward you is not yea and nay</strong> ‘the word of one not to be depended on.’ No wonder that he makes such an appeal; for by such insinuations he feels his whole credit at stake.
