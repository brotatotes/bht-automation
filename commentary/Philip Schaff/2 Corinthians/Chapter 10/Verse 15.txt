<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="2co+10:15">2 Corinthians 10:15</span>. <strong> not glorying ... in other men’s labours, but having hope that, as your faith groweth, we shall be magnified in you . . . unto further abundance</strong> ‘be encouraged by our success with you to extend our labours,’ <strong> so as to preach the gospel even unto the parts beyond you, and not to glory in another’s line of things made ready to our hand. </strong> The principle here laid down was a fixed one with our apostle. See his remarkable statement of it in <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ro+15:18-23">Romans 15:18-23</span>
