2 Corinthians 10:17.  But he that glorieth, let him glory in the Lord.  So often had he to speak of “glorying” (sixteen times, says Stanley, in this section), that he here finds it needful to remind his readers and himself that the one object of all legitimate glorying is “the Lord” ( Jer 9:24 ; 1 Corinthians 1:29; 1 Corinthians 1:31).
