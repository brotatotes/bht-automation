<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="2co+10:9">2 Corinthians 10:9</span>. <strong> that I may not seem as if I would terrify you by my letters</strong> that is, ‘by my letters only, and at a distance:’ ‘Some seem to think that though I can write terrifying letters, I will be as mild as when I was formerly with you; but they shall find it otherwise, if necessity for action shall arise.’ What follows shows this to be the meaning.
