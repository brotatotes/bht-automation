<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="2co+11:14">2 Corinthians 11:14</span>. <strong> And no marvel; for</strong> <strong> Satan fashioneth himself into an angel of light.</strong> His own element is darkness (<span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="2pe+2:4">2 Peter 2:4</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="jude+1:6">Jude 1:6</span>), and all his power over men lies in his power to keep them in the dark (a Cor. <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="2co+4:4">2 Corinthians 4:4</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="eph+6:12">Ephesians 6:12</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="col+1:13">Colossians 1:13</span>). But knowing that men love the light, and hate darkness, he veils his lies in the dress of truth, and hides his poison in the bait of wholesome truth.
