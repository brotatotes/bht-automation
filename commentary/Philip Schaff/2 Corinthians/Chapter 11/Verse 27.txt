<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="2co+11:27">2 Corinthians 11:27</span>. <strong> in labour and travail, in watchings often, in hunger and thirst </strong> (see <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="1co+4:11">1 Corinthians 4:11</span> <strong> ;</strong> <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="php+4:12">Philippians 4:12</span>), <strong> in fastings often</strong> not voluntary fastings, which he would not mention as a privation he had endured, but in the sense of the next clause, <strong> in cold and nakedness</strong> such as he would doubtless often experience in his travels through inhospitable regions.
