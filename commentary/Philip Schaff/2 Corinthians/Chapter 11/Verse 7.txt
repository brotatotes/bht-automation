<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="2co+11:7">2 Corinthians 11:7</span>. Or changing their ground of complaint <strong> did I commit an offence </strong> ( <em> Gr. </em> ‘sin’) <strong> in abasing myself that ye might be exalted, because I preached the gospel of God for nought? </strong> He had claimed for apostles the right to temporal support from their converts (<span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="1co+9:13">1 Corinthians 9:13</span>), but, conscious (as they insinuated) that he himself was not one, he dared not assert it at Corinth.
