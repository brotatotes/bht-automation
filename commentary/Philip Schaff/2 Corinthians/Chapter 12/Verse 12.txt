<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="2co+12:12">2 Corinthians 12:12</span>. <strong> Truly the signs of an apostle were wrought among you in all patience, by signs, and wonders, and mighty works </strong> <em> (Gr. </em> ‘powers’). In <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="heb+2:4">Hebrews 2:4</span>, these are given as evidences of apostleship, as in <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ac+2:22">Acts 2:22</span>, of the Divine mission of Christ Himself. “How at variance with this,” exclaims Meyer, “is the historical criticism which lays down <em> à priori </em> the negation of miracles!”
