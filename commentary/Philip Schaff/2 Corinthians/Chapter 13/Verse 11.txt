<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="2co+13:11">2 Corinthians 13:11</span>. <strong> Finally, brethren, farewell. Be perfect </strong> (‘perfected’). The word ‘adjusted’ (as a net put in order for casting), ‘thoroughly furnished,’ <strong> be</strong> <strong> of good comfort </strong> (‘comforted’); <strong> be of one mind,</strong> the want of which was the first thing he complained of in his First Epistle (<span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="2co+1:10">2 Corinthians 1:10</span>); <strong> live in peace</strong>; <strong> and the God of love and peace shall be with</strong> <strong> you,</strong> compare <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+5:9">Matthew 5:9</span>, “Blessed are the peacemakers, for they shall be called children of God.”
	</p>
