<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="2co+13:7">2 Corinthians 13:7</span>. <strong> Now we pray to God that ye do no evil; not that we may appear approved, but that ye may do that which is honourable, though we be as reprobate:</strong> ‘My prayer is, that when I come I may find no occasion for the exercise of discipline at all, <strong> </strong> but may find you walking consistently and honourably as befits those who are called by the name of Christ; and this not to justify our Procedure towards you, but for the credit of that holy Name, whatever may be thought of us’ (comp. <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ro+9:3">Romans 9:3</span>).
