<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="2co+2:6">2 Corinthians 2:6</span>. <strong> Sufficient to such a man is this punishment inflicted by the many</strong> either ‘the majority,’ in which case the decision was not unanimous, some dissenting, for reasons not hard to seek: or (taking “the many” to refer to the <em> publicity </em> of the act) in presence of the gathered congregation, as directed expressly in <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="1co+5:4">1 Corinthians 5:4</span>.
