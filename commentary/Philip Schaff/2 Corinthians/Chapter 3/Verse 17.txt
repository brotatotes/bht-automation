<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="2co+3:17">2 Corinthians 3:17</span>. <strong> Now the Lord</strong> the glorified Lord of the Church <strong> is the Spirit</strong> that quickening Spirit, who “by the Holy Ghost sent down from heaven” makes the Gospel “spirit and life” to “as many as receive Him” (<span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="joh+1:12-13">John 1:12-13</span>; and see <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="2co+3:6">2 Corinthians 3:6</span> above). ‘Where Christ is (says Bengel) there is the Spirit of Christ; where the Spirit of Christ is, there is Christ’ (<span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ro+8:9-11">Romans 8:9-11</span>).
	</p>
<p><strong> and where the Spirit of the lord is, there is liberty </strong> (<span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ro+8:15">Romans 8:15</span>) the reverse of that “bondage” which is our apostle’s invariable characteristic of the legal economy.
	</p>
