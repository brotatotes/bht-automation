2 Corinthians 4:10.  always bearing about in the body the dying (  Gr.  ‘the putting to death’)  of Jesus, [1] as virtually dying with Him in our daily exposures to death in His cause: compare 2 Corinthians 4:11; 2 Corinthians 4:16; Rom 8:36 ; 1 Corinthians 4:9; 1 Corinthians 15:31; 2 Timothy 2:11-13,  that the life also of Jesus might be made manifest in our body.
	 [1] The received reading “the Lord Jesus” has scarcely any support.
