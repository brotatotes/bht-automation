<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="2co+4:14">2 Corinthians 4:14</span>. <strong> knowing that he which raised up the Lord Jesus</strong> [1] <strong> shall raise up us also with Jesus,</strong> [2] <strong> and shall present us with you</strong> the parent in the faith with his spiritual children (see <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="eph+5:27">Ephesians 5:27</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="1th+2:9">1 Thessalonians 2:9</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="1th+2:20">1 Thessalonians 2:20</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="jude+1:24">Jude 1:24</span>).
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:3.6em;"> [1] Here the received reading is dearly right; that of Lachmann and Tregelles, followed by Stanley and Green, being supported only by one ancient MS.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:3.6em;"> [2] Not “by Jesus,” as in the received text and Authorised Version on next to no evidence
