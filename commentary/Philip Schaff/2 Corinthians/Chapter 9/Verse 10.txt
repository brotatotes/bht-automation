<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="2co+9:10">2 Corinthians 9:10</span>. <strong> And he that supplieth seed to the sower and bread for food, shall supply</strong> [1] <strong> and multiply your seed for sowing, and increase the fruits of your righteousness.</strong> What <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="2co+9:8">2 Corinthians 9:8</span> said that God was “able” to do, it is here said, He “will” do.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:3.6em;"> [1] <em> The future </em> here is clearly the true reading.
