<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="2co+9:3">2 Corinthians 9:3</span>. <strong> But I have sent the brethren, that our glorying on your behalf may not be made void in this respect</strong> (‘as if we had gone too far in commending your eagerness’); <strong> that, even as I said, ye may be prepared: lest by any means, if there come with me any of Macedonia, and find you unprepared, we (that we say not, ye) should be put to shame in this confidence.</strong> [1]
