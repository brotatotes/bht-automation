<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="2co+9:6">2 Corinthians 9:6</span>. <strong> But this I say</strong> ‘Let this as a general principle be borne in mind,’ <strong> He that soweth sparingly</strong> parsimoniously, grudgingly, <strong> shall reap also sparingly; and he that soweth bountifully</strong> ( <em> Gr</em>. ‘with blessings’ his heart going along with his hand in wishing “blessings” on the objects of his charity),
	</p>
<p><strong> shall also reap bountifully.</strong> On the great principle here expressed, see <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ex+25:2">Exodus 25:2</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ex+35:5">Exodus 35:5</span>; 1Ch 29:14 ; <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="1ch+29:17">1 Chronicles 29:17</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ps+112:9">Psalms 112:9</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="pr+11:24-25">Proverbs 11:24-25</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="pr+19:17">Proverbs 19:17</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="pr+22:9">Proverbs 22:9</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+6:38">Luke 6:38</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ac+20:35">Acts 20:35</span>. The figure of sowing and reaping, natural in an agricultural country, and, as such, used by our Lord (<span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+13:3">Matthew 13:3</span>, etc.; <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="joh+4:35-38">John 4:35-38</span>), is a favourite one with our apostle (<span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="1co+9:11">1 Corinthians 9:11</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="1co+15:36-37">1 Corinthians 15:36-37</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="1co+15:42-44">1 Corinthians 15:42-44</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ga+6:7-8">Galatians 6:7-8</span>).
