<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="2co+9:9">2 Corinthians 9:9</span>. <strong> as it is written</strong> (<span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ps+112:9">Psalms 112:9</span>), <strong> He hath scattered abroad</strong> ‘a generous word’ (says Bengel), ‘with a full hand, not anxiously thinking about the single grains;’
	</p>
<p><strong> he hath given to the poor: his righteousness</strong> (in dispersing abroad of his means) <strong> abideth for ever</strong> enabling him always to manifest by his gifts what he is. That this is the sense of ‘righteousness abiding’ here, is evident from <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="2co+9:10">2 Corinthians 9:10</span>.
	</p>
