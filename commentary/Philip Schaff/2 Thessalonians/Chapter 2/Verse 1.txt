<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="2th+2:1">2 Thessalonians 2:1</span>. <strong> Concerning the coming.</strong> The preposition used by Paul means perhaps something more than ‘concerning,’ and is equivalent to ‘in explanation or defence of.’ The coming of Christ had been misrepresented; Paul now does it justice by setting it in a clearer light.
	</p>
<p><strong> Our gathering together.</strong> That is, the gathering together of all Christians to be for ever with Christ, spoken of in the First Epistle, <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="1th+4:17">1 Thessalonians 4:17</span>.
	</p>
