<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="2th+3:11">2 Thessalonians 3:11</span>. For. Paul gives his reason for introducing this subject at all. And he further defines ‘walking disorderly.’ He leaves no doubt as to the persons about whom he speaks. They were those who were excitedly busy, but doing no useful work; running hither and thither, meddling with every one’s business but their own, striving to bring others into the same state of excitement as themselves, and declining all ordinary, steady, profitable, obscure labour.
	</p>
