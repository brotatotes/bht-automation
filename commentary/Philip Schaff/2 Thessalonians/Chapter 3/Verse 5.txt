<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="2th+3:5">2 Thessalonians 3:5</span>. <strong> May the Lord direct,</strong> <em> i.e. </em> may Christ, who is faithful (<span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="2th+3:3">2 Thessalonians 3:3</span>), direct.
	</p>
<p><strong> Into the love of God</strong>. To love God is to have in the heart the root of all activity and endurance, the spring of duty, and the fountain of all virtue.
	</p>
<p><strong> And into the stedfastness of Christ.</strong> The apostle desires they may be enabled to exhibit under trial the same patient endurance which Christ Himself exhibited.
	</p>
