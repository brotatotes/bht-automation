<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="2ti+2:1">2 Timothy 2:1</span>. <strong> Thou therefore, </strong> <em> i.e. </em> as following the example of Onesiphorus.
	</p>
<p><strong> Be strong. </strong> The Greek verb, passive and not middle, implies at once the being strengthened, and the continuance in the state thus reached; and this process and state find the element of their life in the grace which is found in Christ.
	</p>
