<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="2ti+2:12">2 Timothy 2:12</span>. <strong> Shall also reign with him.</strong> The thought, though not the words, enters into our Lord’s teaching: ‘Ye shall sit on thrones, judging the twelve tribes of Israel’ (<span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+19:28">Matthew 19:28</span>).
	</p>
<p><strong> He also will deny us. </strong> The words clearly point to our Lord’s teaching in <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+10:33">Matthew 10:33</span>, and, like other passages in these Epistles, indicate that there was a widely-diffused knowledge of it.
	</p>
