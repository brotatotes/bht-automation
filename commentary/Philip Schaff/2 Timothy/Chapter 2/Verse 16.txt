<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="2ti+2:16">2 Timothy 2:16</span>. <strong> Shun. </strong> The word gives the sense, but hardly the force of the Greek: ‘ <em> Draw back from,’ </em> as a group of men draw back from something horrible and loathsome.
	</p>
<p><strong> Will eat. </strong> Again a strictly medical term: ‘ <em> Will take its course, as if feeding on the flesh.’
