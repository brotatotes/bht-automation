<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="2ti+2:17">2 Timothy 2:17</span>. <strong> Canker. </strong> Another medical word <strong> -</strong> Literally <em> ‘gangrene,’ </em> the state between inflammation and entire mortification. The word is used by Hippocrates sometimes in this special sense, sometimes of cancer.
	</p>
<p><strong> Hymenæus and Philetus. </strong> The former has been mentioned already (<span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="1ti+1:20">1 Timothy 1:20</span>). Of the latter nothing more is known.
