<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="2ti+2:7">2 Timothy 2:7</span>. <strong> Consider</strong> <strong> what I say. </strong> St. Paul contents himself with suggesting the analogies of the two previous verses, and leaves it to Timothy’s reflection to see their bearing on himself.
	</p>
<p><strong> And the Lord give thee.</strong> The better reading gives <em> ‘for the Lord will give thee.’ </em> This gives also a better meaning: ‘Make the effort to reflect, for if thou do, the Lord will give thee the discernment which thou needest.’
	</p>
