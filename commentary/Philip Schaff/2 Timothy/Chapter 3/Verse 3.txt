<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="2ti+3:3">2 Timothy 3:3</span>. <strong> Truce-breakers.</strong> Better, ‘ <em> implacable</em>,’ not the persons who break a truce, but those with whom no truce can be made.
	</p>
<p><strong> False accusers. </strong> Better, ‘ <em> slanderers</em>,’ <em> </em> as in <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="1ti+3:11">1 Timothy 3:11</span>.
	</p>
<p><strong> Fierce. </strong> More definitely, ‘ <em> untameable</em> ’ or ‘ <em> inhuman</em>.’
	</p>
<p><strong> Despisers of them that are good.</strong> Better, ‘ <em> having no love of good</em>.’
	</p>
