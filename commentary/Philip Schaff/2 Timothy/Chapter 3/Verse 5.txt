<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="2ti+3:5">2 Timothy 3:5</span>. <strong> Form. </strong> The Greek word suggests the idea of a manufactured article, the <em> ‘fashion </em> or <em> semblance</em> ’ of piety.
	</p>
<p><strong> Denying.</strong> The Greek participle is in the perfect, <em> ‘having denied’ </em> or <em> ‘repudiated.’
	</em></p>
<p><strong> From such turn away.</strong> The injunction implies, what is in other ways apparent, that. St. Paul thought of the characteristic features of the last days as already present.
	</p>
