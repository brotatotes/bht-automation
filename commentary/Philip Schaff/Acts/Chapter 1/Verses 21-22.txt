<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ac+1:21-22">Acts 1:21-22</span>. The necessary conditions of apostleship were the having been an eye and ear witness of all that had taken place from the day the baptism of John until the day of the ascension.
	</p>
<p>The office <em> </em> of an apostle is briefly summed up in he statement, ‘He must’ ‘be a witness of the resurrection.’ This one event in the history of the Lord on earth is chosen as the central point round which all teaching respecting the life and work of Christ must cluster.
	</p>
