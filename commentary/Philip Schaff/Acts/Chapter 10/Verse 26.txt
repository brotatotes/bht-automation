<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ac+10:26">Acts 10:26</span>. <strong> Stand up: I myself also am a man. </strong> We are at once reminded of the horror expressed by Paul and Barnabas, when the attempt was <strong> </strong> made at Lystra to give them Divine homage (<span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ac+14:14">Acts 14:14</span>), and of the repudiation of this kind of homage by the angel in St. John’s vision (<span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="re+22:8-9">Revelation 22:8-9</span>); and we necessarily contrast with all this our Lord’s calm acceptance of such worship, as is recorded more than once in the Gospel History.
	</p>
