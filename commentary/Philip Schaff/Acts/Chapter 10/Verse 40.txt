<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ac+10:40">Acts 10:40</span>. <strong> Him God raised up on the third</strong> <strong> day. </strong> Here, as everywhere in the Acts of the Apostles, the Resurrection is the culminating point of the apostolic testimony concerning Jesus Christ (see, for instance, <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ac+2:24">Acts 2:24</span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ac+17:31">Acts 17:31</span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ac+26:23">Acts 26:23</span>).
	</p>
<p><strong> Showed him openly.</strong> Literally, ‘gave Him to become visibly manifest.’
