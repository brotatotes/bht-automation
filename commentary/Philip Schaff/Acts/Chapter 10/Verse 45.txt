<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ac+10:45">Acts 10:45</span>. <strong> They of the circumcision which believed were astonished, as many as came with Peter.</strong> The expression in the original is very strong. They were almost out of their mind with wonder. As to the persons whose wonder here forms so prominent a part of the scene, see <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ac+10:23">Acts 10:23</span> and <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ac+11:12">Acts 11:12</span>.
