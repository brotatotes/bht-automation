<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ac+11:14">Acts 11:14</span>. <strong> Words, whereby thou and all thy house shall be saved. </strong> See notes on <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ac+10:6">Acts 10:6</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ac+10:22">Acts 10:22</span>. ‘All thy house’ is a special addition here. The promise is in harmony with the preparation made for the Gospel in the house of Cornelius, as implied in <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ac+10:2">Acts 10:2</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ac+10:7">Acts 10:7</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ac+10:22">Acts 10:22</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ac+10:24">Acts 10:24</span>, and with the results of St. Peter’s preaching, as described in <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ac+10:44">Acts 10:44</span>.
