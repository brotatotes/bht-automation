<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ac+11:8">Acts 11:8</span>. <strong> Hath at any time entered into my mouth. </strong> The same kind of comment may he made here <strong> </strong> as in the other cases. St. Luke has ‘I have never eaten.’ St. Peter expresses the matter more strongly, and with a personal feeling.
