<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ac+13:12">Acts 13:12</span>. <strong> Believed. </strong> That Sergius Paulus was baptized is the natural inference. ‘Believed’ is the ordinary expression used in the early Christian records for turning to the Lord and joining the Church (so <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ac+4:4">Acts 4:4</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ac+11:21">Acts 11:21</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ac+19:18">Acts 19:18</span>). The proconsul of Cyprus is another instance of men of high rank joining the Christian brotherhood in very early times (see note on <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ac+13:1">Acts 13:1</span> of this chapter).</p>
