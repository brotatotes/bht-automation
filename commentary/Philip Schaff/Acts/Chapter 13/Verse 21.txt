<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ac+13:21">Acts 13:21</span>. <strong> By the space of forty years. </strong> The Old Testament does not mention the length of Saul’s reign. The statement here, however, agrees with Josephus, who speaks of Saul reigning eighteen years before Samuel’s death and twenty-two after it.</p>
