<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ac+13:24">Acts 13:24</span>. <strong> When John had first preached before his coming. </strong> The literal translation of the Greek gives a clearer meaning: ‘John having preached before His coming.’ Paul mentions this preaching and testimony of the Baptist to Jesus as a thing well known. A vast number of the Jews seem to have acknowledged John’s authority as a prophet. His mission created a great stir in the Holy Land; and later we read of his disciples at Ephesus, some twenty-five years after the death of the Baptist (<span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ac+19:3">Acts 19:3</span>).</p>
