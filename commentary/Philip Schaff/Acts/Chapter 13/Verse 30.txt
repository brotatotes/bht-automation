<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ac+13:30">Acts 13:30</span>. <strong> But God raised him from the dead.
	</strong></p>
<p>Paul with great force and power here contrasts the work of God with the work of men. <em> Men </em> rejected, scorned, and then crucified Jesus; <em> God </em> raised Him from the dead.</p>
