<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ac+13:32">Acts 13:32</span>. <strong> And we declare unto you. </strong> And thus, Paul went on to say, while the apostles and eyewitnesses are at this moment carrying out <em> their </em> mission in the Holy Land to the Jews, <em> we</em> (Paul and Barnabas) are preaching to you in these distant lands the same glad truths.</p>
