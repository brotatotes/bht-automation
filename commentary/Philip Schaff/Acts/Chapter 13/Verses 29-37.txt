<p><em> The Argument of the following ten verses</em>, 28 - 37 , <strong> </strong> <em> is as follows</em> <strong> : </strong></p>
<p>I. Act 13:28-29 . The Jews put the innocent Jesus to death, and then laid Him in the grave.
	</p>
<p>II. Act 13:30-31 . But God raised Him from the dead; and of the resurrection we possess many eye-witnesses.
	</p>
<p>III. Act 13:32-33 . In this resurrection of Jesus, God hath fulfilled His great promise to the fathers of Israel, for it is His resurrection which is the great proof of His Messiahship.
	</p>
<p>IV. <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ac+13:34-37">Acts 13:34-37</span>. The Risen One, according to the Word of God contained in the writings of the prophets, will never die.</p>
