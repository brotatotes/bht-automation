<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ac+14:26">Acts 14:26</span>. <strong> And thence they sailed to Antioch.</strong> The famous Syrian Antioch is here meant. It was from the Christian Church in Antioch that the Apostles Barnabas and Paul had received their commission to preach in the Gentile churches. They now returned to the same church to give a formal account of their mission.
	</p>
