<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ac+15:11">Acts 15:11</span>. <strong> But we believe that, through the grace of our Lord Jesus Christ, we shall be saved, even as they. </strong> The believing Jew, who has tried to keep the law and failed, will be saved like the Gentile through the power of the blood of Jesus. <em> ‘Their</em> ground of trust is the same as <em> ours, ours</em> <strong> </strong> as <strong> </strong> <em> theirs’</em> (Alford; and see <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ga+2:15">Galatians 2:15</span> and following verses, where this train of thought is more fully carried out by St. Paul).</p>
