<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ac+15:15">Acts 15:15</span>. <strong> And to this agree the words of the prophets. </strong> After referring here to the <em> work of God</em> <strong><em> </em></strong> instanced by Peter, James now shows how completely the <em> word of God</em> in the writings of the prophets agreed with this <em> work.</em> The signs and wonders which accompanied the conversion of Cornelius, and subsequently crowned the missionary labours of Paul and Barnabas, were only the Divine seal of a great work long ago foreshadowed in the Hebrew prophecies.</p>
