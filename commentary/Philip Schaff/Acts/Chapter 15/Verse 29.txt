<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ac+15:29">Acts 15:29</span>. <strong> That ye abstain from meats offered to idols. </strong> The articles in the letter of <strong> </strong> the Council are identical with the points mentioned by James in his speech. They are discussed in the Excursus.
	</p>
<p><strong> Fare ye well </strong> The Greek word rendered ‘fare ye well’ is equivalent to the Latin ‘valete.’ It was the customary conclusion to letters among the Greeks. See the epistle of Claudius Lysias to Festus, <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ac+23:30">Acts 23:30</span>.</p>
