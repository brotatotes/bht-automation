<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ac+15:34">Acts 15:34</span>. <strong> Notwithstanding it pleased Silas to abide there still. </strong> This verse is wanting in the older MSS., <strong><em> </em></strong> and in many of the chief versions. It is evidently a marginal gloss, originally inserted to explain how Silas, notwithstanding the statement of <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ac+15:33">Acts 15:33</span>, was at hand (<span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ac+15:40">Acts 15:40</span>) conveniently for Paul to choose him as companion in travel.</p>
