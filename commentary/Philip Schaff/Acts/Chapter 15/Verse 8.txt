<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ac+15:8">Acts 15:8</span>. <strong> And God, which knoweth the hearts, bare them witness, giving them the Holy Ghost, even as he did unto us. </strong> The Eternal, before whom the secrets of all hearts are open, was able to judge of the sincerity of these Gentiles. He testified that these hitherto despised strangers were acceptable in His sight by giving them the Holy Ghost, just as He had done to the Jews who had turned and believed in Jesus.</p>
