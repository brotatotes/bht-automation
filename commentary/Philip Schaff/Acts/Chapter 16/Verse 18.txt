<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ac+16:18">Acts 16:18</span>. <strong> I command</strong> <strong> thee in the name of Jesus Christ</strong> <strong> to come</strong> <strong> out of</strong> <strong> her. </strong> ‘In My Name,’ said the Lord (<span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+16:17">Mark 16:17</span>), ‘shall they cast out devils.’ It is noticeable how differently such acts as these were performed by the Master and His servants. Christ worked His miracles in His own sovereign power; the apostles worked theirs only in the name of Jesus.
