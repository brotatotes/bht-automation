<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ac+17:12">Acts 17:12</span>. <strong> Also of honourable women which were Greeks, and of men not a few. </strong> To these Jews who accepted the doctrines preached by Paul were added a number of Gentiles, some, of course, proselytes, but most probably idolaters for the most part; and these, the writer of the ‘Acts’ tells us, were men and women of the highest rank among the Greek citizens.
