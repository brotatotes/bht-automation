<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ac+18:11">Acts 18:11</span>. <strong> And he continued there a year and six months. </strong> This year and a half was the whole period of his residence at Corinth. It was during this lengthened stay that the apostle wrote the two epistles to the church of Thessalonica, the earliest letters we possess of St. Paul.
	</p>
