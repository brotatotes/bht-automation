<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ac+19:20">Acts 19:20</span>. <strong> So</strong> <strong> mightily</strong> <strong> grew the word of God and prevailed. </strong> Somewhere about this time Paul wrote his First Epistle to the Corinthians. It is more than probable that when he penned the words, ‘For a great door and effectual is opened unto me, and there are many adversaries’ (<span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="1co+16:9">1 Corinthians 16:9</span>), the apostle was alluding to the events at Ephesus just related, and which led to the state of things the writer of the ‘Acts’ summarised by ‘So mightily grew the <strong> </strong> word of God and prevailed.’
	</p>
