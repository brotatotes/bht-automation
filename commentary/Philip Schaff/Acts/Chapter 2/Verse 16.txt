<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ac+2:16">Acts 2:16</span>. <strong> This is that which was spoken by the prophet Joel. </strong> <em> This, </em> namely, the wonderful utterances of praise, the crowds from so many nations had been just listening to. The quotation, with a few unimportant variations (which will be noticed in their places), is from the LXX., <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="joe+3:1-5">Joel 3:1-5</span>, Hebrew, <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ac+2:28-30">Acts 2:28-30</span>. The passage from Joel is describing the signs which were to herald the beginning of the Messianic period, ‘the last days of the world’s history.’
	</p>
