<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ac+2:31">Acts 2:31</span>. <strong> He seeing this before spake of the resurrection of Christ, that his soul was not left </strong> in <strong> hell, neither did his flesh see corruption. </strong> David as a seer looking ( παίδων ) into the far future, wrote of this great Descendant of his of whom the prophet Nathan had spoken as establishing the throne of his kingdom <em> for ever</em> as One who should die and yet should not see corruption, for He should be raised from the dead.
	</p>
