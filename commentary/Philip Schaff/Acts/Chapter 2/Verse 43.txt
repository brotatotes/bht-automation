<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ac+2:43">Acts 2:43</span>. <strong> And fear came upon every soul. </strong> The general impression on the public mind. A feeling of awe was excited even among those who did not join the company of believers. <strong> And many wonders and signs were done by the apostles. </strong> The healing of the lame man by Peter and John, related in the 3d chapter, is an instance of one of these.
