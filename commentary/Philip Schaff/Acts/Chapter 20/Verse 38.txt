<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ac+20:38">Acts 20:38</span>. <strong> That they should see his face no more. </strong> In this both Paul and his companions in work the elders of Ephesus were no doubt wrong. St. Paul most probably did revisit these shores, and no doubt Ephesus and its church, after his liberation from the Roman imprisonment. See the note on <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ac+20:25">Acts 20:25</span>, where this is discussed at some length.
