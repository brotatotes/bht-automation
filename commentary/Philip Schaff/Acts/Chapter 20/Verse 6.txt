<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ac+20:6">Acts 20:6</span>. <strong> And came unto them in Troas in five days. </strong> This lengthy voyage was, no doubt, owing to contrary winds, or perhaps to a calm. On a former occasion, we read of this voyage being made in two days (see <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ac+16:11">Acts 16:11</span>).
