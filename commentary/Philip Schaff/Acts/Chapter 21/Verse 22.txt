<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ac+21:22">Acts 21:22</span>. <strong> What is it therefore? the multitude must needs come together: for they will hear that thou art come. </strong> ‘Seeing, now, this is the state of things, that you, Paul, are looked upon by a large number of our countrymen with jealous suspicion and dislike, let us consider what is best under the circumstances for you to do; for it if certain that out of all these multitudes of foreign Jews come up to keep Pentecost in Jerusalem, a great number will always be watching you and your actions, to see whether what they have heard alleged against you be just, for the news of your arrival will be soon noised abroad.’
