<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ac+22:13">Acts 22:13</span>. <strong> And the same hour I looked up upon him</strong>. That is to say, Ananias stood before the stricken Saul, and spoke as he was commanded the healing words of power; then Saul turned his heavy blinded eyes in the direction of the voice which spoke to him, and the sight came back, and ‘he looked upon Ananias.’ One commentator thus paraphrases: ‘I looked up with recovered sight upon him.’
	</p>
