<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ac+22:18">Acts 22:18</span>. <strong> Make haste, and get thee quickly out of Jerusalem: for they will not receive thy testimony concerning me</strong>. ‘They’ included both the Jews still strangers to the new covenant, and also the Jewish Christians of the Holy City. By the former he was hated as an apostate; by the latter he was viewed probably as a spy, and as no real Christian.
	</p>
