<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ac+22:27">Acts 22:27</span>. <strong> Art thou a Roman? </strong> The haughty officer, proud of his nationality, could scarcely believe that the poor accused and probably insignificant-looking Jew before him was a citizen of Rome. The pronoun is strangely emphatic: ‘ <em> Thou</em> art <em> thou </em> a Roman?’
