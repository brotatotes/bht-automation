<p><strong> About noon. </strong> This ‘note of time’ does not appear in the former account. A <em> light </em> which could compel attention at such an hour in the full glare of an eastern noon, must be regarded at once as something out of the ordinary course of nature. This mention of the exact time when the Glorious Vision appeared was evidently a <em> personal </em> recollection of the event.
