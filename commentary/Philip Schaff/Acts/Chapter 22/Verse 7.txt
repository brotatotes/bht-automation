<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ac+22:7">Acts 22:7</span>. <strong> Saul, Saul. </strong> <em> Here, </em> and again in Paul’s own account before Agrippa and Bernice at Cæsarea, the language ‘Hebrew’ is specially noticed (chap. <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ac+26:14">Acts 26:14</span>); and also in the narrative of chap. 9, the Aramaic (Hebrew) form of Saul, ‘Saoul,’ is found. The voice from heaven had so imprinted itself on the memory of Paul that he reproduces the call to him as he first heard it.
	</p>
