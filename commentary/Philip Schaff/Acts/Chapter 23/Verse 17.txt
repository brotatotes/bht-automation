<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ac+23:17">Acts 23:17</span>. <strong> Then Paul called one of the centurions unto him, and said, Bring this young man unto the chief captain</strong>. Attention has been justly called here to the fact that, although Paul had just received the Divine promise of protection in all these present dangers, yet he neglected none of the ordinary means of safety which were presented to him, evidently looking on them as the saving hand of the Lord stretched out to him. There was nothing of wild unreasoning enthusiasm in the great missionary apostle.
	</p>
