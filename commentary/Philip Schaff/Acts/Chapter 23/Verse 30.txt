<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ac+23:30">Acts 23:30</span>. <strong> And when it was told me how that the Jews laid wait for the man, I sent straightway to thee</strong>. ‘Though I thought him innocent,’ writes Claudius Lysias, ‘yet, hearing of this further plot against the life of a Roman citizen, I judged it wise to refer the whole matter to you, the chief authority in Judæa, as the affair may be more serious than I have deemed it to be.’ The soldier evidently suspected the affair of Paul was mixed up with some movement against the Roman power.
	</p>
