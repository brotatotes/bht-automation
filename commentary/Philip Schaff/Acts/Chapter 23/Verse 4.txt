<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ac+23:4">Acts 23:4</span>. <strong> And they that stood by said, Revilest thou God’s high priest?</strong> It was and St. Paul knew it well contrary to the law of Moses (see <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ex+22:28">Exodus 22:28</span>, subsequently quoted by him) to revile one placed in a position of authority, such as the high priest, or any one sitting as president of the Sanhedrim council, ‘the father of the house of judgment,’ as the Talmud calls him.
	</p>
