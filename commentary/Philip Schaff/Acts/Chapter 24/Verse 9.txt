Acts 24:9.  And the Jews also assented, saying that these things were so.  The rendering of the better authenticated reading, συνεπε ́ θεντο   instead of συνε ́ θεντο , would be: ‘And the Jews also  assailed  him (or them);’ that is, they joined their voices to their advocate’s in his charges against Paul, and probably against Claudius Lysias also, bearing their testimony to the truth of the facts as alleged in court by Tertullus.
