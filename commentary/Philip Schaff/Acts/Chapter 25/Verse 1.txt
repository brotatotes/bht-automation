<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ac+25:1">Acts 25:1</span>. <strong> Now when Festus was come into the province. </strong> The Greek word translated ‘province’ is an unusual one in the case of a division of one of the greater provincial governments: ε ̓ παρχι ́ α ͅ <em> . </em> The term we find here was perhaps used in consequence of the importance of Judæa at that time, although it was only reckoned as a part of the imperial province of Syria. The proprætor or proconsul ruled over the greater province, the procurator over the smaller division.
