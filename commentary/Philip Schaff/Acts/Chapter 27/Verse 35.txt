<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ac+27:35">Acts 27:35</span>. <strong> He took bread, and gave thanks to God in the presence of them all. </strong> It is difficult to believe that there was not at this moment in his mind a reminiscence of the Sacred Eucharist, especially when we observe that the ‘breaking’ of the bread is specified. See <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+24:30">Luke 24:30</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+24:35">Luke 24:35</span>.
	</p>
<p><strong> He began to eat. </strong> He set the example. We see from what follows that this is by no means .unimportant.
