<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ac+28:12">Acts 28:12</span>. <strong> Landing at Syracuse. </strong> Or rather, ‘putting into harbour at Syracuse.’ This was <strong> </strong> in their direct course. The distance is about eighty miles to the north of Malta.
	</p>
<p><strong> Three days. </strong> From what follows, it seems probable that they were waiting for a fair wind.
