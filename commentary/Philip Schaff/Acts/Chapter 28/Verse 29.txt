<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ac+28:29">Acts 28:29</span>. <strong> And when he had said these words, the Jews departed, and had great reasoning among themselves.</strong> The whole of this verse is wanting in the more ancient MSS. and versions, and must be considered spurious. It was, however, probably added in early times by some scribe, to soften down the apparent abruptness of the close of the account of the interview between Paul and the leading Jews of Rome.
	</p>
