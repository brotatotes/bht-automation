<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ac+3:5">Acts 3:5</span>. <strong> And he gave heed unto them</strong>. The sufferer, perhaps surprised at this unusual notice from a passer-by, gazed up at Peter and John with rapt attention (the Greek word is far stronger than the English equivalent), <em> knowing </em> he was about to receive some kindness, he knew not what, from these holy men, whom doubtless he knew well by sight, having often seen them go up to the Temple.
	</p>
