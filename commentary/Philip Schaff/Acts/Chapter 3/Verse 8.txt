<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ac+3:8">Acts 3:8</span>. <strong> And entered with them into the temple. </strong> Instead of at once going to his home or any other place, his first thought seems to have been: ‘He would go into the sanctuary of his God and there return thanks for his great deliverance.’ Peter and John, guided by the Holy Ghost <strong> , </strong> when they cast their eyes on the poor cripple, were not deceived in their estimate of his character.
