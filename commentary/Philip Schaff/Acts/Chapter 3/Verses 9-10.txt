<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ac+3:9-10">Acts 3:9-10</span>. <strong> And all the people saw him walking. </strong> The crowds in the temple-court knew him as he walked among them for that helpless beggar whom they had seen so many times lying by the ‘Beautiful Gate;’ they saw, him now, who had never walked before, full of life and power, praising God, and were struck with amazement and wonder at the greatness of the miracle.
