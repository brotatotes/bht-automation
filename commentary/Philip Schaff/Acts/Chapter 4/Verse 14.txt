<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ac+4:14">Acts 4:14</span>. <strong> Standing with them. </strong> The attitude of the healed one is mentioned with emphasis. No longer the cripple who had never walked or stood, and who by compassionate friends had been carried daily and laid as a suffering object to ask alms at the beautiful gate, he now <em> stands </em> near his deliverers.
	</p>
