<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ac+6:11">Acts 6:11</span>. <strong> Then they suborned men, which said. </strong> That is, they secretly instructed, having concerted together what should be said.
	</p>
<p><strong> Blasphemous words. </strong> According to the law of Moses, blasphemy consisted in contempt of Moses and his institutions, and was a capital offence (see <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="de+13:6">Deuteronomy 13:6</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="de+13:10">Deuteronomy 13:10</span>). This charge brought against Stephen was the same which was made against Christ, and for which, as far as the Jews were concerned, He was condemned.
	</p>
