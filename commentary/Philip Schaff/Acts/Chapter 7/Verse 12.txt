<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ac+7:12">Acts 7:12</span>. <strong> There was corn in Egypt.</strong> Egypt was the great corn-growing country of the old world. In later times it became the principal granary of Rome (see <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ac+27:6-38">Acts 27:6-38</span>).
