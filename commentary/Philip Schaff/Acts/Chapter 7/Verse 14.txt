<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ac+7:14">Acts 7:14</span>. <strong> And called his father Jacob, and all his kindred, threescore and fifteen souls. </strong> Another memory of Divine favour which Stephen knew would be very grateful to the zealous Jews who sat as judges in that stern council. How the Eternal must have loved the people and prospered them! for from this small family sprang that mighty host which was ‘as the stars of heaven for multitude’ (<span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="de+10:22">Deuteronomy 10:22</span>).
