<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ac+7:17">Acts 7:17</span>. <strong> The people grew and multiplied in Egypt.</strong> They increased so rapidly in power as well as in numbers, that the jealousy of the reigning dynasty was excited against them. The marvellous increase of the little shepherd family, who had been settled in Egypt some two hundred years previously by the minister Joseph, was well calculated to alarm the advisers of a Pharaoh who knew nothing of the claims of the Hebrew tribes upon the goodwill of the country.
	</p>
