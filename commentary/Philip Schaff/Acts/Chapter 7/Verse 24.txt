<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ac+7:24">Acts 7:24</span>. <strong> Suffer wrong. </strong> That is, injured by blows, as in <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ex+2:11">Exodus 2:11</span>.
	</p>
<p><strong> And smote the Egyptian. </strong> He struck the Egyptian who did the wrong so as to kill him. ‘The Egyptian,’ simply without any previous allusion, because the story was so well known.
	</p>
