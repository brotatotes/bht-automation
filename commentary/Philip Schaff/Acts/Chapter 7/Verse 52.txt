<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ac+7:52">Acts 7:52</span>. <strong> The Just One. </strong> This title was used by the Jews as a designation of the Messiah. ‘This sentence (of Stephen’s) seems to have been in the mind of the second apostolic martyr at Jerusalem, St. James, when he wrote his epistle a little before his own martyrdom,’ <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="jas+5:6">James 5:6</span> (Wordsworth).
