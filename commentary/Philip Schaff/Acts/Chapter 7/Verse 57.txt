<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ac+7:57">Acts 7:57</span>. <strong> Then they cried out with a loud voice. </strong> When they heard Stephen in his awful joy saying that he beheld ‘the Crucified’ encircled with the visible glory, thus boldly confessing that the Shekinah belonged to Jesus of Nazareth, they could contain themselves no longer; the purport of their cries no doubt was identical with the memorable expression of the high priest, recorded by St. Matthew (<span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+26:65-66">Matthew 26:65-66</span>), who, when Jesus claimed as belonging to Him the Majesty of heaven, ‘rent his clothes, saying, He hath spoken blasphemy; what think ye? They answered and said, He is guilty of death.’
	</p>
