<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="col+1:4">Colossians 1:4</span>. <strong> Having heard. ‘</strong> After hearing,’ rather than, ‘since we heard,’ or, <strong> ‘</strong> because we heard.’
	</p>
<p><strong> Your faith in Christ Jesus. </strong> He is the sphere and object of the faith; comp. <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="eph+1:15">Ephesians 1:15</span>.
	</p>
<p><strong> The love which ye have to</strong> <strong> all the saints</strong>. The Italics of the E. V. are unnecessary, since the better supported reading <strong> </strong> gives ‘which ye have,’ thus emphasizing the tact that they possess it. </p>
