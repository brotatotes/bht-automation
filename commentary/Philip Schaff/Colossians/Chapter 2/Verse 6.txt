<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="col+2:6">Colossians 2:6</span>. <strong> As then </strong> (in view of this order and stedfastness) <strong> ye received, </strong> <em> i.e., </em> by instruction from your teachers, not, ‘as ye accepted.’
	</p>
<p><strong> Christ etui the Lord. He </strong> was the object in which the instruction centred; the emphasis resting, as the full phrase shows, upon His Person. They had been taught ‘Him;’ comp. <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="eph+4:20">Ephesians 4:20</span>.
	</p>
<p><strong> walk in him. </strong> He is the element of your life; let the life correspond with the teachings you have received,
	</p>
