<p>III. HORTATORY PART: LIVE AS THOSE SHOULD LIVE WHO WERE RAISED WITH CHRIST THE HEAD.
	</p>
<p>(1.) Fellowship with the Exalted Christ the motive for the new life; <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="col+3:1-4">Colossians 3:1-4</span>. (Transitive paragraph.)
	</p>
<p>(2.) General exhortations; <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="col+3:5-17">Colossians 3:5-17</span>. (Negative, <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="col+3:5-11">Colossians 3:5-11</span>; and positive, <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="col+3:12-17">Colossians 3:12-17</span>.)
	</p>
<p>(3.) Special precepts as to household relations; chaps. <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="col+3:18">Colossians 3:18</span> to <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="col+4:1">Colossians 4:1</span>.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:2.28em;"><em> (a.)</em> Wives and husbands (<span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="col+3:18-19">Colossians 3:18-19</span>).
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:2.28em;"> ( <em> b</em>.) Children and parents (<span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="col+3:20-21">Colossians 3:20-21</span>).
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:2.28em;"><em> (c.)</em> Servants and masters (chap. <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="col+3:22">Colossians 3:22</span> to <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="col+4:1">Colossians 4:1</span>).
	</p>
<p>(4.) Concluding exhortation, in relation to prayer and conduct toward those without; <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="col+3:2-6">Colossians 3:2-6</span>.
	</p>
