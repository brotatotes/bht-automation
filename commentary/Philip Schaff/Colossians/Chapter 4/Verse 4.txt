<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="col+4:4">Colossians 4:4</span>. <strong> That I may make it manifest. </strong> This is the end of the speaking, or of the entire petition. They should pray that he might preach; but he should preach in order to make manifest the mystery of Christ.
	</p>
<p><strong> As I ought to speak. </strong> Comp. <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="eph+6:20">Ephesians 6:20</span>; but there the reference is to his labors, while still imprisoned. Here the meaning is: ‘as I ought to do it (namely, freely and unrestrainedly), so as best to advance and further the gospel’ (Ellicott). </p>
