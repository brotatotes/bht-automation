<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="eph+6:19">Ephesians 6:19</span>. <strong> And on my behalf, </strong> especially for him among the saints. Not for himself, but for the sake of the gospel he thus speaks.
	</p>
<p><strong> That</strong> <strong> utterance may be given</strong>; from God.
	</p>
<p><strong> In the</strong> <strong> opening of my month, </strong> <em> i.e., </em> when I speak for God, the reference being, not to the quality or source of the discourse, but to the simple fact of speaking.
	</p>
<p>In boldness <strong> </strong> is emphatic, and to be connected with <strong> to make known, </strong> etc., which indicates the purpose for which he desired utterance and asked their supplications.
	</p>
<p><strong> The mystery of the gospel</strong>; the mystery contained in the gospel; comp. chaps. <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="eph+1:9">Ephesians 1:9</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="eph+3:9">Ephesians 3:9</span>.
	</p>
