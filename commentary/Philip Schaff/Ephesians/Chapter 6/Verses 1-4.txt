<p>2. <em> Christian Duties of Children and Parents.
	</em></p>
<p>These exhortations naturally follow those of the last section and rest, as the previous ones do, upon the common relation to the Lord. For the children of Christian parents, through the vital fellowship with them, are recognized as ‘holy’ (<span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="1co+7:14">1 Corinthians 7:14</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ac+16:15">Acts 16:15</span>), <em> i.e., </em> consecrated to the Lord (so Meyer).
	</p>
