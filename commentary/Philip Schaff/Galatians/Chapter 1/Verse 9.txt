Galatians 1:9.  Before  refers not to Galatians 1:8, which is too near, but to the last visit of Paul to Galatia.
	 Preach  εὐαγγελίζεται implies the actual fact, not the mere possibility, as the hypothetical  should preach  (εὐαγγελίζηται, Galatians 1:8), and thus attacks more directly the Galatian pseudo-apostles.
