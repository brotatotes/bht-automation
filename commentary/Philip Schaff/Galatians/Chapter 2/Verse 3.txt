<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ga+2:3">Galatians 2:3</span>. <strong> Yet not even Titus .... being a Greek, </strong> or although he was a Greek, that is, a heathen. Far from declaring my labors fruitless and disapproving my gospel, the Jewish Apostles did not force even Titus, my companion and co-laborer, much less the body of the Gentile converts, to submit to circumcision, although the Judaizing party peremptorily demanded it as a condition of justification (as appears from <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ga+2:4-5">Galatians 2:4-5</span>, and <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ac+15:5">Acts 15:5</span>).
	</p>
