<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ga+3:5">Galatians 3:5</span>. The present tense <strong> ministereth </strong> and <strong> worketh </strong> is used to indicate the continued communication and abundant supply of the spiritual gifts.
	</p>
<p><strong> Powers, </strong> miraculous powers, <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="1co+12:10">1 Corinthians 12:10</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="1co+12:28-29">1 Corinthians 12:28-29</span>.
	</p>
<p><strong> In</strong> <strong> you, </strong> <em> i.e.,</em> in your heart and will; comp. <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+14:2">Matthew 14:2</span>. Paul probably means the moral miracles of regeneration and conversion. Others understand here physical miracles wrought ‘among you,’ <em> i.e.</em>, in the midst of you.
	</p>
