<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ga+3:6">Galatians 3:6</span>. <strong> </strong> The only reply the Galatians could make to the foregoing question was: ‘By the preaching of faith.’ Taxing this for granted, Paul proceeds (as in <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ro+4:1">Romans 4:1</span>) to give the historical and scriptural proof from the example of Abraham, the father of the faithful. The words are a quotation from <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ge+15:6">Genesis 15:6</span> (Sept.). The emphasis lies on <strong> believed, </strong> <em> i.e.,</em> trusted in God.
	</p>
