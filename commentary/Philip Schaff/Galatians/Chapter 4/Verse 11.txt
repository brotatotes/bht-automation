<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ga+4:11">Galatians 4:11</span>. <strong> I am</strong> <strong> apprehensive of you, last haply I have toiled for you in vain. </strong> This verse is, as it were, bathed in tears, and betrays the deep and painful solicitude of a faithful pastor for his stray sheep, or a tender father for his erring children. It leads to the affectionate appeal, <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ga+4:12">Galatians 4:12</span> ff.
