<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ga+4:23">Galatians 4:23</span>. <strong> But the son from the bondmaid was born after the flesh, </strong> in the regular course of nature. (Used somewhat differently in <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ro+1:3">Romans 1:3</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ro+9:5">Romans 9:5</span>.) <strong> But the son of the freewoman is through the promise, </strong> by virtue of supernatural influence, by the Spirit of God working through the word of promise (as in the conception of our Lord). <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ge+17:16">Genesis 17:16</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ge+17:19">Genesis 17:19</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ge+18:10-11">Genesis 18:10-11</span>; comp. <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ro+4:19">Romans 4:19</span>.
