<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ga+4:28">Galatians 4:28</span>. <strong> But ye</strong>, <strong> brethren, as Isaac was (or, after the manner of Isaac), are children of promise. </strong> Resumes the main subject; comp. <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ga+4:23">Galatians 4:23</span>. Christian believers are born, like Isaac, of the unfruitful Sarah, contrary to the ordinary course of nature, by the supernatural power of the divine promise, and are therefore children of the heavenly Jerusalem.
	</p>
