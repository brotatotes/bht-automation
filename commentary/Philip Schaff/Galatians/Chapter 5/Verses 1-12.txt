<p><em> Exhortation to Steadfastness in Christian Freedom</em>, <em> and Warning against Legal Bondage.
	</em></p>
<p>Here begins the practical part of the Epistle, consisting of exhortations and warnings appropriate to the occasion. First, the Apostle exhorts them to hold fast their spiritual freedom which they enjoy in Christ, and not to relapse again into legal bondage.
