<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ga+6:13">Galatians 6:13</span>. <strong> For not even they who are circumcised keep the law</strong> (in all its details, comp. <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ga+5:3">Galatians 5:3</span>), <strong> but they desire to have you circumcised, that they may glory in your flesh.</strong> The advocates of circumcision are not sincere in their zeal, but want to gratify their vanity in making proselytes. (Comp. <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+23:15">Matthew 23:15</span>.)
