<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ga+6:3">Galatians 6:3</span>. The best motive of forbearance towards others is the sense of our own weakness.
	</p>
<p><strong> Being nothing, </strong> notwithstanding his conceit. Every man is apt to overestimate himself; humility is one of the rarest, but sweetest graces. ‘In Christian morality self-esteem is vanity, and vanity is nothingness.’
	</p>
