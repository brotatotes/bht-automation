<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="heb+1:12">Hebrews 1:12</span>. <strong> As a mantle shalt thou roll them up, as a garment also shall they be changed </strong> a quotation from <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ps+102:0">Psalms 102:0</span>, with the words ‘as a garment’ added, on the authority of the best MSS. The heavens and the earth are to be lolled up as done with, and they are to be changed for a new heaven and a new earth, wherein dwelleth righteousness.
