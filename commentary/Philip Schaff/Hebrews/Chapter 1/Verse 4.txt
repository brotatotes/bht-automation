<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="heb+1:4">Hebrews 1:4</span>. <strong> Having become</strong>, after He had made at nement for sin, as much superior to the <strong> angels, as he has obtained a name far more excellent than they</strong>. His greatness is partly essential and partly acquired (see <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="php+2:6-11">Philippians 2:6-11</span>). The first He had as Son before the world was; the second He obtained through His incarnation, and after He had suffered.
