<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="heb+10:9">Hebrews 10:9</span>. <strong> Then saith he</strong> (literally, hath He said), <strong> He</strong> (that is, Christ) <strong> taketh away the first</strong>, <strong> that he may establish</strong> (set up) <strong> the second</strong>. Legal sacrifices are abolished that there may be substituted for them, the will the good pleasure of God, which Christ came to do by the one sacrifice of Himself.
	</p>
