<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="heb+10:11-14">Hebrews 10:11-14</span>. With this appropriate result that He is exalted as Priest and King to the right hand of his Father.
	</p>
<p><strong> And every priest</strong> (‘high priest’ has less MS. authority and is less appropriate) <strong> standeth</strong> (not permitted to sit in God’s presence as if he were at home and his work were done), <strong> ministering and offering oftentimes</strong>, morning and evening, day after day, <strong> the same sacrifices</strong>, with no result. All that were offered had the same deficiency <strong> that they could nohow and never strip off all round</strong>, <strong> take clean away</strong> the guilt of <strong> sins</strong>. Some sense of relief, some hope they might give; but the sin itself still clung to the worshippers.
	</p>
