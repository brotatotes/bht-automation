<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="heb+11:12">Hebrews 11:12</span>. <strong> Wherefore also</strong> (a common Pauline expression, <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ro+4:22">Romans 4:22</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ro+15:22">Romans 15:22</span>, etc.) <strong> from one</strong> (the emphatic part) <strong> sprang there</strong>, etc. from a single, nay a lifeless, source sprang there a race like the dust of the earth (<span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ge+13:16">Genesis 13:16</span>), the stars of the heaven, the sand on the lip (the margin) of the sea, innumerable; and through faith Abraham became the father and Sarah the mother of them all.
