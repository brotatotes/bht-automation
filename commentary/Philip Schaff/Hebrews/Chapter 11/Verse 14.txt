<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="heb+11:14">Hebrews 11:14</span>. <strong> For</strong> (they proved that they lived and died in faith) <strong> they who say of themselves that they are sojourners </strong> (<span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ge+23:4">Genesis 23:4</span>) of their life that it is a pilgrimage (<span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ge+47:9">Genesis 47:9</span>), a wandering in a foreign land, make it plain that it is a fatherland, a true home, they are seeking, and not the home they have left in the country of Terah, or elsewhere.
