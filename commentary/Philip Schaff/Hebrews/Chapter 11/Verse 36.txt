<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="heb+11:36">Hebrews 11:36</span>. <strong> Others had trial</strong> (experience) <strong> of cruel mockings and scourgings. </strong> The allusion again is to the Maccabees ( 2Ma 7:7-10 ).
	</p>
<p><strong> Yea, moreover</strong> (a harder thing, because of the continuance and depressing influence of it), <strong> of bends of imprisonment </strong> perhaps with reference to Jonathan ( 1Ma 13:12 ), or to Hanani, Micaiah, and especially to Jeremiah (see references).
	</p>
