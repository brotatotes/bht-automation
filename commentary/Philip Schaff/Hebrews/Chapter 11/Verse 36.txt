Hebrews 11:36.  Others had trial (experience)  of cruel mockings and scourgings.  The allusion again is to the Maccabees ( 2Ma 7:7-10 ).
	 Yea, moreover (a harder thing, because of the continuance and depressing influence of it),  of bends of imprisonment  perhaps with reference to Jonathan ( 1Ma 13:12 ), or to Hanani, Micaiah, and especially to Jeremiah (see references).
