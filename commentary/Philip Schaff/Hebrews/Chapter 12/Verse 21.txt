<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="heb+12:21">Hebrews 12:21</span>. <strong> And so terrible was the sight</strong> (what was made to appear) <strong> that Moses</strong> shared their feeling of dread. Such was the access to God which ancient Israel possessed an access that belonged to a visible mountain full of terror; an access rather of repulse and enforced approach, which they prayed might cease.
