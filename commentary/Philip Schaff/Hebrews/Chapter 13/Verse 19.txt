<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="heb+13:19">Hebrews 13:19</span>. <strong> And I beseech you the more exceedingly </strong> (earnestly) <strong> to do this</strong>, <em> i.e</em> <strong><em> </em></strong> to pray for us (comp. <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="phm+1:22">Philemon 1:22</span>), <strong> that I may be restored to you the sooner. </strong> This language agrees remarkably with the deep affection Paul cherished for the Hebrew Church at Jerusalem, a Church he visited many times.
