<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="heb+2:12">Hebrews 2:12</span>. <strong> The church.</strong> The Old Testament name is the congregation. But in modern usage the congregation is one thing, and the church is another; and it is the church that best represents the sense, the exact meaning of the original and the force of the argument.
	</p>
