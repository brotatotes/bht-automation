<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="heb+3:3">Hebrews 3:3</span>. <strong> Builded. </strong> The word implies gathering or making the materials, putting them together, and furnishing the whole, even appointing the servants doing all that is necessary for completing ‘the house’ as a home. Even Moses, therefore, is regarded as part of the house which God prepared.
	</p>
