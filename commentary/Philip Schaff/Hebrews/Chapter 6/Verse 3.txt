<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="heb+6:3">Hebrews 6:3</span>. <strong> And this will we do</strong>. Let us try to raise each other to the higher ground of matured intelligence.
	</p>
<p><strong> If so be that God permit</strong> (favour and help). Whether any of us have so far forfeited His grace as to be incapable of further progress, God only knows; the writer hopes the best (<span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="heb+6:9">Hebrews 6:9</span>); but there is a backsliding, an apostasy, from which it is impossible to return. The position is therefore very solemn, will anyhow need special help, and the work may be even impossible.
	</p>
