<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="heb+7:20-22">Hebrews 7:20-22</span>. A third argument is now introduced. The oath which God sware in making His Son Priest gives to His office higher sanctions.
	</p>
<p><strong> And inasmuch as</strong> (it is) <strong> not without an oath</strong>; rather <strong> a</strong> simpler filling up of the omission than the Authorised Version, though ‘He was made (or came to be) priest’ better represents what is really a new argument.
	</p>
