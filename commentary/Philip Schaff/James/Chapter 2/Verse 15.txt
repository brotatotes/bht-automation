<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="jas+2:15">James 2:15</span>. To prove the uselessness of a barren faith, the apostle illustrates the subject by showing the uselessness of a barren charity, which every one will at once admit; and this illustration is the more appropriate, as love is the indispensable attendant on a <strong> </strong> living faith the instrument by which it works (<span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ga+5:6">Galatians 5:6</span>).
	</p>
<p><strong> If a brother or sister </strong> a Christian brother or sister a <strong> </strong> fellow-believer bringing forward more strongly our duty to assist them, and our culpability if we refuse such assistance.
	</p>
<p><strong> be naked and destitute of daily food </strong> be reduced to a <strong> </strong> state of extreme destitution. By daily food is <strong> </strong> meant the food necessary for each day.
