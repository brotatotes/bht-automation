<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="jas+3:11">James 3:11</span>. Now follow, after the apostle’s method, two illustrations of this incongruity, taken from the natural world. <strong> Doth a fountain send forth at the same place</strong>: literally, ‘at the same hole or fissure’ from the same spring.
	</p>
<p><strong> sweet water and bitter:</strong> literally, ‘the sweet and the bitter.’
	</p>
