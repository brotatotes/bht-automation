<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="jas+3:16">James 3:16</span>. <strong> For,</strong> the reason assigned for the above description of earthly wisdom, <strong> where envying and strife is</strong>; where zeal (in a bad sense) and party-strife are, <strong> there is confusion and every evil work </strong> all kinds of wickedness. Certainly the reference is primarily to religious controversy; but the supposition that the controversy between the Jewish and Gentile Christians is here referred to is without foundation.
	</p>
