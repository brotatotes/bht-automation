<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="joh+1:32">John 1:32</span>. <strong> And John bare witness, saying. I have beheld the Spirit descending. </strong> The effect of what the Baptist had seen had remained, and still remains, with him in all its power: ‘I <em> have</em> <strong><em> </em></strong> beheld.’
	</p>
<p><strong> And it abode upon him. </strong> John had not merely seen the Spirit descend with dove-like motion upon Jesus; he had also seen that it ‘abode’ upon Him, the symbol of an abiding and permanent possession.
	</p>
