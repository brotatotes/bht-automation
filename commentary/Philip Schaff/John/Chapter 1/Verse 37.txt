<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="joh+1:37">John 1:37</span>. <strong> And the two disciples heard him speak, and they followed Jesus.</strong> The witness of the Baptist has its proper effect, an effect, we cannot doubt, foreseen and designed by himself (chap. <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="joh+3:27-30">John 3:27-30</span>). Those who listen to it turn from him, and follow Jesus.
