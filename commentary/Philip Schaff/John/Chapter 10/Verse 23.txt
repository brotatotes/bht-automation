<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="joh+10:23">John 10:23</span>. <strong> And Jesus walked in the temple-courts, in Solomon’s porch. </strong> The ‘porch’ which bore Solomon’s name was a covered colonnade on the eastern side of the outer court of the temple. According to Josephus this ‘porch’ was the work of Solomon: at all events we may well believe that the massive foundations were laid by him, though the cloisters which he built were in ruins when Herod began his restoration of the temple.</p>
