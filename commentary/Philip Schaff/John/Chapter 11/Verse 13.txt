<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="joh+11:13">John 11:13</span>. <strong> Howbeit Jesus had spoken of his death: but they thought that he spake of taking of rest in sleep. </strong> The figure can hardly have been here used by Jesus for the first time. The misconception of His meaning would seem to have arisen from His words in <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="joh+11:4">John 11:4</span>, and from His delay in setting out for Bethany.</p>
