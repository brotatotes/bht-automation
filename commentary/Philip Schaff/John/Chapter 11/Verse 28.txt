John 11:28.  And when she had s o  said, she went away, and called Mary her sister, saying secretly, The Teacher is come, and calleth thee.  We cannot doubt that Mary until now had been in ignorance of the coming of Jesus, or that it was at His bidding that Martha told her sister secretly of His call for her. That which He was about to do   He would have faith, not unbelief, to see; therefore Mary must be called ‘secretly.’
