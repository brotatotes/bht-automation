<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="joh+11:29">John 11:29</span>. <strong> And she, when she heard it, arose quickly, and went unto him. </strong> Mark the characteristic touch in the words ‘arose quickly’ (comp. <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="joh+11:20">John 11:20</span>). <strong> </strong> ‘Went unto,’ <em> i.e.,</em> <strong> </strong> started on her way, for it is in <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="joh+11:32">John 11:32</span> that the actual coming is spoken of.
