<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="joh+11:3">John 11:3</span>. <strong> The sisters therefore sent unto him saying, Lord, behold, he whom thou lovest is sick.</strong> Their confidence in the love and in the power of Jesus is shown by the absence of any request: the message is a tender and delicate expression of their need. With the description of Lazarus compare chap. <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="joh+20:2">John 20:2</span> (where the same verb for ‘love’ is used), ‘the disciple whom Jesus loved.’</p>
