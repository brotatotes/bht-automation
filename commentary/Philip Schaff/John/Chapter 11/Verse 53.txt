<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="joh+11:53">John 11:53</span>. <strong> From that day forth, therefore, they took counsel that they might put him to death. </strong> Not that they might pass sentence of death upon him; that is done: but that they might execute the sentence. Their previous efforts of rage against Jesus had been connected with moments of special excitement; henceforward they are <strong> </strong> deliberate, determined, constant. The cup of iniquity of ‘the Jews’ is full.</p>
