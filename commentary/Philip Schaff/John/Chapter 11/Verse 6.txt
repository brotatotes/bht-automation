<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="joh+11:6">John 11:6</span>. <strong> When he had heard therefore that he was sick, at that time indeed he abode in the place where he was two days. </strong> ‘Therefore’ is explained by the two verses which precede (see the last note). He cannot accept the moment suggested by man (comp. chap. <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="joh+2:4">John 2:4</span>); He cannot follow at once the prompting of His affection for disciples. He will go to assuage their grief, but only at the moment appointed by the Father’s will.</p>
