<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="joh+11:7">John 11:7</span>. <strong> Then after that he saith to the disciples, Let us go into Judea again? </strong> Jesus does not say ‘to Bethany,’ but to ‘Judea;’ for He knows that this visit to Bethany will bring Him again into the midst of His enemies, ‘the Jews,’ and will lead to a development of their hatred and malice which will find satisfaction only in His death. In the full consciousness of what awaits Him He prepares to depart for Bethany.</p>
