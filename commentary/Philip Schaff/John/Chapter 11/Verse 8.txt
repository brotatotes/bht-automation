<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="joh+11:8">John 11:8</span>. <strong> The disciples say unto him, Rabbi, but now the Jews were seeking to stone thee; and goest thou thither again?</strong> The words ‘but now’ (only just now) seem to show that the sojourn in Perea (chap. <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="joh+10:40">John 10:40</span>) was short. The disciples see clearly that to go to Bethany is as perilous as to return to Jerusalem, where He has but now escaped from the rage of ‘the Jews’ (chap. <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="joh+10:31">John 10:31</span>).</p>
