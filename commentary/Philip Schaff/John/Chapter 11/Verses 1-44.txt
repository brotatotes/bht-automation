<p>The manifestation of Jesus by Himself is about to terminate so far at least as the world is concerned, and it does so in His revealing Himself as the Resurrection and the Life, the Conqueror of death in the very height of its power. The raising of Lazarus illustrates this. The account as a whole divides itself into two subordinate parts ( 1 ) <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="joh+11:1-16">John 11:1-16</span>; ( 2 ) <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="joh+11:17-44">John 11:17-44</span>.</p>
