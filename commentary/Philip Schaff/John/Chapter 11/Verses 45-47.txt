<p>The most striking of all the miracles of Jesus has been performed, and His manifestation of Himself to the world has ended. The effect <strong> </strong> is proportionate. On the one hand, faith is awakened in the hearts of ‘many’ of His most determined enemies ‘the Jews.’ On the other hand, final measures are taken to seize and kill Him. Jesus retires to a city near the wilderness along with His disciples. It is the pause before the last journey to Jerusalem, to which He is to go as the Paschal Lamb selected for the true Paschal sacrifice and feast. The subordinate parts are ( 1 ) <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="joh+11:45-46">John 11:45-46</span>; ( 2 ) <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="joh+11:47-53">John 11:47-53</span>; ( 3 ) <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="joh+11:54-57">John 11:54-57</span>.</p>
