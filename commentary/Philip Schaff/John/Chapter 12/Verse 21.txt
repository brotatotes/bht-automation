<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="joh+12:21">John 12:21</span>. <strong> These came therefore to Philip, which was of Bethsaida of Galilee, and asked him saying, Sir, we would see Jesus. </strong> To suppose that their object is to ask Jesus to institute a mission to the Gentiles, or to come to them Himself, is to misapprehend the nature of the situation. It is their own personal faith that John desires to bring out.
	</p>
