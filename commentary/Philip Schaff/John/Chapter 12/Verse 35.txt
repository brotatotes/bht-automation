<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="joh+12:35">John 12:35</span>. <strong> Jesus therefore said unto them, Yet a little while is the light among you. </strong> Not so much words of pity and tenderness in order to clear away the doubts of a sincere desire to learn, as words of solemn warning that they had a day of grace granted them, but that it was now drawing to a close, and that, if they did not pass beyond all doubts to faith, they would be <strong> </strong> overtaken by darkness.
	</p>
<p><strong> Walk as ye have the light, that darkness overtake you not. </strong> That is, ‘Walk in accordance with the fact that the light now shines around you.’
	</p>
<p><strong> And he that walketh in the darkness knoweth not whither he goeth. </strong> If they do not thus walk, thus come to the light (chap. <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="joh+3:21">John 3:21</span>), the darkness will overtake them; and instead of going to the glory to which Jesus ‘goeth,’ they will go blindly to destruction.</p>
