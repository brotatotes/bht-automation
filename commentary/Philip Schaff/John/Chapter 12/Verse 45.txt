<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="joh+12:45">John 12:45</span>. <strong> And he that beholdeth me, beholdeth him that sent me. </strong> In chap. <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="joh+6:40">John 6:40</span> (see note) we have the same combination as in these verses: ‘He that beholdeth the Son and believeth in Him.’ A little later the same thought finds fuller expression in words addressed to disciples (chap. <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="joh+14:9">John 14:9</span>). Compare chap. <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="joh+1:18">John 1:18</span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="joh+15:24">John 15:24</span>.</p>
