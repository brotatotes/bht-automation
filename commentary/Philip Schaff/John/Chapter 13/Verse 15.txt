<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="joh+13:15">John 13:15</span>. <strong> For I gave you an example, that ye also should do even as I did to you. </strong> What the giver of a commission does may well be done by the servant to whom the commission is given. It is important to observe that the act spoken of is only that of ‘washing one another’s feet.’</p>
