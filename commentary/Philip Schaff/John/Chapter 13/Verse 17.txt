<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="joh+13:17">John 13:17</span>. <strong> If ye know these things, happy</strong> <em> </em> <strong> are ye if ye do them. </strong> Simple as might appear the duty to which the disciples were called, Jesus knew that it was a hard and trying task. He connects therefore a promise of blessedness with the actual performance of the duty.</p>
