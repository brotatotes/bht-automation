<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="joh+16:14">John 16:14</span>. <strong> He shall glorify me, because of that which is mine will</strong> <strong> he receive</strong> <strong> and will declare it unto you. </strong> On the glorifying of Jesus here spoken of, see on chap. <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="joh+13:31">John 13:31</span>. This glory will be given Him by the powerful working of the Holy Spirit in the Church, <em> because </em> that which the Spirit applies for the ever increasing growth and efficiency of the Church is only a fuller unfolding of ‘the unsearchable riches of Christ.’ To Him as the Alpha and Omega of our faith, and never beyond Him, the Spirit leads us.
	</p>
