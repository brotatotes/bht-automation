<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="joh+17:16">John 17:16</span>. <strong> They are not of the world, even as I am not of the world. </strong> These words met us in <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="joh+17:14">John 17:14</span>, but they are again introduced in a slightly different order, the emphasis being now thrown on of <strong> </strong> the world, in order to prepare the way for the complete antithesis to be immediately expressed.</p>
