<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="joh+18:12">John 18:12</span>. <strong> The band of soldiers therefore, and the captain, and the officers of the Jews, took Jesus and bound him. </strong> The words addressed by Jesus to Peter <strong> </strong> lend boldness to His cowardly foes. They see that no further resistance is to be offered. A passive victim is before them; and they seize and bind Him.</p>
