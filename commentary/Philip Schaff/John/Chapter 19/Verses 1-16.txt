<p>The dreadful tragedy is still continued; and that it is so in the same line of thought and with the same object as before, is evident from the parallelism between chap. <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="joh+18:33-40">John 18:33-40</span> and chap. <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="joh+19:1-16">John 19:1-16</span>. The subject is the humiliation of Jesus, the half-hearted efforts of Pilate to release Him, and the determined hostility and cruelty of the Jews.</p>
