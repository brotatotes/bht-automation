<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="joh+20:10">John 20:10</span>. <strong> The disciples therefore went away again unto their own home. </strong> We are not told why or in what frame of mind they thus returned to their own homes. One thing is clear: they believed that Jesus was risen, and that it was vain to search for Him in the tomb.
