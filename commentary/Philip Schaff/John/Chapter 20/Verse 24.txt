<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="joh+20:24">John 20:24</span>. <strong> But Thomas, one of the twelve, called Didymus, was not with them when Jesus came. </strong> On the object of thus interpreting the name Thomas, see on chap. <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="joh+11:16">John 11:16</span>. It is impossible to think that the Evangelist translates the word for the mere purpose of mentioning that Thomas had a <strong> </strong> Greek as well as an Aramaic name. The man appears in the name.</p>
