<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="joh+20:26">John 20:26</span>. <strong> And after eight days again his disciples were within, and Thomas with them. </strong> The place of assembly was without doubt the same as before; and that the apostles were assembled on the Sunday appears to indicate that they already regarded the host day of the week as a day which the Risen Lord would peculiarly bless.
	</p>
<p><strong> Jesus cometh when the doors had been shat, and stood in the midst, and said, Peace he unto you. </strong> All is the same as at <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="joh+20:19">John 20:19</span>.
	</p>
