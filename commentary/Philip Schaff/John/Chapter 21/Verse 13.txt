<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="joh+21:13">John 21:13</span>. <strong> Jesus cometh and taketh the loaf, and giveth them, and the fish likewise. </strong> We might have expected to read of the ‘fishes’ rather than the ‘fish,’ for the meal prepared must have included a portion of the ‘fishes’ of <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="joh+21:10">John 21:10</span> as well as the ‘fish’ of <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="joh+21:9">John 21:9</span>. Yet such is the importance which the Evangelist attaches to the latter that he speaks of it alone, and makes no farther allusion to the rest.
	</p>
