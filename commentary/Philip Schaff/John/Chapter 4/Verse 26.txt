<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="joh+4:26">John 4:26</span>. <strong> Jesus saith unto her, I that speak unto thee am he.</strong> She has sought and found the truth. The hope rising in her heart receives full confirmation; and a revelation not yet so clearly and expressly given by Jesus to Israel is granted to this alien, whose heart is prepared for its reception.</p>
