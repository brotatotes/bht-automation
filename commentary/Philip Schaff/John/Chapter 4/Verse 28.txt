<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="joh+4:28">John 4:28</span>. <strong> The woman therefore left her water-pot, and went her way into the city.</strong> ‘Therefore, ‘ because, the conversation being interrupted, there was nothing to restrain her impulse to make known the marvels she had heard. In her eagerness she leaves her waterpot behind: the ‘living water’ has banished the thought of that which came from Jacob’s well.
	</p>
<p><strong> And saith to the men</strong>, whom she would naturally meet on the roads and in the streets.</p>
