<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="joh+4:40">John 4:40</span>. <strong> When therefore the Samaritans were come unto him, they besought him that he would abide with them: and he abode there two days. </strong> Mark the contrast between Judea repelling and Samaria inviting: a dead and petrified orthodoxy may be more proof against the word of life than heresy.</p>
