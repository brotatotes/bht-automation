<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="joh+4:51">John 4:51</span>. <strong> And as he was now going down, his servants met him, saying that his son lived. </strong> The word ‘now’ (or ‘already’) may appear superfluous, but it may possibly imply that some time had elapsed since the words of <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="joh+4:50">John 4:50</span> were spoken, ‘when he had now begun the journey.’ Business may have detained him for a few hours in Cana; and if it did so, it would be a testimony to the firmness of that faith with which he had now believed in Jesus. ‘Going down,’ because Cana is situated in the hilly district, several hundred feet above the level of the Sea of Galilee.</p>
