<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="joh+5:12">John 5:12</span>. <strong> They asked him, Who is the man which said unto thee, Take up, and walk? </strong> The mention of the cure has no effect in leading them to suspend their judgment. It would indeed present to them a new transgression of the law; but they content themselves with passing it by, and laying stress on what they consider an undeniable breach of the very letter of the commandment. This complete indifference to the work of mercy plainly illustrates the hard-hearted malice of ‘the Jews.’</p>
