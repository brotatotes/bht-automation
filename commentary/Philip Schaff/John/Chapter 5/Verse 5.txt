<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="joh+5:5">John 5:5</span>. <strong> And a certain man was there, which had been thirty and eight years in his sickness.</strong> This sufferer (apparently one of the ‘withered,’ though not altogether destitute of the power of motion) had endured thirty-eight years of weakness. How long he had been wont to resort to Bethesda we cannot tell: it may have been only for days or even hours.</p>
