<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="joh+5:8">John 5:8</span>. <strong> Jesus saith unto him, Rise, take up thy bed, and walk.</strong> The cure is performed in the most simple and direct manner. It is not said that Jesus laid His hands on him (<span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+13:13">Luke 13:13</span>), or that He touched him. He speaks: the man hears the voice of the Son of God and lives (<span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="joh+5:25">John 5:25</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="joh+5:28-29">John 5:28-29</span>).</p>
