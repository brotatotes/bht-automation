<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="joh+6:16">John 6:16</span>. <strong> And when even was now come, his disciples went down unto the sea. </strong> Before Jesus retired to the mountain He had constrained His disciples to leave Him for the shore: when they had left He dismissed the people, withdrawing from them, probably by exercising such influence as is implied in chap. <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="joh+5:13">John 5:13</span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="joh+8:59">John 8:59</span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="joh+10:39">John 10:39</span>.</p>
