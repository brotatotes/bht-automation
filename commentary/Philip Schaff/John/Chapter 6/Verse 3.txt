<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="joh+6:3">John 6:3</span>. <strong> And Jesus went up into the mountain, and there he sat with his disciples. </strong> He retired for the purpose of rest and prayer, and that he might instruct his disciples, the twelve who had just returned from their mission (<span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+6:30">Mark 6:30</span>). ‘The mountain’ we must probably understand in a general sense as meaning the high ground near Bethsaida. In this part the eastern hills closely approach the lake.</p>
