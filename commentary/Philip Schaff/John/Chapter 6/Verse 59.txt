<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="joh+6:59">John 6:59</span>. <strong> These things said he, as he was teaching in a synagogue in Capernaum. </strong> These words not only give information as to the place in which the discourse (probably <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="joh+6:41-58">John 6:41-58</span>; see note on <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="joh+6:40">John 6:40</span>) was delivered, but also show the boldness with which Jesus declared truths so new and so surprising to His hearers. He spoke thus in public teaching (comp. chap. <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="joh+18:20">John 18:20</span>), and that too in the presence of His powerful enemies, and in the place where their influence was greatest.</p>
