<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="joh+6:65">John 6:65</span>. <strong> And he said, For this cause have I said unto you, that no one can come unto me, except it have been given unto him of the Father. </strong> They had seemed genuine disciples, but His words had been to them a stumbling-block and had not brought life. They had not really come to Him: they had not received from the Father the gift of ‘coming unto’ Jesus, but the failure had been by their own fault. Having resisted the drawing of the Father, they had lacked the due preparation of heart for receiving the words of Jesus (see the notes on <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="joh+6:37">John 6:37</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="joh+6:44">John 6:44</span>).</p>
