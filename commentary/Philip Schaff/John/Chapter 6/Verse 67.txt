<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="joh+6:67">John 6:67</span>. <strong> Jesus therefore said unto the twelve, Would ye also go?</strong> <strong><em> </em></strong> In contrast with the desertion of many is the strengthened faith of those who, being of the light, are attracted by the light. The ‘Twelve’ are here mentioned by John for the first time.</p>
