<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="joh+7:14">John 7:14</span>. <strong> And when it was already the middle of the feast, Jesus went up into the temple-courts, and taught.</strong> It is evident that the Evangelist means to impress us with the suddenness of this appearance of Jesus in the temple-courts. The Lord suddenly comes to His temple, and, at this feast of peculiar joy and hope, He brings with Him a special message and promise of the new covenant (<span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="joh+7:38">John 7:38</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mal+3:1">Malachi 3:1</span>). His teaching during the latter half of the sacred week is to prepare for His words on the last day of the feast.</p>
