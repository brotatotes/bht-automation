<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="joh+7:26">John 7:26</span>. <strong> And, lo, he speaketh boldly, and they say nothing unto him. Can it be that the rulers know that this is the Christ?</strong> <strong><em> </em></strong> No opinion as to these designs is expressed; there is neither sympathy nor blame; there is only bewilderment, occasioned by the inconsistency between the supposed wishes of the rulers and the boldness and freedom with which Jesus is allowed to speak. Can it be that there is some secret reason for this, that the rulers have really made a discovery, which they will not allow , that this is the Christ?
