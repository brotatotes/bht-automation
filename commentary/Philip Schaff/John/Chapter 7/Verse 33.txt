<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="joh+7:33">John 7:33</span>. <strong> Jesus therefore said, Yet a little while am I with you, and I go unto him that sent me.</strong> In the action now taken by His foes Jesus sees a token of the rapidity with which His hour is approaching. These words, which (<span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="joh+7:35">John 7:35</span>) were spoken in the presence of ‘the Jews,’ declare His perfect knowledge of their designs. But they are also words of judgment, taking from His enemies their last hope.</p>
