<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="joh+7:46">John 7:46</span>. <strong> The officers answered, Never did a man so speak.</strong> A new testimony to Jesus, borne by men who, awed by the majesty of His words, instead of attempting a deed of violence, declare to their very masters that He is more than man.
