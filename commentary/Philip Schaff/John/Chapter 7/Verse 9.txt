<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="joh+7:9">John 7:9</span>. <strong> And when he had said these things unto them he abode still in Galilee.</strong> How long, we are not informed. As, however, it would seem that His brothers were on the point of setting out for Jerusalem, to be present at the beginning of the festival, and as He Himself was teaching in the temple when the sacred week had half expired (<span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="joh+7:14">John 7:14</span>), the interval spent in Galilee can hardly have been more than two or three days.</p>
