<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="joh+7:41-42">John 7:41-42</span>. <strong> Others said, This is the Christ Some said, What, doth the Christ come out of Galilee? Hath not the scripture said. That the Christ cometh of the seed of David, and from Bethlehem, the village where David was? </strong> See <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+2:6">Matthew 2:6</span>. This explanation of the prophecy of Micah (chap. <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="joh+5:2">John 5:2</span>) is found in the Targum, and seems to have been commonly received by the Jews.</p>
