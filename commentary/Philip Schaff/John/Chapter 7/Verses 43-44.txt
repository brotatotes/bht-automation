<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="joh+7:43-44">John 7:43-44</span>. <strong> There arose therefore a division among the multitude because of him. And some of them would have seized him;</strong> but no man laid hands on him. Compare <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="joh+7:30">John 7:30</span>. Here, as there, the result of the division of opinion is a more eager attempt to apprehend Him about whom the dispute has arisen. The last words of <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="joh+7:30">John 7:30</span> may be again supplied in thought: ‘his hour was not yet come.’</p>
