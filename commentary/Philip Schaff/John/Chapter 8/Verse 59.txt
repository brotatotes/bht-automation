<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="joh+8:59">John 8:59</span>. <strong> They took up stones therefore that they might cast them upon him; but Jesus hid himself, and went forth from the temple-courts.</strong> The Jews were enraged at what they considered blasphemy, and in their rage they would have stoned Him (compare chap. <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="joh+10:31">John 10:31</span>). But His hour was not yet come. He hid Himself (whether miraculously or not we cannot tell) and went forth from the temple.
	</p>
