<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="joh+9:25">John 9:25</span>. He <strong> therefore answered, Whether he be a sinner, I know not: one thing I know, that, whereas I was blind, now I see. </strong> His simplicity leaves them no real excuse for condemning: by his steel fast adherence to the one testimony which he alone was competent to <strong> </strong> render, he most effectually brings condemnation on his judges, who, had they been sincere, would first have sought certain knowledge of the fact (see note on <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="joh+9:16">John 9:16</span>).</p>
