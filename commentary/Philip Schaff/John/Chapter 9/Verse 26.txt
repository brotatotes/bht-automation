<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="joh+9:26">John 9:26</span>. <strong><em> </em></strong> <strong> They said therefore to him, What did he to thee? how opened he thine eyes?</strong> Every attempt to overthrow the <em> fact</em> has failed: possibly renewed inquiry as to the <em> mode</em> of cure may disclose something that may be used against Jesus. But the man has now perceived their design: they are not seeking the truth, and he will be the tool of no such judges as they are proving themselves to be.</p>
