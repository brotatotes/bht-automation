<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="joh+9:28">John 9:28</span>. <strong><em> </em></strong> <strong> And they reviled him, and said, Thou art his disciple, but we are Moses’ disciples.</strong> Whether the man distinctly intended such reference to himself or not, it is thus that they understood his words; and this moves them contemptuously to contrast ‘that man’ with their greatest prophet, Moses.</p>
