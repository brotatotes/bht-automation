<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="joh+9:36">John 9:36</span>. <strong> He answered and said, And who is he, Lord, that I may believe in him? </strong> These are not words of a doubter, but of one who seeks to be led to a complete faith. In Jesus he has fullest confidence, and he waits only to hear His declaration respecting the ‘Son of man:’ as such Jesus has not yet manifested Himself to him.</p>
