<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="joh+9:37">John 9:37</span>. <strong> Jesus said unto him, Thou hast both seen him, and he that speaketh with thee is he. </strong> This manifestation is now given; both in word (‘he that speaketh’ . .) and in the half-veiled, yet clear, reference to the work that had been wrought on him (‘thou hast <em> seen</em> Him’) in the gift of physical (and we may certainly add spiritual) eyesight.</p>
