<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="jude+1:16">Jude 1:16</span>. A further description is now given of these teachers by an enumeration of the qualities by which all may identify them. They are characterized by a chronic discontent with everything and everybody, with their own lot especially the providence and ways of God, as we should call it; by intense self-indulgence, by proud and self-sufficient speech, and by gross flattery of the prosperous or great whenever anything is to be gained by it. <strong> Murmurers, complainers</strong> of their lot.
