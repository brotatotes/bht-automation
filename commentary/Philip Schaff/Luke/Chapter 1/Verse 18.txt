<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+1:18">Luke 1:18</span>. <strong> Whereby shall I know this?</strong> What is the sign according to which I may know this. Comp. Abraham’s question, <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ge+15:8">Genesis 15:8</span>, but notice that in Abraham’s case faith was strong (<span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ge+15:6">Genesis 15:6</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ro+4:19">Romans 4:19</span>), while here the unbelief of Zacharias appears in the sign given him and in what follows: <strong> For I am an old man.</strong> Levites could serve up to the age of fifty years (<span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="nu+4:3">Numbers 4:3</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="nu+8:24">Numbers 8:24</span>); but there was no such limitation in the case of priests.
	</p>
