<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+1:19">Luke 1:19</span>. <strong> I am Gabriel;</strong> comp. <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="da+8:16">Daniel 8:16</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="da+9:21">Daniel 9:21</span>. ‘Man of God.’
	</p>
<p><strong> That stand in the presence of God</strong>. One of the chief angels (archangels) nearest to God. According to Tob 12:15 , there were seven such. Comp. <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="re+8:2">Revelation 8:2</span>. The Rabbins say, that the names of the angels were brought from Babylon by the Jews, but this does not prove that the belief in them, or in their rank, was derived from heathenism. Comp. <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="jos+5:13-15">Joshua 5:13-15</span>. The name was known to Zacharias from the book of Daniel, and is announced by Gabriel to assert his authority.
	</p>
<p><strong> To bring thee these glad tidings</strong>. The message was a gospel message.
	</p>
