<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> Of the house of David</strong>. These words refer to Joseph alone, in this instance; but that Mary was also ‘of the house of David,’ seems to be implied in <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+1:32">Luke 1:32</span>, and has been the general belief of Christians. Comp, the genealogy in chap. 3.
