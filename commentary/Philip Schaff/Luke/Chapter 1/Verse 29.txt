<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+1:29">Luke 1:29</span>. <strong> Greatly troubled</strong>; not at the sight of the angel, but <strong> at the saying</strong>. This is further indicated by the clause: <strong> What manner of salutation this might be.</strong> Had she been born without sin, she would have been sufficiently conscious of her fellowship with a holy God, to understand such a salutation at once.
	</p>
