<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+1:64">Luke 1:64</span>. <strong> Immediately</strong>. According to the prediction (<span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+1:20">Luke 1:20</span>), the whole prophecy (<span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+1:13">Luke 1:13</span>), about which Zacharias doubted having now been fulfilled. That this was a miraculous restoration, follows from the character of the entire narrative. The word <strong> loosed</strong> is properly supplied in the E. V.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> Blessing God</strong>. ‘His first use of his recovered faculty is not to utter a complaint, but a doxology: a proof that the cure had taken place in his soul also.’
	</p>
