<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+1:73">Luke 1:73</span>. <em> </em> <strong> The oath</strong> <em> . </em> This explains the word ‘covenant’ in <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+1:72">Luke 1:72</span>. God’s covenant of mercy had been sealed by an oath. This ‘oath’ is found recorded in <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ge+22:16-18">Genesis 22:16-18</span>. The Abrahamic covenant becomes prominent as the coming of the Messiah draws near. Comp. <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ga+3:0">Galatians 3:0</span>.</p>
