<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+10:16">Luke 10:16</span>. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+10:40">Matthew 10:40</span>. Here the connection of thought is: woes on the Galilean cities which had rejected our Lord, would fall on those also that would reject the Seventy. The verse states a principle of general validity, and forms a solemn conclusion.
