<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+10:26">Luke 10:26</span>. <strong> In the law. </strong> These words are emphatic; as if our Lord would say, the answer to your question is in the law you teach.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.06em;"><strong> How readest thou? </strong> This form was used by the Rabbins to call out a quotation from Scripture. ‘How’ means ‘to what purport.’
	</p>
