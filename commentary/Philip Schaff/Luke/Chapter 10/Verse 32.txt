<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+10:32">Luke 10:32</span>. <strong> In like manner a Levite also. </strong> An inferior minister of the law, engaged in the service of the temple.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.06em;"><strong> Came to the place, </strong> etc. The nearest English equivalent for both the Levite’s office and conduct would probably be found in the word ‘beadle.’
	</p>
