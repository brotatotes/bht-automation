<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+10:34">Luke 10:34</span>. <strong> Pouring on them oil and wine. </strong> The usual remedies for wounds in the East.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.06em;"><strong> On his own beast. </strong> So that he walked himself. True philanthropy involves self-sacrifice.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.06em;"><strong> An inn. </strong> Evidently an inn, in our sense of the word, and not a caravanserai.
	</p>
