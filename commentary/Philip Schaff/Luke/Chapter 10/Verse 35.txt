<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+10:35">Luke 10:35</span>. He <strong> took out. </strong> Vivid narration.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.06em;"><strong> Two pence. </strong> Roman <em> denarii. </em> The value of the ‘denarius’ has been variously estimated, from seven and a half to eight and a half pence English (fifteen to seventeen cents). The sum was sufficient to meet the man’s necessities for some days at least
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.06em;"><strong> I.</strong> This is emphatic.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.06em;"><strong> When I come back again. </strong> It has been inferred from this that the Samaritan was a travelling merchant, who would soon return.
