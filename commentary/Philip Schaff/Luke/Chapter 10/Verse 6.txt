<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+10:6">Luke 10:6</span>. <strong> A son of peace, </strong> <em> i.e., </em> one ‘worthy,’ one whose heart was ready to receive the message of peace they brought.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.06em;"><strong> Upon him, </strong> or, ‘it,’ as in E. V. The original may refer either to the man or the house, the former is the more natural sense.
	</p>
