<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+10:7">Luke 10:7</span>. <strong> In that house, </strong> <em> i.e., </em> in the house where they had been received.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.06em;"><strong> Such things as they give. </strong> Lit. ‘the things from them,’ sharing what they have. There is not the slightest reference to eating heathen dishes (as in <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="1co+10:27">1 Corinthians 10:27</span>), for they were not sent among the heathen.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.06em;"><strong> Go not from house to house, </strong> <em> i.e., </em> in search of ease and better entertainment, or for gossip’s sake.
	</p>
