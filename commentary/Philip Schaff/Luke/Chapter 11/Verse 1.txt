<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+11:1">Luke 11:1</span>. <strong> In a certain place. </strong> Our Lord was wont to pray in mountains, hence the conjecture as to the Mount of Olives.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.06em;"><strong> Even at John also taught </strong> (was wont to teach) <strong> his disciples. </strong> We learn of this habit, in itself a very probable one, from this remark alone.
	</p>
