<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+11:38">Luke 11:38</span>. <strong> Washed, </strong> lit, ‘baptized.’ The washing referred to was therefore a <em> ceremonial </em> one, not simply an act of cleanliness. In this ceremony the Pharisees washed their hands, not their whole body.
