<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+11:4">Luke 11:4</span>. <strong> For we ourselves also forgive, </strong> ‘this is our own practice.’ More strongly expressed than in Matthew.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.06em;"><strong> Every one that is indebted to us. </strong> We cannot forgive ‘sins,’ as such, that belongs to God; but only as obligations from man to man represented by the commercial phrase ‘indebted.’
	</p>
