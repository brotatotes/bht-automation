<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+11:53">Luke 11:53</span>. <strong> when he was come out thence. </strong> From the house of the Pharisee.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.06em;"><strong> The scribes and Pharisees </strong> followed Him with malicious intent aroused by His discourse.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.06em;"><strong> To press upon him vehemently, </strong> or, ‘to be very spiteful,’ intensely embittered against Him. The former sense is preferable, as including both their feeling towards Him and their actual following of Him with hostile purpose.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.06em;"><strong> To provoke him to speak of many </strong> (or ‘more’) <strong> things. </strong> To catechize Him on a variety of subjects, so as to take Him off His guard.
	</p>
