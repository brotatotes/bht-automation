<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+12:16">Luke 12:16</span>. <strong> A parable. </strong> Yet a true history constantly repeated.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.06em;"><strong> The ground, </strong> lit., ‘place,’ <em> i.e., </em> estate.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.06em;"><strong> Brought forth plentifully. </strong> By God’s blessing, not by fraud or injustice, did this man’s wealth increase. The seeming innocence of the process is its danger; there is nothing to awaken qualms of conscience as his possessions increase.
	</p>
