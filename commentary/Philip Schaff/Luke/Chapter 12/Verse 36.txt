<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+12:36">Luke 12:36</span>. <strong> When he will return from the marriage feast. </strong> The main thought is simply that He is away at a feast, and expected to return. In the parable of the Ten Virgins (<span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+25:1-13">Matthew 25:1-13</span>), the return of the Bridegroom is the main thought.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.06em;"><strong> Straightway open unto him. </strong> Because they are ready, and have nothing to hide.
	</p>
