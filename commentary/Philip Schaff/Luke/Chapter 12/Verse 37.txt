<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+12:37">Luke 12:37</span>. <strong> Blessed, </strong> etc. The blessedness of these faithful servants is set forth in a figure.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.06em;"><strong> Gird himself, </strong> to serve them. Comp. <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="joh+13:4">John 13:4</span>, which foreshadows the ministering condescension of the master, at His return.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.06em;"><strong> Shall come forward. </strong> A peculiar expression, describing His approach to the guests.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.06em;"><strong> Serve them, </strong> wait upon them at table. In this passage no prominence is given to the wedding feast, and this must be remembered in interpreting it.
	</p>
