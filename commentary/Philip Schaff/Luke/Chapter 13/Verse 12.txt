<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+13:12">Luke 13:12</span>. <strong> Saw her. </strong> There is no evidence, that she asked for a cure. The action of our Lord and the language of the ruler of the synagogue, indicate that she hoped for one.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.06em;"><strong> Thou art loosed from thine infirmity.</strong> Her muscles were released from the influence which bound them. This suggests (as also <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+13:16">Luke 13:16</span>) Satanic power, which our Lord always drove away with a word.
	</p>
