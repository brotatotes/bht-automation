<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+13:30">Luke 13:30</span>. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+19:30">Matthew 19:30</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+20:16">Matthew 20:16</span>. Here the saying seems to be applied to the ingathering of the guests, just spoken of; not simply to the Jews and Gentiles as such, but to individuals and churches and nations all through the ingathering. For example: the church at Jerusalem and her Gentile off-shoots, the Oriental churches. Modern history furnishes many instances.
