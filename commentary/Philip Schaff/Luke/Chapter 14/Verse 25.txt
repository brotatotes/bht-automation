<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+14:25">Luke 14:25</span>. <strong> There went, </strong> etc. A continued journeying with Him is meant. The <strong> multitudes </strong> were probably from different places: Those who originally followed Him from Galilee, others from Perea, and various companies on the way to the approaching Passover feast.
	</p>
