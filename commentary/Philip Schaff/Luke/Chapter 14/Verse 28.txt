<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+14:28">Luke 14:28</span>. <strong> For which of you. </strong> By two illustrations our Lord enforces the requirements just stated.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.06em;"><strong> To build</strong> <strong> a tower, </strong> a structure of some importance, and involving considerable expense. The prudent way is described: first the plan; second, the careful consideration of what is required to carry it out; third, the examination whether the resources will suffice.
	</p>
