<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+14:3">Luke 14:3</span>. <strong> Answering, </strong> <em> i.e.</em>, the thoughts of the Pharisees. </p>
<p style="margin-left:0.06em;"><strong> Is it lawful, </strong> etc.? This unexpected question evidently embarrassed them. If they answered yes, the occasion of finding fault was taken away; if no, they could be charged with want of compassion.
