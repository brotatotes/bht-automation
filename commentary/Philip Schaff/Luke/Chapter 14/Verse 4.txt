<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+14:4">Luke 14:4</span>. <strong> But they held their peace. </strong> They could attend feasts on the Sabbath, but could not say that it was right to heal the sick. Formalism is always thus inconsistent. Their silence was a confession of defeat, however. Then came the healing.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.06em;"><strong> Sent him away. </strong> He was not a guest. The rebuke was not given until after the man had been sent away.
	</p>
