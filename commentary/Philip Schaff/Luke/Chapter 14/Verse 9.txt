<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+14:9">Luke 14:9</span>. <strong> He that bade thee. </strong> The proper person to decide both in the primary and deeper applications of the parable.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.06em;"><strong> And then thou</strong> <strong> shalt begin with shame. </strong> ‘Begin’ hints at the lingering in the coveted place, and the shame rises as the crestfallen one goes lower and lower.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.06em;"><strong> The lowest place. </strong> Farthest away from the honorable places, since the intermediate ones would be al-already occupied.
	</p>
