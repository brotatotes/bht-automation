<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+15:21">Luke 15:21</span>. <strong> Father, </strong> etc. The purposed confession is made, but the conclusion is omitted. ‘The terms are the same, “I have sinned”; but how different is the accent! Luther felt it profoundly: the discovery of the difference between the repentance of fear and that of love was the true principle of the Reformation’ (Godet).
