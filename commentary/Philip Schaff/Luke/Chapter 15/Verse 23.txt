<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+15:23">Luke 15:23</span>. <strong> The fatted calf. </strong> Some calf standing in the stall, probably in readiness for a feast, is to be killed, as the <em> best, </em> for this sudden festivity. There is no allusion to any sacrifice.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.06em;"><strong> Make merry. </strong> The ‘joy in heaven’ (<span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+15:6">Luke 15:6</span>) is again alluded to; the parties feasting are ‘the servants’ (<span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+15:22">Luke 15:22</span>), including the whole family; angels and redeemed men.
	</p>
