<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+15:25">Luke 15:25</span>. <strong> Now his elder son was in the field.</strong> ‘The elder son at the return of the younger brother is not in the house, but has spent the day in hard, self-chosen, slavish service, and now first returns home at evening, when the feast was already in progress’ (Van Oosterzee).
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.06em;"><strong> Music and dancing. </strong> Usual at feasts in the East. Dancing in the East was usually performed by those hired for the purpose.
	</p>
