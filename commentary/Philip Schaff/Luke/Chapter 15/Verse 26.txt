<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+15:26">Luke 15:26</span>. <strong> One of the servants. </strong> Not the same word as in <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+15:22">Luke 15:22</span>; probably an inferior domestic in the permanent employ of the householder, but now standing without.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.06em;"><strong> What these things might be. </strong> Offended that this should take place without his knowledge; jealous of the joy in which he would not share.
	</p>
