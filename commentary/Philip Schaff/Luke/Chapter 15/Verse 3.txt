<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+15:3">Luke 15:3</span>. <strong> What shall I do, </strong> etc. In his uncertainty, he carefully considered the case, and this is the point in which the children of this world are so often wiser than the children of light
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.06em;"><strong> I have not strength to dig. </strong> His life of luxury had unfitted him for that.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.06em;"><strong> To beg I am ashamed. </strong> Because of his past position. This graphic description presents certain points of human character, but cannot be further used in the interpretation.
