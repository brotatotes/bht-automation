<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+15:4">Luke 15:4</span>. <strong> I am resolved, </strong> etc. The plan just strikes him.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.06em;"><strong> They</strong> <em> , i.e., </em> the debtors with whom he intends to deal, <strong> may receive into their houses. </strong> He would thus secure future shelter for himself. Further than this the verse must not be pressed (see <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+15:9">Luke 15:9</span>).
	</p>
