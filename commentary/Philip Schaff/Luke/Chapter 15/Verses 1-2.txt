<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+15:1-2">Luke 15:1-2</span>. THE OCCASION OF THE DISCOURSE. <strong> How all the publicans and sinners.</strong> Not all kinds, nor all without exception, but very many, so that this was the rule.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.06em;"><strong> Were drawing near. </strong> At this time were occupied in thus coming. There was an increasing throng of these classes, with one distinct purpose: <strong> to hear him</strong>. It was precisely these who felt they had no means to build the tower, no forces to meet the opposing King <strong> ;</strong> and hence they sought resources from One who manifested power, and through Him desired ‘conditions of peace.’
	</p>
