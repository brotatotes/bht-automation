<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+15:3-7">Luke 15:3-7</span>. THE PARABLE OF THE LOST SHEEP. Comp. <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+18:12-14">Matthew 18:12-14</span>, where the same parable occurs. There, however, our Lord brings out the preciousness of the one sheep (‘the little one’); here, the mercy of the shepherd in seeking and rejoicing over the one sheep.
	</p>
