<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+16:29">Luke 16:29</span>. <strong> They have Moses and the prophets, </strong> <em> i.e., </em> the Old Testament.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.06em;"><strong> Let them hear them. </strong> This implies that these men, though children of Abraham and possessors of the Old Testament, had never rightly attended to it.
	</p>
