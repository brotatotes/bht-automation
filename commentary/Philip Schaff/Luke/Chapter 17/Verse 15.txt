<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+17:15">Luke 17:15</span>. <strong> One of them, </strong> etc. The description is graphic, the healing took place immediately.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.06em;"><strong> Turned back. </strong> They were still on their way to the priests.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.06em;"><strong> With a loud voice. </strong> There may be an allusion to the clearness of voice resulting from the cure of his leprosy, since that disease would make the voice husky.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.06em;"><strong> Glorifying God. </strong> Glorifying God and love to Jesus Christ are closely joined.
	</p>
