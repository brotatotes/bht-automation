<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+17:18">Luke 17:18</span>. <strong> Save this stranger, </strong> or ‘alien,’ not of Jewish extraction. The nine were Jews, and yet put the ceremonial requirement above gratitude to their own countryman who had healed them; the stranger came, though the Jews had no dealings with the Samaritans (<span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="joh+4:9">John 4:9</span>).
	</p>
