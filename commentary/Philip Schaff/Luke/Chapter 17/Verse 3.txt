<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+17:3">Luke 17:3</span>. <strong> Take heed to yourselves. </strong> Precisely this class needed this caution. For as they had been so lately sinners they would be most likely to give occasion of stumbling; and as new converts of this class are enthusiastic, they would readily stumble themselves.
