<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+17:5">Luke 17:5</span>. <strong> And the apostles said. </strong> This is the only instance in the Gospels, where the Apostles as such make a request in common.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.06em;"><strong> Increase our faith, lit</strong>, ‘add to us faith,’ <em> i.e.,</em> give us more faith. They felt themselves unequal to the duty of forgiving love enjoined upon them (<span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+17:3-4">Luke 17:3-4</span>). They had been taught this before, and no doubt in the mean time had learned their insufficiency. Those who offer the prayer should remember the occasion of it.
	</p>
