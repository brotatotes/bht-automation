<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+17:8">Luke 17:8</span>. <strong> Will not rather</strong>? <strong> </strong> This assumes an affirmative answer.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.06em;"><strong> Make ready, </strong> etc. As a matter of <em> right </em> this was all that could be expected. But compare chap. <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+12:37">Luke 12:37</span>, where the very reverse is promised. There the <em> privileges of a state of grace </em> are spoken of;. here our Lord is telling of what could be expected on <em> the ground of merit.
	</em></p>
