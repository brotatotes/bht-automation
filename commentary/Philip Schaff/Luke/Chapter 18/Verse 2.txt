<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+18:2">Luke 18:2</span>. <strong> In a city a judge. </strong> The ordinary municipal judge, appointed in accordance with <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="de+16:18">Deuteronomy 16:18</span>.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.06em;"><strong> Who feared not God, and regarded not man. </strong> The expression is not an uncommon designation of an unprincipled and reckless person. Religious motives and even social influences set no check to his selfish recklessness.
	</p>
