<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+18:3">Luke 18:3</span>. <strong> A widow in that city. </strong> The Old Testament specially demanded judicial protection for widows. The suitor may represent the <em> church.
	</em></p>
<p style="margin-left:0.06em;"><strong> Avenge me of my adversary. </strong> The justice of her cause is implied throughout. She does more than ask for a decision in her favor, she demands protection and requital. The Church of Christ, persecuted for ages, should proffer this request to God alone.
	</p>
