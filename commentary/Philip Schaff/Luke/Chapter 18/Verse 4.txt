<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+18:4">Luke 18:4</span>. <strong> For a while. </strong> Not necessarily, for a long time.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.06em;"><strong> He</strong> <strong> laid within himself, </strong> etc. This soliloquy reveals the utterly abandoned character of the man: he was not ashamed of his own recklessness.
