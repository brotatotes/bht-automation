<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+19:1">Luke 19:1</span>. <strong> And Jesus. </strong> The E. V. supplies ‘Jesus.’
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.06em;"><strong> Was passing through Jericho. </strong> He had not yet passed entirely through, when He met Zaccheus. Hence it is not necessary to suppose that the house of Zaccheus was outside the city, on the way to Jerusalem. On Jericho, see <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+20:29">Matthew 20:29</span>.
	</p>
