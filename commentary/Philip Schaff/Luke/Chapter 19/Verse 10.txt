<p style="margin-left:0.06em;"><strong> To seek, </strong> as a shepherd, comp. chap. <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+15:4">Luke 15:4</span>. It was ‘the lost sheep of the house of Israel’ to whom the Lord was sent (<span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+15:24">Matthew 15:24</span>). Zaccheus was one of these, and acknowledging himself as such received the Master who was seeking him. </p>
