<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+19:16">Luke 19:16</span>. <strong> Thy pound; </strong> not ‘I <strong> </strong> have gained’ (Matt.). In the latter case, the trust was according to ability, here it was the same in every case; there the gain was proportioned to the trust, but here there was no such proportion; hence the more modest answer. This also favors the view which interprets the ‘pound’ as the one official gift, with varied results.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.06em;"><strong> Hade ten pounds more</strong>, <em> i.e., </em> in addition to itself.
	</p>
