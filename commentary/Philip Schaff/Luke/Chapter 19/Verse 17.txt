<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+19:17">Luke 19:17</span>. <strong> In a very little. </strong> The ‘pound’ was a very small sum. High as the ministerial office is relatively in this world, in the other (and in comparison with the ‘talents’ even here) it is ‘very little;’ certainly is not the sole channel of blessing to the church.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.06em;"><strong> Ten cities. </strong> The reward corresponds with the kingly dignity of the returned Lord. (Comp. on the other hand <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+25:21">Matthew 25:21</span>.)
	</p>
