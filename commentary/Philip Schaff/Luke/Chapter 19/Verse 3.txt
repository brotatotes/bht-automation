<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+19:3">Luke 19:3</span>. <strong> Who he was, </strong> or ‘which (among the crowd) was He.’ Zaccheus had heard of Jesus, but had never seen Him. His curiosity alone is mentioned here; but some better motive, however ill-defined to himself, undoubtedly influenced him.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.06em;"><strong> Could not for the crowd. </strong> He had tried, but failed because of the crowd, his stature making it necessary to get very near in order to see.
	</p>
