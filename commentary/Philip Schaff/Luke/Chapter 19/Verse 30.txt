<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+19:30">Luke 19:30</span>. <strong> And it came to pass. </strong> This leaves room for the intervening events in Bethany on Saturday evening, at the house of Simon the leper. Hence we begin a paragraph here.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.06em;"><strong> High to Bethphage and Bethany. </strong> The village nearest Jerusalem is mentioned first (so Mark).
