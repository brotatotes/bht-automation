<p style="margin-left:0.06em;"><strong> I bring you glad tidings of great joy.</strong> Lit., ‘I evangelize to you great joy.’ The message is a gospel message, a joyous message; therefore they should not be afraid.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.06em;"><strong> To all the people</strong> <em> , i.e., </em> of Israel. First of all to them, then through them to the Gentiles.</p>
