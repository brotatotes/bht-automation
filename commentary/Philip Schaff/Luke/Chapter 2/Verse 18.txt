<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+2:18">Luke 2:18</span>. <strong> Wondered</strong>. With this natural, and probably transient, wonder of those who heard the story, the narrative contrasts the more abiding effect upon Mary. Before Jesus appeared as a teacher, thirty years afterwards, the story was probably forgotten by all but a few earnest souls. If His words and works did not prevent the mass of the Jews from rejecting Him, how little influence would this story have.</p>
