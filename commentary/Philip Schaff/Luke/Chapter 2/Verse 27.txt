<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+2:27">Luke 2:27</span>. <strong> And he came in the spirit into the temple</strong>. His steps were ordered by the Spirit, in the power of which he lived. The Spirit led him thither to meet this child, whom he was enabled, by the same Spirit, to recognize as the Messiah.</p>
