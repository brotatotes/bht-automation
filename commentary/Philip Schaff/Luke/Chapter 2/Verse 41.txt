<p style="margin-left:0.06em;"><strong> At the feast of the Passover</strong>. On the Passover, see <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+26:2">Matthew 26:2</span>. The male Israelites were required to appear at the three yearly feasts (<span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ex+23:14-17">Exodus 23:14-17</span>). Women, according to the teachings of a prominent Rabbi (Hillel), were bound to attend the Passover feast Mary probably went from pious motives, rather than Rabbinical rules.</p>
