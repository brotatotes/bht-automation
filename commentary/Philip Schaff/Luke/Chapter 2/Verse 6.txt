<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+2:6">Luke 2:6</span>. <strong> While they were there</strong>. Apocryphal legends tell how she was overtaken on the way, and sought refuge in a cave. They seem to have arrived in Bethlehem, and sought shelter in vain, before the time spoken of here.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.06em;"><strong> Delivered</strong>, or, ‘bring forth,’ as the same word is translated in <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+2:7">Luke 2:7</span>.</p>
