<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+20:16">Luke 20:16</span>. <strong> And when they heard it, they said. </strong> Who spoke? Some of the crowd, we think, since as yet Luke has not introduced the chief priests in this connection.
