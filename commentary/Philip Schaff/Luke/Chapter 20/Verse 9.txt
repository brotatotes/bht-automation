<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+20:9">Luke 20:9</span>. <strong> Began. </strong> After the discomfiture of the priests, scribes, and elders.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.06em;"><strong> To the people, </strong> but ‘against’ (<span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+20:19">Luke 20:19</span>) His assailants, who were undoubtedly present. Hence there is no disagreement with the other accounts. The description of the <strong> vineyard </strong> is not so full here, but <strong> for a long time </strong> is new.
	</p>
