<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+21:18">Luke 21:18</span>. <strong> And not a hair of your head shall perish. </strong> Some would add: ‘as long as you are needed for the service of Christ;’ others refer it to the safety of the mass of Christians at the time of the destruction of Jerusalem. But the fact that <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+21:16">Luke 21:16</span> points to the death of some makes a reference to the spiritual life more probable. The seeming difficulty led to an early omission of the verse.
	</p>
