<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+22:28">Luke 22:28</span>. <strong> Continued with me in my temptations, </strong> or ‘trials.’ Our Lord does not reproach them, but praises their steadfastness. He speaks of His whole life as one of ‘temptations,’ in accordance with the Scriptural portrayal of His work on earth.
	</p>
