<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+22:30">Luke 22:30</span>. <strong> That ye may eat, </strong> etc. The enjoyments of their reign, with Him in the kingdom appointed by His father, are thus set forth. Comp. <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+22:16">Luke 22:16</span>.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.06em;"><strong> And ye shall lit. </strong> A direct promise.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.06em;"><strong> On thrones, </strong> etc. Not ‘twelve thrones,’ as <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+19:28">Matthew 19:28</span>, possibly on account of Judas. Notice the appropriateness of this verse, first in view of the feast before them; second, in view of the greatness which they anticipated, though so blind as to its character.
	</p>
