<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+22:41">Luke 22:41</span>. <strong> Withdrew himself, </strong> lit, ‘was himself withdrawn.’ Drawn by internal anguish, some suppose.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.06em;"><strong> About a stone’s east. </strong> Not so far as to be out of hearing. This was probably the distance from the three disciples (Matthew, Mark), not from the main body, since the next clause refers to what took place in His solitude, and <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+22:45-46">Luke 22:45-46</span>, to the <em> three </em> disciples.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.06em;"><strong> He kneeled down. </strong> Peculiar to Luke.
	</p>
