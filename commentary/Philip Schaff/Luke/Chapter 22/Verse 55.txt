<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+22:55">Luke 22:55</span>. <strong> Kindled. </strong> Lit., ‘kindled around;’ a large bright fire was made, we infer. All of the Evangelists but Matthew mention the fire, but Mark does not speak of its being kindled.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.06em;"><strong> Sat down. </strong> So Matthew and Mark; out John speaks of his standing. During the night hours, Peter was no doubt restless.
	</p>
