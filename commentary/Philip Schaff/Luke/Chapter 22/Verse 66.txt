<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+22:66">Luke 22:66</span>. <strong> And when it was day. </strong> Roman law forbade a final condemnation before dawn, and Jewish usage forbade even the investigation of capital crime at night.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.06em;"><strong> The assembly of the elders, </strong> lit, ‘the eldership’ <em> (presbyterion); </em> a formal assembly of the Sanhedrin, at the usual place of holding the council.
	</p>
