<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+22:69">Luke 22:69</span>. <strong> But. </strong> This indicates the connection of thought: you have prejudged my case, but, as the time has come to speak, in order that through suffering I may pass to glory, I tell you of that glory and thus confess myself the Christ: <strong> From henceforth</strong>, etc. Comp. <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+26:64">Matthew 26:64</span>. A repetition of this declaration is not at all improbable.
