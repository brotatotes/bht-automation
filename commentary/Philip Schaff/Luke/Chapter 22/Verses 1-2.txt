<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+22:1-2">Luke 22:1-2</span>. THE PLOT OF THE RULERS <strong> Which if called the Passover. </strong> Explanation for Gentile readers.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.06em;"><strong> How they might put him to death; for they feared the people, </strong> who had been hearing Him so attentively (chap. <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+21:38">Luke 21:38</span>); hence the question was <em> how </em> they could carry into effect a purpose already determined. ‘Not on the feast-day’ (Matthew, Mark) is implied here, and also in <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+22:6">Luke 22:6</span>.
	</p>
