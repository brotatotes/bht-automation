<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+23:15">Luke 23:15</span>. <strong> Nor yet Herod, </strong> who knew Jewish affairs so well.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.06em;"><strong> For he sent him back to us. </strong> The correct reading more fully proves Pilate’s assertion.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.06em;"><strong> Hath been done by him, </strong> <em> i.e., </em> Herod’s examination failed to elicit any proof that He had committed a crime.
