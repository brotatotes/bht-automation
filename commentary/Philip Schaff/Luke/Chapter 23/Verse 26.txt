<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+23:26">Luke 23:26</span>. <strong> When they led him away. </strong> See on <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+27:32">Matthew 27:32</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+15:21">Mark 15:21</span>. (John omits this incident.)
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.06em;"><strong> To bear it after Jesus. </strong> The hinder <strong> part </strong> alone was laid upon Simon. The relief was comparatively slight; there is no proof that our Lord was sinking under the load. He who bears the cross <em> after </em> Jesus, bears the lightest end of it.
	</p>
