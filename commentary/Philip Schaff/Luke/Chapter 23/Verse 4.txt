<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+23:4">Luke 23:4</span>. <strong> I find no fault in this man. </strong> Pilate speaks as a judge. Knowing that the Sanhedrin would have no desire to put to death any one for the political crime alleged, he examines our Lord and satisfies himself that no such political crime was involved in His claim to be King of the Jews.
	</p>
