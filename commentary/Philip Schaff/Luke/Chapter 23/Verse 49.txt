<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+23:49">Luke 23:49</span>. <strong> And all his acquaintance. </strong> Peculiar to Luke. ‘All ‘now present in Jerusalem. The Eleven may be included, though John had led Mary home. Possibly they were not there, fearing to come, a view favored by the fact that no mention is made of them in connection with the burial. The account is so brief, that it cannot be <strong> </strong> considered as contradictory, <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="joh+19:25">John 19:25</span>.
