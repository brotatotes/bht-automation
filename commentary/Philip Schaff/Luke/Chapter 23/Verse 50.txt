<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+23:50">Luke 23:50</span>. <strong> A councillor. </strong> A member of the Sanhedrin, as the next verse plainly shows.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.06em;"><strong> Good, </strong> in moral character.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.06em;"><strong> And just. </strong> In the Old Testament sense. ‘Good’ is more than ‘just’ (comp. <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ro+5:7">Romans 5:7</span>), but the former always includes the latter.
	</p>
