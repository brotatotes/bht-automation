<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+23:51">Luke 23:51</span>. <strong> He had not consented. </strong> From chap, <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+22:70">Luke 22:70</span>, we may infer that he was absent from the morning meeting of the Sanhedrin, probably from all.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.06em;"><strong> Their counsel. </strong> The formal decision, which resulted in the <strong> deed, </strong> <em> i.e., </em> crucifixion.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.06em;"><strong> Of</strong>, or ‘from,’ <strong> Arimathea. </strong> He was ‘of’ that city, but it is possible he came ‘from’ that place at this time.
	</p>
