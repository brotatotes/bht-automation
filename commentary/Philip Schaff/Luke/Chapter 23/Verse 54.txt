<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+23:54">Luke 23:54</span>. <strong> And it was the day of the Preparation, </strong> <em> i.e., </em> the day before the Sabbath (<span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+15:42">Mark 15:42</span>)
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.06em;"><strong> And the Sabbath drew on. </strong> The word used of the natural day, is applied here to the legal day, which began at sunset. The time was probably between five and six in the evening.
	</p>
