<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+23:55">Luke 23:55</span>. <strong> And the women, </strong> etc. Matthew and Mark mention the two Marys alone; it is probable that others were with them, but that these two alone remained at the sepulchre. None of them, nor even Nicodemus, seems to have been with Joseph when the body was taken down.
	</p>
