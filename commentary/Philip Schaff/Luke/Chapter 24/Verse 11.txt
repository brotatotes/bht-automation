<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+24:11">Luke 24:11</span>. <strong> These words </strong> (or, ‘sayings’). The original indicates that accounts were given by different persons.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.06em;"><strong> Appeared in their sight. </strong> A full expression, more than ‘seemed to them.’
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.06em;"><strong> Idle talk. </strong> ‘Nonsense and superstitious gossip.’
	</p>
