<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+24:28">Luke 24:28</span>. <strong> He made as though, </strong> etc. It is not implied that He said He would go further, but was about to pass on. As a matter of decorum He must thus do, until they should invite Him to stop. This called forth their desire and request. It was still concealing to reveal more fully.
	</p>
