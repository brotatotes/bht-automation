<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+24:33">Luke 24:33</span>. <strong> That very hour. </strong> Probably leaving the meal untouched. If the hour were six P.M., they would reach Jerusalem at no late hour, since their joy would occasion a rapid gait.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.06em;"><strong> The eleven, </strong> <em> i.e., </em> the Apostles. Thomas was absent. <strong> Gathered together. </strong> According to <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="joh+20:19">John 20:19</span>, ‘the doors were shut’ ‘for fear of the Jews.’ We identify that appearance with that mentioned in the next section.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.06em;"><strong> Them that were with them. </strong> John’s account does not forbid the presence of others. <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ac+1:14">Acts 1:14</span> tells who these persons were.
	</p>
