<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+24:38">Luke 24:38</span>. <strong> Why are ye troubled! </strong> The kindly rebuke was deserved.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.06em;"><strong> And wherefore do questionings, </strong> ‘scruples of a discouraging nature, doubting and gainsaying thoughts,’ <strong> arise in your heart! </strong> These prevented them from at once and unhesitatingly recognizing Him, identifying Him.
	</p>
