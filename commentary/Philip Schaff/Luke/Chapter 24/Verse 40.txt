<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+24:40">Luke 24:40</span>. <strong> He showed them his hands and his feet. </strong> As proof of identity, but also as ‘ <em> signs of victory, </em> proofs of His triumph over death. Moreover therefore and this is properly the deepest sense of His entering salutation as the <em> sign of peace, </em> the peace of the sacrificial death, of the completed atonement’ (Stier).
