<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+24:41">Luke 24:41</span>. <strong> Were still unbelieving for joy. </strong> How natural! The <em> identity </em> was proven, but the <em> reality </em> was still a matter of doubt to them, especially as the fact seemed too glorious to be believed.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.06em;"><strong> Have ye anything to eat! </strong> This question was designed to prove most conclusively that He was not a spirit.
	</p>
