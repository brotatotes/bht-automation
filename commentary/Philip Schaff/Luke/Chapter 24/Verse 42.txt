<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+24:42">Luke 24:42</span>. <strong> And of a honeycomb. </strong> These words are omitted in many ancient authorities, and rejected by some modern editors. We prefer to retain them, since there are a number of reasons to account for their being left out, and more to account for their being put in by the copyists.
	</p>
