<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+24:48">Luke 24:48</span>. Ye. The Apostles, but others may have been present <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ac+1:22">Acts 1:22</span> hints that others saw Him ascend.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.06em;"><strong> Witnesses. </strong> As such they were to proclaim the facts (<span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+24:46">Luke 24:46</span>), and the repentance and remission based upon them; and thus be the fulfillers of the prophecies summed up in <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+24:47">Luke 24:47</span>.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.06em;"><strong> These things. </strong> The Gospel facts respecting Christ, centering in His Death and Resurrection, and including His Ascension. The fulfilment of prophecy and the commission to preach remission and repentance, are not excluded.
	</p>
