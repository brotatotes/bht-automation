<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+24:6">Luke 24:6</span>. <strong> Remember, </strong> etc. This they had forgotten naturally enough in the circumstances.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.06em;"><strong> When he was yet in Galilee, </strong> <em> i.e.</em>, with them in Galilee, their home (<span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+23:55">Luke 23:55</span>). This verse has occasioned difficulty, in view of the fact that according to Matthew and Mark Galilee was spoken of by the angel in a different connection. But we suppose that this reminder preceded the direction of <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+10:7">Mark 10:7</span> (The angelic announcement of <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+28:5-7">Matthew 28:5-7</span> was, we think, made to but two of the women; see notes there.)
