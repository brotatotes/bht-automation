<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+24:7">Luke 24:7</span>. <strong> Saying that the Son of Man, </strong> etc. Comp. <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+9:22">Luke 9:22</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+18:32">Luke 18:32</span>. The announcements in these passages were made to the Twelve, but <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+8:0">Mark 8:0</span> shows that a wider circle heard them. The angel knew of this. The term ‘Son of Man’ is here quoted; it is not otherwise applied to Christ after the resurrection.
	</p>
