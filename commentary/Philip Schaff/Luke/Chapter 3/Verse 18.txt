<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+3:18">Luke 3:18</span>. <strong> With many other exhortations,</strong> etc. The form of the verse is peculiar. The exhorting was varied, different in character as well as repeated. Yet thus <strong> he preached glad tidings</strong>, <em> i.e.,</em> <strong><em> </em></strong> of the coming Messiah. This description of his ministry is peculiarly apt, hinting at the close connection between repentance and belief in the Gospel, and at the relation between John the Baptist and Christ.</p>
