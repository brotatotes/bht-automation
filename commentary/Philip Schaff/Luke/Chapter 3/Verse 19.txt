<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+3:19">Luke 3:19</span>. <strong> But Herod</strong>, etc. This took place afterwards. It is inserted here to complete the sketch of John’s ministry, just as chap. <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+1:80">Luke 1:80</span> does that of his youth.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.06em;"><strong> Herodias</strong>. See <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+14:3">Matthew 14:3</span>.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.06em;"><strong> All the evil things which</strong> <strong> Herod had done</strong>. See <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+6:17-20">Mark 6:17-20</span>, where Herod’s willingness to hear him is brought out.</p>
