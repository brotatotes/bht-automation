<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+4:19">Luke 4:19</span>. <strong> The acceptable </strong> year <strong> of the Lord.</strong> The year, or definite appointed period, when the Lord is gracious, not without a reference to the year of jubilee, which also pointed to the Messiah’s coming and kingdom. It proves nothing as to the length of our Lord’s ministry.</p>
