<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+4:5">Luke 4:5</span>. <strong> And he led him up</strong>. No definite mark of time, hence we think this temptation was the <em> third</em> (as in Matthew). The words: ‘into’ a high mountain, are to be omitted.

<p style="margin-left:0.06em;"><strong> In a moment of</strong> <strong><em> </em></strong> <strong> time,</strong> at once. A supernatural extension of vision is possibly implied.</p>
