<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+5:26">Luke 5:26</span>. <strong> And astonishment seized on them all, </strong> etc. Luke alone mentions all three emotions of wonder, gratitude, and fear. Matthew speaks of the last two; Mark of the first two. Matthew indicates that these feelings were those of the people, not of the scribes and Pharisees.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.06em;"><strong> Strange things.</strong> Our word <em> paradox</em> is taken from the word here used.</p>
