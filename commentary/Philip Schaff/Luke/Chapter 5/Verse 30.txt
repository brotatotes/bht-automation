<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+5:30">Luke 5:30</span>. <strong> Why do ye eat,</strong> etc. Matthew and Mark represent the objection as raised against the conduct of our Lord. But the disciples also ate with the publicans and sinners. The result would be a protest from the Pharisees against both the Master and His disciples.</p>
