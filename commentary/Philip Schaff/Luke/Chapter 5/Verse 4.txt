<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+5:4">Luke 5:4</span>. <strong> Simon.</strong> Evidently the steersman of the boat.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.06em;"><strong> Put out into the deep,</strong> <em> i.e.,</em> the deep water. Luke always uses proper nautical phrases. Addressed in the singular, to Simon.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.06em;"><strong> Let down</strong> <strong> your nets.</strong> Addressed to all the fishermen in the boat. Our Lord first makes a slight request of Simon, then after His discourse a greater one, calling for more confidence in Himself.</p>
