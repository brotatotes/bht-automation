<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+5:6">Luke 5:6</span>. <strong> Having done this.</strong> <em> </em> A number were <strong> </strong> engaged.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.06em;"><strong> Were breaking,</strong> <em> i.e.,</em> ‘began to break,’ just as in <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+5:7">Luke 5:7</span>, ‘were sinking’ means ‘began to sink.’ The nets did not break, nor the boats sink. God sometimes allows dangers to begin, that our faith may be increased.</p>
