<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+5:9">Luke 5:9</span>. <strong> Amazement seized</strong> <em> , </em> etc. This miracle seems more than one of knowledge. It is true the shoals of fish in the lake are very thick, but the promise of <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+5:10">Luke 5:10</span> (‘Henceforth thou shalt catch men’) points to an influence of Christ’s upon the fish.
