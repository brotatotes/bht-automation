<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+5:12-16">Luke 5:12-16</span>. HEALING OF A LEPER. <strong> One of the cities</strong> (<span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+5:12">Luke 5:12</span>). Probably not Capernaum.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.06em;"><strong> Full of leprosy.</strong> A term of medical accuracy, probably referring to the severity of the disease in this case. On this disease, see <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+8:2">Matthew 8:2</span>. In <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+5:14">Luke 5:14</span>, there is a change to the direct address: <strong> but go, and show thyself, </strong> etc. <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+5:16">Luke 5:16</span> breaks off the direct connection of time with what follows; the length of the interval is uncertain.</p>
