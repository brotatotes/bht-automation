<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+5:27-28">Luke 5:27-28</span>. THE CALL OF LEVI. See on <strong> <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+9:9">Matthew 9:9</span></strong>; <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+2:13-14">Mark 2:13-14</span>.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.06em;"><strong> Beheld</strong>, more than ‘saw’ (Matthew and Mark); observed, noticed, looked on.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.06em;"><strong> Forsook all.</strong> Peculiar to Luke. It implies not only the actual relinquishment of what he was then doing, but the spirit in which he followed.</p>
