<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+6:18-19">Luke 6:18-19</span>. Comp. <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+3:10-11">Mark 3:10-11</span>, which suggests that this concourse and pressure of those who would be healed began before the choice of the Twelve and continued after they came down. As however the object of their coming was to hear as well as to be healed (<span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+6:17">Luke 6:17</span>), our Lord teaches them also. The miracles were designed to be a preparation for the instruction.
