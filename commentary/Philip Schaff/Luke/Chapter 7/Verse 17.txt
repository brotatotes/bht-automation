<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+7:17">Luke 7:17</span>. <strong> This report concerning him</strong>. Literally, ‘this saying.’ It can scarcely refer to the saying of the last verse, but rather to the whole account of the miracle.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.06em;"><strong> In the whole of Judea.</strong> <strong><em> </em></strong> Probably meaning all Palestine, and not Judea as opposed to Galilee.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.06em;"><strong> Region round about</strong>, <strong> </strong> <em> i.e.,</em> about Judea, not merely in the district about Nain.
	</p>
