<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+7:2">Luke 7:2</span>. <strong> Who was highly valued by him </strong> as his only and faithful servant. It is further suggested that he was ‘held in honor,’ the object of his master’s attachment, as was frequently the case in these days, between master and slave. The sickness was ‘palsy’ (Matthew).</p>
