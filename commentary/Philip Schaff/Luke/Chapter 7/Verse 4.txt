<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+7:4">Luke 7:4</span>. <strong> He is worthy. </strong> The correct reading makes this verse a quotation of their language. The intercession of the elders is true to nature: a rich man, a man of authority and position, a man of their party, though not ‘to the manner born,’ would enlist their good offices.</p>
