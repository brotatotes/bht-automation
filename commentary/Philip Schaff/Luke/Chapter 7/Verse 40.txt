<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+7:40">Luke 7:40</span>. <strong> Answering,</strong> the thought of the Pharisee, not some outward manifestations of displeasure, though such may have been displayed.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.06em;"><strong> I have somewhat to say unto thee</strong>. Direct personal address, implying a knowledge of Simon’s heart.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.06em;"><strong> Master</strong>, or, ‘Teacher,’ <strong> say on</strong>. The tone is respectful, as if the evidence of our Lord’s insight had already checked the doubt in Simon’s mind.
	</p>
