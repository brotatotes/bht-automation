<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+8:1">Luke 8:1</span>. <strong> Went about through city and village</strong>. From town to town, from village to village, in unwearied activity.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.06em;"><strong> Bringing the glad tidings. </strong> One word in the Greek, hence ‘preaching’ does not govern ‘glad tidings.’
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.06em;"><strong> And the twelve with him, </strong> i.e. <em> ,</em> went about with him, since this is joined closely with the previous clause. Hence after they had been chosen, but before they had been sent out to preach.</p>
