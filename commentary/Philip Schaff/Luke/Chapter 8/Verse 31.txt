<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+8:31">Luke 8:31</span>. <strong> Into the abyss</strong> <strong><em> . </em></strong> There can scarcely be a reasonable doubt that this means ‘hell,’ the place of punishment for evil spirits. We must distinguish between ‘abyss,’ ‘the <em> ad interim</em> place of torment, and the lake of fire into which the devil will be cast by Christ at the end: see <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="re+20:3">Revelation 20:3</span>.’ (Alford.) The respite obtained by their entering into the herd of swine was a very brief one.</p>
