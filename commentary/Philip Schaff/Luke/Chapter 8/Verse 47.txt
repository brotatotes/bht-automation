<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+8:47">Luke 8:47</span>. <strong> Could not be hid.</strong> She felt that He knew of the cure He had wrought.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.06em;"><strong> In the presence of all the people.</strong> Peculiar to Luke, and significant. She sought a cure in secret, but is led to confess it openly. This our Lord desires and deserves. A caution to those believers who do not confess Him before men.</p>
