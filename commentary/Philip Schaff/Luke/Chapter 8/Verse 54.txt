<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+8:54">Luke 8:54</span>. ‘He put them all out’ is to be omitted. Luke has already told of the separation within the house; while Mark, who had told how the crowd outside was prevented from entering, properly adds this (<span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+5:40">Mark 5:40</span>).</p>
