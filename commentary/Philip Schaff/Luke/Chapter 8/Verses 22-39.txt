<p>The voyage across the lake, the storm, the demoniac in the country of the Gerasenes (Gergesenes). Luke is indefinite as to time (<span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+8:22">Luke 8:22</span>), but it was the evening of the day on which the discourse in parables was delivered (<span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+4:35">Mark 4:35</span>). The storm probably occurred at night, and the encounter with the demoniac the succeeding morning.</p>
