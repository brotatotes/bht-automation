<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+8:5-15">Luke 8:5-15</span>. THE PARABLE OF THE SOWER <strong> </strong> and its interpretation. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+13:3-8">Matthew 13:3-8</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+13:18-23">Matthew 13:18-23</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+4:3-20">Mark 4:3-20</span>. The new details are few.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.06em;"><strong> Going on their way.</strong> <strong><em> </em></strong> This indicates carelessness. Some indeed think this refers to a being drawn away by the cares, etc., but this is doubtful.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.06em;"><strong> In patience.</strong> Peculiar to Luke. It means ‘ <em> consistently</em>, through the course of a life spent in duties, and amidst discouragements.’ (Alford).</p>
