<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+9:45">Luke 9:45</span>. <strong> It was hid from them, that they should not perceive it</strong>. Peculiar to Luke. The meaning is plain. They were not permitted to understand the full meaning. Only those who fail to notice the necessity for careful training in the case of the disciples, will doubt the <em> gracious </em> character of this method of concealing in order to reveal.</p>
