<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+9:53">Luke 9:53</span>. <strong> And they did not receive him. </strong> Refused to grant the needed accommodations. This was doubtless done through the messengers. Of course they thus rejected Him as the Messiah.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.06em;"><strong> As though he were going. </strong> ‘As though he were’ is supplied in translating. The ground of rejection was that His going <em> to Jerusalem</em> (not to Gerizim) as the Messiah opposed their Samaritan expectations. What humiliation for the King of heaven that He was refused lodging in an unnamed village! But it was met with love, not with anger.</p>
