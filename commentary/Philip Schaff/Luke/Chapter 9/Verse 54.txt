<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+9:54">Luke 9:54</span>. <strong> Saw this.</strong> On the return of the messengers. Probably the company was now very near the village, and may have noticed some signs of opposition from the inhabitants. Comp. <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ac+8:14-17">Acts 8:14-17</span>, where John’s apostolic visit to Samaria is mentioned. ‘Even as Elijah did’ (<span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="2ki+1:10">2 Kings 1:10</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="2ki+1:12">2 Kings 1:12</span>). This clause is wanting in some of the oldest and best manuscripts, though found in ancient versions. It was readily supplied.</p>
