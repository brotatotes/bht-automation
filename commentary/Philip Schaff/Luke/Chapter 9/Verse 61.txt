<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+9:61">Luke 9:61</span>. <strong> But first suffer me to bid farewell to them that are at my house.</strong> The case of this man is mentioned by Luke only. His request was natural. Some, without good reason, explain: set in order the things in my house, with a view to renouncing them.</p>
