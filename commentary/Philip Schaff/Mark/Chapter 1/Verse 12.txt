<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+1:12">Mark 1:12</span>. <strong> Straightway</strong> <em> .</em> The same favorite word as in <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+1:10">Mark 1:10</span>. The E. V. uses seven different words to represent this one Greek word, which may always be rendered ‘straightway.’
	</p>
<p><strong> The spirit driveth him forth.</strong> Comp. <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+4:1">Matthew 4:1</span>. The expression here used is stronger than ‘led up’ (Matthew), ‘led’ (Luke).
	</p>
