<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+1:14">Mark 1:14</span>. <strong> Now after John was delivered up</strong> <em> ,</em> <em> i.e.</em>, put in prison. On the reason of this imprisonment, see chap. <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+6:17">Mark 6:17</span>.
	</p>
<p><strong> Jesus came into Galilee</strong> <em> .</em> See <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+4:12">Matthew 4:12</span>. Not from fear of Herod, but on account of the opposition of the Pharisees, and also to reach the Galilean masses who had been impressed by the preaching of John.
	</p>
<p><strong> Preaching the gospel of God</strong> <em> .</em> See below and comp. <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+4:17">Matthew 4:17</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+4:23">Matthew 4:23</span>, from the latter passage the words: ‘of the kingdom’ have crept in here.
