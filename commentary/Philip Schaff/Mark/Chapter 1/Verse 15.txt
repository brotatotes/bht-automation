<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+1:15">Mark 1:15</span>. <strong> The time is fulfilled</strong> <em> .</em> The right time, already predicted, has come in fulfilment of prophecy.
	</p>
<p><strong> The kingdom of God is at hand.</strong> <em> </em> Matthew: ‘the kingdom of heaven.’ The reign of the Messiah, which is the kingdom of God, has approached. Comp. <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+3:2">Matthew 3:2</span>.
	</p>
<p><strong> Repent</strong>. Comp, the preaching of John the Baptist; <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+3:2">Matthew 3:2</span>.
	</p>
<p><strong> Believe in the gospel</strong> <em> .</em> Peculiar to Mark. The message of John the Baptist did not include this. As yet our Lord does not preach faith in Himself; that must come later. Yet even here is the germ of faith in a Personal Redeemer. The Jews all hoped for the kingdom of God. Jesus proclaims it, but adds something they do not seem to have expected: repentance and faith in order to enter it.
	</p>
