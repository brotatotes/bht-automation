<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+1:20">Mark 1:20</span>. <strong> With the hired servants.</strong> Peculiar to Mark. Zebedee was not poor, and was not left helpless by this act of his sons
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> Went after him</strong> <em> ,</em> not simply ‘followed Him’ (Matt. and Luke). The great particularity of the brief account suggests that Peter himself told Mark the story. ‘Simon,’ without the addition: ‘called Peter’ (Matthew) is historically more accurate. Hence the order of Mark is probably the more exact, Peter being an eye-witness throughout.
	</p>
