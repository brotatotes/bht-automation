<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+1:25">Mark 1:25</span>. <strong> Rebuked him.</strong> Our Lord refuses the testimony of demons to His Person.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.18em;"><strong> Hold thy peace.</strong> Lit., ‘be thou muzzled,’ silenced. A command joined with enforcing power.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.18em;"><strong> Come out of him.</strong> Two distinct personalities are spoken of, the demon and the possessed man.
	</p>
