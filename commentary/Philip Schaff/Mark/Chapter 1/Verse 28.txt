<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+1:28">Mark 1:28</span>. <strong> And the report of him straightway went out everywhere.</strong> The correct reading presents most graphically the effect of the miracle.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.18em;"><strong> Region of Galilee round about.</strong> Not the regions adjacent to Galilee, but the adjacent regions of Galilee.
	</p>
