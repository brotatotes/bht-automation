<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+1:32">Mark 1:32</span>. <strong> And when evening was come, after the sun was set.</strong> The Sabbath had ended, and they felt at liberty to bring the sick and possessed.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> Them that were possessed with demons,</strong> or, more literally, ‘those demonized, under the power of evil spirits.’ The two afflicted classes are distinguished.
	</p>
