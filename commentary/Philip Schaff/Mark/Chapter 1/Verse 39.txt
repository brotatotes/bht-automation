<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+1:39">Mark 1:39</span>. <strong> In their synagogues.</strong> Lit, ‘into,’ implying that He went into them and reached the ears of the people.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> All Galilee.</strong> Not the next towns only, but throughout the whole region; comp. <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+4:23">Matthew 4:23</span>, and especially <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+4:44">Luke 4:44</span>.
	</p>
