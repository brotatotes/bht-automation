<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+1:43">Mark 1:43</span>. <strong> Solemnly charged him.</strong> This implies strong emotion. Our Lord perceived the man’s disobedient spirit.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> Straightway sent him away.</strong> <strong><em> </em></strong> Not out of the house, for there is no evidence that the miracle was performed in a house. Possibly out of the city into which the leper had come (<span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+4:12">Luke 4:12</span>); but away from Himself, for despite our Lord’s compassion, feelings of grief seem to have been awakened by the man.
	</p>
