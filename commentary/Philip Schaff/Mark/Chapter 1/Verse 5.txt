<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+1:5">Mark 1:5</span>. <strong> And all they of Jerusalem,</strong> lit., ‘the Jerusalemites.’ This expression is peculiar to Mark. These are made prominent among the inhabitants of Judea, since they lived in the capital city. Comp. <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+3:5">Matthew 3:5</span>.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> Confessing their sins</strong>; since ‘repentance’ was the main theme of this preparatory ministry.
	</p>
