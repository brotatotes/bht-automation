<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+10:11">Mark 10:11</span>. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+19:9">Matthew 19:9</span>, where the case of one marrying a divorced woman is added.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> Against her</strong>. It is not clear whether this means the first or second woman. But the marriage with the second is a crime against the first, as well as adultery with the second. The one justifiable ground of divorce is omitted here, being understood as a matter of course.
	</p>
