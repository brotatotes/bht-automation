<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+10:24">Mark 10:24</span>. <strong> Were amazed</strong>. The word is a strong one. Mark introduces this astonishment of the disciples earlier than Matthew, and adds the Lord’s explanation: <strong> Children</strong> (a term of affection to tranquillize them), <strong> how hard it is for them that trust in riches</strong>, etc. That this trust is almost inseparable from the possession of riches, is implied by the connection with <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+10:25">Mark 10:25</span> (on which see <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+19:24">Matthew 19:24</span>). Some ancient authorities omit: ‘for them that trust in riches,’ thus making the statement more general.
	</p>
