<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+10:29">Mark 10:29</span>. <strong> And the gospel’s sake</strong>. A similar addition occurs in chap. <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+8:38">Mark 8:38</span>. Mark perhaps inserts this in both places, in consequence of his own shrinking from suffering on account of the Gospel (<span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ac+13:13">Acts 13:13</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ac+15:38">Acts 15:38</span>); so also, ‘with persecution’ (<span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+10:30">Mark 10:30</span>). He would guard others against his own mistake.
	</p>
