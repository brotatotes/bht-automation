<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+10:42">Mark 10:42</span>. <strong> Accounted to rule over the Gentiles,</strong> have the title of rulers, God being the real Ruler, or are recognized as rulers, the essence of all heathen government being despotism. The latter is perhaps the more suggestive sense.
