<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+10:47">Mark 10:47</span>. Notice the contrast between the title given by the curious crowd: <strong> the Nazarene </strong> (the form used by Mark, and with one exception by him alone), and that in the cry of the blind beggar: <strong> Son of David</strong> (Messiah).
