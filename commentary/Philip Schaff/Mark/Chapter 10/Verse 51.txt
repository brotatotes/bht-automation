<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+10:51">Mark 10:51</span>. <em> </em> <strong> Master </strong> (or my Master). The word is ‘Rabboni’ (as in <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="joh+20:10">John 20:10</span>), the most respectful of the three titles, Rab, Rabbi, Rabboni. Comp. <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+23:7">Matthew 23:7</span>.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> That I may receive my sight</strong>, or, ‘see again.’ Not <em> how or why</em>, but the <em> desire</em>, which he believes the Lord can grant in the best way.
	</p>
