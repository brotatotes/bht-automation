<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+11:16">Mark 11:16</span>. <strong> And he suffered not that any one</strong>. Peculiar to Mark. How He stopped this profanation, we do not know.
	</p>
<p><strong> Should carry a vessel,</strong> including utensils, tools, etc.
	</p>
<p><strong> Through the temple,</strong> <em> i.e.</em>, the court of the Gentiles, which seems to have been used as a thoroughfare. This practice involved the same sin as the others (<span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+11:15">Mark 11:15</span>), and expressed the same contempt for the Gentiles.
	</p>
