<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+11:17">Mark 11:17</span>. <strong> For all the nations</strong>. Part of the original prophecy (<span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="isa+56:7">Isaiah 56:7</span>) and of the quotation also; but the stress cannot be laid upon it, since Matthew and Luke omit it. It shows the independence and accuracy of this Evangelist.
