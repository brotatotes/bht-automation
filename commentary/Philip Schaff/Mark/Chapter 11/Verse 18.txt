<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+11:18">Mark 11:18</span>. <strong> Might destroy him.</strong> The determination to kill Him had been formed before (see <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="joh+11:53">John 11:53</span>). ‘How,’ was now the question. The answer was the treachery of Judas, who probably meditated this step already (from the time of the supper at Bethany on Saturday evening), but first treated with them on the next (Tuesday) evening.
	</p>
