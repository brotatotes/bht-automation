<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+11:2">Mark 11:2</span>. A colt. Matthew mentions the mother, but Mark and Luke the colt only.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> Whereon</strong> <strong> no man ever yet sat.</strong> This agrees with the account that the mother was with it. Animals never yet worked were used for sacred purposes (<span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="nu+19:2">Numbers 19:2</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="de+21:3">Deuteronomy 21:3</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="1sa+6:7">1 Samuel 6:7</span>).
