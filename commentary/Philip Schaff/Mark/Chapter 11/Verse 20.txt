<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+11:20">Mark 11:20</span>. <strong> Withered away from the roots</strong>. The day before the ‘leaves’ were visible ‘afar off;’ today, Tuesday, the blasting was complete. Our verse does not say when this took place, but when they ‘saw’ it Matthew says that it took place ‘immediately.’
