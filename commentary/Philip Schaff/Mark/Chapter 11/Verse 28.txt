<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+11:28">Mark 11:28</span>. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+21:23">Matthew 21:23</span>. Mark with his fondness for solemn repetitions, adds to the second question: <strong> to do these things.</strong> This implies the only authority which could justify such acts is one given for this <em> purpose</em>. Their challenge thus becomes even more definite.
