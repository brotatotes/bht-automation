<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+12:36">Mark 12:36</span>. <em> </em> <strong> David himself said in the Holy Ghost. </strong> Luke: ‘in the book of Psalms.’ The influence of the Holy Spirit upon David in penning the Psalms, is assumed. This passage (<span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ps+110:1">Psalms 110:1</span>) is more frequently referred to in the New Testament than any other.
