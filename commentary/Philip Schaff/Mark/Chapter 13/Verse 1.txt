<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+13:1">Mark 13:1</span>. <strong> Out of the temple.</strong> The final solemn departure (see <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+24:1">Matthew 24:1</span>).
	</p>
<p><strong> One of his disciples.</strong> Mark is most definite here.
	</p>
<p><strong> What</strong> <strong> stones and what buildings.</strong> Luke (<span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+21:5">Luke 21:5</span>): ‘How it was adorned with goodly stones and gifts.’ They seemed almost to intercede for the temple He was leaving.
	</p>
