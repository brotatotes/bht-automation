<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+13:10">Mark 13:10</span>. <em> </em> <strong> Must first be preached unto all the nations. </strong> ‘Preached,’ lit. proclaimed, which is the proper conception of preaching. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+24:14">Matthew 24:14</span>. Here it comes earlier. But the sense is the same. Their martyrdom would spread the gospel, and this spread should precede the end of the woes, in distinction from the beginning (<span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+13:3">Mark 13:3</span>). A twofold fulfilment of this verse is most probable.
	</p>
