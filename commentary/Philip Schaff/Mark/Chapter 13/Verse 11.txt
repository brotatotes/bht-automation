<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+13:11">Mark 13:11</span>. <strong> Be not anxious beforehand. </strong> Peculiar to Mark, though <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+21:14-15">Luke 21:14-15</span>, resembles it. The same thought occurs in <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+10:19-20">Matthew 10:19-20</span>. The two discourses have other points of resemblance. ‘Neither do ye premeditate,’ is to be omitted.
	</p>
