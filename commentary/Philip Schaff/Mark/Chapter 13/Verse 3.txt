<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+13:3">Mark 13:3</span>. <strong> Over against the temple. </strong> A graphic stroke, peculiar to Mark. The summit of Olivet is directly opposite the temple, the city lying spread out like a map before one sitting there.
	</p>
<p><strong> Andrew</strong> (the brother of Peter) is added to the more private company on this occasion.
	</p>
