<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+13:35">Mark 13:35</span>. <strong> Watch therefore.</strong> ‘Ye’ is to be omitted, since ‘watch’ is the emphatic word.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> Whether at even,</strong> etc. With that graphic detail which characterizes this Gospel, four watches of the night (closing at 9 , 12 , 3 , and 6 o’clock.) are mentioned. The coming, unexpected and sudden, will be at night.
	</p>
