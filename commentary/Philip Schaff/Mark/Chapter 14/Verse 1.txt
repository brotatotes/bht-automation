<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+14:1">Mark 14:1</span>. <strong> After two days. </strong> Probably on Tuesday evening after the denunciation of the scribes, etc. Possibly, however, on Wednesday.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> The feast of the Passover and the unleavened bread.</strong> The Passover meal was the beginning of the feast of unleavened bread, which lasted for seven days. On the further details of the conspiracy, see on <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+26:3-5">Matthew 26:3-5</span>.
	</p>
