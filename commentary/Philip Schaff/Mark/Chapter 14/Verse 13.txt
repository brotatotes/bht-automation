<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+14:13">Mark 14:13</span>. <strong> Two of his disciples. </strong> Luke gives their names: ‘Peter and John.’
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> A man bearing a pitcher of water.</strong> This was the sign by which they should know him. The vessel was earthen, but the fact has no necessary significance.
	</p>
