<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+14:15">Mark 14:15</span>. <strong> Upper room</strong>. On the second floor. Some think it was the ‘Alijah,’ or the room on the housetop, as represented in the accompanying cut.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> Furnished</strong>, <em> i.e.,</em> with tables and couches.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> Beady</strong>, <em> i.e., </em> by the householder, who provided the lamb, etc. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+26:17">Matthew 26:17</span>.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> Make ready</strong>. The further preparations necessary for the passover.
	</p>
