<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+14:31">Mark 14:31</span>. <strong> He spake exceeding vehemently.</strong> This might be paraphrased: ‘he went on repeating superabundantly’ The correct reading emphasizes the intensity of the denial.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> And in like manner also,</strong> etc. These protestations occupied some time, probably continuing until they entered Gethsemane.
	</p>
