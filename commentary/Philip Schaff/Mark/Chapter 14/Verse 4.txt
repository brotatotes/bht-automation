<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+14:4">Mark 14:4</span>. <strong> There were some.</strong> Matthew: ‘His disciples;’ John: ‘one of His disciples, Judas,’ etc. The best authorities omit the words, ‘and said.’ Judas alone spoke out; the feeling was general, though no doubt instigated by him. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="joh+12:6">John 12:6</span>.
