<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+14:58">Mark 14:58</span>. <strong> We.... I. </strong> These words are emphatic.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> Made with hands.</strong> <em> ... </em> <strong> made without hands. </strong> Probably our Lord had used these expressions, since, as we <em> now</em> understand them, they express so plainly the correct meaning of the saying mentioned in <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="joh+2:19">John 2:19</span>, etc. An allusion to <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="da+2:34">Daniel 2:34</span>, is possible.
	</p>
