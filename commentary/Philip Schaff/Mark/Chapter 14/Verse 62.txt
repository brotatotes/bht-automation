<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+14:62">Mark 14:62</span>. <em> </em> <strong> I am.</strong> Any allusion to the significant name of God: ‘I Am’ (<span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ex+3:14">Exodus 3:14</span>), is very improbable. Comp. ‘Thou hast said’ (Matthew). ‘From henceforth’ is omitted here. See notes on <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+26:64">Matthew 26:64</span>.
