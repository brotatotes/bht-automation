<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+14:65">Mark 14:65</span>. <strong> Some.</strong> Others than the ‘officers,’ spoken of below. The context (<span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+14:64">Mark 14:64</span>) points to members of the Sanhedrin as engaged in this cruelty.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> The officers.</strong> Probably those who had been by the fire (<span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+14:54">Mark 14:54</span>).
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> Received him with blows of their hands. </strong> The correct reading is thus translated, describing the conduct of the officers when they received Jesus again as their prisoner.
	</p>
