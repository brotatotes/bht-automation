<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+14:68">Mark 14:68</span>. On the different answers, see on Matthew.
	</p>
<p><strong> Into the porch,</strong> or ‘forecourt,’ a different word from that used by Matthew, but referring to the same place.
	</p>
<p><strong> And the cock crew.</strong> The first or mid-night crow. The insertion of this detail is probably due to Peter’s own recollection.
