<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+14:70">Mark 14:70</span>. <strong> And after a little while.</strong> About an hour elapsed (<span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+22:59">Luke 22:59</span>).

	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> For thou art also.</strong> Not, as Jesus is; but, in addition to what has been said, this is another reason. ‘And thy speech,’ etc., was probably inserted from Matthew.
