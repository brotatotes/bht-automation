<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+14:27-31">Mark 14:27-31</span>. <strong> The Way to Gethsemane.</strong> <em> </em> In close agreement with Matthew. The words ‘because of me this night’ (<span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+14:27">Mark 14:27</span>) should be omitted. In <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+14:28">Mark 14:28</span>, Mark introduces a stronger word, howbeit, ‘notwithstanding’ this scattering, you will be gathered again in Galilee. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+26:32">Matthew 26:32</span>.
