<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+15:15">Mark 15:15</span>. <strong> Wishing to content the multitude.</strong> The word ‘wishing’ points to a decision, a determination, neither a hearty desire, nor a mere permission. In <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+1:19">Matthew 1:19</span> the same word is translated ‘was minded.’ Pilate wanted to release Jesus, but in the dilemma (of his own making) concluded to gratify the mob. On the scourging see on <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+27:26">Matthew 27:26</span>.
	</p>
