<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+15:34">Mark 15:34</span>. <strong> Eloi.</strong> This is in the Aramaic dialect then in use. Our Lord probably used the Hebrew form (‘Eli’) given by Matthew, which more closely resembles the name Elijah. A quotation from the Old Testament would naturally be made in Hebrew. On the meaning of the cry, see on <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+27:46">Matthew 27:46</span>.
	</p>
