<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+15:42">Mark 15:42</span>. <strong> The Preparation. </strong> Comp. <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+27:62">Matthew 27:62</span>.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> The day before the Sabbath, </strong> <em> i.e.,</em> Friday. Joseph and the Jews (<span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="joh+19:31">John 19:31</span>) desired ‘that the bodies should not remain upon the cross on the Sabbath.’ The Sabbath of the festival week was, as usual in such cases, a ‘high day’ (<span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="joh+19:31">John 19:31</span>).
	</p>
