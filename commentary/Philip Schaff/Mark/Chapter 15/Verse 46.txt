<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+15:46">Mark 15:46</span>. <strong> And he bought a linen cloth.</strong> It has been argued from this purchase that the day was ‘not the first day of unleavened bread, which was one of sabbatical sanctity,’ but in <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="le+23:7">Leviticus 23:7</span>, labor alone was forbidden on that day. That the tomb belonged to Joseph is implied here, that it was new is omitted by Mark alone.
