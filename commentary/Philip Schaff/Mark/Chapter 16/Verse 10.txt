<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+16:10">Mark 16:10</span>. <strong> She went and told. </strong> Comp. <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="joh+20:18">John 20:18</span>. Emphasis seems to rest on the word ‘she;’ she was the first to tell them, the others probably returning later, after they had seen the Lord on the way (<span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+28:9">Matthew 28:9</span>).
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> Them that had been with him.</strong> An unusual expression for ‘disciples,’ probably including the whole company of His followers.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> As they mourned and wept.</strong> A natural touch, showing how little they anticipated His resurrection.
	</p>
