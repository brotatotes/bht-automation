<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+16:11">Mark 16:11</span>. <strong> Had been seen of her.</strong> Another expression peculiar to this section. But ‘new facts, new words.’
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> Disbelieved</strong>. A different form from ‘believed not’ (<span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+16:12">Mark 16:12</span>). Comp. <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+24:11">Luke 24:11</span>. Their disbelief has been overruled for good; it furnishes abundant proof that they did not <em> invent </em> the story of the resurrection.
	</p>
