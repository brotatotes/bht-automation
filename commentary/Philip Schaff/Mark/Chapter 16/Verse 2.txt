<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+16:2">Mark 16:2</span>. <strong> Very early. </strong> In the East this would mean before sunrise, as the other accounts show. The anxious women would go to the tomb as soon as possible.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> When the sun was risen.</strong> This may be taken literally as referring to the time when they reached the tomb, or less exactly ‘when the sun was about to rise.’
	</p>
