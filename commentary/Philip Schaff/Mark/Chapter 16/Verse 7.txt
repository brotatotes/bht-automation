<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+16:7">Mark 16:7</span>. <strong> But.</strong> Emphatic: instead of lingering here, <strong> go tell,</strong> etc.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> And Peter.</strong> A special token of love to this one who had denied Him, and a recognition of his prominence among his equals.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> Into Galilee.</strong> Comp. <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+24:6-7">Luke 24:6-7</span>. The question: ‘Why seek ye the living,’ etc., probably preceded the words: He is risen (<span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+16:6">Mark 16:6</span>).
	</p>
