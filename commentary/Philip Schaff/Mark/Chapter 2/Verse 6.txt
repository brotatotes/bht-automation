<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+2:6">Mark 2:6</span>. <strong> Certain of the scribes sitting there.</strong> The authorized expounders of the law. Luke defines them more particularly (<span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+5:17">Mark 5:17</span>). These were of the Pharisaical party. From Luke’s account and from the term ‘sitting,’ we infer that they came early; it is probable they were in the upper room where our Lord was, nearer to Him and in the most conspicuous position.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> In their hearts.</strong> That they did not speak, seems clear from the various accounts.
	</p>
