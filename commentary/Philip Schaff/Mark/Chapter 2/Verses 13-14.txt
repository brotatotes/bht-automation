<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+2:13-14">Mark 2:13-14</span>. <strong> The call of Levi</strong> <em> .</em> Undoubtedly the same as Matthew the Apostle and Evangelist. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+9:9">Matthew 9:9</span>. The three accounts agree in matter, but with the usual variation in words. <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+2:13">Mark 2:13</span> is more specific than the parallel passages.
	</p>
<p><strong> Went forth again.</strong> Either with a reference to <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+2:1">Mark 2:1</span> (‘He entered again’), or possibly in allusion to the previous call of four disciples by the sea-side (chap. <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+1:16">Mark 1:16</span>, etc.).
	</p>
