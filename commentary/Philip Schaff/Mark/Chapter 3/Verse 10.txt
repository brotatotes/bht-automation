<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+3:10">Mark 3:10</span>. <strong> They pressed upon him.</strong> Not merely gathered about Him to hear Him, and thus created a pressure, but actually pushed themselves upon Him, <strong> to touch him.</strong> The last clause shows that all were healed, as Matthew states.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> Plagues.</strong> Lit., ‘scourges,’ not a particular class of diseases, as the word ‘plagues’ now implies. On the healing power, comp. <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+6:19">Luke 6:19</span>.
	</p>
