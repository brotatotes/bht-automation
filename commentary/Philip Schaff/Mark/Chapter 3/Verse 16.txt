<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+3:16">Mark 3:16</span>. <strong> He surnamed Peter.</strong> It is not asserted that this name was first given on this occasion. Still the words of our Lord at His first meeting with Simon (<span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="joh+1:42">John 1:42</span>) were prophetic, and Mark seems to have mentioned the name for the first time here, because it was the Apostolic name. On the lists of Apostles, see <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+10:1-4">Matthew 10:1-4</span>.
	</p>
