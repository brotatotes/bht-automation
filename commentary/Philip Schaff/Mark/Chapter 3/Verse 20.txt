<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+3:20">Mark 3:20</span>. Cometh together again. If the last clause of <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+3:19">Mark 3:19</span> means a return to Capernaum, ‘again ‘must refer to chap. <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+2:1">Mark 2:1</span>.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"> They could not so much as eat bread. A vivid description of the thronging. Our Lord and His disciples could not find time to have their regular meals. Notice the excitement and popularity was now at its height; the opposition now takes definite form and stems the tide.
