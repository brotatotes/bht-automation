<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+3:3">Mark 3:3</span>. <strong> Stand forth.</strong> This command is omitted by Matthew. The account of Luke (<span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+6:8">Luke 6:8</span>) is fullest. The subsequent discourse is rendered more impressive by the position of the diseased man.
