<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+4:15">Mark 4:15</span>. <strong> Satan.</strong> Matthew: ‘the wicked one;’ Luke: ‘the devil.’ Being spoken of in the explanation of the parable, or in a didactic way, Satan must be a real personal being, and not merely the symbol of evil.
	</p>
