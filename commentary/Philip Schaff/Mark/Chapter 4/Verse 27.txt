<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+4:27">Mark 4:27</span>. <strong> And should sleep,</strong> etc., <em> i.e.,</em> live as usual without further care of the seed sown.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> He knoweth not how. </strong> The emphasis rests on the word ‘he; ‘he who sows does not know how that takes place which he expects to occur, and to occur for his benefit. A true picture, since such knowledge is not permitted to the wisest of men, and what is known helps the growth very little.
	</p>
