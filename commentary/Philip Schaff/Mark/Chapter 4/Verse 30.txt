<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+4:30">Mark 4:30</span>. <strong> How shall we liken?</strong> Opening a discussion with a question seems to have been a usual mode with Jewish teachers. Here our Lord graciously includes His disciples (‘we’) who were also to teach about the kingdom of God, a hint that Christ’s way of teaching is still to be followed.
	</p>
