<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+4:33">Mark 4:33</span>. <strong> With many such parables.</strong> The many such expressions in the Gospels should put an end to the foolish assumption that each Evangelist intended <em> to tell all he knew.
	</em></p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> As they were able to hear.</strong> Not merely as they had opportunity of listening to His instructions, but ‘according to their capacity of receiving,’ the ability being a moral as well as mental. A wise Teacher! It is taken for granted that He intuitively knew their capacity, a point in which well-meaning instructors may fail.
	</p>
