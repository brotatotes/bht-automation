<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+4:35">Mark 4:35</span>. <strong> And on that day, when evening was come. </strong> Mark is most definite.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> Let us go over unto the other side. </strong> This vivid form of the command indicates a sudden departure. Comp. <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+8:22">Luke 8:22</span>. He would thus seek rest, which could be obtained more easily on a lake subject to storms than in a crowd already excited. Yet unbelief disturbed Him even on the sea.
	</p>
