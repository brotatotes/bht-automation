<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+4:36">Mark 4:36</span>. <strong> And leaving the multitude. </strong> They did not send them away, but left them.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> As he was</strong>; without preparation. He <em> was </em> already <strong> in the boat</strong>, and they set <em> off </em> at once.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> Other boats</strong>. The best authorities do not give the diminutive form, ‘little ships.’ Mark alone tells of this. These other boats were probably separated from them during the subsequent gale.
	</p>
