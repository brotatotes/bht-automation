<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+4:41">Mark 4:41</span>. <strong> Feared exceedingly,</strong> lit., ‘feared a great fear.’ And said one to another. This seems to have been the language of all in the boat.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> Who then is this?</strong> Mark and Luke have a different expression from that given by Matthew: ‘What manner of man.’ ‘Who then,’ <em> i.e.,</em> in view of all we have seen. This command over the wind and sea was a new revelation of Christ even to his disciples.
	</p>
