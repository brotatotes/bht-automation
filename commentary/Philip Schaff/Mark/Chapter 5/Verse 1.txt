<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+5:1">Mark 5:1</span>. <strong> The Gerasenes.</strong> The preferable form here. ‘Gergesenes’ is found in some of the best authorities. The latter is the preferable reading in Luke, although there is good authority for ‘Gerasenes ‘there also. On the locality and in explanation of the cut, see <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+8:28">Matthew 8:28</span>.
