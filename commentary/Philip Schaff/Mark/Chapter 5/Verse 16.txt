<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+5:16">Mark 5:16</span>. <strong> And they that saw it.</strong> Probably the swine herds who had returned, possibly those who had accompanied our Lord in the boat.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.18em;"><strong> How it happened.</strong> Not merely the fact which those coming already perceived, but the way in which the cure had occurred.
	</p>
