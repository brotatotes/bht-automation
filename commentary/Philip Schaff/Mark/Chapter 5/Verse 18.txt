<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+5:18">Mark 5:18</span>. <strong> As he was entering into the boat. </strong> The correct reading shows that he had not yet entered.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.18em;"><strong> Besought him. </strong> The same word used in the last verse. The reason of this request was probably personal gratitude to our Lord. He would thus separate himself from those who rejected his Deliverer. Possibly he feared a relapse.
	</p>
