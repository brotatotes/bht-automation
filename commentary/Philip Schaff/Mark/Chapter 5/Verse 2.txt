<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+5:2">Mark 5:2</span>. <strong> Straightway.</strong> Mark’s favorite word.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> A man.</strong> Matthew tells of ‘two,’ being more particular in this respect. Luke speaks of but one.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> With,</strong> lit., ‘in ‘ <strong> an unclean spirit. </strong> Mark usually prefers this form of describing demoniacal possession.
	</p>
