<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+5:27">Mark 5:27</span>. <strong> When the had heard. </strong> It is not certain how long it was since she heard, but she came because she had heard.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> The things concerning Jesus.</strong> This paraphrase brings out the correct sense. She had heard of His doings, as well as His name.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> In the crowd</strong> (the word usually translated ‘multitude ‘). Mark alone mentions this.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> His garment. </strong> Matthew and Luke are more particular: ‘the hem of His garment.’
	</p>
