<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+5:31">Mark 5:31</span>. <strong> His disciples. </strong> Luke: ‘Peter and they that were with Him.’ The denial of all is mentioned by the same Evangelist. This natural answer of the disciples, according to Luke, called forth an express declaration from our Lord, that He perceived power had gone out from Him.
