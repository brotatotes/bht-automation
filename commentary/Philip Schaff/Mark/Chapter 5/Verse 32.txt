<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+5:32">Mark 5:32</span>. <strong> And he looked round about. </strong> Peculiar in this form to Mark.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> To see her. </strong> This indicates, what is implied in any fair view of the whole transaction, that He knew who had done it.
