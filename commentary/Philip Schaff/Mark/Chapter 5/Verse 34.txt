<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+5:34">Mark 5:34</span>. <strong> Be healed.</strong> Not the same word as in the previous clause.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> Of thy plague,</strong> scourge, affliction. Peculiar to Mark. These words were a gracious and solemn ratification of the healing, which had been stolen, as it were.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> Go in peace.</strong> Lit, ‘into peace.’ The state in which she could now live in contrast with her previous suffering and her unquiet up to this moment.
	</p>
