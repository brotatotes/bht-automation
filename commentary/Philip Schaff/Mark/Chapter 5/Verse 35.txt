<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+5:35">Mark 5:35</span>. <strong> Why troublest thou the master </strong> (Greek: ‘teacher’) <strong> any further?</strong> The underlying thought is: the case is now beyond the help of Jesus, who might have cured, but cannot raise her. The language is kind, and indicates faith.
	</p>
