<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+5:38">Mark 5:38</span>. <strong> Beholdeth a tumult.</strong> Mark gives prominence to the noise common in such circumstances; Matthew, to the ‘minstrels; ‘Luke, to the weeping. Evidently the same scene is described and the accounts derived from eye-wit-nesses. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+9:23">Matthew 9:23</span>.
	</p>
