<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+5:39">Mark 5:39</span>. <strong> When he was come in. </strong> The crowd was kept outside, three disciples accompanying Him. He then speaks to the crowd <em> inside</em>, and after their scornful reply (<span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+5:40">Mark 5:40</span>), they are put out of the house, at least kept from entering into the chamber of death.
