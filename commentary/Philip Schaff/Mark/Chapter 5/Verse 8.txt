<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+5:8">Mark 5:8</span>. <strong> For he said,</strong> or, ‘was saying.’ This and the next verse show that the language just used was that of the demon speaking through the man. The adjuration of the demon and the command of our Lord were uttered about the same moment, the conversation (<span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+5:9-12">Mark 5:9-12</span>) taking place immediately afterwards.
	</p>
