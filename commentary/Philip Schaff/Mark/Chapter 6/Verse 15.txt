<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+6:15">Mark 6:15</span>. <strong> Others said,</strong> were in the habit of saying. The current popular opinions are here given (comp. chap. <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+8:28">Mark 8:28</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+16:14">Matthew 16:14</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+9:19">Luke 9:19</span>), and not what was said to Herod.
	</p>
<p><strong> A prophet as one of the prophets. </strong> The meaning is: A prophet like the old prophets, not Elijah nor the Prophet.
	</p>
