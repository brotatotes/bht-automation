<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+6:16">Mark 6:16</span>. <strong> Heard.</strong> Probably of these opinions as well as of the acts which occasioned them.
	</p>
<p><strong> Whom I beheaded.</strong> The emphasis rests on ‘I,’ and the correct reading is more graphic than the common one. His guilty conscience suggested the thought, which was uttered to his servants (<span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+14:2">Matthew 14:2</span>). Others held the same view (<span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+9:7">Luke 9:7</span>).
	</p>
