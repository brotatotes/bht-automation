<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+6:25">Mark 6:25</span>. <strong> With haste. </strong> She shows no reluctance, but is a genuine daughter of the Herodian family. Her request is put most strongly: <strong> I will,</strong> <em> i.e.,</em> this is my choice.
	</p>
<p><strong> Forthwith,</strong> after as short an interval as possible.
