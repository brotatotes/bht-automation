<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+6:27">Mark 6:27</span>. <strong> A soldier of his guard.</strong> The word is a peculiar one, derived from the Latin. The members of the body-guard would be entrusted with the execution of capital sentences, but that was not their special office. In the prison.
