<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+6:56">Mark 6:56</span>. <strong> Wheresoever he entered. </strong> This implies that a journey of some kind followed.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> Country,</strong> lit,’ fields.’
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> Market-places,</strong> here with a wide sense. This description may refer to a period of some length, and indicates the great number of miracles performed by our Lord. The Passover was at hand (<span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="joh+6:4">John 6:4</span>).
	</p>
