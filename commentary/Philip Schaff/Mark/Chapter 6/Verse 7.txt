<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+6:7">Mark 6:7</span>. <strong> By two and two.</strong> These pairs seem to be indicated in the list given by Matthew, although he does not mention that they were thus sent out. A proof both of truthfulness and of independence.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> Power over the unclean spirits. </strong> Peculiar to Mark, and characteristic of his narrative.
	</p>
