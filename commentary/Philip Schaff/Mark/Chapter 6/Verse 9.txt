<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+6:9">Mark 6:9</span>. <strong> With</strong> <strong> sandals</strong> <em> , i.e.,</em> such as they had on at the time, without waiting for shoes especially adapted for the journey (Matthew: ‘nor shoes’). The construction changes into a direct command in the last clause, as if the memory of one present had supplied it.
