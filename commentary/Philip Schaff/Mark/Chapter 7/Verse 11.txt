<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+7:11">Mark 7:11</span>. <strong> Corban.</strong> This was the Hebrew word used, which Mark translates into Greek for his Greek readers. Both mean a gift to God. The term ‘Corban’ seems to have included all kinds of offerings, though some think it was applied in the time of Christ only to offerings without a sacrifice. On the whole verse, see <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+15:5-6">Matthew 15:5-6</span>.
	</p>
