<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+7:12">Mark 7:12</span>. <strong> Ye no longer suffer him,</strong> etc. Not necessarily that they actively forbade it, but their teachings virtually permitted him to neglect his father and mother altogether. This is the comment of our Lord, not the language of the Pharisees. Comp. <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+15:6">Matthew 15:6</span>.
