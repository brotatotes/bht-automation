<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+7:24">Mark 7:24</span>. <strong> And from thence.</strong> Probably Capernaum, though the locality is nowhere specified.
	</p>
<p><strong> Went. </strong> Matthew: ‘withdrew,’ to avoid the Pharisees.
	</p>
<p><strong> The borders of Tyre and Sidon.</strong> See on <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+15:21">Matthew 15:21</span>. Some ancient authorities omit ‘and Sidon,’ probably to avoid a difficulty in <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+7:31">Mark 7:31</span>.
	</p>
<p><strong> Entered into a house.</strong> To avoid notice.
	</p>
<p><strong> And he could not be hid. </strong> From the desire of the mother who came. She entered the house, and afterwards followed Him in the way. Some however suppose that the first entreaty (<span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+15:22">Matthew 15:22</span>) took place outside the house and the final entreaty within it, so that ‘He could not be hid,’ because she pressed in.
	</p>
