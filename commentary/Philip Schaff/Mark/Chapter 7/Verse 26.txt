<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+7:26">Mark 7:26</span>. <strong> A Greek,</strong> <em> i.e.,</em> a Gentile in religion.
	</p>
<p><strong> Syrophenician by race,</strong> such a nation no longer existed. There were Phenicians at Carthage in Libya (Africa), as well as in Syria. The Phenicians were Canaanites by extraction (comp. <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+15:22">Matthew 15:22</span>).
	</p>
<p><strong> She besought him. </strong> Here occurred all the details given in <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+15:23-25">Matthew 15:23-25</span>.
	</p>
