<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+7:31">Mark 7:31</span>. <strong> And came through Sidon.</strong> Not the city, but the district thus termed. The course was first northward, then eastward, then southward or southwestward, <strong> through the midst of the region of Decapolis </strong> (the northern part) to the <em> eastern</em> shore of the sea of Galilee. See map of Decapolis, p. 271 . In making this circuit, our Lord was seeking needed retirement.
	</p>
