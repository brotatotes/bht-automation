<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+7:35">Mark 7:35</span>. <strong> The string of his tongue, </strong> the impediment, whatever it was, was loosed, was removed.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> And he spake plainly</strong> (or ‘rightly ‘). It is not necessarily implied that he was able to speak in some way before the cure. ‘Mark shows, in his account of the miracles, a preference for those healings, in which the gradual process of the cure, as connected with the instrument and the development of it, is vividly presented’ (Lange).
	</p>
