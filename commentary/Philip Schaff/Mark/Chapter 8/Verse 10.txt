<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+8:10">Mark 8:10</span>. <strong> Into the regions of Dalmanutha. </strong> Matthew: ‘Magadan’ (E. V. ‘Magdala’). The two were probably near each other, north of Tiberias, and our Lord seems to have landed at some retired point between them. See Matt. on <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+15:39">Mark 15:39</span>. The theory that they were on the south-eastern shore of the lake is altogether unsupported, and makes of these journeys of our Lord an aimless wandering.
	</p>
