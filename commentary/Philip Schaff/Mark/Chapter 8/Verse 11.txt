<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+8:11">Mark 8:11</span>. <strong> And the Pharisees.</strong> Matthew: ‘with the Sadducees.’ But the former were the leaders. The skeptical Sadducees were entirely hypocritical in asking a sign from heaven.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> Came forth. </strong> Spying hostility is implied. He had landed at some retired locality (see on <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+8:10">Mark 8:10</span>), where their opposition speedily found Him, since they <strong> began,</strong> at once, to <strong> question with him. </strong> These details are peculiar to Mark.
	</p>
