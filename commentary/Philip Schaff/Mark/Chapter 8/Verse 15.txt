<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+8:15">Mark 8:15</span>. <strong> The leaven of Herod.</strong> Matthew: ‘of the Sadducees.’ Herod was not a professed Sadducee, but our Lord was warning against what all these had in common. On the alliance of the Pharisees and Herodians, see on chap. <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+3:6">Mark 3:6</span>. The one common characteristic of the Pharisees, Sadducees, and Herodians was ‘hypocrisy’ (see on <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+16:12">Matthew 16:12</span>), the last named party coquetting with the other two as politicians do, and of course acting hypocritically.
	</p>
