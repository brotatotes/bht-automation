<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+8:21">Mark 8:21</span>. <strong> Do ye not yet understand,</strong> <em> i.e.</em>, after these miracles. Mark stops with this brief question, because in writing for Gentile readers his main design was to show the condition of the Twelve, rather than to warn against Jewish notions.
	</p>
