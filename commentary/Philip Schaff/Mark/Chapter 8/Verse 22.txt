<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+8:22">Mark 8:22</span>. <strong> And they come to Bethsaida. </strong> They had not landed there, but probably stopped there to procure provisions. Our Lord did not intend to remain there; He was seeking retirement, to prepare His disciples for the future.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> A blind man.</strong> Probably not born blind. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+8:24">Mark 8:24</span>.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> To touch him,</strong> as though the touch was necessary to heal him.
