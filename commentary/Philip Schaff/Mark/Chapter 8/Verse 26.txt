<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+8:26">Mark 8:26</span>. <strong> To his home.</strong> This was not in the village, but elsewhere. Our Lord forbids his return to the village. He was now seeking retirement and avoiding publicity, and there may have been some special reason why it should not be published there. The last clause is to be omitted, though found in many ancient authorities. </p>
