<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+8:27">Mark 8:27</span>. <strong> In the way.</strong> Luke (<span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+9:18">Luke 9:18</span>), without naming the locality, tells that He had been ‘alone praying; ‘an important preparation for the important revelation which was to follow. This was not necessarily ‘in the way ‘from Bethsaida to Cesarea Philippi, but may have been during some journey while in those regions. Mark is less full than Matthew in <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+8:28-30">Mark 8:28-30</span>, but in exact accordance (see on <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+16:14-16">Matthew 16:14-16</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+16:20">Matthew 16:20</span>).
	</p>
