<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+8:7">Mark 8:7</span>. <strong> A few small fishes.</strong> Mark speaks of them separately. The language intimates that they were separately blessed and distributed.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> Having blessed. </strong> A different word from that used in <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+8:6">Mark 8:6</span>. The distinction is slight, however: this one implying <em> praise</em>, and the other <em> thanksgiving</em>. </p>
