<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+9:14">Mark 9:14</span>. <strong> And when they came</strong>. ‘The next day’ (Luke).
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> The scribes questioning with them</strong>. The disciples were not yet prepared to defend themselves, and their failure to cure the lunatic boy was probably used, not only against them, but against their master.
