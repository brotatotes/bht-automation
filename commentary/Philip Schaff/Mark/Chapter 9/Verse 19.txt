<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+9:19">Mark 9:19</span>. <strong> Saith to them</strong>. Not to the man alone (as the incorrect reading implies), though he was included, but to the multitude, whom our Lord addresses as representing that <strong> faithless</strong>, or, ‘unbelieving,’ <strong> generation</strong>.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> How long</strong>, etc.? This indicates ‘holy impatience of their hardness of heart and unbelief. In this the father, disciples, scribes, and multitude are equally involved’ (Alford).
	</p>
