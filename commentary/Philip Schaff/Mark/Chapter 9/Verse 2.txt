<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+9:2">Mark 9:2</span>. <em> Six days. </em> So Matthew. Luke more generally, or perhaps including the day of Peter’s confession: ‘about an eight days’ ( <em> i.e.</em>, a week).
	</p>
<p><strong> By themselves</strong> <em> . </em> Not simply in <em> private</em> (‘apart’), but actually ‘alone.’ The immediate purpose was ‘to pray’ (Luke), the ultimate purpose this revelation.
	</p>
