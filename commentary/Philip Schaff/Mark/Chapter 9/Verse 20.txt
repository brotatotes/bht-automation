<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+9:20">Mark 9:20</span>. <strong> And when he saw him</strong>. When the lad saw Jesus, the spirit convulsed him. But the original gives a stronger hint of the intimate connection between the demon and the possessed person. ‘The kingdom of Satan, in small and great, is ever stirred into a fiercer activity by the coming near of the kingdom of Christ Satan has great wrath, when his time is short’ (Trench).
