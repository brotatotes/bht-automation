<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+9:26">Mark 9:26</span>. <strong> Crying out</strong>, uttering an inarticulate cry. Spoken of the demon, but with the same hint of intimate connection alluded to in <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+9:20">Mark 9:20</span>.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.3em;"><strong> And he became as one dead</strong>. Exhaustion followed the excitement, but this very quietude was a token that the demon was gone.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.3em;"><strong> The most part</strong>, lit., ‘the many,’ according to the correct reading. This was the general verdict.
	</p>
