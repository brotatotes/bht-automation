<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+9:31">Mark 9:31</span>. <strong> For he taught,</strong> or, ‘was teaching,’ habitually, during this private journey.
	</p>
<p><strong> His disciples</strong>. The twelve, as is indicated by the parallel passages. Others may, however, have been included.
	</p>
<p><strong> Is delivered up</strong>. Matthew: ‘shall be delivered up;’ hence the present tense here is prophetic. The delivery was into the hands of men, <em> i.e.</em> <strong> , </strong> by God. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+17:22-23">Matthew 17:22-23</span>.
	</p>
