<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+9:33">Mark 9:33</span>. <strong> When he was.</strong> Literally, ‘being;’ but in the singular number. It was immediately after their entrance.
	</p>
<p><strong> In the house.</strong> Probably a particular house, where He usually resided.
	</p>
<p><strong> In the way. </strong> Probably during the journey to Capernaum.
	</p>
