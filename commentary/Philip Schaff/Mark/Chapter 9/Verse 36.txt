<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+9:36">Mark 9:36</span>. <strong> And taking him in his arms.</strong> Peculiar to Mark. The child seems not to have been brought in, but to have been a member of the household. Tradition says it was the martyr Ignatius, of Antioch, who was therefore called <em> Christophorus</em> (borne by Christ). The little one may have been the child of one of the Apostles, perhaps of Peter, whose house this may have occurred.
