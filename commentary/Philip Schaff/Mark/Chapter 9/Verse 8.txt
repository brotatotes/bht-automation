<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+9:8">Mark 9:8</span>. <strong> And suddenly</strong>, etc. Mark omits some details here. The withdrawal itself was not necessarily sudden, but their perception of it was.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> Save Jesus only</strong>. His authority suffices; His love redeems; His glory is the great end.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> With themselves. </strong> Peculiar to Mark; it hints at the self-consciousness of an eye-witness, and suggests that our Lord was near them as they looked. Matthew tells that they looked up after He touched them. </p>
