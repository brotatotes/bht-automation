<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+1:2">Matthew 1:2</span>. <strong> Abraham begat Isaac. </strong> ‘Begat,’ repeated throughout, makes prominent the idea of a living connection and succession.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> Judah, </strong> the direct ancestor, is named; <strong> his brethren </strong> are added, to indicate the connection with the whole covenant nation.</p>
