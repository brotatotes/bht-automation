<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+1:22">Matthew 1:22</span>. <strong> But all this hath come to pass. </strong> An explanation of the Evangelist, who everywhere points to the fulfilment of prophecy.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> That, </strong> i.e., ‘in order that.’ The event fulfilled God’s purpose as predicted, and therefore took place. The prophecy depends on the fact as purposed in the Divine mind.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> Fulfilled. </strong> This word has its usual sense here as applied to prophecy.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> By the Lord, </strong> who spoke <strong> through the Prophet, </strong> i.e., Isaiah (<span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="isa+7:14">Isaiah 7:14</span>). The writing followed the speaking.</p>
