<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+1:3">Matthew 1:3</span>. <strong> Tamar, </strong> a heathen woman, guilty of intentional incest. The Jews and some commentators seek to excuse her, but the stain must be admitted. The mention of this name not only proves the correctness of the genealogy, but tends to humble Jewish pride and exalt the grace of God.</p>
