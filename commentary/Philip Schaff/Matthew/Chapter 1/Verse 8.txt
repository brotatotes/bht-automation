<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+1:8">Matthew 1:8</span>. Between <strong> Joram </strong> and <strong> Uzziah, </strong> three names are intentionally omitted: Ahaziah, Joash, and Amaziah, probably to reduce the number of generations. These three were chosen, either because personally unworthy, or because descendants to the fourth generation from Jezebel, through Athaliah.</p>
