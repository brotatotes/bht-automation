<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+10:14">Matthew 10:14</span>. <strong> And whosoever shall not receive you, </strong> as guests in the house.
	</p>
<p><strong> Nor hear your words, </strong> as teachers in a town. If refused in one house, they need not leave the town at once, although after inquiring for one ‘worthy,’ such a refusal would probably precede a rejection in the place itself.
	</p>
<p><strong> Shake off the dust of your feet. </strong> To be done immediately after decided rejection in a house or a city. The act was symbolical, expressing an end of all intercourse, and perhaps an end of responsibility. As His representatives, their act implied rejection and consequent judgment (comp. <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+6:11">Mark 6:11</span>).
	</p>
