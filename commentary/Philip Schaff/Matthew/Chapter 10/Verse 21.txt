<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+10:21">Matthew 10:21</span>. <strong> And. </strong> The heavenly ‘Father’ aids; the human relatives may persecute.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> Deliver up. </strong> Become informers. The first prophecy of actual martyrdom. The idea of persecution in general is of course included.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.06em;"><strong> Shall rise up. </strong> A strong word, implying first, rebellion against parental authority, and then, in this connection, a parricidal course of conduct.
	</p>
