<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+10:27">Matthew 10:27</span>. <strong> What I tell you in the darkness, </strong> etc. A further incitement to boldness in preaching. Our Lord must first privately teach, so as to train His disciples; to them the duty of publishing the truth was committed. The verse probably alludes both to the extension of the gospel beyond the narrow limits of Palestine; and also to the future revelation by the Holy Spirit, <strong> in the ear, </strong> which was to be made known everywhere by the Apostles.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.06em;"><strong> Housetops. </strong> From the flat roofs of the Eastern houses with a loud voice the greatest publicity could be obtained. The whole truth is to be publicly made known.
	</p>
