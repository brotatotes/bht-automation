<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+10:38">Matthew 10:38</span>. <strong> Taketh not his cross, </strong> etc. We may supply in thought: as I shall carry my cross. The culprit bore his own cross to the place of crucifixion. The first allusion to the mode of the death, which must have startled the Apostles, even after what had been said.
	</p>
