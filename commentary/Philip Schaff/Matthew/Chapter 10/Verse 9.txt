<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+10:9">Matthew 10:9</span>. Although their labor was to be performed gratuitously and not for gain, they were not to make preparations for the journey, but to go without first providing a store of money: <strong> no gold, nor silver, nor brass. </strong> ‘Brass,’ not even the smaller copper coins.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> In your purses, </strong> <em> i.e., </em> girdles, which were used as pockets or purses.
	</p>
