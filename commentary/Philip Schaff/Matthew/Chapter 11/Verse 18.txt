<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+11:18">Matthew 11:18</span>. <strong> For. </strong> An evidence of the petulant spirit (so <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+11:19">Matthew 11:19</span>).
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> John came neither eating nor drinking. </strong> He came as a prophet, and living in a peculiar manner, ‘neither eating bread nor drinking wine’ (<span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+7:33">Luke 7:33</span>); ‘his meat was locusts and wild honey’ (chap. <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+3:4">Matthew 3:4</span>).
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> And they say, He hath a demon. </strong> A demon of melancholy; he is a fanatic.
	</p>
