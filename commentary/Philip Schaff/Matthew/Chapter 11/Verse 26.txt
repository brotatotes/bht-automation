<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+11:26">Matthew 11:26</span>. <strong> Yea, that it was well-pleasing in thy sight. </strong> Praise for His ‘good pleasure’ which involves His wisdom, prudence, and goodness. When men deny these qualities or we cannot fully perceive them, we may still praise His ‘good pleasure,’ as our Master did.
	</p>
