<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+11:6">Matthew 11:6</span>. <strong> And blessed is he, </strong> etc. This recalls <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="isa+8:14">Isaiah 8:14</span>.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> Offended, </strong> <em> i.e.,</em> ‘made to stumble.’ This does not upbraid, but cautions, implying that Christ knew best what to do in His kingdom. Result of the message: we may well believe that John was not taken away as a martyr to righteousness without having his faith restored. His disciples, after his death and burial,’ came and told Jesus’ (<span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+14:12">Matthew 14:12</span>).
