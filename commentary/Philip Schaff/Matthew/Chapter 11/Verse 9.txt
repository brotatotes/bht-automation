<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+11:9">Matthew 11:9</span>. <strong> To see a prophet. </strong> To this the crowd would answer ‘yes’ (comp. ch. <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+21:26">Matthew 21:26</span>). But our Lord adds, Yea, <strong> </strong> most certainly, <strong> I say unto you, </strong> I who can speak with authority on the subject, <strong> and much more than a prophet. </strong> John saw and pointed out Him whom the prophets only predicted, and he was himself the subject of prophecy.
	</p>
