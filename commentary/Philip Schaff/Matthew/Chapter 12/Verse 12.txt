<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+12:12">Matthew 12:12</span>. <strong> How much, then, is a man better than a sheep? </strong> Some take this as an explanation: ‘Of how much more worth now is a man than a sheep!’ But it is better to regard it as a question. Our Lord recognizes the superiority of man, as well as the superior claims of humanity.
	</p>
<p><strong> Wherefore it is lawful, </strong> etc. (Comp. Mark and Luke.) Works of <em> mercy</em> on the Sabbath are lawful and right. Hypocrites care more for ceremonies than for their cattle, and more for their cattle than for suffering humanity.
	</p>
