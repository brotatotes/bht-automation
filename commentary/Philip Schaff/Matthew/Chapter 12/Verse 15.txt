<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+12:15">Matthew 12:15</span>. <strong> Withdrew.</strong> Not from fear, but to carry out His ministry without interruption from these plotters.
	</p>
<p><strong> Many. </strong> ‘Multitudes’ is to be omitted. It is evident that our Lord did not wish to avoid the people.
	</p>
<p><strong> He healed them all, </strong> <em> i.e.,</em> all who needed healing, possibly, including spiritual healing also. This verse seems to refer to a definite occasion, and not to be a general description of frequent withdrawals, extending over a considerable period. The very detailed account of Mark (<span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+3:7-12">Mark 3:7-12</span>) opposes the latter view.
	</p>
