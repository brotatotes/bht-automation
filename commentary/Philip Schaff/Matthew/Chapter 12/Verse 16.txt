<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+12:16">Matthew 12:16</span>. <strong> And charged them, </strong> etc. Mark tells of the similar command given to ‘evil spirits.’ This more general prohibition was probably given to prevent a rupture between His carnal followers and the Pharisees, so early in His ministry.
