<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+12:22">Matthew 12:22</span>. <strong> Then. </strong> Indefinite, here meaning ‘afterwards.’
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> Was brought. </strong> Such an one could not come alone.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> One possessed, </strong> etc <strong> .</strong>, or, ‘a demoniac,’ <strong> blind and dumb. </strong> A different case from that mentioned in chap. <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+9:32-34">Matthew 9:32-34</span>. The physical effect of the possession was similar, but more unfortunate; the accusation of the Pharisees was similar, but more blasphemous.
	</p>
