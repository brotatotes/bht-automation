<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+12:23">Matthew 12:23</span>. The effect of such a remarkable miracle on the people was astonishment, and they asked: <strong> Is this the Son of David? </strong> The original indicates an expectation or hope of a <em> negative</em> answer (see next verse); so that we must not attribute to the multitude any strong spiritual conviction.
	</p>
