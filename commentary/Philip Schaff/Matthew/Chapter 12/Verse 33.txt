<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+12:33">Matthew 12:33</span>. <strong> Either make the tree good, </strong> etc. The law of God’s creation is: good trees, good fruit; corrupt trees, evil fruit. Judge the tree by its fruit. My works are good, hence I am good; the blasphemous words of the Pharisees show their character. Some explain ‘make’ as meaning ‘exhibit,’ ‘represent,’ but the application is the same.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> For by the fruit the tree is known. </strong> Comp. chap. <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+7:20">Matthew 7:20</span>. The mention of this general principle here favors the view that <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+12:31-32">Matthew 12:31-32</span> are to be applied to a <em> state.
	</em></p>
