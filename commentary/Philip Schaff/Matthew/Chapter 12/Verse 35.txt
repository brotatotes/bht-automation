<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+12:35">Matthew 12:35</span>. The thought of <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+12:33">Matthew 12:33</span>, in another figure; words are represented as fruits.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> The good treasure. </strong> The words: ‘of the heart,’ though not in the text, suggest the correct explanation. The contents of our hearts are known to God alone and partially to ourselves, but our unrestrained utterances show what is laid up there.
	</p>
