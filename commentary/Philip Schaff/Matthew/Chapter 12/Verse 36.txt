<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+12:36">Matthew 12:36</span>. <strong> And I say unto you. </strong> An authoritative revelation, opposed to the common opinion of men, yet preeminently reasonable.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> Every idle word, </strong> <em> i.e.</em>, morally useless. If ‘the idle word’ must be accounted for, much more the wicked ones spoken on this occasion.
