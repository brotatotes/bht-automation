	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> In the judgment, </strong> not ‘in judgment.’
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> With this generation, </strong> <em> i.e.,</em> at the same time, not necessarily against them, although this would be the result.
