<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+12:44">Matthew 12:44</span>. <strong> My house, </strong> <em> i.e.</em> <strong> , </strong> the demoniac.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> He findeth it. </strong> Not in a state of moral purity, but <strong> empty </strong> of a good tenant; <strong> swept </strong> of all that would be forbidding to an evil spirit; <strong> and garnished, </strong> set in order, and adorned, but in a way inviting to the unclean spirit. </p>
