<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+12:9">Matthew 12:9</span>. <strong> And he departed thence. </strong> The miracle which follows, took place on another Sabbath (see <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+6:6">Luke 6:6</span>), probably the next one.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> Went into their synagogue. </strong> The synagogue of His opponents, probably in some important town in Galilee. Luke says He taught there, as seems to have been His habit.
	</p>
