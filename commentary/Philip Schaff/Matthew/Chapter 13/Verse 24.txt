<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+13:24">Matthew 13:24</span>. <strong> Set he before them.</strong> With the double purpose already spoken of; the word being often used in reference to food. </p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> The kingdom of heaven.</strong> The subject in all seven parables. Christ’s reign in the new economy of salvation.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> Is likened,</strong> or ‘made like.’ Not ‘is like,’ as in the succeeding parables. The speedy establishment of the kingdom is implied; hence this parable is referred to the first stage of Christianity. </p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> Good seed,</strong> <em> i.e.</em>, of a good kind and good of its kind.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> His field.</strong> The ‘world’ (<span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+13:38">Matthew 13:38</span>) is <em> ‘</em> His’ though ‘the devil’ works in it.
	</p>
