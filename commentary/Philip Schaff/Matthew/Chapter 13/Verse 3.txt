<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+13:3">Matthew 13:3</span>. <strong> Many things. </strong> Out of the ‘many,’ Matthew selects these parables; for this selection we seek a reason (see note on the whole discourse).
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> Behold, </strong> calling attention to what follows, not to some object in sight, which would have distracted attention from the parable.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> The sower, </strong> standing for the class; <strong> went forth, </strong> <em> i.e.,</em> as usual, pointing rather to a supposed case, than to something occurring before their eyes. </p>
