<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+13:32">Matthew 13:32</span>. <strong> Less than all seeds</strong>, i.e., those sown by the Jews.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> Greater than the herbs</strong>. The literal meaning leaves it uncertain whether the plant referred to was itself an herb. The main point is the rapid growth from a diminutive seed.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> The birds of the heaven</strong> represent the external adherents of the kingdom, nations nominally Christian; oftentimes ‘outward church form,’ since the kingdom itself is not the Church organization.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> Lodge in the branches thereof.</strong> Seeking shelter and remaining there. The permanent external adhesion is thus indicated.
	</p>
