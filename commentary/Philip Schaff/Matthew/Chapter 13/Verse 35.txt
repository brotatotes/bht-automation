<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+13:35">Matthew 13:35</span>. <strong> That,</strong> i.e., ‘in order that.’
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> The prophet.</strong> From <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ps+78:2">Psalms 78:2</span>, the author of which was Asaph, ‘the seer’ (<span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="2ch+29:30">2 Chronicles 29:30</span>), or prophet. The Psalm is historical, but the events it mentions have a reference to Christ (comp. <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="1co+10:6">1 Corinthians 10:6</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="1co+10:11">1 Corinthians 10:11</span>, where the same events are spoken of).
	</p>
