<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+13:42">Matthew 13:42</span>. <strong> And shall cast them,</strong> etc. As the tares were burned, this may be figurative, but it undoubtedly refers to intolerable suffering, resulting not simply from the circumstances of the evildoers in a future state but from their character. </p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> There shall be the weeping.</strong> Comp. chap. <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+8:12">Matthew 8:12</span>. These awful words must mean something positive and punitive.
	</p>
