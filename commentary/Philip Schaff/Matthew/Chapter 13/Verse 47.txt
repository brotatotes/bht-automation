<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+13:47">Matthew 13:47</span>. <strong> A net, that was cast into the sea</strong>. A drag-net or seine is meant. Appropriate for an audience largely made up of fishermen. The parable resembles that of the tares; that, however, represented the two developments of good and evil, side by side in the world (and in the church); this one is applicable rather to the missionary effort of the Church. ‘The sea’ is a Scriptural figure for ‘the nations’ (<span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="re+17:15">Revelation 17:15</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="isa+8:7">Isaiah 8:7</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ps+65:7">Psalms 65:7</span>). Gathered of every kind. This predicted result of Christian effort is sufficiently evident at all times.
