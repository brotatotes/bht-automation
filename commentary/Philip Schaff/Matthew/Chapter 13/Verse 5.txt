<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+13:5">Matthew 13:5</span>. <strong> Upon the rocky places. </strong> Not full of stones, but thin soil over rocks.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> Forthwith they sprung up, because, </strong> etc. The greater heat of the shallow soil would cause a rapid growth upwards.
	</p>
