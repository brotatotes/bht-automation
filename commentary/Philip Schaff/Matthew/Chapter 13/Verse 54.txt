<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+13:54">Matthew 13:54</span>. <strong> Into his own country. </strong> Nazareth as the home of His parents and the place ‘where he had been brought up’ (<span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+4:16">Luke 4:16</span>).
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> Whence hath this man.</strong> As if to say: This is our townsman, what better schooling did he have than we; what his family is, we all know, etc.
	</p>
