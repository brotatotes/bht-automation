<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+13:57">Matthew 13:57</span>. <strong> And they were offended in him, </strong> made to stumble. They were led into error and sin with regard to Him.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> A prophet is not without honor,</strong> etc. The rejection is accounted for by a proverbial expression, verified by human experience. ‘Familiarity breeds contempt,’ ‘Distance lends enchantment to the view,’ are still more general expressions of the same principle.
