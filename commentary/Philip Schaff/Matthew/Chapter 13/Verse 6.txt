<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+13:6">Matthew 13:6</span>. <strong> Scorched, </strong> or ‘burnt.’ The heat of the sun, so necessary to vegetable life, did this; but the effect must be connected with the cause: <strong> they had no root. </strong> Plants need both sunshine and moisture; they get the first from their growth above ground, the second from their growth below ground; the root however being the principal channel of nourishment (comp. Luke: ‘moisture’). Hence these <strong> withered away.
	</strong></p>
