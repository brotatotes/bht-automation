<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+13:7">Matthew 13:7</span>. <strong> Upon the thorns, </strong> <em> i.e.,</em> upon soil where there were roots of thorns, etc., not necessarily among thorn-bushes.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> And the thorns grew up and choked them. </strong> The thorns were of ranker growth. Both ideas are implied in the phrase ‘sprung up.’ <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+13:8">Matthew 13:8</span>. </p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> Good ground. </strong> The proportion of the harvest is large, but not unexampled. Palestine was <strong> </strong> once exceedingly fertile. The remarkable variety in the form of the parallel passages shows independence.
	</p>
