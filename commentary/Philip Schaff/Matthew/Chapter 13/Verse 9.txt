<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+13:9">Matthew 13:9</span>. <strong> He that hath, </strong> etc. Comp. chap. <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+11:15">Matthew 11:15</span>. A <strong> </strong> peculiarly appropriate ending to a parable. It here refers to the understanding of the parable; the parable itself, as our Lord shows, signified the outward hearing. The former would in this case imply the latter.
	</p>
