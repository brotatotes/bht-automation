<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+14:14">Matthew 14:14</span>. <strong> Had compassion on them</strong>. All had followed Him so far and were in a state of spiritual destitution; many of them were sick. His compassion manifested itself in healing their sick, and in giving them instruction (<span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+6:34">Mark 6:34</span>). The approach of the Passover season (<span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="joh+6:4">John 6:4</span>), accounts for the greatness of the multitude; many of them were probably on their way to Jerusalem.
	</p>
