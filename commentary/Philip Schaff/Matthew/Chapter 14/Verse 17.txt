<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+14:17">Matthew 14:17</span>. <strong> We have here</strong>. Andrew said this; a lad who was present had this small store of food (<span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="joh+6:8-9">John 6:8-9</span>). The disciples, though full of perplexity and doubt, tried to <em> obey</em>, and sought food for the multitude. The loaves and fishes thus obtained, of which they said ‘What are they among so many,’ were given by them to the people.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> Five loaves</strong> (‘barley loaves’) and two fishes (‘small fishes,’ probably salt ones). Plain common food.
	</p>
