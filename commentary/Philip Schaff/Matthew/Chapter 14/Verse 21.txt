<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+14:21">Matthew 14:21</span>. <strong> Five thousand men</strong>. All the Evangelists mention the number of men. Matthew alone adds: <strong> besides women and children</strong>. The latter classes were probably not numerous, and would be fed apart from the men. On the effect of the miracle, see <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="joh+6:14-15">John 6:14-15</span>.
	</p>
