<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+14:23">Matthew 14:23</span>. <strong> He went up into the mountain apart to pray</strong>. The attempt to make Him a king was a temptation to be met by prayer.
	</p>
<p><strong> Evening</strong>. Here the second evening. Comp. <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+14:15">Matthew 14:15</span>.
	</p>
<p><strong> He was there alone</strong>. Alone with His Father. Prayer succeeded and preceded His labors for men.
	</p>
