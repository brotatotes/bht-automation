<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+14:26">Matthew 14:26</span>. <strong> It in an apparition</strong> <em> .</em> An unreal appearance of a real person. The word is not that usually rendered, ‘spirit’
	</p>
<p><strong> They cried out for fear</strong> <em> .</em> Matthew is an honest witness to tell of this superstitious fear. As he here discriminates between ‘an apparition’ and a real bodily appearance of our Lord, he cannot mean the former when he writes of the resurrection of Christ.
	</p>
