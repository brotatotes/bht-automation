<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+14:32">Matthew 14:32</span>. <strong> And when they were gone up into the boat.</strong> <em> </em> John (<span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="joh+6:21">John 6:21</span>) speaks of the boat being immediately ‘at the land whither they went’ This was on the western side of the lake, and we may either suppose that the wind during the night had driven them near that shore, or accept another miracle.
