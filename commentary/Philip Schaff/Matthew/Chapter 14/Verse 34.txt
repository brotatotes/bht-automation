<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+14:34">Matthew 14:34</span>. <strong> And when they were passed over.</strong> This points to ordinary, not miraculous sailing.
	</p>
<p><strong> To the land unto Gennesaret.</strong> <em> </em> ‘Gennesaret’ was a fertile district, with a mild climate, on the western shore of the lake (also called the Lake of Gennesaret). It is nearly four miles long and half as broad. Modern name: El-Ghuweir.
	</p>
