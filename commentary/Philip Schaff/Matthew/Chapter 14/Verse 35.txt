<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+14:35">Matthew 14:35</span>. <strong> The men of that place.</strong> Not Capernaum, but a more retired spot. The people who had been fed, came to that city ‘seeking Jesus’ (<span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="joh+6:24">John 6:24</span>): it is implied that they found Him somewhere else. Mark’s account suggests that our Lord passed through other places on His way to Capernaum.
	</p>
<p><strong> Got knowledge of</strong> <em> </em> <strong> him.</strong> When morning came they would recognize Him, as our Lord was personally well known in Galilee.
	</p>
