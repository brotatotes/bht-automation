<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+14:4">Matthew 14:4</span>. <strong> For John said</strong>; not once but habitually, as the original hints. John was a bold preacher of righteousness and repentance, not ‘a reed shaken by the wind’ (chap. <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+11:7">Matthew 11:7</span>). His fidelity led to his imprisonment.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> It is not lawful.</strong> The act of Herod was a crime against his brother, against his wife, and in itself incestuous, since Herodias was his niece (comp. <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="le+18:16">Leviticus 18:16</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="le+20:21">Leviticus 20:21</span>).
	</p>
