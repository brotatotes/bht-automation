<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+14:5">Matthew 14:5</span>. <strong> And when he would have put him to death</strong>. At the instigation of Herodias (<span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+6:19-20">Mark 6:19-20</span>).
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> He feared the multitude</strong>, etc. The character of John also restrained him; but the political motive was needed to overbear the influence of Herodias.
	</p>
