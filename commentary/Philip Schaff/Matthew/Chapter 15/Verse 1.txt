<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+15:1">Matthew 15:1</span>. <em> </em> <strong> From Jerusalem Pharisees and scribes.</strong> Not ‘scribes and Pharisees,’ but representatives of the party of the Pharisees, including ‘scribes.’ Possibly a formal deputation from the Sanhedrin (‘from Jerusalem’. They came apparently with a definite and hostile purpose (comp. <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+7:1">Mark 7:1</span>), probably to aid the Galilean Pharisees, or, as is less likely, in consequence of the conduct of the disciples of our Lord at the recent Passover feast in Jerusalem. (See <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+15:2">Matthew 15:2</span>.)
