<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+15:12">Matthew 15:12</span>. <strong> Then came the disciples.</strong> After He went into the house (<span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+7:17">Mark 7:17</span>).
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> The Pharisees when they heard the saying, took offence.</strong> Probably the saying in <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+15:11">Matthew 15:11</span>, which seemed to be in opposition to the Levitical law. They were ready to take offence from the effect of the previous discourse (<span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+15:3-9">Matthew 15:3-9</span>). The disciples, hearing their disparaging and hostile remarks in the crowd, warn their Master, as their opponents were important personages.
	</p>
