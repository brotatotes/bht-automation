<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+15:19">Matthew 15:19</span>. <strong> For out of the heart proceed evil thoughts</strong>, <strong> </strong> <em> i.e.,</em> reasonings, purposes, not mere notions. The criminality of acts proceeds from the purpose; for these acts man is responsible. The plural form indicates that these sins are common and notorious. Mark adds a number of others.
	</p>
