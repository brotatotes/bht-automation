<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+15:24">Matthew 15:24</span>. <strong> I was not sent,</strong> etc. His personal mission was only to the Jews, as their previous mission had been (chap. <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+10:5-6">Matthew 10:5-6</span>). The exceptions all pointed to the future spiritual significance of the phrase: <strong> house of Israel</strong> <em> .</em> This answer might suggest to the disciples: ‘Is not such a one really a daughter of the spiritual Israel, though a woman of Canaan.’ It was not a refusal, but a postponement, to educate her faith and -train the disciples for their world-wide mission.
