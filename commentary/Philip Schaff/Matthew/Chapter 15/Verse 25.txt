<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+15:25">Matthew 15:25</span>. <strong> But she came.</strong> Perhaps into the house (<span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+7:24">Mark 7:24</span>), but more probably to where He waited for her in the way. Her faith was more manifest, as the Lord gave her opportunity.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> Lord.</strong> Reverential address. </p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> Help me.</strong> A touch of nature in the mothers prayer! Maternal love remains even in heathenism; often leading to Christ.
	</p>
