<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+15:28">Matthew 15:28</span>. <strong> Great is thy faith.</strong> The greatest faith had been shown by Gentiles (comp. chap. <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+8:10">Matthew 8:10</span>); and of this woman’s characteristics, ‘faith’ was not only the crown, but the source.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> And her daughter was healed from that hour.</strong> Mark (<span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+7:30">Mark 7:30</span>) describes her return home. As in the case of the Gentile centurion, the cure was performed at a distance. The intermediate link in both cases was strong faith combined with affection for the person healed. A hint is thus given in regard to intercessory prayer.
	</p>
