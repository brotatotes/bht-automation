<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+15:31">Matthew 15:31</span>. <strong> Wondered.</strong> Comp. <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+7:37">Mark 7:37</span>. The people had probably heard of, but never witnessed, His power.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> The dumb speaking,</strong> etc. This is the form of the original.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> They glorified the God of Israel.</strong> They were not heathen, but Jews. Yet living on the borders, they seem to have been affected by heathen nations, and half recognized other gods.
	</p>
