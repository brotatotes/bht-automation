<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+15:35">Matthew 15:35</span>. <strong> And giving commandment.</strong> The correct reading joins this verse closely with <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+15:36">Matthew 15:36</span>. In the other case the disciples arranged the multitude (<span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+9:14">Luke 9:14</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="joh+6:10">John 6:10</span>).
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> On the ground</strong> <em> ,</em> not ‘on the grass’ (chap. <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+14:19">Matthew 14:19</span>); they were ‘in a wilderness’(<span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+15:33">Matthew 15:33</span>), a desolate region, in this case.
