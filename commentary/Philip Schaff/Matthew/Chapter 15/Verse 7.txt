<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+15:7">Matthew 15:7</span>. <strong> Ye hypocrites.</strong> This word had not quite so strong a sense then as now. It includes those self-deceived.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> Well did Isaiah prophesy of you.</strong> (<span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="isa+29:13">Isaiah 29:13</span>.) ‘Well,’ <em> i.e</em>., aptly. Our Lord assumes that the prophecy properly referred to the Jewish people then, while He does not imply that this was its exclusive or even original application.
	</p>
