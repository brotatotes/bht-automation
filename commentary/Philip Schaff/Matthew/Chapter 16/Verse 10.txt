<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+16:10">Matthew 16:10</span>. <strong> Baskets.</strong> A different word in the original from that used in <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+16:9">Matthew 16:9</span>, but the same one we find in the account of the miracle (chap. <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+15:37">Matthew 15:37</span>).
