<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+16:20">Matthew 16:20</span>. <strong> That they should tell no man.</strong> Until our Lord Himself announced His Messiahship before the Sanhedrin (chap. <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+26:64">Matthew 26:64</span>), the Christian acknowledgment was to be kept separate from the carnal expectations of the Jews.
