<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+16:27">Matthew 16:27</span>. <strong> For</strong>. The reason this transaction is so unprofitable is now given.
	</p>
<p><strong> The Son of man, </strong> who now in humble form asks to be followed on the path of suffering.
	</p>
<p><strong> Shall come in the glory of his Father.</strong> Through suffering to glory. He spoke first of His own sorrows, then of His people’s; now He predicts glory and triumph; their’s also, because His. In this second coming, afterwards more fully spoken of (chaps, 24 , 25 ), He shall appear as Judge of all, in the glory of God the Father, and the attendants shall be <strong> his angels. </strong> Both a threatening and a promise in view of the judgment which it involves.
	</p>
<p><strong> Unto every man according to his doing.</strong> His whole character and conduct. This depends upon the effort either to save the lower life or gain the higher. This ‘doing ‘results from faith or unbelief.
	</p>
