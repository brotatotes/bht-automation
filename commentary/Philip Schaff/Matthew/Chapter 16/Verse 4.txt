<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+16:4">Matthew 16:4</span>. Comp. chap. <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+12:39">Matthew 12:39</span> (exactly the same words). The audience may have been in part the same, hence no explanation is added here.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> And he left them and departed.</strong> Abruptly it would seem. As events proved, He now gave them up to their blindness, but with pain at their unbelief. See on <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+8:12">Mark 8:12</span>: ‘And he sighed deeply in his spirit.’
