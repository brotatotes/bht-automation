<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+17:11">Matthew 17:11</span>. <strong> Elijah indeed cometh.</strong> Our Lord confirms the view, that Elijah should come (<span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mal+4:5">Malachi 4:5</span>).
	</p>
<p><strong> Shall restore</strong> or ‘establish anew,’ <strong> all things.</strong> Comp. <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mal+4:6">Malachi 4:6</span>. The actual work of restoration was however the work of the Messiah, for which Elijah should prepare the way (comp. <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+3:4">Luke 3:4</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ac+3:21">Acts 3:21</span>).
	</p>
