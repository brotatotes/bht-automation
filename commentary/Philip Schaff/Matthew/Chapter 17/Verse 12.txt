<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+17:12">Matthew 17:12</span>. <strong> Elijah is come already.</strong> Comp, chap <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+11:14">Matthew 11:14</span>. The prophecy of Malachi had been fulfilled in John the Baptist, so far as the first coming of the Messiah was concerned.
	</p>
<p><strong> They knew him not.</strong> They recognized, neither John the forerunner of the Messiah, nor the Messiah himself. Like persecution followed like unbelief.
	</p>
