<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+18:16">Matthew 18:16</span>. The next step is less private, but intended to prevent publicity.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> One or two more</strong> as witnesses. The offence must be grave enough to warrant this step.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> Or three</strong>, parenthetical, implying that the offending party may be a witness against himself.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> Established</strong>. It is assumed, not that both are in the wrong, but that the two witnesses, on hearing the facts, pronounce against the party to whom they go.
	</p>
