<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+18:19">Matthew 18:19</span>. <strong> If two of you</strong>. ‘Two’ could still constitute a fellowship.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.18em;"><strong> Shall agree on earth</strong>. This agreement could only be wrought by the Holy Spirit, selfish ends being excluded from the nature of the case. An encouragement to united prayer.
	</p>
