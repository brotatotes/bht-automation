<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+18:2">Matthew 18:2</span>. <strong> A little child</strong>, probably a little boy. An untrustworthy tradition says it was the martyr Ignatius.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> Set him in the midst of them</strong>. He took the child in His arms (Mark). The whole transaction would of itself show the child’s ‘submission and trustfulness.’
