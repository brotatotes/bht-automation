<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+18:29">Matthew 18:29</span>. <strong> Fell down and besought him</strong>. As he had done his greater creditor.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.18em;"><strong> I will pay thee</strong>. The best authorities omit ‘all.’ This may hint that we are far more ready to promise God (<span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+18:26">Matthew 18:26</span>) than men, all we owe, though the first promise cannot be fulfilled.
	</p>
