<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+18:31">Matthew 18:31</span>. <strong> So when his fellow-servants, etc. </strong> Not a warrant for complaints to God against the unforgiving. The fellow-servants were exceeding sorry, not ‘angry;’ the sorrowful cries of God’s people in a world of persecution and oppression are heard.
	</p>
