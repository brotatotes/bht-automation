<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+18:9">Matthew 18:9</span>. <strong> The hell of fire</strong>. The only variation between this verse and the last and a suggestive one (comp. the more detailed form in <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+9:43-48">Mark 9:43-48</span>). Certain and awful future punishment is threatened in cases where some darling sin (or cause of sin) is preferred to Christ.
