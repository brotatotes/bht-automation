<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+19:10">Matthew 19:10</span>. <strong> If the case. </strong> The whole theory of marriage just announced is referred to. The low views then held may be inferred from what the disciples said: <strong> it is not good to marry</strong>; the ideal seemed so high, that its application seemed almost impossible.
