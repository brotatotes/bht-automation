<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+19:11">Matthew 19:11</span>. <strong> All men cannot receive, or, </strong> ‘not all can receive,’ <strong> this saying</strong>. This high ideal can be understood and put into practice only by those who get illumination and power from God. As a rule, the less Christianity, the lower the ideal of marriage, the more numerous the sins against this state.
	</p>
