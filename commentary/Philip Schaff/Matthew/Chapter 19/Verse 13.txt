<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+19:13">Matthew 19:13</span>. <strong> Then were brought unto him</strong>; probably by their parents. An encouragement to parents to bring even ‘infants’ to Christ, since, according to Luke, such were among the little children. Thus the doubts of the disciples about the marriage state were answered.
	</p>
<p><strong> Lay his</strong> <strong> hands on them</strong>. <strong> A </strong> recognition of <strong> </strong> Christ’s power to bless, since He healed by laying on His hands.
	</p>
<p><strong> And the disciples rebuked them</strong>. They were engaged in an interesting discussion about marriage, etc. Abstract theories about household relations should not stand between the Lord and little children.
	</p>
