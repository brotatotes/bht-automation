<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+19:2">Matthew 19:2</span>. <strong> Great multitudes</strong>. Comp. <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+10:1">Mark 10:1</span>: ‘And the people resort to him again, and, as he was wont, he taught them again.’ The harmonists insert here the record of Luke, <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+13:22">Luke 13:22</span> to <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+18:14">Luke 18:14</span>; consisting mainly of parables appropriate to the advanced stage of our Lord’s ministry. This assumes that He was already on the way toward Jerusalem, when the Pharisees came.
	</p>
