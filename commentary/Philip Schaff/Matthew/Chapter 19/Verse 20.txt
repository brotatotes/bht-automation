<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+19:20">Matthew 19:20</span>. <strong> All these have I kept</strong>. Externally moral, perhaps self-righteous, he yet felt that he lacked something. Peace of conscience had not been attained by his keeping of ‘all these.’ He had yet to learn how much he lacked of even comprehending the spirituality of the law.
	</p>
