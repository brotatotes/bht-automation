<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+19:8">Matthew 19:8</span>. <strong> Suffered you</strong>. The Mosaic regulations were merely permissive, growing out of their sinfulness, especially their disposition to be harsh toward their wives.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> But from the beginning it hath not been so</strong>. In the original state in Paradise. Polygamy appears first (<span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ge+4:19">Genesis 4:19</span>) in conjunction with murder, and in the line of Cain.
	</p>
