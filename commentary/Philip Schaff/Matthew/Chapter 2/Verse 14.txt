<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+2:14">Matthew 2:14</span>. <strong> And he arose. </strong> Implying immediate obedience; characteristic of genuine faith.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> By night, </strong> <em> i.e.</em>, the same night.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> Departed, </strong> or, ‘withdrew,’ the same word which was used respecting the Magi.</p>
