<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+2:7">Matthew 2:7</span>. <strong> Privately. </strong> This indicates his evil purpose, and is quite characteristic of political suspicion.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> Learned of</strong> <strong> them earned. </strong> He probably drew some inference from what they told him, and took measures accordingly.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> What time. </strong> This implies how long it had appeared, quite as much as, when it appeared.</p>
