<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+20:1">Matthew 20:1</span>. <strong> A man that is a householder.</strong> The ‘householder’ signifies God; the ‘vineyard’ the kingdom of heaven (comp. <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="isa+5:1-7">Isaiah 5:1-7</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="so+8:12">Song of Solomon 8:12</span> <em> );</em> the ‘steward’ (<span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+20:8">Matthew 20:8</span>) Chris; the ‘twelfth hour’ of the day, or the evening, the coming of Christ; the other ‘hours’ the different periods of calling into service.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.18em;"><strong> Labourers</strong>. Specially the Apostles, yet including all Christians.
	</p>
