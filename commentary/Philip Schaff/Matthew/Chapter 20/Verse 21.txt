<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+20:21">Matthew 20:21</span>. <strong> One on thy right hand, and one on</strong> <strong> thy left hand in thy kingdom</strong>. The highest places of honor, implying special authority also, as is indicated by the answer (<span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+20:25">Matthew 20:25</span>). The request was based upon ignorance (comp. <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+20:22">Matthew 20:22</span>), and prompted by ambition (comp. <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+20:25-27">Matthew 20:25-27</span>), however natural it may have been.
	</p>
