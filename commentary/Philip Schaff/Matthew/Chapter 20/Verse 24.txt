<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+20:24">Matthew 20:24</span>. <strong> The ten</strong>, including Matthew who writes the account. A proof of humility and truthfulness.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> They were sore displeased concerning</strong>. This displeasure was no more praiseworthy than the ambition of the two, and was speedily discountenanced (comp. <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+10:41-42">Mark 10:41-42</span>).
	</p>
