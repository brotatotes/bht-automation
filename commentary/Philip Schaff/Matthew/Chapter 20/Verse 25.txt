<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+20:25">Matthew 20:25</span>. <strong> The rulers of the Gentiles,</strong> <em> i.e. ‘</em> secular princes.’ The Jewish form of government, as ordained by God, was designed to exclude tyranny.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> Exercise lordship,</strong> lord it, <strong> over them,</strong> <em> i.e.,</em> exercise tyrannical and arbitrary power.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> Their great ones</strong>. Either conquerors and usurpers, or the officers of state.
	</p>
