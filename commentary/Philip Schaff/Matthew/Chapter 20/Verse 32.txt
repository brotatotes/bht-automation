<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+20:32">Matthew 20:32</span>. <strong> And Jesus stood still</strong>. He now allows Himself to be publicly called: ‘Son of David;’ comp. His previous conduct in a similar case (chap. <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+9:27-28">Matthew 9:27-28</span>). Mark adds that those about the blind man said: ‘Be of good courage, rise; He calleth thee,’ showing that they too responded to the Lord’s compassion.
	</p>
