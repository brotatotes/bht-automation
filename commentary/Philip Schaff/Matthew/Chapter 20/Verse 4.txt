<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+20:4">Matthew 20:4</span>. <strong> Whatsoever if right I will give you.</strong> The wages promised indefinite; the correct reading in <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+20:7">Matthew 20:7</span> omits all promise of reward. The parable illustrates the truth that salvation is of grace.
