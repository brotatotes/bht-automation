<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+21:11">Matthew 21:11</span>. <strong> The prophet Jesus from Nazareth of Galilee</strong>. The Galileans may have spoken of him with some pride as a well known prophet, but they do not now declare that He <strong> </strong> is the Messiah. The question ‘who is this?’ may have dampened their enthusiasm.
