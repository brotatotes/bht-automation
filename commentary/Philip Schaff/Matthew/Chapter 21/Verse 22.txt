<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+21:22">Matthew 21:22</span>. <strong> And all things</strong>, etc. Mark: ‘therefore,’ showing that the primary application, so far as miraculous power is concerned, was to the Twelve. As applied to all Christians, it is of course confined to prayers of faith (<span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+21:21-22">Matthew 21:21-22</span>), implying agreement with the will of God, and excluding the abuse of this promise. Christ defines believing and effective prayer to be prayer in His name (<span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="joh+14:13">John 14:13</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="joh+15:16">John 15:16</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="joh+16:24">John 16:24</span>).
	</p>
