<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+21:23">Matthew 21:23</span>. <strong> Into the temple</strong>, probably the ‘court of the Israelites.’
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> The chief priests and the</strong> <strong> alders of the people</strong>. Mark and Luke add: ‘the scribes.’ Perhaps a formal delegation from the Sanhedrin.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> By what authority</strong> <strong> doest thou these</strong> <strong> things</strong>! Referring both to His teaching there, and to His cleansing of the temple on the previous day. They were the proper persons to challenge His authority.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> And who gave thee</strong>, etc. ‘Even if you assume to be a prophet, who sent you?’ A hint at the old charge of Satanic power.
	</p>
