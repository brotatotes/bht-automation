<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+21:34">Matthew 21:34</span>. <strong> The season of the fruits</strong>. Probably no definite time is here represented. God expects fruit after such careful preparation; His people, especially those in official stations, are responsible for the trust committed to them.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> He sent his servants</strong>; the prophets of the Old Testament, calling for the fruits of righteousness from the Jewish people.
	</p>
