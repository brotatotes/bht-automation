<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+21:36">Matthew 21:36</span>. <strong> Again</strong>, etc. The second sending probably does not refer to any definite time, but sets forth God’s long-suffering. In Mark’s account the climax is the killing of a servant, here the stoning. The former respects the actual suffering of the servants, the latter the hostility of the husbandmen.
	</p>
