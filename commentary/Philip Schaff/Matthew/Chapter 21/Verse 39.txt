<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+21:39">Matthew 21:39</span>. <strong> Cast him forth out of the vineyard</strong>. This refers either to the excommunication which preceded death, or to the crucifixion outside the gates of Jerusalem; perhaps to both, the latter being a result of the former. Mark inverts the order.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> And slew him</strong>. Our Lord here recognizes the fixed purpose of the rulers to kill Him. Yet there is still love in the warning.
	</p>
