<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+21:45-46">Matthew 21:45-46</span>. They now perceived, if not before, that the parable referred to them; their determination to kill Him became fixed (see <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+12:12">Mark 12:12</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+20:19">Luke 20:19</span>). Avoiding open violence because the multitude <strong> held him for a prophet</strong>, they welcomed treachery and at last carried the multitude with them.
