<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+22:13">Matthew 22:13</span>. <strong> The attendants</strong>. A different word from that used before, referring not to the ‘servants’ who invited, nor to the guests, but probably to angels, as ministers of judgment.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> Bind</strong> <strong> him hand and foot</strong>. For secure transfer to his place of punishment. The best authorities omit, ‘and take him away.’
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> Outer darkness</strong>. See chap <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+8:12">Matthew 8:12</span>. There the fate of ‘the children of the kingdom ‘is referred to; here of a Gentile, who entered in, despising the King; their punishment is the same; their sin was the same, the sin of pride.
	</p>
