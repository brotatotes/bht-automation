<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+22:15">Matthew 22:15</span>. <strong> Then went the Pharisees</strong>. The main element, no doubt, in the deputation which had assailed Him.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> Ensnare him in speech.</strong> This mode of attack was adopted in view of the complete failure of the last attempt, and was the most artful of all.
	</p>
