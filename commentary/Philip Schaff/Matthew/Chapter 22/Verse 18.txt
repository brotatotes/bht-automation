<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+22:18">Matthew 22:18</span>. <strong> Their wickedness.</strong> As just explained.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> Hypocrites</strong>. They were such, both in their flattering address (<span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+22:16">Matthew 22:16</span>) and in their cunning question (<span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+22:17">Matthew 22:17</span>). Men may rightly carry their religious convictions into politics, and religious questions may become political ones; but when this is the case hypocrisy flourishes.
	</p>
