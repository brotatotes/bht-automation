<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+22:29">Matthew 22:29</span>. <strong> Ye do err.</strong> How, is immediately added.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> Not knowing the Scriptures</strong>. ‘In that ye do not understand the Scriptures,’ <em> i.e.,</em> the Old Testament, which they professed to hold free from tradition. That Scripture plainly implies the resurrection.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong><em> N</em></strong> <strong> or the power of God. </strong> His power to raise the dead. Modern Sadducism usually knows the meaning of the Scriptures, but denies ‘the power of God,’ in this as in many other things.
	</p>
