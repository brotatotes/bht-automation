<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+22:31">Matthew 22:31</span>. <strong> But touching the resurrection of the dead</strong>. Proof that the doctrine was implied in the writings of Moses. <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+20:37">Luke 20:37</span> is against the view that our Lord only makes an authoritative statement without really basing His proof on the passage quoted.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> Spoken unto you by God.</strong> Christ assumes the truth of the book of Exodus. The Sadducees are said to have doubted the authority of the prophetical books. The proof is drawn from the Pentateuch, which they acknowledged.
	</p>
