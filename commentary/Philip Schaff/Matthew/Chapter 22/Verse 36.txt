<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+22:36">Matthew 22:36</span>. <strong> What commandment is great in the law? </strong> <em> i.e.</em>, the Mosaic law. Not merely greater than the rest, but ‘great,’ as including the rest. Comp. <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+22:38">Matthew 22:38</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+22:40">Matthew 22:40</span>. If there was a reference to the disputes of the Rabbins about great and small commandments, the meaning would be: ‘What kind of a commandment is great in the law?’ but this sense, though literally correct, does not suit the answer so well.
	</p>
