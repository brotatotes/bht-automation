<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+22:41">Matthew 22:41</span>. <strong> Now while the Pharisees were gathered together.</strong> Probably as they gathered after the last attack.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> Jesus asked them.</strong> Fuller and more exact than Mark and Luke, who seem to imply that the question was put concerning the scribes. This probably took place while His audience was changing: the Pharisees were about to withdraw, no longer daring to question Him; and ‘the multitude’ (<span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+12:37">Mark 12:37</span>) beginning to take the vacated places. Comp. chap. 23 which was addressed ‘to the multitudes’ and ‘to His disciples’ (<span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+22:1">Matthew 22:1</span>).
	</p>
