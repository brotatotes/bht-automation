<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+22:6">Matthew 22:6</span>. <strong> But the rest</strong>. Representing the fanatical rulers of the Jews, the Pharisees.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> Treated</strong> <strong> them shamefully and slew them</strong>. Literally fulfilled, in case of the Apostles and Evangelists. Indifference often passes into hostility, as the more consistent attitude.
	</p>
