<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+22:41-46">Matthew 22:41-46</span>. THE FINAL ENCOUNTER <em> , </em> in which our Lord by His question respecting the Messiah, puts an end to further attempts to ‘ensnare Him by a word.’ Mark and Luke say: ‘No man after that’ ( <em> i.e., </em> the encounter of <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+22:34-40">Matthew 22:34-40</span>) ‘durst ask Him any question,’ while Matthew, in accordance with his rubrical habits, reserves this remark until after this encounter.
