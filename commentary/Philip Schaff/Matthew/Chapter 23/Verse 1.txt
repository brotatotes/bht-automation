<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+23:1">Matthew 23:1</span>. <strong> To the multitudes, and to his disciples.</strong> Luke (<span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+20:45">Luke 20:45</span>): ‘then in the audience of all the people, he said to his disciples.’ His disciples were probably close about Him, the people gathering about them; <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+23:8-12">Matthew 23:8-12</span> appear to be addressed especially to His disciples.
