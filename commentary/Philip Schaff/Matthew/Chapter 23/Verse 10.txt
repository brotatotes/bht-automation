<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+23:10">Matthew 23:10</span>. <strong> Leaders.</strong> Higher than ‘Rabbi,’ leaders of sects, etc.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> For one is your leader, even the Christ.</strong> Hence the disciples were and ought to be called Christians, not by any human name (comp. <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="1co+1:12">1 Corinthians 1:12</span>). As <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+23:9-10">Matthew 23:9-10</span> refer distinctly to the Father and the Son, some have referred <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+23:8">Matthew 23:8</span> to the Holy Ghost; in order to find here a hint of the Trinity. A possible, but improbable, interpretation.
	</p>
