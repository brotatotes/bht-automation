<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+23:2">Matthew 23:2</span>. <strong> The scribes and the Pharisees</strong>. Joined together, because the scribes were mostly Pharisees. Study of the Scriptures would be of comparatively little interest to the indifferent Sadducees. Theologians, from the nature of their pursuits, are in more danger of becoming Pharisees than Sadducees.
	</p>
