<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+23:22">Matthew 23:22</span>. <strong> By heaven</strong>, the great temple of God, hallowed by the presence of God enthroned there. The sum of the whole is: Every oath is by God; hence make no distinctions between oaths; ‘swear not at all’ (chap. <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+5:34">Matthew 5:34</span>). These verses really refer, not only to swearing, but to truthfulness, in word and act; they forbid those false distinctions used to palliate the crime of lying.
	</p>
