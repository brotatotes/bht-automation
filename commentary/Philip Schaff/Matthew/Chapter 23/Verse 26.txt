<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+23:26">Matthew 23:26</span>. <strong> Thou blind Pharisee.</strong> ‘Blind,’ failing to see that the great matter should come first.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> Cleanse first.</strong> Begin with inward purity.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> That the outside thereof may become clean also. </strong> Outward morality is very important, but it naturally follows purity of heart. The former without the latter is not real morality.
	</p>
