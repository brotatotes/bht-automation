<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+23:33">Matthew 23:33</span>. <strong> Ye serpents, ye brood of vipers,</strong> etc. Comp. the similar language of John the Baptist (chap. <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+3:7">Matthew 3:7</span>). That was the first, and this the last recorded address to the unchanged Pharisees. John had said: ‘who hath warned you to flee from the wrath to come,’ our Lord speaks to them, as obdurate: <strong> how shall ye escape the judgment of hell</strong>, <em> i.e., </em> the judgment which condemns to hell, Our Lord speaks as Judge.
	</p>
