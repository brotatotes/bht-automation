<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+23:39">Matthew 23:39</span>. <strong> Ye shall not see me henceforth. </strong> A solemn declaration of His withdrawal from His ministry among them. After this He taught only His own people.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> Till ye shall say,</strong> etc. This refers to the future conversion of the Jews (comp. <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ro+11:25-32">Romans 11:25-32</span>.)
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> Blessed is he that cometh, </strong> etc. Our Lord had been thus greeted by His followers as He entered the city (chap. <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+21:9">Matthew 21:9</span>), but Jerusalem said: ‘Who is this.’ The heavy judgments would inevitably come, but hope still remains.
	</p>
