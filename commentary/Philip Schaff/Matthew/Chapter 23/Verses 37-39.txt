<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+23:37-39">Matthew 23:37-39</span>. Luke (<span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+13:34-35">Luke 13:34-35</span>) inserts this lamentation at an earlier point of the history. It was probably uttered twice, if but once, on this occasion, when it was peculiarly fitting. Comp. also <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+19:41-44">Luke 19:41-44</span>, where we find another lamentation over the city on His triumphant progress towards it.
