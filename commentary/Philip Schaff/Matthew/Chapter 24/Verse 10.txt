<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+24:10">Matthew 24:10</span>. <strong> Then shall many be offended</strong>, or ‘fall away.’ The Apostles understood this of the first century; see the repeated warnings against apostasy in the Epistles. The fulfilment will culminate in the last days.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> Deliver up one another</strong>, <em> i.e.</em>, to tribunals, to heathen magistrates, as was the case in Apostolic times. A natural development of apostasy, then, and to be repeated before ‘the end’ comes.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> Hate one another.</strong> Whenever apostasy occurs, this recurs, since this is the opposite of Christian love. The Great Apostasy (<span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="2th+2:3">2 Thessalonians 2:3</span>) will thus manifest itself.
	</p>
