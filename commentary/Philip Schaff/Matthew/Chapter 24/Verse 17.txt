<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+24:17">Matthew 24:17</span>. <strong> On the house-top.</strong> The flat roofs of eastern dwellings were a favorite place of resort.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> Not go down.</strong> Some suppose this is a command to flee along the house-tops or to go down by the outer stairs as a quicker way. What is distinctly forbidden is to go down <strong> to take the things out of his house.</strong> Extreme haste is enjoined <strong> ;</strong> and being hindered by motives of selfishness or convenience is prohibited. There is probably an allusion to the flight of Lot from Sodom (comp. <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+17:32">Luke 17:32</span>).
	</p>
