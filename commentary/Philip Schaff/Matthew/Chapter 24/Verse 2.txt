<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+24:2">Matthew 24:2</span>. <strong> All these things?</strong> <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+13:2">Mark 13:2</span>: ‘these great buildings.’
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.18em;"><strong> Verily I say unto you,</strong> etc. This prophecy was uttered in a time of profound peace, when the possibility of the destruction of such a magnificent work of art and sanctuary of religion seemed very unlikely; but was literally fulfilled forty years afterwards; and that, too, in express violation of the orders of Titus, who wished to save it.
	</p>
