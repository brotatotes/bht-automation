<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+24:24">Matthew 24:24</span>. <strong> False Christs</strong>. While this may refer to the impostors of the first century, it now points to ‘Antichrist,’ or the many ‘antichrists’ (<span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="1jo+2:18">1 John 2:18</span>), constantly arising. False prophets. Such arose among the Jews, but have arisen ever since.
	</p>
<p><strong> Show great signs and wonders</strong>, in appearance probably, but this cannot be insisted upon. See <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="2th+2:9-12">2 Thessalonians 2:9-12</span>.
	</p>
<p><strong> So as</strong> (the tendency and purpose) <strong> to deceive, if possible</strong>, implying that it is not, <strong> even the elect</strong>. Others will be deceived, led astray from our Lord, the real Messiah and true Prophet. It indicates that a period will come, when the <strong> ‘</strong> deceivableness of unrighteousness’ shall be augmented.
	</p>
