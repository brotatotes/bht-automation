<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+24:33">Matthew 24:33</span>. <strong> So ye also</strong>. Addressed to the disciples, as representing all Christians. It does not mean that they should live to see what He had predicted <strong> ;</strong> two of the four certainly died even before the destruction of Jerusalem.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> All these</strong> <strong> things</strong>, <em> i.e.</em>, the signs mentioned, culminating in these predicted in <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+24:30">Matthew 24:30</span>.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> Know that he is</strong> <strong> nigh</strong>. Christ Himself, since they had asked of His coming (<span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+24:3">Matthew 24:3</span>).
	</p>
