<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+24:42">Matthew 24:42</span>. <strong> Watch therefore</strong>. In view of the suddenness and unexpectedness of this coming, ‘watch.’ Mark: ‘watch and pray.’ Not, be always expecting what will come unexpectedly, nor be seeking to know what cannot be known, but be always in the state of readiness, because of the uncertainty.
	</p>
