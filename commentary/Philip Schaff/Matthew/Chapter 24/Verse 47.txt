<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+24:47">Matthew 24:47</span>. <strong> He will set him over all his goods</strong>. The servant, faithful up to the unexpected arrival of his lord, is rewarded, and is called ‘blessed’ (<span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+24:47">Matthew 24:47</span>). The reward is promotion to be possessor of the full inheritance. Comp. <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ro+8:17">Romans 8:17</span>; also chap. <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+25:21">Matthew 25:21</span> <strong> ;</strong> <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="re+2:26">Revelation 2:26</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="re+3:21">Revelation 3:21</span>. Alford <strong> :</strong> ‘ <em> Each</em> faithful servant shall be over <em> all </em> his master’s goods. That promotion shall not be like earthly promotion, wherein the eminence of one excludes that of another, but rather like the diffusion of love, in which, the more each has, the more there is for all.’
	</p>
