<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+24:15-22">Matthew 24:15-22</span>. These verses certainly refer to the destruction of Jerusalem. Another fulfilment is probable, in accordance with the parallel lines of prophecy we have traced in the preceding section (<span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+24:5-14">Matthew 24:5-14</span>). But precisely because the details are so minute, we must be cautious in applying it to the final catastrophe.
	</p>
