<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+25:10">Matthew 25:10</span>. <strong> And while they went away to buy.</strong> They kept up their endeavor to the very last (see <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+25:11">Matthew 25:11</span>), but probably did not get a supply of oil at that late hour.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> They that were ready</strong> ( <em> i.e., </em> the wise virgins) <strong> went in with him to the marriage</strong> <strong> feast;</strong> comp. <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="re+19:7-9">Revelation 19:7-9</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="re+21:2">Revelation 21:2</span>.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong><em> And</em></strong> <strong> the door was shut.</strong> No more entrance to the feast. The case of those without ( <em> ‘</em> outer darkness;’ comp. chap. <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+8:12">Matthew 8:12</span>) was finally decided.
	</p>
