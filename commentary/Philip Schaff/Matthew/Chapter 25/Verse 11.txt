<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+25:11">Matthew 25:11</span>. <strong> Afterward come also the other virgins.</strong> The story is carried to its conclusion; the foolish virgins did not lack persistent effort. We may understand the verse as a mere carrying out of the story, or as showing the persistent appeals of the self-deceived, without regard to time. Comp. chap. <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+7:22">Matthew 7:22</span>. The more literal application is given below.
