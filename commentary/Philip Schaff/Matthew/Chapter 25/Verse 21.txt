<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+25:21">Matthew 25:21</span>. <strong> I will set thee over many things</strong>. In the kingdom of glory; or on the other theory, during the millenium.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> Into the joy of thy Lord.</strong> In Luke the official position is recognized in the rule over ‘ten cities,’ etc.; here the reward has a reference to the personal spiritual life. ‘The joy;’ the blessed inheritance which Christ’s servants will have with Him. The reference to a ‘feast’ seems unnecessary.
	</p>
