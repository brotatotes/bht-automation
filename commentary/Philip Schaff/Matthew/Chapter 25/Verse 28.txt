<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+25:28">Matthew 25:28</span>. <strong> Take ye away therefore the talent from him.</strong> This command will be given, whether the latter be a spiritual or temporal gift.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.18em;"><strong> Give it unto him that hath the ten talents.</strong> Comp. <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+19:25">Luke 19:25</span>, where this command is questioned. This act of judgment on the slothful servant becomes an act of mercy to the faithful one.
	</p>
