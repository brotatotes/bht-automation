<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+25:44">Matthew 25:44</span>. <strong> When saw we thee,</strong> etc. A self <strong> -</strong> righteous plea of ignorance, implying that they would have done such good works, had they seen Him. The answer of the Lord in <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+25:45">Matthew 25:45</span>, repeats the principle of <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+25:40">Matthew 25:40</span>. Many fancy they would do good to Christ, who fail to see Him in the person of His followers; and the deceitful fancy often continues until the day of retribution.
	</p>
