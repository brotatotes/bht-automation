<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+25:6">Matthew 25:6</span>. <strong> But at midnight</strong>. At a late, dark season, the most unsuitable too for the foolish virgins to make good their lack.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> A cry is made</strong>, <strong> Behold the bridegroom</strong>! This was usual. A sign of the coming of Christ (chap. <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+24:30">Matthew 24:30</span>). For the individual, that cry may come at any time.
	</p>
