<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+25:7">Matthew 25:7</span>. <strong> Arose, and trimmed their lamps</strong>, <em> i.e.</em>, trimmed the wick and put on fresh oil, so as to make a brilliant flame. ‘All’ did this; the foolish virgins were not lacking in effort. But mere trimming does little good, if there is no oil.
	</p>
