<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+26:20">Matthew 26:20</span>. <strong> Even.</strong> Luke: ‘the hour.’ Both point to the regular time.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> He was sitting at meat, </strong> or, ‘reclining at table.’ The original requirement was, to eat the Passover standing (<span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ex+12:11">Exodus 12:11</span>). The Jews altered this when they came to the land of promise and rest.
	</p>
