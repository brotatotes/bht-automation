<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+26:23">Matthew 26:23</span>. <strong> He that hath dipped the hand with me in the dish.</strong> One near Him. There were probably a number of dishes, or bowls, distributed along the table, containing the broth called <em> charoseth</em>, prepared of dates, figs, etc., which was used at the Supper, representing, it is said, the Egyptian bricks or clay. Even this statement may not have definitely pointed out Judas to the others. There is a pathetic tenderness in the language (comp. <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ps+41:9">Psalms 41:9</span>, quoted in <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="joh+13:18">John 13:18</span>).
