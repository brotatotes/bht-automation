<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+26:30">Matthew 26:30</span>. <strong> And when they had sung a hymn </strong> (Psalms 115-118.) <strong> , they went out unto the mount of Olives, </strong> to Gethsemane (<span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+26:36">Matthew 26:36</span>). Between the hymn and the going out we must insert the discourse and prayer of John 14-17. The place of eating the Passover was probably kept concealed, to give time for that closing interview, appropriately called, ‘the Holy of Holies.’
	</p>
