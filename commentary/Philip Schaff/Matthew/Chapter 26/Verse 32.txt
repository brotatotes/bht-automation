<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+26:32">Matthew 26:32</span>. <strong> But after I am raised up. </strong> The resurrection is again announced.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> I will go before you.</strong> The figure of a shepherd is continued. Comp, the remaining words of <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="zec+13:7">Zechariah 13:7</span>: ‘And I will turn my hand upon the little ones.’
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> Into Galilee.</strong> In Galilee He collected His disciples: chap. <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+28:16">Matthew 28:16</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="joh+21:0">John 21:0</span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="1co+15:6">1 Corinthians 15:6</span>. This gathering was the pastoral work after the resurrection, hence the other interviews in Jerusalem are not referred to.
	</p>
