<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+26:43">Matthew 26:43</span>. <strong> For their eyes were heavy. </strong> Drowsiness, not deep sleep, is meant; Mark adds (<span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+14:40">Mark 14:40</span>): ‘and they knew not what they should answer Him.’
