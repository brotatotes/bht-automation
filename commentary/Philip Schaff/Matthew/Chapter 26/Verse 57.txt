<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+26:57">Matthew 26:57</span>. <strong> To Caiaphas the high priest.</strong> Appointed by the Romans, Annas having been deposed, as frequently occurred (comp. <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+26:8">Matthew 26:8</span>).
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> Where the scribes and elders were gathered together. </strong> Mark inserts ‘the chief-priests,’ indicating a meeting of the Sanhedrin or council (<span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+26:59">Matthew 26:59</span>). The examination before Annas would allow time for them to come together. But it was not the final assemblage of that body (see chap. <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+27:1-2">Matthew 27:1-2</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+22:66-71">Luke 22:66-71</span>).
	</p>
