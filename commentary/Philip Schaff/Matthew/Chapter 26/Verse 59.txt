<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+26:59">Matthew 26:59</span>. <strong> The whole council.</strong> The Sanhedrin, Joseph of Arimathea and Nicodemus were probably absent (<span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+23:51">Luke 23:51</span>), since their opposition would have been in vain (comp. <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="joh+7:50">John 7:50</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="joh+9:22">John 9:22</span>). It was not the first time this body had consulted against Him. See <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="joh+7:45-53">John 7:45-53</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="joh+9:22">John 9:22</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="joh+11:57">John 11:57</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="joh+12:10">John 12:10</span>.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> Sought false witness.</strong> Knowing that true witness could not be had, they actually sought ‘false witness.’ Such a sin is greatest in judges.
