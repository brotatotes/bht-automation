<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+26:60">Matthew 26:60</span>. <strong> And found it not, </strong> <em> i.e.,</em> to answer their purpose.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> Many false witnesses came,</strong> as was natural; but two witnesses to one specific point were required (<span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="nu+35:30">Numbers 35:30</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="de+17:6">Deuteronomy 17:6</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="de+19:15">Deuteronomy 19:15</span>).
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> Afterward;</strong> after numerous vain attempts to find two, even apparently concordant, witnesses.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> Two. </strong> The smallest number requisite.
