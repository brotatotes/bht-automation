<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+26:72">Matthew 26:72</span>. <strong> Denied with an oath.</strong> The oath is mentioned by Matthew alone, and was uttered to the maid in the porch.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> I know not the man. </strong> From evasion to perjury, one sin leading to another. The expression is even somewhat contemptuous; Peter was now ‘a stone of stumbling,’ not a ‘rock.’
	</p>
