<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+27:13">Matthew 27:13</span>. <strong> How many things! </strong> Comp. <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+22:5">Luke 22:5</span>, as a specimen of the testimony, or accusations, they brought. The main charge was true in form, but false in fact: His claim to be a king was not a political offence. So as to the evidence: He had stirred up the people, etc., but not to mutiny or for political purposes. Honest advocates at the bar should avoid the tricks of these murderers of Christ.
	</p>
