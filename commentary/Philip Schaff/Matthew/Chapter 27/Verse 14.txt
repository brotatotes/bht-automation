<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+27:14">Matthew 27:14</span>. <strong> And he gave him no answer, </strong> not even to one word. This is the emphatic force of the original.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> Marvelled greatly. </strong> The silence of our Lord continued until just before the final decision (see <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="joh+19:10-11">John 19:10-11</span>). Those accused are not often silent, and Pilate had probably found the Jews tried at his bar especially vehement.
	</p>
