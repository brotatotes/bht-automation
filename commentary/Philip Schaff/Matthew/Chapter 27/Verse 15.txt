<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+27:15">Matthew 27:15</span>. <strong> Now at the feast, </strong> or ‘a feast’ Annually at the Passover.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> Was wont. </strong> Expressly mentioned by three Evangelists. When the custom arose is unknown, but it was undoubtedly designed to soften the Roman yoke. A turbulent people always sympathizes with criminals condemned by hated rulers. That they could choose the prisoner was a prominent feature.
	</p>
