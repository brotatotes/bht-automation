<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+27:18">Matthew 27:18</span>. <strong> For envy,</strong> of His popularity. This implies that Pilate knew something of Jesus before; but it shows his injustice, in not protecting Him as innocent. Still Pilate, while not wishing to directly oppose the rulers, really desired to thwart them.
