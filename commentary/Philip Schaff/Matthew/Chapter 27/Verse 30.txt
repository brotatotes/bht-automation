<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+27:30">Matthew 27:30</span>. <strong> And they spat upon him.</strong> The sport of wicked men wounds; if they are rough, it becomes brutality. Yet the Jews had done this (chap. <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+26:67">Matthew 26:67</span>); Herod had taught these rude soldiers how to mock, and Pilate invited them to do it.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> The reed.</strong> The mock sceptre. There was an alternation of mocking homage and cruel treatment.
