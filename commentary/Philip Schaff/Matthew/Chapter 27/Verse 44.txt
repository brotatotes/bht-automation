<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+27:44">Matthew 27:44</span>. <strong> The robbers also east the same in his teeth,</strong> or ‘cast on him the same reproaches.’ Luke alone tells of the penitence of one (see <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+23:30-43">Luke 23:30-43</span>). Both probably at first reproach Him, out one was afterwards converted, during the three hours they hung side by side. It is not satisfactory to refer ‘the robbers ‘to but one. At this point occurred the touching incident recorded in <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="joh+19:26-27">John 19:26-27</span>.
	</p>
