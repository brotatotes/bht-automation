<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+27:49">Matthew 27:49</span>. <strong> And the rest said, wait,</strong> etc. According to Mark, the man himself says this; giving Him the vinegar, in mingled pity and contempt, he probably responded in the same mocking tone to the jest of the others. The latter say, Wait, do not thus sustain Him; for He is expecting Elijah to help Him, the one who offers it responds: This will sustain Him until Elijah comes.
	</p>
