<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+27:58">Matthew 27:58</span>. <strong> This man went to Pilate.</strong> To the palace. ‘He went in boldly’ (<span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+15:43">Mark 15:43</span>). Although it was the Jewish custom to bury the bodies of the crucified before sunset, Pilate’s consent was necessary. On Pilate’s surprise, see <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+15:44">Mark 15:44</span>. </p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> Then Pilate commanded.</strong> The ready consent may have been owing to the station and character of Joseph.
	</p>
