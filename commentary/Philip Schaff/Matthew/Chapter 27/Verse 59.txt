<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+27:59">Matthew 27:59</span>. <strong> And Joseph</strong> <em> </em> <strong> took it.</strong> The body was taken down by Joseph (Mark, Luke, and John).
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> Wrapped it in a clean linen</strong> <em> </em> <strong> cloth.</strong> A winding sheet. This would enclose the spices used in the temporary embalming, which now took place, Nicodemus having brought the spices (<span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="joh+19:39-40">John 19:39-40</span>). There was not time enough to embalm on Friday evening, so the costly gifts of Nicodemus were used to preserve the body, the women preparing in the interval what they thought necessary for the further anointing.
	</p>
