<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+27:6">Matthew 27:6</span>. <strong> It is not lawful,</strong> etc. Based upon <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="de+23:18">Deuteronomy 23:18</span>. What was put in the treasury was deemed an offering to God.
	</p>
<p><strong> Since it is the price of blood. </strong> They thus stigmatized the crime of their tool, but not their own. Too conscientious to defile the treasury, they were not afraid to defile their own hands. A characteristically Pharisaical scruple.
	</p>
