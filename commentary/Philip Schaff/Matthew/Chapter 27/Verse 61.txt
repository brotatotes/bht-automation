<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> The other Mary.</strong> ‘The mother of Joses’ (<span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+15:47">Mark 15:47</span> <em> ), </em> already mentioned in <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+27:56">Matthew 27:56</span>. According to Luke the female disciples from Galilee all beheld the sepulchre, and returned to prepare spices and ointments, resting on the Sabbath, which began that evening. These two not only saw where He was laid (Mark) but lingered there, <em> sitting </em> over <strong> </strong> <em> against the sepulchre,</em> as the evening came on.
