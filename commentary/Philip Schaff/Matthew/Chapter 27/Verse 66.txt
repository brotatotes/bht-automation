<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+27:66">Matthew 27:66</span>. <strong> Sealing the stone.</strong> A string was stretched across the stone, and sealed to the rock at either end, with wax or sealing-clay. After these precautions, the body could not disappear, except through the miracle of the resurrection.
	</p>
