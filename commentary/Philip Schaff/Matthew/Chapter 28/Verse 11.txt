<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+28:11">Matthew 28:11</span>. <strong> While they were going.</strong> The fact that the soldiers did not go first, indicates that all the occurrences at the sepulchre occupied but a short time.
	</p>
<p><strong> Some of the guard</strong>, etc. They told the truth, possibly hoping for a bribe.
	</p>
<p><strong> Chief priests</strong>, under whose directions they had been, and to whom they ought to report.
	</p>
