<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+28:12">Matthew 28:12</span>. Taken counsel. This was a meeting of the Sanhedrin. Whether public or secret, regular or specially called to meet this emergency, is unknown.
	</p>
<p><strong> They</strong>, <em> i.e., </em> the chief priests and elders.
	</p>
<p><strong> Gave large money</strong>. More than they gave Judas. This is the lowest depth of their malice; and a humiliating position.
	</p>
