<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+28:4">Matthew 28:4</span>. <strong> For fear of him</strong> <em> ,</em> <em> i.e., </em> of the angel. As the angel was there when the women came, and the guard did not go back to the city until after the women departed (<span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+28:11">Matthew 28:11</span>), it seems most probable that all these occurrences took place, just as the women came.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> The watchers did quake</strong> <em> .</em> In their terror they did not prevent the women from going into the sepulchre.
	</p>
