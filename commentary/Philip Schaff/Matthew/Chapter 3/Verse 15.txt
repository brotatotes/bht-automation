<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+3:15">Matthew 3:15</span>. <strong> Suffer it now. </strong> The propriety of John’s scruples is recognized; but he was ‘now’ <em> </em> or ‘as yet’ the minister of the law, which Jesus must fulfil. The relation between them would soon be changed.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> It becometh us. </strong> Both John in his office and Jesus in His.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> Righteousnes. </strong> The requirements of the law, regarded as including all that is right.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> Suffereth him. </strong> More than ‘he baptized him’; Jesus was really the active person, since the rite was administered at His command and by His authority.</p>
