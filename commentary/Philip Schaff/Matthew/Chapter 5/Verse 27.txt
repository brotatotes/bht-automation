<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+5:27">Matthew 5:27</span>. The seventh commandment (<span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ex+20:14">Exodus 20:14</span>) is now cited, with an implied reference to the interpretation given by the scribes, namely, that adultery alone was forbidden.</p>
