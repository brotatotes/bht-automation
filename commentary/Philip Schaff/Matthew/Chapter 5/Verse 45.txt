<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+5:45">Matthew 5:45</span>. <strong> That ye may be</strong> <strong><em> . </em></strong> Such action proves, not makes, the sonship. So doing we show our resemblance to God our Father (a relation springing from our relation to Christ) who <strong> maketh his sun, </strong> etc <strong> .</strong>, whose love of benevolence is universal and not measured by the desert of the persons on whom He showers His providential favors. Christ here teaches the power and providence of God in nature, as well as His character of love.</p>
