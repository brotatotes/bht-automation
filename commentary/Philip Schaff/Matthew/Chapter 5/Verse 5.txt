<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+5:5">Matthew 5:5</span>. <strong> The meek; </strong> the mild, the gentle, opposed to the ambitious, who succeed in such a kingdom as the Jews were looking for. A higher quality than the preceding.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> Inherit the earth, </strong> or ‘the land,’ <em> i.e.</em>, of Canaan, the type of all blessings, not merely of spiritual ones. The literal fulfilment is not infrequent, but the primary reference is to the Messiah’s kingdom.</p>
