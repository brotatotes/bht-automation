<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+5:3-11">Matthew 5:3-11</span>. The beatitudes constitute an ascending series. The same thoughts are found in the Old Testament, but only since Christ has been found there.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> Blessed. </strong> The word, first applied to God, means more than ‘happy.’ Happiness may come from earthly things; blessedness comes from God. It is not bestowed arbitrarily; a reason follows each beatitude.</p>
