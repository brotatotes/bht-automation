<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+7:26">Matthew 7:26</span>. <strong> Doeth them not.</strong> Life is the test, not <em> knowledge</em>, or profession, which may be included here under the word ‘heareth.’
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> Foolish,</strong> <em> i.e., </em> senseless, singularly imprudent
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> The sand. </strong> The transitory teachings and works of man. For moral results, science itself is shifting sand compared to the Rock, Christ.
	</p>
