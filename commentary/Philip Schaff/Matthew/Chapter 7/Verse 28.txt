<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+7:28">Matthew 7:28</span>. <strong> And it came to pass when,</strong> etc. A summary of our Lord’s sayings would not be thus referred to.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> The</strong> <strong> multitudes,</strong> as in <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+7:1">Matthew 7:1</span>. They must have heard Him.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> Were astonished.</strong> A strong word; ‘driven from their customary state of mind by something new and strange.’
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> Teaching,</strong> rather than ‘doctrine;’ the former includes the manner as well as the matter of His instruction, both of which awakened astonishment.
	</p>
