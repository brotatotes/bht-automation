<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+7:8">Matthew 7:8</span>. <strong> For every one that asketh,</strong> etc. An invariable rule; a plain promise, not for the future, but for the present, since our Lord says: <strong> receiveth, findeth, it is opened. </strong> This promise, several times repeated by our Lord, is limited only by the verses which follow; comp., however, <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="jas+4:3">James 4:3</span>, ‘Ye ask and receive not; because ye ask amiss.’ God always answers the right kind of prayer, but in His own right way.
	</p>
