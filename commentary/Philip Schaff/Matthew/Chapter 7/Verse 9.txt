<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+7:9">Matthew 7:9</span>. Or, to view the matter in another light, comparing God’s willingness with that of a human father.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> What man is there of you, more exactly,</strong> ‘who is there among you, a man,’ a mere man.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> Of whom, </strong> etc. In the Greek there are two questions, one broken off: ‘Whom his son shall ask for bread (and who shall no), he will not <strong> give him a stone. </strong> The loaves or cakes, used in the East, resembled somewhat a smooth, flat stone. A deceptive answer is meant.
	</p>
