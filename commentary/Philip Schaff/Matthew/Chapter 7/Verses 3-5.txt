<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+7:3-5">Matthew 7:3-5</span>. A figurative application of the principle just laid down, showing the folly of sinners being censorious, their incapacity for forming a right judgment of others, hinting at the proportionate magnitude which our own faults and those of others should hold in our estimation.
	</p>
