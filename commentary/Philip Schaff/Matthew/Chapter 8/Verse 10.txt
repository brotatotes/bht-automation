<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+8:10">Matthew 8:10</span>. <strong> He marvelled.</strong> Not to be explained away. Our Lord could marvel; a mystery of His humanity.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> To them that followed.</strong> A multitude was probably near, all Jews.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> With no man in Israel have I found so great faith. </strong> This is the sense of the correct reading, which however places last, for emphasis, the phrase, ‘in Israel.’ There greater faith might have been looked for, but a Gentile was the first to acknowledge Christ’s power to heal at a distance.
	</p>
