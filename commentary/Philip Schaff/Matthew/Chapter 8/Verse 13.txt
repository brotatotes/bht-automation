<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+8:13">Matthew 8:13</span>. <strong> As thou hast believed, </strong> etc. The faith of the master resulted in the healing of the servant.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> In that hour,</strong> at once, at the moment. The same kind of faith was exercised by the Syro-Phenician woman; also a heathen (<span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+15:21-28">Matthew 15:21-28</span>). The three believing centurions of the N. T: this one, the one by the cross, and Cornelius.
	</p>
