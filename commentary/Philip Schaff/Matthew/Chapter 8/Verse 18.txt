<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+8:18">Matthew 8:18</span>. <strong> Now when Jesus saw great multitudes.</strong> Some very ancient authorities omit ‘great,’ but it is better to retain it. The ‘multitudes’ had listened to the discourse in parables (chap. 13 ).
	</p>
<p><strong> He gave commandment to depart.</strong> To avoid the crowd, who may have been in an excited condition, and to find repose after a day of conflict and labor (comp, chaps, 12 , 13 ); since this took place in the evening (<span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+4:35">Mark 4:35</span>).
	</p>
<p><strong> To the other side,</strong> of the lake.
	</p>
