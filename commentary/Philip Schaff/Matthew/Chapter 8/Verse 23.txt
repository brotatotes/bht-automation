<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+8:23">Matthew 8:23</span>. <strong> A boat. </strong> The best authorities omit the definite article. It was, however, the boat from which he had been teaching (<span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+4:36">Mark 4:36</span>).
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.18em;"><strong> His disciples,</strong> probably the Twelve, though others followed in other boats (<span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mr+4:36">Mark 4:36</span>).
	</p>
