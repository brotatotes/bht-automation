<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+8:30">Matthew 8:30</span>. <strong> A good way off.</strong> Mark says: ‘Nigh unto the mountains’; Luke: there on the mountain.’ The miracle probably took place on the plain.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> A herd of many swine, </strong> according to Mark, ‘two thousand.’
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> Feeding,</strong> under the care of herdsmen (<span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+8:33">Matthew 8:33</span>). They were the property either of Gentiles or of Jews, engaged in a traffic, which was unclean, according to the Mosaic law.
	</p>
