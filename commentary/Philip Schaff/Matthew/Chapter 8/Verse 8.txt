<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+8:8">Matthew 8:8</span>. <strong> The centurion answered,</strong> through friends (<span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+7:6">Luke 7:6</span>).
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> Lord, I am not worthy, </strong> etc. This humility sprang out of his consciousness that he was a heathen, as well as his esteem of our Lord.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> But only say in a word. </strong> This means one word of command, as <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+8:9">Matthew 8:9</span> shows.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> And my servant shall be healed. </strong> ‘Humility and faith always go hand in hand.’
	</p>
