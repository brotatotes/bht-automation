<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+9:22">Matthew 9:22</span>. Comp, the fuller accounts of Mark and Luke. She was healed at once; our Lord asked, ‘Who touched me?’ and thus constrained her to make public confession, sealed and strengthened her faith, presenting her to the world as healed and clean.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> Daughter, be of good cheer; thy faith hath made thee whole.</strong> Comp. <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+9:2">Matthew 9:2</span>. Her faith is extolled, though so different from that of the paralytic.
	</p>
