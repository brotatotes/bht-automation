<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+9:30">Matthew 9:30</span>. <strong> And their eyes were opened</strong>. A figurative but natural expression for restoration to sight.
	</p>
<p><strong> And Jesus solemnly charged them</strong>, almost equivalent to ‘sternly threatened them.’ These men had already shouted their belief in His Messiahship, in the public street, and their over-ready zeal might provoke over-ready opposition.
	</p>
