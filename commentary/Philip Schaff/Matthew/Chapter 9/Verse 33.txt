<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+9:33">Matthew 9:33</span>. <strong> And when the demon was cast out</strong>, or, ‘the demon having been cast out,’ as a result, <strong> the dumb man spake, and the multitudes marvelled</strong>. The crowds collected on this eventful day had not yet dispersed.
	</p>
<p><strong> It was never so seen</strong>, lit, ‘Never did it thus appear,’ <strong> in Israel</strong>. The double cure was remarkable. Some translate, ‘did he appear,’ referring it to the manifestation of Messianic power. There may be a secondary reference of this character expressed indefinitely through fear of the Pharisees.
	</p>
