<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+9:37">Matthew 9:37</span>. <strong> His disciples. </strong> Probably including more than the twelve.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> The harvest, </strong> etc. The <strong> </strong> people were ready to hear; but could not, if more did not enter into the work. As yet, He was the only laborer. Our weak faith denies the harvest as much as it diminishes the number of laborers.
	</p>
