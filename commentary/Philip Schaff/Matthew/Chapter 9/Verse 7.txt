<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="mt+9:7">Matthew 9:7</span>. <strong> And he arose, and departed to his house. </strong> The test was successfully applied. The intervening moment must have been one of suspense to all, save the Healer and the healed; the one serene in the consciousness of power, the other strong in faith. His walk was truly ‘by faith,’ and he went ‘glorifying God.’ (<span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="lu+5:25">Luke 5:25</span>.)
