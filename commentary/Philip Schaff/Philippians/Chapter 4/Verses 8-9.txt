<p><em> Precepts for the guidance of the Christian Life</em>, <em> </em> 8,9.
	</p>
<p>With much emphasis of language, St. Paul urges on the Philippians that they be mindful of the various virtues which mark the Christian character, and carry out all that he has taught them either by word or example, and thus shall the God of peace dwell with them.
	</p>
