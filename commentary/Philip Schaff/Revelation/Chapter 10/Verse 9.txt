<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="re+10:9">Revelation 10:9</span>. The command is obeyed, and the further instruction is given, <strong> Take it, and eat it up.</strong> For a similar action comp. <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="eze+3:1">Ezekiel 3:1</span>. The eating of the roll can hardly be anything else than a symbol of the complete assimilation of its contents.
