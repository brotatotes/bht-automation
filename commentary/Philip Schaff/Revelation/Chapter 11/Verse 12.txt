<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="re+11:12">Revelation 11:12</span>. Nor that alone. They hear a voice summoning them to ascend into heaven in the presence of the same beholders, and they obey. <strong> They went up into heaven in the cloud,</strong> not in the clouds, or simply in a cloud; but in a distinct and definite cloud, that of the angel of chap. <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="re+10:1">Revelation 10:1</span>, or of Christ in chap. <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="re+14:14-16">Revelation 14:14-16</span>; and their triumph was witnessed by those who killed them.
	</p>
