<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="re+13:5">Revelation 13:5</span>. <strong> And there was given him a month speaking great things and blasphemies.</strong> This is the first of three things spoken of (<span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="re+13:5-7">Revelation 13:5-7</span>) as ‘given,’ <em> i.e </em> given by God to whom in its utmost might the beast is subject. The description is taken from <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="da+8:8">Daniel 8:8</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="da+8:20">Daniel 8:20</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="da+8:25">Daniel 8:25</span>, where similar language is used of the ‘Little Horn.’ The second thing ‘given’ is a <strong> uthority to work forty and two months.</strong> For the time here specified see on chap. <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="re+11:2">Revelation 11:2</span>.
	</p>
