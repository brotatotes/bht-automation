<p>The twelfth chapter has set before us the first great enemy of the Church. This chapter introduces us to other two by means of whom the devil or Satan carries on his warfare against the truth. The first is described in <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="re+13:1-10">Revelation 13:1-10</span>; the second in <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="re+13:11-17">Revelation 13:11-17</span>.
	</p>
