<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="re+16:7">Revelation 16:7</span>. Not only is an acknowledgment of the righteousness of God’s judgment given by the ‘waters;’ the martyred saints also respond. The <strong> altar</strong> (not as in the Authorised Version ‘another out of the altar’) speaks. It is the altar of chap. <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="re+6:9">Revelation 6:9</span> beneath which is the blood, that is the lives, of the saints. They who have suffered own that the <strong> judgments</strong> of the Almighty upon those who persecuted them even unto death <strong> are true and righteous,</strong> conformable to the realities of things and to the demands of perfect righteousness.
	</p>
