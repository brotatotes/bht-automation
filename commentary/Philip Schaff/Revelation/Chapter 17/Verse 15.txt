<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="re+17:15">Revelation 17:15</span>. The fourfold designation of those who constitute the <strong> waters</strong> spoken of in this verse is a clear proof that the harlot exercises her sway over the <em> whole world, </em> in travesty of Him ‘who sitteth upon the flood,’ who ‘sitteth King for ever’ (<span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ps+29:10">Psalms 29:10</span>).
	</p>
