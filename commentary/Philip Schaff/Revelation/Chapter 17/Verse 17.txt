<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="re+17:17">Revelation 17:17</span>. <strong> For God gave it into their hearts to do his mind, and to be of one mind, and to give their kingdom unto the beast.</strong> This ‘giving’ of authority to the beast we have already met with in <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="re+17:13">Revelation 17:13</span>; and in <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="re+17:12">Revelation 17:12</span> it has been intimated that the ten kings held their authority from God. Whatever, therefore, they had done in persecuting the saints had been accomplishing Goo’s purpose (comp. <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ac+2:23">Acts 2:23</span>).
	</p>
<p><strong> Until the words of God should be accomplished;</strong> until all His purposes should be fulfilled.
