<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="re+19:20">Revelation 19:20</span>. The description given in this verse can leave no doubt that we have here the two enemies of chap. 13, the beast and the lamb-like beast with the two horns. The ‘lake of fire’ is again mentioned in chaps. <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="re+20:10">Revelation 20:10</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="re+20:14">Revelation 20:14</span>, and <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="re+21:8">Revelation 21:8</span>.
