<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="re+22:10">Revelation 22:10</span>. <strong> And he saith unto me, Seal not up the words of the prophecy of this book, for the time is at hand.</strong> At chap. <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="re+1:19">Revelation 1:19</span> St. John had been commanded to ‘write,’ now he is commanded to proclaim what he had written. The Apocalypse was not to be a sealed and hidden book like that of Daniel (chaps. <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="da+8:26">Daniel 8:26</span>, <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="da+12:4">Daniel 12:4</span>). It was to be opened for the instruction and the guidance of the Church. There was not a moment to be lost. The Lord was at hand. Let all who believed that truth prepare themselves for His coming.
	</p>
