<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="re+3:11">Revelation 3:11</span>. <strong> I come quickly.</strong> Comp. chap. <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="re+2:25">Revelation 2:25</span> and <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="re+3:3">Revelation 3:3</span>, in both of which the general, rather than any special, coming of the Lord had been spoken of. He was to ‘come’ in the first, to ‘come as a thief’ in the second; now He ‘comes quickly.’
	</p>
<p><strong> That no one take thy crown,</strong> that is, take it away (comp. chap. <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="re+6:4">Revelation 6:4</span>), deprive the church of it. The crown is the crown of future glory, the kingly crown (comp. on chap, <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="re+2:10">Revelation 2:10</span>).
	</p>
