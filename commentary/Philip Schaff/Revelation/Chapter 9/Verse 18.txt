<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="re+9:18">Revelation 9:18</span>. Before the description of the horses is continued, the effect of the three plagues that issue from their mouths is noticed. <strong> By these three plagues was the third part of men killed, </strong> the third part, that is, of men over the whole earth, and whatever the division of the human race to which they belonged.
