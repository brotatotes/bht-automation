<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ro+1:16">Romans 1:16</span>. For I am not ashamed. This gives the reason for his being ready to preach at Rome also (<span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ro+1:15">Romans 1:15</span>), and forms an easy transition to the statement which follows. Rome, the metropolis of the heathen world, with all its pride of power, presented a field, where, if anywhere, one might be tempted to be ashamed of the gospel which centred in a Person whom Roman soldiers had crucified. Comp. <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ga+6:14">Galatians 6:14</span>, and chap. <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ro+5:2">Romans 5:2</span>.
	</p>
