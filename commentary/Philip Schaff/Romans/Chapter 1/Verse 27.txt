<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ro+1:27">Romans 1:27</span>. <strong> The men</strong>; lit., ‘males.’ The vice of sodomy was very prevalent in the ancient world. The description here is more intense, corresponding with the prevalence and intensity of the immorality.
	</p>
<p><strong> Receiving in themselves</strong>; in their own persons. ‘The unseemliness’ points to something well known.
	</p>
<p><strong> That recompense of their error. </strong> The unnatural lusts and vices were the recompense, the due punishment, of their ‘error,’ namely, their departure from God into idolatry.
	</p>
