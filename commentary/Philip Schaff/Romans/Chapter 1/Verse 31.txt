<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ro+1:31">Romans 1:31</span>. In this verse adjectives take the place of the substantives previously used. The long catalogue is thus varied.
	</p>
<p><strong> Without understanding</strong>; the same word as ‘senseless’ (E. V. ‘foolish’), <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ro+1:21">Romans 1:21</span>.
	</p>
<p><strong> Covenant breakers. </strong> In the original there is another play upon the sound of the words. (The best authorities omit ‘implacable.’)
	</p>
<p><strong> Unmerciful. </strong> This concludes the list, marking the absence of the least principle of moral action.
	</p>
