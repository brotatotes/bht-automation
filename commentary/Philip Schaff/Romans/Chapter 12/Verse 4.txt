<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ro+12:4">Romans 12:4</span>. <strong> For as we have many members in one body. </strong> The parallel here set forth (<span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ro+12:4-5">Romans 12:4-5</span>) is more fully carried out in <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="1co+12:12">1 Corinthians 12:12</span>, etc. In Ephesians (throughout) the unity is emphasized, here the variety. This variety is introduced as an explanation of the variety in the measure of faith, and hence as a motive for the humility enjoined.
	</p>
<p><strong> Have not the same office, </strong> or, ‘activity,’ <em> e. g.</em>, eyes, ears, hands, etc.
