<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ro+13:5">Romans 13:5</span>. <strong> Wherefore ye must needs,</strong> etc. In accordance with what has been stated (‘wherefore’), the necessity of obedience rests, not only on grounds of prudence, <strong> because of the wrath,</strong> but on moral grounds, <strong> but also for conscience’ sake</strong>; obedience is a religious duty.
	</p>
