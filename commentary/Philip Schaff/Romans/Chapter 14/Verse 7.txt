<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ro+14:7">Romans 14:7</span>. <strong> For none of us liveth unto himself, </strong> etc. The Christian’s eating or not eating is unto the Lord, because the sum of his earthly existence, living and dying, is not ‘unto himself;’ and this is true in the case of all. This is the negative side; the positive follows.
	</p>
