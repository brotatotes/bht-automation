<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ro+15:21">Romans 15:21</span>. <strong> But, </strong> preaching the gospel in this way, not where others had preached, but, <strong> as it is written </strong> (according to this rule of Scripture), <strong> They to whom no tidings of him came, shall see</strong>; <strong> And they, </strong> etc. From <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="isa+52:15">Isaiah 52:15</span>, following the LXX., which adds ‘of him’ (comp. the E. V., which renders the Hebrew accurately). The prophecy refers to ‘kings,’ but is properly applied to nations whom they represent; the wide extension of the Messiah’s kingdom being the main thought.
	</p>
