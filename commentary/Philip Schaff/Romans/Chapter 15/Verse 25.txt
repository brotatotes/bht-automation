<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ro+15:25">Romans 15:25</span>. <strong> But now. </strong> Partly resumptive of <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ro+15:23">Romans 15:23</span>, since it returns to his present circumstances, but introducing a new thought in contrast with the hope expressed in <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ro+15:24">Romans 15:24</span>.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> I am journeying unto Jerusalem </strong> (on the point of doing so) <strong> ministering unto the saints. </strong> How is afterwards explained. The present participle indicates that the journey is part of the ministering. On this service, see <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="2co+9:1-2">2 Corinthians 9:1-2</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ac+24:27">Acts 24:27</span>.
	</p>
