<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ro+15:29">Romans 15:29</span>. <strong> And I know that, </strong> etc. The Apostle’s humility did not prevent him from knowing this and writing of it. More confidence of <em> this kind </em> would promote humility in the preacher.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.06em;"><strong> In the fulness of the blessing of Christ </strong> ‘Of the gospel’ is a gloss, to be rejected on decisive authority. Christ’s blessing in abundance he knew would attend him at Rome.
	</p>
