<p><strong> III. CONCLUDING PORTION OF THE EPISTLE.
	</strong></p>
<p>This part of the Epistle may be divided into four sections, (1) Personal explanations, similar to those in chap. <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ro+1:8-15">Romans 1:8-15</span> (chap. <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ro+15:14-33">Romans 15:14-33</span>). (2.) Greetings to different persons at Rome (chap. <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ro+16:1-16">Romans 16:1-16</span>). (3.) Closing exhortation, with greetings, from various persons (chap. <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ro+16:17-24">Romans 16:17-24</span>). (4.) Concluding Doxology (chap. <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ro+16:25-27">Romans 16:25-27</span>).
	</p>
