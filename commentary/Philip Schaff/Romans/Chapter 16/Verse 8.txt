<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ro+16:8">Romans 16:8</span>. <strong> Ampliatus;</strong> so the weightier authorities; ‘Amplias’ is an abbreviated form. A common name in itself, it occurs several times in connection with the imperial household (Lightfoot). The same is true of nearly every name in the rest of the section.
