<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ro+2:10">Romans 2:10</span>. <strong> Glory and honor and peace. </strong> (Comp. <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ro+2:7">Romans 2:7</span>.) ‘Peace’ is here used in its fullest sense; in the Old Testament it includes ‘peace, plenty, and prosperity,’ but with more of a temporal reference than in its New Testament use. Comp. chap. <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ro+8:6">Romans 8:6</span>, and similar passages.
