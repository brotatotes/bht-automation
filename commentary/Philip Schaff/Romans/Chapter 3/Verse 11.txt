<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ro+3:11">Romans 3:11</span>. <strong> There is none that understandeth, </strong> etc. Latter half of <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ps+14:2">Psalms 14:2</span>; ‘so quoted that the <em> negative </em> sense which results indirectly from the text in the Hebrew and LXX. is expressed by Paul directly’(Meyer). As regards the meaning, both parts of the verse refer to impiety; sin being represented as folly, and then as failure to seek God.
