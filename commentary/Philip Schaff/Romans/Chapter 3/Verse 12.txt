<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ro+3:12">Romans 3:12</span>. <strong> They have all turned aside, </strong> etc. Accurately quoted from <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ps+14:3">Psalms 14:3</span> (LXX.).
	</p>
<p><strong> Unprofitable. </strong> More literally, ‘useless,’ ‘worthless.
	</p>
<p><strong> Not even one. ‘</strong> There is not even unto one.’ The same form occurs in <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ro+3:1">Romans 3:1</span> of the Psalm, from which <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ro+3:10">Romans 3:10</span> here varies.
	</p>
