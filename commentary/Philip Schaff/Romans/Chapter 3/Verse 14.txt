<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ro+3:14">Romans 3:14</span>. <strong> Whose month</strong>, etc. (From <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ps+10:7">Psalms 10:7</span>.) The variations from the LXX. are slight. The Hebrew is: ‘His mouth is full of oaths, and deceit, and fraud.’ ‘Deceit,’ which occurs in the original, was omitted, because already mentioned (<span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ro+3:3">Romans 3:3</span>).
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.12em;"><strong> Full of cursing and bitterness. </strong> The bitterness which prompts the speech is the cause of the cursing.
	</p>
