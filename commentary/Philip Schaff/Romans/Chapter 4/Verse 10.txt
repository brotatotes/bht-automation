<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ro+4:10">Romans 4:10</span>. <strong> How then was it reckoned? </strong> Not, what was the mode in which it was reckoned, but, ‘how was he situated when this took place?’ The rest of the verse makes this clear.
	</p>
<p style="margin-left:0.06em;"><strong> Not in circumcision, but in uncircumcision. </strong> The ‘reckoning’ took place (<span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ge+15:6">Genesis 15:6</span>) at least fourteen years before the circumcision of Abraham (<span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ge+17:25">Genesis 17:25</span>); consequently the latter was the Divine ratification of grace already received, not the effective cause or condition of the bestowal of grace.
	</p>
