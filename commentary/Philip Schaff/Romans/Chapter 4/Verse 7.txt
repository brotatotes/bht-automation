<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ro+4:7">Romans 4:7</span>. <strong> Blessed are they, </strong> etc. The quotation is made exactly from the LXX.
	</p>
<p><strong> Whoso sins are covered. </strong> The idea of the first clause is repeated under another figure, according to the parallelism of Hebrew poetry. Their sins are hid by God Himself, which is the same as ‘forgiven,’ ‘not reckoned.’
	</p>
