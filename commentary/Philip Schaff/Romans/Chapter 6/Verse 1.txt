<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ro+6:1">Romans 6:1</span>. <strong> What shall we say then?</strong> ‘Then,’ in view of chap. <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ro+5:20-21">Romans 5:20-21</span>. Comp. the similar phrase in chap. <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ro+4:1">Romans 4:1</span>.
	</p>
<p><strong> Shall we continue in sin? </strong> The form of the question in the original indicates that this is the statement of a point to be discussed, or rather of a wrong inference that might be drawn from the abounding of grace. This wrong inference is a standing objection to the gospel, urged by those who have not felt its power. </p>
