<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ro+6:18">Romans 6:18</span>. <strong> And being made free, </strong> etc. This is not the conclusion from what precedes, but a continuation of <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ro+6:17">Romans 6:17</span>. The single act of deliverance and transformation is referred to.
	</p>
<p><strong> Became servants, </strong> <em> i.e.</em>, ‘bondmen,’ personally and wholly belonging to this service. This bondage is real freedom. Compare the opposite thought in <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ro+6:20">Romans 6:20</span>. </p>
