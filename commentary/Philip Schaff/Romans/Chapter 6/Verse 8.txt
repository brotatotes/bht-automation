<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ro+6:8">Romans 6:8</span>. <strong> Vow if we died with Christ. </strong> That this is the fact has been already stated, forming the underlying thought of <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ro+6:3-6">Romans 6:3-6</span>.
	</p>
<p><strong> We believe, </strong> etc. The argument is plain, but the exact force of <strong> live with him </strong> is doubtful. It seems best to accept a primary reference to sanctification, to ethical fellowship with Christ. To this some add the thought of eternal life, others apply the phrase to this exclusively.
	</p>
