<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ro+8:25">Romans 8:25</span>. <strong> With patience we wait</strong> <strong> for it</strong> <em> .</em> Literally, ‘through,’ but it here indicates a characteristic of the waiting.’ Patience,’ as usual, suggests the notion of enduring constancy. Because the Christian hopes for a glory yet to be revealed (<span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ro+8:18">Romans 8:18</span>), he awaits it perseveringly, which even the creation patiently expects; his patient endurance of the present sufferings has one strong motive in this hope.</p>
