<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="ro+9:2">Romans 9:2</span>. <strong> Great grief and continual sorrow</strong>. The cause of this grief obviously is the unbelief of his countrymen, their practical exclusion from the Messianic salvation. This feeling was respecting those who had for years persecuted him with relentless hatred, and who, shortly after this time occasioned him a long imprisonment, thus becoming the immediate cause of his martyrdom.</p>
