<p><em> Character of both the Teachers and the People of Crete, to show the danger to which that Church was exposed, and from which the new Presbyters were to rescue it, </em> 10-16.
	</p>
<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="tit+1:10">Titus 1:10</span>. <strong> Unruly</strong>; for Jews of that age, of whom many inhabited Crete, were noted for seditious tendencies.
	</p>
<p><strong> Vain talkers </strong> (<span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="1ti+1:6">1 Timothy 1:6</span>), or chatterers, and <strong> deceivers, </strong> or misleaders of opinion, are two leading substantives, to which the adjective ‘unruly’ applies.
	</p>
