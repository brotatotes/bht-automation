<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="tit+1:11">Titus 1:11</span>. <strong> Stopped;</strong> literally, muzzled; best done by exposing them as persons who ‘overturn entire houses,’ <em> i.e. </em> families, through anarchic doctrine subversive of domestic authority: probably lax theories of Christian freedom in reference to wedlock and the duties of children and of slaves. Their motive was ‘base gain’ (better than ‘filthy lucre’) <em> </em> to be won from their perverts.
	</p>
