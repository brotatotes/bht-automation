<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="tit+2:3">Titus 2:3</span>. <strong> Likewise,</strong> for the same moral propriety applies here, modified only by sex.
	</p>
<p><strong> Behaviour, </strong> or deportment, a wide term, covering ‘walk, gesture, countenance, speech, silence’ (Jerome).
	</p>
<p><strong> Becometh holiness</strong> (cf. <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="1ti+2:10">1 Timothy 2:10</span>; <span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="eph+5:3">Ephesians 5:3</span>), befitting the solemnity of a consecrated person. Women too are spiritual priests in Christ.
	</p>
<p><strong> False accusers;</strong> frequent failing of this class; the word is literally ‘devils.’ It is the diabolic characteristic to slander the good.
	</p>
