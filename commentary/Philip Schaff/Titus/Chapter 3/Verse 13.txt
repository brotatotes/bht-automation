<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="tit+3:13">Titus 3:13</span>. Hospitality to Christian travellers was an urgent virtue in the early Church. Of Zenas nothing is known. Tradition calls him one of the seventy, and later a bishop at Diospolis.
	</p>
<p><strong> Lawyer</strong> may mean either a Jewish scribe or a jurisconsult of the Roman Empire.
	</p>
<p><strong> Bring on their journey</strong>, etc., means to equip them for further travel with everything needful.
	</p>
