<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="tit+3:14">Titus 3:14</span>. Not Titus alone, but ( <strong> ours</strong>, a unique phrase) the Cretan brethren were to share in this fitting out of the two travellers. It would be an exercise in Christian beneficence and a lesson to ‘practise’ similar ‘good works,’ as often as ‘necessary wants’ (not ‘uses’) arose.
