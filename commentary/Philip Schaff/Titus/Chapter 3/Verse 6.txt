<p><span class="scriptRef" com="scn" lang="eng" ref="tit+3:6">Titus 3:6</span>. <strong> Which</strong>, <em> i.e. </em> the Holy Ghost.
	</p>
<p><strong> Shed, </strong> or poured out (allusion to act of baptizing), including both Pentecost and all the subsequent effusion of the Spirit on the successive members of the Church.
	</p>
<p><strong> Abundantly</strong>; literally, ‘richly.’
	</p>
<p><strong> Through</strong> the mediation and merits of Christ. Note the part assigned to the Three Persons: Father, the fountain who pours; Son, the channel through whom; Spirit, the poured out Water of Life.
	</p>
